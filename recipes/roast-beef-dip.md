## Ingredients

- 1 (13 ounce) can roast beef, drained
- 1 ounce dry onion soup mix
- 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
- 1 (8 ounce) container sour cream

## Instructions

1. Place roast beef in a medium-size mixing bowl.
2. Blend in soup mix, cream cheese and sour cream.
3. Cover and refrigerate before serving.
