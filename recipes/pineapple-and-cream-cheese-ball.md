## Ingredients

- 2 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened
- 1 (16 ounce) can crushed pineapple, well drained
- 1 cup walnut pieces
- 1 bunch chopped fresh parsley

## Instructions

1. Mix cream cheese and pineapple together in a bowl; form into a ball.
2. Roll ball in walnut pieces until ball is completely coated.
3. Set ball on a piece of plastic wrap.
4. Wrap plastic wrap tightly around ball and place in a small bowl.
5. Refrigerate ball to allow flavors to blend, at least 30 minutes.
6. Serve cheese ball on a bed of chopped parsley.
