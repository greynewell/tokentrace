## Ingredients

- ¾ cup boiling water
- 1  chai tea bag
- 1 ½ teaspoons honey
- 1 teaspoon white sugar
- ¾ cup milk

## Instructions

1. Pour boiling water over chai tea bag in a mug; let steep 4 to 6 minutes. Remove and discard tea bag.
2. Stir honey and sugar into tea to dissolve. Stir milk through tea to serve.
