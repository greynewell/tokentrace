## Ingredients

- 6 cups red and yellow cherry tomatoes, some halved
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- ⅔ cup buttermilk
- ¼ cup sour cream
- 3 tablespoons chopped fresh basil
- 1  shallot, minced
- 2 cloves garlic, minced

## Instructions

1. Put tomatoes into a large serving bowl; season with salt and pepper and toss.
2. Beat buttermilk, sour cream, basil, shallot, and garlic together with a whisk in a separate bowl; season with salt and pepper.
3. Drizzle dressing over tomatoes and toss to coat.
