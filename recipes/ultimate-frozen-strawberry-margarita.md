## Ingredients

- 6 fluid ounces tequila
- 2 fluid ounces triple sec
- 8 ounces frozen sliced strawberries in syrup
- 4 fluid ounces frozen limeade concentrate

## Instructions

1. Fill a blender with ice cubes; pulse until ice is crushed.
2. Pour in tequila and triple sec, then add strawberries and limeade concentrate. Blend until smooth, about 30 seconds.
3. Pour into four margarita glasses and serve immediately.
