## Ingredients

- 4 slices bacon, cooked and chopped
- 4  eggs
- ½ cup salsa (such as Pace®)
- ½ cup almond flour

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Grease 8 muffin cups with cooking spray.
3. Blend bacon, eggs, salsa, and almond flour together in a blender on medium speed until thoroughly combined, about 30 seconds.
4. Pour mixture into prepared muffin cups.
5. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center of a muffin comes out clean, about 30 minutes.
6. Let cool 10 minutes before serving.
