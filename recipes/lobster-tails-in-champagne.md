## Ingredients

- 2 (8 ounce) frozen lobster tails, rinsed
- ⅔ cup Champagne
- 2  green onions, minced
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- ¼ cup heavy whipping cream
- ¼ stick unsalted butter, cut into tablespoons

## Instructions

1. Combine lobster tails, champagne, green onions, and salt in a skillet; bring to a boil.
2. Reduce heat to medium-low, cover the skillet, and simmer until lobster is cooked through, about 15 minutes.
3. Transfer lobster to a plate.
4. Stir cream in champagne sauce in skillet.
5. Bring to a boil and cook until sauce is reduced to about 1/3 cup, 5 to 10 minutes.
6. Whisk butter into champagne sauce, 1 tablespoon at a time, until sauce is smooth.
7. Cover the skillet and remove from heat.
8. Remove lobsters tails from shells and slice into medallions.
9. Spoon champagne sauce over lobster medallions.
