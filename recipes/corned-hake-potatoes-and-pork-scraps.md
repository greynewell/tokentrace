## Ingredients

- 3 pounds skinned hake fillets
- 3 tablespoons salt
- 3 tablespoons oil, or to taste
- 2 tablespoons white vinegar, or to taste
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- 1 large onion, thinly sliced, or to taste
- 16  boiling potatoes
- salt to taste
- ½ pound salt pork, thinly sliced

## Instructions

1. Place hake in a deep dish and sprinkle generously with 3 tablespoons salt.
2. Cover with plastic wrap and refrigerate until salt is absorbed, 2 to 3 days.
3. Whisk oil, vinegar, salt, and black pepper together in a bowl.
4. Add onion; mix well to combine.
5. Let marinate in the refrigerator until serving.
6. Rinse hake with cold water and place in a large pot.
7. Pour in water to nearly cover; bring to a boil.
8. Reduce heat and simmer until easily flaked with a fork, about 20 minutes.
9. Remove from water and transfer to serving plates.
10. Place potatoes in a large pot and cover with salted water; bring to a boil.
11. Reduce heat to medium-low and simmer until tender, about 20 minutes.
12. Drain and divide among serving plates.
13. Heat a skillet over medium heat; fry salt pork until lightly browned and crisp, about 5 minutes.
14. Mash hake and potatoes together with a fork.
15. Mix in slices of crispy salt pork and spoonfuls of fat from the skillet.
16. Top with a few sliced onions and serve.
