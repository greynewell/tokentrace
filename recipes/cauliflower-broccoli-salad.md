## Ingredients

- 2 heads broccoli, cut into florets
- 1 head cauliflower, cut into florets
- 1 (20 ounce) package frozen peas, thawed
- 2  carrots, thinly sliced
- 1 (4 ounce) can sliced black olives
- 1 ½ cups creamy salad dressing (such as Miracle Whip®)
- 1 cup sour cream
- 2 teaspoons garlic powder

## Instructions

1. Place broccoli, cauliflower, peas, carrots, and olives in a large bowl.
2. Stir creamy salad dressing, sour cream, and garlic powder together in a bowl until well blended.
3. Stir dressing a spoonful at a time into the broccoli mixture until vegetables are coated.
