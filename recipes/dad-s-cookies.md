## Ingredients

- 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
- 1 teaspoon baking soda
- ¾ teaspoon salt
- 1 cup white sugar
- 1 cup brown sugar
- 1 ½ cups rolled oats
- ½ cup flaked coconut
- ¾ cup vegetable oil
- 2  eggs
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease cookie sheets.
2. Stir together flour, baking soda, salt, white sugar, brown sugar, oats, and coconut in a large bowl. Make a well in the center and pour in oil, eggs, and vanilla. Mix well using your hands or a wooden spoon.
3. Roll dough into walnut-sized balls and place 2 inches apart onto the prepared cookie sheets. Flatten slightly with a fork.
4. Bake in the preheated oven until very light brown and chewy, 10 to 12 minutes. Let cool briefly on the baking sheets before removing to wire racks to cool completely.
