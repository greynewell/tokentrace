## Ingredients

- 2 bunches kale, stems removed and leaves finely shredded
- 1 (15 ounce) can crushed pineapple, undrained
- ⅓ cup lemon juice
- ¼ cup sunflower seed oil
- ½ cup sunflower seeds, or to taste  (Optional)

## Instructions

1. Place shredded kale in a large bowl.
2. Mix pineapple, lemon juice, and sunflower oil together in a bowl; pour over kale and toss.
3. Sprinkle sunflower seeds over salad.
