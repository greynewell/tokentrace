## Ingredients

- 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese
- 1 ½ teaspoons paprika
- 10 dashes hot pepper sauce
- ¼ cup beer

## Instructions

1. In a medium saucepan over low heat, melt cream cheese.
2. Stir in paprika; mixture should be pink.
3. Stir in hot sauce and beer.
4. Serve warm or cold.
