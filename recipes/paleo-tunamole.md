## Ingredients

- 1  avocado, mashed
- ½  lime, juiced
- 1 (5 ounce) can tuna, drained
- ¼ cup chopped tomatoes
- 2 teaspoons capers
- 2 tablespoons sliced scallion
- salt and ground black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Stir mashed avocado with lime juice in a bowl until smooth.
2. Fold tuna, tomatoes, capers, and scallion through the avocado; season with salt and pepper.
