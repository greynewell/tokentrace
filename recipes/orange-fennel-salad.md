## Ingredients

- 1 (10 ounce) bag baby spinach leaves
- 2  oranges, peeled and sectioned
- ½ large fennel  bulb, thinly sliced
- 1 teaspoon chopped fennel fronds
- ¼ cup toasted sliced almonds
- 1 tablespoon chopped fresh mint
- 3 tablespoons olive oil
- 2 tablespoons orange juice
- ½ teaspoon ground cumin
- ½ teaspoon onion powder
- salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Place the spinach, orange sections, sliced fennel, fennel fronds, almonds, and mint into a large mixing bowl; set aside.
2. Whisk together the olive oil, orange juice, cumin, onion powder, salt, and pepper in a small bowl.
3. Pour the dressing over the salad, and toss gently to dress.
4. Serve immediately.
