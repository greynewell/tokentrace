## Ingredients

- 1  lemon, sliced
- 2 sprigs lemon thyme
- 1 (4 pound) striped bass, cleaned and scaled
- 4 pounds kosher salt
- 2  egg whites

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 475 degrees F (245 degrees C).
2. Insert lemon and lemon thyme into the cavity of the fish.
3. Mix salt and egg whites together in a large bowl.
4. Spread 1/2 the salt mixture on a large baking sheet.
5. Lay fish on top.
6. Pour remaining salt mixture on top.
7. Pack down tightly so fish is sealed in salt.
8. Reduce oven temperature to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
9. Bake fish in the preheated oven until an instant-read thermometer inserted through the salt and into the fish reads 120 degrees F (49 degrees C), 15 to 20 minutes.
10. Cool fish for 2 minutes.
11. Crack the salt coffin with a wooden spoon.
12. Peel away the salt and discard.
13. Transfer fish to a serving dish using 2 spatulas.
