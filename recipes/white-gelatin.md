## Ingredients

- 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
- ½ cup white sugar
- 1 (.25 ounce) envelope unflavored gelatin
- ⅓ cup boiling water
- 1 cup milk
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 1 ½ cups frozen whipped topping, thawed

## Instructions

1. In a medium bowl, beat cream cheese and sugar until smooth and well blended.
2. In a separate bowl, dissolve gelatin in boiling water; cool slightly, then stir into cream cheese mixture along with milk, lemon juice, and vanilla. Fold in whipped topping. Pour into a mold if desired, and refrigerate until set, about 3 hours.
