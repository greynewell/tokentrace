## Ingredients

- 6 pounds butternut squash - peeled, seeded and sliced
- 1  egg, beaten
- ½ cup milk
- ½ cup all-purpose flour
- ½ cup cornmeal
- 1 pinch salt
- 1 pinch ground black pepper
- 1 pinch garlic salt
- 1 cup oil for frying

## Instructions

1. Whisk milk and egg together in a small bowl. Combine flour, cornmeal, garlic salt, pepper, and salt together in a separate bowl.
2. Heat oil in a deep skillet over medium-high heat.
3. Dip each squash slice into egg mixture, then dredge in flour mixture to coat.
4. Fry coated squash slices in hot oil, a few at a time, until golden brown on both sides.
