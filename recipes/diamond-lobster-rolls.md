## Ingredients

- 6  hot dog buns, split
- 1 tablespoon soft butter
- 2 cups shredded romaine lettuce
- 2 tablespoons mayonnaise
- 1 teaspoon lime juice
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- ½ teaspoon chopped fresh basil
- 1 dash hot sauce (such as Tabasco®)
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- ½ cup diced red onion
- ¼ cup diced red bell pepper
- ¼ cup diced celery
- 2 pounds cooked and cubed lobster meat
- 6 thin slices  tomato  (Optional)
- ½ cup shredded Cheddar cheese  (Optional)
- ¼ teaspoon chopped fresh dill

## Instructions

1. Spread butter lightly over the insides of the buns.
2. Transfer to a toaster oven; toast until light golden brown, about 5 minutes.
3. Line toasted buns with romaine lettuce.
4. Stir mayonnaise, lime juice, garlic, basil, hot sauce, salt, and pepper together in a bowl.
5. Add red onion, red bell pepper, and celery.
6. Fold in lobster gently.
7. Stuff lobster filling into buns.
8. Top with tomato slices and a light sprinkle of Cheddar cheese and dill.
