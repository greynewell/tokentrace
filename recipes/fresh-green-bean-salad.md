## Ingredients

- 2 pounds fresh green beans, trimmed
- 1 bunch radishes, chopped
- 3  cucumbers, chopped
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 1 tablespoon vinegar
- salt and ground black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Bring a pot of lightly salted water to a boil; cook beans in boiling water until bright green and slightly tender, 3 to 5 minutes.
2. Drain green beans and plunge into ice water until cold; drain.
3. Toss green beans, radishes, and cucumbers together in a large bowl.
4. Whisk olive oil and vinegar together in a small bowl until the oil emulsifies into the vinegar; season with salt and pepper.
5. Drizzle dressing over salad and toss to coat.
