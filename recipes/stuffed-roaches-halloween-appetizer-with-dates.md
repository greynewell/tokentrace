## Ingredients

- 1 (8 ounce) package fat-free cream cheese, softened
- ¼ cup finely chopped walnuts
- 2 (8 ounce) packages whole pitted dates

## Instructions

1. Mix cream cheese and walnuts together in a small bowl.
2. Slice one side of each date lengthwise using a small sharp knife, creating an opening.
3. Carefully spoon cream cheese mixture into the middle of each date; pinch the dates closed.
4. Stack them on a plate with cut sides down.
