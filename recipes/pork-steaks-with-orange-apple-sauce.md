## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons butter, or more to taste
- 1  Gala apple - peeled, cored, and thinly sliced
- ¼ cup orange juice
- 2 tablespoons jellied cranberry sauce  (Optional)
- 2  whole cloves, or more to taste
- 12 ounces pork blade steak, halved

## Instructions

1. Melt butter in a large skillet over medium heat.
2. Cook and stir apple, orange juice, cranberry sauce, and cloves in the melted butter until apples soften, about 2 minutes.
3. Place pork in the skillet with apples.
4. Cover skillet and cook until pork is browned, about 5 minutes per side.
5. Remove cover and cook until pork is no longer pink in the center, about 6 more minutes.
6. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read 160 degrees F (71 degrees C).
