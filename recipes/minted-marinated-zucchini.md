## Ingredients

- ¼ cup olive oil
- 3 large zucchini, thinly sliced
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 cups fresh mint leaves, finely chopped
- ⅓ cup distilled white vinegar
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ground black pepper to taste
- 1 tablespoon olive oil, for drizzling

## Instructions

1. Heat 1/4 cup of olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
2. Add zucchini slices and garlic; cook and stir until starting to brown but you want the squash to stay firm and not get mushy, 3 to 4 minutes.
3. Remove from the heat and mix in the vinegar, mint, salt and pepper.
4. Stir in the remaining olive oil.
5. Spoon into a jar and store covered in the refrigerator.
