## Ingredients

- ½ cup brown sugar
- ⅓ cup water
- ⅓ cup walnuts
- ⅓ cup pecans
- 1 (10 ounce) bag mixed salad greens
- 2 cups strawberries, halved
- 1 cup fresh blackberries
- ¾ cup crumbled blue cheese
- ½ cup dried cranberries
- 4  dates, pitted and chopped
- ¼ cup raspberry vinaigrette dressing, or more to taste

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
2. Line a baking sheet with aluminum foil.
3. Whisk brown sugar and water together in a small saucepan; bring to a boil and cook until reduced to a caramel syrup, 5 to 10 minutes.
4. Arrange walnuts and pecans on the prepared baking sheet.
5. Toast nuts in the preheated oven until fragrant, about 5 minutes.
6. Pour caramel syrup over nuts; toss to coat.
7. Bake until nuts are lightly browned and syrup has thickened, 8 to 10 more minutes.
8. Let cool on baking sheet.
9. Mix salad greens, strawberries, blackberries, blue cheese, cranberries, dates, and vinaigrette dressing; toss to coat.
10. Sprinkle candied nuts over salad.
