## Ingredients

- 24 each Original Snack Factory® Pretzel Crisps®
- 8 ounces thinly sliced deli roast beef, cut or torn into bite-size pieces
- 8 ounces sliced smoked mozzarella cheese, cut into 24 pieces
- ¼ cup thinly sliced red onion
- 2 tablespoons prepared horseradish
- 1 tablespoon chopped fresh chives

## Instructions

1. Arrange Pretzel Crisps® onto a large tray.
2. Top with roast beef and a piece of mozzarella cheese.
3. Add a slice or 2 of red onion.
4. Put a dollop (about 1/4 teaspoon) of horseradish on the onion.
5. Garnish with chives.
