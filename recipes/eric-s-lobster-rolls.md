## Ingredients

- 2 stalks celery, finely chopped
- 2  green onions, finely chopped
- 1  lemon juice, or more to taste
- 2 tablespoons seafood seasoning (such as Old Bay®)
- 2 tablespoons blackening seasoning
- water to cover
- 2  lobster tails
- ½ pound peeled, deveined raw shrimp
- 2 cups seafood salad
- 4 ounces Cajun crab dip
- 6  small croissants

## Instructions

1. Trim celery and green onion ends; place in a pot.
2. Add 2 tablespoons lemon juice, seafood seasoning, and blackening seasoning.
3. Cover with water; bring to a boil.
4. Stir lobster tails and shrimp into the boiling water.
5. Cook until they float to the top, about 7 minutes.
6. Drain.
7. Remove lobster meat from the shell; cut into bite-sized pieces.
8. Place lobster meat and shrimp in a bowl.
9. Refrigerate until cool.
10. Transfer celery and green onions to a bowl.
11. Add seafood salad and crab dip.
12. Drizzle remaining lemon juice on top.
13. Mix in cooled lobster meat and shrimp.
14. Season with a little more lemon juice.
15. Split and stuff croissants with lobster and shrimp salad.
