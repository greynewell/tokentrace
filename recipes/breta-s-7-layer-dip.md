## Ingredients

- 1 (14 ounce) can refried beans
- 1 (9 ounce) can hot bean dip (such as Fritos® Brand)
- 6  avocados - peeled, pitted, and mashed
- 1 cup salsa
- 1 ½ cups sour cream
- 1 cup finely shredded Cheddar cheese
- 1 (4 ounce) can chopped black olives, drained
- 2  tomatoes, diced
- 4  green onions, chopped

## Instructions

1. Mix refried beans with hot bean dip in a bowl; spread the bean mixture in an even layer over a pizza pan.
2. Mix avocados with salsa in a bowl; spread mixture over the bean mixture.
3. Place dollops of sour cream over avocado layer and spread into an even layer; top with shredded Cheddar cheese.
4. Sprinkle black olives, diced tomatoes, and green onions over the dip.
5. Chill at least 1 hour before serving.
