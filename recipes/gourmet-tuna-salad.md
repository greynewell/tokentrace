## Ingredients

- 1 (12 ounce) can albacore tuna in water, drained and flaked
- 2  green onions, chopped
- 1 stalk celery, diced
- ¼ cup pimento-stuffed green olives, chopped
- 2 tablespoons capers, chopped
- ¼ cup blanched slivered almonds
- 1 dash Worcestershire sauce
- ½ cup mayonnaise
- ¼ cup sour cream

## Instructions

1. Combine tuna, green onions, celery, green olives, almonds, and capers in a mixing bowl.
2. Whisk together mayonnaise, sour cream, and Worcestershire sauce in a small bowl.
3. Blend together dressing and tuna mixture. Serve on a bed of lettuce or with croissant as a tuna salad sandwich.
