## Ingredients

- 1 (18.25 ounce) package yellow cake mix
- 1 tablespoon ground cinnamon
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 2  Granny Smith apples - peeled, cored and finely diced

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Prepare cake mix according to package directions for a 10-inch tube pan. Stir in the cinnamon and vanilla extract, then fold in the chopped apples.
3. Pour batter into prepared pan. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center of the cake comes out clean, about 40 minutes. Allow to cool.
