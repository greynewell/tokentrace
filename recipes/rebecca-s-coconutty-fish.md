## Ingredients

- cooking spray
- 1  lime, juiced
- 8  tilapia fillets
- ¼ cup honey-flavored Greek-style yogurt
- ½ cup finely chopped pecans
- ½ cup shredded sweetened coconut

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
2. Lightly coat the bottom of a 13x9-inch baking dish with cooking spray.
3. Drizzle lime juice over one side of each tilapia fillet; place the fillets with the juiced-side down into the prepared baking dish.
4. Spread a thin layer of yogurt atop each fillet.
5. Mix pecans and coconut in a bowl.
6. Divide equally between the tilapia fillets and lightly press mixture into the yogurt layer.
7. Bake in preheated oven until fish flakes easily with a fork, about 15 minutes.
