## Ingredients

- 2 cups cubed, cooked chicken
- ½ cup slivered almonds, toasted
- ½ cup dried cranberries
- ⅓ cup chopped celery
- ¼ cup chopped green onion
- ½ cup mayonnaise
- ¼ cup sour cream
- ½ teaspoon McCormick® Smokehouse Ground Black Pepper

## Instructions

1. Mix chicken, almonds, dried cranberries, celery and green onion in large bowl.
2. Mix mayonnaise, sour cream and Smokehouse Pepper in small bowl. Add to chicken mixture; mix well. Cover.
3. Refrigerate until ready to serve.
