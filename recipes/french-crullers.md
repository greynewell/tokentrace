## Ingredients

- 4 tablespoons white sugar
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon orange zest
- 4 tablespoons shortening
- 1 cup hot water
- 1 cup all-purpose flour
- 3  eggs
- 1 ½ tablespoons shortening
- 1 ½ cups confectioners' sugar
- 3 tablespoons cream
- ⅛ teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

## Instructions

1. Put 4 tablespoons sugar, salt, shortening and orange rind in saucepan with 1 cup hot water.
2. Bring to a boil.
3. Mix in 1 cup of flour.
4. Cook until thick, stirring constantly.
5. Remove from heat, and cool slightly.
6. Beat in one egg at a time, beating each one in thoroughly before adding another.
7. Using a rose tip, press dough through pastry bag, in desired shape, onto a well-greased square of heavy paper.
8. Turn paper upside down and let crullers drop into deep, hot fat (375 degrees F - 190 degrees C).
9. Fry until well puffed up and golden brown in color, about 6 to 7 minutes.
10. Drain on unglazed paper.
11. Ice with confectioners' frosting.
12. To Make Frosting:  Cream 1 1/2 tablespoons shortening and continue creaming while slowly adding sugar.
13. Add cream, salt, and vanilla and mix smooth.
