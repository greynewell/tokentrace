## Ingredients

- 1 (16 ounce) package firm tofu
- 1 cup honey mustard sauce
- 2 tablespoons vegetable oil

## Instructions

1. Preheat your oven to broil.
2. Cut the tofu cake into 4 pieces.
3. Slice each piece very thinly, then lay the tofu strips on a paper towel and blot them dry to remove excess moisture.
4. Place the dry strips in a bowl and cover them with the honey mustard sauce.
5. Cover the bowl and refrigerate for at least 3 hours, stirring occasionally.
6. Broil for 5 minutes, then flip the tofu and broil for another 5 minutes, or until the tofu is hot.
