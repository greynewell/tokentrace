## Ingredients

- ¾ cup all-purpose flour
- ⅓ cup cold butter
- ¼ cup white sugar
- 1 ¼ cups finely chopped fresh rhubarb
- 1  egg
- ¼ cup white sugar
- ¼ cup almond meal

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Process flour, butter, and 1/4 cup sugar together in a food processor until crumbly; press mixture into bottom of 9x9-inch baking pan.
3. Bake in preheated oven until golden brown, about 15 minutes.
4. Stir rhubarb, egg, 1/4 cup sugar, and almond meal together in a bowl until combined; spread mixture over crust.
5. Bake in preheated oven until bubbling, about 30 minutes.
6. Cool to room temperature on a wire rack before cutting into bars.
