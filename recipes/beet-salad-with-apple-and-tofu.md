## Ingredients

- 6  beets
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
- ½ (7.5 ounce) package smoked tofu, diced
- ½  apple, diced
- 1 (8 ounce) container plain yogurt
- 1 teaspoon prepared horseradish
- 1 teaspoon maple syrup

## Instructions

1. Place beets into a large pot, cover with cold, lightly salted water, and bring to a boil. Reduce heat, cover, and simmer until beets are soft and can easily be pierced with a fork, about 40 minutes. Drain and rinse under cold water. Peel and cut into cubes.
2. Heat oil in a skillet over medium heat. Cook and stir smoked tofu until hot, 2 to 3 minutes. Combine tofu, beets, and apple in a bowl.
3. Mix yogurt, horseradish, and maple syrup in a small bowl and season with salt and pepper. Pour over salad and mix well. Let stand until flavors combine, about 15 minutes.
