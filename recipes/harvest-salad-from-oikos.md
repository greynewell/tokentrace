## Ingredients

- 1 cup Dannon Oikos Plain Greek Nonfat Yogurt
- 2 ounces crumbled goat cheese
- 2 tablespoons cider vinegar
- 2 tablespoons chopped parsley
- Salt and cracked pepper
- 3 heads crisp Romaine hearts, shredded
- 1 large apple, cored and sliced
- 8 ounces roasted turkey, julienned
- ½ cup dried cranberries
- ½ cup toasted pecans

## Instructions

1. In a bowl mix together, yogurt, goat cheese, vinegar, parsley, salt and cracked pepper.
2. Let stand 15 minutes.
3. In a large bowl combine remaining ingredients.
4. Toss dressing with greens and toss gently to coat.
5. Divide salad evenly among 6 plates (2 cups per).
