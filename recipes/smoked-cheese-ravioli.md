## Ingredients

- 1 (16 ounce) package frozen cheese ravioli
- 1 cup half-and-half cream
- 1 cup shredded smoked Gouda cheese
- 1 teaspoon chopped fresh parsley
- 1 teaspoon white pepper

## Instructions

1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a rolling boil over high heat; stir in the frozen ravioli and return to a boil.
2. Cook uncovered, stirring occasionally, until the ravioli float to the top and the filling is hot, 6 to 8 minutes.
3. Drain.
4. Meanwhile, bring the half-and-half to a simmer in a saucepan over medium heat.
5. Whisk in the Gouda cheese until melted; season with parsley and white pepper.
6. Pour the sauce over the cooked ravioli to serve.
