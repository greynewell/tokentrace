## Ingredients

- 2 cloves garlic, lightly crushed
- 1 cup Sauvignon Blanc wine
- 8 ounces Gruyere cheese, shredded
- 8 ounces Cheddar cheese, shredded
- 1 tablespoon all-purpose flour

## Instructions

1. Pour enough water into a large pot to be a few inches deep.
2. Set a large stainless steel bowl atop the pot to make a double-boiler.
3. Put garlic cloves into the bowl and turn heat below double-boiler to medium; cook until the garlic is fragrant and warm, 2 to 3 minutes.
4. Pour wine into the bowl; cook until fragrant, 2 to 3 minutes.
5. Remove garlic from wine and discard.
6. Mix Gruyere cheese, Cheddar cheese, and flour in a bowl; gradually add to the wine while stirring continually.
7. Cook and stir the mixture until the cheese is melted and the mixture is smooth, 2 to 3 minutes.
