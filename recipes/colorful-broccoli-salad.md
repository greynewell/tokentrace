## Ingredients

- ½ cup mayonnaise
- ¼ cup white sugar
- 1 teaspoon vinegar
- ½ pound bacon, cut into 1-inch square pieces
- 1 bunch broccoli, cut into florets
- 1  red onion, chopped
- 1 cup shredded sharp Cheddar cheese

## Instructions

1. Whisk mayonnaise, sugar, and vinegar together in a bowl.
2. Place bacon in a large skillet; cook and stir over medium-high heat until evenly browned, about 10 minutes. Drain bacon on paper towels.
3. Mix broccoli, bacon, onion, and Cheddar cheese in a large bowl. Pour dressing over broccoli mixture; toss to coat.
