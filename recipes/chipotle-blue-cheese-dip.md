## Ingredients

- 1 (7 ounce) can chipotle peppers in adobo sauce
- 1 cup mayonnaise
- 2 tablespoons milk
- 1 ½ cups chunky blue cheese dressing

## Instructions

1. Puree the chipotle peppers with the adobo sauce in a blender until smooth.
2. Add the mayonnaise, milk, and dressing; blend again until thoroughly mixed.
