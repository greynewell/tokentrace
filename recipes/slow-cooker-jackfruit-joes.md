## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
- ½ cup diced onion
- ½ cup diced red bell pepper
- 1 (16 ounce) can green jackfruit in brine, drained
- 2 cups plain tomato sauce
- 2 tablespoons brown sugar
- 1 ½ tablespoons brown mustard
- 1 tablespoon tomato paste
- 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
- 1 tablespoon sriracha sauce
- 1 teaspoon white vinegar
- ½ teaspoon garlic powder
- ½ teaspoon dried parsley
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- 6  hamburger buns

## Instructions

1. Turn on a multi-functional pressure cooker (such as Instant Pot®) and select Saute function. Add oil, onion, and bell pepper and cook until slightly softened, 3 to 5 minutes. Turn off Saute function and add jackfruit in a single layer on top of vegetables.
2. Whisk tomato sauce, brown sugar, mustard, tomato paste, Worcestershire sauce, sriracha sauce, vinegar, garlic powder, parsley, salt, and pepper together in a bowl. Pour this mixture evenly over jackfruit in the pressure cooker. Close and lock the lid; choose the Slow Cook option and set the timer for 4 hours.
3. Toast hamburger buns and serve topped with cooked jackfruit.
