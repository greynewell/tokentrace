## Ingredients

- 3 bunches watercress, trimmed
- 1 ½ cups cherry tomatoes, halved
- 1 small red onion, thinly sliced
- ¼ cup crushed peanuts
- 3  limes, juiced, or more to taste
- 1 tablespoon sesame seeds
- 1 tablespoon garlic oil
- 1 tablespoon fish sauce
- ½ teaspoon salt, or more to taste

## Instructions

1. Place watercress in a large bowl; top with tomatoes, onion, peanuts, lime juice, sesame seeds, garlic oil, fish sauce, and salt.
2. Toss mixture well; let sit for flavors to blend, about 10 minutes.
