## Ingredients

- 1 (8 ounce) package egg noodles
- 1 cup cubed fully cooked ham
- 1 (12 fluid ounce) can evaporated milk
- 1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of chicken soup
- 1 small onion, minced
- ⅓ pound processed cheese food (such as Velveeta®), cubed
- ½ cup crushed potato chips

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook egg noodles in the boiling water, stirring occasionally, until cooked through but firm to the bite, about 5 minutes. Drain.
3. Mix egg noodles, ham, evaporated milk, cream of chicken soup, onion, and processed cheese food in a 9x13-inch baking dish until evenly distributed.
4. Bake in preheated oven until bubbly and cheese food is melted, about 1 hour. About 5 minutes before end of baking, sprinkle chips atop casserole and return to oven; bake for 5 minutes more.
