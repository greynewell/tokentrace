## Ingredients

- 25  uncooked medium shrimp, peeled and deveined
- 1 teaspoon blackened seasoning, or to taste
- ½  fresh pineapple, diced
- 1 (15 ounce) can whole kernel corn, drained
- 1 bunch fresh spinach, or to taste

## Instructions

1. Cook the shrimp on medium-high heat until fully pink, seasoning with blackened seasoning while you stir, about 5 minutes. Add pineapple and allow to cook for 5 to 7 minutes. Mix in corn and cook for 5 to 7 minutes.
2. Serve warm on a bed of spinach.
