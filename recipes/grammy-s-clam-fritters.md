## Ingredients

- oil for frying
- 1 cup all-purpose flour
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 1  egg
- 1 cup minced clams
- ground black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Heat oil in a deep-fryer or large saucepan to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
2. Whisk the flour and baking powder together in a bowl; set aside.
3. Beat the egg in a separate bowl until smooth.
4. Stir in the minced clams and black pepper to taste.
5. Stir in the flour to form a batter.
6. Drop the fritters into the hot oil by the heaping tablespoonful.
7. Fry until golden on both sides, about 1 minute per side.
8. Drain on a paper towel-lined plate to serve.
