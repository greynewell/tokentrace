## Ingredients

- 1 (16 ounce) package dried rice vermicelli noodles
- 2 ¼ pounds small shrimp, peeled and deveined
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 1  onion, chopped
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 ½ teaspoons achiote powder
- 2 ¼ pounds ground pork
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- 2 (10.75 ounce) cans  cream of chicken soup
- 3 ¼ cups chicken broth
- 3  hard-boiled eggs, crumbled  (Optional)
- 2  green onions, chopped

## Instructions

1. Soak noodles in a bowl of warm water for 5 minutes.
2. Bring a large pot of water to a rolling boil.
3. Stir in noodles and bring back to a boil.
4. Cook until tender, 4 to 5 minutes.
5. Drain.
6. Bring a saucepan of water to a boil.
7. Add shrimp and boil until just opaque, 2 to 3 minutes.
8. Drain.
9. Heat oil in a skillet over medium heat.
10. Add onion and garlic; cook and stir until onion is translucent, about 5 minutes.
11. Transfer half the mixture to a bowl and set aside.
12. Stir achiote powder into the skillet with the remaining onion and garlic mixture.
13. Add prawns; reduce heat and let simmer until well coated, about 2 minutes.
14. Heat a large pot over medium-high heat.
15. Saute pork until browned, crumbly, and no longer pink, 5 to 7 minutes.
16. Add the remaining 1/2 of the onion-garlic mixture; season with salt and pepper.
17. Add cream of chicken soup; stir until well mixed.
18. Add chicken broth; stir until it becomes a thick sauce, about 5 minutes.
19. Place a portion of the noodles on a plate, ladle a portion of the sauce over, and top with a small portion of the shrimp mixture.
20. Garnish with hard-boiled eggs and green onions.
