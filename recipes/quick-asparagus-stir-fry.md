## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- 1 pound asparagus, trimmed and cut into 1 1/2-inch diagonal pieces
- salt
- 1 squeeze lemon juice, or to taste

## Instructions

1. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add garlic; cook and stir until fragrant, about 30 seconds. Add asparagus and stir-fry until soft but still firm, 4 to 7 minutes.
2. Remove from the heat and season with salt. Squeeze lemon wedge over top and serve.
