## Ingredients

- 3 tablespoons rendered bacon fat
- 3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
- 2 cups chopped stewed tomatoes
- ⅛ cup tomato sauce
- 6 tablespoons minced onion
- 6 tablespoons chopped green bell pepper
- 1 ½ tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
- 1 clove crushed garlic
- 1 ½ cups hot water
- 2 teaspoons salt
- ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
- 7 ounces frozen, peeled and deveined shrimp

## Instructions

1. Melt bacon fat in a large skillet over medium heat.
2. Stir in the flour and cook until browned, stirring constantly to make a brown roux (see Editor's Note).
3. Stir tomatoes, tomato sauce, minced onion, green pepper, parsley, garlic, water, salt and pepper into the roux.
4. Bring sauce to a boil, then reduce heat to medium-low, and simmer for 20 minutes.
5. Add shrimp to mixture and cook until the shrimp turn pink, and have turned opaque in the center.
