## Ingredients

- 1 cup water
- 10 ounces frozen cauliflower
- 2 tablespoons canola oil
- ½ large onion, sliced
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 tablespoons nonfat plain yogurt
- 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley  (Optional)
- 1 teaspoon garlic and herb seasoning blend (such as Mrs. Dash®), or to taste  (Optional)

## Instructions

1. Bring water to a boil in a saucepan.
2. Add cauliflower, reduce heat to medium-low, place a cover on saucepan, and cook cauliflower until tender, about 10 minutes; drain.
3. Set cauliflower aside to cool for about 5 minutes; transfer to a blender.
4. Heat oil in a skillet over medium-high heat.
5. Cook and stir onion and garlic in hot oil until tender, 3 to 5 minutes.
6. Set aside to cool for about 5 minutes; add to blender.
7. Pour yogurt into blender with cauliflower and onion mixture; blend until smooth.
8. Season with parsley and garlic and herb seasoning to serve.
