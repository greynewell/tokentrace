## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 1 bulb fennel, trimmed and sliced
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- ¼ teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes
- 1 cup Swanson® Seafood Stock
- ½ cup white wine
- 24  littleneck clams
- ¼ cup chopped fresh parsley
- 1 loaf Italian or French bread

## Instructions

1. Heat the oil in a 6-quart saucepot over medium heat.
2. Add the fennel, garlic and red pepper and cook and stir for 2 minutes.
3. Add the stock and wine and heat to a boil.
4. Increase the heat to medium-high.
5. Add the clams to the saucepot.
6. Cover and cook for 5 minutes or until the clams open.
7. Discard any clams that do not open.
8. Sprinkle with the parsley.
9. Serve with the bread for dipping.
