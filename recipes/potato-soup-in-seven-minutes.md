## Ingredients

- 2 (10.5 ounce) cans cream of chicken soup
- 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
- 8 cups 2% milk
- 1 (32 ounce) package frozen Southern-style hash brown potatoes
- 1 (14 ounce) package precooked bacon, chopped
- 2 tablespoons butter-flavored granules
- 1 teaspoon salt
- ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper

## Instructions

1. Whisk together cream of chicken soup and cream cheese in a large pot.
2. Whisk in milk, 2 cups at a time, until smooth.
3. Stir in hash brown potatoes, bacon, butter-flavored granules, salt, and pepper.
4. Bring soup to a boil over medium-high heat, then reduce heat to medium-low and simmer until potatoes are tender, 30 to 45 minutes.
