## Ingredients

- 1 (15.25 ounce) can whole kernel sweet corn, drained
- 2 (4 ounce) cans chopped green chilies, drained
- 1 cup shredded Monterey Jack cheese
- 1 cup mayonnaise
- ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
- 1 (4 ounce) can chopped olives

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Mix corn, green chilies, Monterey Jack cheese, mayonnaise, and Parmesan cheese in a 2-quart baking dish.
3. Bake in preheated oven until the cheese is melted and the dip is hot in the center, about 30 minutes.
4. Top with olives to serve.
