## Ingredients

- 1 (7 ounce) package shirataki noodles, drained and rinsed
- 2 tablespoons powdered peanut butter (such as PB2®)
- 1 tablespoon soy sauce
- 2 teaspoons sriracha hot sauce
- 1 pinch stevia powder
- 1 cup peeled, seeded, and sliced cucumber
- 1  green onion, chopped

## Instructions

1. Bring a pot of water to a boil.
2. Cook noodles in the boiling water until heated through, about 1 minute.
3. Drain and rinse noodles under cold water.
4. Mix powdered peanut butter, soy sauce, sriracha sauce, and stevia together in a bowl.
5. Add noodles, cucumber, and green onion and toss to coat.
6. Refrigerate until flavors blend, about 20 minutes.
