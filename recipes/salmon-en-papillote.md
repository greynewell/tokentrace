## Ingredients

- 4 (5 ounce) salmon fillets
- salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
- 2 sprigs fresh dill, stemmed
- 4 slices  lemon
- ½ (8 ounce) container creme fraiche
- 1  lemon, juiced

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
2. Cut a large sheet of aluminum foil into four 8-inch square pieces.
3. Place 1 salmon fillet on each piece of foil; season with salt and pepper.
4. Drizzle olive oil and balsamic vinegar over fillets.
5. Sprinkle dill fronds on top.
6. Place 1 lemon slice over each fillet.
7. Lift up ends of aluminum foil and fold together to create packets around salmon fillets.
8. Transfer packets to a baking sheet.
9. Bake in the preheated oven until salmon flakes easily with a fork, about 20 minutes.
10. Combine creme fraiche, lemon juice, salt, and pepper in a small saucepan over low heat.
11. Stir until sauce is heated through, 3 to 5 minutes.
12. Open foil packets carefully.
13. Pour sauce over salmon.
