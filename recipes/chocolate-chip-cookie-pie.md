## Ingredients

- 2  eggs
- ½ cup all-purpose flour
- ½ cup white sugar
- ½ cup packed brown sugar
- 1 cup butter, melted
- 1 cup semisweet chocolate chips
- 1 cup chopped pecans
- 1 (9 inch) pie shell

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C).
2. In a large mixing bowl, beat eggs until light and foamy. Add flour, white sugar, and brown sugar; beat until well blended. Blend in melted butter. Stir in chocolate chips and nuts. Pour into pie shell.
3. Bake in the preheated oven for 1 hour.
