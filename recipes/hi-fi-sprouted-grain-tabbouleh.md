## Ingredients

- 2 cups barley
- 2  cucumbers, diced
- 2  tomatoes, diced
- ½  onion, diced
- 1 bunch parsley, stemmed and chopped
- ¼ cup olive oil
- 2  lemons, juiced
- 1 tablespoon paprika
- 2 teaspoons pink salt, or more to taste
- 2 cloves garlic, chopped
- 1 teaspoon ground black pepper

## Instructions

1. Place barley in a 1-quart glass jar.
2. Cover with warm water.
3. Let soak at room temperature until doubled in size, 8 hours to overnight.
4. Drain and rinse barley; transfer to a large bowl.
5. Mix cucumbers, tomatoes, onion, parsley, lemon juice, olive oil, paprika, salt, garlic, and black pepper into the barley.
