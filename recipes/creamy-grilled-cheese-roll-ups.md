## Ingredients

- 10 slices sandwich bread, crusts removed
- 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, at room temperature
- 3 tablespoons finely chopped fresh chives
- 2 ½ tablespoons finely chopped white onion
- 1 pinch cayenne pepper
- 10 slices provolone cheese
- 10  toothpicks, or as needed
- ½ cup butter, melted

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
2. Flatten bread slices on a work surface using a rolling pin.
3. Mix cream cheese, chives, onion, and cayenne pepper together in a bowl until smooth and creamy.
4. Spread a thick layer of cream cheese mixture on 1 side of each piece of bread.
5. Place 1 slice provolone cheese over cream cheese layer.
6. Roll bread around cheese filling and secure each roll with a toothpick.
7. Brush each cheese roll with butter and place, seam-side down, on a baking sheet.
8. Bake in the preheated oven for 5 minutes; flip rolls and continue baking until lightly browned, 5 to 10 minutes more.
9. Remove toothpicks and cut each roll in half.
