## Ingredients

- 3 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened
- 1 bunch green onions, finely chopped
- 1 (8 ounce) jar dried beef, chopped
- 1 cup chopped pecans

## Instructions

1. In a medium bowl, mix the cream cheese, green onions and dried beef.
2. Form the mixture into a ball.
3. Sprinkle with pecans.
4. Chill in the refrigerator approximately 2 hours before serving.
