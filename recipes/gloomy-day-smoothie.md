## Ingredients

- 1  mango - peeled, seeded, and cut into chunks
- 1  banana, peeled and chopped
- 1 cup orange juice
- 1 cup vanilla nonfat yogurt

## Instructions

1. Place mango, banana, orange juice, and yogurt in a blender.
2. Blend until smooth.
3. Serve in clear glasses, and drink with a bendy straw!
