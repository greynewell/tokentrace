## Ingredients

- 3 slices bacon
- 1 bunch collard greens - rinsed, trimmed and chopped
- 1  onion, diced
- 1 (6 ounce) smoked pork chop, diced
- ½ teaspoon cayenne pepper
- 1 (14.5 ounce) can chicken broth
- 1 (12 fluid ounce) can beer
- salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Heat a large pot over medium-high heat.
2. Add bacon, and fry until crisp.
3. Remove bacon, and drain off excess fat.
4. Add the onion; cook and stir until slightly browned.
5. Add the pork chop, and season with cayenne pepper.
6. Cook until pork is browned.
7. Add the  , and pour in the chicken broth and beer.
8. Cook over medium-low heat for 30 to 40 minutes, until collards are tender.
9. Crumble bacon on top, and season with salt and pepper before serving.
