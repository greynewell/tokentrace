## Ingredients

- 1 (4 ounce) package Idahoan® Scalloped Homestyle Casserole
- 1 (4 ounce) package Idahoan® Roasted Garlic Flavored Mashed Potatoes, dry
- 3  poblano peppers - broiled, skinned, seeded, and diced
- 3 tablespoons butter
- 1 large onion, chopped
- 2 (32 ounce) cartons chicken broth
- 2 cups milk
- Salt and pepper to taste
- sour cream
- chopped green onions
- shredded cheese
- diced cooked bacon

## Instructions

1. Broil poblanos to blacken skin.
2. Cool, remove skin and seeds, cut into 1/2 dice and set aside.
3. Melt butter over medium heat in a large stock pot.
4. Add onion and saute until starting to soften, about 5 minutes.
5. Add broth and bring to a boil.
6. Crush scalloped potatoes into small pieces and add along with the cheese packet.
7. Cook 30 minutes, or until potatoes are tender.
8. Add milk and bring back to a simmer.
9. Stir in mashed potatoes and diced poblano and simmer 5 to 10 more minutes.
10. Top with assorted toppings and serve.
