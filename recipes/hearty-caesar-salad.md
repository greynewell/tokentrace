## Ingredients

- ¾ cup mayonnaise
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice
- 1 clove garlic, coarsely chopped, or more to taste
- 1  pasteurized egg
- 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
- 1 teaspoon seasoned salt
- 1 pound bacon
- 1 head romaine lettuce, torn
- ½  red onion, sliced
- 6  fresh mushrooms, sliced
- 1 (6 ounce) package croutons

## Instructions

1. Blend mayonnaise, olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, egg, black pepper, and seasoned salt in a blender until smooth.
2. Refrigerate for flavors to blend.
3. Place the bacon in a large skillet and cook over medium-high heat, turning occasionally, until evenly browned, about 10 minutes.
4. Drain the bacon slices on paper towels and crumble.
5. Mix romaine lettuce, bacon, onion, mushrooms, and croutons in a large bowl.
6. Pour dressing over salad; toss to coat.
