## Ingredients

- 1 (8 ounce) container pineapple-flavored cream cheese (such as Philadelphia®)
- 3 (12 inch) flour tortillas
- ½ pound thinly sliced deli ham
- 3  green onions

## Instructions

1. Spread cream cheese onto each tortilla and top with ham.
2. Lay 1 green onion on 1 end of each tortilla.
3. Starting with the green onion end, roll tortilla into a log.
4. Cut ends from each roll to make a clean edge.
5. Cut each log into 1/4- to 1/2-inch pieces.
