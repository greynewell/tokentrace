## Ingredients

- 1 (32 ounce) carton low-sodium chicken broth
- 1 ½ cups brown lentils
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 1 cup chopped yellow onion
- ½ cup chopped carrot
- 1 pound ground turkey
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 (15 ounce) can crushed fire-roasted tomatoes
- ⅓ cup chopped pimiento-stuffed green olives
- 3 tablespoons mild chili powder
- 3 tablespoons tomato paste
- 2 tablespoons cider vinegar

## Instructions

1. Bring broth to a boil in a Dutch oven.
2. Stir in lentils; return to a boil.
3. Reduce heat and simmer, covered, until tender, 20 to 25 minutes.
4. Drain in a colander.
5. Heat oil in the Dutch oven over medium heat.
6. Add onion and carrot; cook until tender, 5 to 6 minutes.
7. Add turkey and garlic; cook, stirring to break up lumps, until turkey is browned.
8. Drain any grease.
9. Stir in tomatoes, olives, chili powder, tomato paste, and vinegar.
10. Bring to a boil; reduce heat and simmer, stirring frequently, until slightly thickened, about 8 minutes.
11. Stir in cooked lentils; cook until heated through, about 2 minutes more.
