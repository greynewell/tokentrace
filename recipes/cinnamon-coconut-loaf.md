## Ingredients

- 2  eggs
- ¼ cup vegetable oil
- 1 cup white sugar
- 1 cup sour cream
- 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
- 1 ½ teaspoons baking powder
- 1 teaspoon baking soda
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- ½ cup flaked coconut
- ¼ cup packed brown sugar
- 2 teaspoons ground cinnamon

## Instructions

1. In a small bowl, stir together coconut, brown sugar, and cinnamon.
2. Measure flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt into a bowl; mix well.
3. In a mixing bowl, beat eggs until frothy.
4. Beat in oil and sugar.
5. Blend in sour cream.
6. Add flour  mixture, and stir until combined.
7. Put 1/2 of the batter in the bottom of a greased 9 x 5 x 3 inch loaf pan.
8. Sprinkle 1/2 of the cinnamon mixture over batter.
9. Top with remaining batter by putting dabs here and there.
10. Sprinkle remaining cinnamon mixture over top.
11. Cut through batter with a knife to give a swirling, marbled effect.
12. Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 1 hour.
13. After loaf stands for 10 minutes, remove from pan to cool on rack.
