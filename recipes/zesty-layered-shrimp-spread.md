## Ingredients

- 1 pound peeled and deveined frozen cooked small or salad shrimp, thawed and patted dry
- 2 tablespoons finely chopped fresh parsley, divided
- 1 teaspoon lemon juice  (Optional)
- ¼ teaspoon coarsely ground pepper
- 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
- 1 (12 ounce) bottle Crosse & Blackwell® Zesty Shrimp Sauce
- 1 cup shredded Mexican cheese blend
- ¾ cup finely chopped red bell pepper
- 2  green onions, finely chopped
- Assorted crackers

## Instructions

1. Press shrimp between layers of paper towels to remove excess moisture.
2. Repeat if necessary.
3. Combine shrimp, 1 tablespoon parsley, lemon juice, if desired, and pepper in medium bowl; mix well.
4. Spread cream cheese in bottom of a 9-inch tart or pie plate or spread into a 9-inch circle on large rimmed serving platter.
5. Top evenly with shrimp mixture and shrimp sauce.
6. Sprinkle evenly with cheese, red pepper, green onions and remaining 1 tablespoon parsley.
7. Cover and chill until ready to serve.
8. Serve with assorted crackers.
