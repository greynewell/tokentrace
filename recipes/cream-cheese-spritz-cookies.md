## Ingredients

- 1 cup butter, at room temperature
- 1 (3 ounce) package cream cheese, at room temperature
- ½ cup white sugar
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 8 drops green food coloring, or as desired  (Optional)
- 2 cups all-purpose flour

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
2. Cream butter and cream cheese together in a bowl using an electric mixer; add sugar and beat well. Mix in vanilla extract and food coloring. Slowly add flour and mix well to combine.
3. Fill a cookie press with dough and form cookies onto ungreased cookie sheets.
4. Bake in the preheated oven until edges are set, 8 to 10 minutes, making sure to remove at the first hint of browning.
