## Ingredients

- ½ cup chickpeas (garbanzo beans) - drained, rinsed, and mashed
- ¼ cup oats
- 2 tablespoons hummus
- 1 tablespoon creamy peanut butter
- 1 dash sriracha sauce  (Optional)

## Instructions

1. Mix chickpeas, oats, hummus, peanut butter, and sriracha together in a bowl until well combined.
2. Roll mixture into a ball then flatten with your hands to form a patty.
3. Cook patty in a skillet over medium heat until both sides are brown, about 10 minutes.
