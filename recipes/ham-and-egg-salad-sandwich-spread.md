## Ingredients

- 4 (1/4 inch thick) slices ham
- 4  hard-boiled eggs, peeled and chopped
- 3 stalks celery, minced
- 1 ½ cups light mayonnaise
- ½ cup dill pickle relish
- ¼ cup minced onion
- 2 tablespoons Dijon mustard  (Optional)
- ground black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Shred ham using the shredding blade on a food processor. Transfer to a large bowl.
2. Add eggs and celery to the bowl, then stir in mayonnaise, relish, onion, mustard, and pepper.
3. Refrigerate for 2 hours before serving.
