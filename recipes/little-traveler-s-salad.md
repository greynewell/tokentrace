## Ingredients

- 1 head romaine lettuce, chopped
- 1 (10 ounce) package baby spinach leaves
- 1 small red onion, thinly sliced
- 2 cups cooked macaroni
- 12 ounces cooked chicken breast, cut into bite-sized pieces
- 1 (12 ounce) jar roasted red peppers, thinly sliced
- 8 slices cooked bacon, crumbled
- ¼ cup poppy seed dressing, or to taste
- ¼ cup French-fried onions, or to taste

## Instructions

1. Mix romaine lettuce, spinach, and red onion together in a bowl; portion lettuce mixture onto serving plates.
2. Top each serving with macaroni, chicken, roasted red peppers, and bacon.
3. Serve with poppy seed dressing and French-fried onions on the side.
