## Ingredients

- 4 ounces cream cheese, softened
- 2 tablespoons butter, softened
- 1 tablespoon cognac
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice
- 1 tablespoon minced onion
- ½ teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
- 4 dashes hot sauce
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- ½ pound skinless, boneless smoked bluefish, flaked

## Instructions

1. Blend cream cheese and butter in a food processor until smooth, 10 to 15 pulses.
2. Scrape down sides of bowl.
3. Add cognac, lemon juice, onion, Worcestershire sauce, hot sauce, salt, and pepper to cream cheese mixture; pulse until blended, scraping down sides 2 to 3 times.
4. Sprinkle fish into cream cheese mixture and pulse just until blended.
5. Transfer pate to a bowl, cover with plastic wrap, and refrigerate until chilled, 3 hours to overnight.
