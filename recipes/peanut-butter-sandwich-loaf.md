## Ingredients

- 2 cups all-purpose flour
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- ¾ teaspoon salt
- ⅓ cup white sugar
- ¾ cup creamy peanut butter
- 1 cup milk
- 1  egg

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degree F (175 degrees C).
2. Coat a 9 x 5 inch loaf pan with vegetable cooking spray.
3. Combine flour, baking powder, salt, and sugar in a bowl.
4. Add the peanut butter, and mix in with a pastry blender.
5. Add milk to beaten egg, and pour this mixture into the flour mixture.
6. Stir until thoroughly combined; do not beat.
7. Spread batter into prepared pan.
8. Bake for 50 to 55 minutes.
9. Cool before slicing.
