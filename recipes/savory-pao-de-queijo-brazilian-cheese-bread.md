## Ingredients

- 1 ¼ cups milk
- ½ cup vegetable oil
- 1 ½ teaspoons salt
- 4 ⅓ cups tapioca flour
- 1 tablespoon tapioca flour
- 2  eggs
- 1 ⅓ cups grated Parmesan cheese

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Grease and flour a baking sheet.
3. Combine milk, vegetable oil, and salt together in a saucepan; bring to a boil.
4. Add 4 1/3 cups plus 1 tablespoon tapioca flour; stir constantly until dough easily pulls away form the sides of the pan.
5. Remove from heat; cool 10 to 15 minutes.
6. Stir in eggs, one at a time.
7. Mix in Parmesan cheese.
8. Shape the dough into small balls with greased hands; place onto baking sheet a few inches apart.
9. Bake in the preheated oven until puffed and golden, about 25 minutes.
