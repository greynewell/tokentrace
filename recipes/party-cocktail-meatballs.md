## Ingredients

- 5 pounds frozen cooked meatballs, thawed
- 1 (28 ounce) bottle ketchup
- 1 (18 ounce) jar apple jelly
- 1 (12 ounce) jar red currant jelly
- ¼ teaspoon hot sauce

## Instructions

1. Fill a large pot with lightly salted water; bring to a boil.
2. Cook meatballs, working in batches of necessary, in the boiling water until they begin floating on top and are heated through, about 3 minutes.
3. Remove meatballs with a slotted spoon, drain in a strainer, and transfer to a large saucepan.
4. Combine ketchup, apple jelly, red currant jelly, and hot sauce in a separate large saucepan; cook and stir over low heat until jelly is dissolved and sauce is heated through, 5 to 10 minutes.
5. Pour sauce over meatballs.
6. Simmer, stirring occasionally, over medium heat until meatballs and sauce are thoroughly heated, 15 to 20 minutes.
