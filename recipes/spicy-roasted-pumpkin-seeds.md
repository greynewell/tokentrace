## Ingredients

- 3 cups pumpkin seeds
- ½ cup butter, melted
- ½ teaspoon garlic powder
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 tablespoon seasoned salt

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 300 degrees F (150 degrees C).
2. In a medium bowl, mix together the pumpkin seeds, butter, garlic powder, salt and seasoned salt until the pumpkin seeds are evenly coated.
3. Spread in an even layer on a cookie sheet.
4. Bake for 1 hour and 15 minutes, stirring every 10 to 15 minutes until toasted.
5. Cool completely on the baking sheet, then transfer to a serving dish.
