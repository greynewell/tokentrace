## Ingredients

- 1  avocado - peeled, pitted, and sliced
- 1  hard boiled egg
- ⅓ cup flaked cooked crabmeat
- 1 teaspoon mustard
- 1 tablespoon mayonnaise
- ¼ teaspoon lemon pepper seasoning
- ¼ teaspoon garlic salt
- ¼ teaspoon cayenne pepper
- ⅛ teaspoon curry powder

## Instructions

1. Mash avocado and egg together in a bowl.
2. Stir crabmeat, mayonnaise, and mustard into the avocado mixture until evenly mixed.
3. Season crab mixture with lemon pepper seasoning, garlic salt, cayenne pepper, and curry powder; stir.
