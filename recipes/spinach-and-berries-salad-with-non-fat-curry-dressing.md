## Ingredients

- 6 ounces fresh spinach, torn in bite-sized pieces
- 1 cup thickly sliced strawberries
- 1 cup blueberries
- 1 small red onion, thinly sliced, pulled into rings
- Â½ cup chopped pecans
- 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
- 2 tablespoons rice vinegar
- 4 teaspoons honey
- 1 teaspoon curry powder
- 2 teaspoons Dijon mustard
- Salt, pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Wash and dry spinach.
2. Whip together dressing.
3. Add to spinach and toss lightly.
4. Add berries, onion and pecans.
5. Toss lightly.
