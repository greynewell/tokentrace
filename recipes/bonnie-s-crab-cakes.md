## Ingredients

- 1 pound lump crabmeat
- 1 ½ tablespoons dry bread crumbs
- 1 ½ tablespoons seafood seasoning (such as
- ®)
- 2 teaspoons chopped fresh parsley
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- 1  egg
- 2 tablespoons mayonnaise
- 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard

## Instructions

1. Mix crabmeat, bread crumbs, seafood seasoning, parsley, salt, and pepper together in a bowl.
2. Beat egg with mayonnaise, and mustard in a separate bowl.
3. Stir egg mixture into crabmeat mixture; shape into 6 patties.
4. Lightly grease a baking sheet.
5. Arrange patties onto baking sheet; refrigerate 30 minutes.
6. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
7. Bake patties in preheated oven until golden brown, about 30 minutes.
