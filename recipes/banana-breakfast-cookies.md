## Ingredients

- 3  very ripe bananas
- 2 cups rolled oats
- 1 cup raisins
- ⅓ cup plain yogurt
- 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Line cookie sheets with parchment paper.
2. Mash bananas in a large bowl. Add oats, raisins, yogurt, and cinnamon. Mix well and allow to sit for 15 minutes.
3. Drop spoonfuls of dough 2 inches apart onto the prepared cookie sheets.
4. Bake in the preheated oven until lightly browned, about 20 minutes.
