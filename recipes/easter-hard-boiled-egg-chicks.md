## Ingredients

- 1 medium carrot, peeled
- 3  hard-boiled eggs, peeled
- 6  sunflower seeds
- 1 leaf kale, finely chopped

## Instructions

1. Thinly slice 6 circular pieces of carrot.
2. Cut 2 small triangles from 3 of the circular pieces to be head feathers.
3. Cut 1 large triangle from the remaining 3 circular pieces to be the feet.
4. Set triangles aside.
5. Slice off the bottom of each egg at the wide end so they can sit upright.
6. Place 3 carrot feet on a plate and place an egg on top of each.
7. Slice the top of each egg about 1 centimeter down using a knife.
8. Wedge a head feather into each egg.
9. Poke a hole in the front of each egg, aligning with head feather.
10. Wedge a larger triangle into each hole as beaks.
11. Poke 2 sunflower seeds into the front of each egg as the chicken's eyes.
12. Serve eggs on a bed of kale.
