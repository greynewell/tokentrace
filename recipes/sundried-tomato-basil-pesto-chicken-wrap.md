## Ingredients

- 4 each Mission® Sundried Tomato Basil Wraps
- 4 (6 ounce) chicken breasts, boneless skinless
- 1 pinch iodized salt to taste
- 1 pinch coarse ground black pepper to taste
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 1 package spring mix lettuce
- ¼ cup pesto (add to taste)
- 1 cup walnut halves
- 1 cup Gorgonzola cheese, crumbled
- 1  Granny Smith apple, thin slices

## Instructions

1. Season chicken breasts with salt and pepper. Broil or saute in olive oil until done, approximately 10 minutes. Slice diagonally.
2. Heat Mission Wraps in a hot skillet 15 seconds on each side. Keep warm.
3. On each wrap, place a portion of spring mix in center. Arrange chicken slices on top of lettuce. Add pesto, walnuts, cheese and apples.
4. Fold bottom edge and roll from left to right, to leave one end open. Serve.
