## Ingredients

- 1 cup instant tea powder
- 2 cups orange-flavored drink mix (e.g. Tang)
- 1 (3 ounce) package powdered lemonade mix
- 2 cups white sugar
- 2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
- ½ teaspoon ground cloves

## Instructions

1. Combine sugar, orange drink mix, tea powder, lemonade powder, ground cinnamon, and ground cloves in a large bowl; mix thoroughly. Store in a sealed jar.
2. To serve: Stir 3 to 4 tablespoons of dry mix into 1 cup hot or cold water.
