## Ingredients

- 1  avocado, chopped
- 4  radishes, minced
- 3 tablespoons minced cilantro, or to taste
- olive oil
- 1 squeeze lime juice
- 1 squeeze lemon juice
- 1 dash salt and ground black pepper to taste
- 2 slices bread
- ½ cup alfalfa sprouts, or to taste

## Instructions

1. Combine avocado, radishes, cilantro, olive oil, lime juice, lemon juice, salt, and pepper in a bowl.
2. Mash roughly, leaving a few chunks of avocado.
3. Toast bread until it is nice and crispy, about 2 minutes.
4. Spread avocado mixture evenly over the bread.
5. Add sprouts and season with salt.
