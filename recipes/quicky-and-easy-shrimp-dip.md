## Ingredients

- 3 pounds cooked salad shrimp
- 6 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese
- 1 pint sour cream
- 1 (12 fluid ounce) can or bottle chile sauce

## Instructions

1. In a mixing bowl, blend together the shrimp, cream cheese, sour cream and chili sauce.
2. Season to taste with salt and ground pepper, serve with favorite crackers.
