## Ingredients

- 4 large apples
- 5 ounces marzipan
- 1 tablespoon orange marmalade
- 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, cut into small pieces
- ¾ cup orange juice

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
2. Peel apples and carefully cut out core using an apple corer. Place in a small baking dish side by side.
3. Knead marzipan and orange marmalade together in a small bowl and stuff mixture into the apples. Top with butter cubes. Pour orange juice into the baking dish.
4. Bake in the preheated oven until apples are soft, 55 to 60 minutes. Baste with orange juice every 10 to 15 minutes.
