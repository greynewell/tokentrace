## Ingredients

- ½ cup melted butter
- ¼ teaspoon garlic powder, or to taste  (Optional)
- 1 (9x9 inch) pan cornbread

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
2. Line 2 large baking sheets with parchment paper.
3. Stir melted butter and garlic powder together in a bowl.
4. Turn cornbread out onto a cutting board.
5. Thinly slice crusts from each of the short ends and discard.
6. Cut cornbread in half lengthwise; cut into 4 1/2 x 1/2-inch slices.
7. Brush both sides of each piece with butter mixture using a pastry brush.
8. Arrange slices on the prepared baking sheet.
9. Bake in the preheated oven until tops are golden and crisp, 4 to 6 minutes.
10. Flip biscotti and continue baking until top side is golden and crispy, 4 to 6 more minutes.
