## Ingredients

- ½ cup whipping cream
- ½ cup smoked almonds
- 4  grilled skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
- ½ cup mayonnaise
- 1 tablespoon minced fresh tarragon
- salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Whip cream to soft peaks.
2. Chop almonds in food processor.
3. Shred chicken or chop finely.
4. In a large bowl combine the cream, almonds, chicken, mayonnaise, tarragon, salt and pepper.
5. Mix well and serve.
