## Ingredients

- 1 cup sour cream
- ½ cup mayonnaise
- 2 teaspoons lemon juice
- 1 package McCormick® Spinach Dip Seasoning Mix
- ½ cup cooked and shredded fresh Maine lobster meat
- 2 tablespoons finely chopped green onions

## Instructions

1. Stir sour cream, mayonnaise, lemon juice, and seasoning mix in medium bowl until well blended.
2. Add lobster meat; gently stir to blend.
3. Cover.
4. Refrigerate at least 1 hour to blend flavors.
5. Stir dip and sprinkle with green onions before serving.
6. Serve with water crackers.
