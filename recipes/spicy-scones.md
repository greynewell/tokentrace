## Ingredients

- 1 cup all-purpose flour
- 1 cup whole wheat flour
- ¼ cup white sugar
- 4 teaspoons baking powder
- 1 ½ teaspoons ground cinnamon
- ½ teaspoon ground nutmeg
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ⅓ cup margarine, chilled
- ½ cup currants
- 1  egg
- ⅔ cup milk
- 2 tablespoons milk
- 2 tablespoons white sugar

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C).
2. Grease one baking sheet.
3. Combine the all-purpose flour, whole wheat flour, white sugar, baking powder, ground cinnamon, ground nutmeg and salt in a bowl and mix well.
4. Cut in the butter until crumbly and stir in the raisins.
5. Make a well in the center.
6. In a small bowl beat the egg until frothy.
7. Mix in the milk and pour into the well made in the dry ingredients.
8. Stir to make a soft dough.
9. Turn dough out on to a lightly floured surface.
10. Knead gently 8 to 10 times.
11. Pat dough into two 6 inch circles and transfer to the prepared baking sheet.
12. Brush tops with milk and sprinkle with sugar.
13. Score each top into 6 pie shaped wedges.
14. Bake at 425 degrees F (220 degrees C) for 15 minutes or until well risen and browned.
15. Serve hot with butter.
16. Maple butter goes very well with these scones.
