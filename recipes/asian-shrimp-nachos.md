## Ingredients

- 1 ½ cups canola oil for frying
- 20  wonton wrappers
- salt to taste
- 2 pounds uncooked medium shrimp, peeled and deveined
- 1 ½ cups cornstarch
- 1 (8.5 ounce) package coleslaw mix
- 3 tablespoons finely sliced green onions
- 1 cup yum yum sauce
- 1 cup sweet chili sauce

## Instructions

1. Heat oil in a cast iron skillet or deep fryer over medium-high heat.
2. Fry wonton wrappers in the hot oil for about 1 minute.
3. Flip and fry until golden brown, about 1 minute more.
4. Transfer to a plate lined with paper towels and sprinkle with salt while piping hot.
5. Cut each shrimp into 4 small pieces and place in a bowl.
6. Toss with cornstarch.
7. Increase heat under skillet to medium-high and fry coated shrimp in batches until crisp, about 6 minutes.
8. Transfer to a plate lined with paper towels.
9. Top each fried wonton with a small amount of coleslaw mix and a pinch of green onion.
10. Drizzle generously with yum yum sauce.
11. Toss fried shrimp and chili sauce in a bowl; place a portion of shrimp on top of each wonton.
