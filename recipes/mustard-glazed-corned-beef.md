## Ingredients

- 1 (5 1/2 pound) corned beef brisket
- 2 tablespoons pickling spice
- ½ cup light corn syrup
- 2 teaspoons prepared yellow mustard, or to taste

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Place the corned beef brisket into a Dutch oven, and season with pickling spice.
3. Fill with enough water to cover the roast, and place the lid on.
4. Bake for 2 1/2 hours in the preheated oven.
5. Then, remove it from the Dutch oven and place on a broiler pan.
6. Preheat the oven's broiler.
7. While the meat is roasting, mix together the corn syrup and mustard in a small saucepan.
8. Bring to a boil, and cook over medium heat until slightly thickened, about 5 minutes.
9. Spread the mustard glaze over the roast, and broil for 5 to 7 minutes, until the glaze is shiny.
