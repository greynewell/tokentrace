## Ingredients

- 1 (5.5 ounce) package dry risotto mix
- 4 ounces goat cheese
- 1 tablespoon olive oil

## Instructions

1. Prepare risotto according to package directions.
2. Chill in the refrigerator at least 1 hour, until cooled.
3. Roll risotto into 2 inch mounds.
4. Place a marble-sized chunk of goat cheese on each mound.
5. Press cheese chunks into mounds, then roll mounds around cheese, creating balls.
6. In a medium saucepan over medium heat, heat the oil and cook the risotto balls, turning occasionally, until evenly golden brown.
7. Drain on paper towels and serve hot.
