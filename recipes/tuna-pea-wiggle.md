## Ingredients

- 1 (5 ounce) can tuna, drained
- 1 (15 ounce) can peas, with liquid
- ¾ cup evaporated milk
- 2 tablespoons butter
- salt and pepper to taste
- 1 cup all-purpose flour
- 1 quart milk

## Instructions

1. In a medium bowl, stir together the tuna, peas, butter, salt and pepper.
2. In a saucepan, stir together the flour and milk until smooth.
3. Stir in the tuna mixture, and cook over medium-low heat until thickened, about 45 minutes, stirring occasionally.
4. Pour over crackers or toast to serve.
