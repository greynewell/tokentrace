## Ingredients

- 2 ounces Baileys® Original Irish Cream
- 1 cup prepared hot chocolate
- Whipped cream
- Chocolate topping

## Instructions

1. Combine hot chocolate and Baileys Original Irish Cream in a mug.
2. Top with whipped cream and drizzle with chocolate syrup.
