## Ingredients

- 1 (8 ounce) package bow-tie pasta
- 6 slices bacon
- ⅔ cup chopped basil
- 3 tablespoons lemon juice
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
- ⅛ teaspoon salt
- 2  avocados, chopped
- ½ cup grated Romano cheese

## Instructions

1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta; cook, stirring occasionally, until tender yet firm to the bite, about 12 minutes. Drain.
2. Place bacon in a large skillet and cook over medium-high heat, turning occasionally, until evenly browned, about 10 minutes. Drain on paper towels; crumble into small pieces.
3. Mix basil, lemon juice, olive oil, garlic, pepper, and salt together in a small bowl.
4. Combine bacon pieces and avocado in a large bowl. Stir in basil mixture. Add pasta; toss to combine. Sprinkle Romano cheese on top.
