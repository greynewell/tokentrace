## Ingredients

- 2 teaspoons olive oil
- ½  onion, chopped
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- 1 pint fresh figs, quartered
- 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
- 4 cups fresh spinach
- ¼ cup crumbled Gorgonzola cheese

## Instructions

1. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium-low heat; cook and stir onion with a pinch of salt until lightly browned and caramelized, 10 to 15 minutes.
2. Add figs and balsamic vinegar to onion; cook over medium heat until vinegar is reduced by half, about 5 minutes.
3. Season fig mixture with salt and pepper.
4. Spread spinach onto serving plates and top with fig mixture and Gorgonzola cheese.
