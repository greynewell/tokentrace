## Ingredients

- 10 cups water
- 1 cup cubed turkey breast meat, with bone, divided
- 1 large onion, chopped
- 4  garlic cloves, crushed
- 1 tablespoon crushed red pepper flakes
- 1 tablespoon sea salt
- 3 cubes  chicken bouillon
- 2 cups chopped broccoli
- 1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of mushroom soup
- 1 cup chopped fresh asparagus
- 1 cup sliced red bell pepper
- 1 (1.8 ounce) tub instant dry lentil soup mix
- ½ cup bean sprouts

## Instructions

1. Place water, turkey breast bone, onion, garlic, pepper flakes, sea salt, and bouillon cubes in a large pot; bring to a boil. Reduce heat; simmer broth for 20 minutes.
2. Stir broccoli, cream of mushroom soup, turkey breast meat, asparagus, bell pepper, and instant soup mix into broth. Return soup to a simmer; cook for 10 minutes. Remove breast bone. Ladle soup into bowls; top with bean sprouts.
