## Ingredients

- 1 ½ cups shredded Cheddar cheese blend
- 2 (10.5 ounce) cans condensed cream of chicken soup
- 2 cups sour cream
- ½ cup melted butter
- ½ cup onion, chopped
- 1 (32 ounce) package shredded hash brown potatoes
- 3 cups coarsely crushed corn flakes
- 2 tablespoons melted butter

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 9x13-inch baking dish.
2. Combine Cheddar cheese, cream of chicken soup, sour cream, 1/2 cup melted butter, and onions in a large bowl. Gently fold in hash browns and spread out in the prepared baking dish. Cover evenly with crushed corn flakes and drizzle with 2 tablespoons melted butter.
3. Bake in the preheated oven for 30 minutes.
