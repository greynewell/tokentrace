## Ingredients

- 1 ½ pounds ground lamb
- 2 medium onions, finely chopped
- ⅔ cup long grain white rice
- ⅔ cup pine nuts
- 1 teaspoon salt
- ¼ teaspoon pepper
- 1 teaspoon chopped fresh mint
- 1 (8 ounce) jar grape leaves, drained and rinsed
- 1 ½ cups water

## Instructions

1. Mix the lamb, onions, rice, pine nuts, salt, pepper, and mint into a bowl until evenly combined.
2. Gently open up a grape leaf, and place rib-side down onto your work surface.
3. Place a rounded tablespoon of the meat mixture into the center of the grape leaf.
4. Fold the bottom of the leaf over the meat, fold in the sides, and roll into a tight cylinder.
5. Place the rolled grape leaf into a large skillet, seam-side down.
6. Repeat with remaining grape leaves, packing them in a tight, single layer.
7. Pour the water into the skillet, and bring to a simmer.
8. Reduce heat to medium-low, cover, and simmer until the rice is tender, 50 to 55 minutes.
9. Check occasionally and add more water if needed.
10. Pour off water before serving.
