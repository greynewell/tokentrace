## Ingredients

- ¼ cup chopped walnuts
- 2 ounces Brie cheese
- ¼ cup fresh raspberries

## Instructions

1. Preheat toaster oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C).
2. Place walnuts in an oven-proof bowl; top with Brie cheese.
3. Sprinkle raspberries over the top.
4. Bake in the preheated toaster oven until cheese has melted, 5 to 10 minutes.
