## Ingredients

- 4 slices bread
- 1 tablespoon mayonnaise
- 1 pinch salt and ground black pepper
- 2 teaspoons marmalade
- 4  potato chips, crushed
- 2 pieces pineapple, thinly sliced
- 4  sardines, drained, or to taste

## Instructions

1. Preheat your sandwich maker according to manufacturer's instructions.
2. Spread mayonnaise over 2 slices of bread.
3. Cover with marmalade.
4. Spread potato chips over marmalade.
5. Lay pineapple over chips.
6. Break apart sardines in a bowl using a fork.
7. Spread on top of the pineapple slices.
8. Season with salt and pepper; cover with the remaining 2 slices of bread.
9. Toast in the preheated sandwich maker until brown and crispy, about 5 minutes.
