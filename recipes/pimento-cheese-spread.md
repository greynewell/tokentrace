## Ingredients

- 1 (4 ounce) jar chopped pimento peppers
- 1 pound shredded Cheddar cheese
- 1 large onion, grated
- 1 pinch garlic powder
- 1 dash Worcestershire sauce
- ground black pepper to taste
- 1 tablespoon mayonnaise

## Instructions

1. In a medium bowl, mash the pimentos using a fork.
2. Mix in cheese, onion, Worcestershire sauce, and mayonnaise.
3. Season to taste with garlic powder and black pepper.
4. Spoon mixture into a container large enough to hold the pimento spread, and store in the refrigerator.
