## Ingredients

- 4 (4 ounce) fillets salmon fillets
- ¼ cup melted butter
- ¼ cup maple syrup
- 4 teaspoons Dijon mustard
- 1 teaspoon soy sauce

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C).
2. Place salmon in the bottom of an 8x8-inch baking dish.
3. Mix melted butter, maple syrup, Dijon mustard, and soy sauce together in a bowl.
4. Pour over salmon, reserving some sauce to pour over baked salmon.
5. Bake in the preheated oven until salmon flakes easily with a fork, about 12 minutes.
6. Remove salmon from the oven and spoon reserved sauce on top.
