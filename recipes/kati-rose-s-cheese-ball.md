## Ingredients

- 2 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened
- 2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese
- ¾ teaspoon garlic powder
- ½ teaspoon salt
- 2 ¼ ounces thinly sliced cooked ham, chopped
- ½ (2.25 ounce) can black olives, drained and chopped
- 1 cup chopped walnuts

## Instructions

1. In a medium bowl, mix cream cheese, Cheddar cheese, garlic powder, salt, ham and olives.
2. Form the mixture into a ball.
3. Roll the ball in the chopped walnuts until heavily coated.
4. Wrap the ball in plastic wrap, and refrigerate for at least one hour before serving.
