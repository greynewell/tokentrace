## Ingredients

- 4 cups torn romaine lettuce, or more to taste
- 1  tomato, chopped
- 1 bunch green onions, chopped
- 1  avocado - peeled, pitted and chopped - or more to taste
- 1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese, or more to taste
- 1 (15 ounce) can ranch-style beans, rinsed and drained
- 2 cups corn chips (such as Fritos®, crushed - or more to taste
- ¼ cup Catalina salad dressing, or to taste

## Instructions

1. Toss the romaine lettuce, tomato, green onions, avocado, and Cheddar cheese in a large salad bowl until thoroughly combined.
2. Stir in the ranch-style beans.
3. Just before serving, toss salad with the corn chips and Catalina dressing until salad is evenly coated.
