## Ingredients

- 1 pound fresh, ground pork sausage
- ½ pound fresh, ground spicy pork sausage
- 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese
- 1 (14.5 ounce) can diced tomatoes with green chile peppers

## Instructions

1. In a large skillet, brown sausage.
2. Drain excess fat.
3. Cut cream cheese blocks into small cubes.
4. Place the cream cheese, tomatoes and sausage into a slow cooker.
5. Heat over a medium heat.
6. Stir continually.
7. Serve as soon as cream cheese melts completely.
