## Ingredients

- 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese
- 1 (3 ounce) package smoked salmon, flaked
- ¼ cup mayonnaise
- ½ stalk celery, chopped
- lemon, juiced
- 1 clove garlic
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- 2  chives, chopped

## Instructions

1. Blend cream cheese, salmon, mayonnaise, celery, lemon juice, garlic, salt, and pepper in a food processor until smooth.
2. Transfer mousse to a serving bowl and garnish with chives.
