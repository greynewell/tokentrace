## Ingredients

- 4  leeks, sliced
- 2 ounces bacon, chopped
- ¼ cup olive oil
- 1 tablespoon chopped fresh thyme
- ½ cup white wine
- 1 ½ pounds whitefish fillets
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 1 sprig fresh thyme

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 475 degrees F (245 degrees C).
2. Toss leeks with bacon and 1/4 cup olive oil and place in a baking pan.
3. Roast in the preheated oven until leeks are softened, about 10 minutes.
4. Add 1 tablespoon thyme and white wine; roast 10 more minutes.
5. Top with whitefish and drizzle with 2 tablespoons olive oil; bake until the fish flakes easily with a fork, about 10 minutes.
6. Garnish with thyme sprig.
