## Ingredients

- 1 cup macaroni
- 3 cups milk
- 3 tablespoons margarine
- salt and pepper to taste
- 1 (14.5 ounce) can diced tomatoes

## Instructions

1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
2. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.
3. In a large saucepan combine cooked macaroni, milk, margarine and salt and pepper.
4. Then stir in tomatoes.
5. Cook over medium heat until quite hot.
