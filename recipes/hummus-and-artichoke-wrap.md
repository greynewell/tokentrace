## Ingredients

- 3 tablespoons hummus
- 1 large whole-wheat sandwich wrap
- 3  marinated artichoke hearts, cut into small pieces
- ¼ cup shredded fat-free mozzarella cheese
- 1  roasted red bell pepper, cut into small pieces
- 1 cup fresh spinach leaves

## Instructions

1. Spread hummus onto sandwich wrap.
2. Arrange artichoke hearts and red bell pepper pieces atop the hummus.
3. Scatter mozzarella cheese over artichokes and bell pepper; top with spinach.
4. Roll the sandwich wrap around the ingredients to serve.
