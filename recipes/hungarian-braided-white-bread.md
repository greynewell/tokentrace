## Ingredients

- 1 (.25 ounce) package active dry yeast
- 1 ¾ cups warm milk (110 degrees F/45 degrees C)
- 1  egg yolk
- 2  eggs
- 2 tablespoons white sugar
- 2 teaspoons salt
- 5 cups all-purpose flour
- 1 tablespoon poppy seeds
- 1  egg, beaten

## Instructions

1. In a small mixing bowl, dissolve yeast in warm milk.
2. Let stand until creamy, about 15 minutes.
3. Mix the egg, egg yolks, sugar, and salt.
4. In a large mixing bowl, combine the yeast mixture with the egg, egg yolks, sugar, salt and 3 cups of the flour; stir until well combined.
5. Add the remaining flour, 1/2 cup at a time, stirring well after each addition.
6. When the dough has pulled together, turn it out onto a lightly floured surface and knead until smooth and supple, about 8 minutes.
7. Turn out onto a floured bread board, and cut into 2 equal parts.
8. Form into cylindrical loaves; let rest for 20 minutes.
9. Divide each loaf in half and roll into a long rope for braiding.
10. Fasten the 4 ends together.
11. Braid.
12. End by fastening the ends, and turning each end under the loaf.
13. Place on cookie sheet, and cover with a towel until doubled in size, about 40 minutes.
14. Brush risen loaf with a beaten egg and sprinkle with poppy seeds.
15. Bake at 400 degrees F (200 degrees C) for 15 minutes.
16. Reduce heat to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) and bake for an additional 45 minutes, or until golden brown.
