## Ingredients

- 12  asparagus spears
- ½ cup all-purpose flour
- 2  eggs, beaten
- 2 cups fresh bread crumbs
- ½ cup vegetable oil for frying, or as needed
- 1 cup mayonnaise
- 2  green chile peppers, finely sliced
- 15 dashes hot pepper sauce (such as Tabasco®), or to taste

## Instructions

1. Dampen asparagus lightly with water.
2. Coat with flour; trying to avoid coating the tips.
3. Dip asparagus in beaten egg.
4. Roll and coat asparagus in a layer of breadcrumbs.
5. Place coated asparagus on a plate and refrigerate, allowing coating to stick, 15 to 20 minutes.
6. Stir mayonnaise, green chilies, and hot pepper sauce together in a small bowl for dipping sauce.
7. Heat vegetable oil in a fry pan over medium heat.
8. Fry coated asparagus in the hot oil until golden brown, 2 to 4 minutes.
9. Serve immediately with dipping sauce.
