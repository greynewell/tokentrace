## Ingredients

- 1 cup fat free sour cream
- 1 cup lowfat cream cheese
- 2 teaspoons lemon pepper
- 1 ½ teaspoons ground white pepper
- 4 drops hot pepper sauce
- 2 (6 ounce) cans crab meat, drained
- 1 cup fat free Catalina salad dressing

## Instructions

1. In a medium sized bowl mix the sour cream, cream cheese, lemon pepper, white pepper (optional), hot sauce, and two cans flaked, undrained crabmeat.
2. Add to taste (approximately 1 cup) your choice of nonfat salad dressing (Catalina, French or Russian)  to make the dip a pinkish color.
3. Mix well.
4. Let the dip chill for 4 to 8 hours.
5. This dip is especially tasty the next day!
