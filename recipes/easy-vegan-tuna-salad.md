## Ingredients

- 1 (15 ounce) can garbanzo beans, drained
- ½ cup vegan mayonnaise
- 2  dill pickles, chopped
- 1  green onion, chopped
- ⅓ cup minced celery
- 1 ½ teaspoons nutritional yeast
- 1 ½ teaspoons soy sauce

## Instructions

1. Mash garbanzo beans with a fork, making sure to flatten all the beans.
2. Mix with mayonnaise, dill pickles, green onion, celery, nutritional yeast, and soy sauce.
