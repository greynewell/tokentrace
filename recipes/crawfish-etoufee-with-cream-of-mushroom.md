## Ingredients

- ½ cup butter
- 2 stalks celery, chopped
- 1  green bell pepper, chopped
- 1  onion, chopped
- 1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of mushroom soup
- 1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed golden mushroom soup
- 1 (10 ounce) can diced tomatoes with green chile peppers
- 1 pound crawfish tails

## Instructions

1. In a heavy saucepan, melt butter or margarine over medium heat..
2. Add celery, bell pepper, and onion; saute for about 10 minutes, or until the vegetables are tender.
3. Stir in condensed soups and canned tomatoes.
4. Reduce heat, cover, and simmer for about 30 minutes.
5. The mixture will look very thick and curdled at first, but will become smooth with cooking.
6. Do not add water.
7. Add the crawfish; cook for about five minutes more.
8. Serve.
