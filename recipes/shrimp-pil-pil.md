## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons butter
- ¼ cup olive oil
- 8 cloves garlic, minced
- ¼ teaspoon cayenne pepper, or to taste
- 3 pounds peeled and deveined medium shrimp (30-40 per pound)
- ¼ cup white wine
- salt and black pepper to taste
- 3 tablespoons sweet paprika

## Instructions

1. Melt the butter and olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
2. Stir in the garlic and cayenne pepper, and cook until fragrant, about 1 minute.
3. Increase the heat to high, and add the shrimp and white wine.
4. Bring to a boil, and cook until the shrimp are no longer transparent in the center, about 3 minutes.
5. Season with salt, pepper, and sweet paprika before serving.
