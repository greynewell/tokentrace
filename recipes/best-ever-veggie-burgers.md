## Ingredients

- 1 (15 ounce) can pinto beans, drained and rinsed
- 1  onion, chopped
- 1 cup cooked brown rice
- 1 cup raw almonds
- 1 cup raw cashews
- ½ pound mushrooms, chopped
- 1 (2.25 ounce) can black olives
- 1 large clove garlic, chopped
- 1 cup Parmesan cheese
- 1  egg, lightly beaten
- ¼ cup ground flaxseed
- 1 teaspoon vegetable bouillon base (such as Better Than Bouillon®)
- 1 dash Worcestershire sauce

## Instructions

1. Process pinto beans, onion, brown rice, almonds, cashews, mushrooms, black olives, and garlic in batches in a food processor until completely chopped; transfer to a large mixing bowl.
2. Mix Parmesan cheese, egg, flaxseed, vegetable bouillon, and Worcestershire sauce into the bean mixture.
3. Cover bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate for 1 hour.
4. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
5. Form bean mixture into 12 patties and arrange onto a non-stick baking sheet.
6. Bake burgers in preheated oven for 10 minutes, turn, and continue baking until cooked through, about 10 minutes more.
7. Heat a skillet over medium heat.
8. Cook burgers in hot skillet until browned, 3 to 5 minutes per side.
