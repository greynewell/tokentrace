## Ingredients

- 1 cup butter
- 8 cloves garlic, minced
- ½ cup soy sauce
- ¼ cup yellow mustard
- ⅓ cup ketchup
- 1  lemon, juiced
- 1 dash Worcestershire sauce

## Instructions

1. Combine the butter, garlic, soy sauce, mustard, ketchup, lemon juice and Worcestershire sauce in a small saucepan.
2. Heat very slowly over medium-low heat.
3. Do not allow it to come to a boil, or it will separate.
4. Serve slightly warmer than room temperature.
