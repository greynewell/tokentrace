## Ingredients

- 2 cups buttermilk pancake mix
- 1 ½ cups water
- 1 cup Amish Friendship Bread Starter

## Instructions

1. Place the pancake mix in a large bowl.
2. Add the water and Amish Friendship Bread Starter and stir until well combined.
3. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium high heat.
4. Pour or scoop the batter onto the griddle, using approximately 1/4 cup for each pancake.
5. Brown on both sides and serve hot.
