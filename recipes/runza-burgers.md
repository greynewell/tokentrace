## Ingredients

- 1 ½ pounds lean ground beef
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1  onion, chopped
- 1 small head cabbage, shredded
- 1 (14 ounce) can beef broth
- 2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
- 2 tablespoons yellow mustard
- salt and pepper to taste
- 10  hamburger buns, split

## Instructions

1. Brown ground beef in a large skillet over high heat.
2. Drain fat.
3. Stir in garlic, onion, and cabbage; cook until vegetables are wilted and soft.
4. Stir in the beef broth, Worcestershire sauce, and mustard.
5. Cook over medium heat, uncovered, until reduced to a sloppy joe consistency.
6. Season with salt and pepper.
7. Place hamburger buns on top of meat mixture; cover skillet to steam, about 1 minute.
8. To serve, fill steamed buns with beef and cabbage mixture.
