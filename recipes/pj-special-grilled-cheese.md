## Ingredients

- 1 cup shredded extra-sharp Cheddar cheese
- 4 teaspoons horseradish mustard (such as Silver Spring® Beer'n Brat®)
- 2 teaspoons minced garlic
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
- 1 pinch cayenne pepper
- 2 teaspoons softened butter, or to taste
- 2 slices whole grain bread

## Instructions

1. Mix Cheddar cheese, horseradish mustard, garlic, salt, pepper, and cayenne pepper together in a bowl.
2. Spread 1 teaspoon butter on one side of a slice of bread.
3. Flip; spread Cheddar cheese mixture evenly over unbuttered side.
4. Top with the other slice of bread.
5. Spread 1 teaspoon butter on top.
6. Preheat a griddle over medium-high heat.
7. Place sandwich on the griddle and press down with a spatula to flatten slightly.
8. Cook until browned, 2 to 3 minutes.
9. Flip and continue cooking until second side is browned, 2 to 3 minutes more.
