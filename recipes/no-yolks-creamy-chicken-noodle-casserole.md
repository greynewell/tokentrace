## Ingredients

- 6 ounces NO YOLKS® Extra Broad Noodles
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 1 (8 ounce) package sliced fresh mushrooms
- 2 ribs celery, chopped
- 3 medium (blank)s medium carrots, peeled and sliced
- 1 small onion, chopped
- 1 teaspoon garlic powder
- 1 quart chicken broth
- 1 sprig fresh thyme
- 1 (8 ounce) package light cream cheese, softened
- ½ cup Parmesan cheese, grated
- 1 ½ tablespoons all-purpose flour
- 2  breast half, bone and skin removed (blank)s cooked chicken breasts, chopped
- 1 cup panko bread crumbs
- 1 tablespoon butter, melted
- 1 pinch Salt and pepper, to taste

## Instructions

1. Combine topping ingredients and set aside.
2. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F.
3. Add olive oil to a 7-quart stock pot, and saute mushrooms, celery, carrots, onions and garlic powder for 3-4 minutes over medium heat.
4. Add chicken broth and thyme sprig and bring to a boil, stirring occasionally.
5. Add cream cheese, Parmesan and noodles.
6. Stir until well blended.
7. Sprinkle flour over top of mixture.
8. Stir and cook for 1-2 minutes, or until mixture begins to thicken.
9. Fold in chicken.
10. Pour into a 3-quart casserole dish.
11. Sprinkle on reserved topping mixture.
12. Bake uncovered 15 minutes.
13. Remove from oven and let stand 10 minutes.
