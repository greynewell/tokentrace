## Ingredients

- 5 slices bacon
- 1 ½ cups chopped onion
- 1 (28 ounce) can tomatoes with liquid
- 1 (8 ounce) can tomato sauce
- 3 cups diced potatoes
- 2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
- 6 dashes hot pepper sauce (such as Tabasco®), or to taste
- salt and pepper to taste
- 2 pounds catfish, cut into bite-sized pieces

## Instructions

1. Place the bacon in a Dutch oven or a large pot with a lid; cook over medium-high heat, turning occasionally, until evenly browned, about 10 minutes.
2. Reserving the drippings in the skillet, remove the bacon to a plate lined with paper towels to drain.
3. Crumble the bacon and set aside.
4. Put the onions in the pot; cover and allow to cook about 5 minutes.
5. Stir the tomatoes, tomato sauce, potatoes, Worcestershire sauce, and hot pepper sauce into the onions.
6. Season with salt and pepper; allow to simmer 30 minutes.
7. Stir the crumbled bacon and catfish into the mixture; continue cooking until the fish flakes easily, about 10 minutes more.
