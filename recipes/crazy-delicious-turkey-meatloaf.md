## Ingredients

- cooking spray
- 1 (14 ounce) package frozen mixed red, yellow, and green bell pepper strips, thawed and drained
- 2 pounds 93% lean ground turkey
- 1 cup Italian-seasoned bread crumbs

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
2. Cover a baking sheet with aluminum foil.
3. Spray foil with nonstick cooking spray.
4. Puree peppers in a blender until smooth; pour into a bowl.
5. Add turkey and bread crumbs to bowl; mix.
6. Form the turkey mixture into a loaf and place on the prepared baking sheet.
7. Bake in preheated oven until no longer pink in the center, 45 to 60 minutes.
8. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 160 degrees F (70 degrees C).
