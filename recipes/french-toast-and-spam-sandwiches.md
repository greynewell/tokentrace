## Ingredients

- 1 (12 ounce) container fully cooked luncheon meat (e.g. Spam)
- 5  eggs
- ½ cup milk
- 8 slices white bread

## Instructions

1. Cut meat into 8 slices.
2. Cook in a large skillet until browned on both sides.
3. Remove from skillet, and keep warm.
4. In a shallow dish, beat eggs and milk together.
5. Dip bread quickly in egg mixture, coating both sides.
6. Fry two at a time in skillet until done on both sides.
