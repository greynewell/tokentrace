## Ingredients

- 1 cup water
- 2 tablespoons margarine
- 2 tablespoons honey
- 2 cups bread flour
- ½ cup whole wheat flour
- ⅓ cup dry milk powder
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 (.25 ounce) package active dry yeast

## Instructions

1. Put ingredients into bread machine in the order suggested by the manufacturer.
2. Process for large (1-1/2 pound) loaf, wheat setting.
