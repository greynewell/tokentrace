## Ingredients

- ¼ cup white sugar
- 3 tablespoons unsalted butter, softened
- ½ cup almond meal
- 1 large egg, lightly beaten
- 1 tablespoon all-purpose flour
- ½ teaspoon vanilla extract, or to taste
- ⅛ teaspoon almond extract, or to taste

## Instructions

1. Beat butter and sugar until creamy. Add almond meal, egg, flour, vanilla extract, and almond extract. Stir until smooth and no traces of flour remain.
2. Use at once or fill in an airtight container and refrigerate to use the same day.
