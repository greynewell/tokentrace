## Ingredients

- 1  garlic and herb pork tenderloin
- 6 large red potatoes
- 16  green beans, trimmed, or more to taste  (Optional)
- 1 cup water
- salt and ground black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat, and lightly oil the grate.
2. Grill pork tenderloin until seared on each side, 2 to 3 minutes per side.
3. Transfer tenderloin to a slow cooker; add potatoes, green beans, and water.
4. Cook tenderloin in slow cooker set to Low until pork is slightly pink in the center, about 4 hours.
5. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 145 degrees F (63 degrees C).
6. Season with salt and ground black pepper to taste.
