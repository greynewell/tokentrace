## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
- 5 fluid ounces heavy cream
- 2 cups finely grated Parmesan cheese
- 1 pinch freshly grated nutmeg, or to taste  (Optional)

## Instructions

1. Melt butter in a saucepan over medium heat. Stir with a wooden spoon and add heavy cream. Stir until combined.
2. Add Parmesan cheese to the cream mixture in the saucepan and stir until blended. Season with nutmeg.
