## Ingredients

- ½ cup plain yogurt
- ¼ cup sour cream
- 1 teaspoon chopped fresh parsley
- 1 teaspoon chopped fresh cilantro
- 1 teaspoon chopped green onion
- ½ teaspoon celery salt

## Instructions

1. In a medium-size mixing or serving bowl, combine yogurt, sour cream, parsley, cilantro, green onion, and celery salt.
2. Mix well, cover, and refrigerate for at least 1 hour before serving.
