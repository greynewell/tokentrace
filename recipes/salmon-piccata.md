## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 2 (3 ounce) fillets salmon fillets
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- ¼ cup all-purpose flour, or as needed
- ¼ cup freshly squeezed lemon juice
- 1 cup chicken broth
- 2 tablespoons capers

## Instructions

1. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat.
2. Season salmon fillets with salt and pepper and dredge in flour.
3. Place fillets in the skillet.
4. Cook until browned, quickly flipping the salmon halfway through, about 5 minutes total.
5. Remove from the skillet for a moment.
6. Add lemon juice to the skillet and scrape the bottom with a wooden spoon.
7. Add broth and capers.
8. Return salmon to the skillet.
9. Cook until fish flakes easily with a fork and sauce is reduced by half, 6 to 8 minutes more.
10. Serve with sauce drizzled on top.
