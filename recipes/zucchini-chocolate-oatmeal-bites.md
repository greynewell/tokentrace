## Ingredients

- 1 ¼ cups gluten-free oats
- ½ cup almond butter
- ½ cup coarsely shredded zucchini
- ¼ cup dairy-free mini chocolate chips
- 3 tablespoons honey
- 3 tablespoons flaxseed meal
- ¼ teaspoon ground cinnamon

## Instructions

1. Mix oats, almond butter, zucchini, chocolate chips, honey, flaxseed meal, and ground cinnamon in a large bowl until combined.
2. Divide mixture into 18 bite-sized pieces and roll into balls. Arrange in a storage container and refrigerate until set, about 1 hour.
