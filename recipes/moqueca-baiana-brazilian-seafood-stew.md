## Ingredients

- 1 (16 ounce) can crabmeat
- ½ pound cooked shrimp, peeled and deveined without tail
- 3 tablespoons lemon juice
- 1 tablespoon lime juice
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 teaspoon salt, divided
- 1 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper, divided
- 2 ½ cups uncooked long-grain white rice
- 3 cups water
- 2 tablespoons water
- 1 (14 ounce) can coconut milk
- 1  yellow onion, coarsely chopped
- 2 ½ tablespoons butter
- 2 teaspoons salt
- 1 clove garlic
- 2 tablespoons dende oil (palm oil)
- 1  yellow onion, sliced
- 1 small yellow bell pepper, seeded and sliced
- 1 small orange bell pepper, seeded and sliced
- 1 teaspoon crushed red pepper
- 3  Roma tomatoes, sliced
- 1  Roma tomato, chopped
- ½ cup chopped green onions
- ½ cup chopped fresh cilantro

## Instructions

1. Place crabmeat and shrimp in a dish; coat with lemon juice, lime juice, 3 cloves garlic, 1/2 teaspoon salt, and 1/2 teaspoon black pepper.
2. Mix well.
3. Cover with plastic wrap; chill until ready to use.
4. Combine rice, 3 cups plus 2 tablespoons water, coconut milk, chopped onion, butter, 2 teaspoons salt, and 1 clove garlic in a large saucepan or rice cooker.
5. Simmer, covered, until water is absorbed, about 15 minutes.
6. Heat dende oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
7. Cook and stir sliced onion until beginning to soften, about 5 minutes.
8. Add yellow bell pepper, orange bell pepper, and crushed pepper.
9. Cook and stir until heated through, 3 to 5 minutes.
10. Add sliced tomatoes, chopped tomato, and green onions.
11. Reduce heat to low; let vegetable mixture simmer for 5 minutes.
12. Add the remaining 1/2 teaspoon salt, 1/2 teaspoon black pepper, and cilantro.
13. Transfer 1/2 the vegetable mixture to a plate.
14. Spread the remaining mixture to evenly cover the bottom of the skillet.
15. Spread crab-shrimp mixture on top.
16. Top with the remaining vegetable mixture and pour on coconut milk.
17. Bring to a simmer over medium heat.
18. Reduce heat to low.
19. Cook without stirring until flavors blend, about 15 minutes.
20. Serve over the rice.
