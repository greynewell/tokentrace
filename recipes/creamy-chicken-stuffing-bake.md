## Ingredients

- 4  skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
- 1 (10.5 ounce) can chicken broth
- ¾ cup water
- 1 (10.75 ounce) can  cream of mushroom soup
- 1 (10.75 ounce) can  cream of chicken soup
- ½ cup margarine, melted
- 1 (6 ounce) package herb-seasoned stuffing mix (such as Pepperidge Farm® Herb-Seasoned Stuffing), crushed

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Grease a 9x13-inch baking dish.
3. Bring a pot of water to a boil and reduce heat to medium-low.
4. Cook chicken breasts in the simmering water until no longer pink inside, 5 to 8 minutes.
5. Remove chicken and cut into bite-sized pieces.
6. Stir chicken broth and water together in a large measuring cup; mixture should total 2 cups.
7. Mix cream of mushroom soup, cream of chicken soup, and 1 cup of chicken broth mixture in a large bowl; stir in chicken.
8. Spread chicken mixture into bottom of prepared baking pan.
9. Mix remaining 1 cup of chicken broth mixture, melted margarine, and stuffing mix in a bowl; stir to moisten.
10. Spread stuffing mix over chicken mixture.
11. Bake in the preheated oven until stuffing is lightly browned and casserole is bubbling, 40 to 45 minutes.
