## Ingredients

- 2 cups sliced carrots
- ½ pound snow peas, trimmed
- 3 tablespoons butter
- ½ teaspoon cornstarch
- 2 tablespoons honey

## Instructions

1. Bring a large saucepan of salted water to a boil.
2. Add carrots and cook until tender crisp, 10 to 12 minutes.
3. Add pea pods and cook until tender crisp; drain and set aside.
4. Melt butter in the same pan and stir in cornstarch.
5. Return carrots and peas to pan and stir in honey.
6. Cook over medium heat, stirring occasionally, until heated through.
