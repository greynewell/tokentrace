## Ingredients

- 1 pound cauliflower florets
- ¼ cup mashed potato flakes
- ¼ cup low-fat milk
- 2 tablespoons margarine
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- ¼ cup shredded reduced-fat Cheddar cheese

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
2. Place a steamer insert into a saucepan and fill with water to just below the bottom of the steamer.
3. Bring water to a boil.
4. Add cauliflower, cover, and steam until tender, about 20 minutes.
5. Transfer cauliflower to a large bowl.
6. Add mashed potato flakes, milk, and margarine; mash with a potato masher or fork until cauliflower mixture is fluffy.
7. Season with salt and black pepper.
8. Pour mixture into a baking dish and sprinkle with Cheddar cheese.
9. Bake in preheated oven until cheese is melted, about 10 minutes.
