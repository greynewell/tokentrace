## Ingredients

- ¼ cup butter
- ¼ cup chopped onion
- ¼ cup all-purpose flour
- ½ teaspoon salt
- 2 cups milk
- 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
- 2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese
- 1 (15.5 ounce) can white beans, drained and rinsed

## Instructions

1. Melt butter in a saucepan over medium heat.
2. Cook and stir onion until soft, about 5 minutes.
3. Stir flour and salt into onion until flour forms a paste, 1 to 2 minutes.
4. Gradually whisk milk and Dijon mustard into onion mixture and bring to a simmer, whisking constantly until the sauce thickens, 5 to 7 minutes.
5. Stir Cheddar cheese and white beans into sauce until cheese melts into sauce, 5 to 7 minutes.
