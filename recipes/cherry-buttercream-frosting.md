## Ingredients

- 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
- 3 cups confectioners' sugar
- 2 tablespoons heavy cream
- ⅓ cup cherry preserves, or more to taste
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

## Instructions

1. Cream butter in a bowl with an electric mixer on medium speed until fluffy. Beat in confectioners' sugar gradually until light and fluffy.
2. Beat in heavy cream, 1 tablespoon at a time, until frosting reaches spreading or piping consistency. Mix in cherry preserves and vanilla extract.
