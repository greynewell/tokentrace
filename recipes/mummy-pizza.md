## Ingredients

- 4 slices French bread
- ¼ cup pizza sauce
- ½ teaspoon dried oregano
- ½ teaspoon garlic powder
- 4  pimento-stuffed green olives, cut in half
- 2  string cheese sticks, pulled into thin strips

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
2. Place bread on a baking sheet; toast in oven 3 to 5 minutes.
3. Spoon pizza sauce evenly over toast.
4. Sprinkle with oregano and garlic powder.
5. Use olive slices to make 2 eyes near one end of the toast.
6. Place strips of string cheese across toast to make "mummy wrap." Bake in preheated oven until cheese melts, 3 to 5 minutes.
