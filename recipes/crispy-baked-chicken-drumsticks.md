## Ingredients

- 6  Tyson® Fresh Chicken Drumsticks
- ⅓ cup flour
- ¼ cup fat-free egg product
- 1 tablespoon water
- 1 cup baked potato chips, coarsely crushed

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F. Spray baking sheet with nonstick cooking spray.
2. Place flour in large resealable plastic bag. Blend egg product and water in small shallow dish. Place crushed potato chips in separate shallow dish.
3. Add drumsticks to flour in bag; shake to coat evenly. One at a time, dip drumsticks in egg mixture; coat evenly with potato chips and place on baking sheet.
4. Bake 30 to 35 minutes or until chicken reaches an internal temperature of 180 degrees F.
