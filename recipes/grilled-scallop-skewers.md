## Ingredients

- 1 cup olive oil
- 2 tablespoons ketchup
- 2 tablespoons chopped fresh oregano
- 2  limes, zested and juiced
- 1 pinch salt-free grilling seasoning (such as Mrs. Dash®), or to taste
- salt to taste
- 2 pounds sea scallops

## Instructions

1. Combine oil, ketchup, oregano, lime zest and juice, grilling seasoning, and salt in a zip-top bag; mix well.
2. Add scallops and refrigerate for 2 hours.
3. Preheat an outdoor grill for medium-high heat and lightly oil the grate.
4. Thread scallops onto skewers.
5. Cook scallops on the preheated grill until opaque, 2 to 3 minutes per side.
