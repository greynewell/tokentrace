## Ingredients

- 3 tablespoons salted butter
- 2 cups chopped sweet onion
- 1 cup diced carrots
- 1 cup diced celery
- 2 ½ cups chopped mushrooms
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 ½ cups uncooked white jasmine rice
- 1 tablespoon mild curry powder
- ¼ teaspoon cayenne pepper
- 2 (32 fluid ounce) containers chicken stock
- 3 (12 ounce) cans canned chicken, drained
- salt and ground black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Melt butter in a Dutch oven over medium heat.
2. Add onion, carrots, and celery; saute until tender, about 5 minutes.
3. Add mushrooms and garlic; stir to combine.
4. Cover and cook for 5 minutes.
5. Add rice, curry powder, and cayenne; cook and stir for 1 minute.
6. Add chicken stock; bring to a boil.
7. Reduce heat to medium-low, cover, and simmer for 10 minutes.
8. Add chicken and return to a simmer.
9. Reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer until rice is tender, 10 to 15 minutes more.
