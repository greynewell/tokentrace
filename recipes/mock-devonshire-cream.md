## Ingredients

- ½ cup heavy whipping cream
- 2 tablespoons confectioners' sugar
- ½ cup sour cream

## Instructions

1. Beat cream in a chilled glass or metal bowl with an electric mixer until foamy.
2. Gradually add sugar, continuing to beat until medium-stiff peaks form.
3. Fold sour cream into the mixture until smooth.
