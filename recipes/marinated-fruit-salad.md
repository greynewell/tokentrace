## Ingredients

- 1  ripe pineapple - peeled, cored, and cut into chunks
- 3  kiwis, peeled and cut into 1/4-inch-thick slices
- 1 cup seedless grapes
- 1  green apple, cored and chopped
- 2  Fuyu persimmons, peeled and thinly sliced
- ¾ cup pineapple-orange-banana juice
- ½ cup orange marmalade
- 2 teaspoons grated fresh ginger
- 2  bananas

## Instructions

1. Combine pineapple, kiwis, grapes, apple, and persimmons in a large serving bowl.
2. Stir together pineapple-orange-banana juice, marmalade, and ginger in a small bowl.
3. Pour over fruit and toss to combine.
4. Cover and refrigerate for 2 to 6 hours.
5. Slice bananas.
6. Remove fruit salad from refrigerator and add bananas; toss gently to combine.
