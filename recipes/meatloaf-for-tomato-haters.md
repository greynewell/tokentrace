## Ingredients

- 1 cup diced onion
- 1 cup diced green bell pepper
- ¾ cup Italian-style bread crumbs
- ¼ cup hot pepper sauce
- 1  egg
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
- 2 pounds ground beef
- 1 (.75 ounce) packet dry brown gravy mix

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Mix onion, bell pepper, bread crumbs, hot pepper sauce, egg, salt, and pepper together in a large bowl. Add ground beef; mix.
3. Press beef mixture into a loaf pan. Sprinkle brown gravy mix over meat.
4. Bake in preheated oven until cooked through and browned, 1 hour. Remove from oven and let rest for 10 minutes before serving.
