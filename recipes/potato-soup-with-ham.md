## Ingredients

- 5  potatoes, diced
- 1 cup diced fully cooked ham
- ⅔ cup finely chopped leek
- ⅓ cup diced celery
- ⅓ cup diced carrots
- ¼ cup butter
- 1 tablespoon garlic powder
- 1 teaspoon dill
- 1  bay leaf
- salt to taste
- coarsely ground black pepper to taste
- 48 fluid ounces low-sodium chicken broth

## Instructions

1. Combine potatoes, ham, leek, celery, carrots, butter, garlic powder, dill, and bay leaf in a slow cooker; season with salt and pepper.
2. Pour chicken broth over the mixture.
3. Cook on Low for 8 hours.
