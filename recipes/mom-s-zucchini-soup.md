## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons margarine
- 4  onions, minced
- 2 cups sliced zucchini
- 4 cups chicken stock, divided
- 1 cup milk
- salt and ground black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Melt margarine in a pot over medium heat.
2. Cook and stir onions in melted margarine until translucent, 5 to 7 minutes.
3. Transfer onions to a blender with a slotted spoon, leaving pot on heat.
4. Cook and stir zucchini in the pot until tender, 7 to 10 minutes.
5. Pour 1 cup chicken stock over the zucchini; stir.
6. Add zucchini and stock mixture to the blender.
7. Blend onions, zucchini, and stock until smooth; return mixture to the pot.
8. Add remaining chicken stock and milk to the mixture; stir.
9. Season with salt and pepper.
10. Cook until heated through, about 5 minutes.
