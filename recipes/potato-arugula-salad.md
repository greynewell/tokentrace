## Ingredients

- 1 ½ pounds red potatoes, cubed
- 3 tablespoons white vinegar
- 2 cloves garlic, minced  (Optional)
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ¼ teaspoon black pepper
- ¼ cup olive oil
- 1 bunch arugula - rinsed, dried and torn

## Instructions

1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
2. Add potatoes and cook until tender but still firm, about 15 minutes.
3. Drain, and cool.
4. Transfer to a large bowl.
5. Meanwhile, mix vinegar, garlic, salt, and pepper in a mixing bowl.
6. Drizzle in olive oil,  whisking until mixture thickens.
7. Toss potatoes with vinegar and oil mixture and arugula.
8. Serve at room temperature.
