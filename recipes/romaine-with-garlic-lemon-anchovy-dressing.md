## Ingredients

- 1 clove garlic, minced
- 2  anchovy fillets, rinsed and patted dry
- 2 teaspoons fresh lemon juice
- ¼ cup extra virgin olive oil
- 1 head romaine lettuce
- ¼ pound Parmesan cheese
- salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Separate romaine leaves.
2. Cut crosswise into 1/2-inch-wide pieces, wash well, and spin dry.
3. With a vegetable peeler, shave 1/3 cup parmesan curls.
4. In a blender puree garlic and anchovies with lemon juice.
5. With motor running add oil in a stream until dressing is emulsified.
6. Season with salt and pepper.
7. In a bowl toss romaine with dressing, 1/4 cup parmesan curls, and salt and pepper to taste.
8. Divide salad between 2 plates and sprinkle with remaining parmesan curls.
