## Ingredients

- ¼ cup margarine, melted
- ½ cup unsweetened cocoa powder
- ⅓ cup milk
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 3 ½ cups confectioners' sugar

## Instructions

1. Beat together cocoa and melted margarine in a large bowl until combined. Add milk and vanilla; beat until smooth.
2. Gradually beat in confectioners' sugar until desired consistency is achieved. Adjust with more milk or confectioners' sugar if necessary.
