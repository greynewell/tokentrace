## Ingredients

- 1 head butter lettuce - rinsed, and torn
- 1 large pear, cored and sliced
- ½ cup dried cherries
- ½ cup wasabi peas
- 2 teaspoons toasted sesame seeds  (Optional)
- ¼ cup poppy seed dressing

## Instructions

1. Place the butter lettuce in a salad bowl; add the pear, dried cherries, wasabi peas, sesame seeds, and poppy seed dressing.
2. Toss to coat.
