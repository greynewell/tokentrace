## Ingredients

- 1  prepared soda bread farl
- 1 tablespoon olive oil for frying
- 1 pinch salt, or to taste

## Instructions

1. Split the soda farl in half horizontally.
2. Heat oil in skillet over medium high heat.
3. Gently fry soda bread for 2 to 3 minutes on each side or until crisp and golden brown.
4. Serve hot, seasoned lightly with salt.
