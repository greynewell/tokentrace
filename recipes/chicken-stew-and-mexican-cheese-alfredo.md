## Ingredients

- 2 cups water
- salt to taste
- 2 ounces spaghetti
- 4 teaspoons butter
- 2 teaspoons all-purpose flour
- ½ cup prepared chicken stew
- 2 teaspoons minced garlic
- 2 teaspoons minced onion
- 2 teaspoons dried oregano
- 1 tablespoon shredded Mexican blend cheese, divided

## Instructions

1. Bring water and salt to a boil in a saucepan.
2. Cook spaghetti at a boil until cooked through but firm to the bite, about 12 minutes; drain.
3. Melt butter in a skillet over medium heat.
4. Stir flour into the melted butter; add chicken stew, garlic, onion, and oregano.
5. Cook and stir the chicken stew mixture until thickened, 5 to 7 minutes.
6. Melt 2 1/2 teaspoons cheese into the chicken mixture.
7. Add spaghetti and toss to combine.
8. Top with remaining cheese to serve.
