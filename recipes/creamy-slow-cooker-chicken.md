## Ingredients

- 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese at room temperature
- 1 (10.75 ounce) can  cream of mushroom soup
- ¼ cup sour cream
- 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
- 2  skinless, boneless chicken breast halves, cut into 1-inch pieces

## Instructions

1. Stir cream cheese, cream of mushroom soup, sour cream, and Italian seasoning in the crock of a slow cooker until mixture is thoroughly combined.
2. Stir in chicken cubes.
3. Set cooker to Low and cook for 6 hours.
