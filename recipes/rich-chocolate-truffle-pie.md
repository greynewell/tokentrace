## Ingredients

- 12 ounces semisweet chocolate chips
- 1 ½ cups heavy whipping cream
- ¼ cup sifted confectioners' sugar
- 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
- 1 (9 inch) prepared chocolate cookie crumb crust

## Instructions

1. In a microwave-safe dish, combine chocolate chips and 1/2 of the cream. Cook on high for 1 to 2 minutes, stirring every 30 seconds until smooth. Cool to room temperature. Stir in sugar and vanilla. Set aside.
2. In a small bowl, beat remaining cream until soft peaks form. Beat in chocolate mixture on high speed, 1/3 at a time. Spoon chocolate cream into crust.
3. Refrigerate for at least 8 hours before serving.
