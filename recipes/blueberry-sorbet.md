## Ingredients

- 3 cups frozen blueberries
- ¾ cup white sugar
- ½ cup water
- 1  lemon, zested and juiced

## Instructions

1. Combine blueberries, sugar, water, lemon zest, and juice in the bowl of a food processor and process until pureed.
2. Pour mixture into an ice cream maker and process for 30 minutes according to manufacturer's instructions. Transfer to a freezer-safe container and freeze for at least 1 hour.
