## Ingredients

- 1  skinless, boneless chicken breast halves - cut into bite size pieces
- 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
- 1 teaspoon olive oil
- 1 tablespoon white sugar
- 1 tablespoon water
- 1 ½ cups mixed salad greens
- 1  hard-cooked egg
- 6 large black olives
- 6 each small green olives
- 2 large (1" to 1-1/4" dia)s radishes
- 3 slices crisp cooked bacon, crumbled

## Instructions

1. Mix together balsamic vinegar, olive oil, white sugar, and water.
2. Combine marinade with chicken pieces in a plastic bag.
3. Leave in bag for 1 hour.
4. Cook drained chicken in a skillet until done, about 10 minutes.
5. Assemble greens, egg, olives, radishes, and crumbled bacon on a dinner plate.
6. Top with chicken.
