## Ingredients

- 8  slices of thick-cut bacon
- 1 ¼ pounds uncooked medium shrimp, peeled and deveined
- ¾ cup chopped green onion
- 3 large cloves garlic, crushed
- 6 cups water
- ¼ cup unsalted butter
- 2 cups quick-cooking grits
- 1 ½ cups shredded sharp Cheddar cheese
- 1 tablespoon barbeque sauce, or to taste

## Instructions

1. Place bacon in a large, deep skillet and cook over medium-high heat, turning occasionally, until evenly browned, about 10 minutes.
2. Drain and cool bacon slices on a paper towel-lined plate; crumble.
3. Reserve bacon drippings in skillet.
4. Heat a stovetop grill over medium heat and cook shrimp until pink and no longer translucent in the center, 8 to 10 minutes.
5. Place cooked shrimp, green onion, garlic, and crumbled bacon in the bacon drippings left in the skillet.
6. Turn heat to low and keep shrimp mixture warm.
7. Bring water to a boil in a large saucepan, melt butter in the water, stir in grits, and cook covered for 5 minutes.
8. Turn heat to low.
9. Stir shrimp mixture with bacon drippings into the grits; stir in Cheddar cheese until thoroughly melted and combined.
10. Serve topped with barbeque sauce.
