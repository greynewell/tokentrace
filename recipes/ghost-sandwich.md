## Ingredients

- 2 slices bread
- 1 teaspoon peanut butter
- 1 teaspoon strawberry jelly
- 2  miniature semisweet chocolate chips

## Instructions

1. Toast bread and spread peanut butter on one of the bread slices and jelly on the other.
2. Sandwich the 2 bread slices together.
3. Cut out a ghost shape with a large cookie cutter.
4. Place 2 chocolate chips on the sandwich for the eyes.
