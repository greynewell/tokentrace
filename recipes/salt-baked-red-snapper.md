## Ingredients

- 1 (2 pound) whole red snapper, cleaned, not scaled
- 3 pounds kosher salt
- 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
- 1 cup water, or more as needed

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C).
2. Line a baking sheet with aluminum foil.
3. Mix salt and flour together in a bowl; mix water in gradually until a thick paste forms.
4. Spread 1/3 of the salt mixture onto prepared baking sheet to form 1/2-inch thick bottom crust.
5. Place snapper onto bottom crust; cover fully with remaining salt mixture to form 1/2-inch thick top crust.
6. Bake in the preheated oven until crust is golden brown, 25 to 30 minutes.
7. Crack crust with a fork; remove crust and skin from snapper to serve.
