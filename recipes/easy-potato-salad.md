## Ingredients

- 6 medium unpeeled red potatoes, cubed
- ½ cup mayonnaise
- ½ cup sour cream
- 1 tablespoon favorite flavor mustard
- 2 teaspoons salt
- ⅛ teaspoon pepper
- ¼ cup chopped celery
- ¼ cup chopped onions

## Instructions

1. Place potatoes and 1/4 cup water in a shallow microwavable dish. Cover tightly and microwave on High 12 to 14 minutes, stirring once, or until potatoes are tender.
2. Meanwhile, mix mayonnaise, sour cream, mustard, salt and pepper in large bowl. Add celery, onions and warm potatoes; toss gently.
3. Cover and refrigerate at least 1 hour or until serving time.
