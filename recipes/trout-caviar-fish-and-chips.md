## Ingredients

- 2 ounces golden trout roe
- 120  potato chips (such as PopChips®)
- ½ cup sour cream, or as needed
- 2 tablespoons chopped fresh chives

## Instructions

1. Spread potato chips on a flat surface.
2. Spoon sour cream into a small resealable plastic bag.
3. Press the sour cream into a corner and cut the corner of the bag off with scissors.
4. Pipe about 1/4 to 1/2 teaspoon sour cream into the center of each chip.
5. Top sour cream with about 1/2 gram trout caviar; garnish caviar with a few pieces of chopped chive.
