## Ingredients

- 3 large beets
- extra-virgin olive oil
- balsamic vinegar
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- 1  avocado -  peeled, pitted, and cut into bite-size pieces
- 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
- 1 (10 ounce) package mixed salad greens
- ¼ cup shelled pistachio nuts
- ¼ cup  dried cherries
- ¼ cup shredded Parmesan cheese
- extra-virgin olive oil
- balsamic vinegar

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
2. Cut stems off beets and place whole on a baking sheet.
3. Roast until fork tender, about 45 minutes.
4. Allow to cool; peel and dice beets into large bite-sized pieces.
5. Toss with 1 tablespoon olive oil and 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar in a bowl; season with salt and black pepper.
6. Toss diced avocado with 2 tablespoons olive oil in a separate bowl; season with salt.
7. To serve, divide salad greens between serving plates.
8. Top each serving with 1/4 of the beets and avocado.
9. Sprinkle each serving with shelled pistachios, dried cherries, and shredded Parmesan cheese.
10. Drizzle with remaining 1 tablespoon olive oil and 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar.
11. Season with salt and ground black pepper to taste.
