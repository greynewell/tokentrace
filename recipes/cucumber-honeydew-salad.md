## Ingredients

- 1  honeydew melon - peeled, seeded and cubed
- 2  cucumbers, peeled and sliced
- ½  red onion, minced
- 1 bunch fresh mint, minced
- ⅓ cup extra-virgin olive oil
- ½  lemon, juiced, or to taste
- salt and ground black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Mix together honeydew melon, cucumbers, red onion and mint in a large bowl.
2. Add olive oil and toss to coat.
3. Stir in lemon juice, salt, and pepper to taste.
4. Cover and refrigerate  until chilled, about 40 minutes.
