## Ingredients

- ¼ cup tarragon vinegar
- ¼ cup white wine vinegar
- 2 bunches green onions
- 1 bunch fresh parsley
- 2 (2 ounce) cans anchovy filets
- 2 cloves garlic
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1  lemon, juiced
- 2 cups mayonnaise

## Instructions

1. Puree vinegars, onions, parsley, anchovies, garlic, salt, and lemon juice in a blender until well combined.
2. Blend in mayonnaise and serve.
