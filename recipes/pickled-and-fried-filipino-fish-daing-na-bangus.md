## Ingredients

- 1 (6 ounce) whole milkfish (bangus), or to taste
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- 5 cloves garlic, minced
- ½ cup white vinegar
- vegetable oil for frying

## Instructions

1. Remove scales from the bangus.
2. Cut a slit into the fish lengthwise along the back to remove gills, intestines, and innards.
3. Spread open the fish to expose the flesh; pat dry all over.
4. Lay fish flat on a ridged plate and sprinkle with salt and pepper.
5. Spread garlic over the flesh and pour vinegar on top.
6. Cover with plastic wrap and marinate in the refrigerator, 1 hour to overnight.
7. Discard marinade and pat fish dry with paper towels.
8. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
9. Fry the fish until golden brown and crispy, 7 to 10 minutes.
