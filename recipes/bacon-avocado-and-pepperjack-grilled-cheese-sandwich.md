## Ingredients

- 8 (3/4 inch thick) slices sourdough bread
- ¼ cup butter
- 8 slices cooked thick bacon
- 8 slices pepperjack cheese
- 1  red onion, sliced and separated into rings
- 1  avocado, halved and cut into 1/4-inch slices

## Instructions

1. Lay bread on a work surface and spread butter on one side of each slice.
2. Layer bacon, 1 slice of cheese, onion rings, avocado slices, and another slice of cheese on top of the unbuttered-side of the bread; top with second slice of bread, buttered-side facing up.
3. Cook sandwiches in a skillet over medium heat, flipping once, until both sides are golden brown and cheese has melted, about 3 to 5 minutes per side.
