## Ingredients

- ¼ cup lemon juice
- 1 teaspoon kosher salt
- 1 teaspoon seasoned salt
- ¼ teaspoon chili powder
- ½ cup olive oil
- 1 cup hot water
- ¾ cup bulgur
- 2 cups chopped fresh flat-leaf parsley
- 1 cup diced tomato
- ½ cup diced green bell pepper
- ½ cup peeled and diced cucumber
- 6  green onions, thinly sliced
- 1 tablespoon chopped fresh mint

## Instructions

1. Whisk lemon juice, kosher salt, seasoned salt, and chili powder together in a bowl.
2. Slowly drizzle in olive oil while whisking rapidly until dressing is thick and creamy.
3. Pour hot water over bulgur in a bowl; let soak until water is absorbed and bulgur is soft, about 30 minutes.
4. Mix parsley, tomato, green bell pepper, cucumber, green onions, and mint together in a large bowl.
5. Add bulgur; toss to combine.
6. Drizzle dressing over bulgur mixture; toss to coat.
