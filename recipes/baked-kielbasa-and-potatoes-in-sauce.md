## Ingredients

- 3 pounds potatoes, peeled and cubed
- 2 pounds kielbasa sausage, cut into 1 inch pieces
- 1 (28 ounce) can tomato sauce
- 1 teaspoon dried basil leaves
- ½ teaspoon garlic powder
- salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 375  degrees F (190 degrees C).
2. In a 9x13 inch baking dish, combine potatoes and kielbasa.
3. Pour tomato sauce evenly over top.
4. Sprinkle with basil, garlic powder, salt and pepper.
5. Bake in preheated oven for 2 hours, or until potatoes are tender.
