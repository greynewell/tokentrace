## Ingredients

- 1 cup chicken broth
- 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
- 1 tablespoon lemon zest
- 1 tablespoon butter
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- ⅔ cup couscous
- 1 (2 ounce) jar sliced pimento peppers, drained
- 2 tablespoons toasted pine nuts
- 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley

## Instructions

1. Stir chicken broth, lemon juice, lemon zest, butter, and salt together in a saucepan; bring to a boil. Add couscous to the liquid and stir to coat completely. Place a cover on the saucepan, remove from heat, and let couscous soak in hot liquid until the moisture is mostly absorbed, about 5 minutes.
2. Fluff couscous with a fork. Stir pimento peppers, pine nuts, and parsley into the couscous.
