## Ingredients

- 3 cups milk
- ⅓ cup white sugar
- 1 ½ tablespoons vanilla extract
- 6 slices white bread, toasted
- 3 pinches ground cinnamon, or to taste

## Instructions

1. Combine milk, sugar, and vanilla extract in a medium saucepan over medium heat; bring to a boil.
2. Divide milk evenly into 3 bowls. Tear toast into bite-sized pieces and distribute evenly amongst bowls. Sprinkle with cinnamon.
