## Ingredients

- 1 cup sour cream
- 1 (8 ounce) container creme fraiche
- ½ cup mayonnaise
- 4 tablespoons chopped fresh dill
- 1 pinch white pepper
- 3 ½ ounces red lumpfish caviar

## Instructions

1. In a bowl, stir together sour cream, creme fraiche, mayonnaise, dill, and white pepper.
2. Carefully mix in caviar.
3. Cover, and refrigerate at least 1 hour before serving.
