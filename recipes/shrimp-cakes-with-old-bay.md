## Ingredients

- 1 pound cooked shrimp, peeled and deveined
- 1 cup dry bread crumbs
- ½ cup diced red bell pepper
- ½ cup diced green bell pepper
- 1 tablespoon seafood seasoning (such as
- ®)
- 2 teaspoons parsley flakes
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
- ½ teaspoon onion salt
- ½ teaspoon Italian seasoning
- ½ teaspoon dry mustard
- 1  egg
- 2 tablespoons chipotle mayonnaise
- 1 teaspoon hot sauce

## Instructions

1. Set an oven rack about 6 inches from the heat source and preheat the oven's broiler to low.
2. Place shrimp in the bowl of a food processor; pulse until shredded.
3. Transfer shrimp to a large bowl and add bread crumbs, bell peppers, seafood seasoning, parsley flakes, salt, pepper, onion salt, Italian seasoning, and dry mustard; mix until well combined.
4. Mix egg, mayonnaise, and hot sauce together in a small bowl. Add to shrimp mixture and mix until fully incorporated. Form into 5 patties and place on a baking sheet.
5. Place under the preheated broiler for 5 minutes. Turn broiler to high and cook until tops are well browned, about 5 minutes longer. Flip shrimp cakes and broil on high until the other sides are browned, about 5 more minutes.
