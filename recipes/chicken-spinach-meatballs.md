## Ingredients

- cooking spray
- ¼ large onion
- 2  jalapeno peppers, seeded
- 1 (1 inch) piece fresh ginger, peeled
- 2  garlic cloves, peeled
- 1 pound ground chicken
- ⅓ (10 ounce) package frozen spinach, thawed, not drained
- ½ cup grated Asiago cheese
- ¼ cup crumbled feta cheese  (Optional)
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ½ teaspoon dried basil
- ¼ teaspoon ground turmeric  (Optional)

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
2. Grease 2 baking sheets with cooking spray.
3. Combine onion, jalapeno peppers, ginger, and garlic in a food processor; pulse until coarsely chopped.
4. Place onion mixture, chicken, spinach, Asiago cheese, feta cheese, salt, basil, and turmeric in a large bowl.
5. Mix together gently.
6. Form mixture into balls.
7. Arrange balls about 1/2 inch apart on the baking sheets.
8. Bake in the preheated oven until no longer pink in the center, about 15 minutes.
