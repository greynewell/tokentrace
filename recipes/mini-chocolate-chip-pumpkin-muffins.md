## Ingredients

- vegetable cooking spray
- 1 (18.25 ounce) package spice cake mix (such as Duncan Hines® Signature)
- 1 (15 ounce) can pure pumpkin (such as Libby's®)
- ½ (12 ounce) package miniature chocolate chips

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C).
2. Spray 12 nonstick muffin cups with cooking spray.
3. Mix spice cake with pumpkin in a bowl with an electric mixer until batter is thick and smooth; stir miniature chocolate chips into batter.
4. Scoop batter into prepared muffin cups.
5. Bake in the preheated oven until lightly golden brown and a toothpick inserted into the center of a muffin comes out clean or with moist crumbs, about 15 minutes.
6. Let muffins rest for about 3 minutes to set before removing from pan to finish cooling on a wire rack.
7. Store in a covered container.
