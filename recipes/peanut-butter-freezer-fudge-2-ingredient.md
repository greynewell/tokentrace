## Ingredients

- ½ cup peanut butter
- ¼ cup maple syrup
- 2  sheets parchment paper

## Instructions

1. Whisk peanut butter and maple syrup together in a bowl until combined. Pour onto a single sheet of parchment paper. Cover peanut butter mixture with the second sheet of parchment paper; flatten.
2. Place flattened peanut butter mixture into the freezer until hardened, about 10 minutes.
