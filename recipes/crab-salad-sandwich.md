## Ingredients

- ½ cup chopped crab meat
- ¼ cup mayonnaise
- 1  English muffin, split and toasted
- sea salt to taste
- 2 tablespoons shredded Cheddar cheese

## Instructions

1. Set oven rack about 6 inches from the heat source and preheat the oven's broiler.
2. Mix crab meat and mayonnaise together in a bowl; spread onto each English muffin half.
3. Sprinkle each crab meat layer with sea salt and Cheddar cheese.
4. Place English muffins on a baking sheet.
5. Broil in the preheated oven until cheese is melted and bubbling, 3 to 5 minutes.
