## Ingredients

- 1 sheet frozen puff pastry, thawed
- 5 ounces Brie cheese
- ½ cup MUSSELMAN'S® Apple Butter
- ¼ cup chopped pecans

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F.
2. Press seams of puff pastry sheet together.
3. Cut sheet into quarters and then each quarter into six pieces.
4. Use a rolling pin to flatten each piece of pastry into a square shape.
5. Place the squares into 24 greased mini muffin cups.
6. Put about 1/2 teaspoon of Brie, 1 teaspoon of apple butter and 1/2 teaspoon of chopped pecans into each muffin cup.
7. Bake for 13-15 minutes.
8. Serve warm.
