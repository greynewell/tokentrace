## Ingredients

- 4  skinless, boneless chicken breasts
- 1 cup chicken broth
- 1 (10.75 ounce) can  cream of chicken soup
- 1 (6 ounce) box stuffing mix
- ½ cup water

## Instructions

1. Place chicken into the bottom of a slow cooker. Pour broth over chicken.
2. Mix soup, stuffing mix, and water in a bowl; spoon on top of chicken.
3. Cook in the slow cooker on Low for 7 hours.
