## Ingredients

- 1 cup Original Bisquick® mix
- 1 cup milk
- ½ teaspoon ground nutmeg
- ½ cup butter or margarine, melted
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 (29 ounce) can sliced peaches, drained

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Stir together Bisquick mix, milk, and nutmeg in an ungreased 8x8-inch baking dish. Stir in melted butter or margarine until blended.
3. Stir together peaches and sugar in a bowl until combined; spoon over batter.
4. Bake in the preheated oven until golden, 50 to 60 minutes.
