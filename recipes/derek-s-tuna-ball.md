## Ingredients

- 1 (8 ounce) can chunk light tuna, drained
- 1 (8 ounce) can albacore tuna, drained
- 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
- 2 tablespoons lemon juice
- 2 teaspoons prepared horseradish
- 2 teaspoons chopped onion
- 1 dash liquid smoke flavoring

## Instructions

1. Combine light tuna, albacore tuna, cream cheese, lemon juice, horseradish, onion, and liquid smoke in a large bowl.
2. Mix by hand until thoroughly incorporated.
3. Shape into a round, flat ball on a serving platter.
4. Refrigerate until firm, at least 6 hours.
