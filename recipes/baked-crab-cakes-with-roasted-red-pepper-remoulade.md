## Ingredients

- 6  buttery round crackers (such as Ritz®), crushed, or more as needed
- ½ cup mayonnaise
- 1  large egg
- 1 tablespoon butter, softened, or more to taste
- 1 tablespoon minced fresh parsley
- 1 ½ teaspoons seafood seasoning (such as Old Bay®)
- ½ teaspoon mustard powder
- ½ teaspoon ground black pepper
- ½ teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
- 1 pound crabmeat, drained
- ½ cup mayonnaise
- ⅓ cup diced roasted red peppers
- 1 teaspoon cayenne pepper

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 360 degrees F (182 degrees C).
2. Butter a baking sheet.
3. Gently mix cracker crumbs, 1/2 cup mayonnaise, egg, butter, parsley, seafood seasoning, mustard powder, black pepper, and Worcestershire sauce together in a bowl; fold in crabmeat.
4. Form crabmeat mixture into patties, about 2 tablespoons per patty.
5. Arrange patties on the prepared baking sheet.
6. Bake in the preheated oven until crab cakes are golden brown, about 30 minutes.
7. Blend 1/2 cup mayonnaise, roasted red peppers, and cayenne pepper together in a food processor or blender until smooth and creamy.
8. Serve alongside crab cakes.
