## Ingredients

- 1 cup lukewarm water
- 1 ½ teaspoons active dry yeast
- ½ cup oat flour
- 1 ¼ cups bread flour
- 1 ¼ cups white whole wheat flour
- ½ teaspoon salt
- 2 tablespoons butter, melted
- 2 tablespoons honey
- 1 tablespoon butter, melted

## Instructions

1. Place lukewarm water, yeast, oat flour, bread flour, white whole wheat flour, salt, 2 tablespoons melted butter, and honey in the bread machine in the order recommended by manufacturer.
2. Cook on the setting for a 1 1/2-pound, whole wheat loaf setting.
3. Brush top of bread with 1 tablespoon butter before slicing.
