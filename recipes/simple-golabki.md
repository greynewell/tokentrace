## Ingredients

- 1 pound lean ground beef
- 1 head cabbage, chopped
- 1  onion, chopped  (Optional)
- 1 clove garlic
- ¼ cup water
- 1 (28 ounce) can diced tomatoes
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
- ¼ cup water

## Instructions

1. Heat a large pot over medium-high heat; cook and stir ground beef until crumbly, evenly browned, and no longer pink, about 5 minutes.
2. Drain and discard any excess grease.
3. Stir in cabbage, onion, garlic, and 1/4 cup water; cook and stir for 1 minute.
4. Add tomatoes and season with salt and pepper.
5. Pour remaining 1/4 cup water into pot; bring a boil.
6. Reduce heat to medium and simmer until cabbage is tender, 7 to 10 minutes.
