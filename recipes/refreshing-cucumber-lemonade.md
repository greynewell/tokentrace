## Ingredients

- 1 cup water
- ½ cup white sugar
- 1  cucumber, sliced
- 6  lemons, juiced

## Instructions

1. Make the simple syrup: Combine water and sugar together in a saucepan over medium heat; heat until just about to boil and sugar has dissolved. Place in refrigerator until cool, about 30 minutes.
2. Blend cucumber in a blender or food processor until mashed into a pulp. Pour cucumber pulp into a fine mesh strainer placed over a bowl; allow to sit until you have about 2/3 cup of cucumber juice in the bowl, about 15 minutes.
3. Stir simple syrup, cucumber juice, and lemon juice together in a pitcher. Serve cold.
