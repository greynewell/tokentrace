## Ingredients

- ½ teaspoon olive oil
- ½ teaspoon lime juice
- ½  carrot, shredded
- ½ cup shredded cabbage
- 4 slices Hass avocado

## Instructions

1. Whisk olive oil and lime juice together in a bowl; add carrots and cabbage and stir to coat.
2. Gently fold avocado slices into salad.
