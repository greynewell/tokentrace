## Ingredients

- 1 bunch fresh cilantro, chopped
- ½ bunch fresh parsley, chopped
- 3 cloves garlic, finely chopped
- 1  shallot, finely chopped
- 2  lemons, zested and juiced
- 1  lime, zested and juiced
- ¼ cup extra-virgin olive oil
- 40  extra-large shrimp, peeled and deveined
- 8  skewers

## Instructions

1. Combine cilantro, parsley, garlic, shallot, lemon zest and juice, and lime zest and juice in a small bowl.
2. Whisk in olive oil slowly until marinade is combined.
3. Place shrimp in a large zip-top bag and pour in marinade mixture.
4. Seal bag and gently massage onto shrimp.
5. Refrigerate for 1 to 6 hours.
6. Preheat an outdoor grill for medium-high heat and lightly oil the grate.
7. Slide 5 shrimp onto each skewer.
8. Grill shrimp kebabs until they are bright pink on the outside and meat is opaque, about 2 minutes per side.
9. Serve hot.
