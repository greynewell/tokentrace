## Ingredients

- ½ cup orange juice
- 3 tablespoons olive oil
- 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar
- ½ teaspoon ground black pepper
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- 1 large head romaine lettuce - torn, washed and dried
- 3 (11 ounce) cans mandarin oranges
- ½ cup slivered almonds

## Instructions

1. Prepare the dressing by whisking together the orange juice, olive oil, vinegar, pepper and salt; set aside.
2. In a large bowl, combine the romaine and oranges.
3. Add enough dressing to coat and toss well.
4. Sprinkle with almonds and serve.
