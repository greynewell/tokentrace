## Ingredients

- 2 cups warm water (110 degrees F/45 degrees C)
- 2 tablespoons margarine, softened
- 1 ½ tablespoons dried oregano
- 2 tablespoons white sugar
- 4 ½ teaspoons active dry yeast
- ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
- 4 ½ cups all-purpose flour
- 2 teaspoons salt
- 1 tablespoon grated Parmesan cheese

## Instructions

1. Sprinkle yeast over water in large bowl.
2. Let stand a few minutes, then stir and dissolve yeast.
3. Add sugar, salt, margarine, 1/2 cup Parmesan cheese, oregano and 3 cups of the flour.
4. Beat at slow speed for 2 minutes.
5. Beat in rest of flour; cover the bowl with a sheet of wax paper and a kitchen towel.
6. Let rise in a warm place 45 minutes, or until doubled in volume.
7. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
8. Lightly grease one round, 2-quart casserole.
9. Set aside.
10. Stir batter down for 1/2 a minute.
11. Turn batter into casserole.
12. Sprinkle with the remaining 1 tablespoon Parmesan cheese.
13. Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 55 minutes.
