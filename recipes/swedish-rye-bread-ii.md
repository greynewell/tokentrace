## Ingredients

- 3 (.25 ounce) packages active dry yeast
- 1 cup warm water (110 degrees F/45 degrees C)
- 4 ½ cups hot water
- ¾ cup honey
- 3 tablespoons salt
- 1 cup white sugar
- 3 tablespoons orange zest
- 1 ½ tablespoons caraway seed
- 6 tablespoons butter, softened
- 7 ½ cups rye flour
- 7 ½ cups all-purpose flour

## Instructions

1. In a small bowl, dissolve the yeast in the warm (110 degree F) water.
2. Let sit until creamy, about 10 minutes.
3. Pour the hot water in a large mixing bowl, add the honey, salt, sugar, orange zest, caraway seeds and butter; briefly stir.
4. Let cool to lukewarm.
5. Combine the yeast mixture with the honey orange mixture.
6. Stir in the rye flour.
7. Mix the bread flour, 1/2 cup at a time, until the dough comes together.
8. Turn the dough out onto a lightly floured surface and knead until smooth and supple, about 8 minutes.
9. Lightly oil a large mixing bowl.
10. Place the dough in the bowl and turn to coat with oil.
11. Cover with a damp towel and let rise in a warm place until doubled in volume, about 1 hour.
12. Meanwhile, lightly grease three 9x5 inch loaf pans.
13. Turn the dough out onto a lightly floured surface and divide into three equal pieces.
14. Form each piece into a loaf and place in prepared pans.
15. Cover with a damp cloth and let rise until doubled in volume, about 40 minutes.
16. Meanwhile, preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
17. Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 40 to 50 minutes, or until the top of the loaves are golden and the bottoms sound hollow when tapped.
