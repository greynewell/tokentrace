## Ingredients

- 2 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened
- 2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese
- 10  jalapeno peppers, halved and seeded
- 1 (12 ounce) package turkey bacon

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Combine cream cheese and Cheddar cheese in a bowl and mix well. Spoon mixture into the center of the jalapeno halves and spread out.
3. Cut bacon slices in half crosswise and wrap 1 bacon strip around the center of each jalapeno half. Place jalapeno poppers on a non-stick baking sheet.
4. Bake in the preheated oven until bacon is cooked and jalapenos are a little tender, 10 to 15 minutes.
