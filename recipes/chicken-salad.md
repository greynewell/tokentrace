## Ingredients

- 6  boneless chicken breast halves, cooked and diced
- 1 cup chopped celery
- 1 teaspoon salt-free herb and spice blend
- 1 cup mayonnaise

## Instructions

1. Dice chicken and place into a large bowl.
2. Add mayonnaise, celery, and spice blend; mix until well combined. Use more or less mayonnaise, depending on your preference.
