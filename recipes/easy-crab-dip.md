## Ingredients

- 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
- 1  small onion, minced
- 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
- 1 ½ tablespoons prepared horseradish
- 1 (6 ounce) can crab meat, drained
- 1 teaspoon hot pepper sauce
- 1 (12 ounce) jar cocktail sauce

## Instructions

1. In a medium size mixing bowl, combine cream cheese, onion, Worcestershire sauce, horseradish, crab meat and hot sauce.
2. Mix well.
3. Spread the mixture into an 8 inch glass pie pan.
4. Cover the cream cheese mixture with cocktail sauce.
5. Refrigerate until you are ready to serve.
