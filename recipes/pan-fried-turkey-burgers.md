## Ingredients

- 1  shallot
- 1 ¼ pounds ground turkey
- 2 tablespoons mayonnaise
- 2 tablespoons steak sauce (such as A1®)
- 2 tablespoons dried parsley
- ¼ teaspoon garlic salt, divided, or to taste
- ground black pepper to taste
- cooking spray

## Instructions

1. Grate shallot with a medium plane of a grater into a large bowl. Be sure to collect the juice. Add turkey, mayonnaise, steak sauce, and parsley. Mix gently with your hands until fully incorporated. Divide into 4 equal portions and gently form into patties. Season tops with garlic salt and pepper.
2. Spray a large skillet with cooking spray and place over medium-high heat. When the pan is hot, place in burgers, seasoned-sides down. Season the opposite sides with more garlic salt and pepper. Cook until browned, 4 to 5 minutes. Flip; continue to cook until no longer pink, 4 to 5 minutes more.
