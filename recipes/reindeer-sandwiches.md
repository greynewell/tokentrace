## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons peanut butter
- 2 slices bread
- 2  cherries
- 4  raisins
- 4  pretzel sticks

## Instructions

1. Spread peanut butter onto 1 bread slice; top with second bread slice to make a sandwich.
2. Cut sandwich in half diagonally, creating 2 triangles.
3. Arrange triangles on a plate.
4. Use 1 cherry on each triangle as the 'nose'.
5. Press 2 raisins on each triangle as 'eyes'.
6. Slice 2 pretzel sticks into the top of each triangle for 'antlers'.
