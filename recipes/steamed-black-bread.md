## Ingredients

- 2 cups buttermilk
- ½ cup molasses
- 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
- 3 tablespoons white sugar
- salt to taste
- 2 teaspoons baking soda
- 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
- 2 cups graham flour

## Instructions

1. Use a canning kettle, and invert the wire rack.
2. Fill with water to just below the wire rack.
3. Bring to a boil on top of the stove.
4. In a large bowl, mix together molasses, oil, milk, sugar, salt, and baking soda.
5. Stir in white and graham flour.
6. Divide batter into two 1 pound coffee cans.
7. Place a double thickness of aluminum foil over top, and secure with rubber bands.
8. Keep water at a slow boil, and steam for one hour.
