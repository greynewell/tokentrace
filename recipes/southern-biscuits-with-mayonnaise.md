## Ingredients

- ⅔ cup buttermilk
- 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
- 3 tablespoons mayonnaise
- 2 cups self-rising flour

## Instructions

1. In a large bowl mix together the buttermilk, oil, and mayonnaise.
2. Gradually stir in the flour and mix until all ingredients are combined.
3. Turn dough out onto a well floured surface.
4. If dough is sticky knead in a little extra flour.
5. Pat the dough out to about a 1/2-inch thickness.
6. Use a biscuit cutter to cut dough into pieces.
7. Place into an oiled cast iron skillet.
8. Allow biscuits to rest in the pan for 10 minutes.
9. Bake in a preheated 425 degree F (220 degrees C) until nicely browned, about 20 minutes.
