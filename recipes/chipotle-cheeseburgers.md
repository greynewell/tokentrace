## Ingredients

- 2 pounds lean ground beef
- 3 tablespoons TABASCO® brand Chipotle Pepper Sauce, divided
- 1 teaspoon salt
- ½ cup mayonnaise
- 6 slices Monterey Jack or Cheddar cheese
- 6  hamburger buns
- 6  red onion slices
- 6  tomato slices
- 6  lettuce leaves

## Instructions

1. Combine ground beef, 2 tablespoons of the TABASCO® Chipotle Sauce and salt in a large bowl and mix well; shape into 6 patties.
2. Combine mayonnaise with remaining 1 tablespoon TABASCO® Chipotle Sauce and mix well; set aside.
3. Grill or broil hamburger patties to desired doneness; place cheese slices on burgers and continue cooking just until cheese melts.
4. Spread mayonnaise on buns and top each with lettuce, burger, tomato and onion.
