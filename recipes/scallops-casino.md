## Ingredients

- ½ cup plain bread crumbs
- 1 tablespoon grated Parmesan cheese
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 2 teaspoons chopped fresh parsley
- 2 slices lean bacon
- ½ pound sea scallops
- 1 tablespoon butter
- ¼  green bell pepper, cut into thin strips
- 1 tablespoon minced onion
- 1 tablespoon chopped roasted red bell peppers
- 1 large clove garlic, minced
- ¼ cup dry white wine
- 2 teaspoons chicken broth
- salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
- ½  lemon, cut into wedges

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C).
2. Combine bread crumbs, Parmesan cheese, olive oil, and parsley in a bowl.
3. Cook bacon in a skillet over medium heat until golden but not crisp, 5 to 7 minutes.
4. Transfer bacon to a paper towel-lined plate.
5. Drain bacon fat from skillet until there is about 1 tablespoon fat left in the skillet.
6. Add scallops and cook until golden brown, 1 1/2 to 2 minutes per side.
7. Remove scallops from skillet.
8. Wipe fat from skillet and add butter; cook and stir green bell pepper, onion, roasted red peppers, and garlic until fragrant and tender, 2 to 3 minutes.
9. Add white wine; cook and stir for 1 minute.
10. Add chicken broth and simmer for 1 minute; season with salt and pepper.
11. Mix bacon and scallops into bell pepper mixture and transfer to a baking dish; top with bread crumb mixture.
12. Bake in the preheated oven for 5 to 6 minutes.
13. Turn on oven's broiler and broil until bread crumb mixture is lightly browned, 1 to 2 minutes.
