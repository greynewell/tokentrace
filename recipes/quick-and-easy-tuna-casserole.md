## Ingredients

- 1 (12 ounce) package egg noodles
- 2 cups frozen green peas
- 2 (10.75 ounce) cans  condensed cream of mushroom soup
- 2 (5 ounce) cans tuna, drained
- 1  onion, chopped
- 10 slices American processed cheese
- ground black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Bring a large pot of water to a boil. Add noodles and frozen peas; simmer for 8 to 10 minutes, or until noodles are al dente. Drain well, then return noodles and peas to the pot.
2. Stir in condensed soup, tuna, cheese, onion, and pepper until well mixed and the cheese has melted. Serve.
