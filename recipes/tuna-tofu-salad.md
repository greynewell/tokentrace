## Ingredients

- 1 (12 ounce) package silken tofu
- ½ cup red wine vinegar
- 1 (5 ounce) can tuna, drained
- 1 large stalk celery, chopped
- 2  carrots, chopped
- 1  onion, chopped
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- 1 pinch cayenne pepper, or to taste

## Instructions

1. Drain and mash tofu.
2. Pour vinegar over tofu and marinate for 15 to 30 minutes.
3. Drain excess vinegar from tofu.
4. Place tofu and tuna in a blender or food processor; blend until smooth.
5. Combine tuna mixture, celery, carrots, onion, garlic and cayenne in a bowl and mix well.
6. Serve.
