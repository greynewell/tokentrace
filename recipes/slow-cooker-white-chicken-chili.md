## Ingredients

- 1  serving PAM® Original No-Stick Cooking Spray
- 2 (15 ounce) cans Great Northern beans, undrained
- 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken thighs
- 1 (10 ounce) can RO*TEL Diced Tomatoes and Green Chilies, undrained
- 1 cup reduced sodium chicken broth
- ¾ cup chopped yellow onion
- 1 ½ teaspoons ground cumin
- 1 teaspoon dried oregano

## Instructions

1. Spray inside of 4-quart slow cooker with cooking spray.
2. Place 1 can of beans in slow cooker; mash with potato masher or spoon until smooth.
3. Add second can of beans and all remaining ingredients to slow cooker; stir to combine.
4. Cover; cook on LOW 8 hours or on HIGH 4 hours.
5. Carefully separate chicken into bite-size pieces.
