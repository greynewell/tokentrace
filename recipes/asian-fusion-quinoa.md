## Ingredients

- 1 ½ tablespoons coconut oil
- ½  onion, chopped
- 2  green onions, chopped
- 3 cloves garlic, chopped
- 1 cup cooked quinoa
- 2 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
- 2 tablespoons orange marmalade
- 1 tablespoon soy sauce, or to taste
- 1 pinch garlic salt
- 1  egg, beaten

## Instructions

1. Melt coconut oil in a skillet over medium-high heat; add onion.
2. Cook and stir until soft, about 3 minutes.
3. Add green onions and garlic to skillet.
4. Cook and stir until garlic is slightly crispy, about 2 minutes.
5. Reduce heat to medium; add cooked quinoa, cilantro, marmalade, soy sauce, and garlic salt to the skillet. cook and stir until heated through, about 2 minutes.
6. Make a well in quinoa-veggie mixture; add egg and cook and stir until egg is firm, about 3 minutes.
7. Stir cooked egg into quinoa-veggie mixture.
