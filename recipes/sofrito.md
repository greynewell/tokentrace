## Ingredients

- 2  green bell peppers, seeded and chopped
- 1  red bell peppers, seeded and chopped
- 10  ajies dulces peppers, tops removed
- 3 medium tomatoes, chopped
- 4  onions, cut into large chunks
- 3 medium heads garlic, peeled
- 25  cilantro stems (with leaves)
- 25  recao stems (with leaves)
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 1 tablespoon black pepper

## Instructions

1. Combine ajies dulces peppers, onions, tomatoes, green peppers, red peppers, and garlic in a food processor; pulse a few times. Add cilantro stems, recao stems, salt, and pepper. Process until a semi-chunky salsa (not watery) consistency is reached.
2. Store in a resealable plastic freezer bag. Use as needed or freeze in portions.
