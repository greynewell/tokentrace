## Ingredients

- 2 cups all-purpose flour
- 1 teaspoon salt
- ⅓ cup butter
- ¼ cup lard
- ⅓ cup water

## Instructions

1. Mix the flour and the salt in a large bowl.
2. Add the butter or margarine and lard; mix with hands until crumb like texture is achieved.
3. Mix in the water.
4. Pat into a ball, and wrap in wax paper.
5. Refrigerate for at least two hours.
