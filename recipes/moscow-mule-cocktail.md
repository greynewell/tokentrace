## Ingredients

- 1 ½ fluid ounces vodka
- ½ fluid ounce lime juice
- ice cubes
- ½ cup ginger beer
- 1  lime wedge for garnish

## Instructions

1. Pour vodka and lime juice into a mug; add ice cubes and ginger beer. Stir to combine.
2. Drop a lime wedge into the mug for garnish.
