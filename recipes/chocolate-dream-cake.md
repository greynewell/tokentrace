## Ingredients

- 1 (18.25 ounce) package chocolate cake mix
- 1 (21 ounce) can cherry pie filling
- 1 (10.5 ounce) package miniature marshmallows

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Grease a 9x13 inch pan.
3. Mix cake batter according to directions on package.
4. Coat bottom of prepared pan with a layer of marshmallows, then pour batter over marshmallows.
5. Spread cherry pie filling on top of batter layer.
6. Bake in the preheated oven for 30 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of the cake comes out clean.
7. Allow to cool.
8. Serve with whipped topping if desired.
