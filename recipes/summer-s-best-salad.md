## Ingredients

- 4  tomatoes, cut into bite-sized pieces
- 1 large bell pepper, cut into bite-sized pieces
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- dried oregano to taste
- garlic salt to taste

## Instructions

1. Combine tomatoes and bell pepper in a large bowl.
2. Pour in the olive oil; stir gently to coat the vegetables.
3. Sprinkle with the oregano and garlic salt to taste.
4. Cover, and refrigerate before serving.
