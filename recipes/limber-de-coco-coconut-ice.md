## Ingredients

- ½ (14 ounce) can coconut milk
- ½ (12 fluid ounce) can evaporated milk
- ¼ cup white sugar
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- ½ teaspoon ground cinnamon

## Instructions

1. Mix coconut milk, evaporated milk, sugar, vanilla extract, and cinnamon together in a bowl or blender until sugar dissolves and mixture is thoroughly combined.
2. Pour mixture into ice trays or popsicle molds. Freeze 8 hours to overnight.
