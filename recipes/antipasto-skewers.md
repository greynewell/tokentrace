## Ingredients

- 10 ounces Havarti cheese, cubed
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 3 tablespoons olive oil
- 1 tablespoon fresh parsley, chopped
- salt and pepper to taste
- 1 (6 ounce) jar mushroom caps, drained
- 6 slices cocktail rye bread, quartered

## Instructions

1. Mix garlic, olive oil, parsley, salt, and pepper together in a medium bowl.
2. Toss in mushrooms, and allow to marinate for 20 minutes.
3. Thread one piece each, bread, mushroom, and havarti onto cocktail toothpicks or small skewers.
4. Arrange on a platter, drizzle with remaining marinade, and serve.
