## Ingredients

- 1 head broccoli, diced
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1  onion, diced
- 6 extra large eggs, beaten
- ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
- 2 cups cornmeal
- ground black pepper to taste
- oil for frying

## Instructions

1. In a large bowl combine broccoli, garlic, onion and eggs; mix well.
2. Stir in Parmesan and cornmeal; mix well.
3. Form into patties.
4. If mixture is too dry, add another egg; if too wet, add more cornmeal.
5. Season with pepper to taste.
6. Heat 1 inch of oil in a large, deep skillet.
7. Place patties in hot oil; cook, turning once, until well browned on each side.
