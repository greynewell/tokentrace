## Ingredients

- ⅓ cup couscous
- ⅓ cup dried cranberries
- ⅔ cup boiling water
- ½  cucumber, diced
- 2 tablespoons crumbled feta cheese
- 2 teaspoons balsamic vinaigrette salad dressing, or to taste
- salt to taste

## Instructions

1. Place the couscous and cranberries in a heatproof bowl.
2. Pour in the boiling water, and stir with a fork.
3. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap, and set aside 5 to 10 minutes.
4. Fluff the couscous with a fork, and fold in the cucumber and feta cheese.
5. Season to taste with balsamic vinaigrette and salt.
