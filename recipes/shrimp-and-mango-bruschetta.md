## Ingredients

- ½ pound chopped shrimp
- 2 cups mango, cut into small dice
- ¼ cup chopped green onion
- ½ cup lime juice
- 2 tablespoons honey, or more to taste
- 1 teaspoon cayenne pepper
- 1 (8 ounce) round Brie cheese, sliced
- 1 (8 ounce) loaf French bread, sliced

## Instructions

1. Stir the shrimp, mango, and green onion together in a bowl; set aside.
2. Whisk the lime juice, honey, and cayenne pepper together in a separate bowl until blended, making sure to scrape along bottom of bowl as needed to incorporate honey; pour over the shrimp mixture.
3. Cover with plastic wrap and refrigerate 30 minutes.
4. Preheat an oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
5. Arrange the bread slices on a baking sheet.
6. Top each bread slice with a slice of Brie cheese.
7. Bake in the preheated oven until slightly browned and the Brie cheese is soft and melted, about 5 minutes.
8. Top each slice with 1 tablespoon of the shrimp mixture to serve.
