## Ingredients

- 6 ounces NO YOLKS® Extra Broad Noodles
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 1 (8 ounce) package sliced fresh mushrooms
- 2 ribs celery, chopped
- 1 ½ cups frozen peas, thawed
- 1 small onion, chopped
- 1 teaspoon garlic powder
- 1 quart vegetable broth
- Dash Worcestershire sauce
- 1 sprig fresh thyme
- 1 (8 ounce) package light cream cheese, softened
- 3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
- 2 (5 ounce) cans tuna, drained and flaked
- 1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
- 1 pinch Fresh ground black pepper to taste
- 1 cup panko bread crumbs
- 1 tablespoon butter, melted
- Salt and pepper, to taste

## Instructions

1. Combine topping ingredients and set aside.
2. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F.
3. Add olive oil to a 7-quart stock pot, and saute mushrooms, celery, onions and garlic powder for 3-4 minutes over medium heat.
4. Add broth, Worcestershire sauce and thyme sprig and bring to a boil, stirring occasionally.
5. Add cream cheese and noodles, stirring until creamy and smooth.
6. Sprinkle flour over top of mixture.
7. Stir and cook for 2 minutes, or until mixture begins to thicken.
8. Fold in tuna and peas.
9. Season with salt and pepper.
10. Pour into a 3-quart casserole dish.
11. Sprinkle on Cheddar cheese and topping mixture.
12. Bake uncovered 15 minutes.
13. Remove from oven and let stand 10 minutes.
