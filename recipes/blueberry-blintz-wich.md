## Ingredients

- 2 slices bread
- 2 tablespoons sour cream, or to taste
- 2 tablespoons blueberry preserves, or to taste

## Instructions

1. Place bread slices on a plate.
2. Spread sour cream onto 1 slice of bread.
3. Gently spread blueberry preserves across the sour cream; top with second slice of bread.
