## Ingredients

- 1 (6 ounce) package lime flavored Jell-O® mix
- 2 cups hot water
- 2 (3 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened
- 1 (8 ounce) can crushed pineapple, drained
- 1 (2 ounce) jar diced pimento peppers, drained
- 1 cup diced celery
- 1 cup chopped pecans
- 1 cup heavy cream

## Instructions

1. In a small bowl, dissolve the lime flavored gelatin in hot water.
2. Allow it to cool for 10 minutes.
3. In a medium bowl, mix together the cream cheese, pineapple, pimento peppers, celery and pecans.
4. Fold in the gelatin.
5. Chill in the refrigerator 1 hour, or until thickened but not firm.
6. Whip the heavy cream in a small bowl until thickened.
7. Fold into the gelatin mixture.
8. Refrigerate 3 hours, or until firmly gelled.
