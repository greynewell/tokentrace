## Ingredients

- 1 cup white sugar
- 1 ½ cups raisins
- 2 teaspoons baking soda
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 tablespoon margarine
- 1 ½ cups boiling water
- 1  egg
- 2 cups all-purpose flour

## Instructions

1. In a large bowl, mix together sugar, raisins, baking soda, salt, margarine and boiling water.
2. Cover and let stand overnight.
3. The following day, preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
4. Lightly grease a 9x5 inch loaf pan.
5. Beat egg and flour into raisin mixture.
6. Pour batter into prepared pan.
7. Bake in preheated oven for 1 hour, or until a toothpick inserted into center of the loaf comes out clean.
