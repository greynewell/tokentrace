## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon butter
- 3  large shallots, sliced thinly
- 4 cloves garlic, chopped
- 1 ½ cups white wine, divided
- ground white pepper to taste
- ¾ cup heavy cream at room temperature
- 1  lemon, juiced
- salt to taste

## Instructions

1. Melt butter in a medium saucepan over low heat. Cook and stir shallots in butter until translucent and tender, 2 to 3 minutes. Stir in garlic; cook until tender and aromatic, about 1 minute.
2. Stir in 1 cup white wine, increase heat to high, and bring to a boil. Add remaining 1/2 cup wine; boil for 10 minutes, then reduce heat to medium-low. Season with white pepper.
3. When sauce is no longer boiling, slowly stir in cream, lemon juice, and salt. Simmer until thickened, 3 to 5 minutes.
