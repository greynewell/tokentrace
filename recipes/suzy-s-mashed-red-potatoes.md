## Ingredients

- 2 pounds small red potatoes, quartered
- ½ cup butter
- ½ cup milk
- ¼ cup sour cream
- salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add potatoes, and cook until tender but still firm, about 10 minutes; drain.
2. Transfer potatoes to a large bowl; add butter, milk, sour cream, salt, and pepper. Mash together until smooth and creamy.
