## Ingredients

- 4 slices bread
- 1 tablespoon mustard
- 6 slices ham
- 1  pear, peeled and thinly sliced
- 2 dashes ground black pepper
- 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
- 1 tablespoon light margarine (such as I Can't Believe It's Not Butter - Light ®)

## Instructions

1. Spread 2 slices of bread with the mustard.
2. Layer each with 3 slices of ham, half of the pear slices, a dash of pepper, and 1/2 cup mozzarella cheese.
3. Top with remaining bread.
4. Lightly spread margarine on the outer sides of each sandwich.
5. Heat a skillet or griddle over medium heat.
6. Grill the sandwiches until the cheese is melted and the bread is golden brown, about 3 minutes per side.
7. Cut each sandwich in half to serve.
