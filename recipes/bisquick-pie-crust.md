## Ingredients

- 1 cup baking mix (such as Bisquick®)
- ¼ cup butter, softened
- 2 tablespoons boiling water, or more as needed

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C).
2. Mix baking mix and butter together in a small bowl. Add water, stirring vigorously, until a soft dough forms.
3. Press dough into a 9-inch pie plate using floured hands.
4. Bake in the preheated oven until light brown, 8 to 10 minutes. Let cool completely; fill with desired filling.
