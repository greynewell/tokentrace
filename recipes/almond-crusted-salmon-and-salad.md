## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons olive oil, divided
- 2 teaspoons lemon juice, divided
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- 2 (8 ounce) fillets wild salmon fillets
- ½ teaspoon Himalayan salt, or to taste
- ½ cup finely crushed almonds
- 1 (6 ounce) package baby spinach leaves
- 1 cup cherry tomatoes

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 500 degrees F (260 degrees C).
2. Grease a baking pan with 1 tablespoon olive oil.
3. Mix remaining olive oil, 1 teaspoon lemon juice, salt, and black pepper together in a bowl to form the salad dressing.
4. Brush salmon fillets with remaining 1 teaspoon lemon juice.
5. Sprinkle with Himalayan salt and black pepper.
6. Cover top and sides of salmon with almonds.
7. Place salmon skin-side down in the prepared baking pan.
8. Sprinkle any remaining almonds on top, pressing gently to adhere.
9. Bake in the preheated oven until salmon flakes easily with a fork, about 15 minutes.
10. Let cool; cut each fillet in half.
11. Place spinach and tomatoes in a bowl.
12. Add dressing; toss to coat.
13. Serve alongside the salmon.
