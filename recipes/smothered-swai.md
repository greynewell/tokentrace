## Ingredients

- 5 (4 ounce) fillets swai fish
- 2 tablespoons lemon juice, or more to taste
- 1 teaspoon dried parsley
- ½ teaspoon garlic powder
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- 1 cup shredded sharp Cheddar cheese
- 1 (15 ounce) can fire-roasted diced tomatoes
- ½ teaspoon dried basil

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Arrange swai fish on an 8x11-inch baking dish.
3. Pour lemon juice over fish and season with parsley, garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
4. Sprinkle Cheddar cheese over fish and top with fire-roasted tomatoes and basil.
5. Bake in the preheated oven until fish flakes easily with a fork, about 30 minutes.
