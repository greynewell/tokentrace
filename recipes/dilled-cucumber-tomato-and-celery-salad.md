## Ingredients

- ⅓ cup water
- 1 tablespoon dill seed
- 1 tablespoon honey
- ⅓ cup buttermilk salad dressing and seasoning mix
- 2 cups mayonnaise
- 1 cup buttermilk
- 1 ⅓ cups milk
- 1 cup white vinegar
- 2 large cucumbers, quartered and thinly sliced
- 3  tomatoes, cubed
- 2 stalks celery, chopped
- ½  onion, sliced and quartered
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 teaspoon salt

## Instructions

1. Place water and dill seed in a small saucepan and bring to a simmer over medium-high heat.
2. Reduce heat to medium-low and simmer 10 minutes.
3. Whisk in honey and salad dressing mix, then pour mixture into a large bowl.
4. Whisk in mayonnaise, buttermilk, milk, and vinegar until smooth.
5. Place cucumbers, tomatoes, celery, onion, garlic, and salt into a separate bowl, and pour half of the dressing onto the vegetables; toss well.
6. Cover, and refrigerate salad for at least 1 hour.
7. Cover and refrigerate remaining dressing for future use.
