## Ingredients

- 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
- 10 ounces extra sharp Cheddar cheese, shredded
- 1 (1 ounce) envelope dry onion soup mix
- 1 cup chopped walnuts

## Instructions

1. Thoroughly mix together the softened cream cheese, Cheddar cheese, and onion soup mix in a bowl.
2. Chill in refrigerator for about 1/2 hour to firm up, if necessary.
3. Form the mixture into a ball or log, and press the outside with walnuts.
4. Serve on an appetizer tray or a decorative plate surrounded by assorted crackers.
