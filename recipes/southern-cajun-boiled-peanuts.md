## Ingredients

- 5 pounds raw peanuts, in shells
- ¾ cup salt
- 1 ¼ cups dill pickle slices, with brine
- 5 small jalapeno peppers, sliced
- 5 tablespoons dry crab boil (such as Zatarain's® Crab and Shrimp Boil)

## Instructions

1. Place peanuts, salt, pickles with brine, jalapenos, and crab boil in a large pot.
2. Pour in water to cover the peanuts; stir to combine.
3. Bring to a boil over medium-high heat, and cook until soft, at least 4 hours, adding water as needed to keep peanuts covered.
4. Scoop peanuts out of the pot with a slotted spoon and serve hot.
