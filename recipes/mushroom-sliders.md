## Ingredients

- 1 pound lean ground beef, or more to taste
- 1  large egg
- 1 small onion, finely chopped
- 1 cup finely chopped mushrooms
- 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
- ½ teaspoon garlic salt
- salt to taste
- 6  portobello mushrooms, or more to taste
- 1  green bell pepper, halved and seeded
- 1  red bell pepper, halved and seeded
- 1  yellow bell pepper, halved and seeded

## Instructions

1. Preheat an outdoor grill for medium-high heat and lightly oil the grate.
2. Mix ground beef, egg, onion, and chopped mushrooms together in a bowl, seasoning with pepper, garlic salt, and salt as you mix.
3. Form mixture into patties about 2 inches in diameter.
4. Grill patties on the preheated grill until browned and desired doneness is reached, 5 to 7 minutes per side.
5. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 160 degrees F (70 degrees C).
6. Grill portobello mushrooms, green bell pepper, red bell pepper, and yellow bell pepper on the grill until tender, 3 to 5 minutes per side.
7. Use 2 mushrooms and as the 'bun' for the burgers.
