## Ingredients

- 2 (8 ounce) packages pimento cheese food
- 8 ounces nonfat cottage cheese
- 2 teaspoons sweet pickle relish  (Optional)

## Instructions

1. Place pimento cheese, cottage cheese, and sweet pickle relish in a blender and blend until smooth.
2. Keep cheese in a closed plastic container until ready to use.
