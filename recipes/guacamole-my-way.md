## Ingredients

- 2  avocados from Mexico, peeled, pitted and diced
- 2  Roma tomatoes, seeded and diced
- 4 tablespoons finely diced onion
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- ½  lime, juiced
- ½ teaspoon salt, or to taste

## Instructions

1. Combine avocados, tomatoes, onion, garlic and lime juice.
2. Stir well, mashing but leaving some chunks of avocado.
3. Season with salt; stir to combine.
4. Serve immediately or chill for 30 minutes.
