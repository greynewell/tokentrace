## Ingredients

- 1 (8 ounce) wedge Brie cheese
- ½ cup salted butter
- 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Remove and discard rind from Brie.
3. Slice cheese into about 12 pieces and place in a microwave-safe bowl.
4. Add cream cheese and butter.
5. Microwave at 50% power, stirring every 45 seconds, until all ingredients are melted together.
6. Stir vigorously until smooth.
7. Transfer to a small (2-cup) oven-proof baking dish.
8. Bake in the preheated oven until dip is bubbly at the edges, 15 to 20 minutes.
