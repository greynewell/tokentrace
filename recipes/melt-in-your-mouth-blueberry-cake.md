## Ingredients

- ½ cup butter
- ½ cup white sugar
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 2  egg yolks
- 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- ⅓ cup milk
- 2  egg whites
- ¼ cup white sugar
- 1 ½ cups fresh blueberries
- 1 tablespoon all-purpose flour
- 1 tablespoon white sugar

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour an 8-inch pan.
2. Beat butter and 1/2 cup sugar together in a large bowl with an electric mixer until soft and fluffy; mix in vanilla and salt. Add egg yolks and beat until creamy.
3. Combine 1 1/2 cups flour and baking powder in a bowl; add alternately with milk to creamed mixture.
4. Toss blueberries with 1 tablespoon flour in a small bowl. Add to the batter and fold in gently.
5. Beat egg whites in a separate bowl until soft peaks form. Add remaining 1/4 cup of sugar, 1 tablespoon at a time, and beat until stiff peaks form. Fold egg whites into cake batter.
6. Pour batter into the prepared pan. Sprinkle remaining 1 tablespoon sugar over top.
7. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted in center comes out clean, about 50 minutes.
