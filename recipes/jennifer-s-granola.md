## Ingredients

- 4 cups rolled oats
- 1 cup wheat germ
- ½ cup flax seed meal
- ½ cup brown sugar
- ½ cup raw sunflower seeds
- ½ cup chopped pecans
- ½ cup sliced almonds
- 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
- 1 ½ teaspoons salt
- ⅓ cup canola oil
- ¼ cup honey
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- ½ cup water

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 300 degrees F (150 degrees C).
2. In a large bowl, mix together the oats, wheat germ, flax seed meal, brown sugar, sunflower seeds, pecans, almonds, cinnamon and salt.
3. In a separate bowl, whisk together the oil, honey, vanilla and water.
4. Pour the wet ingredients over the dry, and mix until evenly blended.
5. Spread in a greased 9x13 or 11x7 inch baking pan.
6. Bake for 1 hour in the preheated oven, stirring every 20 minutes, or until toasted.
7. Let cool completely before storing in an airtight container.
