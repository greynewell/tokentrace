## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon kosher salt
- ½ tablespoon ground black pepper
- 1 teaspoon garlic powder
- 1 teaspoon onion powder
- 1 teaspoon paprika
- 1 teaspoon dried parsley
- ½ teaspoon ground mustard
- ¼ teaspoon celery seed
- 4 pounds chicken drumsticks

## Instructions

1. Combine salt, pepper, garlic powder, onion powder, paprika, parsley, mustard, and celery seed in a small bowl. Stir until evenly combined. Lay drumsticks on a clean work surface and rub all sides with the spice mixture.
2. Preheat an electric smoker to 250 degrees F (120 degrees C).
3. Lay drumsticks directly on the smoker racks and smoke for 2 1/2 hours.
