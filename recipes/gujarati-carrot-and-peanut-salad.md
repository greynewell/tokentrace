## Ingredients

- 2 cups grated carrots
- ½ cup chopped salted peanuts
- 3 tablespoons lemon juice
- ½ teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon white sugar
- 1  green chile pepper, seeded and diced
- 2 tablespoons finely chopped fresh cilantro

## Instructions

1. In a medium serving bowl, toss together the carrots and peanuts.
2. In a separate bowl, whisk together the lemon juice, salt, sugar, chile pepper and cilantro.
3. Pour over the carrots and stir gently to coat.
4. Serve immediately.
