## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 3 cups fresh chopped mushrooms
- 2  green onions, chopped
- 1 clove garlic, crushed
- ½ teaspoon ground cayenne pepper
- 4 ounces cream cheese, softened
- 1 (6 ounce) can crabmeat, drained and flaked
- 1 (17.5 ounce) package frozen puff pastry sheets, thawed

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
2. Heat the olive oil in a medium saucepan over medium heat.
3. Stir in the mushrooms, green onions, garlic and cayenne pepper.
4. Cook 10 minutes, or until tender.
5. Transfer the mushroom mixture to a medium bowl.
6. Blend in the cream cheese and crabmeat.
7. On a lightly floured flat surface, roll each pastry sheet into a 12x12 inch square.
8. Cut each sheet into nine 4x4 inch squares.
9. Place 1 tablespoon of the mushroom mixture onto each pastry square.
10. Fold the squares by bringing the corners to the center, creating an X.
11. Place the squares on a medium baking sheet.
12. Bake 20 minutes in the preheated oven, or until golden brown.
