## Ingredients

- ⅓ cup white sugar
- ½ cup white vinegar
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon ground dry mustard
- 1 teaspoon grated onion
- 1 cup vegetable oil
- 1 tablespoon poppy seeds

## Instructions

1. Place vinegar, sugar, onion, mustard, and salt into a blender or food processor; blend for 20 seconds. With the blender running, gradually add oil in a thin steady stream until combined.
2. Transfer dressing to a bowl and stir in poppy seeds.
