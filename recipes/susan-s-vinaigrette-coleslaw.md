## Ingredients

- 3 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
- 3 tablespoons canola oil
- 3 tablespoons white sugar
- ¼ teaspoon dry mustard
- ¼ teaspoon poppy seeds
- ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- ¼ teaspoon hot pepper sauce  (Optional)
- 4 cups shredded green cabbage
- 2  carrots, shredded

## Instructions

1. Whisk apple cider vinegar, canola oil, sugar, dry mustard, poppy seeds, black pepper, salt, and hot pepper sauce in a bowl, stirring until sugar has dissolved.
2. Mix cabbage with carrots in a large salad bowl and pour dressing over slaw. Stir to coat. Refrigerate at least 2 hours before serving.
