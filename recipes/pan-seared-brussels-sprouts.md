## Ingredients

- 20  Brussels sprouts
- 2 teaspoons Dijon mustard
- 2 teaspoons honey
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 2 tablespoons sliced almonds
- 2 tablespoons dried cherries
- 1 pinch sea salt

## Instructions

1. Slice each Brussels sprout into 5 or 6 slices.
2. Mix Dijon mustard and honey together in a small bowl.
3. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over high heat. Add Brussels sprouts and cook for 1 minute. Pour in Dijon-honey mixture; cook and stir continuously until Brussels sprouts start to brown, 2 to 3 minutes. Add almonds and cherries in the last minute of cooking. Season with sea salt.
