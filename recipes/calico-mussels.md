## Ingredients

- ½ cup white wine
- 1 small onion, minced
- 1  lemon wedge
- 2 pounds mussels, cleaned and debearded
- ¼ cup olive oil
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 small carrot, shredded
- ½  red bell pepper, chopped
- ½  yellow bell pepper, chopped
- ¼ cup chopped fresh parsley
- 3 tablespoons red wine vinegar
- ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
- 1 pinch salt

## Instructions

1. Combine wine, onion, and lemon in a large skillet; add mussels.
2. Bring mixture to a simmer and steam mussels until they break open, about 5 minutes.
3. Transfer mussels to a bowl using a slotted spoon and continue simmering wine mixture until slightly reduced, about 5 more minutes.
4. Remove mussels from shells, reserving some shells for serving.
5. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium-low heat; cook and stir garlic until fragrant, about 2 minutes.
6. Stir reduced wine, carrot, red bell pepper, yellow bell pepper, parsley, vinegar, pepper, and salt into garlic; cook until heated through, 3 to 5 minutes.
7. Mix mussels and vegetable-wine mixture together in a bowl; marinate in refrigerator, about 24 hours.
8. Serve by scooping a mussel and marinade into reserved half shells arranged on a platter.
