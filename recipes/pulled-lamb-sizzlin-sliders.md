## Ingredients

- 3 pounds lamb shanks, on the bone
- ⅔ cup water
- 1  jalapeno, cut in half width-wise
- 8  ciabatta bread dinner rolls
- 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
- 2  green bell pepper, sliced
- 1  onion, sliced
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- 1 cup mayonnaise
- 3 tablespoons Sriracha sauce
- 5 ounces fresh spinach
- 1  jalapeno, seeded and sliced

## Instructions

1. Place lamb shanks into a slow cooker; add water.
2. Stir stem side of the halved jalapeno to the slow cooker, reserving the other end.
3. Set slow cooker to High; cook until lamb is tender and can be easily pulled apart with a fork, 1 to 2 hours.
4. An instant-read thermometer inserted near the bone should read 165 degrees F (74 degrees C).
5. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
6. Place ciabatta rolls onto a baking sheet.
7. Bake in the preheated oven until crisp and browned, 10 to 15 minutes.
8. Remove jalapeno half from the slow cooker; dice, along with the reserved half.
9. Heat oil in a skillet over medium-high heat.
10. Stir in bell peppers, onion, diced jalapeno, salt, and pepper; cook and stir until bell peppers are tender and onion is translucent, 7 to 10 minutes.
11. Combine mayonnaise and chile-garlic sauce in a small bowl; stir until sauce is mixed.
12. Remove lamb from the slow cooker.
13. Separate lamb meat from the bone; discard bones.
14. Shred lamb with a fork; divide into 8 equal portions.
15. Spread sauce onto the top half of the ciabatta; cover with spinach leaves.
16. Spoon lamb and vegetable mixture onto the bottom half of the ciabatta; top with sliced jalapeno.
