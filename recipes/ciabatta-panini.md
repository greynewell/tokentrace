## Ingredients

- 8 slices provolone cheese
- 4  ciabatta rolls, split
- 12 slices pancetta
- 2 cups baby spinach leaves
- 1 large tomato, sliced
- 2  roasted red bell peppers, sliced
- ½ small red onion, thinly sliced
- ¼ cup olive oil
- 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar
- salt and ground black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Preheat panini press and lightly oil the grates.
2. Place a slice of provolone cheese on both top and bottom pieces of ciabatta bread.
3. Layer 3 slices pancetta and 1/4 cup spinach over the 4 bottom slices of bread.
4. Divide tomato, bell peppers, and onion equally among the sandwiches.
5. Drizzle each sandwich with about 1 teaspoon each olive oil and vinegar; season with salt and pepper.
6. Replace tops of ciabatta rolls and brush with the remaining olive oil.
7. Place sandwiches on the panini grill; close the press tightly on top.
8. Cook until golden brown and cheese is melted, about 5 minutes.
