## Ingredients

- 1 pint ripe cherry tomatoes
- ½ cup pepper-flavored vodka
- 3 tablespoons sea salt
- 1 tablespoon lemon pepper

## Instructions

1. Poke 5 or 6 holes with a wooden pick or skewer in each tomato and put in a shallow bowl.
2. Pour vodka over tomatoes.
3. Cover bowl with plastic wrap and marinate until the flavors blend, 1 to 2 hours.
4. Mix sea salt and lemon pepper in a small bowl; serve with the marinated tomatoes for dipping.
