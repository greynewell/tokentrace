## Ingredients

- 2 slices honey whole wheat bread, toasted
- 1 tablespoon mayonnaise
- 2  slices of smoked turkey breast
- 1 tablespoon whole berry cranberry sauce
- 1 tablespoon chopped walnuts
- 1  lettuce leaf

## Instructions

1. Spread one side of each toast piece with mayonnaise.
2. Place smoked turkey breast slices onto one piece of toast and spread turkey with cranberry sauce; sprinkle with walnuts.
3. Top with lettuce leaf and the remaining toast to make a sandwich.
