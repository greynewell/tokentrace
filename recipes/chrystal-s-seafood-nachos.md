## Ingredients

- 30  baked tortilla chips
- 1 (8 ounce) package imitation crabmeat
- ¼ cup reduced-fat sour cream
- ¼ cup reduced-fat mayonnaise
- 2 tablespoons finely chopped onion
- ¼ teaspoon dill weed  (Optional)
- ½ (8 ounce) package shredded reduced-fat Cheddar cheese
- ¼ teaspoon paprika

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Arrange tortilla chips in a single layer on an ungreased baking sheet.
3. Mix imitation crabmeat, sour cream, mayonnaise, onion, and dill together in a bowl.
4. Drop 1 tablespoonful of mixture on each chip.
5. Sprinkle Cheddar cheese and paprika on top.
6. Bake in the preheated oven until Cheddar cheese is melted, 6 to 8 minutes.
