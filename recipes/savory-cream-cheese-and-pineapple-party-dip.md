## Ingredients

- 2 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened
- 1 (8 ounce) can canned crushed pineapple, drained
- 1 cup chopped pecans
- ½ cup chopped green bell pepper
- 2 tablespoons chopped green onion
- 1 teaspoon salt, or to taste

## Instructions

1. Combine cream cheese, crushed pineapple, pecans. bell pepper, most of the green onion, and salt in a bowl, reserving some of the green onion tops for garnish.
2. Serve immediately if desired with reserved green onions sprinkled over, or cover with plastic wrap and refrigerate until ready to serve.
