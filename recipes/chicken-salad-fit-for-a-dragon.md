## Ingredients

- 2 cups cooked chicken, diced
- ½ cup mayonnaise
- 1 large stalk celery, diced
- ½ cup pine nuts
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice
- 1 ½ tablespoons sweet pickle relish
- 1 small jalapeno pepper, seeded and minced
- ¼ teaspoon cracked black pepper, or to taste

## Instructions

1. Stir the chicken, mayonnaise, celery, pine nuts, lemon juice, relish, jalapeno pepper, and black pepper together in a mixing bowl until evenly blended.
2. Cover, and refrigerate 20 to 30 minutes to allow the flavors to blend.
