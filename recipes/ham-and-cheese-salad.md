## Ingredients

- 2 cups chopped ham
- 1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
- 2 stalks celery, chopped
- ⅓ cup mayonnaise
- 1 ½ tablespoons prepared mustard

## Instructions

1. In a food processor, combine the ham and celery; pulse until finely chopped.
2. Add cheese and pulse until mixed.
3. Place mixture in a bowl and add the mayonnaise and mustard.
4. Mix well; serve on sandwich or pita bread.
