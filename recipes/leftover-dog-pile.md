## Ingredients

- 2  hot dogs
- ¾ cup chili with beans
- 1 slice bread
- ¼ cup shredded Cheddar cheese, divided
- 1 tablespoon chopped onion
- 2 tablespoons prepared yellow mustard

## Instructions

1. Place the hot dogs onto a microwave safe plate and cook in the microwave 1 to 2 minutes until hot.
2. Place the chili into a microwave safe bowl and microwave 1 to 2 minutes until hot.
3. Slice the hot dogs in half lengthwise, and place onto the bread slice cut-side down; sprinkle with half of the cheese, the chili, the remaining cheese, onion, and mustard.
