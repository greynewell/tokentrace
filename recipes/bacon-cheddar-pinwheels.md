## Ingredients

- 1 (8 ounce) can Pillsbury® refrigerated crescent dinner rolls or Pillsbury® Crescent Recipe Creations® refrigerated seamless dough sheet
- 2 tablespoons ranch dressing
- ¼ cup cooked real bacon pieces
- ½ cup finely shredded Cheddar cheese
- ¼ cup chopped green onions

## Instructions

1. Heat oven to 350 degrees F.
2. If using crescent rolls: Unroll dough; separate into 2 long rectangles.
3. Press each into 12x4-inch rectangle, firmly pressing perforations to seal.
4. If using dough sheet: Unroll dough; cut lengthwise into 2 long rectangles.
5. Press each into 12x4-inch rectangle.
6. Spread dressing over each rectangle to edges.
7. Sprinkle each with bacon, Cheddar cheese and onions.
8. Starting with one short side, roll up each rectangle; press edge to seal.
9. With serrated knife, cut each roll into 8 slices; place cut side down on ungreased cookie sheet.
10. Bake 12 to 17 minutes or until edges are deep golden brown.
11. Immediately remove from cookie sheet.
12. Serve warm.
