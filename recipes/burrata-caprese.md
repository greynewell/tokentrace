## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
- salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
- 2 medium ripe tomatoes, diced
- 1 (4 ounce) ball burrata cheese, diced
- 6 leaves fresh basil, finely chopped

## Instructions

1. Mix together olive oil, vinegar, salt, and pepper in a large bowl until well combined.
2. Add tomatoes, burrata cheese, and basil. Toss to combine. Serve immediately.
