## Ingredients

- 1  bone-in pork chop
- 4 cups water, or more as needed
- ¼  onion
- 2 teaspoons chicken base
- 1 clove garlic

## Instructions

1. Cut bones and fat from pork chop and cut meat into small pieces.
2. Place bone, fat, and meat in a saucepan; add water, onion, chicken base, and garlic.
3. Bring liquid to a boil, reduce heat to medium-low, cover saucepan, and simmer, adding more water every hour, 5 to 6 hours.
4. Strain broth into a bowl and separate bones from the meat.
5. Put meat and 1/2 of the broth in a blender; blend.
6. Return blended broth to broth in saucepan and mix well.
