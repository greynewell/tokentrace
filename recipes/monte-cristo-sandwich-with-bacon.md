## Ingredients

- 2 slices bacon
- 1 teaspoon vegetable oil, or as needed
- 2 tablespoons milk
- 1  egg, beaten
- 1 pinch ground nutmeg
- 2 slices bread
- 1 tablespoon whole berry cranberry sauce, or more to taste
- 2 slices smoked turkey
- 1 slice provolone cheese
- 1 tablespoon brown mustard, or more to taste
- 2 slices cooked ham

## Instructions

1. Place bacon in a large skillet and cook over medium-high heat, turning occasionally, until evenly browned, about 10 minutes.
2. Drain bacon slices on paper towels.
3. Break each piece in half.
4. Heat oil in a skillet over medium heat.
5. Whisk milk, egg, and nutmeg together in a shallow bowl.
6. Dip each bread slice in the egg mixture.
7. Cook dipped bread slices in the hot oil until   is lightly browned, 2 to 3 minute per side.
8. Transfer French toast to a work surface and turn heat  under skillet to medium-low.
9. Spread cranberry sauce over 1 French toast piece and top with turkey, provolone cheese, and bacon.
10. Spread mustard on the other French toast piece and top with ham; lay on top of bacon layer, ham-side down, creating a sandwich.
11. Cook sandwich in the skillet until cheese is melted and sandwich is heated through, 3 to 4 minutes per side.
