## Ingredients

- 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
- ⅓ cup mayonnaise
- 3  green onions, chopped
- 1 pinch ground black pepper
- 1 pinch ground nutmeg
- 8 ounces shredded Swiss cheese
- ¼ cup sliced almonds

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. In a medium bowl, combine the cream cheese, mayonnaise, onions, pepper and nutmeg.
3. Stir in the Swiss cheese.
4. Transfer to small ovenproof glass bowl.
5. Sprinkle 1/2 of the almonds on top.
6. Bake for 7 minutes.
7. Stir, and bake for an additional 8 minutes.
8. Sprinkle remaining almonds on top, and serve.
