## Ingredients

- 3  Yukon Gold potatoes, cubed
- 3  red potatoes, cubed
- ¼ cup canola oil
- 3 tablespoons distilled white vinegar
- 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
- ½ teaspoon celery salt
- ¼ teaspoon pepper

## Instructions

1. Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat.
2. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil, and cook potatoes 10 to 15 minutes, until tender but firm.
3. Drain, and transfer to a medium bowl.
4. In a small bowl, mix canola oil, vinegar, Dijon mustard, celery salt, and pepper.
5. Toss 1/2 the mixture with the potatoes.
6. Arrange potatoes in a single layer on a sheet of foil, and place on the prepared grill.
7. Turning occasionally, cook 7 to 9 minutes, until lightly browned.
8. Remove from heat, and cool.
9. Toss with remaining oil mixture to serve.
