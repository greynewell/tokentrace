## Ingredients

- 1 pound haddock fillets
- 1 teaspoon salt-free seasoning blend (such as Mrs. Dash®)
- 2  oranges, zested and juiced
- ¾ cup panko bread crumbs
- ¼ cup grated Parmesan cheese
- 2 tablespoons buttery spread (such as Smart Balance®), melted
- ½ teaspoon dried parsley
- ¼ teaspoon ground thyme

## Instructions

1. Arrange haddock fillets in a large baking dish.
2. Season with salt-free seasoning blend.
3. Pour orange juice over fish and cover with plastic wrap, reserve orange zest.
4. Marinate in the refrigerator for at least 2 hours.
5. Preheat oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C).
6. Stir panko bread crumbs, Parmesan cheese, buttery spread, parsley, thyme, and reserved orange zest in a bowl until well mixed.
7. Press crumb mixture over haddock.
8. Bake in the preheated oven until fish is easily flaked with a fork and topping is golden brown, 20 to 25 minutes.
9. Allow fish to rest 5 minutes before serving.
