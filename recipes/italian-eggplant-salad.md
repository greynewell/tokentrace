## Ingredients

- 6  eggplants
- 1 clove garlic, crushed
- 3 tablespoons olive oil
- 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
- 2 tablespoons white sugar
- 1 teaspoon dried parsley
- 1 teaspoon dried oregano
- ¼ teaspoon dried basil
- salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Puncture eggplants with a fork, and place on a baking sheet.
3. Bake for 1 1/2 hours, or until soft, turning occasionally.
4. Cool, then peel and dice.
5. In a large bowl, stir together the garlic, olive oil, vinegar, sugar, parsley, oregano, basil, salt and pepper.
6. Add the diced eggplant, and stir to coat.
7. Refrigerate for at least 2 hours before serving to marinate.
