## Ingredients

- 1 cup shredded Swiss cheese
- ½ cup mayonnaise
- ½ cup sour cream
- 1 (.7 ounce) package dry Italian-style salad dressing mix
- 24 thin slices  baguette
- 1  green bell pepper, minced

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
2. Mix Swiss cheese, mayonnaise, sour cream, and Italian-style salad dressing mix in a bowl until well blended.
3. Spread cheese mixture onto one side of each bread slice.
4. Sprinkle green bell pepper over the cheese mixture.
5. Arrange bread onto a baking sheet.
6. Bake bread slices in preheated oven until the cheese is bubbly and the bread toasted, 10 to 12 minutes.
