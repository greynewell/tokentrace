## Ingredients

- ⅓ cup medium-coarse ground coffee
- 1 ½ cups filtered water
- 1 cup Coffee ice cubes
- 2 packets Sweet'N Low granulated sugar substitute
- 1 teaspoon Milk

## Instructions

1. Fill a tall glass with coffee ice cubes.
2. Pour in the cold brewed coffee.
3. Sweeten to taste with Sweet'N Low.
4. Add milk as desired.
5. To Make Cold-Brewed Coffee: Place the coffee in a glass jar, add the water, and stir to combine.
6. Cover and set aside at room temperature for 12 hours or overnight.
7. Strain the coffee through a large-size paper coffee filter, a fine sieve, or a strainer lined with cheesecloth.
8. Cold-brewed coffee can be refrigerated in a covered jar for up to 24 hours.
9. This recipe can be doubled.
