## Ingredients

- 1 (6.5 ounce) can minced clams, with juice
- 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
- 1 small sweet onion, minced
- 2 drops hot pepper sauce (such as Tabasco®), or to taste

## Instructions

1. With a fork, mash the cream cheese in a bowl, adding a little bit of the clam juice at a time until smooth.
2. Fold the clams and onions into the cream cheese mixture until evenly mixed.
3. Stir the hot pepper sauce into the dip.
