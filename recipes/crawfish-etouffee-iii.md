## Ingredients

- 1  onion, chopped
- 1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed tomato soup
- 1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of mushroom soup
- 1 (10 ounce) can diced tomatoes with green chile peppers
- 1 ½ pounds crawfish, peeled

## Instructions

1. In a large saucepan on medium heat, saute onion until clear.
2. Add tomato soup, mushroom soup, and canned tomatoes.
3. Simmer for 15 minutes.
4. Place the crawfish into the pan and simmer for 25 minutes.
