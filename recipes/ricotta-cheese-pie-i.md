## Ingredients

- 1 ½ cups ricotta cheese
- 2  eggs
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- ½ cup white sugar
- 1 ½ cups milk
- 2 tablespoons cornstarch
- 1 recipe pastry for a 9 inch single crust pie
- ½ teaspoon ground cinnamon

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Dissolve cornstarch in 1/2 cup milk in a large bowl; stir in ricotta, remaining 1 cup milk, eggs, vanilla, and sugar until well blended.
3. Pour filling into unbaked pie crust.
4. Bake in the preheated oven for 1 hour, or until a knife inserted into the center comes out clean. Sprinkle top with cinnamon; cool.
