## Ingredients

- 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
- 1 ½ cups cornmeal
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- ¾ teaspoon salt
- 1 cup warm water (110 degrees F/45 degrees C)

## Instructions

1. In a large bowl, stir together the flour and cornmeal.
2. Add baking powder and salt, and mix together well.
3. Stir in water to form a crumbly dough.
4. Work dough with your hands until it holds together.
5. On a floured surface, knead dough until smooth.
6. Divide dough into 10 to 12 pieces.
7. Roll each into the shape of a ball.
8. Cover lightly with saran wrap and let rest for 10 to 15 minutes.
9. Flatten each piece of dough by hand, then roll into a 8 to 9 inch round.
10. Heat a large skillet over medium heat.
11. Lightly grease the hot pan, and place one tortilla in the pan.
12. Cook tortilla until light brown, turning to ensure equal browning.
13. Repeat with remaining tortillas.
