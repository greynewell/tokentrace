## Ingredients

- 2 cups oat bran
- ½ cup whole wheat flour
- ¼ cup firmly packed brown sugar
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 2 teaspoons pumpkin pie spice
- 1 teaspoon baking soda
- ½ teaspoon salt
- 1 cup milk
- ¼ cup molasses
- 1 large egg, slightly beaten
- 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- ¾ cup pumpkin puree

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C).
2. Spray a 12-cup muffin tin with cooking spray or line with paper liners.
3. Combine oat bran, flour, brown sugar, baking powder, pumpkin pie spice, baking soda, and salt in a bowl and mix thoroughly.
4. Combine milk, molasses, egg, vegetable oil, and vanilla extract in a separate bowl and mix well.
5. Stir into dry ingredients until just moistened.
6. Add pumpkin puree and mix just enough to evenly distribute; do not overstir.
7. Spoon batter into the prepared muffin cups, filling each 3/4 full.
8. Bake in the preheated oven until golden brown, 15 to 17 minutes.
