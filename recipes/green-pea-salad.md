## Ingredients

- 1 (16 ounce) package frozen green peas
- 1 (8 ounce) can sliced water chestnuts, drained and cut into strip
- 6  roma (plum) tomatoes, chopped
- ½  onion, minced
- 2 medium sweet pickles, finely chopped
- ¾ cup mayonnaise
- ⅛ cup lemon juice

## Instructions

1. In a colander, rinse frozen peas in cold water until thawed.
2. Drain, and transfer to a large salad bowl.
3. Add water chestnuts, tomatoes, onion, and sweet pickles; mix well.
4. In a small bowl, mix together mayonnaise and a little pickle or lemon juice.
5. Do not make the mixture to soupy.
6. Pour over vegetables, and gently stir to coat.
