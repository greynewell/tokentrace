## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons butter
- 2 cups uncooked basmati rice
- 1 small onion, chopped
- 4 cups water
- 2 teaspoons salt
- 2  whole cinnamon sticks
- 1 large bay leaf

## Instructions

1. Melt the butter in a large saucepan over medium heat, and cook and stir the basmati rice and onion until the rice kernels are coated with butter and the onion is translucent, 5 to 8 minutes.
2. The rice will give off a slightly toasted fragrance.
3. Pour in water, and mix in salt, cinnamon sticks and bay leaf.
4. Bring the mixture to a boil, and reduce heat to medium-low.
5. Cover the saucepan, and simmer the rice until all the water has disappeared and the rice appears dry, about 18 minutes.
6. Fluff the rice with a fork and serve.
