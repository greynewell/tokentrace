## Ingredients

- 1 (12 ounce) can fully cooked luncheon meat (such as SPAM®), cubed
- ¾ (1 pound) loaf processed cheese food (such as Velveeta®), cubed
- 2 tablespoons sweet pickle relish
- ½ cup creamy salad dressing (such as Miracle Whip®)
- 8  hamburger buns, split

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Stir together luncheon meat, processed cheese, relish, and salad dressing in a bowl. Spoon filling into sandwich buns, and wrap each sandwich individually in aluminum foil. Place sandwiches onto a baking sheet.
3. Bake in the preheated oven until filling is hot, and buns have toasted, 10 to 15 minutes.
