## Ingredients

- ½ cup milk
- 2 cups white sugar
- 3 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa powder
- 3 tablespoons crunchy peanut butter
- ½ cup butter
- 3 cups rolled oats
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

## Instructions

1. Wipe 1" wide band of butter around the rim of a 3 quart pan to prevent boil-over.
2. Combine milk, sugar, cocoa, butter and peanut butter.
3. Stir and bring to boil over medium heat.
4. Let boil for 1 1/2 minutes, do not stir.
5. Remove from heat.
6. Stir in oats and vanilla.
7. Stir until oats evenly distributed.
8. Drop by teaspoon onto waxed paper.
9. Cool.
10. Makes 4 to 5 dozen.
