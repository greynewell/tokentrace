## Ingredients

- 10  hot dogs
- 2 slices Pepper Jack cheese
- 10 slices bacon
- 4 cups oil for deep frying
- 10  hot dog buns
- 20 toothpicks

## Instructions

1. Slice each hot dog 3/4 of the way through lengthwise.
2. Cut each slice of cheese into 5 strips, and insert 1 strip of cheese into each hot dog.
3. Wrap bacon snugly around each hot dog, securing each end with a toothpick.
4. Preheat oil in a saucepan to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
5. Carefully drop the hot dogs in the hot oil, and fry for 1 to 2 minutes, or until bacon is brown and crispy.
6. Remove from oil, and drain on paper towels.
7. Remove and discard toothpicks.
8. Serve on buns with your favorite condiments.
