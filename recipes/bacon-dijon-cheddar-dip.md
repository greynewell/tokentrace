## Ingredients

- 4 slices bacon
- 8 ounces cream cheese, softened
- 1 cup shredded sharp Cheddar cheese
- 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
- 2 tablespoons finely chopped onion
- 1 teaspoon chopped fresh parsley  (Optional)

## Instructions

1. Place the bacon in a large, deep skillet, and cook over medium-high heat, turning occasionally, until evenly browned, about 10 minutes.
2. Drain the bacon slices on a paper towel-lined plate, let cool, and crumble the bacon.
3. Place the cream cheese, Cheddar cheese, and Dijon mustard into a bowl, and mix on medium speed with an electric mixer until the ingredients are thoroughly blended.
4. Stir in the onion and crumbled bacon, and spoon into a serving bowl.
5. Refrigerate for 2 hours to blend the flavors, and sprinkle with parsley to serve.
