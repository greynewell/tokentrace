## Ingredients

- 1 (28 ounce) bottle ketchup
- 2 teaspoons hot pepper sauce
- 1 tablespoon cayenne pepper, or to taste
- 1 tablespoon dried onion granules
- 2 teaspoons granulated garlic
- 2 teaspoons chili powder

## Instructions

1. In a large bowl, stir together the ketchup, hot pepper sauce, cayenne pepper, dried onion, dried garlic and chili powder.
2. Return to the ketchup bottle or another squeeze bottle and store in the refrigerator.
3. Use as you would ketchup.
