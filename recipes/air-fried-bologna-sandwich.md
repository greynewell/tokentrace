## Ingredients

- 2 slices white sandwich bread
- 1 slice Bologna, beef
- 1 teaspoon mayonnaise
- 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
- 1 slice Cheddar cheese
- 1 slice tomato
- 1 leaf lettuce

## Instructions

1. Preheat an air fryer to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
2. Place 1 slice of bread in the air fryer basket and cook for 1 minute. Transfer to a plate. Repeat with remaining slice of bread.
3. Remove casing from bologna. Using a knife, make 4 slits to prevent curling. Place bologna in the basket of the air fryer and cook for 3 minutes. Flip bologna over and cook for 3 minutes more.
4. Spread mayonnaise on 1 slice of bread, mustard on the other. Assemble sandwich by layering cheese, bologna, tomato, and lettuce on 1 piece of bread and top with other piece of bread.
