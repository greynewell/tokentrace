## Ingredients

- 8  beef franks
- 8  hot dog buns, split
- 1 (15 ounce) can HORMEL® Chili No Beans, heated
- Shredded cheese
- 1 teaspoon Sliced green onions

## Instructions

1. Cook franks according to package directions.
2. Place franks in buns; spoon hot chili over franks.
3. Top with cheese, and green onions, if desired.
