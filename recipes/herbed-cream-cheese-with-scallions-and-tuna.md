## Ingredients

- 3  scallions (green onions), roughly chopped
- 1 teaspoon grated lemon zest (peel)
- 1 teaspoon dried basil
- 2 teaspoons lemon juice
- 8 ounces light cream cheese
- 6 ounces canned white albacore tuna, drained and broken into small chunks
- Salt and pepper, to taste

## Instructions

1. Process scallions, zest and basil in a food processor until minced.
2. Add lemon juice and cream cheese; pulse until combined.
3. Transfer to a small bowl.
4. Stir in tuna, and season with salt and pepper.
5. Refrigerate.
