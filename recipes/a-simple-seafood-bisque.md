## Ingredients

- 1 (12 ounce) can evaporated milk
- ½ cup half-and-half
- ½ cup dry white wine
- 1  roasted red pepper, chopped
- 2 teaspoons butter
- 1  bay leaf
- 1 pinch salt
- 1 dash hot pepper sauce (such as Tabasco®)
- 2 (8 ounce) cans oysters, drained and rinsed
- 2 (6.5 ounce) cans chopped clams with juice
- 1 cup chopped portobello mushrooms
- 2  green onions, minced

## Instructions

1. Heat evaporated milk, half-and-half, white wine, roasted red pepper, butter, bay leaf, salt, and hot pepper sauce in a saucepan over medium-low heat, stirring often, until very hot but not simmering, about 5 minutes.
2. Stir in the oysters, clams with juice, and mushrooms.
3. Cover and cook until canned oysters are just heated through, about 3 minutes.
4. Do not boil.
5. Remove bay leaf, sprinkle with green onions, and serve.
