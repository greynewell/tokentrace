## Ingredients

- ¾ cup Old Fashioned Quaker® Oats
- 1 cup finely shredded carrots
- 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
- ½ cup dates, pitted and chopped
- ⅓ cup finely chopped almonds
- ⅓ cup coconut flakes

## Instructions

1. Stir together first 5 ingredients.
2. Form the dough into tablespoon-sized balls, dip in coconut flakes,  and refrigerate an hour before eating.
