## Ingredients

- 2 cups all-purpose flour
- 1 teaspoon cream of tartar
- ½ teaspoon baking soda
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- ¼ cup white sugar
- 3 tablespoons applesauce
- 2 tablespoons water
- ½ cup milk
- 1 cup chocolate chips

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
2. Lightly grease two baking sheets.
3. In a large bowl, combine flour,  cream of tartar, baking soda, salt and sugar.
4. Add the applesauce, water and milk; mix well.
5. Fold in chocolate chips.
6. Drop large rounded spoonfuls of batter onto prepared baking sheets.
7. Bake at 400 degrees F (200 degrees C) for 15 minutes.
8. Let cool before serving.
