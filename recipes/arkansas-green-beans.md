## Ingredients

- 5 (15 ounce) cans green beans, drained
- 7 slices bacon
- ⅔ cup brown sugar
- ¼ cup butter, melted
- 7 teaspoons soy sauce
- 1 ½ teaspoons garlic powder

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Place drained green beans in a 9x13-inch baking pan.
3. Arrange bacon in a single layer on a microwave-safe plate. Cook in the microwave until slightly cooked, about 2 minutes. Lay bacon over green beans.
4. Mix together brown sugar, melted butter, soy sauce, and garlic powder in a small bowl. Pour over green beans and bacon.
5. Bake, uncovered, in the preheated oven for 40 minutes.
