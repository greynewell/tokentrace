## Ingredients

- 2 cups chopped frozen pineapple
- 1 cup fresh spinach
- 1  frozen chopped banana
- ½  avocado
- 2 tablespoons almond butter
- 1 teaspoon maca powder
- 3 cups water

## Instructions

1. Layer pineapple, banana, spinach, avocado, almond butter, and maca powder in a blender; pour in water.
2. Blend until smooth.
