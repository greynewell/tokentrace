## Ingredients

- 1 pound ground beef
- 1 cup diced red onion
- 1 cup chopped carrot
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 4 cups coarsely chopped red cabbage
- 4 cups beef broth
- 2 cups water
- 1 (15 ounce) can pinto beans, rinsed and drained
- 1 (6 ounce) can tomato paste
- 2 teaspoons salt
- 1 teaspoon ground cumin
- 1 teaspoon ground black pepper

## Instructions

1. Heat a large soup pot over medium-high heat. Cook and stir ground beef in the hot pot until browned and crumbly, 5 to 7 minutes. Drain and discard grease.
2. Add onion and carrot to beef. Saute until onion is translucent, about 5 minutes, adding garlic during the last minute. Add cabbage, broth, water, pinto beans, tomato paste, salt, cumin, and pepper; bring to a boil.
3. Reduce heat, cover, and simmer for 2 hours.
