## Ingredients

- 8  mint leaves
- 1 teaspoon simple syrup
- 2 ½ fluid ounces bourbon whiskey
- crushed ice, divided
- 1 sprig fresh mint for garnish

## Instructions

1. Chill a julep cup in the freezer for about 5 minutes.
2. Place mint leaves and simple syrup in the chilled julep cup; mash well with a cocktail muddler.
3. Pour in bourbon and 1/2 cup crushed ice.
4. Stir until chilled and top with remaining 1/2 cup ice.
5. Garnish with fresh mint.
