## Ingredients

- 1  green bell pepper, chopped
- 1  yellow onion, chopped
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 2 tablespoons chicken stock  (Optional)
- 1 pound ground beef
- 3 tablespoons taco seasoning
- 2 large roma (plum) tomatoes, chopped
- ½ teaspoon salt
- 1 (8 ounce) package shredded Cheddar cheese
- 12  large romaine lettuce leaves

## Instructions

1. Cook and stir green bell pepper and yellow onion in a skillet over medium heat with olive oil and chicken broth until onion is translucent, about 5 minutes.
2. Place ground beef in a separate skillet over medium heat. Cook and stir with taco seasoning until beef is browned and crumbly, 5 to 8 minutes. Drain excess grease.
3. Sprinkle tomatoes with salt in a bowl. Place Cheddar cheese into a separate bowl.
4. Fill each lettuce leaf with about 2 tablespoons beef filling; top with 1 to 2 teaspoons green pepper mixture, tomato, and about 1 1/2 tablespoons Cheddar cheese.
