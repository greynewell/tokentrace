## Ingredients

- 1  Smithfield® Golden Rotisserie Pork Tenderloin
- 2 cups shredded cabbage
- 1  mango, thinly sliced
- ½ cup chopped cilantro
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 1  lime, juiced
- 2 teaspoons sriracha sauce, or to taste
- 1 pinch salt
- 8 each slider buns

## Instructions

1. In a medium-sized bowl, toss together cabbage, mango and cilantro.
2. Roast tenderloin in oven at 425 degrees F for 25 to 30 minutes until internal temperature reaches 150 degrees F.
3. Remove from oven and let rest for 5 minutes then slice.
4. In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, lime juice, sriracha and salt.
5. Pour over cabbage mixture and toss to coat.
6. To assemble the sandwiches, halve the rolls then top with a few rounds of pork and a heap of the spicy mango slaw.
