## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon extra-virgin olive oil, or to taste
- ½  white onion, diced
- 3 cloves garlic, diced, or more to taste
- 1 sprig fresh parsley, chopped
- ¼ cup dry white wine, or more as needed
- 1 (8 ounce) fillet striped bass
- ground black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Heat olive oil in a cast iron skillet over medium-low heat.
2. Add onion and garlic; cook and stir until slightly browned, about 5 minutes.
3. Increase heat to medium and add parsley; cook for 1 minute.
4. Pour in enough white wine to cover the bottom of the skillet.
5. Spread onion mixture to the edges of the skillet in a circle.
6. Season both sides of the striped bass with black pepper.
7. Place in the center of the the skillet; push onion mixture up against the sides.
8. Cook until slightly browned and firm, about 4 minutes per side.
