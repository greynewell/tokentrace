## Ingredients

- ¼ cup chicken broth
- 1 pound tiger prawns, peeled and deveined
- ¼ teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes
- ½ cup butter
- 3 cloves garlic, crushed

## Instructions

1. In a large skillet over medium heat, bring chicken broth to a simmer and add prawns; cook until pink.
2. Add red pepper flakes to taste.
3. In a microwave-safe bowl, combine butter and garlic; heat until completely melted.
4. Place prawns on a platter with separate cups of garlic butter for dipping; serve.
