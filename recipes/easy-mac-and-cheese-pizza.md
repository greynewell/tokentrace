## Ingredients

- 1 (12 inch) pre-baked pizza crust
- ¾ cup cavatappi (corkscrew macaroni)
- ⅔ (16 ounce) jar cheese sauce (such as Ragu® Double Cheddar), divided, or as needed
- 1 tablespoon butter
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- ½ cup shredded Cheddar cheese

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C).
2. Place pizza crust on a baking sheet.
3. Bring a small pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
4. Cook cavatappi in the boiling water, stirring occasionally, until cooked through but firm to the bite, 10 to 11 minutes.
5. Drain and return pasta to the pot.
6. Stir 1/2 the cheese sauce and butter into the cavatappi pot over medium heat until butter is melted and pasta-cheese mixture is combined.
7. Season with salt and pepper.
8. Spread a thin layer of cheese sauce over the pizza crust.
9. Sprinkle shredded Cheddar cheese over the cheese sauce onto the crust.
10. Pour pasta-cheese mixture over the Cheddar cheese, and top with another 1/2 the cheese sauce.
11. Bake pizza in the preheated oven until golden and bubbling, 8 to 10 minutes.
12. Let rest for 2 minutes before slicing.
