## Ingredients

- 2 teaspoons olive oil
- ½  onion, chopped
- 1  green bell pepper, chopped
- 2  jalapeno peppers, seeded and chopped
- 1 pound frozen cooked shrimp, thawed
- 1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed tomato soup
- hot pepper sauce (such as Tabasco®), or to taste

## Instructions

1. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat.
2. Cook and stir onion, bell pepper, and jalapeno peppers in hot oil until tender, 3 to 5 minutes.
3. Add shrimp and continue cooking until shrimp are pink and no longer opaque, about 5 minutes more.
4. Pour tomato soup over shrimp mixture; season with hot pepper sauce.
5. Simmer mixture until hot, about 5 minutes.
