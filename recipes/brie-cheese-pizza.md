## Ingredients

- 8 ½ ounces Brie cheese, thinly sliced
- 2 cups sliced almonds
- 1 (14 ounce) package purchased fully baked pizza crust (such as Boboli®)

## Instructions

1. Preheat an oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Arrange Brie slices on pizza crust; cover with sliced almonds.
3. Bake until cheese is melted and almonds are toasted, about 10 minutes.
4. Slice into small wedges to serve.
