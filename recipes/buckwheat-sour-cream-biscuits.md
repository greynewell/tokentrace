## Ingredients

- 2 cups buckwheat flour
- 1 teaspoon gluten-free baking powder
- 1 teaspoon baking soda
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ⅓ cup unsalted butter, at room temperature
- ¾ cup light sour cream
- ¼ cup milk

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
2. Stir buckwheat flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt together in a bowl.
3. Cut in butter until mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
4. Stir in sour cream and milk with a fork until dough forms a ball.
5. Turn dough out on a lightly floured surface and knead about 5 times.
6. Pat out to 3/4-inch thickness.
7. Cut into rounds with a 2- or 3-inch cutter.
8. Transfer biscuits to a nonstick baking sheet.
9. Bake in the preheated oven until bottoms are nicely browned, about 15 minutes.
