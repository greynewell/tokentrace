## Ingredients

- ¼ cup honey
- ¼ cup soy sauce
- 1 tablespoon cornstarch
- 1 clove garlic, or to taste, minced
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 4  skinless, boneless chicken breasts, thinly sliced
- 4 cups warm cooked rice
- 8 (8 inch) soft flour tortillas
- ½ cup carrot matchsticks, or to taste

## Instructions

1. Mix honey, soy sauce, cornstarch, and garlic together in a small bowl.
2. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
3. Cook and stir chicken until no longer pink, 5 to 8 minutes.
4. Pour honey mixture over chicken; cook and stir until sauce thickens and coats chicken, 3 to 5 minutes.
5. Divide warm rice among tortillas; spoon chicken on top.
6. Cover chicken with carrots.
7. Roll up wraps.
