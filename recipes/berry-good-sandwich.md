## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons Neufchatel cheese
- 1 tablespoon strawberry jam
- 2  fresh strawberries, chopped
- 2 slices whole-grain bread

## Instructions

1. In a small bowl, mix together Neufchatel cheese and strawberry jam.
2. Spread strawberry flavored cheese spread over one slice of bread.
3. Top with chopped strawberries and remaining slice of bread to make a sandwich.
4. Remove crusts, and cut into triangles to serve.
