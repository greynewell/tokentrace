## Ingredients

- 1 ¼ cups water
- ⅓ cup honey
- 2 tablespoons butter, softened
- 2 cups bread flour
- 1 ¼ cups whole wheat flour
- 1 ½ teaspoons salt
- ½ teaspoon ground cinnamon
- ¼ teaspoon ground nutmeg
- ¼ teaspoon ground cloves
- 2 teaspoons active dry yeast
- 1 cup sweetened dried cranberries

## Instructions

1. Place all ingredients (except cranberries) in the pan of the bread machine in the order recommended by the manufacturer.
2. Select Sweet Bread cycle; press Start.
3. If your machine has a Fruit setting, add the cranberries at the signal, or about 5 minutes before the kneading cycle has finished.
