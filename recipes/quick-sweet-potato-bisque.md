## Ingredients

- 6 cups skim milk
- 5 large sweet potatoes, peeled and chopped
- 3  apples - peeled, cored, and chopped
- 1 large onion, sliced
- 3  carrots, chopped
- 3 stalks celery, chopped
- 6 cloves garlic, chopped
- 1 teaspoon salt
- ½ teaspoon ground nutmeg
- ½ teaspoon cayenne pepper
- 8  black peppercorns

## Instructions

1. Combine milk, sweet potatoes, apples, onion, carrots, celery, garlic, salt, nutmeg, cayenne pepper, and peppercorns in a large pot; bring to a boil.
2. Reduce heat to low, cover pot, and simmer, stirring occasionally, until potatoes are soft, 20 to 30 minutes.
3. Pour soup into a food processor or blender no more than half full.
4. Cover and hold lid down; pulse a few times before leaving on to blend.
5. Puree in batches until smooth.
