## Ingredients

- 1 pound white corn tortillas
- 1 cup vegetable oil for frying
- 1 pinch salt to taste

## Instructions

1. Heat oil in a deep-fryer or large saucepan to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
2. Slice tortillas with a pizza cutter into desired sizes.
3. Fry 1/2 of the tortillas in the hot oil until golden brown, 3 to 5 minutes.
4. Transfer to paper towels to drain excess oil.
5. Season with salt.
6. Repeat with remaining tortillas.
