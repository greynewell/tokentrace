## Ingredients

- 1 cup cottage cheese
- 1 cup packed brown sugar
- 3  eggs
- 1 ¾ cups all-purpose flour
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- ½ teaspoon baking soda
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ½ cup chopped walnuts
- ½ cup candied mixed fruit peel

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Grease one 9x5x3 inch loaf pan.
3. Smooth cottage cheese in a blender or force through strainer.
4. Put into bowl.
5. Beat in sugar.
6. Add eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition.
7. Set aside.
8. Measure flour, baking powder, soda, salt, walnuts, and mixed peel into a second bowl.
9. Mix together well.
10. Pour into first bowl.
11. Stir to moisten.
12. Scrape into greased 9x5x3 inch loaf pan.
13. Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for about 1 hour.
14. Cool for 10 minutes before turning out to finish cooling on rack.
15. Wrap well.
16. Serve with butter.
