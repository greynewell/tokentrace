## Ingredients

- 4 slices multigrain bread
- 2 large avocados - peeled, pitted, and mashed
- 2 tablespoons diced red onion
- 2 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro, or more to taste
- ¼ cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese, or to taste

## Instructions

1. Set an oven rack about 6 inches from the heat source and preheat the oven's broiler.
2. Place bread slices on a baking sheet under the preheated broiler until toasted, 1 to 2 minutes.
3. Mix together mashed avocados, red onion, and cilantro in a bowl. Spread on toasted bread and sprinkle with Parmesan cheese.
4. Return to broiler until cheese is melted, 2 to 3 minutes.
