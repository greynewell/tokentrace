## Ingredients

- 2 cups whole natural California Almonds, roasted*
- ½ cup honey
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 1 cup turbinado sugar (Sugar in the Raw®)

## Instructions

1. Over medium heat in medium saucepan, heat honey and butter to boiling.
2. Reduce heat to medium-low, simmer 2 minutes, stirring occasionally.
3. Add almonds; simmer and stir 2 minutes.
4. Using slotted spoon, transfer almonds to baking sheet lined with parchment paper or sprayed with nonstick cooking spray; spread in single layer and cool slightly.
5. Toss almonds with sugar to coat using a medium plastic food storage bag.
