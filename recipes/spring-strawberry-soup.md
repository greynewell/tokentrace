## Ingredients

- 2 pints hulled strawberries
- ½ cup plain yogurt
- ¼ cup freshly squeezed orange juice
- ½  lemon, juiced
- 2 tablespoons simple syrup, or more to taste
- ½ teaspoon vanilla extract

## Instructions

1. Combine strawberries, yogurt, orange juice, lemon juice, simple syrup, and vanilla extract in a blender; blend until smooth.
2. Refrigerate soup until chilled, about 30 minutes.
