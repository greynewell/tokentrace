## Ingredients

- 1 cup raisins
- 1 ½ cups rolled oats
- 1  chopped raw almonds
- 1  banana
- ¼ cup unsalted raw peanut butter
- ½ teaspoon vanilla extract
- 1 pinch salt
- 1 tablespoon water  (Optional)

## Instructions

1. Place raisins in a food processor; pulse into a paste.
2. Transfer raisin paste to a large bowl.
3. Add oats and almonds.
4. Place banana in the food processor; blend into a puree.
5. Combine 1/4 cup banana puree, peanut butter, vanilla extract, and salt in a saucepan over medium heat; cook and stir until warmed through, 3 to 5 minutes.
6. Add water if mixture seems too thick.
7. Pour over raisin mixture in the bowl and mix with your hands until combined.
8. Line a square baking dish with parchment paper.
9. Press mixture into the baking dish.
10. Brush remaining banana puree on top.
11. Cover with plastic wrap.
12. Chill until firm, 8 hours to overnight.
13. Cut into squares.
