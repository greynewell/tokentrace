## Ingredients

- ½ cup popcorn kernels, popped in an air popper
- 2 tablespoons salted butter, melted
- 3 tablespoons nutritional yeast
- 1 ½ teaspoons za'atar

## Instructions

1. Put popped popcorn in a large bowl.
2. Drizzle melted butter over the popcorn and mix.
3. Sprinkle nutritional yeast and za'atar over the popcorn; mix.
