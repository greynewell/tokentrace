## Ingredients

- 1 pound cubed beef stew meat
- 3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
- 3 tablespoons shortening
- 1 teaspoon salt
- ⅛ teaspoon ground black pepper
- 4 cups water
- 1  onion, finely diced
- ¼ teaspoon paprika
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
- 1  bay leaf
- 3  carrots, quartered
- 3  potatoes, peeled and quartered
- ¼ cup all-purpose flour
- ½ cup frozen corn kernels

## Instructions

1. Dredge beef in flour and then, in a medium stock pot, brown beef on all sides in shortening.
2. Add salt, pepper, water, onion, paprika, garlic, Worcestershire sauce and bay leave.
3. Bring to a boil, cover and simmer for 2 hours or until meat is tender.
4. Add carrots and potatoes and simmer for 45 minutes or until tender.
5. Remove bay leave and meat from stew, add 1 tablespoon flour or cornstarch and mix, in order to thicken stew.
6. After mixing let sit for a few minutes to thicken, if not to desired thickness add more flour or cornstarch and repeat.
7. Once thickened return meat and add corn to stew, heat through and serve.
