## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons butter
- 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ¼ teaspoon ground white pepper
- 1 teaspoon chicken bouillon powder
- 2 tablespoons chopped onion
- 1 ½ cups half-and-half
- ½ pound medium shrimp - peeled and deveined
- ½ pound crabmeat
- ½ cup white wine

## Instructions

1. In a 2-quart saucepan, melt butter over medium-high heat.
2. Stir in flour, salt, white pepper; cook and stir for 2 minutes.
3. Mix in bouillon granules, onion, and half of the half-and-half.
4. Bring mixture to a boil.
5. Reduce heat to medium; add shrimp and crab meat.
6. Cook, stirring occasionally, until mixture thickens and shrimp are cooked through.
7. Stir in remaining half and half and wine.
8. Serve hot.
