## Ingredients

- 4  hot dogs
- 4  hot dog buns, split
- 1 cup prepared creamy coleslaw
- ¼ cup Carolina-style barbeque sauce
- 2 teaspoons Dijon mustard, or to taste

## Instructions

1. Grill hot dogs until browned and heated through, 5 to 8 minutes.
2. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read 165 degrees F (74 degrees C).
3. Grill buns, cut-side down, until lightly golden, about 1 minute.
4. Place hot dogs in buns and top with coleslaw and barbeque sauce.
5. Drizzle with mustard.
