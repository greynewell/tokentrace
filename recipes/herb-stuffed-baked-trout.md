## Ingredients

- 1  whole trout - cleaned, rinsed, and patted dry
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
- 3 sprigs fresh parsley, or to taste
- 4 leaves fresh sage, or to taste
- 3 sprigs fresh dill, or to taste
- 1  lemon, thinly sliced

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
2. Rub trout with olive oil, salt, and pepper.
3. Rub the cavity of the trout with salt and stuff with parsley, sage, and dill.
4. Make a few slits in the skin on the back of the trout and stuff with lemon slices.
5. Place trout on a lined baking sheet.
6. Bake in preheated oven until flesh flakes easily with a fork, 20 to 30 minutes.
7. Cover with aluminum foil if top starts browning too quickly.
