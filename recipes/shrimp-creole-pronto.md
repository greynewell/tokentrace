## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
- 1 cup chopped onions
- 1 cup chopped celery
- 1  green bell pepper, chopped
- 2 garlic clove (blank)s garlic cloves, minced
- 2 cups chopped, peeled tomatoes
- 1 (8 ounce) can tomato sauce
- ½ cup Holland House® Marsala Cooking Wine
- ¼ teaspoon hot pepper sauce, or to taste
- ½ teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
- 1 pound fresh or frozen, thawed, uncooked shrimp, peeled, deveined
- 1 (10 ounce) package egg noodles, cooked, drained

## Instructions

1. Heat oil in large saucepan over medium-high heat.
2. Add onions, celery, green pepper and garlic; cook 2 to 3 minutes.
3. Add tomatoes; cook 2 to 3 minutes, stirring occasionally.
4. Add remaining ingredients except noodles; cook 2 to 3 minutes or until shrimp turn pink.
5. Serve over cooked noodles or hot cooked rice.
