## Ingredients

- 2 (3 ounce) packages lime-flavored gelatin mix (such as Jell-O®)
- 2 cups boiling water
- 2 cups cold water
- 4  red apples, chopped
- 4 stalks celery, chopped
- ½ cup chopped walnuts, or to taste  (Optional)

## Instructions

1. Dissolve lime gelatin in boiling water in a bowl; stir cold water into gelatin.
2. Pour gelatin into a mold.
3. Stir apples, celery, and walnuts into gelatin.
4. Refrigerate until firm, about 4 hours.
5. Dip mold into hot water to loosen salad; place a plate on top of the mold and invert to release salad from mold.
