## Ingredients

- 1 ⅓ cups whole wheat flour
- ⅓ cup wheat germ
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- ½ teaspoon baking soda
- 1 teaspoon salt
- ½ cup brown sugar
- ⅓ cup butter, melted
- 1  egg
- 1 cup buttermilk
- ¼ cup any flavor fruit jam

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C).
2. Grease muffin cups or line with paper muffin liners.
3. Stir together flour, wheat germ, baking powder, salt and brown sugar.
4. In a separate bowl, mix together butter, egg and buttermilk.
5. Stir milk mixture into dry ingredients; mix just until combined.
6. Fill prepared muffin cups half full with batter.
7. Make a depression in the center of each muffin and drop in 1 teaspoon of jam.
8. Cover jam with additional batter.
9. Bake in preheated oven for 20 to 25 minutes.
