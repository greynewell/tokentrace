## Ingredients

- 4  shucked oysters
- 4 teaspoons hot pepper sauce
- 1 teaspoon prepared horseradish  (Optional)
- ¼ cup vodka, or as needed

## Instructions

1. Place each oyster into a shot glass.
2. Add one teaspoon of hot sauce to each glass, then add horseradish to taste.
3. Fill glasses to the top with vodka, and drink immediately.
