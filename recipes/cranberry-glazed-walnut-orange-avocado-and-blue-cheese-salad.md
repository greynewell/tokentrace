## Ingredients

- 1 (5.5 ounce) package mixed salad greens with spinach
- ¾ cup sweetened dried cranberries
- ¾ cup glazed walnuts
- 1 (15 ounce) can mandarin oranges, drained
- ¾ cup crumbled bleu cheese
- 1  avocado - peeled, pitted and diced
- 2 tablespoons cranberry vinaigrette salad dressing, or to taste

## Instructions

1. Place the salad greens into a salad bowl, and sprinkle with dried cranberries, walnuts, mandarin orange sections, blue cheese, and avocado chunks.
2. Drizzle the salad with the dressing, toss, and serve.
