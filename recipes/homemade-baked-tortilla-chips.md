## Ingredients

- 1 (12 ounce) package flour tortillas
- 1 drizzle olive oil
- 1 pinch salt to taste

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Cut tortillas into 6 wedges each. Place on a baking sheet and drizzle with olive oil. Season with salt.
3. Bake in the preheated oven until golden and crisp, about 5 minutes.
