## Ingredients

- cooking spray (such as Pam®)
- 1 head cauliflower, run through a ricer
- 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
- ½  red bell pepper, diced
- 1  egg, or more if needed
- 1 teaspoon minced garlic  (Optional)
- 1 teaspoon garlic salt
- ½ teaspoon ground black pepper
- ½ teaspoon ground nutmeg
- 1 cup shredded pepperjack cheese

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C).
2. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper and spray with cooking spray.
3. Mix cauliflower, mozzarella cheese, red bell pepper, egg, garlic, garlic salt, black pepper, and nutmeg together in a large bowl until mixture holds together.
4. Spread mixture in a thin layer onto the prepared baking sheet.
5. Bake in the preheated oven for 12 minutes.
6. Sprinkle pepperjack cheese over top and continue baking until cheese is bubbling, 2 to 3 minutes more.
7. Cool and cut into 3- to 4-inch squares.
