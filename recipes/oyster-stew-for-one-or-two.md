## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons butter
- 1 (6 inch) celery rib, finely chopped
- 1 small clove garlic, minced
- 1 tablespoon all-purpose flour
- 1 cup milk
- ¼ cup half-and-half cream
- ¼ teaspoon onion powder
- 1 pinch dried parsley
- ½ teaspoon salt
- 1 cup fresh shucked oysters, undrained
- 1 pinch ground black pepper, to taste

## Instructions

1. Melt the butter in a heavy-bottomed skillet or wok over medium-high heat; cook and stir the celery and garlic in the butter until the celery is soft, 5 to 7 minutes.
2. Add the flour and stir, scraping the bottom of pan until flour is lightly toasted.
3. Slowly add the milk and half-and-half to the roux, stirring constantly.
4. Stir the onion powder, parsley, and salt into the mixture.
5. Reduce heat to medium-low; continue cooking and stirring until the mixture bubbles and thickens, 5 to 7 minutes.
6. Add the oysters with the liquid from the container to the mixture; cook until the edges of the oysters curl, about 5 minutes more.
7. Season with pepper just before serving.
