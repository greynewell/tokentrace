## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 1 teaspoon crushed garlic
- 1 tablespoon chopped fresh cilantro
- 1 teaspoon chile paste
- 1  onion, chopped
- 3 large carrots, peeled and sliced
- 1 large potato, peeled and chopped
- 5 cups vegetable broth

## Instructions

1. Heat oil in a large pot over medium heat.
2. Heat garlic, cilantro and chili paste.
3. Saute onion until tender.
4. Stir in carrots and potato; cook 5 minutes and then pour in vegetable broth.
5. Simmer for 30 to 45 minutes, or until potatoes and carrots are soft.
6. With a hand blender, blend until smooth.
