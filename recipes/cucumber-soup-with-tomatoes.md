## Ingredients

- 4  cucumbers - peeled, quartered, and seeded
- 1 (14.5 ounce) can chicken broth
- 1 cup chopped tomato
- ¼ cup fresh lime juice
- ⅛ teaspoon cayenne pepper

## Instructions

1. Place 2 cucumbers in a blender; pour in chicken stock. Blend cucumber mixture until smooth and pureed; pour cucumber puree into a large bowl.
2. Chop the remaining 2 cucumbers. Stir chopped cucumbers, tomato, lime juice, and cayenne pepper into pureed cucumber until well mixed. Refrigerate until chilled, at least 30 minutes.
