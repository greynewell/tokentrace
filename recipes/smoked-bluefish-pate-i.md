## Ingredients

- ½ pound smoked bluefish
- 4 ounces mascarpone cheese
- 2 tablespoons lemon juice
- 1 tablespoon prepared horseradish
- 1 teaspoon prepared Dijon-style mustard
- 1 pinch ground cayenne pepper
- 2 tablespoons finely chopped red onion
- salt to taste

## Instructions

1. Crumble the smoked bluefish.
2. Place bluefish and mascarpone cheese into a food processor.
3. Blend well using the pulse setting.
4. Transfer bluefish mixture to a medium bowl.
5. Mix in the lemon juice, horseradish, Dijon-style mustard, cayenne pepper, red onion and salt.
