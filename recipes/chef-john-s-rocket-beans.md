## Ingredients

- 4 slices bacon, sliced crosswise into 1/2-inch pieces
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 1 (15 ounce) jar butter beans, drained and rinsed
- 1 pinch salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 cups arugula, coarsely chopped
- ½  lemon, juiced

## Instructions

1. Cook bacon in olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat until crisp, about 8 minutes.
2. Pour butter beans into bacon and drippings; toss lightly.
3. Season with salt and black pepper; cook 3 more minutes to blend flavors.
4. Stir garlic into beans and cook just until fragrant, about 30 seconds.
5. Toss arugula into mixture and cook until wilted; remove from heat.
6. Stir in lemon juice.
