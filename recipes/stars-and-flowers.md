## Ingredients

- 2 (7 inch) whole wheat tortillas
- 2 slices Cheddar cheese, or to taste
- 2 thin slices  ham, or to taste
- 2 tablespoons pimento cheese spread, or to taste
- 4  celery sticks, or to taste
- 4  baby carrots, or to taste
- 5 small black olives
- 2 tablespoons mixed nuts with sweetened cranberries (such as Craisins®)

## Instructions

1. Cut tortillas and Cheddar cheese into star and flower shapes using cookie cutters. Place ham between tortilla and cheese shapes to make sandwiches.
2. Spread pimento cheese over celery sticks and cut into smaller pieces.
3. Assemble bento box with sandwiches, celery pieces, baby carrots, black olives, and mixed nuts.
