## Ingredients

- 3 (15 ounce) cans creamed corn
- 2  eggs
- 1 cup crushed saltine crackers
- ½ cup butter
- ½ teaspoon paprika
- ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Butter an 8x11x2-inch casserole dish.
2. Combine creamed corn, 1/2 cup cracker crumbs, 1/4 cup melted butter, and eggs in a large bowl; mix well. Pour mixture into the prepared dish.
3. Mix remaining 1/2 cup cracker crumbs and remaining 1/4 cup melted butter with paprika and pepper in a small bowl. Sprinkle crumb topping over casserole.
4. Bake in the preheated oven until topping browns slightly and filling bubbles along edges, 30 to 40 minutes.
