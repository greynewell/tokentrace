## Ingredients

- 2 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened
- 4 tablespoons ketchup
- 2 tablespoons prepared horseradish
- 1 pound cooked and peeled shrimp

## Instructions

1. In a medium bowl, mix the cream cheese, ketchup and prepared horseradish.
2. Gently fold in the shrimp.
3. Cover and chill in the refrigerator at least 1 hour before serving.
