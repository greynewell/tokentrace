## Ingredients

- 3  eggs, room temperature
- 1  banana, mashed
- 3 tablespoons honey
- 2 tablespoons melted butter
- ½ teaspoon vanilla extract
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- ⅔ cup sifted coconut flour
- ½ teaspoon baking powder
- ¼ teaspoon ground cinnamon
- ¼ cup chopped walnuts

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Grease or line 6 muffin cups.
3. Blend together eggs, banana, honey, butter, vanilla extract, and salt in a large bowl with an electric mixer until combined.
4. Combine coconut flour, baking powder, and cinnamon in a separate bowl.
5. Whisk into banana batter until there are no lumps.
6. Fold in walnuts.
7. Pour batter into prepared muffin cups.
8. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center of a muffin comes out clean and tops are golden brown, about 20 minutes.
