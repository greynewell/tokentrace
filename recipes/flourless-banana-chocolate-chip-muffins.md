## Ingredients

- 2 ¼ cups quick-cooking oats
- 1 cup mashed bananas
- ½ cup maple syrup
- 2 tablespoons maple syrup
- 2  eggs
- ¼ cup unsweetened vanilla-flavored almond milk
- 3 tablespoons coconut oil, melted
- 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
- ½ teaspoon baking soda
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ½ cup dark chocolate chips

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Grease or line 12 muffin cups with paper liners.
3. Place oats, mashed bananas, 1/2 cup plus 2 tablespoons maple syrup, eggs, almond milk, coconut oil, vanilla extract, baking powder, cinnamon, baking soda, and salt in a blender or food processor; blend or process until batter is smooth.
4. Gently stir chocolate chips into batter using a spoon.
5. Pour batter into the prepared muffin cups, filling them 3/4 full.
6. Bake in the preheated oven until light golden brown, about 22 minutes.
7. Cool for 10 to 15 minutes before eating.
