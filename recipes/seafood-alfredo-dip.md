## Ingredients

- 3 tablespoons butter
- 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
- 1 cup heavy cream
- 2 (4 ounce) cans shrimp, drained
- 1 (6 ounce) can crabmeat, drained
- ¼ cup shredded Parmesan cheese
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ⅛ teaspoon white pepper

## Instructions

1. Melt the butter in a pan over medium heat.
2. Whisk in the flour, and cook to make a smooth paste, about 5 minutes.
3. Gradually stir in the heavy cream.
4. Continue to whisk the mixture until thick and smooth, about 5 minutes.
5. Stir in the shrimp and crabmeat.
6. Add the Parmesan cheese, and stir until melted.
7. Season to taste with salt and white pepper.
8. Serve warm as a dip.
