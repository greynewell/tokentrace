## Ingredients

- 3 cups shredded cooked chicken
- 3  eggs
- ½ cup all-purpose flour, or as needed
- 1  tomato, chopped
- 2  green onions, chopped
- 3 tablespoons chive cream cheese
- 1 tablespoon chopped fresh dill  (Optional)
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- 2 tablespoons olive oil, or as needed
- 1 medium cucumber, diced
- 2 tablespoons sour cream
- 1 tablespoon chive cream cheese
- 1 tablespoon chopped fresh dill  (Optional)

## Instructions

1. Mix shredded chicken, eggs, flour, tomato, green onions, cream cheese, dill, salt, and pepper together in a large bowl.
2. Add additional flour if needed to create a thick batter.
3. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium-high heat.
4. Add spoonfuls of batter and shape into circles using a spoon.
5. Cook for 2 to 3 minutes on each side.
6. Combine cucumber, sour cream, cream cheese, and dill together in a small bowl.
7. Serve dip with fritters.
