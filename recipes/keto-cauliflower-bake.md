## Ingredients

- 1 (16 ounce) package frozen cauliflower
- 1 (8 ounce) package whipped cream cheese
- ½ cup heavy cream
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- 1 ½ cups shredded Cheddar cheese

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Place the cauliflower into a pot and cover with water.
3. Bring to a boil over high heat, then reduce heat to medium-low, cover, and simmer until soft, about 10 minutes.
4. Drain and place cauliflower into a large bowl.
5. Add cream cheese, heavy cream, salt, and pepper to the cauliflower; mash using a potato masher.
6. Pour contents into a glass 8-inch square casserole.
7. Top with Cheddar cheese and cover with aluminum foil.
8. Bake in the preheated oven until cheese is slightly browned and bubbly, about 30 minutes.
