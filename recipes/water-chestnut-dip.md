## Ingredients

- 2 (8 ounce) cans sliced water chestnuts, drained
- 1 envelope dry vegetable soup mix
- ½ cup sour cream
- ½ cup mayonnaise
- ½ teaspoon hot sauce
- ¼ cup sliced green onion

## Instructions

1. Julienne the water chestnuts.
2. In a medium bowl, mix the water chestnuts, vegetable soup mix, sour cream, mayonnaise, hot sauce and green onion.
3. Refrigerate at least 2 hours before serving.
