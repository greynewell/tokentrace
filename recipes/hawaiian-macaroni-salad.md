## Ingredients

- 6 cups elbow macaroni
- 2 cups mayonnaise, or more to taste
- ½ cup milk
- ½ cup shredded carrot
- salt and ground black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook elbow macaroni in boiling water, stirring occasionally, until tender yet firm to the bite, about 8 minutes. Drain and rinse with cold water.
2. Combine macaroni, mayonnaise, milk, and carrot in a large bowl; mix until macaroni is well coated. Add more mayonnaise for a wetter consistency. Season with salt and pepper.
3. Chill in the refrigerator for at least 2 hours or up to overnight.
