## Ingredients

- ¾ cup wheat and barley nugget cereal (e.g. Grape-Nuts™)
- 1 ¼ cups buttermilk
- 1  egg
- ¾ cup white sugar
- 1 teaspoon baking soda
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 2 cups all-purpose flour
- 2 tablespoons melted shortening

## Instructions

1. In a small bowl, soak grape nuts in buttermilk for 1 hour.
2. In a large bowl, combine buttermilk mixture, sugar, salt, egg, and baking soda.
3. Stir until sugar dissolves.
4. Mix in the melted shortening and the flour.
5. Pour batter into a greased and floured 9 x 5 inch loaf pan.
6. Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for about 45 minutes.
7. Cool.
