## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon coconut oil
- 4 teaspoons chopped onion
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 3 ½ tablespoons chopped fresh mushrooms
- 1 tablespoon frozen chopped spinach, thawed and drained
- ⅓ cup almond flour
- ⅓ cup coconut flour
- 2 tablespoons flax seed meal
- ½ teaspoon salt
- 1 pinch ground black pepper
- 2  eggs
- 2 tablespoons water
- 1 tablespoon olive oil

## Instructions

1. Melt coconut oil in a skillet over medium heat; saute onion and garlic until onion is tender, about 5 minutes.
2. Add mushrooms; saute until tender, about 5 minutes.
3. Add spinach and cook until warmed, 2 to 3 minutes.
4. Remove pan from heat and slightly cool vegetables.
5. Mix almond flour, coconut flour, flax seed meal, salt, and pepper in a bowl.
6. Transfer vegetable mixture to a work surface and finely chop.
7. Stir vegetable mixture into flour mixture; add eggs, water, and olive oil.
8. Mix until dough is thoroughly combined and holds together.
9. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
10. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
11. Turn dough onto the prepared baking sheet and press into a flat rectangle.
12. Place a second sheet of parchment paper atop dough and roll dough between the 2 sheets until about 1/16-thick.
13. Remove top parchment paper.
14. Cut edges of dough to make an even rectangle.
15. Add excess dough to 1 end of rectangle and re-roll into even thickness.
16. Cut dough into 1-inch squares using a pizza cutter, keeping dough together.
17. Bake in the preheated oven until edges are browned, 18 to 20 minutes.
18. Cool completely and break into squares.
