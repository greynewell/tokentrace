## Ingredients

- 60  asparagus spears, woody bases removed
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- 12 slices provolone cheese
- 12 slices prosciutto
- toothpicks

## Instructions

1. Fill a large bowl with ice and cold water.
2. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
3. Add asparagus and briefly cook uncovered until just beginning to soften, about 1 minute.
4. Drain in a colander and immediately immerse in ice water for several minutes to stop the cooking process.
5. Drain and season with salt and pepper.
6. Bunch the spears into groups of 5.
7. Place a slice of provolone cheese over the top of each bunch.
8. Wrap a slice of prosciutto, starting at the base of the spears, around the spear-cheese package and secure with a toothpick.
9. Preheat an outdoor grill for medium heat and lightly oil the grate.
10. Place each wrapped asparagus bunch on the grill.
11. Grill until prosciutto is crispy and cheese is melted, about 5 minutes.
