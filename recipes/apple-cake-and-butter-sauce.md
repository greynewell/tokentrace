## Ingredients

- 1 cup white sugar
- ¼ cup butter
- 2 large apples
- 2  eggs
- 1 cup all-purpose flour
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon baking soda
- ½ teaspoon ground nutmeg
- ½ teaspoon ground cinnamon
- ½ cup butter
- 1 cup white sugar
- ½ cup heavy whipping cream
- 1 ½ teaspoons vanilla extract

## Instructions

1. Peel, core, and grate the apples.
2. Cream together 1 cup sugar and 1/4 cup butter or margarine.
3. Stir in the grated apples and eggs.
4. Sift together flour, salt, soda, nutmeg, and cinnamon; mix into the apple mixture.
5. Pour batter into an ungreased 9 inch glass pie plate.
6. Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 50 minutes.
7. Remove from oven.
8. Place 1/2 cup butter or margarine, 1 cup sugar, 1/2 cup cream, and vanilla in the top pan of a double boiler.
9. Heat and stir until sugar has dissolved and butter or margarine has melted.
10. Serve over apple cake.
