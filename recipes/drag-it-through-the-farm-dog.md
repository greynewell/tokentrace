## Ingredients

- 1 (1.5 ounce) Idahoan® Buttery Homestyle Mashed Potato Cup
- 4  Chicken sausages
- 3 tablespoons stone ground mustard
- 4  potato flour buns
- Power greens
- Cherry tomatoes, halved
- Avocado, diced
- Honey mustard salad dressing
- Orange and red bell peppers, diced
- Red onions, diced

## Instructions

1. Grill sausages until done.
2. Prepare Idahoan Buttery Homestyle Mashed Potato Cup according to package directions and stir in stone ground mustard.
3. Place sausage on bun and spread potato mixture on top.
4. Add toppings to your taste.
5. Top with honey mustard dressing.
6. Have fun with your hot dogs!
