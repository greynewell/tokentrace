## Ingredients

- 1 cup whole wheat flour
- 1 cup all-purpose flour
- 1 tablespoon baking powder
- 1 teaspoon salt
- ¼ cup canola oil
- ¾ cup unsweetened soy milk

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 450 degrees F (220 degrees C).
2. Stir together the whole wheat flour, all-purpose flour, baking powder, and salt.
3. Combine the oil and soy milk in a measuring cup.
4. Pour into the dry ingredients all at once, and stir just until the dough pulls away from the sides of the bowl.
5. Drop by heaping spoonfuls onto a baking sheet.
6. Bake for 8 to10 minutes in the preheated oven, until the biscuits are browned on the top and bottom.
