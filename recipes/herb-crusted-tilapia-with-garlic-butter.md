## Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon olive oil, or as needed
- 6 tablespoons butter, melted
- 2 teaspoons roasted garlic, mashed into a paste
- ½ teaspoon garlic powder
- ½ teaspoon oregano
- ½ teaspoon Italian seasoning
- ½ teaspoon dried parsley flakes
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- 4  tilapia fillets
- 1 teaspoon lemon juice, or to taste

## Instructions

1. Set oven rack about 6 inches from the heat source and preheat the oven's broiler.
2. Brush a broiler pan with olive oil.
3. Stir butter and garlic paste together in a bowl.
4. Divide garlic mixture evenly between two bowls, reserving one bowl for serving.
5. Stir garlic powder, oregano, Italian seasoning, parsley, and salt together in a bowl.
6. Place tilapia fillets on prepared broiler pan; brush each fillet with garlic butter and sprinkle each with Italian seasoning mixture, reserving 1/2 the garlic butter in the bowl and 1/2 the Italian seasoning mixture.
7. Broil fillets in the preheated oven for 2 minutes, brush again with garlic butter, and broil for 2 more minutes.
8. Turn fillets over.
9. Brush with garlic butter and sprinkle with remaining Italian seasoning mixture.
10. Broil until tilapia fillets flake easily with a fork, 3 to 5 minutes.
11. Serve with reserved garlic butter and lemon juice.
