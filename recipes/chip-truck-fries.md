## Ingredients

- 6  baking potatoes, cut into 1/4 inch slices
- ¼ cup vegetable oil
- 2 teaspoons seasoned salt

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
2. Coat potato slices with vegetable oil.
3. Sprinkle with seasoned salt.
4. Bake in preheated oven for 45 minutes, or until golden brown.
