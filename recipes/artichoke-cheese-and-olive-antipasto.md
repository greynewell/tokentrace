## Ingredients

- 3 (6.5 ounce) jars marinated artichoke hearts, undrained
- 1 (12 ounce) jar roasted red bell peppers, drained and sliced
- 1 (15 ounce) can black olives, drained
- 1 pound smoked provolone cheese, diced
- ⅓ cup olive oil
- ½ cup balsamic vinegar
- ½ teaspoon dried oregano
- 1 clove garlic, finely chopped
- 1 pinch salt and pepper to taste
- 8  fresh basil leaves, cut into thin strips

## Instructions

1. Pour the jars of artichoke hearts with their liquid into a 1 1/2 quart or larger container with a tight sealing lid.
2. Add the bell peppers, black olives, and provolone cheese.
3. In a medium bowl, whisk together the olive oil, balsamic vinegar, oregano, garlic, salt and pepper.
4. Pour over the ingredients in the container.
5. Seal the lid, and refrigerate for at least 3 hours, shaking gently about once per hour.
6. To serve, allow the mixture to come to room temperature.
7. Soon onto serving plates, and top with basil shreds as a garnish.
