## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon crushed rosemary
- 1 teaspoon rubbed sage
- ½ teaspoon dried oregano
- 8  skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
- ¼ cup fat-free chicken broth
- 1 cup raspberry preserves
- ½ teaspoon honey mustard
- 1 teaspoon chopped fresh rosemary leaves

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. In a small bowl, stir together crushed rosemary, sage, and oregano.
3. Rub one side of each chicken breast with herb mixture.
4. Place chicken herb-side up in a baking dish, and pour broth over the chicken.
5. Bake in preheated oven for 20 minutes.
6. Place raspberry preserves in a microwave-safe bowl, and heat for 20 to 30 seconds to soften.
7. Stir in honey mustard and rosemary.
8. Spread about 1 tablespoon of preserve mixture over each breast.
9. Bake 10 minutes more.
