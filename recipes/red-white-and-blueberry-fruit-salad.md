## Ingredients

- 1 pint strawberries, hulled and quartered
- 1 pint blueberries
- ½ cup white sugar
- 2 tablespoons lemon juice
- 4  bananas

## Instructions

1. Mix strawberries and blueberries together in a bowl, sprinkle with sugar and lemon juice; toss lightly. Refrigerate until cold, at least 30 minutes.
2. About 30 minutes before serving, cut bananas into 3/4-inch thick slices; toss with berries.
