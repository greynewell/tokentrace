## Ingredients

- ½ cup water
- ½ cup coconut milk
- ½ cup uncooked white rice
- ½ teaspoon butter
- 3 ounces firm tofu, cubed
- 1 cup fresh green beans, rinsed and trimmed
- ¼ teaspoon curry powder
- ¼ cup coconut milk

## Instructions

1. Pour water, 1/2 cup coconut milk, and rice into a small saucepan.
2. Bring to a boil over medium-high heat, then reduce heat to medium-low, cover, and simmer until the rice is tender, about 20 minutes.
3. Meanwhile, melt the butter in a skillet over medium-high heat.
4. Add the tofu and cook until golden-brown on all sides, about 5 minutes.
5. When browned, stir in green beans, curry powder, and 1/4 cup of coconut milk.
6. Simmer until the green beans are tender, then serve over rice.
