## Ingredients

- 1 ½ pounds cantaloupe chunks
- ½ cup white sugar
- 2 tablespoons vodka
- 1 ½ tablespoons seasoned rice wine vinegar
- 2 teaspoons fresh lime juice
- ¼ teaspoon cayenne pepper, or more to taste

## Instructions

1. Place melon in bowl of a blender.
2. Add sugar, vodka, rice vinegar, lime juice, and cayenne pepper.
3. Blend on highest speed to completely liquefy, 1 or 2 minutes.
4. Pour into ice cream maker and proceed according to manufacturer's instructions.
5. Smooth out surface and cover with plastic wrap.
6. Freeze until firm, at least 5 hours.
