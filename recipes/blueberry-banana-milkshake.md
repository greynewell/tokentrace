## Ingredients

- 2 cups vanilla ice cream
- 1 cup milk
- 1 ½ cups blueberries
- 1  banana
- ¼ cup white sugar
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

## Instructions

1. Place vanilla ice cream and milk in a blender; blend until smooth.
2. Add blueberries, banana, sugar, and vanilla extract; blend until evenly mixed.
