## Ingredients

- 8  hot dogs
- 8 pinches celery salt
- 8 slices processed American cheese
- 1 (12 ounce) bottle barbeque sauce

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Spray a baking sheet with non-stick cooking spray.
3. Being careful not to slice them into two separate pieces, make a lengthwise slit from the top of every hot dog to the bottom.
4. Spread open the franks, then place them side by side flat side-up on the baking sheet.
5. Season each hot dog with a pinch of celery salt, then put a slice of cheese on top of each frank.
6. Spread a thin layer of barbeque sauce over each piece of cheese.
7. Bake until cheese is slightly browned on edges, about 10 minutes.
