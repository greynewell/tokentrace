## Ingredients

- 1 cup chocolate graham crackers
- ¼ cup white sugar
- ⅛ teaspoon ground cinnamon
- ¼ cup butter

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
2. Crumble graham crackers in a food processor with sugar and cinnamon.
3. Place butter in a microwave-safe bowl and melt in a microwave oven, about 1 minute. Add to the mixture in the food processor and blend until combined into the dough.
4. Roll dough out into a crust and place into a pie plate.
5. Bake in the preheated oven for 7 minutes. Let cool completely before filling, about 30 minutes.
