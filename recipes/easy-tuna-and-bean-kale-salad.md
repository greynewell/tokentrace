## Ingredients

- 1 (8 ounce) package kale, stemmed and chopped
- ½  avocado, diced
- 1  Roma tomato, diced
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- ½ cup canned black beans, drained
- ½ cup chopped cremini mushrooms
- ½ (5 ounce) can tuna, drained
- 1 ½ teaspoons Italian-style salad dressing, or to taste

## Instructions

1. Put kale in a bowl and massage until leaves soften.
2. Place avocado and tomato in a separate bowl and season with salt.
3. Combine black beans, mushrooms, and tuna with the kale.
4. Add avocado and tomato.
5. Add Italian dressing.
6. Finish with black pepper.
