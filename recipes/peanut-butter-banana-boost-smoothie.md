## Ingredients

- ½ cup skim milk
- ½ cup plain low fat Greek-style yogurt
- 2 tablespoons KRAFT All Natural Peanut Butter
- 1 tablespoon oats
- 1 tablespoon honey
- 1 small banana, sliced
- 4  ice cubes

## Instructions

1. Blend all ingredients except ice in blender until smooth.
2. Add ice; blend until thickened.
3. Serve immediately.
