## Ingredients

- 1 ½ cups shredded coconut
- 1 cup white sugar
- 2 cups all-purpose flour
- 2  eggs, room temperature
- ½ cup milk, or more as needed
- ¼ cup simple syrup, or to taste

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 9x13-inch baking pan.
2. Whisk coconut and sugar together in a bowl. Sift in flour and whisk to combine. Form a well in the center.
3. Whisk eggs together in a small bowl and pour into the well. Add enough milk to reach a slightly thickened but runny texture. Pour batter into the prepared pan.
4. Bake in the preheated oven until golden brown on top and a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean, 20 to 25 minutes.
5. Remove from the oven and let cool for 20 minutes. Pour simple syrup over cake before serving.
