## Ingredients

- 2 ½ quarts strawberries, coarsely chopped
- 1 (12 ounce) package fresh or frozen cranberries, coarsely chopped
- 2 (2 ounce) packages powdered fruit pectin
- 1 teaspoon margarine
- 5 pounds white sugar

## Instructions

1. Sterilize jars and lids in boiling water for at least 10 minutes. Let simmer while making jam.
2. In a large saucepan, combine strawberries, cranberries, pectin, and margarine. Bring to a boil. Stir in sugar, and return to a boil. Cook for 1 minute; remove from heat.
3. Quickly fill jars to within 1/2 inch from top. Wipe rims clean, and put on lids. Process in a boiling water bath for 5 minutes.
