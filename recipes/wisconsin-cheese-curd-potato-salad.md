## Ingredients

- 8  red potatoes
- ½  onion, chopped
- 6 ounces Cheddar cheese curds, diced
- 1 ½ cups mayonnaise
- 2 tablespoons prepared yellow mustard
- 1 teaspoon seasoned salt
- 2  hard-boiled eggs, sliced
- black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Place the potatoes into a large pot and cover with salted water.
2. Bring to a boil over high heat, then reduce heat to medium-low, cover, and simmer until tender, about 20 minutes.
3. Drain and allow to steam dry for a minute or two.
4. Let the potatoes cool enough to handle, and peel; allow the potatoes to cool thoroughly.
5. Cut into bite-size pieces.
6. In a bowl, combine the potatoes, onion, cheese curds, mayonnaise, yellow mustard, and seasoned salt; mix thoroughly.
7. Top the potato salad with hard-boiled egg slices and sprinkle with black pepper.
8. Refrigerate at least 2 hours before serving.
