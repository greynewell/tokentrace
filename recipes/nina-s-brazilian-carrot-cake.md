## Ingredients

- 2 ¼ cups all-purpose flour
- 1 tablespoon baking powder
- ¾ teaspoon salt
- 1 cup vegetable oil
- 2 cups white sugar
- 4  large eggs
- 1 ⅔ cups peeled and chopped carrots
- 1 cup whole milk
- 1 (3.5 ounce) bar bittersweet chocolate, broken into small pieces
- 2 tablespoons honey
- 1 tablespoon butter

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Grease and flour a 13x9-inch baking pan.
3. Sift together flour, baking powder, and salt in a bowl.
4. Blend oil, sugar, eggs, and carrots in a blender, stopping to scrape down sides, until creamy and smooth, about 1 minute.
5. Pour puree into a large bowl, then whisk in flour mixture until combined.
6. Pour batter into prepared pan.
7. Bake in preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into center comes out clean, about 40 minutes.
8. Transfer pan to a rack to cool, about 1 hour.
9. Stir together milk, chocolate, honey, and butter in a heavy saucepan and bring to a boil over medium heat, stirring.
10. Reduce heat to medium-low and simmer, stirring constantly, until mixture is smooth, thick, and reduced to about 1 1/8 cups, about 10 minutes.
11. Pour frosting into a metal bowl and set in a larger bowl of ice water.
12. Stir frosting until cooled and thickened, about 5 minutes.
13. Frost cake evenly and cut into 12 pieces to serve.
