## Ingredients

- 2 slices bacon, chopped
- ⅓ cup chopped onion
- 1 package McCormick® Original Country Gravy Mix
- 1 ½ cups milk
- 1 cup shredded white Cheddar cheese, divided
- 1 (6.5 ounce) can chopped clams, drained
- 2 teaspoons McCormick® Parsley Flakes

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
2. Cook bacon in large skillet on medium-high heat until crisp.
3. Remove bacon, reserving drippings in pan.
4. Add onion; cook and stir 2 to 3 minutes or until translucent.
5. Stir in Gravy Mix, milk and 1/2 cup of the shredded cheese.
6. Bring to boil.
7. Reduce heat and simmer 2 minutes or until gravy starts to thicken.
8. Remove from heat.
9. Stir in clams.
10. Pour into 9-inch glass pie plate.
11. Sprinkle with remaining 1/2 cup cheese.
12. Bake 15 minutes or until cheese is melted.
13. Sprinkle with bacon and parsley.
14. Serve with toasted baguette slices or crackers.
