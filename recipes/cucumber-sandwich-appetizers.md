## Ingredients

- 1 (8 ounce) package spreadable cream cheese
- ¼ cup sour cream
- 1 (.7 ounce) package dry Italian-style salad dressing mix
- 1 (1 pound) loaf sliced pumpernickel party bread
- 2  cucumbers, peeled and thinly sliced

## Instructions

1. Mix cream cheese, sour cream, and Italian salad dressing mix in a bowl until well blended.
2. Spread cream cheese mixture atop pumpernickel party bread and top each slice with 1 to 2 cucumber slices.
