## Ingredients

- 1  egg
- ½ cup dry bread crumbs
- 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
- 8  chicken tenderloins

## Instructions

1. Preheat an air fryer to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Whisk egg in a small bowl. Mix bread crumbs and oil together in a second bowl until mixture becomes loose and crumbly.
3. Dip each chicken tenderloin into the bowl of egg; shake off any residual egg. Dip chicken into crumb mixture, making sure it is evenly and fully covered. Lay chicken tenderloins into the basket of the air fryer.
4. Air-fry until no longer pink in the center, about 12 minutes. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 165 degrees F (74 degrees C).
