## Ingredients

- 1 (8 ounce) container frozen whipped topping, thawed
- 1 (3.5 ounce) package instant vanilla pudding mix
- 1 cup milk
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

## Instructions

1. Combine pudding mix with milk and vanilla in a medium bowl. Mix until smooth.
2. Gently fold in whipped topping until no streaks remain. Spread evenly over cake.
