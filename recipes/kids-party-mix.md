## Ingredients

- 1 ½ cups candy-coated chocolate pieces
- 3 cups thin pretzel sticks, broken in half
- 3 cups bite-size Cheddar cheese crackers
- 1 ½ cups raisins

## Instructions

1. Mix chocolate candies, pretzels, crackers, and raisins together and store the mixture in a tightly covered container.
2. Serve as a snack.
