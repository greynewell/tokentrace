## Ingredients

- 1 (14 ounce) package Hillshire Farm® Smoked Sausage
- ¼ cup packed brown sugar
- ⅔ cup mayonnaise
- 2 tablespoons yellow mustard
- 1 tablespoon honey
- ¼ teaspoon black pepper

## Instructions

1. Unwrap and place sausage on a tray or baking sheet.
2. Freeze, uncovered, 30 minutes.
3. Transfer sausage to a cutting board.
4. Use a sharp knife to bias-slice the sausage into 1/4-inch pieces.
5. Preheat oven to 325 degrees F.
6. Line two baking sheets with parchment paper.
7. Arrange sausage slices in a single layer on the baking sheets.
8. Sprinkle with brown sugar.
9. Bake about 20 minutes or until just beginning to brown.
10. Meanwhile, for dip, in a small bowl combine mayonnaise, mustard, honey, and black pepper.
11. Serve with warm sausage slices.
