## Ingredients

- 1 cup dried black-eyed peas
- 4 cups water, or as needed to cover
- 1 tablespoon soy margarine
- 1  turnip, peeled and chopped
- 1 bunch collard greens, chopped
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 2  tomatoes, chopped
- 1 tablespoon balsamic vinaigrette salad dressing
- 1 tablespoon olive oil  (Optional)

## Instructions

1. Place black-eyed peas into a large container and cover with several inches of cool water; let stand 8 hours to overnight.
2. Drain and rinse.
3. In a large pot, cover black-eyed peas with fresh water.
4. Bring to a boil over high heat, then reduce the heat to medium-low; cover and simmer until peas are tender, 40 to 60 minutes.
5. Drain.
6. Heat soy margarine in a skillet over medium heat.
7. Add turnip and  ; cook for 2 minutes.
8. Stir black-eyed peas, garlic, and tomatoes into collard mixture; cook and stir until collards are tender, about 5 minutes.
9. Season with balsamic vinaigrette and olive oil.
