## Ingredients

- 2  beets
- 1 teaspoon balsamic creme (reduced balsamic vinegar), or as needed
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- 4 cups chopped fresh spinach
- ½ cup candied walnuts
- ½ cup crumbled goat cheese
- 14 cups pickled onions
- ¼ cup French-fried onions (such as French's®)
- 1 teaspoon lemon juice
- ¼ cup balsamic creme (reduced balsamic vinegar)
- 1 teaspoon olive oil

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Place beets in a roasting pan; drizzle with 1 teaspoon balsamic creme.
3. Season with salt and black pepper.
4. Roast beets in the preheated oven until tender, 30 to 40 minutes.
5. Cut beets into bite-sized pieces; cool to room temperature.
6. Combine spinach, walnuts, goat cheese, pickled red onion, and French-fried onions in a bowl.
7. Whisk lemon juice, 1/4 cup balsamic creme, olive oil, salt, and black pepper in a bowl; pour over salad.
8. Toss to coat.
