## Ingredients

- 2 cups fresh spinach
- 1 cup diced avocado
- ½ cup reduced-fat sour cream
- ¼ cup chopped red onion
- 1 tablespoon fresh lime juice
- 1 tablespoon chopped seeded jalapeno pepper
- 1 large garlic clove
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ⅛ teaspoon ground black pepper
- hot sauce

## Instructions

1. Process spinach, avocado, sour cream, red onion, lime juice, jalapeno pepper, garlic, salt, black pepper, and hot sauce in a food processor until smooth.
2. Scrape dip into a serving bowl, cover with plastic wrap, and refrigerate until chilled, at least 1 hour.
