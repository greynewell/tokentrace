## Ingredients

- 1 cup mayonnaise
- ½ cup sour cream
- ½ teaspoon dried chives
- ½ teaspoon dried parsley
- ½ teaspoon dried dill weed
- ¼ teaspoon garlic powder
- ¼ teaspoon onion powder
- ⅛ teaspoon salt
- ⅛ teaspoon ground black pepper

## Instructions

1. Whisk together mayonnaise, sour cream, chives, parsley, dill, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and pepper in a large bowl until well-combined.
2. Cover and refrigerate for 30 minutes before serving.
