## Ingredients

- 1 (49.5 fluid ounce) can chicken broth, divided
- 1 (1 ounce) package country style gravy mix (such as McCormick®)
- 6 cups peeled and cubed potatoes
- 1  carrot, diced
- 1  onion, diced
- 2 (1/2 inch thick) slices deli ham, diced

## Instructions

1. Pour 1 cup of the chicken broth, and the contents of the gravy mix packet, into a small bowl.
2. Whisk well to combine.
3. Combine potatoes, carrot, onion, ham, and remaining chicken broth in a large soup pot; bring to a boil.
4. Cook for 5 minutes, and turn down to a simmer.
5. Whisk in the broth and gravy mixture.
6. Simmer soup for one hour.
