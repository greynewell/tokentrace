## Ingredients

- 5 slices bacon
- 1 ¼ cups cornmeal
- 1 teaspoon salt
- ½ teaspoon baking soda
- 1 cup mashed cooked sweet potato
- 1 cup buttermilk
- 2  eggs, lightly beaten

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C).
2. Cook and stir bacon in a 9-inch cast-iron skillet over medium-high heat until browned, about 10 minutes.
3. Remove and chop bacon, leaving 1 tablespoon of bacon drippings in skillet.
4. Whisk cornmeal, salt, and baking soda in a bowl.
5. Stir sweet potato, buttermilk, and eggs together in a separate bowl until thoroughly combined.
6. Stir sweet potato mixture into dry ingredients and mix chopped bacon into batter.
7. Pour batter into the cast-iron skillet.
8. Bake corn bread in the preheated oven until browned and bread feels firm to the touch, about 30 minutes.
9. Turn corn bread onto a serving plate and cut into wedges for serving.
