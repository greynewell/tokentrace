## Ingredients

- ¼ cup shortening
- ¼ cup brown sugar
- ½ cup white sugar
- 1  egg
- 3 tablespoons vanilla extract
- 4 teaspoons instant coffee
- ½ cup warm water
- 1 teaspoon cocoa powder
- ½ teaspoon baking soda
- 1 ⅓ cups all-purpose flour
- 2 cups quick oats
- 6 ounces white chocolate chips, or more to taste

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
2. Mix shortening, brown sugar, white sugar, egg, vanilla extract, instant coffee, warm water, and cocoa powder together in a bowl until smooth. Add baking soda and 1/3 cup flour. Continue adding flour 1/3 cup at a time until fully incorporated. Mix in oats and chocolate chips.
3. Use a small cookie scoop to place 12 cookies on the prepared baking sheet.
4. Bake in the preheated oven for 10 minutes. Remove from the oven and let cookies sit 5 minutes. Transfer cookies to a wire rack to cool completely.
5. Repeat 2 more times with remaining dough to make 36 cookies.
