## Ingredients

- 1 cup almond butter
- ¼ cup raw honey
- 1  egg
- ½ teaspoon baking soda
- ½ teaspoon vanilla extract
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ¼ cup dairy-free and gluten-free chocolate chips (such as Enjoy Life®)

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Line 2 baking sheets with parchment paper.
3. Whisk almond butter, honey, egg, baking soda, vanilla extract, and salt together in a bowl; fold in chocolate chips.
4. Drop batter, about 2 tablespoons per cookie, onto the prepared baking sheet about 1 1/2 inches apart.
5. Bake in the preheated oven until edges start to crisp, 8 to 10 minutes.
6. Cool cookies on baking sheet for 10 minutes before removing.
