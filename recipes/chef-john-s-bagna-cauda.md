## Ingredients

- 6 cloves garlic, roughly chopped, or more to taste
- 1 pinch kosher salt
- 6  anchovy fillets
- ½ cup extra-virgin olive oil
- 3 tablespoons butter
- 2 teaspoons red wine vinegar
- 1 pinch red pepper flakes, or to taste

## Instructions

1. Grind garlic and salt together with a mortar and pestle.
2. Add anchovies; grind with mortar and pestle until a coarse paste forms.
3. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat.
4. Whisk butter into oil until butter melts and foams, about 30 seconds.
5. Reduce heat to low and stir garlic-anchovy mixture into oil mixture; cook and stir until flavors blend and mixture is hot, about 5 minutes.
6. Remove from heat and stir red wine vinegar and red pepper flakes into oil mixture.
