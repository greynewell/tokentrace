## Ingredients

- 1 ½ pounds ground beef
- ½ cup A.1. Original Steak Sauce, divided
- ⅓ cup chopped onion
- ⅓ cup chopped green chilies
- 6 roll (blank)s hamburger buns, split
- ½ cup guacamole
- ¾ cup KRAFT Shredded Cheddar Cheese
- ¾ cup chopped tomato

## Instructions

1. Preheat grill to medium heat. Mix ground beef, 1/4 cup of the steak sauce, the onion and chilies; shape evenly into 6 (1/2-inch-thick) patties.
2. Grill patties, uncovered, 11 to 13 minutes or until cooked through (160 degrees F), turning patties occasionally and brushing with the remaining 1/4 cup steak sauce.
3. Serve in buns topped evenly with the guacamole, cheese and tomatoes.
