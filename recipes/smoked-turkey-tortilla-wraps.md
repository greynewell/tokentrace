## Ingredients

- 10  whole wheat flour tortillas
- 10 slices smoked turkey, cut into thin strips
- 1  avocado - peeled, pitted and sliced
- ½ cup sour cream, for topping
- ½ cup Cheddar cheese

## Instructions

1. Heat flour tortillas in a large skillet over medium heat until slightly browned.
2. Divide turkey strips, avocado, sour cream, and cheese among the warmed tortillas.
3. Fold in half and serve.
