## Ingredients

- 1 cup water
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 ½ teaspoons canola oil
- 1 teaspoon lemon juice
- 3 cups bread flour
- ½ teaspoon ground cinnamon
- 1 teaspoon ground ginger
- 3 tablespoons white sugar
- 1 teaspoon active dry yeast
- ¼ cup chopped crystallized ginger
- 1 tablespoon white sugar

## Instructions

1. Place ingredients except for the crystallized ginger in the pan of the bread machine in the order recommended by the manufacturer.
2. Select dough cycle; press Start.
3. At end of cycle, remove dough onto a floured board.
4. Knead dough briefly, shape into a ball, cover and let rest for 15 minutes.
5. Flatten the dough and gently knead in the crystallized ginger.
6. Form into a loaf shape, place in a lightly greased 9x5 inch loaf pan.
7. Cover and let rise until nearly doubled in size, about 45 minutes.
8. Meanwhile, preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
9. Just before baking, slash top with a razor or sharp knife.
10. Sprinkle with 1 tablespoon sugar if desired.
11. Bake in preheated oven for 30 minutes, or until bottom of loaf sounds hollow when tapped.
12. Remove bread to a wire rack to cool before serving.
