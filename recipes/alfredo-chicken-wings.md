## Ingredients

- 3 pounds chicken wings
- 1 tablespoon seasoned salt
- ¼ cup LAND O LAKES® Salted Butter
- 1 cup heavy cream
- 1 tablespoon minced garlic
- 1 ¼ cups grated Parmesan cheese

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). Grease a large rimmed baking sheet.
2. Place wings on the prepared baking sheet. Sprinkle with seasoned salt; mix to evenly season wings. Arrange in a single layer.
3. Bake in the preheated oven until crisp and cooked through, about 1 hour.
4. Turn on the oven broiler; cook wings under the broiler to crisp both sides, about 1 minute per side. Set aside.
5. Melt butter over medium-low heat in a small saucepan. Add cream and garlic, stirring until small bubbles form. Add cheese and continue to stir until well blended. Remove the pan from heat.
6. Toss wings with cream sauce and serve.
