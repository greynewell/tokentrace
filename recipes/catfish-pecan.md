## Ingredients

- ½ cup cornmeal
- 1 teaspoon salt
- ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
- 1 ¼ pounds catfish
- 3 tablespoons vegetable oil
- ½ cup pecans, finely crushed
- 5 wedges lemon, for garnish
- 5 sprigs fresh parsley, for garnish

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 500 degrees F (260 degrees C).
2. In a large bowl, mix the cornmeal, salt and pepper.
3. Dip the catfish in the cornmeal mixture; coating well.
4. Place catfish on a flat, greased baking sheet.
5. Pour the oil over the fish.
6. Bake in the preheated oven for 15 minutes or until catfish flakes easily when tested with a fork.
7. Sprinkle with pecans toward the end of the cooking period.
8. Garnish with lemon wedges and parsley sprigs.
