## Ingredients

- 2 cups all-purpose flour
- 2 cups white sugar
- 1 teaspoon baking soda
- ½ cup milk
- 2  eggs
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 1 cup water
- ½ cup butter
- ½ cup vegetable oil
- ½ cup peanut butter
- ½ cup butter
- ½ cup peanut butter
- ⅓ cup milk
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 4 ¾ cups confectioners' sugar

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a rimmed baking sheet.
2. Make cake: Whisk together flour, sugar, and baking soda in a large bowl. Beat milk, eggs, and vanilla into flour mixture with an electric mixer on low speed until smooth.
3. Place water, butter, oil, and peanut butter into a saucepan; cook and stir over medium heat until smooth, about 5 minutes. Stir peanut butter mixture into flour mixture until batter is well mixed; pour and spread onto the prepared baking sheet.
4. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center of the cake comes out clean, about 20 minutes.
5. Make icing: Melt butter and peanut butter in a saucepan over medium heat; stir until smooth. Mix in milk and vanilla until combined. Whisk in confectioners' sugar until icing is smooth, 3 to 5 minutes.
6. Pour icing over warm cake.
