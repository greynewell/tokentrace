## Ingredients

- aluminum foil
- ½ pound fresh green beans, trimmed
- ½ tablespoon extra-virgin olive oil
- kosher salt to taste
- ground black pepper to taste
- 1 tablespoon water

## Instructions

1. Preheat an outdoor grill for medium heat and lightly oil the grate. Layer 2 sheets of aluminum foil on a work surface.
2. Toss together green beans and olive oil in a bowl until coated; season with salt and pepper. Place mixture on top of the prepared sheets of foil. Add water, and bring the edges of the foil over the vegetables, sealing to create a pouch.
3. Place the foil pouch on the preheated grill and cook until beans are tender, about 15 minutes.
