## Ingredients

- 2 cups mayonnaise
- 1 cup sweet pickle relish
- ½ teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
- 2 teaspoons salt
- 2 pounds smoked boneless ham, diced
- 1  onion, diced
- 1 small green bell pepper, diced
- 2 stalks celery, diced

## Instructions

1. Stir mayonnaise, relish, salt, and pepper together in a large bowl until well blended. Add diced ham, celery, onion, and bell pepper; toss until coated.
2. Serve immediately, or cover and store in the refrigerator until ready to serve.
