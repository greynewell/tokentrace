## Ingredients

- 2 cups cooked white rice
- ¾ cup all-purpose flour
- 2  eggs
- ½ cup chopped fresh parsley
- ¼ cup grated Parmesan cheese
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- oil for frying

## Instructions

1. Mix white rice, flour, eggs, parsley, Parmesan cheese, salt, and black pepper together with a wooden spoon in a bowl.
2. Shape spoonfuls of the mixture into balls.
3. Heat oil in a large saucepan or deep-fryer over medium heat until hot.
4. Fry balls a few at a time until browned, about 2 minutes per side.
5. Transfer to a plate lined with paper towels.
