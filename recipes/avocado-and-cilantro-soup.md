## Ingredients

- 4  avocados - peeled, pitted and diced
- 1  shallot, finely chopped
- 3 ½ cups cold chicken broth
- 2 tablespoons tomato paste
- 1 teaspoon hot pepper sauce (e.g. Tabasco™)
- 1 tablespoon fresh lime juice, or to taste
- 1 tablespoon tequila  (Optional)
- 2 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
- salt and pepper to taste
- 2 tablespoons sour cream

## Instructions

1. In a large food processor, combine the avocado, shallot, chicken broth, tomato paste, hot pepper sauce, lime juice and tequila.
2. Reserve a little bit of cilantro for garnish, then put the rest into the processor.
3. Process until smooth.
4. If you have an immersion blender, you may do this in a pot or large bowl.
5. Season with salt and pepper.
6. Transfer to a large bowl, cover and refrigerate for at least 2 hours before serving.
7. To serve, divide the soup between four chilled bowls.
8. Top with a dollop of sour cream and a sprig of cilantro.
