## Ingredients

- 2 cups self-rising flour
- ½ cup white sugar
- ½ cup chopped pecans, or to taste  (Optional)
- ¼ cup brown sugar
- 1  egg
- 2 tablespoons vegetable oil

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Combine flour, white sugar, pecans, brown sugar, egg, and oil in a bowl. Mix batter thoroughly. Fill each cup of a muffin tin 2/3-full with batter.
3. Bake in the preheated oven until tops spring back when lightly pressed, 8 to 12 minutes.
