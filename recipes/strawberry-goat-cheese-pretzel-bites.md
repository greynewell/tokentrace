## Ingredients

- 16 each thin pretzel crackers (such as Snack Factory® Pretzel Crisps®)
- 1 (4 ounce) log goat cheese
- 2 tablespoons balsamic glaze, or to taste, divided
- ½ cup sliced fresh strawberries
- 4 leaves fresh basil, cut into strips

## Instructions

1. Arrange pretzel crackers on a serving platter.
2. Spread goat cheese over crackers.
3. Drizzle half of the balsamic glaze on top.
4. Place 3 sliced strawberries on each cracker; scatter basil over strawberries.
5. Drizzle remaining balsamic glaze on top.
