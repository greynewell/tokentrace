## Ingredients

- 3 tablespoons bacon drippings
- ¼ cup all-purpose flour
- 1 ⅔ cups buttermilk, or more as needed
- ½ teaspoon chicken bouillon granules
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper

## Instructions

1. Heat bacon drippings in a skillet over medium heat. Add flour and whisk until well combined and bubbly, about 1 minute. Pour in buttermilk gradually and bring to a boil, about 3 minutes. Reduce heat and simmer, whisking constantly, until thickened and smooth, 2 to 3 minutes.
2. Stir in chicken bouillon, salt, and pepper. If gravy is too thick, whisk in more buttermilk until you reach your desired consistency. Serve immediately.
