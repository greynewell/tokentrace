## Ingredients

- 2 ¼ cups all-purpose flour
- 2 tablespoons sugar
- 1 (.25 ounce) envelope Fleischmann's® RapidRise Yeast
- 1 teaspoon salt
- ¾ cup sour cream
- ¼ cup water
- 2 tablespoons butter or margarine
- 1  large egg

## Instructions

1. Combine 1 cup flour, sugar, undissolved yeast and salt in a large mixer bowl.
2. Heat sour cream, water and butter until very warm (120 to 130 degrees F).
3. Gradually add to flour mixture.
4. Beat 2 minutes at medium speed of electric mixer, scraping bowl occasionally.
5. Add egg and remaining 1-1/4 cups flour to make a soft batter.
6. Spoon evenly into 12 greased (2-1/2 inch) muffin cups.
7. Cover; let rise until doubled in size, about 1 hour.
8. Bake at 400 degrees F for 25 to 30 minutes or until done.
9. Remove from pans; cool on wire rack.
