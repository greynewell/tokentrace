## Ingredients

- 1 ½ cups white sugar
- 3 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa powder
- 2  eggs
- 1 (5 ounce) can evaporated milk
- ¼ cup melted butter
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 1 (9 inch) unbaked pie crust

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Mix sugar and cocoa together in a large bowl. Beat eggs in a medium bowl with an electric mixer until light and fluffy; stir into cocoa mixture. Beat in evaporated milk, butter, and vanilla. Pour mixture into a 9-inch unbaked pie crust.
3. Bake in the preheated oven until set, about 45 minutes. Let cool before slicing.
