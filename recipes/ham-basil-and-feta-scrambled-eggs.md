## Ingredients

- 4  eggs, lightly beaten
- ½ cup diced cooked ham
- ¼ cup crumbled feta cheese
- 1 tablespoon dried basil
- salt and pepper to taste
- 1 ½ teaspoons butter

## Instructions

1. Place a skillet over medium heat.
2. Place the lightly beaten eggs in a bowl; stir in the ham, feta cheese, basil, salt, and pepper.
3. Melt the butter in the skillet.
4. Pour in the egg mixture; cook and stir eggs until firmed and no longer runny, about 5 minutes.
