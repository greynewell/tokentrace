## Ingredients

- 1  russet potato
- 3  carrots
- 3  eggs, or more to taste
- 1 pound bologna, cut into pieces
- 1 (15 ounce) can peas, drained
- 3  dill pickles, diced
- ⅓ cup diced onion
- 1 teaspoon fresh dill, or to taste
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- 2 cups mayonnaise

## Instructions

1. Place potato, carrots, and eggs in a pot and pour in enough water to cover; bring to a boil.
2. Reduce heat and simmer for 15 minutes.
3. Remove carrots using a slotted spoon, cool on a work surface, and dice.
4. Continue simmering eggs and potato for 5 more minutes.
5. Remove eggs with a slotted spoon and transfer to a bowl of ice water until cooled, about 5 minutes.
6. Peel eggs and dice.
7. Continue cooking potato until tender, about 5 more minutes.
8. Drain pot, cool potato on a work surface; dice.
9. Combine potato, carrots, eggs, peas, pickles, onion, dill, salt, and pepper in a bowl.
10. Stir mayonnaise into salad until evenly coated.
