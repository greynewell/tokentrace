## Ingredients

- 5  tomatoes, diced
- 1  onion, chopped
- 1  cucumber, sliced
- 1  green bell pepper, chopped
- ½ cup chopped fresh basil
- ½ cup chopped parsley
- 2 tablespoons crushed garlic
- salt and pepper to taste
- 2 tablespoons white wine vinegar

## Instructions

1. Combine tomatoes, onion, cucumber, bell pepper, basil, parsley, garlic, and vinegar in a large bowl; toss well. Season with salt and pepper.
2. Chill salad in the refrigerator for 15 minutes before serving.
