## Ingredients

- vegetable oil for frying
- 8  all-beef hot dogs
- 8 slices bacon
- 16  toothpicks
- 1 (16 ounce) jar pushcart-style onions in sauce
- 8  hot dog rolls
- 1 (8 ounce) package corn chips, crushed

## Instructions

1. Heat oil in a deep-fryer or large saucepan to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Wrap each hot dog with a slice of bacon, securing ends with toothpicks.
3. Fry hot dogs 2 at a time in hot oil until they float to the surface, 3 to 4 minutes.
4. Drain on paper towels.
5. Pour onions into a small pot over low heat.
6. Stir until heated through, about 5 minutes.
7. Arrange fried hot dogs in rolls.
8. Garnish with onions and crushed corn chips.
