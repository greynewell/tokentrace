## Ingredients

- 2 ½ cups sifted all-purpose flour
- ½ cup flax seed meal
- ¼ cup raw sugar
- 1 tablespoon baking powder
- ½ teaspoon ground cinnamon
- 1 (12 fluid ounce) can or bottle amber ale
- 1 ounce cranberries
- ¼ cup melted butter

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
2. Grease a 5x9-inch loaf pan.
3. Gently whisk flour, flax meal, sugar, baking powder, and cinnamon together in a large bowl.
4. Whisk ale into flour mixture; fold in cranberries.
5. Pour batter into the prepared pan; drizzle melted butter over batter.
6. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted in the middle comes out clean, about 1 hour.
