## Ingredients

- 2 (.25 ounce) packages active dry yeast
- ¾ cup warm water
- 2 cups milk
- 3 tablespoons white sugar
- 2 ½ teaspoons salt
- 3 tablespoons margarine
- 6 ¼ cups all-purpose flour
- 1 (1 ounce) package dry onion soup mix

## Instructions

1. Warm the milk in a small saucepan until it bubbles, then remove from heat.
2. Let cool until lukewarm.
3. In a large bowl, stir together yeast, warm water, and 1 teaspoon sugar.
4. Set aside to proof.
5. Add milk, 2 2/3 tablespoons sugar, salt, butter or margarine, flour, and soup mix to the yeast in the bowl.
6. Combine until dough forms, and then turn out on a lightly floured surface.
7. Knead until dough is elastic.
8. Oil a large bowl.
9. Place the dough in the bowl, and turn several times to coat.
10. Cover the bowl with a damp cloth, and set aside in a warm place to rise for 30 minutes.
11. Divide dough in half.
12. Shape into loaves, and place into two greased 8 1/2 x 4 1/2 inch bread pans.
13. Set aside to rise for 30 minutes.
14. Bake at 375 degrees F (190 degrees C) for 40 minutes.
15. Loaf will sound hollow when done.
