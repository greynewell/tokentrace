## Ingredients

- 4 tablespoons butter, softened
- 4 slices  sourdough bread
- 8  Borden® Mild Cheddar Slices
- 4 tablespoons roasted raspberry chipotle sauce

## Instructions

1. Place a skillet over medium heat.
2. Butter 4 slices of bread with a tablespoon of butter each.
3. Place 2 slices butter side down onto skillet.
4. Place 2 slices of cheese on each.
5. Spread 2 tablespoons of the roasted raspberry chipotle sauce onto each sandwich, and top each with 2 more slices of cheese.
6. Top each with remaining bread, butter side up.
7. Grill until bottom is lightly browned; flip sandwiches over.
8. Grill other side until browned and cheese has melted.
