## Ingredients

- 1 slice French bread
- 2 teaspoons balsamic vinegar
- ¼  avocado, sliced
- 1 dash paprika, or to taste
- salt and freshly cracked black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Toast the French bread lightly, about 1 minute. Sprinkle vinegar on the toasted bread.
2. Layer avocado slices on the bread. Sprinkle paprika, salt, and pepper on top.
