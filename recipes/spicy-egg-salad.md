## Ingredients

- 12  large eggs hard-boiled eggs, chopped
- ¼ cup diced sweet onion
- ¼ cup mayonnaise
- ¼ cup sweet-hot pickle relish
- 1 tablespoon dried celery flakes
- 1 tablespoon spicy mustard
- 1 teaspoon ground chipotle powder
- 1 teaspoon Greek seasoning (such as Cavender's®)
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
- 2 dashes lemon juice
- 2 pinches ground paprika

## Instructions

1. Combine eggs, onion, mayonnaise, relish, celery flakes, mustard, chipotle powder, Greek seasoning, salt, pepper, lemon juice, and paprika in a bowl.
2. Place in the refrigerator for 2 hours before serving.
