## Ingredients

- 1 cup baby spinach leaves
- 1 cup shredded beets
- 1 cup garbanzo beans (chickpeas), drained and rinsed
- 1 cup shredded carrots
- 5  half-pint canning jars with lids and rings
- ¼ cup extra-virgin olive oil
- ¼ cup apple cider vinegar
- 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
- 2 teaspoons honey
- salt and ground black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Layer 1/4 cup spinach, 1/4 cup beets, 1/4 cup garbanzo beans, and 1/4 cup shredded carrots in each of 4 jars.
2. Combine olive oil, vinegar, mustard, honey, salt, and pepper in the remaining jar; place lid on jar.
3. Shake until dressing is evenly combined.
4. Pour 1/4 the dressing over each salad.
