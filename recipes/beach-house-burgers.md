## Ingredients

- 1 pound ground sirloin
- 1 pound ground chuck
- ½ cup chopped onion
- kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
- 1 ½ cups chopped fresh tomatoes
- 2 tablespoons chopped fresh basil
- 8 slices mozzarella cheese
- 8  hamburger buns, split

## Instructions

1. Preheat an outdoor grill for medium-high heat and lightly oil the grate.
2. Mix sirloin, chuck, onion, kosher salt, and pepper together in a bowl with your hands.
3. Form meat mixture into eight 3/4-inch patties.
4. Stir tomatoes and basil together in a bowl until well-combined.
5. Cook patties on the preheated grill until they start to firm, and are reddish-pink and juicy in the center, 3 to 4 minutes per side.
6. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read 130 degrees F (54 degrees C).
7. Place a slice of mozzarella cheese onto each burger during the last minute of cooking.
8. Place 1 burger on the bottom half of each bun, spoon some tomato mixture onto each burger, and place top bun on top.
