## Ingredients

- 4 cups water
- 1 ½ cups farro
- 4  tomatoes, chopped
- 1 large onion, chopped
- 2 tablespoons capers, chopped, or to taste
- 2 cloves garlic, chopped
- 10 leaves fresh basil, chopped
- 3 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil, or to taste

## Instructions

1. Bring water to a boil in a saucepan; add farro.
2. Reduce heat to medium-low, cover, and simmer until farro is tender, about 30 minutes.
3. Drain and let cool.
4. Mix farro, tomatoes, onion, capers, garlic, and basil together in a bowl.
5. Drizzle olive oil over the top.
