## Ingredients

- ⅓ cup raisins
- 1 pound sweet potatoes, peeled and cut into 1/2-inch cubes
- 2 cups water
- 2 tablespoons honey
- 2 tablespoons butter
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ½ teaspoon ground cinnamon
- ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
- ⅛ teaspoon ground turmeric
- 3  saffron threads, crumbled, or to taste

## Instructions

1. Place raisins into a bowl, cover with boiling water, and let stand to plump.
2. Place sweet potatoes into a saucepan, cover with 2 cups water, and bring to a boil.
3. Add honey, butter, salt, cinnamon, black pepper, turmeric, and saffron to potatoes; reduce heat to low.
4. Simmer until potatoes are tender but not mushy, 8 to 10 minutes.
5. Drain raisins and stir into sweet potato mixture.
6. Continue to simmer until mixture has thickened, about 10 more minutes.
7. Let cool before serving.
