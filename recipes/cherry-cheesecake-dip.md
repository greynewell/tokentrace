## Ingredients

- 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
- ½ (7 ounce) jar marshmallow cream (such as Marshmallow Fluff®)
- 1 (8 ounce) container frozen whipped topping (such as Cool Whip®), thawed
- 1 (12 ounce) can cherry pie filling
- 1 individual package graham crackers

## Instructions

1. Mix cream cheese and marshmallow cream until smooth.
2. Fold in whipped topping.
3. Spread into a serving dish.
4. Cover with cherry pie filling.
5. Break graham crackers into sticks and arrange them around the dip.
