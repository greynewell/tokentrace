## Ingredients

- 1 (12 ounce) package firm tofu, drained
- 1  apple, cut into bite-sized pieces
- ¼ cup mustard
- ¼ cup raisins
- ½ teaspoon ground thyme, or to taste
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- 8 slices bread, or to taste

## Instructions

1. Pat tofu dry with a paper towel. Cut into small pieces.
2. Mix tofu, apple, mustard, raisins, thyme, salt, and pepper in a large bowl. Spoon mixture onto bread.
