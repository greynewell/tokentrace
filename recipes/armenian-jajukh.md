## Ingredients

- 2  cucumbers, peeled and diced
- 2 tablespoons nonfat plain yogurt (such as Mountain High®)
- ½ clove garlic, crushed, or to taste
- 1 teaspoon crushed dried mint, or to taste
- 2 pinches salt

## Instructions

1. Mix cucumbers and yogurt together in a bowl.
2. Mix in garlic until fully incorporated.
3. Add mint and salt; mix well.
