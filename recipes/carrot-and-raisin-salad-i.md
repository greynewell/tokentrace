## Ingredients

- 2 cups shredded carrots
- ½ cup diced celery
- ½ cup raisins
- ⅓ cup mayonnaise
- 1 tablespoon distilled white vinegar

## Instructions

1. In a mixing bowl, combine the carrots, celery, raisins, mayonnaise and vinegar.
2. Mix together and refrigerate until chilled.
