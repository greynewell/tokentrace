## Ingredients

- 48 large tiger prawns, peeled and deveined
- 4  limes, zested and juiced
- 4  green chile peppers, seeded and chopped
- 4 cloves garlic, crushed
- 1 (2 inch) piece fresh ginger root, chopped
- 1  medium onion, coarsely chopped
- skewers

## Instructions

1. Place the prawns and lime zest in a large, non-metallic bowl.
2. Place the lime juice, chile pepper, garlic, ginger, and onion in a food processor or blender, and process until smooth.
3. You may need to add a little oil to facilitate blending.
4. Pour over the bowl of prawns, and stir to coat.
5. Cover, and refrigerate for 4 hours.
6. Preheat grill for medium-high heat.
7. Thread prawns onto skewers, piercing each first through the tail, and then the head.
8. Brush grill grate with oil.
9. Cook prawns for 5 minutes, turning once, or until opaque.
