## Ingredients

- 3 cups white cornmeal
- 4 cups boiling water
- 1 ¼ cups white sugar
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 2 cups milk
- 3  eggs
- ½ cup butter, melted

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). Butter a 9x13-inch baking pan.
2. Place cornmeal in a large bowl. Pour in boiling water, stirring until blended. Add sugar and salt; blend using an electric mixer.
3. Beat milk and eggs together; stir into the cornmeal mixture. Add melted butter and blend well. Pour into the prepared pan.
4. Bake in the preheated oven until top is slightly browned, about 1 hour.
