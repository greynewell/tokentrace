## Ingredients

- 1 ½ pounds cooked cocktail shrimp
- 1 (8 ounce) package shredded pepper Jack cheese
- 1  yellow bell pepper, chopped
- ½ cup chopped green onion
- 1 tablespoon Creole seasoning
- 6  skin-on, boneless chicken thighs
- 6 slices thick-cut bacon

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C).
2. Mix shrimp, pepper Jack cheese, yellow bell pepper, and green onion together in a bowl.
3. Stir in Creole seasoning.
4. Place chicken thighs skin-side up on a cutting board.
5. Pound with a meat mallet until somewhat flattened.
6. Flip.
7. Pack an even amount of the shrimp mixture over each chicken thigh.
8. Roll up chicken; wrap tightly with bacon slices.
9. Transfer rolls to a large baking pan.
10. Bake in the preheated oven until juices run clear from chicken and bacon is crisp, about 45 minutes.
