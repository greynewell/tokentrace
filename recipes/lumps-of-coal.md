## Ingredients

- ¼ cup butter
- 4 cups miniature marshmallows
- 1 (20 ounce) package chocolate sandwich cookies (such as Oreo®), crushed

## Instructions

1. Butter a 9x13-inch baking dish.
2. Melt butter in a large saucepan over low heat; add marshmallows and stir until melted. Cook and stir mixture for 2 minutes more. Remove saucepan from heat. Stir crushed cookies into marshmallow mixture until well coated.
3. Press cookie mixture firmly into the prepared baking dish using a buttered spatula or waxed paper; cool. Cut into 2-inch squares.
