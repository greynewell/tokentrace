## Ingredients

- 1 cup bran cereal (such as Kellogg's® All Bran® Original)
- 1 cup mashed bananas
- ¾ cup plain yogurt
- 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- 1 teaspoon baking soda
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ½ cup chopped nuts and/or raisins
- 2 each eggs
- ½ cup packed brown sugar
- ¼ cup cooking oil

## Instructions

1. Combine bran cereal, bananas and yogurt in a medium bowl.
2. Let stand for a couple minutes to allow cereal to soften.
3. Meanwhile, combine flour, baking powder, baking soda, salt, and nuts and/or raisins in a large bowl; set aside.
4. Stir eggs, sugar and oil into bran mixture, then add to dry ingredients; stir just until combined.
5. Spoon batter evenly into 12 greased or paper-lined muffin cups.
6. Bake in a preheated 400 degrees F (200 degrees C) until tops spring back when pressed, about 17 to 20 minutes.
7. Let muffins cool in pan for a few minutes, then remove to a wire rack to finish cooling.
8. Store in an airtight container.
