## Ingredients

- 1  whole-wheat English muffin, split
- 2 tablespoons peanut butter
- ½  banana, thinly sliced
- 2 teaspoons honey
- 2 teaspoons chia seeds

## Instructions

1. Toast English muffin until golden brown, about 2 minutes.
2. Spread 1 tablespoon peanut butter over each half; arrange banana slices on top.
3. Drizzle honey over banana.
4. Sprinkle chia seeds on top.
5. Serve the 2 halves open-faced.
