## Ingredients

- 1 (4.1 ounce) package Idahoan® Roasted Garlic & Parmesan Baby Reds
- 2 (6 ounce) cans lump crabmeat, drained
- 2 (15 ounce) cans water packed artichoke hearts
- 1 cup Brie cheese, broken into chunks
- 1 cup shredded Gruyere cheese
- ½ cup shredded Parmesan cheese
- 1 cup boiling water

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F.
2. Add all ingredients in a large mixing bowl and fold carefully.
3. Use the water from the canned artichokes along with the boiling water.
4. Place in a 9 x 12 baking dish that has been sprayed with olive oil.
5. Bake until bubbling and golden brown.
6. Serve with baguettes or crackers.
