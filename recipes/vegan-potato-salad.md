## Ingredients

- 3 pounds potatoes, cut into bite-size pieces, or more to taste
- 1 cup avocado flesh
- 4 tablespoons olive oil
- 1 small bunch parsley
- ¼  onion
- 2  garlic cloves
- 2 tablespoons lemon juice
- 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
- salt and ground black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Fill a large pot with salted water and bring to a boil.
2. Add potatoes.
3. Reduce heat to medium-low and simmer until fork-tender, 10 to 20 minutes.
4. Drain immediately and rinse with cold water.
5. Set aside.
6. Combine avocado, oil, parsley, onion, garlic, lemon juice, and mustard in a blender.
7. Blend until dressing is smooth.
8. Thin, if necessary, by adding 1 tablespoon of water at a time.
9. Transfer cooked potatoes to a large bowl and pour dressing on top.
10. Gently mix until thoroughly coated.
11. Season with salt and pepper.
