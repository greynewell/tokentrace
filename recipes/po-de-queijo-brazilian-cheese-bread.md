## Ingredients

- 1 cup half-and-half
- ½ cup vegetable oil
- 1 pinch salt
- 2 cups tapioca flour
- 2 cups finely grated Pecorino Romano cheese
- 2  eggs

## Instructions

1. Heat half-and-half, vegetable, and salt together in a saucepan over medium heat; constantly whisk until mixture just starts to boil, about 5 minutes.
2. Remove from heat and pour mixture into a large deep bowl.
3. Stir tapioca flour into half-and-half mixture using a wooden spoon.
4. Mixture will thicken quickly.
5. Cool to room temperature, about 20 minutes.
6. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
7. Mix Pecorino Romano cheese and eggs into tapioca mixture using an electric mixer or by hand until dough has a paste-like texture.
8. Continue mixing until dough is soft and stretchy.
9. Roll dough into 2- or 3-inch balls and arrange on a baking sheet.
10. Bake in the preheated oven until cheese balls are puffed and hardened on the outside, 20 to 25 minutes.
11. Serve warm.
