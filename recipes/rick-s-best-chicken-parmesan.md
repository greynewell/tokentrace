## Ingredients

- 4  skinless, boneless chicken breasts
- ¼ cup all-purpose flour
- ¼ cup crushed fish-shaped crackers (such as Goldfish®)
- 3 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese, divided
- ½ teaspoon garlic powder
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ½ teaspoon ground black pepper
- 2  eggs
- ¾ cup vegetable oil

## Instructions

1. Place chicken breasts between 2 sheets of heavy plastic on a solid, level surface.
2. Firmly pound with the smooth side of a meat mallet to 1/2-inch thickness.
3. Combine flour, crackers, 1 1/2 tablespoons Parmesan cheese, garlic powder, salt, and pepper in a resealable plastic bag.
4. Beat eggs in a shallow bowl.
5. Dip chicken breasts into eggs to coat; place in flour mixture and seal bag.
6. Toss chicken in the flour mixture until well covered.
7. Heat oil in a skillet over high heat.
8. Add chicken breasts; cook until underside is browned and crispy, 4 to 5 minutes.
9. Flip; sprinkle 1 teaspoon Parmesan cheese over each chicken breast.
10. Spoon hot oil over cheese until melted.
11. Drain on paper towels for 2 to 3 minutes before serving.
