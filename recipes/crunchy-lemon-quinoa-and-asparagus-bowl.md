## Ingredients

- 1 pound fresh asparagus, trimmed
- 2 teaspoons coconut oil
- ½  onion, diced
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 cup cooked red quinoa
- ½ cup low-sodium vegetable stock
- 1 tablespoon ground turmeric
- ½ cup nutritional yeast
- ½ large lemon, juiced

## Instructions

1. Fill a saucepan halfway with water; bring to a boil.
2. Add asparagus and cook uncovered until tender but still crispy, 2 to 3 minutes.
3. Drain in a colander and rinse with cold water to stop the cooking process.
4. Heat coconut oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
5. Add the onion and garlic; cook until onion is translucent, about 5 minutes.
6. Stir in cooked quinoa, vegetable stock, and turmeric; cook until flavors combine, 5 to 6 minutes.
7. Stir in blanched asparagus, nutritional yeast, and lemon juice; cook and stir, 3 to 4 minutes.
