## Ingredients

- ¾ cup pecan halves
- 1 teaspoon butter
- 3 tablespoons pure maple syrup, or more to taste
- 2 pinches salt

## Instructions

1. Heat a skillet over medium heat; add pecans and lower heat to medium-low.
2. Cook, stirring constantly, until pecans are lightly crisp and turn a darker brown, about 5 minutes.
3. Remove skillet from stove and slightly cool pecans.
4. Mix butter into warm pecans and toss to coat.
5. Turn heat to medium; add maple syrup and stir pecans with a heat-proof or wooden spoon until syrup begins to bubble, 1 to 2 minutes.
6. Turn heat down to low and continue stirring pecans until syrup begins to caramelize onto the pecans, about 5 minutes more.
7. Remove skillet from heat, sprinkle salt over pecans, and stir.
