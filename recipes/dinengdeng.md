## Ingredients

- 2 (8 ounce) fillets milkfish (bangus)
- 1  tomato, quartered
- 1  onion, chopped
- 2 tablespoons shrimp paste (bagoong)
- 1 cup water
- salt and pepper to taste
- ½ pound long beans, cut into bite-size pieces
- ½ pound zucchini, cut into bite-size pieces
- ½ pound fresh okra

## Instructions

1. Preheat an outdoor grill for medium heat and lightly oil the grate.
2. Grill the milkfish fillets until the flesh flakes easily with a fork, 2 to 3 minutes per side.
3. Combine the grilled fillets, tomato, onion, shrimp paste, and water to the pot; bring to a boil for 5 minutes.
4. Season with salt and pepper.
5. Add the long beans and zucchini; stir.
6. Cover the pot and cook over medium heat for 5 minutes.
7. Stir the okra into the mixture and cook another 5 minutes.
8. Serve hot.
