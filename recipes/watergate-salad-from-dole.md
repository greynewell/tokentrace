## Ingredients

- 1 (4 serving size) package instant pistachio flavor pudding mix
- 1 (20 ounce) can DOLE® Crushed Pineapple, undrained
- 1 cup miniature marshmallows
- ½ cup chopped pecans
- 1 ½ cups thawed whipped topping

## Instructions

1. Mix dry pudding mix, crushed pineapple, marshmallows and pecans in large bowl until well blended. Gently stir in whipped topping; cover.
2. Refrigerate 1 hour or until ready to serve.
