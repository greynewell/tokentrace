## Ingredients

- 2 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened
- 1 (16 ounce) jar sliced jalapeno peppers, finely chopped
- 3 ½ cups grated Cheddar cheese
- 2 ¼ cups finely chopped walnuts, divided

## Instructions

1. Combine cream cheese, jalapeno peppers, Cheddar cheese, and 1/4 cup walnuts in a bowl; mix well.
2. Divide cheese mixture in half and shape into balls or logs.
3. Roll balls in remaining 2 cups walnuts until well coated.
4. Cover and refrigerate cheese balls until firm, 3 hours to overnight.
