## Ingredients

- 4 (4 ounce) boneless, skinless chicken breast halves
- 1 teaspoon chicken seasoning blend
- 1 (8 ounce) package Borden® Mozzarella Cheese Slices
- 4  ciabatta rolls, sliced horizontally
- Olive oil
- 2 cups packaged spring mix lettuce blend
- 1  tomato, cut into wedges
- ¼ cup prepared balsamic vinaigrette
- ½ cup prepared sun-dried tomato mayonnaise
- 1  ripe avocado, pitted, peeled and sliced  (Optional)

## Instructions

1. Preheat grill.
2. Season chicken breasts with chicken seasoning.
3. Grill over medium heat for 5 to 8 minutes on each side or until fully internal temperature reaches 165 degrees F.
4. Top chicken with sliced cheese keep on grill until cheese melts; remove from grill and keep warm.
5. Brush cut sides of rolls lightly with oil; grill for 1 minute.
6. Toss lettuce blend and tomatoes with vinaigrette in a medium bowl.
7. Spread mayonnaise equally on cut sides of sandwich rolls.
8. Layer lettuce blend, grilled chicken breast and avocado slices (optional) on each roll.
9. Serve sandwiches immediately.
