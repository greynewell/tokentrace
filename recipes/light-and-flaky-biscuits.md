## Ingredients

- 1 cup milk
- 1 large egg
- 3 cups all-purpose flour
- 2 tablespoons white sugar
- 4 ½ teaspoons baking powder
- ¾ teaspoon cream of tartar
- ¾ teaspoon salt
- ¾ cup cold margarine

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C).
2. Whisk milk and egg together.
3. Mix flour, sugar, baking powder, cream of tartar, and salt together in a bowl. Add margarine and cut in with a pastry blender or 2 knives. Add milk-egg mixture and mix just enough to combine.
4. Knead dough briefly, 5 to 7 times. Roll to a 1 1/2-inch thickness and cut out 14 biscuits.
5. Bake in the preheated oven until golden brown, about 12 minutes.
