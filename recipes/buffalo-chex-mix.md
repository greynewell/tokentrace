## Ingredients

- 4 cups Rice Chex® cereal
- 4 cups Wheat Chex® cereal
- 2 cups Parmesan-flavored crackers
- 2 cups pretzel twists
- 6 tablespoons butter
- 2 ½ tablespoons hot sauce
- 1 (1 ounce) packet ranch dressing mix
- 2 teaspoons celery seed

## Instructions

1. In large microwavable bowl, mix cereals, crackers and pretzels; set aside.
2. In  small microwavable bowl, microwave butter uncovered on High about 40 seconds or until melted.
3. Stir in hot sauce and seasonings.
4. Pour over cereal mixture; stir until evenly coated.
5. Microwave uncovered on High 4 to 5 minutes, thoroughly stirring every 2 minutes.
6. Spread on paper towels to cool.
7. Store in airtight container.
