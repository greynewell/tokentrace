## Ingredients

- 1 pound ground beef
- 2 ½ cups water
- 1 (8 ounce) package ZATARAIN'S® Dirty Rice Mix, Original
- 2 tablespoons butter, divided
- ¼ cup pecan pieces
- ¼ cup chopped red bell pepper
- ¼ cup chopped yellow bell pepper
- ½ cup raisins
- ½ cup crumbled cooked bacon
- ½ cup thinly sliced green onions

## Instructions

1. Cook ground beef in large skillet on medium-high heat until no longer pink.
2. Drain fat; set aside.
3. Add water to same skillet; bring to boil.
4. Stir in Rice Mix and ground beef; return to boil.
5. Reduce heat to low; cover and simmer 25 minutes or until rice is tender.
6. Remove from heat.
7. Let stand 5 minutes.
8. Melt 1 tablespoon of the butter in medium skillet on medium heat.
9. Add pecans; cook and stir 2 minutes or until lightly browned.
10. Add to rice mixture.
11. Melt remaining 1 tablespoon butter in same skillet.
12. Add bell peppers and raisins; cook and stir 3 minutes or until tender.
13. Add to rice mixture; stir until well mixed.
14. Sprinkle with bacon and green onions before serving.
