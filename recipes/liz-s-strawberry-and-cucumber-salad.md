## Ingredients

- 1  cucumber - peeled, seeded, and sliced
- 2 cups sliced fresh strawberries
- 1 cup plain yogurt
- 1 clove garlic, chopped
- 2 tablespoons minced fresh mint
- 1 tablespoon minced fresh parsley

## Instructions

1. Place cucumber and strawberries in a bowl.
2. Mix yogurt, garlic, mint, and parsley together in a separate bowl; pour over cucumber and strawberries.
3. Stir to combine.
4. Chill for 1 hour before serving.
