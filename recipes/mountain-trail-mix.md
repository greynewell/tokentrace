## Ingredients

- ½ cup cashews
- ¼ cup peanuts
- ¼ cup almonds  (Optional)
- ½ cup raisins
- 1 (1.69 ounce) package candy-coated milk chocolate pieces (such as M&M's®)

## Instructions

1. Combine the cashews, peanuts, almonds, raisins, and chocolate in a bowl.
2. Store in an airtight container to store.
