## Ingredients

- cooking spray
- 1 ½ pounds uncooked elbow macaroni
- 1 (15 ounce) jar Alfredo sauce
- 1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of mushroom soup
- 1 cup heavy cream
- 3 tablespoons parsley, divided
- 8 ounces cooked salmon, flaked
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- 2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese
- 1 cup crushed cheese-flavored snack chips (such as Sunchips®)

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Coat a 9x11-inch baking dish with cooking spray.
3. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
4. Cook elbow macaroni in the boiling water, stirring occasionally, until tender yet firm to the bite, about 8 minutes.
5. Drain.
6. Combine  , condensed mushroom soup, cream, and 2 tablespoons parsley in a large pot and bring to a simmer, about 5 minutes.
7. Fold in cooked macaroni and salmon.
8. Season with salt and pepper.
9. Spoon mixture into the prepared baking dish.
10. Top with Cheddar cheese followed by crushed chips.
11. Bake in the preheated oven until melty and bubbly, about 15 minutes.
12. Garnish with remaining parsley.
