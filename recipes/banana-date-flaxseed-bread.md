## Ingredients

- ½ cup flax seed
- 3  bananas, mashed
- ¼ cup vegetable oil
- ½ cup white sugar
- 2  eggs
- 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
- ½ teaspoon baking powder
- ½ teaspoon baking soda
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ¼ cup flax seed
- ½ cup chopped pitted dates

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly grease an 8x4-inch loaf pan.
2. Grind 1/2 cup flax seed in an electric coffee grinder or food processor; set aside.
3. Beat together banana, oil, sugar, and eggs in a large bowl. Mix together flour, baking powder, baking soda, salt, ground flax seed, and 1/4 cup whole flax seed in a separate bowl. Gradually stir flour mixture into banana mixture. Fold in dates. Spoon batter into the prepared loaf pan.
4. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the loaf comes out clean, 55 to 60 minutes.
