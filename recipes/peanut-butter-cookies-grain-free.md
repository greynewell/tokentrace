## Ingredients

- 2 cups creamy natural peanut butter
- 1 cup honey
- 2 tablespoons honey
- 3  eggs
- ½ cup coconut flour
- 1 ½ teaspoons vanilla extract
- ½ teaspoon baking soda

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C).
2. Mix peanut butter, 1 cup plus 2 tablespoons honey, eggs, coconut flour, vanilla extract, and baking soda together in a large bowl until cookie dough is well-combined.
3. Drop teaspoonfuls of cookie dough onto ungreased baking sheets, spacing them about 2 inches apart.
4. Bake in the preheated oven until golden, about 15 minutes.
5. Use a fork to press a crisscross pattern into cookies immediately after removing from the oven.
