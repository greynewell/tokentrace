## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 1  onion, chopped
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1  carrot, sliced
- 1 stalk celery, sliced
- 3 ¼ cups chicken broth
- 2 ¼ pounds mussels, cleaned and debearded
- 1 bunch fresh spinach, leaves torn in half

## Instructions

1. Heat the olive oil in a large pot over medium heat.
2. Cook the onion and garlic in the hot oil until softened, 5 to 7 minutes.
3. Stir the carrot and celery into the mixture and continue cooking until the carrot and celery have also softened, another 7 to 10 minutes.
4. Pour the chicken broth into the pot and bring to a boil; cook at a boil for 5 minutes.
5. Add the mussels; cover the pot and allow the mussels to simmer in the broth mixture just until they begin to open, about 5 minutes.
6. Discard any mussels which do not open.
7. Stir the spinach into the broth just before serving hot.
