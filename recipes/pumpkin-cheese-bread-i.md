## Ingredients

- 2 ½ cups white sugar
- 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese
- ½ cup margarine, softened
- 4  eggs
- 1 (15 ounce) can canned pumpkin puree
- 3 ½ cups all-purpose flour
- 2 teaspoons baking soda
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
- ½ teaspoon baking powder
- ¼ teaspoon ground cloves
- 1 cup chopped nuts

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Grease two 9x5 inch loaf pans.
3. In a large mixing bowl, cream together sugar, cream cheese and margarine.
4. Add the eggs, one at a time, mixing well after each addition.
5. Blend in pumpkin; mixing until completely incorporated.
6. Combine the flour, baking soda, salt, cinnamon, baking powder and cloves.
7. Mix the dry ingredients into the pumpkin mixture; stirring just until moistened.
8. Fold in the walnuts.
9. Divide the batter evenly between the two pans.
10. Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 60 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of a loaf comes out clean.
11. Let cool in the pans for 5 minutes then remove from pans to cool completely on a wire rack.
