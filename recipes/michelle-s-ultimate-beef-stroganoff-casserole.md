## Ingredients

- 1 (16 ounce) package egg noodles
- 1 pound ground beef
- 1 pinch salt and ground black pepper to taste
- 1 pinch garlic powder  (Optional)
- 2 (10.75 ounce) cans  cream of mushroom soup
- 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese
- ½ cup milk, plus more to taste
- 1 (11 ounce) can diced carrots

## Instructions

1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook egg noodles in boiling water, stirring occasionally, until tender yet firm to the bite, 8 to 10 minutes. Drain and transfer to a large dish.
2. Season ground beef with salt, pepper, and garlic powder.
3. Heat a large skillet over medium heat. Add seasoned beef; cook and stir until browned, 6 to 8 minutes. Add cream of mushroom soup, cream cheese, and milk; cook and stir until cream cheese melts and mixture is saucy, about 6 minutes.
4. Pour mixture over noodles. Add carrots; stir until evenly combined.
