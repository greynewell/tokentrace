## Ingredients

- 2 pounds octopus, head and tentacles separated
- 1 pound peeled raw shrimp
- 1 large red onion, diced
- 1 tablespoon kosher salt
- 3  bay leaves
- 1 pinch freshly ground black pepper to taste
- 10  Roma tomatoes, diced
- 2 teaspoons sriracha sauce

## Instructions

1. Cut octopus tentacles away from the piece that holds them together; cut into smaller pieces.
2. Cut head in half.
3. Combine octopus pieces and shrimp in a skillet over medium-high heat; cover and cook in their juices until octopus is almost tender, about 25 minutes.
4. Transfer octopus and shrimp to a bowl, reserving juices in the skillet.
5. Stir onion, salt, bay leaves, and black pepper into the skillet; increase heat and cook until juices are reduced and onion takes on the color of the juices, 3 to 5 minutes.
6. Stir in tomatoes and sriracha sauce; cook for 5 minutes.
7. Return octopus and shrimp to the skillet.
8. Reduce heat to a simmer and cook until flavors combine and sauce thickens, about 30 minutes.
