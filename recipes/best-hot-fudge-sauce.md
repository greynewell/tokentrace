## Ingredients

- 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips
- 1 cup butter
- 4 cups confectioners' sugar
- 2 ⅔ cups evaporated milk
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- ⅛ teaspoon salt

## Instructions

1. In a large saucepan, combine chocolate chips and butter. Cook and stir over low heat until melted.
2. Gradually mix in confectioners' sugar and milk. Increase heat to a boil. Cook, stirring constantly, for 8 minutes.
3. Remove from heat, then stir in vanilla and salt. Serve warm. Store in refrigerator.
