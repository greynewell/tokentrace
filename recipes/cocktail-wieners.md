## Ingredients

- 1 cup barbeque sauce
- ¾ cup grape jelly
- 2 (16 ounce) packages cocktail sausages (such as Lit'l Smokies®)

## Instructions

1. Mix barbeque sauce and grape jelly in a slow cooker; add cocktail sausages and stir until well coated.
2. Cook on Low for 2 to 3 hours.
