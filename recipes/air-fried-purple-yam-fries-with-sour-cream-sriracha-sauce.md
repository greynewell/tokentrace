## Ingredients

- 1 ⅓ pounds ube (purple yam), peeled and cut into 1/4-inch-thick fries
- 2 teaspoons olive oil
- 1 teaspoon sriracha-flavored salt
- ⅓ cup sour cream
- 2 teaspoons sriracha sauce, or more to taste

## Instructions

1. Preheat an air fryer to 320 degrees F (160 degrees C).
2. Place yam fries into a bowl. Add olive oil and sriracha-flavored salt; toss to coat evenly. Pour into the air fryer basket.
3. Cook in the preheated air fryer for 16 minutes. Increase heat to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Shake basket and cook for 5 minutes more.
4. Mix sour cream and sriracha sauce together in a bowl. Serve alongside yam fries.
