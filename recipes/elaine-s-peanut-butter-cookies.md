## Ingredients

- 1 (18.25 ounce) package yellow cake mix
- 1 cup creamy peanut butter
- ½ cup vegetable oil
- 2  eggs
- 2 tablespoons water

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Pour the cake mix into a large bowl.
3. Make a well in the center, and add peanut butter, oil, eggs, and water.
4. Mix until well blended.
5. Drop by teaspoonfuls onto ungreased cookie sheets.
6. Flatten slightly using a fork dipped in water.
7. Bake for 10 to 12 minutes in the preheated oven.
8. Let cookies set on cookie sheet for 2 to 3 minutes before carefully removing from the cookie sheet to cool on wire racks.
