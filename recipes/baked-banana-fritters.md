## Ingredients

- ⅓ cup dry bread crumbs
- 2 tablespoons dry shredded coconut
- 1 teaspoon white sugar
- ¼ teaspoon ground cinnamon
- 1  egg white
- 2  bananas, cut into bite-size pieces

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
2. Combine bread crumbs, coconut, sugar, and cinnamon together in a bowl. Beat egg white in a small bowl until frothy. Dip each banana piece in egg white and press into bread crumb mixture. Place the breaded bananas on prepared baking sheet; do not stack.
3. Bake in preheated oven until golden brown, about 10 minutes.
