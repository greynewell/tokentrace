## Ingredients

- cooking spray
- 2 ½ cups fresh cranberries
- 1 ¼ cups white sugar, divided
- 1 cup all-purpose flour
- ¾ cup butter, melted
- 2  eggs

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C). Spray an 8-inch baking dish with cooking spray.
2. Spread cranberries into the prepared baking dish and sprinkle with 1/4 cup sugar.
3. Stir together remaining 1 cup sugar, flour, and melted butter in a large bowl. Whisk eggs in a separate bowl, then stir into sugar-flour mixture until smooth. Pour over cranberries in the baking dish.
4. Bake in the preheated oven until berries are bubbling and topping is lightly browned, about 1 hour.
