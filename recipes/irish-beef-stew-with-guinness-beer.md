## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 1  onion, chopped
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 teaspoon salt
- ½ teaspoon ground black pepper
- 2 pounds beef stew meat, cubed
- 3 cups stout beer (such as Guinness®)
- 2  potatoes, peeled and sliced
- 2  potatoes, peeled and quartered
- salt and ground black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Heat the olive oil in a large pot over medium heat.
2. Stir in the onion, garlic, salt, and pepper.
3. Cook and stir until the onion has softened and turned translucent, about 5 minutes.
4. Stir in the beef, beer, sliced potatoes, and quartered potatoes.
5. Bring to a boil over high heat, then reduce heat to medium-low, cover, and simmer until the beef is tender, about 2 hours.
6. Season to taste with salt and pepper before serving.
