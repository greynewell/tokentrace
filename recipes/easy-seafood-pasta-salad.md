## Ingredients

- 1 ½ (8 ounce) packages tri-color pasta
- 1 cup mayonnaise, or to taste
- 3 tablespoons milk
- 2 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
- 1 ½ tablespoons white sugar
- 1 teaspoon salt, or to taste
- ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper, or to taste
- 1 pound imitation crabmeat
- 2 cups frozen green peas, thawed
- 6  carrots, diced
- 3 stalks celery, diced
- 1  green bell pepper, diced
- ½  sweet onion, diced

## Instructions

1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
2. Cook the tri-color pasta at a boil, stirring occasionally, until cooked through yet firm to the bite, 8 to 10 minutes.
3. Rinse pasta under cold water until cool; drain.
4. Whisk mayonnaise, milk, vinegar, sugar, salt, and black pepper together in a large bowl until smooth.
5. Add pasta, imitation crabmeat, peas, carrots, celery, green pepper, and onion; mix until evenly coated.
6. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate to let flavors combine, at least 5 hours.
