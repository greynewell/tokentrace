## Ingredients

- 1 cup white rice flour
- ¼ cup steel cut oats
- 1 tablespoon flax seed
- 1 teaspoon baking soda
- 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
- ½ teaspoon ground nutmeg
- ½ teaspoon arrowroot powder  (Optional)
- ⅔ cup plain yogurt
- 1  egg, lightly beaten
- 1 ½ tablespoons butter, melted
- 2  Gala apples - peeled, cored, and chopped
- ¼ cup cranberry juice

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Lightly grease a 6 cup large muffin pan.
3. In a bowl, whisk together the rice flour, steel cut oats, flax seed, baking soda, cinnamon, nutmeg, and arrowroot powder.
4. In a separate bowl, mix the yogurt, egg, butter, apples, and cranberry juice.
5. Thoroughly mix the dry ingredients into the wet ingredients to form a slightly runny batter.
6. Spoon evenly into the prepared muffin tin cups.
7. Bake 30 minutes in the preheated oven, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of a muffin comes out clean.
