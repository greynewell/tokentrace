## Ingredients

- ¼ cup all-purpose flour
- 2 pounds beef round steak, cut into strips
- 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
- 1 (14.5 ounce) can cream of mushroom soup
- 1 (14.5 ounce) can Cheddar cheese soup
- ½ small onion, chopped
- 1 tablespoon chopped green bell pepper, or more to taste
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
- ½ teaspoon browning sauce (such as Kitchen Bouquet®), or to taste
- salt and ground black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Place flour into a bowl. Dredge strips of round steak in flour to coat.
2. Heat vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium heat; brown strips of round steak in the hot oil, 7 to 8 minutes per side.
3. Transfer beef strips to a slow cooker. Add cream of mushroom soup, Cheddar cheese soup, onion, green bell pepper, garlic, Worcestershire sauce, and browning sauce; stir. Season mixture with salt and black pepper.
4. Cook on High and cook 4 hours.
