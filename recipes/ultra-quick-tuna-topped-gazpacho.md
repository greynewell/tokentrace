## Ingredients

- 2  sweet tomatoes, halved, or more to taste
- ½  cucumber, roughly chopped
- 2 cloves garlic, or more to taste, peeled
- 1 cup olive oil
- 1 teaspoon hot sauce (such as Frank's RedHot ®), or more to taste
- 1 pinch salt and ground black pepper to taste
- 1 tablespoon water as needed  (Optional)
- ½  green bell pepper
- 1 (5 ounce) can tuna, or more to taste, drained and flaked

## Instructions

1. Combine tomatoes, cucumber, and garlic in a high-powered blend; blend until smooth, 15 to 30 seconds.
2. Add olive oil, hot sauce, salt, and pepper and blend until smooth, 5 to 15 seconds.
3. Add about 1 teaspoon water at a time if mixture is too thick and blend until smooth.
4. Add bell pepper to tomato mixture; blend until finely chopped but not liquified, 1 to 2 seconds.
5. Chill gazpacho in the refrigerator for at least 2 hours.
6. Top gazpacho with tuna to serve.
