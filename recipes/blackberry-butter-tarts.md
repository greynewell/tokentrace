## Ingredients

- 12 (3 inch) unbaked tart shells
- 1 cup fresh blackberries, rinsed and patted dry
- 1  lemon, zested
- ¾ cup brown sugar
- ⅓ cup honey
- 1  egg
- 2 tablespoons melted butter
- 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
- 1 pinch salt

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Place tart shells on a baking sheet.
2. Mash blackberries and lemon zest together lightly in a small bowl. Divide blackberry mixture evenly into the bottom of tart shells.
3. Whisk brown sugar, honey, egg, butter, vanilla extract, and salt together in a bowl; spoon mixture evenly over blackberries in shells.
4. Bake in the preheated oven until filling is bubbly and shells are golden, 22 to 27 minutes.
