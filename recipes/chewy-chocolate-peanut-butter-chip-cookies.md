## Ingredients

- 1 ½ cups butter, melted
- 2 cups white sugar
- 2  eggs
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 2 cups all-purpose flour
- ¾ cup unsweetened cocoa powder
- 1 teaspoon baking soda
- ½ teaspoon salt
- 2 cups peanut butter chips

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. In a large bowl, mix together butter and sugar.
3. Beat in eggs and vanilla.
4. Combine flour, cocoa, baking soda, and salt; gradually stir into the butter mixture.
5. Mix in peanut butter chips.
6. Drop by rounded teaspoons onto ungreased cookie sheets.
7. Bake 8 to 10 minutes in preheated oven.
8. Cool for 1 minute before placing on wire racks to cool completely.
