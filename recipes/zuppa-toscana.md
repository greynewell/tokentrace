## Ingredients

- 1 (16 ounce) package smoked sausage
- 2  potatoes, cut into 1/4-inch slices
- ¾ cup chopped onion
- 6 slices bacon
- 1 ½ teaspoons minced garlic
- 2 cups kale - washed, dried, and shredded
- 2 tablespoons chicken bouillon powder
- 1 quart water
- ⅓ cup heavy whipping cream

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 300 degrees F (150 degrees C).
2. Arrange sausage links onto a baking sheet; bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes, or until cooked through. Cut in half lengthwise, then cut at an angle into 1/2-inch slices.
3. Place onions and bacon in a large saucepan; cook and stir over medium heat until bacon is crispy and onions are tender. Remove bacon and crumble; set aside.
4. Add garlic; cook for 1 minute. Add bouillon powder, water, and potatoes; simmer for 15 minutes.
5. Add crumbled bacon, sausage, kale, and cream. Simmer for 4 minutes and serve.
