## Ingredients

- 2 cups diced tomatoes
- 1 cup diced fresh mozzarella cheese
- 1 small shallot, minced
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- 1 tablespoon chopped fresh basil  (Optional)
- 2 teaspoons balsamic vinegar
- 2 teaspoons extra-virgin olive oil
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ½ teaspoon ground black pepper
- 1 ¼ cups water
- 1 cup pearl (Israeli) couscous

## Instructions

1. Combine tomatoes, mozzarella cheese, shallot, garlic, basil, balsamic vinegar, olive oil, salt, and black pepper in a large bowl.
2. Refrigerate for 20 to 30 minutes.
3. Bring the water to a boil in a saucepan.
4. Remove from the heat, and stir in the couscous.
5. Cover and let stand for 10 minutes; fluff with a fork.
6. Allow couscous to cool, then toss with tomato mixture.
