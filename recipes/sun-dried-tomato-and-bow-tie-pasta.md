## Ingredients

- 4 tablespoons dried basil
- 1 tablespoon minced pine nuts
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 3 ounces sun-dried tomatoes
- ⅛ cup olive oil
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 8 ounces fresh mushrooms, sliced
- ½ teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon cayenne pepper
- 1 (16 ounce) package bow tie pasta

## Instructions

1. Combine basil, pine nuts, and 1 tablespoon of olive oil in a small bowl; set pesto aside.
2. Blanch sun-dried tomatoes for 30 seconds in boiling water. Drain well and slice into small pieces.
3. Heat 1/8 cup olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat; add garlic and sauté until golden brown. Stir in mushrooms and sauté until tender. Add prepared pesto, sun-dried tomatoes, cayenne pepper, and salt; reduce heat to low and simmer sauce.
4. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook bow-tie pasta at a boil, stirring occasionally, until tender yet firm to the bite, about 12 minutes; drain well. Toss pasta and sauce together until well coated.
