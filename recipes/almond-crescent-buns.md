## Ingredients

- ¼ cup margarine, softened
- ½ cup packed brown sugar
- 2 teaspoons all-purpose flour
- 2 tablespoons milk
- ⅜ teaspoon almond extract
- 2 (8 ounce) cans refrigerated crescent rolls
- 2 tablespoons butter, melted
- ¼ cup chopped toasted almonds
- ½ cup confectioners' sugar

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (195 degrees C).
2. Combine softened butter or margarine, brown sugar, flour, 1 tablespoon milk, and 1/8 teaspoon almond extract.
3. Mix well.
4. Spoon mixture into 12 ungreased muffin cups.
5. Separate dough into 4 long rectangles.
6. Firmly press perforations to seal.
7. Combine melted butter and 1/8 teaspoon almond extract; brush on dough.
8. Sprinkle with nuts, and roll dough up jellyroll fashion.
9. Cut each roll of dough into 3 slices.
10. Place in muffin cups.
11. Place muffin pan on a baking sheet.
12. Bake for 15 to 20 minutes, or until golden brown.
13. When done, invert rolls immediately onto cooling rack.
14. Combine confectioners' sugar, 1 tablespoon milk, and 1/8 teaspoon almond extract.
15. Drizzle over hot rolls.
