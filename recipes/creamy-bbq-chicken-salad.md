## Ingredients

- 4  large pieces frozen, breaded chicken
- 1 head romaine lettuce, chopped
- 1 tablespoon milk
- 1 tablespoon sour cream
- 1 teaspoon ranch dressing mix
- 3 tablespoons barbeque sauce (such as Sweet Baby Ray's®)
- 1 (15 ounce) can kidney beans, rinsed and drained
- 1 cup frozen corn, thawed
- 1  tomato, chopped

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C).
2. Spread chicken onto a baking sheet.
3. Bake chicken in the preheated oven until cooked through, 20 to 30 minutes.
4. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 165 degrees F (74 degrees C).
5. Put lettuce in a large bowl.
6. Whisk milk, sour cream, and ranch dressing mix together in a bowl until smooth; add to lettuce and toss to coat.
7. Cut cooked chicken into small pieces and toss with barbeque sauce in a bowl.
8. Mix barbeque chicken, kidney beans, corn, and tomato with lettuce.
