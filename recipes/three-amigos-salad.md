## Ingredients

- 1  Haas avocado, cut into bite-sized pieces
- 1  mango, cut into bite-sized pieces
- 1  grapefruit, cut into bite-sized pieces
- ½  lime

## Instructions

1. Place the avocado, mango, and grapefruit into a mixing bowl.
2. Squeeze the lime over the fruit, and stir gently until mixed.
