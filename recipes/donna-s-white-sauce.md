## Ingredients

- ¼ cup butter
- 3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
- 1 quart heavy cream
- 4 ounces Havarti cheese

## Instructions

1. Melt butter in a medium sauce pan over medium heat.
2. Whisk in flour.
3. Remove from heat and add a small amount of cream, stirring to prevent clumping.
4. Return to low heat and add cheese.
5. Stir until cheese melts and sauce thickens.
