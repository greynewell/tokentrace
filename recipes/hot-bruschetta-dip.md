## Ingredients

- 2  roma (plum) tomatoes, chopped
- 1 small onion, chopped
- 1 clove garlic, peeled and minced
- 1 teaspoon chopped fresh basil
- 1 teaspoon chopped fresh parsley
- ¼ cup olive oil
- 1 (8 ounce) wheel Brie cheese

## Instructions

1. In a small bowl, mix roma (plum) tomatoes, onion, garlic, basil, parsley and olive oil.
2. Cover and chill in the refrigerator at least 1 hour.
3. Remove top layer of rind from brie.
4. Place brie in a small microwave safe serving dish.
5. Microwave on high 1 minute, or until the cheese begins to soften.
6. Spoon roma tomato mixture over the cheese.
7. Microwave on high 1 minute, or until slightly melted.
8. Serve immediately with Paris toasts or other dried breads.
