## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons butter
- 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
- 1 ½ cups warm milk
- 1 (8 ounce) jar dried beef
- 1 pinch cayenne pepper

## Instructions

1. Melt butter in a medium saucepan over low heat. Add flour and whisk until smooth. Add milk a little at a time, whisking well after each addition.
2. Bring to a boil over medium-high heat and cook, stirring, until thickened. Stir in beef and cayenne; cook until warmed through.
