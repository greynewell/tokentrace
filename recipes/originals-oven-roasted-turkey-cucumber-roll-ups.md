## Ingredients

- 1  cucumber
- 5 slices Dietz & Watson Originals Oven Roasted Turkey Breast
- 3 slices Dietz & Watson Originals Cheddar Cheese
- 4 ounces ranch veggie dip

## Instructions

1. Using a vegetable peeler or mandolin, peel the cucumber into thin sliced strips.
2. Slice Dietz & Watson Originals Turkey breast into thin sliced strips.
3. Slice Dietz & Watson Originals Cheddar Cheese into 1/2 inch sliced strips.
4. Layer one slice of D&W Turkey, folded in half, and two D&W Cheese pieces onto cucumber slices.
5. Roll cucumber and place toothpick in the middle to hold the roll together.
6. Serve with Ranch Veggie Dip for dipping.
