## Ingredients

- 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
- 1 cup mayonnaise
- ¼ cup sour cream
- 8  imitation crab legs
- 1 tablespoon dried dill weed
- 1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
- 1 (2.25 ounce) can black olives, chopped
- ¼ teaspoon vinegar-based hot pepper sauce

## Instructions

1. In a medium-size mixing bowl, stir together cream cheese, mayonnaise and sour cream.
2. Stir in imitation crabmeat, dill weed, black olives, and hot pepper sauce.
