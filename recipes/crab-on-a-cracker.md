## Ingredients

- 1 (6 ounce) can crabmeat
- 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
- 1 (8 ounce) can crushed pineapple
- 4 dashes Worcestershire sauce
- 2 tablespoons ketchup
- 1 pinch garlic powder

## Instructions

1. In a medium-sized mixing bowl combine crabmeat and cream cheese into the bowl.
2. Mix the pineapple into the mixture, and season with Worcestershire sauce, ketchup and garlic powder.
3. Refrigerate overnight.
