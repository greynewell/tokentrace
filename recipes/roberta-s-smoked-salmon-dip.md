## Ingredients

- 1 (6 ounce) can salmon, bones and skin removed
- ½ cup mayonnaise
- ⅓ cup finely chopped celery
- ⅓ cup bread crumbs  (Optional)
- 3 tablespoons lemon juice
- 3 tablespoons chopped fresh dill
- 1 tablespoon liquid smoke flavoring
- 1 tablespoon Cajun seasoning  (Optional)
- 1  lemon, sliced  (Optional)
- 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley, or to taste  (Optional)

## Instructions

1. Mix salmon, mayonnaise, celery, bread crumbs, lemon juice, dill, liquid smoke, and Cajun seasoning together in a bowl.
2. Form salmon mixture into a mold on a plate and loosely cover with plastic wrap; refrigerate, 8 hours to overnight.
3. Garnish with lemon slices and parsley.
