## Ingredients

- 3  green bell peppers, cut into large chunks
- ½ cup sliced jalapeno peppers
- 1 pinch dried oregano
- 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese

## Instructions

1. Preheat a grill for medium heat and lightly oil the grate.
2. Place pepper chunks, skin-side up, on the preheated grill; cook until slightly charred, 3 to 5 minutes.
3. Turn peppers over; place jalapeno slices on them. Top with mozzarella cheese and sprinkle with oregano. Grill until cheese melts; transfer to a plate and serve.
