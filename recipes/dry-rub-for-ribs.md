## Ingredients

- 3 tablespoons brown sugar
- 1 ½ tablespoons paprika
- 1 ½ tablespoons salt
- 1 ½ tablespoons ground black pepper
- 1 teaspoon garlic powder

## Instructions

1. Mix brown sugar, paprika, salt, pepper, and garlic powder together in a small bowl. Rub into pork ribs.
2. For best results, allow ribs to marinate overnight.
