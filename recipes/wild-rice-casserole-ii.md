## Ingredients

- 2 (14.5 ounce) cans chicken broth
- 1 cup uncooked wild rice
- ½ cup water chestnuts, sliced
- ¼ cup butter
- 1 teaspoon salt
- ½ pound fresh mushrooms, sliced
- ½ cup onion, chopped

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Bring chicken broth to a boil in a medium saucepan.
3. Mix chicken broth, uncooked wild rice, water chestnuts, 2 tablespoons butter, and salt in a 1 1/2-quart baking dish.
4. Cover and bake in the preheated oven for 30 minutes.
5. While rice mixture is baking, melt remaining butter in a medium skillet over medium heat. Place mushrooms and onion in the skillet; slowly cook and stir until tender. Stir mushrooms and onion into wild rice mixture.
6. Cover and continue baking until liquid has been absorbed, about 30 more minutes.
