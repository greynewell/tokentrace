## Ingredients

- 2 (10 ounce) packages frozen chopped broccoli
- 8 ounces processed cheese food, shredded
- Â¼ pound butter
- 32  buttery round crackers, crushed

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Place broccoli in casserole dish and cover with cheese.
3. In a large skillet over medium heat, melt the butter; add the cracker crumbs and stir until evenly coated.
4. Sprinkle over cheese.
5. Bake for 30 minutes.
