## Ingredients

- 8 tablespoons reduced-fat peanut butter, or to taste
- 8  whole wheat tortillas
- 8 teaspoons honey, or to taste
- 8  bananas, peeled
- 1 cup high-protein crisp rice and wheat cereal (such as Kellogg's® Special K®), or to taste

## Instructions

1. Spread 1 tablespoon peanut butter onto each tortilla.
2. Place 1 banana in the center of each tortilla; drizzle honey over each banana.
3. Sprinkle about 2 tablespoons cereal over peanut butter and banana.
4. Fold ends of each tortilla over the 2 ends of each banana and roll tortilla around banana.
5. Cut in half or serve whole.
