## Ingredients

- 3 large ripe mangoes, peeled and cut into long, thin strips
- 2  red bell peppers, cut into long, thin strips
- 2  jicamas, peeled and cut into long, thin strips
- 1 pint fresh blueberries, or more to taste
- 4  limes, each cut into 6 wedges

## Instructions

1. Toss mango, red bell pepper, and jicama together in a large salad.
2. Add blueberries and toss gently to mix.
3. Cut a slit into each lime wedge and arrange around the edge of the salad bowl to be squeezed over individual salad portions.
