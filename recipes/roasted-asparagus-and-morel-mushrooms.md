## Ingredients

- 1 pound asparagus spears, trimmed and halved
- 1 pound fresh morel mushrooms, sliced
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 1 teaspoon salt, or to taste
- 2 tablespoons butter, melted
- 1 cup arugula
- 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar, or to taste
- 1 tablespoon chopped fresh thyme

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C).
2. Line a baking sheet with aluminum foil.
3. Arrange asparagus and mushrooms on the prepared baking sheet.
4. Drizzle with oil and sprinkle with salt; toss to coat.
5. Roast, stirring occasionally, about 15 minutes.
6. Add butter and thyme.
7. Toss to coat, and roast an additional 5 minutes.
8. Arrange vegetables on a serving platter.
9. Top with arugula, sprinkle with salt, and drizzle with balsamic vinegar.
