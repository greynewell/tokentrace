## Ingredients

- ½ cup brandy
- ¼ cup lemon juice
- ⅓ cup frozen lemonade concentrate
- ⅓ cup orange juice
- 1 (750 milliliter) bottle dry red wine
- ½ cup triple sec
- 1  lemon, sliced into rounds
- 1  orange, sliced into rounds
- 1  lime, sliced into rounds
- ¼ cup white sugar  (Optional)
- 8  maraschino cherries
- 2 cups carbonated water  (Optional)

## Instructions

1. Mix together red wine, brandy, triple sec, lemonade concentrate, orange juice, lemon juice, and sugar in a bowl. Add orange, lemon, and lime slices and maraschino cherries.
2. Serve immediately over ice, or refrigerate 8 hours to overnight for best flavor.
3. For a fizzy sangria, add club soda just before serving.
