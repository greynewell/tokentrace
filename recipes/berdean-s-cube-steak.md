## Ingredients

- 4 (4 ounce) cube steaks
- salt and pepper to taste
- ¼ cup all-purpose flour
- ⅓ cup vegetable oil
- 1 teaspoon beef bouillon granules

## Instructions

1. Season cube steaks on both sides with salt and pepper. Place flour in a shallow bowl; press steaks into the flour and shake off any excess.
2. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add steaks and cook until browned on both sides, about 3 minutes per side.
3. Pour enough water into the skillet to almost cover steaks. Stir in beef bouillon and salt; bring to a boil. Reduce heat to medium-low, cover, and simmer until very tender, about 2 hours.
