## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons butter
- ¼ cup chopped onion
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 6 cups chicken broth
- 1 (16 ounce) jar Alfredo sauce
- 1 (8 ounce) carton sour cream
- 2 cups cubed cooked chicken
- 1 (20 ounce) package refrigerated three-cheese tortellini
- 1 (10 ounce) package fresh spinach
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- ¼ cup shredded Parmesan cheese  (Optional)

## Instructions

1. Melt butter in a 5-quart pot over medium heat; cook and stir onion and garlic in melted butter until onion is translucent, about 5 minutes.
2. Stir chicken broth and   into onion mixture; cook until heated through, about 5 minutes.
3. Stir in sour cream and cooked chicken; simmer for 15 minutes.
4. Mix cheese tortellini and spinach into chicken mixture, cover pot, and simmer for 20 minutes.
5. Season with salt and pepper.
6. Divide soup into bowls and sprinkle with Parmesan cheese.
