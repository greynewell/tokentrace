## Ingredients

- ¾ cup pecans
- 2  ripe peaches
- 4 cups baby spinach, rinsed and dried
- ¼ cup poppyseed salad dressing

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Arrange pecans on a single layer on a baking sheet and roast in preheated oven for 7 to 10 minutes, until they just begin to darken. Remove from oven and set aside.
3. Peel peaches (if desired) and slice into bite-sized segments. Combine peaches, spinach, and pecans in a large bowl.
4. Toss with dressing until evenly coated, adding a little additional dressing, if necessary.
