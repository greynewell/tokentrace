## Ingredients

- cooking spray
- 4 (4 ounce) fillets swai fish
- 2 tablespoons margarine
- ¼ cup dry white wine
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice
- 1 tablespoon chopped fresh cilantro  (Optional)
- 1 teaspoon minced garlic
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
- 1 teaspoon paprika

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a shallow pan or baking sheet with cooking spray.
2. Place fish fillets into the prepared pan.
3. Melt margarine in a saucepan over medium heat. Mix white wine, lemon juice, cilantro, garlic, salt, and black pepper into melted margarine; simmer for 2 minutes. Generously spoon sauce over fish fillets. Sprinkle fillets with paprika.
4. Bake in the preheated oven until fish flakes easily with a fork, 10 to 12 minutes.
