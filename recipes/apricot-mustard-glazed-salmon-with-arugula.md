## Ingredients

- ¼ cup apricot preserves
- 1 ½ tablespoons whole-grain mustard
- 1 teaspoon balsamic vinegar
- 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
- 2 teaspoons apricot preserves
- 1 teaspoon whole-grain mustard
- ⅛ teaspoon salt
- ⅛ teaspoon ground black pepper
- ¼ cup extra-virgin olive oil
- 2 teaspoons extra-virgin olive oil
- 2 (8 ounce) salmon fillets, cut 1 1/4-inch thick
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- 3 cups baby arugula
- 1 cup cherry tomatoes  (Optional)

## Instructions

1. Mix 1/4 cup apricot preserves, 1 1/2 tablespoon mustard, and 1 teaspoon balsamic vinegar in a bowl to make glaze.
2. Divide glaze between 2 separate bowls.
3. Whisk 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar, 2 teaspoon apricot preserves, 1 teaspoon mustard, salt, and pepper together in a bowl.
4. Drizzle in 1/4 cup olive oil, whisking constantly, until dressing is emulsified.
5. Preheat Panasonic Countertop Induction Oven to Medium-High on the "Grill" setting.
6. Brush 1 teaspoon of olive oil over both sides of each piece of salmon.
7. Season with salt and pepper.
8. Place salmon flesh-side down in the center of the grill pan.
9. Set timer for 3 1/2 minutes and press "Start." Flip salmon so skin side is down.
10. Brush with glaze from one of the bowls.
11. Set timer for 4 minutes and press "Start." Remove salmon to a plate and spoon glaze from the second bowl on top.
12. Let salmon rest for 5 minutes.
13. Place 1 1/2 cup arugula and 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes on each serving plate.
14. Drizzle with dressing.
15. Top with cooked salmon fillets.
