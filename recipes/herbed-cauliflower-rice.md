## Ingredients

- 1 head cauliflower, cut into florets
- 2 teaspoons chopped fresh chives
- 2 teaspoons chopped fresh parsley
- ¼ teaspoon garlic powder
- ¼ teaspoon onion powder
- salt and ground black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Place a steamer insert into a pot and fill with water to just below the bottom of the steamer.
2. Bring water to a boil.
3. Add cauliflower, cover, and steam until fork-tender, 10 to 15 minutes.
4. Remove cauliflower and steamer from the pot.
5. Drain all water and return cauliflower to the empty pot.
6. Add chives, parsley, garlic powder, and onion powder.
7. Set pot over medium-low heat.
8. Mash cauliflower with a potato masher to the consistency of rice grains.
9. Stir until excess moisture evaporates and the 'rice' appears fluffy, 5 to 7 minutes.
10. Season with salt and pepper.
