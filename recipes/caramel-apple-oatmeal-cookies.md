## Ingredients

- 1 ¾ cups all-purpose flour
- 1 teaspoon baking soda
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ½ teaspoon apple pie spice
- 2 cups firmly packed brown sugar
- 1 cup LAND O LAKES® Butter, softened
- ¼ cup applesauce
- 1  LAND O LAKES® Egg
- 2 teaspoons vanilla
- 2 ½ cups uncooked old-fashioned oats
- ½ cup caramel bits
- ½ cup finely chopped dried apple rings

## Instructions

1. Heat oven to 375 degrees F.
2. Line cookie sheet with parchment paper; set aside.
3. Combine flour, baking soda, salt and apple pie spice in bowl; set aside.
4. Combine brown sugar and butter in another bowl; beat until well mixed.
5. Add applesauce, egg and vanilla; beat, scraping sides of bowl occasionally, until well mixed.
6. Add flour mixture; beat at low speed until just combined.
7. Stir in oats, caramel bits and dried apples.
8. Drop dough by rounded tablespoonfuls, 2 inches apart, onto prepared cookie sheet.
9. Bake 8-10 minutes or until edges are lightly browned.
10. Let stand 3 minutes on cookie sheet; remove to cooling rack.
