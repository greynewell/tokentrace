## Ingredients

- 1 sheet whole wheat or regular lavash bread
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 3 tablespoons freshly grated Parmesan cheese, or to taste

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Brush lavash with olive oil.
3. Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese.
4. Using a very sharp knife or a pizza cutter, cut lavash into strips, squares, or triangles.
5. Place lavash pieces on a baking sheet.
6. Bake in the preheated oven until crisp, about 10 minutes.
7. Remove from oven to cool completely; store in an airtight container.
