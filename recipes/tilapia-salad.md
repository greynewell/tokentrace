## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 2  tilapia fillets
- 1 ½ tablespoons Chinese five-spice powder, or as needed
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- 1 (8 ounce) package baby spinach leaves
- 1  orange bell pepper, diced
- 1  grapefruit, peeled and sectioned
- 1  tomato, diced
- 1  avocado - peeled, pitted and diced
- 2 tablespoons Asian-style salad dressing
- 1 ½ tablespoons toasted almonds, chopped

## Instructions

1. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat.
2. Sprinkle both sides of the tilapia fillets with Chinese five-spice powder, salt, and black pepper; cook tilapia fillets in the skillet until lightly browned and the flesh is white and opaque, about 3 minutes per side.
3. Remove from heat.
4. Place the spinach leaves into a large salad bowl, and top with the orange bell pepper, grapefruit, tomato, and avocado.
5. Drizzle the salad with the Asian-style dressing, sprinkle with almonds, and top with the cooked tilapia fillets.
