## Ingredients

- 2 (14.5 ounce) cans evaporated milk
- 2 (14.5 ounce) cans sweetened condensed milk
- ¾ cup lemon juice, or more to taste
- ⅔ (20 ounce) package Mexican Maria cookies (galletas Maria)

## Instructions

1. Combine evaporated milk, condensed milk, and lemon juice in a bowl; whisk until mixture is thick and smooth.
2. Line a glass bowl or trifle dish with Maria cookies and cover them with a generous layer of the milk mixture. Add another layer of biscuits and milk. Continue the layering, ending with a layer of the milk mixture.
3. Refrigerate for at least 6 hours, but better if overnight. Remove from the fridge just before serving.
