## Ingredients

- 8 prunes pitted prunes
- ¼ cup dried apricots
- 1 cup apple juice
- 1  cinnamon stick
- ½ teaspoon whole cloves
- 1  banana, peeled and sliced
- 1 large orange, peeled, sectioned, and cut into bite-size
- 1  apple, cored and chopped
- ½ cup orange juice
- 3 tablespoons orange marmalade
- ¼ cup chopped pecans

## Instructions

1. Combine prunes, apricots, apple juice, cinnamon stick, and cloves in saucepan.
2. Bring to a boil, remove from heat and let soak overnight in refrigerator.
3. Remove the dried fruit and divide the prunes, apricots, banana, orange and apple among 4 dishes.
4. Strain apple juice and add orange juice and orange marmalade.
5. Mix together and pour over fruit.
6. Top with chopped pecans.
