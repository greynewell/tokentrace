## Ingredients

- 3 packets CARNATION BREAKFAST ESSENTIALS® Rich Milk Chocolate Powdered Drink Mix
- ½ cup almond butter
- ¾ cup old-fashioned oats
- 1 small ripe banana, mashed
- ¼ cup unsweetened shredded coconut
- cooking spray

## Instructions

1. Combine drink mix, almond butter, oats, banana, and coconut in a medium bowl.
2. Line an 8-inch baking pan with foil. Spray foil with cooking spray.
3. Press mixture evenly into the pan and place freeze until firm, 2 to 3 hours. Cut into bars. Serve frozen, or let soften in the refrigerator.
