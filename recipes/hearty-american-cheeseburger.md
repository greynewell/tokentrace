## Ingredients

- ½ cup A.1. Thick & Hearty Steak Sauce
- ⅓ cup CLAUSSEN Sweet Pickle Relish
- 1 ½ pounds ground beef
- 1 large yellow or red onion, sliced
- 6 each KRAFT Singles
- 6 each onion rolls, partially split
- 6 large leaf (blank)s lettuce leaves
- 6 slices tomato

## Instructions

1. Preheat grill to medium-hot heat. Mix 1/4 cup of the steak sauce and relish until well blended. Set aside. Shape meat into 6 (1/4-inch-thick) patties.
2. Grill or broil burgers and onion slices 5 to 7 minutes on each side or until burgers are cooked through and onion slices are tender, occasionally brushing with remaining 1/4 cup steak sauce. Top burgers with Singles; continue grilling or broiling until Singles begin to melt.
3. Cover bottom halves of rolls with lettuce; top with cheeseburgers, onion, tomato, relish mixture and tops of rolls.
