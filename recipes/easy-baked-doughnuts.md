## Ingredients

- 2 cups baking mix (such as Bisquick ®)
- ⅓ cup milk
- 1  egg
- 2 tablespoons white sugar
- ⅛ teaspoon ground cinnamon
- ¼ cup butter, melted
- ½ cup white sugar

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
2. Mix baking mix, milk, egg, 2 tablespoons sugar, and cinnamon together in a bowl with a whisk, beating vigorously for 20 strokes. Transfer dough to a floured work surface and knead 5 times. Roll dough into 1/2-inch thickness and cut with a floured doughnut cutter. Place doughnuts onto a baking sheet.
3. Bake in the preheated oven until cooked through and lightly browned, 7 to 9 minutes.
4. Pour melted butter into a bowl. Place 1/2 cup sugar in a separate bowl. Dip doughnuts into butter; transfer to sugar and lightly press until doughnuts are coated.
