## Ingredients

- 1 head cauliflower, broken into florets
- 1  egg
- ½ cup finely shredded mozzarella cheese
- ½ teaspoon oregano
- 1 pinch salt

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
2. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
3. Place cauliflower florets in a food processor; pulse until chopped into grains the size of rice.
4. Fill a large pot with about 1 inch of water; bring to a boil.
5. Add cauliflower "rice"; cook until softened, 4 to 5 minutes.
6. Drain in a fine sieve and cool until running water.
7. Pour cauliflower onto a clean dish towel; roll up and twist edges.
8. Wring out as much water as possible.
9. Transfer cauliflower to a large bowl.
10. Add egg, mozzarella cheese, oregano, and salt; mix well.
11. Spread on the lined baking sheet to about 1/2-inch thickness.
12. Bake in the preheated oven until firm to the touch and light golden brown in color, about 35 minutes.
