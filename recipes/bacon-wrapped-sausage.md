## Ingredients

- cooking spray
- 1 (13 ounce) package smoked sausage, cut into 1/2-inch slices
- 1 (16 ounce) package bacon slices, halved crosswise
- 1 (16 ounce) package brown sugar

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Spray a 9x13-inch baking dish with cooking spray.
2. Wrap each slice of sausage with a piece of bacon and arrange in the prepared baking dish; sprinkle with brown sugar.
3. Bake in the preheated oven until bacon is crispy and sugar is caramelized, about 1 hour.
