## Ingredients

- 2 cups uncooked orecchiette pasta
- ⅓ cup butter
- ½ cup chopped shallots
- 3 cloves garlic, chopped
- ¼ cup chopped green onion
- 1 ½ teaspoons Cajun seasoning, or to taste
- 1 teaspoon cracked black pepper
- 1 cup white wine
- 1 cup diced plum tomatoes
- 1 pound medium shrimp - peeled and deveined
- 1 cup baby spinach

## Instructions

1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
2. Add orecchiette pasta, and cook for 9 to 11 minutes, until almost al dente; drain.
3. Melt butter in a medium skillet over medium heat.
4. Stir in shallots, garlic, and green onion.
5. Season with Cajun seasoning and pepper, and cook about 2 minutes.
6. Mix in wine, tomatoes, and shrimp.
7. Continue to cook and stir until shrimp are opaque.
8. Mix in the pasta and spinach, cover, and simmer 3 to 5 minutes, until pasta is al dente and spinach has wilted.
