## Ingredients

- 4 ears fresh corn, shucked
- 4 dampened paper towels
- 1 storage bag

## Instructions

1. Wrap each ear of corn in a damp paper towel. Place corn in a storage bag; do not seal the bag.
2. Place in the microwave oven and cook on high for 4 minutes. Flip the bag over and cook on high an additional 4 minutes.
