## Ingredients

- 1 (.25 ounce) package instant yeast
- 1 cup warm water
- ¼ cup honey
- 4 teaspoons vegetable oil
- 3 cups whole wheat flour
- ¼ cup wheat bran  (Optional)
- 1 teaspoon salt
- ⅓ cup sunflower seeds
- ⅓ cup shelled, toasted, chopped pumpkin seeds

## Instructions

1. Place the ingredients into the bread machine in the order recommended by the manufacturer.
2. Select the whole wheat setting and press start.
3. If your machine has a signal for fruit or nuts, add the sunflower and pumpkin seeds at the beep.
