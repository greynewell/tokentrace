## Ingredients

- 1 (15 ounce) jar pasteurized process cheese spread (such as Cheez Whiz®)
- ⅓ cup prepared horseradish, or to taste
- 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
- ½ teaspoon dry mustard
- ¼ teaspoon garlic powder, or more to taste

## Instructions

1. Mix cheese spread, horseradish, Worcestershire sauce, dry mustard, and garlic powder in a mixing bowl.
2. Refrigerate 8 hours to overnight.
