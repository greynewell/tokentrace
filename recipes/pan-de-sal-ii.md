## Ingredients

- 2 cups milk
- ¾ cup white sugar
- ½ cup butter
- 1 (.25 ounce) package active dry yeast
- 1 ½ teaspoons salt
- ½ teaspoon baking soda
- ½ teaspoon baking powder
- 6 cups all-purpose flour
- 2 tablespoons dry bread crumbs for topping

## Instructions

1. Heat the milk in a small saucepan until it bubbles; remove from heat.
2. Add the butter and sugar; stir until melted.
3. Let cool until lukewarm.
4. Pour the cooled milk mixture into a large mixing bowl.
5. Add the yeast and stir to dissolve.
6. Add the salt, baking soda, baking powder and 4 cups of the flour; stir well to combine.
7. Add the remaining flour, 1/2 cup at a time, stirring well after each addition.
8. When the dough has pulled together, turn it out onto a lightly floured surface and knead until smooth and supple, about 8 minutes.
9. Lightly oil a large mixing bowl, place the dough in the bowl and turn to coat with oil.
10. Cover with a damp cloth and let rise in a warm place until doubled in volume, about 1 hour.
11. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
12. Deflate the dough and turn it out onto a lightly floured surface.
13. Divide the dough into 12 equal pieces and form into rounds.
14. Place the rolls on 2 lightly greased baking sheets.
15. Cover with a damp cloth and let rise until doubled in volume, about 40 minutes.
16. Sprinkle with bread crumbs and bake at 375 degrees F (190 degrees C) for about 15 to 20 minutes or until the tops are golden brown.
