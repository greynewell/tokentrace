## Ingredients

- ½ cup balsamic vinegar
- ¼ cup olive oil
- 6 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tablespoon dried basil
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- 2 pounds skinless, boneless chicken breast, thinly sliced

## Instructions

1. Combine vinegar, oil, garlic, basil, salt, and pepper in a large bowl.
2. Add chicken; mix to coat.
3. Cover with plastic wrap; refrigerate for at least 20 minutes.
4. Heat a cast iron grill pan over medium-high heat.
5. Add chicken; cook until golden on the outside, no longer pink in the center, and the juices run clear, 7 to 10 minutes per side.
6. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 165 degrees F (74 degrees C).
