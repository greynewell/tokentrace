## Ingredients

- cooking spray
- 8  boneless, skinless chicken tenders
- 4 teaspoons butter, softened
- ⅓ cup lemon juice
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ¼ teaspoon paprika

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C).
2. Coat an 8x11-inch baking pan with cooking spray.
3. Place chicken tenders in a single layer on the prepared baking pan.
4. Spread butter on the tenders.
5. Sprinkle evenly with lemon juice, salt, and paprika.
6. Bake, uncovered, in the preheated oven until chicken is no longer pink in the center and juices run clear, 25 to 30 minutes.
