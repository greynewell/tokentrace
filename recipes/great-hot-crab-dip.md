## Ingredients

- 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
- ¼ pound crabmeat
- 1 tablespoon minced onion
- ¼ cup mayonnaise
- 2 tablespoons cocktail sauce
- ½ teaspoon prepared horseradish
- ½ cup sliced almonds

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. In a mixing bowl, combine the softened cream cheese, crabmeat, onion, mayonnaise, cocktail sauce and horseradish.
3. Mix thoroughly.
4. Place mixture in a small casserole dish.
5. Top mixture with sliced almonds.
6. Bake in a preheated oven for 30 minutes or until bubbly
