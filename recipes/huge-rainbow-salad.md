## Ingredients

- 1 cup fresh spinach leaves
- 1 cup torn lettuce
- 1 cup sliced fresh strawberries
- 1 cup sliced white button mushrooms
- 1  avocado - peeled, pitted, and sliced
- ¼ cup black olives
- 2  hard-boiled eggs, sliced  (Optional)
- ¼ cup thinly sliced red bell pepper
- ¼ cup thinly sliced yellow bell pepper
- ¼ cup thinly sliced orange bell pepper
- ¼ cup broccoli florets
- ¼ cup cauliflower florets

## Instructions

1. Combine spinach and lettuce in the bottom of a large salad bowl.
2. Top with strawberries, mushrooms, avocado, black olives, hard-boiled eggs, red bell pepper, yellow bell pepper, orange bell pepper, broccoli, and cauliflower.
