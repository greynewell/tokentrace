## Ingredients

- 2 teaspoons butter, or as needed
- 1 (8 ounce) container sour cream
- ½ teaspoon baking soda
- 1 cup all-purpose flour
- 3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
- 3  eggs
- ½ cup mayonnaise
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 (6.5 ounce) can salmon, drained and flaked

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
2. Butter a cast iron skillet or pie plate.
3. Mix sour cream and baking soda together in a large bowl.
4. Add 1 cup plus 3 tablespoons flour, eggs, mayonnaise, and salt.
5. Mix to form a thin batter of pouring consistency.
6. Pour 1/2 the batter into the prepared skillet or pie plate.
7. Mash fish with a fork; spread on top of the batter.
8. Pour in the rest of the batter.
9. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean, about 30 minutes.
