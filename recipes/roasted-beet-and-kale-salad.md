## Ingredients

- 3 large beets
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- 1 bunch fresh kale, cut into bite-size pieces
- ½ cup chopped cashews
- ¼ cup  dried cherries
- 2 tablespoons golden raisins
- ½ cup apple cider
- ½  lemon, juiced
- 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 teaspoons apple cider vinegar
- 2 tablespoons olive oil, or more to taste

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Line a baking sheet with aluminum foil.
3. Trim roots and stems from beets.
4. Coat beets with 1 tablespoon olive oil and sprinkle with salt and black pepper.
5. Place beets onto prepared baking sheet.
6. Roast beets for 30 minutes; turn beets over and continue roasting until tender, 30 minutes to 1 hour more.
7. Let beets cool.
8. Peel skins from beets and cut into 1-inch cubes.
9. Toss cooked beets with kale, cashews, dried cherries, and golden raisins in a large salad bowl.
10. Whisk apple cider, lemon juice, Dijon mustard, garlic, and cider vinegar in a bowl.
11. Slowly drizzle 2 tablespoons olive oil into apple cider mixture, whisking constantly, until dressing is combined.
12. Pour dressing over salad and toss to coat.
13. Refrigerate at least 1 hour for flavors to blend before serving.
