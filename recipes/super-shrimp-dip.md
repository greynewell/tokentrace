## Ingredients

- 2 cups cooked shrimp meat
- 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese
- ½ cup mayonnaise (such as Best Foods®)
- ½ cup cocktail sauce
- ½ cup chopped celery
- ½ cup chopped cooked bacon
- ½ cup shredded Cheddar cheese
- ½ cup chopped green onions
- 2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
- 1 tablespoon hot sauce
- 2 teaspoons seafood seasoning (such as Old Bay®)
- 1 large green bell pepper, cored and seeded

## Instructions

1. Mix shrimp, cream cheese, mayonnaise, cocktail sauce, celery, bacon, Cheddar cheese, green onions, Worcestershire sauce, hot sauce, and seafood seasoning in a large bowl.
2. Chill shrimp mixture until flavors combine, at least 1 hour.
3. Spoon into green bell pepper.
