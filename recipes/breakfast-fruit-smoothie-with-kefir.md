## Ingredients

- ½ cup kefir
- ½ cup frozen blueberries
- ½ small banana
- 1 tablespoon almond butter
- 2 teaspoons honey

## Instructions

1. Combine kefir, blueberries, banana, almond butter, and honey in a blender.
2. Process until smooth.
