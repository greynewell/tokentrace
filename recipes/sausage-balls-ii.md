## Ingredients

- 1 ½ cups biscuit mix
- 1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
- 1 pound fresh, ground pork sausage

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. In a large bowl, mix together biscuit mix, Cheddar cheese, and sausage.
3. Roll mixture into walnut-size balls and arrange on an ungreased cookie sheet.
4. Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 5 to 15 minutes, or until completely cooked and browned.
5. Place on paper towels to drain and cool.
