## Ingredients

- 4  eggs
- 1 cup milk
- 1 ½ cups diced zucchini
- 8 ounces chopped razor clams
- 1 small onion, diced
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- ½ cup bread crumbs
- ½ cup all-purpose flour
- 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
- 1 pinch salt and black pepper to taste
- 1 cup oil for frying
- 2 tablespoons butter

## Instructions

1. Whisk together the eggs and milk in a bowl until evenly blended.
2. Mix in the zucchini, razor clams, onion, and garlic; set aside.
3. Stir together the bread crumbs, flour, parsley, salt, and pepper in a separate large bowl.
4. Pour the egg and vegetable mixture into the flour mixture and stir until well combined.
5. Heat oil and butter in a large, deep skillet over medium-high heat.
6. Drop fritter batter by spoonfuls into the skillet in batches of 4.
7. Cook until golden brown on the bottom; flip and cook until the other side is golden brown.
8. Drain the fritters on a paper towel-lined plate.
