## Ingredients

- 3  eggs, beaten
- 2 cups white sugar
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 1 cup vegetable oil
- 2 cups grated zucchini
- 3 cups all-purpose flour
- 1 teaspoon baking soda
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- 1 teaspoon salt
- ½ cup raisins
- 1 cup chopped pecans
- 1 cup crushed pineapple, drained

## Instructions

1. Mix together the eggs, sugar, vanilla, oil, and zucchini in a large bowl.
2. In a separate bowl mix together the flour, soda, baking powder, and salt.
3. Add to the zucchini batter and mix well.
4. Stir in raisins, nuts, and pineapple until just blended.
5. Pour into 2-9x5x3 inch loaf pans.
6. Bake in a preheated 325 degree F (165 degrees C) for one hour, or until browned.
