## Ingredients

- 1 recipe pastry for a 9-inch single pie crust
- ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
- 2 large tomatoes, seeded and sliced
- 3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
- 1  sweet onion (such as Vidalia®), minced
- ½ cup chopped fresh basil
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ½ teaspoon ground black pepper
- 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese, divided
- ½ cup Dijon mustard
- ¼ cup mayonnaise

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
2. Press pie crust pastry into a 9-inch pie pan; sprinkle with 1/2 cup Parmesan cheese.
3. Dip tomato slices in flour, coating both sides.
4. Arrange a layer of coated tomato slices atop the Parmesan cheese.
5. Top with half the onion, half the basil, half the salt, and half the black pepper.
6. Repeat layering with 1/2 cup Parmesan cheese and remaining tomatoes, onion, basil, salt, and black pepper, ending with 1/2 cup Parmesan cheese.
7. Stir mustard and mayonnaise together in a bowl.
8. Spread over Parmesan cheese layer.
9. Bake in the preheated oven until cheese is melted and bubbling, about 25 minutes.
