## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon white sugar
- 1 cup ice, or as needed
- 2 fluid ounces Ruby red grapefruit juice
- 1 fluid ounce vodka
- 1 fluid ounce triple sec

## Instructions

1. Pour sugar onto a small plate. Wet the rim of a martini glass with cold water, then dip into sugar to coat.
2. Fill a cocktail shaker with ice, then pour in grapefruit juice, vodka, and triple sec. Cover shaker with lid and shake. Strain and pour mixture into the prepared martini glass.
