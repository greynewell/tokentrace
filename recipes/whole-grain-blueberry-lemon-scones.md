## Ingredients

- 1 cup all-purpose flour
- ¾ cup whole wheat pastry flour
- 2 ½ teaspoons baking powder
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- ½ cup very cold unsalted butter, cut into 1/2-inch cubes
- 1 cup blueberries
- 2 teaspoons lemon zest
- 7 tablespoons heavy whipping cream, divided
- ¼ cup honey
- 1  egg

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C).
2. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
3. Whisk all-purpose flour, whole wheat pastry flour, baking powder, and salt together in a large bowl.
4. Rub in cubed butter with your fingers until mixture resembles coarse meal.
5. Fold in blueberries and lemon zest.
6. Whisk 5 tablespoons heavy cream, honey, and egg together in a small bowl.
7. Pour over the flour mixture; mix gently with your hands until dough just comes together.
8. Turn dough out onto a lightly floured work surface.
9. Pat dough into a 6-inch round about 1 inch thick.
10. Cut dough into 12 rounds using a round cutter.
11. Transfer rounds to the prepared baking sheet.
12. Drizzle remaining 2 tablespoons heavy cream over the tops.
13. Bake in the preheated oven until golden brown, 12 to 15 minutes.
