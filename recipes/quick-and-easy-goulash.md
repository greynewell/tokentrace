## Ingredients

- 1 (12 ounce) package egg noodles
- 1 pound lean ground beef
- 1 (26 ounce) jar spaghetti sauce
- 1 (15.25 ounce) can whole kernel corn
- 1  onion, chopped
- 1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese

## Instructions

1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add egg noodles and cook until al dente, 8 to 10 minutes; drain.
2. Brown beef in a skillet over medium heat; drain extra grease.
3. Mix in spaghetti sauce, corn, onion, and cheese with the beef over medium heat. Add in noodles and stir together to heat through.
