## Ingredients

- 2 cups amaranth flour
- 2 teaspoons xanthan gum
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 1 teaspoon white sugar
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- 1 pinch dried oregano
- 1 pinch dried basil
- 1 pinch rubbed sage
- 1 pinch dried thyme leaves
- 1 ½ cups sparkling water
- 2 tablespoons olive oil

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C).
2. Grease a pizza pan.
3. Mix amaranth flour, xanthan gum, baking powder, sugar, salt, oregano, basil, sage, and thyme together in a large bowl.
4. Pour in sparkling water and olive oil; mix until a sticky dough forms.
5. Spread dough over the prepared pan, using moistened hands to keep from sticking.
6. Bake in the preheated oven until lightly golden, about 20 minutes.
