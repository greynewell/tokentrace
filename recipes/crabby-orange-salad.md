## Ingredients

- 1 (6 ounce) can crabmeat, drained and flaked
- 3 large  oranges, peeled and segmented
- ⅓ cup orange juice
- ¼ cup mayonnaise
- ½ cup sweetened whipped cream
- 2 tablespoons white sugar

## Instructions

1. In a medium bowl, toss together the crabmeat and oranges.
2. Cover and chill.
3. In a small bowl, whisk together the orange juice, mayonnaise, whipped cream and sugar.
4. Serve over the chilled crab mixture.
