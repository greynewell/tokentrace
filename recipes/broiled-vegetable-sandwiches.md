## Ingredients

- 2 medium tomatoes, chopped
- 1 large onion, chopped
- 1  red bell pepper, chopped
- 1  green bell pepper, chopped
- 2 teaspoons olive oil
- 6 slices white American cheese
- 6 slices white bread

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven's broiler.
2. In a medium bowl, toss together the tomatoes, onion, red bell pepper, and green bell pepper.
3. Place the slices of bread on a baking sheet, and place under the broiler.
4. Broil for about 2 minutes, just until lightly toasted.
5. Remove from the oven, and turn the bread untoasted side up.
6. Place a handful of the vegetable mixture on top of each slice.
7. Drizzle with a bit of olive oil, then top with a slice of cheese.
8. Return the bread slices to the broiler, and cook until the cheese is melted.
9. Serve immediately.
