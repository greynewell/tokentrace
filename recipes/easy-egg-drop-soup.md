## Ingredients

- 1 (49.5 fluid ounce) can chicken broth
- 1 bunch chopped green onions
- ¼ teaspoon white sugar
- 2 tablespoons soy sauce
- 3 tablespoons cornstarch
- ⅓ cup cold water
- 3  eggs
- 2 tablespoons cold water

## Instructions

1. Heat chicken broth in a large pot over medium-high heat; stir in green onions.
2. Mix 1/3 cup of cold water, cornstarch, soy sauce, and sugar in a bowl until smooth; stir into broth. Bring to a boil and simmer for 1 minute; reduce heat to medium-low.
3. Whisk eggs and 2 tablespoons of cold water together in a small bowl with a fork. Drizzle egg, a little at a time, from the fork into hot broth. Remove soup from heat and serve.
