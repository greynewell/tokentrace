## Ingredients

- 2 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened
- 1 (16 ounce) package fresh strawberries, hulled
- 1 loaf sliced white bread
- ½ cup chopped pecans, or more to taste  (Optional)

## Instructions

1. Place cream cheese and strawberries in a food processor; pulse until smooth.
2. Spread cream cheese mixture onto half the bread and sprinkle with pecans.
3. Place remaining bread slices on top to create sandwiches.
