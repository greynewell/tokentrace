## Ingredients

- 5 ¼ cups water, divided
- 1 ¼ cups white sugar
- 1 cup sliced fresh rhubarb
- 1 pinch salt
- 1 cup fresh lemon juice
- ice cubes
- Fresh rhubarb stalks, for garnish

## Instructions

1. Stir 1 1/4 cups water, sugar, rhubarb, and salt in a small saucepan.
2. Bring to a boil, stirring to dissolve sugar.
3. Reduce heat to medium-low and simmer to the consistency of syrup, about 6 minutes.
4. Strain through a fine-mesh sieve set over a Mason jar; discard solids.
5. Chill, covered, at least 1 hour.
6. Stir chilled rhubarb syrup, remaining 4 cups water, and lemon juice in a large pitcher.
7. Chill until ready to serve.
8. Pour into ice-filled glasses.
9. Garnish with fresh rhubarb stalks.
