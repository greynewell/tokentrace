## Ingredients

- 1 cup cornmeal
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon white sugar
- 1 tablespoon shortening
- ¾ cup boiling water

## Instructions

1. Combine cornmeal, salt, and sugar in a medium bowl. Add boiling water and 1 tablespoon bacon grease; stir until bacon grease melts melts.
2. Pour bacon grease to a depth of 1/2-inch in a large skillet; heat to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
3. Shape cornmeal mixture into 12 flattened balls using a heaping tablespoon as a measuring guide.
4. Fry, turning once, in hot oil until crisp and golden brown, about 5 minutes. Drain on paper towels and serve warm.
