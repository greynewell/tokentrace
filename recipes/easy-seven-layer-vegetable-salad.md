## Ingredients

- 1 head lettuce, torn into small pieces
- 1 (10 ounce) package frozen green peas, thawed
- ½ cup chopped green bell pepper
- 12 slices bacon
- 1 ½ cups small cauliflower florets
- ½ cup chopped celery
- 2 cups mayonnaise
- 3 tablespoons white sugar
- 4 ounces shredded Cheddar cheese

## Instructions

1. Place bacon in a large, deep skillet.
2. Cook over medium high heat until evenly brown.
3. Drain and set aside.
4. In a 9x13 inch pan layer the lettuce followed by the peas, green pepper, bacon, cauliflower and celery.
5. In a small bowl combine the mayonnaise and the sugar.
6. Spread mixture over salad.
7. Sprinkle cheese over top.
8. Cover and chill for at least 8 to 12 hours before serving.
