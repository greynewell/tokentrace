## Ingredients

- 4 ears fresh corn, shucked and desilked
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 1 medium onion, chopped
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- ¼ pound pork, cubed
- ¼ pound uncooked prawns, peeled and deveined
- salt and pepper to taste
- 4 cups chicken broth
- 1 bunch fresh spinach, washed and dried

## Instructions

1. Slice the kernels from the cob and scrape off the remaining bits and juice into a bowl.
2. Set aside.
3. Heat the olive oil in a large pot over medium heat; cook and stir the onion and garlic in the hot oil until fragrant, about 5 minutes.
4. Add the pork and cook until no longer pink, about 5 minutes; add the prawns and cook until bright pink on the outside and the meat is no longer transparent in the center, about 5 minutes more.
5. Stir the corn into the pot; season with salt and pepper.
6. Pour the chicken broth over the mixture; bring to a simmer and cook another 10 minutes.
7. Add the spinach to the soup and cook until just wilted, 2 to 3 minutes.
8. Serve hot.
