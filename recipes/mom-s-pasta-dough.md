## Ingredients

- 2 cups all-purpose flour, divided
- 2  eggs
- 1 teaspoon olive oil
- ¾ teaspoon salt
- 2 tablespoons warm water, or as needed

## Instructions

1. Pour 1 3/4 cups flour into a large, shallow bowl and make a well in the center.
2. Crack eggs into the well and mix flour and eggs together with a fork or your hands.
3. Stir oil and salt into mixture; gradually add water and remaining flour until a dough starts to come together.
4. Knead dough until dough is elastic and soft.
5. Transfer dough to a lightly oiled bowl and cover the bowl with plastic wrap.
6. Store in the refrigerator.
