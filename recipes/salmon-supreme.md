## Ingredients

- 2 (4 ounce) fillets salmon
- 1 small yellow squash, chopped
- 1 small zucchini, chopped
- 2  roma (plum) tomato, thinly sliced
- 1  carrot, sliced
- 1 cup sliced mushrooms
- 2 tablespoons Creole-style hot pepper marinade
- 2 slices  lemon

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Place salmon fillets in a piece of foil large enough create a sealed packet, and layer with squash, zucchini, tomato, carrot and mushrooms.
3. Drizzle with Creole-style hot pepper marinade, and top each fillet with a lemon slice.
4. Seal foil tightly around salmon and vegetables.
5. Place packet on a medium baking sheet.
6. Bake 25 minutes in the preheated oven, or until vegetables are tender and fish is easily flaked with a fork.
