## Ingredients

- 7  red potatoes, shredded, or more to taste
- ½  onion, shredded
- 2  eggs
- 3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
- ½ teaspoon garlic powder
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ½ teaspoon ground black pepper

## Instructions

1. Stir potatoes, onion, eggs, flour, garlic powder, salt, and black pepper together in a bowl.
2. Heat a lightly oiled large skillet over medium heat.
3. Drop potato mixture by large spoonfuls onto the skillet and cook until edges are dry and golden, 3 to 4 minutes.
4. Flip and cook until browned on the other side, 3 to 4 minutes.
5. Repeat with remaining potato mixture.
