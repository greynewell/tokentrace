## Ingredients

- 1 (14.75 ounce) can salmon, drained and flaked
- 2 (5 ounce) cans tuna, drained
- ¾ cup bread crumbs, or more to taste
- ½ cup nonfat plain yogurt
- 1  egg
- 3 tablespoons ketchup, or more to taste
- 1 tablespoon dried minced onion
- 1 tablespoon dried dill
- cooking spray

## Instructions

1. Mix salmon, tuna, bread crumbs, yogurt, egg, ketchup, onion, and dill together in a bowl.
2. Form mixture into 6 patties.
3. Spray a skillet with cooking spray and heat over medium-high heat.
4. Cook fish cakes in hot skillet, turning once, until cooked through and lightly browned on each side, 5 to 10 minutes.
