## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 1 pound pork tenderloin, cut into 1 inch cubes
- 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley
- 1 (10 ounce) bag fresh spinach leaves
- 1  Asian pear, cored and sliced
- ¼ cup chopped walnuts
- ½ cup balsamic vinaigrette salad dressing, or to taste

## Instructions

1. Heat the oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
2. Add the pork and parsley; cook and stir until pork is browned on the outside, and cooked through.
3. Remove from the heat and set aside.
4. Make a bed of spinach on individual serving plates or on a large platter.
5. Arrange slices of pear over the spinach.
6. Top with the cooked pork and sprinkle with walnuts.
7. Drizzle the balsamic vinaigrette over the whole salad.
