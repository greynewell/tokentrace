## Ingredients

- 1 cup all-purpose flour
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- ½ teaspoon salt
- 1 cup milk
- 1  large egg
- ½ cup blueberries
- 2 tablespoons chia seeds  (Optional)
- cooking spray

## Instructions

1. Whisk flour, baking powder, and salt together in a bowl. Make a well in the center and add milk and egg. Whisk batter until combined. Fold in blueberries and chia seeds gently.
2. Heat a nonstick skillet over medium-high heat. Grease the pan lightly with cooking spray. Pour 1/4 cup batter into the hot pan; cook until edges look dry and bubbles on top begin to pop, about 2 minutes. Flip and continue to cook for 1 minute.
