## Ingredients

- 1 pound ground beef
- ½ cup bread crumbs
- 1 large egg
- 2 tablespoons grated onion
- ⅛ teaspoon ground black pepper
- ⅛ teaspoon ground nutmeg
- salt to taste

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
2. Mix ground beef, bread crumbs, egg, onion, black pepper, nutmeg, and salt together in a bowl; form into small balls.
3. Arrange balls on a baking sheet.
4. Bake in the preheated oven for 10 minutes.
5. Flip meatballs and continue baking until cooked through, 12 to 15 minutes.
6. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 160 degrees F (70 degrees C).
