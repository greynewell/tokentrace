## Ingredients

- 1 (6.5 ounce) can small shrimp, drained
- 3 tablespoons creamy salad dressing, e.g. Miracle Whip ™
- 1 tablespoon minced onion
- ⅛ teaspoon curry powder
- paprika

## Instructions

1. In a medium bowl, place the small shrimp.
2. Mash with a fork.
3. Mix in creamy salad dressing, e.g.
4. Miracle Whip ™, curry powder and onion.
5. Thoroughly blend.
6. Garnish with paprika.
7. Cover and chill in the refrigerator at least 45 minutes before serving.
