## Ingredients

- ¼ cup mayonnaise
- 1 (4 ounce) can diced green chile peppers, drained
- 1 (2.25 ounce) can sliced black olives, drained
- ¼ cup sliced green onion
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- ¼ pound peeled and deveined small shrimp
- 2 ounces crumbled blue cheese

## Instructions

1. In a bowl, mix mayonnaise, green chile peppers, black olives, and green onion.
2. Heat the olive oil in a skillet over medium heat.
3. Place shrimp in skillet and cook 2 to 5 minutes, until opaque.
4. Stir in the mayonnaise mixture and continue cooking 2 to 5 minutes, until mixture has heated through.
5. Remove skillet from heat.
6. Gently fold in blue cheese.
7. Transfer dip to a serving bowl.
