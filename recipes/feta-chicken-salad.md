## Ingredients

- 3 cups diced cooked chicken
- 2 large stalks  celery, diced
- 1  red bell pepper, seeded and diced
- ½  red onion, diced
- 6 tablespoons mayonnaise
- 6 tablespoons sour cream
- 1 (4 ounce) package feta cheese, crumbled
- 2 teaspoons dried dill weed
- 1 pinch salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Mix chicken, celery, and red onion together in a serving bowl.
2. Stir mayonnaise, sour cream, feta cheese, and dill together in a separate bowl. Pour over the chicken mixture, and stir to blend. Taste, and season with salt and pepper as needed.
3. Serve immediately, or refrigerate until serving.
