## Ingredients

- 1 (16.3 ounce) can refrigerated biscuit dough (such as Pillsbury Grands!®)
- 1 teaspoon olive oil
- 1 pound ground chicken
- ¼ cup salted butter
- 1 teaspoon minced garlic
- 1 pinch onion powder
- ¼ cup hot pepper sauce (such as Frank's RedHot®)
- 6 tablespoons blue cheese dressing, divided, or to taste

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Place biscuits 1 to 2 inches apart on an ungreased baking sheet.
3. Bake until golden brown, 13 to 17 minutes.
4. Remove from oven and cool until easily handled, about 5 minutes.
5. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
6. Add chicken; cook and stir until golden, 5 to 7 minutes.
7. Add butter, garlic, and onion powder; stir well to combine.
8. Stir in hot pepper sauce.
9. Split biscuits open; spread blue cheese dressing over each half.
10. Top with chicken mixture.
