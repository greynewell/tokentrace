## Ingredients

- 3  green bell pepper, minced
- 2 (4 ounce) cans diced jalapeno peppers
- 1 ½ cups distilled white vinegar
- 6 ½ cups white sugar
- ½ teaspoon cayenne pepper
- 1 (6 fluid ounce) container liquid pectin
- 5 drops green food coloring

## Instructions

1. In a large, stainless steel saucepan, combine peppers, vinegar, sugar, and cayenne pepper.
2. Cook over medium high heat.
3. Stir frequently until mixture begins to boil.
4. Stir in pectin; boil 5 minutes longer, stirring constantly, and mix in food coloring.
5. Skim off foam, and remove from heat.
6. Ladle into sterilized jars.
7. Seal, and process in a boiling-water canner for 5 minutes.
