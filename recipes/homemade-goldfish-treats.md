## Ingredients

- 8 ounces shredded sharp Cheddar cheese
- 1 cup all-purpose flour
- ¼ cup butter, cut into cubes
- ¾ teaspoon salt
- 2 tablespoons cold water

## Instructions

1. Combine Cheddar cheese, flour, butter, and salt in a food processor; pulse until dough is consistency of course sand.
2. Add water, 1 tablespoon at a time, until dough is moistened.
3. Wrap dough in plastic wrap and refrigerate for 20 minutes.
4. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
5. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
6. Roll dough onto a lightly floured work surface and cut into desired shapes using a cookie cutter.
7. If you choose to use a fish cut-out, use a toothpick to make faces.
8. Arrange shapes on the prepared baking sheet.
9. Bake in the preheated oven until golden brown and crispy, 15 to 16 minutes.
