## Ingredients

- 1 ¼ cups water
- 3 tablespoons honey
- 3 tablespoons butter, softened
- ¼ cup spent grain
- 1 ½ tablespoons powdered milk
- 1 teaspoon white sugar
- 1 teaspoon salt
- ½ cup rye flour
- 1 ½ cups whole wheat flour
- 1 ½ cups bread flour
- ¼ cup vital wheat gluten
- 1 teaspoon active dry yeast

## Instructions

1. Place ingredients in the pan of the bread machine in the order recommended by the manufacturer.
2. Select whole wheat cycle; press Start.
3. If using the delay timer, decrease water by 1 tablespoon.
