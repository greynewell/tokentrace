## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons butter
- 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
- 1 cup milk
- 7 slices processed American cheese
- ½ teaspoon salt

## Instructions

1. Melt butter in a medium saucepan over medium heat. Whisk in flour until a paste forms. Pour in milk and stir until mixture thickens.
2. Add cheese and salt; cook and stir until cheese has melted, about 15 minutes.
