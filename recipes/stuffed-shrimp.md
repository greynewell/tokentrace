## Ingredients

- 20  extra-large shrimp - peeled, deveined, and tails removed
- 20 leaves fresh basil
- ¼ cup creamy goat cheese
- 10 thin slices  prosciutto, halved crosswise
- 1 tablespoon olive oil

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
2. Make thin slit in the back of each shrimp, being careful not to cut all the way through.
3. Tuck 1 basil leaf into the opening of each shrimp, followed by 1/2 teaspoon of goat cheese.
4. Wrap 1/2 of a prosciutto slice around each shrimp from bottom to tail.
5. Arrange stuffed shrimp on a baking sheet.
6. Brush each lightly with olive oil.
7. Bake in the preheated oven until shrimp is pink and prosciutto is crispy, 4 to 5 minutes.
