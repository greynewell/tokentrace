## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon canned jellied cranberry sauce
- 1 tablespoon mayonnaise
- ¼ teaspoon celery seed
- 2 slices bread
- 1 tablespoon mashed leftover sweet potato casserole with marshmallow topping
- 1 tablespoon gravy  (Optional)
- 1 tablespoon dry leftover stuffing
- ¼ cup shredded leftover cooked turkey
- 1 tablespoon coarsely chopped leftover green bean casserole
- 1 tablespoon chopped French-fried onions

## Instructions

1. Mash cranberry sauce in a bowl with a fork until smooth.
2. Add mayonnaise and celery seed; mix thoroughly.
3. Spread sauce over bread slices.
4. Spread sweet potato casserole onto 1 slice of bread.
5. Mash gravy and stuffing together in a bowl.
6. Mix in turkey.
7. Spoon the mixture over the sweet potatoes; top with green bean casserole and onions.
8. Place second slice of bread on top.
