## Ingredients

- 4 (6 ounce) fillets sole
- 2  eggs, beaten
- 1 cup dry bread crumbs
- 1 quart vegetable oil for frying
- 1 cup butter
- 2 cloves garlic, crushed
- 1 cup chicken broth
- ¼ cup white wine
- 1  lemon, juiced
- salt and pepper to taste
- 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley

## Instructions

1. Dip fillets in beaten egg, and then in bread crumbs.
2. In an electric fryer, heat oil to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
3. Fry fish until golden brown.
4. Transfer to a dish, and keep warm.
5. In a small saucepan, melt butter over medium heat.
6. Add garlic, and saute for 1 to 2 minutes.
7. Stir in chicken broth, wine, and lemon juice; bring to a boil.
8. Season with salt and pepper.
9. Remove from heat, and stir in chopped parsley.
10. Serve butter sauce over fish fillets.
