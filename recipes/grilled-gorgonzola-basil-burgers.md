## Ingredients

- 1 pound ground sirloin, or extra lean beef
- 1 tablespoon minced garlic
- ⅛ teaspoon onion powder
- 1 (4 ounce) container crumbled Gorgonzola cheese
- ¼ cup fresh basil leaves, chopped
- salt and black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Preheat an outdoor grill for medium-high heat.
2. Lightly oil grill grate, and place about 4 inches from heat source.
3. Mix the ground sirloin, garlic, onion powder, Gorgonzola cheese, and basil together in a large bowl until evenly blended.
4. Divide mixture evenly to form four patties.
5. Cook patties on preheated grill until meat is evenly browned and center is no longer pink, about 5 minutes on each side.
