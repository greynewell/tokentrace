## Ingredients

- 1 (14 ounce) package breaded frozen fish sticks
- 1 (16 ounce) package frozen French fries
- 1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of celery soup
- ¾ cup milk
- ⅓ cup mayonnaise
- 1 teaspoon curry powder

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Line the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish with fish sticks.
3. Sprinkle fries in a layer over the fish.
4. Mix together the cream of celery soup, milk, and mayonnaise; pour over the fish and fries.
5. Bake uncovered for 45 minutes in the preheated oven.
6. Cool slightly before serving.
