## Ingredients

- 24 large shrimp
- 2 cups all-purpose flour
- 2 large eggs
- 2 tablespoons milk
- 2 (8 ounce) packages sweetened flaked coconut
- 1 quart vegetable oil for frying, or as needed

## Instructions

1. Peel and devein shrimp, leaving tails on.
2. Use a sharp knife and butterfly each shrimp by cutting down the back of the shrimp, but not all the way through.
3. Press shrimp open flat.
4. Place flour in a bowl.
5. Beat eggs and milk in a second bowl.
6. Place coconut in a third bowl.
7. Dredge shrimp in flour, then dip in egg mixture; repeat once more.
8. Gently roll coated shrimp in coconut, pressing in to coat.
9. Heat oil in a deep-fryer or large saucepan to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
10. Deep-fry shrimp in the preheated oil until golden brown, 2 to 3 minutes.
11. Drain on paper towels and serve.
