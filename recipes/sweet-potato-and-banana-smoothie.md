## Ingredients

- 1 large sweet potato
- 1  banana
- 2 cups soy milk
- ¼ teaspoon ground cinnamon

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Bake sweet potato in the preheated oven until tender and cooked through, about 1 hour. Remove skin, then cool in the refrigerator 8 hours to overnight.
3. Blend sweet potato, banana, soy milk, and cinnamon together in a blender until smooth.
