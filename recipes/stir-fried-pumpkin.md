## Ingredients

- 3 tablespoons olive oil
- ½ cup chopped onion
- 1 teaspoon minced garlic
- 4 cups cubed fresh pumpkin
- ½ cup grated carrot
- 2 tablespoons soy sauce
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
- 2 teaspoons black sesame seeds

## Instructions

1. Heat the oil in a large skillet.
2. Add the onion and garlic; cook and stir until the onions begin to brown.
3. Stir in the pumpkin, carrot, soy sauce, salt, and pepper; cook another 5 to 7 minutes, until pumpkin is tender.
4. Sprinkle with sesame seeds before serving.
