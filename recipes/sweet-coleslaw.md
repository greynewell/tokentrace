## Ingredients

- 1 ¼ cups mayonnaise
- ¼ cup sour cream
- ¼ cup white sugar
- 2 tablespoons white balsamic vinegar
- 1 tablespoon orange juice
- ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
- 2 ½ heads cabbage, shredded
- 2  carrots, shredded

## Instructions

1. Stir mayonnaise, sour cream, sugar, white balsamic vinegar, orange juice, and pepper together in a bowl.
2. Toss cabbage and carrots together in a large bowl. Drizzle dressing over the cabbage mixture and toss to coat.
