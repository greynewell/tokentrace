## Ingredients

- 1 (10 ounce) package fresh spinach
- ½ cup chopped almonds
- ⅓ cup sliced red onion
- ¼ cup grated Asiago cheese
- ½ cup raspberry vinaigrette dressing
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- 2  fresh peaches, sliced

## Instructions

1. Combine spinach, almonds, onion, and Asiago cheese in a large bowl.
2. Drizzle dressing over salad and season with salt and pepper.
3. Add peaches and toss.
