## Ingredients

- 3 cups watermelon chunks
- 3 cups pineapple chunks
- 2 cups sliced strawberries
- ¼ cup silver tequila, or more to taste
- ¼ cup agave nectar, or more to taste
- 3  limes, zested and juiced

## Instructions

1. Mix watermelon, pineapple, and strawberries in a serving bowl.
2. Whisk tequila, agave nectar, lime zest, and lime juice together in a bowl; pour dressing over fruit. Toss to coat.
