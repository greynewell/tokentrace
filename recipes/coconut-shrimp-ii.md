## Ingredients

- 2 quarts vegetable oil for frying
- 10 ounces orange marmalade
- 3 tablespoons prepared horseradish
- 3 tablespoons prepared mustard
- ¾ cup all-purpose flour
- ¼ teaspoon baking powder
- ¼ teaspoon paprika
- ¼ teaspoon curry powder
- ⅛ teaspoon salt
- ⅛ teaspoon cayenne pepper
- ⅓ cup beer
- 1 pound large shrimp, peeled and deveined with tails attached
- ¼ cup all-purpose flour
- 8 ounces unsweetened flaked coconut

## Instructions

1. Heat oil in a deep fryer to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
2. In a small bowl, stir together marmalade, horseradish and mustard; set aside.
3. Combine 3/4 cup flour, baking powder, paprika, curry powder, salt and cayenne.
4. Stir in beer.
5. Dredge shrimp in 1/4 cup flour, dip in beer batter and roll in coconut.
6. Fry shrimp in hot oil until golden on both sides.
7. Drain briefly and serve with reserved dipping sauce.
