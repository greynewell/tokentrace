## Ingredients

- 1 pound Brussels sprouts, trimmed
- 1 (16 ounce) bottle zesty Italian-style salad dressing
- 3 dashes hot sauce, or to taste  (Optional)

## Instructions

1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook Brussels sprouts in boiling water, stirring occasionally, until tender, about 15 minutes. Drain and transfer to a large bowl.
2. Pour Italian dressing and hot sauce over Brussels sprouts; toss to coat.
3. Marinate in the refrigerator, stirring occasionally, for 4 hours.
