## Ingredients

- 2 pounds spinach, washed and chopped
- 1  onion, chopped
- ½ pound fresh mushrooms, sliced
- 3 tablespoons vegetable oil
- 2 cups ricotta cheese
- 2 tablespoons dried basil
- 2  eggs
- 1 cup feta cheese
- ¾ cup butter, melted
- 1 (16 ounce) package phyllo dough

## Instructions

1. Steam the spinach until just wilted.
2. In a medium skillet heat the oil until hot and saute the onions and mushrooms.
3. Stir together with the spinach.
4. In a medium bowl, beat the eggs well.
5. Stir in the ricotta, feta, and basil.
6. Butter a deep dish pie pan.
7. Place a sheet of phyllo on the bottom of the pan, and brush melted butter over the sheet.
8. Repeat until there are 5 or 6 sheets in the pan.
9. Spread the ricotta mixture over the phyllo.
10. Layer 5 or 6 more sheets of filo brushed with butter on top of the cheese.
11. Spread spinach mixture over the phyllo.
12. Top the pie with 5 or 6 more sheets of phyllo brushed with butter.
13. Trim dough to the edge of the pie dish.
14. Bake at 375 degrees F (175 degrees C) for about 1 hour, or until pie is golden brown.
