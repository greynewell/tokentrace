## Ingredients

- 1 (15.25 ounce) package white cake mix
- 2 large eggs
- 6 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
- ½ cup powdered sugar

## Instructions

1. Position a rack in the center of the oven and preheat to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
2. Stir cake mix, eggs, and butter together in a large bowl to form a soft cookie dough.
3. Shape dough into 1-inch balls; roll in powdered sugar to coat. Set balls about 2 inches apart on ungreased baking sheets.
4. Bake in the preheated oven, one pan at a time, just until edges are firm and cracks appear slightly moist, 9 to 11 minutes. Cool on pans for 3 minutes; transfer to a wire rack to cool completely.
