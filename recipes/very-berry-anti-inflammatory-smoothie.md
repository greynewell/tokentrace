## Ingredients

- 1 cup unsweetened vanilla almond milk
- 1 cup fresh cherries, pitted
- ¾ cup fresh strawberries
- ¾ cup seedless red grapes
- ½ cup frozen blueberries
- ½ teaspoon ground turmeric  (Optional)

## Instructions

1. Pour almond milk into a high-frequency blender (such as Vitamix®). Add cherries, strawberries, grapes, blueberries, and turmeric.
2. Secure lid, and turn onto variable speed 1. Increase speed to variable 10. Blend for 30 to 45 seconds.
