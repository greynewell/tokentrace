## Ingredients

- 1 pound bacon
- 3 (10.75 ounce) cans  cream of mushroom soup
- 1 (14 ounce) can chicken broth
- 2  chicken breasts, cut into chunks
- 1 (6 ounce) can sliced mushrooms, drained
- ½ cup wild rice
- ¼ cup chopped onion
- ¼ cup chopped celery
- ¼ cup chopped green bell pepper
- water, as needed

## Instructions

1. Place the bacon in a large skillet and cook over medium-high heat, turning occasionally, until evenly browned, about 10 minutes.
2. Drain the bacon slices on paper towels.
3. Crumble bacon into a large pot; add cream of mushroom soup, chicken broth, chicken, mushrooms, wild rice, onion, celery, and bell pepper.
4. Bring the mixture to a boil, reduce heat to medium-low, and cook at a simmer until the rice is completely tender, about 2 hours.
5. Stir water into the soup as needed to keep it from being too thick.
