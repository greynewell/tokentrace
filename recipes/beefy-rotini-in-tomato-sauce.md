## Ingredients

- 1 pound lean ground beef
- 2 medium onions, chopped
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 (46 fluid ounce) bottle tomato juice
- 1 (8 ounce) can tomato sauce
- 3 cubes beef bouillon
- 2 teaspoons chili powder
- 1 teaspoon garlic salt
- 1 pinch ground paprika
- 1 pinch garlic powder, or to taste
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- 1 (16 ounce) package rotini pasta

## Instructions

1. Heat a large, heavy pot over medium-high heat.
2. Cook and stir ground beef, onions, and garlic in the hot skillet until beef is browned and crumbly and onions and garlic are softened, 5 to 7 minutes.
3. Add tomato juice, tomato sauce, bouillon cubes, chili powder, garlic salt, paprika, garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
4. Bring to a boil, while stirring.
5. Reduce heat and simmer, stirring occasionally, until reduced, 30 to 40 minutes.
6. Meanwhile, bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
7. Cook spaghetti in the boiling water, stirring occasionally, until tender yet firm to the bite, about 12 minutes.
8. Drain.
9. Stir pasta into sauce and serve hot.
