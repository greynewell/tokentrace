## Ingredients

- 1  egg
- 1 (18.25 ounce) package yellow cake mix
- Â½ cup butter, softened
- 8 ounces cream cheese, softened
- 1 (16 ounce) package confectioners' sugar, sifted
- 2  eggs
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Grease and flour one 9x13 inch pan.
3. Beat together 1 egg, cake mix, and butter for 5 minutes.
4. Mix and pour into prepared pan.
5. Cream together cream cheese, confectioners' sugar, 2 eggs, and vanilla extract.
6. Pour this on top of the cake base.
7. Bake 35-40 minutes until browned.
8. Cool 15 minutes and cut into squares.
