## Ingredients

- 2 pounds mahi mahi fillets
- ½ cup dark rum
- ½ cup fresh lime juice
- 2  onion, sliced into thin rings
- 1  lemon, sliced
- 2 teaspoons dried oregano
- 4 tablespoons butter
- ground black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Arrange the fish fillets in an oven proof 9x13 glass baking dish.
2. Pour the rum and lime juice over the fish and place a slice onion on each fillet.
3. Cover and refrigerate for 2 to 4 hours.
4. Preheat oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C).
5. Remove fish from the refrigerator and pour off about 3/4 of the liquid.
6. Leave the onion slices in the dish and place a thick slice of lemon on each fish fillet.
7. Sprinkle with oregano and black pepper to taste.
8. Place a pat of butter or margarine on each fillet.
9. Bake, covered, at 350 degrees F (165 degrees C) for about 20 to 30 minutes or until fish flakes easily with a fork.
10. Be careful not to over cook the fish or it will be dry.
11. Serve with the cooked onion and lemon slices.
