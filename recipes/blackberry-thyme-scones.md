## Ingredients

- 1 cup all-purpose flour
- 1 cup oats
- ⅓ cup butter, cut in small pieces
- 1 tablespoon sugar, or more to taste
- ½ cup half-and-half
- 2  eggs, beaten
- 1 cup fresh blackberries
- 1 teaspoon chopped fresh thyme

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C).
2. Process flour, oats, butter, and sugar together in a food processor until mixture resembles a coarse meal.
3. Stir half-and-half and eggs together in a bowl, reserving 1 tablespoon. Add egg mixture to flour mixture in food processor and pulse until it forms a soft dough.
4. Turn out dough onto lightly floured work surface. Press dough into a circle about 1-inch thick. Gently press blackberries into top of dough; brush with reserved 1 tablespoon egg mixture and sprinkle with thyme. Cut into six wedges and transfer to a baking sheet.
5. Bake in preheated oven until scones turn golden brown, about 15 minutes.
