## Ingredients

- 1 (8 ounce) container sour cream
- 8 ounces chive and onion cream cheese
- 1 (1 ounce) package taco seasoning mix
- 1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
- 1 cup chopped tomatoes
- 2  chopped green onions

## Instructions

1. In a bowl, combine the sour cream, cream cheese and  .
2. Spread the mixture into a pie dish.
3. Sprinkle the Cheddar cheese over the mixture in an even layer.
4. Top the cheese with the tomatoes and green onions.
