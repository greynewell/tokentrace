## Ingredients

- 1 (20 ounce) bag Tyson Grilled & Ready® Pulled Chicken Breast, thawed
- 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
- 1 cup sour cream
- ½ cup Buffalo sauce
- 1 (12 ounce) package tortilla chips

## Instructions

1. Combine the cream cheese, sour cream, and Buffalo sauce in a mixing or microwave-safe bowl.
2. Fold in the pulled chicken once thawed, and serve cold or microwave on high for 2 minutes, stirring until the dip is heated throughout.
3. Serve alongside your favorite tortilla chips.
