## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon belacan (shrimp paste)
- 8  fresh red chile peppers, coarsely sliced
- salt to taste
- 2 small kasturi limes, halved

## Instructions

1. Place belacan in a skillet over medium heat; cook and stir until fragrant and toasted, about 5 minutes.
2. Grind red chile peppers with salt using a mortar and pestle until coarsely ground.
3. Add belacan and grind until evenly distributed.
4. Squeeze limes over paste just before serving.
