## Ingredients

- cooking spray
- 2 cups quick-cooking oats
- ½ cup confectioners' sugar
- ½ cup butter, melted
- ½ teaspoon ground cinnamon
- ¼ teaspoon ground nutmeg  (Optional)
- ⅛ teaspoon salt

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly spray a 9-inch pie dish with cooking spray.
2. Combine oats, confectioners' sugar, butter, cinnamon, nutmeg, and salt together in a bowl; press into the prepared pie dish.
3. Bake in the preheated oven until lightly browned, 15 to 20 minutes. Cool completely.
