## Ingredients

- cooking spray
- 1 cup shortening
- 1 cup white sugar
- ½ cup brown sugar
- 2  eggs
- 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
- 2 cups all-purpose flour
- 1 ½ teaspoons salt
- 1 teaspoon baking soda
- 1 (12 ounce) bag semisweet chocolate chips

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly spray an 18-inch pizza pan with cooking spray.
2. Combine shortening, white sugar, brown sugar, eggs, and vanilla extract in a large bowl. Beat with an electric mixer until creamy.
3. Mix flour, salt, and baking soda together. Mix into the shortening mixture. Stir in chocolate chips to finish cookie dough.
4. Spoon dough into the center of the prepared pizza pan. Press dough from the center towards the rim of the pan.
5. Bake in the preheated oven until edges are firm and center looks set, 10 to 12 minutes. Let cool before slicing.
