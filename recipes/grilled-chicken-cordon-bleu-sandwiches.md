## Ingredients

- 2 slices whole wheat bread
- 2 tablespoons butter, softened
- 1 tablespoon sour cream
- 2 slices Swiss cheese
- 1 thick slice deli cooked chicken breast meat
- 1 slice deli ham

## Instructions

1. Spread butter or margarine on the outsides of the bread slices.
2. Spread sour cream on the insides of the bread slices.
3. Then layer sandwich as follows: Bread slice, cheese slice, chicken, ham, 2nd cheese slice, 2nd bread slice.
4. In a small skillet over medium heat, grill sandwich until meat is heated through and cheese has melted.
