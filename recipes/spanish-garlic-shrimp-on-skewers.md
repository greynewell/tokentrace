## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons GOYA® Extra Virgin Olive Oil
- 1 teaspoon GOYA® Adobo with Pepper
- 1 teaspoon GOYA® Minced Garlic
- ¼ teaspoon GOYA® Crushed Red Pepper
- 1 pound jumbo shrimp, peeled and deveined
- 1 large green and/or red bell pepper, cut into 1-inch pieces
- 1  lemon, cut into 8 slices
- 4  skewers

## Instructions

1. In zip-top plastic bag, or plastic container with a lid, mix together oil, Adobo, garlic and pepper flakes.
2. Add shrimp, tossing to combine; transfer to refrigerator.
3. Chill shrimp at least 15 minutes, or up to 30 minutes.
4. Remove shrimp from marinade; reserve any remaining liquid.
5. Alternately thread peppers, shrimp and lemons on skewers, so that each skewer starts and ends with peppers and contains 4 shrimp pieces.
6. Using pastry brush, brush reserved marinade onto shrimp and vegetables.
7. Prepare grill to medium-high heat, or heat a heavy-bottomed grill pan over medium-high heat.
8. Place shrimp on hot surface and cook until shrimp turn pink and opaque, flipping once, 3 - 5 minutes.
