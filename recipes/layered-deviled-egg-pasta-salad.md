## Ingredients

- 6  eggs
- 2 ½ tablespoons mayonnaise
- 2 teaspoons prepared yellow mustard
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- 2 sprigs fresh dill
- 3 cups farfalle (bow-tie) pasta
- 2 tablespoons mayonnaise
- 1 pound cherry tomatoes, halved
- 2 cups chopped celery
- 2 cups diced cooked ham
- 4 cups chopped lettuce
- 4  spring onions, thinly sliced

## Instructions

1. Place eggs in a large saucepan and cover with cold water.
2. Bring water to a boil.
3. Remove saucepan from heat; let eggs stand in hot water for 10 minutes.
4. Immerse eggs in a bowl of ice water and let cool, 1 to 2 minutes.
5. Peel eggs and halve them lengthwise.
6. Scoop yolks into a bowl.
7. Arrange egg whites cut-side up on a plate.
8. Mash egg yolks into a crumbly paste with a fork.
9. Mix in 2 1/2 tablespoons mayonnaise and mustard with the fork.
10. Season with salt and pepper.
11. Spoon egg yolk mixture into a small piping bag; pipe into the egg whites.
12. Garnish with fresh dill.
13. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
14. Cook bow-tie pasta at a boil, stirring occasionally, until tender yet firm to the bite, about 12 minutes.
15. Drain and let cool, about 15 minutes.
16. Mix cooled pasta with 2 tablespoons mayonnaise in a bowl.
17. Season with salt and pepper.
18. Spoon pasta into the bottom of a large glass trifle bowl.
19. Layer tomatoes, celery, ham, and lettuce on top.
20. Arrange deviled eggs on top before serving.
21. Garnish with spring onions.
