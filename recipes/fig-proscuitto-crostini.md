## Ingredients

- 24  French bread slices, 1/2 inch thick
- ⅔ cup fig jam
- ¼ cup chopped toasted walnuts
- ¼ pound prosciutto, cut into 24 pieces
- 1 ½ cups KRAFT Shredded Triple Cheddar Cheese with a TOUCH OF PHILADELPHIA
- 1 tablespoon chopped fresh rosemary

## Instructions

1. Heat broiler.
2. Place bread slices in single layer on baking sheet; spread with jam.
3. Top with remaining ingredients.
4. Broil, 4 inches from heat, 3 min. or until cheese is melted.
