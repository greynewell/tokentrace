## Ingredients

- 1 head lettuce
- 1 cup chopped cooked chicken breast
- 1 (8 ounce) can chopped water chestnuts
- 1 bunch green onions, chopped
- 1 cup sliced almonds
- 1 (5 ounce) can chow mein noodles
- ½ cup vegetable oil
- 4 tablespoons vinegar
- 4 tablespoons white sugar
- ½ teaspoon salt
- 1 pinch ground black pepper
- 1 tablespoon poppy seeds

## Instructions

1. In a small bowl, whisk together the oil, vinegar, sugar, salt, pepper, and poppy seeds, and set aside.
2. Wash lettuce and tear into bite-sized pieces.
3. To a large bowl, first add the lettuce, then the onions, water chestnuts, and chicken.
4. Just before serving, top with the almonds, noodles and dressing, and toss lightly.
