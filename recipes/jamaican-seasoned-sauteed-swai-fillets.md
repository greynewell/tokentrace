## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 1 tablespoon Jamaican jerk seasoning
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 5 (3 ounce) fillets swai fish

## Instructions

1. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat; add butter.
2. Sprinkle Jamaican jerk seasoning and salt evenly over swai fish.
3. Arrange fish in the skillet in 1 layer, working in batches if needed; cook until fish flakes easily with a fork, 3 to 4 minutes per side.
