## Ingredients

- ¾ pound shrimp, peeled and deveined
- 2 teaspoons all-purpose flour
- salt and pepper to taste
- 2  egg yolks
- 2  egg whites
- 4 slices whole wheat bread
- ½ cup sesame seeds
- 2 cups oil for frying

## Instructions

1. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium high heat to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Chop and mash shrimp using a large knife.
3. Mix in flour, egg yolks, salt and pepper.
4. In a separate bowl,  whisk the egg whites to stiff peaks and fold into the shrimp mixture.
5. Slice bread into 4 pieces each.
6. Put 2 teaspoons of the shrimp mixture onto each piece and sprinkle with sesame seeds.
7. Starting with the shrimp side down, fry for 3 minutes.
8. Turn, and fry for another 3 minutes or until golden brown.
9. Remove from oil, drain on paper towels and serve warm.
