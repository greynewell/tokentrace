## Ingredients

- 1 pound ground beef
- 1 small onion, chopped
- ½ cup seasoned bread crumbs
- ½ cup water
- ½ cup soy sauce
- 2 cloves garlic, crushed
- 1 (1.5 fluid ounce) jigger whiskey

## Instructions

1. In a medium bowl, mix ground beef, onion and seasoned bread crumbs.
2. Form the mixture into bite-sized balls.
3. In a large skillet over medium high heat, cook the balls until evenly brown throughout.
4. In a large bowl, mix water, soy sauce, garlic and whiskey.
5. Place the balls into the mixture.
6. Cover and chill in the refrigerator at least 1 hour.
7. Transfer the balls and sauce mixture to a slow cooker set to low heat.
8. Cook at least 30 minutes before serving.
