## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons butter, divided
- 1 slice brown rye bread
- 2 ounces sliced ham
- 2 ounces sliced Gruyere cheese
- 1  egg
- 1 teaspoon chopped fresh parsley, or to taste
- salt and ground black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Butter the bread with 1 tablespoon butter and place on a large serving plate.
2. Heat a skillet over medium heat.
3. Fry ham lightly, 3 to 4 minutes.
4. Cover with Gruyere cheese and melt, 1 to 2 minutes more.
5. Set aside and keep warm.
6. Heat remaining butter in another skillet over medium heat.
7. Add egg and cook until white is set, about 3 minutes.
8. Flip carefully and cook until set but yolk is still runny, 1 minute more.
9. Cover the bread with ham and cheese and top with the egg.
10. Sprinkle with parsley, salt, and pepper.
