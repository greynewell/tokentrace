## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
- 1 cup sweet corn kernels
- 2 cups fish stock
- salt and pepper to taste
- 1  egg, beaten
- 1 (6 ounce) can crabmeat, drained and flaked

## Instructions

1. Heat the oil in a saucepan over medium-high heat.
2. Add the corn, and fry for about 30 seconds.
3. Pour in the fish stock, and season with salt and pepper.
4. Simmer over medium-low heat for a few minutes.
5. Drizzle in the egg while slowly stirring the soup to create a white swirl.
6. To serve, divide the crabmeat into 4 bowls, and ladle the hot soup over it.
