## Ingredients

- ½ cup soy sauce
- ¼ cup packed brown sugar
- ½ cup orange juice
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- 1 teaspoon ground black pepper  (Optional)

## Instructions

1. In a small bowl, stir together soy sauce, brown sugar, orange juice, garlic, and pepper.
2. Pour over beef, pork, or chicken.
3. Cover, and marinate in the refrigerator for 4 hours, or overnight.
4. The longer the meat is marinated, the stronger the flavor will be.
