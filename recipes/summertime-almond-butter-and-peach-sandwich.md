## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons almond butter
- 2 slices wheat bread
- 1  peach, or to taste, sliced

## Instructions

1. Spread almond butter onto one side of each slice of bread.
2. Pile the sliced peaches atop one almond butter-covered slice and sandwich with the other.
