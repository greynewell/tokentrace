## Ingredients

- 2 pounds Yukon gold potatoes
- ¼ cup white vinegar
- ¼ cup olive oil
- 2 teaspoons garlic powder
- 1 teaspoon sea salt
- 1 teaspoon dried sage
- 1 teaspoon dried tarragon

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C).
2. Scrub the potatoes and dry with paper towels.
3. Cut into small chunks and place in a large bowl.
4. Add vinegar, oil, garlic powder, salt, sage, and tarragon to the bowl with the potatoes.
5. Toss to coat.
6. Pour into a baking dish.
7. Bake in the preheated oven until crispy on the outside and soft on the inside, about 45 minutes.
8. Serve immediately.
