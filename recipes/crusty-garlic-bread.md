## Ingredients

- ½ cup butter, softened
- 3 tablespoons dried minced garlic
- 1 teaspoon dried basil
- ½ teaspoon salt
- 1 loaf French bread, halved lengthwise

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
2. Beat butter, garlic, basil, and salt together in a bowl until smooth.
3. Spread butter mixture onto each half of bread.
4. Wrap each half of bread in aluminum foil and place on a baking sheet.
5. Bake in the preheated oven until golden and crusty, about 20 minutes.
