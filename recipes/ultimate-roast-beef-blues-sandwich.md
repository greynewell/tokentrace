## Ingredients

- 4  whole wheat sub rolls, lightly toasted
- ⅓ cup light mayonnaise
- 16  fresh basil leaves
- 1 pound thinly sliced cooked deli roast beef
- ¾ cup crumbled blue cheese
- ½ cup toasted walnuts

## Instructions

1. Open the rolls and spread each side with 1 to 2 tablespoons of mayonnaise.
2. Place 4 basil leaves on four of the rolls.
3. Layer the same four rolls with the roast beef, 1 to 2 tablespoons of blue cheese, and a sprinkling of walnuts.
4. Top each with the remaining half rolls.
