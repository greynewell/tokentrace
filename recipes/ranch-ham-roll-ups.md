## Ingredients

- 2 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened
- ¾  avocado - peeled, pitted, and mashed
- 1 (1 ounce) package ranch salad dressing mix
- 3  green onions, chopped
- ½  green bell pepper, chopped
- 11 (8 inch) flour tortillas
- 22 slices thinly sliced ham

## Instructions

1. Beat cream cheese, avocado, and salad dressing mix in a small bowl; stir in onions and bell peppers.
2. Spread 3 tablespoons cream cheese mixture over each tortilla; top each tortilla with two slices ham.
3. Roll tortillas up tightly and wrap in plastic wrap; refrigerate until firm, about 30 minutes.
4. Unwrap and cut into 3/4-inch slices.
