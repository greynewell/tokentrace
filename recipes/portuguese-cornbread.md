## Ingredients

- 1 ½ cups cornmeal
- 1 ½ teaspoons salt
- 1 cup boiling water
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 1 (.25 ounce) package active dry yeast
- 1 teaspoon white sugar
- ¼ cup warm water (110 degrees F/45 degrees C)
- 2 cups all-purpose flour

## Instructions

1. In a small bowl, sprinkle or crumble yeast and sugar over the lukewarm water.
2. Let stand for 2 or 3 minutes, then stir to dissolve.
3. Set the bowl in a warm, draft-free place for 8 to 10 minutes, or until yeast doubles in volume.
4. Pulverize the cornmeal in a blender until fine.
5. In a large mixing bowl, combine 1 cup cornmeal, salt, and boiling water.
6. Stir vigorously until smooth.
7. Stir in 1 tablespoon olive oil.
8. Cool mixture to lukewarm.
9. Stir proofed yeast into the cornmeal mixture.
10. Gradually add 1/2 cup cornmeal and flour.
11. Gather the dough into a ball, and place it in a greased bowl.
12. Cover.
13. Set it aside in a warm place for about 30 minutes, or until doubled in bulk.
14. With a pastry brush, coat the bottom and sides of a 9 inch pie pan with remaining 1 tablespoon olive oil.
15. Turn the dough out into pie pan, and cover.
16. Let it rise in a warm place for another 30 minutes, or until it doubles in bulk again.
17. Bake in the middle of a preheated 350 degree F (175 degree C) oven for 40 minutes, or until top is golden.
18. Transfer to a rack to cool.
