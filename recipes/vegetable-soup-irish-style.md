## Ingredients

- 3  carrots, chopped
- 3 large potatoes - peeled and cubed
- 1  parsnip, peeled and diced
- 1  turnip, peeled and diced
- 1  leek, sliced
- ½  onion, chopped
- ¼ cup dry potato flakes  (Optional)
- salt and pepper to taste
- 1 cup water, or as needed

## Instructions

1. Place the carrots, potatoes, parsnip, turnip, leek and onion into a large saucepan.
2. Fill with enough water to cover.
3. Bring to a boil and cook until the vegetables are tender.
4. Drain off water and puree vegetables in a blender or using a stick blender.
5. Return the puree to the saucepan and stir in water to reach your desired thickness.
6. Heat to a simmer and season with salt and pepper.
7. Serve and enjoy.
