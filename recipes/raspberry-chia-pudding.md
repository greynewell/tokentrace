## Ingredients

- 1 cup coconut milk
- 1 cup unsweetened rice milk
- 6 tablespoons chia seeds
- 2 tablespoons maple syrup
- 2 cups fresh raspberries
- 2 tablespoons chia seeds
- ¾ teaspoon vanilla extract

## Instructions

1. Combine coconut milk, rice milk, 6 tablespoons chia seeds, and maple syrup in a bowl.
2. Let sit for 10 minutes.
3. Whisk until smooth.
4. Cover with plastic wrap and chill until the consistency of pudding, 8 hours to overnight.
5. Mash raspberries in a bowl.
6. Stir in 2 tablespoons chia seeds and vanilla extract.
7. Cover with plastic wrap and chill until gelled, 8 hours to overnight.
8. Spoon some of the raspberry mixture into the bottom of each serving glass.
9. Stir pudding with a spoon until smooth; divide evenly among the glasses.
10. Spoon remaining raspberry mixture on top.
