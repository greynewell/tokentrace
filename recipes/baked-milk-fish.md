## Ingredients

- 1 ½ pounds cod fillets
- 1 ½ cups 1% milk
- 1  onion, chopped
- 1 teaspoon lemon pepper
- 1 teaspoon dried parsley
- salt to taste
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Lightly coat an 8x8 baking dish with cooking spray.
3. Rinse cod filets and pat dry.
4. Place fish in a single layer in the baking dish.
5. Mix together milk, onion, lemon pepper, parsley and salt; pour over fish filets.
6. Drizzle filets with lemon juice.
7. Bake in preheated oven for 15 minutes, or until fish is firm and can be flaked with a fork.
