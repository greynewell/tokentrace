## Ingredients

- 4 cups milk
- 2 (3.4 ounce) packages instant vanilla pudding mix
- 2 (8 ounce) containers whipped topping (such as Cool Whip®)
- 30  chocolate sandwich cookies (such as Oreo®), crushed, or to taste

## Instructions

1. Whisk together milk and instant pudding mix in a large serving bowl until thickened, about 2 minutes. Fold in whipped topping until incorporated.
2. Stir crushed cookies into pudding until well blended. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap. Refrigerate until chilled and thickened, at least 1 hour to overnight.
