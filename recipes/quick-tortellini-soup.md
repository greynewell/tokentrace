## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 5 cloves garlic, chopped
- 3 cups chicken broth, or as needed
- 2 (14.5 ounce) cans diced tomatoes
- 1 cup spinach
- 1 teaspoon red pepper flakes  (Optional)
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- 2 cups cheese tortellini

## Instructions

1. Heat olive oil in a pot over medium heat; cook and stir garlic in the hot oil until lightly browned, 1 to 2 minutes.
2. Pour in chicken broth and bring to a simmer.
3. Add tomatoes and spinach; simmer until flavors blend, about 25 minutes.
4. Season with red pepper flakes, salt, and black pepper.
5. Fill a large pot with lightly salted water and bring to a rolling boil.
6. Once the water is boiling, stir in tortellini and return to a boil.
7. Cook until tortellini float to the top, 3 to 4 minutes; drain.
8. Mix tortellini into tomato mixture; simmer for 5 more minutes.
