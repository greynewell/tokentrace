## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 1 small onion, diced
- 2 cloves garlic, crushed
- ½ pound pork loin, cut into small cubes
- ½ pound tiger prawns, peeled and deveined
- 1  tomato, chopped
- salt and pepper to taste
- 1  bitter melon, seeded and sliced

## Instructions

1. Heat the olive oil in a skillet over medium heat.
2. Cook the onion and garlic in the hot oil until fragrant, about 5 minutes.
3. Mix the pork into the onion and garlic; cook until no longer pink, about 5 minutes.
4. Stir the prawns into the mixture and cook until no longer translucent, about 5 minutes more.
5. Add the tomatoes; season with salt and pepper.
6. Continue cooking until the tomatoes soften, about 5 minutes.
7. Stir the bitter melon through the mixture and cook until the melon is tender, about 5 minutes.
8. Serve hot.
