## Ingredients

- 1 (8 ounce) package linguini pasta
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 1  onion, chopped
- 1 clove crushed garlic
- 1 pound large shrimp - peeled and deveined
- 1 ¼ cups frozen artichoke hearts
- ½ cup white wine
- 2 tablespoons lemon juice
- 2 teaspoons lemon zest
- salt to taste
- ground black pepper to taste
- 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley

## Instructions

1. Cook pasta in boiling water.
2. While pasta is cooking, saute onion and garlic in oil for about 5 minutes.
3. Remove to bowl.
4. Add shrimp to remaining drippings, and cook for about 3 minutes.
5. Stir in artichoke hearts, wine, lemon juice, lemon peel, salt, pepper, and cooked onion.
6. Heat to boiling over high heat.
7. Reduce heat to low; simmer, covered, for 4 to 5 minutes.
8. Toss with pasta and parsley.
9. Serve immediately.
