## Ingredients

- 3 pounds catfish fillets
- 2 tablespoons Cajun seasoning (such as Tex Joy® All Purpose), or to taste
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- ½ (16 ounce) package saltine crackers, crumbled
- 1 (2.45 ounce) package sliced almonds
- 2  eggs, beaten
- 2 cups milk
- 1 teaspoon pressed garlic
- 1 quart peanut oil, for frying

## Instructions

1. Season catfish with Cajun seasoning, salt, and black pepper.
2. Refrigerate fillets 1 to 3 hours.
3. Mix saltine crackers and almonds in a large, wide bowl.
4. Stir milk, beaten eggs, and garlic together in a separate bowl.
5. Soak the catfish fillets in the milk mixture for 5 to 10 minutes.
6. Heat peanut oil in a deep-fryer or large saucepan to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
7. Gently press the catfish fillets into the cracker mixture to coat completely; shake to remove any loose breading.
8. Fry fillets in hot peanut oil until cooked through and floats atop the oil, about 5 minutes.
