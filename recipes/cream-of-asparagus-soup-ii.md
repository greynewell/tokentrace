## Ingredients

- 1 pound fresh asparagus
- 3 ½ cups chicken broth
- ¼ cup margarine
- ¼ cup all-purpose flour
- ½ cup half-and-half
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ⅛ teaspoon ground black pepper

## Instructions

1. Trim the coarse ends of the asparagus and cut asparagus into one inch pieces.
2. In a medium skillet over medium high heat, saute the asparagus in 1 cup of the broth for about 7 minutes, or until tender.
3. In a large saucepan over medium high heat, melt the butter or margarine and remove from heat.
4. Add the flour and stir well until smooth.
5. Gradually add the remaining 2 1/2 cups broth.
6. Return the saucepan to the heat and continue to cook, stirring occasionally, until slightly thickened.
7. Add the half-and-half, salt, pepper and cooked asparagus with liquid.
8. Stir well and heat thoroughly.
9. Serve hot and enjoy!
