## Ingredients

- 1 (16 ounce) package frozen cauliflower
- 1 (10 ounce) package frozen chopped broccoli, thawed
- 2  cooked boneless chicken breast halves, chopped
- 1 (16 ounce) jar Alfredo sauce
- 2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese, or to taste, divided
- 1 envelope onion soup mix
- 1 tablespoon minced garlic

## Instructions

1. Place a steamer insert into a saucepan and fill with water to just below the bottom of the steamer.
2. Bring water to a boil.
3. Add cauliflower, cover, and steam until tender, about 5 minutes.
4. Transfer cauliflower to a food processor; process using the shredding blade.
5. Transfer cauliflower to the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking pan.
6. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
7. Refill steamer with water and return to a boil.
8. Add broccoli, cover, and steam until tender, about 5 minutes.
9. Arrange broccoli on top of the shredded cauliflower.
10. Cover with cooked chicken.
11. Combine  , 1 cup Cheddar cheese, onion soup mix, and garlic in a saucepan over medium heat.
12. Cook and stir until hot and melted, about 2 minutes.
13. Spread sauce over the chicken.
14. Top with remaining Cheddar cheese.
15. Bake in the preheated oven until bubbly on top, 30 to 40 minutes.
