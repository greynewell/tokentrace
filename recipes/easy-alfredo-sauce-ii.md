## Ingredients

- ½ cup butter, room temperature
- ¾ cup grated Parmesan cheese
- 1 cup heavy cream

## Instructions

1. In a medium bowl, beat butter and Parmesan with an electric mixer until fluffy.
2. Add cream, a little at a time, until mixture resembles softly scrambled eggs.
3. Toss with hot pasta to serve.
