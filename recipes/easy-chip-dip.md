## Ingredients

- ¾ cup sour cream
- ¼ cup salsa
- 1 (1.8 ounce) packet dry vegetable soup mix

## Instructions

1. Mix together the sour cream, salsa, and the soup mix in a small bowl.
2. Cover and chill in refrigerator at least 15 minutes.
