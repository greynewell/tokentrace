## Ingredients

- 2 bunches dino kale, cut into 2-inch pieces
- ¼ cup olive oil
- 1 teaspoon sea salt
- ½  lemon, juiced
- 1 tablespoon honey

## Instructions

1. Combine the kale pieces, olive oil, lemon juice, and sea salt in a resealable bag.
2. Seal and massage the mixture for 3 minutes.
3. The kale will be starting to break down a bit.
4. Drizzle the honey over the mixture and again massage gently for another 3 minutes; the kale will now be half the bulk you started with.
5. Lay flat and even in a food dehydrator; cook at 115 degrees F (46 degrees C) until crispy, about 8 hours.
6. Alternately, you can bake in an oven at 145 degrees F (65 degrees C) until crispy, 4 to 5 hours.
