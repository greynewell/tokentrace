## Ingredients

- 1 cup brown sugar
- 1 cup all-purpose flour
- ¼ cup butter, diced

## Instructions

1. Mix together sugar and flour in a medium bowl. Cut in butter with a fork or stand mixer just until the topping is crumbly.
2. Top your pie before baking.
