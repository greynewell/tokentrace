## Ingredients

- 6 large carrots, shredded
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- ¼ cup finely chopped walnuts
- ¼ cup light mayonnaise

## Instructions

1. Stir together the carrots, garlic, walnuts, and mayonnaise until evenly blended.
2. Serve.
