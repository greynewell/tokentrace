## Ingredients

- 1 (5 ounce) can chicken chunks, drained
- 1 tablespoon hot pepper sauce
- 1 slice Cheddar cheese
- 1  hot dog bun, split

## Instructions

1. Warm chicken and hot sauce in a nonstick skillet over medium heat, stirring to coat chicken evenly with sauce, until heated through, about 3 minutes.
2. Scrape chicken into the center of the pan to form a shape similar to the hot dog bun. Cut Cheddar cheese slice in half and place over chicken; cook until Cheddar is melted, 1 to 2 minutes.
3. Place bottom half of hot dog bun over chicken and cheese mixture, slide a spatula underneath, and invert onto a plate. Place top half of hot dog bun over melted cheese.
