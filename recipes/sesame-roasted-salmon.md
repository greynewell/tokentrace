## Ingredients

- cooking spray
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 teaspoons soy sauce
- 2 teaspoons Dijon mustard
- 2 teaspoons honey
- 1 ¼ pounds skin-on salmon fillet, cut into 4 pieces
- 1 tablespoon black sesame seeds

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
2. Line a baking sheet with aluminum foil and spray with cooking spray.
3. Combine garlic, soy sauce, Dijon mustard, and honey in a bowl.
4. Place salmon, skin-side down, onto the prepared baking sheet.
5. Prick salmon several times with a fork.
6. Spread garlic mixture over salmon; top with sesame seeds.
7. Bake in the preheated oven until fish flakes easily with a fork, 10 to 20 minutes.
