## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon ground cayenne pepper
- 1 tablespoon ground jalapeno pepper
- 1 tablespoon ground habanero pepper
- ½ teaspoon dried oregano
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ⅛ teaspoon white sugar
- ⅛ teaspoon ground black pepper
- ⅛ teaspoon ground cinnamon
- 12  chicken wings, thawed if frozen

## Instructions

1. Combine cayenne pepper, jalapeno pepper, habanero pepper, oregano, salt, sugar, black pepper, and cinnamon in a small bowl; mix well to make dry rub.
2. Place chicken wings in a large resealable bag or container. Add dry rub mixture to wings; seal container and shake well until evenly distributed.
3. Preheat a grill for medium heat and lightly oil the grate.
4. Cook the chicken wings on the preheated grill, turning occasionally, until the chicken is well-browned and no longer pink, 15 to 20 minutes.
