## Ingredients

- 1  Granny Smith apple, cored and julienned
- 1 cup balsamic vinaigrette salad dressing
- 1 cup crumbled blue cheese
- ½ cup chopped pecans
- 4 slices turkey bacon
- 6 cups torn romaine lettuce

## Instructions

1. Place apple into a bowl with balsamic vinaigrette. Refrigerate for at least 1 hour to marinate.
2. Cook bacon in the microwave or in a skillet over medium-high heat until browned. Drain and crumble.
3. In a salad bowl, combine romaine, blue cheese, pecans, and bacon; toss to blend.
4. When ready to serve, pour apples and dressing over romaine mixture.
