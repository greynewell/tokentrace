## Ingredients

- 1 small onion, diced
- 1 (6 ounce) can salmon, drained and flaked
- 8  crackers, crushed, or more to taste
- 1  egg, beaten
- 1 tablespoon butter, softened
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice
- 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
- 3 tablespoons vegetable oil

## Instructions

1. Mix onion, salmon, crackers, egg, butter, lemon juice, and black pepper together in a bowl; form into 4 patties.
2. Heat oil in a skillet over medium heat; fry patties in the hot oil until crispy and golden brown, about 5 minutes per side.
