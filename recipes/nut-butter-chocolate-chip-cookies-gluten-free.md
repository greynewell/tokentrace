## Ingredients

- 2 cups almond butter
- 1 cup fine-grind almond flour
- ⅔ cup coconut palm sugar
- 1  egg, slightly beaten
- ½ teaspoon vanilla extract  (Optional)
- 1 pinch sea salt
- 1 cup dark chocolate chips

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 360 degrees F (182 degrees C).
2. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
3. Mix almond butter, almond flour, palm sugar, egg, vanilla extract, and salt together in a bowl; fold in chocolate chips.
4. Roll dough into 24 balls and arrange on the prepared baking sheet.
5. Press each ball lightly with a fork dipped in water to form fork marks.
6. Bake in the preheated oven until lightly browned, 8 to 10 minutes.
