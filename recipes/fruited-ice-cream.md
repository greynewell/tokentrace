## Ingredients

- 2 (14 ounce) cans sweetened condensed milk
- 5 cups milk
- 2 cups heavy cream
- 2 tablespoons vanilla extract
- ½ teaspoon salt
- 3 cups chopped strawberries

## Instructions

1. Combine condensed milk, milk, cream, vanilla, salt and fruit in freezer canister of ice cream maker.
2. Freeze according to manufacturer's directions.
