## Ingredients

- 2 cups all-purpose flour
- 1 tablespoon baking powder
- 1 teaspoon salt
- ½ cup butter, softened
- ½ cup packed brown sugar
- ½ cup real maple syrup
- 2  eggs
- ¾ cup milk
- ½ cup chopped walnuts

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). Line a 12-cup muffin pan with paper baking cups.
2. Sift together flour, baking powder, and salt in a large bowl; set aside.
3. Cream butter and sugar in a mixing bowl until light and fluffy. Stir in maple syrup and eggs; beat until well blended. Add flour mixture alternately with milk, beating just until combined. Stir in walnuts. Spoon batter into the prepared muffin cups, filling each about 3/4 full.
4. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center of a muffin comes out clean, about 20 minutes. Remove to a wire rack to cool completely.
