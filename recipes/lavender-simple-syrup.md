## Ingredients

- 1 cup water
- 1 cup white sugar
- 1 tablespoon fresh lavender blossoms

## Instructions

1. Combine water, sugar, and lavender blossoms in a small saucepan over medium-high heat. Bring to a boil, stirring until sugar dissolves. Reduce heat and simmer for 1 minute. Remove from heat and let syrup steep for about 30 minutes.
2. Pour syrup into a sterilized glass jar through a mesh strainer to remove blossoms; let cool.
