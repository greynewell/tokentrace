## Ingredients

- 1 cup water
- 2 teaspoons honey
- 1 teaspoon fresh lemon juice
- 1 teaspoon white sugar, or to taste

## Instructions

1. Pour water into a mug. Add honey and heat in the microwave for 1 minute 30 seconds.
2. Stir in lemon juice, mixing until honey is dissolved. Stir in sugar.
