## Ingredients

- ¾ cup olive oil
- ¼ cup balsamic vinegar
- 1 tablespoon dark brown sugar
- 2 ½ teaspoons minced garlic
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ½ teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
- 6 cups mixed baby greens
- 3  pears, thinly sliced
- 1 cup dried cranberries
- 1 cup toasted pecans
- ¾ cup cubed Havarti cheese
- ½ cup toasted sliced almonds

## Instructions

1. Whisk olive oil, balsamic vinegar, brown sugar, garlic, salt, and black pepper in a medium bowl.
2. Combine greens, pears, cranberries, pecans, and Havarti cheese in a large bowl. Pour balsamic vinegar dressing over salad and toss. Garnish with almonds to serve.
