## Ingredients

- cooking spray
- 3 tablespoons water
- 1 tablespoon flax seed meal
- 1 cup tapioca flour
- ½ cup potato starch
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- 1 teaspoon salt
- ½ cup shredded mozzarella-style vegan cheese (such as Daiya®)
- ⅔ cup almond milk
- ⅓ cup olive oil

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
2. Grease a 24-cup mini muffin tin with cooking spray.
3. Stir water and flaxseed meal together in a small bowl.
4. Let stand until thickened, about 5 minutes.
5. Combine tapioca flour, potato starch, and baking powder in the bowl of a food processor; blend until well combined.
6. Add vegan Mozzarella cheese; mix well.
7. Mix in flaxseed mixture, almond milk, and olive oil, with the processor running, and blend until a smooth and soft batter forms.
8. Spoon batter into the prepared mini muffin tin, filling each 2/3 to the top.
9. Bake in the preheated oven until cheese breads are puffy and lightly golden, about 20 minutes.
