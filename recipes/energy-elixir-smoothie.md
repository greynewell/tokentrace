## Ingredients

- 1 cup spring salad greens, or to taste
- 1 cup frozen red grapes
- 1  chopped frozen banana
- 1  cored and chopped frozen pear
- 2 tablespoons walnuts
- water as needed

## Instructions

1. Layer salad greens, red grapes, banana, pear, and walnuts in a high-powered blender; add enough water to cover.
2. Blend mixture until smooth, adding more water to reach desired consistency.
