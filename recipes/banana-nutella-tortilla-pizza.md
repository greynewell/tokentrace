## Ingredients

- ½ cup chocolate-hazelnut spread (such as Nutella®), or to taste
- 1 large tortilla
- 1  banana, cut into 1/4-inch-thick slices

## Instructions

1. Spread chocolate-hazelnut spread onto 1 side of the tortilla and top with banana slices.
2. Place tortilla in a large skillet and cook over high heat until tortilla is crispy and sturdy, about 5 minutes.
3. Transfer "pizza" to a plate and cool, 3 to 4 minutes.
4. Cut into 4 slices.
