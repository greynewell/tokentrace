## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons walnuts
- 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
- 2 tablespoons chopped chives
- 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
- ½ cup blue cheese, crumbled

## Instructions

1. Place the walnuts, parsley, and chives in the bowl of a food processor, and process until finely chopped.
2. Place herb mixture into a small bowl, and stir in blue cheese.
3. Set aside.
4. Place the cream cheese in the bowl of a food processor, and process until smooth.
5. Add the herb mixture to the cream cheese, and process until thoroughly blended.
6. Serve at room temperature.
