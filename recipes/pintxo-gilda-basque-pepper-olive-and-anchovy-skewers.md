## Ingredients

- 12  green Manzanillo olives, pitted
- 12  short skewers
- 12  oil-packed anchovy fillets
- 12  pepperoncini

## Instructions

1. Thread 1 olive onto a skewer.
2. Thread the end of 1 anchovy fillet onto the skewer and add a piece of pepperoncini.
3. Thread the other end of the anchovy fillet onto the skewer, wrapping around the pepper.
4. Repeat with remaining olives, anchovies, and pepperoncini.
