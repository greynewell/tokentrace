## Ingredients

- 4 cups confectioners' sugar
- Â½ cup shortening
- 5 tablespoons milk
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- food coloring  (Optional)

## Instructions

1. Beat shortening in a large bowl until creamy. Gradually mix in confectioners' sugar in small amounts, alternating with 5 tablespoons milk, until smooth. Mix in vanilla and continue to beat until frosting is stiff and glossy, about 5 minutes, adding more milk if needed.
2. Mix in food coloring, separating into smaller bowls if using multiple colors.
3. Spread frosting on cookies or fill piping bags and decorate cookies as desired.
