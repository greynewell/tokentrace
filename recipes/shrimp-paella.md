## Ingredients

- ½ cup uncooked white rice
- 1 ½ cups water
- ½ teaspoon minced garlic
- 1 pound fresh shrimp, shelled and deveined without tails
- 1 (14.5 ounce) can chicken broth
- 1 (14.5 ounce) can peeled and diced tomatoes with juice
- 4  saffron threads

## Instructions

1. In a saucepan bring water to a boil.
2. Add rice and stir.
3. Reduce heat, cover and simmer for 20 minutes.
4. In a large skillet over medium heat, saute the garlic and shrimp for about 5 minutes or until pink.
5. Pour in broth, tomatoes and saffron; bring to a boil, stirring frequently.
6. Stir in 2 cups of the cooked rice and reduce heat to low.
7. Cook for an additional 5 minutes.
