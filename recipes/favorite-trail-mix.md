## Ingredients

- 1 cup sweetened dried cranberries
- ½ cup dried tart cherries
- ½ cup dried blueberries
- ½ cup dried pineapple pieces
- 1 cup candy-coated dark chocolate pieces (such as M&M's Dark Chocolate ®)
- 2 ½ cups salted deluxe mixed nuts (without peanuts)

## Instructions

1. Combine the cranberries, cherries, blueberries, pineapple, chocolate, and mixed nuts until evenly blended.
2. Store in an airtight container.
