## Ingredients

- 4 (4 ounce) cod fillets
- 1 pinch salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
- ½ cup warm water
- ½  fish bouillon cube
- ¾ cup dry bread crumbs
- ¼ cup butter, cut into small chunks
- 1 bunch fresh parsley, chopped
- 1 tablespoon chopped fresh dill
- 2 teaspoons dried tarragon

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Lay cod fillets in a baking dish and season lightly with salt and pepper.
2. Combine water and fish bouillon cube in a bowl. Stir to combine and pour over cod.
3. Mix bread crumbs, butter, parsley, dill, tarragon, salt, and pepper in a bowl with a fork until crumbly. Sprinkle evenly over cod.
4. Bake in the preheated oven until fish flakes easily with a fork and the crust is golden and crunchy, about 20 minutes.
