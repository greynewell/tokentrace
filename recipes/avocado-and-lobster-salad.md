## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons butter
- 1 ½ cups cooked lobster meat, diced
- 1 teaspoon seafood seasoning
- 1  tomato, diced
- ½  avocado, diced
- 2 cups chopped iceberg lettuce
- ¼ cup crumbled feta

## Instructions

1. Melt butter in a skillet over medium heat, add lobster, and gently reheat until hot.
2. Season with seafood seasoning, then transfer to a serving dish.
3. Toss with tomato, avocado, and lettuce.
4. Sprinkle with crumbled feta cheese.
