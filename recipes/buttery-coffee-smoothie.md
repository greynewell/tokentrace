## Ingredients

- 1 cup brewed coffee
- 2  large pasteurized egg yolks
- ¼ cup avocado
- ¼ cup ice cubes
- 1 tablespoon coconut sugar
- 2 tablespoons coconut oil, melted

## Instructions

1. Combine coffee, egg yolks, avocado, ice cubes, and coconut sugar in a blender; blend until smooth.
2. Add coconut oil and blend until smooth.
