## Ingredients

- 1 cup vegetable oil
- 1 pound chicken wings
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ½ teaspoon ground white pepper
- ½ cup butter
- ½ teaspoon hot pepper sauce
- 1 cup mayonnaise
- 4 tablespoons minced onion
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- ⅓ cup chopped fresh parsley
- ½ cup sour cream
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice
- 1 tablespoon distilled white vinegar
- ½ cup blue cheese, crumbled
- ⅛ teaspoon salt
- ⅛ teaspoon ground black pepper

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. In a large frying pan or deep fryer, heat oil to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
3. Fry wings for approximately 8 minutes.
4. Transfer wings to a paper towel and let drain.
5. Season with salt and white pepper.
6. After the wings have drained, place them in a large mixing bowl.
7. In a small saucepan, melt butter or margarine.
8. Stir in hot pepper sauce.
9. Pour mixture over chicken wings, toss to coat the wings.
10. Place wings in a baking dish.
11. Bake 15 to 20 minutes.
12. While the chicken wings are baking, make the blue cheese dressing: In a medium size mixing bowl, combine mayonnaise, onion, garlic, parsley, sour cream, lemon juice, vinegar and blue cheese.
13. Blend the mixture well.
14. Arrange the baked chicken wings on a serving platter.
15. Serve with blue cheese dressing.
