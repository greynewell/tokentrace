## Ingredients

- 1 head cauliflower, grated
- ¼  lemon, juiced
- 2 ½ tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
- 1 ½ tablespoons heavy whipping cream
- 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- ¼ teaspoon garlic powder
- ¼ teaspoon ground white pepper
- ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
- 1 packet zero-calorie sweetener
- 3 ounces cooked jumbo shrimp
- 1 tablespoon reduced-sodium soy sauce

## Instructions

1. Wring out excess moisture from cauliflower using a cheesecloth or towel to make 'rice.' Combine lemon juice, olive oil, cream, parsley, salt, garlic powder, white pepper, black pepper, and sugar substitute in a small bowl.
2. Heat a large nonstick saucepan over medium heat.
3. Add shrimp; cook for 1 minute.
4. Add cream mixture; cook until shrimp are lightly browned, 3 to 4 minutes more.
5. Transfer to a bowl.
6. Combine cauliflower rice and soy sauce in the same saucepan over medium heat.
7. Cook and stir until cauliflower is tender, 2 to 4 minutes.
8. Serve shrimp on top of the cauliflower rice.
