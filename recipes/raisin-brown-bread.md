## Ingredients

- 2 cups boiling water
- 1 cup rolled oats
- 2 tablespoons shortening
- 3 tablespoons brown sugar
- 4 teaspoons salt
- 1 cup molasses
- ½ cup warm water (110 degrees F/45 degrees C)
- 1 teaspoon white sugar
- 2 tablespoons active dry yeast
- 3 cups warm water (110 degrees F/45 degrees C)
- 12 cups bread flour
- 1 ½ cups raisins
- 1 ½ teaspoons ground cinnamon

## Instructions

1. Combine boiling water, oats, shortening, brown sugar, salt, and molasses.
2. Allow to cool.
3. Stir 1/2 cup warm water and 1 teaspoon white sugar until sugar is dissolved.
4. Sprinkle yeast over this mixture, and proof while oats are cooling.
5. Add 3 cups of warm water to the cooled oat mixture.
6. Stir in yeast.
7. Begin stirring in flour 1 cup at a time until it begins to get hard to stir.
8. Dredge raisins in flour, and mix with cinnamon; mix into the dough.
9. Turn onto a lightly floured surface, and knead in enough flour to make a soft but not sticky dough.
10. Cover.
11. Let rise for 1 to 2 hours in a warm place, or until dough doubles in size.
12. Divide dough into 5 parts.
13. Shape loaves, and place into greased 9 x 5 inch loaf pans.
14. Bake in a preheated 375 degree F (190 degree C) oven for 20 minutes.
15. Reduce heat to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C), and bake an additional 20 minutes.
16. Cool on wire racks.
