## Ingredients

- 3 cups biscuit baking mix
- 1 pound bulk pork sausage
- 4 cups shredded Cheddar cheese
- ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
- ½ cup milk
- 1 ½ teaspoons dried parsley

## Instructions

1. Preheat an oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Grease a broiler pan or other pan with rack.
3. Mix together the baking mix, sausage, Cheddar cheese, Parmesan cheese, milk, and parsley.
4. Shape the mix into 1-inch balls and place on the prepared pan.
5. Bake the sausage balls in the preheated oven until brown, about 25 minutes.
