## Ingredients

- 4 cups oil for frying
- 2  eggs
- ½ cup milk
- ½ cup honey
- 2 cups all-purpose flour
- ⅔ cup white sugar
- ⅓ cup ground cinnamon
- 4  skinless, boneless chicken breast halves - cut into bite-size pieces

## Instructions

1. Heat oil in skillet or fryer to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Arrange paper towels on a baking sheet.
3. In a medium bowl, beat together eggs, milk , and honey; set aside.
4. Combine flour, sugar, and cinnamon in a large resealable plastic bag; shake until blended.
5. Dip chicken in egg mixture, and then add to flour mixture.
6. Seal the bag, and shake to coat chicken with flour mixture.
7. Place 10 to 12 coated chicken nuggets into hot oil.
8. Cook till done, about 7 to 10 minutes (nuggets will be a dark bronze color).
9. Remove nuggets from oil, and drain well on paper towels.
10. Repeat till all nuggets are cooked.
