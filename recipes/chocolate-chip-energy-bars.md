## Ingredients

- 3 cups rolled oats
- 1 cup chopped walnuts
- 1 cup olive oil
- 1 cup chocolate chips
- ½ cup honey
- 6  eggs, beaten
- 4 teaspoons vanilla extract

## Instructions

1. Grease a 9x13-inch baking pan.
2. Mix oats, walnuts, olive oil, chocolate chips, honey, eggs, and vanilla extract together in a large bowl.
3. Press oat mixture firmly into the prepared baking pan.
4. Place pan in a cold oven.
5. Heat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) and bake energy bars until heated through, 25 minutes.
6. Cool completely before cutting into squares.
