## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons butter
- 3 (10 ounce) cans refrigerated biscuit dough
- 1 (16 ounce) jar salsa
- ½ cup chopped onions
- 2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese
- ½ cup sliced black olives

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Use the butter to grease a 9x13 inch baking dish.
3. Quarter biscuits.
4. Mix biscuits and salsa in a large bowl.
5. Pour into prepared dish; top with onions, cheese, and black olives.
6. Bake in the preheated oven for 35 to 40 minutes.
