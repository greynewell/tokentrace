## Ingredients

- ⅓ cup fresh lemon juice
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 1 tablespoon peeled and grated fresh ginger root
- 1 tablespoon minced garlic
- 1 tablespoon white sugar, or to taste
- 1 teaspoon ground coriander
- 2 cups water
- 1 cup bulgur
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 (10 ounce) package frozen lima beans
- ½ teaspoon garlic salt
- 1 cup seeded and chopped tomato
- 1 cup peeled, seeded, and chopped cucumber
- ¼ cup thinly sliced green onion
- ⅓ cup finely chopped fresh mint

## Instructions

1. Whisk lemon juice, olive oil, ginger, garlic, sugar, and coriander together in a bowl.
2. Combine water, bulgur, and salt in a saucepan; bring to a boil.
3. Cover saucepan, reduce heat, and simmer until water is absorbed, 15 to 20 minutes.
4. Remove from heat and fluff with a fork.
5. Bring a separate saucepan of water to a boil; add lima beans and garlic salt.
6. Cook until lima beans are tender, 5 to 7 minutes; drain.
7. Mix bulgur, lima beans, tomato, cucumber, green onion, and mint together in a large bowl; drizzle dressing over bulgur mixture.
8. Toss to coat.
9. Cover bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate to blend flavors, 2 hours to overnight.
