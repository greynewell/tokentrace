## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon ground black pepper
- 1 tablespoon paprika
- 2 teaspoons chili powder
- ½ teaspoon celery salt
- ½ teaspoon ground cayenne pepper
- ½ teaspoon garlic powder
- ¼ teaspoon mustard powder
- 1 (4 pound) rump roast
- ½ cup water

## Instructions

1. Mix pepper, paprika, chili powder, celery salt, cayenne pepper, garlic powder, and mustard powder together in a small bowl.
2. Rub seasoning mixture over surface of roast. Place roast into a slow cooker and pour in water.
3. Cover and cook on Low until meat is tender and well done, 8 to 10 hours.
4. Transfer roast to a serving platter. Skim fat from juices and strain. Serve juice with meat.
