## Ingredients

- 8  pork chops
- 1 cup cola-flavored carbonated beverage
- 1 cup ketchup
- 4 tablespoons brown sugar

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Place pork chops in a 9x13 inch baking dish.
3. In a small bowl, mix together the cola and ketchup.
4. Pour over chops and sprinkle with brown sugar.
5. Bake uncovered for about 1 hour, or until pork is cooked through and internal temperature has reached 145 degrees F (63 degrees C).
