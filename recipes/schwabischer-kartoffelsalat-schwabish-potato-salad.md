## Ingredients

- 5 pounds white potatoes with skin
- 2 cubes  chicken bouillon
- ¾ cup boiling water
- ¼ cup balsamic vinegar
- 1  hard-cooked egg, diced
- freshly ground black pepper to taste
- 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley
- 2 teaspoons mayonnaise
- 1 cup olive oil

## Instructions

1. Place the potatoes into a large pot, and fill with enough water to cover.
2. Bring to a boil, and cook for about 20 minutes, or until easily pierced with a fork.
3. Drain, and let them cool slightly.
4. If you wish to peel them, do it while they are still hot.
5. It will be much easier.
6. Allow to cool completely, then slice thinly.
7. Place the potatoes in a large bowl.
8. Dissolve the chicken bouillon in boiling water, and pour in with the potatoes.
9. Gently stir in the vinegar, egg, pepper, mayonnaise and parsley.
10. Mix in the olive oil last.
11. Let stand at room temperature for 15 minutes before serving.
12. This salad is best at room temperature.
