## Ingredients

- 1 cup all-purpose flour
- ⅔ cup brewed coffee
- ¼ cup powdered coffee creamer
- 1 tablespoon white sugar

## Instructions

1. Mix flour, coffee, creamer, and sugar together in a microwave-safe mug.
2. Microwave on high power until cake looks done in the middle, about 3 minutes. Let stand for 1 minute before serving.
