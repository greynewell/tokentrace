## Ingredients

- 3 whole lobster tails
- 4 pounds beef tenderloin filet
- 1 cup butter
- 2 teaspoons lemon juice
- 1 teaspoon garlic powder
- 3 cups white wine
- ¼ cup olive oil
- 1 tablespoon cracked black pepper
- 1 teaspoon garlic salt

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C).
2. Bring a large pot of  water to a boil.
3. Add lobster tails, and cook for 5 to 6 minutes.
4. Remove from water, rinse and remove shells.
5. Cut tail meat in half lengthwise.
6. Cut tenderloin lengthwise, to within 3/4 inch of the bottom.
7. Fill center of meat with lobster tails.
8. In a saucepan, melt butter with lemon juice, garlic powder and 1 cup of the wine.
9. Drizzle about 1/4 of the butter mixture over the lobster.
10. Tie the tenderloin with kitchen string.
11. Rub tenderloin with olive oil and place in a roasting pan.
12. Pour remaining wine in bottom of pan.
13. Sprinkle with cracked pepper.
14. Bake in preheated oven for 45 minutes to 1 hour.
15. Stir garlic salt into remaining butter mixture, and serve with slices of roast.
