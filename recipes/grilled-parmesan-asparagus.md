## Ingredients

- 1 pound fresh asparagus, trimmed
- 3 tablespoons olive oil
- ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese, or to taste
- salt to taste

## Instructions

1. Preheat an outdoor grill on medium heat, and lightly oil the grate.
2. Toss asparagus and olive oil in a bowl until evenly coated.
3. Sprinkle Parmesan cheese and salt onto asparagus until evenly distributed.
4. Place asparagus spears on grill crosswise.
5. Cook until tips are almost blackened, 20 minutes; turning spears over after 10 minutes.
