## Ingredients

- 1 pound spicy ground pork sausage
- 1  onion, diced
- 1 (2 ounce) package dried beef, shredded
- 1 (16 ounce) can sauerkraut, drained and minced
- 1 teaspoon prepared mustard
- 1 tablespoon dried parsley
- 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
- 1 teaspoon garlic salt
- 2 quarts oil for frying
- 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
- 1 ¼ cups milk
- 1  egg, lightly beaten
- 2 cups seasoned bread crumbs

## Instructions

1. Place spicy ground pork sausage, onion and dried beef in a large, deep skillet.
2. Cook over medium high heat until sausage is evenly brown and onions are soft, about 10 minutes.
3. Drain and transfer to a large bowl.
4. Mix sauerkraut, prepared mustard, parsley, cream cheese and garlic salt with the sausage mixture.
5. Cover and chill in the refrigerator approximately 2 hours.
6. Heat oil in deep-fryer to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
7. Place flour in a small bowl.
8. Blend milk and egg together in another small bowl.
9. Place seasoned bread crumbs in a medium bowl.
10. Roll the chilled sausage mixture into 1 inch balls.
11. Dredge sausage balls one at a time in flour, the egg and milk mixture and the bread crumbs.
12. In small batches deep fry sausage balls in the preheated oil until golden brown, about 3 minutes.
13. Drain on paper towels and serve warm, or freeze for later use.
