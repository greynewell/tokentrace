## Ingredients

- 5 pounds red potatoes, cut into chunks
- 1 tablespoon minced garlic, or to taste
- 3 cubes  chicken bouillon
- 1 (8 ounce) container sour cream
- 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
- ½ cup butter
- salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Place potatoes, bouillon, and garlic in a large pot and cover with water; bring to a boil. Reduce heat to medium-low and simmer until tender, about 15 minutes. Drain in a colander over a large bowl, reserving cooking water.
2. Mash potatoes with sour cream and cream cheese, adding reserved water as needed to reach desired consistency.
3. Transfer mashed potato mixture to a slow cooker, cover, and cook on Low for 2 to 3 hours. Just before serving, stir in butter and season with salt and pepper to taste.
