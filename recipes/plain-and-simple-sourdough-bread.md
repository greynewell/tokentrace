## Ingredients

- ¾ cup warm water (110 degrees F/45 degrees C)
- 1 cup sourdough starter
- 1 ½ teaspoons salt
- 2 ⅔ cups bread flour
- 1 ½ teaspoons active dry yeast

## Instructions

1. Add all ingredients in order suggested by your manufacturer.
2. Select white bread setting and push start.
