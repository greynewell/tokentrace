## Ingredients

- 2 (6 ounce) skinless salmon fillets, about 1-inch thick, cut into 2-inch strips
- ⅓ cup lemon juice
- ¼ cup white wine
- 1 tablespoon chopped fresh mint
- 1 tablespoon chopped fresh dill
- 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
- 2 tablespoons minced garlic
- 1 pinch crushed red pepper flakes
- ¼ cup olive oil

## Instructions

1. Preheat grill for medium-low heat.
2. Lie the salmon in the bottom of a baking dish.
3. Whisk together the lemon juice, wine, mint, dill, parsley, garlic, and red pepper flakes in a bowl.
4. Slowly drizzle in the olive oil while whisking vigorously.
5. Pour the mixture over the salmon.
6. Marinate the salmon in the refrigerator for no more than 30 minutes.
7. Thread the salmon onto metal or soaked wooden skewers lengthwise.
8. Cook on preheated grill until opaque in the center, about 4 minutes per side.
9. Serve immediately.
