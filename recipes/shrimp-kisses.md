## Ingredients

- 1 (8 ounce) package Monterey Jack cheese, cut into strips
- 40  large shrimp - peeled, deveined and butterflied
- 20 slices bacon, cut in half

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 450 degrees F (220 degrees C).
2. Place a small piece of cheese into the butterflied opening of each shrimp.
3. Wrap half of a slice of bacon around each one to conceal the cheese, securing with toothpicks.
4. Place on a cookie sheet.
5. Bake for 10 to 15 minutes in the preheated oven, until bacon is browned.
