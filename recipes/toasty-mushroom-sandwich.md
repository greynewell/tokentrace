## Ingredients

- 1  whole wheat sandwich roll
- 2 teaspoons butter
- 1 clove garlic, halved
- 1 slice Swiss cheese, or more to taste
- 1  portobello mushroom cap

## Instructions

1. Slice sandwich roll in half and place in the toaster oven for 5 to 6 minutes. Spread 1 teaspoon butter over each half. Scrape garlic over the bread to give it a garlic flavor.
2. Place Swiss cheese over mushroom cap and toast until cheese is melting, about 5 minutes. Sandwich between buttered bread halves.
