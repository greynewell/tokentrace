## Ingredients

- 3 tablespoons butter
- ¼ cup chopped onion
- 4 cloves garlic, coarsely chopped
- 1 (14.5 ounce) can chicken broth
- 2 tablespoons cornstarch
- ¼ cup Parmesan and Romano cheese blend (such as Sargento's®), divided
- 1 pound large shrimp - peeled, deveined, and tails removed
- ¼ cup chopped sun-dried tomatoes
- ¼ cup chopped fresh parsley
- 3 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
- 1 (9 ounce) package fresh fettuccine (such as Buitoni®)

## Instructions

1. Melt butter in a large skillet over medium heat.
2. Add onion and garlic.
3. Cook until onion is tender, about 5 minutes.
4. Add chicken broth and cornstarch; mix until smooth.
5. Cook until sauce is thickened, about 5 minutes more.
6. Add 2 tablespoons Parmesan-Romano cheese blend, stirring until smooth.
7. Add shrimp, sun-dried tomatoes, parsley, and lemon juice.
8. Cook until shrimp turns pink, about 5 minutes more.
9. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
10. Cook fettuccine in the boiling water until tender yet firm to the bite, 1 to 3 minutes; drain.
11. Toss pasta with shrimp mixture.
12. Garnish with remaining Parmesan and Romano cheese blend.
