## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons barbeque seasoning (such as KC Masterpiece®), or to taste
- 2 pounds beef tips, trimmed
- 1 pound bacon, strips cut in half
- toothpicks

## Instructions

1. Rub barbeque seasoning into beef tips until meat has an orange hue, 3 to 5 minutes. Cut beef into 1-inch cubes.
2. Wrap each beef cube in a bacon slice and secure bacon slice with a toothpick.
3. Heat a skillet over medium-heat. Working in batches, cook wrapped beef tips in hot skillet until beef is browned all over and bacon is crispy, 5 to 10 minutes.
