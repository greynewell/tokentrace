## Ingredients

- 1 (15 ounce) can peas, drained
- ½ cup sweet pickles, halved and sliced
- ½ cup roasted Spanish peanuts
- ¼ cup creamy salad dressing (such as Miracle Whip®), or to taste

## Instructions

1. Lightly mix peas, sweet pickles, peanuts, and creamy salad dressing in a bowl.
2. Chill until cold, about 45 minutes.
