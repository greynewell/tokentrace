## Ingredients

- ½ cup mayonnaise
- 2 ½ tablespoons distilled white vinegar
- 1  apple - peeled, cored, and chopped
- 1 large stalk celery, diced
- ½ cup raisins
- ½ cup chopped walnuts
- salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Whisk together mayonnaise and vinegar in a mixing bowl.
2. Add apples, celery, raisins, and walnuts to the bowl. Season with salt and pepper; mix well.
3. Place in the refrigerator until chilled, about 30 minutes.
