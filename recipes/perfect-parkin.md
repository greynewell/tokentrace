## Ingredients

- ½ cup butter
- ⅔ cup packed dark brown sugar
- ½ cup golden syrup (such as Lyle's®)
- 1 tablespoon golden syrup (such as Lyle's®)
- ⅓ cup molasses
- 1 ½ cups self-rising flour
- ½ teaspoon baking soda
- 1 cup quick cooking oats
- 1  large egg
- ½ cup whole milk

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 300 degrees F (150 degrees C).
2. Grease a 9-inch square baking pan and line with parchment paper.
3. Stir together butter, sugar, 1/2 cup plus 1 tablespoon golden syrup, and molasses in a saucepan and cook over low heat, stirring constantly, until butter is melted and mixture is smooth, 4 to 5 minutes.
4. Remove from heat and let mixture cool 5 minutes.
5. Whisk flour, baking soda, and oats together in a large bowl.
6. Stir in butter mixture.
7. Add egg, then milk, and stir until well combined.
8. Pour batter into prepared pan and spread evenly.
9. Bake until a toothpick inserted into center comes out clean, about 1 hour 20 minutes.
10. Remove from oven and let cool completely in pan, about 1 1/2 hours.
11. Cover pan tightly with foil or plastic wrap, without letting it touch surface of cake, and let stand at room temperature for at least 1 day and up to 5.
12. Cut into squares and serve.
