## Ingredients

- cooking spray
- 8 teaspoons plain bread crumbs
- 4 teaspoons chopped almonds
- 2 ¼ teaspoons chopped fresh thyme  (Optional)
- ⅛ teaspoon ground black pepper
- ½ pound trout fillets
- 2 tablespoons milk
- 2  thyme sprigs for garnish  (Optional)

## Instructions

1. Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat, and coat a grill basket with cooking spray.
2. Mix bread crumbs, almonds, chopped thyme, and black pepper together in a shallow dish.
3. Dip each trout fillet in milk, then dredge each in the bread crumb mixture.
4. Coat each fillet with cooking spray.
5. Place dredged fillets in prepared grill basket and cook until golden and fish flakes easily with a fork, about 5 minutes on each side.
6. Garnish with thyme sprigs.
