## Ingredients

- 2  Granny Smith apples, thinly sliced
- ½  lemon, juiced
- 6 cups thinly sliced kale
- 4 slices cooked bacon, crumbled
- ½  avocado, thinly sliced
- ¼ cup chopped almonds
- ½ cup olive oil
- ¼ cup apple cider vinegar
- 2 tablespoons maple syrup
- 2 teaspoons grainy mustard
- 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
- ½ teaspoon kosher salt

## Instructions

1. Toss apples with lemon juice in a large bowl to coat.
2. Add kale, bacon, avocado, and almonds; toss to mix.
3. Whisk olive oil, vinegar, maple syrup, mustard, pepper, and salt together in a bowl until smooth; drizzle over salad and toss to coat.
