## Ingredients

- 4  potatoes, or more to taste
- 4  carrots, or more to taste
- 3  beets, or more to taste
- ½ cup finely chopped onion
- 2 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
- ½ cup frozen peas
- 1 tablespoon sunflower seed oil
- 1 tablespoon dill
- ½ teaspoon sea salt, or to taste
- ¼ teaspoon freshly ground black pepper, or to taste

## Instructions

1. Place potatoes, carrots, and beets into a large pot and cover with water; bring to a boil and turn off heat.
2. Allow pot to sit on burner until potato mixture cools, 1 to 2 hours.
3. Place onion in a large bowl and cover with vinegar; set aside.
4. Peel and chop potatoes, carrots, and beets; add to onion mixture.
5. Mix peas, sunflower seed oil, dill, sea salt, and black pepper into potato mixture and stir well.
6. Cover bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate, 4 hours to overnight.
