## Ingredients

- 1 cup olive oil
- 1 cup fresh basil, chopped
- 3 teaspoons minced garlic
- 1 (8 ounce) loaf Calabrese bread (crusty Italian bread), cut into 1/2-inch slices
- salt and coarsely ground black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Mix oil, basil, and garlic in a bowl.
2. Preheat an outdoor grill for medium heat.
3. Cook bread slices on the grill until underside is toasted, 2 to 3 minutes.
4. Flip bread over and baste the grilled sides with about 1/2 of the oil mixture.
5. Grind salt and pepper on top.
6. Grill until bottoms are toasted, 1 to 2 minutes.
7. Flip bread slices over once more; baste with remaining oil mixture.
8. Season with salt and pepper and remove from heat.
