## Ingredients

- 3  andouille sausage links
- 8 ½ cups chicken stock
- 8 ounces dry great Northern beans
- 1  bay leaf
- 1 pinch crushed saffron threads
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 1  onion, finely chopped
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 1  red bell pepper, finely chopped
- 1 tablespoon sweet paprika
- 12 ounces kale, stems removed and leaves coarsely chopped

## Instructions

1. Bring 2 cups of water to a boil in a large skillet.
2. Add sausages, and cook over medium heat for 7 minutes on each side.
3. Remove from the pan, dice, and set aside.
4. In a large pot, combine the chicken stock and dry beans.
5. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat to low.
6. Add the bay leaf and a pinch of salt.
7. Simmer, partially covered, for 2 hours.
8. Discard bay leaf.
9. Remove 1/2 cup of the stock to a small bowl.
10. Stir in saffron, and set aside to soak.
11. Heat the oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
12. Add the sausage, and brown for about 5 minutes.
13. Remove from the pan, and reduce the heat to low.
14. Add the onion, and cook for about 5 minutes, then stir in the garlic and cook for another minute.
15. Mix in the red bell pepper and paprika.
16. Cook for a few minutes before adding the mixture to the soup pot along with the sausage.
17. Bring the soup to a low boil, and stir in the kale.
18. Simmer for 10 minutes, until kale is tender.
19. Serve hot.
