## Ingredients

- ⅓ cup apple juice
- ⅓ cup maple syrup
- 3 tablespoons soy sauce
- 2 tablespoons finely chopped onion
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 4 (8 ounce) salmon fillets

## Instructions

1. In a large resealable bag, combine the apple juice, maple syrup, soy sauce, onion and garlic.
2. Remove 1/2 cup of the mixture and set aside in a separate bowl for basting.
3. Place the salmon fillets in the bag and seal.
4. Refrigerate, turning occasionally for 3 hours to marinate.
5. Preheat a grill for high heat.
6. Lightly oil the grate.
7. Place fish fillets on the grill and discard the bag with the marinade.
8. Grill for 5 to 10 minutes per side depending on thickness, basting occasionally with the reserved marinade.
