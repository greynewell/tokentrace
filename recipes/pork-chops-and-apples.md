## Ingredients

- 5  pork chops
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- ¼ cup all-purpose flour
- 1 teaspoon ground cumin
- 1 tablespoon oil, or as needed
- 2  apples - peeled, cored, and thinly sliced
- 1 cup chicken broth
- 4 tablespoons red wine vinegar
- 2 tablespoons honey
- 2 tablespoons ketchup
- 2 tablespoons finely chopped shallot

## Instructions

1. Season pork chops with salt and pepper.
2. Dredge in flour and cumin and shake off excess.
3. Heat oil in a skillet over medium-high heat.
4. Saute pork chops until browned, about 5 minutes per side.
5. Transfer to a plate.
6. Add apples; cook and stir just until tender, about 2 minutes.
7. Transfer to a plate.
8. Add broth, vinegar, honey, ketchup, and shallot to the skillet and cook for 1 minute.
9. Return pork and apples to the skillet, cover, and cook until pork is only slightly pink in the center, about 10 minutes more.
