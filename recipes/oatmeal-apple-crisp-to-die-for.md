## Ingredients

- cooking spray
- 1 cup brown sugar
- 1 cup rolled oats
- 1 cup all-purpose flour
- ¾ cup melted butter
- 4  Red Delicious apples - peeled, cored, and thinly sliced
- ¾ cup white sugar
- 3 tablespoons ground cinnamon

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Spray an 8-inch square baking dish with cooking spray.
2. Combine brown sugar, oats, flour, and butter together in a bowl until crumbly. Spread 1/2 of the oat mixture into the prepared baking dish and press firmly to form a crust. Arrange apple slices over the crust.
3. Mix white sugar and cinnamon together in a bowl; sprinkle over apples. Crumble remaining oat mixture over apples.
4. Bake in the preheated oven until topping is golden brown, 40 to 45 minutes.
