## Ingredients

- 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese
- 1 (14 ounce) can sweetened condensed milk
- ⅓ cup lemon juice
- 2 cups cherry pie filling
- 1 (9 inch) prepared graham cracker crust

## Instructions

1. Cream together condensed milk and cream cheese in a mixing bowl until smooth. Add lemon juice and blend well.
2. Pour cream cheese mixture into graham cracker crust, then top with cherry pie filling.
3. Chill in the refrigerator for 2 hours before serving.
