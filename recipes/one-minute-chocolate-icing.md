## Ingredients

- 1 cup white sugar
- ½ cup unsweetened cocoa powder
- ¼ cup milk
- ¼ cup butter
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

## Instructions

1. Combine sugar, cocoa powder, milk, butter, and vanilla in a saucepan. Bring to a rolling boil, stirring constantly; cook for 1 minute. Remove from heat.
2. Beat icing with an electric mixer until mixture cools and thickens to spreading consistency, about 3 minutes.
