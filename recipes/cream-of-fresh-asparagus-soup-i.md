## Ingredients

- 1 ½ pounds fresh asparagus
- 1 ½ cups chopped onion
- 6 tablespoons butter
- 1 pinch salt
- 6 tablespoons all-purpose flour
- 2 cups water
- 4 cups hot milk
- 1 teaspoon dried dill weed
- 1 teaspoon salt
- ½ teaspoon ground white pepper
- 2 tablespoons tamari

## Instructions

1. Break off and discard tough asparagus bottoms.
2. Break off tips; set aside.
3. Coarsely chop stalks.
4. Cook in skillet over medium heat with onion in butter, salting lightly, for 8 to 10 minutes.
5. When onions are clear, sprinkle with flour.
6. Continue to stir over lowest possible heat 5 to 8 minutes.
7. Slowly add water or stock, stirring constantly.
8. Cook 8 to 10 minutes, stirring frequently, until thickened.
9. Cool slightly.
10. In blender, puree sauce bit-by-bit with milk until thoroughly smooth.
11. Return puree to 3-quart pan, preferably a double boiler.
12. Add dill, 1 teaspoon salt, pepper, and tamari.
13. Heat gently but don't boil.
14. As soup heats, cook asparagus tips in boiling water until tender, but still very green, about 2 minutes; drain.
15. Add whole pieces to soup.
