## Ingredients

- 2 pounds lean ground beef
- 1 (10.5 ounce) can condensed French onion soup
- ½ cup fine dry bread crumbs
- ½ cup rolled oats
- 1  egg, lightly beaten
- 1 tablespoon dried parsley
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Combine ground beef, condensed French onion soup, bread crumbs, oats, beaten egg, dried parsley, salt, and pepper in a bowl. Mix well. Pack into a loaf pan.
3. Bake in the preheated oven until no longer pink in the center, about 1 hour and 15 minutes. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 160 degrees F (70 degrees C). Let rest 5 minutes before cutting.
