## Ingredients

- 2 ½ cups cooked quinoa (at room temperature)
- 4  eggs, beaten
- 1  sweet yellow onion, finely chopped
- ⅓ cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese
- 3 cloves garlic, finely chopped
- ½ teaspoon fine sea salt
- 1 cup whole-grain bread crumbs
- 1 teaspoon butter, or as needed

## Instructions

1. Mix quinoa, eggs, yellow onion, Parmesan cheese, garlic, and sea salt in a bowl; add bread crumbs and stir.
2. Form into 9 small patties.
3. Melt butter in a flat skillet over-medium high heat.
4. Cook the patties in the hot butter until crispy, 8 to 10 minutes per side.
