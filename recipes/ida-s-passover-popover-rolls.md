## Ingredients

- 1 ½ cups water
- ½ cup vegetable oil
- 1 pinch white sugar
- 1 pinch salt
- 2 cups matzo meal
- 6  eggs

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
2. Bring water, oil, sugar, and salt to a boil in a saucepan.
3. Remove from heat and quickly stir in matzo meal.
4. Beat eggs one at a time into matzo mixture until blended.
5. With wet hands, roll dough into 18 rolls and arrange on a baking sheet.
6. Cut an 'X' into each roll.
7. Bake in the preheated oven until golden brown, 30 to 45 minutes.
