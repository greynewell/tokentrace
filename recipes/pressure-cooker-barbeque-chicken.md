## Ingredients

- 2 pounds boneless, skinless chicken thighs
- 1 teaspoon ground paprika
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- 1  onion, minced
- ½ cup chile sauce
- ½ cup water
- 2 tablespoons vinegar

## Instructions

1. Heat pressure cooker over medium heat; cook chicken until browned, 2 to 3 minutes per side. Sprinkle paprika, salt, and pepper onto chicken.
2. Combine onion, chile sauce, water, and vinegar in a bowl; pour over chicken.
3. Cover pressure cooker with lid and cook according to manufacturer's instructions, about 15 minutes. Remove from heat and release pressure through natural release method; open pressure cooker carefully.
