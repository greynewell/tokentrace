## Ingredients

- 1 (8 ounce) package brick cream cheese, softened
- 36  OREO Cookies, finely crushed
- 3 (4 ounce) packages white baking chocolate, melted
- 1 teaspoon black decorating gel
- 2 teaspoons orange decorating icing

## Instructions

1. Mix cream cheese and cookie crumbs until blended.
2. Shape into 48 (1-inch) balls.
3. Freeze 10 min.
4. Dip balls in melted chocolate; place in single layer in shallow waxed paper-lined pan.
5. Decorate with remaining ingredients to resemble snowmen.
6. Refrigerate 1 hour or until firm.
7. Keep refrigerated.
