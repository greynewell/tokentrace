## Ingredients

- 20  green grapes
- 1 tablespoon cream cheese, softened
- 10  miniature semisweet chocolate chips
- skewers

## Instructions

1. Pick out the 5 nicest looking grapes.
2. Place 2 small dollops of cream cheese on each one for the eyes and stick in mini chocolate chips for the pupils.
3. Thread 3 to 4 grapes, depending on length of your skewers, lengthwise onto a skewer, followed by the grape with the eyes horizontally.
4. Repeat with the other grapes.
