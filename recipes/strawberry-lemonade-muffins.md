## Ingredients

- 2 cups chopped fresh strawberries
- 4 teaspoons lemon extract
- 1 teaspoon white sugar
- 2 ¾ cups all-purpose flour
- ½ cup light brown sugar
- ½ cup white sugar
- 1 tablespoon baking soda
- ¾ teaspoon salt
- ¾ cup milk
- ½ cup vegetable oil
- 2  eggs

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
2. Line 24 muffin cups with paper muffin liners.
3. Stir strawberries, lemon extract, and 1 teaspoon white sugar together in a bowl.
4. Whisk flour, brown sugar, 1/2 cup white sugar, baking soda, and salt together in a large bowl.
5. Pour milk, oil, and eggs into flour mixture and mix until batter is well-combined.
6. Stir strawberry mixture into batter.
7. Spoon batter into prepared muffin cups.
8. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center of a muffin comes out clean, about 25 minutes.
9. Transfer muffins to a wire rack to cool completely.
