## Ingredients

- 1 cup pecan halves or pieces
- 1 cup honey
- ¾ cup butter
- ¼ cup vegetable oil
- 4 (4 ounce) fillets salmon
- salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Spread pecans in a single layer on a baking sheet.
3. Bake 8 to 10 minutes, or until slightly browned.
4. Stir 2 or 3 times to toast uniformly.
5. In a small saucepan over medium-high heat, combine honey with half of the butter.
6. Stir in the toasted pecans, and cook for 8 to 10 minutes to infuse the flavors.
7. Remove from heat, and keep warm.
8. Melt remaining butter with oil in a large skillet over high heat.
9. Season fish with salt and pepper.
10. Arrange fillets in skillet, and cook until firm to the touch, about 4 to 5 minutes per side.
11. Salmon is done when it flakes easily with a fork.
12. Transfer to serving plate, and spoon glaze over top.
