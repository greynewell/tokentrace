## Ingredients

- 1  egg
- 18 large prawns, peeled and deveined, tails left on
- 1 (10 ounce) bag nacho-cheese flavored corn chips, finely crushed

## Instructions

1. Rinse prawns and pat dry.
2. Whisk egg in a bowl.
3. Place crushed chips in another bowl.
4. Dip a prawn in the whisked egg and then in the crushed chips; place on a plate.
5. Repeat with remaining prawns.
6. Preheat an air fryer to 350 degrees F (180 degrees C).
7. Place prawns in the air fryer and set the timer for 8 minutes.
8. Cook until prawns are opaque.
