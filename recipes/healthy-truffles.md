## Ingredients

- ½ cup smooth peanut butter
- ¼ cup cocoa powder
- ¼ cup honey
- ¼ cup coconut oil
- 1 teaspoon butter  (Optional)
- 1 teaspoon vanilla bean paste
- ¼ cup unsweetened coconut flakes, or more as needed

## Instructions

1. Combine peanut butter, cocoa powder, honey, coconut oil, butter, and vanilla bean paste in a saucepan over low heat; cook, stirring constantly, until melted and smooth, 3 to 5 minutes.
2. Remove saucepan from heat and transfer peanut butter mixture to a bowl.
3. Refrigerate mixture, stirring every 5 minutes, until truffle mixture is firm yet sticky and soft enough to shape, about 15 minutes.
4. Pour coconut flakes into a bowl.
5. Drop truffle mixture, about 1 teaspoon per truffle, into coconut and roll to coat.
6. Refrigerate until firm, about 15 minutes.
