## Ingredients

- Canola cooking spray
- 2  egg whites, beaten
- 1 pound large shrimp, uncooked
- ½ cup Silk® Coconutmilk Original
- ½ cup panko bread crumbs
- ½ cup sweetened flaked coconut
- ½ cup orange marmalade
- 2 tablespoons stone-ground mustard
- 1 teaspoon olive oil
- 1 teaspoon prepared horseradish
- Salt and pepper, to taste

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees F.
2. Lightly spray a baking sheet with canola oil.
3. Peel and devein shrimp.
4. Using a small, sharp knife, butterfly shrimp by making an incision head to tail down back of shrimp and opening slightly, like a book.
5. Beat egg whites and Silk in a shallow bowl.
6. Combine panko and coconut in another shallow bowl.
7. Dip shrimp in coconut/egg mixture then into crumb mixture, pressing to coat.
8. Place shrimp on baking sheet.
9. Lightly spray shrimp with oil and bake 5-7 minutes per side until lightly golden brown and cooked through.
10. To serve, combine dipping sauce ingredients in a small bowl and serve with warm shrimp.
