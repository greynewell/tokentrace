## Ingredients

- ¼ cup butter
- 3 ounces thinly sliced prosciutto, chopped
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- ½ cup pine nuts
- ⅓ cup fresh lemon juice
- 2 pounds fresh asparagus, trimmed
- salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Melt butter in a skillet over medium heat; cook and stir the prosciutto, garlic, and pine nuts in the melted butter until the garlic is fragrant and the nuts begin to turn brown, about 2 minutes.
2. Stir in the lemon juice.
3. Bring a pot filled with lightly salted water to boil.
4. Gently boil the asparagus until the bright green and barely tender, about 3 minutes; drain.
5. Add the asparagus to the prosciutto mixture and toss to mix evenly.
6. Season with salt and pepper; serve hot.
