## Ingredients

- 1 ½ teaspoons egg replacer (dry)
- 2 tablespoons water
- 1 cup yellow cornmeal
- ½ cup all-purpose flour
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 2 tablespoons white sugar
- 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
- 1 cup water
- ½ teaspoon salt

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C).
2. Grease six muffin cups or line with paper muffin liners.
3. In a small bowl, beat together egg replacer and water.
4. In a separate bowl, combine cornmeal, flour, baking powder, sugar and salt.
5. Add egg mixture, oil and water; stir until smooth.
6. Spoon batter into prepared muffin tins, filling each about halfway.
7. Bake in pre-heated oven for 10 to 15 minutes, until a toothpick inserted into the center of a muffin comes out clean.
