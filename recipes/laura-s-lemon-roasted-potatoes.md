## Ingredients

- ¼ cup canola oil
- ½ cup butter, melted
- 2 tablespoons lemon juice
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tablespoon dried dill weed
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
- 5 large potatoes, peeled and cubed

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
2. In a large bowl, stir together canola oil, melted butter, lemon juice, garlic, dill, salt, and pepper. Add potatoes and toss to coat. Spread the potatoes out on a baking sheet and drizzle any remaining liquid from the bowl over them.
3. Bake in the preheated oven until potatoes are brown and crispy, about 45 minutes.
