## Ingredients

- 1 cup warm water
- 5 tablespoons white sugar
- 3 cups all-purpose flour
- ¾ teaspoon salt
- 2 tablespoons dry milk powder
- 2 tablespoons dry potato flakes
- 1 tablespoon active dry yeast
- ¼ teaspoon vanilla extract
- ¼ teaspoon lemon extract
- 2  eggs
- 4 tablespoons margarine

## Instructions

1. Measure ingredients into the bread machine in the order suggested by the manufacturer.
2. Set machine for dough.
3. Place dough in a 9x5 inch loaf pan.
4. Allow to rise until doubled.
5. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
6. Bake in the preheated oven for about 30 minutes.
7. Crust should be golden and bread should sound hollow when thumped.
