## Ingredients

- 4  cooked lobster tails, chopped into bite-sized pieces
- ½ cup mayonnaise
- 2 stalks celery, diced
- 3 tablespoons freshly squeezed lemon juice
- 2 tablespoons Dijon mustard
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- ¼ cup butter, or to taste
- 4  white hot dog buns (such as Wonder®)
- 2 cups plain potato chips
- 8 large dill pickles, thinly sliced
- ¼ cup thinly sliced chives, or as needed
- 1 tablespoon parsley, or to taste

## Instructions

1. Combine lobster meat, mayonnaise, celery, lemon juice, mustard, salt, and black pepper in a bowl.
2. Refrigerate until needed.
3. Preheat the oven to 250 degrees F (120 degrees C).
4. Butter hot dog buns evenly and toast in the preheated oven until lightly golden, 3 to 5 minutes.
5. Fill toasted buns evenly with lobster mixture.
6. Garnish with potato chips, pickles, chives, and parsley.
