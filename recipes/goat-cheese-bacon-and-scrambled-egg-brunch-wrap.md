## Ingredients

- 2 slices turkey bacon
- 1  cooking spray
- 1 large egg
- 1 (6 inch) flour tortilla
- 1 ounce honey-flavored goat cheese
- 1 tablespoon chopped pitted Kalamata olives
- 4 leaves baby spinach

## Instructions

1. Place bacon in a large skillet and cook over medium-high heat, turning occasionally, until evenly browned, 5 to 8 minutes. Drain on paper towels.
2. While bacon cooks, spray a small skillet with cooking spray; warm over medium-high heat. Whisk egg in a bowl. Pour into the skillet; cook, stirring frequently, until scrambled and set, about 5 minutes.
3. Heat tortilla in a microwave until soft and pliable, about 15 seconds.
4. Spread goat cheese evenly over the tortilla. Layer olives, 1 slice of bacon, egg, spinach, and remaining bacon slice in the center of the tortilla. Wrap up and serve.
