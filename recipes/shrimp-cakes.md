## Ingredients

- 1 pound shrimp - peeled, deveined and chopped
- 1 ¼ cups coarsely crushed buttery round crackers
- ¼ cup chopped onion
- 2  jalapeno peppers, seeded and chopped
- 1 cup mayonnaise
- 1 tablespoon prepared yellow mustard
- 10 dashes hot pepper sauce (e.g. Tabasco™)
- celery salt to taste
- 1 tablespoon Parmesan cheese
- 1 quart oil for frying

## Instructions

1. In a medium bowl, combine the shrimp, crackers, onion and jalapeno.
2. Stir in the mayonnaise and mustard, and season with Tabasco and celery salt.
3. Mix in Parmesan cheese.
4. Form into 8 patties.
5. Heat about 1/2 inch of oil in a deep heavy skillet over medium-high heat.
6. When oil is nice and hot, fry the patties for about 5 minutes per side, or until golden brown.
