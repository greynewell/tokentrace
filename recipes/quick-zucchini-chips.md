## Ingredients

- 1  zucchini, thinly sliced into rounds
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- sea salt to taste
- ground black pepper to taste
- 1 pinch paprika, or more to taste

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C).
2. Toss zucchini, olive oil, salt, and pepper together in a bowl.
3. Turn zucchini slices out onto a baking sheet.
4. Sprinkle paprika over the top of the slices.
5. Bake in the preheated oven until golden and crisp, 25 to 30 minutes.
