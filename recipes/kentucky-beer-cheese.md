## Ingredients

- 1 (8 ounce) package sharp Cheddar cheese, cut into chunks
- ¼ teaspoon garlic powder
- ¼ teaspoon cayenne pepper
- ¼ teaspoon hot sauce
- ¼ cup warm beer

## Instructions

1. Process Cheddar cheese chunks in the bowl of a food processor until crumbly. Add garlic powder, cayenne, and hot sauce. Begin pouring in the beer a little at a time, mixing until desired consistency has been reached.
2. Refrigerate for at least 30 minutes before serving.
