## Ingredients

- 1 (5 ounce) can tuna in water (such as StarKist® Chunk Light), drained
- ¼ cup Italian-style salad dressing
- 2 teaspoons prepared yellow mustard
- 1 teaspoon tomato relish
- 1 teaspoon celery seed
- 1 teaspoon onion powder
- salt and ground black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Mix tuna, salad dressing, mustard, relish, celery seed, onion powder, salt, and pepper together in a bowl.
2. Place in the refrigerator for 1 hour.
