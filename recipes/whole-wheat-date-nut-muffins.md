## Ingredients

- 1  serving cooking spray  (Optional)
- 1 cup buttermilk
- ⅓ cup honey
- ½  banana, mashed
- ¼ cup canola oil
- 1  egg
- 2 tablespoons egg white
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 2 ½ cups whole wheat flour
- 1 tablespoon baking powder
- 1 tablespoon flax seeds, or more to taste
- ½ teaspoon baking soda
- ½ teaspoon ground cinnamon, or more to taste
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- 18  dates, pitted and chopped, or more taste
- ½ cup chopped walnuts

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
2. Grease muffin tin with cooking spray or line with paper liners.
3. Beat buttermilk, honey, mashed banana, canola oil, egg, egg white, and vanilla extract together in a bowl.
4. Combine whole wheat flour, baking powder, flax seeds, baking soda, cinnamon, and salt in a large bowl.
5. Mix in dates and walnuts.
6. Pour in buttermilk mixture; mix batter until blended.
7. Scoop batter into the prepared muffin tin.
8. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean, 20 to 25 minutes.
9. Transfer to a wire rack to cool, about 15 minutes.
