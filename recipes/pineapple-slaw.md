## Ingredients

- 1 medium head green cabbage, chopped
- 1  fresh pineapple - peeled, cored, and cut into small pieces
- 4  green onions, chopped
- 1  bell pepper, chopped
- ⅓ cup white vinegar
- ¼ cup mayonnaise
- 1 tablespoon white sugar
- 1 teaspoon salt
- ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper

## Instructions

1. Mix cabbage, pineapple, green onions, and bell pepper together in a large bowl.
2. Whisk vinegar, mayonnaise, sugar, salt, and pepper together in a separate bowl; drizzle over cabbage mixture and toss to coat.
3. Cover bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate at least 2 hours before serving.
