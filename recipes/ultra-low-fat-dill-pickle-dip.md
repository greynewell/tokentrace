## Ingredients

- 1 (8 ounce) package 95% fat-free cream cheese
- 2 tablespoons dill pickle juice, or more to taste
- 2 tablespoons finely chopped dill pickles
- ½ teaspoon minced onion
- 1 dash salt

## Instructions

1. Mix cream cheese and pickle juice together in a bowl until smooth.
2. Add dill pickles, onion, and salt; mix well.
3. Chill until flavors blend, at least 1 hour.
