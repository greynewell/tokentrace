## Ingredients

- 2  soft-shell crabs, cleaned
- ¼ cup cheriyaki glaze
- 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
- 1 tablespoon mirin
- 1 tablespoon soy sauce

## Instructions

1. In a deep skillet, mix together the cheriyaki glaze, Worcestershire sauce, mirin and soy sauce.
2. Bring to a boil over medium heat.
3. Place the crabs side by side in the sauce, with the top shell down.
4. Cook for about 5 minutes, then turn and cook for 5 more minutes.
5. Remove to serving plates, and serve with plenty of napkins!
