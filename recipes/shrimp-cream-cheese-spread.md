## Ingredients

- 2 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened
- 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
- 1 teaspoon hot sauce
- 1 (4 ounce) can small shrimp, drained
- 2  green onions, finely chopped
- ½ (12 ounce) jar cocktail sauce

## Instructions

1. In a medium bowl, beat together the cream cheese, lemon juice and hot sauce until well blended and fluffy.
2. Mix in the shrimp and green onions.
3. Form into a mound on a medium serving platter.
4. Cover and chill in the refrigerator 1 hour, or until firm.
5. Top with cocktail sauce before serving.
