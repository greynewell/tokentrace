## Ingredients

- 6  eggs, well-beaten
- 1 teaspoon salt
- ¼ teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
- 1 tablespoon cooking oil, divided, or as needed
- 2 cups bean sprouts
- 1 cup cooked shrimp, finely chopped
- 6  green onions, chopped
- 1 rib celery, thinly sliced
- 1 slice fresh ginger root, minced

## Instructions

1. Combine eggs, salt, and pepper together in a bowl.
2. Heat a few drops of oil in a skillet over medium-high heat.
3. Add bean sprouts, shrimp, green onion, celery, and ginger; cook and stir until celery is translucent but still crisp, 3 to 4 minutes.
4. Remove from heat, cool slightly, and stir into egg mixture.
5. Heat remaining oil in skillet over medium heat.
6. Pour 1/4 egg-shrimp mixture into skillet; tilt pan to spread mixture evenly.
7. Cook until golden brown, 1 to 3 minutes per side.
8. Repeat with remaining egg-shrimp mixture.
