## Ingredients

- 8 jumbo shrimp, peeled and deveined with tails left on
- ½ teaspoon steak seasoning
- 4 strips thin sliced bacon, cut in half

## Instructions

1. Preheat an air fryer to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
2. Place shrimp in a bowl, add steak seasoning, and toss to coat.
3. Starting just above the tail, wrap half a piece of bacon around each shrimp. Place in the basket of the air fryer seam-side down.
4. Cook for 5 minutes. Flip and cook for 5 minutes more.
