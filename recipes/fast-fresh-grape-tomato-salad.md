## Ingredients

- 2 cups halved grape tomatoes
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 1 tablespoon sweetened rice vinegar
- ¼ teaspoon garlic powder
- ¼ teaspoon dried oregano, or to taste
- 1 pinch salt to taste

## Instructions

1. Mix tomatoes, olive oil, rice vinegar, and garlic powder together in a bowl.
2. Crumble oregano between fingers to release flavor and add to the tomatoes; stir to coat.
3. Season with salt.
4. Let flavors marinate before serving, 5 minutes or up to an hour.
