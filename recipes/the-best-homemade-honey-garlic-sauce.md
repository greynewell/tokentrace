## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon canola oil
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- ¾ cup ketchup
- ½ cup honey
- ¼ cup soy sauce

## Instructions

1. Heat oil in a nonstick skillet over medium heat. Add garlic; cook and stir until translucent, 3 to 4 minutes.
2. Stir in ketchup, honey, and soy sauce and bring to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer, stirring occasionally, until flavors combine, about 5 minutes.
