## Ingredients

- ½ cup olive oil
- ¼ cup balsamic vinegar
- 1 teaspoon honey
- 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
- 1  shallot, minced
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- salt and ground black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Combine olive oil, balsamic vinegar, honey, Dijon mustard, shallot, garlic, salt, and black pepper together in a glass jar with a tight-fitting lid.
2. Replace the lid on the jar and shake vigorously until thoroughly combined.
