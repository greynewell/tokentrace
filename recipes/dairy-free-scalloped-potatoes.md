## Ingredients

- cooking spray
- 12  potatoes, peeled and thinly sliced
- 2  onions, thinly sliced
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- ⅓ cup non-dairy butter
- ⅓ cup all-purpose flour
- 3 cups vegetable broth
- 1 pinch paprika, or to taste

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Spray two 9x13-inch baking dishes with nonstick cooking spray.
2. Layer potatoes and onions in the prepared baking dishes, seasoning each layer with salt and pepper.
3. Melt non-dairy butter in a medium saucepan over medium heat. Stir in flour until blended. Whisk in vegetable broth, stirring constantly, until sauce boils and thickens. Pour sauce over the potatoes and onions; sprinkle paprika over the top.
4. Bake in the preheated oven until potatoes are tender and golden brown, about 50 minutes.
