## Ingredients

- cooking spray
- 1 ½ cups blanched almond flour
- 5 tablespoons psyllium husk
- 1 teaspoon sea salt
- 1 cup boiling water
- 3  egg whites
- 2 teaspoons white vinegar
- 2 tablespoons sesame seeds

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Grease a baking sheet with cooking spray.
3. Combine almond flour, psyllium husk, and salt in a bowl; mix well.
4. Add water, egg whites, and vinegar; whisk with an electric mixer until well combined and a thick dough is formed, about 1 minute.
5. Wet your hands and shape dough into 8 rolls.
6. Arrange rolls on the prepared baking sheet.
7. Sprinkle sesame seeds on top.
8. Bake in the preheated oven until golden, about 55 minutes.
