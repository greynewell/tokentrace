## Ingredients

- 1 pound hot Italian sausage links or sweet Italian sausage links, sliced
- 1 small onion, sliced
- 1 (10 ounce) package frozen chopped spinach, thawed and squeezed dry
- 1 (24 ounce) jar RAGÚ® Sauce
- 1  (16-inch) loaf Italian bread or French bread, cut into 4 pieces

## Instructions

1. In large nonstick skillet, brown sausage over medium-high heat.
2. Stir in onion, spinach and mushrooms.
3. Cook, stirring occasionally, 5 minutes or until sausage is done.
4. Stir in Sauce; heat through.
5. For each sandwich, split open each bread piece, and evenly spoon in sausage mixture.
6. Sprinkle, if desired, with crushed red pepper flakes.
