## Ingredients

- 2 fluid ounces bourbon
- ½ fluid ounce sweet vermouth
- 1  maraschino cherry  (Optional)
- ½ cup ice

## Instructions

1. Place ice into a shaker.
2. Pour bourbon and vermouth over ice; shake well.
3. Pour into a cocktail glass and garnish with a cherry.
