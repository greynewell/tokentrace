## Ingredients

- ½ cup warm water
- 2 (.25 ounce) packages active dry yeast
- 1 cup warm milk (110 degrees F/45 degrees C)
- ½ cup white sugar
- 1 teaspoon salt
- ½ cup melted shortening
- 2 cups sifted all-purpose flour
- 2  eggs
- 3 ½ cups all-purpose flour
- 2  egg yolks
- 3 tablespoons white sugar
- ⅛ teaspoon salt
- 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
- 1 cup raisins
- 2 cups cottage cheese

## Instructions

1. Pour yeast into 1/2 cup of lukewarm water.
2. Set aside to soak.
3. In a separate bowl mix together 1 cup milk, 1/2 cup sugar, 1 teaspoon salt.
4. Add 1/2 cup melted shortening and 2 cups sifted flour.
5. Beat well.
6. Add in 2 beaten eggs and yeast mixture and mix well.
7. Set aside to rest until bubbly and spongy.
8. Add 3 1/2 cups of flour and blend well.
9. Set aside to rise for 1 hour.
10. Punch down and let rise again for 1 hour.
11. To Make Filling:  Beat together 2 egg yolks, 3 tablespoons sugar, 1/8 teaspoon salt, 2 tablespoons flour and 1 cup of raisins.
12. Add cottage cheese and mix well.
13. Pinch off 1 inch balls from dough and press an indentation in the center of each.
14. Put filling in.
15. Let rise 15 minutes, then bake at 425 degrees F (220 degrees C) until golden -  12-18 minutes.
