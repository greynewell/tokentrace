## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
- ½ cup cornmeal, or as needed
- 6  green tomatoes, cored and cut into 1/16-inch slices
- salt and ground black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Heat vegetable oil in a skillet over medium heat.
2. Place cornmeal in a bowl; dip and coat each tomato slice until thoroughly coated.
3. Place coated tomato slices side by side in the hot oil; fry until coating is browned and crusty, 1 to 2 minutes per side.
4. Remove with a slotted spoon and transfer to a paper towel-lined plate to drain.
5. Season with salt and black pepper.
6. Repeat with remaining tomato slices.
