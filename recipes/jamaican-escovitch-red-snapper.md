## Ingredients

- 1 pound red snapper, gutted and scaled
- 1 cup lime juice
- 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
- ⅛ cup sifted all-purpose flour
- 1 tablespoon seasoned salt, or more to taste
- 1 tablespoon ground black pepper, or more to taste
- 1 cup vegetable oil for frying
- 1  onion, sliced
- ½  Scotch bonnet pepper, seeded and sliced
- ½ tablespoon whole black peppercorns
- 1 teaspoon ground allspice
- 1 cup white vinegar
- 1 tablespoon ketchup

## Instructions

1. Wash red snapper thoroughly with water and lime juice.
2. Let soak in lime water with 1 teaspoon black pepper in the refrigerator, 10 to 20 minutes.
3. Pat dry with paper towels.
4. Mix flour, salt, and 1 tablespoon pepper in a shallow bowl.
5. Pat snapper dry with paper towels and lightly coat both sides with flour mixture.
6. Heat oil in a heavy frying pan until very hot.
7. Deep-fry snapper until golden brown, 4 to 5 minutes per side.
8. Transfer to a plate.
9. Pour out all but 3 tablespoons of the oil.
10. Add onion, Scotch bonnet pepper, whole peppercorns, and allspice to the oil in the frying pan.
11. Cook until onion softens, about 5 minutes.
12. Pour in vinegar and stir until heated through.
13. Add ketchup and simmer for 1 minute.
14. Pour over hot snapper and let soak in.
15. Serve hot or cold.
