## Ingredients

- 1 pound fresh okra
- ¼ cup melted butter
- ¼ cup Cajun seasoning

## Instructions

1. Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat, and lightly oil the grate.
2. Roll the okra in the melted butter and then in the cajun seasoning. Grill the okra until charred, about 2 minutes per side.
