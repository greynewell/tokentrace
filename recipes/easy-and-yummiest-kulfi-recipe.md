## Ingredients

- 2 (5 ounce) cans evaporated milk
- 2 (7.6 ounce) cans canned table cream (media crema)
- 1 (14 ounce) can sweetened condensed milk
- 2 slices white bread
- ¼ teaspoon ground cardamom
- 12  blanched almonds

## Instructions

1. Pour evaporated milk, canned cream, and sweetened condensed milk into a blender; tear bread into pieces and add to blender.
2. Add cardamom and almonds; blend until liquified, 3 to 4 minutes.
3. Pour into a 9x13-inch glass dish and freeze 8 hours or overnight before cutting into squares and serving.
