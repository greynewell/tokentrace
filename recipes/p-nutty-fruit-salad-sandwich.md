## Ingredients

- ½  apple, cored and diced
- ½  banana, sliced
- ¼ cup apple juice
- 1 tablespoon strawberry jam
- 2 tablespoons peanut butter
- 2 slices whole-wheat bread, toasted

## Instructions

1. Mix apple, banana, apple juice, and strawberry jam together in a bowl.
2. Spread peanut butter on both slices of bread.
3. Top one piece of bread with fruit mixture.
4. Place second slice of bread over the fruit mixture.
