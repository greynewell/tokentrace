## Ingredients

- 2  oval pita breads
- 2 tablespoons mayonnaise
- 2 tablespoons corn relish
- 3 leaves lettuce, chopped
- 10  frozen chicken nuggets, thawed

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to Grill/Broil.
2. Carefully split open pita pockets.
3. Place pitas on serving plates.
4. Spread mayonnaise, then corn relish on inside of pitas.
5. Fill each pocket with lettuce and set aside.
6. Grill/broil chicken nuggets in preheated oven for 2 to 3 minutes each side, or until cooked through and juices run clear.
7. Put chicken nuggets in pita pockets and serve hot.
