## Ingredients

- ½ cup margarine, melted
- 1 ½ cups graham cracker crumbs
- 1 cup chopped walnuts
- 1 cup semisweet chocolate chips
- 1 ⅓ cups flaked coconut
- 1 (14 ounce) can sweetened condensed milk

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (180 degrees C).
2. Pour melted margarine into a 9x13-inch dish. Sprinkle graham crumbs evenly over melted margarine, followed by chopped nuts, then chocolate chips. Top with coconut and pour milk over all.
3. Bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes or until lightly browned on top. Cool for 15 minutes before cutting into finger-length bars.
