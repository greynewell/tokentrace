## Ingredients

- 20  saltine crackers
- 1 (6 ounce) can tomato paste
- 10 (4 ounce) links sausage (such as Eckrich® Smok-y Breakfast Sausage)
- 5 slices American cheese

## Instructions

1. Set oven rack about 6 inches from the heat source and preheat the oven's broiler.
2. Lay crackers salt side down on a baking sheet.
3. Spread tomato paste on top.
4. Microwave sausages in a microwave-safe plate until heated through, about 1 minute.
5. Slice in half lengthwise, making sure not to cut all the way through.
6. Place sliced side-down onto the crackers.
7. Quarter each slice of cheese and lay over the sausage.
8. Broil until the cheese is melted, 2 to 5 minutes.
