## Ingredients

- 6 ears  white corn, husked and cleaned
- 3 tablespoons melted butter
- ⅓ cup fresh lime juice
- ½  red bell pepper, diced
- 4  sun-dried tomatoes (not packed in oil), snipped into small pieces
- 1 tablespoon kosher salt, or to taste
- 1 teaspoon cracked black pepper
- 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
- 2 tablespoons garlic powder

## Instructions

1. Preheat an outdoor grill for medium-high heat.
2. Grill corn on preheated grill until the kernels are tender and have browned in spots, about 8 minutes.
3. Set aside to until cool enough to handle.
4. Whisk together the melted butter and lime juice in a large bowl.
5. Slice the kernels off of the corn and place into the bowl along with the red pepper and sun-dried tomatoes.
6. Season with salt, pepper, parsley, and garlic powder.
7. Toss until well combined.
