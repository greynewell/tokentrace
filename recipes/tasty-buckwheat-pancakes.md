## Ingredients

- ½ cup whole wheat flour
- ¼ cup buckwheat flour
- ¼ cup all-purpose flour
- ¼ cup quick cooking oats
- 3 teaspoons baking powder
- 1 cup skim milk
- 3 tablespoons safflower oil
- 2 tablespoons honey
- 1  egg, lightly beaten

## Instructions

1. In a large bowl, stir together wheat flour, buckwheat flour, all-purpose flour, oats, and baking powder.
2. Stir in milk, oil, honey, and egg.
3. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium heat.
4. Pour or scoop the batter onto the griddle, using approximately 1/4 cup for each pancake.
5. Brown on both sides, and serve hot.
