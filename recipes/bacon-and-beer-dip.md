## Ingredients

- ⅓ pound white American cheese
- 3 ounces light beer, or more if needed
- 3 slices cooked bacon, crumbled
- 1 stalk green onion, thinly sliced

## Instructions

1. Break cheese into pieces and place in a microwave-safe bowl. Add 3 ounces beer. Microwave on high for 1 minute. Stir, then continue to cook in 30-second increments until cheese is melted. If dip is too thick, add more beer.
2. Add crumbed bacon and green onion; stir to incorporate. Dip will thicken as it cools.
