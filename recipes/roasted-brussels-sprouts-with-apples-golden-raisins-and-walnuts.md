## Ingredients

- 1 pound Brussels sprouts, halved
- 1 cup chopped cauliflower
- 2  apples - peeled, cored, and cut into chunks
- 1  sweet onion, sliced
- 1 tablespoon olive oil, or more if needed
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- ½ cup golden raisins
- ¼ cup chopped walnuts
- 1 tablespoon Chardonnay wine, or more to taste

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
2. Line a baking sheet with aluminum foil.
3. Spread Brussels sprouts, cauliflower, apples, and onion onto the prepared baking sheet; drizzle with olive oil.
4. Season with salt and black pepper; stir until evenly coated.
5. Bake in the preheated oven until Brussels sprouts and cauliflower are tender, 20 to 25 minutes.
6. Transfer Brussels sprouts mixture to a serving bowl.
7. Fold in raisins and walnuts; drizzle with wine.
8. Toss to coat.
