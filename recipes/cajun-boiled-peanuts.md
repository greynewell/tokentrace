## Ingredients

- 5 pounds raw peanuts, in shells
- 1 (6 ounce) package dry crab boil
- 1 (4 ounce) can sliced jalapeno peppers, with liquid

## Instructions

1. Place peanuts in a slow cooker.
2. Sprinkle with dry crab boil.
3. Cover with water.
4. Stir in sliced jalapeno peppers and their liquid.
5. Cover the slow cooker, and cook the peanuts on low 8 hours, or overnight, until peanuts float to the top of the water.
