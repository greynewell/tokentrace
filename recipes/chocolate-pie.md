## Ingredients

- 1 (9 inch) pie shell, baked
- 1 (5 ounce) package non-instant chocolate pudding mix
- 2 cups low-fat milk
- 1 (8 ounce) container frozen whipped topping, thawed
- ¼ cup chocolate shavings

## Instructions

1. Whisk milk and pudding mix together in a large bowl until smooth; cover pudding with waxed paper and set aside for 5 minutes.
2. Pour pudding into pie shell and refrigerate until set, at least 1 hour.
3. Top with whipped topping before serving and garnish with chocolate shavings.
