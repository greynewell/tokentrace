## Ingredients

- 1 pound fresh green beans, trimmed
- 1 pound small new potatoes, halved
- water to cover
- 2 (1 inch) cubes salt pork, or more to taste

## Instructions

1. Layer green beans and potatoes in the bottom of a slow cooker. Add just enough water to barely cover. Top with salt pork.
2. Cover and cook on Low until tender, 6 to 8 hours.
