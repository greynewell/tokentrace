## Ingredients

- 2 cups chopped cooked chicken
- 1 cup chopped celery
- ½ cup cashews
- 1 cup mayonnaise
- 1 small onion, grated
- ½ teaspoon salt
- 2 tablespoons lemon juice
- ½ cup shredded Cheddar cheese
- 1 cup crushed potato chips

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
2. In a medium bowl, mix together the chicken, celery, cashews, mayonnaise, onion, salt and lemon juice.
3. Transfer the mixture to a casserole dish, then top with shredded cheese and potato chips.
4. Bake uncovered for 20 minutes in the preheated oven, or until heated through and bubbly.
