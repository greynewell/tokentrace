## Ingredients

- 2 pounds ground beef
- 2  eggs
- ¾ cup instant oatmeal
- 3 tablespoons dried minced onion
- ¾ cup shredded mild Cheddar cheese
- ¼ cup ketchup
- 1 teaspoon salt
- ½ teaspoon pepper
- 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
- 1 (12 ounce) bottle barbeque sauce

## Instructions

1. In a large bowl, mix together the ground beef, eggs, oatmeal, onion, Cheddar cheese, ketchup, salt, and pepper using your hands.
2. Form into tiny meatballs about 1 inch wide.
3. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
4. Heat the oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
5. Add the meatballs, and cook, turning frequently, until browned, about 10 minutes.
6. Use a slotted spoon to transfer the meatballs to a baking dish.
7. Cover with barbeque sauce.
8. Bake for 30 minutes in the preheat oven, until sauce is thick and meatballs are cooked through.
9. Serve hot.
