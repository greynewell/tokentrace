## Ingredients

- 1 pound fresh broccoli, cut into spears
- 1 ½ cups cubed cooked chicken or turkey
- 1 (10.75 ounce) can  Campbell's® Condensed Broccoli Cheese Soup or Campbell's® Condensed 98% Fat Free Broccoli Cheese Soup*
- ⅓ cup milk
- ½ cup shredded Cheddar cheese
- 2 tablespoons dry bread crumbs
- 1 tablespoon butter or margarine, melted

## Instructions

1. Arrange broccoli and chicken in 9-inch pie plate or 2-quart shallow baking dish.
2. Mix soup and milk and pour over all.
3. Sprinkle with cheese. Mix bread crumbs with butter and sprinkle on top.
4. Bake 400 degrees F for 25 minutes or until hot.
