## Ingredients

- 6 cups chicken broth
- 3 cups frozen O'Brien hash brown potatoes
- 1 (14.5 ounce) can diced tomatoes
- 1 (12 ounce) package frozen mixed vegetables (corn, peas, carrots, green beans, and lima beans)
- 1  celery rib, sliced
- 2 teaspoons Worcestershire sauce
- 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
- 1 teaspoon dried oregano
- ½ teaspoon onion powder
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ½ teaspoon freeze-dried dill
- ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper

## Instructions

1. Place chicken broth, hash brown potatoes, tomatoes, frozen vegetables, celery, Worcestershire sauce, paprika, oregano, onion powder, salt, dill, and pepper into a multi-functional pressure cooker (such as Instant Pot). Stir to combine.
2. Close and lock the lid. Select high pressure according to manufacturer's instructions; set timer for 0 minutes. Allow 30 minutes for pressure to build.
3. Release pressure carefully using the quick-release method according to the manufacturer's instructions, about 5 minutes. Ladle soup into bowls.
