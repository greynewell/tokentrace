## Ingredients

- 3  ripe bananas
- 1 (15 ounce) can chickpeas (garbanzo beans), drained and rinsed
- ½ cup melted butter
- ½ cup chopped pitted dates
- ½ cup almond flour
- ¼ cup honey
- 4  eggs
- 1 teaspoon baking soda
- 1 teaspoon grated fresh ginger
- 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- ¾ teaspoon salt

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Grease 1 large loaf pan.
3. Blend bananas, chickpeas, butter, dates, almond flour, honey, eggs, baking soda, ginger, cinnamon, vanilla extract, and salt together in a blender or food processor until batter is smooth.
4. Pour batter into the prepared loaf pan.
5. Bake in the preheated oven until golden brown and a knife inserted in the center of the loaf comes out clean, about 1 hour.
6. Cool in pan for 15 minutes before slicing.
