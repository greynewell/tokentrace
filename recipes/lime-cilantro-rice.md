## Ingredients

- 2 cups water
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 cup long-grain white rice
- 1 teaspoon lime zest
- 2 tablespoons fresh lime juice
- ½ cup chopped cilantro

## Instructions

1. Bring water to a boil in a small saucepan. Stir in rice and butter. Reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer until rice is tender, about 20 minutes.
2. Just before serving, add cilantro, lime juice, and lime zest.
