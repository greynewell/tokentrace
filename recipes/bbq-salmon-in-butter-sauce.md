## Ingredients

- ⅓ cup butter
- ½ small onion, or to taste, diced
- 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
- ¼ teaspoon paprika
- 4  salmon steaks

## Instructions

1. Preheat grill for medium heat.
2. Melt butter in a saucepan; stir onion, Worcestershire sauce, and paprika into the melted butter.
3. Arrange four squares of aluminum foil on a flat work surface.
4. Place a salmon steak in the middle of each piece of foil.
5. Roll edges toward the salmon to make miniature pans and pour about 1/4 of the butter sauce over each steak.
6. Carefully set 'pans' onto the hot grill and cook until the fish flakes easily with a fork, 20 to 25 minutes.
