## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon butter
- ⅓ cup sliced almonds
- ¼ cup vegetable oil
- 2 tablespoons white sugar
- 2 tablespoons white wine vinegar
- 2 tablespoons cider vinegar
- 1 tablespoon sesame seeds, toasted
- 1 ½ teaspoons poppy seeds
- 1 teaspoon minced onion
- ⅛ teaspoon paprika
- 1 (10 ounce) package fresh spinach
- ½ cup pomegranate arils
- 1 tablespoon toasted sesame seeds

## Instructions

1. Melt butter in a small saucepan over medium heat.
2. Add almonds; cook, stirring frequently, until lightly toasted.
3. Whisk oil, sugar, vinegars, sesame seeds, poppy seeds, onion, and paprika together in a bowl for the dressing.
4. Toss spinach, toasted almonds, and pomegranate arils together in a large bowl.
5. Drizzle with dressing and toss to coat.
