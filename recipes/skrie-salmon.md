## Ingredients

- ¼ teaspoon anise seed
- ⅛ teaspoon crushed dried mint
- 1 ¼ cups water, divided
- ¾ teaspoon honey
- 3  strips lemon peel
- 3 tablespoons butter, melted
- 2 pounds salmon fillets

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. In a small saucepan, stir together anise seeds and mint.
3. Stir in 1 cup water, and bring to a boil over high heat.
4. Boil 5 minutes, then stir in honey.
5. Continue to boil until most of the water has boiled off.
6. Remove from heat, and stir in remaining 1/4 cup water.
7. Place lemon strips in a bowl, and pour melted butter over the strips.
8. Let sit 5 minutes.
9. Place salmon on baking sheet.
10. Brush thoroughly with anise sauce.
11. Place in a preheated oven, and bake until done, about 15 minutes.
12. Remove from oven, and brush liberally with lemon butter.
