## Ingredients

- 1 ½ cups warm water
- ¼ cup molasses
- 1 (.25 ounce) package active dry yeast
- 2 tablespoons canola oil
- 1 ½ cups bread flour, plus more for kneading
- 1 cup whole-wheat flour
- 1 cup rye flour
- ⅓ cup cornmeal
- ¼ cup unsweetened cocoa powder
- 2 tablespoons vital wheat gluten
- 1 teaspoon salt

## Instructions

1. Stir warm water, molasses, and yeast together in the bowl of a stand mixer.
2. Let stand until the yeast softens and begins to form a creamy foam, about 5 minutes.
3. Add canola oil.
4. Mix bread flour, whole-wheat flour, rye flour, cornmeal, cocoa powder, vital wheat gluten, and salt in a bowl.
5. Add slowly to yeast mixture.
6. Fit the mixer with the dough hook attachment; turn to medium speed and let dough mix for 15 minutes.
7. Turn dough onto a floured surface; knead by hand until no longer sticky, but smooth and elastic, adding more flour as needed, 3 to 5 minutes.
8. Grease a large bowl.
9. Transfer dough to greased bowl, turning to coat dough on all sides.
10. Cover bowl; let rise in a warm area until doubles in size, 30 minutes.
11. Grease a loaf pan.
12. Punch dough down and transfer to greased loaf pan.
13. Cover and let rise in a warm area for 1 hour.
14. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
15. Bake in preheated oven until loaf sounds hollow when tapped, 40 to 50 minutes.
16. Cool on a wire rack.
