## Ingredients

- ½ cup complete pancake mix
- ⅓ cup water
- cooking spray
- 9  chocolate sandwich cookies (such as Oreo®)
- 1 tablespoon confectioners' sugar, or to taste

## Instructions

1. Preheat the air fryer to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Line the air fryer basket with parchment paper. Spray parchment paper with cooking spray.
2. Mix pancake mix and water together in a bowl until well combined.
3. Dip each cookie into pancake mixture and place in the prepared basket, making sure they are not touching; cook in batches if necessary.
4. Cook in the preheated air fryer for 4 to 5 minutes; flip cookies and cook until golden brown, 2 to 3 minutes more. Sprinkle with confectioners' sugar.
