## Ingredients

- 2 cups milk
- ½ cup honey
- 6 cups all-purpose flour
- 2 teaspoons baking soda
- ½ cup white sugar
- ½ cup ground cinnamon
- 1 cup butter, melted
- ½ cup white sugar
- ½ cup ground cinnamon

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
2. Grease 2 baking sheets.
3. Stir milk and honey together in a bowl; let sit for 5 minutes.
4. Sift flour and baking soda together in a large bowl; mix milk-honey mixture into flour mixture until dough is soft.
5. Knead dough on a lightly floured surface for about 3 minutes.
6. Roll dough using a rolling pin into a rectangle about 1/2-inch thick; cut into 24 equal pieces.
7. Roll each piece of dough into a l0-inch cylinder; fold ends together into a pretzel-shape.
8. Mix 1/2 cup sugar and 1/2 cup cinnamon in a bowl; sprinkle over pretzels.
9. Arrange coated pretzels on the prepared baking sheet.
10. Bake in the preheated oven until pretzels are golden brown, about 8 minutes.
11. Mix melted butter with 1/2 cup sugar and 1/2 cup cinnamon; brush warm pretzels with butter mixture.
