## Ingredients

- 2 pounds Brussels sprouts, trimmed and halved lengthwise
- 1 cup coarsely chopped pecans
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 2 cloves garlic, finely chopped
- kosher salt and ground black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
2. Place the Brussels sprouts, pecans, olive oil, and garlic onto a large rimmed baking sheet, and toss the ingredients together until well combined and coated with oil.
3. Spread out into a single layer, and turn the Brussels sprouts to face cut sides down.
4. Roast in the preheated oven until golden brown and tender, 20 to 25 minutes.
