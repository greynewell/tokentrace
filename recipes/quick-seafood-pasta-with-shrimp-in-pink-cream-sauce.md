## Ingredients

- 1 (16 ounce) package dry fettuccine pasta
- ¼ cup olive oil
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 cup dry white wine
- 3 tablespoons tomato paste
- 1 ½ pounds medium shrimp - peeled, deveined, and cut into 1/2-inch pieces
- 1 cup heavy whipping cream
- 1 pinch salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Fill a large pot with lightly salted water and bring to a rolling boil.
2. Cook fettuccine at a boil until tender yet firm to the bite, about 8 minutes.
3. Drain.
4. Heat olive oil in a saucepan over medium heat and cook garlic until fragrant, 1 to 2 minutes.
5. Stir wine and tomato paste together in a small bowl; add to the saucepan.
6. Simmer over low heat until sauce starts to thicken, about 10 minutes.
7. Stir shrimp into the sauce.
8. Cook until shrimp are pink, 3 to 5 minutes.
9. Remove about 2/3 of the shrimp with a slotted spoon and put in the bowl of a food processor; pulse until pureed.
10. Return pureed shrimp to the saucepan and stir in heavy cream.
11. Heat until warm, about 5 minutes.
12. Season with salt and pepper.
13. Serve with the fettuccine.
