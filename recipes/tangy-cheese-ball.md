## Ingredients

- 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
- 1 (8 ounce) package shredded sharp Cheddar cheese
- 2 teaspoons dill pickle juice
- 1 (.7 ounce) package dry Italian-style salad dressing mix
- 10 ounces pecans, chopped

## Instructions

1. In a large bowl combine cream cheese, Cheddar cheese, pickle juice and salad dressing mix.
2. Blend well, mixing by hand.
3. Shape into a ball and roll in pecans.
4. Refrigerate at least 3 hours before serving.
