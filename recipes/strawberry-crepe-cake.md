## Ingredients

- 3 cups whole milk
- 4  eggs
- ⅓ cup white sugar
- 3 cups whole milk
- 3 cups all-purpose flour
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ½ teaspoon almond extract
- ½ teaspoon vanilla extract
- 8 tablespoons butter, melted, divided
- 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
- 2 cups heavy cream
- ½ cup confectioners' sugar
- ⅓ cup strawberry jam
- 1 cup strawberries

## Instructions

1. Pulse milk, eggs, and sugar in a blender until combined.
2. Add flour, salt, and extracts.
3. Blend until smooth, about 3 minutes.
4. Transfer batter to an airtight container and let chill for at least 4 hours or overnight.
5. Gently whisk chilled batter to recombine.
6. Place a small 9-inch nonstick pan over medium heat.
7. Coat pan with a portion of the melted butter; let melt and sizzle.
8. Remove from heat and pour a scant 1/4 cup of batter into one side of the pan.
9. Swirl to evenly coat pan and return to heat.
10. Cook until edges are golden brown and center is pale gold, 2 to 3 minutes.
11. Lift one side of the crepe with a flexible spatula and flip using your fingertips.
12. Continue to cook until pale golden, 30 seconds to 1 minute.
13. Slide crepe out of the pan and let cool on a wire rack.
14. Continue until you have 20 crepes.
15. Transfer cooled crepes to a parchment paper-lined baking sheet.
16. Whisk cream cheese until light and fluffy in the bowl of a stand mixer, about 4 minutes.
17. Add cream and confectioners' sugar and whisk on medium-high speed until soft peaks form.
18. Stir in strawberry jam.
19. Place 1 crepe on the center of a cake board or platter and spread a thin layer of the whipped cream mixture on top.
20. Top with another crepe and continue layering until all crepes have been used.
21. Top final layer with the last of the cream and garnish with fresh strawberries.
22. Chill at least 30 minutes before slicing and serving.
