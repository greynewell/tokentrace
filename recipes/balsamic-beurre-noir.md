## Ingredients

- 2 teaspoons butter
- 1 clove garlic, sliced
- ⅓ cup aged balsamic vinegar
- 1 teaspoon minced red chile pepper
- ½ teaspoon tomato paste
- salt and pepper to taste
- 2 tablespoons cold unsalted butter

## Instructions

1. Melt butter in a skillet over medium heat until it foams.
2. Stir in garlic; cook until edges just start to turn golden, about 2 minutes.
3. Stir balsamic vinegar into skillet, bring to a simmer and cook over medium heat until vinegar is reduced by 2/3 to create a thick sauce, 1 to 2 minutes.
4. Stir in chile peppers and reduce heat to low.
5. Whisk in tomato paste, salt and black pepper.
6. Remove skillet from the heat.
7. Stir in butter; whisk constantly until the butter melts and is completely incorporated into the sauce.
