## Ingredients

- avocado oil nonstick cooking spray
- 1 (14.75 ounce) can salmon, drained and flaked
- ¾ cup panko bread crumbs
- ½ cup diced onion
- ⅓ cup mayonnaise
- ½ medium lemon, juiced
- 1 large egg, beaten
- 1 teaspoon dried dill
- 1 teaspoon seafood seasoning (such as Old Bay®)
- salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
- 3 large roll or bun (3-1/2" dia)s brioche buns, halved
- 3 teaspoons mayonnaise, or to taste

## Instructions

1. Preheat an air fryer to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Line the air fryer basket with parchment paper and spray with avocado oil.
2. Mix salmon, panko, onion, 1/3 cup mayonnaise, lemon juice, egg, dill, seafood seasoning, salt, and pepper in a bow until well combined. Shape into 3 equal-sized patties.
3. Place salmon patties into the basket and spray with avocado oil.
4. Air fry until patties are golden brown, 8 to 10 minutes. Flip patties over, spray with avocado oil, and air fry an additional 4 to 7 minutes. Serve on buns spread with remaining mayonnaise.
