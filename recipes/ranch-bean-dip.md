## Ingredients

- 1 (16 ounce) can refried beans
- 2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese
- 1 (1 ounce) package ranch dressing mix
- 1 cup sour cream

## Instructions

1. In a small saucepan, combine beans, cheese, ranch dressing mix, and sour cream.
2. Heat the mixture over a medium heat, stir until the ingredients are well blended and warm.
