## Ingredients

- ½ cup almond flour
- 5  eggs
- 2 teaspoons honey
- 1 pinch salt
- 2 teaspoons butter, or as needed

## Instructions

1. Mix almond flour, eggs, honey, and salt together in a bowl.
2. Heat butter in a skillet over medium heat.
3. Pour a few tablespoons of batter into the skillet and rotate the skillet to spread batter out in a thin layer.
4. Cook until lightly browned, 1 to 2 minutes per side.
5. Transfer to a plate and repeat with remaining batter.
