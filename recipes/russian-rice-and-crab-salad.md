## Ingredients

- 1 cup water
- ½ cup uncooked white rice
- 1 (15.25 ounce) can whole kernel corn, drained
- 1 cup imitation crabmeat, flaked
- 4  hard-boiled eggs, mashed
- 1  onion, minced
- 4  cornichons, minced
- 1 pinch salt and ground black pepper to taste
- 3 tablespoons mayonnaise
- 1 clove garlic, finely chopped
- 1 bunch fresh dill, chopped

## Instructions

1. Bring water and rice to a boil in a saucepan.
2. Reduce heat to medium-low, cover, and simmer until rice is tender and water has been absorbed, 20 to 25 minutes.
3. Remove from heat and cool for a minimum of 4 hours or overnight.
4. Combine cooked and cooled rice, corn, imitation crab, eggs, onion, and cornichons in a bowl.
5. Season with salt and pepper.
6. Stir together mayonnaise and garlic in a small bowl; pour over salad.
7. Mix well.
8. Sprinkle with chopped dill.
