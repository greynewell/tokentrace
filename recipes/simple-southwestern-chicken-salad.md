## Ingredients

- 2 1/2 breast, bone removeds boneless chicken breast halves, cooked and cubed
- ¾ cup mayonnaise
- ¼ cup chopped celery
- chopped fresh cilantro to taste
- 1 (1 ounce) package
- mix

## Instructions

1. In a medium bowl, combine the chicken, mayonnaise, celery, cilantro and seasoning mix.
2. Mix well.
3. Cover bowl and refrigerate for 1 hour.
