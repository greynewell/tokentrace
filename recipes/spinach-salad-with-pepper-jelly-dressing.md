## Ingredients

- 3 tablespoons mild pepper jelly
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- ⅛ teaspoon salt
- ⅛ teaspoon Dijon mustard
- 2 cups baby spinach leaves
- 2 ounces goat cheese, sliced
- 2 tablespoons chopped walnuts

## Instructions

1. In a small bowl, whisk together the pepper jelly, olive oil, salt and mustard to make the dressing.
2. Heat in the microwave for 30 seconds.
3. Let cool.
4. Place the spinach in a large bowl, and toss with the dressing.
5. Divide between two serving bowls.
6. Top each one with slices of goat cheese and sprinkle with walnuts.
