## Ingredients

- 2 quarts vegetable oil for frying
- 3 cups cornmeal
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 1 ½ teaspoons salt
- 1 ½ cups milk
- ½ cup water
- 1  egg, beaten
- 1  small onion, minced

## Instructions

1. Heat vegetable oil to 365 degrees F (185 degrees C).
2. In a large mixing bowl, combine corn meal, baking powder, salt, milk, and water.
3. Mix in egg and chopped onion.
4. Shape the batter into small balls, approximately 1 tablespoon each.
5. Use a long handled spoon to gently slide the hush puppies into the hot oil.
6. Cook 8 or 10 at a time, until golden brown.
7. Remove from the oil with a slotted spoon, and roll on paper towels to briefly drain.
8. Serve hot.
