## Ingredients

- 2 cups baking mix (such as Bisquick®)
- ½ cup applesauce
- ½ cup white sugar, divided
- ¼ cup milk
- 1 large egg
- 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
- 1 ¼ teaspoons ground cinnamon, divided
- 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease 30 mini muffin cups.
2. Mix baking mix, applesauce, 1/4 cup sugar, milk, egg, oil, and 1 teaspoon cinnamon together in a bowl until just blended.
3. Spoon batter into the prepared muffin cups, filling each 3/4 full.
4. Bake in the preheated oven until golden, 12 to 15 minutes.
5. While puffs are baking, place melted butter in a shallow bowl, and combine remaining sugar and cinnamon in a second shallow bowl.
6. Dip warm puffs in melted butter, then in the cinnamon-sugar mixture.
