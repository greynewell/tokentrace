## Ingredients

- 1 (16 ounce) package frozen meatballs
- 1 (10.5 ounce) can condensed beef and mushroom soup
- ½ cup sour cream

## Instructions

1. Combine frozen meatballs, condensed soup, and sour cream in a slow cooker.
2. Cover and cook on High until heated through, about 2 hours.
