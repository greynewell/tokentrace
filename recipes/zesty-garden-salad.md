## Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
- 1 sprig fresh dill, chopped  (Optional)
- 1 tablespoon chopped green onion
- 2 tablespoons shredded Cheddar cheese
- ½ cup sweet corn kernels
- ½ cup sugar snap peas
- ⅓ cup frozen shelled edamame (green soybeans)
- 2 cups iceberg lettuce
- 1 pinch salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Stir the Dijon mustard, dill, green onion, Cheddar cheese, corn, peas, and edamame in a bowl until evenly combined.
2. Stir in the iceberg lettuce, season to taste with salt and pepper, and toss to mix.
