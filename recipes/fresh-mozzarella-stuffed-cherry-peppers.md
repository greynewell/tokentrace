## Ingredients

- 5 ounces BelGioioso Fresh Mozzarella Ciliegine cheese
- 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
- ½ teaspoon dried basil
- ½ teaspoon dried oregano
- ⅛ teaspoon red pepper flakes
- Salt and pepper to taste
- 1 (16 ounce) jar pickled sweet cherry peppers or peppadew peppers

## Instructions

1. Toss BelGioioso Fresh Mozzarella Ciliegine with olive oil, basil, oregano, red pepper flakes, salt and pepper.
2. Slice off pepper stem and stuff with a ball of Fresh Mozzarella.
