## Ingredients

- ¼ cup butter
- ½ cup packed brown sugar
- ¼ cup orange juice

## Instructions

1. Boil cut-up sweet potatoes until tender, about 10 minutes.
2. Melt butter and brown sugar together in a frying pan until bubbly. Add orange juice and stir until smooth. Add the sweet potatoes and cook slowly, turning occasionally until caramelized, about 20 minutes.
