## Ingredients

- ¼ cup pine nuts
- ¼ cup butter
- 1 bunch kale - stems removed, roughly chopped, and rinsed
- 1 teaspoon distilled white vinegar
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- ½ cup shredded Parmesan cheese

## Instructions

1. Toast pine nuts in a skillet over low heat until golden brown and fragrant, about 5 minutes.
2. Melt butter in a large skillet over medium heat.
3. Place kale in the melted butter and cook until tender, 10 minutes.
4. Toss cooked kale with vinegar in a serving bowl; season with salt and pepper.
5. Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese to serve.
