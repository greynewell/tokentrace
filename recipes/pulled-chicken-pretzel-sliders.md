## Ingredients

- 12 (7.2 ounce) packages Original Snack Factory® Pretzel Crisps®
- 6 tablespoons shredded chicken in BBQ sauce, warmed
- 2 ounces white Cheddar cheese, cut into 12 small squares
- 2 thick bacon slices cooked thick bacon strips, cut into 12 pieces

## Instructions

1. Arrange pretzels on serving platter.
2. Top with 1/2 tablespoon heated shredded BBQ chicken, a piece of cheese, and bacon.
3. Allow cheese to melt slightly before serving.
