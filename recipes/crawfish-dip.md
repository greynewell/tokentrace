## Ingredients

- 1 cup butter
- 2 bunches green onions, chopped
- ¼ cup chopped fresh parsley
- 2 pounds peeled crawfish tails
- 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
- 2  egg yolks, beaten
- 1 (12 fluid ounce) can evaporated milk
- 1 (1 pound) loaf processed cheese food, cubed
- salt and black pepper to taste
- hot pepper sauce to taste

## Instructions

1. Heat butter in a large pot over medium heat.
2. Stir in green onions, and cook briefly.
3. Add parsley and crawfish, and simmer about 7 minutes, stirring frequently.
4. Stir in flour to coat crawfish.
5. In a small bowl, mix together egg yolks and evaporated milk.
6. Stir into crawfish, and reduce heat to medium-low.
7. Gradually add cheese cubes, stirring continuously until the cheese has melted.
8. Season with salt, pepper, and hot pepper sauce.
9. Remove from heat, and bring to room temperature before serving.
