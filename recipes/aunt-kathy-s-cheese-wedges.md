## Ingredients

- 1 ½ cups grated Cheddar cheese
- 1 cup mayonnaise
- ½ cup butter, softened
- 1 cup chopped black olives, drained
- ½ teaspoon seasoned salt
- ½ teaspoon curry powder
- 6  English muffins, split

## Instructions

1. Gently stir together the Cheddar cheese, mayonnaise, butter, olives, seasoned salt, and curry powder in a bowl.
2. Preheat oven broiler.
3. Lightly toast the English muffins in a toaster.
4. Spread each muffin with a portion of the cheese mixture.
5. Arrange muffins on a large baking sheet.
6. Broil topped muffins under the broiler until the cheese bubbles, 3 to 5 minutes.
7. Cut into halves to serve.
