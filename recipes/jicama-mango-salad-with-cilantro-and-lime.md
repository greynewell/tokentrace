## Ingredients

- 1 large jicama, peeled and cut into matchsticks
- 1 small red bell pepper, cut into matchsticks
- 1 large firm mango, peeled and cut into matchsticks
- ½  red onion, cut into matchsticks
- ½ cup chopped cilantro leaves
- 2  limes, juiced
- ¼ cup honey
- 1 teaspoon salt
- ⅛ teaspoon cayenne pepper, or more to taste

## Instructions

1. Toss jicama, red pepper, mango, and red onion together in a large bowl. Set aside.
2. Stir cilantro, lime juice, honey, salt, and cayenne pepper together in a bowl.
3. Pour the cilantro mixture over the jicama mixture and toss to coat. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 15 minutes.
