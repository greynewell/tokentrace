## Ingredients

- 2 ¼ cups all-purpose flour
- 1 teaspoon baking soda
- ½ teaspoon salt
- 1 cup Becel® Buttery Taste or Original Margarine*
- 1 cup firmly packed light brown sugar
- ¼ cup granulated sugar
- 2  large Naturegg™ Omega 3 eggs
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- ¾ cup HERSHEY'S® Chipits® SKOR™ Toffee Bits
- ¾ cup HERSHEY'S® Chipits® Butterscotch Flavoured Baking Chips
- 1 teaspoon coarse sea salt

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 C).
2. Combine flour, baking soda and salt in medium bowl; set aside.
3. Beat Becel Buttery Taste margarine with sugars in large bowl.
4. Beat in eggs and vanilla until blended.
5. Gradually add in flour mixture; beat just until blended.
6. Stir in toffee and butterscotch chips.
7. Drop by tablespoonfuls on ungreased baking sheets, 2-inches (5 cm) apart.
8. Evenly sprinkle with sea salt.
9. Bake 7 minutes or until edges are golden.
10. Cool cookies on sheets 2 minutes on wire rack; remove cookies from sheets and cool completely.
