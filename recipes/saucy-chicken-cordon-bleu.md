## Ingredients

- 4  skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
- 4 slices ham
- 4 slices Swiss cheese
- 1 cup all-purpose flour
- 1 teaspoon salt
- ½ teaspoon ground black pepper
- ½ teaspoon paprika
- 2  eggs, beaten
- ⅓ cup milk
- 1 cup dry bread crumbs
- ¼ cup olive oil
- 1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of mushroom soup
- ½ pound fresh mushrooms, sliced
- ¼ teaspoon garlic powder
- ⅛ teaspoon curry powder
- ¼ cup white wine
- ½ cup sour cream
- 2 sprigs fresh parsley, for garnish

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Flatten chicken breasts without breaking through meat.
3. Roll each ham slice in a cheese slice, then roll up in chicken breasts.
4. In a shallow dish or bowl season flour with salt, pepper and paprika.
5. In a separate dish or bowl beat together eggs and milk.
6. Dip chicken rolls in seasoned flour, then egg mixture, then bread crumbs.
7. Heat oil in a large skillet and fry chicken in hot oil until golden brown.
8. Set aside.
9. To Make Sauce: In a large bowl combine the soup, mushrooms, garlic powder, curry powder, wine and sour cream.
10. Mix all together.
11. Place browned chicken in a 9x13 inch baking dish.
12. Pour sauce mixture over chicken and bake in the preheated oven for 15 to 20 minutes.
13. Garnish with fresh parsley sprigs.
