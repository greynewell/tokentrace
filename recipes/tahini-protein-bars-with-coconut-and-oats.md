## Ingredients

- ½ cup old-fashioned oats
- ½ cup unsweetened coconut flakes
- ½ teaspoon flaked sea salt
- 1 ¼ cups tahini
- 3 tablespoons agave syrup
- 2 scoops whey protein powder  (Optional)
- 3 tablespoons cashew milk

## Instructions

1. Line a 9x13-inch baking pan with parchment paper.
2. Blend oats, coconut flakes, and salt in a food processor until a powder-like consistency is reached.
3. Add tahini and agave syrup; blend until a fluffy paste is formed.
4. Mix in protein powder until well combined.
5. Blend in milk 1 tablespoon at a time, thinning the paste until it is spreadable.
6. Spread paste into the prepared pan.
7. Refrigerate until firm, about 30 minutes.
8. Cut into squares and serve chilled.
