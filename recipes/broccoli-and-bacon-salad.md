## Ingredients

- 1 pound bacon
- 2 heads broccoli, cut into florets
- 1 cup sunflower seeds
- ½ cup chopped green onions
- ½ cup mayonnaise
- 6 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
- ¼ cup white sugar

## Instructions

1. Place bacon in a large skillet and cook over medium-high heat, turning occasionally, until evenly browned, about 10 minutes. Drain bacon slices on paper towels. Tear bacon into small pieces.
2. Combine broccoli, bacon, sunflower seeds, and green onions in a bowl.
3. Whisk mayonnaise, vinegar, and sugar together in a bowl; pour over broccoli mixture and toss to coat.
