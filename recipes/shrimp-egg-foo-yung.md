## Ingredients

- 8 ounces fresh bean sprouts, rinsed and drained
- 1 cup cooked small shrimp
- 8  eggs, beaten
- 1 (4.5 ounce) can sliced mushrooms
- 2 large green onions, chopped
- ½ teaspoon salt
- 3 tablespoons vegetable oil
- 1 ½ cups chicken broth
- 2 tablespoons soy sauce
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- ground white pepper, to taste
- 2 tablespoons cornstarch
- 2 tablespoons cold water

## Instructions

1. In a mixing bowl, combine the bean sprouts, shrimp, eggs, mushrooms, scallions and 1/2 teaspoon of salt.
2. Mix thoroughly.
3. Heat a deep skillet over high heat until 1 or 2 drops of water bubble when sprinkled in.
4. Add oil and turn heat back to Medium-High.
5. Pour in 1/2 cup of egg mixture into pan.
6. Push cooked egg up over shrimp with a spatula to form a patty.
7. Fry until golden brown, turning once, about 4 minutes.
8. Repeat with remaining egg mixture.
9. If you need to, add more oil.
10. Keep patties warm.
11. In a saucepan over medium heat, combine chicken broth, soy sauce, 1/4 teaspoon salt and white pepper to taste.
12. Bring to a boil.
13. Mix cornstarch and water; stir into broth mix.
14. Cook and stir until thickened (about 10 seconds).
15. Pour over patties.
