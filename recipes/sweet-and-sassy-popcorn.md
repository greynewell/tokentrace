## Ingredients

- ⅓ cup vegetable oil
- 3 tablespoons honey
- 1 teaspoon paprika
- 1 teaspoon cayenne pepper, or to taste
- ¼ teaspoon black pepper
- 1 (2.9 ounce) bag microwave popcorn, popped

## Instructions

1. Stir oil, honey, paprika, cayenne pepper, and black pepper together in a small, microwave-safe bowl.
2. Heat on high until the honey is melted, about 30 seconds; stir.
3. Pour popped popcorn into a large serving bowl.
4. Drizzle oil mixture over popcorn and toss to coat.
