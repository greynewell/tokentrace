## Ingredients

- 1 cup walnut halves
- 1 pound radishes, trimmed and sliced into thin rounds
- 3 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
- 2 teaspoons fresh lemon juice
- ¾ teaspoon kosher salt (such as Diamond Crystal®)
- ½ teaspoon honey
- 20  fresh mint leaves

## Instructions

1. Toast walnuts in a dry skillet over medium heat, shaking occasionally, until fragrant and a shade darker, 2 to 4 minutes.
2. Chop coarsely.
3. Place radishes in a serving bowl.
4. Whisk oil, lemon juice, salt, and honey in a small bowl until combined; drizzle over radish slices and toss to coat.
5. Stack mint leaves, roll tightly, and slice crosswise into thin ribbons.
6. Fold mint and walnuts into salad.
