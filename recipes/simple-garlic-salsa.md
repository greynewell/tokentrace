## Ingredients

- 1 (14.5 ounce) can diced tomatoes, drained
- ½ cup olive oil
- 1 tablespoon minced garlic
- 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley
- 1 pinch salt
- 1 pinch ground black pepper

## Instructions

1. In a medium glass bowl, mix together tomatoes, oil, garlic, parsley, salt and pepper.
2. Cover, and refrigerate overnight, allowing the flavors to 'mix and mingle'.
