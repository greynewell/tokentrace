## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon salted butter
- 3 tablespoons finely ground almond flour
- 1 tablespoon shredded Cheddar cheese
- ¼ teaspoon onion powder
- ¼ teaspoon baking powder
- 1 large egg, lightly beaten

## Instructions

1. Melt butter in a 4-inch ramekin in the microwave on high, about 25 seconds. Carefully remove from the microwave with a pot holder.
2. Add almond flour, Cheddar cheese, onion powder, and baking powder; mix until combined. Stir in egg until incorporated.
3. Microwave on high until set, about 90 seconds. Remove from the microwave and let stand for 1 minute.
4. Run a butter knife around the edges and invert onto a plate. Cut horizontally to make 2 pieces of bread.
