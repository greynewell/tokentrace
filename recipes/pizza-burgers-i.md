## Ingredients

- 1 pound ground beef
- 1 (14 ounce) can pizza sauce
- 4  hamburger buns
- 4 slices shredded mozzarella cheese

## Instructions

1. Heat a large, deep skillet over medium-high heat. Cook and stir ground beef in the hot skillet until evenly browned and crumbly, 5 to 7 minutes.
2. Stir in pizza sauce and cook until heated through, about 3 minutes.
3. Place bun bottoms onto a microwave-safe plate. Spoon beef mixture onto each bun and place a mozzarella cheese slice over top.
4. Microwave on high until cheese is melted, 15 to 20 seconds.
5. Place bun tops over melted cheese and serve.
