## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- ½ cup chopped red bell pepper
- ¼ large red onion, chopped
- ¼ cup crumbled blue cheese
- 1 (4 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
- ¼ cup heavy cream
- ¼ cup milk
- 1 (14.5 ounce) can diced tomatoes
- 1 ½ cups tomato juice
- 2 teaspoons dried basil
- ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
- 1 teaspoon granular no-calorie sucralose sweetener (such as Splenda®)

## Instructions

1. Heat the olive oil in a saucepan over medium heat.
2. Stir in the garlic, bell pepper, and onion; cook and stir until the onion has softened and turned translucent, about 5 minutes.
3. Stir in the blue cheese, cream cheese, heavy cream, and milk.
4. Heat until the cheese is melted and the mixture is simmering, 5 to 7 minutes.
5. Stir in the diced tomatoes, tomato juice, basil, sweetener, and pepper.
6. Continue to cook and stir until the soup is heated through, 15 to 20 minutes.
