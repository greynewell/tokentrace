## Ingredients

- 3 tablespoons butter
- 1  onion, chopped
- 2 cups fresh sliced mushrooms
- Â½ cup dates, pitted and chopped
- 1 (8 ounce) wheel Brie cheese

## Instructions

1. In a medium skillet over medium heat, melt butter and caramelize onions and mushrooms; add dates and warm mixture for about 2 minutes.
2. Slice the Brie wheel in half down the center so that there is a top and a bottom; fill with the onion mixture.
3. Replace the top of the Brie.
4. Microwave Brie for 1 to 3 minutes or until bubbly; serve.
