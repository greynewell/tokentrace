## Ingredients

- 2 quarts vegetable oil for deep frying
- ½ cup all-purpose flour
- 1 teaspoon salt
- ½ teaspoon ground black pepper
- 12 ounces shucked oysters, drained
- 2  eggs, lightly beaten
- ¾ cup fine bread crumbs

## Instructions

1. Heat oil in a deep fryer to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
2. Combine flour, salt, and pepper in a shallow bowl. Place eggs in another bowl. Place bread crumbs in a third bowl.
3. Dredge oysters in flour mixture, then dip in egg and roll in bread crumbs to coat thoroughly.
4. Cook oysters, 5 at a time, in hot oil until golden brown, about 2 minutes. Drain on a paper towel-lined plate. Serve hot.
