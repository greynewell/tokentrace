## Ingredients

- 4  sweet potatoes, cut into large French fries
- 1 tablespoon water
- 2 teaspoons Italian seasoning
- ½ teaspoon lemon pepper
- 1 pinch salt and pepper to taste
- 2 tablespoons olive oil

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
2. Place sweet potatoes and water into a microwave-safe dish. Cook in the microwave on full power for 5 minutes; drain. Add olive oil, Italian seasoning, lemon pepper, salt, and pepper; toss to coat.
3. Arrange fries on a baking sheet in a single layer.
4. Bake in the preheated oven, turning once, until fries are crispy on the outside, about 30 minutes.
