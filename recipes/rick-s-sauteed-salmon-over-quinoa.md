## Ingredients

- 3 tablespoons olive oil, divided
- 1 (3 ounce) fillet salmon fillet
- 1  shallot, finely diced
- 1 clove garlic, thinly sliced
- 4 ounces fresh spinach, roughly chopped
- ½ cup cooked quinoa
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- 1  lemon wedge

## Instructions

1. Heat a skillet over high heat and add 1 tablespoon olive oil.
2. Add salmon, cover, and cook 4 minutes.
3. Heat another skillet over medium heat.
4. Add remaining olive oil, shallot, and garlic.
5. Cook until slightly browned, about 2 minutes.
6. Add spinach.
7. Turn salmon over.
8. Cook, uncovered, until fish flakes easily with fork, an additional 4 minutes.
9. Toss spinach until wilted; add quinoa.
10. Season with salt and pepper and mix well.
11. Plate spinach-quinoa mixture and lay salmon on top.
12. Add lemon wedge.
