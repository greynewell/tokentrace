## Ingredients

- 2 slices leftover meatloaf
- 4 thin slices  Cheddar cheese
- 1 teaspoon mayonnaise, or to taste
- 1 teaspoon ketchup, or to taste
- 1 teaspoon mustard, or to taste  (Optional)
- 2 slices bread, toasted

## Instructions

1. Place meatloaf slices in a nonstick pan over medium heat.
2. Lay 2 Cheddar cheese slices over each.
3. Cook until meatloaf is heated through and browned on the bottom, 3 to 5 minutes.
4. Flip 1 piece of meatloaf over the other so cheese is in the middle.
5. Spread mayonnaise, ketchup, and mustard over toast.
6. Add cheesy meatloaf and sandwich the toast together.
