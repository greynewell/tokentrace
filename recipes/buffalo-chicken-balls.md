## Ingredients

- 1 pound ground chicken
- 2 cups whole wheat bread crumbs
- 1 stalk celery, minced
- 1  egg
- ¼ cup melted butter
- ⅓ cup hot pepper sauce (such as Frank's RedHot®)
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- cooking spray

## Instructions

1. Combine ground chicken, bread crumbs, celery, and egg in a bowl.
2. Melt butter in a saucepan over low heat; stir in hot sauce.
3. Pour into chicken mixture; knead until well incorporated.
4. Season mixture with salt and pepper.
5. Cover and refrigerate for 1 hour.
6. Preheat oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C).
7. Spray a large baking dish with cooking spray.
8. Roll chicken mixture into 1-inch balls; place in a single layer on baking dish.
9. Bake in the preheated oven until no longer pink in the center, about 30 minutes.
10. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 165 degrees F (74 degrees C).
11. Remove from oven and rest for 5 minutes before serving.
