## Ingredients

- 1 (9 inch) frozen pie crust, thawed
- 1 ¾ pounds sliced bacon
- 3  eggs, lightly beaten
- 1 (12 ounce) can evaporated milk
- ½ teaspoon spicy brown mustard
- ¼ teaspoon ground nutmeg
- ½ cup all-purpose flour, or as needed
- 1 ½ cups shredded Swiss cheese

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Prick bottom and sides of pie crust and bake 10 minutes, until lightly brown; set aside to cool.
3. Place bacon in a skillet over medium-high heat and cook until evenly brown.
4. Drain, crumble and set aside.
5. In a bowl, mix eggs, milk, mustard and nutmeg.
6. In a separate bowl, toss bacon with flour to lightly coat.
7. Spread 3/4 bacon mixture and Swiss cheese over bottom of pie crust.
8. Pour egg mixture over bacon mixture.
9. Top with remaining bacon mixture.
10. Bake 1 hour in the preheated oven, or until a fork inserted in the center comes out clean.
11. Cool 10 minutes before serving.
