## Ingredients

- 36  fresh cheese tortellini
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 1 tablespoon minced garlic
- 24  large cooked shrimp
- 12  skewers
- 12  cherry tomatoes
- 1 (16 ounce) container sour cream
- 1 (1 ounce) package dry ranch dressing mix (such as Hidden Valley Ranch®)

## Instructions

1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
2. Cook tortellini in the boiling water until tender yet firm to the bite, 2 to 3 minutes; drain.
3. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat; cook and stir garlic until fragrant and tender, 1 to 2 minutes.
4. Add shrimp and cook until heated through, 2 to 3 minutes.
5. Mix tortellini into shrimp mixture, remove skillet from heat, and cool until easily handled.
6. Thread tortellini, shrimp, tortellini, shrimp, and tortellini onto a skewer, ending with a tomato.
7. Repeat with remaining ingredients.
8. Mix sour cream and ranch dressing mix together in a bowl; serve with skewers.
