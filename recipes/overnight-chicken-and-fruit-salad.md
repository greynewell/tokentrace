## Ingredients

- 3 cups cubed cooked chicken
- 1 pound seedless red grapes, halved
- 1 (15 ounce) can pineapple chunks, drained
- 1 cup sliced celery
- 1 (8 ounce) can sliced water chestnuts, drained
- 1 cup mayonnaise
- 1 tablespoon soy sauce
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice
- 1 teaspoon curry powder
- 1 cup toasted slivered almonds
- 1  papaya - peeled, seeded, and sliced
- 1  avocado - peeled, pitted, and sliced

## Instructions

1. Combine chicken, grapes, pineapple chunks, celery, and water chestnuts in a large salad bowl.
2. Mix mayonnaise, soy sauce, lemon juice, and curry powder in a separate bowl.
3. Pour dressing over salad and toss lightly to coat.
4. Cover and chill 8 hours to overnight.
5. Stir almonds into salad just before serving; garnish with papaya and avocado slices.
