## Ingredients

- ½ cup mayonnaise
- 1 clove garlic, crushed
- ⅛ teaspoon paprika
- ½ cup couscous
- ⅔ cup orange juice
- 1 (14.75 ounce) can red salmon, drained
- 1 (10 ounce) package frozen chopped spinach - thawed, drained and squeezed dry
- 2  egg yolks, beaten
- 2 cloves garlic, crushed
- 1 teaspoon ground cumin
- ½ teaspoon ground black pepper
- ½ teaspoon salt
- 3 tablespoons olive oil

## Instructions

1. In a small stainless steel or glass bowl, stir together mayonnaise, garlic, and paprika.
2. Set aside.
3. Prepare couscous according to package directions using the 2/3 cup of orange juice in place of water.
4. In a mixing bowl, combine the cooked couscous, red salmon, drained spinach, egg yolks, garlic, cumin, black pepper, and salt.
5. Form into patties.
6. In a large skillet over medium heat, heat the olive oil and fry patties until golden brown turning once, about 8 to 10 minutes.
7. Serve with garlic mayonnaise.
