## Ingredients

- 4 slices whole grain bread
- 4 ounces sliced fontina cheese or brie cheese
- ¼ cup whole berry cranberry sauce
- 1 cup baby kale
- 4 ounces sliced roasted turkey
- 2 tablespoons Hellmann's® or Best Foods® Real Mayonnaise

## Instructions

1. Evenly top 2 bread slices with cheese, cranberry sauce , kale, then turkey.
2. Top with remaining 2 bread slices.
3. Spread outside of sandwiches evenly with Hellmann's® or Best Foods® Real Mayonnaise.
4. Cook sandwiches in large nonstick skillet over medium heat, turning once, until golden brown and cheese is melted, about 5 minutes.
