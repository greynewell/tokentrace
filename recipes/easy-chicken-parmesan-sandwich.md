## Ingredients

- 6  frozen chicken nuggets
- 2 tablespoons tomato sauce
- 1  hamburger bun, split
- 1 slice American cheese
- 1 tablespoon grated Parmesan cheese

## Instructions

1. Place the chicken nuggets on a microwave-safe plate.
2. Cook in microwave until hot in the middle, about 2 minutes.
3. Spread 1 tablespoon tomato sauce on one piece of the hamburger bun; cover the sauce with the American cheese slice.
4. Arrange the chicken nuggets onto the cheese.
5. Pour the remaining tomato sauce over the nuggets; sprinkle with the Parmesan cheese.
6. Top with the other half of the hamburger bun to finish the sandwich.
