## Ingredients

- 1 head iceberg lettuce, quartered
- 1 cup Marie's® Chunky Blue Cheese Dressing
- 4 slices cooked bacon, chopped
- 2  green onions, diced
- 2 small tomatoes, chopped

## Instructions

1. Divide iceberg head into 4 quarters and place each wedge on individual salad plates.
2. Top each wedge with 1/4 cup Marie's Chunky Blue Cheese dressing.
3. Sprinkle bacon, green onions, and tomatoes on top of each wedge.
