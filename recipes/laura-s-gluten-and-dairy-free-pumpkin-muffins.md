## Ingredients

- 1 ½ cups rolled oats
- 1 cup coconut flour
- 1 teaspoon baking soda
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
- ¼ teaspoon ground nutmeg
- ¼ teaspoon ground cloves
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- 1 cup applesauce
- 1 cup pumpkin puree
- ¾ cup almond milk
- 4  eggs, slightly beaten
- ¼ cup coconut oil
- ¼ cup agave nectar
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
2. Place cupcake liners in 18 muffin cups.
3. Mix rolled oats, coconut flour, baking soda, baking powder, cinnamon, nutmeg, cloves, and salt together in a bowl.
4. Mix applesauce, pumpkin puree, almond milk, eggs, coconut oil, agave nectar, and vanilla extract together in a separate bowl until thoroughly blended.
5. Add flour mixture and stir just until flour is incorporated into the batter, about 1 minute.
6. Fill muffin cups evenly with batter.
7. Bake in preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into a muffin comes out clean, 20 to 25 minutes.
