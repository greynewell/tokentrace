## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon butter
- 2 (16 ounce) packages baby carrots
- 3 tablespoons honey
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 teaspoon salt
- ½ teaspoon black pepper
- ⅓ cup water

## Instructions

1. Melt butter in a large skillet over medium heat. Stir in carrots, honey, garlic, salt, and pepper. Pour in water, cover, and bring to a boil. Cook for 4 minutes.
2. Remove the lid and continue to cook, stirring often, until carrots are tender, 10 to 15 minutes.
