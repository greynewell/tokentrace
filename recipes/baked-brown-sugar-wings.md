## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
- 2 teaspoons packed brown sugar
- 2 teaspoons onion powder
- 2 teaspoons garlic powder
- 2 teaspoons chili powder
- 2 teaspoons paprika
- 1 teaspoon sea salt
- 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
- 2 pounds frozen chicken wings, thawed

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C). Line a baking sheet with foil.
2. Combine oil, brown sugar, onion powder, garlic powder, chili powder, paprika, salt, and black pepper in a bowl. Rub over chicken wings and transfer to the prepared baking sheet.
3. Bake in the preheated oven until browned and no longer pink in the centers, about 25 minutes.
