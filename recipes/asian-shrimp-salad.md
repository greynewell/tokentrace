## Ingredients

- ⅓ pound small shrimp, peeled, deveined and cooked
- 1 large orange, peeled and cut into half slices
- ⅓ cup chopped red bell pepper
- 3 tablespoons sliced green onions
- Asian Pineapple Sesame Dressing (recipe below)
- 4 cups DOLE® Hearts of Romaine or other DOLE Salad blend

## Instructions

1. Combine shrimp, orange, bell pepper, and green onions in medium bowl.
2. Toss with Asian Pineapple Dressing, to taste.
3. Divide salad on 2 large plates.
4. Spoon shrimp mixture over each salad.
5. Refrigerate leftovers.
