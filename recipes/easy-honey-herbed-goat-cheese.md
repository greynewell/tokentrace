## Ingredients

- 8 ounces goat cheese
- 1 tablespoon shallot, minced
- 1 ½ tablespoons Aunt Sue’s® Raw & Unfiltered Honey, plus more to finish
- 1 tablespoon creme fraiche  (Optional)
- 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
- 2 teaspoons chopped fresh chives, plus optional chive batons to garnish
- 1 teaspoon salt

## Instructions

1. Fold all ingredients well to combine.
2. Serve with toasted baguette and drizzle with Aunt Sue's® Raw & Unfiltered Honey.
3. Garnish with chive batons if desired.
