## Ingredients

- 2 large zucchini (Italian squash)
- 2 large Gold Rush zucchini
- 2 large yellow (crookneck) squash
- ¼ pound bacon, diced
- 1 medium red onion, diced

## Instructions

1. Quarter zucchini, Gold Rush zucchini, and crookneck squash lengthwise and cut into 1/2-inch chunks.
2. Heat a skillet over medium heat.
3. Cook and stir bacon and onion in the hot skillet until onion is tender, about 5 minutes.
4. Add zucchini, Gold Rush zucchini, and crookneck squash and cook until tender and bacon is crispy, about 10 minutes.
