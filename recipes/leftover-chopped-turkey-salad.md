## Ingredients

- 2 cups cooked turkey meat, chopped
- 2 stalks celery with leaves, chopped
- ½ small red onion, chopped
- ½  avocado, peeled, pitted, and chopped
- ⅓ cup mayonnaise, or more to taste
- ⅛ cup reduced-fat balsamic vinaigrette dressing
- ½ teaspoon garlic salt
- freshly ground black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Mix turkey, celery, red onion, avocado, mayonnaise, balsamic dressing, garlic salt, and pepper together in a medium-sized bowl.
2. Adjust to taste.
3. Cover with plastic wrap and chill in a refrigerator before serving.
