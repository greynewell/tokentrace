## Ingredients

- ½ (1 ounce) package dry onion soup mix
- 1 (1.8 ounce) package dry leek soup mix
- ½ teaspoon dried dill weed
- ½ cup mayonnaise
- 1 (16 ounce) container sour cream

## Instructions

1. In a medium bowl, mix together dry onion soup mix, dry leek soup mix, dried dill weed, mayonnaise and sour cream.
2. Allow the mixture to chill in the refrigerator at least 1 hour before serving.
