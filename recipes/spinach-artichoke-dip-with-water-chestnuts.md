## Ingredients

- 1 (14 ounce) can artichoke hearts, drained and chopped
- ½ (10 ounce) package frozen chopped spinach, thawed
- ½ (8 ounce) can sliced water chestnuts, drained and finely chopped
- ½ cup sour cream
- ¼ cup mayonnaise
- ¼ cup cream cheese
- ¼ cup grated Romano cheese
- ¼ teaspoon minced garlic

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
2. Mix artichoke hearts, spinach, water chestnuts, sour cream, mayonnaise, cream cheese, Romano cheese, and garlic together in a small baking dish. Cover dish with aluminum foil.
3. Bake in the preheated oven until cooked through and bubbling, about 25 minutes.
