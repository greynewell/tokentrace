## Ingredients

- 2 slices bread
- 2 slices Cheddar cheese
- ¼ cup chopped broccoli
- ¼ cup chopped zucchini
- ¼ cup chopped green bell pepper
- 1 tablespoon chopped jalapeno pepper
- 3 tablespoons butter

## Instructions

1. Arrange bread slices on a work surface.
2. Place Cheddar cheese slices on top of 1 bread slice.
3. Layer broccoli, zucchini, green bell pepper, and jalapeno pepper on top of cheese.
4. Place the second piece of bread on top of vegetables, creating a sandwich.
5. Heat a non-stick skillet over medium-high heat.
6. Melt 1 1/2 tablespoons butter in the hot skillet.
7. Add sandwich to skillet, cover with a lid, and cook until bread is golden, about 45 seconds.
8. Flip sandwich, add remaining butter, and cook until bottom is golden, about 45 seconds more.
