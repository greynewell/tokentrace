## Ingredients

- 2 cups all-purpose flour
- 1 teaspoon salt
- ¼ teaspoon ground white pepper
- ¼ teaspoon dry mustard
- ¾ cup butter, chilled
- ½ cup shredded Cheddar cheese
- 6 tablespoons cold water, or as needed

## Instructions

1. In a medium bowl, stir together the flour, salt, white pepper and mustard.
2. Cut in butter with a fork until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
3. Stir in Cheddar cheese.
4. Stir in water one tablespoon at a time, until the dough is able to hold together.
5. Press the mixture into a ball, wrap and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.
6. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
7. Line baking sheets with parchment paper.
8. On a lightly floured surface, roll the dough out to a rectangle that is 16x12 inches.
9. The dough should be about 1/8 inch thick.
10. Cut into 1x3 inch strips.
11. Place the strips onto the prepared baking sheets spaced about 1 inch apart.
12. Bake for 10 to 12 minutes in the preheated oven, or until golden and crispy.
13. Cool completely before storing.
