## Ingredients

- 2 ⅛ cups mayonnaise
- 2 cups shredded smoked Gouda cheese
- 1 cup shredded Gouda cheese with chipotle peppers (such as Boar's Head®)
- ¾ cup chopped onion
- 4 ounces chopped cooked bacon

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Mix mayonnaise, smoked Gouda, Gouda with chipotle peppers, onion, and bacon together in a bowl; spread into a baking dish.
3. Bake in preheated oven until mixture is bubbly and lightly browned along the edges, 15 to 20 minutes.
