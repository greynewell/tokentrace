## Ingredients

- 8 cups water
- 1 teaspoon garlic powder
- 1 tablespoon hot pepper sauce
- 2  carrots, sliced thin
- 4 ounces fresh mushrooms
- 1 (10 ounce) package frozen okra, thawed and sliced
- ¼ cup uncooked wild rice
- 1  skinless, boneless chicken breast half - cut into cubes
- 1 ½ cups uncooked rotini pasta
- salt to taste
- ground black pepper to taste
- 3  green onions, thinly sliced

## Instructions

1. Bring water to a boil. Season with garlic powder and hot pepper sauce. Add in carrots and mushrooms. Cook for 5 minutes.
2. Add okra, wild rice, and chicken. Reduce the heat to low, and cook for 3 hours.
3. Add pasta and cook for 10 minutes. Season with salt and pepper. Serve hot, garnished with green onions.
