## Ingredients

- 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
- 1 (8 ounce) jar cocktail sauce
- ½ pound cooked baby shrimp
- 3  green onions, chopped
- 1 (2.25 ounce) can sliced black olives, drained

## Instructions

1. Spread cream cheese on a medium round serving platter.
2. Layer with cocktail sauce.
3. Top with baby shrimp, green onions and olives.
4. Chill in the refrigerator at least 30 minutes before serving.
