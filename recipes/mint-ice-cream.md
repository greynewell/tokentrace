## Ingredients

- 4 cups fresh mint leaves, or to taste
- 2 cups milk
- 1 cup white sugar
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- ½ teaspoon salt
- 2 cups heavy cream
- 1 cup mini chocolate chips

## Instructions

1. Blend mint, milk, sugar, vanilla, and salt together on high speed until the mint leaf bits are tiny, about 1 minute. Add heavy cream and blend until uniform, about 15 seconds.
2. Pour mixture into an ice cream maker and freeze according to manufacturer's instructions, about 20 minutes, adding chocolate chips during the last 5 minutes. Transfer to an airtight container and freeze until firm, about 4 hours.
