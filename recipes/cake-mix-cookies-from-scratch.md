## Ingredients

- 1 cup all-purpose flour
- ¾ cup white sugar
- 3 tablespoons dry milk powder
- 1 ½ teaspoons baking powder
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- 1  egg
- ¼ cup vegetable oil
- ½ teaspoon vanilla extract
- ⅓ cup chocolate chips

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Mix flour, sugar, dry milk, baking powder, and salt in a medium bowl.
3. Combine egg, oil and vanilla extract in a small bowl; mix well. Add to flour mixture and stir until no longer dry but still slightly crumbly. Stir in chocolate chips.
4. Roll dough into a large ball or tube; form into 1- to 1 1/2-inch balls. Arrange on cookie sheets and flatten if desired.
5. Bake in the preheated oven until edges are golden, 7 to 8 minutes. Do not overbake. Cool on the cookie sheets for 1 minute before removing to a wire rack to cool completely.
