## Ingredients

- ½ cup bread crumbs
- 3 (4 ounce) fillets swai fish
- ½ cup creamy salad dressing (such as Miracle Whip®)
- ¾ cup shredded Asiago cheese
- salt and ground black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Put bread crumbs in a shallow dish.
3. Press swai fish, skin-side down, in bread crumbs.
4. Spread creamy salad dressing onto exposed side of each fillet.
5. Arrange fillets in a baking dish and top with Asiago cheese; season with salt and pepper.
6. Bake in the preheated oven until fish flakes easily with a fork, 45 to 60 minutes.
