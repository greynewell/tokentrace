## Ingredients

- 8 ounces Gorgonzola cheese, crumbled
- ⅓ cup minced red onion
- ½ cup minced fresh parsley
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice
- ⅓ cup olive oil

## Instructions

1. Mix together the Gorgonzola cheese, onion, parsley, and garlic in a bowl until well blended.
2. Whisk together the vinegar, lemon juice, and olive oil in a separate bowl until well blended.
3. Stir the vinegar mixture into the cheese mixture until well blended.
4. Refrigerate at least 4 hours, or longer.
5. Serve at room temperature.
