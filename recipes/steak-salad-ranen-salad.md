## Ingredients

- 1 ½ pounds beef sirloin steak
- 8 cups romaine lettuce, torn into bite-size pieces
- 6  roma (plum) tomatoes, sliced
- ½ cup sliced fresh mushrooms
- ¾ cup crumbled blue cheese
- ¼ cup walnuts
- ⅓ cup vegetable oil
- 3 tablespoons red wine vinegar
- 2 tablespoons lemon juice
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ⅛ teaspoon ground black pepper
- 3 teaspoons Worcestershire sauce
- ⅛ teaspoon liquid smoke flavoring

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven on broiler setting.
2. Broil steaks for 3 to 5 minutes per side, or to desired doneness.
3. Allow to cool, then slice into bite-size pieces.
4. On chilled plates, arrange lettuce, tomatoes, and mushrooms.
5. Sprinkle with blue cheese and walnuts.
6. Top with steak slices.
7. In a small bowl, whisk together oil, vinegar, lemon juice, salt, pepper, Worcestershire sauce, and smoke flavoring.
8. Drizzle over salad.
