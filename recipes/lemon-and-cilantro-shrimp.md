## Ingredients

- 20  jumbo shrimp, peeled and deveined
- 6  lemons, zested
- 2 cups chopped fresh cilantro
- ¼ cup extra virgin olive oil
- salt to taste
- 1  lemon, juiced
- ground black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. In a bowl, toss shrimp with lemon zest and cilantro to coat.
2. Heat the oil in a skillet over medium heat.
3. Sprinkle oil with salt, then stir in the shrimp, lemon zest, and cilantro.
4. Cook, stirring, 5 minutes, or until shrimp are opaque.
5. Transfer to a serving bowl, drizzle with remaining oil, lemon zest, and olive oil from the skillet.
6. Squeeze lemon juice over shrimp, season with pepper, and serve immediately.
