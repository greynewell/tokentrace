## Ingredients

- 1 ounce Baileys® Original Irish Cream or Baileys® Red Velvet
- ½ ounce Ketel One Vodka
- Whipped Cream
- Multi-colored Sprinkles

## Instructions

1. Mix ingredients in a cocktail shaker with ice.
2. Strain into a shot glass.
3. Add whipped cream and sprinkles.
