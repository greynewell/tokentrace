## Ingredients

- 1 ½ ounces tequila
- ¾ ounce lime juice
- ¼ ounce orange juice
- ¾ ounce Truvia® Simple Syrup**
- 1 pinch Sea salt and lime for garnish

## Instructions

1. In a cocktail shaker add all the ingredients together and fill halfway with ice.
2. Shake vigorously.
3. Double strain into glass filled with ice and garnish.
