## Ingredients

- 2 pints heavy cream, chilled
- ⅓ cup unsweetened cocoa powder
- ¾ cup chocolate syrup
- 2 (14 ounce) cans sweetened condensed milk
- ¼ teaspoon ground cinnamon

## Instructions

1. In a large bowl, beat cream with cocoa until stiff peaks form.
2. Stir in chocolate syrup, sweetened condensed milk and cinnamon.
3. Pour into a shallow dish or plastic container, cover and freeze 8 hours or until firm.
