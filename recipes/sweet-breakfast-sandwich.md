## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons chocolate-hazelnut spread (such as Nutella®)
- 2  English muffins, split and toasted
- 1  banana, sliced
- 2 tablespoons marshmallow cream

## Instructions

1. Spread 1 tablespoon chocolate-hazelnut spread on 1 half of each English muffin; arrange banana slices on top.
2. Spread 1 tablespoon marshmallow cream on the other half.
3. Press both halves together to create a sandwich.
