## Ingredients

- 3 cups shredded cooked or leftover beef
- 1 cup ketchup
- 2 cups barbecue sauce
- 4  hamburger buns
- ½ cup sliced onion

## Instructions

1. Heat meat in a skillet over medium heat. Stir in the ketchup and barbecue sauce. Heat stirring constantly until the sauces and meat are sticky.
2. Serve meat on hamburger buns with sliced onion.
