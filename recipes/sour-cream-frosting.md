## Ingredients

- 4 tablespoons butter, softened
- ½ cup sour cream
- ½ teaspoon vanilla extract
- ½ teaspoon lemon juice
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- 2 ¾ cups confectioners' sugar

## Instructions

1. Beat sour cream, butter, vanilla, lemon juice, and salt in a mixing bowl until well combined, 2 to 3 minutes.
2. Gradually mix in confectioners' sugar until smooth.
