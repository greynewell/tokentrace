## Ingredients

- 3 tablespoons olive oil, divided, or more as needed
- 1  shallot, minced
- ¾ cup chopped hazelnuts
- 2 ¾ cups broccoli florets
- 1 tablespoon finely chopped fresh parsley
- 1 teaspoon chopped fresh dill
- 1 pinch salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
- 1 pinch ground nutmeg

## Instructions

1. Heat 1 tablespoon olive oil in a skillet and cook shallot until soft and translucent, about 5 minutes.
2. Add hazelnuts and cook until toasted, about 2 minutes.
3. Remove from heat and cool for 10 minutes.
4. Meanwhile, bring a pot of lightly salted water to a boil and cook broccoli until softened, about 3 minutes.
5. Drain and rinse under cold water.
6. Drain well.
7. Combine shallot-hazelnut mixture, broccoli, parsley, and dill in the bowl of a food processor; pulse until blended.
8. Pour 2 tablespoons olive oil in slowly, with the processor running, until spread is smooth.
9. Add more olive oil if needed.
10. Season with salt, pepper, and nutmeg.
