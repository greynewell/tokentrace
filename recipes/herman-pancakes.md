## Ingredients

- 1 cup all-purpose flour
- ¾ teaspoon baking soda
- ½ teaspoon salt
- 2 tablespoons white sugar
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- 1 cup Herman Sourdough Starter
- ⅓ cup vegetable oil
- 2  eggs
- ½ cup milk

## Instructions

1. In a large bowl, mix together flour, baking soda, salt, sugar and baking powder.
2. Add the sourdough starter, oil, eggs and milk; beat well.
3. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium high heat.
4. Pour or scoop the batter onto the griddle, using approximately 1/4 cup for each pancake.
5. Brown on both sides and serve hot.
