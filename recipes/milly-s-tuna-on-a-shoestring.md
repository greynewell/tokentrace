## Ingredients

- 1 (5 ounce) can tuna, drained
- 1 cup shredded carrot
- 1 cup diced celery
- ¼ cup minced onion
- ¾ cup mayonnaise, or to taste
- ½ (9 ounce) can shoestring potatoes

## Instructions

1. Break the tuna apart in a bowl.
2. Add the carrot, celery, onion, and mayonnaise; stir to mix.
3. Fold in the shoestring potatoes just before serving.
