## Ingredients

- 1 cup hulled and sliced strawberries
- ½ teaspoon white sugar
- 1 tablespoon brandy-based orange liqueur (such as Grand Marnier®)
- 1 (16 ounce) package prepared polenta
- 1 tablespoon olive oil, or as needed
- 1 pinch salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
- cooking spray

## Instructions

1. Place the sliced strawberries into a small bowl, add sugar and brandy, and stir to coat. Let stand while you prepare the polenta.
2. Cut polenta into eight 1/2- to 3/4-inch-thick slices. Lightly brush the slices with olive oil and sprinkle with salt and pepper.
3. Preheat an outdoor grill for medium-high heat and lightly spray the grate.
4. Grill the polenta slices for 7 to 10 minutes per side, turning once. Place 2 slices on a dessert plate and top with 1/4 of the strawberry mixture.
