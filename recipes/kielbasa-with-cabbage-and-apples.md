## Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon olive oil
- 1 ½ pounds kielbasa sausage, cut into 1-inch pieces
- 1 (12 fluid ounce) bottle hard apple cider
- ½  onion
- 1 head green cabbage, coarsely chopped
- 1  Granny Smith apple, cored and sliced
- ½ cup applesauce
- 1 teaspoon caraway seeds
- ½ teaspoon kosher salt
- ½ teaspoon freshly ground black pepper

## Instructions

1. Heat oil in a heavy-bottomed pot over medium heat.
2. Add kielbasa; cook and stir until browned, about 5 minutes.
3. Pour in some apple cider.
4. Scrape the browned bits off of the bottom of the pot with a wooden spoon.
5. Stir onion into the pot; cook and stir until softened, about 5 minutes more.
6. Add cabbage, cider, Granny Smith apple, applesauce, caraway, salt, and pepper.
7. Reduce heat to low; simmer until cabbage softens, 20 to 25 minutes.
