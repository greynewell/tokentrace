## Ingredients

- 1 (16 ounce) package soft style cream cheese with pineapple
- ¾ pound shredded Swiss cheese
- 2 cups dried cranberries
- 2 tablespoons orange juice
- ¼ cup apple juice

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 375  degrees F (190 degrees C).
2. In a medium bowl, blend the cream cheese, Swiss cheese, cranberries, orange juice, and apple juice.
3. Scoop into a 9 inch pie pan.
4. Bake 15 minutes in the preheated oven, until bubbly and lightly browned.
