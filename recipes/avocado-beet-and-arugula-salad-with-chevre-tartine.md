## Ingredients

- 4 ounces goat cheese, softened
- 1 teaspoon dried basil
- 4  (1/2 inch thick) slices crusty bread
- 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
- 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
- salt and pepper to taste
- ¼ cup olive oil
- 1 (15 ounce) can sliced beets, drained and diced
- 1 (10 ounce) package mixed salad greens with arugula
- 2  avocados - peeled, pitted and diced
- ⅓ cup chopped toasted hazelnuts

## Instructions

1. Preheat broiler for high heat.
2. Line a baking sheet with foil.
3. Mix the goat cheese with the basil in a bowl.
4. Spread cheese mixture evenly on four slices of bread.
5. Place on prepared baking sheet.
6. Cook under preheated broiler until cheese has begun to turn golden brown, 3 to 5 minutes.
7. Remove and set aside.
8. To make the vinaigrette, whisk together the mustard and vinegar in a small bowl until blended; season with salt and pepper to taste.
9. Slowly pour in olive oil while continually whisking until dressing is smooth.
10. Place diced beets and mixed greens into a large bowl.
11. Drizzle vinaigrette over top; toss to coat.
12. Divide salad onto four chilled plates.
13. Top with avocado and hazelnuts.
14. Serve with goat-cheese tartines.
