## Ingredients

- 10 cups water
- 1 (1 pound) package smoked sausage, sliced
- 1 ½ cups chopped carrot
- 1 cup chopped celery
- 1  onion, chopped
- 5 cubes  chicken bouillon
- 2  bay leaves
- ½ teaspoon garlic powder
- ½ teaspoon dried oregano
- 1 pound dried black-eyed peas

## Instructions

1. Pour 10 cups water into a 5-quart slow cooker crock; add sausage, carrot, celery, onion, chicken bouillon, bay leaves, garlic powder, and oregano.
2. Pour black-eyed peas into a saucepan; add enough water to cover by a few inches.
3. Bring water to a boil, remove saucepan from heat, drain the peas, and add them to the slow cooker.
4. Cook on Low for 8 hours.
5. Remove and discard bay leaves before serving.
