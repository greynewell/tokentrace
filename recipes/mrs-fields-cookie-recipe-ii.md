## Ingredients

- 2 cups butter, softened
- 2 cups white sugar
- 2 cups packed brown sugar
- 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 2 teaspoons baking soda
- 4  eggs
- 4 cups all-purpose flour
- 5 cups rolled oats
- 4 cups semisweet chocolate chips
- 8 ounces finely grated chocolate bar
- 3 cups chopped walnuts

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (180 degrees C).
2. Cream together butter, white sugar, and brown sugar. Add eggs and vanilla.
3. Pulverize oats in a blender.
4. Mix pulverized oats, flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt together in a separate bowl. Stir into butter mixture until combined.
5. Add chocolate chips, walnuts, and chocolate and stir until combined.
6. Place golfball-sized balls of dough onto greased cookie sheets, about 2 inches apart.
7. Bake in the preheated oven, working in batches as needed, until golden, 8 to 10 minutes. Do not overbake.
