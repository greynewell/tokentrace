## Ingredients

- 2 cups plain yogurt
- 2 cloves crushed garlic
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
- ¼ cup chopped fresh mint leaves
- 1 large cucumber - peeled, seeded and shredded

## Instructions

1. Use a cheese cloth to strain the yogurt over a bowl for 3 to 4 hours, until most of the water has drained.
2. Press excess liquid out of the shredded cucumber.
3. In a medium bowl, stir together the cucumber and strained yogurt.
4. Mix in the garlic, salt, pepper and mint.
5. Chill the mixture for 1 to 2 hours.
