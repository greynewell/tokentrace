## Ingredients

- ¾ cup yellow stone-ground grits
- 3 cups milk
- ⅛ cup extra virgin olive oil
- 1 teaspoon butter
- 1 small onion, finely chopped
- 1 pound shrimp, peeled and deveined
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- ⅛ teaspoon white pepper

## Instructions

1. In a medium saucepan, bring the milk to a boil.
2. Stir in the grits, and reduce heat to low.
3. Cook, stirring occasionally, until silky, about 10 minutes.
4. Meanwhile, heat olive oil and butter in a skillet over medium heat.
5. Saute onions until tender, then toss in shrimp.
6. Season with salt and pepper, and cook 4 to 5 minutes, or until shrimp turn pink.
7. Stir shrimp mixture into grits, and continue cooking for 10 to 15 minutes.
8. Serve hot.
