## Ingredients

- ¼ cup olive oil, divided
- 1  onion, diced small
- 1  carrot, diced small
- 3 cloves garlic, pressed
- 2 links Merguez sausage, casing removed and discarded
- 1 (16 ounce) can butter beans (large lima beans), drained and rinsed
- 1 cup water
- 1  tomato, diced small
- 4 leaves kale, stems removed and leaves coarsely chopped
- 2 tablespoons basil pesto
- 10 leaves fresh basil leaves

## Instructions

1. Heat 2 tablespoons olive oil in a heavy pot over medium heat; cook and stir onion, carrot, and garlic in hot oil until the onion is translucent, about 3 minutes.
2. Break sausage meat into small pieces and stir into vegetable mixture; cook and stir until meat is slightly golden, about 5 minutes.
3. Stir butter beans, water, and tomato into sausage mixture; bring to a simmer and cook until tomatoes soften, about 5 minutes.
4. Stir kale and pesto into the sausage mixture; cook and stir until kale wilts slightly, 3 to 5 minutes.
5. Remove pot from heat and stir basil leaves into soup.
