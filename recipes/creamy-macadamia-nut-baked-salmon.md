## Ingredients

- butter-flavored cooking spray
- 6 tablespoons sour cream
- 1 (1 ounce) package vegetable dip mix, or to taste
- 1 pinch garlic salt, or to taste
- 1 pinch Creole seasoning, or to taste
- 4 (4 ounce) salmon fillets
- ½ cup chopped macadamia nuts

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C).
2. Line a baking sheet with aluminum foil and spray with cooking spray.
3. Mix sour cream, vegetable dip mix, garlic salt, and Creole seasoning together in a bowl.
4. Arrange salmon fillets, skin-side down, on the prepared baking sheet.
5. Spread sour cream mixture over each fillet.
6. Bake in the preheated oven until fish almost flakes easily with a fork, 7 to 8 minutes; sprinkle macadamia nuts over fillets.
7. Turn on oven's broiler.
8. Broil until nuts are browned and fish flakes easily with a fork, 2 to 3 minutes more.
