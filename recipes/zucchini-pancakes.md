## Ingredients

- 4  eggs
- 2 cups grated zucchini
- ¾ cup all-purpose flour
- ½ teaspoon white sugar
- ½ teaspoon salt
- 3 tablespoons olive oil
- 4 teaspoons baking powder
- ¼ cup butter, melted

## Instructions

1. Preheat grill to 425 to 450 degrees F (220 to 225 degrees C).
2. In a large mixing bowl, beat eggs, add shredded zucchini and mix well using a fork.
3. Add flour, sugar, salt and vegetable oil and stir to blend well.
4. Finally, add baking powder and mix well using a  large spoon.
5. The batter's consistency should be like heavy whipping cream.
6. Spoon batter on hot grill (about 2 tablespoons) for each pancake.
7. Cook until there are no longer bubbles forming in  the pancake about 2 minutes; turn over and cook for 2 minutes longer.
8. Rub pancakes with melted butter and serve immediately.
