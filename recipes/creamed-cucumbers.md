## Ingredients

- salt to taste
- 3  cucumbers, peeled and thinly sliced
- 1 (12 ounce) can evaporated milk
- 2 tablespoons white vinegar
- ground black pepper, to taste

## Instructions

1. Sprinkle salt over the cucumber slices in a large bowl, making sure all slices get some salt.
2. Set aside until liquid drains from cucumber slices, 3 to 4 hours.
3. Drain liquid.
4. Squeeze cucumber slices with paper towel to express as much moisture from them as possible.
5. Mix evaporated milk and vinegar together in a glass bowl until thickened.
6. Fold cucumber slices into the milk mixture; season with black pepper.
