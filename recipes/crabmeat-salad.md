## Ingredients

- 1 (16 ounce) package imitation crabmeat
- ¾ cup creamy salad dressing (e.g. Miracle Whip)
- 3 stalks celery, chopped
- 5 tablespoons finely chopped onion
- 1  carrot, shredded

## Instructions

1. Shred imitation crab meat into bite sized pieces.
2. Combine with salad dressing, celery, onions and carrot.
3. Chill for 1 hour before serving.
