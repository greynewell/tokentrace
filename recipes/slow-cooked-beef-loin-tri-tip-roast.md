## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons olive oil, or as needed, divided
- 2 ¼ pounds seasoned beef loin tri-tip roast
- 1  yellow onion, halved and sliced
- ½ cup water
- 1  beef bouillon cube
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- 1 pinch adobo seasoning (such as Goya®), or more to taste

## Instructions

1. Coat the inside of a slow cooker with about 1 tablespoon olive oil.
2. Coat the inside of a skillet with about 1 tablespoon olive oil and heat over medium-high heat. Cook roast in the hot oil until browned, about 5 minutes per side. Cook and stir onion in the hot oil during the last 5 minutes of browning the roast. Transfer roast to the slow cooker, reserving onion in the skillet.
3. Pour water over onion; crumble bouillon cube into water. Add garlic and adobo seasoning to onion mixture; cook and stir until onion is tender, about 5 minutes. Pour mixture over roast.
4. Cook on High until meat is cooked through and tender, about 4 hours.
