## Ingredients

- 1 head cauliflower, cut into florets
- ¼ cup butter
- 1 small sweet onion (such as Vidalia®), diced
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- 9  pitted kalamata olives
- 1 teaspoon dried parsley
- salt to taste
- 1  lemon, zested

## Instructions

1. Cut cauliflower into very finely chopped pieces similar to real couscous or rice.
2. Melt butter in a medium skillet over medium heat.
3. Add onion and garlic; cook and stir until onion has softened, about 2 minutes.
4. Add cauliflower and cook on medium heat for about 40 minutes.
5. Stir every 5 minutes until entire batch is golden and nutty.
6. Mix kalamata olives, parsley, salt, and lemon zest into cauliflower.
