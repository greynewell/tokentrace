## Ingredients

- 1 ½ cups ice
- 2 fluid ounces tequila
- 4 fluid ounces orange juice
- 1 cup ice
- ¾ fluid ounce grenadine syrup

## Instructions

1. Fill a highball glass with 1 1/2 cups ice and set aside.
2. Combine orange juice and tequila in a cocktail shaker; add remaining 1 cup ice. Cover and shake until the outside of the shaker has frosted. Strain into the prepared highball glass. Slowly pour in grenadine and let settle. Stir before drinking.
