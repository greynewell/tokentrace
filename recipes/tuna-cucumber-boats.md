## Ingredients

- 1 (5 ounce) can tuna, drained
- 1 medium carrot, finely chopped
- 1 stalk celery, finely chopped
- 2 tablespoons finely chopped onion
- 2 tablespoons relish
- 1 ½ tablespoons prepared yellow mustard
- 1 ½ tablespoons Dijon mustard
- 1 medium cucumber, halved lengthwise and seeded

## Instructions

1. Combine tuna, carrot, celery, onion, relish, yellow mustard, and Dijon mustard in a bowl; mix until well blended.
2. Fill each cucumber half with the tuna mixture.
