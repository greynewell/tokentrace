## Ingredients

- 2 teaspoons butter, or as needed
- 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
- 1  tomato, diced
- ½ cup diced cooked ham
- ½ teaspoon onion powder
- ¼ teaspoon dried parsley
- 9  eggs
- ¾ cup shredded Cheddar cheese
- 2 tablespoons milk
- salt and ground black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Coat a large 6-cup muffin tin with butter.
3. Heat oil in a skillet over medium heat.
4. Add tomato, ham, onion powder, and parsley.
5. Cook and stir until tomato is warmed through and juice has evaporated, about 5 minutes.
6. Whisk eggs, Cheddar cheese, and milk together in a large bowl with salt and pepper.
7. Divide the ham mixture among the 6 muffin cups.
8. Cover with egg mixture until about 3/4 full.
9. Place muffin tin in a larger baking pan filled with hot water.
10. Bake in the preheated oven until set, about 25 minutes.
11. Cover with aluminum foil if tops start browning too much.
