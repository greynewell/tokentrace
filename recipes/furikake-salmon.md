## Ingredients

- 4  salmon steaks
- ½ teaspoon garlic salt, or to taste
- ground black pepper to taste
- 1 tablespoon mayonnaise, or more to taste
- 2 teaspoons honey
- 1 (1.7 ounce) container nori fumi furikake (seaweed and sesame rice seasoning)
- ¼ cup oil for frying, or as needed

## Instructions

1. Season salmon with garlic salt and black pepper.
2. Combine mayonnaise and honey in a small bowl and spread on top side of salmon steaks.
3. Place furikake in a shallow bowl.
4. Dip top side of each salmon steak in furikake; press down lightly for coating to stick.
5. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
6. Place salmon coated-side down and cook until golden brown, about 5 minutes.
7. Turn over and continue cooking until salmon flakes easily with a fork, about 5 minutes more.
