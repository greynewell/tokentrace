## Ingredients

- 1 ½ cups high protein crisp rice and wheat cereal
- 1 ¼ cups warm water (110 degrees F)
- 1 tablespoon honey
- 1 tablespoon molasses
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 tablespoon butter, softened
- 2 cups bread flour
- 2 cups whole wheat flour
- 1 ½ teaspoons active dry yeast

## Instructions

1. Place all  ingredients in the pan of the bread machine in the order recommended by the manufacturer.
2. Select normal cycle; press Start.
