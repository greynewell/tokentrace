## Ingredients

- 1 pound bacon
- 4 cups broccoli florets
- 5  green onions, chopped
- ¼ cup sunflower seeds
- ¼ cup golden raisins
- 1 cup mayonnaise
- ½ cup white sugar
- 6 tablespoons red wine vinegar

## Instructions

1. Place bacon in a large skillet. Cook over medium-high heat until evenly browned. Cool, crumble, and set aside.
2. Toss together broccoli, green onions, sunflower seeds, raisins, and crumbled bacon in a large bowl.
3. Mix together mayonnaise, sugar, and red wine vinegar in a small bowl. Toss with vegetables to coat. Cover and chill in the refrigerator until serving.
