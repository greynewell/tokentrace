## Ingredients

- 1 ½ cups cubed cooked chicken
- 3 tablespoons chopped onion
- 3 tablespoons mayonnaise
- 2 tablespoons chopped water chestnuts
- 4 teaspoons sweet pickle relish
- 1 tablespoon chopped pecans
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- ⅛ teaspoon ground black pepper
- 4  lettuce leaves
- 1  hard-boiled egg, sliced

## Instructions

1. Mix chicken, onion, mayonnaise, water chestnuts, sweet pickle relish, pecans, salt, and black pepper together in a bowl.
2. Serve on lettuce leaves and garnish each serving with a slice or 2 of hard-boiled egg.
