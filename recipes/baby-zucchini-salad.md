## Ingredients

- 5  baby zucchini, sliced thinly
- ½  red onion, sliced thin
- 1  red bell pepper, diced
- 2 leaves fresh basil, minced
- 2 tablespoons smoked olive oil
- 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
- salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Toss the zucchini, onion, bell pepper, and basil together in a large bowl.
2. Drizzle the olive oil and balsamic vinegar over the mixture and toss again to evenly coat.
3. Season with salt and pepper to serve.
