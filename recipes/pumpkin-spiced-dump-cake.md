## Ingredients

- cooking spray
- 1 (15 ounce) can pumpkin puree
- 1 (12 fluid ounce) can evaporated milk
- 1 ½ cups white sugar
- 4  eggs
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- ½ teaspoon ground cinnamon
- ¼ teaspoon ground nutmeg
- 1 (15.25 ounce) package yellow cake mix
- 2 cups chopped pecans
- ¾ cup butter, melted

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C). Grease a 9x13-inch baking pan with cooking spray.
2. Mix pumpkin, evaporated milk, sugar, eggs, vanilla extract, cinnamon, and nutmeg together in a bowl; pour into the prepared baking pan.
3. Sprinkle cake mix evenly over the pumpkin mixture; top with pecans. Drizzle melted butter on top.
4. Bake in the preheated oven until golden brown, about 1 hour.
