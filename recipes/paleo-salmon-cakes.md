## Ingredients

- 1 (14.75 ounce) can wild Alaskan salmon, drained
- ⅓ cup flaxseed meal
- 1  egg
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- 1 tablespoon olive oil

## Instructions

1. Combine salmon, flaxseed meal, egg, salt, and pepper in a bowl.
2. Mix well and form into 4 cakes.
3. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium-high heat.
4. Fry salmon cakes until browned, about 4 minutes per side.
5. Let sit about 2 minutes.
