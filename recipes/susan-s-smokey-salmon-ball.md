## Ingredients

- 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice
- 1 tablespoon finely minced onion
- 1 teaspoon prepared horseradish
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- 1 dash liquid smoke flavoring
- 2 (7 ounce) pouches skinless, boneless salmon, drained and flaked
- 1 sleeve buttery round crackers (such as Ritz®), crushed

## Instructions

1. Thoroughly mix cream cheese, lemon juice, minced onion, horseradish, salt, and liquid smoke in a bowl.
2. Fold salmon through the mixture to distribute evenly; shape into a ball.
3. Refrigerate ball until firm, about 30 minutes.
4. Spread crackers onto a plate.
5. Roll ball in crackers to coat.
6. Keep chilled until serving.
