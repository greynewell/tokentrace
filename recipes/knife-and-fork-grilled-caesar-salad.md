## Ingredients

- 1  long thin baguette
- ¼ cup olive oil, divided
- 2 cloves garlic, halved
- 1 small tomato, halved and seeded
- 1 head romaine lettuce, outer leaves discarded and head cut into quarters
- salt and coarsely ground black pepper to taste
- 1 cup Caesar salad dressing, or to taste
- ½ cup Parmesan cheese shavings

## Instructions

1. Preheat grill for low heat and lightly oil the grate.
2. Cut baguette on a severely sharp diagonal to make 4 long slices about 1/2-inch thick.
3. Lightly brush each cut side with about half of the olive oil.
4. Grill baguette slices on the preheated grill until lightly crispy, 2 to 3 minutes per side.
5. Rub each side of baguette slices with the cut-side of garlic and cut-side of tomatoes.
6. Set aside to cool.
7. Brush 2 cut sides of romaine quarters with remaining olive oil.
8. Grill romaine quarters until lightly seared, 2 to 3 minutes per side.
9. Sprinkle grilled romaine with salt and set aside to cool.
10. Place a grilled romaine quarter, cut-side up, on top of a grilled baguette slice.
11. Drizzle each with Caesar dressing and top with Parmesan cheese.
12. Season with salt and black pepper.
