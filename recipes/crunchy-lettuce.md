## Ingredients

- 6 slices bacon
- 1 head iceberg lettuce - rinsed, dried, and shredded
- ⅓ cup toasted and sliced almonds
- ¼ cup sesame seeds, toasted
- 4  green onions, chopped
- ¾ cup chow mein noodles
- ½ cup vegetable oil
- ¼ cup white sugar
- 2 tablespoons white wine vinegar
- 1 teaspoon salt
- ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper

## Instructions

1. Place bacon in a large, deep skillet.
2. Cook over medium high heat until evenly brown.
3. Drain, crumble and set aside.
4. In a salad bowl, combine the bacon, lettuce, almonds, sesame seeds, green onions and chow mien noodles; toss and refrigerate.
5. In a glass jar with a tight fitting lid, combine the oil, sugar, vinegar, salt and pepper.
6. Shake well and chill for 1 hour.
7. Before serving, shake dressing and pour over salad; toss and serve.
