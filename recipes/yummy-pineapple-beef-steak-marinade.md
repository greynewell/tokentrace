## Ingredients

- ¾ cup Worcestershire sauce
- ⅔ cup pineapple juice
- 2 tablespoons packed brown sugar
- ⅓ cup olive oil

## Instructions

1. Mix together the Worcestershire sauce, pineapple juice, brown sugar, and olive oil in a bowl.
2. Marinate the meat of your choice for 1 to 2 hours, and cook as desired.
