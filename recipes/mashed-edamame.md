## Ingredients

- 1 (12 ounce) package frozen shelled edamame (green soybeans)
- 2 cloves garlic, smashed and peeled
- ½ teaspoon kosher salt
- water to cover
- ½ teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
- 3 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil, or more as needed

## Instructions

1. Combine edamame, garlic, and salt in a saucepan; add enough water to cover edamame.
2. Bring to a low boil and cook for 30 minutes.
3. Drain liquid from edamame and transfer edamame and garlic to a food processor; season with black pepper.
4. Process mixture on high, adding olive oil while continuing to mix, until smooth.
