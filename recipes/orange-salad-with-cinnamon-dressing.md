## Ingredients

- 8  seedless oranges, peeled and sliced into circles
- 4 cups mixed salad greens
- 1 teaspoon orange zest, or to taste  (Optional)
- 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, at room temperature
- ⅓ cup orange juice
- 1 tablespoon honey
- 2 teaspoons grated orange zest  (Optional)
- ¾ teaspoon ground cinnamon

## Instructions

1. Arrange orange slices and salad greens on serving plates.
2. Sprinkle 1 teaspoon orange zest among the salads.
3. Beat cream cheese, orange juice, honey, 2 teaspoons orange zest, and cinnamon together in a bowl until smooth.
4. Spoon dressing onto each salad.
