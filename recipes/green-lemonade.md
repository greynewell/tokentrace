## Ingredients

- 1 head romaine lettuce
- 4 leaves kale
- 1 (1/2 inch) piece ginger
- 2  apples, quartered
- 1  lemon

## Instructions

1. Process romaine lettuce, kale, ginger, apples, and lemon through a juicer.
2. Stir before drinking.
