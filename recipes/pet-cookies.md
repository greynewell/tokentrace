## Ingredients

- 2 cups wheat germ
- 3 (2.5 ounce) jars strained chicken baby food
- 1 tablespoon water

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. In a medium bowl, mix the wheat germ, baby food, and water together.
3. Add more water if necessary to form a dough.
4. Roll dough into 1 inch balls and place on cookie sheet.
5. Flatten slightly with a fork dipped in water.
6. Bake for 20 to 25 minutes in the  preheated oven.
