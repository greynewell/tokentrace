## Ingredients

- 2 cups red seedless grapes, halved
- ⅓ cup capers, drained and rinsed
- 1  shallot, minced
- 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- salt and black pepper to taste
- 4 (8 ounce) tuna steaks
- ¼ cup fresh lemon juice

## Instructions

1. Preheat an outdoor grill for medium-high heat and lightly oil grate.
2. Stir together grapes, capers, shallot, parsley, and olive oil in a bowl; season to taste with salt and pepper, and set aside.
3. Place tuna steaks onto a plate, and brush with lemon juice.
4. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
5. Cook tuna steaks on preheated grill until cooked to desired degree of doneness, 2 to 3 minutes per side for medium-rare.
6. Serve with the grape and caper salsa.
