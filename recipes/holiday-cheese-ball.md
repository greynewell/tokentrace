## Ingredients

- 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese
- 1 (2.5 ounce) package smoked sliced beef, chopped
- 3  green onions, finely chopped
- 1 dash hot pepper sauce

## Instructions

1. Combine cream cheese, beef, onions and pepper sauce with hands or an electric mixer.
2. Shape into a ball.
3. Chill at least 1 hour before serving with crackers.
