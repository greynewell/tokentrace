## Ingredients

- 6 slices bacon
- 2 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened
- 1 (1 ounce) package buttermilk Ranch dressing mix
- ½ cup shredded sharp Cheddar cheese
- 4  green onions, chopped
- 3 tablespoons chopped black olives
- 1 ½ cups chopped pecans  (Optional)

## Instructions

1. Place the bacon in a large, deep skillet, and cook over medium-high heat, turning occasionally, until evenly browned, about 10 minutes.
2. Drain the bacon slices on a paper towel-lined plate.
3. Chop the cooled bacon into small pieces.
4. Stir together the cream cheese and ranch dressing mix in a bowl until smooth.
5. Add the bacon, Cheddar cheese, green onions, and olives; stir until well combined.
6. Shape the mixture into a ball and roll in the chopped pecans.
7. Refrigerate overnight.
