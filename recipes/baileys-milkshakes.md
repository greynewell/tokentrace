## Ingredients

- 1 scoop vanilla ice cream
- 1 scoop chocolate ice cream
- 1 tablespoon chocolate sauce
- 1 tablespoon caramel sauce
- 2 fluid ounces Baileys® Irish Cream
- 2 cups whole milk
- Chocolate Icing
- Whipped Cream
- Sprinkles
- Mini Marshmallows
- Chopped Nuts
- Crumbled Cookies
- Candied Bacon
- Caramel Sauce
- Chocolate Shavings

## Instructions

1. Mix all milkshake ingredients in a blender and blend until frothy.
2. Rim a Mason jar with chocolate icing and dip into your choice of toppings from the toppings bar. Pour milkshake from the blender into the jar - and complete with more toppings.
