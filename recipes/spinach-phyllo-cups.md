## Ingredients

- 1  egg, beaten
- 2 cloves garlic, finely minced
- 1 (8 ounce) package crumbled feta cheese
- 2 (10 ounce) boxes frozen chopped spinach, thawed and squeezed dry
- 3 (2.1 ounce) packages pre-baked mini phyllo dough shells

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
2. Mix the egg, garlic, and feta in a large bowl.
3. Stir in the spinach.
4. Place the phyllo shells on flat baking sheets, and fill with the spinach mixture.
5. Bake in preheated oven until hot, 6 to 8 minutes.
