## Ingredients

- 1 (8 ounce) package bulk pork sausage
- 4  large hard-boiled eggs, peeled
- 1 large egg
- ½ cup Italian bread crumbs

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C). Line a baking pan with aluminum foil. Place a wire rack over the foil.
2. Cut and form pork sausage into 2-ounce patties. Place a hard-boiled egg in the center of each patty and press sausage around the egg.
3. Beat raw egg in a small bowl. Place bread crumbs in another bowl. Dip each patty into the egg; roll in the bread crumbs to coat. Place coated patties on the wire rack.
4. Bake in the preheated oven until golden brown, 20 to 22 minutes. Serve hot.
