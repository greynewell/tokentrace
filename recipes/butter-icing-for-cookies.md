## Ingredients

- 2 cups confectioners' sugar
- 2 ½ tablespoons cream
- 4 tablespoons butter
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

## Instructions

1. Warm cream and butter in a saucepan over medium heat until butter is melted. Stir in vanilla and confectioners' sugar until combined.
2. Remove from heat and beat with an electric mixer until thick and smooth.
