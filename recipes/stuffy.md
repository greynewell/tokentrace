## Ingredients

- ¼ cup mayonnaise, or to taste
- 4  hoagie rolls, split lengthwise
- 1 cup leftover roast turkey meat, shredded
- 1 cup prepared stuffing
- ½ cup leftover turkey gravy
- ½ cup cranberry sauce

## Instructions

1. Preheat an oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Spread mayonnaise on each half of the split hoagie rolls.
3. Layer 1/4 cup turkey, 1/4 cup stuffing, 2 tablespoons gravy, and 2 tablespoons cranberry sauce on each of the four hoagie halves.
4. Top each sandwich with the remaining 4 pieces of bread.
5. Wrap each sandwich in aluminum foil and place in the oven for 15 minutes or until the sandwiches are heated through.
