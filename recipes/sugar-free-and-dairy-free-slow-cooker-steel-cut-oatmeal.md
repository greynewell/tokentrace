## Ingredients

- 2  bananas, mashed
- 5 cups water, divided
- 1 cup steel cut oats
- ¼ cup raisins  (Optional)
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

## Instructions

1. Put bananas into a blender with 1 cup water; puree and pour into a slow cooker.
2. Add remaining water, oats, raisins, cinnamon, and vanilla extract.
3. Cook on Medium, stirring every 30 minutes, for 3 hours.
