## Ingredients

- ½ cup milk
- 2 tablespoons butter, softened
- 2  eggs
- ¼ cup white sugar
- ¼ cup brown sugar
- 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon baking soda
- 1 (.25 ounce) package active dry yeast
- 2 small ripe bananas, sliced
- ½ cup chopped walnuts

## Instructions

1. Place ingredients in the pan of the bread machine in the order recommended by the manufacturer.
2. Select cycle; press Start.
3. If your machine has a Fruit setting, add the bananas and nuts at the signal, or about 5 minutes before the kneading cycle has finished.
