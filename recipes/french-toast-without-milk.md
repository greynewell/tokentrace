## Ingredients

- 2 large eggs
- 1 tablespoon white sugar
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- ½ teaspoon ground cinnamon
- ¼ teaspoon ground nutmeg
- 1 tablespoon unsalted butter
- 4 slices white bread

## Instructions

1. Beat eggs in a wide mixing bowl; mix in sugar, vanilla extract, cinnamon, and nutmeg.
2. Melt butter in a skillet over medium-low heat.
3. Dip 2 slices of bread into egg mixture, ensuring both sides are covered; place in the skillet and cook until golden brown, about 4 minutes per side. Repeat with remaining 2 slices of bread. Serve warm.
