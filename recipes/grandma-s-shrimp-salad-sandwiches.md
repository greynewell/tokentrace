## Ingredients

- 1 (3 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
- 2 tablespoons mayonnaise
- 1 tablespoon ketchup
- 1 teaspoon prepared mustard
- 1 cup chopped cooked shrimp
- ¼ cup finely chopped celery
- 1 teaspoon finely chopped onion
- ⅛ teaspoon garlic powder, or to taste
- 8 slices white bread

## Instructions

1. Stir cream cheese and mayonnaise together until smooth; add ketchup and mustard and stir until smooth.
2. Mix shrimp celery, onion, and garlic powder together in a separate bowl; add cream cheese mixture and mix to coat.
3. Divide shrimp mixture between 4 bread slices and spread into an even layer; top with remaining bread slices.
