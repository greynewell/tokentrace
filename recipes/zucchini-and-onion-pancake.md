## Ingredients

- 3  zucchini
- 1 large onion
- 1 teaspoon dried oregano
- salt and pepper to taste
- ¼ cup butter

## Instructions

1. Grate zucchini and onion into a medium bowl, and drain off excess juices.
2. Stir in the oregano and salt and pepper to taste.
3. Heat a frying pan over medium high heat and melt butter in the pan.
4. When butter has coated the pan, arrange the zucchini in a flat layer over the bottom of the pan.
5. Fry until golden brown on both sides, about 5 minutes per side.
6. Serve warm.
