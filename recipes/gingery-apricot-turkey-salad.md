## Ingredients

- 2 cups diced, cooked turkey
- 1  Granny Smith apple - peeled, cored and diced
- 1 cup diced celery
- ½ cup chopped cashews
- ¼ cup dried apricots, finely chopped
- ½ cup low-fat mayonnaise
- ¼ cup low-fat sour cream
- 2 tablespoons apricot preserves
- ⅛ teaspoon ground nutmeg
- ¼ teaspoon ground ginger
- ⅛ teaspoon ground black pepper

## Instructions

1. In a large bowl, toss together the turkey, apple, celery, apricot and cashew pieces.
2. Set aside.
3. In a small bowl, whisk together the mayonnaise, sour cream, apricot preserves, nutmeg, ginger and pepper.
4. Spoon over the salad mixture and fold dressing in until well-blended.
5. Serve over a bed of lettuce in a tortilla wrap or pita pocket.
