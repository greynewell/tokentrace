## Ingredients

- olive oil
- 4  bone-in pork chops
- 1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of mushroom soup
- 1 (1 ounce) package dry onion soup mix
- ⅓ cup milk
- 1 tablespoon dry sherry

## Instructions

1. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat and brown the pork chops, 5 to 8 minutes per side.
2. Mix cream of mushroom soup, dry onion soup mix, milk, and sherry together in a bowl and pour over the pork chops; cover and reduce heat to medium-low.
3. Simmer until pork chops are tender and no longer pink inside, 15 to 20 minutes.
