## Ingredients

- PAM® Original No-Stick Cooking Spray
- 1 (2 pound) boneless beef chuck roast
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ½ teaspoon dried Italian seasoning
- ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
- 1 cup lower sodium beef broth
- 1 (14.5 ounce) can Hunt's® Diced Tomatoes, undrained
- 6  hoagie rolls, split lengthwise and toasted
- 6 thin slices  provolone cheese, cut in half
- ½ cup giardiniera
- ¼ cup mild yellow banana pepper rings

## Instructions

1. Spray inside of 4.5-quart slow cooker and large skillet with cooking spray.
2. Heat skillet over medium-high heat.
3. Sprinkle beef with salt, Italian seasoning and pepper; cook 3 to 5 minutes or until browned on each side, turning once.
4. Place beef in slow cooker.
5. Add broth to skillet, stirring to loosen brown bits from bottom of skillet; pour over beef in slow cooker.
6. Pour undrained tomatoes over beef.
7. Cook on LOW 8 to 10 hours or HIGH 4 to 6 hours, until beef is very tender.
8. Remove beef from slow cooker and shred with two forks; return to slow cooker.
9. To serve: Place 2 pieces of cheese on bottom of each roll.
10. Top evenly with shredded beef, giardiniera and peppers.
11. Serve with cooking juices for dipping, if desired.
