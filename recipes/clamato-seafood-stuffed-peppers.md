## Ingredients

- 3 tablespoons olive oil
- 1 small onion, diced
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 pound uncooked medium shrimp, peeled, deveined and diced
- 1  tilapia fillet, cut into bite-size pieces
- 4 ounces small bay scallops  (Optional)
- 1 cup Clamato® Tomato Cocktail
- 1 tablespoon ReaLemon® Juice
- 1 tablespoon seafood seasoning
- 2 cups cooked white rice
- 10  basil leaves, chopped
- 8 ounces shredded Monterey Jack cheese
- 4  Cubanelle peppers, cut in half and hollowed out
- 8 ounces shredded Cheddar cheese

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
2. In a large saute pan on medium-high heat, heat olive oil; add onions and garlic; cook for 2 minutes.
3. Add shrimp, tilapia and bay scallops; cook 3-4 minutes until about halfway done.
4. Add Clamato®, lemon juice, seafood seasoning, basil and rice; mix well.
5. Cook to heat through.
6. Remove from heat; blend in Monterey Jack.
7. Spoon mixture into peppers, place in a lightly greased baking dish, cover with foil and bake for 20 minutes.
8. Remove foil and top with Cheddar; bake 5 more minutes or until the cheese is melted.
