## Ingredients

- 4 (4 ounce) salmon fillets
- 1 cup fat-free milk
- ½ cup PHILADELPHIA 1/3 Less Fat than Cream Cheese
- 2 cups chopped cucumbers
- 2 tablespoons chopped fresh dill
- 2 cups hot cooked long-grain white rice

## Instructions

1. Heat large skillet sprayed with cooking spray on medium-high heat.
2. Add fish; cook 5 min. on each side or until fish flakes easily with fork.
3. Remove from skillet; cover to keep warm.
4. Add milk and reduced-fat cream cheese to skillet; cook and stir until cream cheese is completely melted and mixture is well blended.
5. Stir in cucumbers and dill.
6. Return fish to skillet.
7. Cook 2 min. or until heated through.
8. Serve over rice; top with cream cheese sauce.
