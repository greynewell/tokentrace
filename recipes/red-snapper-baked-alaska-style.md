## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons butter
- 2 pounds red snapper fillets
- salt and white pepper to taste
- 2  egg whites
- 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
- Dijon mustard
- 1 teaspoon dried tarragon
- 3 tablespoons fresh bread crumbs

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
2. Spread butter into the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish.
3. Arrange fish fillets in a single layer over the bottom of the pan, and sprinkle with salt and pepper.
4. Bake for about 12 minutes in the preheated oven.
5. Meanwhile, whip egg whites in a clean dry bowl until stiff peaks form.
6. Fold in the parsley, mustard and tarragon.
7. Spread the egg white mixture over the fish in a layer about 1 inch thick.
8. Sprinkle bread crumbs over the top.
9. Return to the oven, and bake for about 6 more minutes, or until meringue is golden, and fish flakes with a fork.
