## Ingredients

- 1 (15 ounce) jar salsa
- 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
- 1 cup shredded lettuce
- 2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese

## Instructions

1. Combine salsa and cream cheese in a bowl; beat with an electric mixer until almost completely blended. Transfer to a serving bowl.
2. Layer cream cheese mixture with 1/2 of the lettuce and 1/2 of the Cheddar cheese. Repeat layers once more.
