## Ingredients

- 2 pounds boneless round steak, cut into 6 pieces
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- 1  onion, sliced
- ½ cup water
- 1 cube beef bouillon
- 1 teaspoon dill weed
- ¼ cup cold water
- ¼ cup all-purpose flour
- 3 tablespoons sour cream, or more to taste

## Instructions

1. Season steaks with salt and pepper and place in a slow cooker; add onion, 1/2 cup water, bouillon, and dill.
2. Cook on Low until steaks are tender, about 6 hours.
3. Remove steaks from broth and place on a serving platter.
4. Whisk 1/4 cup cold water and flour together in a bowl until smooth; pour into broth.
5. Increase slow cooker heat to High and cook gravy until thickened, about 10 minutes.
6. Stir sour cream into thickened gravy; serve alongside steaks.
