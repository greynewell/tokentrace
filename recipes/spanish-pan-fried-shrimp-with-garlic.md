## Ingredients

- ½ cup olive oil, or as needed
- 1 head garlic, cloves peeled and coarsely chopped
- 1  dried red chile pepper, seeded and chopped, or to taste
- 1 pound uncooked medium shrimp, peeled and deveined
- salt to taste
- 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley, or to taste

## Instructions

1. Heat 1/4 inch olive oil in a skillet over medium heat.
2. Add garlic and chile pepper and saute until fragrant, about 1 minute.
3. Remove garlic and chile peppers with a slotted spoon and set aside.
4. Add shrimp to the hot oil.
5. Cook and stir until opaque in color, about 3 minutes.
6. Return garlic and chile peppers to the skillet.
7. Season with salt and cook for 2 more minutes.
8. Sprinkle with fresh parsley and serve immediately.
