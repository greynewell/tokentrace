## Ingredients

- 1 cup white sugar
- ½ cup corn syrup
- 1 ½ cups pecan pieces
- 3 tablespoons butter
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 1 teaspoon baking soda

## Instructions

1. Combine sugar and corn syrup in a microwave-safe bowl. Heat in a microwave at 70%, or 700W, power for 4 minutes. Remove and stir. Microwave again at 70% power for 4 minutes. Add pecans, butter, and vanilla extract.
2. Microwave at 100%, or 1000W, power for 3 minutes. Add baking soda and stir until frothy.
3. Pour out onto a parchment paper-lined cookie sheet and let cool for 15 minutes.
