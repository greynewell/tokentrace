## Ingredients

- 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
- 1 (8 ounce) container sour cream
- ½ cup mayonnaise
- 1 (1 ounce) package ranch dressing mix
- 18  green onions, chopped
- 1  jalapeno pepper, seeded and minced  (Optional)

## Instructions

1. Combine cream cheese, sour cream, and mayonnaise in a blender; puree until smooth.
2. Sprinkle in the ranch dressing mix, green onions, and optional jalapeno; puree until well combined.
3. For best results, refrigerate overnight before serving.
