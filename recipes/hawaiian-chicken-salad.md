## Ingredients

- 2 (3 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened
- ⅓ cup creamy salad dressing, e.g. Miracle Whip ™
- 1 (8 ounce) can pineapple tidbits, juice reserved
- 3 (5 ounce) cans chunk chicken, drained
- 1 cup blanched slivered almonds
- 1 ½ cups seedless grapes, halved

## Instructions

1. Beat cream cheese in a medium bowl until fluffy. Mix in salad dressing and 2 tablespoons reserved pineapple juice.
2. Stir in pineapple tidbits, chicken, almonds, and grapes until evenly coated. Chill in the refrigerator until serving.
