## Ingredients

- 1  egg
- 1 ⅓ cups milk
- ¼ cup vegetable oil
- 2 cups self-rising corn meal mix
- 1 (8 ounce) can cream-style corn
- 1 cup sour cream

## Instructions

1. Heat oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C).
2. Grease a 9 inch iron skillet.
3. In a large bowl, beat the egg.
4. Add milk, oil, sour cream, cream corn, and cornmeal mix; stir until cornmeal is just dampened.
5. Pour batter into greased skillet.
6. Bake for 25 to 30 minutes, or until knife inserted in center comes out clean.
