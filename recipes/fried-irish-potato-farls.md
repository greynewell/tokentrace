## Ingredients

- 4  prepared potato bread farls
- 1 tablespoon olive oil, or as needed
- 1 pinch salt

## Instructions

1. Heat oil in a skillet over medium heat.
2. Gently fry potato farls for 2 to 3 minutes on each side or until golden brown.
3. Season with salt and serve immediately.
