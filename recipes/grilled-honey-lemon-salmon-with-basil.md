## Ingredients

- 5  salmon fillets, rinsed
- ½ cup white vinegar
- 1 teaspoon ground ginger  (Optional)
- 1 teaspoon garlic powder
- ground black pepper to taste
- kosher salt to taste
- cooking spray
- ⅓ cup honey
- 3 tablespoons lemon juice
- 2 tablespoons minced fresh basil

## Instructions

1. Place salmon fillets in a plastic container and add vinegar, ginger, garlic powder, black pepper, and salt.
2. Lightly massage into salmon for about 1 minute.
3. Spray both sides of fish with cooking spray.
4. Refrigerate fish while you prepare the rest of the ingredients.
5. Mix honey, lemon juice, and basil together in a bowl.
6. Preheat a clean outdoor grill for medium heat and lightly oil the grate.
7. Grill salmon fillets on the hot grill.
8. Cook the first side for 3 to 5 minutes, depending on thickness of the fish.
9. Turn fish and lightly baste with honey-lemon mixture.
10. Grill another 3 minutes and turn so honey side is down.
11. Baste top of salmon with honey mixture and grill until just warmed on this side, approximately 1 minute more.
