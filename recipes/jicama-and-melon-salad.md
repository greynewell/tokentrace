## Ingredients

- 2 cups thin slivers jicama
- 2 cups cubed honeydew melon
- 3 tablespoons fresh lime juice
- 3 tablespoons chopped fresh mint leaves
- 1 teaspoon lime zest
- 2 teaspoons honey

## Instructions

1. Toss together the jicama, honeydew, lime juice, mint, lime zest, and honey in a bowl; cover.
2. Refrigerate 2 hours.
