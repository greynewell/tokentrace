## Ingredients

- ¾ cup water (80 degrees F/27 degrees C)
- ¾ cup milk (80 degrees F/27 degrees C)
- 1 ½ tablespoons vegetable oil
- 6 tablespoons honey
- 1 ½ teaspoons salt
- ¾ cup oats
- 1 cup oat flour
- 1 ¼ cups wheat flour
- 1 ¼ cups bread flour
- 1 ½ teaspoons active dry yeast

## Instructions

1. Place warm water, milk, oil, honey, salt, oats, oat flour, wheat flour, bread flour, and yeast in the pan of a bread machine in the order listed.
2. Set cycle for light crust according to manufacturer's instructions.
