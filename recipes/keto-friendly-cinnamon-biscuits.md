## Ingredients

- 1 cup blanched super-fine almond flour
- 2 ½ teaspoons baking powder
- 1 teaspoon sea salt
- 3 ounces shredded mozzarella cheese
- 3 ½ tablespoons sour cream
- 2 tablespoons avocado oil
- 1 large egg
- 2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
- 1 tablespoon low-calorie natural sweetener (such as Swerve®)

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
2. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
3. Combine almond flour, baking powder, and sea salt in a bowl with a whisk.
4. Add mozzarella cheese; coat well with the almond flour mixture.
5. Mix in sour cream, avocado oil, and egg using a spatula.
6. Add cinnamon and sweetener to mixture; combine.
7. Form dough into 6 biscuits and place on the prepared baking sheet.
8. Bake in the preheated oven until golden brown, about 15 minutes.
