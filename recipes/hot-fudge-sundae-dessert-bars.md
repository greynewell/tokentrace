## Ingredients

- 1 pound chocolate sandwich cookies, crushed
- ½ cup margarine, melted
- 1 ¾ cups confectioners' sugar
- 1 (12 fluid ounce) can evaporated milk
- 1 cup semisweet chocolate chips
- ½ cup margarine
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- ½ gallon vanilla ice cream
- 1 ½ cups dry-roasted peanuts

## Instructions

1. Combined crushed cookies and melted margarine and press into a 9x13 inch dish.
2. Chill 1 hour in refrigerator.
3. In a saucepan over medium heat, combine confectioners' sugar, evaporated milk, chocolate chips and 1/2 cup margarine.
4. Bring to a boil, stirring constantly, and boil 8 minutes.
5. Remove from heat and stir in vanilla.
6. Set aside to cool.
7. Slice vanilla ice cream into 3/4 inch slices, and place them in a single layer over the chilled crust.
8. Smooth the seams.
9. Sprinkle the peanuts over the ice cream.
10. Top with the cooled chocolate sauce.
11. Cover and freeze 8 hours or overnight.
