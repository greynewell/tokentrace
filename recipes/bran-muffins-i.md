## Ingredients

- 2 cups raisin bran cereal
- 4 cups whole bran cereal
- 2 cups boiling water
- 1 cup shortening
- 3 cups white sugar
- 4  eggs
- 4 cups buttermilk
- 5 cups all-purpose flour
- 5 teaspoons baking soda
- 1 ½ teaspoons salt

## Instructions

1. Mix cereal and boiling water together in a metal bowl, and allow to cool.
2. Cream shortening, sugar, and eggs in a large bowl.
3. Add buttermilk and cooled cereal mixture.
4. Combine flour, baking soda, and salt; stir into the creamed mixture until flour is moistened.
5. Batter should be lumpy.
6. Put batter into a one gallon wide mouth jar.
7. When using mix, use a ladle to get batter out of the jar; don't mix or stir.
8. When ready to use, spoon batter into muffin tins.
9. Bake at 375 degrees F (195 degrees C) for approximately 15 minutes.
