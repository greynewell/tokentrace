## Ingredients

- ½ cup mayonnaise
- 2 tablespoons prepared yellow mustard
- 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
- 2 tablespoons honey
- ½ tablespoon lemon juice

## Instructions

1. Mix mayonnaise, honey, mustards, and lemon juice together in a bowl.
2. Cover and chill in the refrigerator overnight.
