## Ingredients

- ½ cup almond milk
- ¼ cup brown sugar
- ¼ cup white sugar
- ⅛ teaspoon salt
- 1 tablespoon margarine (such as Country Crock®)

## Instructions

1. Combine almond milk, brown sugar, white sugar, and salt in a saucepan over medium heat. Heat, stirring frequently, until milk reaches a soft boil and mixture has thickened slightly, about 5 minutes.
2. Remove from heat. Stir in margarine until melted.
