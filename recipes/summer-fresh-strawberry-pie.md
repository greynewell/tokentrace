## Ingredients

- ¾ cup white sugar
- 3 tablespoons cornstarch
- 1 pinch salt
- 1 cup water, divided
- 3 tablespoons lemon juice
- ½ teaspoon almond extract
- 3 drops red food coloring, or as desired  (Optional)
- 3 cups fresh strawberries, sliced
- 1 (9 inch) baked pie crust
- 8 ounces heavy whipping cream
- 3 tablespoons confectioners' sugar, or to taste

## Instructions

1. Stir sugar, cornstarch, and salt together in a medium saucepan. Add 3 tablespoons water and mix into a paste.
2. Bring remaining water to a boil in a pot. Add to sugar mixture slowly. Let mixture simmer over low heat, stirring constantly, until thick and clear, about 5 minutes. Remove from heat. Add lemon juice, almond extract, and red food coloring.
3. Let mixture cool for at least 15 minutes; fold in strawberries. Pour into the prepared pie crust. Chill until set, at least 1 hour.
4. Whip heavy cream in a mixing bowl using an electric mixer; add confectioners' sugar slowly until combined. Spread topping over chilled pie.
