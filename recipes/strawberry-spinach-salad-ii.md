## Ingredients

- ½ cup white sugar
- 2 tablespoons sesame seeds
- ½ teaspoon sesame oil
- 1 tablespoon poppy seeds
- 1 ½ teaspoons dried minced onion
- ¼ teaspoon paprika
- ½ cup vegetable oil
- ½ cup balsamic vinegar
- 2 bunches fresh spinach - chopped, washed and dried
- 1 pint strawberries, halved

## Instructions

1. Whisk together the sugar, sesame seeds, sesame oil, poppy seeds, dried onion, paprika, oil and vinegar.
2. Refrigerate until chilled.
3. In a salad bowl, combine the spinach and strawberries.
4. Drizzle with dressing, toss lightly and serve.
