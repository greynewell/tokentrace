## Ingredients

- ½ cup milk
- ⅓ cup water
- 1  egg
- 2 ¼ cups bread flour
- 1 tablespoon white sugar
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 ½ teaspoons active dry yeast

## Instructions

1. Place ingredients in the pan of the bread machine in the order recommended by the manufacturer.
2. Select Basic White Bread cycle; press Start.
3. After the baking cycle ends, remove bread from pan, place on metal rack and allow to cool one hour before slicing.
