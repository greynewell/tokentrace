## Ingredients

- 1 (12 ounce) can corned beef, flaked
- 1 cup shredded Swiss cheese
- ¾ cup mayonnaise
- 3 tablespoons sweet pickle relish
- 6 (1.5 ounce) hamburger buns

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 300 degrees F (150 degrees C).
2. Mix corned beef, Swiss cheese, mayonnaise, and relish together in a bowl. Spread onto hamburger buns and wrap each sandwich in aluminum foil.
3. Bake in the preheated oven until heated through, 20 to 25 minutes.
