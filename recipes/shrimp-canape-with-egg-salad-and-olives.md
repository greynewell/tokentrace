## Ingredients

- 3  eggs
- ½ pound uncooked medium shrimp, peeled and deveined
- 2 tablespoons mayonnaise, or more to taste
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 sprigs dill, finely chopped
- salt and freshly ground black pepper
- 5 slices bread - crusts removed, lightly toasted, and cut into 4 squares
- 20  sliced black olives

## Instructions

1. Place eggs in a saucepan and cover with water.
2. Bring to a boil, remove from heat, and let eggs stand in hot water for 15 minutes.
3. Remove eggs from hot water, cool under cold running water, and peel.
4. Bring a large pot with lightly salted water to a boil while eggs are cooking.
5. Add shrimp and cook until shrimp are pink and opaque, 1 to 3 minutes.
6. Drain and rinse shrimp in a colander under cold running water.
7. Chop hard-boiled eggs and mix with mayonnaise, garlic, and dill in a bowl.
8. Season egg salad with salt and pepper.
9. Lay out bread pieces on a serving platter.
10. Place a dollop of egg salad on each piece of bread and add 1 shrimp on top.
11. Garnish with 1 olive slice.
