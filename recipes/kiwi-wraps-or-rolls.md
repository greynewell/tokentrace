## Ingredients

- 1  whole wheat tortilla
- 1 tablespoon peanut butter
- 1 tablespoon cream cheese
- ½  kiwi, peeled and thinly sliced

## Instructions

1. Cut rounded edges from tortilla to form a square.
2. Spread peanut butter on one half of the tortilla; spread cream cheese on remaining half.
3. Place kiwi slices in a single layer over cream cheese.
4. Starting with the cream cheese end, gently roll up the tortilla to form a log shape.
5. Serve as a wrap or sliced into thick rounds.
