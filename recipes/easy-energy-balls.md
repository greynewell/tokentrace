## Ingredients

- 1 cup instant oats
- ⅔ cup flaked coconut
- ½ cup peanut butter
- ½ cup flax seeds
- ½ cup chocolate chips
- ¼ cup honey

## Instructions

1. Stir oats, coconut, peanut butter, flax seeds, chocolate chips, and honey together in a large bowl until well-combined.
2. Roll into golf ball-size balls.
