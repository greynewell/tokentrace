## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons ricotta cheese
- 1 ½ teaspoons orange juice
- 1 tablespoon granular no-calorie sucralose sweetener (such as Splenda®)
- ½ teaspoon honey
- 1 tablespoon nonfat dry milk powder

## Instructions

1. Mix together the ricotta cheese, orange juice, sweetener, honey, and dry milk powder in a small bowl until the mixture forms a spreadable, even texture.
2. Refrigerate leftovers.
