## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
- 2 (16 ounce) packages kielbasa sausage, cut into 1 inch pieces
- 1 (16 ounce) bottle barbeque sauce
- ¼ cup grape jelly
- 1 (15 ounce) can pineapple chunks, drained
- 1 (4 ounce) jar maraschino cherries
- 1 tablespoon crushed red pepper flakes
- 2 dashes cayenne pepper hot sauce

## Instructions

1. Heat the oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
2. Cook the sausage in the hot oil until browned; add the barbecue sauce, grape jelly, pineapple, cherries, red pepper flakes, and cayenne pepper and stir; simmer 20 minutes.
