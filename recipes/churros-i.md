## Ingredients

- 1 ¼ cups hot water
- 1 ½ cups buttermilk biscuit mix
- 4 cups vegetable oil for frying
- ½ cup white sugar
- 1 tablespoon ground cinnamon

## Instructions

1. Fill a deep frying pan 1 1/2 inches deep with vegetable oil.
2. Heat to medium-high heat.
3. In a medium bowl, combine the water and baking mix.
4. Using a wooden spoon, beat for about 3 to 4 minutes, until the mixture becomes spongy and uniform.
5. Using a pastry bag, pipe 5-inch long strips of batter into the hot oil.
6. For best results, fry only a couple at a time.
7. Cook until golden brown, then drain on paper towels.
8. In a small bowl or saucer, stir together the sugar and cinnamon.
9. Dip fried churros in the sugar to coat.
10. Serve hot.
