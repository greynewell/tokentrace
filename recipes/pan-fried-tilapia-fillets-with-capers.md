## Ingredients

- 2 (8 ounce) fillets tilapia fillets, halved
- salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
- ¼ cup all-purpose flour, or as needed
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 1 cup heavy whipping cream
- 1 pinch ground nutmeg
- 1 (4 ounce) jar capers, drained and rinsed

## Instructions

1. Season tilapia fillets with salt and pepper on all sides.
2. Dredge in flour and shake off excess.
3. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat and pan-fry tilapia until golden brown, about 3 minutes per side.
4. While tilapia is cooking, bring cream to a boil in a second skillet and season with salt and nutmeg.
5. Add tilapia and capers.
6. Simmer until cream thickens, about 5 minutes.
