## Ingredients

- 12 large potatoes, peeled and halved
- 2 (10.5 ounce) cans cream of chicken soup
- 2 cups sour cream
- 2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese, divided
- salt and ground black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Place potatoes into a large pot and cover with salted water; bring to a boil. Reduce heat to medium-low and simmer until tender, about 15 minutes. Drain. Cut potatoes into cubes and place into a large bowl.
3. Stir chicken soup, sour cream, and half the Cheddar cheese into potatoes until evenly incorporated; season with salt and black pepper. Spread potato mixture into a 9x13-inch casserole dish; sprinkle with remaining Cheddar cheese.
4. Bake in the preheated oven until cooked through and Cheddar cheese is melted, about 30 minutes.
