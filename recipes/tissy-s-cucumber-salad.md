## Ingredients

- 3  cucumbers, cut into chunks
- 3  tomatoes, cut into chunks
- 1  onion, diced
- ¾ cup mayonnaise
- ½ cup zesty Italian dressing
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 1 tablespoon ground black pepper
- 1 tablespoon minced garlic

## Instructions

1. Mix cucumbers, tomatoes, and onion together in a bowl.
2. Stir mayonnaise, Italian dressing, salt, pepper, and garlic together in a small bowl until smooth; pour over the cucumber mixture and toss to coat.
