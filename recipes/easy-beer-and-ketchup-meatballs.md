## Ingredients

- 1 (28 ounce) bottle ketchup
- 24 fluid ounces beer
- 1 ½ pounds ground beef
- 2 teaspoons garlic powder
- 1  onion, chopped

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
2. Place the beer and ketchup in a slow cooker on high setting and allow to simmer..
3. Meanwhile, in a large bowl, combine the ground beef, garlic powder and onion, mixing well.
4. Form mixture into meatballs about 3/4 inch in diameter.
5. Place meatballs in a 9x13 inch baking dish.
6. Bake at 400 degrees F (200 degrees C) for 20 minutes.
7. Transfer meatballs to the slow cooker with the beer and ketchup and simmer for 3 hours; sauce will thicken.
