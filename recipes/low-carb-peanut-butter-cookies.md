## Ingredients

- 1 cup peanut butter
- 1 cup white sugar substitute
- 1 large egg
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Line a baking sheet with a silicone baking mat.
3. Mix peanut butter, sugar substitute, egg, and vanilla extract together in a bowl.
4. Drop 16 spoonfuls of dough 1 inch apart onto the prepared baking sheet.
5. Bake in the preheated oven until set, about 12 minutes.
