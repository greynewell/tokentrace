## Ingredients

- 1 cup butter, softened
- 6 ounces cream cheese, softened
- 2 tablespoons white sugar
- 2 cups all-purpose flour
- ¾ cup dried apricots
- 1 ½ cups water
- ¾ cup white sugar

## Instructions

1. To make Filling:  Combine apricots and water in heavy saucepan and cook covered over medium heat for 10 minutes or until apricots are soft.
2. Cook UNCOVERED 5 to 10 minutes or until most of the water has been absorbed.
3. Mash apricots, stir in 2 tablespoons of sugar and let filling cool.
4. Set aside.
5. To make Dough:   Cream butter and cream cheese until fluffy; add 3/4 cup sugar and beat well.
6. Add flour and mix well.
7. Shape dough into ball and chill 1 hour.
8. Turn dough onto well-floured surface, working with only half the dough.
9. Roll into 1/8 inch thickness and cut into 2 inch squares.
10. Spoon 1/2 teaspoon of apricot filling into the center of the square.
11. Bring four corners to center, pinching to seal.
12. Bake at 400 degrees F for about 15 minutes.
13. Optional - you may baste with butter halfway thru baking period.
14. Optional - drizzle LIGHTLY with powdered sugar/milk frosting if you desire sweeter kolaches.
