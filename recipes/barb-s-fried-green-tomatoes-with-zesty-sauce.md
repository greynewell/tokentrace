## Ingredients

- 1 cup potato flakes
- 36  thin wheat crackers (such as Wheat Thins®)
- ¼ cup all-purpose flour
- ¼ cup butter-flavored granules (such as Butter Buds®)
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
- ½ cup butter flavored shortening (such as Crisco®)
- 5  green tomatoes, cut into 1/2-inch thick slices
- 3  eggs, beaten
- ¼ cup buttermilk ranch dressing
- ¼ cup sour cream
- 2 tablespoons hot pepper sauce (such as Frank's RedHot®)

## Instructions

1. Place potato flakes, wheat crackers, flour, butter-flavored granules, salt, and pepper into a food processor. Pulse until the crackers are finely crushed, then transfer mixture to a bowl.
2. Melt the butter flavored shortening in a skillet over medium-high heat. Dip the tomato slices one at a time into the eggs, then the cracker mixture. Pan fry the tomatoes in the shortening until golden brown on both sides, 7 to 10 minutes.
3. Mix the ranch dressing, sour cream, and hot pepper sauce in a bowl. Serve the fried tomatoes with topped with the sauce.
