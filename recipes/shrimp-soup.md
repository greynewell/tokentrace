## Ingredients

- 2 (14.5 ounce) cans chicken broth
- 2 (14.5 ounce) cans beef broth
- 2 (10.5 ounce) cans shrimp consomme
- 2 ½ cups frozen shrimp, thawed
- 1 teaspoon fresh dill weed
- salt and pepper to taste
- 2 tablespoons sliced green onions

## Instructions

1. In a large saucepan over medium heat, combine chicken broth, beef broth and shrimp consomme. Bring to a simmer, then strain.
2. Return broth to pot and add shrimp and dill. Heat through and adjust seasonings. Garnish with chopped green onion.
