## Ingredients

- 1  egg
- 2 tablespoons virgin coconut oil
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 2 cups hot coffee
- 1 pinch ground cinnamon

## Instructions

1. Place egg, coconut oil, and butter in a blender.
2. Add hot coffee; blend on low until combined, about 1 minute.
3. Pour into a coffee cup and sprinkle cinnamon on top of the froth.
