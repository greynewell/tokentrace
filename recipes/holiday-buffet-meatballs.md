## Ingredients

- 1 (38 ounce) package frozen cooked meatballs, thawed
- 1 cup ketchup
- ¾ cup packed brown sugar
- 3 tablespoons vinegar
- 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
- ½ teaspoon dry mustard
- ¼ teaspoon ground cloves
- 1 teaspoon garlic salt
- 1  beef bouillon cube

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 325 degrees F (160 degrees C). Place meatballs in a casserole dish.
2. Bake in preheated oven until warmed through, about 10 minutes.
3. Combine ketchup, brown sugar, vinegar, Worcestershire sauce, mustard, cloves, garlic salt, and beef bouillon cube in a saucepan; bring to a boil, stirring often. Reduce heat to low and simmer for 5 minutes. Pour sauce over meatballs; cover dish with aluminum foil.
4. Continue baking in oven until meatballs are hot, about 20 minutes.
