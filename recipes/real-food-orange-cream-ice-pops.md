## Ingredients

- 1 cup freshly squeezed orange juice
- 1 cup canned coconut milk
- 3 tablespoons raw honey
- ½ teaspoon pure vanilla extract
- 6  wooden pop sticks

## Instructions

1. Whisk orange juice, coconut milk, honey, and vanilla extract together in a large measuring cup or bowl with a spout.
2. Pour mixture into ice pop molds or small paper cups; add wooden pop sticks.
3. Place in freezer until solid, 8 hours to overnight.
