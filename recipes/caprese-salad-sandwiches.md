## Ingredients

- 8 slices artisan-style whole wheat bread
- 2 tablespoons white truffle oil, or to taste
- ½ pound buffalo mozzarella, thinly sliced
- ½ cup fresh basil leaves, or to taste
- 1 large heirloom tomato, thinly sliced
- coarse sea salt and ground black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Drizzle each slice of bread evenly with truffle oil and cover 4 bread slices with slices of buffalo mozzarella cheese, basil leaves, and tomato slices.
2. Season to taste with sea salt and black pepper.
3. Top each sandwich with a remaining bread slice.
