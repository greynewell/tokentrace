## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon unsalted butter
- 1  yellow onion, diced
- 2 teaspoons dried dill weed
- ½ cup water
- 2 tablespoons water
- ½ cup heavy cream
- 2 tablespoons heavy cream
- ½  lemon, juiced
- 14 ounces cod, cut into 1/2-inch cubes
- 1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed tomato soup
- 4 ounces peeled and deveined small shrimp

## Instructions

1. Melt butter in a pot over medium heat; stir in onion.
2. Cook and stir until the onion has softened and turned translucent, about 5 minutes.
3. Stir in dill and cook until fragrant, about 1 minute.
4. Add water, cream, and lemon juice; bring to a simmer.
5. Add cod and cook until fish flakes easily with a fork, 5 to 10 minutes.
6. Stir in tomato soup until dissolved.
7. Add shrimp and cook until bright pink on the outside and the meat is opaque, 2 to 5 minutes.
