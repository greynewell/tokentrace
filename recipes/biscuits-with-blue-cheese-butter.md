## Ingredients

- 1 (7.5 ounce) package refrigerated biscuit dough
- ½ cup unsalted butter, melted
- 2 tablespoons crumbled blue cheese

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
2. Grease a 9x13-inch baking dish.
3. Separate biscuits and cut into quarters; place biscuit pieces into prepared baking dish.
4. Mix unsalted butter and blue cheese together in a bowl until thoroughly combined and brush the butter mixture over the biscuits.
5. Bake in the preheated oven until biscuits are golden brown, 10 to 15 minutes.
6. Pull apart to serve.
