## Ingredients

- 1 cup water
- 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
- ¼ cup honey
- 1 teaspoon salt
- ½ cup rolled oats
- 2 ⅓ cups bread flour
- 1 teaspoon active dry yeast

## Instructions

1. Place ingredients in bread machine pan in the order suggested by the manufacturer.
2. Select Light Crust or Basic setting, and press Start.
