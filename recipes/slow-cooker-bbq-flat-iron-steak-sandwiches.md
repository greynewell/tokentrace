## Ingredients

- ½ cup ketchup
- ½ cup Italian dressing
- 2 tablespoons soy sauce
- 1 tablespoon molasses
- 2 pounds flat iron steak, cubed
- 1 tablespoon dried chopped onion
- 4  hoagie rolls, split lengthwise and toasted
- 1 cup prepared coleslaw  (Optional)

## Instructions

1. Mix the ketchup, Italian dressing, soy sauce, and molasses in a small bowl.
2. Place the steak in slow cooker, sprinkle with onions, and pour the ketchup mixture over the steak.
3. Turn the slow cooker to High and cook for 1 hour.
4. Reduce the heat to Low and continue cooking until the meat is tender, about 4 hours.
5. Serve on hoagie buns and top with 1/4 cup coleslaw, if desired, for a Southern BBQ flavor.
