## Ingredients

- 22 ounces spaghetti
- 1 cup chopped celery
- 1 (4.5 ounce) can small shrimp, drained
- 1  onion, chopped
- 1 cup frozen green peas
- 1 (16 ounce) bottle ranch-style salad dressing

## Instructions

1. Break pasta in half and cook in a pot of boiling salted water until al-dente.
2. Drain and cool under cold water.
3. In a large bowl, combine the pasta, celery, seafood, onion and peas.
4. Toss with enough dressing to coat.
5. Refrigerate until chilled and serve.
