## Ingredients

- 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
- 1 tablespoon chopped fresh dill
- 4 (8 inch) flour tortillas
- 4 ½ ounces sliced smoked salmon, finely chopped
- 16  fresh spinach leaves
- 16 (1/4 inch x 3 inch) strips red bell pepper

## Instructions

1. Mix cream cheese and dill together in a bowl.
2. Spread about 1/4 cup cream cheese mixture onto each tortilla.
3. Sprinkle 1/4 cup salmon over cream cheese layer.
4. Arrange 3 to 4 spinach leaves and 4 red bell pepper strips over salmon layer.
5. Roll tortilla tightly around filling and wrap securely in plastic wrap; refrigerate for at least 2 hours and up to 24 hours.
6. Remove plastic wrap and cut rolls into 1-inch pieces.
