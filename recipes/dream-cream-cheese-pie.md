## Ingredients

- 1 cup confectioners' sugar
- 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese
- 1 (12 ounce) container frozen whipped topping, thawed
- 3  bananas
- 2 (9 inch)  prepared graham cracker crusts
- 1 (21 ounce) can apple pie filling

## Instructions

1. Slice bananas, and arrange on the bottom of each pie crust.
2. Mix confectioners' sugar, cream cheese, and nondairy whipped topping with an electric mixer for about 2 minutes.
3. Divide mixture into pie shells.
4. Top with fresh fruit or pie filling of you choice.
5. Chill in freezer for 2 hours, or leave in refrigerator over night.
6. Serve!!!
