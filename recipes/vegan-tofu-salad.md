## Ingredients

- 2 (12 ounce) packages tofu, drained and cubed
- 1 cup vegan mayonnaise (such as Follow Your Heart® Veganaise®)
- 2 ½ tablespoons cider vinegar
- 1 tablespoon brown rice syrup
- 2 teaspoons poppy seeds
- 4 stalks celery, chopped
- 1 ½ cups grapes, halved
- ¾ cup chopped green onions

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Spread tofu evenly on a baking sheet.
3. Bake in the preheated oven until golden, 10 to 15 minutes.
4. Mix vegan mayonnaise, vinegar, brown rice syrup, and poppy seeds together in a large bowl.
5. Add celery, grapes, green onions, and baked tofu.
6. Mix together and chill in the refrigerator until ready to serve.
