## Ingredients

- 4 cups crushed ice
- 2 (1.5 fluid ounce) jiggers vodka
- 2 (1.5 fluid ounce) jiggers coffee flavored liqueur
- 2 (1.5 fluid ounce) jiggers Irish cream liqueur
- 2 tablespoons chocolate syrup
- ½ cup whipped cream

## Instructions

1. Combine crushed ice, vodka, coffee liqueur, and Irish cream liqueur in a blender. Drizzle in chocolate syrup. Blend until smooth.
2. Pour into glasses and garnish with whipped cream.
