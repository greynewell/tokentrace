## Ingredients

- 1  serving cooking spray
- 4 (4 ounce) tilapia fillets
- 1 tablespoon lime juice
- ½ teaspoon lemon-pepper seasoning
- ¼ cup mayonnaise
- 2 teaspoons Dijon mustard
- 1 teaspoon honey
- 4 each hamburger buns, split
- 4 leaves lettuce
- 4 slices tomato

## Instructions

1. Preheat an outdoor grill for medium-high heat and lightly oil the grate with cooking spray.
2. Brush tilapia with lime juice and sprinkle with lemon-pepper.
3. Cook on the preheated grill, covered, until fish flakes easily with a fork, 5 to 6 minutes.
4. While fish is grilling, combine mayonnaise, Dijon mustard, and honey in a bowl. Spread over bottom half of buns and top with lettuce and tomato. When fish is finished, place on prepared buns and enjoy!
