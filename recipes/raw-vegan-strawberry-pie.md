## Ingredients

- 1 cup almonds
- 1 cup shredded coconut
- 1 cup pitted dates
- 2 tablespoons coconut oil
- 1 pinch salt
- 2 cups strawberries
- 2 cups shredded coconut
- 2 tablespoons coconut oil
- 1 pinch salt
- 3  strawberries, sliced

## Instructions

1. Place almonds, 1 cup coconut, dates, 2 tablespoons coconut oil, and salt in a blender.
2. Puree until ingredients form a dough.
3. Press dough into a pie pan to form the crust.
4. Place 2 cups strawberries, 2 cups coconut, 2 tablespoons coconut oil, and salt in a blender.
5. Puree into a cream.
6. Pour cream over the crust.
7. Top with sliced strawberries.
8. Refrigerate until cream is thickened, about 2 hours.
