## Ingredients

- 2 pounds Roma tomatoes, halved lengthwise
- ½ cup olive oil
- 1 clove garlic, crushed
- 1 tablespoon dried oregano
- 1 teaspoon white sugar
- 1 teaspoon salt

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 250 degrees F (120 degrees C).
2. Arrange tomatoes, cut-side up, in a shallow baking dish; drizzle with olive oil and sprinkle garlic, oregano, sugar, and salt evenly on top.
3. Bake in the preheated oven for 1 hour.
4. Turn tomatoes over and continue baking until caramelized and very soft, 1 to 1 1/2 hours. Remove from the oven and set aside to cool to room temperature, about 1 hour.
5. Transfer tomato mixture to a blender or food processor; purée until smooth.
