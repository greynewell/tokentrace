## Ingredients

- ½ (1 ounce) packet Hidden Valley® Original Ranch® Salad Dressing & Seasoning Mix
- ½ cup milk
- ½ cup mayonnaise
- 3 tablespoons olive oil or vegetable oil
- 2  zucchini, cut into 1/4-inch slices
- 1 cup frozen broccoli florets, steamed
- 1  red bell pepper or green bell pepper, cut into small chunks
- 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
- 4 medium radishes, thinly sliced
- 3  green onions, chopped
- 1 (16 ounce) package rotini (corkscrew) pasta
- 2 tablespoons drained capers  (Optional)

## Instructions

1. Cook pasta according to package directions.
2. Rinse with cool water and drain.
3. Combine Hidden Valley® Original Ranch Salad® Dressing & Seasoning Mix with milk and mayonnaise in a bowl, creating 1 cup dressing.
4. In a small bowl, whisk the dressing together with the oil until emulsified.
5. In a large bowl, add the pasta and the remaining ingredients.
6. Add the dressing and toss until well coated.
7. Chill covered for 1 hour before serving.
