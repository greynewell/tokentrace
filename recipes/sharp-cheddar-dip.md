## Ingredients

- 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
- 5 ounces sharp processed cheese spread
- 1 pinch garlic powder
- 6  bagels

## Instructions

1. In an electric blender, combine cream cheese, sharp Cheddar cheese spread, and garlic powder. Blend until creamy.
2. Cut bagels in half, and then into quarters. Arrange the bagels around the dip on a serving platter.
