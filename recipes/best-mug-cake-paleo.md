## Ingredients

- 3 tablespoons chocolate chips, or more to taste
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 2 tablespoons coconut flour
- 2 tablespoons water
- ⅛ teaspoon baking soda
- 1  egg
- 1 drop coffee-flavored extract  (Optional)

## Instructions

1. Combine chocolate chips and olive oil in a microwave-safe mug; heat in microwave until melted, 20 to 30 seconds.
2. Stir coconut flour, water, and baking soda into chocolate mixture using a fork.
3. Add egg and coffee extract to chocolate mixture; stir well.
4. Heat in microwave until cake is cooked through, about 1 1/2 minutes.
5. Cool cake for about 2 minutes before eating.
