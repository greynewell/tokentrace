## Ingredients

- 1 (16 ounce) package shredded sharp Cheddar cheese
- ¾ cup mayonnaise
- ½ cup diced roasted red bell peppers
- 2 teaspoons Worcestershire sauce

## Instructions

1. Blend the shredded cheese with the mayonnaise in a bowl.
2. Stir in the roasted peppers and the Worcestershire sauce and blend thoroughly.
3. Cover, and chill for at least 4 hours or overnight.
