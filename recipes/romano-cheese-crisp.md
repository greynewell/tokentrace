## Ingredients

- 1 cup grated Romano cheese
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
- 1 (1 pound) loaf French bread

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to broil.
2. Slice bread into 1/2 inch slices.
3. In a medium-size mixing bowl, combine Romano cheese, garlic, and cream cheese.
4. Spread the cheese mixture onto the sliced French bread.
5. Broil for about 3 minutes, or until the cheese melts.
