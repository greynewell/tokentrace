## Ingredients

- 4 cups all-purpose flour
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 tablespoon baking powder
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 1 ½ cups milk

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
2. Lightly grease a baking sheet.
3. In a large bowl, stir together flour, salt and baking powder.
4. Cut in butter until mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
5. Stir in milk, mixing just until dough holds together.
6. Form dough into a round and place onto prepared baking sheet.
7. Bake in preheated oven for 30 minutes, or until a butter knife inserted into center of the loaf comes out clean.
