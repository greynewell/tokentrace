## Ingredients

- 1 cup mayonnaise
- ½ teaspoon salt
- 1 large head iceberg lettuce - rinsed, dried, and chopped
- 1  onion, chopped
- 1  tomato, chopped
- 1 (5 ounce) can tuna, drained and flaked

## Instructions

1. In a large bowl, whisk together the mayonnaise and salt.
2. Stir in the lettuce, onion, tomato and tuna until all ingredients are coated.
3. Enjoy!
