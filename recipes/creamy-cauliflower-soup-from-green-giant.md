## Ingredients

- 1 (20 ounce) package Green Giant® Garlic & Herb Mashed Cauliflower
- 1 cup milk (or chicken or vegetable broth)
- 2 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese

## Instructions

1. Cook Green Giant® Garlic & Herb Mashed Cauliflower according to package directions.
2. Combine Green Giant® Garlic & Herb Mashed Cauliflower with milk in microwave safe container.
3. Microwave on High 1 to 2 minutes or until hot.
4. Stir in Parmesan cheese and garnish with fresh herbs, if desired.
