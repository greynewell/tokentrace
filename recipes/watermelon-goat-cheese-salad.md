## Ingredients

- ¼ cup extra-virgin olive oil
- 2 tablespoons lemon juice
- 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- Ground pepper, to taste
- 4 cups spring mix
- 4 cups seedless watermelon, diced
- ½ (4 ounce) log Montchevre® honey goat cheese
- ½ cup hazelnuts, chopped

## Instructions

1. Whisk together olive oil, lemon juice, vinegar, salt, and pepper.
2. In a large bowl, gently toss spring mix and watermelon together with vinaigrette.
3. Top with goat cheese and hazelnuts to serve.
