## Ingredients

- 1 pound lean (at least 80%) ground beef, cooked, drained
- ¾ cup barbeque sauce
- 1 (10.2 ounce) can Pillsbury® Grands!® Flaky Layers refrigerated biscuits
- ¾ cup shredded Cheddar cheese

## Instructions

1. In 10-inch nonstick skillet, mix cooked beef and barbecue sauce.
2. Heat over medium-high heat, stirring constantly, until hot.
3. Pull each biscuit apart into 2 layers; press each into 4-inch round.
4. Place 1 round in each of 8 greased regular-size muffin cups.
5. Firmly press in bottom and up side, forming 1/4-inch rim.
6. Fill with beef mixture; sprinkle with cheese.
7. Bake at 375 degrees F 13 to 15 minutes or until edges of biscuits are golden brown.
8. Cool 1 minute; remove from pan.
