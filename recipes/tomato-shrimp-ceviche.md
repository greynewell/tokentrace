## Ingredients

- 1  medium cucumber, peeled and diced
- 1 large avocado, diced
- ¾ cup diced red onion
- ¾ cup chopped green onion
- 1 ½ pounds cooked small shrimp
- 1 (15 ounce) can tomato sauce
- 1 (6 ounce) can tomato paste
- ½ cup water
- 2 teaspoons salt
- 1 teaspoon pepper
- 1 teaspoon garlic salt
- hot pepper sauce to taste
- salt to taste

## Instructions

1. Combine the cucumber, avocado, red onion, green onion, and shrimp in a large bowl.
2. Pour in the tomato sauce, tomato paste, and water, mix well, thinning with additional water if needed.
3. Season to taste with salt, pepper, garlic salt, and hot pepper sauce.
4. Cover, and refrigerate for one hour.
