## Ingredients

- 1  whole artichoke
- ¼ cup butter, cut into small cubes
- ⅛ teaspoon garlic powder
- ⅛ teaspoon seasoned salt (such as LAWRY'S®)

## Instructions

1. Trim artichoke stem and about 3/4 inch of the top using a knife.
2. Trim thorns from leaves using scissors.
3. Place artichoke into a large pot and cover with water; bring to a boil.
4. Reduce heat to medium-low and simmer until tender, at least 1 hour.
5. Drain and cool artichoke, 15 to 30 minutes.
6. Preheat grill to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
7. Remove leaves from artichoke and scrape the hairy part from heart using a spoon with light pressure; discard hair.
8. Place leaves and heart into a vegetable griller.
9. Sprinkle butter over leaves and heart; season with garlic powder and seasoned salt.
10. Place griller on the preheated grill and cook, shaking griller occasionally, until edges of leaves are charred, about 10 minutes.
