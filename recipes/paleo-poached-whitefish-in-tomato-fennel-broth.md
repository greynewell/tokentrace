## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 2 bulbs fennel, chopped
- 1  onion, chopped
- 1 ½ pounds whitefish fillets
- 1 pinch saffron
- 1 tablespoon fennel seeds
- 1 cup diced tomatoes
- 1 cup water, or more as needed

## Instructions

1. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat.
2. Stir in fennel bulbs and onion; cook and stir until the onion has softened and turned translucent, about 5 minutes.
3. Add whitefish, saffron, fennel seeds, tomatoes, and water.
4. Bring to a boil, cover, and remove from heat.
5. Let sit covered until fish flakes easily with a fork, about 10 minutes.
