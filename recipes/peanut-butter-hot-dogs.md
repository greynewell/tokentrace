## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons peanut butter
- 2 slices whole wheat bread
- 1  banana, peeled

## Instructions

1. Spread a tablespoon of peanut butter onto one side of each slice of bread.
2. Break the banana into two pieces, and place one half onto the center of each peanut buttered bread slice.
3. Wrap the bread around each banana and eat like a hot dog!
