## Ingredients

- 4 large  cucumbers, peeled and thinly sliced
- 1 large cold onion, thinly sliced
- 4 teaspoons salt, divided
- 1 (8 ounce) container sour cream

## Instructions

1. Layer 1/4 of the sliced cucumbers and 1/4 of the onion in a large bowl.
2. Season with 1 teaspoon of salt.
3. Repeat the layers 3 more times.
4. Cover and allow to rest on the counter until the cucumbers and onion releases about 1 inch of liquid and are softened, 3 to 4 hours.
5. Drain cucumbers and onion in a colander and thoroughly rinse to remove any remaining salt.
6. Let the vegetables continue to drain in the colander for 30 minutes.
7. Transfer the vegetables to a bowl, then stir in the sour cream.
8. Cover and refrigerate until chilled, about 30 minutes.
