## Ingredients

- ¼ pound margarine
- 2 medium yellow onions, finely chopped
- 3 quarts water
- 1 ¾ pounds fresh or frozen haddock fillets, thawed and cut into 1/2 inch pieces
- 5 medium white potatoes, 1/2 inch dice
- 2 teaspoons salt
- 1 ½ cups non-dairy creamer

## Instructions

1. In a large stock pot, melt margarine over medium high heat.
2. Cook and stir chopped onion until translucent, but not darkened.
3. Carefully add 3 quarts of water, increase heat to high, and bring to a low boil.
4. Once boiling, stir in diced haddock and potatoes.
5. Reduce heat to medium, and cook at a low boil for 30 minutes, stirring frequently to prevent sticking.
6. Remove pot from heat, and allow to cool until mildly warm.
7. Stir non-dairy creamer into cooled soup, slowly and thoroughly to avoid lumps from forming.
8. Season to taste with salt.
9. Cover and refrigerate 8 hours, or overnight, to develop flavors.
10. To serve, reheat on the stove or in the microwave.
11. Adjust seasoning as needed with salt.
12. Serve with soda crackers and sweet pickles.
