## Ingredients

- 12 each fresh oysters in shells, rinsed and scrubbed clean
- 2 pinches salt
- 2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce

## Instructions

1. Preheat an electric smoker to 225 degrees F (110 degrees C).
2. Shuck oysters and add them to a bowl along with their liquor. Add salt and let marinate for 20 minutes.
3. Meanwhile, wash 1/2 of the shells. Drain liquor from the oysters and place an oyster in each half shell.
4. Place shells directly on the grate and smoke for 1 1/2 hours. Top each oyster with a dash of Worcestershire sauce before serving.
