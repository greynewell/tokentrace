## Ingredients

- 1 pound pizza dough
- ½ cup pizza sauce, divided
- 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese, divided
- 16 slices pepperoni

## Instructions

1. Preheat a waffle iron according to manufacturer's instructions.
2. Roll pizza dough to 1/2-inch thickness on a flat work surface.
3. Cut dough into 16 rounds using a 2-inch cutter.
4. Press rounds out with your fingers until each measures about 3 inches across.
5. Spoon 1 teaspoon pizza sauce into the center of 8 rounds.
6. Top each with 2 teaspoons mozzarella cheese and 1 slice of pepperoni.
7. Cover with remaining 8 rounds, pressing around edges to seal.
8. Cook the sealed rounds, in batches, on the preheated waffle iron until lightly browned, about 2 minutes.
9. Top each one with another pepperoni slice and a little more mozzarella cheese.
10. Close waffle iron and continue cooking until golden brown, 1 to 2 minutes more.
