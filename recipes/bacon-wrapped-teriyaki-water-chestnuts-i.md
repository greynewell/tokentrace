## Ingredients

- 2 (8 ounce) cans water chestnuts
- 2 cups teriyaki sauce
- 1 pound bacon

## Instructions

1. Drain chestnuts and place them in a bowl.
2. Cover chestnuts with teriyaki sauce and marinate in the refrigerator for 2 hours or more.
3. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
4. Remove water chestnuts from refrigerator and drain well.
5. Cut bacon strips in half so that strips are about 3 inches long.
6. Wrap each chestnut with bacon and secure bacon closed with a toothpick.
7. Arrange all wrapped chestnuts on a baking sheet.
8. Place in oven until bacon is crispy.
9. Remove from oven and enjoy.
