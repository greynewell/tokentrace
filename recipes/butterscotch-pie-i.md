## Ingredients

- 1 ½ cups packed brown sugar
- 4 tablespoons cornstarch
- 3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
- 2 cups milk
- 3  egg yolks
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 1 (9 inch) pie shell, baked
- 3  egg whites
- ½ teaspoon cream of tartar
- ½ cup white sugar
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

## Instructions

1. In a double boiler, whisk together the brown sugar, flour, and cornstarch.
2. Add just enough milk to make a paste.
3. Mix in beaten egg yolks, and then add the remainder of the milk.
4. Cook slowly, stirring constantly; when thick, remove from heat.
5. Add butter or margarine and 1 teaspoon vanilla.
6. Pour the filling into the baked pie shell.
7. In medium mixing bowl, beat egg whites until stiff.
8. Beat in cream of tarter,  white sugar, and 1 teaspoon vanilla.
9. Spread meringue over pie filling.
10. Bake at 375 degree F (190 degrees C) until meringue peaks turn a golden brown.
