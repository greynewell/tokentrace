## Ingredients

- 1 (10 ounce) bag bag tortilla chips
- 1 pound shredded Monterey Jack cheese
- ¾ cup Alfredo Sauce
- 1 small red onion, diced
- ⅓ cup sun-dried tomatoes, chopped
- ⅓ cup chopped pepperoncini
- ¼ cup pitted Kalamata olives, chopped

## Instructions

1. Arrange the tortilla chips on a large, microwave-safe platter.
2. Scatter the cheese over the chips; drizzle with  .
3. Heat in microwave until the cheese has melted, 2 to 3 minutes.
4. Top nachos with onion, tomato, pepperoncini, and olives to serve.
