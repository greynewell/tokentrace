## Ingredients

- 1 slice center-cut slice of watermelon, 1 to 2 inches thick
- 2 tablespoons creamy peanut butter
- ½  banana
- 1 teaspoon honey, or to taste
- 1 tablespoon chopped peanuts

## Instructions

1. Cut watermelon slice into 8 wedges, but keep them together for a nice presentation. Pat dry with a paper towel or clean dishcloth.
2. Spread peanut butter evenly over the watermelon, stopping at the rind. Top with sliced banana. Drizzle with honey and top with peanuts. Serve immediately.
