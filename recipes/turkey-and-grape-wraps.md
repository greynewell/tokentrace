## Ingredients

- 4 cups chopped cooked turkey
- 2 cups red seedless grapes, halved
- ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
- ½ cup mayonnaise
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- 4 (8 inch) flour tortillas
- 4  large fresh spinach leaves

## Instructions

1. Lightly mix the turkey, grapes, Parmesan cheese, and mayonnaise in a bowl until well combined.
2. Season to taste with salt and black pepper.
3. Place the 4 tortillas out onto a work surface, and place 1 spinach leaf in the middle of each.
4. Spoon 1/4 of the turkey filling into each spinach leaf, in a line.
5. Fold up the bottom of each tortilla to hold the filling, then wrap tightly.
6. Leave the tops open.
