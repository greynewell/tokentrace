## Ingredients

- 18 ounces fettuccine
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- 1 pound fresh spinach, chopped
- 1 cup heavy whipping cream
- 1 ½ cups grated Parmesan cheese
- ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper

## Instructions

1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a rolling boil.
2. Cook fettuccine at a boil until cooked through yet firm to the bite, about 8 minutes; drain.
3. Melt butter with olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
4. Cook and stir garlic in the butter mixture until softened, 2 to 3 minutes; add spinach and cook to wilt slightly, about 1 minute.
5. Stir fettuccine with the spinach mixture.
6. Pour cream over the pasta and spinach.
7. Increase heat to medium-high and cook until the cream is slightly thickened and reduced in volume, about 5 minutes.
8. Stir Parmesan cheese into the sauce to melt; season with black pepper.
9. Toss to coat noodles with the cheesy sauce.
