## Ingredients

- 2 cups bulgur
- 4 cups boiling water
- 3 tablespoons olive oil
- 2  lemons, zested
- 2  lemons, juiced
- ¼ cup chopped fresh mint
- ¼ cup minced fresh parsley
- salt and black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. In a large bowl, pour boiling water over bulgur.
2. Stir in olive oil, lemon zest, lemon juice, mint, and parsley.
3. Set aside for 30 minutes.
4. Fluff bulgur with a fork, and season to taste with salt and pepper.
