## Ingredients

- ¼ cup powdered buttermilk
- 1 teaspoon garlic powder
- 1 teaspoon dried dill weed
- 1 teaspoon dried parsley
- 1 teaspoon dried chives
- ½ teaspoon onion powder
- ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- 2 cups low-fat plain yogurt

## Instructions

1. Combine buttermilk, garlic powder, dill weed, parsley, chives, onion powder, pepper, and salt in a bowl, making sure to break up any clumps.
2. Gently fold in the yogurt until combined, being careful not to over mix.
3. Refrigerate for 1 hour before serving.
4. Pour off any whey before serving.
