## Ingredients

- 4  (1 1/4 inch thick) pork chops
- salt and pepper to taste
- 1 tablespoon butter
- ¾ cup milk
- ¼ cup water
- 1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of mushroom soup

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Season pork chops with salt and pepper. Melt butter in a large skillet over medium-high heat; add pork chops and sauté for about 5 minutes per side.
3. Combine milk, water, and condensed soup in a separate medium bowl. Arrange pork chops in a 9x13-inch baking dish; pour soup mixture over chops.
4. Bake in the preheated oven for 45 minutes. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read 145 degrees F (63 degrees C).
