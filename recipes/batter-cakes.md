## Ingredients

- 1 cup buttermilk
- ⅔ cup cornmeal
- 1  egg, slightly beaten
- 3 tablespoons butter, melted
- ¾ teaspoon salt
- ½ teaspoon baking soda
- cooking spray

## Instructions

1. Mix buttermilk, cornmeal, egg, butter, salt, and baking soda in a bowl until just combined.
2. Heat a griddle or large skillet over medium heat.
3. Spray with non-stick cooking spray.
4. Drop by heaping tablespoonful onto the griddle.
5. Cook until bubbles form on the top, 2 to 3 minutes; flip and brown on the other side, another 2 to 3 minutes.
