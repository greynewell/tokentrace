## Ingredients

- 1 (9 inch) refrigerated pie crust
- 1 cup Egg Beaters® Original
- ½ cup granulated sugar
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 2 cups fat free milk
- 2 cups assorted fresh fruit (such as blueberries, sliced strawberries, and/or peaches)
- 8 tablespoons Reddi-wip Original Whipped Light Cream

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
2. Place pie crust in 9-inch pie plate, pressing crust firmly into bottom and up sides of plate.
3. Flute edge.
4. Beat Egg Beaters, sugar and vanilla in large bowl with wire whisk until blended.
5. Gradually beat in milk until combined.
6. Place pastry shell in oven.
7. Carefully pour filling into prepared crust.
8. Bake 40 minutes or until knife inserted near center comes out clean.
9. Cool completely on wire rack.
10. Chill until firm, about 2 hours.
11. Arrange fruit on top.
12. Cut pie into 8 slices.
13. Top each slice with a serving of Reddi-wip just before serving, if desired.
