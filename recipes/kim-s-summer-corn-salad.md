## Ingredients

- 1 (15.25 ounce) can whole kernel corn, drained
- 1 (15.25 ounce) can white corn, drained
- 2  tomatoes, chopped
- ½ (15 ounce) can sweet peas, drained
- 1  cucumber, diced
- 1  purple onion, diced
- 3 tablespoons pimentos
- 1 cup mayonnaise
- salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. In a large bowl, mix the corn, white corn, tomatoes, sweet peas, cucumber, onion, and pimentos.
2. Fold in the mayonnaise.
3. Season with salt and pepper.
4. Cover, and chill at least 1 hour in the refrigerator.
