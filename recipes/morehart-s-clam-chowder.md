## Ingredients

- 6 large potatoes, cubed
- 1 (8 ounce) package sliced fresh mushrooms
- 1  yellow onion, chopped
- ½  green bell pepper, minced
- 2 quarts half-and-half
- 5 (6 ounce) cans clams in clam juice, undrained
- 2 (10.75 ounce) cans  condensed cream of mushroom soup (such as Campbell's®)
- ½ cup butter
- 1 tablespoon chopped parsley
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- 1 pinch seafood seasoning (such as Old Bay®), or to taste

## Instructions

1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil and add potatoes, mushrooms, onion, and green pepper.
2. Reduce heat to low; cook vegetables at a simmer until potatoes are tender, about 30 minutes.
3. Drain.
4. Place boiled vegetables back into the pot and raise heat to medium.
5. Stir half-and-half, clams with their juice, cream of mushroom soup, butter, and parsley into vegetables; bring to a boil, stirring occasionally.
6. Reduce heat to low and simmer until thickened, about 30 minutes; stir often.
7. Season with salt, black pepper, and seafood seasoning.
