## Ingredients

- 2 pounds fingerling potatoes, cut into large chunks
- 1 (8 ounce) container lowfat plain yogurt
- ¼ cup sour cream
- 1  lemon, zested and juiced
- 1 tablespoon coarse-grained mustard, or more to taste
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- 2 cups torn arugula leaves

## Instructions

1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil and add potatoes.
2. Boil until potatoes are tender, 10 to 13 minutes; drain.
3. Let cool until potatoes are warm.
4. Whisk yogurt, sour cream, lemon zest, lemon juice, mustard, salt, and black pepper together in a bowl.
5. Gently fold potatoes into dressing.
6. Stir arugula leaves into potato mixture and serve.
