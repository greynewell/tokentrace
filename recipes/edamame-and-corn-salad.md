## Ingredients

- 2  tomatoes, diced
- 1 cup shelled, cooked edamame
- 1 cup frozen sweet corn kernels, thawed
- 4  green onions, sliced
- 2 tablespoons fresh lime juice
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 1 tablespoon chopped fresh cilantro
- 2 cloves garlic, minced, or to taste
- 1 pinch coarse sea salt to taste  (Optional)

## Instructions

1. Mix tomatoes, edamame, corn, green onions, lime juice, olive oil, cilantro, and garlic together in a bowl. Season with sea salt.
2. Cover bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate until flavors blend, at least 1 hour.
