## Ingredients

- 4  skinless, boneless chicken breasts
- 4 cups plain non-fat yogurt
- 2 cups cornflakes cereal

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Crush the cornflake crumbs between 2 pieces of wax paper.
3. Dip the chicken breasts in the yogurt, coating both sides.
4. Roll in crushed cornflake crumbs to coat all sides, then place in a 9x13 inch baking dish.
5. Bake the chicken in the preheated oven for 30 minutes.
