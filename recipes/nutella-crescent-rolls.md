## Ingredients

- cooking spray
- 1 (8 ounce) package refrigerated crescent rolls
- ¼ cup chocolate-hazelnut spread (such as Nutella®)

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). Line a rimmed baking sheet with foil sprayed with nonstick spray.
2. Roll out and separate crescent dough on a work surface. Place chocolate-hazelnut spread in a small resealable bag and snip off one corner. Use a zig-zag motion to pipe chocolate-hazelnut spread onto each crescent. Roll up, starting at the larger edge. Place on the prepared baking sheet.
3. Bake in the preheated oven until puffed up and lightly browned, about 14 minutes. Serve immediately.
