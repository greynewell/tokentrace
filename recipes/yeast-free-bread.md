## Ingredients

- 2 ½ cups self-rising flour
- 3 tablespoons white sugar
- 1 (12 fluid ounce) can or bottle lemon-lime flavored carbonated beverage

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Grease one 9x5 inch loaf pan.
3. Combine the flour, sugar and carbonated beverage.
4. Mix well and place into the prepared pan.
5. Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 45 minutes.
6. Note: If using a sweetened carbonated beverage cut back on the sugar a little.
