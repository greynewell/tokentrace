## Ingredients

- 10 cups all-purpose flour
- ½ cup baking powder
- ¼ cup white sugar
- 2 teaspoons salt
- 2 cups shortening

## Instructions

1. In a large bowl mix together the flour, baking powder, sugar, and salt.
2. Cut in shortening until mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
3. Store in an airtight container for up to 6 weeks.
