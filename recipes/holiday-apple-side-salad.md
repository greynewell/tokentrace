## Ingredients

- 2 (16 ounce) cans dark red kidney beans, drained and rinsed
- 2 large Granny Smith apples - peeled, cored and diced
- 2 stalks celery, diced
- 1 bunch red globe grapes, halved and seeded
- 1 ½ cups creamy salad dressing, e.g. Miracle Whip ™

## Instructions

1. In a large bowl mix together the kidney beans, celery, and grapes.
2. Stir in the creamy salad dressing a little at a time.
3. Adjust the amount of creamy salad dressing to suit your taste.
