## Ingredients

- 2 cups quick-cooking rolled oats
- 3 large bananas, mashed  (Optional)
- ½ cup shredded coconut
- ¼ cup chopped pitted dates
- ¼ cup chopped walnuts
- 3 tablespoons agave nectar
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract  (Optional)
- 1 pinch ground nutmeg, or to taste
- 1 pinch salt

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Mix oats, bananas, coconut, dates, walnuts, agave, vanilla extract, nutmeg, and salt together in a bowl by hand; press into a 9-inch square baking dish.
3. Bake in preheated oven until browned on top, about 30 minutes.
4. Cool in baking dish on a wire rack until completely cooled before cutting into bars.
