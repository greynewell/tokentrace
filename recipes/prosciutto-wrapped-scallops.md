## Ingredients

- ¼ cup sun-dried tomatoes
- ¼ cup black olives
- 1 tablespoon dried basil
- ¼ cup olive oil
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ½ teaspoon ground black pepper
- 20  scallops
- 10 slices prosciutto

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Place the tomatoes, olives, basil, olive oil, salt and pepper into the bowl of a food processor and pulse until all of the ingredients are well blended and the consistency is almost smooth.
3. Scrape the tomato and olive marinade into a large bowl; toss the scallops in the mixture until well coated.
4. Cut prosciutto slices in half lengthwise and wrap each piece around one scallop.
5. Arrange wrapped scallops on a baking sheet and bake in preheated oven for twenty minutes.
