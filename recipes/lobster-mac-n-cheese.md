## Ingredients

- ½ (16 ounce) package bow tie pasta
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 2  shallots, minced
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 cups heavy cream
- 6 ounces grated Grana Padano cheese, divided
- 4 ounces shredded Havarti cheese
- 4 ounces shredded sharp white Cheddar cheese
- ½ (8 ounce) container mascarpone cheese
- 4 ounces shredded Gruyere cheese
- ½ teaspoon ground white pepper
- 1 pound lobster meat, chopped
- ½ cup panko bread crumbs

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
3. Cook bow tie pasta at a boil, stirring occasionally, until tender yet firm to the bite, about 12 minutes.
4. Meanwhile, melt butter in a large skillet over medium heat.
5. Saute shallots and garlic until translucent, about 5 minutes.
6. Transfer shallot-garlic mixture to a double boiler.
7. Add heavy cream, 1/2 of the Grana Padano, Havarti, Cheddar, mascarpone, and Gruyere cheeses.
8. Stir until smooth, about 5 minutes.
9. Season with white pepper.
10. Stir in lobster meat.
11. Drain pasta and mix into the sauce.
12. Pour pasta and sauce into a large baking dish.
13. Sprinkle with remaining Grana Padano and panko on top.
14. Bake in the preheated oven until bread crumbs are golden brown, 10 to 15 minutes.
