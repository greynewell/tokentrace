## Ingredients

- ½ cup orzo pasta
- 1 (12 fluid ounce) can or bottle beer
- 2 teaspoons canola oil
- 1 pound boneless chicken thighs
- ½  onion, minced
- 3 cloves garlic, crushed
- 1 cup low-sodium chicken broth
- 1 cup tomato sauce
- ½ cup pimentos, drained
- 1 tablespoon ground turmeric
- ½ cup frozen petite peas
- salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Place the orzo in a small bowl.
2. Pour enough beer into  the bowl to cover the orzo; set the remaining beer aside.
3. Heat the oil in a large, non-stick skillet over medium-high heat.
4. Brown the chicken on both sides, about 4 minutes per side.
5. Drain the fat from the skillet and lower heat to medium.
6. Add onion and garlic; cook and stir until the onions are translucent.
7. Pour in the orzo mixture, the rest of the beer, chicken broth, tomato sauce, pimentos, and turmeric; cover and simmer for 10 minutes.
8. Stir in the peas; simmer another 5 minutes.
9. Season with salt and pepper.
