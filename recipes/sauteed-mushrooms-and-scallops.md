## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons butter
- 1 (10 ounce) can sliced mushrooms, drained
- ¼ teaspoon ground cumin
- ⅛ teaspoon ground black pepper
- 1 small onion, finely chopped
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 9 ounces bay scallops

## Instructions

1. Melt butter in a medium pan over medium heat. Add mushrooms, cumin, and pepper. Saute until mushrooms are lightly browned, about 5 minutes.
2. Add onion and garlic; saute until translucent, about 5 minutes. Add scallops; cook and stir until opaque, about 3 minutes. Remove from heat.
