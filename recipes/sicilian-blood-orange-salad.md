## Ingredients

- 6  blood oranges, peeled and sectioned
- ¼ cup green onions, sliced
- extra virgin olive oil
- ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Slice each blood orange section in half and remove and discard any seeds.
2. Place oranges in a medium bowl.
3. Add green onions; toss to combine.
4. Drizzle with olive oil until just coated.
5. Lightly sprinkle with pepper.
6. Chill until ready to serve.
