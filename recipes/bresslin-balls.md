## Ingredients

- 1 (16 ounce) package ground sausage
- 12  hard-boiled eggs, peeled
- 1 cup bread crumbs

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
3. Divide sausage into 12 pieces.
4. Roll each piece into a ball the size of an egg.
5. Flatten each ball of sausage and wrap around a hard-boiled egg, covering it completely.
6. Pour bread crumbs into a shallow bowl.
7. Roll sausage-wrapped egg in bread crumbs until coated; transfer to the prepared baking sheet.
8. Bake in the preheated oven, turning every 10 minutes, until golden brown and crispy, about 40 minutes.
