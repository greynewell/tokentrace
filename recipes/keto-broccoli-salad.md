## Ingredients

- 1 pound bacon
- 2 cups broccoli florets, chopped
- 2 cups cauliflower florets, chopped
- 1  carrot, chopped
- ¼ cup chopped green onions
- ½ cup mayonnaise
- ¼ cup distilled white vinegar
- 1 tablespoon sour cream
- 1 pinch garlic powder
- 1 pinch dried basil
- ¼ cup shredded Cheddar cheese
- 1 tablespoon chopped fresh cilantro, or to taste

## Instructions

1. Place bacon in a large skillet and cook over medium-high heat, turning occasionally, until evenly browned, about 10 minutes. Drain bacon slices on paper towels. Chop and set aside.
2. Combine broccoli, cauliflower, carrot, and green onions in a large bowl.
3. Combine mayonnaise, vinegar, and sour cream in a separate bowl. Beat until smooth. Season with garlic powder and basil.
4. Pour dressing over vegetables and toss to coat evenly. Chill until flavors meld, about 1 hour.
5. Serve salad garnished with Cheddar cheese and cilantro.
