## Ingredients

- ½ cup corn syrup
- ½ cup peanut butter
- ½ cup packed brown sugar
- 2 cups crispy rice cereal
- 1 cup confectioners' sugar
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 2 tablespoons milk
- 1 pinch salt
- 2 teaspoons unsweetened cocoa powder

## Instructions

1. In saucepan, over medium heat, melt corn syrup, peanut butter and brown sugar.
2. Remove from heat and add cereal.
3. Mix well and press into buttered 9  x  9 inch square pan.
4. To make icing:  Combine the rest of the ingredients.
5. Add more milk, if necessary, until smooth and spreadable.
