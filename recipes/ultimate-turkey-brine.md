## Ingredients

- 1 gallon water
- 2 cups kosher salt
- 1 ½ cups dark brown sugar
- 12  whole cloves
- 3  bay leaves
- 1 tablespoon whole black peppercorns
- 2 ½ teaspoons dried rosemary
- 2 ½ teaspoons dried thyme

## Instructions

1. Bring water to a boil in a large stockpot. Stir in salt and brown sugar until dissolved; return to a boil.
2. Add bay leaves, peppercorns, rosemary, thyme and cloves. Reduce heat to medium-low and simmer for 15 to 20 minutes.
3. Remove from the heat and let cool completely before using.
