## Ingredients

- 2 cups whole wheat flour
- 4 teaspoons baking powder
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 2 tablespoons vegan butter
- 2 tablespoons coconut oil
- 1 cup almond milk

## Instructions

1. Preheat an oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C).
2. Mix flour, baking powder, and salt together in a bowl.
3. Cut vegan butter and coconut oil into flour mixture using a fork until crumbly; stir in almond milk until just combined.
4. Drop batter onto an ungreased baking sheet.
5. Bake in the preheated oven until biscuits are soft and lightly browned, 10 to 12 minutes.
