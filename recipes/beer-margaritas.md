## Ingredients

- 1 (12 fluid ounce) can frozen limeade concentrate
- 12 fluid ounces tequila
- 12 fluid ounces water
- 12 fluid ounces beer
- ice
- 1  lime, cut into wedges

## Instructions

1. Pour limeade, tequila, water, and beer into a large pitcher. Stir until limeade has melted.
2. Add plenty of ice to the pitcher and garnish with lime wedges. Adjust with additional water, if needed.
