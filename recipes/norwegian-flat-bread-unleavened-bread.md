## Ingredients

- 2 cups flour
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ½ cup butter, softened
- ½ cup milk

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Stir flour and salt together in a bowl.
3. Cut butter into flour mixture with a fork or pastry blender until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
4. Stir milk into mixture and knead with hands to form dough.
5. Turn dough out onto a lightly-floured work surface and roll to 1/4-inch thickness.
6. Cut dough into sandwich-size pieces and poke each piece several times with a fork.
7. Transfer slices to a baking sheet.
8. Bake in the preheated oven until just golden brown, about 30 minutes.
