## Ingredients

- 2 cups spring mix
- ¾ cup cherry tomatoes, halved
- ⅔ cup chopped cucumber
- ½ cup sliced salami
- ⅓ cup chopped red onion
- ⅓ cup blue cheese
- ⅓ cup red wine vinegar
- 1 tablespoon extra-virgin olive oil

## Instructions

1. Combine spring mix, tomatoes, cucumber, salami, onion, and blue cheese in a bowl.
2. Whisk vinegar and oil together in a small bowl and pour over salad; toss to coat.
