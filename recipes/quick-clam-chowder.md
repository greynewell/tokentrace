## Ingredients

- 1 (10.75 ounce) can  New England clam chowder
- 1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of potato soup
- 1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of celery soup
- 1 (6.5 ounce) can minced clams
- ½ cup chopped onion
- ½ cup chopped celery
- 1 tablespoon margarine
- 4 cups half-and-half cream

## Instructions

1. In large pan cook onion and celery in butter or margarine.
2. Add clam chowder, cream of potato soup, cream of celery soup, clams, and half-and-half, and heat through.
3. NOTE: Half-and-half is best but milk will do.
