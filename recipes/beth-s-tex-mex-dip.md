## Ingredients

- 1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
- 1 (16 ounce) container sour cream
- 1 (4 ounce) can chopped green chile peppers
- 1 (1 ounce) package taco seasoning mix

## Instructions

1. In a medium bowl, mix the Cheddar cheese, sour cream, green chile peppers and   mix.
2. Chill at least 2 hours in the refrigerator before serving.
