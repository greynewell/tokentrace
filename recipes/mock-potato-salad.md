## Ingredients

- 4 cups cooked brown rice
- 1 ½ cups thinly sliced celery
- 8  radishes, thinly sliced
- 1  cucumber, thinly sliced
- ½ cup chopped sweet onion
- 3  hard-boiled eggs, chopped  (Optional)
- 1 cup reduced-fat mayonnaise
- ¼ cup spicy mustard
- ¾ teaspoon salt

## Instructions

1. Mix rice, celery, radishes, cucumber, and onion together in a bowl; fold in eggs.
2. Stir mayonnaise, mustard, and salt into rice mixture; toss to coat.
3. Refrigerate until flavors blend, 8 hours to overnight.
