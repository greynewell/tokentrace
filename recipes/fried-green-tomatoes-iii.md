## Ingredients

- ½ cup vegetable oil
- 4 large green tomatoes
- 2 cups all-purpose flour
- 2  eggs, beaten
- salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Heat vegetable oil in a large frying pan over a medium-high heat.
2. Slice tomatoes 1/4 inch thick.
3. Place flour in a small bowl.
4. Dip tomatoes into the beaten egg, then dredge the tomatoes in the flour.
5. Fry the tomatoes in the oil.
6. Fry for approximately 2 minutes, flip the tomato and fry on the other side another 2 minutes.
7. Sprinkle with salt and pepper to taste.
