## Ingredients

- 1 (18.25 ounce) package German chocolate cake mix with pudding
- ¾ cup melted butter
- ⅓ cup evaporated milk
- 1 cup chopped pecans
- 13 ounces individually wrapped caramels, unwrapped
- ⅓ cup evaporated milk
- 1 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Spray one 9x13 inch pan with non-stick coating .
3. Combine the cake mix, butter and 1/3 cup evaporated milk.
4. Mix well and pour 2/3 of the batter into pan.
5. Press pecans into batter and bake for 8 to 10 minutes.
6. In a saucepan over medium heat, combine the caramel and 1/3 cup evaporated milk.
7. Stir until melted and smooth; pour over cooled cake mix.
8. Sprinkle chocolate chips on top of caramel and top with spoonfuls of remaining cake mix.
9. Bake for additional l5 to l8 minutes; cool and cut.
