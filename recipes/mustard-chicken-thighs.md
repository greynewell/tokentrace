## Ingredients

- 1 cup Dijon mustard
- 3 tablespoons olive oil
- 3 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
- 3 tablespoons minced garlic
- 2 tablespoons Louisiana-style hot sauce
- 1 tablespoon paprika
- 6  bone-in chicken thighs

## Instructions

1. Mix Dijon mustard, olive oil, Worcestershire, garlic, hot sauce, and paprika together in a bowl.
2. Add chicken thighs and coat well.
3. Cover and let sit in the refrigerator 2 hours to overnight.
4. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
5. Lightly grease a broiling pan.
6. Place chicken thighs in the prepared pan; discard excess marinade.
7. Bake in the preheated oven until chicken is no longer pink in the center and juices run clear, about 45 minutes.
