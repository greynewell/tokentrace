## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon vegetable oil, or as needed
- 1  onion, chopped
- 1 ½  portobello mushrooms, stemmed and sliced
- 8 cups low-sodium chicken broth, divided
- 2 tablespoons miso paste
- sea salt and ground black pepper to taste
- 2 cups cubed, cooked chicken
- 5  green onions, trimmed and chopped

## Instructions

1. Heat oil in a skillet over medium heat; stir in onion.
2. Cook and stir onion until golden, about 5 minutes.
3. Add mushrooms and cook until softened, about 5 minutes more.
4. Pour in 1/2 cup broth; simmer, stirring occasionally, until liquid is reduced, 8 to 10 minutes.
5. Whisk miso paste and 2 tablespoons broth together in a bowl.
6. Pour into the vegetable mixture.
7. Season with salt and pepper.
8. Cook and stir to coat the vegetables, about 1 minute.
9. Add chicken meat and the remaining broth.
10. Reduce heat to low and simmer soup until flavors are well blended, about 30 minutes.
