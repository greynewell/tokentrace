## Ingredients

- 3 cups dry sherry
- 3 cloves garlic, crushed
- 1 cube chicken bouillon
- 6 tablespoons butter
- 1 tablespoon Italian seasoning
- 3 slices  fresh lemon
- 3 quarts fresh mussels, scrubbed and debearded

## Instructions

1. Place the dry sherry, garlic, bouillon, butter, Italian seasoning, and lemon into a large pot.
2. Bring to a simmer over medium heat.
3. Add the mussels, cover, and simmer until the mussels have opened, about 5 minutes.
4. Discard any unopened mussels.
