## Ingredients

- 1 ½ pounds ground beef
- 1  onion, chopped
- 1 (14.75 ounce) can cream-style corn
- 1 (15 ounce) can chili with beans
- 1 (15 ounce) can baked beans with pork
- 1 (15 ounce) can tomato sauce
- 1 (4 ounce) can diced green chiles

## Instructions

1. Crumble the ground beef into a large skillet or Dutch oven over medium-high heat.
2. Add onion; cook and stir until beef is no longer pink.
3. Drain off grease.
4. Reduce heat to medium-low and stir in the corn, chili with beans, baked beans, tomato sauce and green chilies.
5. Cover and simmer for 30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
