## Ingredients

- 1 pound beef rib-eye steak, thinly sliced
- 2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
- 1 tablespoon reduced-sodium soy sauce
- 1 medium onion, sliced into petals
- 4 ounces sliced baby portobello mushrooms
- ½  green bell pepper, thinly sliced
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ½ teaspoon ground mustard
- ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
- 4  hoagie rolls
- 4 slices Provolone cheese

## Instructions

1. Place steak in a bowl and add Worcestershire and soy sauce. Cover and refrigerate 4 hours to overnight. Remove from the refrigerator and let come to room temperature, about 30 minutes.
2. Preheat the air fryer to 380 degrees F (190 degrees C).
3. Combine onion, mushrooms, and bell pepper in a large bowl. Add olive oil, salt, ground mustard, and pepper; stir to coat.
4. Place hoagie rolls in the basket of the air fryer and cook until toasted, about 2 minutes. Transfer rolls to a plate.
5. Place steak in the basket of the air fryer and cook for 3 minutes. Stir and cook for 1 more minute. Transfer to a plate.
6. Add vegetable mix to the basket of the air fryer and cook 5 minutes. Stir and cook until softened, about 5 more minutes.
7. Stir steak into the vegetable mixture. Place cheese slices on top, slightly overlapping. Cook until cheese is melted and bubbly, about 3 minutes. Spoon mixture onto toasted rolls and serve immediately.
