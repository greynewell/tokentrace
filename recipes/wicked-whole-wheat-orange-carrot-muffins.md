## Ingredients

- 1 cup whole wheat flour
- 1 cup all-purpose flour
- 1 tablespoon baking powder
- 2 teaspoons baking soda
- ¼ teaspoon ground cinnamon
- 4  eggs
- ¾ cup brown sugar
- ⅔ cup applesauce
- ⅓ cup vegetable oil
- 3 cups shredded carrots
- 1  orange, zested

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease 12 muffin cups or line with paper muffin liners.
2. Sift whole wheat flour, all-purpose flour, baking powder, baking soda, and cinnamon together in a bowl.
3. Whisk eggs, brown sugar, applesauce, and vegetable oil together in a bowl. Stir egg mixture into flour mixture just until ingredients are moistened; fold in carrots and orange zest. Spoon batter into prepared muffin cups.
4. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted near the center comes out clean, about 30 minutes.
