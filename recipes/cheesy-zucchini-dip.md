## Ingredients

- 3 cups shredded zucchini
- 2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese
- ⅓ cup shredded mozzarella cheese
- 1 ½ cups mayonnaise
- ¼ cup sour cream
- 1 cup chopped pecans
- ¼ cup finely sliced red bell pepper
- salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. In a medium bowl, mix together zucchini, Cheddar cheese, mozzarella cheese, mayonnaise, sour cream, pecans, red bell pepper, salt and pepper.
2. Chill in the refrigerator at least 1 hour before serving.
