## Ingredients

- 1 (8 ounce) package Neufchatel cheese, softened
- Â¼ cup crushed pineapple
- 4 tablespoons sliced almonds
- 8 slices whole-grain bread

## Instructions

1. In a small bowl, mix together softened Neufchatel cheese and crushed pineapple.
2. Spread 1/4 of the pineapple and cheese mixture on a slice of bread.
3. Top with a tablespoon of  almonds, and cover with another slice of bread to make a sandwich. repeat with remaining ingredients.
