## Ingredients

- ¾ pound asparagus, trimmed and cut into 1-inch pieces
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- ¼ cup chopped onion
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 3 cups low-sodium chicken broth
- 1 cup thinly sliced carrots
- ¼ cup minced fresh parsley
- ¼ cup minced fresh basil
- 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ½ cup half-and-half

## Instructions

1. Separate asparagus tips and stalk pieces, and set asparagus tips aside.
2. Heat olive oil in a saucepan over medium heat; cook and stir onion until tender, 5 to 10 minutes.
3. Add garlic; cook and stir until fragrant, about 1 minute.
4. Mix chicken broth, asparagus stalk pieces, carrots, parsley, basil, pepper, and salt into onion mixture; bring to a boil.
5. Reduce heat to medium and simmer until asparagus and carrots are tender, 10 to 15 minutes.
6. Pour soup into a blender no more than half full.
7. Cover and hold lid down; pulse a few times before leaving on to blend.
8. Puree in batches until smooth.
9. Return soup to saucepan, add asparagus tips, and stir in half-and-half.
10. Simmer soup until tips are tender, about 5 minutes.
