## Ingredients

- 1 (5.4 ounce) package Knorr® Rice Sides™ - Herb & Butter
- 1  orange bell pepper, seeded and chopped
- 1  cucumber, peeled and sliced
- 1 (10.5 ounce) container cherry or grape tomatoes, halved
- 1  red onion, chopped
- 1 (10 ounce) package frozen shelled edamame, prepared according to package directions
- 2  limes, juiced
- 3 tablespoons olive oil
- 2 tablespoons white wine vinegar
- Salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Prepare rice according to package directions.
2. Transfer to a large bowl and let cool.
3. Add bell peppers, cucumbers, tomatoes, onion, edamame, lime juice, olive oil, salt and pepper.
4. Mix well and refrigerate until ready to serve!
