## Ingredients

- 1  avocado - peeled, pitted, and mashed
- ½ (15 ounce) can black beans, drained and rinsed
- 1 tablespoon lime juice
- 1 clove garlic, minced, or more to taste
- ⅓ cup shredded Cheddar cheese
- 1 pinch sea salt to taste

## Instructions

1. Mix avocado, black beans, lime juice, and garlic together in a microwave-safe bowl. Sprinkle with Cheddar cheese.
2. Microwave dip on high until cheese is melted, about 1 minute. Season with sea salt if desired.
