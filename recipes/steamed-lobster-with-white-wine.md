## Ingredients

- 2  lobster tails
- ½ cup white wine
- ¼ cup cherry tomatoes, or more to taste  (Optional)
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 2 tablespoons garlic powder, divided
- 1 teaspoon salt

## Instructions

1. Carefully cut lobster tails down the center using scissors, making a wide enough incision for the meat to "flower" out during cooking.
2. De-vein the meat, if desired.
3. Combine wine, cherry tomatoes, butter, 1 tablespoon garlic powder, and salt in a large pot; add lobster tails.
4. Bring heat to medium and steam lobster tails for 4 minutes.
5. Add the remaining 1 tablespoon garlic powder and continue steaming until meat is cooked through and tender, about 3 minutes more.
