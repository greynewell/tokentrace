## Ingredients

- 2 ¾ cups baking mix
- ½ teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes
- ¾ teaspoon garlic powder
- 1 cup milk
- 1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
- 2 tablespoons butter, melted

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C).
2. Combine biscuit mix, pepper, and 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder in a large bowl.
3. With a fork, stir in milk and cheese until mixture forms a soft dough.
4. Drop by 1/4 cupfuls onto greased cookie sheet.
5. Combine butter and remaining 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder, and brush on top of biscuits.
6. Bake for 10 to 12 minutes, or until golden brown.
