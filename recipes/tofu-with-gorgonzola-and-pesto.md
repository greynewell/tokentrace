## Ingredients

- 1 (12 ounce) package firm tofu
- 1 pint cherry tomatoes, halved
- ½ cup prepared basil pesto sauce
- 1 cup crumbled Gorgonzola cheese
- ½ cup chopped fresh basil leaves

## Instructions

1. Drain the tofu and slice horizontally into 4 slices.
2. Pat each slice dry and place on a serving plate.
3. Divide the tomatoes evenly amongst the tofu portions.
4. Top each with about 2 tablespoons of basil pesto and 1/4 cup of Gorgonzola cheese.
5. Garnish with fresh basil leaves.
6. Serve cold.
