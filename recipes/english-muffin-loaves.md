## Ingredients

- 2 (.25 ounce) packages active dry yeast
- 6 cups all-purpose flour
- 1 tablespoon white sugar
- 2 cups milk
- ¼ teaspoon baking soda
- ½ cup water
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 2 tablespoons cornmeal

## Instructions

1. In a small saucepan, heat the milk and water until very warm.
2. Combine 3 cups flour, yeast, sugar, salt, and soda in a large bowl.
3. Add  the warm milk and water, and beat well.
4. Stir in enough of the remaining flour to make a stiff batter.
5. Spoon into two 9 x 5 inch loaf pans that have been greased and dusted with the cornmeal.
6. Cover, and let rise in a warm place for 45 minutes.
7. Bake at 400 degrees F (205 degrees C) for 25 minutes.
8. Remove from pans immediately, and let cool.
