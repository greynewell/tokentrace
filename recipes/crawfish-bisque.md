## Ingredients

- 6 tablespoons butter
- 1  onion, chopped
- ¼ cup all-purpose flour
- 2 cups chicken broth
- 3 cups half-and-half cream
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 ½ pounds peeled crawfish tails
- 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
- 1 pinch cayenne pepper, or to taste

## Instructions

1. Melt the butter in a large saucepan over medium heat, and cook and stir the onion until golden, about 8 minutes.
2. Stir in the flour until smooth, and cook and stir the mixture until it turns a pale golden color, about 5 minutes.
3. Whisk in the chicken broth, stirring constantly to avoid lumps, then mix in the half-and-half, salt, crawfish tails, Worcestershire sauce, and cayenne pepper.
4. Reduce heat to medium-low, and continue whisking as soup comes to a simmer.
5. Allow the bisque to simmer for about 5 minutes, until slightly thickened.
6. Do not boil.
