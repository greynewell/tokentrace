## Ingredients

- 1  fennel bulb, trimmed and diced
- 1 (10 ounce) can mandarin orange segments
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 2 tablespoons white wine vinegar
- salt and ground black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Combine the fennel and mandarin oranges with a tablespoon of the juice from the canned oranges in a bowl.
2. Add the olive oil and vinegar.
3. Season with salt and pepper; stir.
4. Allow to sit 10 to 15 minutes before serving.
