## Ingredients

- 2  eggs
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
- 2 cups panko bread crumbs
- 1 head cauliflower, broken into florets

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
2. Whisk eggs, salt, and pepper together in a bowl. Place panko bread crumbs in another bowl.
3. Dip each cauliflower floret in egg mixture to coat, then dip each floret into bread crumbs to coat; place on prepared baking sheet.
4. Bake in the preheated oven until tender and lightly browned, turning once, about 25 minutes.
