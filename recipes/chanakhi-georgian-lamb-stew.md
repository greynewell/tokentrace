## Ingredients

- 1 ¼ pounds cubed lamb meat
- 1  onion, chopped
- 2 ¼  potatoes, cubed
- 3  eggplants, cubed
- 2 cloves garlic, finely chopped
- 1 tablespoon tomato paste
- 2  tomatoes, sliced
- 1  red bell pepper, chopped
- 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
- 2 tablespoons chopped fresh dill
- salt and ground black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. In a heavy casserole dish, layer the following order: lamb, onion, potatoes, eggplants, garlic, tomato paste, tomatoes, red bell pepper, parsley, and dill, seasoning each layer with salt and pepper.
3. Pour a few spoonfuls of water on top.
4. Cover with a lid.
5. Bake in the preheated oven until lamb is tender, about 2 hours.
