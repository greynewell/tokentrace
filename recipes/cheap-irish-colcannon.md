## Ingredients

- 2 ½ cups thinly sliced green cabbage
- 1 cup chopped broccoli
- 3 cups hot prepared instant mashed potatoes
- 4 tablespoons butter, or as needed
- 1 pinch garlic salt
- ground black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Place the cabbage and broccoli in a saucepan, and fill with just enough water to cover the vegetables.
2. Cover, and bring the water to a boil over high heat.
3. Reduce the heat to medium, and simmer until vegetables are tender, about 5 minutes; drain.
4. Stir in the prepared mashed potatoes, butter, garlic salt, and black pepper.
