## Ingredients

- 2 ½ tablespoons flax seeds
- 1 cup almonds
- ¾ cup sesame seeds
- ½ cup sunflower seeds
- ½ cup cocoa nibs
- ¼ cup raw cocoa powder
- 3 tablespoons raw maca powder  (Optional)
- 3 tablespoons chia seeds  (Optional)
- 1 ½ teaspoons spirulina powder
- 1 teaspoon ground cardamom
- ¼ teaspoon ground cinnamon
- 1 pinch salt
- 1 cup Medjool dates, pitted
- ½ cup tahini
- ½ cup almond butter
- ⅓ cup honey
- ½ teaspoon vanilla extract
- ½ cup shredded unsweetened coconut

## Instructions

1. Soak flax seeds in a bowl of water for 10 minutes.
2. Drain.
3. Blend almonds in a food processor or blender until finely chopped; transfer to a bowl.
4. Mix sesame seeds, sunflower seeds, cocoa nibs, cocoa powder, maca powder, chia seeds, spirulina powder, cardamom, cinnamon, and salt into almonds.
5. Process dates in the food processor or blender; add to almond mixture.
6. Combine tahini, almond butter, honey, and vanilla extract in a separate bowl.
7. Slowly mix almond mixture into tahini mixture until it holds together.
8. Form mixture into bite-size balls.
9. Spread coconut in a shallow bowl.
10. Roll the balls in the coconut until coated.
11. Chill balls in the refrigerator, at least 30 minutes.
