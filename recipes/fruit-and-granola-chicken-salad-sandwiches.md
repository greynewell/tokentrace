## Ingredients

- 2 cups plain chicken salad
- ¾ cup crushed roasted almond crunchy granola
- ½ cup light creamy salad dressing
- ¼ cup seedless green grapes, quartered
- ¼ cup chopped fresh pineapple
- ¼ cup chopped red apple
- ¼ cup drained canned mandarin orange segments
- 1 tablespoon packed brown sugar, or more to taste
- 6  croissants

## Instructions

1. Mix chicken salad, granola, salad dressing, grapes, pineapple, apple, mandarin orange segments, and brown sugar together in a medium bowl. Refrigerate until needed or use immediately.
2. Cut croissants in half horizontally. Fill with the chicken salad.
