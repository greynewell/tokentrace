## Ingredients

- 4  (6-inch) crusty sandwich rolls, unsplit
- 1 pound lean ground beef
- 1 (15 ounce) can sloppy joe sauce
- 1 cup Borden® Mild Cheddar Shreds

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
2. Cut a hole at the top of each sandwich roll and hollow out the bread leaving about a 1/2 inch shell (discard the extra bread or save for another use).
3. Place rolls on a rimmed baking sheet.
4. Lightly oil a skillet and place over medium to medium-high heat.
5. Cook ground beef and stir to crumble.
6. Cook until no longer pink, about 10 minutes.
7. Use a paper towel to sop up any grease, if necessary.
8. Pour sloppy joe sauce over the beef and continue cooking until heated through, about 5 minutes.
9. Evenly spoon beef mixture into each sandwich roll and top with shredded Cheddar cheese.
10. Bake in preheated oven until cheese is melted, about 10 minutes.
