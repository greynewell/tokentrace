## Ingredients

- 2 large  cucumbers, peeled and sliced
- ½ cup diced sweet onion (such as Vidalia®)
- 2 tablespoons white vinegar
- 2 tablespoons white sugar
- 1 ½ tablespoons sour cream
- 1 teaspoon salt
- ½ teaspoon cracked black pepper

## Instructions

1. Combine cucumbers, onion, vinegar, sugar, sour cream, salt, and pepper together in a bowl.
2. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate until chilled, 20 to 30 minutes.
