## Ingredients

- 2 cups white sugar
- ½ cup butter
- ½ cup milk
- 3 tablespoons cocoa powder
- 3 cups quick cooking oats
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

## Instructions

1. Place sugar, butter, and milk in a medium saucepan. Bring to a rolling boil over medium-low heat; boil for one minute, stirring constantly.
2. Remove from heat and mix in oats, cocoa powder, and vanilla. Drop by teaspoonfuls onto waxed paper. Let cool until hardened.
