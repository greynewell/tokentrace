## Ingredients

- 4 large beets
- ¼ cup water
- 3 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
- 1 tablespoon honey
- 1 ½ teaspoons red wine vinegar
- ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
- ¼ cup chopped sweet onion
- ¼ cup chopped walnuts
- ⅓ cup crumbled blue cheese
- 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
- salt to taste

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
2. Arrange beets in a baking dish and pour in water; cover with aluminum foil.
3. Bake in the preheated oven until beets are tender, about 1 hour.
4. Allow beets to cool overnight; remove outer skin and chop beets into bite size pieces.
5. Whisk balsamic vinegar, honey, red wine vinegar, and black pepper together in a bowl.
6. Stir beets, onion, and walnuts into vinegar dressing.
7. Sprinkle with blue cheese, parsley, and salt to serve.
