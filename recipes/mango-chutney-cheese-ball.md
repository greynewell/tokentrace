## Ingredients

- 11 ounces cream cheese, at room temperature
- 1 cup golden raisins
- 1 (2.1 ounce) package cooked bacon, diced
- 1 bunch green onions, chopped
- 3 tablespoons sour cream
- 1 (9 ounce) jar mango chutney

## Instructions

1. In a medium bowl, stir together the cream cheese, raisins, bacon, green onions and sour cream.
2. Form into a ball, and wrap in plastic wrap.
3. Refrigerate for at least 3 hours or overnight before serving.
4. To serve, place the cheese ball on a serving tray, and pour the mango chutney over it.
5. Surround with crackers for dipping.
