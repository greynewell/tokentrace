## Ingredients

- 4 slices bacon
- ¾ pound fresh spinach
- 2  hard-cooked eggs, sliced
- 2 tablespoons sweet onion, chopped
- 2 tablespoons chopped raw almonds
- 1 tablespoon raw sunflower seeds
- ½ cup vegetable oil
- ½ cup ketchup
- ¼ cup cider vinegar
- 2 tablespoons brown sugar
- 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- 1 teaspoon salt
- ½ teaspoon ground black pepper

## Instructions

1. Arrange bacon in a large skillet and cook over medium-high heat, turning occasionally, until evenly browned, about 10 minutes. Drain bacon on paper towels and crumble.
2. Layer spinach, bacon, eggs, and onion in a large bowl. Sprinkle with almonds and sunflower seeds. Whisk vegetable oil, ketchup, vinegar, brown sugar, Worcestershire sauce, garlic, salt, and black pepper in a bowl; pour over spinach mixture and toss. Serve immediately.
