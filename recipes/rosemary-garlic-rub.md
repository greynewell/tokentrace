## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon ground black pepper
- 1 tablespoon kosher salt
- 3 tablespoons chopped fresh rosemary
- 1 tablespoon dried rosemary
- 8 cloves garlic, diced
- ⅓ cup olive oil

## Instructions

1. Mix black pepper, kosher salt, fresh rosemary, dried rosemary, and garlic in a small bowl.
2. Gradually stir enough olive oil into spice mixture to form a thick paste. Rub into desired meats before grilling.
