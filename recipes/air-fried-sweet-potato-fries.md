## Ingredients

- 1  sweet potatoes, peeled and cut into 1/2 inch wide fries
- 1 tablespoon canola oil
- ½ teaspoon kosher salt
- ¼ teaspoon pepper
- ⅛ teaspoon garlic powder
- ⅛ teaspoon ground sweet paprika

## Instructions

1. Preheat the air fryer to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
2. Cut sweet potato into 1/2-inch wide fries. Place in a bowl with canola oil; toss to coat. Season with salt, pepper, garlic powder, and paprika; mix until all fries are evenly coated.
3. Working in batches if necessary, place an even layer of fries in the air fryer basket.
4. Cook in the preheated air fryer until golden, about 10 minutes. Repeat to cook remaining fries.
