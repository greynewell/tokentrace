## Ingredients

- 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese
- 1 (8 ounce) container sour cream
- 8 ounces imitation crabmeat, flaked
- ¼  one lemon, juiced
- ¼ teaspoon garlic salt

## Instructions

1. In a large bowl, mix together cream cheese, sour cream, crab meat, lemon juice and garlic salt.
2. Cover and chill in the refrigerator for at least 2 hours before serving.
