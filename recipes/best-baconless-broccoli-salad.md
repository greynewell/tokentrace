## Ingredients

- 6 cups chopped fresh broccoli
- ¼ cup chopped red onion
- ¾ cup dried cranberries
- ½ cup pumpkin seeds
- 2 tablespoons flax seeds
- ½ cup mayonnaise
- 2 tablespoons raspberry vinegar
- 2 tablespoons white sugar
- ½ cup chopped pecans

## Instructions

1. Make the salad by tossing together the broccoli, onion, cranberries, pumpkin seeds, and flax seeds in a large bowl.
2. Whisk together the mayonnaise, vinegar, and white sugar; pour over the salad. Toss to evenly coat. Chill at least 30 minutes. Sprinkle with pecans before serving.
