## Ingredients

- ½ (8 ounce) package cream cheese
- ½ (8 ounce) container sour cream
- 6 tablespoons shredded Cheddar cheese
- 1 tablespoon sliced green onion
- 3 teaspoons mustard
- 6 (8 inch) flour tortillas
- ½ pound diced cooked chicken
- 1 ½ cups shredded napa cabbage

## Instructions

1. Combine cream cheese, sour cream, Cheddar cheese, green onion, and mustard in a bowl; stir well.
2. Spread 2 tablespoons of the mixture over each tortilla.
3. Top each tortilla with 1/4 cup chicken and 1/4 cup cabbage.
4. Roll up and serve.
