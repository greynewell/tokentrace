## Ingredients

- 2  boneless, skinless chicken breasts, halved horizontally
- 8 ounces crumbled blue cheese
- 6 ounces walnut halves
- 8 slices bacon

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Pound chicken slices until they are even in thickness and about 1/4-inch thick.
3. Spread blue cheese and walnuts on top of each chicken piece.
4. Roll chicken breasts over filling.
5. Place 2 bacon slices side by side on a work surface.
6. Place each chicken roll at one end of bacon slices and roll bacon around chicken; secure with toothpicks.
7. Repeat with remaining chicken rolls and bacon.
8. Heat a skillet to medium-high heat and cook bacon-wrapped chicken rolls in the hot skillet until the bacon is browned and crisp, 4 to 5 minutes per side.
9. Transfer chicken rolls to preheated oven; bake until chicken is no longer pink in the center and the juices run clear, 25 to 35 minutes.
