## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
- 1 ½ tablespoons honey
- 2 tablespoons finely chopped sweet onion
- 2 teaspoons coarse-grain mustard
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
- ¼ cup olive oil
- 4  hard-cooked eggs, peeled
- 8 cups mixed greens
- 1 ½ cups grape tomatoes, halved
- 4 slices bacon, cooked and chopped

## Instructions

1. Dressing: In a small bowl combine apple cider vinegar, honey, onion, mustard, salt and pepper.
2. Slowly whisk in oil.
3. Set aside.
4. Salad: Finely grate or chop 2 eggs; slice remaining 2 eggs.
5. In a large salad bowl, combine greens, tomatoes, bacon and grated eggs.
6. Toss with dressing to coat and garnish with remaining egg slices.
7. Serve immediately.
