## Ingredients

- 2 (6 ounce) containers raspberry nonfat yogurt
- 2 (3.25 ounce) cups Snack Pack® Vanilla Pudding
- 1 ½ cups Reddi-wip® Fat Free Dairy Whipped Topping
- 1 (9 inch) prepared angel food cake, cut into 1-inch pieces
- 4 cups assorted fresh berries (such as blueberries, raspberries and blackberries)

## Instructions

1. Stir together yogurt and pudding in medium bowl until blended.
2. Fold in Reddi-wip.
3. Place about 8 cake pieces each in 8 small jars or glasses.
4. Top each with 2 tablespoons pudding mixture and 6 berries.
5. Repeat cake and pudding mixture layers.
6. Cover; refrigerate at least 2 hours.
7. Top with additional Reddi-wip and remaining berries just before serving.
