## Ingredients

- 2 cups whole wheat flour, or as needed
- 2 cups spelt flour
- 2 cups quick-cooking rolled oats
- ¼ cup flax seeds
- 2 ½ cups warm water
- ½ cup natural peanut butter

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Combine wheat flour, spelt flour, oats, and flax seeds in large bowl.
3. Stir in water and peanut butter.
4. Knead dough on a lightly floured surface, mixing in more flour as needed until dough is smooth and no longer sticky.
5. Roll out dough to 1/4 inch thick.
6. Cut with small cookie cutters into desired shapes, and place 3/4 inch apart on greased cookie sheets.
7. Bake 40 minutes.
8. Turn oven off; leave biscuits in oven until completely cool.
