## Ingredients

- 1 (8 ounce) jar pitted green olives
- 1 (5 ounce) jar pitted kalamata olives, drained
- 2  bay leaves
- ¼ teaspoon dried rosemary
- ½ teaspoon fennel seed
- ½ teaspoon dried thyme
- 6 tablespoons distilled white vinegar

## Instructions

1. Place olives in a colander, and rinse in cool, running water.
2. Rinse the olive jars out with water.
3. In a large mixing bowl, combine bay leaves, rosemary, fennel, thyme, and white vinegar.
4. Add olives, and toss until olives are coated.
5. Place the mixture into the two jars; make sure each jar gets one bay leaf.
6. Fill any empty space in the jars up with water.
7. Cap jar, and shake well.
8. Refrigerate for at least 2 days.
