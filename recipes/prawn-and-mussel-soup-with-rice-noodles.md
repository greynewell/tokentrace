## Ingredients

- 4 ounces mussels, cleaned and debearded
- ½ (8 ounce) package dried rice noodles
- 1 quart water
- 1  tomato, diced
- ⅓ cup diced onion
- 2 tablespoons tomato puree
- 1 tablespoon sauerkraut
- 2 teaspoons chile oil
- 1 teaspoon sliced garlic
- ½ teaspoon salt
- 4 ounces prawns, peeled and deveined
- 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley

## Instructions

1. Soak mussels in a bowl of cold salted water for 20 minutes.
2. Drain.
3. Bring a small pot of water to a boil.
4. Add noodles; simmer until tender, 2 to 3 minutes.
5. Drain and rinse with cold water.
6. Bring 1 quart of water to a boil in the same pot.
7. Add tomato, onion, tomato puree, sauerkraut, chile oil, garlic, and salt.
8. Simmer until onion softens, about 10 minutes.
9. Stir in mussels; simmer for 1 minute.
10. Stir in prawns; simmer until opaque, 1 to 2 minutes.
11. Stir in rice noodles.
12. Garnish with parsley.
