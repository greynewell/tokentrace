## Ingredients

- 2 cups whole wheat flour
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- ½ cup vegetable oil
- ½ cup pure maple syrup
- 4  eggs, separated
- 2 tablespoons milk
- 3  bananas, mashed
- ½ cup chopped walnuts

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Lightly grease a 9x5 inch loaf pan.
3. In a large mixing bowl, combine flour and salt.
4. Add oil, syrup and egg yolks; stir well.
5. Add milk and mashed bananas; stir well.
6. Add chopped nuts and stir.
7. Beat egg whites until stiff; fold into batter.
8. Pour batter into prepared loaf pan.
9. Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 50 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into center of the loaf comes out clean.
