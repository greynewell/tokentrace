## Ingredients

- 2 teaspoons butter
- 1  onion, chopped
- 2 cloves garlic, chopped, or more to taste
- 2 (16 ounce) packages frozen peas
- ½ teaspoon dried dill, or to taste
- 1 (900 ml) carton chicken broth, or as needed
- ⅓ cup milk  (Optional)
- salt and ground black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Melt butter in a large pot over medium-high heat. Saute onion in hot butter until golden and translucent, about 10 minutes. Stir garlic into onions and saute until fragrant, about 1 minute.
2. Stir peas and dill into onion mixture and pour in enough chicken broth to cover. Bring to a boil, reduce heat to medium-low, and simmer until hot, 10 to 15 minutes. Stir milk into soup.
3. Puree soup with an immersion blender or pour pea soup into a blender no more than half full. Cover and hold lid down; pulse a few times before leaving on to blend. Puree in batches until smooth. Season with salt and pepper.
