## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon butter, softened
- 1  glazed raised ring doughnut, halved horizontally
- 2 slices Gouda cheese

## Instructions

1. Spread butter onto the cut sides of the doughnut.
2. Preheat a skillet over medium heat.
3. Place 1 doughnut slice, butter side down, into the skillet.
4. Lay Gouda cheese on doughnut slice; top with remaining doughnut slice, butter side out.
5. Flatten with a spatula.
6. Cook, flipping once, until lightly browned on both sides and cheese is melted, about 4 minutes.
