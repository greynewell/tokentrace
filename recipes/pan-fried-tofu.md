## Ingredients

- 1 (16 ounce) package water-packed firm tofu, drained and rinsed
- 6 tablespoons nutritional yeast
- ½ teaspoon garlic powder, or to taste  (Optional)
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- 1 ½ tablespoons olive oil

## Instructions

1. Slice tofu into 1/4-inch-thick slabs.
2. Place nutritional yeast on a plate. Mix in garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
3. Roll tofu slices in nutritional yeast mixture until evenly coated.
4. Heat oil in a nonstick skillet over low to medium heat. Add tofu slices; cook until golden brown and crispy, 3 to 5 minutes per side.
