## Ingredients

- ⅓ cup lemon juice
- 3 tablespoons butter
- 1 tablespoon lemon zest
- 1 tablespoon minced garlic, or more to taste
- 1 cup frozen cooked salad shrimp, thawed
- 1 pinch ground black pepper

## Instructions

1. Heat a skillet over high heat.
2. Melt lemon juice, butter, and lemon zest together.
3. Add shrimp and garlic; season with pepper.
4. Cook until shrimp are hot, about 3 minutes.
