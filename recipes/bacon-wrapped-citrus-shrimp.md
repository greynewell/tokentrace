## Ingredients

- 1 (8 ounce) can pineapple chunks, drained
- 12  jumbo shrimp, peeled and deveined
- 6 slices bacon, halved crosswise
- 12  toothpicks
- ½ cup lime vinaigrette dressing
- ¼ cup chopped green onion

## Instructions

1. Place a pineapple chunk in the inner curve of each shrimp and wrap shrimp with bacon.
2. Secure by threading a toothpick through the bacon.
3. Arrange wrapped shrimp on a baking sheet and drizzle with lime vinaigrette dressing.
4. Refrigerate for 30 minutes.
5. Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat; arrange shrimp on the skillet and fry until bacon is crispy and browned, 2 to 3 minutes per side.
6. Transfer shrimp to a serving tray and sprinkle with green onion.
