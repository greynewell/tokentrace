## Ingredients

- 10  ripe tomatoes, quartered
- ½  red onion, diced
- 1  serrano chile pepper, seeded and diced
- ½ cup chopped fresh cilantro, or to taste
- 2  limes, juiced
- 3 cloves garlic, peeled
- 1 teaspoon red wine vinegar, or to taste
- salt to taste
- ¼ cup extra-virgin olive oil  (Optional)

## Instructions

1. Blend tomatoes, red onion, and serrano chile pepper in a blender until smooth; add cilantro, lime juice, garlic, vinegar, and salt; blend.
2. Slowly pour olive oil into mixture while the blender is running.
3. Continue blending for 10 more seconds.
