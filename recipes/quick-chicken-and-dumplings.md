## Ingredients

- 1 ½ cups milk
- 1 cup frozen green peas and carrots
- 1 cup cut-up cooked chicken
- 1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of chicken mushroom soup
- 1 cup Original Bisquick® mix
- ⅓ cup milk
- Paprika, if desired

## Instructions

1. Heat 1 1/2 cups milk, the peas and carrots, chicken and soup to boiling in 3-quart saucepan, stirring frequently.
2. Stir Bisquick mix and 1/3 cup milk until soft dough forms.
3. Drop dough by 8 spoonfuls onto chicken mixture (do not drop directly into liquid).
4. Sprinkle with paprika.
5. Cook uncovered over low heat 10 minutes.
6. Cover and cook 10 minutes longer.
