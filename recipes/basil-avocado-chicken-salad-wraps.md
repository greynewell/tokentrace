## Ingredients

- 2  ripe avocados - peeled, pitted, and mashed
- 1  lime, juiced
- 2 tablespoons chopped fresh basil
- ½ teaspoon garlic salt
- ½ teaspoon ground black pepper
- 4 cups chopped cooked chicken
- ¼ cup raisins
- ¼ cup chopped walnuts
- 2 heads Bibb lettuce, leaves separated

## Instructions

1. Mix mashed avocados, lime juice, basil, garlic salt, and pepper together in a bowl.
2. Add chicken, raisins, and walnuts to avocado mixture and stir until evenly coated.
3. Spoon chicken mixture into lettuce leaves and roll leaves around filling.
