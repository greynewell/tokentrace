## Ingredients

- 4  dried scallops
- 1 pound pork spare ribs, cut into 1-inch pieces
- 3  lotus roots, peeled and cut into 1/3-inch slices
- water to cover
- salt to taste

## Instructions

1. Place dried scallops in a small bowl and cover with water.
2. Let soak until softened, 30 minutes to 1 hour.
3. Drain and cut into small pieces.
4. Bring a large pot of water to a boil.
5. Add pork spare ribs; cook for 5 to 8 minutes.
6. Drain and rinse under cold water.
7. Combine scallops, spare ribs, and lotus roots in a pot.
8. Add three times the volume of water; bring to a boil.
9. Cook at a boil for 30 minutes.
10. Reduce heat to medium and simmer, adding more water if needed, until lotus roots are soft and water is reduced by a third, 2 to 3 hours.
11. Stir and season with salt.
