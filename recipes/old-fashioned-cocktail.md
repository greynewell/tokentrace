## Ingredients

- 1  sugar cube
- 1 teaspoon water
- 1 dash bitters
- 2 fluid ounces whiskey (rye or bourbon)
- 1  lemon twist
- ice cubes
- 1  orange slice, for garnish
- 1  maraschino cherry, for garnish

## Instructions

1. Muddle sugar cube, water, and bitters in an old fashioned glass for 1 minute. Pour in whiskey; stir for 1 minute.
2. Squeeze lemon twist over the glass and drop it in. Add ice cubes. Garnish with orange slice and maraschino cherry; serve with a swizzle stick.
