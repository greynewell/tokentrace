## Ingredients

- 5 (6.5 ounce) cans minced clams, drained
- 1 large potato, diced
- 1  onion, chopped
- ½ teaspoon salt
- 1 pinch ground white pepper
- 4 slices bacon
- 1 large carrot, grated
- 8 ounces tomato sauce
- 2  tomatoes, chopped
- 1 stalk celery, chopped
- 2 cups water

## Instructions

1. Dice bacon, cook in large saucepan with onion and celery until crisp and onion golden.
2. Pour off most of fat.
3. Add 2 cups water, potatoes, carrots, salt, pepper, thawed tomatoes, and clam juice from cans.
4. Simmer 1 hour.
5. Add tomato sauce and clams.
6. Simmer 15-20 minutes or until just boiling.
