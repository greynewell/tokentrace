## Ingredients

- 3 tablespoons sliced almonds
- 4  trout fillets
- ¾ cup all-purpose flour
- ½ cup unsalted butter
- 3 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil, divided
- 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
- 1 large tomato, diced
- 1 tablespoon capers

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
2. Spread almonds onto a baking sheet.
3. Roast in the preheated oven until almonds start to turn golden brown and become fragrant, 3 to 4 minutes.
4. Place trout on a flat work surface.
5. Slice horizontally through the middle, being careful not to cut all the way through to the other side.
6. Open the fillet like an open book.
7. Dredge trout lightly in flour.
8. Repeat with remaining fillets.
9. Melt 1/2 cup butter and 2 tablespoons olive oil in a skillet over medium-high heat.
10. Place trout skin-side down on the hot skillet and cook until bottom is firm and lightly browned, 3 to 4 minutes.
11. Turn over and cook until trout flakes easily with a fork, 2 to 3 minutes more.
12. Heat 2 tablespoons butter and remaining olive oil in a saucepan over medium-high heat.
13. Saute tomato and capers until juices are released, 2 to 3 minutes.
14. Place each trout fillet skin-side down on a plate and top with the roasted almonds and tomato-caper saute.
