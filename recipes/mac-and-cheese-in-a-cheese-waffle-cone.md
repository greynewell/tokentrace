## Ingredients

- 5 ounces Cheddar cheese, finely grated
- 1 (7.25 ounce) package macaroni and cheese mix
- ¼ cup whole milk
- ¼ cup butter
- 1 ounce sliced pepperoni
- 1 teaspoon salt

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
3. Sprinkle Cheddar cheese onto the prepared baking sheet.
4. Bake in the preheated oven until edges start to brown and cheese is bubbling, 5 to 10 minutes.
5. Remove from oven and cool to room temperature.
6. Cut melted cheese into squares and form into cones.
7. Freeze cones for 5 minutes.
8. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
9. Cook elbow macaroni in the boiling water, stirring occasionally until cooked through but firm to the bite, 8 minutes.
10. Drain and return pasta to pot.
11. Mix milk, butter, pepperoni, salt, and cheese packet into pasta until evenly combined.
12. Fill cheese cones with macaroni and cheese.
