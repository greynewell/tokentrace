## Ingredients

- 1 ½ cups plain non-fat yogurt
- ⅓ cup honey
- ½ cup prepared Dijon-style mustard
- 1 tablespoon chopped green onion
- 1 dash chili sauce

## Instructions

1. In a medium bowl, mix together plain non-fat yogurt, honey, prepared Dijon-style mustard, green onion and chili sauce.
2. Chill in the refrigerator approximately 1 hour before serving.
