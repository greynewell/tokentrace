## Ingredients

- 6 large tomatoes, peeled and quartered
- 1  green bell pepper, sliced in rings
- 1  red onion, sliced in rings
- ¾ cup white wine vinegar
- 1 ½ teaspoons celery salt
- 1 ½ teaspoons mustard seed
- 4 ½ teaspoons white sugar
- ⅛ teaspoon cayenne pepper
- ⅛ teaspoon ground black pepper
- ¼ cup water
- 1 large cucumber

## Instructions

1. In a large bowl, combine the tomatoes, bell peppers and onions.
2. Prepare the dressing in a saucepan by combining the vinegar, celery salt, mustard seed, sugar, cayenne pepper, black pepper and water.
3. Boil for 1 minute and pour hot dressing over vegetables.
4. Refrigerate until chilled.
5. Before serving, peel and slice cucumber; add to vegetables and toss.
