## Ingredients

- 5 tablespoons olive oil
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 pound fresh mushrooms, sliced
- 3 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
- 3 tablespoons white wine
- salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium-low heat. Add garlic and saut√© until fragrant but not brown, 1 to 2 minutes.
2. Add mushrooms and cook, stirring occasionally, until they begin to release their juices, about 2 minutes. Stir in balsamic vinegar and wine and cook until mushrooms are tender, about 2 more minutes. Season with salt and pepper.
