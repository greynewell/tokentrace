## Ingredients

- 32  vanilla wafers, crushed
- 1  egg, beaten
- ¾ cup water
- ⅓ cup apricot nectar
- 2 teaspoons cornstarch
- ¼ cup packed brown sugar
- 3 tablespoons red wine vinegar
- 1 tablespoon ketchup
- 2 cups vegetable oil
- ¾ pound medium shrimp - peeled and deveined

## Instructions

1. In a small bowl mix vanilla wafers, egg, and water until well blended.
2. Refrigerate 1 to 2 hours.
3. To make dipping sauce: In a small sauce pan blend nectar into cornstarch.
4. Stir in brown sugar, vinegar and catsup.
5. Heat mixture over a medium heat, stirring constantly until mixture thickens and comes to a boil.
6. Set aside.
7. In a stockpot or deep fryer heat 2 cups oil to 375 degrees F (175 degrees C).
8. Dip shrimp in vanilla wafer batter and fry 4 to 6 shrimp at a time until golden brown.
9. Drain well.
10. Serve hot with dipping sauce.
