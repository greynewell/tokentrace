## Ingredients

- 1 cup peanut butter
- ½ cup milk powder
- ¼ cup honey
- ½ cup chocolate chips  (Optional)

## Instructions

1. Stir peanut butter, milk powder, honey, and chocolate chips together in a bowl until evenly mixed.
2. Scoop up about 1 1/2 tablespoon of mixture per serving, and shape into round balls.
3. Arrange on a plate.
4. Freeze until set, about 30 minutes.
