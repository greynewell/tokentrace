## Ingredients

- 2 cups white sugar
- 3  egg whites
- 3 tablespoons light corn syrup
- 3 tablespoons cold water
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

## Instructions

1. In the top of a double boiler, combine the sugar, egg whites, corn syrup, water and vanilla.
2. Place over boiling water and beat with an electric mixer on high speed for 7 minutes, or until frosting forms peaks when beaters are raised.
3. Remove from heat.
4. Use immediately to frost a cooled cake.
