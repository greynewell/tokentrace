## Ingredients

- ½ (16 ounce) package squid ink linguine
- 2 cups white wine
- 1  leek, chopped
- ¼ cup unsalted butter, softened
- 4  green onions, chopped
- 1  shallot, chopped
- 4 cloves garlic, chopped
- 6 large fresh scallops, or more to taste
- 12  New Zealand green-lipped mussels, cleaned
- salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
- ½  lemon, sliced, divided
- ¼ cup heavy whipping cream
- ½  baguette, sliced and toasted

## Instructions

1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
2. Cook linguine at a boil until tender yet firm to the bite, about 11 minutes; drain.
3. Combine white wine, leek, butter, green onions, shallot, and garlic in a large pot; bring to a boil.
4. Reduce heat and simmer until leek and shallot soften, 2 to 4 minutes.
5. Stir scallops, mussels, salt, and pepper into the pot.
6. Cover and cook, stirring occasionally, until scallops are opaque and mussels open, 2 to 4 minutes.
7. Squeeze in juice from 2 lemon slices.
8. Remove from heat; stir in heavy cream.
9. Divide linguine between serving bowls.
10. Scoop scallop and mussels on top.
11. Serve with remaining lemon slices and toasted baguette.
