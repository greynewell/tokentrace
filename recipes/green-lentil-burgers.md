## Ingredients

- 1 cup green lentils, rinsed
- 2 cups water
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 2 tablespoons canola oil
- 1 cup finely chopped celery
- 1  red onion, finely chopped
- 1 tablespoon fancy molasses
- 1 teaspoon ground ginger
- 1 teaspoon dry mustard
- 1 tablespoon canola oil, or as needed

## Instructions

1. Combine water, lentils, and salt in a saucepan; bring to a boil, reduce heat to medium, and cook until all the water has been absorbed and lentils are nicely cooked, about 20 minutes.
2. Transfer to a large bowl.
3. Mash lentils with a potato masher; set aside.
4. Heat 2 tablespoons canola oil in a skillet over medium-high heat.
5. Saute celery and red onion in hot oil until tender, 5 to 7 minutes.
6. Drain and discard excess oil and transfer celery and onion to a bowl.
7. Stir molasses, ginger, and mustard together in a small bowl; stir into the celery mixture until the celery and onion are evenly coated.
8. Stir celery mixture into the mashed lentils.
9. Shape lentil mixture into 6 patties.
10. Heat 1 tablespoon canola oil in a skillet over medium heat; fry patties in hot oil hot and firm in the center, 5 to 7 minutes per side.
