## Ingredients

- 1 cup raisins  (Optional)
- ¾ cup unsweetened applesauce
- 3 tablespoons unsweetened applesauce
- ⅔ cup chocolate-hazelnut spread (such as Nutella®)
- ½ cup brown sugar
- ¼ cup honey
- 2 tablespoons corn syrup
- 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
- 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 3 ⅓ cups old-fashioned oats
- ⅔ cup all-purpose flour

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Grease an 8x11-inch baking dish.
3. Stir raisins, 3/4 cup plus 3 tablespoons applesauce, chocolate-hazelnut spread, brown sugar, honey, corn syrup, vanilla extract, ground cinnamon, and salt together in a bowl.
4. Combine oats and flour together in a resealable plastic bag, shake until mixed.
5. Stir oat mixture into chocolate-hazelnut mixture; spread granola into prepared baking dish.
6. Bake in preheated oven until lightly brown, about 15 minutes.
7. Cool completely on a wire rack before cutting into bars, about 1 hour.
