## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
- ⅔ cup quinoa
- 1  carrot, diced
- 1 stalk celery, diced
- ½  onion, finely chopped
- ½  green bell pepper, seeded and chopped
- 2 cloves garlic, crushed
- 2 (15 ounce) cans chicken broth
- 3 ½ cups water
- 2 large tomatoes, finely chopped
- ¼ head cabbage, chopped
- salt and pepper to taste
- ¼ cup chopped fresh parsley, for garnish

## Instructions

1. Heat the vegetable oil in a large pot on medium-high heat.
2. Stir in the quinoa, carrot, celery, onion, bell pepper, and garlic.
3. Cook for a few minutes, until lightly browned, stirring frequently.
4. Pour in the chicken broth, water, tomatoes, and cabbage.
5. Increase heat to high and bring to a boil.
6. Reduce heat to medium and simmer until the quinoa and vegetables are tender, about 10 minutes.
7. Season to taste with salt and pepper.
8. Garnish with parsley before servings.
