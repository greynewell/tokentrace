## Ingredients

- 16 ounces semisweet chocolate
- 1 (14 ounce) can sweetened condensed milk
- 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
- 1 ½ cups chopped walnuts

## Instructions

1. Line an 8x8 inch square dish with aluminum foil.
2. Chop chocolate and place in a large, microwave safe bowl with condensed milk.
3. Microwave on high, stirring once or twice until chocolate is soft, 2 to 3 minutes.
4. Remove from oven and stir until completely smooth.
5. Stir in vanilla and walnuts.
6. Spread in prepared pan.
7. Refrigerate 2 hours, until firm.
8. Cut into squares.
