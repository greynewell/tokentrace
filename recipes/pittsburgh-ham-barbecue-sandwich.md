## Ingredients

- 1 pound chipped chopped ham
- 2 (12 ounce) bottles tomato-based chili sauce
- 1 (12 fluid ounce) can or bottle cola-flavored carbonated beverage
- 1 stalk celery, finely chopped
- ½  onion, finely chopped
- ½  dill pickle, chopped
- 2 tablespoons brown sugar
- 8  hamburger buns

## Instructions

1. Place the chopped ham, chili sauce, cola, celery, onion, dill pickle, and brown sugar into a slow cooker, and stir to mix.
2. Set the cooker to high, and cook for 1 1/2 hours. You may set cooker to low and cook for 3 hours. Serve on buns.
