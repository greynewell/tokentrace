## Ingredients

- 2 cups white rice
- 9 ounces shelled and deveined shrimp
- 1 tablespoon sesame oil
- 1 tablespoon rice wine
- 12 cups water
- salt to taste

## Instructions

1. Rinse rice and set aside for 2 hours or until moistened.
2. Heat sesame oil in a saucepan.
3. Add shrimp and rice wine and gently fry.
4. Add rice and fry for 1 minute.
5. Pour water into saucepan and boil over medium heat.
6. When the rice and shrimp mixture is thickened, or the rice expands about 3 times, reduce the heat to low.
7. Continue to cook for 10 minutes, or until heated through, stirring constantly.
8. Season with salt and serve hot.
