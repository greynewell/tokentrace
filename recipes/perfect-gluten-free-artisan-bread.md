## Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon active dry yeast
- ⅔ cup lukewarm water, divided, or as needed
- 2 cups gluten-free all-purpose flour
- ½ teaspoon baking soda
- ½ teaspoon sea salt
- 3  eggs
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 2 tablespoons apple cider vinegar

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 500 degrees F (260 degrees C).
2. Heat a Dutch oven with the lid on in the oven.
3. Combine yeast and 1/3 cup lukewarm water in a large bowl; whisk until fully dissolved.
4. Let stand for 10 minutes.
5. Combine flour, baking soda, and salt in a separate bowl; mix well.
6. Add flour mixture to yeast mixture; whisk until flour is absorbed.
7. Whisk remaining 1/3 cup water, eggs, olive oil, and vinegar in a separate bowl.
8. Add to flour mixture and whisk well until fully combined and a soft dough is formed.
9. Cover dough with plastic wrap and let rise for 45 minutes.
10. Spoon dough into the center a large piece of parchment paper.
11. Smooth out loaf with an offset spatula; sprinkle top with gluten-free flour.
12. Place loaf with parchment paper inside the hot Dutch oven; cover with the lid and place in the oven.
13. Bake in the preheated oven until golden, about 45 minutes.
14. Remove bread with a spatula and transfer to a wire rack.
