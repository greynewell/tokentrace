## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon olive oil, or as desired
- ½ cup chopped yellow onion
- 1 clove garlic, minced, or more to taste
- 2 teaspoons dried rosemary
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 1 tablespoon all-purpose flour
- 2 cups chicken broth
- 1 (15.5 ounce) can white beans
- 1 (15 ounce) can asparagus, drained
- 1 cup heavy whipping cream
- salt and ground black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Pour enough olive oil into a skillet to coat the bottom; cook and stir onion, garlic, and rosemary over medium heat just until onion is soft, 5 to 10 minutes.
2. Add butter and flour; cook and stir until butter is melted and flour is dissolved, 3 to 5 minutes.
3. Mix broth, white beans, asparagus, cream, salt, and pepper into onion mixture; bring to a boil.
4. Reduce heat to medium, cover skillet, and simmer for 10 minutes.
5. Remove skillet from heat and cool for at least 5 minutes.
6. Pour soup into a food processor or blender no more than half full.
7. Cover and pulse a few times before leaving on to blend for about 3 minutes.
8. Puree in batches until smooth.
9. Pour soup through cheesecloth or strainer.
