## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons cooking oil
- 1  onion, chopped
- 2 cloves garlic, crushed
- 4 (4 ounce) catfish fillets
- salt and pepper to taste
- 1 ½ cups coconut milk

## Instructions

1. Heat the oil in a skillet over medium heat.
2. Cook and stir the onion and garlic in the hot oil until the garlic is lightly browned and the onion is glossy, 7 to 10 minutes.
3. Add the catfish; season with salt and pepper.
4. Cover and cook for 2 minutes.
5. Add the coconut milk and stir; cover again and cook until the coconut milk appears oily, about 10 minutes.
