## Ingredients

- 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
- 2 tablespoons bacon-flavored mayonnaise (such as Baconnaise®)
- 6  jalapeno pepper, halved lengthwise
- 12 slices bacon
- 12  toothpicks  (Optional)

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Mix cream cheese and bacon-flavored mayonnaise together in a bowl.
3. Fill each jalapeno pepper half with cream cheese mixture.
4. Wrap each filled pepper with a bacon slice, securing with a toothpick if needed.
5. Arrange peppers in a baking dish.
6. Bake in the preheated oven until bacon is crisp and peppers are tender, about 1 hour.
7. Transfer peppers to a serving plate and allow to cool, 3 to 5 minutes.
