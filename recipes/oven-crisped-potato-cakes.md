## Ingredients

- cooking spray
- 16 ounces shredded frozen potatoes, thawed
- 1 large onion, grated
- ½ cup all-purpose flour
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
- 1 large egg, beaten
- 2 ⅔ tablespoons canola oil, divided

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C).
2. Coat baking sheet with cooking spray.
3. Put potatoes and onion on a clean towel.
4. Roll up towel and squeeze out as much liquid as possible.
5. Transfer mixture to a large bowl.
6. Stir flour, salt, and black pepper into potato mixture; mix well.
7. Stir egg and 2 teaspoons canola oil into potato mixture until combined.
8. Heat remaining 2 tablespoons oil in a large non-stick skillet over medium-high heat.
9. Scoop up potato mixture with a 1/4-cup measuring cup and drop into hot oil.
10. Flatten into patties with a spatula.
11. Cook patties until golden brown, 2 to 3 minutes on each side.
12. Transfer cakes to prepared baking sheet.
13. Bake in preheated oven until cakes are browned and crisp, 12 to 15 minutes.
