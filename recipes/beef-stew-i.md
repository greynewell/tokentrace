## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 2 pounds cubed beef stew meat
- 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
- 6 cups water
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ½ teaspoon ground black pepper
- 4 cups cubed potatoes
- 2 cups chopped carrots
- ½ teaspoon browning sauce
- 2 teaspoons dried rosemary
- 1 cup fresh corn kernels
- 1 cup fresh green beans, cut into 1 inch pieces
- ¼ cup instant rice

## Instructions

1. Heat oil in large pot.
2. Add beef and flour, stirring to coat beef; brown.
3. Add water, salt, and pepper.
4. Heat to boiling; reduce heat, and simmer 1 hour.
5. Stir in potatoes, carrots (or turnips), browning sauce, and rosemary.
6. Simmer 1-2 hours.
7. Add corn, green beans, and rice; simmer for 30 minutes longer.
