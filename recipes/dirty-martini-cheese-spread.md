## Ingredients

- 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
- 1 tablespoon mayonnaise
- ¼ cup pitted green olives, chopped
- 2 tablespoons juice from the jar of olives
- ¼ cup vodka
- ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper

## Instructions

1. Stir together the cream cheese, mayonnaise, and chopped olives until evenly blended.
2. Stir in the olive juice, vodka, and pepper.
3. Refrigerate at least 2 hours before serving.
4. Serve in a martini glass.
