## Ingredients

- 1 cup nonfat sour cream
- 1 pinch salt
- ⅓ cup chili sauce
- 1 tablespoon pickle relish
- 1 teaspoon cider vinegar
- 1 tablespoon minced green onions
- 1 (4.5 ounce) can shrimp, drained and chopped
- ½  one lemon, juiced
- 1 pinch ground black pepper

## Instructions

1. In a medium bowl, combine sour cream, salt, chili sauce, relish, cider vinegar, onion, shrimp, lemon juice and ground black pepper.
2. Mix well.
3. Adjust seasoning and chill before serving.
