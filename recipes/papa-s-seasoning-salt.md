## Ingredients

- 26 ounces salt
- 1 cup ground black pepper
- 6 tablespoons onion powder
- ¼ cup garlic powder
- 1 tablespoon cayenne pepper
- 1 teaspoon ground ginger

## Instructions

1. Mix an entire 26-ounce round container of salt with the pepper, onion powder, garlic powder, ground cayenne, and ground ginger.
2. Store it in a dispenser with large holes for sprinkling.
