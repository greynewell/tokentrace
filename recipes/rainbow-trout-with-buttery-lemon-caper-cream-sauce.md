## Ingredients

- cooking spray
- 1  lemon, zested and halved
- 3 (4 ounce) fillets rainbow trout
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 2 tablespoons Dijon mustard
- 1 pinch dried marjoram
- 1 pinch dried tarragon
- 1 ½ tablespoons sour cream
- 2 teaspoons capers
- 1 pinch dried parsley flakes, or to taste  (Optional)

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Line a baking dish with aluminum foil and spray with cooking spray.
3. Juice 1/2 the lemon and cut the other half into 6 thin slices.
4. Place slices in 3 columns of 2 into the prepared baking dish.
5. Season trout with salt and pepper.
6. Place each fillet, skin-side down, over 2 of the lemon slices in the baking dish.
7. Bake in the preheated oven until trout flakes easily with a fork, about 15 minutes.
8. Melt butter in a small saucepan over medium-high heat.
9. Add Dijon mustard; stir.
10. Add the lemon juice, marjoram, and tarragon.
11. Cook and stir until flavors meld, about 2 minutes.
12. Reduce heat to medium-low.
13. Add sour cream and capers.
14. Stir until sauce is smooth but capers are not yet cooked through, about 2 minutes more.
15. Reduce heat to low and let sauce sit until trout is baked.
16. Place each piece of trout on a plate and garnish with the lemon slices.
17. Pour sauce evenly on top.
18. Sprinkle lemon zest and parsley over the sauce.
