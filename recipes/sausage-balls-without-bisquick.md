## Ingredients

- 1 ½ cups cornbread stuffing mix
- ¾ cup hot water
- 1 pound bulk pork sausage
- ½ cup grated Cheddar cheese
- ⅛ cup chopped green onion
- ½ teaspoon baking powder

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C).
2. Cover a large baking dish with aluminum foil.
3. Mix stuffing mix and water in a bowl until moistened.
4. Add pork, Cheddar cheese, green onion, and baking powder.
5. Mix well.
6. Shape into 1-inch balls and place into the prepared baking dish.
7. Bake in the preheated oven until browned and no longer pink in the centers, 18 to 20 minutes.
