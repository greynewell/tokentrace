## Ingredients

- ¼ cup steak sauce (such as A1®)
- ¼ cup ketchup
- 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
- 1 tablespoon soy sauce
- 1 tablespoon minced garlic
- ¼ cup water
- 4 (6 ounce) country style pork ribs
- 1  onion, cut into rings  (Optional)

## Instructions

1. Whisk together steak sauce, ketchup, water, Worcestershire sauce, soy sauce, and garlic in a bowl until smooth.
2. Place pork ribs in a saucepan with a lid, and pour the sauce over the ribs. Spread raw onion rings over the ribs, and cover the pan. Bring to a boil over medium heat, and simmer the ribs in the sauce until tender, about 45 minutes.
