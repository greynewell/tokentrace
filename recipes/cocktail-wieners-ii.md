## Ingredients

- 1 (16 ounce) package little smokie sausages
- 1 (8 ounce) jar cocktail sauce
- 1 (8 ounce) jar grape jelly

## Instructions

1. In a saucepan over medium heat, combine the cocktail sauce and grape jelly.
2. Mix in the little smokie sausages.
3. Cook 5 minutes, or until the sausages are hot.
