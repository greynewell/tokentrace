## Ingredients

- 2 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese
- 1 (.7 ounce) package dry Italian-style salad dressing mix
- 1 cup chopped nuts

## Instructions

1. In a large bowl, mix together cream cheese and salad dressing mix.
2. Shape into 1 large ball and roll in chopped nuts.
3. Refrigerate cheese ball for several hours or overnight.
