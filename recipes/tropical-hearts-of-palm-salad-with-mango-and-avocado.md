## Ingredients

- 2 (14 ounce) cans hearts of palm, drained and sliced into 1/2-inch rounds
- 1  mango - peeled, seeded, and diced
- 1  avocado, diced
- ¾ cup watercress
- ⅓  onion, sliced
- 1 tablespoon chopped cilantro
- ¼ cup rice wine vinegar
- 1 teaspoon white sugar
- ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
- ¼ teaspoon salt

## Instructions

1. Combine hearts of palm, mango, avocado, watercress, onion, and cilantro together in a bowl.
2. Whisk vinegar, sugar, black pepper, and salt together in a bowl; pour over salad and toss to coat.
