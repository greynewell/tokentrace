## Ingredients

- 1  baguette, sliced
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 1  red onion, sliced
- ¼ cup red wine
- ⅓ cup mayonnaise
- 1 tablespoon horseradish
- ½ pound thinly sliced deli roast beef

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C).
2. Spread baguette slices on a baking sheet.
3. Heat oil in a skillet over medium-low heat.
4. Cook and stir red onion until it starts to soften, about 5 minutes.
5. Cover and cook, stirring occasionally, until completely soft, about 10 minutes.
6. Uncover and pour in red wine; increase heat and stir constantly until wine evaporates, 3 to 5 minutes.
7. Remove from heat and let cool.
8. Bake baguette slices in the preheated oven until lightly toasted, about 5 minutes.
9. Mix mayonnaise and horseradish together in a bowl.
10. Spread mayonnaise-horseradish mixture over baguette slices; cover with onion and roast beef.
