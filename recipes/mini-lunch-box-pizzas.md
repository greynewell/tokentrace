## Ingredients

- ¼ cup broccoli florets
- 1 (10 ounce) can refrigerated biscuits
- ½ cup pizza sauce
- 1 cup chopped pepperoni
- ¾ cup shredded Italian cheese blend

## Instructions

1. Place a steamer insert into a saucepan and fill with water to just below the bottom of the steamer.
2. Bring water to a boil.
3. Add broccoli, cover, and steam until tender, 2 to 6 minutes.
4. Chop broccoli finely.
5. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
6. Press each biscuit into a nonstick muffin cup.
7. Mix pizza sauce and broccoli together.
8. Spoon 1 tablespoon of the sauce over each biscuit.
9. Top with pepperoni and shredded cheese.
10. Bake in the preheated oven until golden brown, 10 to 13 minutes.
