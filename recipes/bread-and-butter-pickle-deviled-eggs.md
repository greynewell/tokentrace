## Ingredients

- 6  hard-boiled eggs
- ¼ cup mayonnaise
- 2 tablespoons sliced bread-and-butter pickles
- 1 tablespoon honey mustard, or more to taste
- ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
- 1 pinch paprika, or as needed  (Optional)

## Instructions

1. Halve eggs lengthwise. Carefully remove yolks to a small bowl and mash with a fork; add mayonnaise, pickles, honey mustard, and black pepper. Blend the mixture until smooth.
2. Scoop yolk mixture into egg halves; garnish with paprika. Keep refrigerated until ready to serve.
