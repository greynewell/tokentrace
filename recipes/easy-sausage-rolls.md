## Ingredients

- 1 pound breakfast sausage
- 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, at room temperature
- 2 (8 ounce) packages refrigerated crescent rolls
- 1  egg white

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
2. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat.
3. Cook and stir sausage in the hot skillet until browned and crumbly, 5 to 7 minutes; drain and discard grease.
4. Stir in cream cheese.
5. Unroll crescent dough and separate into individual pieces; spoon 2 to 3 teaspoons sausage mixture onto each piece of dough.
6. Roll dough around filling.
7. Brush each crescent roll with egg white.
8. Arrange crescent rolls on a baking sheet.
9. Bake in the preheated oven until rolls are lightly browned, about 15 minutes.
