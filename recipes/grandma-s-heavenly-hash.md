## Ingredients

- 2 (7 ounce) bars milk chocolate bars (such as Hershey's®)
- 1 (12 ounce) package semi-sweet chocolate chips
- 1 cup butter
- 1 cup chopped walnuts
- ½ (10.5 ounce) package miniature marshmallows
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

## Instructions

1. Heat chocolate bars, chocolate chips, and butter in the top of a double boiler over simmering water, stirring frequently and scraping down the sides with a rubber spatula to avoid scorching.
2. Remove chocolate mixture from heat; stir in walnuts, marshmallows, and vanilla until completely coated.
3. Spread mixture into 9x13-inch baking pan, gently pressing down with spatula to evenly flatten out.
4. Refrigerate until hardened, 4 to 5 hours.
