## Ingredients

- 3 ounces HORMEL® NATURAL CHOICE® Oven Roasted Deli Turkey
- ¼ cup Swiss chard, chopped
- 3 sprigs daikon radish sprouts
- 1 medium red bell pepper, cut into matchsticks
- 1 medium yellow bell pepper, cut into matchsticks
- 1 medium orange bell pepper, cut into matchsticks
- 1 (6 inch) whole grain tortilla or flatbread
- 2 tablespoons rice wine vinegar
- 1 ½ teaspoons soy sauce
- 1 teaspoon honey
- salt and ground black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Lay tortilla on plate or flat surface.
2. Layer Swiss chard, sprouts and pepper on tortilla.
3. Pile turkey evenly in center of wrap.
4. In a small bowl, whisk together vinegar, soy sauce, honey, salt and pepper to taste.
5. Drizzle over turkey.
6. Fold sides of tortilla to center.
7. Fold bottom of tortilla up.
