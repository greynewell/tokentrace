## Ingredients

- ¾ cup whole wheat flour
- ¼ cup all-purpose flour, plus more for kneading
- 1 teaspoon onion powder
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ¾ cup low-fat yogurt
- 2 tablespoons sour cream
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 1 tablespoon dried mint
- 2 tablespoons vegetable oil, or as needed

## Instructions

1. Mix whole wheat flour, all-purpose flour, onion powder, and salt together in a large bowl.
2. Mix yogurt, sour cream, and olive oil together in a separate bowl; stir in dried mint.
3. Pour over flour mixture and mix with a wooden spoon until well-combined.
4. Turn dough out onto a well-floured work surface.
5. Knead dough until smooth and elastic, dusting with flour until it is no longer sticky, 8 to 10 minutes.
6. Cover the dough with plastic wrap and let it rest, about 10 minutes.
7. Divide dough into 6 small balls and roll each piece out into a thin circle.
8. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat.
9. Brush with vegetable oil.
10. Carefully transfer one flatbread into the hot skillet.
11. Cook until bottom side is golden brown and slightly charred, about 1 minute.
12. Brush raw side with oil and flip.
13. Cook until golden, about 30 seconds.
14. Transfer to serving dish.
15. Repeat with remaining flatbreads.
