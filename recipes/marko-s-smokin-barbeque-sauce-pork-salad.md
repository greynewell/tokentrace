## Ingredients

- ½ cup chopped lettuce
- ¼ cup canned black beans, drained
- ¼ cup Mexican style corn, drained
- ¼ cup shredded smoked pork
- 6  cherry tomatoes, halved, or more to taste
- 1 tablespoon shredded Cheddar cheese, or to taste
- ¼ cup barbeque sauce (such as Marko's Original Smokin' BBQ sauce)

## Instructions

1. Combine lettuce, black beans, corn, pork, and tomatoes in a bowl.
2. Add cheese; mix thoroughly.
3. Drizzle barbeque sauce on top.
