## Ingredients

- 4 (7 ounce) halibut steaks
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- 4 ounces bacon, cut crosswise into 1/4-inch pieces
- 8 ounces fresh green beans, tips trimmed, sliced into 1/8-inch pieces
- 1 pinch salt
- 1 tablespoon water
- 2 tablespoons lemon juice
- salt and freshly ground pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Season halibut steaks generously with salt and black pepper.
2. Refrigerate until needed.
3. Place bacon in a skillet and cook over medium heat.
4. When bacon is about half cooked, 4 to 6 minutes, stir in beans and pinch of salt.
5. Cook, stirring, until bacon is crisp and beans are almost tender, 4 to 6 minutes.
6. Transfer bacon and beans to a bowl and return skillet with bacon drippings to the stove over medium-high heat.
7. When bacon drippings just begin to smoke, place 2 halibut steaks in the skillet; cook until browned and meat is just starting to pull away from the bone, about 4 minutes per side.
8. Transfer steaks to a plate and repeat for remaining 2 steaks.
9. Remove skillet from heat and pour in water; bring to a boil while scraping any browned bits off the bottom of the pan.
10. Return beans and bacon to the skillet and stir in lemon juice; cook and stir 1 to 2 minutes.
11. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
12. Spoon hot bean and bacon relish over halibut steaks and serve.
