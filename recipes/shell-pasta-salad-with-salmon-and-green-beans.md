## Ingredients

- 1 cup seashell pasta
- ¼ pound fresh green beans, cut into 1-inch lengths
- ¼ cup low-fat cottage cheese
- ¼ cup plain yogurt
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice
- ¼ cup chopped fresh dill weed
- 2 (6 ounce) cans salmon, drained
- ground black pepper to taste
- 8 leaves lettuce

## Instructions

1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil, add pasta and cook until al dente.
2. Drain and rinse pasta under cold running water.
3. Drain again and set aside.
4. Blanch green beans in boiling water for 2 minutes.
5. Drain and rinse under cold running water.
6. Drain again and set aside.
7. In food processor or through a sieve, puree cottage cheese.
8. Combine with yogurt and lemon juice; mix well In a large bowl, combine pasta, green beans, yogurt mixture and dill; stir to mix.
9. Discard skin from salmon and break into chunks; add to salad and stir gently to mix.
10. Add pepper to taste.
11. Line serving plate with lettuce leaves and mound salad on top.
