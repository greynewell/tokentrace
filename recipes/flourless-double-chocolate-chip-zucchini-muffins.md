## Ingredients

- ½ cup almond butter
- 1  ripe banana, mashed
- ¼ cup unsweetened cocoa powder
- 2 tablespoons ground flax seeds
- 1 tablespoon honey
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- ½ teaspoon baking soda
- 1 cup finely grated zucchini, excess moisture squeezed out
- ¼ cup semisweet chocolate chips
- ¼ cup bittersweet chocolate chips, or to taste

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
2. Line muffin cups with aluminum foil liners.
3. Mix almond butter, banana, cocoa powder, ground flax seeds, honey, vanilla extract, and baking soda together in a bowl until batter is well blended; fold in zucchini and semisweet chocolate chips.
4. Scoop batter into the prepared muffin cups.
5. Top each with bittersweet chocolate chips.
6. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean, about 25 minutes.
7. Place muffins on a wire rack to cool.
