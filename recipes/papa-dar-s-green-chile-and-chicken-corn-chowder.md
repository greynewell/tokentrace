## Ingredients

- ½ pound smoked bacon, chopped
- 1  onion, chopped
- 4 cloves garlic, chopped
- 6 (4 ounce) cans chopped mild green chile peppers
- 2 ½ pounds skinless, boneless chicken breast halves, cubed
- 1 ½ pounds russet potatoes, cubed
- ¼ cup butter
- 2 (16 ounce) cans cream-style corn
- 1 pint heavy whipping cream
- salt and ground black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Place bacon in a large stockpot; cook and stir over medium-high heat until lightly browned, about 10 minutes.
2. Add onion and saute until onion is tender, about 10 minutes.
3. Add garlic and cook until fragrant, about 2 minutes.
4. Stir green chiles into bacon-onion mixture and bring to a simmer; add chicken.
5. Simmer mixture until chicken is almost fully cooked, about 10 minutes.
6. Add potatoes and butter to green chilies-chicken mixture and reduce heat to medium; cook until potatoes are almost tender, 10 to 15 minutes.
7. Stir corn and cream into mixture and reduce heat to medium-low; cook, stirring frequently and scraping bottom of pot with a wooden spoon, until potatoes are tender, about 10 more minutes.
8. Season with salt and pepper.
