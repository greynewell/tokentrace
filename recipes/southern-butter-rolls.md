## Ingredients

- 4 ½ cups all-purpose flour
- ¼ cup shortening
- ¼ cup white sugar
- 2 ½ teaspoons salt
- 1 ½ tablespoons active dry yeast
- 1 ½ cups cold water
- 1 tablespoon vegetable oil

## Instructions

1. Mix together flour, sugar, salt, and instant yeast in a bowl. Add shortening and mix well. Mix in water.
2. Turn dough out onto a lightly floured surface. Knead by hand for 20 minutes, or by machine for 12 to 14 minutes. Toss dough with vegetable oil in a bowl; cover the bowl and let proof for 1 hour.
3. Form dough into 24 rounds, and place on a greased baking tray. Cover and let proof for 45 minutes more.
4. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
5. Bake in the preheated oven until golden, 12 to 14 minutes.
