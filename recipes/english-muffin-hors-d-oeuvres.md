## Ingredients

- 1 ¼ cups mayonnaise
- 1 ½ cups shredded Cheddar cheese
- 6  green onions, chopped
- 1 pinch salt
- 1 pinch garlic salt
- 1 pinch ground black pepper
- 1 (12 ounce) package English muffin, split

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven broiler. Set the oven rack about 6 inches from the heat source.
2. Mix together Cheddar cheese, mayonnaise, green onions, salt, garlic salt, and pepper in a bowl. Spread cheese mixture over each English muffin half. Cut muffins into quarters and place them on a baking sheet.
3. Broil in the preheated oven until cheese is bubbly, 4 to 6 minutes.
