## Ingredients

- 1 (4 ounce) package Idahoan® Four Cheese Mashed Potatoes
- 16  fresh jalapenos
- 32 slices bacon

## Instructions

1. Prepare mashed potatoes following package directions, set aside to cool.
2. Split jalapenos lengthwise and remove the seeds and membrane.
3. Stuff with 2 tablespoons of the mashed potatoes and wrap with a bacon slice.
4. Broil until bacon is slightly crisp
