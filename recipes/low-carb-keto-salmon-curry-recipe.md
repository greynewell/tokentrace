## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons coconut oil
- ½ medium onion, diced
- 2 cups diced green beans
- 2 cups chicken stock
- 1 pound salmon fillets, diced
- 1 ½ tablespoons curry powder
- 1 teaspoon garlic powder
- 1 (14 ounce) can chilled coconut milk, solid cream only
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- 2 tablespoons chopped fresh basil

## Instructions

1. Heat coconut oil in a pot over medium-high heat.
2. Add onion and cook until translucent, about 5 minutes.
3. Add green beans and saute until nearly tender, about 3 minutes.
4. Pour in stock and bring to a boil.
5. Add salmon, curry powder, and garlic powder.
6. Add coconut cream, reduce heat, and simmer until salmon flesh flakes easily with a fork, 3 to 5 minutes.
7. Season with salt and pepper; garnish with basil.
