## Ingredients

- 4  Mission® Medium Flour Tortillas
- 4 tablespoons fat-free cream cheese
- 1 teaspoon mustard
- 8 thin slices  ham
- 4 tablespoons grated Cheddar cheese
- 1 small apple, sliced into toothpick-sized slivers

## Instructions

1. Spread 1 tablespoon cream cheese over entire surface of each tortilla.
2. Dot middle of each tortilla with 1/4 teaspoon mustard.
3. Top with 2 slices ham, 1 Tablespoon cheese, and 1/4 of the slivered apple.
4. Roll tortillas tightly into a wrap.
5. Wrap tightly in plastic wrap and refrigerate.
6. To serve: Remove from plastic wrap and cut in half.
