## Ingredients

- ⅔ cup liquid sourdough starter
- ½ cup water
- 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
- 2 tablespoons honey
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ½ cup high protein wheat flour
- 2 cups bread flour
- 1 teaspoon active dry yeast

## Instructions

1. Place ingredients in the pan of the bread machine in the order recommended by the manufacturer.
2. Select basic white cycle; press Start.
3. Add water or flour 1 tablespoon at a time, if needed, to obtain a smooth dough ball.
