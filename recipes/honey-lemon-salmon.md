## Ingredients

- 3 tablespoons butter
- 4 (4 ounce) fillets salmon fillets
- ½ cup white wine
- ¼ cup honey
- ¼ cup lemon juice
- salt and ground black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Melt butter in a large skillet over medium-low heat.
2. Add salmon fillets.
3. Pour white wine, honey, and lemon juice over salmon.
4. Season with salt and pepper.
5. Simmer until liquid cooks down to a syrupy consistency, 10 to 12 minutes.
6. Make sure to turn salmon fillets every few minutes.
7. Plate salmon and drizzle syrup on top.
