## Ingredients

- 2 fluid ounces passion fruit-orange-guava (POG) juice
- 1 fluid ounce coconut-flavored rum
- 2 fluid ounces cold Champagne, or as needed
- 1  pineapple ring
- 1  maraschino cherry

## Instructions

1. Pour passion fruit-orange-guava juice and rum into a Champagne glass.
2. Top with Champagne and garnish with pineapple and cherry.
