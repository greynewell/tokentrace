## Ingredients

- 12 ounces ground chorizo or Italian sausage
- ½ cup chopped green bell pepper
- 1 (20 ounce) package Simply Potatoes® Southwest Style Hash Browns
- 2 cups shredded Monterey Jack or Cheddar cheese
- 4 each eggs
- 1 cup milk
- 1 medium tomato, seeded, chopped

## Instructions

1. Heat oven to 350 degrees F.
2. Spray 8 or 9-inch square baking dish with nonstick cooking spray.
3. In 10-inch skillet cook sausage and green pepper over medium-high heat 8 to 10 minutes or until sausage is browned and cooked through; drain.
4. Place half of the Simply Potatoes in prepared baking dish.
5. Top with half of the sausage and 3/4 cup of cheese; repeat.
6. Combine eggs and milk in medium bowl; beat well with fork.
7. Pour egg mixture evenly over layered mixture in pan.
8. Cover with foil; bake for 45 minutes.
9. Uncover; add chopped tomato and remaining 1/2 cup cheese.
10. Bake, uncovered, for an additional 10 to 15 minutes or until knife inserted in center comes out clean.
11. Let stand 10 minutes before serving.
