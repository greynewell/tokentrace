## Ingredients

- 1 bunch kale, stems removed and cut into bite-sized pieces
- 1  apple, peeled and diced
- ½ cup sunflower seeds
- ½ cup dried cranberries
- ½ cup extra-virgin olive oil
- 2 tablespoons honey
- 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
- 1 tablespoon apple cider vinegar
- 1 pinch ground cinnamon
- salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Combine kale, apple, sunflower seeds, and cranberries in a large bowl.
2. Whisk together olive oil, honey, lemon juice, apple cider vinegar, cinnamon, salt, and pepper in a separate bowl.
3. Drizzle vinaigrette over kale and mix with salad by hand, massaging dressing into leaves.
4. Cover and refrigerate for at least 1 hour before serving.
