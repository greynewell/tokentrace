## Ingredients

- 1 fluid ounce brewed espresso
- 1 ½ fluid ounces vodka
- 1 ½ fluid ounces coffee-flavored liqueur (such as Kahlua®)
- 1 fluid ounce white creme de cacao
- 1 cup ice

## Instructions

1. Pour vodka, coffee liqueur, crème de cacao, and espresso into a cocktail shaker; add ice, cover and shake until chilled.
2. Strain into a chilled martini glass.
