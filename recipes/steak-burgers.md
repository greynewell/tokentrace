## Ingredients

- 1 pound lean ground beef
- 1 teaspoon Montreal steak seasoning
- 4  hamburger buns, split

## Instructions

1. Mix ground beef and Montreal steak seasoning together in a bowl. Form mixture into 4 patties.
2. Preheat grill for medium heat and lightly oil the grate. Grill patties until browned, 5 to 6 minutes per side.
3. Serve grilled patties on hamburger buns.
