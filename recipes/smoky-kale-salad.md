## Ingredients

- 1 bunch green kale, stemmed and leaves roughly chopped
- 1 bunch red kale, stemmed and leaves roughly chopped
- ½ cup chopped pecans
- ¼ cup apple cider vinegar
- 1 tablespoon honey
- 1 ½ teaspoons chipotle pepper powder
- 1 ½ teaspoons smoked paprika
- 1 teaspoon liquid amino acid (such as Bragg®)
- 1  avocado - peeled, pitted, and chopped

## Instructions

1. Combine green kale, red kale, pecans, apple cider vinegar, honey, chipotle pepper powder, paprika, and liquid amino acid together in a bowl.
2. Massage the kale mixture together using your hands until leaves are evenly coated and starting to soften, about 3 minutes; fold in avocado.
