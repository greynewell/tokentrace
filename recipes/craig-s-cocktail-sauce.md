## Ingredients

- 1 (36 ounce) bottle ketchup
- 3 tablespoons steak sauce
- 3 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
- 6 tablespoons lemon juice concentrate
- 3 tablespoons prepared horseradish
- 15 drops hot pepper sauce, or to taste
- 1 teaspoon salt

## Instructions

1. Squeeze ketchup into a bowl.
2. Pour in steak sauce, Worcestershire sauce, and lemon juice.
3. Stir in horseradish, hot pepper sauce, and salt, and mix well.
