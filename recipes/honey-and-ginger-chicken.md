## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 2 large boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cubed
- ¼ cup honey
- 2 tablespoons finely chopped ginger
- 2  red bell peppers, chopped
- 1 large onion, cut into 8 wedges
- 1 large head broccoli, cut into florets
- 1 cup peeled and cubed fresh pineapple
- ½ cup honey

## Instructions

1. Heat olive oil in a skillet or wok over medium heat.
2. Add chicken cubes, 1/4 cup honey, and ginger.
3. Cook and stir until chicken is golden brown, about 10 minutes.
4. Add bell peppers, onion, broccoli, pineapple, and remaining 1/2 cup honey.
5. Cover and cook over medium-high heat until vegetables are tender, 5 to 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
