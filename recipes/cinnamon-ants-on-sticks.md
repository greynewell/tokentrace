## Ingredients

- 1 large stalk celery, cut into 3 pieces
- 3 tablespoons peanut butter
- 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
- 2 tablespoons raisins

## Instructions

1. Place the celery pieces on a clean surface, hollow part  facing up, and sprinkle evenly with cinnamon.
2. Spoon peanut butter into the hollow, and arrange raisins on top.
