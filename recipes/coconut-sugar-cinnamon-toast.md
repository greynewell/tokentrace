## Ingredients

- ¼ cup butter, softened
- 1 tablespoon coconut sugar
- 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
- 2 drops vanilla extract
- 2 slices bread, toasted

## Instructions

1. Mash butter in a bowl until smooth; stir in coconut sugar, cinnamon, and vanilla extract.
2. Spread butter mixture onto each piece of toast.
