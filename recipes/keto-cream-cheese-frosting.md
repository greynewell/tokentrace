## Ingredients

- 1 (6 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
- 3 ounces butter, softened
- 1 drop liquid sweetener (such as SucraDrops®), or more to taste

## Instructions

1. Mix cream cheese, butter, and sweetener with an electric mixer until well blended.
2. Adjust sweetener to taste.
3. Refrigerate until use.
