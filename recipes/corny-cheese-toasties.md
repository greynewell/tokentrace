## Ingredients

- 2 slices bread
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 teaspoon mayonnaise
- 1 slice canned corned beef
- 1 slice American processed cheese

## Instructions

1. Preheat skillet over medium heat.
2. Butter each slice of bread on 1 side only.
3. Spread mayonnaise on the un-buttered sides.
4. Place 1 slice of bread, buttered side down, in the skillet.
5. Layer corned beef and cheese slices on bread.
6. Cover with remaining slice of bread, buttered side up.
7. Cook for 5 minutes on each side, or until toast is golden brown, and cheese is melted.
