## Ingredients

- 1 (8 ounce) package refrigerated crescent rolls
- ¼ cup shredded Cheddar cheese
- 8  hot dogs

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
2. Roll crescent roll dough onto a baking sheet. Sprinkle Cheddar cheese over dough. Lay a hot dog at the base of each triangle and roll dough around hot dog.
3. Bake in the preheated oven until rolls are golden and hot dogs are plump, about 12 minutes.
