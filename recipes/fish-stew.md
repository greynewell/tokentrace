## Ingredients

- 1 (15 ounce) can mackerel, undrained
- 1 (10.75 ounce) can  tomato soup (not condensed)  (Optional)
- 4  potatoes, sliced
- 1  onion, sliced
- red pepper flakes (to taste)
- 1  egg  (Optional)

## Instructions

1. Place undrained mackerel, tomato soup (if using), potatoes, onion, and red pepper flakes in a medium stock pot.
2. Add just enough water to cover.
3. Bring to a boil over medium-high heat, reduce to a simmer, and cook 30 minutes, or until potatoes are tender.
4. Break the egg over the stew, if desired, and serve.
