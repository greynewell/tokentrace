## Ingredients

- 20  graham crackers, finely crushed
- ½ cup confectioners' sugar
- ¼ cup butter, melted
- 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, at room temperature
- 1 (8 ounce) container whipped topping (such as Cool Whip®)
- 1 cup confectioners' sugar
- 1 (12 ounce) can cherry pie filling

## Instructions

1. Mix graham cracker crumbs, 1/2 cup confectioners' sugar, and butter together in a bowl; press into a 9x9-inch pan.
2. Whisk cream cheese, whipped topping, and 1 cup confectioners' sugar together in a bowl until smooth; spread over graham cracker crust. Pour cherry pie filling over cream cheese mixture layer. Refrigerate until firm, at least 1 hour.
