## Ingredients

- ½ cup lemon juice
- 1 tablespoon agave syrup
- 1 tablespoon dried minced onion
- 1 clove garlic, crushed
- 1 teaspoon chili powder
- ½ teaspoon paprika
- ½ teaspoon ground ginger
- 1  haddock fillet

## Instructions

1. Whisk lemon juice, agave, onion, garlic, chili powder, paprika, and ginger together in a bowl.
2. Pour mixture into a nonstick skillet and bring to a simmer over medium heat.
3. Add haddock; cook until fish flakes easily with a fork, 7 to 10 minutes, turning once halfway though cooking time.
4. Serve fish with pan drippings drizzled over the top.
