## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 1  onion, halved and sliced
- salt and freshly ground black pepper
- 1  red bell pepper - cored, seeded and thinly sliced
- 2  zucchini, diced
- 2  tomatoes, diced
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- ½ cup dry white wine
- 1 pound squid, cleaned and sliced into rings
- 1 bunch fresh basil, chopped

## Instructions

1. Heat olive oil in a skillet and cook onion until softened, about 3 minutes.
2. Season with salt.
3. Add bell pepper and zucchini and cook until softened, 3 to 5 minutes.
4. Stir in tomatoes, garlic, salt, and black pepper.
5. Pour in white wine and bring to a boil.
6. Add squid and cook until squid is just cooked through, about 5 minutes.
7. Do not overcook.
8. Sprinkle with chopped basil to serve.
