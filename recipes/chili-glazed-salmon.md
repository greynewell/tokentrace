## Ingredients

- 4 (6 ounce) salmon fillets, skinless
- ½ cup VH® Sweet Thai Chili Sauce
- 2 tablespoons VH® Soya Sauce
- 2 tablespoons orange juice
- 1  serving PAM® Cooking Spray

## Instructions

1. Spray glass baking dish with PAM®. Place salmon in dish.
2. Stir together VH® Sweet Thai Chili sauce, VH® Soya sauce and orange juice. Pour over salmon.
3. Bake in a 400 degrees F (205 degrees C) for 15 minutes or until salmon is cooked through.
