## Ingredients

- ¼ cup steel-cut oats
- 1 (8 ounce) container plain yogurt
- 2 ounces fresh blueberries
- ½ ounce stevia powder (such as Pure Via®)
- 4 ½ ounces coconut milk

## Instructions

1. Place oats into the bottom of a pint-sized Mason jar. Stir in yogurt, blueberries, and stevia powder. Pour in coconut milk and stir to combine.
2. Cover the jar and refrigerate 8 hours to overnight.
