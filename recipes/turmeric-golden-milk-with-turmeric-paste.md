## Ingredients

- ½ cup water
- ¼ cup ground turmeric
- ½ teaspoon ground black pepper
- 1 teaspoon water, or as needed
- 2 cups almond milk
- 1 teaspoon extra-virgin coconut oil
- 1 teaspoon ground ginger
- 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
- 1 teaspoon raw honey

## Instructions

1. Make paste: Combine 1/2 cup water, turmeric, and pepper in a small saucepan over low heat. Cook, stirring constantly, until thickened, 7 to 9 minutes. Stir in 1 teaspoon water and continue cooking until thickened into a paste, 7 to 9 minutes. Allow to cool completely, 15 to 20 minutes. Store turmeric paste in an airtight glass jar in the refrigerator.
2. Make milk: Whisk together almond milk, 1 teaspoon turmeric paste, coconut oil, ginger, and cinnamon in a saucepan. Place over low heat until hot, about 5 minutes. Remove from heat; stir in honey. Strain milk mixture into 2 mugs.
