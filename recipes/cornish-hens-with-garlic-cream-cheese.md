## Ingredients

- 2  Cornish game hens, giblets removed
- 3 tablespoons garlic and herb seasoning (such as McCormick®), divided
- 2 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, divided
- 1 tablespoon butter, melted
- salt to taste

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C).
2. Line the inside of a roasting pan with aluminum foil.
3. Place hens, breast-side up, in the prepared roasting pan.
4. Mix 1 tablespoon garlic and herb seasoning with 1 package cream cheese in a bowl.
5. Stuff cream cheese mixture in 1 hen.
6. Repeat with 1 tablespoon garlic and herb seasoning and remaining 1 package cream cheese; stuff the second hen.
7. Brush hens with melted butter; sprinkle with remaining 1 tablespoon garlic and herb seasoning.
8. Season with salt.
9. Cover hens with aluminum foil.
10. Bake in the preheated oven for 1 hour.
11. Remove aluminum foil and bake until no longer pink at the bone and the juices run clear, about 15 more minutes.
12. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the thickest part of the thigh, near the bone, should read 165 degrees F (74 degrees C).
