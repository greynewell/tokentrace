## Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
- ½ teaspoon garlic powder
- ½ teaspoon onion powder
- ¼ teaspoon ground cumin
- ⅛ teaspoon cayenne pepper
- ⅛ teaspoon salt
- .063 teaspoon ground allspice
- ¼ cup butter, melted
- 1 tablespoon honey
- 12 cups popped popcorn, or to taste

## Instructions

1. Mix cinnamon, garlic powder, onion powder, cumin, cayenne pepper, salt, and allspice together in a bowl.
2. Stir butter and honey together in a separate bowl until evenly mixed.
3. Add cinnamon mixture with butter mixture and mix well.
4. Place popcorn in a bowl and pour butter mixture over popcorn, flipping often to evenly coat each piece.
