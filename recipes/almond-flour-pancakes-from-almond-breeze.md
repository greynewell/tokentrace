## Ingredients

- 1 ½ cups almond meal
- 3  eggs
- ½ cup Original Unsweetened Almond Breeze Almondmilk Coconutmilk Blend
- 2 tablespoons maple syrup or sugar
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
- ½ teaspoon baking powder
- ¼ teaspoon salt

## Instructions

1. In a medium bowl, mix all ingredients.
2. In a non-stick skillet over medium-low heat, scoop 1/4 cup spoonfuls of batter into pan.
3. Cook 3 to 4 minutes or until golden brown, then flip and cook an additional 1 to 2 minutes on the other side.
4. Serve with fresh fruit & maple syrup.
