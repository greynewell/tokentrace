## Ingredients

- 6  sweet bread and butter pickles
- ¼  red onion, cut into large chunks
- 2 stalks celery, cut into large chunks
- 10 ounces baked ham, trimmed and cut into 1-inch chunks
- 2 tablespoons mayonnaise
- 1 tablespoon whole-grain Dijon mustard
- ½ teaspoon Cajun seasoning  (Optional)
- 8 slices rye bread
- 8  lettuce leaves

## Instructions

1. Combine pickles, onion, and celery in a food processor; pulse to a medium-fine consistency.
2. Add ham; pulse into pieces about 1/4 inch in diameter.
3. Stir ham mixture, mayonnaise, Dijon mustard, and Cajun seasoning together in a bowl.
4. Divide ham salad mixture over 4 slices of rye bread; lay 2 lettuce leaves over each.
5. Cover with remaining bread slices.
