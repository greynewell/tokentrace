## Ingredients

- 2 (8 ounce) flat iron steaks
- ½ teaspoon lemon pepper seasoning, or to taste
- ½ teaspoon onion powder, or to taste
- ½ teaspoon garlic powder, or to taste

## Instructions

1. Season the steaks with lemon pepper, onion powder and garlic powder on both sides.
2. Wrap in plastic wrap and marinate for at least 2 hours in the refrigerator.
3. Preheat the grill for medium-high heat and allow the steaks to come to room temperature.
4. Unwrap steaks and place on the preheated grill.
5. Cook to your desired degree of doneness, about 3 minutes per side for medium rare.
6. Allow steaks to rest for a few minutes before serving.
