## Ingredients

- 1 cup zesty Italian-style salad dressing
- ½ cup mayonnaise
- 1 (10 ounce) package frozen peas
- 1 cup chopped celery
- 1 cup chopped peanuts
- ¼ cup chopped red onion
- 5 slices cooked bacon, crumbled

## Instructions

1. Mix together Italian-style dressing and mayonnaise in a bowl.
2. Fold in peas, celery, peanuts, and onion until well mixed.
3. Refrigerate until serving.
4. Crumble bacon on top just before serving.
