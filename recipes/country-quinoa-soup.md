## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 2 cups chopped onion
- 1  red bell pepper, seeded and chopped
- 4 cloves garlic, chopped
- 1 ½ tablespoons mild curry powder
- 2 teaspoons ground ginger
- ¼ teaspoon cayenne pepper
- 6 cups vegetable broth
- 2 cups chopped tomatoes
- 1  Granny Smith apple, peeled and chopped
- 1 cup quinoa
- 3 tablespoons dried currants
- ½ cup chopped fresh cilantro
- 1 pinch sea salt and ground black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium-high heat; cook and sir onion and red bell pepper until softened, 6 to 8 minutes.
2. Add garlic, curry, ginger, and cayenne pepper to onion mixture; cook and stir until evenly coated, about 30 seconds.
3. Mix vegetable broth, tomatoes, Granny Smith apple, quinoa, and currants into onion mixture; bring to a boil.
4. Reduce heat to low, cover pot, and simmer until quinoa is tender, 15 to 20 minutes.
5. Stir cilantro into soup and season with sea salt and pepper.
