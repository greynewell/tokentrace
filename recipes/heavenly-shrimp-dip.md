## Ingredients

- 2 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese
- 1 (8 ounce) jar cocktail sauce
- Â½ pound shrimp, peeled and deveined
- 1  lime
- 1 (16 ounce) package buttery round crackers

## Instructions

1. Place cream cheese in center of serving platter.
2. Mix together cocktail sauce and shrimp. Pour mixture over cream cheese.
3. Slice lime in half; squeeze one half over cocktail sauce. Slice other half into wedges for garnish.
4. Place crackers and lime wedges around edge of plate and serve.
