## Ingredients

- 1 medium pumpkin - peeled and cubed
- 5 cups chicken broth, divided
- 6  dried red chile peppers, stemmed and seeded
- 1 teaspoon butter
- 1 large onion, chopped
- 2 pounds leeks, chopped
- 1 teaspoon cumin
- 1  bay leaf
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- 1 cup buttermilk

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C).
2. Bake the pumpkin on a baking sheet in the preheated oven until soft, 30 to 40 minutes.
3. Combine 2 cups chicken broth and chile peppers in a saucepan over medium-low heat; simmer for 30 minutes.
4. Melt butter in a large stockpot over medium heat.
5. Cook and stir onion in the butter until caramelized, about 45 minutes.
6. Transfer to a bowl.
7. Cook leeks in the same stockpot until tender, 10 to 15 minutes.
8. Add pumpkin, chicken broth with chile peppers, caramelized onion, remaining 3 cups chicken broth, and cumin.
9. Simmer until flavors combine, about 5 minutes.
10. Puree soup with an immersion blender until smooth.
11. Add bay leaf, salt, and pepper.
12. Remove from heat; stir in buttermilk.
13. Discard bay leaf before serving.
