## Ingredients

- 1 (4 ounce) ball Burrata cheese, at room temperature
- 1  peach, sliced
- 1 tablespoon thinly sliced fresh basil leaves
- 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
- 3 tablespoons raspberry balsamic vinegar
- flaked sea salt
- 1 pinch freshly ground black pepper

## Instructions

1. Gently tear open the Burrata cheese to expose the soft, creamy core and place it in the middle of a plate.
2. Arrange peach slices in and around the cheese.
3. Sprinkle basil leaves over cheese and top with olive oil.
4. Bring raspberry balsamic vinegar to a boil in a small saucepan; reduce heat to medium-low and simmer until vinegar reduces to a thick syrup, about 5 minutes.
5. Spoon balsamic syrup over peaches and cheese; season with sea salt and black pepper.
