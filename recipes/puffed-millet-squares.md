## Ingredients

- ½ cup natural peanut butter
- ½ cup honey
- ½ teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 3 ½ cups puffed millet cereal
- ½ cup chopped dried mixed fruit

## Instructions

1. Grease an 8-inch square pan with cooking spray.
2. Combine peanut butter, honey, and salt in a large saucepan; mix to combine and bring to a boil. Stir in vanilla.
3. Remove from the heat and stir in millet and dried fruit. Press mixture into the prepared pan. Refrigerate until chilled, about 1 hour.
4. Cut into 16 squares.
