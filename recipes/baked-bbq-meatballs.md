## Ingredients

- 1 pound ground beef
- 1 cup cracker crumbs
- ¼ cup chopped onion
- ¼ cup milk
- 2  eggs
- ¾ teaspoon salt
- ⅔ cup ketchup
- ⅓ cup water
- 6 tablespoons brown sugar
- ½ teaspoon ground nutmeg

## Instructions

1. Preheat an oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Mix ground beef, cracker crumbs, chopped onion, milk, eggs, and salt in a large bowl. Roll out 20 meatballs and arrange on a baking sheet with a rim. Whisk together the ketchup, water, brown sugar, and nutmeg in a bowl. Pour mixture over meatballs.
3. Bake in the preheated oven until the meatballs are no longer pink in the center, about 45 minutes.
