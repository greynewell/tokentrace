## Ingredients

- 3 cups vegetable oil
- 3 cups water
- ¼ cup sea salt, divided
- 2 large potatoes, thinly sliced
- 2 tablespoons ground black pepper

## Instructions

1. Heat oil in a deep-fryer or large saucepan to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
2. Stir water and 2 tablespoons sea salt together in a bowl until salt is dissolved.
3. Place potato slices in water.
4. Transfer 2 handfuls of potatoes using tongs from water to hot oil, cover the pot or fryer, and cook in hot oil until chips are crispy and light brown, 5 to 7 minutes.
5. Transfer to a paper towel-lined plate.
6. Repeat with remaining potatoes.
7. Place chips in a large bowl and sprinkle remaining salt and pepper over the top; toss to coat.
