## Ingredients

- ¾ (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
- 2 tablespoons Dijon mustard
- 2 tablespoons dry sherry
- 1 teaspoon caraway seeds
- 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
- 1 pound shredded Cheddar cheese
- 3 tablespoons caraway seeds, for rolling

## Instructions

1. Beat the cream cheese in a large bowl until fluffy and soft.
2. Stir in the mustard, sherry, and 1 teaspoon of caraway seeds, mixing thoroughly.
3. Beat in the Cheddar cheese and vegetable oil.
4. Shape mixture into a ball and roll in the remaining 3 tablespoons of caraway seeds.
5. Wrap coated ball in plastic and refrigerate until firm, at least 4 hours.
