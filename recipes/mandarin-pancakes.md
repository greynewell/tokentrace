## Ingredients

- 2 cups all-purpose flour
- 1 cup boiling water
- 1 ½ teaspoons dark sesame oil

## Instructions

1. Combine flour and boiling water in a large bowl until dough comes together.
2. Turn dough onto a lightly floured work surface and knead until smooth and elastic, 5 to 8 minutes.
3. Shape dough into a 1 1/2-inch thick log and divide into 16 equal portions.
4. Roll each piece of dough into a 6-inch circle on a lightly floured work surface.
5. Brush 8 dough circles with sesame oil.
6. Place an unoiled dough circle on top of an oiled dough circle, gently pressing them together.
7. Heat a nonstick skillet over medium-high heat.
8. Place 1 dough circle stack in the skillet and cook until slightly puffed, about 1 minute per side.
9. Remove from the skillet and cool slightly.
10. Peel pancakes apart.
11. Repeat cooking with remaining dough circles.
