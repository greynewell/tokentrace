## Ingredients

- 1 (6 ounce) sweet potato
- ¼ cup water
- 1  orange - peeled, segmented, and seeded
- 1 tablespoon raisins
- 2 teaspoons lemon juice
- 1 teaspoon honey
- 1 pinch salt
- 4  walnut halves, chopped

## Instructions

1. Place sweet potato into a microwave steamer with a rack; add water.
2. Cook in the microwave until sweet potato is soft but not mushy, about 8 minutes.
3. Set aside until cool enough to handle, about 5 minutes.
4. Peel cooled sweet potato and cut into 1/2-inch cubes.
5. Cut each orange segment into 3 pieces.
6. Place sweet potato and orange pieces in a bowl; add raisins.
7. Mix lemon juice and honey together in a bowl; pour over potato-orange mixture.
8. Add a pinch of salt and sprinkle walnuts over salad.
