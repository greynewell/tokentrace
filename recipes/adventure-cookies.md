## Ingredients

- 1 cup chopped dates
- ¾ cup white sugar
- 1 tablespoon milk
- 1 stick butter
- 1  egg, well beaten
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- ⅛ teaspoon salt
- 2 cups crispy rice cereal (such as Rice Krispies®)
- ½ cup chopped walnuts
- 1 (14 ounce) package sweetened flaked coconut (such as Bakers® Angel Flake®)

## Instructions

1. Combine dates, sugar, milk, and butter in a saucepan and cook over medium-low heat until thick, 5 to 10 minutes. Set aside to cool.
2. Add egg, vanilla extract, and salt to the date mixture; stir to combine. Add crispy rice cereal and walnuts and mix very well.
3. Pour coconut into a shallow bowl. Shape the date mixture into balls and roll in the coconut.
