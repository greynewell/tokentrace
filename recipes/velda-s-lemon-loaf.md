## Ingredients

- 1 (18.25 ounce) package lemon cake mix
- 4  eggs
- ¾ cup water
- ⅔ cup vegetable oil
- 1 (3.4 ounce) package instant lemon pudding mix

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C).
2. Grease 2 loaf pans.
3. Combine cake mix, eggs, water, vegetable oil, and pudding mix in a large bowl; beat until combined and smooth.
4. Divide batter between loaf pans.
5. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean, about 1 hour.
