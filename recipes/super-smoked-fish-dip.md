## Ingredients

- ½ cup mayonnaise
- ½ cup sour cream
- 3 tablespoons chopped fresh dill weed
- ½ pound smoked marlin

## Instructions

1. In a medium-sized mixing bowl, combine mayonnaise, sour cream, and dill.
2. Mix well.
3. Chunk or flake the smoked fish into the mixture, and gently fold until the dip is well combined.
