## Ingredients

- 8  hot dogs
- 8 slices bread
- 8 slices American cheese
- ketchup and mustard to taste

## Instructions

1. Make a slit down the middle of one end of each hot dog; this will be the legs.
2. At the other end, make a small slit each side; these will be the arms.
3. Bring a saucepan of water to a boil, and add the hot dogs.
4. Boil for about 5 minutes until the slits open up.
5. Place slices of cheese onto slices of bread.
6. Set the hot dog men on top.
7. Use ketchup and mustard to make a face, hair and clothes or buttons.
