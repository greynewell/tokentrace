## Ingredients

- cooking spray
- 6 ounces grated Parmesan cheese
- 2 teaspoons crushed dried rosemary
- 1 teaspoon garlic salt

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C).
2. Line 2 baking sheets with parchment paper and spray them with cooking spray.
3. Drop tablespoonfuls of Parmesan cheese 2 inches apart on the prepared baking sheet.
4. Flatten them out to the size of a potato chip.
5. Sprinkle rosemary and garlic salt on top.
6. Bake in the preheated oven just until edges start to turn golden brown, 3 to 5 minutes.
7. Cool on the baking sheets for 2 minutes; lift off with a spatula and transfer to a wire rack to cool until firm, about 5 minutes.
