## Ingredients

- 1 (12 ounce) can corned beef
- ⅓ cup ketchup
- 2 tablespoons prepared yellow mustard
- 2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
- 1  onion, chopped
- 1 (8 ounce) package shredded white Cheddar cheese
- 8  hot dog buns

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Mash the corned beef into a saucepan, and stir in ketchup, mustard, Worcestershire sauce, onion, and white Cheddar cheese over medium heat.
3. Bring to a boil, reduce heat to medium-low, and simmer until the cheese has melted and the onion is tender and translucent, about 10 minutes.
4. Fill each hot dog bun with about 1/2 cup of the mixture, and wrap each filled bun in aluminum foil.
5. Place the wrapped buns onto a baking sheet.
6. Bake in the preheated oven until buns are slightly toasted, about 15 minutes.
7. If desired, make a big batch of filled and wrapped buns, freeze them, and bake frozen for 35 minutes.
