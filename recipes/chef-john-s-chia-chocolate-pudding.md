## Ingredients

- 1 cup milk
- ¼ cup chia seeds
- 2 tablespoons cocoa powder
- 1 tablespoon brown sugar
- 1 tablespoon white sugar
- ½ teaspoon instant coffee granules
- ¼ teaspoon vanilla extract
- 1 tiny pinch salt
- 1 tablespoon chocolate covered hemp seeds, or to taste

## Instructions

1. Whisk milk, chia seeds, cocoa powder, brown sugar, white sugar, coffee granules, vanilla extract, and salt together in a bowl until pudding is smooth.
2. Let sit for 5 minutes; stir.
3. Repeat sitting and stirring process at least 2 times.
4. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate, 8 hours to overnight.
5. Spoon into bowls and top with chocolate hemp seeds.
