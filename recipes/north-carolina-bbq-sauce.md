## Ingredients

- 1 gallon white vinegar
- 1 ⅓ cups cayenne pepper
- 1 ⅛ cups ground black pepper
- ¾ cup mustard powder
- ½ cup salt
- 3  lemons
- 2 (10 fluid ounce) bottles Worcestershire sauce

## Instructions

1. Combine the vinegar, cayenne pepper, black pepper, mustard powder, salt, lemons, and Worcestershire sauce in a large pot; bring to a simmer.
2. Bring to a boil, then turn heat to low and simmer for at least 30 minutes.
