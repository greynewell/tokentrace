## Ingredients

- 1 cup olive oil
- ⅓ cup distilled white vinegar
- ½ cup white sugar
- 1 (3 ounce) package chicken flavored ramen noodles, crushed, seasoning packet reserved
- 1 large head fresh broccoli, diced
- 2  carrots, grated
- 2 bunches green onions, chopped
- 1 cup sunflower seeds

## Instructions

1. In a small bowl combine oil, vinegar, sugar and the seasoning packet from the ramen noodles.
2. Mix well and refrigerate at least one hour before serving, or overnight.
3. In a large bowl combine broccoli, carrots, green onions and sunflower seeds.
4. Crush ramen noodles and stir in.
5. Pour dressing over salad about 10 minutes before serving.
