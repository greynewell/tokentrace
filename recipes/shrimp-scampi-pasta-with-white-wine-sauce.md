## Ingredients

- 12 ounces angel hair pasta
- ¼ cup olive oil
- 2 tablespoons butter
- ½  yellow onion, diced
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 small tomato, diced
- ¼ cup olives, chopped
- ¼ cup Chardonnay wine
- ¼ cup chopped leeks
- ½  lemon, juiced
- 2 teaspoons capers
- 2 teaspoons salt
- ½ teaspoon cayenne pepper, or to taste
- 1 pound uncooked medium shrimp, peeled and deveined
- ¼ cup heavy whipping cream
- 2 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro

## Instructions

1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
2. Cook angel hair pasta in the boiling water, stirring occasionally, until tender yet firm to the bite, 4 to 5 minutes.
3. Drain.
4. Heat olive oil and butter in a large skillet over medium heat.
5. Add onion and garlic; cook until onion is transparent, about 4 minutes.
6. Add tomato, olives, Chardonnay wine, leeks, lemon juice, capers, salt, and cayenne pepper.
7. Bring to a boil.
8. Mix in shrimp and simmer until pink, about 10 minutes.
9. Stir in heavy whipping cream and cilantro.
10. Serve on top of angel hair pasta.
