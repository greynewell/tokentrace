## Ingredients

- 1  red bell pepper, sliced
- 1  tomato, chopped
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- 1 teaspoon dried oregano, or to taste
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- 1  eggplant, sliced
- 1  zucchini, sliced
- 1  tomato, sliced
- 1  red onion, sliced
- 4 teaspoons olive oil
- 4 slices sourdough bread
- 4 slices mozzarella cheese

## Instructions

1. Preheat a panini press according to manufacturer's instructions.
2. Grill red bell pepper slices in the panini press on high heat until soft, about 5 minutes.
3. Transfer to a blender.
4. Place chopped tomato and garlic in the blender.
5. Blend with bell pepper until a smooth sauce forms, about 2 minutes.
6. Season sauce with oregano, salt, and black pepper.
7. Grill eggplant, zucchini, tomato, and onion slices in the panini press until softened, about 3 minutes per side.
8. Drizzle olive oil over bread slices; flip and spread with a generous amount of sauce.
9. Place 2 slices bread, oil-side down, on the panini press; add 1 slice of mozzarella cheese.
10. Top with eggplant mixture and second slice of mozzarella cheese.
11. Place second slice of bread, oil-side up, on top.
12. Grill on medium-high until golden and cheese is melted, about 5 minutes.
