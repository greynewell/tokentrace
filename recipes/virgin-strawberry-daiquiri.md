## Ingredients

- 2 large strawberries, hulled
- ¼ cup white sugar
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice
- ¾ cup chilled lemon-lime soda
- 4 cubes  ice

## Instructions

1. Combine strawberries, sugar, lemon-lime soda, and lemon juice in the container of a blender. Add ice and blend until smooth.
2. Pour into a fancy glass to serve.
