## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon mayonnaise
- 2 slices thick-cut rye bread
- 2 slices Swiss cheese
- 2 slices leftover turkey meat, or to taste
- ¼ cup baby spinach, or to taste

## Instructions

1. Set oven rack about 6 inches from the heat source and preheat the oven's broiler.
2. Spread mayonnaise onto 1 side of each bread slice.
3. Layer Swiss cheese, turkey, and spinach onto the mayonnaise-side of 1 bread slice; top with second bread slice.
4. Place sandwich on a baking sheet.
5. Broil in the preheated oven until heated through and cheese is bubbling, about 5 minutes.
