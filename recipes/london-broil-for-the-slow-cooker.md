## Ingredients

- 1 ½ pounds London broil
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 (10.75 ounce) can  cream of mushroom soup
- ½ cup water
- ½ teaspoon dried basil
- ½ teaspoon dried oregano
- ½ teaspoon salt

## Instructions

1. Place London broil in a slow cooker; add the garlic, condensed soup, water, basil, oregano, and salt; cover.
2. Cook on Low 6 to 7 hours.
