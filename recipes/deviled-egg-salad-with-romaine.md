## Ingredients

- 1 cup white sugar
- 1 cup mayonnaise
- 3 tablespoons yellow mustard
- 2 teaspoons water, or as needed  (Optional)
- 3 heads romaine lettuce, chopped
- 4  hard-boiled eggs, peeled and sliced
- 1 pinch paprika, or to taste

## Instructions

1. Whisk sugar, mayonnaise, and mustard together in a bowl until smooth. Add water, 1 teaspoon at a time, if dressing is too thick. Refrigerate dressing until chilled, at least 30 minutes.
2. Place romaine lettuce in a bowl; add dressing and toss to coat. Arrange eggs around the lettuce and season with paprika.
