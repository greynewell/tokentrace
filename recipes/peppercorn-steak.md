## Ingredients

- 1 (12 ounce) top sirloin, rib-eye, or T-bone steak, 1-inch thick
- salt to taste
- 2 tablespoons whole black peppercorns, coarsely ground
- oil, or as needed
- ½  shallot, minced
- ¼ cup brandy
- ¼ cup beef stock
- ¼ cup heavy whipping cream

## Instructions

1. Season both sides of the steak with salt.
2. Press peppercorns onto both sides of the steak to form a crust.
3. Heat oil in a skillet over high heat.
4. Add steak and cook for 4 minutes on each side.
5. Remove steak and let it rest.
6. Discard oil from the skillet.
7. Return skillet to heat and add shallot.
8. Cook and stir shallot for 1 minute.
9. Pour in brandy.
10. Reduce heat slightly; add stock and heavy cream.
11. Cook until liquid has reduced to a creamy consistency, 3 to 5 minutes.
12. Season with salt.
13. Serve steak with the sauce.
