## Ingredients

- 9 cloves garlic, coarsely chopped
- 4  green bell peppers, seeded and chopped
- 2  red bell peppers, seeded and chopped
- 2  green chile peppers, seeded and chopped
- 2  onions, chopped

## Instructions

1. Place the garlic, green bell peppers, red bell peppers, green chile peppers, and onions into a food processor, and process to a coarse puree.
2. Spoon the sofrito into ice cube trays and freeze.
3. Once the cubes are solidly frozen, transfer them to a resealable freezer bag.
4. Keep frozen for use as needed.
