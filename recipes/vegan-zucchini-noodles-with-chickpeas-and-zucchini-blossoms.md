## Ingredients

- 4 tablespoons olive oil
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 medium zucchini, cut into noodles with a spiralizer
- 12  zucchini blossoms, pistils removed, cut into strips
- ½ cup canned chickpeas, drained and rinsed
- salt
- 6  fresh basil leaves, cut into strips, or to taste

## Instructions

1. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over low heat and cook garlic until softened, about 10 minutes.
2. Add zucchini and zucchini blossoms; mix well with olive oil.
3. Add chickpeas and stir to combine.
4. Season with salt and stir in basil.
5. Serve immediately.
