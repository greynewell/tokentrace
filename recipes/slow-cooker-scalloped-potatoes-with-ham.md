## Ingredients

- 3 pounds potatoes, peeled and thinly sliced
- 1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
- ½ cup chopped onion
- 1 cup chopped cooked ham
- 1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of mushroom soup
- ½ cup water
- ½ teaspoon garlic powder
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- ¼ teaspoon black pepper

## Instructions

1. Place sliced potatoes into the slow cooker. Mix together shredded cheese, ham, and onion in a bowl. Add to potatoes in the slow cooker; mix well.
2. Stir together condensed soup and water in the bowl. Season with garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Pour evenly over potato mixture.
3. Cook on High for 4 hours.
