## Ingredients

- 1  egg white
- 1 tablespoon water
- 1 pound walnut halves
- 1 cup white sugar
- ½ teaspoon ground cinnamon
- ¼ teaspoon salt

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 250 degrees F (120 degrees C).
2. Beat egg white and water together in a bowl using an electric mixer until frothy. Add walnuts to egg white mixture and stir until coated.
3. Mix sugar, cinnamon, and salt together in a bowl; add to walnuts and stir until coated. Spread coated walnuts onto a baking sheet.
4. Bake in the preheated oven, stirring every 15 minutes, until walnuts are fragrant and crisp, about 1 hour.
