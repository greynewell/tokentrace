## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons peanut butter, or to taste
- 1 (7 inch) whole wheat tortilla
- 1  apple, sliced
- 4  grape tomatoes, or to taste
- 4  yellow grape tomatoes, or to taste
- 3  celery sticks, or to taste
- 4  baby carrots, or to taste
- 2 tablespoons ranch dressing, or to taste

## Instructions

1. Spread peanut butter over tortilla. Arrange apple slices end to end down the middle of the tortilla. Roll tortilla up tightly; cut into small pieces.
2. Assemble bento box with tortilla pieces, grape tomatoes, golden tomatoes, celery, and carrots. Serve ranch dressing alongside.
