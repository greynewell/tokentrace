## Ingredients

- 1 cup canned shrimp
- 2  hard-cooked eggs, chopped
- 1 ½ cups finely grated carrots
- 1 tablespoon minced onion
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ⅛ teaspoon ground black pepper
- ½ cup mayonnaise
- 2 tablespoons lemon juice
- ½ teaspoon prepared mustard
- 4  medium tomatoes
- 4  leaves of lettuce

## Instructions

1. Add shrimp, eggs, carrots, onion, salt, and pepper to the bowl of a food processor.
2. Pulse until just combined.
3. Blend mayonnaise with lemon juice and mustard; mix into shrimp mixture with a fork.
4. Core tomatoes.
5. Cut into quarters without cutting all the way to the bottom.
6. Place each tomato on a lettuce leaf, and open.
7. Spoon shrimp mixture into the center.
8. Serve
