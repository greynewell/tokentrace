## Ingredients

- 8  carrots, peeled
- 3 tablespoons olive oil
- ¼ cup honey
- salt and ground black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Preheat an oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Place whole carrots in a baking dish, and drizzle with olive oil. Mix until carrots are completely covered with oil. Drizzle honey over top, then season with salt and pepper; mix until evenly coated.
3. Bake in the preheated oven until carrots are just tender, about 30 minutes, or longer if you prefer softer carrots.
