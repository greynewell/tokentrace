## Ingredients

- 1 (16 ounce) can garbanzo beans (chickpeas), rinsed and drained
- 1  mango - peeled and diced
- 1 cup chopped hearts of romaine  (Optional)
- ½ cup diced cucumber
- ½ cup diced carrot
- ¼ cup diced celery  (Optional)
- 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
- salt to taste
- 1 cup chopped pineapple

## Instructions

1. Toss together the garbanzo beans, mango, romaine lettuce, cucumber, carrot, celery, lemon juice, and salt in a large bowl.
2. Let the mixture set for 30 minutes.
3. Top the salad with the pineapple to serve.
