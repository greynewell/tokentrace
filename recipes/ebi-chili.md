## Ingredients

- 12  large shrimp, peeled and deveined
- 1 tablespoon water
- 1 tablespoon cornstarch
- ¾ teaspoon salt, divided
- 1 tablespoon tomato ketchup
- 1 teaspoon chili bean paste (doubanjiang)
- 1 teaspoon sake
- 1 teaspoon vinegar
- 1 teaspoon white sugar
- 1 teaspoon cornstarch
- ¼  scallion, minced
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- 1 teaspoon minced fresh ginger
- 3 leaves green lettuce, torn into bite-sized pieces
- 1 tablespoon mixed sprouts

## Instructions

1. Combine shrimp, water, 1 tablespoon cornstarch, and 1/2 teaspoon salt in a microwave-safe bowl; mix well with your hands.
2. Rinse under cold running water.
3. Pat shrimp dry with paper towels; return to the bowl.
4. Whisk ketchup, chili bean paste, sake, vinegar, sugar, 1 teaspoon cornstarch, and 1/4 teaspoon salt together in a bowl.
5. Mix in scallion, garlic, and ginger.
6. Pour ketchup mixture evenly over the shrimp.
7. Cover bowl with microwave-safe plastic wrap.
8. Microwave on 600W power until shrimp turn opaque, about 3 minutes.
9. Spread lettuce pieces on a serving plate; pour shrimp on top.
10. Garnish with sprouts.
