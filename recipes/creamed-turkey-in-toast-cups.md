## Ingredients

- 16 slices soft white bread, crusts removed
- 3 tablespoons butter, melted
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 small onion, diced
- 1 small green bell pepper, diced
- 1 (10.75 ounce) can  reduced-fat condensed cream of mushroom soup
- ¾ cup skim milk
- 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
- 1 teaspoon rubbed sage, or more to taste
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- 2 ½ cups cubed cooked turkey
- 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley, or as desired

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Roll bread slices flat on a work surface using a rolling pin.
3. Brush 1 side of each flattened bread slice with melted butter.
4. Fit bread slices into muffin cups, butter-side up.
5. Bake in the preheated oven until cups are lightly browned, 12 to 15 minutes.
6. Cool completely.
7. Heat olive oil and 1 tablespoon butter in a large skillet over medium-high heat; cook and stir onion and green bell pepper until softened, 5 to 10 minutes.
8. Reduce heat to medium and gradually stir cream of mushroom soup and milk into onion mixture until smooth; add Italian seasoning, sage, salt, and pepper.
9. Stir turkey into soup mixture; cook and stir until bubbling, about 5 minutes.
10. Place 2 or 3 bread cups on each place.
11. Ladle soup mixture into each cup; top with parsley.
