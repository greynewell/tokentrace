## Ingredients

- 3 tablespoons vegetable oil
- 4 (1/4 inch thick) slices fresh scrapple
- 8 slices bread, toasted
- 2 teaspoons prepared spicy mustard

## Instructions

1. Heat the vegetable oil in a large nonstick skillet over medium-low heat.
2. Cook the scrapple until crispy and browned on both sides, 5 to 7 minutes per side.
3. Drain the scrapple slices on a paper towel-lined plate.
4. Place a scrapple slice onto a piece of toasted bread, spread with mustard, and top with another slice of bread to assemble.
