## Ingredients

- 1 cup corn syrup
- ¾ cup natural peanut butter
- 1 cup gluten-free chocolate chips, divided
- ½ cup chopped almonds
- ½ cup chopped cashews
- 2 cups gluten-free oatmeal
- 2 cups crispy rice cereal
- ⅓ cup flax seed meal

## Instructions

1. Mix syrup and peanut butter in a saucepan over low heat until smooth, 3 to 5 minutes.
2. Remove saucepan from heat.
3. Stir about half the chocolate chips into the syrup mixture.
4. Mix remaining chocolate chips, almonds, cashews, oatmeal, crispy rice cereal, and flax seed meal in a bowl.
5. Pour the mixture from the saucepan over the mixture in the bowl; stir to evenly coat.
6. Transfer mixture to a 9x13-inch baking dish and press into an even layer; allow to cool completely before cutting into bars.
