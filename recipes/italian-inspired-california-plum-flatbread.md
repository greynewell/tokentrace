## Ingredients

- 1 pound whole wheat pizza dough
- 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil, divided
- 2 cups shredded Italian blend cheese
- 2 fruit (2-1/8" dia)s fresh California plums, pitted and very thinly sliced
- 3 cups baby or chopped arugula
- ½ cup slivered red onion
- 2 tablespoons snipped fresh basil
- 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
- 1 pinch Freshly ground pepper and sea salt to taste

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees F.
2. Divide dough into 3 pieces; roll each into a very flat oval on a lightly floured board (about 7x11-inches).
3. Place dough on baking sheets and brush with 1 tablespoon oil.
4. Bake for 10 minutes. (May be prepared ahead to this point.) Just before serving, top each flat bread with 2/3 cup cheese.
5. Bake at 425 degrees F for 5 minutes.
6. To prepare plum salad, toss remaining oil with remaining ingredients.
7. Place equal amounts on each warm cheese flat bread and cut into 3-inch slices to serve.
