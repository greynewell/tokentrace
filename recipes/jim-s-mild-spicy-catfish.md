## Ingredients

- 1 cup cornmeal
- 1 teaspoon paprika
- ½ teaspoon onion powder
- ½ teaspoon garlic powder
- ¼ teaspoon cayenne pepper, or to taste
- 1 cup milk
- 2  eggs, beaten
- 6 (4 ounce) fillets catfish
- 2 tablespoons vegetable oil

## Instructions

1. Mix cornmeal, paprika, onion powder, garlic powder, and cayenne pepper on a plate.
2. Beat milk and eggs together in a bowl.
3. Dip catfish fillets in milk mixture, then gently press fillets into cornmeal mixture, coating both sides.
4. Shake off excess breading.
5. Heat vegetable oil in a large skillet; pan-fry catfish in hot oil until the fish is golden brown and flakes easily with a fork, about 4 minutes per side.
