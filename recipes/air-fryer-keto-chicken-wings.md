## Ingredients

- 3 pounds chicken wings
- 1 tablespoon taco seasoning mix
- 2 teaspoons olive oil

## Instructions

1. Combine chicken wings, taco seasoning, and oil in a resealable plastic bag. Shake to coat.
2. Preheat the air fryer to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 2 minutes.
3. Place wings in the air fryer and cook for 12 minutes, flipping after 6 minutes.
