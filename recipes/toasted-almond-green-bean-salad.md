## Ingredients

- 2 pounds fresh green beans, trimmed
- ½ cup olive oil
- 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
- ½ cup sliced almonds

## Instructions

1. Place beans in a large saucepan, and cover with water.
2. Boil beans until soft but still bright green, about 15 minutes.
3. Transfer beans immediately to an ice water bath to cool.
4. Drain after 3 minutes.
5. Place beans in large bowl.
6. Toss with balsamic vinegar, stir in th olive oil.
7. Place the almonds in a heavy skillet over low heat; cook until golden.
8. Toss almonds into the green bean salad.
9. Serve  at room temperature or chilled.
