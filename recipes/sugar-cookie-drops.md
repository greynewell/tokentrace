## Ingredients

- 1 cup butter
- 1 cup vegetable oil
- 1 ½ cups confectioners' sugar
- 1 cup white sugar
- 2  eggs
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 4 ½ cups all-purpose flour
- 1 teaspoon baking soda
- 1 teaspoon cream of tartar
- ½ cup white sugar for decoration

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. In a large bowl, cream together the butter, oil, confectioners' sugar, and 1 cup white sugar until light and fluffy.
3. Beat in eggs one at a time, and stir in the vanilla.
4. Combine the flour, baking soda, and cream of tartar; stir into the creamed mixture.
5. Roll dough into 1 inch balls, and then roll the balls in remaining white sugar.
6. Place onto ungreased cookie sheets, and flatten with a fork.
7. Bake for 8 to 10 minutes in the preheated oven, or until the edges begin to brown.
8. Cool on wire racks.
