## Ingredients

- ½ cup fat-free mayonnaise
- ¼ cup reduced-fat buttermilk
- 3 tablespoons thinly sliced green onions
- 1 ½ tablespoons fresh lemon juice
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- kosher salt to taste
- 6 medium heirloom tomatoes, cored and cut into wedges
- ½ cup thinly sliced onion
- 1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice
- kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
- 2 ounces crumbled soft goat cheese

## Instructions

1. Whisk mayonnaise, buttermilk, green onions, lemon juice, garlic, and salt for dressing together in a small bowl. Chill in the refrigerator until ready to use if not serving immediately.
2. Combine tomatoes, onion, lemon juice, salt, and pepper for salad in a bowl.
3. Divide mixture between plates. Drizzle with dressing and top with crumbled goat cheese.
