## Ingredients

- 6  dates, pitted
- 1 cup raw cashews
- 1 tablespoon flax seed meal
- ⅛ teaspoon ground cinnamon
- 1 pinch salt
- 1 tablespoon coconut oil

## Instructions

1. Combine dates, cashews, flax meal, cinnamon, and salt in a food processor or blender; blend until very well mixed.
2. While the processor is running, add coconut oil and blend until mixture holds together.
3. Roll mixture into 20 balls (about the size of a large gum ball) and arrange on a plate or baking sheet.
4. Freeze for at least 30 minutes.
5. Keep energy balls stored in the freezer or refrigerator.
