## Ingredients

- 1 ½ pounds ground chuck
- ¼ cup butter
- 8 slices white bread
- 8 slices American cheese
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 1 large onion, sliced

## Instructions

1. Preheat an outdoor grill for medium-high heat, and lightly oil the grate.
2. Divide the beef in half, and form each half into a patty.
3. Grill the beef patties for 8 to 10 minutes per side, or to desired doneness.
4. While the burgers are grilling, melt 1/4 cup of butter in a large skillet.
5. Lay out 4 slices of bread, and top each slice with 2 slices of American cheese.
6. Top each sandwich with a slice of bread to make 4 cheese sandwiches.
7. Pan-fry the sandwiches in the butter until golden brown, about 3 minutes per side.
8. Set the sandwiches aside.
9. Melt the remaining 2 tablespoons of butter in the skillet, and cook the sliced onions until browned, stirring occasionally, about 8 minutes.
10. To assemble, top each grilled burger with half the onions, and place each between 2 grilled cheese sandwiches.
