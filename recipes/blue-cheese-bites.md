## Ingredients

- 1 (10 ounce) package refrigerated biscuit dough
- ½ cup butter, melted
- 5 ounces blue cheese, crumbled

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Lightly grease a 9-inch pie plate with nonstick cooking spray.
2. Slice each biscuit into quarters and arrange them in the prepared pie plate. Pour melted butter over biscuits, then sprinkle with blue cheese.
3. Bake in the preheated oven for 8 to 10 minutes.
