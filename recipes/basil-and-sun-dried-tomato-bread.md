## Ingredients

- 2 ¼ teaspoons active dry yeast
- 3 cups bread flour
- 3 tablespoons wheat bran
- ⅓ cup quinoa
- 3 tablespoons instant powdered milk
- 1 tablespoon dried basil
- ⅓ cup chopped sun-dried tomatoes
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 ¼ cups water
- 1 cup boiling water to cover

## Instructions

1. In a small bowl, pour boiling water over sun-dried tomato halves to cover.
2. Soak for 10 minutes, drain, and cool to room temperature.
3. With scissors, snip into 1/4 inch pieces.
4. Place all ingredients into the pan of the bread machine in the order recommended by the manufacturer.
5. Select the Basic or White Bread cycle, and Start.
