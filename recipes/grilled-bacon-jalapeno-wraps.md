## Ingredients

- 6  fresh jalapeno peppers, halved lengthwise and seeded
- 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese
- 12 slices bacon

## Instructions

1. Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat and lightly oil the grate.
2. Spread cream cheese in each jalapeño half; wrap with bacon. Secure with a toothpick if needed.
3. Cook on the preheated grill until bacon is crispy, turning often, about 10 minutes.
