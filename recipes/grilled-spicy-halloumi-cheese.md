## Ingredients

- 8 ounces halloumi cheese, sliced
- 1 tablespoon Sriracha sauce
- 2 teaspoons olive oil
- ¼ teaspoon garlic powder
- ¼ teaspoon black pepper

## Instructions

1. Preheat an outdoor grill for medium heat and lightly oil the grate.
2. Mix Sriracha, olive oil, garlic powder, and black pepper in a shallow bowl. Dip each halloumi cheese slice into Sriracha mixture and let rest 5 minutes.
3. Place each halloumi slice on the hot grill and cook for 3 minutes. Carefully flip slices and cook an additional 3 minutes. Serve immediately.
