## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons coconut oil
- 3 tablespoons agave nectar
- 2 tablespoons dried chipotle chili pepper
- ½ cup unpopped popcorn

## Instructions

1. Heat the coconut oil, agave nectar, and chipotle powder in a large saucepan or soup pot over medium-high heat.
2. Once the mixture starts to form tiny bubbles, pour in the popcorn, and use a fork to stir it around to coat the kernels.
3. Place the lid on the pan.
4. When the corn starts to pop, shake the pan constantly until the popping stops.
5. Remove from heat, and pour popped corn into a large bowl.
