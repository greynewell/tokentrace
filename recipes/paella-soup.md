## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 1 cup chopped celery
- 1 cup chopped onion
- ¾ cup chopped carrots
- 1  red bell pepper, chopped, or more to taste
- 1 cup cubed fully cooked ham
- 1 ½ quarts chicken broth
- ¼ teaspoon saffron threads, or more to taste
- 1 cup frozen petite peas
- 3 ⅓ cups water
- 2 (5.6 ounce) packages Spanish rice mix (such as La Preferida®)
- 1 (10 ounce) can diced tomatoes and green chiles (such as RO*TEL®)
- 2 pounds frozen cooked shrimp, thawed and tails removed

## Instructions

1. Heat olive oil in a saucepan over medium-high heat.
2. Saute celery, onion, carrots, and red pepper until soft, about 10 minutes.
3. Add ham; cook and stir for 2 minutes.
4. Add chicken broth and saffron.
5. Bring to a boil; reduce to a simmer.
6. Add peas.
7. Meanwhile, bring water to a boil in a separate pot.
8. Add rice mixes and stir well.
9. Bring back to a boil; reduce heat, cover, and simmer for 3 minutes.
10. Remove from heat and let stand, covered, for 8 minutes.
11. Add diced tomatoes and green chiles; stir.
12. Add rice mixture to the soup.
13. Remove soup from heat and let cool for 5 minutes.
14. Add shrimp and let stand until warmed through.
