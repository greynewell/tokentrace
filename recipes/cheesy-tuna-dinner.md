## Ingredients

- 1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of mushroom soup
- 1 ½ cups milk
- 2 (6 ounce) cans tuna, drained, flaked
- 1 cup frozen green peas, thawed
- 2 cups Minute® White Rice, uncooked
- 1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
- 1 cup French fried onions or crushed potato chips

## Instructions

1. Mix soup and milk in medium saucepan.
2. Bring to boil on medium heat, stirring frequently.
3. Add tuna and peas; mix well.
4. Return to boil.
5. Stir in rice and cheese; cover.
6. Reduce heat to low; cook 5 minutes.
7. Stir until cheese is melted.
8. Garnish with canned French fried onions or crushed  potato chips just before serving, if desired.
