## Ingredients

- 3  sweet potatoes, peeled and chopped
- 2 cups beef broth
- 1 cup unsweetened coconut milk
- 1 small shallot, minced
- 2 tablespoons grade B maple syrup
- 2 teaspoons curry powder
- 1 teaspoon sea salt
- 1 teaspoon chili powder
- 1 teaspoon Spanish smoked paprika

## Instructions

1. Combine sweet potatoes and beef broth in a pot; cook over medium-high heat until tender, 5 to 10 minutes.
2. Mash sweet potatoes into the broth using a potato masher.
3. Mix coconut milk, shallot, maple syrup, curry powder, sea salt, chili powder, and paprika into sweet potato mixture; cook and stir until heated through, 5 to 10 minutes.
4. Reduce heat to low and blend soup with a hand blender until smooth.
