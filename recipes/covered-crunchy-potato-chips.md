## Ingredients

- 1 ounce kettle-cooked potato chips
- ¼ cup shredded sharp Cheddar cheese
- 2  cherry tomatoes, chopped
- 1 tablespoon chopped green onion
- 1 tablespoon chopped cooked bacon
- 1 tablespoon sour cream

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
2. Spread chips into a baking dish.
3. Bake in the preheated oven until chips are warm, about 3 minutes.
4. Sprinkle Cheddar cheese over chips and bake in the oven until cheese is melted, 3 to 5 minutes.
5. Top chips with tomatoes, green onion, bacon, and sour cream.
