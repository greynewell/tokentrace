## Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon vegetable oil
- 1 ½ tablespoons Dijon mustard
- 2 teaspoons rice vinegar
- ½ teaspoon Sriracha hot sauce  (Optional)
- salt to taste
- 2 (5 ounce) salmon fillets

## Instructions

1. Set oven rack about 6 inches from the heat source and preheat the oven's broiler.
2. Line a baking sheet with aluminum foil and rub with vegetable oil.
3. Stir mustard, vinegar, Sriracha sauce, and salt together in a bowl until glaze is smooth.
4. Lay salmon fillets, skin-side down, on the prepared baking sheet.
5. Brush fillets with mustard glaze.
6. Cook in the preheated oven until salmon is golden and flakes easily with a fork, 5 to 8 minutes.
