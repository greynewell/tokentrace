## Ingredients

- 1 (3 pound) cauliflower
- ¼ cup water
- ¾ cup mayonnaise
- ¼ cup grated Parmesan cheese
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tablespoon chopped fresh chives

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees F).
2. Cut leaves and bottom core off cauliflower. Place in a shallow microwave-safe baking dish and add about 1 inch of water.
3. Microwave on high for 8 minutes. Drain.
4. Combine mayonnaise, Parmesan cheese, and garlic in a bowl. Spread onto the cauliflower.
5. Bake cauliflower in the preheated oven until tender and golden brown, 15 to 20 minutes. Sprinkle with chives.
