## Ingredients

- 2 cups spinach leaves
- ½  red bell pepper, seeded and diced
- ½ tablespoon crumbled feta cheese
- cracked black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Place the spinach on a plate or in a bowl.
2. Sprinkle the red pepper and feta cheese over it.
3. Season with cracked black pepper, and enjoy this healthy meal!
