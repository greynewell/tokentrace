## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
- 1  onion, finely diced
- 1  green bell pepper, chopped
- 1 rib celery, thinly sliced
- 1 (14 ounce) can chopped tomatoes
- 3 cups chicken broth, or more to taste
- ¼ cup chopped fresh parsley
- 1 ½ pounds cod, cut into 3/4-inch cubes
- salt and pepper to taste
- 1 dash red pepper flakes

## Instructions

1. Heat oil in a soup pot over medium heat.
2. Add onion, bell pepper, and celery and cook until wilted, about 5 minutes.
3. Add tomatoes and broth and bring to a simmer, about 5 minutes.
4. Cook 15 to 20 minutes.
5. Add cod, parsley, and red pepper flakes and simmer until fish flakes easily with a fork, 8 to 10 minutes more.
6. Season with salt and black pepper.
