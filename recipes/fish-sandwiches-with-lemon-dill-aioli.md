## Ingredients

- 1 cup mayonnaise
- ½ medium lemon, juiced
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- 2 teaspoons chopped fresh dill, or to taste
- 1 dash cayenne pepper
- 1 pinch salt and ground black pepper to taste
- 1 tablespoon chili powder
- ½ tablespoon garlic powder
- ¾ teaspoon dried thyme
- ¾ teaspoon cayenne pepper, or to taste
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
- 2 tablespoons olive oil, or as needed
- 4 (4 ounce) tilapia fillets
- 4 each sandwich buns, split and toasted

## Instructions

1. Combine mayonnaise, lemon juice, garlic, dill, cayenne, salt, and pepper for aioli in a bowl.
2. Let flavors mingle for at least 10 minutes.
3. Meanwhile, combine chili powder, garlic powder, thyme, cayenne, salt, and black pepper in a small bowl.
4. Sprinkle a little of the seasoning mixture over each side of the tilapia.
5. Place a medium to large pan on the stovetop over medium heat.
6. Add just enough olive oil to coat the bottom of the pan and allow oil to heat, 1 to 2 minutes.
7. Add fish to the hot oil and cook until it flakes easily with a fork, 3 to 4 minutes on each side.
8. Spread a little aioli onto each half of the toasted buns.
9. Place cooked fish on the bottom half; top with remaining half.
10. Serve with extra aioli on the side.
