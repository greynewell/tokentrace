## Ingredients

- 2 pounds ground beef
- 2  onions, chopped
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 stalks celery, finely chopped
- 1  carrot, shredded
- 4 (14.5 ounce) cans stewed tomatoes
- 1 (15 ounce) can tomato sauce
- 2 tablespoons chili powder
- 2 teaspoons Italian seasoning
- 2 teaspoons salt
- 1 (15 ounce) can kidney beans with liquid

## Instructions

1. Combine ground beef, onions, and garlic in a large stockpot; cook and stir over medium heat until beef is browned and crumbly, about 10 minutes.
2. Drain excess grease.
3. Add celery and carrot to beef mixture; cook and stir until celery and carrot are slightly tender, about 5 minutes.
4. Stir stewed tomatoes, tomato sauce, chili powder, Italian seasoning, and salt into beef mixture, breaking up tomatoes while stirring.
5. Bring liquid to a boil, reduce heat to medium-low, cover stockpot, and simmer, stirring occasionally, until flavors blend, about 1 hour.
6. Stir beans into chili and simmer, stirring occasionally, for 20 minutes.
