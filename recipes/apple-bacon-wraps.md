## Ingredients

- 6 slices bacon, cut in half
- 4 (10 inch) flour tortillas
- ¼ cup honey
- ½ cup shredded Cheddar cheese  (Optional)
- 8 slices deli ham
- 1  Granny Smith apple, cut into thin bite-size pieces

## Instructions

1. Place bacon in a large skillet and cook over medium-high heat, turning occasionally, until evenly browned, about 10 minutes.
2. Drain bacon slices on paper towels.
3. Place a tortilla on a work surface and drizzle 1 tablespoon honey down the middle of the tortilla; top with 3 bacon pieces.
4. Layer 2 tablespoons Cheddar cheese over bacon and top with 2 slices ham and 1/4 of the sliced apples.
5. Roll tortilla around filling.
6. Repeat to create 3 more wraps.
