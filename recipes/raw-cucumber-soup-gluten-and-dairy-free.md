## Ingredients

- 2 cups chopped cucumber
- 1 cup chopped zucchini
- 1 cup peeled and chopped avocado
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- 2 cups lukewarm water
- ½  large lemon, juiced
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- ½ teaspoon salt

## Instructions

1. Blend cucumber, zucchini, avocado, and garlic in a food processor or blender until very finely chopped, about 30 seconds.
2. Whisk together water, lemon juice, olive oil, and salt in a bowl.
3. Turn on the food processor and pour water mixture slowly through food processor's feed tube while processing cucumber mixture.
4. Process until smooth, about 1 minute.
