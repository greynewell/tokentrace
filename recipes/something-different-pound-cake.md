## Ingredients

- 1 cup butter
- ½ cup shortening
- 3 cups white sugar
- 5  eggs
- 3 cups all-purpose flour
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ½ teaspoon baking powder
- ½ cup milk
- ½ cup evaporated milk
- 1 teaspoon rum flavored extract
- 1 teaspoon coconut extract
- 1 cup white sugar
- 1 teaspoon rum flavored extract
- 1 teaspoon coconut extract
- 1 teaspoon almond extract
- ½ cup water

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C).
2. Grease a 10 inch tube pan and line with parchment paper.
3. Grease the parchment paper.
4. Sift flour, salt and baking powder together and set aside.
5. In a large bowl, cream butter, shortening and 3 cups sugar until light and fluffy.
6. Add eggs one at a time, beating well after each.
7. Add 1 teaspoon rum extract and 1 teaspoon coconut extract.
8. Add flour mixture alternately with milk and evaporated milk.
9. Mix until smooth.
10. Pour batter into 10 inch tube pan.
11. Bake at 325 degrees F (165 degrees C) for 1 hour and 15 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into cake comes out clean.
12. Cool.
13. Make the glaze: in a saucepan, combine 1 cup sugar, 1/2 cup water, 1 teaspoon rum extract, 1 teaspoon coconut extract and 1 teaspoon almond extract.
14. Bring to a boil, stirring constantly.
15. Use a toothpick to pierce all over the top of the cake.
16. Pour the glaze over the cooled cake and allow it to soak in.
