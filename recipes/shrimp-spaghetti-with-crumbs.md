## Ingredients

- 1 (16 ounce) package spaghetti
- ⅔ cup olive oil
- 4 cloves garlic, halved
- 1 cup dry bread crumbs
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 1 cup cooked shrimp
- ½ teaspoon dried rosemary
- ½ teaspoon dried basil
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
3. Cook spaghetti in the boiling water, stirring occasionally, until cooked through but firm to the bite, about 12 minutes.
4. Drain and transfer to a large bowl.
5. Heat 2/3 cup olive oil and garlic in a skillet over medium-low heat until garlic is fragrant, about 5 minutes.
6. Remove from heat and discard garlic cloves.
7. Toss bread crumbs with about 2 tablespoons garlic olive oil in a bowl and spread bread crumbs over a baking sheet.
8. Pour remaining garlic olive oil over spaghetti and toss to coat.
9. Bake bread crumbs in the preheated oven until toasted, about 5 minutes.
10. Transfer bread crumbs to the bowl with spaghetti and toss.
11. Melt butter in a clean skillet over medium heat; cook and stir shrimp, rosemary, and basil until shrimp is coated and heated through, about 5 minutes.
12. Toss shrimp with spaghetti mixture and season with salt and pepper.
13. Garnish with parsley.
