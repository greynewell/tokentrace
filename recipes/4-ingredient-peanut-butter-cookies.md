## Ingredients

- 1 cup white sugar
- 1 cup light corn syrup
- 1 ½ cups peanut butter
- 4 cups cornflakes

## Instructions

1. Combine sugar and corn syrup in a large skillet over medium heat. Cook and stir until sugar has dissolved and mixture reaches a full boil. Remove from the heat. Add peanut butter; stir until thoroughly mixed. Stir in cornflakes.
2. Drop spoonfuls of dough onto waxed paper and let cool.
