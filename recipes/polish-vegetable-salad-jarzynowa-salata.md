## Ingredients

- 4 large parsnips
- 6  carrots
- 8  eggs
- 8  dill pickles, finely chopped
- 1 (14 ounce) can peas, drained
- 1  onion, finely chopped
- 1 small apple, peeled and finely chopped
- 1 cup mayonnaise
- ⅓ cup mustard
- 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
- ½ teaspoon salt

## Instructions

1. Place parsnips, carrots, and eggs into a large pot and cover with salted water; bring to a boil.
2. Cook until vegetables are tender and eggs are hard-boiled, about 6 minutes .
3. Remove eggs from hot water, cool under cold running water, and peel.
4. Drain vegetables and let cool until safe to handle.
5. Place cooled eggs, parsnips, and carrots on a work surface.
6. Chop into small pieces the size of peas, and transfer to a large bowl.
7. Drain pickles of any excess juice and add to the bowl along with peas, onion, and apple.
8. Add mayonnaise, mustard, pepper, and salt; stir thoroughly until everything is coated.
9. Cover with plastic wrap and refrigerate until flavors sink in, about 24 hours.
