## Ingredients

- 1  French baguette, thinly sliced
- 6 ounces goat cheese, or more to taste
- 5 sprigs fresh rosemary, minced
- 1 cup chopped walnuts
- ½ cup honey, or to taste

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Place baguette slices on a baking sheet.
3. Spread a thin layer of goat cheese over slices.
4. Sprinkle rosemary on top.
5. Bake in preheated oven until crispy at the edges, about 10 minutes.
6. Toast walnuts in a skillet over medium heat, stirring constantly, about 5 minutes.
7. Sprinkle walnuts on top of baguette slices; drizzle lightly with honey.
