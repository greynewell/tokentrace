## Ingredients

- 1 pound onions, sliced
- 3 tablespoons vegetable oil
- 2 (14 ounce) cans beef broth
- 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
- 1 pinch ground black pepper
- 6 slices French bread, toasted
- â…” cup shredded Swiss cheese

## Instructions

1. Cook and stir onions in vegetable oil in a covered large saucepan over medium heat for 20 minutes.
2. Stir in the beef broth, Worcestershire sauce and black pepper; heat to a boil.
3. Remove from heat.
4. Place bread on a baking sheet; sprinkle bread with cheese.
5. Broil 4-to-5 inches from heat for about 1 minute or until cheese is melted and golden.
6. Ladle soup into bowls and top with bread.
