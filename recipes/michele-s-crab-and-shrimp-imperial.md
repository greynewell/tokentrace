## Ingredients

- 1  serving cooking spray  (Optional)
- 2 pounds peeled and deveined extra large shrimp
- 2 extra large eggs
- ¾ cup mayonnaise
- 1 tablespoon prepared yellow mustard
- 3 dashes hot pepper sauce
- 2 teaspoons seafood seasoning (such as Old Bay®)
- 1 tablespoon dried tarragon
- salt and pepper to taste
- 2 (6 ounce) cans crabmeat, drained

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Coat an 8x8 inch baking dish with nonstick cooking spray.
3. Place shrimp in the work bowl of a food processor; pulse until shrimp forms a thick paste.
4. Mix eggs, mayonnaise, mustard, hot pepper sauce, seafood seasoning, dried tarragon, and salt and pepper in a large bowl.
5. Stir in shrimp; carefully fold in crab.
6. Spoon into prepared baking dish.
7. Bake in preheated oven until lightly browned and heated through, about 30 minutes.
