## Ingredients

- 1 ½ tablespoons olive oil
- 6  chicken tenders, cut in 1-inch pieces
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- 3 tablespoons cayenne pepper sauce (such as Frank's® RedHot®)
- 1 tablespoon butter
- ½ head lettuce, chopped, or to taste
- ½ cup chopped red cabbage
- 1  tomato, diced
- 1  avocado, diced
- ⅓  red onion, diced
- 4 tablespoons ranch dressing, or to taste

## Instructions

1. Heat olive oil in a pan on medium heat.
2. Season chicken tenders with salt and pepper.
3. Place in the hot oil and cook until golden brown, 4 to 5 minutes per side.
4. Remove chicken to a bowl and set aside.
5. Add cayenne pepper sauce and butter to the pan.
6. Cook and stir until thickened, about 1 minute.
7. Pour over the chicken.
8. Mix well until all chicken pieces are well covered.
9. Divide lettuce, cabbage, tomato, avocado, and red onion between 2 bowls.
10. Place chicken on top.
11. Drizzle with ranch dressing.
