## Ingredients

- 1 (8 ounce) package uncooked egg noodles
- ½ cup sour cream
- 3 tablespoons milk
- 1 pinch garlic powder to taste
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- 1 (15 ounce) can peas, drained
- 1 (5 ounce) can tuna, drained

## Instructions

1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
2. Add the egg noodles and cook until tender, about 6 minutes.
3. Drain.
4. Mix sour cream, milk, garlic, and salt together in a saucepan.
5. Stir in drained noodles along with peas and tuna.
6. Cook over low heat until heated through, about 15 minutes.
