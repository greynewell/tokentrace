## Ingredients

- 4  jalapeno peppers, seeded and minced
- 3 tablespoons prepared horseradish
- ¼ cup roasted garlic
- 1 cup mayonnaise
- 4 (6 ounce) salmon fillets

## Instructions

1. In the container of a food processor, combine the jalapeno peppers, horseradish, garlic and mayonnaise.
2. Set aside 3/4 of the mixture in the refrigerator.
3. Brush or spread the remaining 1/4 onto the salmon fillets.
4. Cover and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes to marinate.
5. Preheat a grill for medium-high heat.
6. When hot, lightly oil the grate.
7. Grill the salmon fillets until they are able to be flaked with a fork, about 7 minutes per side.
8. Baste occasionally with the remaining marinade.
