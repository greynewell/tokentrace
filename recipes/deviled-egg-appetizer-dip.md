## Ingredients

- 12  eggs
- 1 cup mayonnaise
- 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
- 2 tablespoons white wine vinegar
- 1 teaspoon red hot sauce

## Instructions

1. Place eggs in a saucepan and cover with water.
2. Bring to a boil; remove saucepan from heat and let eggs cook in hot water off the heat for 12 minutes.
3. Pour off hot water and place eggs in a bowl full of ice water to cool.
4. Peel and halve eggs.
5. Chop about half the egg whites and place into a bowl.
6. Put remaining egg whites and yolks in a food processor with mayonnaise, Dijon mustard, white wine vinegar, and hot sauce; process till smooth and transfer to a bowl.
7. Fold chopped egg whites into the mixture to serve.
