## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 4 (4 ounce) fillets salmon
- 4 ounces Brie cheese, sliced
- 1 teaspoon butter
- 2  mangos - peeled, seeded, and diced

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Heat the olive oil in a large oven-proof skillet over medium high heat, and sear the salmon about 4 minutes on each side.
3. Top salmon with Brie cheese.
4. Cover skillet, and place in the preheated oven.
5. Bake 15 minutes, or until the salmon is easily flaked with a fork.
6. In a saucepan over medium heat, melt the butter and simmer the mangos 15 minutes, or until soft and smooth.
7. Serve over the cooked salmon.
