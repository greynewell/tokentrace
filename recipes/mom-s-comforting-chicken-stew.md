## Ingredients

- 2  potato, diced
- 2  carrots, diced
- ½ cup green peas
- ¼ cup butter
- 1 cup diced boneless chicken
- 1 clove garlic, or more to taste, minced
- 10  mushrooms, sliced
- 1  green onion, chopped
- 1 tablespoon all-purpose flour
- 1 cup chicken stock
- ½ cup heavy whipping cream
- ½ teaspoon mustard powder
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- 2 tablespoons chopped cilantro

## Instructions

1. Bring a saucepan of water to a boil.
2. Add potatoes and carrots; cook until softened, about 8 minutes.
3. Add peas; cook until tender, about 2 minutes.
4. Drain.
5. Heat butter in a heavy skillet until melted.
6. Add chicken and garlic; cook and stir until lightly browned, about 5 minutes.
7. Stir in mushrooms and green onion; cook until softened, about 2 minutes.
8. Stir in flour and cook for 1 minute.
9. Stir chicken stock, heavy cream, mustard powder, salt, and pepper into the skillet.
10. Simmer until thickened, about 5 minutes.
11. Fold in potatoes, carrots, and peas; cook until warmed through, about 2 minutes.
12. Remove stew from heat and garnish with cilantro.
