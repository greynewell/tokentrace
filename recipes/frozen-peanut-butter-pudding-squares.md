## Ingredients

- 1 ½ cups cold milk
- 1 (3.9 ounce) package instant chocolate pudding mix
- 1 cup peanut butter, melted
- 16  whole graham crackers, broken into two square halves

## Instructions

1. Pour milk into a bowl; add pudding mix and beat continually until the powder is completely dissolved and the mixture begins to thicken, about 2 minutes.
2. Beat melted peanut butter into the pudding mixture.
3. Chill pudding mixture in refrigerator until slightly thickened, about 3 minutes.
4. Spread about 1 heaping tablespoon pudding mix onto each graham cracker square; sandwich squares together to get 16 pudding-filled squares.
5. Arrange onto a baking sheet and cover lightly with plastic wrap.
6. Chill in freezer until the pudding is firm, at least 1 hour.
7. Store in a resealable plastic bag in the freezer.
