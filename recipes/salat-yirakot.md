## Ingredients

- 2  tomatoes, diced
- 2  green bell peppers, diced
- 1  red bell pepper, diced, or more to taste
- ½  cucumber, diced
- ½ cup olive oil
- ½  red onion, diced
- 2  scallions, chopped
- 2 tablespoons lemon juice
- 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley, or to taste
- 1 tablespoon chopped fresh mint, or to taste
- 1 tablespoon chopped fresh dill, or to taste
- 2 teaspoons vinegar
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- ½ teaspoon ground sumac
- salt and ground black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Combine tomatoes, green bell peppers, red bell pepper, cucumber, olive oil, red onion, scallions, lemon juice, parsley, mint, dill, vinegar, garlic, sumac, salt, and pepper in a large bowl; mix thoroughly.
2. Chill tomato mixture until flavors combine, about 25 minutes.
