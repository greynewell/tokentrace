## Ingredients

- 8 cups spelt flour
- ½ cup sesame seeds
- ½ teaspoon salt, or to taste
- 1 tablespoon blackstrap molasses
- 2 teaspoons baking soda
- 4 ¼ cups milk

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease two 9x5-inch loaf pans.
2. Combine spelt flour, milk, sesame seeds, molasses, baking soda, and salt in a large bowl until well blended; divide batter evenly between the prepared pans.
3. Bake in the preheated oven until golden, about 1 hour and 10 minutes.
