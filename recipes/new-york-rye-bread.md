## Ingredients

- 1 ⅛ cups warm water
- 1 ⅓ tablespoons vegetable oil
- 2 tablespoons honey
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 2 ⅔ teaspoons caraway seed
- 1 ⅓ cups rye flour
- 2 ⅓ cups bread flour
- ¼ cup vital wheat gluten
- ¼ cup dry milk powder
- 2 ½ teaspoons active dry yeast

## Instructions

1. Place all ingredients in to your bread machine in the order specified by your machine's manual.
2. Set the cycle to Basic or White.
