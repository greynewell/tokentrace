## Ingredients

- 10 (9 inch)  flour tortillas
- 1 (8 ounce) package whipped cream cheese
- 1 pound shaved deli ham
- 1 (16 ounce) bottle ranch dressing
- 1 (8 ounce) package shredded carrots
- 1 (8 ounce) package shredded Cheddar cheese
- 2 cups shredded lettuce

## Instructions

1. Spread each tortilla with a thin layer of cream cheese.
2. Lay shaved ham thinly over cream cheese layer.
3. Spread a thin layer of ranch dressing on ham.
4. Sprinkle lightly with carrots, followed by Cheddar cheese.
5. Top with a light layer of lettuce.
6. Fold side of tortilla shell towards the center.
7. Roll up unfolded sides.
8. Secure with a toothpick if necessary.
