## Ingredients

- 1 large cucumber, halved lengthwise and thinly sliced crosswise
- 2 medium tomatoes, diced
- 1 small onion, thinly sliced
- 2 cups cubed seedless watermelon
- 3 tablespoons red wine vinegar
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 1 teaspoon sea salt
- 1 teaspoon white sugar
- ½ teaspoon dried oregano
- ½ teaspoon dried basil
- ½ teaspoon ground thyme
- ½ teaspoon ground black pepper

## Instructions

1. Combine cucumber, tomatoes, onion, and watermelon in a large bowl.
2. Mix vinegar, olive oil, salt, sugar, oregano, basil, thyme, and pepper together in a separate, smaller bowl and pour over salad. Stir to coat well.
3. Cover and refrigerate for 20 minutes. Serve chilled.
