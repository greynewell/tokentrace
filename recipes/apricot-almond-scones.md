## Ingredients

- Crisco® Original No-Stick Cooking Spray
- 2 ½ cups Martha White® Self-Rising Flour
- ⅓ cup sugar
- ½ cup butter, cut into pieces
- 1 (7 ounce) package dried apricots, chopped
- 1  large egg, beaten
- ¾ cup buttermilk, or as needed
- ¼ teaspoon almond extract
- 2 tablespoons butter, melted

## Instructions

1. Heat oven to 400 degrees F.
2. Spray a large cookie sheet with no-stick cooking spray.
3. Combine flour and sugar in large bowl; mix well.
4. Cut in 1/2 cup butter with pastry blender or fork until mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
5. Stir in apricots.
6. Beat egg in 1-cup measuring cup.
7. Add enough buttermilk to egg to make 1 cup.
8. Stir in almond extract.
9. Add buttermilk mixture to flour mixture; stir gently until soft dough forms.
10. Drop dough by 1/4 cupfuls about 2 inches apart onto prepared cookie sheet.
11. Bake 20 to 25 minutes or until light golden brown.
12. Brush scones with 2 tablespoons melted butter.
13. Serve warm or cool.
