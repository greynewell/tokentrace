## Ingredients

- 1 (8 ounce) package refrigerated crescent rolls
- 4 slices mozzarella cheese
- 4 teaspoons tomato paste
- ½ teaspoon dried oregano

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Unwind the roll dough, and separate into 4 rectangles.
3. Pinch together perforations to seal.
4. Place 2 rectangles on a 9x13 inch baking sheet.
5. Moisten edges of rectangles with water.
6. Place 2 slices mozzarella on each rectangle.
7. Top each with 2 teaspoons tomato paste.
8. Sprinkle each with 1/4 teaspoon oregano.
9. Cover each with remaining dough.
10. Press the edges firmly with a fork to seal.
11. Bake in the preheated oven 10 to 12 minutes, or until golden brown.
