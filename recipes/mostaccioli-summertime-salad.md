## Ingredients

- 1 (16 ounce) package mostaccioli pasta
- 1 cup olive oil
- 1 cup white vinegar
- 1 cup white sugar
- 1 (2 ounce) jar sliced pimento peppers
- 1 tablespoon parsley flakes
- 1 ½ teaspoons ground black pepper
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon garlic salt
- 1 bunch green onions, thinly sliced
- 1 medium cucumber, peeled and diced

## Instructions

1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook, stirring occasionally, until tender yet firm to the bite, about 11 minutes.
2. While pasta is cooking, mix oil, vinegar, sugar, pimento peppers, parsley, pepper, salt, and garlic salt together in a bowl. Stir in green onions and cucumber.
3. Drain pasta. Pour dressing mixture over pasta and mix well. Refrigerate for at least 4 hours, or overnight, before serving.
