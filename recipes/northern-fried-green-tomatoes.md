## Ingredients

- 1 cup panko bread crumbs
- ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
- 1 teaspoon garlic powder
- 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
- 2  eggs
- 1 cup all-purpose flour
- 1 cup vegetable oil for frying, or as needed
- 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
- 2 large green tomatoes, cut into 1/4-inch slices
- salt to taste

## Instructions

1. Combine panko bread crumbs, Parmesan cheese, garlic powder, and black pepper together in a bowl.
2. Beat eggs in a separate bowl.
3. Pour flour into a third bowl.
4. Pour in enough vegetable oil to measure 1/2-inch in a large skillet; add olive oil.
5. Heat oil mixture to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
6. Dredge each tomato slice in egg; coat with flour.
7. Dredge through egg again and press into panko mixture until evenly coated.
8. Fry coated tomato slices in the hot oil until golden brown, 2 to 3 minutes per side.
9. Remove fried green tomatoes with a slotted spoon and transfer to a paper plate or paper towel-lined plate.
10. Sprinkle with salt.
