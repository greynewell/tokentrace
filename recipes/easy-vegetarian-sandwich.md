## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon almond butter, or to taste
- 2 slices whole-grain bread
- ½  Granny Smith apple, cored and thinly sliced
- ¼ cup shredded carrots
- ¼ cup shredded Cheddar cheese

## Instructions

1. Spread almond butter onto 1 side of each bread slice.
2. Arrange apples neatly over almond butter on 1 slice of bread; top with carrots and Cheddar cheese.
3. Place second piece of bread on top to make a sandwich.
