## Ingredients

- 6 cups fresh spinach, torn into bite-size pieces
- 1 cup thickly sliced strawberries
- 1 cup blueberries, trimmed
- 1 small red onion, thinly sliced
- Â½ cup chopped pecans
- 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
- 2 tablespoons rice vinegar
- 4 teaspoons honey
- 1 teaspoon curry powder
- 2 teaspoons Dijon mustard
- 1 pinch Salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Wash and dry spinach.
2. Whip together dressing ingredients.
3. Add to spinach and toss lightly.
4. Add berries, onion and pecans.
5. Toss lightly and serve.
