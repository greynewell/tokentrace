## Ingredients

- 2 pounds jicama, peeled and julienned
- ¼ cup chopped cilantro leaves
- salt and pepper to taste
- ¼ cup lime juice

## Instructions

1. Combine the julienned jicama, chopped cilantro, and salt and pepper in a large bowl.
2. Sprinkle with lime juice and toss to coat.
3. Refrigerate until ready to serve.
