## Ingredients

- ½ cup milk
- 1  egg
- 3 tablespoons olive oil
- 1 cup all-purpose flour
- ¼ cup white sugar
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- ½ cup blackberries

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Line a muffin tin with paper liners.
2. Mix milk, egg, and olive oil together in a bowl. Add flour, sugar, and baking powder; mix well. Fold blackberries into the batter.
3. Spoon batter into the muffin tin, filling each cup 1/2 way.
4. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean, about 20 minutes.
