## Ingredients

- 1 loaf ciabatta bread, vertically cut into 4 equal pieces
- 3 tablespoons canola oil
- ¼ large white onion, thinly sliced
- 1 (8 ounce) package sliced fresh mushrooms
- 1 (8 ounce) package sweet pea shoots, chopped into 2-inch pieces
- 4 slices Muenster cheese
- 12 thin slices  deli ham

## Instructions

1. Slice each of the 4 pieces of ciabatta bread horizontally in half.
2. Heat a skillet over medium-low heat; add oil.
3. Cook and stir onion in the hot oil over low heat until onion begins to soften and lightly brown, 10 to 15 minutes.
4. Add mushrooms; cook and stir until tender and browned, 5 to 10 minutes more.
5. Add pea shoots and cook until wilted, 2 to 3 minutes.
6. Remove skillet from heat.
7. Open the ciabatta pieces, creating 4 sandwiches.
8. Evenly distribute the pea shoot mixture among the bottom pieces of each sandwich; add 1 slice Muenster cheese and 3 slices deli ham.
9. Replace the top pieces of ciabatta to make a sandwich.
