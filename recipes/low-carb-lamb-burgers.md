## Ingredients

- 1 pound ground lamb
- 1 tablespoon soy sauce
- ⅓ cup chopped onion
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- ⅛ cup avocado oil
- ⅓ cup crumbled feta cheese  (Optional)

## Instructions

1. Put lamb, soy sauce, and onion in a bowl and season with salt and pepper to taste.
2. Mix with hands to combine.
3. Form lamb mixture into 4 patties.
4. Heat avocado oil in a skillet over medium heat.
5. Cook lamb patties until browned, 5 to 8 minutes on each side.
6. An instant-read meat thermometer, inserted into the center of a patty, should read at least 145 degrees F (65 degrees C).
7. Sprinkle with feta cheese and serve.
