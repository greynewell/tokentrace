## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- ¼ cup balsamic vinegar
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- 4  portobello mushroom caps
- ⅓ cup mayonnaise
- 2 tablespoons orange juice
- 1 (12 ounce) jar roasted red bell peppers
- 4 rolls sourdough bread
- 4 slices smoked Gouda cheese
- 1 (10 ounce) bag mixed salad greens

## Instructions

1. Whisk together olive oil, balsamic vinegar, and garlic until well blended.
2. Pour over mushroom caps in a resealable bag to coat, then seal and allow to marinate for 30 minutes.
3. Stir together mayonnaise and orange juice, and set aside.
4. Preheat an outdoor grill for medium heat.
5. Remove mushroom caps from the marinade and shake off excess.
6. Place each mushroom cap upside down on a square piece of foil.
7. Place roasted peppers on top, and seal.
8. Cook on preheated grill until tender, about 15 minutes, turning occasionally.
9. When the mushrooms are nearly done, cut open the sourdough rolls and grill until golden brown on the cut sides.
10. To assemble the sandwiches, spread the cut sides of the rolls with orange mayonnaise, and layer with mushroom, roasted pepper, Gouda cheese, and the mixed greens.
