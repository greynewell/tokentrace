## Ingredients

- 1 large head kale, stemmed, leaves cut into bite-size pieces
- ½ cup balsamic vinegar
- ⅓ cup extra-virgin olive oil
- ½  lemon, juiced
- ¼ cup slivered raw almonds
- coarse sea salt to taste

## Instructions

1. Place kale in a large bowl.
2. Drizzle balsamic vinegar, olive oil, and lemon juice over the top; toss to coat.
3. Add almonds and salt; toss to combine.
4. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate until flavors combine, 15 to 30 minutes.
