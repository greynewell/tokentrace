## Ingredients

- 1 ½ cups chicken broth
- 2 tablespoons lemon juice
- 2 teaspoons prepared mustard
- 1 pinch garlic powder
- black pepper to taste
- 2 tablespoons cornstarch
- ¼ cup water

## Instructions

1. Pour broth and lemon juice into a saucepan, and set over high heat.
2. Stir in mustard, garlic powder, and black pepper.
3. Bring to a boil.
4. In a small bowl, stir together cornstarch and water.
5. Pour into boiling mixture, and stir well.
6. Reduce heat to medium, and simmer until sauce thickens.
