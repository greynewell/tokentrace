## Ingredients

- 4 cups chicken broth
- 3 large Yukon Gold potatoes, peeled and cubed
- 3 large sweet potatoes, peeled and cubed
- 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
- 1 ½ cups chopped cooked ham
- 4  leeks, chopped
- 1 cup half-and-half
- salt and ground black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Bring chicken broth to a boil in a large Dutch oven.
2. Add Yukon gold and sweet potatoes, bring back to a boil, and turn heat to medium-low.
3. Simmer until tender, 10 to 20 minutes.
4. Heat vegetable oil in a skillet over medium-high heat.
5. Add ham; cook and stir for 2 minutes.
6. Add leeks; cook and stir until leeks are soft, about 5-10 minutes.
7. Do not allow the leeks to brown.
8. Stir leek and ham mixture into potatoes and broth.
9. Add half-and-half; season to taste with salt and ground black pepper.
