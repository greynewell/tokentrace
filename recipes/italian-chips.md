## Ingredients

- 12  wonton wrappers
- 1  egg white, beaten
- ½ teaspoon crumbled dried oregano
- ¾ cup freshly grated Parmigiano-Reggiano cheese

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
2. Prepare a baking sheet with cooking spray.
3. Arrange the wonton wrappers on the baking sheet in a single layer.
4. Brush each wrapper with egg white; sprinkle with oregano and Parmigiano-Reggiano cheese.
5. Cut each into two triangles using a pizza cutter.
6. Bake in the preheated oven until the edges are brown, 6 to 7 minutes.
7. Transfer to a cooling rack to cool completely.
8. Store in an airtight container up to 2 days.
