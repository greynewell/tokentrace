## Ingredients

- 1 (16 ounce) box linguine pasta
- ¾ cup sweet cream salted butter
- ½ cup diced sweet onion (such as Vidalia®)
- ¼ cup dry white wine
- 2 tablespoons lemon juice
- 2 tablespoons Dijon mustard
- 2 tablespoons minced garlic
- 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
- 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley
- 1 teaspoon salt
- ¾ teaspoon cayenne pepper  (Optional)
- ½ teaspoon black pepper
- 2 pounds uncooked medium shrimp, peeled and deveined
- ¾ cup shredded Asiago cheese

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C).
2. Bring a large pot of salted water to boil over high heat.
3. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
4. Cook linguine at a boil until tender yet firm to the bite, about 11 minutes.
5. Drain.
6. Melt butter in a saucepan over medium-high heat.
7. Add onion, white wine, lemon juice, Dijon mustard, garlic, Worcestershire sauce, parsley, salt, cayenne pepper, and black pepper; cook and stir until sauce is combined, 3 to 5 minutes.
8. Arrange shrimp in a flat layer in a shallow baking dish.
9. Pour the sauce over the shrimp and sprinkle Asiago cheese evenly over the top.
10. Bake in the preheated oven until the shrimp are pink, 12 to 15 minutes.
11. Serve shrimp and sauce over linguine.
12. Be sure to spoon equal portions of butter sauce over pasta.
