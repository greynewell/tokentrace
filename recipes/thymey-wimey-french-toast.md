## Ingredients

- ¼ cup melted butter
- ¼ cup milk
- 2 thick slices crusty bread
- 2 teaspoons dried thyme leaves
- ⅓ cup shredded sharp Cheddar cheese
- salt and cracked black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
2. Whisk melted butter and milk together with a fork.
3. Soak the bread slices in the milk mixture, turning to cover both sides completely.
4. Sprinkle half of the thyme over one side of the bread pieces.
5. Heat a dry skillet over medium-low heat, and fry the bread, thyme-side down, until lightly browned, about 5 minutes.
6. Sprinkle the remaining thyme over the bread; turn over and fry until lightly browned, about 5 minutes more.
7. Transfer to a baking sheet; sprinkle with the Cheddar cheese.
8. Bake in the preheated oven until the cheese is bubbly, about 10 minutes.
9. Remove and serve immediately with salt and cracked pepper.
