## Ingredients

- 2 cups cauliflower florets
- ⅓ cup olive oil
- ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
- 1 teaspoon lemon juice
- 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
- 1 teaspoon cayenne pepper
- 2 slices whole-grain bread
- 1 tablespoon light mayonnaise  (Optional)
- 1  avocado, thinly sliced

## Instructions

1. Place cauliflower in a skillet over medium heat; drizzle with olive oil.
2. Cook and stir until light golden, 5 to 7 minutes.
3. Pour cooked cauliflower into a blender with Parmesan cheese, lemon juice, and black pepper.
4. Blend until the mixture resembles mashed potatoes, 10 to 15 seconds.
5. Toast bread, about 2 minutes, and spread light mayonnaise equally on each slice.
6. Spread cauliflower mash equally over the toast.
7. Place avocado slices over the cauliflower.
