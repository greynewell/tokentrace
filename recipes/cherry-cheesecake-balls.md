## Ingredients

- 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
- ½ cup white sugar
- 2 teaspoons butter, at room temperature
- ½ teaspoon vanilla extract
- ½ cup flaked coconut
- 8  maraschino cherries, chopped, or more to taste
- ½ cup graham cracker crumbs

## Instructions

1. Beat cream cheese, sugar, butter, and vanilla extract together in a bowl using an electric mixer. Beat in coconut and cherries.
2. Drop batter by teaspoonfuls into graham cracker crumbs and form into balls; roll again and place on wax paper-lined baking sheets.
3. Refrigerate for 2 hours before serving.
