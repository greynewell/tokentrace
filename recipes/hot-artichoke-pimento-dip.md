## Ingredients

- 2 (8 ounce) cans artichoke hearts, drained and chopped
- 2 (4 ounce) cans green chile peppers, drained and chopped
- 1 (4 ounce) jar pimento peppers, drained and chopped
- 1 ½ cups mayonnaise
- ½ (8 ounce) package shredded Monterey Jack cheese
- ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese, divided

## Instructions

1. In an oven safe bowl, combine artichoke hearts, chile peppers, pimento peppers, mayonnaise, Monterey Jack cheese and almost all of the Parmesan cheese; sprinkle remaining Parmesan cheese on top.
2. Refrigerate for 1 hour.
3. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
4. Bake in preheated oven for 30 minutes, or until lightly browned.
