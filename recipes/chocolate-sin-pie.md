## Ingredients

- cooking spray (such as Pam®)
- 1 ½ cups self-rising flour
- 1 cup chopped pecans
- ¾ cup butter, melted
- 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
- 1 cup frozen whipped topping (such as Cool Whip®), thawed
- 1 cup confectioners' sugar
- 3 cups milk
- 1 (5.9 ounce) package instant chocolate pudding mix
- 1 (12 ounce) container frozen whipped topping (such as Cool Whip®), thawed

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). Spray a 9x13-inch baking dish with cooking spray.
2. Stir flour, pecans, and butter together in a bowl; press into the bottom of the prepared baking dish.
3. Bake in the preheated oven until crust is golden and crisp, about 15 minutes. Cool completely.
4. Stir cream cheese, 1 cup whipped topping, and confectioners' sugar together in a bowl until smooth; spread over cooled crust.
5. Whisk milk and pudding together in a bowl until slightly thickened, about 2 minutes. Let sit until pudding is set, about 5 minutes. Spread pudding mixture over cream cheese layer. Top cream cheese layer with 12 ounces whipped topping. Refrigerate until flavors blend and pie is set, about 24 hours.
