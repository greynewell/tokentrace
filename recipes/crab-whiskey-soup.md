## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 4  shallots, minced
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 (32 fluid ounce) container chicken broth
- 1 cup milk
- 3 ½ tablespoons all-purpose flour
- ¼ teaspoon ground cayenne pepper
- salt and pepper to taste
- 1 ½ pounds cooked crabmeat, flaked
- ⅓ cup whiskey

## Instructions

1. Heat the olive oil in a large pot over medium heat, and saute the shallots and garlic until tender.
2. Pour in the chicken broth, and bring to a boil.
3. In a small bowl, mix the milk and flour.
4. Stir the mixture into the pot, and continue cooking until soup is slightly thickened.
5. Season the soup with cayenne pepper, salt, and pepper.
6. Mix in the crab and whiskey, and cook until heated through.
