## Ingredients

- 2 cups milk
- ½ cup white sugar
- 3 tablespoons cornstarch
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 1 tablespoon butter

## Instructions

1. Heat milk in a medium saucepan over medium heat until bubbles form at the edges.
2. Mix sugar, cornstarch, and salt together in a small bowl; add to hot milk, a little at a time, stirring until thick enough to coat the back of the spoon. Remove saucepan from heat, and stir in vanilla and butter.
3. Spoon pudding evenly into 5 serving dishes; chill in the refrigerator before serving.
