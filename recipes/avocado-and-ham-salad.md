## Ingredients

- 1 cup shredded Romaine lettuce
- ½ cup mixed salad greens
- ½ cup arugula
- ½ cup cooked mixed beans, drained
- ½ cup chopped cucumber
- ⅓ cup chopped sweet red pepper
- 3  radishes, sliced
- 1  tomato, sliced
- 1 small avocado, peeled, pitted, and chopped
- 1 slice ham, chopped

## Instructions

1. Toss Romaine lettuce, mixed salad greens, arugula, mixed beans, cucumber, sweet red pepper, radishes, tomato, avocado, and ham together in a large bowl.
2. Serve cold.
