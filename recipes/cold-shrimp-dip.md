## Ingredients

- 1 (16 ounce) container sour cream
- 1 (.7 ounce) package dry Italian-style salad dressing mix
- ½ pound cooked baby shrimp

## Instructions

1. In a medium bowl, mix sour cream, dry Italian-style salad dressing mix and baby shrimp.
2. Chill in the refrigerator until serving.
