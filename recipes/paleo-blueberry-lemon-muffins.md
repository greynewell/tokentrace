## Ingredients

- 3  egg, at room temperature
- ½ cup coconut oil, melted
- ¼ cup coconut sugar
- 1  lemon, zested
- 1 teaspoon lemon extract
- ¾ teaspoon sea salt
- ½ teaspoon baking soda
- ¼ teaspoon baking powder
- 1 ½ cups almond meal
- 1 cup fresh blueberries
- ½ cup coconut butter, melted
- ½ cup raw honey
- lemon, juiced

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Line muffin cups with paper liners.
3. Whisk eggs, coconut oil, coconut sugar, lemon zest, and lemon extract together in a bowl.
4. Sift salt, baking soda, and baking powder together in a separate bowl; stir in almond meal using a rubber spatula.
5. Mix egg mixture into almond meal mixture until batter is smooth; fold in blueberries.
6. Scoop batter using an ice cream scooper into prepared muffin cups, filling 3/4-full.
7. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean, 30 to 35 minutes.
8. Cool.
9. Mix coconut butter, honey, and lemon juice together in a bowl until smooth; drizzle over muffins.
