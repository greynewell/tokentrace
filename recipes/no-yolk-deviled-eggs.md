## Ingredients

- 6  eggs
- 1 medium potato, peeled and diced
- 1 tablespoon sweet pickle relish
- 1 teaspoon sugar
- 1 tablespoon mayonnaise
- 1 teaspoon prepared yellow mustard, or to taste
- 1 pinch paprika, for garnish

## Instructions

1. Place the eggs in a pan of cold water and bring to a boil.
2. Once they boil, remove from the heat, cover and let stand for 10 minutes.
3. Drain, cool and peel.
4. Place the potato in a saucepan with enough water to cover.
5. Bring to a boil, and cook until tender.
6. Drain.
7. Cut the eggs in half lengthwise, and remove the yolks; reserve for other uses or discard.
8. Place the potatoes in a medium bowl.
9. Mash with a whisk until smooth, then mix in the relish, sugar, mayonnaise and mustard.
10. Spoon into the egg whites, and dust with paprika.
11. Refrigerate or serve.
