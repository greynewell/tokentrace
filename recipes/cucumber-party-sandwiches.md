## Ingredients

- 1  cucumber - peeled, seeded, and minced
- kosher salt to taste
- 8 ounces cream cheese, softened
- ¼ teaspoon seasoned salt
- ¼ teaspoon ground cumin
- 1 (1 pound) loaf sliced bread

## Instructions

1. Place cucumber into a strainer and sprinkle with kosher salt; allow cucumber to drain for about 20 minutes.
2. Transfer cucumber to a piece of cheesecloth and squeeze dry.
3. Mash cream cheese with seasoned salt and cumin in a bowl.
4. Stir cucumber into cream cheese mixture.
5. Spread the cucumber mixture on a slice of bread; top with a second slice.
6. Press the slices together lightly and trim off the crusts.
7. Slice each sandwich into 4 triangles by cutting an X shape.
8. Chill sandwiches until serving time.
