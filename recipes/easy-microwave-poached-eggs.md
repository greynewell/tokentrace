## Ingredients

- ¼ cup cold water
- 1 tablespoon white vinegar
- 2  cold large eggs

## Instructions

1. Pour water into a microwave-safe cup or bowl; add vinegar. Cracks eggs into the cup.
2. Cook eggs in the microwave for 1 minute 20 seconds. Drain.
