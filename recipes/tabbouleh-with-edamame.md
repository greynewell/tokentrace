## Ingredients

- 1 cup bulgur
- 1 cup boiling water
- 1 (1 pound) package frozen shelled edamame
- 2 cups halved cherry tomatoes
- 2 cups chopped fresh parsley
- 1  cucumber, seeded and chopped
- 1 bunch green onions, thinly sliced
- ¼ cup olive oil, or as needed
- 2  large lemons, juiced
- 2 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
- salt and ground black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Stir bulgur and boiling water together in a pot; cover and remove from heat.
2. Set aside until water is absorbed, about 1 hour.
3. Bring a separate pot of water to a boil; cook edamame in the boiling water until tender, about 5 minutes.
4. Drain.
5. Mix cooked bulgur, edamame, tomatoes, parsley, cucumber, and green onions together in a large bowl.
6. Whisk olive oil, lemon juice, and vinegar in a separate bowl; pour over bulgur mixture.
7. Season with salt and pepper; toss to coat.
8. Cover with plastic wrap and refrigerate to blend flavors, at least 6 hours.
