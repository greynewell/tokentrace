## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon butter
- ⅓ cup blanched almond flour
- 1  egg
- ½ teaspoon baking powder
- 1 pinch salt

## Instructions

1. Microwave butter in a microwave-safe mug until melted, about 15 seconds. Swirl the mug to coat with butter.
2. Add almond flour, egg, baking powder, and salt to the mug; whisk until smooth.
3. Microwave on high until set, about 90 seconds. Let cool for 2 minutes before slicing.
