## Ingredients

- 1 (3 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
- ½ pound imitation crabmeat
- 2  green onions, chopped
- 12 small (4 inch) pita breads, halved

## Instructions

1. Place cream cheese, crab meat, and green onions into the bowl of a food processor.
2. Process until smooth.
3. Spoon 1 tablespoon of the crab mixture into each pita half.
4. Serve immediately.
