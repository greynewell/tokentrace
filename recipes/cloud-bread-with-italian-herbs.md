## Ingredients

- 2  eggs, separated
- ¼ teaspoon baking powder
- 2 tablespoons cream cheese, softened
- 2 teaspoons Italian herb blend
- 1 pinch salt

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 300 degrees F (150 degrees C).
2. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
3. Beat egg whites in a glass, metal, or ceramic bowl until foamy.
4. Add baking powder and continue to beat until stiff peaks form.
5. Whisk egg yolks, cream cheese, Italian herbs, and salt in a separate bowl until well blended.
6. Gently fold cream cheese mixture into the beaten egg whites with a spatula until just mixed and yellow.
7. Spoon mixture into about 6 rounds of about 3 inches diameter on the baking sheet.
8. Smooth the tops with the back of a spoon.
9. Bake in the preheated oven until golden brown, about 25 minutes.
10. Remove from the oven and serve warm, or allow to cool.
