## Ingredients

- 1  egg, beaten
- ½ cup white sugar
- ⅓ cup white vinegar
- 4 ½ teaspoons dry mustard

## Instructions

1. Beat egg and sugar in a saucepan over medium heat.
2. Mix vinegar and mustard in a small bowl; slowly stir into the egg mixture.
3. Cook and stir the mixture until hot.
