## Ingredients

- ½ cup coconut milk
- ½ cup canola oil
- ⅓ cup red wine vinegar
- 1 teaspoon ground mustard
- 1 teaspoon garlic powder
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 5 cups chopped lettuce
- 3 cups cubed cooked chicken
- 2 cups seedless red grapes, halved
- ¾ cup macadamia nuts, chopped
- ¾ cup chopped celery
- ½ cup shredded coconut

## Instructions

1. Blend coconut milk, oil, vinegar, mustard, garlic powder, and salt together in an electric blender.
2. Mix lettuce, chicken, grapes, macadamia nuts, celery, and coconut together in a large bowl.
3. Pour dressing over salad and toss to coat.
