## Ingredients

- 1 ⅓ cups whole wheat flour
- 1 ⅓ cups all-purpose flour
- ¼ cup vegetable oil
- 1 teaspoon baking soda
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ¾ cup buttermilk, room temperature

## Instructions

1. In a large bowl combine the whole wheat flour, all-purpose flour, oil, baking soda, and salt.
2. Add enough buttermilk to make a stiff dough.
3. Knead dough for 30 seconds on a well-floured surface.
4. Cover dough to prevent drying.
5. Roll 1/4 cup handfuls of dough into balls and pat into a flat circle.
6. Using a rolling pin, flatten dough into 10 inch circles.
7. Place onto ungreased cookie sheet.
8. Score pieces into pie shapes by running a knife along but not through dough.
9. Repeat process with remaining dough.
10. Bake in a preheated 350 degree F (175 degrees C) oven for 8 to 10 minutes.
11. Cool on wire rack briefly before breaking along scored lines.
