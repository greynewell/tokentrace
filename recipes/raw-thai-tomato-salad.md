## Ingredients

- 1  tomato, sliced
- 2 tablespoons pine nuts
- 10  almonds, or more to taste
- 1 tablespoon dried goji berries
- 1 tablespoon Thai peanut sauce, or more to taste
- 1 teaspoon black sesame seeds

## Instructions

1. Arrange tomato slices on a plate.
2. Mix pine nuts, almonds, goji berries, peanut sauce, and sesame seeds in a bowl; spoon over tomatoes.
