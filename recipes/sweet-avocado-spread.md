## Ingredients

- 2 medium (blank)s ripe avocados from Mexico, peeled and pitted
- ⅛ teaspoon ground cinnamon
- 1 tablespoon low-fat cream cheese, softened
- 1 ½ tablespoons honey
- 4 slices cinnamon bread

## Instructions

1. Combine avocados, cinnamon, cream cheese and honey in a blender; blend until smooth.
2. Refrigerate for at least 1 hour before serving.
3. Spread on cinnamon bread, or any bread or bagel of your choice.
