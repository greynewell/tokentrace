## Ingredients

- 1  sourdough baguette
- 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, at room temperature
- 25 large fresh basil leaves, washed and dried
- 25 thin slices  capicola

## Instructions

1. Slice baguette on the diagonal into 25 slices about 1 inch thick.
2. Spread slices generously with cream cheese.
3. Place a single basil leaf on top of the cream cheese and press down slightly so basil adheres to cheese.
4. Top with a thin slice of capicola.
