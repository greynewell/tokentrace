## Ingredients

- 1 (14 ounce) package egg roll wrappers
- 24 (1 ounce) pieces string cheese
- 25 (4 ounce) packages sliced pepperoni sausage, cut into strips
- 1 (6 ounce) can sliced mushrooms, drained
- 2 tablespoons minced garlic
- 2 cups oil for frying, or as needed
- 1 (14 ounce) jar marinara sauce

## Instructions

1. Lay out the egg roll wrappers one at a time.
2. Place one piece of string cheese in the center near the edge, then arrange some pepperoni, sliced mushrooms and garlic around the cheese.
3. Roll up the string cheese a little, fold in the sides, then continue to roll up, and seal the edge by moistening with water.
4. Repeat with remaining wrappers, cheese and fillings.
5. Heat oil in a large heavy skillet, or deep fryer to 365 degrees F (185 degrees C).
6. Fry enough to fit loosely in the pan, turning once after about 5 minutes.
7. Continue to fry for 3 to 5 more minutes or until golden brown.
8. Drain on paper towels.
9. Warm the marinara sauce in a serving bowl in the microwave.
10. Serve Pizza Stix on a platter with the bowl of sauce for dipping.
