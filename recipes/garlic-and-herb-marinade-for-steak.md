## Ingredients

- ½ cup Worcestershire sauce
- ¼ cup olive oil
- ¼ cup apple cider vinegar
- 2 tablespoons minced garlic
- 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
- 2 tablespoons chopped fresh oregano
- ¼ teaspoon salt

## Instructions

1. Mix Worcestershire sauce, oil, vinegar, garlic, parsley, oregano, and salt together in a bowl.
2. Reserve 1/4 cup in the refrigerator.
3. Place steak(s) in the remaining marinade and refrigerate 3 to 24 hours; discard used marinade when ready to cook.
4. Brush reserved marinade onto steaks while cooking.
