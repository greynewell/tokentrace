## Ingredients

- 1 (17.5 ounce) pouch sugar cookie mix
- ¾ cup all-purpose flour
- ½ cup butter
- 1  egg
- ⅓ cup white sugar
- 34  white chocolate kisses with candy cane stripes (such as Hershey's®)

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
2. Combine cookie mix, flour, butter, and egg in a mixing bowl. Mix until well blended.
3. Pour sugar into a shallow dish. Roll dough into 1-inch balls; roll each in sugar. Place on ungreased cookie sheets.
4. Bake in the preheated oven until edges are set and golden, 10 to 12 minutes.
5. Remove cookies from the oven. Press a candy cane kiss in the center of each warm cookie. Let cool completely, 20 to 30 minutes.
