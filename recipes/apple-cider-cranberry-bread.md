## Ingredients

- cooking spray
- 2 cups all-purpose flour
- ¾ cup white sugar
- ¾ cup dried cranberries
- ¾ cup chopped walnuts  (Optional)
- 1 ½ teaspoons baking powder
- ½ teaspoon baking soda
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ¾ cup apple cider
- 2  eggs
- ⅛ cup vegetable oil

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Spray a 9x5-inch loaf pan with cooking spray.
2. Combine flour, sugar, cranberries, walnuts, baking powder, baking soda, and salt in a large bowl.
3. Beat apple cider, eggs, and vegetable oil in a separate bowl until frothy. Mix apple cider mixture into flour mixture until moist; pour into the prepared loaf pan.
4. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean, 40 to 45 minutes.
