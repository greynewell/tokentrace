## Ingredients

- 1 (8 ounce) package lowfat cream cheese, softened
- 1 (8 ounce) container lite sour cream
- 1 (8 ounce) container lite frozen whipped topping, thawed
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 3 (1 ounce) packages instant sugar-free vanilla pudding mix
- ½ packet artificial sweetener
- 5 cups nonfat milk
- 1 (11 ounce) package Cookies, vanilla wafers, lower fat
- 7  bananas, sliced

## Instructions

1. In a medium bowl, beat cream cheese with sour cream.
2. Stir in whipped topping and vanilla.
3. Set aside.
4. In a large bowl, combine pudding mix, sweetener and milk.
5. Stir until sugar and mix are dissolved.
6. Combine with cheese mixture.
7. In a large glass serving dish, layer pudding mixture, wafers and bananas until all ingredients are used.
8. Chill until serving.
