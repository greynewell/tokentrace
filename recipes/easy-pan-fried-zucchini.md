## Ingredients

- 1  zucchini, peeled and sliced
- 1 clove garlic, peeled
- 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
- 1  egg, beaten
- 2 tablespoons dry bread crumbs, or more as needed
- 2 tablespoons olive oil, or more as needed

## Instructions

1. Rub zucchini slices with garlic.
2. Place flour, egg, and bread crumbs separately in 3 small bowls. Turn zucchini slices in flour, dusting off excess. Dip in egg and turn in bread crumbs.
3. Heat oil in a skillet over medium-high heat and pan-fry breaded zucchini in batches on both sides until lightly browned, about 5 minutes.
