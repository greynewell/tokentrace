## Ingredients

- 2  organic onions, quartered with skin left on
- 4 cups water
- 1 teaspoon honey, or to taste

## Instructions

1. Place onions in a pot and cover with water; bring to a boil over medium heat. Reduce heat and simmer for about 1 hour.
2. Strain hot liquid through a sieve. Pour into a cup, sweeten to taste with honey, and drink as hot as possible.
