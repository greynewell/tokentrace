## Ingredients

- ½ cup shredded cabbage
- ½  carrot, shredded
- 1  egg
- ½ teaspoon soy sauce
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 2 slices bread, toasted

## Instructions

1. Mix the cabbage and carrot together in a bowl.
2. Stir the egg and soy sauce into the vegetables and mix until combined.
3. Heat the butter in a large skillet over medium heat.
4. Make a patty with the vegetable mixture the size of a slice of bread.
5. Place the patty into the skillet, and cook until browned on both sides, about 3 minutes per side.
6. Place the patty between two slices of toasted bread to serve.
