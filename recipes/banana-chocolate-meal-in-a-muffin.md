## Ingredients

- cooking spray
- 4  bananas, mashed
- ¼ cup brown sugar
- 2  eggs
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
- 2 ½ cups whole wheat flour
- ¼ cup cocoa powder
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
- ¼ cup chocolate chips
- ¼ cup chopped walnuts  (Optional)

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Spray a 12-cup muffin tin with cooking spray.
3. Mix mashed bananas and brown sugar together in a large bowl.
4. Add eggs, olive oil, and vanilla extract; mix well.
5. Mix flour, cocoa powder, baking powder, and cinnamon in a separate bowl.
6. Stir banana mixture into flour mixture.
7. Fold chocolate chips and walnuts into batter.
8. Distribute batter into the muffin cups.
9. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted in the center of a muffin comes out clean, 35 to 40 minutes.
