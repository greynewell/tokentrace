## Ingredients

- 1 bunch fresh asparagus, trimmed
- ⅓ cup Italian-style dressing (such as Bernstein's Restaurant Recipe®)
- ¾ teaspoon lemon pepper
- salt and ground black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Preheat grill for medium heat and lightly oil the grate.
2. Lay asparagus flat in a 9x9-inch pan.
3. Add Italian dressing, lemon pepper, salt, and black pepper; toss to coat.
4. Transfer asparagus using tongs to the grill.
5. Grill asparagus on preheated grill until tender, 3 to 5 minutes per side.
6. Return asparagus to pan and toss with remaining dressing mixture.
