## Ingredients

- 1 cup large flake rolled oats
- ½ cup KRAFT All Natural Peanut Butter
- ¼ cup honey
- 2 tablespoons MAXWELL HOUSE Original Roast Coffee
- 2 ounces BAKER'S Semi-Sweet Chocolate, chopped
- 2 ounces BAKER'S White Chocolate, chopped

## Instructions

1. Mix together all ingredients in a medium bowl until blended.
2. Refrigerate for 20 to 30 min.
3. Roll into 20 (1-inch) balls, using about 2 Tbsp. for each.
