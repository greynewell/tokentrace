## Ingredients

- 4  cucumbers - peeled, seeded, and coarsely chopped
- 1 small garlic clove, chopped
- 3 cups chicken broth, divided
- 2 ½ cups sour cream
- 3 tablespoons white vinegar
- 2 tablespoons seasoned salt (such as LAWRY'S®)

## Instructions

1. Blend cucumbers, garlic, and 1 cup chicken broth in a blender until smooth.
2. Add remaining 2 cups chicken broth, sour cream, vinegar, and seasoned salt; blend until smooth.
3. Refrigerate soup until completely chilled, at least 1 hour.
