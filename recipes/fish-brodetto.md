## Ingredients

- 1 pound lobster tail
- 1 pound sole fillets
- 1 pound medium shrimp - peeled and deveined
- 3 tablespoons lemon juice
- ¼ cup olive oil
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 large onion, sliced
- 1 (15 ounce) can whole tomatoes
- 1 cup balsamic vinegar
- 3 cups red wine
- 2 quarts water
- 2 sprigs fresh basil leaves, torn
- 3 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
- salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Cut the lobster and sole into 1 inch chunks.
2. Toss the lobster and sole with the shrimp and lemon juice, set aside.
3. Heat the olive oil in a large pot over medium heat.
4. Add the garlic and onions; cook and stir until translucent.
5. Pour in the tomatoes, vinegar, and wine; bring to a simmer and cook for about 10 minutes.
6. Pour in water; add the marinated fish, basil, and parsley; simmer for about 30 minutes, until the lobster has cooked through.
7. Season to taste with salt and pepper.
8. Serve with a nice, crusty bread.
