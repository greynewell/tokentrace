## Ingredients

- 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
- 1 (8 ounce) container sour cream
- ½ cup chopped celery
- 3  green onions, chopped
- 4 tablespoons lemon juice
- 1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
- ½ pound imitation crabmeat, chopped
- 5 drops hot pepper sauce

## Instructions

1. In a medium bowl, blend the cream cheese and sour cream.
2. Mix in the celery, green onions, lemon juice, Cheddar cheese, imitation crab and hot pepper sauce.
3. Serve chilled.
