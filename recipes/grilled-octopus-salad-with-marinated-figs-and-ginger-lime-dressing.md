## Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
- ½ teaspoon honey
- 1 teaspoon crushed ginger
- 2 tablespoons fresh lime juice
- ½  lime, zested
- 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
- ½ teaspoon ground pink pepper
- 1 small octopus, head and tentacles separated
- 1 tablespoon extra-virgin olive oil
- 6  celery ribs, thinly sliced
- 1 cup flat-leaf parsley leaves
- ⅓ cup chopped inner celery leaves
- ⅓ cup marinated figs

## Instructions

1. Combine Dijon mustard and honey together in a bowl.
2. Add crushed ginger; stir until blended.
3. Stir in lime juice and lime zest until blended.
4. Stir in olive oil, 1 tablespoon at a time.
5. Season with ground pink pepper; mix until dressing is combined.
6. Preheat an outdoor grill for medium-high heat and lightly oil the grate.
7. Coat tentacles in 1 tablespoon olive oil.
8. Grill tentacles on the preheated grill until lightly browned, 1 to 2 minutes per side.
9. Combine celery, parsley, celery leaves, figs, and grilled tentacles in a large bowl.
10. Add dressing; toss until combined.
