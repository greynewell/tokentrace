## Ingredients

- 1 (5 ounce) can tuna in water, drained
- ⅓ cup dried cranberries
- ½  sweet apple, cut into 1/4-inch pieces
- 2 tablespoons light mayonnaise
- 1  green onion, chopped
- sea salt to taste
- ground black pepper to taste
- 4 cups chopped fresh spinach

## Instructions

1. Mix tuna, cranberries, apple, mayonnaise, green onion, sea salt, and black pepper together in a bowl; refrigerate 1 hour.
2. Put 2 cups spinach on each of 2 plates. Top spinach with about half the tuna salad.
