## Ingredients

- 2 (14.5 ounce) cans fire-roasted diced tomatoes, undrained
- 1 small onion, chopped
- 1 ½ tablespoons sucralose sugar substitute (such as Splenda®)  (Optional)
- 2 teaspoons salt
- ⅛ teaspoon ground cloves
- ⅛ teaspoon ground ginger
- ½ teaspoon ground black pepper
- 1 (12 fluid ounce) can fat-free evaporated milk
- 1  cooked chicken breast, shredded
- 5 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese

## Instructions

1. Combine tomatoes, onion, sugar substitute, salt, cloves, ginger, and black pepper in a microwave-safe bowl; cook in microwave on high for 15 minutes.
2. Add evaporated milk and chicken to tomato mixture; cook in microwave until warmed, about 45 seconds.
3. Ladle soup into 5 bowls and sprinkle 1 tablespoon Parmesan cheese onto each serving.
