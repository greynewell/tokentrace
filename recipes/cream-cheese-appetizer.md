## Ingredients

- 1 pound cream cheese, room temperature
- 1 cup Jamaican hot pepper sauce
- assorted snack crackers

## Instructions

1. Place the cream cheese onto the center of a plate, and pour the hot pepper sauce over so that it drips down the sides.
2. Serve with assorted crackers.
