## Ingredients

- 2 cups fresh broccoli florets
- 2 cups fresh cauliflower florets
- 2  large eggs eggs
- 1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of mushroom soup
- 1 cup shredded sharp Cheddar cheese
- ½ cup mayonnaise
- 1 medium onion, finely chopped
- ½ cup butter, melted
- 1 (15 ounce) package seasoned croutons, finely crushed

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Lightly grease a casserole dish.
3. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
4. Add broccoli and cauliflower.
5. Cook, uncovered, until tender, 3 to 4 minutes.
6. Drain in a colander and rinse under cold running water.
7. Transfer to the prepared casserole dish, making sure vegetables are evenly distributed.
8. Beat eggs in a bowl.
9. Mix in cream of mushroom soup, 1/2 cup of Cheddar cheese, mayonnaise, and onion.
10. Pour evenly over vegetables in the casserole dish.
11. Sprinkle the remaining 1/2 cup of Cheddar cheese over the top and level with a spoon.
12. Pour melted butter over the entire casserole.
13. Sprinkle with crushed croutons.
14. Bake in the preheated oven until casserole is hot and bubbling, about 40 minutes.
