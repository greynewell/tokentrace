## Ingredients

- ¼ cup butter
- 8 ounces fresh mushrooms, quartered
- 1 teaspoon Cajun seasoning
- 1 teaspoon minced garlic
- 24  shucked oysters, quartered
- ½ cup whole corn kernels, blanched
- ⅔ cup French-style green beans, chopped
- 2 tablespoons chopped pimento peppers
- ½ cup seafood stock
- 10 ounces fresh linguine pasta
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
- 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
- 3 tablespoons thinly sliced green onion
- 4 ounces crabmeat

## Instructions

1. In a small saucepan, melt 2 tablespoons butter or margarine.
2. Stir in flour to make a paste.
3. Set roux aside.
4. Saute mushrooms, Cajun spice, and garlic in 1/4 cup butter or margarine over medium-high heat for 2 minutes.
5. Add oysters, corn, string beans, and pimento.
6. Saute for 1 1/2 minutes.
7. Add stock and linguine, and bring to a slight simmer.
8. Fold in roux until sauce thickens, then reduce heat.
9. Fold in parsley and scallions.
10. Fold in lump crabmeat, and heat through.
11. Serve immediately.
