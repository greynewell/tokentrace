## Ingredients

- 4 (1 ounce) squares unsweetened chocolate
- ½ cup butter
- ½ teaspoon salt
- 3 cups white sugar
- 1 (12 fluid ounce) can evaporated milk

## Instructions

1. Fill the lower pot of a double boiler halfway with water and bring to a boil. Melt chocolate, butter, and salt together in the upper pot. Add sugar, 1/2 cup at a time, stirring after each addition.
2. Gradually add evaporated milk, a little at a time, and continue stirring until well mixed.
3. Serve hot over ice cream. Extra sauce may be stored in the refrigerator and reheated in the microwave.
