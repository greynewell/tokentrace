## Ingredients

- ⅔ cup skim milk
- ⅔ cup peanut butter
- ¼ cup applesauce
- 1  large egg
- 2 tablespoons honey
- 1  apple, finely chopped
- 7  dried apricots, chopped
- 8  dates, pitted and chopped
- ½ cup chopped mixed nuts
- 2 ¼ cups old-fashioned rolled oats
- 2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
- 1 teaspoon ground nutmeg
- 1 teaspoon baking soda

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Grease a 9x9-inch baking dish.
3. Mix milk, peanut butter, applesauce, egg, honey, apple, apricots, dates, and nuts together in a large bowl until well-combined.
4. Stir in oats, cinnamon, nutmeg, and baking soda until evenly distributed.
5. Spread mixture into prepared baking dish.
6. Bake in preheated oven until a knife inserted into the center comes out clean, 35 to 40 minutes.
7. Cool in the pans for 10 minutes before cutting into 12 bars.
