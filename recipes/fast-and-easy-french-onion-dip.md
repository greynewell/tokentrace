## Ingredients

- 1 ¼ cups nonfat sour cream
- ½ cup fat-free mayonnaise
- ⅛ cup chopped green onion
- 1 (1 ounce) package dry onion soup mix
- ¼ teaspoon dried minced onion
- salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. In a medium bowl, stir together nonfat sour cream, fat-free mayonnaise, green onion, dry onion soup mix, dry minced onion, salt and pepper.
2. Chill until serving.
