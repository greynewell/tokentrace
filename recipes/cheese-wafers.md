## Ingredients

- ½ cup butter, softened
- 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
- ½ pound shredded Cheddar cheese
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 pinch crushed red pepper flakes
- 1 dash hot pepper sauce
- ¾ cup chopped pecans

## Instructions

1. Blend together butter and flour in a medium bowl. Mix in Cheddar cheese, salt, red pepper flakes, hot pepper sauce, and pecans. Form dough into a roll and wrap in parchment paper. Refrigerate dough 8 hours to overnight.
2. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
3. Slice dough into very thin wafers and place onto a baking tray.
4. Bake wafers in the preheated oven until crisp, 15 to 20 minutes.
