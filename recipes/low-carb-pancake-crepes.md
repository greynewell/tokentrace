## Ingredients

- 3 ounces cream cheese, softened
- 2  eggs, beaten
- 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
- 1 tablespoon sugar-free syrup
- 1 teaspoon butter

## Instructions

1. In a bowl, mash the cream cheese with beaten eggs, about 1 teaspoon at a time at first, until the mixture is smooth and free of lumps.
2. Beat in the cinnamon and sugar-free syrup.
3. Melt the butter in a nonstick skillet over medium heat.
4. When the butter has stopped foaming, reduce heat to medium-low, and pour in several tablespoons of the batter.
5. Swirl to coat the bottom of the skillet.
6. Allow to cook until set, about 4 minutes on the first side; flip the crepe with a spatula and cook the other side until the crepe shows small browned spots, 1 to 2 more minutes.
