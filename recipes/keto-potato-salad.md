## Ingredients

- 1 large head cauliflower
- ¾ cup light mayonnaise
- ¼ cup chopped onion
- ¼ cup dill pickle relish
- 3 tablespoons mustard
- salt and ground black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Chop cauliflower into bite-sized pieces, discarding the stems.
2. Transfer to a microwave-safe dish.
3. Cover and cook on high until tender, about 6 minutes.
4. Mix mayonnaise, onion, pickle relish, mustard, salt, and pepper into the cauliflower.
5. Mash slightly to get a creamier consistency.
6. Chill before serving.
