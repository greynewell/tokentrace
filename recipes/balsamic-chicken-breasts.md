## Ingredients

- 2  sweet potatoes, peeled and cut into 2-inch pieces
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- salt and pepper to taste
- 2  skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
- ½ cup balsamic vinegar
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- ½ cup balsamic vinegar

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
2. Place the potatoes on a baking sheet; drizzle olive oil over potatoes and season with salt and pepper.
3. Place the chicken breasts in a baking dish.
4. Pour 1/2 cup of balsamic vinegar over the breasts; season with salt and pepper.
5. Cover with aluminum foil.
6. Place the potatoes in the preheated oven and bake for 10 minutes; place the dish with the chicken in the oven and cook both the potatoes an chicken another 20 minutes; flip both the potatoes and chicken; reduce the oven heat to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
7. Bake another 20 minutes.
8. Pour 1/2 cup of balsamic vinegar into a small saucepan and place over medium heat.
9. Cook until reduced to about 1/4 cup.
10. Place the chicken breasts atop the potatoes; drizzle with the reduced balsamic vinegar to serve.
