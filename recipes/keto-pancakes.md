## Ingredients

- 3 tablespoons butter
- 1 cup blanched almond flour
- ¼ cup coconut flour
- 1 tablespoon baking powder
- 1 tablespoon low-calorie natural sweetener (such as Swerve®)
- 1 tablespoon chia seeds  (Optional)
- 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon  (Optional)
- ⅛ teaspoon salt
- 4 large eggs
- ⅓ cup milk, or more as needed
- 2 ounces cream cheese, softened in the microwave
- 1 teaspoon maple extract
- 1 tablespoon coconut oil, or as needed

## Instructions

1. Melt butter in a large skillet over medium heat; cook, stirring frequently, until starting to brown, about 5 minutes.
2. Remove from heat and allow to cool while preparing the batter.
3. Whisk almond flour, coconut flour, baking powder, sweetener, chia seeds, cinnamon, and salt together in a bowl.
4. Add browned butter, eggs, milk, softened cream cheese, and maple extract; mix until batter is no longer lumpy.
5. Mix in more milk if batter is too thick.
6. Heat some coconut oil in the same skillet over medium-low heat.
7. Drop spoonfuls of batter into the skillet, making 2-inch silver dollar pancakes.
8. Cook until pancakes appear set, 2 to 3 minutes.
9. Flip and continue cooking until browned on the second side, 2 to 3 minutes.
10. Transfer to a plate.
11. Repeat with remaining coconut oil and batter.
