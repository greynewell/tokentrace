## Ingredients

- 3 ounces butter
- 4 cloves garlic, crushed
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- 1 pinch dried oregano, or to taste
- 2  salmon steaks

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Place butter in a microwave-safe bowl and melt in the microwave, about 30 seconds. Add crushed garlic to butter and stir. Season sauce with salt, pepper, and oregano.
3. Place salmon fillets on a large sheet of aluminum foil and pour sauce on top. Wrap tightly to enclose; place on a baking sheet.
4. Bake in the preheated oven until fish flakes easily with a fork, about 20 minutes.
