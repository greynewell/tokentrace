## Ingredients

- 10 strips frozen French fried potatoes
- 2 slices Italian bread
- 5 slices deli cooked roast beef
- 2 slices provolone cheese
- 2 slices tomato
- ¼ cup prepared coleslaw

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven and prepare french fries according to package instructions.
2. Layer Italian bread with roast beef, provolone cheese, tomato, coleslaw, and freshly cooked fries.
3. Serve immediately.
