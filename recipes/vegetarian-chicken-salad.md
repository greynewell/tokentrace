## Ingredients

- 4 cups vegetarian fried chicken (such as FriChik®), diced
- 2 cups thinly sliced celery
- ¾ cup lemon juice
- ¾ cup mayonnaise
- ¼ cup whipping cream
- ¼ cup seedless red and green grapes, chopped  (Optional)
- 2 tablespoons curry powder
- 2 tablespoons dried dill weed
- ½ teaspoon salt

## Instructions

1. Combine vegetarian fried chicken and celery together in a large bowl.
2. Mix together lemon juice, mayonnaise, whipping cream, grapes, curry powder, dill, and salt in a separate bowl. Add to chicken mixture and toss well. Chill until serving.
