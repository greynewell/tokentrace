## Ingredients

- 1 cup mayonnaise
- ½ cup ketchup
- 1 (0.53 ounce) package McCormick® French Onion Dip Seasoning Mix
- 2 teaspoons chopped pickles

## Instructions

1. Mix all ingredients in small bowl until well blended.
2. Cover.
3. Refrigerate 1 hour or until ready to serve.
4. Stir before serving.
5. Serve with French fries.
