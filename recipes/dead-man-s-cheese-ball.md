## Ingredients

- 1 ¼ pounds sharp Cheddar cheese, shredded
- 1 cup sour cream
- ¼ cup butter, melted and cooled
- 1 tablespoon prepared horseradish
- 1 teaspoon prepared hot mustard
- 2 dashes chipotle hot sauce
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- ¼ cup port wine
- ½ cup black sesame seeds

## Instructions

1. In the container of a food processor, combine the Cheddar cheese, sour cream, butter, horseradish, hot mustard, chipotle sauce, garlic and port wine.
2. Pulse until smooth.
3. You can use a potato masher in a large bowl but it will take longer.
4. Line a bowl with plastic wrap, and fill with the cheese mixture.
5. Cover and refrigerate for at least 2 hours, or overnight.
6. Toast the sesame seeds in a dry skillet over medium heat just until fragrant, about 3 minutes.
7. Gather the plastic wrap around the cheese mixture and form into a ball.
8. Remove the plastic wrap and roll the ball in sesame seeds to coat.
9. Place in the center of a platter and surround with your favorite chips or crackers.
