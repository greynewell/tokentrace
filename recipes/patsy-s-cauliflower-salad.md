## Ingredients

- 1 head cauliflower, cut into bite-size pieces
- 1 bunch green onions, thinly sliced
- 1 bunch radishes, thinly sliced
- ½ cup mayonnaise
- ½ cup sour cream
- 5 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese
- 1 teaspoon garlic powder

## Instructions

1. Combine cauliflower, green onions, and radishes in a large bowl.
2. Whisk mayonnaise, sour cream, Parmesan cheese, and garlic powder in another bowl; fold mayonnaise mixture into vegetables.
3. Refrigerate until chilled.
