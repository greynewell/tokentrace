## Ingredients

- 6 tablespoons margarine
- 1 cup milk
- 1 (.25 ounce) package active dry yeast
- 1 teaspoon salt
- ⅓ cup white sugar
- 5 cups all-purpose flour
- 2  eggs
- ½ cup white sugar
- ⅔ cup all-purpose flour
- 3 ½ tablespoons margarine
- 2  egg yolks
- 2 tablespoons cocoa powder  (Optional)
- 1  egg
- 2 tablespoons milk

## Instructions

1. In a small pan, combine 6 tablespoons butter or margarine and 1 cup milk; heat to 110 degrees.
2. In a large mixing bowl, combine yeast, salt, 1/3 cup sugar, and 2 cups of the flour.
3. Pour in warmed milk mix.
4. Beat, scraping often, with an electric mixer on medium speed for 2 minutes.
5. Blend in 2 whole eggs and 1 cup flour; beat on high for 2 minutes.
6. With a spoon , beat in enough of the remaining flour to form a stiff dough.
7. Knead on a floured board till smooth, about 5 minutes.
8. Place in a greased bowl, turn to grease top, cover, and let rise until doubled.
9. While dough is rising make streusel.
10. Stir together 1/2 cup sugar and 2/3 cup flour.
11. Mix in 3 1/2 tablespoons cold butter or margarine.
12. Mix to get fine crumbs.
13. Blend in 2 egg yolks with a fork.
14. For chocolate streusel, mix 2 tablespoons cocoa powder with flour.
15. Punch dough down, turn onto floured board.
16. Divide into 14 pieces.
17. Shape each into a ball.
18. Shape 7 into seashells.
19. Squeeze 1/4 cup of streusel into a firm ball; and press over the top of each round.
20. Score with slightly curved parallel lines to resemble a scallop shell.
21. Roll remaining dough into 4 by 8 inch ovals.
22. Top each with 3 tablespoons streusel.
23. To make horns, roll oval from short end; stop half way, fold in sides, and finish rolling.
24. Curl ends in to form a crescent.
25. Place buns about 2 inches apart on a greased baking sheet.
26. Cover lightly, and allow to rise until doubled, about 45 minutes.
27. In a small bowl, beat together 1 egg and 2 tablespoons milk.
28. Brush buns with egg mixture.
29. Bake at 375 degrees F (190 degrees C) until lightly browned, about 15 to 17 minutes.
