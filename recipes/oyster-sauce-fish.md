## Ingredients

- 2 pounds fish fillets
- butter flavored cooking spray
- 2 teaspoons canola oil or corn oil
- ¼ cup oyster sauce
- lemon pepper
- garlic powder

## Instructions

1. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Place fillets in skillet; spray fillets with butter spray. Sprinkle with lemon pepper and garlic powder. Cook 4 to 5 minutes.
2. Pour in oyster sauce, then gently turn fillets. Cook until fish begins to flake, about 5 minutes.
