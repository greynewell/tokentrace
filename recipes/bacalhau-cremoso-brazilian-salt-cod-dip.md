## Ingredients

- 1 pound dried salted cod
- 1 cup olive oil
- 2  garlic cloves, minced
- 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley
- salt and ground white pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Soak cod in a large bowl of water in the refrigerator for 24 hours, changing soaking water 4 to 5 times to remove the salt.
2. Rinse the cod under running water; drain.
3. Remove skin and bones.
4. Transfer cod to a saucepan and cover with water.
5. Heat over low heat until it is soft and flakes easily with a fork, about 30 minutes.
6. Drain cod and let cool until easily handled, about 15 minutes.
7. Shred finely and transfer to a large saucepan.
8. Place saucepan over low heat.
9. Pour in olive oil in a slow, steady stream, beating vigorously, until mixture thickens to the consistency of mayonnaise, about 5 minutes.
10. Stir in garlic, parsley, salt, and white pepper.
11. Let cool before serving, about 15 minutes.
