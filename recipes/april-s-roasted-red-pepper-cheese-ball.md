## Ingredients

- 2 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened
- 1 (8 ounce) package finely shredded Cheddar cheese
- 1 teaspoon garlic powder
- 1 pinch cayenne pepper
- 1 ½ teaspoons Worcestershire sauce
- ⅓ cup jarred roasted red pepper, drained and chopped
- ½ cup chopped fresh parsley

## Instructions

1. In a medium bowl, mix together the cream cheese, Cheddar cheese, garlic powder, cayenne pepper and Worcestershire sauce.
2. I use a hand mixer.
3. Stir in the roasted red peppers last.
4. Spoon the mixture onto plastic wrap, and cover with another piece of plastic wrap.
5. Shape into a ball, and roll in parsley to coat.
6. Refrigerate until serving.
