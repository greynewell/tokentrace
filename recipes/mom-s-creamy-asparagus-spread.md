## Ingredients

- 1 (15 ounce) can asparagus tips, drained
- 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
- ½ cup finely chopped almonds
- 3 tablespoons mayonnaise
- 1 teaspoon finely minced onion

## Instructions

1. Mash asparagus in bowl; pour off any remaining liquid.
2. Mix in the cream cheese, almonds, mayonnaise, and onion  until thoroughly blended.
3. Cover, and refrigerate until serving.
