## Ingredients

- 2 (7 ounce) cans sweet corn, drained
- 2  green onions, chopped
- ⅓ medium red bell pepper, diced
- 1  serrano pepper, finely diced
- ½ cup shredded Cheddar cheese, or more to taste
- 1 tablespoon coarsely chopped fresh cilantro
- 1 tablespoon bacon bits  (Optional)
- 1 tablespoon mayonnaise
- 1 tablespoon sour cream
- ½ teaspoon garlic powder
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- 8 ounces chili cheese-flavored corn chips (such as Fritos®)

## Instructions

1. Place corn, green onions, red bell pepper, serrano pepper, Cheddar cheese, cilantro, bacon bits, mayonnaise, sour cream, garlic powder, salt, and pepper into a medium-sized bowl.
2. Stir well.
3. Cover and refrigerate until ready to serve, at least 1 hour.
4. Stir in corn chips just before serving.
