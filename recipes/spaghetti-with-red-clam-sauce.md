## Ingredients

- 1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
- 1 (14.5 ounce) can diced tomatoes
- 1 (15 ounce) can tomato sauce
- 1 ½ tablespoons capers
- 1 tablespoon minced garlic
- 2 teaspoons white sugar
- 2 teaspoons dried parsley
- 1 teaspoon garlic powder
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ¼ teaspoon dried basil
- ¼ teaspoon dried oregano
- ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
- 1 pinch crushed red pepper flakes
- 2 (6.5 ounce) cans minced clams, with juice
- 1 pound cooked and drained spaghetti

## Instructions

1. In a large pot combine the crushed tomatoes, diced tomatoes, tomato sauce, capers, minced garlic, sugar, parsley, garlic powder, salt, oregano, basil, black pepper and red pepper.
2. Cover and bring to a boil.
3. Lower heat to a simmer and cook for 45 to 60 minutes.
4. Add the clams plus some of their juice during the last 5 minutes of cooking.
5. Pour sauce over hot spaghetti.
