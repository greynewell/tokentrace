## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon sea salt
- 4 (6 ounce) lobster tails
- ½ cup butter, melted

## Instructions

1. Pour about 1 inch of water into the bottom of a large stockpot and bring to a boil. Add salt and place a steamer insert inside the pot so that it is just above the water level. Place lobster tails on the steamer rack and cover the pot.
2. Steam lobster tails for 8 minutes. And don't peek! Remove from the steamer and serve with melted butter.
