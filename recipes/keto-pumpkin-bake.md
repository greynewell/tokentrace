## Ingredients

- 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
- 4 large eggs
- 1 (15 ounce) can pumpkin puree
- ½ cup granular sucrolose sweetener (such as Splenda®)
- 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
- 1 teaspoon pumpkin pie spice

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Place cream cheese in a bowl; beat with an electric mixer until smooth.
3. Add eggs and beat until well combined.
4. Add pumpkin puree, sweetener, cinnamon, and pumpkin pie spice; mix well.
5. Pour batter into an ungreased 8x8-inch baking pan.
6. Bake in the preheated oven until center is set, about 45 minutes.
7. Remove from oven and cool completely.
8. Slice into serving-size pieces.
