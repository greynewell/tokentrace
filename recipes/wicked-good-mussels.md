## Ingredients

- 2 cups white wine
- 2  shallots, finely chopped
- 10 cloves garlic, minced, divided
- 4 pounds mussels, cleaned and debearded
- 1 ¼ sticks butter
- ½ cup chopped Italian parsley
- 2 tablespoons lemon juice
- 1 teaspoon lemon zest

## Instructions

1. Mix wine, shallots, and 4 cloves garlic in a large saucepan over medium heat.
2. Bring to a simmer.
3. Add mussels.
4. Cook until shells open, 10 to 15 minutes.
5. Discard any unopened mussels.
6. Drain the rest and place in a bowl.
7. Melt butter in a pan; add remaining garlic and heat until fragrant, 30 seconds to 1 minute.
8. Add parsley, lemon juice, and lemon zest.
9. Pour over the steamed mussels.
