## Ingredients

- ¼ cup coconut oil
- 1 (1 pound) whole pompano fish, gutted and cleaned
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- 1  lime, juiced

## Instructions

1. Preheat grill to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
2. Line a baking dish with aluminum foil.
3. Place dish in the grill, away from direct heat.
4. Drop coconut oil onto the foil and close grill long enough to melt oil.
5. Place fish in the oil and turn until it is coated in oil on both sides; season with salt and pepper.
6. Close grill.
7. Cook on the grill until fish flakes easily with a fork, about 20 minutes.
8. Carefully transfer fish and foil onto a plate and slide fish off the foil.
9. Squeeze lime juice over fish.
