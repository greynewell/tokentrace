## Ingredients

- 1 ½ cups green lentils
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 2  onions, diced
- 1 pound mushrooms, diced
- 3 stalks celery, diced
- ¼ cup olive oil
- ¼ cup soy sauce
- 2 tablespoons honey
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice, or to taste
- 2 cloves garlic, crushed
- 1 cup chopped almonds

## Instructions

1. Bring a pot of lightly salted water to a boil; add lentils.
2. Reduce heat and simmer until lentils are tender, 20 to 25 minutes.
3. Drain.
4. Heat 1 tablespoon olive oil in a skillet over medium heat; cook and stir onions until slightly browned, 10 to 15 minutes.
5. Add mushrooms and celery; cook until mushrooms have softened, about 7 minutes.
6. Whisk 1/4 cup olive oil, soy sauce, honey, lemon juice, and garlic together in a bowl.
7. Stir almonds into onion mixture; mix well.
8. Carefully fold in lentils and dressing; stir to coat.
9. Serve warm or cold.
