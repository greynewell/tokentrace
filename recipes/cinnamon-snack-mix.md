## Ingredients

- 5 cups honey graham cereal
- 3 cups cinnamon-flavored bear-shaped graham cookies
- 2 cups ramen noodles, crushed
- ¾ cup sliced almonds
- 1 cup golden raisins
- ⅓ cup butter
- ⅓ cup honey
- 1 teaspoon orange juice

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 375  degrees F (190 degrees C).
2. In a large bowl, mix honey graham cereal, bear-shaped graham cookies, ramen noodles, almonds, and golden raisins.
3. In a small saucepan over low heat, melt butter, and blend in honey and orange juice.
4. Spread over the honey graham cereal mixture, and toss to coat.
5. Spread mixture onto a large baking sheet.
6. Bake 10 minutes in the preheated oven.
