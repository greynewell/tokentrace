## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons vegetable oil, divided
- 1 ½ tablespoons all-purpose flour
- 1 pound shrimp, peeled and deveined
- 2 medium onions, chopped
- 1 cup chopped celery
- 1 medium bell pepper, chopped
- 1 ½ pounds sliced fresh okra
- 4 cups water
- 1 (14.5 ounce) can diced tomatoes, undrained
- 2 large bay leaves
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- 1 (6 ounce) can tomato paste
- ½ pound lobster meat
- 1 teaspoon gumbo file powder, or to taste

## Instructions

1. Heat 1 1/2 tablespoons oil in a saucepan over medium heat until a pinch of flour sprinkled into the oil will just begin to bubble.
2. Whisk in flour to form a thick paste the consistency of cake frosting.
3. Continue cooking, whisking constantly, until the flour turns the color of melted chocolate, about 45 minutes.
4. Add shrimp and cook for 3 minutes.
5. Remove from the heat and set aside.
6. Meanwhile, heat remaining oil in a large saucepan over medium heat.
7. Add onions, celery, and bell pepper; cook until soft, about 5 minutes.
8. Add okra; cook until it ceases to rope, about 30 minutes.
9. Add water, undrained tomatoes, bay leaves, salt, and pepper.
10. Stir in shrimp mixture, cover, and bring to a simmer.
11. Simmer for 15 minutes.
12. Add tomato paste and simmer for 15 minutes more.
13. Stir in lobster meat and gumbo file.
14. Remove bay leaves before serving.
