## Ingredients

- ⅓ cup natural peanut butter
- 1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice
- 2 teaspoons tamari sauce
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- 2 tablespoons water
- 1 cup celery, finely chopped

## Instructions

1. Mix the peanut butter together in a bowl with the lemon juice, tamari sauce, garlic, and water until smooth.
2. Stir in the celery and toss until evenly blended.
3. Spread on your choice of sliced bread.
