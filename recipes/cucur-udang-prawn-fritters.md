## Ingredients

- 10  shrimp - peeled, deveined, and chopped
- 1  onion, chopped
- 1  potato, diced
- 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
- 1 cup water
- ½ cup frozen corn
- 1  egg
- 1 teaspoon chicken bouillon granules
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- 1 teaspoon baking soda
- ¼ cup hot water
- vegetable oil for frying

## Instructions

1. Combine shrimp, onion, potato, flour, 1 cup water, corn, egg, chicken bouillon, salt, baking powder, and baking soda in a bowl. Mix in hot water until well combined. Let stand for 5 minutes.
2. Meanwhile, heat oil in a wok over medium- to medium-high heat. Drop spoonfuls of batter into the hot oil. Cook fritters in batches, flipping halfway, until browned on both sides, 3 to 4 minutes. Drain on a paper towel-lined plate.
