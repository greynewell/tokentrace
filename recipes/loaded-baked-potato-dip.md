## Ingredients

- 3 pounds bacon, cut into 1/2-inch pieces
- 6 cups sour cream
- ½ pound shredded extra-sharp Cheddar cheese
- 1 cup chopped green onion
- 1 pinch cayenne pepper
- freshly ground black pepper to taste
- ¼ cup sour cream, or as needed
- 1 ½ cups chopped green onion, green parts only, or more as needed

## Instructions

1. Place bacon in a large skillet over medium-high heat; cook, turning occasionally, until evenly browned and crisp, about 10 minutes.
2. Pour bacon into a colander set over a bowl to drain and cool to room temperature; finely chop cooled bacon.
3. Stir 6 cups sour cream, Cheddar cheese, 1 cup chopped green onion, cayenne pepper, and black pepper together in a large bowl until well-mixed.
4. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate until chilled, at least 1 hour.
5. Spread sour cream mixture into a football shape in the center of a serving platter.
6. Spread bacon over the top to resemble the outside of a football.
7. Place 1/4 cup sour cream in a piping bag; pipe 1 line on each end of the "football" and 1 set of "laces" down the center of the "football".
8. Spread 1 1/2 cups chopped green onion around the "football" to resemble "grass".
