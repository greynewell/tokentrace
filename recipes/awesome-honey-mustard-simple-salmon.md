## Ingredients

- 2 (8 ounce) skinless, boneless salmon fillets
- ¼ cup Dijon mustard (such as Grey Poupon®)
- 2 tablespoons honey

## Instructions

1. Place salmon fillets in a baking pan.
2. Set oven rack about 6 inches from the heat source and preheat the oven's broiler.
3. Combine Dijon mustard and honey in bowl.
4. Spread over the salmon fillets.
5. Broil in the preheated oven until the mustard begins to blacken, about 6 minutes.
6. Turn fillets over; coat with the honey-mustard mixture from the bottom of the pan.
7. Return to the oven.
8. Broil until mustard begins to blacken and fish flakes easily, about 6 minutes more.
