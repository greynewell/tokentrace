## Ingredients

- ½ cup lemon juice
- 2  anchovy fillets
- 2 cloves garlic, peeled
- ½ teaspoon Dijon mustard
- ½ cup olive oil
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- ground black pepper to taste
- 6 cups kale leaves
- 1 cup croutons
- ¼ cup grated Parmesan cheese

## Instructions

1. Combine lemon juice, anchovies, garlic, and mustard in a blender or food processor fitted with a steel blade.
2. Process until thoroughly combined.
3. Pour in olive oil very gradually through the feed tube while machine is running, first 1 tablespoon at a time, then gradually increasing the amount.
4. Season with salt and pepper.
5. Stack and bunch the kale leaves together on a cutting board and cut across the stack into skinny slivers.
6. Place the kale, croutons, and Parmesan cheese in the bowl and drizzle with about a 1/4 of the dressing.
7. Toss and taste the salad and add more dressing if desired, then toss again.
