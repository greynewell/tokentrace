## Ingredients

- 1 pound fresh green beans, trimmed
- ¼ cup olive oil
- 1 teaspoon minced garlic
- 1 teaspoon kosher salt

## Instructions

1. Combine green beans, olive oil, garlic, and salt in a bowl; toss to coat. Allow green beans to marinate for 30 minutes.
2. Preheat the grill for medium heat and lightly oil the grate. Arrange green beans on a grill pan.
3. Place the grill pan on the preheated grill; cook and stir green beans until lightly charred, about 10 minutes.
