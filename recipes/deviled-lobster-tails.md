## Ingredients

- ¼ cup mayonnaise
- 2 tablespoons chopped tarragon
- 1 tablespoon sriracha sauce
- 1 teaspoon ground paprika
- 1 teaspoon lemon juice
- ¼ teaspoon ground cayenne pepper
- 1 pinch salt
- 4  frozen lobster tails - thawed, split in half, and deveined

## Instructions

1. Mix mayonnaise, tarragon, sriracha sauce, paprika, lemon juice, cayenne pepper, and salt together in a bowl.
2. Preheat oven to 500 degrees F (260 degrees C).
3. Set oven rack about 8 inches from the heat source and preheat the oven's broiler.
4. Loosen lobster meat from the shell, leaving it attached near the tip of the tail.
5. Place tails on a baking sheet lined with crinkled aluminum foil to help keep them in place.
6. Spoon some of the mayonnaise mixture beneath the lobster meat and spread a generous amount on top.
7. Broil in the preheated oven until lobster tails are browned and cooked through, 5 to 7 minutes.
