## Ingredients

- 1 cup white rice
- 2 cups water
- 3 tablespoons peanut oil
- 1 pound tofu, cubed
- ½  white onion, chopped
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- ¾ (6 ounce) can tomato paste
- ½ cup peanut butter

## Instructions

1. Bring rice and water to a boil in a pot.
2. Cover, reduce heat to low, and simmer 20 minutes, or until rice is tender and water has been absorbed.
3. Heat the oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
4. Cook the tofu in the oil until browned, about 10 minutes.
5. Add the onion and garlic; cook and stir until the onion is translucent, 3 to 5 minutes.
6. Mix in the tomato paste and peanut butter; cook and stir until peanut butter has melted completely, about 5 minutes.
7. Serve over cooked rice.
