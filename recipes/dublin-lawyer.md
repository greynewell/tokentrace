## Ingredients

- 1 (2 pound) fresh lobster
- 4 tablespoons butter
- 1 small onion, finely diced
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- ½ cup heavy cream
- 3 fluid ounces Irish whiskey

## Instructions

1. Cut lobster in half.
2. Remove organs and discard; remove meat.
3. Cut into large chunks and set aside.
4. Bring a large pot of water to a boil; drop in lobster shells to clean, about 5 minutes.
5. Set aside.
6. Heat butter in a skillet over medium heat until just beginning to brown, 3 to 4 minutes.
7. Add lobster meat, onion, and garlic; cook and stir until onions and garlic are softened, and meat is cooked through but not colored, about 5 minutes.
8. Stir in whiskey and set on fire carefully with a lighter.
9. Add cream once alcohol has burned off, or when flames are out.
10. Cook until cream is heated through, 3 to 4 minutes more.
11. Return meat and cream sauce to cleaned shell halves to serve.
