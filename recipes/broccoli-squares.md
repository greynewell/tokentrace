## Ingredients

- 2 (8 ounce) packages refrigerated crescent roll dough
- 2 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened
- 1 cup mayonnaise
- 1 (1 ounce) package dry Ranch-style dressing mix
- 1 head fresh broccoli, chopped
- 3  roma (plum) tomatoes, chopped
- 1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 375  degrees F (190 degrees C).
2. Lightly grease a medium baking sheet.
3. Arrange the dinner roll dough in 4 rectangles on the baking sheet.
4. Bake in the preheated oven 12 minutes, or until golden brown.
5. Remove from heat and allow to cool completely.
6. In a medium bowl, mix the cream cheese, mayonnaise and dry ranch-style dressing mix.
7. Spread evenly on the crescent rolls.
8. Sprinkle with broccoli and tomatoes.
9. Top with Cheddar cheese and serve.
