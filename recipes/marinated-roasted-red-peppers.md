## Ingredients

- 4 large red bell peppers, halved, with stems and seeds removed
- ¼ cup olive oil
- 2 cloves garlic, pressed
- 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ½ teaspoon dried basil
- ¼ teaspoon seasoned pepper

## Instructions

1. Set an oven rack about 4 inches from the heat source and preheat the oven's broiler.
2. Line a baking sheet with aluminum foil.
3. Place bell peppers, skin side-up, on the prepared baking sheet.
4. Broil peppers in the preheated oven until skin is blistered and blackened, 15 to 20 minutes.
5. Remove peppers from the oven and immediately transfer to a plastic or paper bag, seal, and set aside for 20 minutes.
6. Peel off blackened skin and cut peppers into 1/2-inch strips.
7. Combine olive oil, garlic, balsamic vinegar, salt, basil, and seasoned pepper in a wide-mouth jar and shake until marinade is well blended.
8. Place pepper slices into the jar, shake, and refrigerate until fully cooled and flavors have melded, 3 hours to overnight.
