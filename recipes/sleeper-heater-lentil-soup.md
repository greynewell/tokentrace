## Ingredients

- 3 cups brown lentils
- ¼ cup chopped fresh parsley
- ¼ cup curry paste
- 1 tablespoon grated fresh ginger root
- 2 tablespoons chopped fresh oregano
- 2 cloves garlic, chopped
- 1 tablespoon all-purpose flour
- 1 teaspoon paprika

## Instructions

1. Place the lentils, parsley, curry paste, ginger, oregano, garlic, flour and paprika into a 2.5 quart (5 liter) slow cooker.
2. Mix until blended.
3. Fill with water to within 1/2 inch of the top.
4. Cover, and cook on high for 4 hours, or longer if you can.
