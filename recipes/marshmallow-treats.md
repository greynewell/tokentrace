## Ingredients

- Â¼ cup butter
- 4 cups miniature marshmallows
- 5 cups crisp rice cereal

## Instructions

1. Melt butter in a large saucepan over low heat; add marshmallows and stir until melted and well combined. Cook 2 minutes longer, stirring constantly. Remove from heat.
2. Stir in crispy rice cereal until well coated.
3. Press mixture evenly and firmly into a buttered 9x13-inch pan using a buttered spatula or waxed paper. Cut into 2-inch squares when cool.
