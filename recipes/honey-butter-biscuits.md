## Ingredients

- 2 cups all-purpose flour
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 1 teaspoon salt
- ⅓ cup vegetable shortening
- ¾ cup milk
- ½ cup honey
- ½ cup butter

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Sift flour, baking powder, and salt together into a bowl.
3. Combine shortening with flour mixture using fingers until the mixture is crumbly and resembles cornmeal.
4. Stir milk in gradually until dough is well combined.
5. Place dough on a floured surface; shape into a 1/2-inch thick rectangle about 12 inches wide.
6. Put honey and butter into a microwave-safe bowl; microwave until melted, 15 to 20 seconds.
7. Stir.
8. Spread 1/2 the honey-butter mixture on the dough surface; roll up like a jellyroll and cut into 12 slices, about 1 inch thick.
9. Arrange biscuits on a baking sheet 1/2-inch apart; spread remaining honey-butter on each.
10. Bake in the preheated oven until golden, 12 to 15 minutes.
