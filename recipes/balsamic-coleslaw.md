## Ingredients

- 1  serving cooking spray
- 1 cup shredded cabbage
- 1 cup shredded red cabbage
- ½ cup shredded carrots
- 1 teaspoon garlic powder
- 1 teaspoon onion powder
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ½ teaspoon black pepper
- ¼ cup balsamic vinegar

## Instructions

1. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat.
2. Spray with cooking spray.
3. Cook the cabbage, red cabbage, and carrots in the hot skillet until they begin to soften, about 2 minutes.
4. Stir in the garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and pepper.
5. Continue to cook and stir until cabbage and carrots are soft, 8 to 10 minutes.
6. Stir balsamic vinegar into the cabbage mixture just before removing from heat.
