## Ingredients

- Vegetable oil for frying
- 1 cup McCormick® Beer Batter Seafood Batter Mix
- 1 tablespoon Old Bay® Seasoning
- ⅔ cup beer or water
- 1 ½ pounds cod fillets, cut into serving-size pieces

## Instructions

1. Pour oil into deep fryer or large heavy skillet, filling no more than 1/3 full.
2. Heat to 375 degrees F on medium heat.
3. Mix Batter Mix and   in medium bowl.
4. Add beer or water; stir until smooth.
5. Dip fish into batter.
6. Shake off excess.
7. Fry fish, a few pieces at a time, 3 to 5 minutes or until golden brown, turning once to brown evenly.
8. Drain on paper towels.
