## Ingredients

- 1 medium head cabbage, shredded
- 1  red bell pepper, seeded and chopped
- 1  yellow bell pepper, seeded and chopped
- 1  onion, chopped
- 3  carrots, grated
- 2 stalks celery, chopped
- 1 ½ cups cider vinegar
- 1 cup vegetable oil
- ½ cup white sugar
- 2 teaspoons dry mustard
- 3 teaspoons dried dill weed

## Instructions

1. In a large bowl, toss together cabbage, red pepper, yellow pepper, onion, carrots and celery. Refrigerate until serving.
2. In a small bowl, whisk together the vinegar, oil, sugar, mustard and dill. Pour over salad and toss to coat just before serving.
