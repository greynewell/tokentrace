## Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon butter, or as needed
- 1 cup white sugar
- 1  lemon, zested
- 1 cup plain yogurt
- 3  eggs
- ⅓ cup canola oil
- ½ teaspoon vanilla extract
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- ¼ teaspoon salt

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a metal loaf pan with butter.
2. Whisk sugar and lemon zest together in a large bowl. Whisk in yogurt, eggs, oil, and vanilla extract until well blended.
3. Mix flour, baking powder, and salt together in a separate bowl. Whisk into the yogurt mixture until just blended. Pour batter into the prepared loaf pan.
4. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean, about 1 hour. Cool in the pan for 10 minutes. Invert cake onto a cooling rack and cool completely.
