## Ingredients

- ½ cup mayonnaise
- ½ cup Parmesan cheese
- 1 (2 ounce) can anchovies
- 3 tablespoons lemon juice
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 6 cloves garlic
- 1  jalapeno pepper, seeded
- ¼ teaspoon salt

## Instructions

1. Blend mayonnaise, Parmesan cheese, anchovies, lemon juice, olive oil, garlic, jalapeno pepper, and salt together in a food processor until smooth.
2. Refrigerate dressing for flavors to blend, at least 4 hours.
