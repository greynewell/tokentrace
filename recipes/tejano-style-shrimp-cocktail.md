## Ingredients

- 1 pound cooked medium shrimp, chilled
- ½ large cucumber, cut into 1/2 inch cubes
- ½ large tomato, cut into 1/2 inch cubes
- 8 medium (4-1/8" long)s green onions, thinly sliced
- 1 ounce fresh cilantro, finely chopped
- 1  serrano pepper, thinly sliced
- 1 (8 ounce) can tomato sauce
- 2 tablespoons white vinegar
- 1  lime

## Instructions

1. In a large bowl, combine shrimp, cucumber, tomato, green onion, cilantro, and serrano pepper.
2. Stir in tomato sauce, and vinegar.
3. Squeeze lime juice over mixture.
