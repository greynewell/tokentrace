## Ingredients

- 1 ½ pounds ground sirloin
- 1 pound VELVEETA®, cut into 1/2-inch cubes
- 1 (10 ounce) can RO*TEL Diced Tomatoes and Green Chilies, undrained
- 6  hamburger buns
- 6  pieces leaf lettuce
- 1 large tomato, cut into 6 slices

## Instructions

1. Heat grill to medium heat.
2. Shape meat into 6 (1/2-inch-thick) patties.
3. Grill 4 to 5 min. on each side or until done (160 degrees F).
4. Meanwhile, microwave VELVEETA and RO*TEL in microwaveable bowl on HIGH 5 min. or until VELVEETA is completely melted, stirring after 3 min.
5. Place burgers on bottom halves of buns; top each with 2 Tbsp.
6. VELVEETA sauce.
7. Cover with lettuce, tomatoes and tops of buns.
