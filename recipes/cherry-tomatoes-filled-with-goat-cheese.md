## Ingredients

- ¼ pound goat cheese
- ¼ cup minced fresh basil
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ½ teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
- 24  cherry tomatoes

## Instructions

1. In a medium-sized bowl, combine the cheese, basil, salt and pepper.
2. Mix with a fork until well blended.
3. Cut the top off each cherry tomato.
4. Using a small spoon, carefully scoop out the pulp to make a hollow, yet sturdy, shell.
5. Drain off any juice that may be in the tomato shells.
6. Using a small spoon, scoop about 1 teaspoon of the cheese mixture and carefully stuff into a tomato shell.
7. Repeat until tomatoes or cheese mixture is used up.
8. Arrange the filled tomatoes on a platter to serve.
