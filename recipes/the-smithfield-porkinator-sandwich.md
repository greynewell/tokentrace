## Ingredients

- 1  Smithfield® Peppercorn & Garlic Seasoned Pork Tenderloin
- ½ cup ranch salad dressing
- 3 tablespoons barbecue sauce
- 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
- 1 medium sweet yellow onion, thinly sliced
- 6  large ciabatta rolls, sliced open horizontally and toasted
- 12 slices Smithfield® Deli Style Ham
- ¾ cup shredded sharp Cheddar cheese
- 12 slices Smithfield® Hometown Original Hickory Smoked Bacon, cooked crisp and drained
- Arugula or baby spinach leaves

## Instructions

1. Heat oven to 425 degrees F.
2. Place tenderloin on rack in shallow roasting pan.
3. Roast about 30 minutes until internal temperature reaches 150 degrees F.
4. Let stand 10 minutes before slicing very thin.
5. Meanwhile, mix together salad dressing and barbecue sauce; set aside.
6. Saute onion slices in oil over medium heat until very tender and dark golden brown.
7. Place bottom half of each toasted roll on baking sheet; spread with barbecue ranch dressing.
8. Top with ham, pork tenderloin, caramelized onions, shredded cheese and bacon.
9. Place under broil until cheese is melted, 1 to 2 minutes.
10. Remove from oven, top with arugula and crowns of rolls to serve.
