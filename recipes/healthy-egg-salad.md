## Ingredients

- 6  hard-boiled eggs
- ¼ cup fat-free mayonnaise
- 1  celery stalk, chopped
- 1  carrot, shredded
- 2 tablespoons minced red onion
- 2 tablespoons Dijon mustard
- 1 tablespoon white wine vinegar
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ½ teaspoon hot pepper sauce

## Instructions

1. Mash hard-boiled eggs using a fork in a large bowl; add mayonnaise, celery, carrot, red onion, Dijon mustard, vinegar, salt, and hot pepper sauce.
2. Mix well.
3. Refrigerate until chilled, at least 30 minutes.
