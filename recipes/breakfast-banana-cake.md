## Ingredients

- 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
- ½ cup rolled oats
- 1 teaspoon baking soda
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon, or to taste
- ½ cup brown sugar
- 2  eggs, beaten
- 1 cup bananas, sliced
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 1 tablespoon grated orange zest
- ½ cup melted butter
- ¼ cup flaked coconut
- ¼ cup sliced almonds, or as needed

## Instructions

1. Preheat an oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C).
2. Grease and flour a 9-inch round cake pan.
3. Combine the flour, oats, baking soda, salt, and cinnamon in a mixing bowl; set aside.
4. Beat the brown sugar, eggs, bananas, vanilla extract, orange zest, and butter in a bowl until smooth.
5. Stir the dry mixture into the egg mixture until smooth; fold in the coconut and almonds.
6. Pour into the prepared cake pan.
7. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean, about 35 minutes.
8. Cool in the pan for 10 minutes before removing to cool completely on a wire rack.
