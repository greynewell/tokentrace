## Ingredients

- 4 cups crispy corn cereal squares
- 1 cup dried mixed fruit, chopped
- ¼ cup yogurt covered raisins
- ⅓ cup chopped mixed nuts
- .66 cup banana chips

## Instructions

1. In a medium bowl, mix together crispy corn cereal squares, dried fruit mix, yogurt covered raisins, mixed nuts and banana chips.
2. Store in sealed, airtight containers.
