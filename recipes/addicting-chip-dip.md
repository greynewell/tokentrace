## Ingredients

- 2 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened
- ¼ cup milk
- 1 ½ teaspoons anchovy paste
- 3 tablespoons grated onion
- 1 pinch garlic powder

## Instructions

1. In a medium-sized mixing bowl, combine cream cheese and milk until creamy.
2. Add more milk for a thinner dip.
3. Using a fork, whip the anchovy paste, onion, and garlic powder into the mixture.
4. Cover and refrigerate this dip at least one hour before serving.
