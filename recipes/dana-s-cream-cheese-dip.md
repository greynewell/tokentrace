## Ingredients

- ⅓ cup toasted pine nuts
- 1 cup fresh basil leaves
- 2 cloves garlic
- 2 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened
- 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar

## Instructions

1. Place the pine nuts, basil, and garlic in a food processor.
2. Pulse until coarsely chopped.
3. Mix the cream cheese and balsamic vinegar together in a bowl until well blended, then stir in the pine nut mixture.
