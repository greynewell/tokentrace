## Ingredients

- 1 cup chopped dates
- ½ cup raisins
- ½ cup chopped pitted prunes
- 1 cup water
- ½ cup butter, cut into pieces
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- 2  eggs, lightly beaten
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 1 cup all-purpose flour
- 1 teaspoon baking soda
- ½ teaspoon baking powder
- ½ cup chopped walnuts  (Optional)

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Grease and flour a muffin pan, or use paper liners.
3. In a saucepan over medium-high heat, combine dates, raisins, prunes, and water.
4. Boil for 5 minutes, then stir in butter and salt; set aside to cool.
5. When fruit mixture is cool, transfer to a large bowl.
6. Beat in eggs and vanilla.
7. Stir together flour, baking soda and baking powder, then blend into fruit mixture. fold in nuts.
8. Spoon into muffin cups 2/3 to 3/4 full.
9. Bake in the preheated oven for 15 to 20 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
10. Allow to cool.
