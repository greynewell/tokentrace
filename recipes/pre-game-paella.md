## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
- 2 cups uncooked regular long-grain white rice
- 4 cups Swanson® Chicken Stock, heated
- 1 cup Pace® Thick & Chunky Salsa
- 1 teaspoon ground turmeric
- 1 (16 ounce) package turkey kielbasa, sliced
- 12 ounces frozen peeled and deveined small cooked shrimp, thawed
- 1 (10 ounce) package refrigerated fully-cooked chicken breast strips

## Instructions

1. Heat the oil in a 12-inch skillet over medium heat.
2. Add the rice and cook for 30 seconds, stirring constantly.
3. Stir in the stock, salsa and turmeric and heat to a boil.
4. Reduce the heat to low.
5. Cover the skillet and cook for 15 minutes.
6. Stir in the kielbasa, shrimp and chicken.
7. Cover the skillet and cook for 5 minutes or until the rice is tender and most of the liquid is absorbed.
