## Ingredients

- 2 slices applewood smoked bacon
- 2 slices whole wheat bread
- 2 tablespoons peanut butter
- 1 tablespoon honey

## Instructions

1. Place the bacon in a skillet, and cook over medium-high heat, turning occasionally, until evenly browned and crisp, about 10 minutes.
2. Drain the bacon slices on a paper towel-lined plate.
3. Toast the slices of whole wheat bread in a toaster, and spread a toasted slice with peanut butter and honey.
4. Place the bacon on the honey, top with the remaining toasted bread slice, and serve.
