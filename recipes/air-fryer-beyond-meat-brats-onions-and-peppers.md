## Ingredients

- 2 links vegetarian bratwurst sausage (such as Beyond Meat®), sliced
- 1 medium onion, sliced into petals
- ½  green bell pepper, sliced into thin strips
- ½  red bell pepper, sliced into thin strips
- 2 teaspoons olive oil
- 1 pinch salt and ground black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Preheat the air fryer to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Combine vegetarian sausage slices, onion, bell peppers, olive oil, salt, and pepper in a bowl; stir to coat. Pour mixture into the basket of the air fryer.
3. Cook in the preheated air fryer for 10 minutes. Shake basket and cook for 5 more minutes. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center of sausage should read 165 degrees F (74 degrees C).
