## Ingredients

- 2 pounds Yukon Gold potatoes, peeled and cubed
- ½ pound broccoli rabe, thick stems peeled
- ½ cup olive oil
- 4  anchovy filets, rinsed and chopped
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 ½ pounds mussels, cleaned and debearded
- 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
- ½ cup water
- salt to taste

## Instructions

1. In a medium saucepan, cover the potatoes with cold water.
2. Add salt, and bring to a boil.
3. Cook until tender, about 15 minutes.
4. Drain.
5. Bring a saucepan of salted water to a boil.
6. Add the broccoli rabe, and cook until just tender; it should be bright green.
7. Drain well, and cut into 2 inch lengths.
8. Combine the oil, anchovies, and garlic in a large, deep skillet.
9. Cook over high heat, mashing the anchovies, for about 1 minute.
10. Spread the mussels in the skillet, top with the potatoes, broccoli rabe, and chopped parsley.
11. Add 1/2 cup water, and season with salt.
12. Cover, and cook until mussels are open.
13. Serve.
