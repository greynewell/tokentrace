## Ingredients

- 1 large head cauliflower, cut into florets
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 2 tablespoons hoisin sauce
- 1 tablespoon Sriracha sauce
- salt and ground black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
2. Combine cauliflower florets, olive oil, hoisin sauce, Sriracha sauce, salt, and pepper in a large bowl; toss to combine. Spread out in a single layer on a baking sheet.
3. Bake in the preheated oven until cauliflower is soft and cooked through, about 30 minutes, turning once after 20 minutes.
