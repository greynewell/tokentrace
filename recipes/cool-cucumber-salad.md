## Ingredients

- 2 large  cucumbers, peeled and thinly sliced
- ½ (8 ounce) carton sour cream
- 1  green onion, chopped
- 2 tablespoons chopped fresh dill
- ½ teaspoon kosher salt
- ½ teaspoon cracked black pepper

## Instructions

1. Place cucumbers into a salad bowl and combine with sour cream, green onion, dill, kosher salt, and pepper.
2. Cover bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate salad for 30 minutes before serving.
