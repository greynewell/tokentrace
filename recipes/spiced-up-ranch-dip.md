## Ingredients

- 1 (16 ounce) container sour cream
- 2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese
- 1 cup bacon bits
- 1 (1 ounce) package ranch dressing mix (such as Hidden Valley Ranch ®)
- 1 teaspoon chili powder
- 1 teaspoon cayenne pepper

## Instructions

1. Stir the sour cream, Cheddar cheese, bacon bits, ranch mix, chili powder, and cayenne pepper in a bowl.
2. Chill in refrigerator 1 hour before serving.
