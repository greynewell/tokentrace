## Ingredients

- 3 (15 ounce) cans peas, drained
- 1 (4 ounce) jar pimento peppers, drained and chopped
- ½ pound Colby cheese, cut into 1/4-inch cubes
- ½  onion, diced
- 1 cup mayonnaise
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper

## Instructions

1. Mix peas, pimento peppers, Colby cheese, onion, mayonnaise, salt, and black pepper together in a bowl with a lid.
2. Cover and refrigerate for at least 12 hours before serving for best flavor; stir again before serving.
