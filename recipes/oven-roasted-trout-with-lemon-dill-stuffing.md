## Ingredients

- 1 cup soft bread crumbs
- ½  sweet onion, minced
- 2 tablespoons chopped fresh dill
- 2 teaspoons grated lemon zest
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- ¼ cup olive oil, divided
- salt and pepper to taste
- 4  trout, cleaned and head removed

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Line a baking sheet with aluminum foil.
2. Toss bread crumbs with onion, dill, lemon zest, garlic, and 1/2 of the olive oil. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Press stuffing into trout, then brush with remaining olive oil; season with salt and pepper. Place trout onto the prepared baking sheet.
3. Roast in the preheated oven until skin begins to brown, and the flesh flakes easily with a fork, about 20 minutes.
