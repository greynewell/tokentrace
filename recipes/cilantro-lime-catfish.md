## Ingredients

- ¼ cup butter
- ¼ cup chopped fresh cilantro
- ½ teaspoon chopped garlic
- 5 (5 ounce) fillets catfish
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- ½  lemon, juiced
- ½  lime, juiced

## Instructions

1. Melt butter in a skillet over medium heat.
2. Stir cilantro and garlic into the melted butter; cook about 1 minute.
3. Season catfish fillets with salt and black pepper; lay gently into the butter mixture.
4. Place a cover on the skillet.
5. Cook fillets 10 minutes, turn, and continue cooking until the fish flakes easily with a fork and is lightly browned, about 10 minutes more.
6. Squeeze lemon juice and lime juice over the fillets to serve.
