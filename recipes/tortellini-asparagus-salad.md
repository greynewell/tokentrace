## Ingredients

- 1 (9 ounce) package refrigerated cheese tortellini
- 8 ounces fresh asparagus, trimmed and cut into 2-inch pieces
- ½ cup bottled ranch salad dressing
- ¼ cup ReaLime® lime juice from concentrate
- 2 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese
- ¼ teaspoon Cracked pepper

## Instructions

1. Cook tortellini with asparagus in large saucepan according to package directions for pasta.
2. Drain.
3. Meanwhile, combine ranch dressing, ReaLime® and Parmesan cheese in a large bowl.
4. Add pasta mixture; toss gently to coat.
5. Chill at least 2 hours.
6. Before serving, sprinkle with pepper.
