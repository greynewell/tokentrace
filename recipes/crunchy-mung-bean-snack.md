## Ingredients

- 1 cup dried mung beans
- ¼ cup vegetable oil, or more as needed
- 1  green onion, sliced
- 1  Thai chile pepper, or more to taste, sliced  (Optional)
- 1 pinch salt

## Instructions

1. Put mung beans into a large container and cover with several inches of cool water.
2. Soak beans for 1 hour.
3. Drain mung beans and spread them out onto a flat surface.
4. Pat gently with paper towel to blot some of the water; air-dry beans for 15 minutes.
5. Heat oil in a skillet over medium heat.
6. Cook and stir beans in hot oil until evenly browned and crunchy, about 5 minutes.
7. Drain beans and transfer to a mixing bowl; add chile pepper, green onion, and salt and stir to coat.
