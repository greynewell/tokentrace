## Ingredients

- 5  Yukon Gold potatoes, peeled and thinly sliced
- 1 small onion, chopped
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- 1 cup shredded sharp Cheddar cheese
- 1 ½ cups cubed fully cooked ham
- 1 quart heavy cream

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Place potatoes, onion, garlic, Cheddar cheese, and ham in a 9x13-inch baking dish. Gently stir until well combined. Pour cream over the potato mixture.
3. Bake in the preheated oven until the potatoes are tender, about 1 hour.
