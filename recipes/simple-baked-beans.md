## Ingredients

- 2 (16 ounce) cans baked beans with pork
- ¼ cup molasses
- ¼ cup chopped onions
- 4 tablespoons brown sugar
- 1 tablespoon prepared mustard
- 2 tablespoons ketchup
- 2 slices bacon, chopped

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a casserole dish with cooking spray.
2. Mix together baked beans with pork, molasses, onions, brown sugar, ketchup, and mustard together in a large bowl. Transfer mixture to the prepared dish and top with chopped bacon.
3. Cover and bake in the preheated oven until thickened, about 3 hours.
