## Ingredients

- 1 (32 ounce) jar dill pickles
- 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
- 1 pound sliced ham
- 10 (6 inch) flour tortillas

## Instructions

1. Spread cream cheese on one side of a tortilla.
2. Place a slice or two of ham over this.
3. Spread another layer of cream cheese over the ham.
4. Roll a pickle up in the tortilla and slice the roll  into finger food-sized pieces.
5. Refrigerate the rolls if you aren't serving them immediately.
