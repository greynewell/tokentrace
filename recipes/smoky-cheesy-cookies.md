## Ingredients

- ½ cup cold unsalted butter, cut into 16 pieces
- 4 ounces smoked Gouda cheese, cut into tiny cubes
- 2 ounces sharp Cheddar cheese, shredded
- ¾ teaspoon sea salt
- ¼ teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
- 1 pinch cayenne pepper
- 1 ¼ cups all-purpose flour

## Instructions

1. Combine butter, Gouda cheese, Cheddar cheese, sea salt, black pepper, and cayenne pepper in a food processor; pulse until butter is in bits and the mixture forms small curds.
2. Add flour; pulse until dough looks moist and forms large popcorn-sized curds.
3. Turn dough out onto a flat surface; knead gently just until it comes together and you can shape it into a ball.
4. Divide into 2 pieces.
5. Pat each piece into a disk.
6. Place 1 disk between 2 sheets of parchment paper.
7. Roll to a thickness of 1/4 inch.
8. Repeat with second disk.
9. Stack sheets of dough on a baking sheet.
10. Freeze until firm, about 1 hour.
11. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
12. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper or a silicone baking mat.
13. Peel parchment paper off 1 sheet of frozen dough.
14. Cut into cookies using a 1 1/2-inch-diameter cutter; arrange 1 inch apart on the prepared baking sheet.
15. Repeat with the second sheet of dough.
16. Bake cookies in the preheated oven until lightly golden on the bottom, about 9 minutes.
17. Rotate baking sheet.
18. Continue baking until bottoms are golden brown and tops are lightly golden, about 5 minutes more.
19. Cool on the baking sheet for 2 minutes.
20. Transfer to a rack to cool completely, about 10 minutes.
21. Gather dough scraps, roll to a thickness of 1/4-inch, and freeze until firm, about 15 minutes.
22. Cut into cookies and bake.
