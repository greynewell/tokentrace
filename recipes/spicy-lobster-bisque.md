## Ingredients

- 3 tablespoons butter
- ¼ cup chopped onion
- 3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
- 1 ½ cups milk
- 1 ½ cups heavy whipping cream
- 1 cup cooked and cubed lobster, or more to taste
- 1 ½ tablespoons cooking sherry
- 1 tablespoon paprika
- ⅛ teaspoon Cajun seasoning
- salt and ground black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Melt butter in a large pot over medium heat.
2. Add onion; cook and stir until tender, about 10 minutes.
3. Add flour; stir until combined and cooked, 1 to 2 minutes.
4. Pour in milk; cook and stir until thickened, about 5 minutes.
5. Add heavy cream and lobster, stirring constantly, until bisque re-thickens, about 5 minutes.
6. Stir cooking sherry, paprika, and Cajun seasoning into bisque.
7. Cook until flavors combine, 2 to 4 minutes.
8. Season with salt and black pepper.
