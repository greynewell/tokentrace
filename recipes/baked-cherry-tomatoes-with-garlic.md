## Ingredients

- 1 pint cherry tomatoes
- 4 cloves garlic, slivered
- 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil  (Optional)
- 1 pinch kosher salt

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Cut a slit in one side of the cherry tomatoes, and insert a sliver of garlic into each. Arrange tomatoes in a single layer on a baking sheet. Drizzle with olive oil and sprinkle with salt.
3. Bake tomatoes about 20 minutes in the preheated oven, until slightly shriveled. Serve warm.
