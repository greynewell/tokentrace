## Ingredients

- 1 pound ground beef
- 3 tablespoons chili seasoning mix
- 2  chipotle peppers in adobo sauce, minced
- ½ fluid ounce beer
- ¼ cup mayonnaise
- 1  chipotle pepper in adobo sauce, minced
- 6 (1 ounce) slices white bread
- 6 (1/2 ounce) slices pepperjack cheese

## Instructions

1. Mix together the ground beef, chili seasoning mix, 2 minced chipotle peppers with adobo sauce, and the beer in a bowl.
2. Divide the mixture evenly into three patties.
3. Stir together the mayonnaise and 1 minced chipotle pepper with adobo sauce in a small bowl.
4. Divide the mixture between the bread slices and spread evenly.
5. Place a slice of pepperjack cheese on top of the mayonnaise mixture on each slice of bread.
6. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat.
7. Cook the patties in the skillet until no longer pink in the center, 5 to 7 minutes each side for well done.
8. Remove each burger to a slice of bread, sandwiching them with the remaining slices.
9. Drain the skillet, reserving 2 tablespoons of the grease.
10. Heat the reserved grease in the skillet over medium-high heat.
11. Grill the sandwiches in the skillet until the bread is golden brown and the cheese is melted, 1 to 2 minutes per side.
