## Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon salt
- 4  broccolini stalks, bottoms trimmed
- 2 teaspoons olive oil
- 1 pinch red pepper flakes
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- 1 ½ cups baby greens mix (such as spinach, chard, kale, and arugula)
- 2 teaspoons lemon vinaigrette, or to taste
- coarse sea salt to taste

## Instructions

1. Bring a pot of water to a boil; add 1 teaspoon salt.
2. Reduce heat and add broccolini stalks; simmer until broccolini turns bright green, about 1 minute.
3. Transfer broccolini to a bowl of ice water; drain when cool.
4. Chop broccolini into 1-inch pieces.
5. Return pot to stove; heat olive oil and red pepper flakes over medium heat.
6. Add broccolini; cook until warmed through.
7. Stir in garlic; cook until fragrant but not brown, 30 seconds to 1 minute.
8. Remove pot from heat.
9. Stir baby greens, vinaigrette, and sea salt into broccolini mixture; toss just until greens are warmed through but not wilted, 1 to 2 minutes.
