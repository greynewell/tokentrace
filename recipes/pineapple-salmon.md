## Ingredients

- 1 ½ pounds salmon fillets
- 1  serving cooking spray
- ½ cup water
- 1 (8 ounce) can crushed pineapple
- ¼ cup reduced-sodium teriyaki sauce
- 3 tablespoons pineapple preserves
- 3 tablespoons Asian sweet chili sauce
- 2 teaspoons reduced-sodium teriyaki sauce

## Instructions

1. Heat a large saute pan over medium-high heat for 2 minutes. Coat both sides of salmon fillets with cooking spray and add to the hot pan. Cook for 4 minutes. Flip, cover, and cook for 4 minutes more.
2. Whisk water, crushed pineapple, and 1/4 cup teriyaki sauce together in a bowl. Pour sauce over salmon; reduce heat to medium-low, cover, and cook for 2 minutes.
3. Stir pineapple preserves, chili sauce, and remaining 2 teaspoons teriyaki sauce together. Spoon evenly over salmon, cover, and remove from heat. Let stand for 1 to 2 minutes.
