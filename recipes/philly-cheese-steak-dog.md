## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- ½ large onion, diced
- ½ pound shaved rib-eye, chopped
- 8  hot dogs
- 8  hot dog buns
- 1 (8 ounce) jar processed cheese spread (such as Cheese Whiz®)

## Instructions

1. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat; cook and stir onion in the hot oil until tender, 5 to 10 minutes.
2. Transfer onion to a bowl.
3. Cook and stir rib-eye in the same skillet until meat is no longer red and liquid is evaporated, 5 to 10 minutes.
4. Stir onion into cooked rib-eye.
5. Bring a pot of water to a boil; cook hot dogs in the boiling water until cooked through, 5 to 10 minutes.
6. Place each cooked hot dog into a bun.
7. Spoon rib-eye mixture over hot dog; top with cheese spread.
