## Ingredients

- 2 cups chicken broth, or more as needed
- 1 cup whole milk, divided
- 2 teaspoons poultry seasoning
- 1 teaspoon ground turmeric
- ½ teaspoon onion powder
- ½ teaspoon granulated garlic
- ½ teaspoon ground paprika
- ¼ teaspoon black pepper
- 1 pinch cayenne pepper
- 1 cup cream
- 1 cup all-purpose flour, or more as needed

## Instructions

1. Combine 2 cups chicken broth, 1/2 cup milk, poultry seasoning, turmeric, onion powder, garlic, paprika, pepper, and cayenne in a saucepan over medium heat; bring to a boil.
2. Combine cream, flour, and remaining milk in a bowl. Add a ladle or two of hot broth mixture and whisk quickly to combine. Pour the mixture back into the saucepan; whisk together and bring to a boil. Cook until thickened, 2 to 3 minutes.
3. Add additional broth or flour to adjust consistency. Check seasoning and adjust as needed.
