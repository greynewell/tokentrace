## Ingredients

- 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese
- 1 (4 ounce) can small shrimp, drained
- ½ (12 ounce) bottle cocktail sauce

## Instructions

1. Spread the cream cheese on the bottom of a 8 inch square serving dish.
2. Layer the shrimp over the cream cheese.
3. Pour the cocktail sauce over the top.
