## Ingredients

- 8 cups popped popcorn
- 4 cups crispy rice cereal squares
- 2 cups small pretzel twists
- 1 cup pecan halves
- 1 cup packed brown sugar
- ½ cup butter
- ¼ cup light corn syrup
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- ½ teaspoon baking soda

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 300 degrees F (150 degrees C).
2. In a large bowl, combine the popcorn, cereal, pretzels and pecans.
3. In a saucepan over medium heat, combine the brown sugar, butter and corn syrup.
4. Cook and stir until mixture comes to a boil.
5. Reduce heat to medium low; cook without stirring for 5 minutes.
6. Remove saucepan from heat and stir in vanilla and baking soda.
7. Pour over popcorn mixture and toss until evenly coated.
8. Bake at 300 degrees F (150 degrees F) for 30 minutes, stirring after 15 minutes.
9. Transfer to large piece of parchment paper and cool completely.
10. Break into chunks once cool.
