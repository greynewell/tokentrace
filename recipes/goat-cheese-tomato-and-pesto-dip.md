## Ingredients

- 1 (8 ounce) package goat cheese, softened
- 1 (8 ounce) jar pesto, or as needed
- 3  tomatoes, chopped
- 1 (8 ounce) loaf French bread, sliced

## Instructions

1. Spread goat cheese into 1/4-inch layer on a large serving plate.
2. Spread a thin layer of pesto over goat cheese layer.
3. Top with chopped tomatoes.
4. Serve with French bread slices.
