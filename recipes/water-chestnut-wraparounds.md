## Ingredients

- Â¼ cup shoyu (soy and wheat soy sauce)
- 2 tablespoons white sugar
- 2 (5 ounce) cans water chestnuts, drained
- 10 slices bacon, cut in half

## Instructions

1. Combine shoyu sauce and sugar.
2. Stir in water chestnuts.
3. Marinate chestnuts overnight.
4. Wrap bacon slice around each chestnut.
5. Fasten with a wooden pick.
6. Place on a rack in shallow baking pan.
7. Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 30-35 minutes.
8. Serve hot.
