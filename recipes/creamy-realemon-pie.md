## Ingredients

- 3  egg yolks
- 1 (14 ounce) can sweetened condensed milk
- ½ cup ReaLemon® lemon juice from concentrate
- 2 drops yellow food coloring  (Optional)
- 1  (8- or 9-inch) baked pastry shell or graham cracker crumb crust
- ⅓ cup Whipped cream or whipped topping

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 325 degrees F.
2. Beat egg yolks in medium-sized bowl with rotary beater or fork; gradually beat in condensed milk and ReaLemon®.
3. Stir in food coloring if desired.
4. Pour into pastry shell or crumb crust.
5. Bake 30 to 35 minutes or until set.
6. Remove from oven.
7. Cool 1 hour.
8. Chill at least 3 hours.
9. Before serving, spread whipped topping over pie.
10. Refrigerate leftovers.
