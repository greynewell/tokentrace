## Ingredients

- 1 (1 pound) loaf cocktail rye bread
- 1 (3 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
- 1 large cucumber, peeled and thinly sliced
- 1  onion, halved and thinly sliced
- 6 tablespoons mayonnaise

## Instructions

1. Place half of the cocktail rye bread on a clean work surface.
2. Spread each slice with cream cheese.
3. Place 1 or 2 slices of cucumber on each, and then 1 or 2 slices of onion.
4. Spread remaining slices with mayonnaise and place atop onions to complete the sandwiches.
