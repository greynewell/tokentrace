## Ingredients

- ½ cup spicy brown mustard
- 1 cup honey
- 1 ½ cups mayonnaise
- 2 tablespoons lemon juice
- 1 tablespoon onion powder
- ¼ cup finely chopped green onion  (Optional)

## Instructions

1. In a medium bowl, stir together the mustard, honey, mayonnaise, lemon juice and onion powder.
2. For best results, use an electric hand mixer, but do not whip.
3. Stir in green onion.
4. Use immediately, or refrigerate until needed.
