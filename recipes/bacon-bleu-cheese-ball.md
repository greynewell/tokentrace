## Ingredients

- 2 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened
- 1 (3 ounce) package bacon bits (such as Oscar Mayer®)
- 3 ounces crumbled blue cheese
- ¼ cup blue cheese salad dressing, or to taste
- 2  green onions, chopped
- ½ teaspoon garlic powder
- ¼ cup dried parsley, or as needed  (Optional)

## Instructions

1. Stir cream cheese, bacon bits, blue cheese, salad dressing, green onions, and garlic powder together in a bowl.
2. Form mixture into 2 balls, cover each with plastic wrap, and refrigerate until chilled and firm, 3 hours or overnight.
3. Remove cheese balls from plastic wrap and roll in parsley.
