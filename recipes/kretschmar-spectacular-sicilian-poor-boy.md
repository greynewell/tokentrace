## Ingredients

- 1  white hoagie (sub) roll, split
- 3 tablespoons cream cheese, softened
- 2 tablespoons (purchased) basil pesto
- 2 tablespoons sliced ripe olives, drained
- 1  pepperoncini, drained and thinly sliced
- 2 piece (blank)s canned artichoke hearts, packed in brine, drained and thinly sliced vertically
- 2 slices  KRETSCHMAR® Genoa Salami
- 2 slices  KRETSCHMAR® Peppered Hickory Turkey Breast
- 2 slices  KRETSCHMAR® Brown Sugar Ham
- 1 medium Roma tomato, cored and thinly sliced vertically
- 3 leaves radicchio, washed and patted dry

## Instructions

1. In a small mixing bowl, mix cream cheese with basil pesto until smooth and creamy. Stir in olives and sliced pepperoncini.
2. Open hoagie roll and evenly spread both insides with cream cheese mixture. Place artichoke heart slices over cream cheese. Place on top half of roll, layer Genoa, turkey and ham.
3. Place on bottom half of roll, radicchio and tomato slice. Carefully place top onto bottom half of roll and press gently. Secure with long toothpicks, cut in half. Place on a plate and garnish with a whole pepperoncini and radicchio leaves (optional).
