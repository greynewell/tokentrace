## Ingredients

- ¾ cup olive oil
- ¼ cup balsamic vinegar
- 1 teaspoon chopped garlic
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ½ teaspoon ground black pepper
- 2 teaspoons olive oil, or to taste
- ½ pound bay scallops
- 1 bunch fresh asparagus, trimmed
- 1 (10 ounce) bag fresh spinach

## Instructions

1. Beat 3/4 cup olive oil, vinegar, garlic, salt, and pepper together in a bowl until smooth.
2. Heat 2 teaspoons olive oil in a skillet over medium-high heat.
3. Sear scallops and asparagus in hot oil until caramelizing, 2 to 3 minutes per side.
4. Put spinach in a large mixing bowl.
5. Drizzle dressing over spinach and toss to coat; top with scallops and asparagus.
