## Ingredients

- 2  bananas
- 1 cup frozen pineapple
- 2 tablespoons almond butter
- 1 tablespoon chia seeds
- 1 cup water

## Instructions

1. Combine bananas, pineapple, almond butter, and chia seeds in a blender; add water.
2. Blend until smooth.
