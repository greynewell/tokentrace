## Ingredients

- 1 (16 ounce) package mostaccioli pasta
- 1 (1 pound) pre-cooked ham steak, diced
- 1 pint heavy cream
- ⅜ cup white sugar
- 1 (12 ounce) can tomato paste
- 1 tablespoon soy sauce
- 1 teaspoon salt
- ½ pound shredded Swiss cheese

## Instructions

1. Bring a large pot of lightly-salted water to a boil.
2. Add pasta and cook until al dente, 8 to 10 minutes; drain well.
3. Place the pasta in a large bowl.
4. Heat a skillet over medium heat.
5. Cook the ham in the skillet for 3 minutes.
6. Add the cream, sugar, tomato paste, soy sauce, and salt; stir.
7. Reduce heat to medium low and cook and stir until the mixture reaches a low boil.
8. Pour the sauce over the drained pasta.
9. Sprinkle the Swiss cheese over the top of the pasta; stir until the pasta is evenly coated.
