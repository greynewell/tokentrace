## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 1  onion, chopped
- 2 cloves garlic, crushed
- 3 (6 ounce) lean pork tenderloins, sliced into small pieces
- 4 cups uncooked medium shrimp, peeled and deveined
- ½ small jicama (singkamas or yambean), peeled and cut into matchsticks
- 1 ½ cups bean sprouts
- ⅔ cup chickpeas, drained
- 1 cup fresh green beans, trimmed
- 2 stalks celery, diced
- 1 large carrot, minced
- 1 cup crushed salted peanuts
- 1 cup crushed salted peanuts, or to taste
- 1 head green leafy lettuce, or to taste
- 1 (16 ounce) package lumpia wrappers

## Instructions

1. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat.
2. Add onion and garlic; saute until onion is softened and browned, 10 to 15 minutes.
3. Transfer onion and garlic to a plate.
4. Cook pork and shrimp in the same skillet until browned, 5 to 7 minutes.
5. Add back the onion and garlic; season with salt and pepper.
6. Add jicama, bean sprouts, chickpeas, green beans, celery, and carrot.
7. Cook and stir until vegetables are tender, about 5 minutes.
8. Place filling mixture in a large bowl.
9. Add peanuts and mix well.
10. Place a lettuce leaf on top of a lumpia wrapper.
11. Place 1 tablespoon filling onto the lettuce.
12. Roll up the wrapper, folding 1 end over the filling.
13. Place on a serving platter.
14. Repeat with remaining lettuce, lumpia, and filling.
