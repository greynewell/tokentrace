## Ingredients

- 1 (2 to 3 pound) whole chicken, cut into pieces
- 1 teaspoon salt
- ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
- ¼ teaspoon garlic powder
- ¼ teaspoon paprika
- ¼ teaspoon dried thyme
- ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
- 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley
- ½ cup seasoned dry bread crumbs
- ¼ cup butter, melted
- ½ cup dry white wine

## Instructions

1. Measure salt and pepper, garlic powder, paprika, thyme, Parmesan cheese, parsley, and bread crumbs into a bag.
2. Shake chicken pieces in bag until well coated.
3. Oil roasting pan lightly, and arrange chicken in pan.
4. Sprinkle with melted butter or margarine.
5. Bake, uncovered, at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 45 minutes.
6. Remove from oven, and pour wine over all.
7. Cover, and bake an additional 15 minutes.
