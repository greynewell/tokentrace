## Ingredients

- 1 (11 ounce) can mandarin oranges, drained
- 1 (8 ounce) can crushed pineapple, drained
- 1 (6 ounce) package orange flavored Jell-O® mix
- 16 ounces cottage cheese
- 8 ounces frozen whipped topping, thawed

## Instructions

1. Combine oranges, pineapple, and gelatin mix in a mixing bowl. Mix well and chill for 30 minutes.
2. Add cottage cheese to the fruit mixture; stir to combine.
3. Gently fold in whipped topping; chill and serve.
