## Ingredients

- 2 cups thinly sliced fresh basil
- 9 tablespoons olive oil, divided
- ⅓ cup toasted sliced almonds, divided
- 1 tablespoon red wine vinegar
- 1  shallot, coarsely chopped
- 2 cloves garlic, coarsely chopped
- 1  lemon, zested
- ¼ teaspoon red pepper flakes
- 1 pound shrimp, peeled and deveined
- 5  zucchini
- kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Blend basil, 1/2 cup olive oil, 1/4 cup almonds, vinegar, shallot, garlic, lemon zest, and red pepper flakes in a blender until lemon basil dressing is smooth.
2. Heat 1 tablespoon olive oil in a skillet over medium-high heat; saute shrimp until cooked through and pink, 2 to 4 minutes.
3. Remove skillet from heat and mix shrimp with 2 tablespoons dressing in a bowl.
4. Run zucchini through a spiralizer to create spaghetti-size noodle shapes.
5. Add to skillet; cook and stir over medium heat until zucchini noodles are tender, 1 to 2 minutes.
6. Add 2 tablespoons lemon basil dressing and toss to coat.
7. Remove skillet from heat.
8. Arrange shrimp on top of zucchini noodles; season with salt and black pepper.
9. Top with remaining almonds.
