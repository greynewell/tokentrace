## Ingredients

- 2  Granny Smith apples, cut into cubes
- 1 large avocado - peeled, pitted, and cut into chunks
- ½  lemon, juiced
- 1 tablespoon honey, or more to taste
- 1 pinch ground cinnamon, or to taste

## Instructions

1. Combine apples and avocado together in a bowl.
2. Whisk lemon juice and honey together in a separate bowl; drizzle over apples and avocado.
3. Sprinkle cinnamon over salad and fold until evenly mixed.
4. Refrigerate until chilled, at least 30 minutes.
5. Serve cold.
