## Ingredients

- 1 pound seashell pasta
- 1 (10 ounce) package frozen chopped spinach
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 7 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 teaspoon dried red pepper flakes  (Optional)
- salt to taste

## Instructions

1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
2. Add pasta and spinach and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until pasta is al dente; drain and reserve.
3. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
4. Add garlic and red pepper flakes; saute for 5 minutes or until the garlic turns light gold.
5. Add cooked pasta and spinach to the skillet and mix well.
6. Season with salt and toss; serve.
