## Ingredients

- 4  string cheese sticks, or more as needed
- 12  pretzel sticks
- 1 leaf fresh spinach, cut into strips, or more as needed

## Instructions

1. Cut each string cheese stick into thirds. Snip the ends of each third into thin strings to look like a broom.
2. Place a pretzel stick into each piece of string cheese to create the broom handle. Tie a spinach strip around the cheese.
