## Ingredients

- 4 slices Dietz & Watson Gourmet Smoked Bacon
- 2 slices multigrain bread
- 6 slices Dietz & Watson Originals Herbed Turkey
- 4 slices Dietz & Watson Originals Cheddar Cheese
- 2 leaves lettuce
- 4  cherry tomatoes
- 2 ounces Dietz & Watson Hoagie Dressing

## Instructions

1. Cook Bacon in a cold pan on low until crisp.
2. While bacon is cooking, cut bread, D&W Originals Cheddar, D&W Originals Herbed Turkey, and lettuce into 2 inch squares.
3. Cut slices of cooked bacon into thirds.
4. On a 10- to 12-inch skewer, layer bread, lettuce, cheese, bacon and cherry tomato.
5. Repeat once more, then top with last piece of bread.
6. Serve with a side of D&W Hoagie Dressing for dipping.
