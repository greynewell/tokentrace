## Ingredients

- 1 cup confectioners' sugar
- 2 tablespoons milk
- 1 (1 ounce) square unsweetened chocolate, melted

## Instructions

1. Mix sugar, milk and unsweetened chocolate until smooth.
2. Use this icing for dipping the Truffle Cookies.
