## Ingredients

- 3 heads   endive - washed, dried, and separated into individual leaves
- ⅓ cup crumbled blue cheese
- ⅓ cup sweetened dried cranberries (such as Craisins®)
- ⅓ cup glazed walnuts
- 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar, or to taste

## Instructions

1. Arrange endive leaves on a serving tray.
2. Stir blue cheese, cranberries, and walnuts together in a bowl. Place a small amount of the blue cheese mixture on each leaf. Drizzle with balsamic vinegar.
