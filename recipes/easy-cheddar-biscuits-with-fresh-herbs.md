## Ingredients

- 2 ¼ cups all-purpose baking mix
- ½ cup shredded Cheddar cheese
- 2 tablespoons fresh rosemary, chopped
- ¼ cup sour cream
- 2 tablespoons Dijon mustard
- ⅓ cup milk
- 1  egg, lightly beaten

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C).
2. In a medium bowl, stir together baking mix, cheese, and rosemary.
3. In a separate bowl, stir together sour cream, mustard, and milk.
4. Stir wet mixture into dry mixture until well combined.
5. Drop dough in 2-inch rounds onto a baking sheet.
6. Brush tops with beaten egg.
7. Bake in preheated oven 12 to 15 minutes.
8. Serve warm.
