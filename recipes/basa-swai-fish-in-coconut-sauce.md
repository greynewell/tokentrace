## Ingredients

- ¾ cup coconut milk
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 teaspoons soy sauce
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- ⅛ teaspoon hot sauce
- .063 teaspoon ground black pepper
- 4 (4 ounce) fillets swai fish, cut into 3-inch pieces
- 1 tablespoon cornstarch
- 1 tablespoon cold water

## Instructions

1. Mix coconut milk, garlic, soy sauce, salt, hot sauce, and black pepper together in a large skillet or wok; add swai fish.
2. Cook over medium-high heat until fish flakes easily with a fork, about 10 minutes.
3. Stir cornstarch and cold water together in a bowl; mix into fish mixture and cook until sauce is thickened, 2 to 3 minutes.
