## Ingredients

- 1  Yukon Gold potato, peeled and diced
- ½ head cauliflower, chopped
- 12  baby carrots
- ¼ cup warm milk
- 1 tablespoon butter, divided
- ¼ cup diced onion
- ¼ cup diced celery
- ½ teaspoon dried thyme
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- ¾ cup diced ham
- 1 (14 ounce) can navy beans, undrained
- 1 cup chicken broth
- 1 (14 ounce) can navy beans, drained
- 1 clove garlic

## Instructions

1. Place a steamer insert into a saucepan and fill with water to just below the bottom of the steamer.
2. Bring water to a boil.
3. Add potato, cauliflower, and carrots; cover and steam until very tender, about 20 minutes.
4. Transfer potato mixture to a bowl and add milk and 1 1/2 teaspoons butter.
5. Mash with a potato masher or hand mixer until smooth.
6. Melt 1 1/2 teaspoons butter in a large saucepan over medium heat; saute onion, celery, thyme, salt, and pepper until fragrant, about 2 minutes.
7. Add ham and cook and stir until ham is warmed, about 2 more minutes.
8. Stir undrained navy beans and chicken broth into ham mixture, increase heat to high, and bring to a boil.
9. Reduce heat and simmer.
10. Blend drained navy beans and garlic together in a blender until smooth.
11. Stir bean mixture into soup; simmer, stirring often, until soup thickens, about 10 minutes.
12. Stir potato mixture into soup and simmer, stirring often, until thickened, about 5 minutes.
