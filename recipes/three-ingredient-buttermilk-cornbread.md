## Ingredients

- vegetable oil as needed
- 1 ½ cups buttermilk
- 1 ½ cups cornmeal
- ½ cup all-purpose flour

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C).
2. Pour enough oil into a skillet to coat the bottom; place into oven.
3. Mix buttermilk, cornmeal, and flour together in a bowl until smooth.
4. Remove skillet from oven; pour in buttermilk mixture.
5. Bake in the preheated oven until cornbread is golden brown, 20 to 25 minutes.
