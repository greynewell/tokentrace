## Ingredients

- 6 cups baking potatoes, peeled and sliced
- ¼ cup yellow mustard
- ½ cup all-purpose flour
- 3 ½ cups milk
- 1 teaspoon salt
- ½ cup condensed cream of mushroom soup
- 2 (5 ounce) cans tuna, drained
- 1 ½ cups thinly sliced onions

## Instructions

1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
2. Add potatoes and cook until tender but still firm, about 10 minutes; Drain.
3. Preheat oven to 300 degrees F (150 degrees C).
4. In a saucepan, combine yellow mustard, flour, milk, salt and 1/2 cup of soup.
5. Mix thoroughly and heat until thickened.
6. Starting with the potatoes, arrange the potatoes, tuna and onions, in alternating layers in a 9x13 inch baking dish.
7. Pour the mustard sauce all over the top.
8. Bake in a preheated oven for 1 hour or until hot and bubbly.
