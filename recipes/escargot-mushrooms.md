## Ingredients

- 4 tablespoons butter
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 6  helix snails, without shells
- 6 large fresh mushrooms

## Instructions

1. Over medium heat in a medium saucepan, heat butter and garlic.
2. Place the snails in the saucepan and slowly cook until tender, about 5 minutes.
3. Remove and discard mushroom stems.
4. Fill the mushroom caps with 1/2 teaspoon melted butter with garlic from the saucepan.
5. Microwave 2 to 3 minutes.
6. Insert the snails in the mushroom caps.
7. Microwave another 3 minutes.
