## Ingredients

- 1  nectarine, pitted
- ¾ cup strawberries, hulled
- ¾ cup blueberries, rinsed and drained
- ⅓ cup nonfat dry milk powder
- 1 cup crushed ice

## Instructions

1. In a blender combine nectarine, strawberries, blueberries, milk powder and crushed ice.
2. Blend until smooth. pour into glasses and serve.
