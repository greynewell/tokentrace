## Ingredients

- 1  spaghetti squash, halved and seeded
- 2 tablespoons olive oil, divided
- 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
- 1 tablespoon red pepper flakes
- salt and ground black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Coat inside of squash with 1 tablespoon olive oil. Place squash cut-side down on a baking sheet.
3. Bake in the preheated oven until squash is tender, about 30 minutes. Cool squash for 10 minutes.
4. Shred inside of squash with a fork and transfer to a bowl. Add remaining olive oil, parsley, red pepper flakes, salt, and pepper to shredded squash and toss to coat.
