## Ingredients

- 5 pounds chuck roast
- 2 cubes  beef bouillon
- 2 tablespoons salt
- 2 teaspoons garlic salt
- 2  bay leaves
- 2 tablespoons whole black peppercorns
- 2 teaspoons dried oregano
- 1 ½ teaspoons dried rosemary

## Instructions

1. Place roast in a large pot with water to cover.
2. Mix in bouillon, salt, and garlic salt.
3. Place the bay leaves, peppercorns, oregano, and rosemary in a coffee filter and secure tightly with a rubber band.
4. Add this to the pot.
5. Bring to a boil over high heat, then reduce heat to low, cover with a lid, and simmer for 6 to 8 hours.
6. Remove coffee filter and discard.
7. Remove roast from the pot and shred with two forks.
8. Reserve broth for dipping if desired.
