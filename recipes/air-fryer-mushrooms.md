## Ingredients

- 1 (8 ounce) package cremini mushrooms, halved or quartered
- 2 tablespoons avocado oil
- 1 teaspoon low-sodium soy sauce (such as Bragg®)
- ½ teaspoon garlic granules
- salt and ground black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Preheat the air fryer to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
2. Combine mushrooms, avocado oil, soy sauce, garlic granules, salt, and pepper in a bowl; toss to coat. Transfer to the air fryer bowl.
3. Cook mushrooms in the air fryer for 10 minutes, shaking occasionally.
