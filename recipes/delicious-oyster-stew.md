## Ingredients

- ½ cup butter
- 1 pint fresh shucked oysters
- 1 small onion, finely chopped
- 1 quart heavy whipping cream
- salt and ground black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Melt the butter in a pot over low heat.
2. Stir the oysters and onion into the melted butter, increase heat to medium-low, and cook and stir until the edges of the oysters begin to curl, 7 to 10 minutes.
3. Slowly stream the cream into the oyster mixture as you stir.
4. Bring the mixture to a simmer, but do not boil; cook at a simmer until heated through, about 5 minutes.
5. Season with salt and black pepper to serve.
