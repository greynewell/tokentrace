## Ingredients

- 1 (6 ounce) can canned crabmeat, drained
- 1 cup fresh bread crumbs
- 1 cup mayonnaise
- ¾ cup milk
- 6  hard-cooked eggs, chopped
- ⅓ cup onion, diced
- 1 teaspoon salt

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Place eggs in a saucepan and cover with cold water.
3. Bring water to a boil and immediately remove from heat.
4. Cover and let eggs stand in hot water for 10 to 12 minutes.
5. Remove from hot water, cool, peel and chop.
6. In a mixing bowl, combine the crab, bread crumbs, mayonnaise, milk, chopped eggs, onion, and salt.
7. Mix thoroughly.
8. Pour into a 1 quart casserole dish.
9. Bake in a preheated oven for 25 minutes or until the top is lightly brown and bubbly.
