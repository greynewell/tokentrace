## Ingredients

- ¼ cup olive oil
- 2 tablespoons distilled white vinegar
- 1 tablespoon honey
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- 1 teaspoon brown mustard
- 1 pound Brussels sprouts, shredded
- ½ cup dried cherries
- ½ cup slivered almonds
- ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese

## Instructions

1. Whisk olive oil, vinegar, honey, garlic, and mustard together in a bowl until dressing is smooth.
2. Combine Brussels sprouts, dried cherries, almonds, and Parmesan cheese together in a bowl; add dressing and toss to coat.
3. For best flavor, refrigerate for several hours before serving.
