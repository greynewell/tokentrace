## Ingredients

- 10 medium red potatoes, diced
- 1 small onion, chopped
- ½ cup diced roasted red peppers
- 1 (4 ounce) can sliced black olives, drained
- 1 (10 ounce) can quartered artichoke hearts, drained
- ½ cup balsamic vinegar
- 3 teaspoons olive oil
- 1 teaspoon dried oregano
- 1 teaspoon dried basil
- ½ teaspoon mustard powder
- 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley

## Instructions

1. Place potatoes in a saucepan with enough water to cover.
2. Bring to a boil, then cook for 5 to 10 minutes, until tender.
3. Drain, and transfer to a large bowl.
4. Add the onion, red peppers, olives, and artichokes to the bowl with the potatoes.
5. In a separate bowl, whisk together the balsamic vinegar, olive oil, oregano, basil, mustard powder and parsley.
6. Pour over the vegetables, and stir to coat.
7. Chill for at least 4 hours or overnight before serving.
