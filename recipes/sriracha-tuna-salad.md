## Ingredients

- 1 (5 ounce) can tuna, or more to taste, drained and flaked
- 2  hard-boiled eggs, chopped
- 3  mini sweet peppers, or more to taste
- 2 tablespoons Sriracha hot chili sauce, or more to taste
- 2 tablespoons mayonnaise
- 2 tablespoons honey mustard

## Instructions

1. Mix tuna, eggs, and peppers in a bowl.
2. Stir sriracha, mayonnaise, and honey mustard into the tuna mixture.
3. Refrigerate tuna salad for 5 minutes.
