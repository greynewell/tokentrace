## Ingredients

- 3 cups lowfat buttermilk
- 3  avocados - peeled, pitted and chopped
- lemon, juiced
- 2 cloves fresh garlic
- ½ teaspoon salt
- 2 drops hot pepper sauce (e.g. Tabasco™), or to taste  (Optional)

## Instructions

1. Combine buttermilk, avocados, lemon juice, garlic, salt, and hot pepper sauce in blender; blend until smooth and light green in color.
2. Adjust flavors if desired.
