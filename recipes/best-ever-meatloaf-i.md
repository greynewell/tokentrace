## Ingredients

- 1 ½ pounds lean ground beef
- 1 cup tomato juice
- ¾ cup rolled oats
- 1  egg
- ¼ cup chopped onion
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. In a large bowl, combine meat, tomato juice, oats, egg, chopped onion, salt, and pepper. Mix lightly but thoroughly. Press into an 8x4 inch loaf pan.
3. Bake for 1 hour, or until meat is no longer pink and juices run clear. Drain. Let stand 5 minutes before serving.
