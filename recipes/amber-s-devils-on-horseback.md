## Ingredients

- 1 pound bay or sea scallops, rinsed and drained
- ¼ cup lemon juice
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- lemon pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Pat scallops dry with paper towels and place in a lightly greased 1 quart casserole dish.
3. Bake scallops in preheated oven for 5 minutes.
4. In a small bowl, combine lemon juice and garlic.
5. Remove scallops from oven.
6. Spoon lemon/garlic mixture over scallops and sprinkle generously with lemon pepper.
7. Return scallops to oven and bake until firm, about 10 minutes.
8. Serve warm.
