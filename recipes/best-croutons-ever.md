## Ingredients

- ¼ cup butter
- 4 slices bread
- ¼ teaspoon garlic powder

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). Line a baking sheet with aluminum foil.
2. Melt butter in a pot over medium heat.
3. Cut each bread slice into 16 cubes; add bread cubes and garlic powder to melted butter. Stir to coat. Transfer coated bread cubes to the prepared baking sheet.
4. Bake in the preheated oven until crispy, 8 to 10 minutes.
