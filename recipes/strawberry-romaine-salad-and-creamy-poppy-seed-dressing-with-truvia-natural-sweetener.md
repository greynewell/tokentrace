## Ingredients

- 1 bunch romaine lettuce, torn
- ½ small red onion, thinly sliced
- 2 cups fresh strawberries, halved
- ¼ cup slivered almonds
- ¾ cup light mayonnaise (not salad dressing)
- ¼ cup skim milk
- 2 tablespoons Truvia® natural sweetener, plus
- 1 teaspoon Truvia® natural sweetener*
- 1 teaspoon vinegar
- 1 teaspoon poppy seeds

## Instructions

1. Combine romaine, onion and strawberries in a large bowl.
2. In a separate small bowl, whisk together the mayonnaise, milk, Truvia® natural sweetener, vinegar and poppy seeds.
3. Drizzle dressing over salad and toss.
4. Sprinkle with almonds.
