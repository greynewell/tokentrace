## Ingredients

- 1 cup Almond Breeze Vanilla almondmilk
- 1 cup lemon yogurt
- 2 tablespoons maple syrup
- 1  lemon, zested and juiced
- 1 dash vanilla extract

## Instructions

1. Whisk ingredients together and pour into ice pop molds.
2. Freeze until hard (about 2 hours).
