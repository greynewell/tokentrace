## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
- 3 cloves garlic, chopped
- 1 pound medium raw shrimp, peeled and deveined
- 1  lemon, zested and juiced
- 1 ¾ cups chicken broth
- 1 cup basmati rice
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 2 cups artichoke hearts
- 2 cups fresh baby spinach
- ½ cup sliced green onion

## Instructions

1. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
2. Add garlic; saute until fragrant, about 2 minutes.
3. Add shrimp, lemon zest, and lemon juice; saute until shrimp turn opaque, about 2 minutes.
4. Stir chicken broth, basmati rice, butter, and salt into the skillet.
5. Reduce heat to low; simmer until rice absorbs broth, about 15 minutes.
6. Add artichokes on top; simmer until heated through, about 5 minutes.
7. Spread spinach and green onion over artichokes.
8. Cook until spinach wilts, 3 to 5 minutes; fold into the rice mixture.
