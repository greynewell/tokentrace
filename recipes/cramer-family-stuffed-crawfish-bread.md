## Ingredients

- ¼ cup butter
- 1  onion, chopped
- ½ (12 fluid ounce) can evaporated milk, or as needed
- 2 pounds processed cheese food (such as Velveeta®), cubed
- ¾ pound cooked and peeled whole crawfish tails
- 24  brown and serve pistolette rolls
- 2 quarts vegetable oil for frying

## Instructions

1. Melt the butter in a saucepan over medium heat.
2. Stir in the onion and cook until the onion has softened and turned translucent, about 5 minutes.
3. Pour in the evaporated milk, cheese, and crawfish tails.
4. Cook and stir until the cheese has melted and the sauce is smooth, about 5 minutes more.
5. Use the end of a spoon to poke a hole into each pistolette roll.
6. Fill each roll with about 1 tablespoon of the crawfish filling.
7. Heat oil in a deep-fryer or large saucepan to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
8. Fry the rolls a few at a time in the hot oil until golden brown, about 3 minutes.
9. Drain on a paper towel-lined plate and serve hot.
