## Ingredients

- ⅓ cup wheat germ
- 1 cup vanilla yogurt
- ⅓ cup peanut butter
- ½ cup white sugar
- 3 cups sauerkraut, drained, rinsed and squeezed dry

## Instructions

1. Toast the wheat germ in a skillet over medium-high heat, stirring constantly, until browned and fragrant, about 5 minutes.
2. Pour wheat germ onto a plate, and set aside to cool.
3. Place yogurt, peanut butter, and sugar into a blender, and blend until smooth.
4. Toss sauerkraut with peanut dressing; refrigerate for 30 minutes to allow the flavors to blend.
5. Toss with toasted wheat germ just before serving.
