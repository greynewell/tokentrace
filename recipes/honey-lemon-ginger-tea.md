## Ingredients

- 4 cups water
- ¾ cup brown sugar
- ¼ cup grated ginger root
- 3  tea bags
- 2  lemons, juiced
- 3 tablespoons honey

## Instructions

1. Stir water, brown sugar, and grated ginger root together in a saucepan; bring to a boil, reduce heat to medium-low, and cook at a simmer for 20 minutes.
2. Remove saucepan from heat and add tea bags; steep tea to desired strength, 3 to 5 minutes. Remove and discard tea bags.
3. Stir lemon juice and honey into the tea; strain into a pitcher.
