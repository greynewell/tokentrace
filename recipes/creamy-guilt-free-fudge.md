## Ingredients

- ⅔ cup low-fat sweetened condensed milk
- 1 ½ cups semi-sweet chocolate chips
- ¾ teaspoon vanilla extract
- 1 pinch salt

## Instructions

1. Combine chocolate chips and condensed milk in a medium saucepan over medium-low heat; stir gently until chips are melted. Stir in vanilla and salt, then pour mixture into an ungreased 10-inch square pan.
2. Place in the refrigerator and chill until set, about 3 hours.
3. Cut chilled fudge into 25 squares.
