## Ingredients

- 1 pound braunschweiger liver sausage
- 1 tablespoon milk
- 1 tablespoon finely chopped onion
- ½ (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
- ½ teaspoon sugar
- ½ teaspoon minced garlic
- 1 teaspoon chili powder
- 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce

## Instructions

1. Mix together sausage, cream cheese, milk, and onion in a medium bowl. Season with Worcestershire sauce, chili powder, sugar, and garlic, and mix until well blended.
2. Wrap in plastic wrap, and refrigerate for at least 2 hours, until firm.
