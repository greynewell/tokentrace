## Ingredients

- ½ gallon water
- 1  onion, chopped
- 1  lemon, sliced
- 3  bay leaves
- 6  whole black peppercorns
- ½ tablespoon salt
- 2  whole lobsters
- ½ cup mayonnaise
- 3 tablespoons ketchup
- 1 tablespoon brandy

## Instructions

1. Fill a large pot with water, and mix in the onion, lemon, bay leaves, peppercorns, and salt.
2. Bring to a boil.
3. Place the lobsters in the boiling water, and cook until bright red, about 12 minutes for 1 pound, and about 5 more minutes for each additional pound.
4. Remove the cooked lobsters from the pot, and rinse under cold water.
5. Chill in the refrigerator, and serve cold.
6. In a small bowl, mix the mayonnaise, ketchup, and brandy.
7. Serve as a dipping sauce with the lobster.
