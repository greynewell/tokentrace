## Ingredients

- 1 cup dried bread crumbs
- 1 cup crab meat
- 1 cup small shrimp
- 3  green onions, chopped
- 1 cup chopped celery
- 1 cup mayonnaise
- 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
- ½ cup chopped parsley

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. In a large bowl, toss together 3/4 cup breadcrumbs, crab, shrimp, green onions, celery, mayonnaise, Worcestershire sauce and parsley.
3. Mix well, and spoon mixture into clean, dry seafood shells or ramekins.
4. Place stuffed shells on a foil-lined cookie sheet, and sprinkle with remaining 1/4 cup bread crumbs.
5. Bake in the preheated oven for 30 minutes.
