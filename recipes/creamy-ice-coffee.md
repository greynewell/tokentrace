## Ingredients

- 1 ½ quarts brewed coffee, room temperature
- 1 cup milk
- 1 cup half-and-half cream
- ⅓ cup white sugar
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 2 tablespoons creme de cacao

## Instructions

1. In a pitcher, combine cooled coffee, milk and half-and-half.
2. Stir in sugar, vanilla and creme de cacao.
3. Chill in refrigerator until ready to serve.
