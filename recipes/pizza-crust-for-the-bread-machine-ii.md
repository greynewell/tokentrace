## Ingredients

- ¾ cup water
- 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
- 1 teaspoon lemon juice
- ½ teaspoon salt
- 1 tablespoon white sugar
- 1 tablespoon dry milk powder
- 2 ¼ cups bread flour
- 1 teaspoon active dry yeast

## Instructions

1. Place ingredients in the pan of the bread machine in the order recommended by the manufacturer.
2. Select Dough cycle; press Start.
3. Remove dough from pan after rise cycle and use for your favorite pizza recipe.
