## Ingredients

- ⅔ cup beef broth
- ⅓ cup dry quinoa
- 1 (10 ounce) package frozen chopped spinach, thawed and drained
- ½ cup chopped onion
- ½ cup shredded Parmesan cheese
- 2 cloves garlic, or more to taste
- ½ cup fresh bread crumbs
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon red pepper flakes
- ½ teaspoon garlic powder
- ¼ teaspoon fresh ground black pepper
- 2  egg whites
- 1  egg
- cooking spray
- 4  hamburger buns

## Instructions

1. Bring beef broth to a strong boil in a saucepan over high heat.
2. Stir in quinoa; reduce heat to low and cover.
3. Cook until broth is absorbed, about 15 minutes.
4. Remove from heat; let stand for 5 minutes.
5. Fluff with a fork; let cool.
6. Chop spinach in a food processor; transfer to a bowl.
7. Chop onion, Parmesan cheese, and garlic in the food processor; add to the spinach.
8. Mix in cooled quinoa, bread crumbs, salt, red pepper flakes, garlic powder, and black pepper.
9. Add egg whites and egg; stir burger mixture to combine.
10. Shape burger mixture into 4 equal patties.
11. Heat a skillet over medium-high heat and coat with cooking spray.
12. Cook patties until cooked throughout, 4 to 6 minutes per side.
13. Serve on hamburger buns.
