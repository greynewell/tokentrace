## Ingredients

- 1 (16 ounce) package frozen French-cut green beans
- ⅓ cup almonds
- ¼ cup tamari

## Instructions

1. Lightly spray a skillet with cooking spray and set over medium heat.
2. Place the green beans in the skillet; cook and stir until cooked through, about 8 minutes.
3. Add the almonds and tamari.
4. Continue to cook and stir until the green beans are lightly browned.
