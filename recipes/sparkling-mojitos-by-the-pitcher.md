## Ingredients

- 1 cup superfine sugar
- 30 leaves fresh mint
- 20  limes, juiced, or more to taste
- 1 cup white rum
- 25 fluid ounces sparkling water, or more to taste

## Instructions

1. Combine sugar and mint leaves in a pitcher; mash well with a cocktail muddler.
2. Stir in lime juice and rum until sugar is dissolved.
3. Stir in sparkling water.
