## Ingredients

- ¾ cup self-rising cornbread mix (such as Martha White®)
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- 2  yellow squash, cut into 1/8-inch slices
- ¼ cup olive oil, or more as needed

## Instructions

1. Place cornbread mix in a 1-gallon resealable bag; season with salt and black pepper. Add squash, seal the bag, and shake until evenly coated. Remove squash from the bag and shake off any excess cornmeal.
2. Heat about 1/4 inch of olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
3. Working in batches, fry squash until the center is cooked and the edges are crisp, 2 to 3 minutes per side. Remove with a slotted spoon and drain on a paper towel-lined plate.
