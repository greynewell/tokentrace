## Ingredients

- 2 (8 ounce) cans oysters, drained
- 32 fluid ounces half-and-half
- ⅓ cup butter

## Instructions

1. Melt the butter in a medium saucepan over medium heat.
2. Cook the drained oysters in the butter over medium heat until warmed through.
3. Stir in half-and-half and bring to a slight simmer.
4. Reduce heat, and stir occasionally to prevent boiling or scorching the bottom of the pan.
5. Serve hot.
