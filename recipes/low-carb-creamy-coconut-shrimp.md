## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons coconut oil
- 1 pound frozen peeled and deveined shrimp, thawed
- 1 cup heavy cream
- ½ cup fresh spinach
- 2 tablespoons coconut flour
- 1 tablespoon garlic powder
- salt to taste
- ½ cup pine nuts

## Instructions

1. Heat coconut oil in a cast iron skillet over medium to medium-high heat.
2. Stir in shrimp, heavy cream, spinach, flour, garlic powder, and salt.
3. Bring to a boil and cook until shrimp changes color to pink, about 3 minutes.
4. Turn off heat and add pine nuts.
