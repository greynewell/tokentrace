## Ingredients

- 1 (8 ounce) package whole-wheat fettuccine
- 1 pound large dry sea scallops
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- 1 tablespoon extra-virgin olive oil
- 1 (8 ounce) bottle clam juice
- 1 cup low-fat milk
- 3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
- ¼ teaspoon ground white pepper
- ⅛ teaspoon salt
- 3 cups frozen green peas, thawed
- ¾ cup shredded Romano cheese, divided
- ⅓ cup chopped fresh chives
- ½ teaspoon lemon zest
- 1 teaspoon lemon juice

## Instructions

1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil; cook the fettuccini at a boil until cooked through yet slightly firm, about 6 minutes; drain.
2. Season the scallops salt.
3. Heat olive oil in a large non-stick skillet over medium-high heat and cook the scallops until golden brown, 2 to 3 minutes per side.
4. Transfer scallops to a plate.
5. Pour the clam juice into the skillet.
6. Whisk together the low-fat milk, flour, white pepper, and 1/8 teaspoon salt until the mixture is smooth; whisk into the clam juice.
7. Reduce heat to low and bring the sauce to a simmer, whisking constantly.
8. Cook until thickened, 2 to 3 minutes.
9. Return the scallops and any accumulated juices to the skillet and stir in the peas until thoroughly combined.
10. Return to a simmer.
11. Gently mix the cooked fettuccine, 1/2 cup of Romano cheese, chives, lemon zest, and lemon juice into the scallops and sauce.
12. Sprinkle with remaining 1/4 cup of Romano cheese to serve.
