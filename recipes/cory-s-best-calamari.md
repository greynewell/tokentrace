## Ingredients

- 2 pounds squid, cleaned and sliced into rounds
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 cup all-purpose flour
- ½ cup vegetable oil
- 1 pinch ground black pepper

## Instructions

1. Rinse and drain squid well.
2. Pat squid dry with paper towels.
3. Sprinkle salt on squid.
4. Put flour and a few squid in a small paper or plastic bag.
5. Shake the squid rounds in the bag until coated.
6. Remove squid and place more in the sack to coat.
7. Heat oil in a medium size frying pan over a high heat.
8. Fry the calamari in the oil until golden brown and crispy.
9. Drain the calamari on paper towels.
10. Serve hot.
