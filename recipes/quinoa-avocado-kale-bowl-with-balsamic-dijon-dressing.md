## Ingredients

- 1 ½ teaspoons olive oil
- 1 teaspoon balsamic vinegar
- ¼ teaspoon Dijon mustard
- ¼ teaspoon lime juice
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- ½ cup cooked quinoa
- 1 leaf kale, stemmed and chopped
- 4  cherry tomatoes, sliced into thirds
- ¼  avocado, sliced
- 1 tablespoon diced cucumber
- 1 tablespoon diced red onion
- 1 tablespoon diced green bell pepper
- 1 tablespoon crumbled goat cheese

## Instructions

1. Whisk olive oil, balsamic vinegar, Dijon mustard, and lime juice together in a bowl to make dressing.
2. Season with salt and pepper.
3. Place quinoa in a bowl.
4. Massage kale pieces with your hands until slightly softened; spread over quinoa.
5. Layer tomatoes, avocado, cucumber, red onion, and green bell pepper over kale.
6. Drizzle dressing on top.
7. Garnish with goat cheese.
