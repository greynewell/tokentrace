## Ingredients

- 8 ounces diced cooked ham
- 1 cup finely diced celery
- 1 cup finely diced red onion
- ⅓ cup mayonnaise, or more to taste
- 1 teaspoon dried rosemary leaves, crumbled
- 2 cups torn romaine lettuce, or to taste
- ¼ cup ranch dressing

## Instructions

1. Mix ham, celery, and red onion in a large bowl; add mayonnaise and rosemary and stir to coat.
2. Refrigerate ham mixture for at least 1 hour.
3. Put lettuce into a large bowl.
4. Spoon ham mixture over the lettuce.
5. Serve with ranch dressing on the side.
