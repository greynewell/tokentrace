## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons butter
- ⅓ cup mayonnaise
- ⅓ cup sour cream
- ¼ cup fresh lemon juice
- 1 pound cod fillets

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. In a mixing bowl, combine butter, mayonnaise, sour cream and lemon juice.
3. Place cod fillets in a 8x8 inch baking dish.
4. Pour mayonnaise mixture over fish.
5. Bake in a preheated oven for 30 minutes or until fish flakes easily with a fork.
