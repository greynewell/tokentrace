## Ingredients

- 1 (10 ounce) can asparagus tips, drained
- ½ cup mayonnaise
- 2 tablespoons finely chopped onion
- 1 pinch seasoning salt
- 1 (1 pound) loaf soft, sliced white bread

## Instructions

1. Set asparagus spears onto paper towels to absorb excess liquid.
2. In a small bowl, mix together the mayonnaise, onion and seasoning salt.
3. Remove crusts from bread slices, and spread mayonnaise mixture thinly on each slice.
4. Place a spear onto the slice and roll up.
5. Cut each roll in half, and arrange on a serving tray.
6. Cover with plastic wrap, and refrigerate until serving.
