## Ingredients

- 4 cups popped popcorn
- ½ (10.5 ounce) bag corn chips (such as Fritos®)
- ½ (14 ounce) package candy corn
- 1 (10 ounce) can mixed nuts
- 1 cup jelly beans or other small candies, or as desired
- 1 (12 ounce) package white chocolate chips

## Instructions

1. Mix popcorn, corn chips, candy corn, mixed nuts, and jelly beans together in a large bowl.
2. In a microwave-safe glass bowl, melt the white chocolate chips on Low setting until barely melted, 1 or 2 minutes.
3. Stir until smooth.
4. Pour the white chocolate over the popcorn mixture, and stir to coat.
5. Allow to cool, and break apart into small pieces.
