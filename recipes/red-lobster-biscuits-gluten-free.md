## Ingredients

- 2 cups gluten-free all purpose baking flour
- 1 tablespoon baking powder
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon dried parsley
- ½ teaspoon onion powder
- ½ teaspoon garlic powder
- ⅔ cup whole milk
- ⅓ cup vegetable oil
- ¾ cup shredded Cheddar cheese
- ¼ cup dried parsley
- 2 tablespoons melted butter
- ½ teaspoon garlic powder

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C).
2. Line a baking sheet with aluminum foil.
3. Whisk flour, baking powder, salt, 1 teaspoon parsley, onion powder, and 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder together in a bowl.
4. Add milk and vegetable oil; stir until smooth.
5. Mix in Cheddar cheese until dough is combined.
6. Roll dough into 2-inch balls and place 2-inches apart on prepared baking sheet.
7. Bake in the preheated oven until golden brown, 10 to 12 minutes.
8. Stir 1/4 cup parsley, melted butter, and 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder together in a bowl.
9. Brush butter mixture onto hot biscuits; let biscuits cool 10 to 15 minutes.
