## Ingredients

- vegetable oil for frying
- 1  egg, beaten
- ⅓ cup milk
- 4 (6 ounce) fillets cod
- 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour for coating
- 2 cups quick-cooking oats
- salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
2. Whisk together egg and milk in a bowl. Coat cod fillets with flour; shake off excess. Dip cod in egg mixture; allow excess to drip off. Coat cod with oats.
3. Place fish one at a time in hot oil; fry for 2 to 3 minutes, then turn over, and fry 2 to 3 minutes more. Remove to paper towels. Season to taste with salt and pepper.
