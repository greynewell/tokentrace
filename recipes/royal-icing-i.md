## Ingredients

- 4  egg whites
- 4 cups sifted confectioners' sugar
- 1 teaspoon lemon extract

## Instructions

1. Beat egg whites in a clean, large bowl with an electric mixer at high speed until foamy.
2. Gradually add sugar and lemon extract. Beat on high speed until thickened.
