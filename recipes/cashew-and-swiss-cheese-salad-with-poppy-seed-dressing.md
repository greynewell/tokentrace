## Ingredients

- 1 cup olive oil
- ¾ cup white sugar
- ⅓ cup vinegar
- ⅓ cup grated onion
- 1 teaspoon poppy seeds
- ½ teaspoon mustard
- 1 pinch salt
- 1 head green leaf lettuce, chopped, or more to taste
- 2 cups grated Swiss cheese
- 1 cup chopped cashews

## Instructions

1. Whisk oil, sugar, vinegar, onion, poppy seeds, mustard, and salt together in a bowl until dressing is smooth. Cover bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate for 24 hours.
2. Put lettuce in a bowl and drizzle dressing over lettuce; toss to coat. Add Swiss cheese and cashew and toss to coat.
