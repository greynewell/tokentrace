## Ingredients

- 60  unopened, fresh, live medium oysters
- 5  limes
- 2 tablespoons ground black pepper

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
2. Place the oysters on a large baking sheet and cook 8 to 15 minutes in the preheated oven, or until the shells have opened.
3. Squeeze the juice and pulp from the limes into a small serving bowl.
4. Mix in the pepper.
5. Serve oysters on the half shell with the sauce.
