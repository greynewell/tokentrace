## Ingredients

- ½ cup butter
- ½ cup superfine sugar
- 2  eggs
- 1 cup self-rising flour
- 2 teaspoons cocoa powder
- 1 teaspoon baking soda

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Grease 6 cups in a muffin pan.
3. Cream together butter and sugar in a mixing bowl.
4. Mix in eggs.
5. Add flour, cocoa powder, and baking soda and blend thoroughly.
6. Spoon batter into muffin cups.
7. Bake 15 to 20 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
8. Cool and decorate.
