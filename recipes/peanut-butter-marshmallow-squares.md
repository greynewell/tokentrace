## Ingredients

- ½ cup butter
- 1 (11 ounce) package butterscotch chips
- 1 cup peanut butter
- 1 cup flaked coconut
- 1 cup chopped walnuts
- 3 ½ cups miniature marshmallows

## Instructions

1. Grease a 9x13-inch pan; set aside.
2. Place butterscotch chips, peanut butter, and butter in the top of a double boiler over simmering water. Stir frequently, scraping down the sides with a rubber spatula to avoid scorching, until melted and combined, about 5 minutes. Remove from heat.
3. Stir marshmallows, coconut, and walnuts into butterscotch mixture until evenly combined.
4. Pour mixture into prepared pan. Refrigerate until set, about 1 hour. Cut into 18 squares and store in the refrigerator.
