## Ingredients

- ½ cup reduced-fat ranch dressing
- 2 tablespoons orange juice
- 1 tablespoon prepared horseradish
- 4 cups chopped broccoli
- 1 large mango, peeled and cubed
- ½ cup cashews
- 1 small red onion, cut into thin wedges
- 1 (11 ounce) can mandarin oranges, drained

## Instructions

1. Combine ranch dressing, orange juice, and horseradish in a bowl; mix well and set aside.
2. Toss together the chopped broccoli, cubed mango, cashews, and wedged onion in a salad bowl.
3. Pour the dressing over the broccoli mixture; toss to coat.
4. Refrigerate at least 4 hours.
5. Add the drained oranges to the salad just before serving.
