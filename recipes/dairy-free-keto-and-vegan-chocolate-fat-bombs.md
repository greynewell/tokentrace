## Ingredients

- ½ cup melted coconut oil
- ½ cup almond butter
- ½ cup unsweetened cocoa powder

## Instructions

1. Blend coconut oil, almond butter, and cocoa powder in a food processor until creamy, about 2 minutes.
2. Fill mini muffin cups with the mixture and chill in the refrigerator until set, about 30 minutes.
