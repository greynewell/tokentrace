## Ingredients

- 1 large carrot, sliced diagonally into 1/4 inch thick slices
- 1 (5 ounce) can tuna packed in water, drained
- 3  green onions, chopped
- 1 tablespoon mayonnaise

## Instructions

1. Arrange slices of carrot on a plate or serving tray.
2. In a small bowl, mix together the tuna, green onions and mayonnaise.
3. Spoon some onto each slice of carrot and serve immediately.
