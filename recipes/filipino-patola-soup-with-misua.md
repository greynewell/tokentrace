## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 1  onion, chopped
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- ⅓ pound prawns, peeled and deveined
- 1 tablespoon fish sauce
- ⅛ teaspoon ground black pepper
- 3 ¼ (14.5 ounce) cans chicken broth
- 1  patola (luffa squash), peeled and cut crosswise into chunks
- 1 (8 ounce) package misua (salted wheat) noodles, or to taste

## Instructions

1. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium-high heat.
2. Saute onion and garlic until onion is translucent, about 5 minutes.
3. Add prawns; cook and stir until starting to turn color, about 2 minutes.
4. Add fish sauce and pepper; cover and cook until slightly reduced, about 2 minutes.
5. Pour chicken broth into the skillet with prawns.
6. Cover, reduce heat, and simmer until flavors begin to combine, about 2 minutes.
7. Add patola; re-cover and cook until mostly softened, about 5 minutes.
8. Add misua noodles; let simmer until tender yet firm to the bite, about 2 minutes.
