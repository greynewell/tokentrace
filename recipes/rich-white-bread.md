## Ingredients

- 3 cups all-purpose flour
- 2 tablespoons white sugar
- 1 (.25 ounce) package rapid rise yeast
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- 3 tablespoons butter
- 1  egg
- ¾ cup warm water (110 degrees F/45 degrees C)

## Instructions

1. Insert metal blade in food processor bowl.
2. Add flour, sugar, yeast, and salt; process 10 seconds to combine.
3. Place butter and egg in food processor.
4. Begin processing, then slowly pour warm water through feed tube just until dough forms a ball, about 10 to 15 seconds.
5. Continue processing for 60 seconds to knead dough.
6. Carefully remove dough from processor bowl  and place on a lightly floured surface.
7. Cover and let rest for 10 minutes.
8. Form dough into a loaf and place in a lightly greased 8x4 inch loaf pan.
9. Cover and let rise until doubled, about 45 minutes.
10. Meanwhile, preheat oven to 375  degrees F (190 degrees C).
11. Bake in the preheated oven for about 30 minutes or until the top is golden brown and the bottom of the loaf sounds hollow when tapped.
