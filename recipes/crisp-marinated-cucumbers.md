## Ingredients

- ½ cup white vinegar
- ½ cup white sugar
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ¼ teaspoon celery seed
- 2  cucumbers, sliced
- ¼ cup sliced sweet onion

## Instructions

1. Whisk vinegar, sugar, salt, and celery seed together in a large bow. Add cucumbers and onion and stir until well coated.
2. Cover and refrigerate 8 hours to overnight. Serve cold.
