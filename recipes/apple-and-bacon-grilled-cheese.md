## Ingredients

- 2 slices cracked wheat bread
- 2 thin slices  aged Cheddar cheese
- 2 slices Honeycrisp apple, or more to taste
- 2  cooked bacon strips

## Instructions

1. Preheat a sandwich/panini press according to manufacturer's instructions.
2. Set one slice of bread on a work surface.
3. Top bread with 1 slice cheese, apple slices, bacon slices, and remaining 1 slice cheese.
4. Place remaining bread slice on top.
5. Cook sandwich in the preheated panini press until bread is lightly toasted and cheese begins to melt, about 5 minutes.
