## Ingredients

- ½ cup blue cheese salad dressing
- ½ cup mayonnaise
- salt and pepper to taste
- 1 (16 ounce) bag coleslaw mix
- 1 (2.5 ounce) package cooked real bacon pieces (such as Hormel™)
- 1 cup quartered cherry tomatoes

## Instructions

1. Combine mayonnaise, salad dressing, salt, and pepper in a large bowl.
2. Stir in the coleslaw mix and bacon.
3. Add the tomatoes, and toss gently.
4. Cover, and refrigerate for 1 hour or overnight before serving.
