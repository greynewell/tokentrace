## Ingredients

- 1 pound lean ground beef
- 1  onion, sliced
- 1 medium head cabbage, sliced
- 2 (10.75 ounce) cans  condensed tomato soup
- 10 ¾ fluid ounces water
- salt to taste
- ground black pepper to taste
- ½ cup sour cream

## Instructions

1. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat.
2. Cook and stir beef and sliced onion in the hot skillet until browned and crumbly, 5 to 7 minutes; drain and discard grease.
3. Stir in sliced cabbage, tomato soup, water (use the empty soup can to measure), salt, and pepper.
4. Cover and simmer until cabbage is tender, stirring occasionally, about 25 minutes.
5. Stir in sour cream just before serving.
