## Ingredients

- 1 pound ground turkey
- 8 cups water
- 8 cubes beef bouillon cube
- 4  potatoes, peeled and cubed
- 2  carrots, sliced
- 1 small head cabbage, shredded
- 1 cup pearl barley

## Instructions

1. In a large pot, combine water, bouillon, potatoes, carrots, and cabbage.
2. Bring to a boil.
3. Lower temperature, and simmer.
4. In a separate saucepan, brown the turkey.
5. Put in pot with other ingredients.
6. Slowly simmer for 1 hour.
7. Add barley for last 15 minutes of cooking time.
8. Serve.
