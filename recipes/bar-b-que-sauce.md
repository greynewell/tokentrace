## Ingredients

- ½ cup ketchup
- 2 tablespoons brown sugar
- 2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
- 1 tablespoon cider vinegar
- 1 dash hot pepper sauce
- 1 teaspoon garlic powder
- ¼ teaspoon mustard powder
- ¼ teaspoon salt

## Instructions

1. Stir together ketchup, brown sugar, Worcestershire sauce, vinegar, garlic powder, mustard powder, salt, and hot pepper sauce in a small saucepan.
2. Bring to a simmer over medium heat. Remove from heat and allow to cool slightly before brushing on your favorite meat.
