## Ingredients

- 2 (5 ounce) cans tuna packed in water, drained
- 2 tablespoons mayonnaise, or to taste
- ¾ teaspoon celery salt
- ¾ teaspoon onion powder
- ¾ teaspoon garlic powder
- ⅓ cup chopped celery
- ⅓ cup chopped onion
- ¼ cup chopped yellow bell pepper
- 1 tablespoon butter, or to taste
- 3  English muffins, split
- 6 slices American cheese
- 6 slices fresh tomato

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Flake the tuna into a medium bowl.
3. Mix in the mayonnaise, celery salt, onion powder, and garlic powder.
4. Stir in celery, onion, and yellow bell pepper.
5. Butter English muffin halves, place them on a baking sheet.
6. Bake in the preheated oven until lightly browned, about 3 minutes.
7. Spoon tuna mixture onto each muffin half and top each with a slice of cheese.
8. Return to the oven and continue baking until the cheese has melted, about 8 minutes.
9. Top each with a tomato slice before serving.
