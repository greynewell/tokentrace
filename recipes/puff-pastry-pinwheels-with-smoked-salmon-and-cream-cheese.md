## Ingredients

- ½ (17.5 ounce) package frozen puff pastry, thawed
- ½ (8 ounce) container herbed cream cheese
- 7 ounces sliced smoked salmon
- 1 sprig fresh dill, finely chopped, or to taste

## Instructions

1. Dust a flat work surface lightly with flour.
2. Unroll puff pastry and roll out to a rectangle.
3. Spread a thin layer of herbed cream cheese over the puff pastry.
4. Cover with smoked salmon slices and sprinkle with dill, leaving a 1-inch border.
5. Roll puff pastry up tightly like a jelly roll and wrap in plastic wrap.
6. Refrigerate for 15 minutes.
7. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
8. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
9. Slice puff pastry roll into 1/4-inch-thick slices and lay on the prepared baking sheet.
10. Bake on the center rack of the preheated oven until lightly browned and puffed up, 20 to 25 minutes.
