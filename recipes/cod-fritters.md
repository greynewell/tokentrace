## Ingredients

- 1 ½ pounds cod
- ½ medium onion, chopped
- ¼ cup water
- 2 large eggs
- 1 cup all-purpose flour
- ½ teaspoon baking soda
- ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
- ⅛ teaspoon cayenne pepper
- 1 cup vegetable oil for frying

## Instructions

1. Bring a pot of water to a boil.
2. Add cod and continue to boil until soft, about 10 minutes.
3. Drain, transfer fish to a bowl, and shred with 2 forks.
4. Heat oil in a skillet over medium-high heat.
5. Saute onion in the hot oil until translucent, 5 to 7 minutes.
6. Add to fish with water and eggs.
7. Mix flour and baking soda together and add to fish mixture; stir in black pepper and cayenne.
8. Measure out scoops of mixture with a tablespoon.
9. Heat oil in a deep pan over medium to low heat.
10. Fry fritters in batches in the hot oil until golden brown, 5 to 7 minutes, flattening out with the back of a spoon.
