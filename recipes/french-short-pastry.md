## Ingredients

- 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
- ⅛ teaspoon salt
- ½ cup unsalted butter, chilled and cut into small pieces
- 6 tablespoons ice water

## Instructions

1. In a food processor, mix together flour and salt.
2. Add butter, and process until mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
3. Add water 1 tablespoon at a time.
4. Pulse briefly until dough begins to form in clumps.
5. Remove dough from processor onto a floured surface, and form into a ball.
6. Allow dough to rest at room temperature for 30 to 45 minutes before rolling out.
7. On a lightly floured surface, roll out dough to fit pie pan.
8. Place in pan, and crimp edge.
9. Prick the bottom of dough several times with a fork.
10. Place in freezer for 15 to 20 minutes.
11. Meanwhile, preheat oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C).
12. Fill crust with beans, or use pie weights.
13. Bake in preheated oven for 15 minutes.
14. Remove pie weights, and bake another 5 minutes.
15. If necessary, cover with foil to prevent overbrowning.
