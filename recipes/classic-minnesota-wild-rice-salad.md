## Ingredients

- 1 cup wild rice
- 4 cups chicken broth
- 3 cups cubed, cooked chicken
- 2 cups seedless red grapes
- 1 cup roasted salted cashews
- 1 (8 ounce) can sliced water chestnuts, drained
- 2 cups mayonnaise
- 1 ½ teaspoons seasoned salt
- 1 teaspoon soy sauce
- ¼ teaspoon curry powder

## Instructions

1. Bring the wild  rice and chicken broth to a boil in a saucepan.
2. Reduce heat to medium-low, cover, and simmer until the rice is tender but not mushy, 30 to 45 minutes.
3. Drain off any excess liquid, fluff the rice with a fork, and cook uncovered 5 minutes more.
4. Remove from heat and let the rice cool thoroughly in refrigerator, about 1 hour.
5. Transfer wild rice to a large salad bowl and toss with cooked chicken, grapes, cashews, and water chestnuts.
6. Whisk mayonnaise, seasoned salt, soy sauce, and curry powder in a separate bowl.
7. Pour the dressing over the salad and toss again.
8. Chill thoroughly, at least 1 hour or overnight for best flavor.
