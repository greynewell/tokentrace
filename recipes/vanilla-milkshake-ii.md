## Ingredients

- 1 scoop vanilla ice cream
- 1 cup half-and-half cream
- ⅛ teaspoon vanilla extract

## Instructions

1. In a blender, combine ice cream, half-and-half and vanilla extract.
2. Blend until smooth.
3. Pour into glass and serve.
