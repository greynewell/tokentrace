## Ingredients

- 1 (8 ounce) container small fresh mozzarella balls
- 1 (4 ounce) package fresh basil leaves
- 1 (10.5 ounce) container cherry tomatoes, halved
- 24  small skewers, or as needed
- salt and ground black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Wrap each mozzarella ball with a basil leaf.
2. Thread a tomato half, a basil-wrapped mozzarella ball, and another tomato half onto a skewer. Repeat until all tomatoes are on skewers. Sprinkle with salt and pepper.
