## Ingredients

- 1 cup unsweetened peanut butter, softened
- 1 cup coconut oil
- ¼ cup unsweetened vanilla-flavored almond milk
- 2 teaspoons vanilla liquid stevia, or as needed  (Optional)

## Instructions

1. Line a loaf pan with parchment paper.
2. Combine peanut butter and coconut oil in a microwave-safe dish.
3. Microwave 30 seconds until slightly melted.
4. Add to blender with almond milk and stevia; blend until well combined.
5. Pour into loaf pan and refrigerate until set, about 2 hours.
