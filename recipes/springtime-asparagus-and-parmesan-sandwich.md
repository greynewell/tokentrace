## Ingredients

- 1 bunch asparagus spears, trimmed and cut in half
- ⅓ cup water
- 12 slices French bread
- ¼ cup butter
- ⅓ cup mayonnaise
- ⅓ cup shaved Parmesan cheese
- 12  Bibb lettuce leaves
- freshly ground black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Place the asparagus and 1/3 cup water in a microwave-safe bowl.
2. Cook in the microwave on High 1 minute, or just until tender.
3. Rinse under cold water, and blot dry with paper towels.
4. Spread 1 side of 6 bread slices with 1/4 teaspoon butter and about 1 tablespoon mayonnaise each.
5. Layer with asparagus, and sprinkle with Parmesan cheese.
6. Place lettuce over the asparagus, season with pepper, and top with remaining 6 bread slices to form 6 sandwiches.
7. Slice on a diagonal; this is a sandwich that exudes spring !
