## Ingredients

- 2 cups cold mashed potatoes
- 2  eggs, lightly beaten
- 1 tablespoon all-purpose flour
- ⅛ teaspoon garlic powder
- 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
- 1 tablespoon chopped fresh chives

## Instructions

1. Place potatoes, eggs, flour, and garlic powder in a bowl; mix well.
2. Heat oil in a skillet over medium-high heat. Transfer potato mixture to the skillet, pressing with a spatula to flatten evenly. Cover and cook until bottom is crispy, about 10 minutes.
3. Invert onto a serving platter and garnish with fresh chives.
