## Ingredients

- 2  apples
- 2 tablespoons brown sugar
- 1 teaspoon ground nutmeg
- 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
- 2 teaspoons butter

## Instructions

1. Core apples, leaving the bottom intact.
2. Mix brown sugar, cinnamon, and nutmeg together in a bowl. Spoon half of the sugar mixture into each apple and put 1 teaspoon butter on each top. Place apples in a deep, microwave-safe casserole dish and cover.
3. Microwave until tender, 3 1/2 to 4 minutes. Let apples sit for 2 minutes before serving.
