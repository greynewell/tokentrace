## Ingredients

- 2 pounds stew meat, trimmed and cubed
- 3 (10.75 ounce) cans  condensed cream of chicken soup
- 1 (16 ounce) package egg noodles

## Instructions

1. Spray the inside of a slow cooker with the vegetable cooking spray.
2. Add the meat and the soups to the slow cooker.
3. Cook on low setting for 8 to 10 hours.
4. Prepare noodles according to package directions.
5. When stew is ready, pour over the noodles and serve hot.
