## Ingredients

- cooking spray
- 1  sweet potato
- 1 ¾ cups sifted whole wheat flour
- 1 teaspoon baking soda
- ¾ teaspoon ground cinnamon
- ¼ teaspoon ground nutmeg
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- 1 pinch ground ginger
- 1 ¼ cups applesauce
- 2  eggs
- ¼ cup maple syrup
- ⅓ cup chopped walnuts  (Optional)

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Spray a 9x5-inch loaf pan with cooking spray.
3. Place sweet potato on a microwave-safe plate and pierce skin 5 or 6 times.
4. Cook in the microwave, rotating halfway through, until tender, 5 to 8 minutes.
5. Cool until easily handled, about 5 minutes.
6. Peel and mash in a small bowl.
7. Mix whole wheat flour, baking soda, cinnamon, nutmeg, and salt together in a large bowl.
8. Whisk applesauce, eggs, and maple syrup together in a separate bowl.
9. Mix in 1 cup mashed sweet potato.
10. Pour applesauce mixture over flour mixture; stir to combine.
11. Fold in walnuts.
12. Pour into the prepared loaf pan.
13. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean, 45 to 55 minutes.
14. Cool in the pan for 15 minutes; invert onto a wire rack and let cool completely, about 20 minutes.
