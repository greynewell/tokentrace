## Ingredients

- ¼ cup olive oil
- 1 cup minced onion
- ¼ teaspoon onion powder
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 tablespoons dried oregano
- 2 (6 ounce) cans tomato paste
- 2 (15 ounce) cans tomato sauce

## Instructions

1. Heat oil in a medium saucepan over medium heat.
2. Saute onion, onion powder, garlic and oregano until clear.
3. Stir in tomato paste and tomato sauce; reduce heat to low and simmer for 15 minutes.
4. Let cool and spread over prepared pizza crust.
