## Ingredients

- 10 slices provolone cheese
- 1 (14 ounce) package frozen cooked meatballs
- 1 (28 ounce) jar spaghetti sauce
- 5  hoagie rolls, split lengthwise

## Instructions

1. Place the meatballs into a glass baking dish and heat in the microwave until hot, about 45 seconds per 6 meatballs.
2. Remove from the microwave, add the spaghetti sauce, cover and return to the microwave.
3. Heat until bubbly, 2 to 3 minutes depending on your microwave.
4. Spoon the meatballs and sauce onto the rolls and top each one with two slices of provolone cheese.
5. Return to the microwave and heat until cheese melts, about 15 seconds.
