## Ingredients

- 6 (1 1/4 pound) live lobsters
- 1 cup butter
- 2 teaspoons fresh lemon juice
- 2 teaspoons chopped fresh flat-leaf parsley
- 2 teaspoons chopped fresh chives
- 2 teaspoons chopped fresh basil
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- 1  lemon, cut into wedges

## Instructions

1. Bring 2 large pots of salted water to a boil.
2. Divide lobsters between the pots and boil until shells are pink and lobsters are opaque, about 11 minutes.
3. Meanwhile, melt butter in a small saucepan.
4. Add lemon juice, parsley, chives, and basil.
5. Season with salt and pepper.
6. Serve lobsters with warm herb butter and lemon wedges.
