## Ingredients

- 3  eggs
- 1 cup all-purpose flour
- ½ cup plain bread crumbs
- ½ cup panko bread crumbs
- ½ tablespoon kosher salt
- 1 teaspoon onion powder
- 1 teaspoon garlic powder
- 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
- 1 teaspoon paprika
- 1 teaspoon seafood seasoning (such as Old Bay®)
- 1 pound small cremini mushrooms
- 1  serving nonstick cooking spray

## Instructions

1. Preheat the air fryer to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
2. Beat eggs in a bowl.
3. Combine flour, plain bread crumbs, panko bread crumbs, kosher salt, onion powder, garlic powder, pepper, paprika, and seafood seasoning in a separate bowl.
4. Dip mushrooms in beaten eggs, then in seasoned bread crumb mixture. Dip back into eggs and into seasoned bread crumb mixture again. Place on a plate.
5. Place a batch of mushrooms in the air fryer basket, making sure none are overlapping. Spray with nonstick cooking spray. Cook in the preheated air fryer for 6 minutes. Shake basket and spray any chalky spots with nonstick cooking spray. Cook for 6 minutes more. Repeat with remaining mushrooms.
