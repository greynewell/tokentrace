## Ingredients

- 1 pound carrots, peeled and cut in chunks
- 1 small onion, quartered
- ⅓ cup raw cashews
- 1 (14 ounce) can unsweetened light coconut milk
- 1 teaspoon curry powder
- ½ teaspoon ground ginger
- ½ teaspoon kosher salt  (Optional)
- 1 cup vegetable stock

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
2. Place carrots and onion in an ungreased shallow baking pan; roast 30 to 35 minutes or until tender.
3. Transfer to Oster® Versa® Performance Blender; add cashews, coconut milk and seasonings.
4. PULSE on HIGH 2 to 3 times.
5. Blend on HIGH for 1 to 2 minutes or until creamy.
6. Remove to a medium saucepan; stir in stock.
7. Cook over medium heat until heated through, about 5 minutes.
