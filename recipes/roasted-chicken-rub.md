## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon celery flakes
- 1 tablespoon kosher salt
- 1 tablespoon paprika
- 1 tablespoon garlic powder
- 1 tablespoon onion powder
- 1 tablespoon ground thyme
- 2 teaspoons dried sage
- 1 ½ teaspoons ground black pepper
- 1 ½ teaspoons dried rosemary
- ½ teaspoon cayenne pepper

## Instructions

1. Stir together celery flakes, salt, paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, thyme, sage, black pepper, rosemary, and cayenne pepper in a bowl.
2. Transfer spice mixture to a spice grinder or food processor; blend until smooth.
