## Ingredients

- 4  (3/4 inch-thick) pork chops
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 1 tablespoon all-purpose flour, or as needed
- 1 large shallot, chopped
- ½ cup freshly squeezed tangerine juice
- ½ cup Marsala wine
- 1  orange, zested
- 1 tablespoon cold butter

## Instructions

1. Season pork chops generously with salt and black pepper; dust lightly with flour.
2. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat and add olive oil to the hot skillet.
3. Brown pork chops in the hot oil, about 5 minutes per side.
4. Reduce heat to medium and scatter shallot around chops.
5. Continue to cook meat, flipping once, until chops are fork-tender, 10 to 15 minutes.
6. Stir occasionally to prevent shallots from burning.
7. Remove chops and keep warm.
8. Raise heat to medium-high.
9. Pour tangerine juice and Marsala wine into skillet; bring to a boil.
10. Cook until juice and wine reduce to a glaze, 3 to 5 minutes.
11. Return pork chops to skillet and sprinkle with orange zest; add butter to skillet.
12. Flip chops in glaze until coated.
