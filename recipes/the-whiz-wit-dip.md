## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 1 large onion, diced
- 1 pound thinly sliced rib-eye steak, chopped
- ½ (4 ounce) jar diced jalapeno peppers  (Optional)
- ½ (4 ounce) jar diced pimento peppers  (Optional)
- 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese (such as Philadelphia®), softened
- 1 (15 ounce) jar processed cheese sauce (such as Cheez Whiz®)

## Instructions

1. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat; cook and stir onion in the hot oil until tender, 5 to 10 minutes.
2. Remove onion from skillet to a bowl with a slotted spoon, leaving oil in skillet.
3. Cook and stir steak in the same skillet over medium-high heat until browned and liquid has evaporated, 5 to 10 minutes.
4. Stir onion, jalapeno peppers, and pimento peppers into steak until thoroughly mixed.
5. Add cream cheese to steak mixture; cook and stir until cream cheese is melted, about 5 minutes.
6. Stir cheese sauce into steak mixture; cook and stir until dip is heated through, 3 to 5 minutes.
7. Serve hot.
