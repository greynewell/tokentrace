## Ingredients

- 1 (5 ounce) can tuna, drained
- 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
- 3 tablespoons salsa

## Instructions

1. In a small bowl, blend the tuna, cream cheese and salsa.
2. Serve immediately.
