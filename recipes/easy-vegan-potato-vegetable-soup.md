## Ingredients

- 1  onion, chopped
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 1 pound potatoes, peeled and cubed
- 3  carrots, coarsely shredded
- 1  sweet potato, peeled and cubed
- 1 ¾ cups vegetable broth
- salt and freshly ground pepper to taste
- 1 pinch ground nutmeg, or more to taste

## Instructions

1. Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat and cook onion until soft and translucent, about 5 minutes. Add potatoes, carrots, sweet potato, and vegetable broth and bring to a boil. Cover, reduce heat, and simmer until potatoes are soft, about 20 minutes.
2. Puree vegetables with an immersion blender until smooth. Season with salt, pepper, and nutmeg.
