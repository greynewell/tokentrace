## Ingredients

- ¼ cup olive oil
- ¼ cup butter, or as needed
- 3 stalks celery, diced
- 1 large onion, diced
- 2 cups chopped tomatoes
- 1 tablespoon minced garlic
- 8 cups chicken broth
- 2 cups water
- 1 cup dry white wine
- ¼ cup chopped fresh parsley
- ⅛ teaspoon saffron
- salt and pepper to taste
- 2 pounds whiting (merluzzo), chopped into small chunks
- 12  mussels, in shells and cleaned
- 12  clams in shell, scrubbed

## Instructions

1. Heat olive oil and butter in a large pot over medium heat.
2. Add celery and onion and cook about 3 minutes.
3. Add tomatoes with juices and garlic.
4. Simmer for 10 minutes.
5. Add chicken broth, water, and white wine to the pot with the vegetables and bring to a boil, about 5 minutes.
6. Add parsley and saffron, and season with salt and pepper.
7. Simmer broth 15 minutes more.
8. Add fish to the soup.
9. Place a steamer insert into a saucepan and fill with water to just below the bottom of the steamer.
10. Bring water to a boil, about 5 minutes.
11. Add mussels and clams, cover, and steam until shells open, 5 to 10 minutes.
12. Transfer steamed mussels and clams and the steaming liquid to the pot with the soup.
13. Simmer soup 10 minutes more.
