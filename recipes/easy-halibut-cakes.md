## Ingredients

- ¼ cup vegetable oil, divided
- 1 pound halibut fillets
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
- ¼ cup mayonnaise
- 1  egg
- 1 tablespoon mustard
- ½ teaspoon dried oregano
- ½ teaspoon dried thyme
- ½ teaspoon dried rosemary
- 1 cup bread crumbs

## Instructions

1. Heat 2 tablespoons oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
2. Add halibut fillets; season with salt and pepper.
3. Cook until flesh flakes easily with a fork, about 4 minutes per side.
4. Transfer to a plate; cool until easily handled, about 5 minutes.
5. Flake into small chunks.
6. Whisk mayonnaise, egg, and mustard together in a large bowl.
7. Mix in oregano, thyme, and rosemary.
8. Stir in flaked halibut and bread crumbs.
9. Shape mixture into 6 cakes.
10. Heat remaining 2 tablespoons oil in the skillet over medium-high heat.
11. Add halibut cakes and cook until browned, about 5 minutes per side.
