## Ingredients

- 2 pounds red potatoes, cut into quarters
- 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
- 1 teaspoon salt
- ½ teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
- ½ teaspoon dried rosemary, crushed

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 450 degrees F (250 degrees C).
2. Place potatoes in a large roasting pan and toss with oil, salt, pepper, and rosemary until evenly coated. Spread out potatoes in a single layer.
3. Bake, stirring occasionally, in the preheated oven until tender, about 20 minutes. Serve warm.
