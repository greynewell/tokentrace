## Ingredients

- 5 pounds Yukon Gold potatoes
- ½ cup butter
- 2 cups Parmesan cheese
- 1 cup chopped fresh chives
- 1 ½ cups cream cheese
- ½ medium head garlic, peeled and minced
- 1 pinch salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Peel and quarter potatoes; place in a large pot. Cover with salted water and bring to a boil. Cook until tender but still firm, about 15 minutes. Drain and return potatoes to the pot over low heat to dry for 1 to 2 minutes.
2. Add Parmesan cheese, cream cheese, chives, butter, garlic, salt, and pepper. Use a potato masher to mash until smooth and serve.
