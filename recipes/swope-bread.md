## Ingredients

- 2 cups all-purpose flour
- 4 cups whole wheat flour
- 1 cup white sugar
- 2 teaspoons salt
- 4 teaspoons baking soda
- 4 cups buttermilk

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Lightly grease two 9x5 inch loaf pans.
3. In a large bowl, stir together all-purpose flour, whole wheat flour, sugar and salt.
4. In a separate bowl, dissolve baking soda in buttermilk.
5. Stir buttermilk into flour mixture.
6. Beat well.
7. Pour batter into prepared pans.
8. Bake in preheated oven for one hour.
