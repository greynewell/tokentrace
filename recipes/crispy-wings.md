## Ingredients

- oil for deep frying
- 1 cup all-purpose flour, divided
- 8 tablespoons garlic powder, divided
- 6  eggs, beaten
- 3 cups crushed cornflake crumbs
- 24  chicken wings
- ½  carrot, cut into thick strips

## Instructions

1. Preheat oil in a large, deep skillet or deep fryer.
2. In a shallow dish or bowl mix together 3/4 cup flour with 2 tablespoons garlic powder; in a second shallow dish or bowl combine eggs and 4 tablespoons garlic powder and mix together.
3. In a third shallow dish or bowl mix together cornflake crumbs, remaining 1/4 cup flour and remaining 2 tablespoons garlic powder.
4. Dip each wing in the flour mixture, crumb mixture, egg mixture then back in the crumb mixture.
5. Drop carrot into hot oil; when it starts to fry, drop in the wings.
6. Fry about 10 minutes, or until cooked through - they'll be a deep golden color.
