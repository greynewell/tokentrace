## Ingredients

- 1 pound purple sprouting broccoli
- 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
- 2 tablespoons toasted sesame oil
- 6 cloves garlic, thinly sliced
- salt to taste
- 2 teaspoons toasted sesame seeds, or to taste

## Instructions

1. Bring a large wide pot of salted water to a boil. Drop in broccoli and boil until dark green, 3 to 4 minutes. Drain.
2. Heat vegetable and sesame oils in a large skillet over medium-low heat. Add garlic and cook, stirring frequently, until fragrant and light brown, 2 to 3 minutes. Add broccoli and stir-fry until well seasoned with garlic oil, 2 to 3 minutes. Season with salt.
3. Serve sprinkled with sesame seeds.
