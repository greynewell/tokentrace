## Ingredients

- 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
- ½ cup shredded Cheddar cheese
- 2  jalapeno peppers, seeded and minced
- 4 (6 ounce) skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
- 1 cup seasoned bread crumbs
- ¼ cup vegetable oil

## Instructions

1. Stir together the cream cheese, Cheddar cheese, and jalapenos in a small bowl.
2. Cut a pocket horizontally through the thickest side of each chicken breast, and fill with 1/4 of the cheese mixture.
3. Secure with toothpicks.
4. Roll the chicken breasts in bread crumbs to coat.
5. Heat the oil in a skillet over medium-high heat, and cook chicken breasts until no longer pink in the center and the juices run clear, about 8 minutes per side.
6. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 165 degrees F (74 degrees C).
