## Ingredients

- 7 cups chicken broth
- 3  skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
- 1  onion, quartered
- 1 cup chopped carrots
- 1 small turnip, chopped
- 2 stalks celery, chopped
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
- 1 cup frozen French-cut green beans
- ⅔ cup converted rice (such as Uncle Ben's®)
- ¾ (8 ounce) package cream cheese

## Instructions

1. Bring chicken broth, chicken breasts, onion, carrots, turnip, and celery to a boil in a large pot until chicken is no longer pink in the center, about 20 minutes.
2. Season with salt and pepper.
3. Remove chicken from broth; strain and discard vegetables.
4. Add green beans and rice; continue to simmer until rice is tender, about 20 minutes more.
5. Cut chicken into chunks.
6. Stir chicken and cream cheese into broth until cheese is melted, about 5 minutes.
