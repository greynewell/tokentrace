## Ingredients

- 1 clove garlic, peeled and cut in half
- ¼ cup fresh lemon juice
- ¼ cup olive oil
- 1 pound fresh green beans, trimmed
- 1 teaspoon salt

## Instructions

1. Mix together the garlic, lemon juice, and olive oil in a large bowl; set aside.
2. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
3. Cook the green beans in the water for 5 to 6 minutes; drain.
4. Cool beans for about 10 minutes.
5. Place cooled beans in the large bowl and toss with the lemon juice mixture; season with salt.
6. Allow beans to rest for 2 minutes before stirring again.
7. Repeat 2 minutes rest and stirring once more.
8. Remove garlic before serving.
