## Ingredients

- 1 (10 ounce) package hearts of romaine lettuce, leaves separated
- ½ cup shredded Cheddar cheese
- 8 slices cooked bacon, crumbled
- 4  hard-boiled eggs, chopped
- 3  fully cooked breaded chicken breasts, sliced, or more to taste
- 2  tomatoes, chopped
- 2  avocados, peeled and chopped
- 1  cucumber, peeled and sliced
- 1  avocado, peeled and pitted
- 1  lemon, juiced
- ¼ cup ranch dressing, or to taste

## Instructions

1. Arrange lettuce leaves on 4 plates.
2. Arrange rows of Cheddar cheese, bacon, eggs, chicken, tomatoes, chopped avocados, and cucumber, in that order, over lettuce.
3. Mash pitted avocado in a small bowl; add lemon juice and mix well.
4. Stir ranch dressing into avocado-lemon juice mixture until dressing is smooth.
5. Top salad with dressing.
