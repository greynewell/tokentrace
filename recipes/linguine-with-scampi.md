## Ingredients

- 1 (12 ounce) package linguini pasta
- ¾ cup butter
- 1 pound shrimp, peeled and deveined
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 tablespoons lemon juice
- 3 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
- salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
2. Place the butter or margarine in 9x13 inch glass baking dish, and place in oven until butter melts.
3. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
4. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente.
5. Drain, and transfer to a large serving bowl.
6. Stir the shrimp, garlic, and lemon juice into the melted butter.
7. Return dish to oven.
8. Bake for 3 minutes.
9. Remove from the oven, and mix in parsley; continue baking until shrimp are opaque, about 2 minutes longer.
10. Season with salt and pepper.
11. Spoon the shrimp and butter sauce over the linguini, and toss to coat the pasta.
12. Serve immediately.
