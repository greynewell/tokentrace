## Ingredients

- 8 ounces crabmeat
- 4 ounces uncooked linguine pasta
- 2  eggs, beaten
- 3  green onions, finely chopped
- ¼ cup fine dry bread crumbs
- 2 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
- 2 teaspoons olive oil
- ⅛ teaspoon salt
- ⅛ teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
- 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
- ¼ cup mayonnaise
- 1 tablespoon finely chopped cilantro
- ½ teaspoon finely grated lime zest
- 1 tablespoon fresh lime juice

## Instructions

1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
2. Place pasta in boiling water and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente.
3. Drain, and cut into 1 inch pieces.
4. In a mixing bowl, combine crab, noodles, eggs, green onion, bread crumbs, cilantro, olive oil and salt and pepper.
5. Mix thoroughly.
6. On wax paper, divide mixture into 6 equal piles; form each pile into a patty.
7. Make sure noodles are incorporated and are not sticking out.
8. In a medium skillet, heat vegetable oil and cook patties over medium heat for 3 minutes on each side until golden.
9. Drain on paper towels.
10. Serve with cilantro-lime mayonnaise.
11. For the cilantro-lime mayonnaise, mix mayonnaise, 1 tablespoon cilantro, lime zest and lime juice in a small mixing bowl.
12. Mix thoroughly.
13. Chill until ready to serve.
