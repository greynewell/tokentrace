## Ingredients

- 4 slices prosciutto
- 8 leaves basil, or more to taste
- 1 ½ pounds barramundi (Asian sea bass) fillets
- 2 tablespoons olive oil

## Instructions

1. Lay 2 prosciutto slices on a flat work surface so that they overlap slightly.
2. Scatter 4 basil leaves on top.
3. Add 1 barramundi fillet; wrap up.
4. Repeat with remaining prosciutto, basil leaves, and barramundi.
5. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
6. Add prosciutto-wrapped barramundi; cook until flesh flakes easily with a fork and prosciutto is slightly crisp, 3 to 5 minutes per side.
