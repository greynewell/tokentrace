## Ingredients

- 4 (4 ounce) fillets mahi mahi fillets
- 1 cup Italian-style salad dressing
- 1 pinch lemon-pepper seasoning, or to taste
- garlic powder, or to taste
- 4  raw onion rings
- 1  lemon, quartered

## Instructions

1. Arrange 4 large pieces of aluminum foil on a work surface.
2. Place 1 mahi mahi fillet onto each aluminum foil piece.
3. Pour Italian dressing over each fillet.
4. Sprinkle each fillet with lemon-pepper seasoning and garlic powder; flip fillets and season other side with lemon-pepper seasoning and garlic powder.
5. Place 1 onion ring atop each fillet and squeeze lemon quarter over each fillet.
6. Fold aluminum foil over each fillet, crimping all the corners to completely seal.
7. Place packets in a glass baking dish in the refrigerator to marinate, about 2 hours.
8. Preheat oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C).
9. Bake in the preheated oven until fish flakes easily with a fork, about 30 minutes.
10. Carefully open packets and remove onion before serving.
