## Ingredients

- 6 slices Dietz & Watson Originals Oven Roasted Turkey
- 1 slice Dietz & Watson Colby Jack Cheese
- 1 tablespoon mayonnaise
- 1  spinach tortilla
- 1 thin slice avocado
- ¾ cup vinegar-pickled red cabbage
- ½ cup spring mix

## Instructions

1. Spread the mayo lightly across the spinach tortilla.
2. Layer the slices of Dietz & Watson Originals Oven Roasted Turkey, Dietz & Watson Colby Jack Cheese, and avocado on the tortilla, then add the cup of vinegar pickled red cabbage and spring mix.
3. Wrap up the tortilla and cut it in half.
