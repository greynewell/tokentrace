## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon olive oil, or more as needed
- 4 (8 ounce) bone-in pork loin chops
- 1 cup unsweetened pineapple juice
- ½ cup teriyaki sauce
- 1 teaspoon dried crushed rosemary
- 1 teaspoon garlic powder
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper

## Instructions

1. Heat oil in a skillet over medium-high heat. Add pork chops and brown for about 3 minutes. Transfer chops to a slow cooker.
2. Combine pineapple juice, teriyaki sauce, rosemary, garlic powder, salt, and pepper in a bowl. Pour over pork chops.
3. Cover and cook on Low, 4 to 5 hours. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read 145 degrees F (63 degrees C).
