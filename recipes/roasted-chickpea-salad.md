## Ingredients

- 1  Spanish onion, finely chopped
- 1 (16 ounce) package bella mushrooms, sliced into thirds
- 1  orange bell pepper, diced
- 1 (16 ounce) can chickpeas (garbanzo beans), drained
- 2 tablespoons Hungarian hot paprika
- 1 tablespoon dried oregano
- sea salt to taste

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C).
2. Cook and stir onion, mushrooms, and bell pepper in a nonstick skillet over medium heat until just slightly tender, 5 to 10 minutes.
3. Discard any excess water from vegetables.
4. Transfer vegetables to a 2-quart casserole dish.
5. Add garbanzo beans, paprika, oregano, and salt; stir to coat.
6. Bake in the preheated oven until vegetables are tender, about 15 minutes.
