## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 1 teaspoon minced garlic
- 1 (15.5 ounce) can black beans, drained
- 1  green bell pepper, chopped
- ½  sweet onion, chopped
- ½ (12 ounce) can light beer
- 1 tablespoon fresh chopped cilantro, for garnish

## Instructions

1. Heat the oil in a skillet over medium heat; cook the garlic in the oil 15 seconds.
2. Stir in the onion and green pepper; cook and stir 10 minutes.
3. Pour in the black beans and beer; simmer until thick, about 15 minutes; garnish with cilantro.
