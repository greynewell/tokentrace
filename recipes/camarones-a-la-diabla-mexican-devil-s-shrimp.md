## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons butter
- 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
- 1 pound large shrimp, in their shells
- 10 cloves garlic, pressed
- ½ (12 ounce) bottle Mexican-style hot sauce (such as Valentina® or Cholula®), or to taste
- ½ cup ketchup
- 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
- ½ teaspoon dried oregano
- salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
- 1 cup beer, or as needed

## Instructions

1. Heat butter and oil in a skillet over medium heat.
2. Add shrimp and garlic; cook until shrimp are pink, about 3 minutes.
3. Add hot sauce, ketchup, Worcestershire sauce, oregano, salt, and pepper.
4. Cook until flavors are well blended, 5 to 10 minutes.
5. Pour in beer a little at a time, stirring until dish has desired consistency, about 4 minutes.
6. Cook for 5 minutes more.
