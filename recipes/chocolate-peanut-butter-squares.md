## Ingredients

- 1 cup butter
- 4 cups confectioners' sugar
- 2 cups peanut butter
- 1 ½ cups graham cracker crumbs
- ½ cup butter
- 1 cup semisweet chocolate chips

## Instructions

1. Melt 1 cup butter or margarine over low heat.
2. Remove from heat and stir in confectioners' sugar, peanut butter and graham cracker crumbs.
3. Spread mixture in a jelly roll pan.
4. Pat down evenly.
5. To Make Topping:   Melt together 1/2 cup butter or margarine with 1 cup chocolate chips.
6. Spread this mixture over peanut butter mixture.
7. Refrigerate 1/2 hour.
8. Cut into squares.
