## Ingredients

- 2 ounces Baileys Original Irish Cream
- 5 ounces coffee
- Cinnamon dust and cinnamon stick

## Instructions

1. Make up a glass or mug of 5 ounces black coffee.
2. Add Baileys Original Irish Cream. Stir it all together. Garnish with cinnamon dust and cinnamon stick.
