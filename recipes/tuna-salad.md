## Ingredients

- 1 (5 ounce) can tuna, drained
- 1 (15 ounce) can chickpeas (garbanzo beans), drained
- 2  hard-boiled eggs, chopped
- 1  cucumber, peeled and diced
- 1 head iceberg lettuce, torn into bite-sized pieces
- ½ cup French dressing

## Instructions

1. In a large mixing bowl, combine tuna, chickpeas (garbanzo beans), chopped hard-boiled eggs, diced cucumber, and iceberg lettuce.
2. Toss well and add French dressing to taste.
