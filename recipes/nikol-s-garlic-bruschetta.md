## Ingredients

- 3 tablespoons olive oil
- 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
- 3 tablespoons chopped fresh basil
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 7  roma (plum) tomatoes, chopped
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- 1  sourdough baguette, cut into 1/2 inch thick slices
- 1 (4 ounce) package semi-soft cheese with garlic and herbs (such as Alouette®)

## Instructions

1. Whisk together the olive oil, balsamic vinegar, basil, and garlic in a bowl.
2. Stir in the chopped tomatoes; season to taste with salt and pepper.
3. Cover; place in the refrigerator to marinate for 20 minutes.
4. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
5. Spread the bread slices in a single layer on a large baking pan.
6. Bake in the oven until golden, about 10 minutes.
7. Remove to cool on a wire rack.
8. When the bread is cool, spread the semi-soft cheese evenly over each slice.
9. Using a slotted spoon, spoon some of the tomato mixture over each slice.
