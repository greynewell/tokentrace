## Ingredients

- ½ cup butter
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 cup buttermilk
- 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
- 1 tablespoon corn syrup
- ¼ teaspoon ground cinnamon
- ½ teaspoon baking soda

## Instructions

1. Bring butter, sugar, buttermilk, vanilla extract, corn syrup, and cinnamon to a simmer in a large saucepan over medium-high heat.
2. Whisk in baking soda; cook for 10 seconds before removing from heat. Serve warm.
