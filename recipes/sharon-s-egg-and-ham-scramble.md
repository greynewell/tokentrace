## Ingredients

- 8  eggs, beaten
- 3 tablespoons milk, or more to taste
- ¼ teaspoon seasoned salt (such as Season-All®)
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- ¼ cup olive oil
- 1  jalapeno pepper, seeded and minced
- ½ cup chopped deli slices applewood-smoked ham
- 1 cup finely shredded Cheddar cheese, divided

## Instructions

1. Beat eggs, milk, seasoned salt, salt, and black pepper together in a bowl.
2. Heat olive oil in a large non-stick skillet over medium-high heat; saute jalapeno in hot oil until slightly softened, 2 to 3 minutes. Add ham to jalapeno and cook until heated through, about 1 minute.
3. Pour egg mixture into ham mixture. Cook and stir until eggs are set but not dry, 3 to 5 minutes. Sprinkle 1/2 of the Cheddar cheese over eggs; cook and stir until cheese is melted. Transfer eggs to a plate and sprinkle remaining cheese over the top.
