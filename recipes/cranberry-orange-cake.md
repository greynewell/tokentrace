## Ingredients

- baking spray
- 3 cups all-purpose flour
- 2 cups white sugar
- 1 cup vegetable oil
- 4  eggs
- ⅔ cup orange juice
- 1 tablespoon baking powder
- 2 ½ teaspoons vanilla extract
- ½ teaspoon salt
- 1 ½ cups fresh cranberries
- 1  orange, zested

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 10-inch Bundt pan with nonstick spray.
2. Combine flour, sugar, oil, eggs, orange juice, baking powder, vanilla, and salt in a large bowl; beat with an electric mixer until thoroughly incorporated. Stir in cranberries and orange zest; pour batter into the prepared pan.
3. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean, about 1 hour. Let cake rest for 15 minutes, then invert onto a wire rack to cool for 1 hour.
