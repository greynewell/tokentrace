## Ingredients

- ½ large banana, cut into pieces and frozen
- 2 large fresh strawberries, rinsed and sliced
- ¼ cup blueberries
- ½ cup milk
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 2 tablespoons vanilla yogurt
- 2  ice cubes

## Instructions

1. Place the banana pieces, strawberries, blueberries, milk, vanilla extract, yogurt, and ice cubes in a blender.
2. Blend until smooth.
