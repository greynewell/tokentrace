## Ingredients

- 1 (20 ounce) can white hominy
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- 1 tablespoon chopped jalapeno pepper, or to taste
- ½ cup sour cream
- 1 tablespoon butter, or to taste
- 1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Combine hominy, garlic, and jalapeño in a saucepan. Bring to a boil, reduce heat, and simmer until hominy is tender, 15 to 20 minutes. Strain out liquid and pour hominy mixture into a baking dish. Stir sour cream and butter into hominy mixture; top with Cheddar cheese.
3. Bake in the preheated oven until cheese melts, about 25 minutes.
