## Ingredients

- 1 (15 ounce) can chickpeas (garbanzo beans), drained
- ½  onion, chopped
- ½  cucumber, sliced
- 1 small tomato, chopped
- ½ cup red wine vinegar
- ½ cup balsamic vinegar

## Instructions

1. In a medium bowl combine chickpeas, onion, cucumber, tomato, red wine vinegar and balsamic vinegar.
2. Mix well and serve.
