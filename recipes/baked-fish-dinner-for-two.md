## Ingredients

- 4 tablespoons olive oil, divided
- 2  baking potatoes, cut into 1/2 inch slices
- salt and pepper to taste
- ½ cup water
- ½  red bell pepper, chopped
- 1  leek, bulb only, chopped
- 1  zucchini, thickly sliced
- 2 stalks celery, cut into thin 3 inch long slices
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 pound white fish fillets
- 1  tomato, seeded and chopped
- 2 sprigs fresh parsley or fennel, for garnish

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C).
2. Lightly oil a shallow, 2 quart baking dish.
3. In a medium bowl, toss the potatoes with 2 tablespoons olive oil to coat evenly.
4. Season with salt and pepper.
5. Spread potatoes in baking dish with 1/4 cup water.
6. Cover, and bake for 15 minutes.
7. Combine bell peppers, leek, zucchini, and celery in a medium bowl.
8. Toss with garlic and 2 tablespoons olive oil,.
9. Season to taste with salt and pepper.
10. Spread vegetables over potatoes, add another 1/4 cup water, and cover.
11. Bake for 10  to 15 minutes.
12. Rinse, and cut fish into 1 to 1 1/2 inch cubes, and spread over vegetables.
13. Cover.
14. Bake for 10 minutes, or until fish flakes easily.
15. Serve with tomato and parsley or fennel garnish.
