## Ingredients

- 1 cup cold water
- 1 small banana
- ½ cup spinach, or more to taste
- 1 teaspoon spirulina powder

## Instructions

1. Place cold water, banana, and spinach leaves in a blender; blend to combine, about 5 seconds.
2. Add spirulina powder and blend until smooth, 1 minute more.
