## Ingredients

- 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
- 1 cup sour cream
- 3 tablespoons mayonnaise
- 1 ½ cups chopped yellow onion
- 6  hard-cooked eggs, peeled and chopped
- 3 ½ ounces black caviar
- 3 ½ ounces red caviar
- 4  green onions

## Instructions

1. In a medium bowl, mix together the cream cheese, sour cream and mayonnaise.
2. Stir in the onion and chopped egg.
3. Spread the mixture in a shallow baking dish or glass pie plate.
4. Spoon red and black caviar in a checkerboard pattern over the top.
5. Slice green onions into long thin strips, and use the strips to outline the checkerboard pattern.
6. Chill until serving.
