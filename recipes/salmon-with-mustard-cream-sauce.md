## Ingredients

- 1 (270 g) tub PHILADELPHIA Herb & Garlic Cooking Creme Cream Cheese Product
- ½ cup 25%-less-sodium chicken broth
- 3 tablespoons grainy Dijon mustard, divided
- 6 (4 ounce) salmon fillets, skin removed

## Instructions

1. Heat greased barbecue to medium heat.
2. Cook and stir cooking creme, broth and 1 Tbsp. mustard in saucepan on medium heat 3 min. or until heated through. Remove from heat; cover to keep warm.
3. Brush fish with remaining mustard; grill 2 to 3 min. on each side or until fish flakes easily with fork. Serve with warm sauce.
