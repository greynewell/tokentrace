## Ingredients

- 8  eggs
- 1 ¾ cups milk
- ⅓ cup brown sugar
- ¼ cup butter, melted
- 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
- 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
- ¼ teaspoon ground nutmeg
- 12 slices wheat bread, cubed
- 2  bananas, sliced

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease 9x13-inch baking dish.
2. Whisk eggs, milk, brown sugar, butter, vanilla extract, ground cinnamon, and ground nutmeg together in a bowl. Stir bread cubes and bananas into egg mixture until coated; pour into prepared baking dish.
3. Bake in preheated oven until a knife inserted in center comes out clean, 35 to 40 minutes.
