## Ingredients

- 1 small head green cabbage, cored and shredded
- 1  carrot, peeled and shredded
- 1 small green bell pepper, seeded and chopped
- 1 small onion, chopped
- 3 tablespoons mayonnaise
- 2 tablespoons white vinegar
- 1 tablespoon sugar
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- 4 slices bacon

## Instructions

1. In a large bowl, toss together the cabbage, carrot, green pepper and onion.
2. In a small bowl, whisk together the mayonnaise, vinegar, sugar and salt.
3. Pour over the cabbage mixture, and toss to coat.
4. Cover and refrigerate for at least 4 hours, or overnight.
5. Place the bacon in a large deep skillet.
6. Cook over medium heat until evenly browned, turning occasionally.
7. Drain, cool and crumble.
8. Toss crumbled bacon with salad just before serving.
