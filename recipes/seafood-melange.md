## Ingredients

- 4  sole, patted dry
- 10  bay scallops, raw
- ¾ cup crabmeat
- ¾ cup cooked shrimp
- ½ cup shredded Monterey Jack cheese
- ½ cup butter
- 2  egg yolks
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice
- ½ teaspoon mustard powder
- ⅛ teaspoon salt
- 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
- ¼ teaspoon paprika

## Instructions

1. Butter two 2-cup au gratin dishes.
2. Place 1 fillet on bottom of each, then layer with scallops, crabmeat, shrimp, cheese and a second fillet; set aside.
3. Preheat oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C).
4. Melt butter.
5. In a medium mixing bowl, combine yolks, lemon juice, mustard and salt; mix on high and slowly add butter in a steady stream until sauce is thick and creamy.
6. Pour sauce over fillets.
7. Bake in preheated oven for 10 to 15 minutes; sprinkle with parsley and paprika.
8. Serve.
