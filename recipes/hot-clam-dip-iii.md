## Ingredients

- 2 (6.5 ounce) cans minced clams, drained with juice reserved
- ⅓ cup lemon juice
- ¾ cup seasoned bread crumbs
- ⅓ cup minced onion
- 1 teaspoon dried parsley
- 1 teaspoon dried oregano
- 1 dash ground black pepper
- garlic powder to taste
- 6 tablespoons melted margarine
- ¼ cup grated Parmesan cheese
- paprika to taste

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C).
2. In a medium saucepan over medium heat, slowly cook and stir minced clams in the lemon juice until heated and tender, abou 5 minutes.
3. Remove from heat.
4. Transfer clams to a medium baking dish.
5. Mix in seasoned bread crumbs, onion, parsley, oregano, black pepper, garlic powder, margarine and approximately 3/4 of the reserved clam juice.
6. Top with grated Parmesan cheese and paprika.
7. Bake clam mixture in the preheated oven 30 minutes, or until lightly browned.
