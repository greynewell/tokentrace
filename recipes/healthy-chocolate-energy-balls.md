## Ingredients

- 1 cup raw hemp seeds
- 6  Medjool dates, pitted
- ⅓ cup cocoa powder (such as Hershey's®)

## Instructions

1. Grind hemp seeds into a powder in a food processor; add dates and cocoa powder and process until smooth.
2. Roll dough into balls, pressing tightly.
