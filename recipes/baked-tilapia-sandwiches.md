## Ingredients

- 4 (4 ounce) tilapia fillets
- 2 tablespoons olive oil, divided, or as needed
- ½ teaspoon Cajun seasoning, or to taste
- 1 pinch salt and ground black pepper to taste
- 2 medium onions, thinly sliced
- 1 (14.5 ounce) can diced tomatoes, drained
- 2 tablespoons capers
- 4  submarine sandwich rolls, split

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
2. Brush tilapia lightly with 1 tablespoon olive oil and then sprinkle with Cajun seasoning. Season both sides with salt and pepper and place on a baking sheet.
3. Bake in the preheated oven until fish flakes easily with a fork, about 15 minutes.
4. While fish is cooking, heat remaining olive oil in a pan over medium-high heat. Add onions and saute until tender, 5 to 7 minutes. Season with salt and pepper. Add tomatoes and capers; cook until heated through, 3 to 5 minutes.
5. Place fish on rolls and top with onion, tomato, and caper mixture.
