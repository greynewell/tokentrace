## Ingredients

- 2 cups gluten-free all purpose baking flour
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- ½ teaspoon salt
- 2 tablespoons shortening
- 1 cup hot water

## Instructions

1. Combine flour, baking powder, and salt in a bowl.
2. Cut shortening into flour mixture until mixture resembles corn meal.
3. Stir in hot water, 1 to 2 tablespoons at a time, until mixture forms into a ball.
4. Knead dough until evenly combined.
5. Cut dough into 8 pieces; roll each piece into a ball.
6. Press each ball into a flat tortilla-shape.
7. Heat a griddle or skillet over medium-high heat.
8. Cook each tortilla in the hot griddle until lightly browned, 1 to 2 minutes per side.
