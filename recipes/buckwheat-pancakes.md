## Ingredients

- 1 cup buckwheat flour
- 1 ½ teaspoons white sugar
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- ¼ teaspoon baking soda
- 1 ¼ cups buttermilk
- 1  large egg, beaten
- ¼ teaspoon vanilla extract
- 1 tablespoon unsalted butter, or as needed

## Instructions

1. Whisk buckwheat flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt together in a medium bowl.
2. Beat buttermilk, egg, and vanilla together in a large bowl. Pour flour mixture into buttermilk mixture; whisk until batter is thick and smooth. Let batter rest until bubbles form and batter relaxes, about 5 minutes
3. Melt butter on a griddle over medium heat.
4. Drop batter by large spoonfuls onto the hot griddle and cook until bubbles form and the edges dry, 3 to 4 minutes. Flip and cook until browned on the other side, 2 to 3 minutes. Repeat with remaining batter.
