## Ingredients

- 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese (such as Philadelphia®), softened
- 1 cup frozen whipped topping (such as Cool Whip®), thawed
- 1 cup confectioners' sugar
- 1 (9 inch) prepared graham cracker crust
- 2 (3.9 ounce) packages instant chocolate pudding mix
- 3 cups milk
- 1 cup frozen whipped topping (such as Cool Whip®), thawed - or to taste

## Instructions

1. Beat cream cheese, 1 cup whipped topping, and confectioners' sugar together in a bowl; spread into the prepared graham cracker crust.
2. Whisk chocolate pudding mix and milk together in a bowl; pour over the cream cheese layer before it has a chance to set.
3. Refrigerate until pudding sets, about 10 minutes.
4. Top pie with 1 cup whipped topping; refrigerate at least 1 hour more before serving.
