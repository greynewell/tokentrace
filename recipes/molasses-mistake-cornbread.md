## Ingredients

- ⅔ cup cornmeal
- ¾ cup all-purpose flour
- 1 tablespoon baking powder
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1  egg, beaten
- 1 cup milk
- 2 tablespoons molasses

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
2. Lightly grease an 8x8 inch baking pan.
3. In a large bowl, stir together cornmeal, flour, baking powder and salt.
4. In a separate bowl, stir together egg, milk and molasses.
5. Stir egg mixture into flour mixture; mix until blended.
6. Pour batter into prepared pan.
7. Bake in preheated oven for 15 to 20 minutes, until golden brown and a toothpick inserted into center of pan comes out clean.
