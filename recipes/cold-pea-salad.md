## Ingredients

- 2 cups frozen sweet peas, thawed
- ¾ cup pecans
- ¼ cup drained, crushed pineapple
- ¼ teaspoon lime juice

## Instructions

1. Mix peas, pecans, pineapple, and lime juice together in a bowl.
2. Refrigerate 30 to 60 minutes before serving.
