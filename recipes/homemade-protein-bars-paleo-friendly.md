## Ingredients

- 2 cups walnuts
- 1 cup pecans
- 2 cups almonds
- 1 cup pumpkin seeds
- 1 cup dried cranberries
- 1 cup vanilla protein powder
- ½ cup pitted dates
- ½ cup raisins
- ½ cup coconut flour
- ¼ cup maple syrup
- 3 tablespoons coconut oil
- 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
- 1 ½ teaspoons ground cinnamon
- 1 ½ teaspoons molasses

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 220 degrees F (104 degrees C).
2. Arrange walnuts and pecans on a baking sheet.
3. Bake in the preheated oven until nuts are roasted and fragrant, about 30 minutes.
4. Remove nuts from oven.
5. Increase oven temperature to 230 degrees F (110 degrees C).
6. Grease a 9x13-inch pan.
7. Pulse roasted walnuts, roasted pecans, and almonds together in a food processor until mixture is resembles a coarse meal; transfer to a large bowl.
8. Add pumpkin seeds, cranberries, protein powder, dates, raisins, coconut flour, maple syrup, coconut oil, vanilla extract, cinnamon, and molasses; mix well.
9. Press mixture into the prepared pan.
10. Bake in the preheated oven until golden brown, about 40 minutes.
