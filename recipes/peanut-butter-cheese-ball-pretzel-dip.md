## Ingredients

- 1 cup creamy peanut butter
- 1 (4 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
- ¼ cup butter, softened
- ½ teaspoon vanilla extract
- 1 ½ cups confectioners' sugar
- ½ cup mini chocolate chips
- ¼ cup chocolate sprinkles, or as needed  (Optional)
- 1 (7.2 ounce) package thin pretzel crackers (such as Snack Factory® Pretzel Crisps®)

## Instructions

1. Combine peanut butter, cream cheese, and butter in a bowl; beat together with an electric mixer until creamy.
2. Beat in vanilla extract.
3. Mix in confectioners' sugar, 1/2 cup at a time, until incorporated.
4. Fold in chocolate chips.
5. Pour chocolate sprinkles onto a plate.
6. Shape peanut butter mixture into a ball and roll in chocolate sprinkles until completely coated.
7. Transfer to a serving dish.
8. Arrange pretzel crackers on the dish around the ball.
9. Chill until firm, at least 1 hour.
