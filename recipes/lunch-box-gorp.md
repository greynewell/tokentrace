## Ingredients

- ½ cup raisins
- ½ cup toasted oat cereal (such as Cheerios®)
- ½ cup chocolate chips
- ½ cup butterscotch chips
- ½ cup miniature fish-shaped crackers (such as Goldfish®)

## Instructions

1. Mix raisins, oat cereal, chocolate chips, butterscotch chips, and fish-shaped crackers in a small mixing bowl.
2. Divide into snack bags or small containers.
