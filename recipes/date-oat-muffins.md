## Ingredients

- 1 cup all-purpose flour
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- ½ teaspoon baking soda
- ½ teaspoon salt
- 1 cup rolled oats
- 1 cup milk
- ⅓ cup packed brown sugar
- 2  egg whites
- ⅓ cup butter, melted
- ¼ cup applesauce
- ½ cup chopped pitted dates

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Grease a muffin pan or line with paper muffin liners.
3. In a large bowl, sift together flour, baking powder, baking soda and salt.
4. Stir in the oats, milk, sugar, egg whites, butter, applesauce and dates; mix just to combine.
5. Spoon batter into prepared muffin cups.
6. Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 30 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into a muffin comes out clean.
