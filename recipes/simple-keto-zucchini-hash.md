## Ingredients

- 4 small zucchini, grated and squeezed dry
- 3 tablespoons coconut oil
- 1 tablespoon butter
- ⅓ cup grated Parmesan cheese
- 1 teaspoon chili powder, or more to taste
- 1 teaspoon sea salt
- 1 teaspoon cayenne pepper  (Optional)
- 2  eggs, beaten

## Instructions

1. Combine zucchini with coconut oil and butter in a medium skillet over medium heat.
2. Add Parmesan cheese, chili powder, salt, and cayenne pepper.
3. Stir until cheese melts.
4. Reduce heat to low and add eggs; stir quickly until fully mixed in.
5. Increase heat back to medium and cook, stirring and flipping occasionally, until edges of the hash are lightly browned, about 15 minutes.
