## Ingredients

- 1 (8 ounce) package whipped pineapple cream cheese
- 1 ½ cups crushed pineapple in juice, well drained
- 6  (8 inch) flour tortillas
- 6 ounces sliced fully cooked deli ham

## Instructions

1. Mix the cream cheese and pineapple in a bowl until evenly blended.
2. Place the tortillas on a clean work surface.
3. Spread 2/3 of each tortilla with the cream cheese mixture.
4. Place a slice of ham over the cream cheese.
5. Roll up each tortilla towards its uncovered edge.
6. If needed, seal the edge with a dollop of cream cheese.
7. Wrap each tortilla roll in plastic wrap, sealing tightly.
8. Refrigerate at least 2 hours.
9. Unwrap, and cut into 1/2 inch wide pieces.
