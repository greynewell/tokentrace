## Ingredients

- ½ pound large shrimp - peeled and deveined
- 2 cups fresh broccoli florets
- 1 (6 ounce) jar sun-dried tomatoes packed in oil, drained and chopped
- ½ teaspoon minced garlic
- ½ cup butter
- 1 (8 ounce) package angel hair pasta

## Instructions

1. Put the heat on under the pasta pot full of water.
2. In a large saute pan heat the oil from the drained sun-dried tomatoes.
3. Add butter.
4. When butter is melted saute the garlic about 1 minute, add the sun-dried tomatoes, heat for about 1 minute.
5. Add shelled and deveined shrimp.
6. Cook until shrimp are done, about 5-8 minutes.
7. When pasta water comes to a boil, toss in the broccoli florets.
8. Cook about 2-3 minutes.
9. Then add angel hair.
10. Cook according to package instructions.
11. Drain and turn into large past bowl.
12. Spoon shrimp mixture over pasta and serve.
