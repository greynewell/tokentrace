## Ingredients

- 2 ½ cups water
- 1 packet dry onion soup mix
- 2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
- 1 teaspoon garlic powder
- 1 teaspoon dried marjoram
- 1 teaspoon dried thyme
- 1 teaspoon dried oregano
- 4 pounds chuck roast
- 1 (10 ounce) package frozen bell pepper stir-fry mix

## Instructions

1. In a slow cooker combine the water, soup mix, Worcestershire sauce, garlic powder, marjoram, thyme and oregano.
2. Add the meat and the stir-fry mix.
3. Cook for half a day on high setting or all day on low setting, or until the meat falls apart.
4. Pull the meat apart and stir all together.
