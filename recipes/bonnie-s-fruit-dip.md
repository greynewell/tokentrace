## Ingredients

- 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
- ½ cup sour cream, for topping
- 1 cup whipped cream
- ¼ cup brown sugar
- ¼ cup white sugar
- 1 tablespoon maple syrup
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

## Instructions

1. In a mixing bowl, combine the cream cheese, sour cream, whipped cream, brown sugar, sugar, maple syrup and vanilla extract.
2. Mix until smooth.
3. Serve immediately or chill for later.
