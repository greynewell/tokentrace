## Ingredients

- 1 cup mayonnaise
- 2 tablespoons lemon juice
- 2 tablespoons agave nectar
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- 1 tablespoon apple cider vinegar
- fresh ground black pepper
- sea salt
- 6 cups broccoli florets, chopped
- 1  apple - peeled, cored, and chopped
- ½ cup slivered almonds
- ½ cup grated carrots
- ¼ cup diced red onion
- ¼ cup dried cranberries

## Instructions

1. Mix mayonnaise, lemon juice, agave nectar, garlic, apple cider vinegar, black pepper, and sea salt together in a bowl.
2. Combine broccoli, apple, almonds, carrots, red onion, and cranberries in a separate bowl.
3. Pour dressing into the salad; toss until well-coated.
4. Chill in the refrigerator for 30 minutes.
