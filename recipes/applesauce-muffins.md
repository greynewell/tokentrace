## Ingredients

- 2 cups all-purpose flour
- 1 teaspoon baking soda
- ½ teaspoon ground allspice
- ½ teaspoon ground nutmeg
- ½ teaspoon ground cinnamon
- ½ teaspoon ground cloves
- ¼ teaspoon ground ginger
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- ⅓ cup shortening
- ¼ cup honey
- 1 cup unsweetened applesauce
- 1 cup raisins
- ¼ cup crushed walnuts

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Line 12 muffin cups with paper liners.
3. Mix flour, baking soda, allspice, nutmeg, cinnamon, cloves, ginger, and salt together in a bowl.
4. Beat shortening with an electric mixer in a separate large bowl until smooth; add honey and mix for 2 minutes.
5. Stir flour mixture into shortening mixture, alternating with applesauce, until batter is smooth.
6. Fold in raisins and walnuts.
7. Spoon about 2 1/2 tablespoons batter into each muffin cup.
8. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted in the center of a muffin comes out clean, 20 to 25 minutes.
