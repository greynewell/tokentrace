## Ingredients

- 2 cups all-purpose flour
- ¼ teaspoon baking soda
- 1 ½ teaspoons baking powder
- 2 tablespoons butter, melted
- ⅓ cup raspberry syrup
- ⅓ cup buttermilk
- ¼ cup heavy cream
- 1 cup raspberries

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Lightly grease a baking sheet.
3. In a large bowl, stir together flour, baking soda and baking powder.
4. Mix in butter and raspberry syrup.
5. Add buttermilk and cream; mix just until moistened.
6. Fold in raspberries.
7. Form into an oblong loaf and place on a baking sheet.
8. Bake in preheated oven for 12 to 15 minutes.
9. Remove from the oven and cut loaf into 6 wedge shaped pieces.
10. Place pieces on baking sheet and continue baking for 15 minutes, until golden.
