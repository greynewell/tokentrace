## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
- 2 (6 ounce) filets arctic char
- 1 teaspoon kosher salt
- freshly ground black pepper
- lemon juice
- 1 (8 ounce) package mixed salad greens
- 1  mango - peeled, seeded, and sliced
- 6  strawberries, sliced
- ¼ cup roasted pecans
- 2 teaspoons extra-virgin olive oil

## Instructions

1. Heat 2 tablespoons olive oil in a grill pan or skillet over medium-high heat.
2. Season arctic char with kosher salt and black pepper.
3. Cook fillets on grill pan skin side up until lightly browned, 3 to 4 minutes.
4. Flip fillets and continue to cook until fish flakes easily with a fork, 3 to 4 minutes more.
5. Pour lemon juice over fish; remove from heat.
6. Divide salad greens over two plates and arrange mango, strawberries, and pecans on top; season with salt and black pepper.
7. Carefully place an arctic char fillet on each portion and drizzle pan juices and remaining 2 teaspoon olive oil over salads.
