## Ingredients

- 2 cups milk chocolate chips, or more to taste
- 1 cup peanut butter
- ½ cup butter
- 1 (17.5 ounce) package crispy rice cereal squares (such as Rice Chex ®)
- 1 tablespoon confectioners' sugar, or to taste
- 1 (12 ounce) can salted peanuts
- 1 (10.5 ounce) package miniature marshmallows
- 1 (3 ounce) package sunflower seeds
- 1 (1.69 ounce) package candy-coated milk chocolate pieces (such as M&M's®)
- 1 (1.5 ounce) box raisins

## Instructions

1. Cook and stir chocolate chips, peanut butter, and butter together in a pot over low heat until melted, about 5 minutes.
2. Place cereal squares in a bowl and pour chocolate mixture over cereal and stir until evenly coated.
3. Add confectioners' sugar to coated cereal and stir to coat.
4. Allow puppy chow to cool.
5. Mix puppy chow, peanuts, marshmallows, sunflower seeds, chocolate pieces, and raisins together in a large bowl.
