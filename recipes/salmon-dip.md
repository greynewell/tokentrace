## Ingredients

- 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
- ½ cup sour cream
- ¼ cup butter, softened
- 2 tablespoons chopped pimento peppers
- 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley
- 1 teaspoon grated onion
- 1 (7 ounce) can salmon, drained, bones and skin removed
- ¼ teaspoon dried dill weed

## Instructions

1. In a medium bowl, combine the cream cheese, sour cream, butter, pimentos, parsley and onion; beat with an electric mixer until smooth.
2. Stir in the salmon and dill.
3. Cover and refrigerate until serving.
