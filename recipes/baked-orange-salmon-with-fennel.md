## Ingredients

- 1 small fennel bulb, trimmed and diced
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 2 (8 ounce) salmon fillets
- ½ cup orange juice
- 1 pinch Old Bay Seasoning™

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (150 degrees C).
2. In a medium skillet over medium heat, cook fennel in olive oil until translucent, about 20 minutes.
3. Place the fillets skin side down in a glass baking dish.
4. Pour the orange juice over the fillets.
5. Sprinkle fennel over in an even layer, and season with  .
6. Cover the dish with aluminum foil.
7. Bake for 20 to 25 minutes, or until fish flakes easily with a fork.
