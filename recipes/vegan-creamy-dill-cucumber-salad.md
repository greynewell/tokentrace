## Ingredients

- 3 large cucumbers, peeled and diced
- ¾ cup vegan mayonnaise (such as Follow Your Heart® Veganaise®)
- 3 stalks celery, diced
- ½  lemon, juiced
- ¾ cup minced fresh dill
- 2 tablespoons fresh garlic, minced
- 1 teaspoon pink Himalayan salt
- 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
- ½ teaspoon dried dill weed

## Instructions

1. Combine cucumbers, vegan mayonnaise, celery, lemon juice, fresh dill, garlic, salt, black pepper, and dried dill in a bowl.
2. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap.
3. Refrigerate at least 2 hours before serving.
