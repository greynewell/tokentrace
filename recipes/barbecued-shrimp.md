## Ingredients

- 2 cups butter
- 2 cups olive oil
- ¾ cup Worcestershire sauce
- 6 tablespoons ground black pepper
- 4  lemons, sliced
- ½ teaspoon hot pepper sauce
- 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 teaspoon paprika
- 4 teaspoons salt
- 8 pounds large shrimp - peeled and deveined

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C).
2. Heat butter and oil in a large saucepan.
3. Add Worcestershire sauce, black pepper, lemons, hot sauce, Italian seasoning, garlic, paprika and salt to the saucepan.
4. Mix well and simmer 5 to 7 minutes.
5. Divide shrimp between two Dutch ovens.
6. Pour 1/2 of the sauce into one Dutch oven and 1/2 of the sauce into the other.
7. Heat the sauce in both Dutch ovens to a simmer and cook the shrimp for 6 to 8 minutes; until the shrimp begin to turn pink.
8. When the shrimp have begun to turn pink pour the shrimp and sauce from both Dutch ovens into a large baking pan (or two pans depending on how much sauce you have).
9. Bake the shrimp for 10 minutes, stirring once.
