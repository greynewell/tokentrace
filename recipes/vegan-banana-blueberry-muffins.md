## Ingredients

- 2  very ripe bananas, mashed
- ½ cup white sugar
- ½ teaspoon baking powder
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ¾ cup all-purpose flour
- ½ cup whole wheat pastry flour
- 1 ½ teaspoons egg replacer (dry)
- 2 tablespoons water
- ½ cup blueberries

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Grease muffin cups or line with paper muffin liners.
3. In a large bowl combine mashed bananas, sugar, baking powder, salt and flours; mix until smooth.
4. In a small bowl or cup combine egg replacer and water; stir into banana mixture.
5. Fold in blueberries.
6. Spoon batter evenly, about 1/4 cup each, into muffin cups.
7. Bake in preheated oven for 20 to 25 minutes, or until golden brown.
