## Ingredients

- ¾ cup beer
- ¾ cup teriyaki sauce
- ½ cup Worcestershire sauce
- ⅓ cup soy sauce
- ⅓ cup water
- 3 tablespoons liquid smoke
- 4 ½ teaspoons ketchup
- 1 teaspoon red pepper flakes
- 1 ½ teaspoons salt
- ½ teaspoon onion salt
- ¼ teaspoon cracked black pepper, or to taste
- ¼ teaspoon garlic powder
- 1 pound beef round steak

## Instructions

1. Stir together beer, teriyaki sauce, Worcestershire sauce, soy sauce, water, liquid smoke, and ketchup in a large bowl.
2. Stir in red pepper flakes, salt, onion salt, pepper, and garlic powder until blended.
3. Cut the round steak into long strips about 1 inch wide by 1/4 inch thick.
4. Toss the meat with the marinade until the strips are well coated.
5. Tightly cover the bowl, and marinate in the refrigerator at least 6 hours.
6. Prepare jerky in a food dehydrator according to manufacturer's directions.
