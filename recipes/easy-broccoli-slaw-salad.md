## Ingredients

- ⅔ cup olive oil
- ⅓ cup cider vinegar
- ⅔ cup white sugar
- 2 (3 ounce) packages chicken ramen noodles, broken
- 10 ounces broccoli coleslaw mix
- ¾ cup sliced almonds
- ¾ cup golden raisins  (Optional)
- ½ cup sliced green onions
- ½ cup chopped green bell pepper

## Instructions

1. Whisk olive oil, vinegar, sugar, and ramen noodle seasoning packets together in a bowl.
2. Combine broccoli coleslaw mix, broken ramen noodles, almonds, raisins, green onions, and green bell pepper together in a bowl; fold in dressing. Refrigerate at least 2 hours. Stir again before serving.
