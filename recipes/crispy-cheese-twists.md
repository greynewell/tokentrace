## Ingredients

- ½ cup Parmesan cheese
- ¾ teaspoon ground black pepper
- ½ teaspoon garlic powder
- 1 (17.5 ounce) package frozen puff pastry, thawed
- 1  egg white

## Instructions

1. Combine parmesan cheese, pepper and garlic powder.
2. Unfold pastry sheets onto cutting board.
3. Brush lightly with egg white; sprinkle each sheet with 1/4 of the cheese mixture.
4. Lightly press into pastry, turn over; repeat.
5. Cut each sheet into 12 (1-inch) strips; twist.
6. Place on ungreased cookie sheet and bake in 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) oven for 15 minutes or until golden brown.
