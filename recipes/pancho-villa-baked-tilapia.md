## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 4 (4 ounce) fillets tilapia
- 1 (10 ounce) can diced tomatoes with green chile peppers
- 1  lime, juiced
- 4 tablespoons minced fresh cilantro
- 1  lime, thinly sliced

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Coat one side of 4 (8x10 inch) pieces of aluminum foil with olive oil.
3. Center each fillet on a foil square.
4. Spoon a generous amount of diced tomatoes with juices over fish.
5. Sprinkle with lime juice and cilantro.
6. Position 2 slices of lime on top of each fillet.
7. Close and seal foil packets, and place on a baking tray.
8. Bake in preheated oven for approximately 20 minutes, or until fish flakes easily with a fork.
