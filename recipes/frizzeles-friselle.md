## Ingredients

- 3 ½ cups all-purpose flour
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
- 1 cup olive oil
- 1 cup cold water

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
2. Whisk flour, baking powder, salt, and black pepper together in a bowl.
3. Stir olive oil and cold water into dry ingredients, mixing to make a soft dough.
4. Pinch off a handful of dough, roll into a thick log between your hands,  and use a scissors to cut into pieces about 2 inches long.
5. Place biscuits about 1 1/2 inches apart on baking sheets.
6. Bake in the preheated oven on middle rack for 15 minutes; flip biscuits and bake until golden brown on both sides, about 15 minutes more.
7. Let cool on pieces of waxed paper.
8. Place biscuits into a sealed container and refrigerate overnight for best flavor.
