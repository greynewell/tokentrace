## Ingredients

- 2 cups elbow macaroni
- 1 (9 ounce) can tuna, drained
- 1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of mushroom soup
- 1 (10.75 ounce) can  milk
- 1 cup shredded sharp Cheddar cheese
- 1 cup crushed croutons

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
3. Cook macaroni in boiling water for 8 to 10 minutes, or until al dente; drain.
4. In a medium bowl, combine tuna, condensed soup, and milk.
5. Mix with the macaroni, and pour into 2 quart casserole dish.
6. Sprinkle Cheddar cheese and crushed croutons over top.
7. Bake in a preheated oven for 20 to 30 minutes, or until bubbling and browned on the top.
