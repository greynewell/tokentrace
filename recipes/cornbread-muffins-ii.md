## Ingredients

- 1 ¾ cups cornmeal
- ¾ cup all-purpose flour
- 4 teaspoons baking powder
- ¼ teaspoon baking soda
- 1 teaspoon salt
- ¼ cup white sugar
- 2 cups buttermilk
- 2  eggs
- ¼ cup vegetable oil

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C).
2. Lightly grease or line 12 muffin cups.
3. In a large bowl, mix together cornmeal, all-purpose flour, baking powder, baking soda, salt and sugar.
4. In a separate bowl, combine buttermilk, eggs and vegetable oil; beat well.
5. Pour buttermilk mixture into flour mixture; stir until blended.
6. Pour batter into prepared muffin cups.
7. Bake in preheated oven for 15 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of a muffin comes out clean.
