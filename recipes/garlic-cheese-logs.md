## Ingredients

- 1 pound shredded sharp Cheddar cheese
- 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
- 1 cup chopped pecans
- 2 tablespoons mayonnaise
- 2 tablespoons lemon juice
- 2 cloves garlic, chopped
- ½ teaspoon paprika

## Instructions

1. In a medium bowl, mix the Cheddar cheese, cream cheese, pecans, mayonnaise, lemon juice and garlic.
2. Form into 2 or 3 logs.
3. Sprinkle each log with paprika.
4. Chill the logs until firm, about 2 hours.
5. Slice each log into rounds as needed, and serve with crackers.
