## Ingredients

- 8  graham crackers, crushed
- 1 pound semisweet chocolate chips
- 1 (14 ounce) can sweetened condensed milk
- ¼ cup unsalted butter
- 2 tablespoons vanilla extract
- 1 (10.5 ounce) package miniature marshmallows

## Instructions

1. Line an 11x7-inch baking dish with parchment paper. Sprinkle 1/2 of the crushed graham crackers on the bottom of the dish. Set aside.
2. Melt chocolate chips, condensed milk, butter, and vanilla in the top of a double boiler over simmering water, stirring frequently and scraping down the sides with a rubber spatula to avoid scorching.
3. Pour 1/2 of the melted chocolate mixture into the prepared baking dish; sprinkle 1/2 of the marshmallows over chocolate mixture. Repeat layers with remaining crushed graham crackers, chocolate mixture, and marshmallows.
4. Chill in the refrigerator until solid, 3 hours to overnight.
