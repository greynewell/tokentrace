## Ingredients

- ½ (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
- ¼ cup white sugar
- ½ teaspoon lemon zest
- ⅛ teaspoon vanilla extract
- 2  eggs
- ¾ cup milk
- ½ cup vegetable oil
- 2 cups all-purpose flour
- ⅓ cup white sugar
- 3 teaspoons baking powder
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ¼ cup confectioners' sugar

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Oil bottoms only of medium muffin tins.
3. Mix together cream cheese, 1/4 cup white sugar, lemon peel, vanilla, and 1 egg with an electric mixer.
4. Set filling aside.
5. In a large bowl, mix together flour, 1/3 cup white sugar, baking powder, and salt until well blended.
6. Beat remaining egg, and stir in milk and oil; pour into flour mixture, and stir until moistened.
7. Fill muffin cups about 1/2 full.
8. Spoon 1 teaspoon filling onto batter.
9. Top with batter to 3/4 full.
10. Bake 30 to 35 minutes.
11. Don't brown these; muffins should be light in color.
12. Roll while still hot in sifted confectioners' sugar.
