## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
- 1  onion, sliced
- 2  tomatoes, chopped
- 2 pounds zucchini, peeled and cut into 1 inch thick slices
- 1  green bell pepper, chopped
- salt to taste
- ground black pepper to taste
- ¼ cup uncooked white rice
- ½ cup water

## Instructions

1. Heat oil in a saute pan over medium heat.
2. Add onion, and cook and stir for 3 minutes.
3. Add tomatoes, zucchini, and green pepper.
4. Stir.
5. Season to taste with salt and black pepper.
6. Reduce heat, cover, and simmer for 5 minutes.
7. Stir in rice and water.
8. Cover, and cook over low heat for 20 minutes.
