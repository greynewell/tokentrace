## Ingredients

- 3 tablespoons chopped walnuts
- 8 ounces chilled goat cheese, rolled into 8 balls
- 8  bamboo toothpicks
- 8 (2 ounce) roasted beet wedges
- 24  fresh spinach leaves
- 3 tablespoons honey

## Instructions

1. Pour walnuts into a shallow bowl.
2. Roll goat cheese balls in walnuts to coat completely.
3. Thread 1 coated goat cheese ball onto a toothpick; add beet wedge and 4 baby spinach leaves to the toothpick.
4. Repeat with remaining toothpicks, goat cheese, beets, and spinach.
5. Drizzle each skewer with honey.
