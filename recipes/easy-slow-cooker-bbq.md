## Ingredients

- 1 (3 pound) boneless pork shoulder (Boston Butt) roast
- ¾ cup cider vinegar
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- 1 cup barbeque sauce, or to taste

## Instructions

1. Place pork shoulder in the slow cooker. Pour vinegar over pork, coating all sides; season with salt and pepper.
2. Cook on Low for 10 to 12 hours.
3. Transfer pork to a platter. Drain and discard juices from the slow cooker. Chop pork and return to the slow cooker.
4. Add barbeque sauce and continue cooking for 30 minutes.
