## Ingredients

- 2 (7 ounce) packages chunk light tuna in water, drained and flaked
- ⅓ cup ranch dressing
- ½ teaspoon Dijon mustard
- 1 tablespoon grated Parmesan cheese
- 4  chipotle peppers in adobo sauce, chopped
- ¼ cup green onions, chopped
- 1 pinch salt to taste

## Instructions

1. Mix tuna, ranch dressing, Dijon mustard, and Parmesan cheese in a bowl.
2. Add chipotle peppers and green onions; fold through the tuna mixture.
3. Season with salt.
