## Ingredients

- 2 teaspoons ground dried sage
- 1 ½ teaspoons ground dried thyme
- 1 teaspoon ground dried marjoram
- ¾ teaspoon ground dried rosemary
- ½ teaspoon ground nutmeg
- ½ teaspoon finely ground black pepper

## Instructions

1. Mix sage, thyme, marjoram, rosemary, nutmeg, and black pepper in a small bowl.
2. Transfer seasoning to an airtight container. Label and store with your other spices.
