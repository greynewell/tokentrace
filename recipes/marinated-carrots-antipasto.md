## Ingredients

- 1 ⅓ pounds carrots, peeled
- 6 tablespoons olive oil
- 5 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
- 1 pinch dried oregano
- 1 pinch salt
- 1 pinch cayenne pepper
- 3 cloves garlic, minced

## Instructions

1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil and cook carrots until softened slightly yet firm to the bite, 5 to 10 minutes.
2. Make sure not to overcook because they continue cooking even after they have been taken off the stove.
3. Drain and cut into 1-inch long strips.
4. Stir together olive oil, balsamic vinegar, oregano, salt, and cayenne pepper in a small bowl.
5. Add garlic to marinade and mix to combine.
6. Place carrot strips into a container with a tight-fitting lid and pour marinade on top.
7. Cover and marinate in the refrigerator for 24 hours.
