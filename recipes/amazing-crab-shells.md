## Ingredients

- 36  jumbo pasta shells
- 2 (8 ounce) packages Neufchatel cheese
- 1 pound imitation crabmeat
- 6 ounces cooked tiny shrimp
- 1  onion, minced
- 2 stalks celery, chopped
- ⅓ cup mayonnaise
- 2 tablespoons white sugar
- 1 ½ teaspoons salt
- ½ teaspoon ground black pepper
- 1 teaspoon lemon juice

## Instructions

1. Bring a large pot of salted water to boil, and add pasta shells; boil until al dente.
2. Drain well.
3. In a large mixing bowl, combine cream cheese, crab, shrimp, onion, celery, mayonnaise, sugar, salt, pepper and lemon juice; mix well.
4. Stuff cream cheese mixture into the jumbo pasta shells.
5. Chill for at least 2 hours before serving.
