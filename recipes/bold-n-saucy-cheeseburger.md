## Ingredients

- 1 pound extra lean ground beef
- ¼ cup A.1. Bold & Spicy Steak Sauce, divided
- 4 slices  red onion
- 4 each KRAFT 2% Milk Singles
- 4 roll (blank)s whole wheat hamburger buns, split
- 4 slices tomato

## Instructions

1. Preheat grill to medium-high heat. Mix meat and 2 tablespoons of the steak sauce in medium bowl. Shape into 4 (1/2-inch-thick) patties. Spray both sides of onion slices with cooking spray and set aside.
2. Grill patties 4 minutes on each side or until cooked through, adding onions to the grill after 2 minutes and cooking until crisp-tender. Top the burgers with the 2% Milk Singles and continue grilling until the 2% Milk Singles are melted.
3. Place burgers on bottom halves of buns. Top evenly with remaining 2 tablespoons steak sauce. Cover with onions, tomatoes and top halves of buns.
