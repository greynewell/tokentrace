## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon lemon juice
- 1 ½ teaspoons white sugar
- 1 cup finely chopped strawberries
- 1 (8 ounce) can unsweetened crushed pineapple, drained
- 3  kiwis, peeled and finely chopped
- 1  naval orange, peeled and cut into small pieces

## Instructions

1. Stir lemon juice and sugar together in a bowl to dissolve the sugar; add strawberries, pineapple, kiwis, and orange pieces and stir to coat in the lemon juice mixture.
2. Cover bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate at least 30 minutes.
