## Ingredients

- 1 cup all-purpose flour
- ½ cup white sugar
- 1 (3.9 ounce) package instant chocolate fudge pudding mix
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ½ teaspoon baking powder
- 1 cup mashed overripe bananas
- 1 cup miniature milk chocolate chips
- 3  large eggs
- ½ cup olive oil

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a loaf pan.
2. Mix flour, sugar, pudding mix, salt, and baking powder together in a bowl. Stir in bananas, 3/4 of the chocolate chips, eggs, and oil. Pour mixture into the prepared pan and sprinkle the rest of the chocolate chips on top.
3. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean, about 1 hour 20 minutes.
