## Ingredients

- 5 tablespoons butter
- 5 tablespoons all-purpose flour
- 1 tablespoon ketchup
- ½ teaspoon chili powder
- ¼ teaspoon garlic powder
- 1 ¾ cups milk
- 1 (10 ounce) can diced tomatoes with green chile peppers (such as RO*TEL®), liquid drained and reserved
- 2 (8 ounce) packages processed cheese food (such as Velveeta ®), cut into small chunks

## Instructions

1. Melt butter in a pan over medium-low heat; whisk flour into the melted butter until smooth, about 1 minute.
2. Stir in ketchup, chili powder, and garlic powder until incorporated.
3. Slowly stir milk into mixture until smooth.
4. Add tomatoes with green chile peppers; whisk in processed cheese food.
5. Bring to a boil, stirring constantly, until cheese is melted, 3 to 4 minutes.
6. Stir in reserved juice from tomatoes if dip is too thick.
