## Ingredients

- ⅞ cup tahini
- ⅓ cup honey, or more to taste
- 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon

## Instructions

1. Combine tahini, honey, and cinnamon in a bowl.
2. Stir until well combined.
3. Taste and add more honey if desired.
