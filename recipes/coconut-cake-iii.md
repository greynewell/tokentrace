## Ingredients

- 1 (18.25 ounce) package yellow cake mix
- 1 (3.5 ounce) package instant vanilla pudding mix
- 1 ⅓ cups water
- 4  eggs
- ¼ cup vegetable oil
- 2 cups flaked coconut
- 1 cup chopped walnuts
- 4 tablespoons butter, melted
- 2 cups flaked coconut
- 2 teaspoons milk
- ½ teaspoon vanilla extract
- 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese
- 3 ½ cups confectioners' sugar

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Grease a 9x13 inch pan.
3. In a large bowl, combine cake mix, pudding mix, water, eggs and oil.
4. Blend for 4 minutes.
5. Stir in 2 cups coconut and the chopped nuts.
6. Pour into a greased 9x13 inch pan.
7. Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 30 minutes or until done.
8. Allow to cool.
9. To Make Coconut Cream Cheese Frosting: Melt 2 Tablespoons of butter over low heat.
10. Add 3/4 cup of the coconut and stir until browned.
11. Dry on paper towel.
12. Cream other 2 tablespoons butter with cream cheese.
13. Alternately add milk and powdered sugar.
14. Add vanilla.
15. Stir in remaining  1-1/4 cup coconut.
16. Spread Icing on cake and sprinkle with browned coconut.
