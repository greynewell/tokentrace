## Ingredients

- 1 head cabbage, cored and shredded
- ⅛ cup olive oil
- 1 teaspoon garlic powder
- 2 teaspoons lemon juice
- 1 tablespoon chopped fresh cilantro

## Instructions

1. Place the shredded cabbage in a large bowl, and drizzle olive oil over it.
2. Toss until evenly coated.
3. Season with garlic powder, lemon juice and cilantro, and stir to blend in all of the flavors.
