## Ingredients

- 1 (1 1/2-pound) salmon fillet
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 1 teaspoon seafood seasoning (such as Old Bay®)
- 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
- 1 (1 inch) piece fresh ginger root, peeled and thinly sliced
- 6 cloves garlic, minced
- 1  lime, thinly sliced

## Instructions

1. Set oven rack about 6 to 8 inches from the heat source and preheat the oven's broiler; if broiler setting has Low setting, set broiler to that.
2. Line a baking sheet with aluminum foil.
3. Place salmon, skin side down, on the prepared baking sheet; rub salmon with olive oil.
4. Season with seafood seasoning and black pepper.
5. Arrange ginger slices atop salmon and sprinkle with garlic.
6. Place lime slices over ginger-garlic layer.
7. Broil salmon until hot and beginning to turn opaque, about 10 minutes; watch carefully.
8. If broiler has a High setting, turn broiler to that setting and continue broiling until salmon is cooked through and flakes easily with a fork, 5 to 10 more minutes.
