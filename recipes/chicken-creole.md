## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- 1  onion, thinly sliced
- 1 stalk celery, sliced thin
- 1  green bell pepper, minced
- 2 (16 ounce) cans diced tomatoes
- 1  bay leaf
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ⅛ teaspoon cayenne pepper
- 4  skinless, boneless chicken breasts

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. In a large skillet, heat oil over medium heat. Add garlic, onion, celery, and bell pepper. Cook, stirring occasionally, until tender (about 4 minutes). Add tomatoes, bay leaf, salt, and cayenne pepper. Cook the sauce 3 minutes longer, stirring often.
3. Arrange chicken breasts in an 8x11-inch baking dish. Pour the Creole sauce over chicken.
4. Bake 15 to 20 minutes, until chicken is tender and white throughout.
