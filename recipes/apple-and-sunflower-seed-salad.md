## Ingredients

- 2  green apples - washed, cored and cubed
- ½ cup sunflower seeds
- 1 head romaine lettuce - rinsed, dried, and chopped
- 2  dill pickles, diced
- 2  tomatoes, diced
- ½ cup ranch-style salad dressing

## Instructions

1. In a large bowl, mix together the apples, sunflower seeds, lettuce, pickles and tomato.
2. Pour dressing on top, toss and enjoy!
