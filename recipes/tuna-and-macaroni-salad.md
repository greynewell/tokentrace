## Ingredients

- 1 (12 ounce) package bow-tie pasta
- 1 cup frozen peas
- 2 (6 ounce) cans flaked tuna, drained
- 1 cup diced celery
- ½ cup chopped green onions
- ⅓ cup chopped fresh dill
- ¼ cup grated carrot
- 1 cup mayonnaise
- 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard, or more to taste
- salt and ground black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook bow-tie pasta at a boil, stirring occasionally, until almost tender, about 10 minutes. Add peas and continue cooking for 2 minutes. Drain pasta and peas and chill quickly by rinsing with cold water.
2. Transfer pasta and peas to a large bowl. Mix in tuna, celery, green onion, dill, and carrot until well blended.
3. Mix mayonnaise, mustard, salt, and pepper together in a small bowl. Blend into macaroni mixture.
