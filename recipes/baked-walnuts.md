## Ingredients

- 2  egg whites, stiffly beaten
- 1 cup white sugar
- ½ cup butter, softened
- 1 pound walnut halves

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. To the stiff egg whites add sugar, butter and walnuts.
3. Mix until nuts are well coated.
4. Spread out on a cookie sheet.
5. Bake in preheated oven for 20 to 30 minutes, stirring a couple of times, until browned.
