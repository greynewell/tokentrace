## Ingredients

- 1 pound uncooked jumbo shrimp, in shell with heads attached
- ⅛ cup cornstarch, or more as needed
- 3 tablespoons peanut oil, divided
- 10 cloves garlic, pressed
- ¼ cup onion, minced
- 1 tablespoon minced ginger
- 3  scallions, chopped
- 1 ½ tablespoons minced jalapeno pepper
- ½ teaspoon kosher salt
- ½ teaspoon freshly cracked black pepper

## Instructions

1. Use kitchen shears to cut long whiskers and sharp head point off of the shrimp.
2. Place cornstarch in a bowl.
3. Roll in cornstarch to coat lightly and evenly, brushing off excess.
4. Heat 2 tablespoons peanut oil in a skillet over medium-high heat.
5. Add shrimp and saute until red and golden, about 2 minutes per side.
6. Transfer shrimp to a paper towel-lined plate and wipe out skillet.
7. Heat remaining peanut oil in the same skillet.
8. Add garlic, onion, and ginger; cook and stir until fragrant and soft, about 1 minute.
9. Add scallions, jalapeno, and coated shrimp.
10. Cook and stir for 30 seconds while seasoning with most of the salt and pepper.
11. Garnish with remaining salt and pepper before serving.
