## Ingredients

- 1 cup warm water
- 1 (.25 ounce) package active dry yeast
- 1 tablespoon molasses
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 ½ cups whole wheat flour
- 1 ½ cups soy flour
- cooking spray
- cornmeal for dusting

## Instructions

1. In a bowl mix the water, yeast, molasses, and salt.
2. Let sit 5 to 10 minutes, then gradually mix in the whole wheat flour and soy flour.
3. Turn out onto a floured surface, and knead until smooth.
4. Place in a large bowl lightly coated with cooking spray.
5. Cover with a towel, and let rise in a warm place for 1 hour.
6. Punch down the dough, and knead for 5 to 10 minutes.
7. Divide into 6 pieces.
8. On a lightly floured surface, flatten each piece to about 1/8 inch thick with a rolling pin.
9. Cover with a towel, and let rise 30 minutes.
10. Preheat oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C).
11. Warm a baking sheet in the preheating oven for about 2 minutes, then remove from heat and sprinkle with cornmeal.
12. Arrange the dough rounds on the prepared baking sheet, and bake 6 minutes in the preheated oven.
13. Remove from heat and cover the bread with a moist towel to soften.
14. When cooled, slice in half and use a knife to cut pockets in the bread.
