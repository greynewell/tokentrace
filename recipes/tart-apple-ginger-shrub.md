## Ingredients

- 1  Granny Smith apple, shredded
- ⅓ cup unfiltered apple cider vinegar
- ¼ cup white sugar
- 1 teaspoon grated fresh ginger

## Instructions

1. Place apple in a glass jar with a tight-fitting lid.
2. Add apple cider vinegar, sugar, and grated ginger; stir well.
3. Close tightly.
4. Let sit in the refrigerator, 4 to 5 days.
5. Strain syrupy liquid into a clean bottle, discarding apple scraps.
6. Close tightly and chill.
