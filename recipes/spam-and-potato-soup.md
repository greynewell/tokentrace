## Ingredients

- 6 medium baking potatoes
- 1 (12 ounce) can SPAM® Classic or other variety, cut into cubes
- 5 tablespoons butter
- 5 tablespoons all-purpose flour
- 4 cups milk
- ½ teaspoon salt and ground black pepper to taste
- 1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C).
2. Prick potatoes several times with a fork and place onto a baking sheet.
3. Bake in the preheated oven until potatoes are easily pierced with a fork, 50 minutes to 1 hour.
4. When potatoes are cool enough to handle, scoop potato flesh out into a bowl and mash lightly with a fork.
5. Cook and stir SPAM® Classic in a skillet over medium heat until browned (optional).
6. Melt butter in a large pot over medium-low heat; stir in flour to form a smooth roux.
7. Cook and stir roux until well combined and bubbling; stir in milk.
8. Season with salt and pepper.
9. Cook milk mixture, stirring continuously, until it thickens, about 5 minutes; stir in potatoes and SPAM® Classic and cook until heated through.
10. Serve in bowls and garnish each with about 1/4 cup of Cheddar cheese.
