## Ingredients

- 2 small zucchini, cut lengthwise into slightly less than 1/4-inch slices
- 4 slices smoked fontina cheese, halved
- 8 slices thick cut bacon

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
2. Place zucchini directly on oven rack near the bottom.
3. Cook until slightly softened, 5 to 10 minutes.
4. Place bacon slices on paper towels.
5. Heat in microwave on High until cooked but not crisp, about 3 minutes.
6. Place a layer of bacon on each zucchini slice; top with a slice of fontina cheese.
7. Tightly roll each layered zucchini strip and secure with a toothpick or skewer.
8. Place on baking sheet.
9. Bake zucchini rolls in the preheated oven until cheese is melted, about 10 minutes.
