## Ingredients

- ½ cup olive oil
- 2 tablespoons white wine vinegar
- 2 teaspoons honey Dijon mustard
- 2 teaspoons grated red onion
- 1 teaspoon sea salt
- ½ teaspoon ground black pepper
- ¼ teaspoon lemon juice
- ⅓ cup white sugar, or more to taste

## Instructions

1. Whisk olive oil, white wine vinegar, Dijon mustard, red onion, sea salt, black pepper, and lemon juice together in a microwave-safe bowl.
2. Cook oil mixture in the microwave until hot and almost boiling, 1 1/2 to 2 minutes. Gradually whisk sugar into hot mixture until dressing is thick and creamy.
