## Ingredients

- 2 (12 fluid ounce) cans or bottles light beer
- 2 tablespoons dry crab boil
- 2 pounds large shrimp

## Instructions

1. Bring beer with dry crab boil to a boil in a large pot.
2. Add shrimp to boiling beer and place a cover on the pot.
3. Bring the beer again to a boil, reduce heat to medium-low, and cook at a simmer for 5 minutes.
4. Remove pot from heat and leave shrimp steeping in the beer another 2 to 3 minutes; drain.
5. Serve immediately.
