## Ingredients

- 1 (15 ounce) can black beans, drained
- 1 (15 ounce) can kidney beans, drained
- 1 (15 ounce) can garbanzo beans, drained
- 1  green bell pepper, diced
- 1 large red onion, diced
- 1 bunch cilantro, chopped
- 1 stalk celery, diced
- 2 tablespoons Italian-style salad dressing
- 1  lemon, juiced
- salt and ground black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Mix black beans, kidney beans, garbanzo beans, green bell pepper, red onion, cilantro, and celery in a large bowl.
2. Add Italian dressing, lemon juice, salt, and pepper; toss to coat.
3. Let stand about 5 minutes for flavors to blend.
4. Chill if desired.
