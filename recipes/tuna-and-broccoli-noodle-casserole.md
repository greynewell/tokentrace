## Ingredients

- 1 (16 ounce) package tri-colored pasta assortment
- 2  carrots, sliced
- 1 cup chopped broccoli
- 3 (10.75 ounce) cans  condensed cream of mushroom soup
- 2 (5 ounce) cans tuna, drained
- 3 stalks celery, chopped
- ½  onion, chopped
- 1  red bell pepper, chopped
- 2 tablespoons shredded Cheddar cheese

## Instructions

1. Bring a large pot of slightly salted water to a boil.
2. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente.
3. Add carrots and broccoli for 5 minutes or less before removing pasta; drain.
4. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
5. In a large bowl, combine pasta, carrots, broccoli, soup, tuna, celery, onion, and red bell pepper; mix all together.
6. Place mixture evenly in a 9x13 inch baking dish, and sprinkle cheese on top.
7. Bake in preheated oven for 20 minutes.
