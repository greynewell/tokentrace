## Ingredients

- 4  black tiger shrimp, peeled and deveined, tails off
- ½  fresh peach, pitted and cut into thirds
- 1 tablespoon barbecue sauce (such as Sweet Baby Ray's®), or more to taste

## Instructions

1. Preheat an outdoor grill for medium-high heat and lightly oil the grate.
2. Alternately thread shrimp and peach onto a skewer. Brush with barbecue sauce.
3. Place on the hot grill and cook until shrimp are bright pink on the outside and the meat is opaque, about 5 minutes, turning occasionally and basting with barbecue sauce.
