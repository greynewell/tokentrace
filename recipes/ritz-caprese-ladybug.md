## Ingredients

- 24  RITZ Crackers
- 6  bocconcini (1-inch fresh mozzarella cheese balls), each cut into 4 slices
- 24  fresh basil leaves
- 12  cherry tomatoes, halved
- 6  pitted black olives, quartered
- 1  green onion, green part only cut into 48 thin strips
- 2 tablespoons balsamic glaze
- ¼ teaspoon black sesame seeds

## Instructions

1. Top each cracker with 1 EACH cheese slice and basil leaf.
2. Add 1 tomato half to each for the lady bug's body, 1 olive piece for the head and 2 green onion strips for the antennae.
3. Brush with balsamic glaze; sprinkle with sesame seed.
