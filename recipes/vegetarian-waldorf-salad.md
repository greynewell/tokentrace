## Ingredients

- 2 cups chopped apples
- 1 ½  lemons, juiced
- ¼ cup chopped celery
- ¼ cup chopped walnuts
- ¼ cup raisins
- ¼ cup seedless grapes, halved
- ½ cup heavy cream, whipped
- ¼ cup mayonnaise
- 1 pinch ground nutmeg

## Instructions

1. In a salad bowl, toss together the apples, lemon juice, celery, walnuts, raisins and grapes.
2. In a separate bowl, fold together the whipped cream and mayonnaise; add to salad and mix well.
3. Sprinkle with nutmeg and chill before serving.
