## Ingredients

- 4  chopped sun-dried tomatoes  (Optional)
- ¼ pound cod fillets
- 1 tablespoon flour, or as needed
- salt and pepper to taste
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 tablespoon chopped onion
- 4 ounces tomatoes, sliced
- 4 ounces zucchini, sliced  (Optional)
- 2 ounces creme fraiche
- 1 teaspoon dried rosemary
- 1 pinch salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
- 1 tablespoon tomato paste, or as needed

## Instructions

1. Place sun-dried tomatoes in a bowl and cover with warm water.
2. Soak for 15 minutes.
3. Drain and chop.
4. Lightly dust cod with flour and season with salt and pepper.
5. Heat butter in a skillet and cook cod until lightly browned, about 3 minutes per side.
6. Transfer cod to a baking dish with a lid.
7. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
8. Add onion to skillet and cook over medium heat until soft and translucent, about 5 minutes.
9. Add drained sun-dried tomatoes, tomatoes, zucchini, creme fraiche, rosemary, salt, and pepper.
10. Cook for 2 minutes.
11. Stir in tomato paste to thicken sauce.
12. Pour mixture over fish in the baking dish and cover.
13. Bake in preheated oven until fish flakes easily with a fork, about 30 minutes.
