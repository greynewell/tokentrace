## Ingredients

- 1 cup old-fashioned oatmeal
- 2 ⅓ cups boiling water
- ⅓ cup molasses
- ½ cup butter
- 2 teaspoons salt
- 6 cups bread flour, or more as needed
- 2 teaspoons active dry yeast

## Instructions

1. Pour boiling water over oats in a large bowl; let soften, about 1 hour.
2. Add molasses, butter, and yeast; mix well.
3. Add flour, 1 cup at a time, mixing well after each addition until dough sticks together.
4. Cover with a towel and let rise in a warm place until doubled in volume, about 1 hour.
5. Butter two 9x5-inch loaf pans.
6. Punch down dough and turn onto a lightly floured surface.
7. Shape into 2 balls and let rest for 10 minutes.
8. Form balls into loaves and place into the prepared pans.
9. Cover loaves with a damp cloth and let rise until doubled in volume, about 30 minutes.
10. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
11. Bake loaves in the preheated oven until tops are golden brown, about 40 minutes.
