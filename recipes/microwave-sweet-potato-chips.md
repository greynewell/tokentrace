## Ingredients

- 1  sweet potato, thinly sliced
- 2 teaspoons olive oil, or as needed
- coarse sea salt

## Instructions

1. Combine sweet potato slices and olive oil in a large bowl; toss to coat. Arrange sweet potato slices in a single layer on a large microwave-safe plate; season with salt.
2. Cook in microwave until chips are dry, crisp, and slightly browned, about 5 minutes. Cool chips on plate before transferring to a bowl. Repeat with remaining sweet potato slices.
