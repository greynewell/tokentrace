## Ingredients

- 1 (12 ounce) package bow tie pasta (farfalle)
- 2 ½ cups diced fresh tomatoes
- ¾ cup cooked crumbled bacon
- ½ head romaine lettuce, torn
- ¾ cup mayonnaise
- ¾ cup ranch dressing
- 1 pinch onion powder, or to taste
- 1 pinch garlic salt, or to taste
- ground black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
2. Cook bow tie pasta at a boil, stirring occasionally, until cooked through yet firm to the bite, about 12 minutes; drain.
3. Transfer to a large bowl.
4. Stir tomatoes, bacon, and romaine lettuce into bow tie pasta.
5. Whisk mayonnaise, ranch dressing, onion powder, garlic salt, and black pepper together in a small bowl.
6. Pour over pasta mixture; toss to coat.
7. Chill in refrigerator for 2 to 3 hours before serving.
