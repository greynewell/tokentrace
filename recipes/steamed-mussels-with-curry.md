## Ingredients

- ¼ cup curry powder
- 2 teaspoons minced garlic
- 2 pounds mussels, cleaned and debearded

## Instructions

1. Fill a large pot with 1 inch of water.
2. Stir in the curry powder and garlic, and bring to a boil.
3. Add the mussels, and steam over high heat until they are all open, 5 to 10 minutes.
4. Serve immediately.
