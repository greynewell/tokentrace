## Ingredients

- 5  red potatoes
- ½ cup reduced-fat mayonnaise
- 2 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese
- 1 ½ tablespoons Dijon mustard
- 4 teaspoons cider vinegar
- ½ teaspoon cayenne pepper
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
- ¼ teaspoon paprika
- ½  red onion, thinly sliced
- 2  green onions, chopped

## Instructions

1. Place potatoes into a large pot and cover with salted water; bring to a boil. Reduce heat to medium-low, cover, and simmer until tender, about 20 minutes. Drain and cut into cubes.
2. Whisk mayonnaise, Parmesan cheese, Dijon mustard, vinegar, cayenne pepper, salt, black pepper, and paprika together in a large bowl. Add potatoes, red onion, and green onions; gently toss to combine.
