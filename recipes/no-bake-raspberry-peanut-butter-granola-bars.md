## Ingredients

- ½ cup natural peanut butter (such as NuttZo®)
- ¼ cup mild honey
- 1 cup gluten-free rolled oats
- ½ cup fresh raspberries
- ¼ cup golden flaxseed meal
- 1 tablespoon chia seeds

## Instructions

1. Combine peanut butter and honey in a microwave-safe bowl; heat in microwave until melted and smooth, about 30 seconds.
2. Add oats, raspberries, flaxseed meal, and chia seeds and mix well.
3. Line an 8-inch baking dish with parchment paper.
4. Press oat mixture into the prepared baking dish, using your fingers to flatten into the corners.
5. Refrigerate until set, about 3 hours.
6. Cut into bars.
