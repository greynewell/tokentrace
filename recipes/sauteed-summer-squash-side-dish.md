## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 1 bunch scallions, diced
- 1 teaspoon minced garlic, or more to taste
- 1  zucchini, sliced 1/4-inch thick, or more to taste
- 1  summer squash, sliced 1/4-inch thick, or more to taste
- 4 pinches dried Italian seasoning, or to taste
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- 1 teaspoon chopped fresh basil, or to taste

## Instructions

1. Heat olive oil over medium heat in a medium-sized skillet, about 1 minute. Add scallions and garlic; saute in the hot oil until tender, 2 to 3 minutes. Add zucchini and squash; season with Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper. Saute until squash is tender, 8 to 12 minutes.
2. Divide mixture evenly among plates. Garnish with basil.
