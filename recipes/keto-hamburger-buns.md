## Ingredients

- 1 ¼ cups extra-fine almond flour
- 5 tablespoons psyllium husk
- 1 tablespoon stevia sweetener (such as Truvia®)
- 1 tablespoon nutritional yeast
- 1 teaspoon salt
- ¾ teaspoon unflavored gelatin
- 1 cup very hot water
- 3  egg whites
- 2 teaspoons cider vinegar
- 1 teaspoon active dry yeast

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
3. Combine almond flour, psyllium husk, sweetener, nutritional yeast, salt, and gelatin in a large bowl.
4. Mix completely.
5. Add hot water and mix thoroughly into dry ingredients.
6. Add egg whites and vinegar; mix.
7. Add active yeast and mix dough.
8. Knead dough until all fluid is absorbed.
9. Allow to sit 10 minutes.
10. Fill a small bowl with water and keep nearby to moisten fingers and prevent dough from sticking.
11. Form dough into 6 equal parts and shape into desired bun shapes.
12. Place on the prepared baking sheet.
13. Bake until set, 50 to 60 minutes.
