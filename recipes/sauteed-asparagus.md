## Ingredients

- 1 bunch fresh asparagus, trimmed
- 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
- ¼ cup water
- ⅛ teaspoon salt
- ⅛ teaspoon pepper

## Instructions

1. Drizzle olive oil into a large skillet over medium-high heat. Once oil is hot, add asparagus spears; cover and cook for 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.
2. Add water and cover to steam asparagus and keep it from drying out. Continue cooking for another 5 minutes or until asparagus is tender. Season with salt and pepper.
