## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon butter, divided
- 1  plain bagel, split
- 4 slices ham
- 2  eggs
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- 2 slices American cheese
- 2 tablespoons maple syrup

## Instructions

1. Heat 1 1/2 teaspoons butter in a skillet over medium heat; arrange bagel halves, cut sides down, in the melted butter.
2. Cook until bagel is very slightly toasted and softened, 1 to 2 minutes per side.
3. Transfer bagel to a plate.
4. Cook ham slices in the same skillet until heated through, 1 to 2 minutes.
5. Arrange 2 ham slices onto each bagel half.
6. Heat remaining 1 1/2 teaspoons butter in the same skillet; cook eggs in the melted butter until whites are set but yolks are still runny, about 3 minutes.
7. Gently flip eggs and cook until whites are set, about 1 more minute.
8. Season eggs with salt and pepper.
9. Layer eggs and American cheese slices onto 1 bagel half; top with the second bagel half, creating a sandwich.
10. Place bagel sandwich back in the skillet and cover with a lid; heat until cheese is melted, about 45 seconds per side.
11. Transfer bagel sandwich to a plate; drizzle with maple syrup.
