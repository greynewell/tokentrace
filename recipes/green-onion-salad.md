## Ingredients

- 3 bunches green onions, chopped
- 2  hard-cooked eggs, peeled and chopped
- 1 cup mayonnaise
- 1 (4 ounce) packet saltine crackers, crushed

## Instructions

1. Toss together the green onions and hard-cooked eggs until blended.
2. Stir in the mayonnaise.
3. Carefully stir in the crackers until well coated.
4. Serve immediately so the crackers do not get soggy.
