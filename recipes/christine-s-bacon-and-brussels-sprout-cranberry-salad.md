## Ingredients

- ½ pound thick-cut bacon, cut into 1-inch chunks
- 1 pound Brussels sprouts, cut into 1/4-inch slices
- ½ cup dried cranberries
- ½  gala apple, diced
- ¼ cup water
- ¼ cup chopped, unsalted, and roasted almonds
- ¼ cup balsamic vinaigrette dressing, or to taste

## Instructions

1. Place bacon in a wok or large skillet; cook and stir until evenly browned, about 10 minutes.
2. Drain excess fat.
3. Mix Brussels sprouts, cranberries, apple, water, and almonds into bacon; cook and stir over medium-high heat until Brussels sprouts are bright green and tender, 7 to 10 minutes.
4. Transfer sprouts mixture to a bowl and top with dressing.
