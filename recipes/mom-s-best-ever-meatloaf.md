## Ingredients

- 2 pounds ground beef
- ¾ cup bread crumbs
- 2 (10.75 ounce) cans  golden mushroom soup, divided
- 1  egg, beaten
- 1 tablespoon dried minced onion
- 1 teaspoon salt
- ⅓ cup water

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
2. Mix ground beef, bread crumbs, 1/2 can golden mushroom soup, egg, dried minced onion, and salt together in a bowl until smooth; shape into an 8x4-inch loaf and move to a baking dish.
3. Bake in the preheated oven until no longer pink in the center, about 75 minutes. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 160 degrees F (70 degrees C).
4. Stir remaining soup with hot water in a saucepan; bring to a simmer. Ladle soup mixture over meatloaf.
