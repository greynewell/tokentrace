## Ingredients

- 1 pound cooked shrimp, peeled and deveined
- 1  onion, chopped
- ¼ inch piece fresh ginger root, minced
- 2  egg whites
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ½ teaspoon ground black pepper
- 8 slices white bread, crusts trimmed
- ½ cup fine dry bread crumbs
- 1 quart oil for frying
- 1  lemon, for garnish

## Instructions

1. In a blender or food processor, blend shrimp, onion, ginger, egg whites, salt and pepper until smooth.
2. Cut each white bread slice into 4 triangles.
3. Spread both sides of triangles with shrimp mixture and coat with bread crumbs.
4. Cover and chill in the refrigerator approximately 1 hour.
5. Heat oil in a large, heavy skillet to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
6. In small batches, fry bread triangles 3 minutes on each side, or until golden brown.
7. Drain on paper towels.
8. Garnish with lemon juice before serving.
