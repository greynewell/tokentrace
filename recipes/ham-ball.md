## Ingredients

- 2 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, slightly softened
- 4 slices ham, chopped
- 4  green onions, chopped
- ¼ teaspoon seasoned salt, or to taste
- 1 cup chopped pecans, or more to taste

## Instructions

1. Mix cream cheese, ham, green onions, and seasoned salt until evenly mixed; roll into a ball.
2. Spread chopped pecans onto a flat surface. Roll ham ball in chopped pecans to coat.
