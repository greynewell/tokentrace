## Ingredients

- 1 (4 ounce) can chopped green chiles, drained
- 1  onion, chopped
- 1 (5 ounce) jar green olives, chopped (reserve brine)
- 1 (6 ounce) can chopped black olives
- 1 ½ cups shredded Cheddar cheese
- ground black pepper to taste
- garlic salt to taste
- 2  fresh red tomatoes, chopped

## Instructions

1. Chill a serving bowl in the freezer while you make the salsa.
2. In a mixing bowl, combine the chopped green chiles, onion, green olives, and black olives.
3. Lightly mix in the Cheddar cheese and tomatoes; season to taste with garlic salt and black pepper.
4. If desired, mix in a little bit of the olive brine.
5. Serve in the chilled bowl.
