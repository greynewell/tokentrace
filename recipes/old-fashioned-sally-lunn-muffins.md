## Ingredients

- 2 cups all-purpose flour
- 3 teaspoons baking powder
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ¼ cup shortening
- ¼ cup white sugar
- 1  egg, beaten
- 1 cup milk

## Instructions

1. In a medium bowl mix together the flour, baking powder, and salt.
2. In a separate bowl cream together the shortening and sugar.
3. Stir in beaten egg, and milk.
4. Add flour mixture alternately with liquid mixture.
5. Fill greased muffin tins two-thirds of the way full.
6. Bake in a preheated 375 degree F(190 degrees C) oven for 30 minutes.
