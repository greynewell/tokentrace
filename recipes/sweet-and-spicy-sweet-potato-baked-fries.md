## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 1 tablespoon honey
- 1 teaspoon cayenne pepper
- ½ teaspoon cracked black pepper
- 1  sweet potato, cut into long thin slices, or more as needed

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
2. Mix olive oil, honey, cayenne pepper, and black pepper in a large bowl.
3. Add sweet potato slices; toss to coat.
4. Arrange coated sweet potatoes on a baking sheet.
5. Bake in the preheated oven until fries are slightly crisp, 30 to 40 minutes.
