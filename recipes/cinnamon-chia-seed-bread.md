## Ingredients

- 1 cup self-rising flour
- ¾ cup white sugar
- ¼ cup chia seeds
- 3 tablespoons ground cinnamon
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- ½ cup coconut milk beverage
- 1 large egg
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease an 8x4-inch loaf pan.
2. Combine flour, sugar, chia seeds, cinnamon, and salt in a large bowl.
3. Combine coconut milk beverage, egg, and vanilla extract in a separate bowl. Slowly stir into the flour mixture until combined. Pour batter into the prepared pan.
4. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean, 25 to 35 minutes. Allow to cool before serving.
