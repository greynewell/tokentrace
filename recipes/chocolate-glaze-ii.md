## Ingredients

- 2 cups sifted confectioners' sugar
- 2 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa powder
- 1 tablespoon butter, softened
- Â½ teaspoon vanilla extract
- 4 tablespoons milk

## Instructions

1. In a small bowl, combine butter with sugar and cocoa.
2. Add vanilla.
3. Add milk gradually until smooth.
4. Drizzle over cake.
