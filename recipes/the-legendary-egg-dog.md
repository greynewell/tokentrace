## Ingredients

- ¼ teaspoon butter
- 1  egg, beaten
- 1  hot dog bun
- 1 dash hot sauce, or to taste

## Instructions

1. Melt butter in a nonstick skillet over medium-high heat. Add egg to melted butter and cook until egg starts to bubble, about 1 minute. Flip egg and cook until set, 2 to 3 minutes more.
2. Roll egg, place in hot dog bun, and top with hot sauce.
