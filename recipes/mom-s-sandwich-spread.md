## Ingredients

- 1 pound bologna, ground
- 1 cup mayonnaise
- 1 teaspoon light corn syrup
- 2 tablespoons sweet pickle relish

## Instructions

1. Place bologna in a medium bowl.
2. Mix in mayonnaise a few tablespoons at a time until the desired consistency is reached.
3. Stir in light corn syrup and sweet pickle relish.
4. Cover and chill in the refrigerator at least 1 hour before serving.
