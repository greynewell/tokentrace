## Ingredients

- 1 (9.6 ounce) package Johnsonville® Fully Cooked Original Recipe or Turkey Breakfast Sausage
- 1 (12 ounce) can Pillsbury® Grands!® refrigerated biscuits
- 2 slices American cheese, quartered

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
2. Grease a nonstick muffin pan with cooking spray.
3. Remove biscuit dough from can.
4. Separate 8 uncooked biscuits.
5. Divide each biscuit into two even layers, horizontally.
6. Place the bottom dough layers in the muffin pan; top each with cheese and a sausage patty.
7. Place the top dough layers on top of each sausage patty.
8. Bake for 18-20 minutes or until golden brown and the biscuit is fully baked.
