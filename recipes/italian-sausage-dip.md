## Ingredients

- 1 pound ground Italian sausage
- 2  tomatoes, chopped
- 2  chopped green bell peppers
- 2  onions, chopped
- 2 (4 ounce) cans chopped green chile peppers
- 1 (16 ounce) container sour cream
- 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese

## Instructions

1. Place Italian sausage in a large, deep skillet.
2. Cook over medium high heat until evenly brown.
3. Drain and set aside.
4. In a large bowl, mix together sausage, tomatoes, green bell peppers, onions, green chile peppers, sour cream and cream cheese.
5. Transfer mixture to a slow cooker.
6. Cook on high heat approximately 1 hour, or until vegetables are soft.
7. Reduce heat and simmer until serving.
