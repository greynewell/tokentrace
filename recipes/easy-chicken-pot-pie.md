## Ingredients

- 2 (9 inch)  deep dish frozen pie crusts, thawed
- 1 (15 ounce) can mixed vegetables, drained
- 2 cups cooked, diced chicken breast
- 1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of chicken soup
- ½ cup milk

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Combine chicken, mixed vegetables, condensed soup, and milk in a bowl. Pour the mixture into the bottom pie crust. Cover with second crust. Seal the edges and poke holes in top crust.
3. Bake in the preheated oven until crust is golden brown, about 30 minutes.
