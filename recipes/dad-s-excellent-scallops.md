## Ingredients

- 2 pounds shelled, large sea scallops
- ½ pound prosciutto, thinly sliced
- ½ cup butter, melted
- toothpicks, soaked in water

## Instructions

1. Preheat grill for medium-high heat.
2. Wrap each scallop with a thin slice of prosciutto, and secure with a toothpick.
3. Lightly oil grill grate.
4. Arrange scallops on the grill, and baste with butter.
5. Cook for 5 minutes, turn, and baste with butter.
6. Cook for another 8 minutes, or until opaque.
