## Ingredients

- 1 cup all-purpose flour
- 2 cups whole wheat flour
- 1 teaspoon baking soda
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- 1 cup molasses
- 1 ½ cups buttermilk
- ⅔ cup raisins

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
2. Grease a 9x5 inch loaf pan.
3. In a large bowl, stir together all-purpose flour, whole wheat flour, baking soda and salt.
4. Mix in molasses and buttermilk.
5. Stir in raisins or currants.
6. Pour batter into prepared loaf pan.
7. Bake for 1 hour in the preheated oven, or until a tester inserted into the center of the loaf comes out clean.
