## Ingredients

- 1 ½ cups soy flour
- ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
- 1  egg, beaten
- 3 tablespoons water, or as needed

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Grease a pizza stone.
3. Stir soy flour, Parmesan cheese, and egg together in the bowl of a stand mixer; add water.
4. Switch mixer attachment to a dough hook; mix, adding water as necessary, until a stiff dough forms.
5. Roll dough out onto prepared pizza stone.
6. Bake in the preheated oven until crisped, 15 to 20 minutes.
