## Ingredients

- 1 pound cooked peeled shrimp, chilled
- 3  green onions, coarsely chopped
- 1 small onion, coarsely chopped
- 1 tablespoon chopped fresh tarragon
- 1 ½ teaspoons dried parsley
- 1 teaspoon minced fresh rosemary
- ½ teaspoon chili powder
- 1 teaspoon lemon juice
- 1 tablespoon finely grated carrot
- 1 cup low-fat mayonnaise
- 1 cup low-fat sour cream
- salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Add shrimp, green onions, onion, tarragon, parsley, rosemary, chili powder, and lemon juice to the jar of a blender or food processor; process the mixture until shrimp are finely chopped.
2. Scrape the chopped shrimp mixture into a bowl and stir in the carrot, mayonnaise, and sour cream, mixing until well blended.
3. Season to taste with salt and pepper.
4. Chill at least 1 hour before serving.
