## Ingredients

- ½ cup whipped cream cheese
- 2 tablespoons Dijon mustard
- 1 tablespoon honey
- 4  bagels, split
- 8 slices deli-style ham

## Instructions

1. Mix the cream cheese, mustard, and honey in a small bowl.
2. Spread cream cheese mixture over cut sides of 4 bagel halves.
3. Top evenly with ham.
4. Sandwich with the remaining bagel halves.
