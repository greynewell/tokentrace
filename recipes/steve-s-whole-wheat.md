## Ingredients

- 1 ⅓ cups water
- 1 ½ tablespoons dry milk powder
- 1 tablespoon molasses
- 1 tablespoon honey
- 3 tablespoons margarine
- 1 teaspoon white sugar
- 1 teaspoon salt
- ½ cup rye flour
- 2 cups whole wheat flour
- 1 cup bread flour
- 2 ¾ teaspoons active dry yeast

## Instructions

1. Place the ingredients in the pan of the bread machine in the order recommended by the manufacturer.
2. Select Whole Wheat setting, and Start.
