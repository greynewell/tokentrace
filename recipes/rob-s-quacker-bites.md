## Ingredients

- 1 cup steak sauce, such as Dale's®
- 1 cup whiskey
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- 1 pound bacon strips, cut in half
- 6  duck breasts, deboned and cut into cubes
- 1 box wooden toothpicks

## Instructions

1. To make the marinade, stir the steak sauce and whiskey together in a bowl.
2. Pour the marinade into a resealable plastic bag and add the duck cubes.
3. Seal the bag, turn once or twice to thoroughly coat the duck, and place in the refrigerator for 1 hour.
4. Preheat a grill for medium heat.
5. Soak the toothpicks in a bowl of water at least 1/2 hour to prevent burning.
6. Remove the duck from the marinade, and discard the sauce.
7. Wrap a piece of bacon around each duck cube and secure with a toothpick.
8. Cook the duck bites on the preheated grill until bacon is crisp and duck is no longer pink, turning once, 5 to 10 minutes.
9. Place on a serving plate, cool slightly, and serve.
