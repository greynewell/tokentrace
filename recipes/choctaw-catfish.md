## Ingredients

- 4 (4 ounce) fillets catfish
- 2 cups buttermilk
- 1 cup yellow cornmeal
- ½ tablespoon onion salt
- 1 tablespoon steak seasoning
- ¼ cup vegetable oil

## Instructions

1. In a medium bowl, place catfish fillets in buttermilk.
2. Cover, and marinate in the refrigerator 45 minutes.
3. In a resealable plastic bag, mix cornmeal, onion salt, and steak seasoning.
4. One at a time, drain catfish fillets and place in the bag to coat with the cornmeal mixture.
5. Heat oil in a large, heavy skillet over medium heat.
6. Fry each fillet 7-10 minutes per side, or until exterior is lightly browned and flesh is easily flaked with a fork.
7. Drain on paper towels.
