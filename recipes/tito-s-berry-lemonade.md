## Ingredients

- 1 ½ ounces Tito's Handmade Vodka
- 4 ounces lemonade
- Fresh berries, sliced or whole

## Instructions

1. Lightly muddle berries into a glass. Add Tito's Handmade Vodka, lemonade, and ice.
2. Stir and garnish with a strawberry or lemon slice.
