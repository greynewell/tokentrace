## Ingredients

- nonstick cooking spray
- ¾ cup grated Parmesan cheese
- ½ teaspoon dried thyme
- ½ teaspoon dried oregano
- ½ teaspoon dried basil
- ¼ teaspoon garlic powder
- kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
- 1 head cauliflower, chopped into bite-size pieces
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley leaves

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Spray a cooling rack with nonstick cooking spray; place on a baking sheet and set aside.
3. Combine Parmesan cheese, thyme, oregano, basil, garlic powder, salt, and pepper in a small bowl.
4. Place cauliflower onto the prepared cooling rack.
5. Drizzle with olive oil and sprinkle with Parmesan cheese mixture.
6. Bake in the preheated oven until tender and easily pierced with a knife, about 15 minutes.
7. Remove from the oven.
8. Set an oven rack about 6 inches from the heat source and preheat the oven's broiler.
9. Broil in the preheated oven until cauliflower is golden brown, 2 to 3 minutes.
10. Remove from the oven, garnish with parsley, and serve immediately.
