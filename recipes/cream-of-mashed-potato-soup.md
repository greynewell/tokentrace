## Ingredients

- 2 cups milk, or as needed
- 4 cups leftover mashed potatoes
- 2 cups cubed fully cooked ham
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- ½ cup shredded Cheddar cheese, or to taste

## Instructions

1. Heat milk in a large saucepan over medium heat.
2. Stir mashed potatoes into the warm milk until the mixture is smooth, adding more milk as needed to make it smooth; add ham and stir.
3. Cook the mixture until the ham cubes are hot in the middle, 5 to 10 minutes; season with salt and pepper and top with Cheddar cheese.
