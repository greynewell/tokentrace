## Ingredients

- 1 (6 ounce) can chunk light tuna, drained
- 2 tablespoons chopped onion
- 1 tablespoon light mayonnaise
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- 3 leaves green lettuce, rinsed and patted dry
- 2 tablespoons finely shredded low-fat Cheddar cheese  (Optional)

## Instructions

1. Combine tuna, mayonnaise, onion, salt, and pepper in a bowl.
2. Lay lettuce leaves out. Divide tuna mixture among the leaves, placing it in the middle of each leaf. Sprinkle Cheddar cheese over the tuna. Roll leaves up to create wraps.
