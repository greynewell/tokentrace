## Ingredients

- ½ cup butter, softened
- 3 tablespoons cream cheese, softened
- ½ cup low-calorie natural sweetener (such as Swerve®)
- 1  egg
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 1 cup coconut flour
- ½ teaspoon baking powder
- ¼ teaspoon salt

## Instructions

1. Whisk butter, cream cheese, and sweetener together in a bowl with an electric mixer until creamy, about 3 minutes.
2. Add egg and vanilla extract; whisk until smooth.
3. Combine coconut flour, baking powder, and salt in a bowl; whisk into butter mixture until a sticky dough is formed.
4. Wrap dough with parchment paper into a log shape; chill in the fridge for 30 minutes.
5. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
6. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
7. Slice dough into 1/2-inch thick cookies and place 2 inches apart onto the prepared baking sheet.
8. Bake in the preheated oven until golden, 15 to 17 minutes.
