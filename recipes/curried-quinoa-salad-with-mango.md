## Ingredients

- 1 ½ cups chicken stock
- ¾ cup quinoa
- 1 ½ teaspoons curry powder
- ¼ teaspoon garlic powder
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ¼ teaspoon black pepper
- 1  mango - peeled, seeded and diced
- 3  green onions, chopped

## Instructions

1. Bring chicken stock, quinoa, curry powder, garlic powder, salt, and pepper to a boil in a saucepan over high heat.
2. Reduce heat to medium-low, cover, and simmer until the quinoa is tender, 15 to 20 minutes.
3. Once done, scrape the quinoa into a shallow dish and allow to cool to room temperature.
4. Stir in the mango and green onions.
5. Serve either at room temperature or cold.
