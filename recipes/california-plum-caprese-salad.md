## Ingredients

- ½ cup pomegranate juice
- 2 tablespoons white balsamic vinegar
- ½ teaspoon sugar
- 2 fruit (2-1/8" dia)s black California plums, pitted and thinly sliced
- 1  green California plum, pitted and thinly sliced
- 1 (8 ounce) ball fresh mozzarella, thinly sliced
- 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
- 2 tablespoons snipped fresh basil
- 1 teaspoon grated lemon zest

## Instructions

1. Place pomegranate juice in a saucepan and simmer over medium heat for about 10 minutes until reduced to 2 tablespoons.
2. Stir in vinegar and sugar; cover and chill until ready to use.
3. To prepare salad, arrange plum slices alternately with mozzarella on a platter.
4. Drizzle with chilled pomegranate mixture and olive oil; sprinkle with basil and lemon zest.
5. Serve as a salad or as an appetizer with grilled baguette slices.
