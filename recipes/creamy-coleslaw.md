## Ingredients

- 1  red onion, thinly sliced
- 1  red bell pepper, thinly sliced
- 3 cups shredded cabbage
- 1 large carrot, shredded
- ½ cup raisins
- ¼ cup mayonnaise
- ½ cup sour cream
- 2 tablespoons Dijon-style prepared mustard
- 1 teaspoon white wine vinegar

## Instructions

1. In a large bowl, combine the onion, red pepper, cabbage and carrot.
2. Add raisins and toss well.
3. In a small bowl, whisk the mayonnaise, sour cream, mustard and vinegar together.
4. Pour dressing over vegetable mixture and refrigerate for at least 2 hours before serving.
