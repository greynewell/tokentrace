## Ingredients

- ½ pound scallops
- 2 tablespoons sherry
- ½ teaspoon white sugar
- ½ teaspoon salt
- 6 slices bacon, cut in half
- 6  water chestnuts, sliced
- 1  green onion, cut into 1 inch pieces

## Instructions

1. In a large bowl, stir together sugar and sherry.
2. Add scallops to the bowl, and marinate for 30 minutes.
3. Remove scallops from marinade, and sprinkle with salt.
4. Center a scallop or scallop piece, a slice of water chestnut, and a slice of scallion on a piece of bacon.
5. Wrap, and secure with a toothpick.
6. Arrange on a broiling pan.
7. Place pan under broiler for 10 minutes, turning once, until bacon is golden and cooked.
