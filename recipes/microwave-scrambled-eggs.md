## Ingredients

- cooking spray
- 8  eggs
- salt and ground black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Spray a large glass bowl with cooking spray.
2. Crack 1 egg into a small ramekin, then pour into the prepared bowl. Whisk egg. Repeat with remaining eggs, whisking well after each addition.
3. Cook eggs in the microwave for 30 seconds; remove and stir. Continue cooking in 30-second intervals, stirring after each, until eggs are almost set, about 3 minutes total. Season with salt and pepper.
