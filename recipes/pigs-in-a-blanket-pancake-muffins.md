## Ingredients

- 1 cup Hungry Jack® Buttermilk Pancake & Waffle Mix
- ⅔ cup milk
- 2  eggs
- 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
- 1 tablespoon Hungry Jack® Original Syrup, plus more for serving if desired
- 4  fully cooked sausage links

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
2. Add the pancake mix, milk, eggs, oil, and syrup to a medium mixing bowl and whisk to combine.
3. Chop the cooked sausage links into small pieces and stir into the batter.
4. Pour batter 2/3 full into a greased or lined muffin tin.
5. Bake for 13 minutes or until a toothpick inserted comes out clean.
6. Serve with extra syrup, as desired.
