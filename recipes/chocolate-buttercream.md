## Ingredients

- 12 (1 ounce) squares bittersweet chocolate
- 2 cups unsalted butter
- 1 pinch salt
- 2  eggs
- 4 cups confectioners' sugar
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

## Instructions

1. Melt the bittersweet chocolate, and allow to cool slightly.
2. Place the butter or margarine, salt, and vanilla in a mixing bowl.
3. Beat with a mixer until very light and airy, about 4 minutes.
4. Add the powdered sugar a little at a time while beating on low speed.
5. Mix well, and beat on medium speed for about 4 minutes.
6. Add the eggs one at a time, and beat for 5 minutes more.
7. Add the melted chocolate, and beat 4 minutes.
