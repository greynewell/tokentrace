## Ingredients

- 1 pound sushi-grade tuna, cut into 1/2-inch cubes
- 1  avocado, cubed
- 2  green onions, sliced
- 2 tablespoons soy sauce
- 1 medium lime
- 2 tablespoons mayonnaise
- 2 teaspoons sriracha sauce
- 30  tortilla chips, crushed
- 2 teaspoons black sesame seeds  (Optional)

## Instructions

1. Combine tuna, avocado, green onion, and soy sauce in a bowl.
2. Roll lime on a hard surface, cut into wedges, and squeeze juice over tuna mixture.
3. Combine mayonnaise and sriracha sauce in a small resealable plastic bag.
4. Seal bag and massage sauce to mix.
5. Divide chips between 2 bowls.
6. Pour 1/2 of the tuna mixture into each bowl.
7. Cut a small corner off the bag containing the sauce and drizzle over tuna.
8. Sprinkle with sesame seeds to garnish.
