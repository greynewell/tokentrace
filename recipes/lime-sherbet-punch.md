## Ingredients

- 2 quarts lime sherbet
- 2 (2 liter) bottles ginger ale
- 1 (46 fluid ounce) can pineapple juice
- 1 (4 ounce) jar maraschino cherries, drained
- 1  lemon, sliced
- 1  lime, sliced

## Instructions

1. Scoop lime sherbet into a punch bowl; pour in ginger ale and pineapple juice. Stir well.
2. Stir in maraschino cherries and float lemon and lime slices in the punch.
