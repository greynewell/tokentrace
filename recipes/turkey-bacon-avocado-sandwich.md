## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon reduced-fat mayonnaise  (Optional)
- 2 slices bread, toasted
- 1 slice provolone cheese
- 4 thin slices  deli turkey breast
- 4 slices precooked bacon, microwaved according to package directions
- ½  avocado - peeled, pitted, and thinly sliced
- 1 slice ripe tomato
- 1 leaf lettuce

## Instructions

1. Spread mayonnaise on one side of both slices of toasted bread.
2. Top a bread slice with provolone cheese, turkey, bacon, avocado, tomato, and lettuce.
3. Place the remaining bread slice on top, slice in half, and serve.
