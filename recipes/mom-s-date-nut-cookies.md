## Ingredients

- ½ cup butter
- 1 cup white sugar
- 1 cup chopped dates
- 1 cup chopped walnuts
- 1  egg, beaten
- 2 ½ cups crisp rice cereal
- 2 cups flaked coconut, or to taste

## Instructions

1. Melt butter in a large skillet over medium heat. Add sugar, dates, walnuts, and beaten egg. Bring to a boil, stirring occasionally. Boil until dates have softened and melted into the mixture, 3 to 5 minutes. Remove from the heat and stir in rice cereal.
2. Place coconut in a shallow bowl.
3. Scoop rounded teaspoonfuls of dough and drop them into the coconut, rolling and pressing until coated.
