## Ingredients

- 2 ½ cups creamy salad dressing (such as Miracle Whip®)
- ¾ cup prepared yellow mustard
- ¼ cup white sugar
- 2 tablespoons white vinegar
- 12  hard-boiled eggs, peeled and cut into 1/4-inch slices

## Instructions

1. Mix creamy salad dressing, mustard, sugar, and vinegar together with a whisk in a large bowl; add egg slices and gently stir to coat.
2. Cover bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate until chilled, at least 30 minutes.
