## Ingredients

- cooking spray
- 3 tablespoons butter
- ½ pound salmon fillets, skin removed
- 1 (14.5 ounce) can asparagus cuts and tips, drained
- ½  onion, chopped
- 3 tablespoons chopped garlic
- ⅛ teaspoon ground black pepper
- ⅛ teaspoon ground nutmeg
- ½ cup shredded Asiago cheese
- 4  eggs
- 1 cup half-and-half
- 1 pinch salt
- ½ cup shredded Asiago cheese
- 1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
2. Coat a 10-inch pie dish with cooking spray.
3. Melt butter in a large skillet over medium heat; cook salmon in the hot butter until fish flakes with a fork, about 5 minutes per side.
4. Separate fillets into pieces; stir asparagus, onion, and garlic into skillet with salmon.
5. Cook and stir until liquid clinging to asparagus pieces has evaporated, about 15 minutes.
6. Mix pepper, nutmeg, and 1/2 cup Asiago cheese into salmon mixture.
7. Transfer filling into prepared pie dish.
8. Whisk eggs, half-and-half, and salt in a bowl; pour egg mixture over filling in pie dish.
9. Bake in the preheated oven for 15 minutes; sprinkle quiche with 1/2 cup Asiago cheese and Cheddar cheese.
10. Continue baking until quiche is set, a knife inserted into the middle comes out clean, and cheese topping is melted, 35 to 40 minutes.
