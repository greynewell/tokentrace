## Ingredients

- 1 (3 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
- 1 teaspoon margarine
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
- 2 tablespoons chopped onion
- 2 tablespoons milk
- 2 cups cubed, cooked chicken
- 1 (8 ounce) package refrigerated crescent rolls
- 2 tablespoons bread crumbs

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Stir together the cream cheese, margarine, salt, pepper, milk, and onion together.
3. Fold the chicken into the cream cheese mixture.
4. Separate crescent rolls into 4 rectangles and smooth the perforations.
5. Spoon 1/2 cup of the chicken mixture into the center of each rectangle.
6. Fold the corners over the chicken mixture and pinch along the edges to seal into a ball shape.
7. Arrange on a baking sheet.
8. Sprinkle bread crumbs over the top of the newly formed 'baseballs.' Bake in preheated oven until browned, about 20 minutes.
