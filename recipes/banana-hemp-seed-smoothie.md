## Ingredients

- 2  bananas
- 1 cup frozen peach slices
- 2 tablespoons almond butter
- 1 tablespoon hemp seeds
- 2 cups water

## Instructions

1. Layer bananas, peach slices, almond butter, and hemp seeds in a blender; pour in water.
2. Cover and blend until smooth.
