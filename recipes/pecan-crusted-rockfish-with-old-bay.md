## Ingredients

- 1  serving cooking spray
- 2 tablespoons melted butter
- 2 pounds frozen rockfish
- 1 cup finely chopped pecans
- 1 cup panko bread crumbs
- 2 teaspoons seafood seasoning (such as
- ®)

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C). Line a rimmed baking sheet with foil or parchment paper. Spray with cooking spray.
2. Pour melted butter over rockfish. Combine pecans, panko, and seafood seasoning in a bowl. Evenly distribute mixture over the fillets, pressing gently into the top.
3. Bake in the preheated oven until fish easily flakes with a fork, about 20 minutes. Set an oven rack about 6 inches from the heat source and turn on the oven's broiler. Broil for 1 to 2 minutes, making sure not to burn the crust. Serve immediately.
