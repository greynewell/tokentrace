## Ingredients

- 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
- 1 ½ teaspoons baking powder
- ½ teaspoon baking soda
- 1 ½ teaspoons ground cinnamon
- 1 cup quick cooking oats
- ¾ cup packed brown sugar
- ¾ cup raisins
- 1 ¼ cups applesauce
- ⅓ cup vegetable oil
- 2  eggs, beaten
- ¼ cup milk
- ¼ cup apple juice

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Grease and flour an 8 1/2 x 4 1/2 inch loaf pan.
3. Soak raisins in apple juice.
4. In a large bowl, whisk together flour, baking powder, soda, cinnamon, oats, and brown sugar.
5. Add applesauce, oil, eggs, and milk; stir until just combined.
6. Mix in raisins.
7. Spread batter into prepared pan.
8. Bake for 55 to 60 minutes.
9. Remove from pan immediately, and cool on a wire rack.
