## Ingredients

- 3 pounds russet potatoes
- ½ pound bacon, cut into 1/2-inch pieces
- 1 large onion, diced
- 3 stalks celery, sliced
- 6  hard-boiled eggs, quartered and sliced
- 2 cups mayonnaise
- ¼ cup milk
- 3 tablespoons sour cream  (Optional)
- ¼ cup white vinegar
- 1 teaspoon celery salt
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon ground black pepper

## Instructions

1. Place potatoes into a large pot and cover with salted water; bring to a boil.
2. Reduce heat to medium-low and simmer until slightly tender, 15 to 20 minutes.
3. Drain and cool.
4. Remove skin from potatoes; quarter and slice.
5. Place bacon in a large skillet and cook over medium-high heat, turning occasionally, until evenly browned, about 10 minutes.
6. Drain on paper towels.
7. Combine potatoes, bacon, onion, celery, and eggs together in a bowl.
8. Whisk mayonnaise, milk, sour cream, vinegar, celery salt, salt, and black pepper together in a separate bowl.
9. Fold mayonnaise mixture into potato mixture until incorporated.
10. Refrigerate 8 hours or overnight to allow flavors to blend.
