## Ingredients

- 1  egg, lightly beaten
- 1 cup mayonnaise
- ⅛ teaspoon ground black pepper
- 3 cups finely shredded Cheddar cheese
- 1  onion, finely chopped

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C).
2. Spray a 1-quart baking dish with cooking spray.
3. In a bowl, mix the egg, mayonnaise, and pepper.
4. Stir in the Cheddar cheese and onion, and transfer to the prepared dish.
5. Bake 25 minutes in the preheated oven, until golden brown.
