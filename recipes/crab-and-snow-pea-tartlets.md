## Ingredients

- 24 (3 inch) tart shells, baked
- 30 pods snow peas, blanched
- 1 cup crabmeat
- 2  hard-cooked eggs, chopped
- 2 tablespoons mayonnaise
- 2 tablespoons minced celery
- 2 tablespoons thinly sliced green onion
- ¼ teaspoon prepared Dijon-style mustard

## Instructions

1. Place pea pods and the crabmeat in a food processor, pulse just until chopped.
2. Add the egg, mayonnaise, celery, onion and mustard.
3. Pulse briefly, just until mixed together.
4. Spoon into tart shells and chill.
