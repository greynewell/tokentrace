## Ingredients

- 1 (9 inch) prepared graham cracker crust
- Â¾ cup frozen lemonade concentrate, thawed
- 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
- 1 (14 ounce) can sweetened condensed milk
- 1 (8 ounce) container frozen whipped topping, thawed

## Instructions

1. In a blender, combine lemonade concentrate, cream cheese, and condensed milk.
2. Blend on medium speed until mixture is smooth.
3. Transfer mixture to a bowl and fold in whipped topping.
4. Pour mixture into graham cracker crust.
5. Chill before serving.
