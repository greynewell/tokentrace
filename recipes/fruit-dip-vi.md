## Ingredients

- 2 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened
- 1 cup brown sugar
- 2 teaspoons vanilla extract

## Instructions

1. In a medium bowl, blend the cream cheese, brown sugar and vanilla extract.
2. Chill in the refrigerator 1 hour before serving.
