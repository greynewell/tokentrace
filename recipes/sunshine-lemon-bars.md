## Ingredients

- 1 package Duncan Hines® Moist Deluxe® Lemon Cake Mix
- ½ cup butter
- 1  large egg
- 1 (14 ounce) can sweetened condensed milk
- ½ cup lemon juice

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
2. Stir together cake mix, butter and egg.
3. Spread mixture evenly into bottom of a 13x9-inch baking pan.
4. Blend together sweetened condensed milk and lemon juice.
5. Pour mixture over top of cake mix mixture.
6. Bake for 23 to 27 minutes.
7. Cool completely.
8. Cut into 1 1/2x3 1/4-inch bars.
