## Ingredients

- 2 cups all-purpose flour
- 2 cups white sugar
- 4  eggs
- 1 cup milk
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 2 teaspoons vanilla extract

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
2. Sift together flour and baking powder.
3. Heat milk in a small saucepan until just boiling, then remove from heat and set aside.
4. Beat eggs until foamy, then add sugar.
5. Continue beating on medium-high speed until thick and lemon colored.
6. Add vanilla, reduce mixer speed to medium and add hot milk in a steady stream.
7. Quickly beat in flour mixture.
8. Pour into a 10 inch tube pan.
9. Bake in preheated oven 45 minutes, or until top springs back when lightly tapped.
