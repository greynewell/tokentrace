## Ingredients

- 1  butternut squash - peeled, seeded and cut into 1-inch dice
- 3  carrots, cut into 1 inch pieces
- 1 large sweet potato, cut into 1-inch cubes
- 1  rutabaga, peeled and cut into 1-inch pieces
- 3  parsnips, peeled and cubed
- 3  turnips, peeled and cut into 1-inch dice
- ¼ cup extra virgin olive oil
- kosher salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C).
2. Place butternut squash, carrots, sweet potato, rutabaga, parsnips, and turnips into a large mixing bowl. Drizzle with olive oil, and toss to coat. Season to taste with kosher salt and pepper: place vegetables into a deep roasting pan.
3. Roast in the preheated oven until vegetables are tender and golden brown around the edges, about 45 minutes, stirring once during roasting to ensure even cooking.
