## Ingredients

- 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
- ¼ cup white sugar
- ½ teaspoon vanilla extract
- ¾ cup sliced fresh strawberries, mashed
- 1 cup heavy whipping cream
- ¼ cup confectioners' sugar
- ¼ cup sliced fresh strawberries
- 1 (9 inch) prepared graham cracker crust

## Instructions

1. Mix cream cheese, white sugar, and vanilla extract in a large bowl until smooth. Stir 3/4 cup mashed strawberries into cream cheese mixture.
2. Beat heavy cream and confectioners' sugar in a glass or metal bowl until stiff peaks form. Lift your beater or whisk straight up: the cream will form sharp peaks. Fold whipped cream into cream cheese mixture until evenly mixed; fold in remaining 1/4 cup sliced strawberries.
3. Spoon strawberry mixture into graham cracker crust. Chill at least 3 hours before serving.
