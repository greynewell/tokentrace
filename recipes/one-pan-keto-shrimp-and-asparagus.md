## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon avocado oil
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 2 teaspoons minced garlic
- ¾ pound large shrimp, peeled and deveined
- salt to taste
- ½ teaspoon red pepper flakes
- ½ bunch fresh asparagus, trimmed
- ground black pepper to taste
- 1 medium lemon, halved
- ¼ cup finely shredded Parmesan cheese

## Instructions

1. Heat avocado oil and butter together in a skillet over medium heat.
2. Add garlic and cook until lightly browned, 1 to 3 minutes.
3. Place shrimp into one side of the pan; season with salt and red pepper flakes.
4. Add asparagus to the other side of the pan and season with salt and black pepper.
5. Flip shrimp after about 2 minutes and roll asparagus over.
6. Squeeze lemon juice on top of shrimp and continue cooking about 3 minutes more.
7. Gently sprinkle Parmesan cheese over shrimp.
8. Transfer to a plate and pour pan juices over shrimp.
