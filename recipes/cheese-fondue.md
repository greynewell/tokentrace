## Ingredients

- 1 cup dry white wine
- ½ pound shredded Swiss cheese
- ½ pound shredded Gruyere cheese
- 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- ¼ teaspoon ground nutmeg
- 1 (1 pound) loaf French bread, cut into 1 inch cubes

## Instructions

1. Simmer wine in a fondue pot. Add Swiss and Gruyere cheeses, 1/4 pound at a time. Stir after each addition of cheese until melted. Stir in flour. When cheese has melted, stir in salt and nutmeg.
2. Serve with cut-up French bread.
