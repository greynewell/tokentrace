## Ingredients

- ½ cup margarine
- 1  onion, chopped
- 1 ½ cups sliced fresh mushrooms
- 1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of mushroom soup
- 8 ounces jalapeno processed cheese spread
- 1 pound fresh shrimp, peeled and deveined
- 1 pound angel hair pasta

## Instructions

1. In a large skillet over medium heat, melt margarine and saute onions and mushrooms; add soup and processed cheese spread and blend together.
2. Add shrimp and cook for about 10 minutes.
3. Meanwhile, bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
4. Add pasta and cook for 5 to 7 minutes or until al dente; drain.
5. Pour sauce over pasta and serve.
