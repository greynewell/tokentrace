## Ingredients

- 3 tablespoons butter
- 1 tablespoon minced garlic
- 1 pound shredded cooked chicken
- 2 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened
- 1 cup creamy Parmesan salad dressing
- ¼ teaspoon apple cider vinegar
- 1 ¼ cups shredded Monterey Jack cheese
- ½ cup shredded Parmesan cheese

## Instructions

1. Mix butter and garlic in a skillet over medium-high heat until butter is melted.
2. Add chicken; cook and stir until chicken is heated through, 2 to 4 minutes.
3. Stir cream cheese, Parmesan salad dressing, and vinegar into chicken mixture; cook until well-blended and warm, about 5 minutes.
4. Mix 1/2 of the Monterey Jack cheese and 1/2 of the Parmesan cheese into chicken mixture; transfer to a slow cooker.
5. Sprinkle remaining Monterey Jack cheese and Parmesan cheese over dip.
6. Cook on Low until hot and bubbling, 30 to 40 minutes.
