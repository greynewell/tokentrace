## Ingredients

- 1 cup water
- 1  onion, sliced
- 2 cloves garlic, crushed
- ½ teaspoon red pepper flakes
- 1 tablespoon coarse sea salt
- 3 cups champagne
- 2  lobsters

## Instructions

1. Pour the water into the bottom of a large pot over medium heat.
2. Add the onion, garlic, red pepper, and salt to the water and bring to a boil.
3. Stir in the champagne and return the mixture to a boil.
4. Place the lobsters in a steamer basket and lower into the pot.
5. Steam the lobsters until they turn a bright red color, about 12 minutes.
