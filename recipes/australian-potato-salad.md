## Ingredients

- 4 pounds white potatoes, cut into 1 inch cubes
- ¾ cup bottled French salad dressing
- 1 cup chopped celery
- 6  green onions, chopped
- 1 ¼ cups mayonnaise
- 1 cup sour cream
- 1 tablespoon coarse grained mustard
- ½ cup chopped fresh mint leaves
- ½ cup chopped fresh dill weed
- ground black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Place the potatoes in a large pot and fill with enough water to cover.
2. Bring to a boil with the pot uncovered, then remove from the heat, cover and let stand for 25 minutes.
3. Drain and transfer to a serving bowl.
4. Stir in the French salad dressing while the potatoes are still warm.
5. Cool for at least 1 hour.
6. Add the celery and green onions to the potatoes and mix in the mayonnaise, sour cream, mustard, mint, dill and pepper.
7. Refrigerate until serving.
