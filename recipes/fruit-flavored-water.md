## Ingredients

- 5 cups water
- 1 cup ice cubes  (Optional)
- ½ cup small strawberries
- 1  lime, sliced

## Instructions

1. Pour water into a pitcher.
2. Add ice and strawberries.
3. Squeeze lime slices to release some of their juice into the water before adding them in.
4. Stir to combine flavors.
