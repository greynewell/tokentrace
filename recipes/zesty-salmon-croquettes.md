## Ingredients

- 1 (14.75 ounce) can salmon - drained, deboned, and flaked
- ½ cup all-purpose flour
- ½ cup bread crumbs
- ½ cup minced onion
- 1  egg, beaten
- 1 tablespoon seafood seasoning (such as Old Bay®)
- 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
- 1 tablespoon mayonnaise
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- ⅛ teaspoon ground black pepper
- oil for frying

## Instructions

1. Combine salmon, flour, bread crumbs, onion, egg, seafood seasoning, Worcestershire sauce, mayonnaise, salt, and pepper and mix very well.
2. Shape mixture into 6 even-sized patties.
3. Heat oil in a deep skillet over medium-low heat.
4. Cook patties until nicely browned, about 5 minutes per side.
