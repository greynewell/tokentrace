## Ingredients

- ¼ cup plain instant oatmeal
- 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
- 2 tablespoons milk
- 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 2 tablespoons butter, or more to taste

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C).
2. Combine 1 cup water and 1/4 cup quick-cooking oats together in a microwave-safe bowl.
3. Microwave on full power until mixture is thick and creamy, 1 1/2 to 2 minutes.
4. Mix oatmeal, flour, milk, vegetable oil, and salt together in a bowl.
5. Spread evenly on a baking sheet; top with bits of butter.
6. Bake in the preheated oven until brown on top, about 30 minutes.
