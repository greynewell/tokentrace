## Ingredients

- 18  fresh okra pods, sliced 1/3 inch thick
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 2 teaspoons kosher salt, or to taste
- 2 teaspoons black pepper, or to taste

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C).
2. Arrange okra slices in a single layer on a foil-lined baking sheet. Drizzle with olive oil and sprinkle with salt and pepper.
3. Roast in the preheated oven for 10 to 15 minutes.
