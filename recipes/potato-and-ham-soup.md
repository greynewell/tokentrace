## Ingredients

- 5 cups peeled and diced potatoes
- 1 cup diced onion
- ¾ cup diced celery
- ¾ cup diced carrot
- 2 teaspoons salt, or to taste
- ½ teaspoon ground black pepper, or to taste
- 4 cups water
- 1 ½ cups diced ham
- 2 (10.75 ounce) cans  condensed cream of celery soup
- 1 cup heavy cream

## Instructions

1. Bring the potatoes, onion, celery, carrot, salt, pepper, and water to a boil in a large pot.
2. Cook until the vegetables are tender, about 15 minutes.
3. Add the ham, cream of celery soup, and heavy cream; return to a simmer and cook for 5 minutes.
4. Adjust the seasoning and serve.
