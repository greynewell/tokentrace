## Ingredients

- ½  Smithfield® Applewood Smoked Bacon Seasoned Pork Loin Filet, cooked and finely diced
- 1 (12 ounce) package pimento cheese spread
- 3 tablespoons barbecue sauce
- 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
- 4 (10 inch) flour tortillas

## Instructions

1. Combine pimiento cheese spread, barbecue sauce and paprika in small bowl; mix well.
2. Divide cheese mixture between 4 tortillas; evenly spread on each tortilla.
3. Sprinkle chopped pork over top of cheese spread.
4. Tightly roll up each tortilla and wrap in plastic wrap.
5. Place in refrigerator 2 hours or up to 2 days.
6. Cut each tortilla into 1/2-inch slices.
7. Serve with a light drizzle of barbecue sauce
