## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 6  frozen pot stickers
- 1 cup frozen shelled edamame (green soybeans)
- 1 cup water
- 4 cups chopped iceberg lettuce
- 2 tablespoons ginger-sesame salad dressing
- salt to taste

## Instructions

1. Heat the olive oil in a skillet over medium heat.
2. Place the pot stickers into the hot oil flat-side-down.
3. Cook until the bottom has turned golden brown, 1 to 2 minutes.
4. Add the edamame and pour in the water.
5. Cover, and bring to a simmer over medium-high heat.
6. Cook until the pot stickers have completely thawed and are hot on the inside, 8 to 10 minutes.
7. Scrape the pot stickers and edamame into a mixing bowl, and add the lettuce and salad dressing.
8. Season to taste with salt, and toss to mix.
9. Serve immediately.
