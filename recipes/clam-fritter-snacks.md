## Ingredients

- 1 (10 ounce) can minced clams, drained
- 1 cup baking mix
- 1  egg, beaten
- 2 tablespoons vegetable oil

## Instructions

1. Drain the clams.
2. In a medium bowl, beat 1 egg and stir in the cup of baking mix and clams.
3. In a medium skillet, heat the oil and fry the clams in pieces until golden brown.
4. Drain on a plate lined with paper towels.
