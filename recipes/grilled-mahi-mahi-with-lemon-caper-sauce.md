## Ingredients

- 6 (6 ounce) mahi mahi fillets (or other firm white fish)
- 2 tablespoons Crisco® Pure Olive Oil
- 2  limes, juiced
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ¼ teaspoon pepper
- 1 cup mayonnaise
- 1 (4 ounce) jar Crosse & Blackwell® Capers, rinsed and drained
- 1  lemon, juiced
- 6 cups mixed salad greens

## Instructions

1. Place fish in a shallow dish and rub with olive oil, lime juice and salt and pepper.
2. Cover and leave at room temperature 2 hours.
3. Heat grill to medium-high.
4. Grill fish 4 minutes on each side.
5. Combine mayonnaise, capers and lemon juice.
6. Serve fish on bed of mixed greens and top with mayonnaise mixture.
