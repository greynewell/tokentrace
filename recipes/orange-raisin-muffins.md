## Ingredients

- 1 ¾ cups all-purpose flour
- 3 teaspoons baking powder
- ¾ teaspoon salt
- ¼ cup white sugar
- 1  egg, beaten
- 1  orange, zested
- ¼ cup orange juice
- ½ cup milk
- ⅓ cup vegetable oil
- ¾ cup raisins

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
2. Grease muffin cups or line with paper muffin liners.
3. In a large bowl, sift together flour, baking powder, salt and sugar.
4. In a separate bowl, combine egg, orange zest, orange juice, milk and vegetable oil.
5. Stir egg mixture into flour mixture just until moistened; fold in raisins.
6. Spoon batter into prepared muffin cups.
7. Bake in preheated oven for 20 to 25 minutes, until a toothpick inserted into center of a muffin comes out clean.
