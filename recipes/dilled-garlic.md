## Ingredients

- 2 pounds garlic, peeled
- 1 sprig chopped fresh dill
- cayenne pepper to taste
- 4 ½ cups distilled white vinegar
- 4 tablespoons salt

## Instructions

1. Fill sterile containers with garlic, chopped fresh dill and cayenne pepper to within an inch of the top.
2. Mix the distilled white vinegar and salt in a medium saucepan.
3. Bring to a boil.
4. Pour the hot distilled white vinegar and salt mixture into the containers with the garlic mixture.
5. Fill to approximately 1/4 inch from the top.
6. Seal and store in the refrigerator.
