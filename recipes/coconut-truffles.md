## Ingredients

- 2 (16 ounce) boxes confectioners' sugar
- 1 (14 ounce) can sweetened condensed milk
- 1 cup butter
- 2 ½ cups chopped walnuts
- 1 (14 ounce) package shredded coconut
- 1 (24 ounce) bag chocolate chips

## Instructions

1. Line a baking sheet with waxed paper.
2. Mix sugar, sweetened condensed milk, and butter together in a bowl.
3. Stir walnuts and coconut into dough.
4. Cover bowl with plastic wrap and freeze until dough is firm, at least 1 hour.
5. Form dough into 1-inch balls, place on prepared baking sheet, and freeze until firm, at least 30 minutes.
6. Melt chocolate chips in a double boiler or in a bowl set over a pan of simmering water, stirring frequently until smooth.
7. Dip balls into melted chocolate to coat; cool on prepared baking sheet until chocolate has hardened.
