## Ingredients

- 1 ½ pounds catfish fillets
- ⅓ cup tahini
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- ¼ cup water
- ¼ cup fresh lemon juice
- salt to taste
- 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley

## Instructions

1. Preheat an oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
2. Grease a baking dish and arrange the catfish in a single layer.
3. Bake in the preheated oven until the fish flakes easily with a fork, 45 to 60 minutes.
4. Meanwhile, whisk together the tahini, garlic, water, and lemon juice; season with salt.
5. Once the fish is done, cut it into 1 inch cubes, and pour the tahini sauce overtop.
6. Sprinkle with parsley to garnish.
