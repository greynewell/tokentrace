## Ingredients

- 1 (9 ounce) package frozen cut green beans
- 1 clove garlic, peeled and crushed
- ¼ cup water
- 1 teaspoon fresh lemon juice
- 3  anchovy fillets, oil reserved
- freshly ground black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Place the green beans in a medium saucepan, and mix in the garlic, water, and lemon juice.
2. Cover, and bring to a boil.
3. Cook, stirring occasionally, 8 to 10 minutes, until tender but crisp.
4. Drain, and transfer to a medium bowl.
5. In a small bowl, mash the anchovy fillets and about 1 tablespoon reserved oil.
6. Toss into the bowl with the green beans.
7. Season with pepper.
8. Serve immediately.
