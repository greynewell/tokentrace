## Ingredients

- 1 cup water
- 1 extra large egg, beaten
- 2 tablespoons dry milk powder

- 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
- 1 ½ teaspoons salt
- 1 cup bread flour
- 2 cups all-purpose flour
- 1 ¼ teaspoons active dry yeast
