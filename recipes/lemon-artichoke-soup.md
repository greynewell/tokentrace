## Ingredients

- ¼ cup butter
- ½ cup minced onion
- ½ cup minced celery
- ½ cup minced carrots
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- 3 cups chicken broth
- 2 cups chopped artichoke hearts
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
- 1  lemon, halved
- 3 cups half and half

## Instructions

1. Melt the butter in a saucepan over medium heat.
2. Add the onion, celery, carrot, and garlic; cook until the onion and garlic are translucent.
3. Stir in the chicken broth, artichoke hearts, salt, and pepper and simmer 10 minutes.
4. Transfer mixture to a blender and puree until smooth; return the mixture to the saucepan.
5. Squeeze one lemon half into the pan.
6. Place the other lemon half into the saucepan.
7. Bring to a boil.
8. Reduce heat to low and simmer another 10 minutes.
9. Remove the lemon half from the pan and pour in the half and half; stir.
10. Continue to simmer until thoroughly heated, about 10 minutes.
