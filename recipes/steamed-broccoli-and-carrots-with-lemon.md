## Ingredients

- 1 cup broccoli florets
- ½ cup julienne-cut carrots
- 2 tablespoons lemon juice
- 1 teaspoon seasoned salt, or to taste

## Instructions

1. Place a steamer insert into a saucepan and fill with water to just below the bottom of the steamer. Bring water to a boil. Add broccoli florets and carrots, cover, and steam until tender, about 5 minutes.
2. Transfer vegetables to a bowl; add lemon juice and seasoned salt and toss to coat.
