## Ingredients

- 4 sheets aluminum foil
- cooking spray
- 4 (4 ounce) fillets swai fish
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- 1  lemon, thinly sliced and seeds removed
- 6  mushrooms, thinly sliced
- ½  onion, thinly sliced
- 2 tablespoons capers

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Arrange aluminum foil on a work surface and lightly spray cooking spray onto 1 side of each.
3. Place 1 swai fish fillet onto the middle of each piece of foil; season with salt and pepper.
4. Layer lemon slices, mushroom slices, onion slices, and capers onto each swai fillet.
5. Fold foil over each swai fillet creating a packet.
6. Place packets on a baking sheet.
7. Bake in the preheated oven until fish flakes easily with a fork, about 15 minutes.
