## Ingredients

- 4 ears  corn, shucked
- ¼ cup melted butter
- ¼ cup mayonnaise
- ½ cup grated cotija cheese
- 4 wedges lime  (Optional)

## Instructions

1. Preheat an outdoor grill for medium-high heat.
2. Grill corn on the preheated grill until hot and lightly charred all over, 7 to 10 minutes.
3. Roll corn in melted butter, then spread evenly with mayonnaise. Sprinkle with cotija cheese and serve with a lime wedge.
