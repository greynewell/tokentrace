## Ingredients

- 3  hard-boiled egg whites, chopped
- 3 tablespoons prepared hummus
- 1 pinch celery seed
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- 1 dash hot pepper sauce  (Optional)

## Instructions

1. Mix chopped egg whites, hummus, celery seed, salt, black pepper, and hot pepper sauce in a bowl.
2. Refrigerate for 2 hours to overnight.
