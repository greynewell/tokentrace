## Ingredients

- 1 pound firm tofu, cubed
- 4 cups water
- ¼ cup white vinegar
- 1 pinch salt

## Instructions

1. In a large pot over medium-high heat bring water, vinegar and salt to a boil.
2. Stir in tofu and return to a boil.
3. Reduce heat to medium and simmer 15 minutes.
4. Drain.
