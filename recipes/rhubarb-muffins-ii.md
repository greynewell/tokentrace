## Ingredients

- 2 ½ cups all-purpose flour
- 1 teaspoon baking soda
- 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
- 1 teaspoon salt
- ½ cup packed brown sugar
- 1 cup buttermilk
- 1  egg
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 2 ½ cups chopped fresh rhubarb
- ½ cup chopped walnuts
- ½ cup white sugar
- 2 tablespoons butter, softened

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C).
2. Grease muffin cups or line with paper muffin liners.
3. In a large bowl, combine flour, baking soda, cinnamon and salt.
4. Set aside.
5. In a medium bowl, combine brown sugar, buttermilk, egg and vanilla; mix.
6. Fold in rhubarb and walnuts.
7. Add to flour mixture, mixing just until moist.
8. Spoon batter into prepared paper liners.
9. In a small bowl, blend sugar and butter and sprinkle over each muffin.
10. Bake in preheated oven for 20 minutes, or until toothpick inserted in middle comes out clean.
