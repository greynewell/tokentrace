## Ingredients

- 1 cup mayonnaise
- 1 cup sour cream
- ½ cup green onions, sliced
- ½ cup fresh parsley
- 1 teaspoon Dijon-style prepared mustard
- 1 clove garlic, minced

## Instructions

1. In an electric blender or food processor, combine mayonnaise, sour cream, green onion, parsley, mustard, and garlic.
2. Blend until smooth.
3. Cover and chill before serving.
