## Ingredients

- 48  littleneck clams, shucked and drained
- 1 cup 2% milk
- ½ cup gluten-free all purpose baking flour
- ½ cup masa harina
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
- ⅛ teaspoon ground cayenne pepper
- oil for frying

## Instructions

1. Place shucked clams in a small bowl.
2. Pour in milk; toss clams gently with a fork to cover evenly.
3. Let soak in the refrigerator for 30 minutes.
4. Combine flour,  , salt, pepper, and cayenne pepper in a plastic container with a lid.
5. Seal container and shake to blend flour mixture.
6. Remove clams one at a time from the milk and place in the flour mixture; shake gently to coat.
7. Shake off excess flour and place on a plate.
8. Heat 3/4 inch oil in a large saucepan or deep-fryer.
9. Fry clams in small batches until golden brown and crispy, about 1 minute.
10. Transfer to a paper towel-lined plate using a slotted spoon.
