## Ingredients

- cooking spray
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- ½ cup finely diced onion
- 1 (10 ounce) package fresh spinach, chopped
- 6  large eggs
- ¼ cup milk, or as needed
- ¼ cup shredded sharp Cheddar cheese
- ¼ cup shredded Asiago cheese
- ¼ cup grated Parmesan cheese
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- ¾ cup imitation crab meat, finely chopped

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
2. Spray 24 miniature muffin cups with cooking spray.
3. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat and cook onion until softened, about 2 minutes.
4. Stir spinach into onion and cook, stirring often, until spinach is wilted and onions are translucent, about 3 more minutes.
5. Drain juice.
6. Beat eggs with milk in a large bowl until thoroughly combined; stir in Cheddar, Asiago, and Parmesan cheeses and season with salt and black pepper.
7. Divide spinach mixture into the prepared muffin cups (about 1 teaspoon per cup) and top spinach with imitation crab meat.
8. Slowly and carefully pour egg-cheese mixture over the imitation crab, filling the mini muffin cups just to the top.
9. Bake in the preheated oven until the egg is set, 12 to 15 minutes.
