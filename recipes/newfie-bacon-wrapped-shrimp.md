## Ingredients

- ¾ cup boiling water
- 2 tablespoons butter
- ½ (6 ounce) box chicken-flavored dry bread stuffing mix
- 9 slices maple-cured bacon, halved crosswise
- 18  cooked medium shrimp, peeled and deveined

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
2. Grease a baking sheet.
3. Boil water with butter in a saucepan; stir in stuffing mix.
4. Remove saucepan from heat, cover, and let stand for 5 minutes.
5. Fluff with a fork.
6. Lay out halved bacon strips.
7. Place about 1 teaspoon stuffing on end of each strip, top with a shrimp, and roll up.
8. Secure with a toothpick.
9. Place bacon wraps on prepared baking sheet.
10. Bake in the preheated oven until bacon is crisp, about 30 minutes.
11. Remove with tongs to a paper towel-lined plate to cool slightly before serving, about 2 minutes.
