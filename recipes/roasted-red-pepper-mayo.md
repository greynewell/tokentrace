## Ingredients

- ⅓ cup mayonnaise
- 2 tablespoons chopped roasted red peppers
- 1 squeeze lemon juice
- ½ teaspoon lemon zest
- ⅛ teaspoon salt

## Instructions

1. Mix mayonnaise and roasted red peppers together in a small bowl.
2. Add lemon juice, lemon zest, and salt; mix well.
3. Refrigerate until serving.
