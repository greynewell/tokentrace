## Ingredients

- ½ cup popcorn kernels
- ¼ cup canola oil
- 1  lime, zested and juiced
- ½ teaspoon salt
- 1 tablespoon unsalted butter, melted
- 1 tablespoon sriracha sauce

## Instructions

1. Combine popcorn kernels and oil in a deep pot over high heat and place a cover on the pot.
2. Cook the popcorn, frequently shaking the pot to move the kernels, until the sound of kernels popping slows to nearly a stop, about 7 minutes.
3. Carefully pour popcorn into a large mixing bowl.
4. Sprinkle lime zest and salt over the popcorn.
5. Whisk lime juice, melted butter, and sriracha sauce together in a bowl; drizzle over popcorn and toss to coat.
