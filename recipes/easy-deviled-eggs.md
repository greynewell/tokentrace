## Ingredients

- 6  large eggs hard-cooked eggs
- ¼ cup mayonnaise
- 1 teaspoon favorite flavor mustard
- 1 teaspoon cider vinegar

## Instructions

1. Cut eggs in half and pop out yolks into resealable food-safe plastic bag.
2. Add remaining ingredients, seal bag and squeeze to mix.
3. Cut off one corner of plastic bag and squeeze to pipe yolk filling into whites.
