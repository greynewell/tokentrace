## Ingredients

- 2  eggs
- 1 (5 ounce) can tuna, drained
- 1 cup low-fat cottage cheese
- ¼ cup chopped celery
- ¼ cup chopped green onions
- 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
- 1 teaspoon lemon pepper
- ¼ teaspoon celery salt
- 1 teaspoon lemon juice
- ¼ cup mayonnaise

## Instructions

1. Place eggs in a saucepan, and cover completely with cold water.
2. Bring water to a boil.
3. Cover, remove from heat, and let eggs stand in hot water for 10 to 12 minutes.
4. Remove from hot water, and cool.
5. Peel, and chop.
6. In a large bowl, flake tuna.
7. Add cottage cheese, eggs, celery, green onions, parsley, lemon pepper seasoning, celery salt, lemon juice, and mayonnaise; mix well.
8. Chill.
