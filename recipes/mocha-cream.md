## Ingredients

- 2 cups heavy whipping cream
- 2 tablespoons white sugar
- 1 tablespoon instant coffee powder
- 1 tablespoon boiling water

## Instructions

1. Beat whipping cream and sugar together in a mixing bowl until stiff.
2. Dissolve coffee granules in boiling water. Cool. Add to whipped cream and beat in. Keep mocha cream refrigerated.
