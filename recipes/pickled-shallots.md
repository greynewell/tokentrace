## Ingredients

- 3 tablespoons butter
- 2 tablespoons liquid honey
- 1 pound shallots, peeled and quartered
- 4 sprigs fresh thyme, chopped
- salt and pepper to taste
- 7 tablespoons balsamic vinegar

## Instructions

1. Melt butter in a medium skillet over medium heat; stir in honey and shallots.
2. Season with thyme, salt, and pepper and cook until lightly browned, 2 to 3 minutes.
3. Turn shallots to brown evenly.
4. Pour balsamic vinegar into the skillet with the shallots.
5. Reduce heat to low and simmer until shallots are soft, about 7 minutes.
6. Serve warm or store shallots in the refrigerator in a sealed container until chilled.
