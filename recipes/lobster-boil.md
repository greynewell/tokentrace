## Ingredients

- 12 cups water
- 1 cup dry white wine
- ¼ cup kosher salt
- 1 tablespoon whole white peppercorns, coarsely ground
- 1 tablespoon paprika
- 2 teaspoons lemon zest
- 1 teaspoon garlic powder
- 1 teaspoon onion powder
- ¼ teaspoon cayenne pepper
- 2  bay leaves, crushed
- 2 (1 pound) whole lobsters

## Instructions

1. Bring water to a boil in a tall stockpot.
2. Add white wine, salt, peppercorns, paprika, lemon zest, garlic powder, onion powder, cayenne pepper, and bay leaves.
3. Stir and return to a boil.
4. Lower lobsters carefully into the stockpot.
5. Cover and cook over medium heat until lobsters are bright red and claws are easily pulled off, about 10 minutes.
