## Ingredients

- ¼ cup black truffle-infused olive oil, divided
- 1 (10 ounce) package crimini mushrooms, sliced
- ¼ cup diced red bell pepper
- ¼ cup minced flat-leaf parsley
- 2 cloves garlic, pressed, or more to taste
- 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- ½ large baguette, sliced into thin rounds

## Instructions

1. Heat 2 tablespoons truffle-infused oil in a skillet over medium heat; cook and stir mushrooms until most of the liquid has evaporated, about 5 minutes.
2. Add red bell pepper, parsley, and garlic to mushrooms; cook and stir until mushrooms are golden and lightly browned, about 5 minutes more.
3. Remove skillet from heat.
4. Set oven rack about 6 inches from the heat source and preheat the oven's broiler.
5. Stir remaining truffle-infused oil and cream cheese into mushroom mixture until cheese melts from the heat of the skillet; season with salt and pepper.
6. Spread mushroom-cheese mixture onto each baguette round.
7. Arrange rounds on a baking sheet.
8. Broil bruschetta until bread is warmed, 2 to 3 minutes.
