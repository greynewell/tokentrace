## Ingredients

- 1  egg white, beaten
- 2 cups mixed nuts
- 6 tablespoons granulated sugar
- 1 tablespoon ground cinnamon
- ⅛ teaspoon ground nutmeg
- ⅛ teaspoon ground cloves
- 1 cup raisins
- ½ teaspoon salt  (Optional)

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C).
2. Beat egg white until smooth; stir in nuts.
3. In a small bowl combine sugar, cinnamon, nutmeg and cloves; stir into nut mixture.
4. Spread on a baking sheet.
5. Bake in preheated oven for 20 minutes, stirring occasionally.
6. Remove from oven and stir in raisins and salt.
7. Cool before storing in an airtight container.
