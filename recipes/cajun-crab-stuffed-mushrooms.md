## Ingredients

- 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
- ½ cup shredded Colby-Monterey Jack cheese
- 1 teaspoon seafood seasoning (such as Old Bay®)
- ½ teaspoon Cajun seasoning
- ¼ teaspoon cayenne hot pepper sauce, or to taste  (Optional)
- ¼ teaspoon garlic powder
- 1 (8 ounce) package imitation crabmeat, flaked
- ¼ cup Italian seasoned bread crumbs
- 1 (8 ounce) package crimini mushrooms, stems removed

## Instructions

1. Preheat an oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Grease a 9x5 inch baking dish.
3. Stir the cream cheese, Colby-Monterey Jack cheese, seafood seasoning, Cajun seasoning, hot pepper sauce, and garlic powder in a mixing bowl until smooth.
4. Stir in the crabmeat and bread crumbs until evenly blended.
5. Spoon the cheese mixture into the mushroom caps; set them filling-side-up into the prepared baking dish.
6. Bake in the preheated oven for 7 minutes; set the oven to broil and broil until the tops are crisp and brown, about 3 minutes.
