## Ingredients

- 10  hot dogs, cut into thirds
- 15 slices bacon, cut in half
- ½ cup packed brown sugar

## Instructions

1. Wrap 1/2 slice of bacon around each piece of hot dog.
2. Secure the bacon with a toothpick.
3. Place hot dogs into a slow cooker on low heat.
4. Pour brown sugar over hot dogs, let cook for 5 to 6 hours; until the bacon is cooked.
