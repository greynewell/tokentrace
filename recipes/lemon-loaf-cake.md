## Ingredients

- ½ cup butter
- ½ cup white sugar
- 2  eggs
- ½ cup sour cream
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 1 teaspoon freshly grated lemon peel
- 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
- ¼ cup cornstarch
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ½ teaspoon baking soda
- 3 tablespoons lemon juice
- 2 cups confectioners' sugar

## Instructions

1. Preheat an oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C).
2. Beat the butter and sugar with an electric mixer in a large bowl until light and fluffy.
3. The mixture should be noticeably lighter in color.
4. Add the room temperature eggs one at a time, allowing each egg to blend into the butter mixture before adding the next.
5. Beat in the sour cream, vanilla, and lemon peel.
6. Beat for an additional 2 minutes.
7. Batter will look curdled.
8. Combine flour, cornstarch, salt, and baking soda in a separate bowl.
9. Pour the flour mixture into the egg mixture, mixing until just incorporated.
10. Pour the batter into a prepared 8x4 inch loaf pan.
11. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean, 30 to 35 minutes.
12. Cool in the pans for 5 minutes before removing to cool completely on a plate.
13. To make glaze:  Whisk together the lemon juice and powdered sugar.
14. Pour glaze over entire cake and allow cake to cool while the glaze is soaked in.
