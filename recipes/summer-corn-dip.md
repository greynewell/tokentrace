## Ingredients

- 1 (15.25 ounce) can whole kernel corn, drained
- 1  red onion, minced
- 1 small roma (plum) tomato, seeded and chopped
- 1 small cucumber, chopped
- ½ cup fresh mint leaves, chopped
- ½ cup fresh basil leaves, chopped
- 2  Thai chile peppers, seeded and chopped
- 1 tablespoon lime juice
- ¼ cup mayonnaise

## Instructions

1. Stir the corn, onion, tomato, cucumber, mint, basil, Thai chile peppers, lime juice, and mayonnaise together in a bowl.
2. Refrigerate overnight or 8 hours before serving.
