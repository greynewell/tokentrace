## Ingredients

- 6  avocados - peeled, pitted, and cubed
- 1 teaspoon fresh lemon juice
- 5  hard-cooked eggs, chopped
- ½  red onion, minced
- ½ cup light mayonnaise
- 1 tablespoon milk
- 3  hard-cooked eggs, sliced
- ½ teaspoon paprika

## Instructions

1. Toss avocado cubes with lemon juice in a serving bowl; mix avocado with chopped egg and onion.
2. Whisk together the mayonnaise and milk; add to avocado mixture and gently mix.
3. Arrange the remaining eggs sliced atop the salad in rows.
4. Sprinkle top with paprika.
