## Ingredients

- ½ cup white sugar
- ½ cup water
- ½ teaspoon Chinese five-spice powder
- 1 pound seedless grapes

## Instructions

1. Combine sugar and water in a saucepan; simmer over medium heat until sugar is dissolved, about 2 minutes. Remove saucepan from heat and stir Chinese five-spice powder into sugar mixture.
2. Pierce each grape all the way through using a fork. Add grapes and sugar mixture to a resealable bag; refrigerate, turning every few hours, until grapes are marinated, about 24 hours.
