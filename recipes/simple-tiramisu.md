## Ingredients

- 16  ladyfingers
- .66 cup strong brewed coffee, room temperature
- ⅓ cup maple syrup
- 1 (3 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
- ½ cup sour cream
- ½ cup maple syrup
- 1 (12 ounce) container frozen whipped topping, thawed
- 1 tablespoon unsweetened cocoa powder, for dusting

## Instructions

1. Split ladyfingers lengthwise and use them to line the bottom and sides of an 8x8 inch dish.
2. Stir together coffee and 1/3 cup maple syrup until smooth.
3. Spoon mixture over ladyfingers.
4. In a large bowl, beat together cream cheese, sour cream and 1/2 cup maple syrup until smooth.
5. Fold in whipped topping.
6. Spread mixture over soaked ladyfingers.
7. Cover and chill 15 minutes.
8. Dust with sifted cocoa just before serving.
