## Ingredients

- 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese
- 1 (.25 ounce) package unflavored gelatin
- 1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of mushroom soup
- ¾ cup chopped celery
- ¾ cup chopped green onions
- ¾ cup water chestnuts, drained
- 1 ½ cups cooked shrimp, peeled and deveined

## Instructions

1. Place the cream cheese and gelatin in a medium bowl and microwave on high approximately 1 minute, or until melted.
2. Blend until creamy.
3. Stir in the cream of mushroom soup, celery, green onions, water chestnuts and shrimp.
4. Chill in the refrigerator approximately 1 hour before serving.
