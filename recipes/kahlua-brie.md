## Ingredients

- ¾ cup chopped pecans
- 1 (14 ounce) round Brie cheese
- ¼ cup coffee-flavored liqueur (such as Kahlua®)
- 3 tablespoons brown sugar

## Instructions

1. Spread pecans onto a microwave-safe plate; heat in microwave, stirring every 2 minutes, until pecans are fragrant and lightly toasted, 4 to 6 minutes.
2. Place Brie cheese on a microwave-safe plate.
3. Stir pecans, coffee-flavored liqueur, and brown sugar together in a bowl; spread on top of Brie cheese.
4. Heat Brie cheese mixture in the microwave until softened, 1 1/2 to 2 minutes.
