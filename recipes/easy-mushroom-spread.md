## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons butter
- ½ teaspoon fresh rosemary
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 3 cups chopped fresh mushrooms
- ¼ cup fresh parsley
- 3 tablespoons vegetable stock
- 1 teaspoon cornstarch
- 1 tablespoon Scotch whiskey

## Instructions

1. In a large skillet over medium heat, melt butter and saute rosemary and garlic until tender.
2. Stir in mushrooms and saute until juices run.
3. Add parsley and stir occasionally to prevent sticking.
4. When mushrooms are tender, stir in stock and mix well before adding cornstarch.
5. Cook for 1 to 2 minutes, then add whiskey and cook for 1 minute more.
