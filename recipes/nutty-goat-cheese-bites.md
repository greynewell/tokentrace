## Ingredients

- 1  French baguette, cut into 1/2 inch slices
- 1 (8 ounce) tub spreadable goat cheese
- ½ cup finely chopped pecans
- 12  red seedless grapes, halved

## Instructions

1. Pour chopped nuts into a bowl, and set aside.
2. Spread each bread slice with approximately 1 tablespoon goat cheese.
3. Dip into the pecans to thoroughly coat the cheese.
4. Arrange on a plate, and push a grape half into the top of  the cheese on each slice.
