## Ingredients

- 4 pounds seedless green grapes
- 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese
- 1 (8 ounce) container sour cream
- ½ cup white sugar
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 4 ounces chopped pecans
- 2 tablespoons brown sugar

## Instructions

1. Wash and dry grapes.
2. Mix together cream cheese, sour cream, sugar, and vanilla in a large bowl. Add grapes and mix until evenly incorporated.
3. Sprinkle with brown sugar and pecans, mix again and refrigerate until serving.
