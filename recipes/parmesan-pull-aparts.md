## Ingredients

- 3 tablespoons melted butter
- ¼ teaspoon dill weed
- ¼ teaspoon celery seed
- ¼ teaspoon minced onion
- 1 tablespoon grated Parmesan cheese
- 1 (10 ounce) can refrigerated biscuit dough, separated and cut into half circles
- 1 tablespoon grated Parmesan cheese

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C).
2. Pour melted butter into the bottom of a 9-inch pie dish; swirl dish to evenly coat.
3. Mix dill, celery seed, onion, and 1 tablespoon Parmesan cheese in a small bowl; sprinkle into the pie dish.
4. Place 1 biscuit half in the center of the pie dish; place remaining biscuit halves around the center biscuit in a clockwise arrangement.
5. Sprinkle biscuits with 1 tablespoon Parmesan cheese.
6. Bake in the preheated oven until biscuits are golden brown, 15 to 18 minutes.
7. Flip dish upside-down onto a plate and pull apart to serve.
