## Ingredients

- 4 cups baking mix
- 3 ounces Cheddar cheese, shredded
- 1 ⅓ cups water
- ½ cup melted butter
- 1 teaspoon garlic powder
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- ⅛ teaspoon onion powder
- ⅛ teaspoon dried parsley

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
2. Combine baking mix, water, and cheese in a large bowl; mix until dough comes together. Use a small scoop to portion dough onto the prepared pan.
3. Bake in the preheated oven until golden brown, 10 to 12 minutes.
4. While biscuits are baking, combine melted butter, garlic powder, salt, onion powder, and parsley in a small bowl. Brush over hot biscuits.
