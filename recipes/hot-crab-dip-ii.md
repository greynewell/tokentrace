## Ingredients

- ½ cup milk
- ⅓ cup salsa
- 3 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese
- 1 pound imitation crabmeat, flaked
- 1 cup chopped green onions
- 1 (4 ounce) can diced green chiles

## Instructions

1. In a small bowl, combine milk and salsa.
2. Coat a slow cooker with non-stick cooking spray.
3. Transfer the milk and salsa mixture into the slow cooker.
4. Stir in cream cheese, crab, onions, and chilies.
5. Cover and cook on low for 3 to 4 hours, stirring every 30 minutes.
