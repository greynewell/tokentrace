## Ingredients

- ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
- 1 teaspoon onion powder
- 1 teaspoon dried oregano leaves
- ½ teaspoon blood orange zest
- 1 pound skinless rainbow trout fillets
- 2 tablespoons butter
- salt and ground black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Mix Parmesan cheese, onion powder, oregano leaves, and blood orange zest together in a shallow dish.
2. Press trout fillets into the Parmesan mixture to coat.
3. Shake off excess and place fillets on a plate.
4. Melt butter in a nonstick skillet over medium-low heat.
5. Add fillets; cook until brown and crispy, about 3 minutes.
6. Flip and continue cooking until flesh flakes easily with a fork, about 3 minutes more.
7. Season with salt and pepper.
