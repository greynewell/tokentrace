## Ingredients

- ½ cup Holland House® White Cooking Wine
- 1 pound boneless chicken breasts, skinned
- 1 cup pea pods, blanched
- ¾ cup sliced celery
- ¼ cup sliced green onions
- ½ cup mayonnaise
- 1 teaspoon grated lemon peel
- 1 pinch Pepper if desired
- ½ cup toasted slivered almonds
- Lettuce

## Instructions

1. Place cooking wine and chicken in large saucepan.
2. Add enough water to cover.
3. Bring to a boil; reduce heat.
4. Simmer 10 minutes or until chicken is tender and no longer pink.
5. Let chicken cool in liquid 45 minutes.
6. Drain; cut chicken into bite-sized pieces.
7. In large bowl, combine chicken, pea pods, celery, onions, mayonnaise, lemon peel and pepper; mix well.
8. Cover; refrigerate 1 to 2 hours to blend flavors.
9. Just before serving, stir in almonds.
10. To serve, spoon chicken salad onto lettuce-lined plates.
