## Ingredients

- 6  large portobello mushroom, stems removed
- 1  eggplant, sliced into 1/2 inch rounds
- 1 medium yellow squash, cut into 1/4-inch slices
- 1  zucchini, cut into 1/4-inch slices
- 1 (16 fl oz) bottle balsamic vinaigrette
- 1 (4 ounce) package crumbled blue cheese
- 6  hamburger buns, split and lightly toasted

## Instructions

1. Place the mushrooms, eggplant,  , and zucchini into a shallow bowl or baking dish.
2. Drizzle with the balsamic vinaigrette, turning to coat evenly.
3. Cover, and refrigerate at least 2 hours, or up to 24 hours, stirring occasionally.
4. Preheat grill to medium-high heat.
5. Drain vegetables and discard marinade.
6. Place vegetables on preheated grill, and cook until lightly browned, turning once, or about 3 minutes on each side. (Note that cooking times vary between grills.) Sprinkle the mushrooms with blue cheese, and let the cheese melt slightly.
7. Open the 6 hamburger buns and divide the eggplant, squash, and zucchini among bottom halves.
8. Place the portobello mushrooms on top of the vegetables, cheese side up.
9. Top with the remaining 6 hamburger bun halves.
10. If necessary, use toothpicks to hold the buns in place.
11. Serve immediately.
