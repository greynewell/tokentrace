## Ingredients

- 2 cups all-purpose flour
- 1 tablespoon baking powder
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ¾ cup unsalted butter
- ½ cup white sugar
- 1 cup packed brown sugar
- 1 tablespoon orange zest
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 2  eggs
- 1 ½ cups semisweet chocolate chips
- 1 ½ cups chopped pecans

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease cookie sheets.
2. Sift flour, baking powder, and salt into a medium bowl and set aside.
3. Cream butter, sugars, orange zest, and vanilla extract together in a large bowl until light and fluffy.
4. Add eggs, one at a time, beating well after addition. Add dry ingredients and stir until combined. Stir in chocolate chips and pecans; mix until well-blended.
5. Drop dough by slightly rounded tablespoonfuls 2 inches apart on cookie sheets; flatten slightly.
6. Bake cookies in the preheated oven until golden, 15 to 16 minutes. Let cookies cool on the cookie sheets.
