## Ingredients

- ⅔ cup corn flake cereal crumbs*
- ½ cup all-purpose flour
- 2  Land O'Lakes® Eggs, slightly beaten
- 1 (1 ounce) package dry ranch-flavored dressing mix
- 1 pound boneless skinless chicken tenders
- 3 squares Land O'Lakes® Garlic & Herb Saute Express®
- Ranch dressing

## Instructions

1. Place corn flake crumbs, flour and eggs each into individual shallow bowls.
2. Add ranch dressing mix to flour; stir until combined.
3. Coat each chicken tender with flour mixture, shaking off excess.
4. Dip into beaten egg.
5. Press into corn flake crumbs.
6. Melt Saute Express® squares in 12-inch nonstick skillet over medium-low heat just until bubbles begin to form.
7. Place coated chicken tenders into skillet.
8. Cook, turning occasionally, 10-12 minutes or until chicken is no longer pink.
9. Serve with ranch dressing.
