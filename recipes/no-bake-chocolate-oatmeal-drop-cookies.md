## Ingredients

- ½ cup milk
- 2 cups white sugar
- ½ cup butter
- ½ cup cocoa powder
- 3 cups quick oats
- ½ cup shredded coconut
- ½ cup peanut butter
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

## Instructions

1. Mix milk, sugar, butter, and cocoa powder in a large saucepan and bring to a rolling boil. Boil, stirring frequently, for about 1 minute. Remove from heat and add oats, coconut, peanut butter, and vanilla. Mix thoroughly.
2. Drop tablespoon-sized balls of cookie dough onto waxed paper and let cool in refrigerator for at least 1 hour.
