## Ingredients

- 2 cups all-purpose flour
- ½ cup white sugar
- 3 teaspoons baking powder
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ¾ cup apple juice
- ⅓ cup vegetable oil
- 1  egg
- 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
- 1 cup apples - peeled, cored and finely diced

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (205 degrees C). Grease the bottoms only of 12 muffin cups or line with baking cups.
2. In a medium bowl, combine flour, sugar, baking powder, cinnamon, and salt; mix well. In a small bowl, combine apple juice, oil, and egg; blend well. Add dry ingredients all at once; stir just until dry ingredients are moistened and a few lumps remain. Stir in chopped apples. Fill the prepared muffin cups 2/3 full.
3. Bake in the preheated oven for 18 to 22 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean. Cool for 1 minute before removing from the pan. Serve warm.
