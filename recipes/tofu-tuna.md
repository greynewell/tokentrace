## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
- 1  onion, minced
- 2 stalks celery, minced
- 1 (12 ounce) package silken tofu
- 3 tablespoons mayonnaise
- 3 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
- 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
- 1 teaspoon dill weed
- 1 (5 ounce) can tuna, drained and flaked, or more to taste

## Instructions

1. Heat oil in a skillet over medium-high heat; saute onion and celery until softened, 5 to 10 minutes.
2. Combine tofu, mayonnaise, lemon juice, mustard, and dill weed in a bowl; mash with a potato masher until tofu is smooth and mixture is combined.
3. Fold tuna into tofu mixture.
4. Stir tofu-tuna mixture into onion mixture; cook and stir until heated through, 5 to 10 minutes.
