## Ingredients

- cooking spray
- 2 pounds ground turkey
- 1 (8 ounce) package Cheddar cheese, cubed  (Optional)
- 1  onion, diced
- 1 cup seasoned bread crumbs
- ¾ cup milk
- 2  eggs
- 2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
- 2 cloves garlic, diced
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon ground black pepper

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). Grease a muffin tin with cooking spray.
2. Combine ground turkey, Cheddar cheese, onion, bread crumbs, milk, eggs, Worcestershire sauce, garlic, salt, and pepper in a large bowl; mix well with your hands.
3. Drop scoops of the turkey mixture into the muffin tin using a large ice cream scoop.
4. Bake in the preheated oven until golden brown, about 45 minutes.
