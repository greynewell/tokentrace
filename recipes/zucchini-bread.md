## Ingredients

- 3 cups all-purpose flour
- 3  eggs
- 2 cups white sugar
- 1 cup vegetable oil
- 2 cups grated zucchini
- 1 cup semisweet chocolate chips
- 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
- 1 teaspoon baking soda
- ¼ teaspoon baking powder
- ½ cup sour cream

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease two 9x5-inch loaf pans.
2. Beat together eggs, sugar, and oil. Blend in grated zucchini, and then sour cream. Mix in flour, baking powder, soda, and cinnamon. Stir in chocolate chips. Pour batter into the prepared pans.
3. Bake in the preheated oven until a tester inserted in the center of each loaf comes out clean, about 80 minutes. Cool on wire rack.
