## Ingredients

- 2 pounds swordfish steaks
- 2 tablespoons lemon juice
- ½ cup dry sherry
- 1 tablespoon minced fresh ginger root
- 1 cup mango chutney

## Instructions

1. Place swordfish steaks in a large, shallow bowl.
2. In a small bowl, mix lemon juice, dry sherry, ginger and mango chutney.
3. Pour the mixture over swordfish steaks, turning steaks to coat.
4. Cover, and marinate in the refrigerator 1 hour.
5. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
6. Place swordfish steaks in a medium baking dish.
7. Cover with marinade.
8. Bake in the preheated oven 20 to 30 minutes, basting occasionally with marinade, until fish is easily flaked with a fork.
