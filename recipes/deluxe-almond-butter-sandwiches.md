- 2 slices whole-grain bread

## Instructions

1. Spread almond butter over one side of one slice of bread.
2. Arrange strawberries and bananas over almond butter.
3. Spread jam over one side of remaining slice of bread.
4. Place over fruit to make a sandwich.
