## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons peanut butter
- 2  whole wheat hot dog buns
- 2  bananas
- 2 tablespoons raspberry jelly
- 1 tablespoon raisins  (Optional)

## Instructions

1. Spread 1 tablespoon of peanut butter onto each hot dog bun.
2. Place a banana into each one as if it were a hot dog.
3. Squeeze jelly over the banana from a squeeze bottle, or spread over the peanut butter.
4. Sprinkle with raisins, if using.
