## Ingredients

- ⅔ cup water
- ⅔ cup white sugar
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 5 leaves fresh mint
- 1  lime, juiced
- 1 pinch kosher salt
- 10 cups cubed seeded watermelon
- 8 ounces labneh (thick Middle Eastern-style yogurt)
- 1 tablespoon honey, or to taste
- 1 teaspoon lemon juice, or to taste
- ½  lemon, zested
- 3 tablespoons water, or as needed

## Instructions

1. Combine water, sugar, vanilla extract, and fresh mint in a heavy bottomed saucepan; bring to boil.
2. Remove from heat; mix in lime juice and salt.
3. Place watermelon in batches in a blender.
4. Cover and blend until smooth.
5. Strain through a sieve into a large bowl, using a spoon to press out as much juice as possible; stir in sugar mixture.
6. Pour watermelon mixture into a 10x14-inch glass pan.
7. Freeze, scraping the surface every 30 minutes with a fork until ice crystals form throughout, 1 1/2 to 2 1/2 hours.
8. Mix labneh, honey, lemon juice, and lemon zest together in a small bowl.
9. Whisk in water 1 teaspoon at a time until consistency is light and creamy.
10. Top granita with cream.
