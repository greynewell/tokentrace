## Ingredients

- 1 (14 ounce) package pizza dough
- ¼ cup pizza sauce
- 4 ounces Farmland® Cubed Ham
- 4 ounces Farmland® Original Breakfast Sausage Links, cooked
- 4  eggs, lightly scrambled
- 2  roasted red peppers, sliced
- 1 cup fresh spinach
- ½ cup grated Provolone cheese
- 1  egg mixed with 1 tablespoon water, for egg wash
- 1 teaspoon dried parsley
- ¼ cup grated Parmesan cheese

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 500 degrees F.
2. Roll pizza dough into rough rectangle and place on parchment-lined baking sheet.
3. Spread pizza sauce over dough, leaving 1-inch border on 3 sides.
4. Top with ham, sausage, scrambled eggs, peppers, spinach, and Provolone cheese.
5. Brush edges with egg wash.
6. Fold up sides about 1 inch and brush with more egg wash.
7. Roll up like a jelly roll lengthwise, starting along bottom edge of dough.
8. Place seam-side down, brush with egg wash, and top with parsley and Parmesan cheese.
9. Gently cut slits along top.
10. Place in preheated oven and cook for 8 to 10 minutes, or until golden brown.
