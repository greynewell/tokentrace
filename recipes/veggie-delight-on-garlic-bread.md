## Ingredients

- ⅛ cup olive oil
- 1 clove garlic, chopped
- 1  medium eggplant, cubed
- 1  zucchini, cubed
- 1  medium tomato - peeled, seeded and chopped
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 2 teaspoons minced fresh oregano
- 2 teaspoons minced fresh basil  (Optional)
- 1  French baguette
- 4 teaspoons garlic powder
- 6 teaspoons butter, softened

## Instructions

1. Place olive oil and garlic in a large skillet.
2. Fry (stirring occasionally) over a medium heat until the garlic is golden brown; approximately 2 minutes.
3. Add eggplant and zucchini to the skillet, fry until the eggplant is brown and tender; approximately 5 to 7 minutes.
4. Add the tomato chunks  to the skillet,  stir the mixture until the tomato becomes a pulp.
5. Mix in salt, oregano, and basil.
6. Stir the entire mixture over the heat for 2 minutes, then remove pan from heat and let the mixture cool.
7. Preheat oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C).
8. Slice the bread into 12 (1-inch thick) slices.
9. Distribute garlic powder and butter evenly over the top of each slice.
10. Place the slices directly onto the oven rack (do not use a cookie sheet, the bread will get crisper this way).
11. Let the bread heat for 3 to 5 minutes.
12. Remove the bread from the oven and arrange them on a serving platter.
13. Spread the vegetable mixture over the 12 bread slices, distribute the topping as evenly as you can.
14. Serve immediately.
