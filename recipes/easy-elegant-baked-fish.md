## Ingredients

- 2 pounds swai fish
- ¼ cup butter, melted
- ¼ teaspoon garlic powder, or to taste
- ¼ teaspoon garlic pepper seasoning, or to taste
- salt to taste
- 1  lemon, thinly sliced
- 1 tablespoon capers

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Place fish in a baking dish and drizzle melted butter over fish; season with garlic powder, garlic pepper seasoning, and salt.
3. Top fish with lemon slices and capers.
4. Bake in the preheated oven until fish flakes easily with a fork, 15 to 20 minutes.
