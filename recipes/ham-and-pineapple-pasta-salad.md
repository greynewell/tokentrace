## Ingredients

- 1 (12 ounce) package medium shell pasta
- 3 tablespoons salted butter
- 1 cup cubed cooked ham
- ¼ cup packed brown sugar
- ½ cup mayonnaise
- ½ cup sour cream
- 1 (8 ounce) can pineapple chunks, drained
- ½ cup red grapes
- ½ cup cubed Cheddar cheese
- 2 tablespoons pineapple juice, or more to taste

## Instructions

1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add shells and cook, stirring occasionally, until tender yet firm to the bite, about 9 minutes. Drain and let cool.
2. Heat butter in a medium skillet over medium heat and add ham and brown sugar. Cook for 2 to 3 minutes; do not overcook. Set aside and let cool.
3. Mix mayonnaise and sour cream together in a small bowl. Combine pineapple, grapes, Cheddar cheese, and pineapple juice in a large bowl; mix in mayonnaise mixture, ham mixture, and pasta. Mix well before every serving.
