## Ingredients

- ¾ cup Kikkoman Teriyaki Marinade & Sauce
- 2 tablespoons brown sugar, packed
- 1 teaspoon grated fresh ginger root
- 4 steaks salmon steaks*

## Instructions

1. Combine Kikkoman teriyaki marinade, brown sugar, and grated ginger root in a bowl. Pour mixture over salmon steaks in a large resealable bag; seal well.
2. Marinate in the refrigerator for 30 minutes, turning the bag over once. Cook salmon as desired.
