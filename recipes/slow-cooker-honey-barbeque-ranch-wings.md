## Ingredients

- 1 ½ cups barbeque sauce (such as Sweet Baby Ray's®)
- 1 cup honey
- 3 (1 ounce) packages ranch dressing mix (such as Hidden Valley Ranch®)
- 8  chicken wings

## Instructions

1. Mix barbeque sauce, honey, and ranch dressing together in a large bowl.
2. Dip wings in the sauce until coated.
3. Transfer wings to a 6-quart slow cooker; pour any remaining sauce on top.
4. Cook on High until no longer pink at the bone and the juices run clear, about 4 hours.
5. An instant-read thermometer inserted near the bone should read 165 degrees F (74 degrees C).
