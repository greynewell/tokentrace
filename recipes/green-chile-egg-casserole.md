## Ingredients

- 1 pound shredded Mexican cheese blend
- 8  eggs
- 1 (16 ounce) package cottage cheese
- 1 cup baking mix (such as Bisquick®)
- 1 cup milk
- ½ cup margarine, melted
- 1 (4 ounce) can chopped green chilies

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 9x13-inch baking dish.
2. Mix Mexican cheese blend, eggs, cottage cheese, baking mix, milk, margarine, and green chiles together in a bowl; transfer to the prepared baking dish.
3. Bake in the preheated oven until cooked through and bubbling, about 40 minutes.
