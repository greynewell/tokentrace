## Ingredients

- 1 ¼ cups walnuts
- 1 ½ cups whole wheat flour
- ½ cup all-purpose flour
- 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
- ¾ teaspoon baking soda
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ¼ teaspoon ground nutmeg
- 1 ½ cups mashed overripe bananas, or more to taste
- ½ cup raw honey
- 2  eggs, lightly beaten
- ¼ cup plain yogurt
- ¼ cup coconut oil, melted and cooled
- 2 tablespoons butter, melted and cooled
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Spread walnuts onto a baking sheet.
3. Toast in preheated oven until nuts start to turn golden brown and become fragrant, 10 to 15 minutes.
4. Coarsely chop walnuts.
5. Place walnuts in a large bowl.
6. Add whole wheat flour, all-purpose flour, cinnamon, baking soda, salt, and nutmeg; mix until combined.
7. Mix mashed bananas, honey, eggs, yogurt, coconut oil, butter, and vanilla extract together in another large bowl.
8. Add banana mixture to flour mixture, mixing with a wooden spoon or rubber spatula until batter is just blended.
9. Grease a 9x5-inch loaf pan.
10. Pour batter into prepared pan.
11. Bake in the preheated oven until edges and top are golden brown and a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean, 50 to 60 minutes.
