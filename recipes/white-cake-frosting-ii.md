## Ingredients

- ½ cup shortening
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- ½ teaspoon vanilla extract
- 4 tablespoons warm water
- 1 (16 ounce) package confectioners' sugar

## Instructions

1. In a large bowl, combine shortening, salt, vanilla, water and confectioners' sugar.
2. Beat until light and fluffy.
