## Ingredients

- 2 fluid ounces rye whiskey
- ½ fluid ounce sweet vermouth
- 1 dash Angostura bitters
- 1 cup ice cubes
- 1  maraschino cherry

## Instructions

1. Combine whiskey, vermouth, and bitters in a cocktail mixing glass. Add ice and stir until chilled.
2. Strain into a chilled cocktail glass and garnish with a maraschino cherry.
