## Ingredients

- ⅔ cup milk
- ½ cup peanut butter
- ½ cup oats
- ¼ cup chocolate chips, or to taste
- 2 scoops protein powder
- ½ teaspoon ground cinnamon

## Instructions

1. Combine milk, peanut butter, oats, chocolate chips, protein powder, and cinnamon in a medium mixing bowl and mix.
2. Scoop and shape into balls using a spoon. Place into a freezer bag and store in the freezer.
