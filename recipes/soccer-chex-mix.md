## Ingredients

- 6 tablespoons butter, melted
- 2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
- 1 ½ teaspoons seasoned salt
- ¾ teaspoon garlic powder
- ½ teaspoon onion powder
- 3 cups crispy rice cereal squares (such as Rice Chex ®)
- 3 cups crispy corn cereal squares (such as Corn Chex®)
- 1 ½ cups crispy wheat cereal squares (such as Wheat Chex®)
- 2 cups bite-sized pretzels
- 1 ½ cups Parmesan-flavored goldfish crackers
- 1 cup bite-sized garlic-flavored bagel chips
- 1 cup peanuts

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 250 degrees F (120 degrees C).
2. Mix butter, Worcestershire sauce, seasoned salt, garlic powder, and onion powder in a bowl.
3. Combine rice cereal squares, corn cereal squares, wheat cereal squares, pretzels, goldfish crackers, bagel chips, and peanuts in a bowl.
4. Mix about 1/4 the cereal mixture with 1/4 the butter mixture in a separate bowl until coated; continue adding more cereal mixture and more butter mixture until cereal mixture is fully coated in butter mixture. Pour onto a baking sheet.
5. Bake in the preheated oven, stirring every 15 minutes, until butter mixture is baked into cereal mixture, about 1 hour.
