## Ingredients

- 2  eggs
- ¼ cup mayonnaise, plus
- 2 tablespoons mayonnaise
- 2 tablespoons prepared yellow mustard
- 2 tablespoons seafood seasoning (such as Old Bay®)
- 1 teaspoon chili powder
- 2  sweet potatoes, peeled and shredded
- 7 ounces imitation crabmeat, flaked
- 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
- ¼ cup lemon zest
- salt and black pepper to taste
- ¼ cup vegetable oil
- 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley for garnish

## Instructions

1. Beat the eggs in a mixing bowl; whisk in the mayonnaise, mustard, seafood seasoning, and chili powder.
2. Add the sweet potatoes, crabmeat, mozzarella cheese, and lemon zest.
3. Season to taste with salt and pepper; mix until evenly combined.
4. Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
5. Scoop heaping spoonfuls of the sweet potato mixture into the heated pan.
6. Flatten into 3-inch circles and cook until golden brown, about 3 minutes per side.
7. Repeat with remaining sweet potato mixture.
8. Sprinkle with parsley to garnish.
