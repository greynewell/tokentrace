## Ingredients

- 1 cup lukewarm milk
- 2 (.25 ounce) packages active dry yeast
- 2 ½ cups all-purpose flour
- 6  eggs
- 1 ½ cups grated Romano cheese
- ¼ cup butter, melted

## Instructions

1. In a small bowl, stir together milk and yeast; set aside.
2. In a separate bowl, combine flour, eggs, yeast mixture, cheese and butter; mix well.
3. Spoon batter into two lightly greased 9x5 inch loaf pans.
4. Let rise until doubled, about 60 minutes.
5. Meanwhile, preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
6. Bake in preheated oven for 35 to 45 minutes, until golden brown and bottom of loaf sounds hollow when tapped.
