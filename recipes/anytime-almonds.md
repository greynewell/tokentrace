## Ingredients

- 2 cups dry roasted almonds, no salt added
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 2 cloves garlic, finely chopped
- 1 sprig fresh rosemary, chopped
- 1 ½ teaspoons kosher salt (do not use regular table salt)

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. In a bowl, mix together almonds, olive oil, garlic, and rosemary until nuts are completely coated.
3. Season with salt.
4. Spread evenly on a baking sheet.
5. Bake in a preheated oven for 10 minutes.
