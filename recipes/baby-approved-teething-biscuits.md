## Ingredients

- 2  bananas
- ¾ cup white sugar
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 1 cup whole wheat flour
- 1 cup all-purpose flour  (Optional)
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- ½ cup all-purpose flour, or as needed  (Optional)
- cooking spray

## Instructions

1. Mash bananas in a bowl with sugar and vanilla extract.
2. Add whole wheat flour, 1 cup all-purpose flour, and baking soda.
3. Mix until dough stiffens, adding additional all-purpose flour as needed.
4. Roll into 1 1/2-inch thick strands of dough.
5. Cover with a dish towel and let sit out, 8 hours to overnight.
6. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
7. Grease 2 cookie sheets with cooking spray.
8. Cut cookies into 1- to 2-inch pieces and place on the prepared cookie sheets.
9. Bake in the preheated oven until hard and golden, 12 to 15 minutes.
