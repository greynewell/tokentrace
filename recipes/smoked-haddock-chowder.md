## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 1  onion, diced
- 1 large carrot, peeled and diced
- 1 ½ teaspoons chopped fresh thyme
- 3 ¼ cups fish stock
- 14 ounces smoked haddock
- 2 large potatoes, quartered
- ⅓ cup light cream
- salt and freshly ground pepper to taste
- 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley

## Instructions

1. Heat olive oil in a large saucepan over medium heat.
2. Add onion and carrot; cook and stir until slightly softened, about 5 minutes.
3. Stir in thyme.
4. Pour in fish stock.
5. Stir smoked haddock into the saucepan; cook for 5 minutes.
6. Transfer to a plate using a slotted spoon.
7. Add potatoes to the saucepan; cook until tender, about 15 minutes.
8. Return the haddock to the saucepan.
9. Pour in cream and cook until heated through, about 5 minutes.
10. Season chowder with salt and pepper.
11. Garnish with parsley.
