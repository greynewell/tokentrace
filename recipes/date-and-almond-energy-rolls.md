## Ingredients

- 1 cup Medjool dates, pitted
- 1 cup hot water  (Optional)
- ½ cup shredded coconut
- 1 cup smoked and salted almonds
- 1 tablespoon coconut oil
- ½ teaspoon pure vanilla extract

## Instructions

1. Line a plate with parchment paper.
2. Put the dates in a small bowl and add enough boiling water to cover them. (Skip this step if dates are soft).
3. Soak for 5 minutes, then drain.
4. Combine dates and coconut in the bowl of a food processor; pulse for 30 seconds or until dates are coarsely chopped.
5. Add almonds, coconut oil, and vanilla extract.
6. Pulse until finely processed, about 15 seconds.
7. Scoop 1 tablespoon of the mixture and place on a sheet of plastic wrap.
8. Protecting your fingertips with the plastic wrap, mold each scoop into a roll that is 3 inches long.
9. Place rolls onto a plate lined with parchment paper and refrigerate for 1 hour.
