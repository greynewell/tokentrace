## Ingredients

- 2 (6 ounce) packages orange flavored Jell-O®
- 4 cups boiling water
- 1 quart orange sherbet
- 2 (11 ounce) cans mandarin oranges
- 3  bananas, sliced

## Instructions

1. Combine the gelatin and boiling water; stir until dissolved.
2. Add sherbet and fruit; mix well.
3. Pour into a 9x13 dish and chill until set.
4. Serve cold.
