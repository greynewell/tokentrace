## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons butter, softened
- 1  baguette, cut into 3/4-inch-thick slices
- 2  avocados, peeled and pitted
- 10  cherry tomatoes, chopped
- ½  onion, minced  (Optional)
- ½ cup shredded mozzarella cheese

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Spread butter onto 1 side of each baguette slice.
3. Mix avocados, tomatoes, and onion in a bowl until smooth and consistent in texture; scoop onto butter-side of baguette slices. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese over avocado mixture layer. Arrange baguette slices on a baking sheet.
4. Bake in the preheated oven until cheese is melted, about 7 minutes.
