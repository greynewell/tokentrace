## Ingredients

- 1 bunch broccolini
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- 1  lemon wedge
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- 1 pinch red pepper flakes  (Optional)

## Instructions

1. Place broccolini in a large skillet with about 2 inches of water; bring to a boil and cook until bright green, 1 to 2 minutes. Drain.
2. Heat olive oil in the same skillet over medium heat. Stir in garlic and cook until golden and fragrant, 1 to 2 minutes. Add broccolini; cook and stir until heated through, 2 to 3 minutes. Squeeze lemon juice over broccoli and season with red pepper flakes, salt, and pepper.
