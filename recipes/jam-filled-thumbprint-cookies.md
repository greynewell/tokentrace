## Ingredients

- 1 cup unsalted butter, room temperature
- ⅔ cup granulated sugar
- 2  large egg yolks
- 1 ½ teaspoons vanilla extract
- 2 cups all-purpose flour
- ¾ teaspoon salt
- ½ cup assorted stone fruit jam such as peach, apricot or plum
- Reynolds® Parchment Paper

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees F.
2. Line a baking sheet with Reynolds® Parchment Paper.
3. Beat together butter and sugar with an electric mixer until light and fluffy, about 3 minutes, stopping once or twice to scrape down the sides of bowl with a rubber spatula.
4. Beat in egg yolks and vanilla extract.
5. Reduce mixer speed and add flour and salt and mix until just incorporated.
6. Form dough into 1-inch balls and arrange on prepared baking sheet.
7. Using your thumb, flatten balls slightly to make an indentation in the center of each cookie.
8. Bake cookies for 8-10 minutes or until bottoms are just golden.
9. Remove baking sheet from oven.
10. Fill each indentation with a heaping 1/2 teaspoon of jam.
11. Bake for an additional 5 to 6 minutes, or until the edges of cookies are lightly golden.
12. Transfer cookies to wire racks to cool.
