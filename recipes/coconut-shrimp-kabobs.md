## Ingredients

- 1  jalapeno pepper, seeded and coarsely chopped, or more to taste
- 1  lime, zested and juiced
- 2 cloves garlic, chopped
- ½ cup flaked coconut
- ¼ cup olive oil
- 1 (16 ounce) package Market Pantry™ Frozen Raw Jumbo Shrimp, thawed
- ½  fresh pineapple, cored and cut into 1-inch pieces
- 1  red onion, cut into 1-inch pieces
- 1  green bell pepper, cut into 1-inch pieces
- 12  (12-inch) skewers*

## Instructions

1. Mix first 5 ingredients in food processor or blender.
2. Process on high until pureed (mixture will not be smooth).
3. Transfer to large food storage bag.
4. Add thawed shrimp; seal bag.
5. Massage bag to coat shrimp.
6. Refrigerate 1 to 2 hours to marinate.
7. Heat grill.
8. Thread shrimp and remaining ingredients on skewers.
9. Grill 5 to 7 minutes until shrimp are opaque and cooked through, turning once or twice.
