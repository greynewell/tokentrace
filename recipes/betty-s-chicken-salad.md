## Ingredients

- 10 ounces seashell pasta
- 4 cups cubed cooked chicken
- 3 cups chopped celery
- ⅔ cup blanched slivered almonds
- 2 cups reduced-fat mayonnaise
- ¼ cup lemon juice
- 1 teaspoon salt
- ⅔ teaspoon ground black pepper
- 8  hard-cooked eggs, chopped

## Instructions

1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
2. Cook shell pasta in the boiling water, stirring occasionally until cooked through but firm to the bite, 8 minutes.
3. Rinse in cold water until cool and drain.
4. Combine pasta, chicken, celery, and almonds in a large bowl.
5. Mix mayonnaise, lemon juice, salt, and pepper in another bowl.
6. Pour mayonnaise mixture into pasta mixture; stir well.
7. Fold eggs into pasta.
8. Cover and refrigerate for 6 hours.
