## Ingredients

- 2 slices white bread, toasted
- 1 tablespoon butter, softened
- 2 tablespoons whipped cream cheese
- 1  hard-boiled egg, sliced
- 5 dashes hot pepper sauce (such as Frank's RedHot®), or to taste
- 1 pinch salt and ground black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Butter each slice of toast. Spread 1 tablespoon cream cheese onto each slice.
2. Layer hard-boiled egg slices evenly onto 1 slice of toast. Add hot sauce, salt, and pepper. Top with other slice of toast.
