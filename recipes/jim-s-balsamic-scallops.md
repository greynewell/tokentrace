## Ingredients

- 2 pounds sea scallops
- ½ cup all-purpose flour
- 4 teaspoons ground black pepper
- 4 teaspoons garlic powder
- 2 teaspoons paprika
- 2 teaspoons dried marjoram
- 2 teaspoons salt
- 1 teaspoon cayenne pepper
- ¼ cup olive oil
- ½ cup balsamic vinegar

## Instructions

1. Pat scallops dry with paper towels and set aside.
2. Place flour, black pepper, garlic powder, paprika, marjoram, salt, and cayenne pepper in a large plastic resealable bag and shake well to mix.
3. Add scallops to the bag and toss to coat with flour mixture.
4. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
5. Add all scallops in a single layer and sear until golden, 3 to 4 minutes per side.
6. Drizzle balsamic vinegar over seared scallops.
