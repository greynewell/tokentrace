## Ingredients

- ½ cup butter, softened
- ⅓ cup chocolate-hazelnut spread (such as Nutella®), at room temperature
- ⅓ cup cocoa powder, sifted
- 2 cups powdered sugar, sifted
- 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
- 1 ½ tablespoons milk, or more as needed

## Instructions

1. Beat butter with an electric mixer until fluffy. Add chocolate-hazelnut spread and blend lightly. Mix in cocoa powder until well combined.
2. Add powdered sugar, 1 cup at a time, mixing well after each addition. Blend in vanilla. Add milk; mix to desired consistency.
