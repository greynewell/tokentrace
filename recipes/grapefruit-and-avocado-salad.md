## Ingredients

- 4 large red grapefruit, peeled and sectioned
- 4  avocados - peeled, pitted, and sliced
- ⅓ cup orange juice
- ⅓ cup olive oil
- 3 tablespoons lime juice
- 2 tablespoons honey
- salt and cracked black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Arrange the grapefruit sections and avocado slices attractively in a shallow serving dish.
2. In a bowl, whisk together the orange juice, olive oil, lime juice, and honey until well combined; drizzle the dressing over the grapefruit and avocado slices.
3. Season to taste with salt and black pepper.
4. Serve immediately.
