## Ingredients

- 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ½ teaspoon baking powder
- ½ teaspoon baking soda
- 1 cup white sugar
- 2 tablespoons butter, softened
- 3  eggs, at room temperature
- ⅓ cup lemon juice
- 2 teaspoons lemon zest
- 1 teaspoon lemon extract
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- ½ cup vegetable oil
- 1 cup powdered sugar
- 1 tablespoon powdered sugar
- 2 tablespoons milk
- ½ teaspoon lemon zest
- ½ teaspoon lemon extract

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C). Grease and flour a 9x5-inch loaf pan.
2. Whisk flour, salt, baking powder, and baking soda in a bowl. Cream sugar and butter in a separate bowl with an electric mixer. Add eggs, lemon juice, lemon zest, lemon extract, and vanilla; beat until incorporated.
3. Pour egg mixture into flour mixture; whisk until smooth. Add oil and mix well. Pour into the prepared loaf pan.
4. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean, about 45 minutes. Cool on a wire rack.
5. While the cake is cooling, mix powdered sugar, milk, lemon zest, and lemon extract in a bowl until smooth. Pour over cooled cake; slice when icing sets.
