## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
- ¼ cup chopped red bell pepper
- ¼ cup chopped celery
- 3 cups chicken stock
- 1 cup chopped cauliflower
- 1 (8 ounce) can baby clams
- 6  medium shrimp
- 6 slices bacon, cooked and crumbled
- 1 teaspoon seafood chowder spice (such as St. Jean's®)
- 8 spears asparagus, chopped
- ½ cup light cream (table cream)
- salt and ground black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Melt butter in a saucepan over medium heat and add bell pepper and celery.
2. Cook and stir until softened, about 5 minutes.
3. Add chicken stock and bring to a boil.
4. Reduce heat to a simmer and add cauliflower, clams, shrimp, bacon, and seafood spice.
5. Simmer gently for 15 minutes.
6. Add asparagus and simmer until just tender, about 5 minutes more.
7. Divide chowder between 2 soup bowls and add cream to each.
8. Season with salt and pepper.
