## Ingredients

- 1 (16 ounce) can cannellini beans, drained and rinsed
- 1 (7 ounce) jar roasted red bell peppers, drained and rinsed
- 1 small avocado, peeled and pitted
- ¼ cup chopped fresh basil
- 1 ½ tablespoons minced garlic
- 1 teaspoon lemon juice
- 1 teaspoon balsamic vinegar
- ½ teaspoon smoked paprika
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ½ teaspoon ground black pepper
- ¼ teaspoon curry powder
- 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil

## Instructions

1. Put cannellini beans, roasted red bell peppers, avocado, basil, garlic, lemon juice, balsamic vinegar, paprika, salt, pepper, and curry powder in a food processor bowl; process until smooth.
2. With the processor running, slowly stream olive oil into the mixture until smooth.
