## Ingredients

- cooking spray
- ½  onion, chopped
- 1 (16 ounce) package kielbasa sausage, sliced
- ½ head broccoli, cut into florets
- 3  potatoes, peeled and sliced
- salt and ground black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Spray a large skillet with cooking spray and heat over medium-low heat. Add onion; cook and stir until onion has softened and turned translucent, about 5 minutes. Add kielbasa; fry until sausage is lightly browned, stirring occasionally, about 5 minutes more.
2. Stir in potatoes and broccoli; season with salt and pepper. Cook, without stirring, until broccoli begins to soften, about 15 minutes.
3. Stir well and continue to cook until vegetables are completely tender, 10 to 15 minutes more.
