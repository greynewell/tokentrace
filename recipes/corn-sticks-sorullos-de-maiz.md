## Ingredients

- 1 ½ cups cornmeal
- ½ cup white sugar
- 1 ¼ teaspoons salt
- 1 cup boiling water
- 4 slices American cheese, cut in half
- 1 quart vegetable oil for frying

## Instructions

1. Stir the cornmeal, sugar, salt, and water together in a mixing bowl until a dough has formed.
2. Set aside to cool for 10 minutes.
3. Once cool enough to handle, place a spoonful of dough into the palm of your hand; make a depression in the center.
4. Fold a half slice of American cheese into a wad and place into the hole.
5. Spoon some more dough overtop; roll the corn stick into a sausage shape, making sure that the cheese is not exposed.
6. Repeat with the remaining dough and cheese.
7. Heat oil in a large skillet to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
8. Cook the corn sticks in the hot oil until they turn golden brown and float to the top of the oil, about 4 minutes per side.
9. Drain on a paper towel-lined plate before serving.
