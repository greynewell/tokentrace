## Ingredients

- ¾ cup water
- ¼ cup vegetable oil
- ¼ cup sour cream
- 1  egg
- 1  egg yolk
- 2 ¼ teaspoons active dry yeast
- 2 ⅔ cups bread flour
- ¾ cup buckwheat flour
- 1 tablespoon white sugar
- ¾ teaspoon salt

## Instructions

1. Combine water, vegetable oil, sour cream, egg, and egg yolk in the bread machine.
2. Combine yeast, bread flour, buckwheat flour, sugar, and salt in a bowl. Add to wet ingredients in bread machine all at once.
3. Run the Bread cycle. Remove bread from the machine after the cycle is done and cool bread on a wire rack.
