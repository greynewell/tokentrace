## Ingredients

- 3 tablespoons butter
- 2 teaspoons garlic powder
- 2 teaspoons chopped fresh rosemary
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ½ teaspoon ground black pepper
- 6  boneless, skinless chicken thighs
- 2 tablespoons honey
- 2 tablespoons lemon juice

## Instructions

1. Melt butter in a large skillet over medium heat.
2. Add garlic powder, rosemary, salt, and pepper; cook until fragrant, about 1 minute.
3. Add chicken thighs to skillet and turn to coat.
4. Cook until browned on 1 side, about 5 minutes.
5. Reduce heat to low and flip chicken.
6. Cover and cook for 15 minutes, or until an instant-read thermometer inserted into thickest parts registers 170 degrees F (86 degrees C).
7. Remove chicken from skillet and skim fat off pan juices.
8. Return chicken to skillet.
9. Stir honey and lemon juice together in a small bowl; drizzle over chicken.
10. Continue cooking until heated through, 3 to 5 minutes.
