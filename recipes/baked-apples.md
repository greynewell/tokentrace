## Ingredients

- 4  tart green apples
- ½ cup brown sugar
- 4 tablespoons butter
- 2 teaspoons ground cinnamon

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Remove core from the top of each apple, leaving a well.
2. Stuff each apple with 2 tablespoons brown sugar and 1 tablespoon butter. Place in a shallow baking dish and sprinkle with cinnamon.
3. Bake in the preheated oven until sugar begins to caramelize and apples are tender, 15 minutes.
