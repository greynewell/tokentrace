## Ingredients

- 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken thighs, or more to taste
- 2 cups dry white wine (such as Pinot Grigio)
- 2 cups chicken broth
- ¾ cup plain yogurt
- 1 teaspoon mayonnaise
- ½ cup chopped scallions
- 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
- 1 teaspoon dried tarragon
- ½ teaspoon sea salt
- ¼ teaspoon onion powder
- ¼ teaspoon dried kelp flakes  (Optional)
- 2 pinches ground white pepper

## Instructions

1. Place chicken in a large pot.
2. Pour white wine and chicken broth over chicken; bring to a boil.
3. Cook chicken until no longer pink in the center, about 45 minutes.
4. Transfer chicken to a work surface using a slotted spoon.
5. Shred chicken.
6. Mix chicken, yogurt, and mayonnaise together in a bowl.
7. Add scallions, mustard, tarragon, sea salt, onion powder, kelp, and white pepper; mix well.
8. Cover bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate, 1 hour to overnight.
