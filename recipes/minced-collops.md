## Ingredients

- 1 pound lean ground beef
- ½  onion, minced
- 1 teaspoon salt, to taste
- ½ teaspoon ground black pepper
- ⅔ cup steel-cut oats
- 1 ½ cups beef stock
- 2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce

## Instructions

1. Crumble the ground beef into a skillet over medium heat; add onion, salt, and pepper.
2. Cook and stir the ground beef until no longer pink, 7 to 10 minutes.
3. Stir oats into beef mixture to coat in beef drippings.
4. Pour beef stock over beef mixture; bring to a boil, place cover on the skillet, and reduce heat to low.
5. Cook at a simmer until the oats are cooked, about 30 minutes.
6. Stir Worcestershire sauce through the beef mixture to serve.
