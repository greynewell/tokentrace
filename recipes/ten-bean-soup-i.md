## Ingredients

- 1 (16 ounce) package dry mixed beans
- 4 slices bacon
- ½  onion, chopped
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 3 stalks celery, chopped
- salt to taste
- ground black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Soak beans in water overnight.
2. Fry bacon in a medium skillet over medium heat until partially cooked, then add onion, garlic, and celery. Cook until onion is tender, 3 to 4 minutes. Remove from heat.
3. Place drained beans in a large pot. Add water to cover beans. Add bacon mixture to the pot.
4. Cook at a low boil until beans are tender, about 1 1/2 hours. Season with salt and pepper.
