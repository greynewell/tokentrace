## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons butter
- 1 small onion, finely chopped
- 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
- 1 cup plain yogurt
- 2 (6 ounce) cans lump crabmeat, drained
- 6 ounces smoked salmon, chopped
- ¼ teaspoon garlic salt
- 1 pinch ground black pepper
- 1 tablespoon chopped Italian flat leaf parsley

## Instructions

1. Melt butter in small skillet over medium heat.
2. Add onion; cook and stir until onion is translucent, about 5 minutes.
3. Set aside to cool.
4. Stir together cream cheese, yogurt , crab meat, and salmon in a bowl.
5. Stir in cooled onion.
6. Season with garlic salt and black pepper.
7. Pour mixture into a shallow serving bowl and sprinkle with parsley.
