## Ingredients

- 1 pound halibut
- ½ cup white wine
- ⅛ teaspoon ground white pepper
- 2 tablespoons butter
- ½ cup chopped button mushrooms
- ¼ cup minced onion
- ¼ cup minced red bell pepper
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
- 1 cup evaporated milk
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- ⅛ teaspoon ground white pepper
- ½ pound crabmeat, flaked
- ¼ cup shredded Monterey Jack cheese

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Butter 8 small ovenproof ramekins.
3. Cut fish into 8 pieces, and arrange in a shallow 9x13 inch baking dish.
4. Pour wine over fish, and sprinkle with 1/8 teaspoon ground white pepper.
5. Bake in preheated oven for 20 minutes, or until fish flakes easily; keep oven on for final step.
6. Melt 2 tablespoons butter in a large saucepan over medium heat.
7. Saute mushrooms, onion and red bell pepper until tender; remove from heat and set aside.
8. Melt remaining 2 tablespoons butter in the saucepan over low heat.
9. Mix in flour, and cook for 1 minute, stirring constantly until smooth.
10. Gradually add milk and increase heat to medium; stir constantly until mixture is thick and bubbly.
11. Stir in mushroom mixture, salt and remaining 1/8 teaspoon ground white pepper to saucepan, stirring until well combined.
12. Remove sauce from heat and set aside.
13. Remove fish from wine carefully with a slotted spoon, and place 1 piece into each ramekin.
14. Sprinkle crabmeat evenly on tops, followed by 2 tablespoons sauce and 1 1/2 teaspoons cheese in each dish.
15. Bake in preheated oven for 5 minutes or until cheese is hot and bubbly; serve immediately.
