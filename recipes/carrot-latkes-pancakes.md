## Ingredients

- oil for frying, or as needed
- 2 cups grated carrots
- 6  green onions, chopped
- ½ cup all-purpose flour
- ½ teaspoon baking powder
- 3  eggs
- ¾ teaspoon salt
- 1 pinch freshly ground black pepper

## Instructions

1. Pour about 1/2-inch depth of oil in a large saucepan or electric skillet and heat to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Mix carrots, green onion, flour, and baking powder together in a bowl until carrots are evenly coated.
3. Beat eggs, salt, and pepper together in a separate bowl; pour over carrot mixture.
4. Stir mixture until combined and moistened.
5. Spoon small mounds of carrot mixture, working in batches, into the hot oil; cook until browned, 2 to 3 minutes per side.
6. Transfer latkes using a slotted spoon to a paper towel-lined plate.
