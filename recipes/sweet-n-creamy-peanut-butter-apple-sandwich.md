## Ingredients

- 1  Granny Smith apple, thinly sliced
- 2 tablespoons peanut butter
- 2 tablespoons low-fat ricotta cheese
- ¼ teaspoon vanilla extract
- ¼ teaspoon ground cinnamon
- ¼ teaspoon white sugar
- 4 slices sandwich bread

## Instructions

1. Stir peanut butter and ricotta cheese together in a bowl until smooth. Add vanilla extract, cinnamon, and white sugar to peanut butter mixture; stir until completely blended.
2. Spread peanut butter mixture on one side of two of the pieces of bread; top peanut butter mixture with apples. Place remaining slices of bread over the apples.
