## Ingredients

- 4  skinless, boneless chicken breast halves - cut into bite size pieces
- 2 cups dry white wine, divided
- 1 teaspoon garlic powder
- 1 teaspoon onion powder
- 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
- 1  onion
- 1  green bell pepper, diced
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 2 tablespoons teriyaki sauce

## Instructions

1. To Marinate: Place chicken in a nonporous glass dish or bowl.
2. Pour 1 1/2 cups of wine over chicken, then sprinkle with garlic powder and onion powder.
3. Cover and refrigerate to marinate for at least 1 hour.
4. Heat vegetable oil in a small skillet over medium heat.
5. Saute onion and bell pepper for 5 to 7 minutes, or until soft.
6. Remove from skillet and set aside.
7. Remove chicken from marinade, discarding any remaining marinade.
8. Heat olive oil in a medium skillet over medium high heat and brown chicken, adding teriyaki sauce to 'sear'.
9. Braise with remaining white wine and simmer for 8 to 10 minutes, or until chicken is cooked through and no longer pink inside.
10. Add onion/bell pepper mixture to chicken and heat through, stirring.
11. Serve hot.
