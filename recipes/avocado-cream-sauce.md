## Ingredients

- 2  avocados
- 2 cloves garlic
- ½  lime, juiced, or to taste
- 2 tablespoons chopped green onions
- 1 tablespoon chopped fresh cilantro
- ½ tablespoon chopped fresh tarragon
- 2 tablespoons butter
- ¼ cup heavy cream
- salt to taste

## Instructions

1. Combine avocados, garlic, lime juice, green onions, cilantro, and tarragon in a food processor; puree until smooth.
2. Melt butter in a saucepan over low heat. Stir in avocado puree and cook over low heat, stirring occasionally, until heated through, about 5 minutes. Add cream and cook until heated through, but not boiling. Season with salt.
