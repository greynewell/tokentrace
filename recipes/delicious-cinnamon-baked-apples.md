## Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon butter
- 2 tablespoons brown sugar
- 3 teaspoons vanilla sugar
- 3 teaspoons ground cinnamon
- 1 teaspoon ground nutmeg
- 6 large apples - peeled, cored, and sliced
- 3 ½ tablespoons water

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a large baking dish with butter.
2. Mix brown sugar, vanilla sugar, cinnamon, and nutmeg in a small bowl. Layer about 1/3 of the apples in the prepared baking dish; sprinkle with 1/3 of the sugar mixture. Repeat layers twice more.
3. Bake in preheated oven for 30 minutes. Pour water over apples and continue baking until tender, about 15 minutes more.
