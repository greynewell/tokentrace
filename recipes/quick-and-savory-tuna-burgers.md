## Ingredients

- 2 (5 ounce) cans tuna, drained
- 2  eggs
- 7 tablespoons bread crumbs
- 7 tablespoons chopped onion
- 7 tablespoons chopped celery
- 1 tablespoon prepared horseradish
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
- 2 tablespoons vegetable oil, or as needed

## Instructions

1. Mix tuna, eggs, bread crumbs, onion, celery, horseradish, lemon juice, garlic, and black pepper in a bowl until mixture holds together.
2. Divide mixture into 4 portions and form each portion into a patty.
3. Heat vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium heat and cook patties in the hot oil until golden brown, about 5 minutes per side.
