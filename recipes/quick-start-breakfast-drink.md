## Ingredients

- 2 cups pineapple juice
- 2  bananas
- 2 cups vanilla yogurt
- 1 cup strawberries, hulled
- ¼ cup wheat germ
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

## Instructions

1. In a blender combine pineapple juice, bananas yogurt, strawberries, wheat germ and vanilla extract.
2. Blend until smooth.
