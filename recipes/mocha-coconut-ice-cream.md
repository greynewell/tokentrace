## Ingredients

- 10 ounces dark chocolate, broken into pieces
- 2 (14 ounce) cans coconut milk
- 3 tablespoons strong brewed coffee
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 1 pinch salt

## Instructions

1. Place chocolate into a small heatproof bowl; place bowl over hot water.
2. Stir chocolate occasionally until melted and smooth, about 5 minutes.
3. Whisk coconut milk, coffee, vanilla extract, and salt together in a bowl; whisk in melted chocolate until smooth.
4. Place coconut milk mixture in refrigerator until cool, about 30 minutes.
5. Freeze in ice cream maker according to manufacturer's instructions.
