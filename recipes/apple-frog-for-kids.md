## Ingredients

- ¼  Granny Smith apple, cored
- 3  green grapes, halved
- 1 teaspoon cream cheese, softened
- 2  raisins

## Instructions

1. Lay the apple sideways on a plate; cut out a thin horizontal sliver for the mouth.
2. Arrange 1 grape half on each side of the apple as arms.
3. Cut out 2 little triangles from the other 4 grapes so they look like toes and fingers and arrange them as hands and feet.
4. Shape cream cheese into 2 little balls and stick a grape into each as a pupil.
5. Stick creme cheese eyes on top of the quartered apple.
