## Ingredients

- 1  cucumber, peeled and diced
- ½  green bell pepper, minced
- ½  Spanish onion, minced
- 1 cup Italian-style salad dressing (such as Wishbone®)
- 2 tablespoons minced fresh basil
- 1 (14 ounce) can diced tomatoes
- 1 cup tomato juice

## Instructions

1. Combine cucumber, green bell pepper, and onion in a large bowl; stir in Italian dressing and basil.
2. Add tomatoes and tomato juice and stir well.
3. Refrigerate until chilled, at least 30 minutes.
