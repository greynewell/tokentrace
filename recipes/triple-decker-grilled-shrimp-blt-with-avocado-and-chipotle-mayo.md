## Ingredients

- 1 cup mayonnaise
- 1  chipotle pepper in adobo sauce
- ½  lime, juiced
- 1 pinch salt
- 1 pinch ground black pepper
- 4 slices bacon
- 8  extra-large shrimp - peeled, deveined, and tails removed
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- 1  avocado, peeled, pitted and sliced
- 2  leaves romaine lettuce
- 4 slices ripe red tomato
- 6 slices  sourdough bread, toasted

## Instructions

1. Combine mayonnaise, chipotle pepper, lime juice, and a pinch of salt and pepper in a bowl.
2. Puree until smooth with a stick blender.
3. Alternately, you can use a food processor to puree the ingredients.
4. Cover and refrigerate until ready to assemble sandwiches.
5. Place the bacon in a large, deep skillet, and cook over medium-high heat, turning occasionally, until evenly browned, about 10 minutes.
6. Drain the bacon slices on a paper towel-lined plate.
7. Preheat an outdoor grill for medium-high heat, and lightly oil the grate.
8. Toss the shrimp in a bowl with olive oil and salt and pepper to taste.
9. Cook shrimp on the preheated grill until they are bright pink on the outside and the meat is no longer transparent in the center, about 3 minutes on each side.
10. To assemble sandwiches: Spread prepared mayonnaise dressing generously on 1 slice of bread.
11. Arrange half of the shrimp and avocado slices on top.
12. Place another slice of bread over the avocado, and spread another layer of the dressing.
13. Top with a lettuce leaf and 2 slices of tomato followed by a third slice of bread.
14. Repeat with the remaining ingredients for the second sandwich.
