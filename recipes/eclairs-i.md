## Ingredients

- 1 cup water
- ½ cup butter
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- 1 cup all-purpose flour
- 4  eggs
- 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
- 2 cups milk
- 1 (5 ounce) package instant vanilla pudding mix
- 1 (1 ounce) square unsweetened chocolate, chopped
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 cup confectioners' sugar
- 2 tablespoons boiling water

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
2. For icing:  In small saucepan, melt chocolate and 1 tablespoon butter over low heat.
3. Remove from heat and, with electric mixer, beat in confectioners' sugar and 2 tablespoons hot water.
4. Beat until smooth.
5. Drizzle over filled eclairs.
6. For shells: In medium saucepan bring 1 cup water to a boil, stir in 1/2 cup butter and salt.
7. Stir until melted.
8. Add flour all at once and stir vigorously until mixture forms a ball.
9. Remove from heat and beat in eggs, one at a time, until smooth.
10. Drop by tablespoons onto baking sheet.
11. Bake 15 minutes in the preheated oven, then reduce heat to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C) and bake until golden, 10 to 15 minutes.
12. Cool completely.
13. For filling:  In large bowl, beat 1/2 cup of milk, a little at a time, into softened cream cheese, until creamy.
14. Beat in remaining 1 1/2 cups milk and pudding mix.
15. Stir until thick, 1 to 2 minutes.
16. Cut tops off cooled pastry shells and fill.
17. Replace tops.
