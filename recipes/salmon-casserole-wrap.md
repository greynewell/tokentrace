## Ingredients

- 1 (15 ounce) can sliced potatoes
- 1 (8 ounce) can cream of broccoli soup
- 1 (7 ounce) can salmon
- 2  flour tortillas, or more to taste

## Instructions

1. Mix potatoes, cream of broccoli soup, and salmon together in a saucepan; cook and stir over medium heat until heated through, about 5 minutes.
2. Spoon filling in a line across the middle of a tortilla.
3. Fold opposing edges of the tortilla to overlap the filling.
4. Roll 1 of the opposing edges around the filling into a burrito.
5. Repeat with remaining filling and tortilla.
