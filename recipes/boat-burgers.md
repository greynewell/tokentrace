## Ingredients

- 1 pound ground beef
- 1  egg
- 1 cup instant oatmeal
- ½ cup ketchup
- 2 tablespoons prepared mustard
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
- 6  hot dog buns
- 6 slices American cheese

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Mix ground beef and egg together in a large bowl; add oatmeal, ketchup, mustard, salt, and pepper.
3. Mix well.
4. Arrange hot dog buns on a baking sheet.
5. Spoon ground beef mixture into each bun.
6. Bake in the preheated oven for 10 minutes.
7. Add an American cheese slice to each 'boat burger'; continue baking until ground beef is fully cooked and buns are crispy, 10 to 12 more minutes.
8. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 160 degrees F (70 degrees C).
