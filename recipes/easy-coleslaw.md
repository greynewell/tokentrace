## Ingredients

- 1 large head cabbage, shredded
- 1  green bell pepper, diced
- 1  onion, finely diced
- 1 ½ large carrots, shredded
- 1 cup white wine vinegar
- 1 cup vegetable oil
- 1 cup white sugar

## Instructions

1. Combine cabbage, bell pepper, onion, and carrots in a large bowl.
2. Whisk together vinegar, oil, and sugar in a medium bowl. Pour enough dressing over cabbage mixture to coat; mix well. Cover and refrigerate for 6 to 8 hours.
3. Drain any excess liquid, toss, and serve cold.
