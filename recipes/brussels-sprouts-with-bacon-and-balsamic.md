## Ingredients

- 2 pounds Brussels sprouts, trimmed and halved lengthwise
- 6 slices bacon, or more to taste, thinly sliced
- 1 pinch salt and ground black pepper to taste
- ⅔ cup reduced balsamic vinegar, divided

## Instructions

1. Combine Brussels sprouts and bacon in a cast iron skillet; season with salt and pepper. Cook, stirring occasionally, until Brussels sprouts start to brown, 15 to 20 minutes. Drizzle 1/2 cup of balsamic vinegar on top.
2. Transfer Brussels sprouts and bacon to a serving platter; drizzle remaining balsamic vinegar on top.
