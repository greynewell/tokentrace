## Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon salt
- 2  cucumbers, peeled, halved lengthwise, seeded, and chopped
- 1 cup whipping cream
- 2 tablespoons prepared mustard
- 2 teaspoons chopped fresh tarragon
- 2 cups white wine
- salt to taste
- 1  bay leaf
- 2 (3 ounce) fresh tilapia fillets

## Instructions

1. Sprinkle salt over the cucumber pieces; allow to sit 1 hour.
2. Strain liquid from cucumbers.
3. Combine the whipping cream, mustard, and tarragon in a saucepan over medium heat; add the cucumbers and simmer 8 minutes.
4. Pour the wine into a large skillet over medium heat.
5. Season with salt and add the Bay leaf.
6. Bring the wine to a boil.
7. Lay the tilapia into the skillet, reduce heat to medium-low and cook until the tilapia flakes easily with a fork, 8 to 10 minutes.
8. Serve fish with cucumbers on the side.
