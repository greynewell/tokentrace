## Ingredients

- 1 pound frozen calamari rings
- 2 cups 2% milk, or as needed
- ½ cup corn flour
- ½ cup gluten-free all purpose baking flour
- oil for frying

## Instructions

1. Separate calamari rings and place them in a lidded plastic container with enough milk to cover them.
2. Seal the container and allow to soak at room temperature for at least 1 hour.
3. Heat the oil in a deep fryer.
4. Combine corn flour and gluten-free all-purpose flour in a shallow pan.
5. Transfer about 6 calamari rings from the milk directly into the flour mixture to coat.
6. Place coated calamari into the hot oil without crowding, and cook until just starting to brown, 2 to 3 minutes per batch.
7. Drain on paper towels.
8. Repeat with remaining calamari.
