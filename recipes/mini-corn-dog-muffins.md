## Ingredients

- 1 (8.5 ounce) package corn muffin mix
- 2 ounces VELVEETA®, cut into 24 small cubes
- 4  OSCAR MAYER Beef Franks, each cut into 6 pieces
- ½ cup GREY POUPON Savory Honey Mustard

## Instructions

1. Heat oven to 375 degrees F.
2. Prepare muffin batter as directed on package; spoon into 24 mini muffin pan cups sprayed with cooking spray.
3. Press 1 each VELVEETA cube and hot dog slice into batter in center of each muffin cup Bake 10 to 12 min. or until golden brown.
4. Cool 5 min. before removing from pans.
5. Serve warm with mustard.
