## Ingredients

- ⅓ cup balsamic vinegar
- ⅓ cup extra-virgin olive oil
- 2 tablespoons honey
- 1  lime, juiced
- 1 (16 ounce) bag coleslaw mix
- 5 ounces baby kale, chopped into bite-size pieces
- ½  red onion, finely chopped
- 2 tablespoons sunflower seeds, or more to taste
- salt and ground black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Whisk balsamic vinegar, olive oil, honey, and lime juice in a large bowl.
2. Add coleslaw mix, baby kale, red onion, and sunflower seeds; toss to combine.
3. Season with salt and pepper.
