## Ingredients

- 5  potatoes, peeled and sliced
- 1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed cheddar cheese soup
- 1 ¼ cups milk
- ¼ cup butter, melted

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Grease a 9x9-inch baking dish.
3. Layer the potatoes into the prepared baking dish.
4. Mix together the soup, milk, and melted butter in a bowl until thoroughly combined; pour the mixture over the potatoes.
5. Bake uncovered in the preheated oven on a lower rack until the potatoes are tender, about 1 hour.
