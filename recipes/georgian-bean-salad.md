## Ingredients

- 1 ½ cups dry kidney beans
- salt to taste
- ¼ teaspoon cayenne pepper
- ¼ cup shelled walnuts
- 2  garlic cloves
- 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar
- ⅓ cup water
- 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley
- 1 tablespoon chopped fresh cilantro
- ¼ teaspoon cayenne pepper
- 1 small white onion, minced
- 2  cooked chicken breast halves, chopped  (Optional)
- 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley
- 2 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro

## Instructions

1. Place beans in a bowl; cover with plenty of water.
2. Soak for 8 hours or overnight.
3. Drain beans and transfer to a large pot; cover with plenty of water, salt, and 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper.
4. Bring to a boil, reduce heat, and simmer until beans are soft, about 90 minutes.
5. Drain and season with salt.
6. Grind walnuts and garlic to a fine paste in a food processor.
7. Add vinegar, 1/3 cup water, 1 tablespoon parsley, 1 tablespoon cilantro, and 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper.
8. Process until smooth.
9. Mix beans, onion, chicken, and dressing in a bowl.
10. Chill in refrigerator for at least 1 hour before serving.
11. Garnish with 1 tablespoon parsley and 2 tablespoons cilantro.
