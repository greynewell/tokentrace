## Ingredients

- 6  whole sweet onions (such as Vidalia®)
- 6  beef bouillon cubes
- 6  ice cubes  (Optional)
- 6 tablespoons butter, divided
- 6  12x12-inch squares aluminum foil

## Instructions

1. Preheat an outdoor grill for medium heat.
2. Peel brown outer layers from an onion.
3. Using a sharp knife, cut a cone-shaped core from the top of the onion, about 2/3 of the way into the vegetable.
4. The core should be about 1 1/2 inches wide at the top.
5. Insert a bouillon cube into the hollowed-out space, followed by an ice cube (for more moisture, if desired).
6. Top the ice cube with 1 tablespoon of butter.
7. Wrap the filled onion in a sheet of aluminum foil, leaving a small vent at the top.
8. Repeat with the rest of the onions and filling ingredients.
9. Set the foil-wrapped onions on the preheated grill vent-sides up, and grill until onions are tender, 20 to 30 minutes.
