## Ingredients

- 1 (1.5 fluid ounce) jigger citron vodka
- ½ fluid ounce Blue Curacao
- 2 fluid ounces sour mix
- 1 (12 fluid ounce) can or bottle lemon-lime flavored carbonated beverage
- 1  lemon - cut into wedges, for garnish

## Instructions

1. Fill a Collins glass with ice. Pour sour mix, vodka, and blue curacao over ice.
2. Fill to the top with lemon-lime soda. Garnish with lemon wedge; serve with a straw.
