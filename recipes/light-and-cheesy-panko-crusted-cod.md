## Ingredients

- Non-stick cooking spray
- 1 (9.74 ounce) package Idahoan Signature™ Russets Mashed Potatoes
- 6 (6 ounce) cod fillets
- ¼ cup honey mustard
- ½ cup panko bread crumbs
- ⅓ cup grated Parmesan cheese
- 1 teaspoon garlic powder
- 1 teaspoon dried parsley
- salt and pepper to taste
- ½ cup plain yogurt
- 1 tablespoon honey mustard
- 1 tablespoon garlic powder
- 2 tablespoons chopped fresh chives
- 1 tablespoon mayonnaise
- salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
2. Lightly spray a baking pan with cooking spray.
3. Prepare Idahoan Signature™ Russets Mashed Potatoes according to package directions.
4. Keep warm.
5. Mix bread crumbs, Parmesan cheese, garlic powder, salt, pepper, and parsley together in a bowl.
6. Sprinkle each piece of cod with the bread crumb mixture.
7. Lightly brush cod with the 1/4 cup honey mustard.
8. Bake in preheated oven until fish starts to flake and becomes a golden brown, about 15 minutes.
9. Mix yogurt, mayo, honey mustard, garlic powder, chives, salt, and pepper together in a bowl.
10. Spoon over the fish when plating.
11. Serve with a side of Idahoan Signature™ Russets Mashed Potatoes.
