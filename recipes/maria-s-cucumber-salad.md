## Ingredients

- 2  cucumbers, peeled and cut into 1/4-inch thick slices
- 1 teaspoon chili powder
- lemon, juiced
- ½ teaspoon salt

## Instructions

1. Toss the cucumber slices in a bowl with chili powder, lemon juice, and salt until well mixed.
2. Serve immediately.
