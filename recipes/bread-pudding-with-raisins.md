## Ingredients

- 6 cups day-old bread cubes, crust removed
- 1 cup raisins, or to taste
- 4 cups milk
- 8  eggs
- ⅔ cup white sugar
- 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- ½ teaspoon salt

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Fill an 11x12-inch pan with dried bread cubes and sprinkle with raisins.
3. Combine milk, eggs, sugar, cinnamon, vanilla extract, and salt in a bowl. Beat with an electric mixer until smooth. Pour milk-egg mixture over bread cubes and raisins.
4. Bake in the preheated oven until a knife inserted in the center comes out clean, 1 to 1 1/2 hours.
