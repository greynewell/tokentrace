## Ingredients

- 4 cups finely chopped broccoli florets
- 2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese
- 2  eggs
- ½ cup dry bread crumbs

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
2. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
3. Mix broccoli, Cheddar cheese, eggs, and bread crumbs together in a large bowl.
4. Roll into small balls with your hands.
5. Flatten the balls into thick patties and place on the baking sheet.
6. Bake in the preheated oven until tops are golden brown, about 15 minutes.
7. Flip patties over and continue baking until opposite side is golden brown, about 10 minutes more.
