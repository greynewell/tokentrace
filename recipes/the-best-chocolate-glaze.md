## Ingredients

- 6 tablespoons cocoa powder
- 5 tablespoons butter
- 1 cup confectioners' sugar
- ¾ teaspoon vanilla extract
- 2 tablespoons hot water, divided

## Instructions

1. Melt cocoa and butter together in a small saucepan over low heat, about 5 minutes. Remove from heat.
2. Stir in confectioners' sugar and vanilla extract. Stir in hot water, 1 teaspoon at a time, until glaze is thick and smooth.
