## Ingredients

- 1  purple sweet potato
- 1 teaspoon coconut oil
- 3 tablespoons boiled peanuts
- 1 tablespoon peanut butter
- 1 tablespoon coconut cream, or more to taste

## Instructions

1. Place sweet potato into a pot and cover with salted water; bring to a boil.
2. Reduce heat to medium-low and simmer until tender, about 15 minutes.
3. Drain.
4. Peel sweet potato; mash in a bowl.
5. Heat coconut oil in a skillet over medium heat; cook and stir mashed sweet potato until moisture evaporates, about 5 minutes.
6. Place sweet potato in a bowl and top with boiled peanuts, peanut butter, and coconut cream.
