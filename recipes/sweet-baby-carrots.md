## Ingredients

- 1 (16 ounce) package baby carrots
- ½ cup butter
- 3 tablespoons honey
- ½ cup brown sugar

## Instructions

1. Place carrots in a large pot and cover with salted water. Bring to a boil over high heat and cook until carrots are tender, about 15 minutes. Remove from the stove and drain carrots in a strainer.
2. Return the pot to the stove, add butter, and reduce the heat to medium-low. When butter is melted, stir in brown sugar and honey. Add carrots and stir until evenly coated. Cook for 5 more minutes before serving.
