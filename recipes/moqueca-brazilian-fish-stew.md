## Ingredients

- 2 pounds sea bass fillets, cut into large pieces
- 1  lime, juiced
- 1 pinch salt and ground black pepper to taste
- 2 tablespoons palm oil
- 3 large tomatoes, diced
- 1 large onion, chopped
- 1  green bell pepper, diced
- 1  red bell pepper, diced
- 1 bunch cilantro, chopped
- 2 tablespoons chopped green onions
- 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 3 cups coconut milk
- 1 pound raw shrimp, peeled and deveined   (Optional)

## Instructions

1. Combine sea bass fillets, lime juice, salt, and pepper in a large bowl.
2. Marinate in the refrigerator for 30 minutes.
3. Heat palm oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
4. Add tomatoes, onion, green bell pepper, red bell pepper, cilantro, green onions, parsley, and garlic; cook and stir until fragrant, about 2 minutes.
5. Add marinated sea bass and coconut milk.
6. Reduce heat to low; cook for 12 minutes.
7. Add shrimp.
8. Continue cooking until sea bass flakes easily with a fork and shrimp is opaque, about 3 minutes more.
