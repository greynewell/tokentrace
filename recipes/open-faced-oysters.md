## Ingredients

- 1 (8 ounce) package fresh mushrooms, finely chopped
- 1 bunch fresh cilantro, chopped
- ¼ cup olive oil
- 3  green onions, thinly sliced
- 1  lime, zested and juiced
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 20  freshly shucked oysters on the half shell
- 20 drops hot sauce, or to taste

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Arrange oysters on a baking dish.
3. Measure 1 tablespoon lime juice into a bowl; reserve.
4. Combine mushrooms, cilantro, olive oil, green onions, lime juice, lime zest, and garlic in a bowl.
5. Place a spoonful of the mixture over each oyster.
6. Add a drop of hot sauce to each oyster.
7. Drizzle reserved lime juice on top.
8. Bake in the preheated oven until warmed through, 10 to 15 minutes.
