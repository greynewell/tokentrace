## Ingredients

- 1 (16 ounce) package dry fusilli pasta
- 1 pound cooked medium shrimp, peeled and deveined
- 1  tomato, chopped
- 1  green bell pepper, chopped
- 2 tablespoons mayonnaise
- salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil, add pasta and cook until al dente.
2. Rinse under cold water and drain.
3. In a large bowl combine shrimp, tomato, green pepper, mayonnaise and salt and pepper to taste.
4. Mix well.
5. Add pasta and toss to coat.
6. Chill for approximately one hour and serve.
