## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 1  pear - peeled, cored and diced
- ⅔ cup all-purpose flour
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- ⅛ teaspoon black pepper
- 1  egg
- 3 tablespoons milk
- oil for deep frying

## Instructions

1. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium-high heat.
2. Saute the pears until caramelized; set aside to cool.
3. Meanwhile, in a medium bowl combine flour, baking powder, salt and pepper; form a well in the center.
4. In a small, separate bowl beat the egg with the milk and pour into the dry ingredients; mix well.
5. Gently fold pears into the batter.
6. Heat deep fryer to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
7. Drop batter by rounded spoonfuls into hot oil, and fry until golden brown.
8. Drain briefly on paper towels.
9. Serve hot.
