## Ingredients

- 1 (12 ounce) can SPAM® with Bacon
- ½ teaspoon ground coriander
- ¼ cup prepared yellow mustard
- 2  French rolls
- 4 slices provolone cheese

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
2. Shred SPAM® using the large holes on a cheese grater.
3. Fry shredded SPAM® with coriander in a large skillet on medium-high heat until browned, about 5 minutes.
4. Spread 1 tablespoon of yellow mustard on each half of the French rolls; place 2 slices of provolone cheese on the top half of each roll; place rolls on a baking sheet.
5. Bake until cheese has melted, about 5 minutes.
6. Divide cooked SPAM® between rolls, fold and serve immediately.
