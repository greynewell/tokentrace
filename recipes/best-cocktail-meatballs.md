## Ingredients

- 1 pound lean ground beef
- ⅔ cup evaporated milk
- 2 (1 ounce) packages dry onion soup mix
- ¼ cup Worcestershire sauce
- 1 cup ketchup
- ½ cup packed brown sugar

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to broil.
2. In a medium-sized mixing bowl, combine hamburger, milk, onion soup mix, and Worcestershire sauce.
3. Roll the mixture into small balls.
4. Arrange meatballs on a cookie sheet.
5. Broil for 10 minutes, or until cooked through.
6. In a small bowl, mix ketchup and brown sugar.
7. Transfer mixture to a slow cooker.
8. Add the meatballs to the slow cooker.
9. Cook meatballs on high for approximately 30 minutes.
