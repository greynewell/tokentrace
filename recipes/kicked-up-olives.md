## Ingredients

- 4 cups mixed olives with pits
- 5 cloves garlic, peeled
- 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
- ¼ teaspoon red pepper flakes
- 2  tangerines
- 3 slices sweet onion
- 4 sprigs fresh rosemary
- ⅔ cup extra-virgin olive oil
- 1 teaspoon fennel seeds, or to taste

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 250 degrees F (120 degrees C).
2. Mix olives, garlic, balsamic vinegar, and red pepper flakes together in a bowl.
3. Carefully peel tangerines and cut peels into strips.
4. Squeeze juice from 1 tangerine over olive mixture.
5. Add tangerine peel strips to olive mixture and toss well.
6. Make a packet with a large piece of aluminum foil.
7. Pour olive mixture into packet.
8. Arrange onion slices and rosemary over olives; drizzle with olive oil.
9. Sprinkle fennel seeds over mixture.
10. Fold and seal packet; place on a baking sheet.
11. Bake in the preheated oven until warmed, about 90 minutes.
12. Serve warm.
