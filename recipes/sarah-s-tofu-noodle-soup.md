## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons butter
- 2 cups sliced carrots
- 1 ½ cups chopped onion
- 1 ½ cups chopped celery
- 1 ½ teaspoons minced garlic
- 12 cups vegetarian chicken-flavored broth
- 2 cups egg noodles
- 1 (14 ounce) container extra-firm tofu, drained and cubed
- ¼ cup raisins
- ½ teaspoon dried basil
- ½ teaspoon dried oregano
- ¼ teaspoon poultry seasoning
- ¼ teaspoon dried thyme
- ¼ teaspoon dried rosemary
- ¼ teaspoon dried marjoram
- ¼ teaspoon black pepper
- ¼ cup cornstarch
- 3 tablespoons cold water

## Instructions

1. Melt the butter in a stockpot over medium heat.
2. Stir in the carrots, onion, celery, and garlic and cook until just tender, about 10 minutes.
3. Pour in the broth and bring to a boil over high heat.
4. Once boiling, add the noodles, tofu, raisins, basil, oregano, poultry seasoning, thyme, rosemary, marjoram, and pepper.
5. Dissolve the cornstarch and water in a small bowl.
6. Stir the cornstarch mixture into the soup.
7. Return soup to a boil, then reduce heat to medium-low, cover, and simmer for 30 minutes.
