## Ingredients

- 1  orange, peeled
- ½ cup water
- 1 leaf kale, torn into several pieces
- 2  ripe bananas, peeled

## Instructions

1. Blend orange in a blender until mostly juice.
2. Add water and kale; blend again on High speed until kale is liquefied.
3. Break bananas into chunks and add to the blender. Start blending at a lower speed until banana is incorporated. Increase speed to blend the mixture into a pudding-like texture.
