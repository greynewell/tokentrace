## Ingredients

- 3 ⅓ cups water
- ⅔ cup uncooked grits
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- ¼ cup butter
- ½ teaspoon minced garlic
- 2  green onions, diced
- 1 (16 ounce) package frozen shrimp, peeled and deveined - thawed
- 1 teaspoon lemon juice
- 2 pinches cayenne pepper, or to taste
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- ¼ cup butter
- ¾ cup cubed processed cheese food (such as Velveeta®)

## Instructions

1. Bring water to a boil in a saucepan; stir grits and salt into the boiling water, cover, and reduce heat to low.
2. Simmer grits until tender, 15 to 20 minutes.
3. Remove from heat and keep warm.
4. Melt 1/4 cup butter in a pan over medium heat; cook and stir garlic until softened, about 2 minutes.
5. Stir in green onions, cooking until green onions are wilted, another 4 minutes.
6. Mix shrimp into the garlic and green onions and cook until shrimp are firm and pink, about 5 minutes.
7. Stir in lemon juice, cayenne pepper, salt, and black pepper.
8. Just before serving, mix 1/4 cup butter into the cooked grits, followed by processed cheese.
9. Mix thoroughly until creamy and cheese is melted.
10. Season with salt and black pepper.
11. Place grits in a bowl, top with the shrimp, and pour pan sauce over shrimp and grits to serve.
