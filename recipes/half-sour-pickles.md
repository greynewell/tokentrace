## Ingredients

- 10 pounds pickling cucumbers
- 10 quarts water
- 2 ½ cups salt
- ½ cup whole coriander seeds
- ¼ cup whole mustard seeds
- ¼ cup whole caraway seeds
- 8 peppers dried red chile peppers
- 2 heads   garlic, separated into cloves and crushed
- 4 each allspice berries
- 2 leaf (blank)s bay leaves

## Instructions

1. Combine cucumbers, water, salt, coriander seeds, mustard seeds, caraway seeds, chile peppers, garlic, allspice berries, and bay leaves in an airtight jar.
2. Let sit for 2 days.
