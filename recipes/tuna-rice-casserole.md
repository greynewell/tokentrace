## Ingredients

- 1 ½ cups instant rice
- 1 ½ cups water
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- 2 tablespoons finely chopped onion
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ½ teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
- 1 (5 ounce) can tuna, drained
- ½ cup mozzarella cheese, shredded
- ¼ cup tomatoes, chopped

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. In a medium saucepan, mix water, garlic, onion, salt and black pepper.
3. Cover and bring to a boil.
4. Let boil for 1 minute, then stir in the rice.
5. Cover and remove from heat.
6. Let stand for 5 minutes.
7. Line the bottom of a 9x5 inch loaf pan with half of the rice.
8. Top with drained tuna, cheese, tomatoes and the remaining rice.
9. Bake in a preheated oven for 20 minutes.
