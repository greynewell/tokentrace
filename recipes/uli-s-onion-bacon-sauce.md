## Ingredients

- 1 stick butter
- 6 ounces thick-cut bacon, diced
- ¾ cup diced sweet onion

## Instructions

1. Melt butter in a skillet over medium-high heat.
2. Add bacon; cook, stirring, until almost crisp, 3 to 4 minutes.
3. Stir in onions.
4. Cook, stirring, until bacon is crisp and onions are soft and translucent, 8 to 10 minutes.
