## Ingredients

- ⅔ cup packed brown sugar
- ½ cup all-purpose flour
- 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
- 6 tablespoons cold butter
- 1 cup chopped walnuts
- 1  apple - peeled, cored, and chopped
- 2 cups all-purpose flour
- 1 tablespoon baking powder
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 cup white sugar
- ⅓ cup butter, softened
- 1 cup milk
- 1 large egg
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 9x13-inch pan.
2. Mix brown sugar, flour, and cinnamon for topping in a bowl. Cut in cold butter. Stir in walnuts and apple.
3. Combine flour, baking powder, and salt for cake in a bowl. Beat white sugar and butter together in a large bowl with an electric mixer until creamy. Add dry ingredients. Mix in milk, egg, and vanilla extract. Spread into the prepared pan and sprinkle streusel topping over top. Swirl a knife through both mixtures.
4. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center of the cake comes out clean, 35 to 40 minutes.
