## Ingredients

- 1 (2 pound) package frozen hash brown potatoes, thawed
- ½ cup butter, softened
- 1 teaspoon salt
- ½ teaspoon ground black pepper
- ½ cup chopped onion
- 1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of chicken soup
- 2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Spray a 9x13-inch dish with nonstick cooking spray.
2. Gently mix potatoes, soup, cheese, onion, butter, salt, and pepper in a large bowl. Pour into the prepared dish.
3. Bake in the preheated oven until browned, about 1 hour.
