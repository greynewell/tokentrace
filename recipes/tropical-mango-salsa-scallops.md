## Ingredients

- 2  potatoes, thinly sliced
- olive oil
- 1 pinch sea salt and ground black pepper to taste
- 1  mango - peeled, seeded, and diced
- 1  Spanish onion, minced
- 2  fresh chives, minced
- 1  tomato, chopped
- 1 small fresh red chile pepper, minced
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- 3  limes, juiced
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 12  scallops

## Instructions

1. Arrange the potato slices on a large plate.
2. Cook in microwave on High until tender about 3 minutes.
3. Heat 1/4 cup olive oil in a skillet over medium heat.
4. Add the potatoes to the skillet, season with the salt and pepper, and cook in the hot oil until golden on both sides, about 5 minutes.
5. Set aside on a plate lined with paper towels to drain.
6. Stir the mango, onion, chives, tomato, red chile pepper, garlic, and lime juice together in a bowl.
7. Heat 1 tablespoon olive oil in a skillet over medium heat.
8. Cook the scallops in the hot oil until browned on the bottom, about 2 minutes.
9. Turn and cook for 1 minute longer.
10. Remove scallops and place each individual scallop on top of a potato chip as a base, then smother liberally with the salsa.
11. They should be big enough to cut up with a knife or put into the mouth whole.
