## Ingredients

- 1 (4 roll) package Hawaiian bread rolls, split
- 8 slices Cheddar cheese
- white sugar, or to taste

## Instructions

1. Top each bread roll half with a slice of Cheddar cheese.
2. Toast topped rolls in toaster oven until cheese is melted, 5 to 7 minutes.
3. Sprinkle sugar over the melted cheese.
