## Ingredients

- 4  lobster tails
- 8 tablespoons butter, divided
- 4  top-sliced hot dog buns
- 1 pinch paprika
- 1 pinch kosher salt
- 4 leaves Bibb lettuce  (Optional)

## Instructions

1. Bring 1 cup water to a boil in a steamer.
2. Add lobster tails; steam for 3 minutes.
3. Remove from steamer and rinse under cold water until cool enough to handle.
4. Remove the shells and dice lobster meat into bite-sized pieces.
5. Melt 1 tablespoon butter in a 12-inch frying pan.
6. Add 2 buns and turn to coat with butter.
7. Toast over low heat, turning once, about 2 minutes.
8. Transfer to a plate.
9. Repeat with 1 tablespoon butter and remaining buns.
10. Melt remaining 6 tablespoons butter in the same pan.
11. Toss in lobster pieces.
12. Cook over low heat, stirring almost constantly, for 3 minutes.
13. Add paprika and salt; continue to stir until lobster is opaque, about 2 minutes more.
14. Line toasted buns with lettuce.
15. Fill with lobster.
16. Drizzle any remaining butter on top.
