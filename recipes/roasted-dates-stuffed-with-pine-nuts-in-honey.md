## Ingredients

- 1 cup toasted pine nuts
- ½ cup white wine, or as needed
- ¼ cup honey, or as needed
- ½ cup crumbled Gorgonzola cheese
- 2 cups dates - pitted and cut lengthwise to the center
- ground black pepper to taste
- salt to taste
- 1 cup honey, warmed
- ½ cup slivered, toasted almonds

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Grease a baking dish.
3. Soak pine nuts in a bowl with white wine and 1/4 cup honey to infuse flavor, at least 30 minutes.
4. Drain.
5. Mix Gorgonzola cheese and pine nuts together in a bowl.
6. Spread dates open slightly; season the inside of each date with black pepper.
7. Spoon Gorgonzola mixture into each date.
8. Season the outside of each date with salt.
9. Arrange stuffed dates in a baking dish.
10. Cover with 1 cup honey.
11. Bake in the preheated oven until warmed, about 10 minutes.
12. Transfer cooked dates to a serving platter.
13. Garnish with almonds.
