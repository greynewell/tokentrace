## Ingredients

- 1 quart vegetable stock
- 1 cup water
- 1 ½ teaspoons lobster base
- 4  potatoes, cut into bite-size pieces
- 1 tablespoon butter, or to taste
- ½  onion, halved and sliced
- 2 teaspoons dried tarragon
- 2 teaspoons arrowroot powder
- 1 cup half-and-half

## Instructions

1. Place vegetable stock, water, and lobster base in a large stockpot; bring to a boil.
2. Stir in potatoes; boil until tender, about 15 minutes.
3. Drain potatoes, reserving stock.
4. Melt butter in the stockpot over medium heat.
5. Add onions and tarragon; cook and stir until onions are translucent, about 5 minutes.
6. Add arrowroot; stir until thoroughly combined.
7. Pour reserved stock back into the stockpot.
8. Stir in half-and-half and bring bisque to a boil.
9. Return potatoes to the stockpot; lower heat and simmer until heated through, about 5 minutes.
