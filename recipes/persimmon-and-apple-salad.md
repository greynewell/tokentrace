## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon walnut pieces
- 2 tablespoons fresh orange juice
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 1 tablespoon white wine vinegar
- 1  apple, cored and thinly sliced
- 1  persimmon, stemmed and thinly sliced
- 1 dash salt
- 1 pinch ground cinnamon
- 2 cups shredded romaine lettuce, or to taste

## Instructions

1. Heat a small skillet over medium-low heat; add walnut pieces and cook until fragrant, about 2 minutes.
2. Whisk orange juice, olive oil, and white wine vinegar together in a large bowl.
3. Add apple, persimmon, and toasted walnut pieces; mix to coat.
4. Season with salt and cinnamon.
5. Add lettuce and toss to combine.
