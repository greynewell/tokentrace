## Ingredients

- 3 tablespoons butter
- 1 pound fresh mushrooms, sliced
- 1 small onion, chopped
- 1 ½ tablespoons all-purpose flour
- 1 cup sour cream
- ½ teaspoon lemon juice
- 1 pinch salt and pepper to taste
- ⅓ cup Parmesan cheese

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 375  degrees F (190 degrees C).
2. Melt the butter in a skillet over medium heat.
3. Stir in the mushrooms and onion, and cook until tender.
4. Sprinkle with the flour.
5. Reduce heat to low, and continue cooking 5 minutes.
6. Mix in sour cream and lemon juice, and season with salt and pepper.
7. Transfer to a small casserole dish, and top with Parmesan cheese.
8. Bake 15 minutes in the preheated oven, until bubbly.
