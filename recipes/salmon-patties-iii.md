## Ingredients

- ½ pound salmon
- 1  red potato, peeled and chopped
- 1  shallot, minced
- 1  egg, beaten
- ¼ cup Italian seasoned bread crumbs
- 1 teaspoon dried Italian seasoning
- salt and pepper to taste
- ½ cup cornflake crumbs
- 2 tablespoons olive oil

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) and lightly grease a small baking dish.
2. Place salmon in the prepared baking dish, cover, and bake 20 minutes, or until easily flaked with a fork.
3. Place potato in a small saucepan with enough water to cover, and bring to a boil.
4. Cook 15 minutes, or until tender.
5. Drain and mash.
6. In a medium bowl, mix salmon, potato, shallot, egg, and bread crumbs.
7. Season with Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper.
8. Place the cornflake crumbs in a small bowl.
9. Form the salmon mixture into 1 inch balls, and roll in the cornflakes to coat.
10. Heat olive oil in a medium saucepan over medium heat.
11. Press the balls into patties, and fry 3 to 5 minutes per side, or until golden brown.
