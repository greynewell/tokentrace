## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 1  onion, chopped
- 1 cup seafood stock
- ¼ cup tomato paste
- 1 head bok choy, chopped
- 3 cloves garlic, chopped
- 1 teaspoon gumbo file powder
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- 2  saffron threads, or to taste
- 3 pounds tilapia fillets

## Instructions

1. Heat oil in a skillet over medium heat.
2. Add onion; cook and stir until translucent, about 5 minutes.
3. Add seafood stock and tomato paste.
4. Add bok choy, garlic, gumbo file, salt, pepper, and saffron; cook and stir until well incorporated, about 5 minutes.
5. Add tilapia.
6. Let simmer until flavors blend and fish flakes easily with a fork, 15 to 20 minutes.
