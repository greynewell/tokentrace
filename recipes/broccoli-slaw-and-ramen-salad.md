## Ingredients

- 1 (3 ounce) package ramen noodles, crushed, with seasoning packet
- ¾ cup apple cider vinegar
- 2 tablespoons white sugar
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ½ teaspoon ground black pepper
- 1 (12 ounce) package broccoli coleslaw mix
- ½ cup dried cranberries  (Optional)

## Instructions

1. Whisk ramen noodle seasoning packet, vinegar, sugar, olive oil, salt, and pepper together in a bowl to make the salad dressing.
2. Add ramen noodles and broccoli slaw; toss together.
3. Let sit until flavors meld, 1 to 2 hours.
