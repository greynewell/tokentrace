## Ingredients

- 1 (8 ounce) can crushed pineapple with juice
- 3 cups all-purpose flour
- 2 ¾ cups white sugar
- 1 tablespoon baking soda
- 2 ½ teaspoons ground cinnamon
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 ½ cups vegetable oil
- 4  eggs, beaten
- 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
- 1 ½ cups shredded carrots
- 1 ¼ cups flaked coconut
- 1 cup chopped walnuts
- 1 cup confectioners' sugar

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Grease and flour a 10 inch Bundt pan.
3. Drain pineapple and reserve the juice, set aside.
4. In a large bowl, mix flour, sugar, baking soda, cinnamon and salt.
5. Make a well in the center of flour mixture and add oil, eggs and vanilla.
6. Beat until combined.
7. Fold in carrots, coconut, walnuts and pineapple.
8. Pour into a 10 inch Bundt pan.
9. Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 45 to 50 minutes, or until cake pulls away from pan.
10. Cool in pan 15 minutes, then turn out onto a wire rack and cool completely.
11. In a medium bowl, combine confectioners sugar and 2 tablespoons pineapple juice.
12. Beat until smooth, then drizzle over cake.
