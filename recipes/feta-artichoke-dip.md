## Ingredients

- 1 (13.75 ounce) can artichoke hearts, drained, coarsely chopped
- 1 (8 ounce) package crumbled feta
- 1 cup mayonnaise
- ½ cup shredded Parmesan cheese
- 2 ounces pimientos, drained and diced
- 3 cloves garlic, diced
- 1  tomato, chopped
- 3  green onions, sliced

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Lightly grease 9x9 inch baking dish.
3. Combine the artichoke hearts, feta, mayonnaise, Parmesan cheese, pimientos, and garlic in a bowl; blend thoroughly.
4. Spoon into prepared baking dish.
5. Bake in preheated oven until top is lightly brown, 20 to 25 minutes.
6. Remove and garnish with tomatoes and green onions.
