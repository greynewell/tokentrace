## Ingredients

- ¼ cup olive oil
- 2 tablespoons white vinegar
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon basil
- ½ teaspoon ground black pepper
- ½ teaspoon oregano
- 1 (12 ounce) package firm tofu, drained and cut into 1/2-inch cubes
- 2 tablespoons capers
- 1 head leafy dark green lettuce -  torn, washed, and spun dry
- 10  cherry tomatoes, halved
- ¼  red onion, thinly sliced for garnish (or as preferred)

## Instructions

1. Whisk olive oil, vinegar, salt, basil, pepper, and oregano together in a bowl.
2. Put tofu cubes in a large bowl.
3. Pour marinade over tofu; add capers.
4. Marinate tofu, stirring occasionally, for at least 10 minutes.
5. Combine lettuce and tomatoes in a separate large bowl.
6. Pour tofu mixture over lettuce and toss.
7. Garnish with red onion.
