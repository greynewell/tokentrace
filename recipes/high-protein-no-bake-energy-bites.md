## Ingredients

- 1 cup rolled oats
- ½ cup honey
- ½ cup peanut butter
- ½ cup chocolate chips
- 2 scoops protein powder
- 2 tablespoons chopped macadamia nuts

## Instructions

1. Combine oats, honey, peanut butter, chocolate chips, protein powder, and macadamia nuts in a bowl; form into balls and arrange on a plate or baking sheet.
2. Refrigerate until firm, about 1 hour.
