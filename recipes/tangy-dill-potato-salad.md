## Ingredients

- 3 pounds new potatoes, scrubbed and quartered
- ½ cup Italian-style salad dressing
- ¾ cup mayonnaise
- ¼ cup chopped green onions
- 2 teaspoons chopped fresh dill
- 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard  (Optional)
- 1 teaspoon lemon juice
- ⅛ teaspoon pepper

## Instructions

1. Bring a large pot of water to a boil.
2. Add potatoes, and cook for about 10 minutes, or until tender.
3. Drain, and set aside to cool.
4. Meanwhile, in a large bowl, stir together the salad dressing, mayonnaise, green onions, dill, mustard, lemon juice, and pepper.
5. When the potatoes are cooled, stir into the bowl until coated.
6. Refrigerate for a couple of hours to blend flavors before serving.
