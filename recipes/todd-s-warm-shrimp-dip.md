## Ingredients

- 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
- 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese with chives
- 1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of shrimp soup
- 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
- 1 (4.5 ounce) can shrimp, coarsely chopped

## Instructions

1. In a large skillet, combine cream cheese, and cream cheese with chives.
2. When the cheese has melted, mix in shrimp soup, Worcestershire sauce, and shrimp.
3. Stir until the mixture is heated through.
