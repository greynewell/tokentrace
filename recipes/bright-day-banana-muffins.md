## Ingredients

- 4  bananas
- 1 cup brown sugar
- 2  egg whites
- 1  egg
- 3 tablespoons canola oil
- 1 ¼ cups whole wheat flour
- 1 cup rolled oats
- ½ teaspoon baking soda
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ¼ teaspoon baking powder
- 12  paper muffin cup liners

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Line 12 muffin cups with paper liners.
2. Mash bananas in a bowl until no lumps remain. Add brown sugar, egg whites, egg, and canola oil; mix thoroughly. Stir flour, oats, baking soda, salt, and baking powder into the batter until just combined.
3. Fill muffin cup 2/3-full with batter.
4. Bake in the preheated oven until tops spring back when lightly pressed, 20 to 25 minutes.
