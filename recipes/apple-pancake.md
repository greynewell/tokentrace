## Ingredients

- 3 tablespoons butter
- 1 large apple, cored and sliced
- ½ cup white sugar, divided
- 2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
- 4  eggs
- ⅓ cup milk
- ⅓ cup all-purpose flour
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 1 pinch salt

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
2. Melt butter in an oven-safe skillet over medium heat. Add apple slices, 1/4 cup sugar, and cinnamon; cook and stir until apples are tender, about 5 minutes. Remove from the heat.
3. Beat eggs, milk, flour, remaining 1/4 cup sugar, baking powder, vanilla, and salt in a large bowl until smooth. Pour batter evenly over apples.
4. Bake in the preheated oven until pancake is golden brown, about 10 minutes.
5. Remove from the oven. Run a spatula around the edges to loosen. Invert carefully over a large plate to serve.
