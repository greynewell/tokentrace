## Ingredients

- 1 pound frozen pumpkin-filled ravioli
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 1 (6 ounce) can crabmeat
- 2 teaspoons crushed dried thyme
- 1 pint light cream
- ¼ cup shredded Parmesan cheese
- salt and ground white pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
2. Stir in ravioli and return to a boil.
3. Cook uncovered, stirring occasionally, until ravioli float to the top and filling is hot, 6 to 8 minutes.
4. Drain.
5. Melt butter in a large saucepan over medium heat.
6. Add crabmeat and thyme; cook and stir until thyme is fragrant, about 2 minutes.
7. Pour in cream.
8. Increase heat to medium-high and cook, stirring, until cream begins to steam, 4 to 5 minutes.
9. Reduce heat and add Parmesan cheese; stir until melted, about 2 minutes.
10. Ladle cream sauce into the bottom of a serving dish; top with ravioli.
