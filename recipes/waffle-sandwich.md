## Ingredients

- 2 links pork sausage
- 1 slice Cheddar cheese
- 2  frozen waffles, toasted
- ¼  Red Delicious apple, sliced very thin
- ½ teaspoon cinnamon-sugar

## Instructions

1. Heat a small skillet over medium heat.
2. Cook the sausages in the hot skillet, turning occasionally, until no longer pink in the center, about 5 minutes.
3. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read 160 degrees F (70 degrees C).
4. Put Cheddar cheese atop 1 toasted waffle.
5. Arrange apple slices over the cheese.
6. Sprinkle cinnamon-sugar over the apple slices.
7. Put sausage links atop the apples and finish sandwich with remaining waffle.
8. Cut sandwich into halves.
