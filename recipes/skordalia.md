## Ingredients

- 4 large potatoes, peeled and cubed
- ¼ cup minced garlic
- ⅓ cup distilled white vinegar
- 1 ½ cups olive oil
- salt to taste

## Instructions

1. Place potatoes in a saucepan and cover with lightly salted water.
2. Bring to a boil over high heat, then reduce heat to medium-low and simmer until very tender, about 20 minutes.
3. Drain and return to the saucepan.
4. Mash together the potatoes, garlic, vinegar and olive oil with a potato masher or a large fork.
5. Continue mixing until all ingredients are well blended.
6. Season with salt to taste.
7. This sauce can be served chilled or at room temperature.
