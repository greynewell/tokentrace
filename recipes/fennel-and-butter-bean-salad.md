## Ingredients

- 1 cup canned butter beans, drained and rinsed
- 1 bulb fennel, cored and finely chopped
- 3 stalks scallions, sliced
- ¼ cup lemon juice
- ¼ cup olive oil
- 2 tablespoons chopped fresh dill
- ½ teaspoon honey
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- ¼ teaspoon pepper
- 1 clove garlic, minced

## Instructions

1. Combine butter beans, fennel, and scallions in a bowl.
2. Whisk lemon juice, olive oil, dill, honey, salt, pepper, and garlic together in a separate bowl. Pour dressing over the bean mixture; toss together to coat.
3. Refrigerate at least 1 hour before serving, or up to 1 day.
