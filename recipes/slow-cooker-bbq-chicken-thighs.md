## Ingredients

- 9  Tyson® Fresh Boneless, Skinless Chicken Thighs
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ¼ teaspoon pepper
- 1 ½ cups barbecue sauce
- ¼ cup honey
- 2 teaspoons Creole mustard
- ¼ teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
- ¼ teaspoon hot pepper sauce

## Instructions

1. Season chicken with salt and pepper and place in a slow cooker.
2. Combine remaining ingredients in a bowl and mix well. Pour mixture over chicken thighs and stir to coat.
3. Cook on Low for approximately 5 hours or until internal temperature of the chicken reaches 180 degrees F.
