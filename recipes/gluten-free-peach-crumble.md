## Ingredients

- 6 cups peeled, sliced fresh peaches
- ¼ cup packed brown sugar
- 3 tablespoons gluten-free all purpose baking flour (such as Pillsbury™)
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon
- 1 teaspoon lemon juice
- ½ teaspoon grated lemon zest
- 1 cup gluten-free all purpose baking flour (such as Pillsbury™)
- 1 cup white sugar
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- ¼ teaspoon nutmeg
- 1  egg, beaten
- ½ cup melted coconut butter

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). Grease a shallow 11x7-inch baking dish.
2. Place sliced peaches in the prepared baking dish. Mix together brown sugar, flour, cinnamon, lemon juice, and lemon zest in a small bowl; sprinkle over peaches.
3. Mix together flour, sugar, baking powder, salt, and nutmeg in a medium bowl for the topping. Stir in egg until mixture resembles coarse crumbs; sprinkle topping over peaches. Drizzle melted coconut butter evenly over topping.
4. Bake in the preheated oven until peach juices are bubbling and topping is golden brown, about 38 minutes.
