## Ingredients

- ½ cup butter
- 1 cup all-purpose flour
- ¼ teaspoon butter flavored extract
- 2 tablespoons water
- 1 cup water
- ½ cup butter
- ¼ teaspoon butter flavored extract
- 1 cup all-purpose flour
- 3  eggs
- 1 teaspoon almond extract
- 1 cup confectioners' sugar
- ½ teaspoon almond extract
- ¼ teaspoon butter flavored extract
- 1 tablespoon milk

## Instructions

1. Preheat an oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Cut 1/2 cup butter into 1 cup flour with a knife or pastry blender in a bowl until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
3. Stir in 1/4 teaspoon butter extract.
4. Add the water a tablespoon at a time, tossing with a fork, until the flour mixture is moistened.
5. Press mixture onto the bottom of a 10x13-inch baking sheet.
6. Bring 1 cup water, 1/2 cup butter, and 1/2 teaspoon butter extract to a boil in a saucepan over medium-high heat.
7. Remove from heat, then beat in 1 cup flour.
8. Beat in eggs one at a time until blended.
9. Stir in 1 teaspoon almond extract, and pour over crust.
10. Bake in the preheated oven until golden brown, about 55 minutes.
11. Meanwhile, mix the confectioners' sugar, 1/2 teaspoon almond extract, 1/4 teaspoon butter extract, and 1 tablespoon milk in a bowl until smooth.
12. Spread mixture over the hot pastry.
