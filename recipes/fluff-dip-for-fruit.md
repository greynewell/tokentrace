## Ingredients

- 2 (3 ounce) packages instant vanilla pudding mix
- 1 cup milk
- ½ cup amaretto liqueur
- 1 (16 ounce) container frozen whipped topping, thawed

## Instructions

1. In a medium bowl, mix together the vanilla pudding mix, milk and amaretto liqueur.
2. Beat in the thawed frozen whipped topping.
3. Chill 1 hour in the refrigerator before serving.
