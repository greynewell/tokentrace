## Ingredients

- ½ cup mayonnaise
- 2 teaspoons ground dried chipotle pepper
- 1  lime, juiced
- 1  large egg white
- ¼ cup ice water
- ½ teaspoon kosher salt
- ¼ cup cornstarch
- ¼ cup self-rising flour
- 2 ears  fresh sweet white corn, kernels removed from cob
- 1 cup vegetable oil for frying, or as needed
- 1 ½ cups flaked fresh Dungeness crab meat
- 1  fresh lime, cut into wedges

## Instructions

1. Whisk mayonnaise, chipotle powder, and lime juice together in a mixing bowl.
2. Cover with plastic wrap and refrigerate until needed for serving.
3. Whisk egg white, ice water, and salt together in a large mixing bowl until mixture is light and foamy, 1 or 2 minutes.
4. Add cornstarch and flour; whisk together until thoroughly mixed.
5. Batter should resemble a thin pancake batter.
6. Stir in corn.
7. Heat 1/2 inch vegetable oil to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) in a seasoned cast iron skillet.
8. Spoon about 2 large spoonfuls of batter into oil for each fritter.
9. Do not crowd, cook in batches, if necessary.
10. Fry until browned and crispy, about 2 minutes per side.
11. Turn fritters carefully to avoid spattering the oil.
12. Drain on paper-towel-lined plate.
13. Serve topped with fresh Dungeness crab and drizzle with the chipotle dressing.
14. Serve with a wedge of lime.
