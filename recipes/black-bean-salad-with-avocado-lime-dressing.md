## Ingredients

- 1  avocado, mashed
- ¼ cup chopped fresh cilantro
- 2 tablespoons lime juice
- ½ cup pumpkin seeds
- 4 cups shredded romaine lettuce
- 2 (15 ounce) cans no-salt-added black beans, drained and rinsed
- 1 cup grape tomatoes, halved
- 1 cup corn kernels
- 1 small red bell pepper, chopped

## Instructions

1. Beat avocado, cilantro, and lime juice together in a large bowl until smooth.
2. Heat a small skillet over medium heat.
3. Toast pumpkin seeds in skillet until lightly browned and fragrant, about 5 minutes.
4. Stir pumpkin seeds, romaine lettuce, black beans, grape tomatoes, corn kernels, and red bell pepper into the dressing to coat.
