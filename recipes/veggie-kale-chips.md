## Ingredients

- 1 bunch kale, stems removed and leaves ripped into bite-size pieces
- 1 tablespoon olive oil, or more as needed
- 1 clove garlic, pressed  (Optional)
- 1 tablespoon vinegar
- salt to taste

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 250 degrees F (120 degrees C).
2. Place kale leaves in a large resealable bag.
3. Mix olive oil and garlic together in a bowl; pour over kale.
4. Seal and shake bag until kale leaves are evenly coated.
5. Add vinegar to bag, reseal bag, and shake until coated.
6. Spread leaves evenly onto a baking sheet.
7. Bake in the preheated oven until crisp, 20 to 25 minutes.
8. Season with salt.
