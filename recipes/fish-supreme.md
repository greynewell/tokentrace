## Ingredients

- 2 (6 ounce) fillets cod
- salt and pepper to taste
- 1 cup tomato sauce
- 3  potatoes, peeled and sliced paper-thin

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
2. Place fish fillets in a 8x12 inch baking dish and season with salt and pepper.
3. Pour tomato sauce over fillets and layer sliced potatoes on top.
4. Season to taste with salt and pepper.
5. Loosely cover dish with foil.
6. Bake in the preheated oven for 15 to 20 minutes or until fish flakes with a fork and potatoes are tender.
