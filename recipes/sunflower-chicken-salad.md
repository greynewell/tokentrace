## Ingredients

- 2 cups cubed cooked chicken breast meat
- 1 cup cubed Cheddar cheese
- ¼ cup sunflower seeds
- ¼ cup thinly sliced celery
- ½ cup seedless green grapes, halved
- ½ cup mayonnaise
- salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. In a large bowl combine the chicken, cheese, sunflower seeds, celery, grapes, mayonnaise and salt and pepper to taste.
2. Mix all together and serve on rolls or lettuce leaves, if desired.
