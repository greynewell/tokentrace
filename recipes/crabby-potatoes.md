## Ingredients

- 1 ½ cups butter, melted and divided
- 1 (16 ounce) package frozen hash brown potatoes
- 1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of chicken soup
- 1 (16 ounce) container sour cream
- 1 pound crabmeat
- 1 (16 ounce) package Cheddar cheese, shredded

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Pour 1 cup melted butter in a 9x13 inch casserole dish.
3. Spread frozen hash browns in the bottom of the dish.
4. In a small bowl combine chicken soup and sour cream;  spread over hash browns.
5. Sprinkle crab meat evenly over potatoes and top with cheese.
6. Pour remaining 1/2 cup melted butter over cheese.
7. Bake in preheated oven for 25 minutes.
