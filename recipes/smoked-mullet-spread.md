## Ingredients

- 1 whole large smoked mullet
- 2 (12 ounce) containers whipped cream cheese, softened
- 1 (5 ounce) bottle sweet hot sauce

## Instructions

1. Pick the meat off of bones, discard any dark meat (it has a nasty flavor), and make sure you chop the meat up fine afterwards.
2. In a small mixing bowl combine the meat, cream cheese and hot sauce.
3. Mash the mixture well.
4. Refrigerate overnight.
