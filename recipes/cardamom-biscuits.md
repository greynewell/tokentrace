## Ingredients

- 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
- ½ cup nonfat dry milk powder
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 1 teaspoon ground cardamom
- ⅛ teaspoon salt
- ⅓ cup warm milk
- ⅓ cup honey
- 3 tablespoons canola oil
- ½ teaspoon vanilla extract
- 1 tablespoon turbinado sugar

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C).
2. Spray a baking sheet with cooking spray, and set aside.
3. Combine the flour, dry milk powder, baking powder, cardamom, and salt in a  bowl.
4. In another  bowl, stir together warm milk, honey, oil, and vanilla extract.
5. Pour the milk mixture into the flour mixture, and stir a few times, until just combined.
6. Gather the dough together and turn out onto a floured surface.
7. Gently pat the dough into a 9x3 inch rectangle, and cut the dough into 3 3-inch squares with a sharp knife.
8. Cut each square on the diagonal to make 6 triangles, and place the triangles onto the prepared baking sheet.
9. Bake for 6 minutes in the preheated oven, remove from oven, and sprinkle the tops of the biscuits with turbinado sugar.
10. Return the biscuits to the oven, and bake for 8 more minutes.
11. Let cool on rack before serving.
