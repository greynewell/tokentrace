## Ingredients

- 2 pounds medium shrimp - peeled and deveined
- 4 cloves garlic, peeled and minced
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 8  plum tomatoes, finely chopped
- salt
- cayenne pepper
- 1 tablespoon butter  (Optional)

## Instructions

1. In a large skillet over medium heat, cook the garlic in the olive oil until just golden, approximately 2 minutes.
2. Be careful not to burn the garlic.
3. Add the tomatoes and sprinkle with salt and cayenne pepper.
4. Bring to a gentle simmer and cook, stirring occasionally, for 15 minutes.
5. Season the shrimp with salt and add to the tomatoes.
6. Cook 3 to 5 minutes, or until no longer pink.
7. Stir in 1 tablespoon of butter for added richness, if desired.
