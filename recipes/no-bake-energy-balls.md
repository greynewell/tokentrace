## Ingredients

- 1 cup old-fashioned oats
- ½ cup peanut butter
- ½ cup ground flax seed
- ½ cup chocolate chips
- ⅓ cup honey
- 1 tablespoon chia seeds  (Optional)
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

## Instructions

1. Combine oats, peanut butter, ground flaxseed, chocolate chips, honey, chia seeds, and vanilla extract together in a bowl. Cover and chill the dough in the refrigerator for 30 minutes.
2. Remove chilled dough from the refrigerator; roll into twenty 1-inch diameter balls.
