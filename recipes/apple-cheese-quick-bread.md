## Ingredients

- ½ cup butter, softened
- ⅓ cup white sugar
- ⅓ cup honey
- 2  eggs
- 1 cup whole wheat flour
- 1 cup all-purpose flour
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- ½ teaspoon baking soda
- ½ teaspoon salt
- 2  Granny Smith apples - peeled, cored and shredded
- 4 ounces Swiss cheese, shredded
- ½ cup chopped walnuts

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Lightly grease a 9x5 inch loaf pan.
3. In a large bowl, cream together the butter and sugar until light and fluffy.
4. Beat in the honey and eggs.
5. In a separate bowl, sift together the whole wheat flour, all-purpose flour, baking powder, soda and salt.
6. Add the flour mixture to the butter and egg mixture; mix until combined.
7. Fold in the apple, cheese and walnuts.
8. Spoon batter into prepared pan.
9. Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 50 to 60 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of the loaf comes out clean.
10. Turn the loaf out onto a wire rack and let cool completely.
