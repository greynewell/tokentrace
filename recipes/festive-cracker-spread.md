## Ingredients

- 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
- 1 (8 ounce) package finely shredded sharp Cheddar cheese
- ½ cup mayonnaise
- ¾ cup chopped pimento-stuffed olives
- ½ cup chopped celery
- ⅓ cup chopped onion
- ¼ cup chopped green bell pepper
- 2 teaspoons dried parsley

## Instructions

1. Beat the cream cheese, Cheddar cheese, and mayonnaise with an electric mixer in a bowl until smooth.
2. Fold in the olives, celery, onion, bell pepper, and parsley; mixing just enough to evenly combine.
3. Cover and chill for at least 1 hour.
