## Ingredients

- ¾ cup white sugar
- 1 tablespoon corn syrup
- 2 ½ tablespoons butter
- ¼ cup milk
- ½ (16 ounce) package chopped marshmallows
- 2 tablespoons water
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

## Instructions

1. Combine sugar, milk, butter, and corn syrup in a saucepan over low heat; stir until sugar dissolves. Bring to a boil, then lower heat and simmer for 5 minutes.
2. Meanwhile, melt marshmallows in the top of a double boiler, melt the marshmallows with the water.
3. Stir melted marshmallows into the hot syrup mixture until no streaks remain. Remove from the heat and stir in vanilla.
