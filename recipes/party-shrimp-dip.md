## Ingredients

- ¼ cup milk
- 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
- 1 teaspoon lemon juice
- 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
- ½ teaspoon garlic salt
- 1 (4.5 ounce) can tiny shrimp, rinsed and drained

## Instructions

1. Blend milk gradually into cream cheese with wire whisk in a bowl; whisk in lemon juice, Worcestershire sauce, and garlic salt.
2. Stir shrimp through cheese mixture until evenly mixed.
3. Cover bowl with plastic wrap; refrigerate at least 1 hour.
