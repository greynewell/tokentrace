## Ingredients

- 12 large hard-boiled eggs, peeled
- ⅓ cup mayonnaise
- 1 tablespoon pickle relish
- 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
- 1 teaspoon yellow mustard
- 1 teaspoon apple cider vinegar
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
- 1 teaspoon ground paprika

## Instructions

1. Slice eggs in half lengthwise and remove yolks. Place yolks in a medium bowl and set whites aside.
2. Mash yolks with a fork. Add mayonnaise, relish, Dijon, yellow mustard, vinegar, salt, and pepper; stir until well combined and smooth. Transfer to a large zip-top bag.
3. Arrange egg whites on a tray. Snip off the bottom corner of the zip-top bag. Fill each white with about 1 1/2 to 2 teaspoons filling. Sprinkle with paprika.
