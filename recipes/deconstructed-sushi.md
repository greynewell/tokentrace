## Ingredients

- 1 ¼ cups chicken broth
- 1 cup jasmine rice
- 1 teaspoon grated fresh ginger
- 1 teaspoon grated fresh garlic
- ½ teaspoon salt
- 1  bay leaf
- ½  cucumber, cut into matchstick-size pieces
- 1 pinch salt, or as needed
- 4 ounces imitation crabmeat, shredded
- ½  zucchini, cut into matchstick-size pieces
- ½  yellow squash, cut into matchstick-size pieces
- 1 tablespoon toasted sesame seeds

## Instructions

1. Combine chicken broth, rice, ginger, garlic, 1/2 teaspoon salt, and bay leaf in a pot; bring to a boil.
2. Reduce heat to medium-low, cover pot, and simmer until rice is tender, 15 to 20 minutes.
3. Remove pot from heat and allow rice to cool, at least 30 minutes.
4. Place cucumber in a bowl and sprinkle 1 pinch salt over cucumber.
5. Mix crabmeat, cucumber, zucchini, squash, and sesame seeds into rice mixture.
