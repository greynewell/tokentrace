## Ingredients

- ⅓ cup butter
- 1 clove crushed garlic
- ¼ tablespoon dried basil
- 2 teaspoons dried oregano

## Instructions

1. Melt butter in a small saucepan. Add garlic , and saute until cooked and fragrant. Add oregano and basil and stir until heated through.
2. Serve warm.
