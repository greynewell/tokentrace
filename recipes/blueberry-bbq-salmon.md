## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons barbeque sauce
- 2 teaspoons blueberry jam
- 1 (1 pound) fillet salmon

## Instructions

1. Set an oven rack about 6 inches from the heat source and preheat the oven's broiler.
2. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
3. Mix together barbecue sauce and blueberry jam in a small bowl until well combined.
4. Place salmon on the prepared baking sheet and cover with 2/3 of the sauce.
5. Reserve remaining sauce.
6. Broil the salmon in the preheated oven until fish flakes easily with a fork, 5 to 10 minutes.
7. Remove from the oven and cover with the remaining sauce to serve.
