## Ingredients

- 2  eggs
- ¾ cup warm water
- 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
- 2 tablespoons molasses
- 2 ½ cups rye flour
- ¼ cup cornstarch
- 2 teaspoons lecithin
- 1 ¼ teaspoons sea salt
- 3 teaspoons active dry yeast

## Instructions

1. Place ingredients in the pan of the bread machine in the order recommended by the manufacturer.
2. Select Regular cycle and medium crust setting; press Start.
3. While dough is mixing, check the consistency, it should be slightly sticky.
