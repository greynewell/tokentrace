## Ingredients

- 1 cup bread flour
- 1 cup whole wheat flour
- 1 cup cornmeal
- 1 cup molasses
- 2 cups water
- 1 teaspoon baking soda
- 1 teaspoon salt

## Instructions

1. In large bowl, stir together bread flour, whole wheat flour, cornmeal, baking soda.
2. Stir in molasses and water; mix until combined.
3. Pour batter into two lightly greased coffee cans, fill about 2/3 full.
4. Place loaves into a steamer, cover and steam over low heat.
5. Steam for 1 hour and 45 minutes, until loaves are set.
