## Ingredients

- ½ cup unpopped popcorn
- 3 tablespoons butter, melted
- 2 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese
- ½ teaspoon garlic salt, or more to taste
- ½ teaspoon dried crushed rosemary  (Optional)

## Instructions

1. Pop popcorn in a air popper according to manufacturer's instructions; transfer to a large bowl.
2. Drizzle butter over popcorn; stir to evenly coat.
3. Sprinkle Parmesan cheese, garlic salt, and rosemary over popcorn; toss to coat.
