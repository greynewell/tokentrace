## Ingredients

- ½ cup oil-packed sun-dried tomatoes
- ¼ cup basil leaves
- 8 ounces cream cheese

## Instructions

1. Place sun-dried tomatoes in the work bowl of a food processor; pulse until coarsely ground.
2. Add basil; pulse until finely ground.
3. Add cream cheese; process until well-mixed.
4. Transfer to a serving bowl or storage container.
