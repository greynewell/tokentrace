## Ingredients

- 2 cups cold milk
- 1 (3.9 ounce) package instant chocolate pudding mix
- 2  whole graham crackers, crushed
- 1 ½ cups vanilla ice cream, softened

## Instructions

1. Beat milk and pudding mix together in a bowl using an electric mixer until thickened, about 2 minutes.
2. Measure 1 1/2 cup pudding and set remaining pudding aside for another use.
3. Sprinkle graham cracker crumbs into the bottom of each ice pop mold; add about 1/4 cup ice cream.
4. Sprinkle another layer of graham cracker crumbs and top with about 1/4 cup pudding.
5. Add a final layer of graham cracker crumbs.
6. Freeze until completely frozen, about 3 hours.
