## Ingredients

- 6 (6 ounce) fillets halibut
- 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
- ½ pound cooked salad shrimp
- salt to taste
- ground black pepper to taste
- ½ teaspoon dried basil
- 1  lemon

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C).
2. Line a cookie sheet with foil.
3. In a small bowl, mix together cream cheese and shrimp.
4. Season to taste with basil, salt and pepper.
5. Make a pocket in each of the fillets, making sure you do not cut through on three sides.
6. Divide the cream cheese mixture into 6 parts, and stuff each fillet.
7. Place on foil lined pan.
8. Squeeze lemon juice over fish.
9. Bake for 20 minutes, or until fish is thoroughly done.
