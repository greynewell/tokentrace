## Ingredients

- ¼ cup margarine, softened
- 1 cup white sugar
- 1  egg
- 1 ½ cups mashed bananas
- ¼ cup sour milk
- ¾ teaspoon baking soda
- 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
- ¼ teaspoon salt

## Instructions

1. Cream together butter or margarine, sugar, and salt.
2. Mix in egg.
3. Stir in bananas and milk.
4. Stir in flour and baking soda with a few swift strokes; do not overbeat.
5. Spread batter into a greased and floured 9 x 5 inch loaf pan.
6. Bake at 325 degrees F (165 degrees C) for 45 to 60 minutes, or until a tester inserted in the center comes out clean.
