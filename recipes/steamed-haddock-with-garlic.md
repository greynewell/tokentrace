## Ingredients

- 2 (6 ounce) fillets haddock fillets
- 3 tablespoons butter
- 1  onion, chopped
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 3 pinches dried dill weed
- ground black pepper to taste
- 1  lemon, halved

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Place haddock fillets on an 18x18-inch piece of aluminum foil; top with butter.
3. Sprinkle onion, garlic, dill, and pepper over butter and haddock.
4. Squeeze juice from 1/2 lemon evenly on top.
5. Lift up bottom and top ends of the aluminum foil towards the center; fold together to 1 inch above the haddock.
6. Flatten short ends of the aluminum foil; fold over to within 1 inch of the sides of the haddock.
7. Place foil package on a baking sheet.
8. Bake in the preheated oven until haddock flakes easily with a fish, about 45 minutes.
9. Let sit, about 5 minutes.
10. Open ends of the packet carefully; squeeze juice from the remaining 1/2 lemon on top.
