## Ingredients

- ½ cup butter, softened
- 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
- 4 cups confectioners' sugar
- 5 tablespoons premium bourbon

## Instructions

1. Combine butter and cream cheese in a large bowl; beat with an electric mixer until smooth and creamy.
2. Add confectioners' sugar and bourbon. Beat until frosting is smooth and spreadable.
