## Ingredients

- 4 ounces frozen French fries
- ½ pound thinly sliced roast turkey
- 4 slices white bread, lightly toasted
- 10 slices American cheese

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
2. Spread French fries out on a baking sheet.
3. Bake in the preheated oven until heated through and lightly browned, about 20 minutes.
4. Meanwhile, warm the sliced turkey in a skillet set over medium heat.
5. Divide turkey between two slices of bread.
6. Place the slices of cheese in a microwave-safe dish and heat in the microwave until melted, about 3 minutes.
7. Top the turkey with French fries then pour melted cheese over them.
8. Top with the remaining slices of bread and serve immediately.
