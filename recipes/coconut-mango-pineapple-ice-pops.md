## Ingredients

- 8 ounces coconut milk
- 7 ounces canned sweetened mango pulp
- 17 ounces unsweetened pineapple juice

## Instructions

1. Whisk together coconut milk and mango pulp in a 4-cup measuring cup. Add enough pineapple juice to make 4 cups. Mix again.
2. Pour into popsicle molds and freeze until solid, at least 4 hours.
