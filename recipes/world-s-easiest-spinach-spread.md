## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
- 8 ounces fresh baby spinach
- 1 whole head garlic, peeled
- ¼ cup grated Romano cheese

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Wrap garlic cloves in aluminum foil, and bake for 45 minutes, or until soft.
3. Bring 3 inches of water to a boil in a large saucepan.
4. Add spinach, cover, and cook for about 2 minutes,  just until wilted and soggy.
5. Remove from heat and drain.
6. Mix the garlic, spinach, olive oil, and Romano cheese.
7. In small batches, use a food processor to process to your desired consistency.
8. Transfer to a serving dish.
