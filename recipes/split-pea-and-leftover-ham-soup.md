## Ingredients

- ¼ pound dried white navy beans  (Optional)
- 1  leftover ham bone
- 1 cup chopped onion
- 2 cloves garlic, diced
- 2 teaspoons ground black pepper
- 1 cup chopped carrots
- 1 ½ pounds dried yellow split peas, rinsed and drained
- 1 cup chopped leftover ham
- salt to taste

## Instructions

1. Soak white navy beans in a bowl of cold water in the refrigerator for 8 hours to overnight.
2. Rinse thoroughly.
3. Place ham bone in a large pot with water to just cover.
4. Bring to a boil and cook for at least 4 hours.
5. Add onion, garlic, and pepper and simmer 1 hour more.
6. Remove ham bone; strip any meat from the bone and return it to the pot.
7. Add chopped leftover ham, soaked and rinsed navy beans, split peas, and carrots and bring soup to a simmer.
8. Cook until peas begin to break down, about 1 hour.
9. Season with salt.
