## Ingredients

- 4  veal shanks, sliced for ossobuco
- 1 dash all-purpose flour
- 1 tablespoon butter, or to taste
- 1 tablespoon olive oil, or to taste
- 1 tablespoon chopped onion
- 1 tablespoon chopped carrot
- ½ cup white wine
- 1 tablespoon tomato puree
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- 1 cup beef stock
- ¼ cup chopped fresh parsley
- ½  lemon, zested

## Instructions

1. Slit the skin around the veal shanks in 2 or 3 places so that slices do not curl when cooked.
2. Dust veal shanks with flour.
3. Heat butter and olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
4. Add onion and carrot; cook and stir until softened, about 5 minutes.
5. Add veal shanks; cook until browned, 3 to 5 minutes per side.
6. Pour in white wine and simmer until reduced, about 5 minutes.
7. Stir tomato puree, salt, and pepper into the skillet.
8. Pour in beef stock gradually, stirring between each addition.
9. Reduce heat to medium-low, cover, and cook, turning veal shanks occasionally, until tender, about 55 minutes.
10. Mix parsley and lemon zest together in a small bowl.
11. Stir into the skillet.
12. Cook until flavors combine, about 5 minutes.
