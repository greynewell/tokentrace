## Ingredients

- 1 pound ground beef
- ¼ cup A.1. Original Steak Sauce, divided
- 4 roll (blank)s hamburger buns, split
- 4 large leaf (blank)s lettuce leaves
- 4 slices tomato

## Instructions

1. Preheat grill to medium-high heat. Mix meat and 2 tablespoons of the steak sauce; shape into four 1/2-inch-thick patties.
2. Grill 5 minutes on each side or until cooked through (160 degrees F), placing buns, cut-sides down, on grill grate for the last 2 minutes.
3. Place lettuce on bottom halves of buns; top with tomatoes, burgers and remaining 2 tablespoons steak sauce. Cover with tops of buns.
