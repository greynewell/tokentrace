## Ingredients

- 1 (8 ounce) package small seashell pasta
- 2 (5 ounce) cans tuna, drained and flaked
- 1 stalk celery, diced
- 2 tablespoons diced green bell pepper
- 1 tablespoon diced onion
- ⅔ cup creamy salad dressing, e.g. Miracle Whip ™

## Instructions

1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
2. Add shell macaroni and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain and rinse with cool water.
3. In a medium bowl, mix together the tuna, celery, green bell pepper, onion and creamy salad dressing.
4. Stuff the shell macaroni with the tuna mixture.
