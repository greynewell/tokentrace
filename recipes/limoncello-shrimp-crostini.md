## Ingredients

- 1  baguette, sliced
- 3 tablespoons butter
- 3 tablespoons olive oil
- ¼ teaspoon dried savory
- ¼ teaspoon dried basil
- ¼ teaspoon dried thyme
- 2  shallots, finely chopped
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 24  large shrimp, peeled and deveined
- 1 cup limoncello liqueur (such as Caravella®)
- ¼ cup fresh lemon juice
- ⅓ cup heavy cream
- salt and ground black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
2. Place 24 baguette slices onto a baking sheet.
3. Bake in the preheated oven until toasted, 15 to 20 minutes.
4. Remove from the oven and let cool.
5. Heat oil and butter in a large pan over medium-high heat.
6. Stir in savory, basil, and thyme; cook for 1 minute.
7. Add shallots and cook until transparent, 5 to 7 minutes.
8. Stir in garlic and cook, being careful not to burn, about 1 minute more.
9. Saute shrimp in the same pan over medium to medium-high heat until they are bright pink on the outside and the meat is opaque, 2 to 3 minutes per side.
10. Transfer shrimp quickly to a separate dish; do not overcook.
11. Pour limoncello and lemon juice into the same pan and cook over medium heat until mixture is reduced by about half, 7 to 9 minutes.
12. Add heavy cream gradually.
13. Season with salt and pepper.
14. Add shrimp back into the pan and heat, stirring to coat, 2 to 3 minutes.
15. Spoon 1 shrimp with sauce onto each baguette slice.
16. Serve.
