## Ingredients

- ½ pound ground pork sausage (such as Odom's Tennessee Pride®)
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 1 cup milk
- 1 package McCormick® Pork Gravy Mix
- 8 ounces processed cheese product (such as Velveeta®), cubed
- 1 (10 ounce) can diced tomatoes and green chiles (such as RO*TEL®), undrained

## Instructions

1. Brown sausage in large skillet on medium-high heat.
2. Drain fat.
3. Set aside.
4. Melt butter in large saucepan on medium heat.
5. Stir in milk and Gravy Mix with a whisk.
6. Stirring frequently, cook on medium heat until gravy comes to boil.
7. Reduce heat and simmer 2 minutes, stirring occasionally.
8. Stir in cooked sausage, cheese and tomatoes.
9. Cook 3 to 5 minutes or until cheese is melted, stirring frequently.
