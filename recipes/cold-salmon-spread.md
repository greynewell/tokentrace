## Ingredients

- 1 (8 ounce) package reduced fat cream cheese, softened
- 4 ounces smoked salmon, chopped
- 2 ½ tablespoons capers, plus
- 1 tablespoon caper juice
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice
- 3 tablespoons dried chives
- 2 tablespoons dried dill weed
- buttery crackers

## Instructions

1. In a bowl, mix together the cream cheese, smoked salmon, capers, caper juice, lemon juice, chives, and dill weed until well blended.
2. Cover, and chill for 1 hour.
3. Serve with buttery crackers.
