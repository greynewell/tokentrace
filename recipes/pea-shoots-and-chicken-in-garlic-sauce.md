## Ingredients

- 2  skinless, boneless chicken breast halves - cut into thin strips
- 1 tablespoon cornstarch
- 1 ½ teaspoons sesame oil
- 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 bunch pea shoots, trimmed
- ¾ cup chicken broth
- salt and black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Toss the strips of chicken breast with the cornstarch in a mixing bowl.
2. Sprinkle with the sesame oil and toss until evenly mixed.
3. Heat the vegetable oil in a wok or large skillet over high heat.
4. Stir in the chicken and cook until lightly browned, about 5 minutes.
5. Add the garlic and cook until fragrant.
6. Stir in the pea shoots just until wilted; pour in the chicken broth and bring to a simmer.
7. Cook and stir a  few minutes until the chicken broth has thickened.
8. Season to taste with salt and pepper.
