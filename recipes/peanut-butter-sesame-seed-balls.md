## Ingredients

- 1 cup whole wheat flour
- ¼ cup oats
- 3 tablespoons ground flax seed
- ½ teaspoon ground cinnamon
- ¼ teaspoon salt  (Optional)
- ⅔ cup crunchy peanut butter
- ½ cup honey
- ½ cup sesame seeds

## Instructions

1. Whisk flour, oats, ground flax seed, cinnamon, and salt together in a bowl; add peanut butter and honey and mix until thoroughly combined.
2. Shape mixture into teaspoon-size balls.
3. Pour about 1/2 the sesame seeds into a large frying pan and cook over low heat, stirring or shaking the pan every minute until fragrant and toasted, 3 to 5 minutes.
4. Transfer toasted seeds to a bowl and toast remaining sesame seeds.
5. Spread toasted seeds into a wide, shallow bowl.
6. Roll peanut butter balls in the toasted sesame seeds to evenly coat.
