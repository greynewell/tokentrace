## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon butter
- ½ cup finely diced onion
- 1 teaspoon minced garlic
- ½ cup chopped fresh green chile peppers
- 1 (5 ounce) can chunk chicken
- 1 ½ teaspoons ground cumin
- 1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of chicken soup
- 1 ¼ cups half-and-half cream
- 1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
- ¼ cup sour cream

## Instructions

1. Melt butter in a large saucepan over medium-high heat. Sauté onion until translucent, 3 to 4 minutes. Stir in garlic, green chiles, chicken meat, and cumin. Cook until flavors are blended, about 1 minute. Stir in cream of chicken soup and half-and-half. Cook until heated through, about 5 minutes.
2. Ladle hot soup into bowls. Top with cheese and a dollop of sour cream.
