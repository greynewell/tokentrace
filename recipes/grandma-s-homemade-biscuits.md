## Ingredients

- butter for greasing
- 2 cups self-rising flour (such as White Lily®)
- ½ cup cold salted butter
- ⅔ cup buttermilk

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 500 degrees F (260 degrees C).
2. Grease a cast iron pan with butter.
3. Pour flour into a large mixing bowl.
4. Cut cold butter into flour using a pastry blender until crumbly.
5. Add buttermilk and mix well.
6. Pat out mixture on a floured surface.
7. Cut out biscuits using a floured cutter.
8. Place into the prepared pan with sides touching.
9. Bake in the preheated oven until golden, about 10 minutes.
