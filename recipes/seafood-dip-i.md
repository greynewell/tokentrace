## Ingredients

- 1 ½ cups cocktail sauce
- ¾ cup imitation crabmeat
- 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened

## Instructions

1. In a bowl, mix the cocktail sauce and crabmeat.
2. Place block of cream cheese on serving dish, and pour cocktail sauce mixture over the top.
