## Ingredients

- 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
- 1 ½ teaspoons baking powder
- ½ cup white sugar
- ½ teaspoon salt
- 1 cup milk
- 1  egg
- ⅓ cup butter, melted
- 1 cup semisweet chocolate chips

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly grease a 12-cup muffin tin or line with paper liners.
2. Combine flour, baking powder, sugar, and salt in a large bowl. Stir in milk, egg, butter, and chocolate chips until combined. Pour batter into the prepared muffin cups.
3. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center of a muffin comes out clean, 15 to 20 minutes.
