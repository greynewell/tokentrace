## Ingredients

- 1 cup evaporated milk
- 1 cup brown sugar
- 3  egg yolks
- ½ cup butter
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 1 cup chopped pecans
- 1 ⅓ cups flaked coconut

## Instructions

1. Combine evaporated milk, brown sugar, butter, egg yolks, and vanilla in a saucepan over medium heat. Cook, stirring constantly, at a low boil until mixture turns a light caramel color and thickens, about 10 minutes.
2. Remove from heat and stir in coconut and pecans. Let cool to room temperature before spreading on cake.
