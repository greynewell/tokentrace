## Ingredients

- 1 cup water
- ⅔ cup quinoa
- 5  carrots, diced
- ⅓ cup currants
- ¼ cup chopped fresh mint
- ¼ cup chopped fresh parsley
- ¼ cup green onions, chopped
- ½ cup olive oil
- ¼ cup lime juice
- 1 teaspoon agave nectar
- ½ teaspoon ground cumin
- ½ teaspoon salt

## Instructions

1. Bring water and quinoa to a boil in a saucepan.
2. Reduce heat to medium-low, cover, and simmer until quinoa is tender and water has been absorbed, about 15 minutes.
3. Remove from heat and let quinoa rest, covered, for 5 minutes.
4. Uncover the saucepan and cool to room temperature.
5. Stir quinoa, carrots, currants, mint, parsley, and green onions together in a bowl.
6. Combine olive oil, lime juice, agave nectar, cumin, and salt in a jar; seal the jar with a lid and shake vigorously until dressing is well-blended.
7. Pour dressing over quinoa mixture and toss to coat.
8. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate until flavors blend, at least 30 minutes.
