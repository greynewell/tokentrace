## Ingredients

- 8  frankfurters
- 8 slices American processed cheese
- 1 (10 ounce) package refrigerated biscuit dough

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Shape a section of biscuit dough into a 3-inch diameter circle. Lay a slice of cheese on dough; place a frankfurter in the center and roll it up. Place roll on a cookie sheet seam-side down. Repeat with remaining sections of dough.
3. Bake in the preheated oven until biscuits are golden brown, about 10 to 15 minutes. Serve hot.
