## Ingredients

- ½ cup firmly packed brown sugar, divided
- ⅓ cup sliced almonds
- ¼ cup all-purpose flour
- 3 tablespoons cold Parkay® Original-stick
- PAM® Baking Spray
- 1 (15 ounce) package refrigerated pie crusts
- 1 pound apples (such as Braeburn), cored, chopped
- ½ teaspoon ground cinnamon
- ½ teaspoon vanilla extract
- Reddi-wip® Original Dairy Whipped Topping

## Instructions

1. Stir together 6 tablespoons brown sugar, almonds and flour in small bowl.
2. Cut in Parkay with pastry blender until mixture resembles coarse crumbs; refrigerate until ready to use.
3. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F.
4. Spray 18 medium nonstick muffin cups with baking spray.
5. Roll each pie crust into a 13-inch circle on floured work surface.
6. Trim and discard edges to make two 10-1/2-inch square crusts.
7. Cut each crust into 9 square pieces.
8. Carefully press pieces into muffin cups; set aside.
9. Combine apples, remaining 2 tablespoons brown sugar, cinnamon and vanilla in large bowl.
10. Spoon 1/4 cup apple mixture into each crust-lined muffin cup.
11. Bake 20 minutes.
12. Top each with crumble mixture; bake 8 to 10 minutes more or until crumble browns.
13. Cool in pan 5 minutes; carefully remove from muffin cups.
14. Top each with Reddi-wip just before serving.
