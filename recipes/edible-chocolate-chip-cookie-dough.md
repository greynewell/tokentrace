## Ingredients

- 1 cup butter, softened
- ¼ cup milk
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 2 ¼ cups all-purpose flour
- ¾ cup brown sugar
- ¾ cup white sugar
- 1 teaspoon baking soda
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 cup chocolate chips
- ½ cup chopped walnuts  (Optional)

## Instructions

1. Mix butter, milk, and vanilla extract together in a large bowl.
2. Combine flour, brown sugar, white sugar, baking soda, and salt in a separate bowl; slowly mix into butter mixture, beating on low speed with an electric mixer until dough is just combined.
3. Fold chocolate chips and walnuts into dough; drop by rounded tablespoon onto waxed paper.
