## Ingredients

- 1 (8 ounce) package deluxe American cheese, diced
- 3 (15 ounce) cans baby peas, drained
- 1 cup creamy salad dressing (such as Miracle Whip®)
- 1 (4 ounce) jar diced pimentos, drained
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ⅛ teaspoon ground black pepper

## Instructions

1. Place the diced cheese into a salad bowl.
2. Gently stir in the peas, salad dressing, pimentos, salt, and pepper until the diced cheese separates into small squares and the salad is thoroughly mixed.
3. Cover and refrigerate 3 hours or overnight to chill and blend the flavors.
