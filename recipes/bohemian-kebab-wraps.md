## Ingredients

- 4 (4 ounce) beef skirt steaks, cut into 1-inch cubes
- 2 large yellow onions, cut into 1-inch pieces
- 2 large green bell peppers, cut into 1-inch pieces
- 2 large red bell peppers, cut into 1-inch pieces
- 1 large yellow bell pepper, cut into 1-inch pieces
- 8  wooden skewers, soaked in water
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
- 1 teaspoon paprika
- 12 (10 inch) flour tortillas
- 1 cup butter, melted
- 1 clove garlic, crushed

## Instructions

1. Skewer steak, onions, green bell peppers, red bell peppers, and yellow bell peppers, leaving 1 inch at the end, until each skewer is full.
2. Season with salt, pepper, and paprika.
3. Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat and lightly oil the grate.
4. Grill the skewers until steak is browned on all sides, about 8 minutes.
5. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center of the steak should read 160 degrees F (70 degrees C).
6. Put tortillas on a microwaveable plate and cover with a damp paper towel.
7. Microwave in 30-second intervals until the tortillas are heated through.
8. Slide the meat and vegetables off the skewers into the tortillas and roll up.
9. Mix butter and garlic in a small bowl until combined.
10. Portion into individual cups and serve alongside the wraps.
