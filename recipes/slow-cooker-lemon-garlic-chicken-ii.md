## Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon dried oregano
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
- 2 pounds skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
- 2 tablespoons butter
- ¼ cup water
- 3 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 teaspoon chicken bouillon granules
- 1 teaspoon chopped fresh parsley

## Instructions

1. Mix together oregano, salt, and pepper in a small bowl. Rub spice mixture all over chicken breasts.
2. Melt butter in a skillet over medium heat. Cook chicken in butter until lightly browned, 3 to 5 minutes per side. Place chicken in a slow cooker.
3. Mix water, lemon juice, garlic, and bouillon in the same skillet; bring to boil. Pour over chicken in the slow cooker.
4. Cover and cook on Low for 6 hours or High for 3 hours. Add parsley to the slow cooker 15 to 30 minutes before the end of cook time.
