## Ingredients

- cooking spray
- 1 pound thinly sliced ham
- ½ pound sliced Swiss cheese
- 2 (12 count) packages Hawaiian bread rolls (such as King's®)
- 1 cup butter, melted
- 4 tablespoons brown sugar
- 2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
- 2 tablespoons prepared yellow mustard
- 2 tablespoons poppy seeds

## Instructions

1. Spray a cookie sheet with cooking spray. Place ham and Swiss cheese slices on the bottom of each bread roll and cover with tops; arrange rolls in an even layer on the prepared sheet.
2. Mix butter, brown sugar, Worcestershire, mustard, and poppy seeds together in a small bowl; spoon over sandwich tops. Let sit, 4 hours to overnight.
3. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
4. Bake in the preheated oven until golden and cheese is melted, about 20 to 25 minutes.
