## Ingredients

- 2 large potatoes, peeled and coarsely shredded
- ¼ cup all-purpose flour
- 1  egg
- 2 slices prosciutto, chopped
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- ¼ cup extra-virgin olive oil, or as needed

## Instructions

1. Place shredded potatoes in a bowl; add flour, egg, and ham and mix well.
2. Season mixture with salt and pepper.
3. Form mixture into fritters.
4. Heat oil in a skillet over medium heat; fry fritters until golden, about 5 minutes per side.
