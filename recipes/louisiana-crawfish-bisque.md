## Ingredients

- ½ cup butter
- ½ cup chopped onion
- ½ cup chopped green onion
- 1 pound crawfish tails
- 2 (10.75 ounce) cans  cream of potato soup
- 1 (10.75 ounce) can  cream of mushroom soup
- 1 (15 ounce) can yellow corn, drained
- 1 (14 ounce) can white corn, drained
- 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese
- 1 pint half-and-half
- 3 tablespoons Creole seasoning (such as Tony Chachere's®)
- salt and ground black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Melt butter in a large pot over medium heat. Add onion and green onion; sauté until softened, about 5 minutes.
2. Add crawfish tails, condensed soups, canned corns, and cream cheese; cook, stirring often, until cream cheese melts and all ingredients are well combined, about 5 minutes.
3. Stir in half-and-half and Creole seasoning; season with salt and pepper. Cook until hot, about 15 minutes.
