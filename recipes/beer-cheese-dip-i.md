## Ingredients

- 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
- 1 (8 ounce) package processed cheese food, diced
- ½ (12 fluid ounce) can beer, room temperature
- 1 clove garlic, peeled and crushed

## Instructions

1. In a medium bowl, whip together cream cheese, processed cheese food, beer and garlic.
2. Continue whipping until smooth.
