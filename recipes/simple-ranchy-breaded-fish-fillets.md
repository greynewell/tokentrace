## Ingredients

- ¾ cup Italian seasoned bread crumbs
- 1 (1 ounce) package dry Ranch-style dressing mix
- 2 ½ tablespoons vegetable oil
- 1 pound tilapia fillets
- 2 teaspoons butter

## Instructions

1. Place bread crumbs in a bowl. In a shallow dish, mix dressing mix and oil to form a paste. Coat tilapia fillets with the paste, then dredge in bread crumbs to lightly coat.
2. Melt butter in a skillet over medium heat. Place fillets in the skillet, and cook 5 minutes on each side, or until golden brown and easily flaked with a fork.
