## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon pesto, or to taste
- 1 slice dense and hearty bread
- 1 slice Swiss cheese
- 2 slices deli-sliced honey ham
- 2 tablespoons butter, divided
- 1  egg

## Instructions

1. Spread pesto over one side of the bread.
2. Top with Swiss cheese.
3. Cover the cheese completely with ham.
4. Heat a skillet over medium-high heat.
5. Add sandwich ham-side down; cook until ham starts to caramelize and cheese begins to melt, 3 to 4 minutes.
6. Spread 1 tablespoon butter carefully over the top side of the bread.
7. Flip and cook sandwich until buttered side is golden brown, 3 to 4 minutes.
8. Meanwhile, melt butter in another skillet.
9. Crack egg into the hot butter and fry until white is set, 3 to 5 minutes.
10. Transfer sandwich to a plate and top with the fried egg.
