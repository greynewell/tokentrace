## Ingredients

- 4  carrots, grated
- 2  apples, chopped
- 1 stalk celery, chopped
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice
- ½ cup raisins
- ½ cup vanilla yogurt
- ½ cup chopped walnuts
- ½ teaspoon ground cinnamon, or to taste

## Instructions

1. Stir the carrots, apples, celery, lemon juice, raisins, yogurt, walnuts, and cinnamon together in a mixing bowl until evenly blended.
2. Refrigerate 1 hour before serving.
