## Ingredients

- 1 pound Italian sausage
- ½ cup diced onion
- 1 tablespoon minced garlic
- ½ teaspoon Italian seasoning
- 1 (48 fluid ounce) can chicken broth
- 1 large carrot, sliced
- 1 (10 ounce) package frozen chopped spinach
- ¼ cup uncooked pearl barley

## Instructions

1. Cook sausage, onion, and garlic in a skillet over medium heat until the sausage is evenly brown. Season with Italian seasoning. Remove from heat and drain.
2. Add sausage mixture, chicken broth, carrot, spinach, and barley into a slow cooker.
3. Cover and cook on High for 4 hours, or Low for 6 to 8 hours.
