## Ingredients

- 2 cups ice cubes
- ½ (1.5 fluid ounce) jigger vodka
- ½ (1.5 fluid ounce) jigger tequila
- ½ (1.5 fluid ounce) jigger rum
- ½ (1.5 fluid ounce) jigger gin
- ½ (1.5 fluid ounce) jigger Blue Curacao
- 1 dash sour mix
- 2 fluid ounces lemon-lime flavored carbonated beverage
- 1 slice fresh lemon

## Instructions

1. Fill a cocktail shaker with ice. Combine vodka, tequila, rum, gin, Blue Curacao, sour mix, and lemon-lime soda in the cocktail shaker. Shake vigorously to get a frothy head on the drink.
2. Pour the contents of the shaker, including ice, into a tall bar glass and garnish with slice of lemon.
