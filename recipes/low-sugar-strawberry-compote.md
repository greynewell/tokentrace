## Ingredients

- 2 cups strawberries, rinsed, hulled, and quartered
- 1 tablespoon honey
- 1 teaspoon lemon juice

## Instructions

1. Heat a saucepan over medium-low heat. Add strawberries, honey, and lemon and crush with a hand-held food chopper or a potato masher. Simmer, uncovered, until strawberries reduce down, about 8 minutes.
2. Stir strawberries and adjust heat to medium-high. Cook for an additional 5 minutes. Serve immediately.
