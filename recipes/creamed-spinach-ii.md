## Ingredients

- 2 (10 ounce) packages frozen chopped spinach
- 1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of mushroom soup
- 1 tablespoon butter
- garlic salt to taste

## Instructions

1. Prepare spinach according to package directions; drain well.
2. In a large saucepan over medium-high heat, combine spinach, mushroom soup and butter. Bring to a low boil. Season with garlic salt to taste.
