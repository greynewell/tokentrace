## Ingredients

- 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
- 3 tablespoons butter, softened
- 1 cup crumbled bleu cheese
- 1 tablespoon chopped fresh chives
- ½ cup chopped green olives

## Instructions

1. In a medium bowl, mix together the cream cheese, butter and blue cheese.
2. Stir in the olives and chives.
3. Spoon onto plastic wrap and gather into a ball.
4. Refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.
5. To serve, unwrap and place on a serving tray.
6. Surround with your favorite crackers.
