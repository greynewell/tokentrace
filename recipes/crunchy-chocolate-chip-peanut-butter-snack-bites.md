## Ingredients

- 1 cup large flake rolled oats
- ½ cup KRAFT Crunchy Peanut Butter
- ¼ cup honey
- ½ cup salted peanuts, chopped
- ½ cup BAKER'S Semi-Sweet Chocolate Chips

## Instructions

1. Mix together all ingredients in a medium bowl until blended. Refrigerate for 20 to 30 min.
2. Roll into 20 (1-inch) balls, using about 2 Tbsp. for each.
