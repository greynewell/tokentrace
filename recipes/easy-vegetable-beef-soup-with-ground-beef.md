## Ingredients

- 2 pounds lean ground beef
- 4 (15 ounce) cans mixed vegetables
- 4 (16 ounce) cans diced tomatoes
- 1  onion, chopped
- ground black pepper to taste
- salt to taste

## Instructions

1. In a large soup pot, cook ground meat over medium heat until browned. Drain grease from the pot.
2. Add chopped onion, mixed vegetables, and tomatoes. Give it a stir. Reduce heat, and simmer for about 3 to 4 hours. Season to taste with salt and pepper.
