## Ingredients

- 2 slices bacon, chopped
- 2  onions, chopped
- 1  green bell pepper, chopped
- 1 stalk celery, chopped
- 1 clove garlic
- 3 cups water, or as needed
- 2 (10 ounce) packages frozen okra, thawed
- 1 (10 ounce) box frozen chopped spinach, thawed
- ¼ teaspoon hot pepper sauce, or to taste
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- 2 (6 ounce) cans crab, drained  (Optional)

## Instructions

1. Heat bacon in a saucepan over medium heat until fat begins to render, about 2 minutes; stir in onions, green bell pepper, celery, and garlic.
2. Cook and stir mixture until onions are translucent, about 5 minutes.
3. Pour water into bacon mixture; stir in okra and spinach.
4. Bring soup to a simmer; cook for 30 minutes.
5. Season with hot pepper sauce, salt, and pepper.
6. Blend soup until smooth using an immersion blender.
7. Alternatively, pour soup into a blender no more than half full.
8. Cover and hold lid down; pulse a few times before leaving on to blend.
9. Puree in batches until smooth.
10. Return soup to a simmer in saucepan over low heat; stir in crab.
11. Simmer until crab is heated through, about 5 minutes.
