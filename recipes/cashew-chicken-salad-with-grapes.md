## Ingredients

- 1 (16 ounce) package rotini pasta
- 4 cups cubed cooked chicken
- 1 (20 ounce) can pineapple tidbits, drained
- 1 ½ cups sliced celery
- 1 cup seedless green grapes, halved
- 1 cup seedless red grapes, halved
- ½ cup Onions, spring or scallions (includes tops and bulb), raw
- 1 (6 ounce) package dried cranberries
- 1 cup ranch dressing
- ⅔ cup mayonnaise
- 2 cups cashews

## Instructions

1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil; cook rotini at a boil until tender yet firm to the bite, about 8 minutes. Drain and run under cold water. Drain again and transfer to a large bowl.
2. Add chicken, pineapple tidbits, celery, green and red grapes, green onions, and cranberries; mix to combine.
3. Stir ranch dressing and mayonnaise together in a second bowl until well mixed. Add to pasta mixture and stir to coat.
4. Refrigerate for at least 1 hour before serving.
