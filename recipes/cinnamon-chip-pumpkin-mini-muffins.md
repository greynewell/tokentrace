## Ingredients

- cooking spray
- 1 (15.25 ounce) package yellow cake mix
- 1 (15 ounce) can pumpkin pie mix
- 1 (10 ounce) package cinnamon chips (such as Hershey's®)

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Spray a mini muffin tin with cooking spray.
2. Combine cake mix, pumpkin pie mix, and cinnamon chips in a large bowl; mix well with a wooden spoon.
3. Scoop mixture into muffin tin, filling each cup 3/4 of the way full.
4. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean, 17 to 20 minutes.
