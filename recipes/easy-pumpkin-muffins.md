## Ingredients

- 1 (18.25 ounce) package yellow cake mix
- 1 (15 ounce) can pumpkin puree
- 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
- ½ teaspoon ground nutmeg
- ¼ teaspoon ground cloves

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 12-cup muffin pan or line with paper liners.
2. Mix together cake mix, pumpkin puree, cinnamon, nutmeg, and cloves in a large bowl until smooth. Spoon equal amounts of batter into the prepared muffin cups.
3. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted in the center of one comes out clean, 20 to 25 minutes.
