## Ingredients

- 1 pint heavy cream
- ¼ cup white sugar
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

## Instructions

1. Place a small bowl and mixer beaters in the freezer for about 5 minutes.
2. Pour cream into the chilled bowl and beat with an electric mixer until frothy. Add sugar and vanilla extract gradually, continuing to beat until soft peaks form.
