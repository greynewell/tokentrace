## Ingredients

- 12 ounces zucchini, halved and cut into 1/4-inch sliced
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 2 teaspoons olive oil
- 2 teaspoons minced garlic
- 1 teaspoon salted fermented shrimp
- ½  yellow onion, sliced
- 1 teaspoon sesame seeds
- ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper

## Instructions

1. Place zucchini slices in a bowl; sprinkle salt on top.
2. Let stand for 10 minutes.
3. Drain off excess moisture.
4. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat.
5. Add zucchini, garlic, and salted shrimp; cook and stir until zucchini softens, about 5 minutes.
6. Add onion, sesame seeds, and black pepper; cook and stir until onion is translucent, about 5 minutes more.
