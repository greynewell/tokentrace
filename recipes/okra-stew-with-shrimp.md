## Ingredients

- 1 pound medium shrimp - peeled and deveined
- ½  lime, juiced
- 4 tablespoons margarine
- 2  green bell peppers, seeded
- 6 tablespoons minced shallots
- 1 cup frozen corn kernels
- 1 cup chopped okra
- 3  tomatoes - blanched, peeled and chopped
- 1 tablespoon tomato paste
- ¼ teaspoon dried thyme
- 1  bay leaf
- salt and pepper to taste
- 1  green chile pepper

## Instructions

1. Place shrimp in mixing bowl and squeeze lime juice on top, trying to coat shrimp evenly.
2. Heat butter in a stock pot and saute the green pepper with shallots or chives for 2 to 3 minutes.
3. Mix in corn, okra, tomatoes, tomato paste, thyme, bay leaf and chili pepper.
4. Season with salt and pepper and  simmer for 10 minutes.
5. Add the shrimp, return to a boil and simmer for another 5 minutes.
6. Remove bay leaf and chili before serving.
