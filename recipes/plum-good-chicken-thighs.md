## Ingredients

- 3  green onions
- 1 pound skinless, boneless chicken thighs
- 1 teaspoon Chinese five-spice powder
- ½ teaspoon salt
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- ½ cup plum jam
- ½ cup chicken broth
- 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar

## Instructions

1. Finely chop 2 tablespoons of green onions and set aside for the garnish.
2. Cut remaining green onions into 1-inch pieces.
3. Toss chicken thighs with five-spice powder and salt in a bowl.
4. Heat oil in a skillet over medium heat.
5. Add chicken thighs and cook until browned, about 5 minutes per side.
6. Transfer to a plate, leaving drippings in the skillet.
7. Cook green onion pieces in the drippings until lightly browned, about 4 minutes.
8. Stir in plum jam, chicken broth, and balsamic vinegar.
9. Bring to a boil.
10. Reduce heat to medium and return thighs to the skillet.
11. Cook until syrupy, turning a few times to coat, about 5 minutes.
12. Garnish with reserved green onions.
