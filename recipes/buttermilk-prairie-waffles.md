## Ingredients

- 2 cups sifted flour
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- 1 teaspoon baking soda
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 4 large eggs, at room temperature
- 2 cups buttermilk
- 1 cup melted butter

## Instructions

1. Preheat a waffle iron according to manufacturer's instructions.
2. Spray waffle iron with non-stick cooking spray or brush with oil.
3. Whisk flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt together in a bowl.
4. Beat eggs in a large bowl until light and fluffy.
5. Stir flour mixture into eggs, one third at a time, alternating with buttermilk, until just combined; stir in butter.
6. Pour batter into preheated waffle iron; cook until golden brown, about 3 minutes.
