## Ingredients

- ½ cup hazelnuts
- 2 tablespoons butter, softened
- 1 cup butter
- 3  egg yolks
- 1 pinch salt
- 1 pinch white pepper
- 1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
2. Place the hazelnuts on a baking sheet and toast in the oven for about 8 minutes, or until fragrant.
3. Remove from the oven and cool.
4. When cool enough to handle, place the nuts on a kitchen towel, fold the towel over to cover them and rub to remove the skins.
5. Place the cooled hazelnuts into a food processor or blender and grind to a fine powder.
6. Add 2 tablespoons of butter to the nuts and blend thoroughly.
7. Set the hazelnut butter aside.
8. Melt 1 cup of butter in a small saucepan, and keep hot.
9. In the container of a blender, combine the egg yolks, salt, pepper and lemon juice.
10. Cover, leaving the hole in the lid open, and blend for about 5 seconds.
11. Continue to blend at high speed while pouring butter in through the lid in a thin stream.
12. Stir in the hazelnut butter while still warm.
13. Keep warm until serving or serve immediately.
