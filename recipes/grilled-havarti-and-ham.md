## Ingredients

- 4 thin slices  smoked honey ham
- ¼ cup chopped spinach
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- 2 slices potato bread
- 4 slices Havarti cheese
- 1 tablespoon butter

## Instructions

1. Place ham in a skillet over medium-high heat.
2. Saute until lightly browned, about 2 minutes per side.
3. Remove and transfer to a plate.
4. Cook spinach in the same skillet until heated through, about 2 minutes.
5. Season with salt and pepper.
6. Place ham on a slice of bread.
7. Add Havarti cheese and spinach.
8. Cover with the second slice.
9. Allow cheese to melt slightly, about 1 minute.
10. Melt butter in the skillet over medium heat.
11. Place sandwich in the hot butter; cook until golden brown, about 3 minutes per side.
