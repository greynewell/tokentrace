## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- ¼ cup chopped Vidalia onion
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 3 pounds young green jackfruit packed in water, drained
- 2 cups barbeque sauce, or more to taste

## Instructions

1. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Cook and stir onion and garlic until slightly softened, about 3 minutes. Add jackfruit; cook and stir until lightly browned and relatively free of moisture, about 10 minutes.
2. Transfer jackfruit mixture to a slow cooker; cover with barbeque sauce. Cook on Low, stirring occasionally, until jackfruit shreds easily with a fork, about 6 hours.
