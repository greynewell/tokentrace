## Ingredients

- ½ sheet puff pastry
- 3 tablespoons butter
- ¼ pound mushrooms, sliced
- 2 cloves garlic, crushed
- ½ cup crumbled goat cheese
- 4 teaspoons finely chopped fresh parsley

## Instructions

1. Preheat an oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
2. Lightly grease a baking tray.
3. Lay puff pastry out on a lightly floured surface and cut into 8 squares.
4. Prick the squares in several places with a fork.
5. Place the puff pastry squares on prepared baking sheet.
6. Bake the puff pastry squares for 7 minutes.
7. Remove tray from the oven, flatten the squares using the backside of a spatula.
8. Return puff pastry to the oven to bake until golden brown, 5 to 8 minutes.
9. Remove from oven.
10. Meanwhile, melt butter in a skillet over high heat.
11. Cook and stir the mushrooms and garlic in the butter  until mushrooms are tender, 5 to 7 minutes.
12. Remove from heat.
13. Top the baked puff pastry squares evenly with the mushroom mixture.
14. Crumble goat cheese over mushrooms and sprinkle with parsley.
