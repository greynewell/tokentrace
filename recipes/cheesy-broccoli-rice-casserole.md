## Ingredients

- 1 (8 ounce) package processed cheese, cubed
- 1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of chicken soup
- 1 cup cooked wild rice
- 1 (10 ounce) package frozen chopped broccoli, thawed
- ½ cup chopped onion
- ½ cup chopped celery
- 1 dash hot sauce
- ground black pepper to taste
- 1 (2.8 ounce) can French-fried onions

## Instructions

1. Place cheese in a 2-quart microwave-safe casserole dish; cook in microwave until mostly melted, 1 to 2 minutes.
2. Stir in chicken soup, wild rice, broccoli, chopped onion, celery, hot sauce, and black pepper.
3. Microwave until fully cooked and bubbling, 12 to 14 minutes, rotating halfway through cooking.
4. Sprinkle French-fried onions over casserole; cook for 1 more minute.
