## Ingredients

- ⅓ cup honey
- 6 cloves garlic
- 4  chipotle peppers in adobo sauce
- 6  skinless, boneless chicken breasts, or more to taste
- 2 (15 ounce) cans black beans, drained and rinsed

## Instructions

1. Blend honey, garlic, and chipotle peppers in adobo sauce together in a food processor until smooth.
2. Place chicken breasts in the bottom of a slow cooker and mix in honey-chipotle sauce.
3. Cook in the slow cooker on Low for 5 hours.
4. Transfer chicken to a bowl and shred using 2 forks. Return chicken to the slow cooker and stir in black beans.
5. Continue to cook on Low until cooked through, about 30 minutes more.
