## Ingredients

- 1 cup sourdough starter
- ⅓ cup vegetable oil
- ¾ teaspoon baking soda
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- 1 cup all-purpose flour

## Instructions

1. Sift flour, salt, and soda together into a large bowl.
2. Mix starter with oil, and stir into the sifted ingredients.
3. Drop dough by tablespoons onto an ungreased baking sheet.
4. Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 10 to 15 minutes.
5. Serve warm.
