## Ingredients

- 12 ounces fresh mushrooms, stems removed
- 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
- 1 (6 ounce) can crabmeat
- 2 teaspoons fresh lemon juice
- 3 tablespoons butter
- 3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
- 1 ¼ cups milk
- 2  egg yolks
- 6 ounces sharp Cheddar cheese, shredded
- 2 tablespoons cooking sherry

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to broil.
2. Place mushroom caps on a baking sheet, and brush with oil.
3. Broil until soft and slightly browned.
4. Meanwhile, melt butter in a saucepan over medium heat.
5. Stir in flour to make a paste.
6. Whisk in milk; cook, stirring frequently, until hot.
7. In a small bowl, beat egg yolks together, then pour a small amount of the hot milk mixture into the yolks.
8. Whisk this yolk mixture into milk in saucepan.
9. Remove from heat, and stir in 1 1/4 cups cheese.
10. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
11. Place mushroom caps, hollow side up,  in an 8 inch, round baking dish.
12. Cover with crab, and sprinkle with lemon juice.
13. Pour cheese sauce over mushrooms and crab, and top with remaining 1/4 cup cheese.
14. Bake in preheated oven for 20 minutes, or until hot and bubbly.
