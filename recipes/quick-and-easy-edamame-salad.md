## Ingredients

- 3 cups hulled edamame
- 1 (15.5 ounce) can black beans, rinsed and drained
- ¾ cup frozen corn, thawed
- ½ cup diced red onion
- ½ cup diced red bell pepper
- 2 tablespoons ginger sesame dressing, or to taste
- 2 teaspoons salt

## Instructions

1. Combine edamame, black beans, corn, red onion, red pepper, ginger sesame dressing, and salt in a bowl; stir until well mixed.
2. Place in the refrigerator until ready to serve.
