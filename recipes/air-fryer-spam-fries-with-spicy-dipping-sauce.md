## Ingredients

- ½ cup mayonnaise
- 2 teaspoons Sriracha sauce
- 1 teaspoon ketchup
- 1 (12 ounce) container fully cooked luncheon meat (such as SPAM®), sliced into fries

## Instructions

1. Preheat the air fryer to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
2. Whisk mayonnaise, Sriracha, and ketchup together in a small bowl. Set dipping sauce aside.
3. Place luncheon meat fries into the air fryer basket without overcrowding. Work in batches if needed. Fry for 6 minutes. Flip fries using tongs. Cook to desired crispness, about 4 minutes more. Transfer to a paper towel-lined plate. Serve with dipping sauce.
