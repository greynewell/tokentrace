## Ingredients

- ½ cup butter
- 2  egg yolks
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice
- 1 tablespoon milk
- salt and ground black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Heat butter in a microwave-safe measuring cup in the microwave until melted, about 30 seconds.
2. Whisk egg yolks, lemon juice, milk, salt, and black pepper together in a microwave-safe bowl. Whisk melted butter, a bit at a time, into egg yolk mixture until sauce is smooth.
3. Cook sauce in the microwave on medium, whisking at 15-second intervals, until sauce is hot and thick, about 1 minute.
