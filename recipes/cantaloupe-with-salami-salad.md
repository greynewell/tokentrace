## Ingredients

- ½  cantaloupe - peeled, seeded, and thinly sliced into strips
- ¼ pound thinly sliced spicy salami, cut into strips
- 2 ounces Parmesan cheese, thinly shaved
- ¼ cup arugula, chopped
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
- 1  lime, juiced

## Instructions

1. Arrange cantaloupe, salami, and Parmesan cheese on a platter.
2. Sprinkle arugula over cantaloupe mixture; season with salt and pepper.
3. Drizzle olive oil and lime juice over salad.
