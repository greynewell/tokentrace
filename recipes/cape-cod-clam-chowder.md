## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons butter
- ½  yellow onion, chopped
- 2 (8 ounce) bottles clam juice
- 1  baking potato, peeled and diced
- ½ cup heavy cream
- 2 (6.5 ounce) cans minced clams, drained
- salt and ground black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Melt butter in a saucepan over medium-low heat.
2. Cook onions in melted butter until clear, 3 to 4 minutes.
3. Pour clam juice into the saucepan.
4. Add the potato; bring the mixture to a boil.
5. Cook at a boil until potatoes are tender, about 10 minutes.
6. Reduce heat to medium-low.
7. Stir heavy cream into the mixture; add clams.
8. Continue cooking until the clams are warmed, 2 to 3 minutes.
9. Season with salt and pepper.
