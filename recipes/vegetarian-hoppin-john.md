## Ingredients

- 2 ½ cups vegetable broth
- 1 cup long grain rice
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 1  onion, chopped
- 2 (15 ounce) cans black-eyed peas, rinsed and drained
- ½ teaspoon Cajun seasoning

## Instructions

1. Bring broth and rice to a boil in a saucepan. Reduce heat to medium-low, cover, and simmer until rice is tender and liquid has been absorbed, 20 to 25 minutes.
2. Heat olive oil in a pot over medium-high heat. Sauté onion in hot oil until translucent, 5 to 7 minutes. Stir cooked rice, black-eyed peas, and Cajun seasoning into onion.
3. Cover the pot with a lid and cook until flavors blend, about 10 minutes.
