## Ingredients

- 5  skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
- 1 ½ (1 ounce) packages dry onion soup mix
- 2 cups water
- 1 (15 ounce) can pineapple, drained
- 1 large orange, sliced in rounds
- 1 tablespoon vegetable oil

## Instructions

1. Spray large frying pan or electric skillet with vegetable spray.
2. Brown chicken breasts in pan, meat side down.
3. Turn chicken over; add pineapple chunks, onion soup, and water.
4. Cover, and reduce heat to low.
5. Simmer for 30 minutes.
6. Garnish with cooked pineapple chunks and fresh orange slices.
