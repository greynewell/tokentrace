## Ingredients

- 1 (15.25 ounce) can sweet corn, drained
- 1 cup cornbread mix (such as Jiffy®)
- 1 cup pancake mix with buttermilk
- ½ cup evaporated milk
- ½ cup white sugar
- ⅓ cup vegetable oil
- 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, softened
- 1 tablespoon honey
- ½ teaspoon salt

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 9x13-inch baking pan.
2. Combine corn, cornbread mix, pancake mix, evaporated milk, sugar, oil, butter, honey, and salt in a large bowl; whisk together. Pour into the prepared baking pan.
3. Bake in the preheated oven until golden and a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean, about 30 minutes.
