## Ingredients

- 1 (1 pound) loaf French bread
- 10 ounces ahi (yellowfin) tuna, sushi-grade - sliced 1 inch long and 1/8 inch thick
- ¼ cup onion, cut into 1/8-inch dice
- 2 tablespoons capers, drained
- 3 ounces olive oil
- 3 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
- 1 teaspoon kosher salt
- ¼ teaspoon freshly ground black pepper

## Instructions

1. Slice the French bread 1/4 inch thick, and set aside.
2. Place the tuna slices in a single layer in a flat nonreactive dish.
3. Sprinkle the tuna with onion and capers; drizzle with olive oil and lemon juice.
4. Sprinkle with kosher salt and black pepper.
5. Serve slices of tuna on sliced French bread.
