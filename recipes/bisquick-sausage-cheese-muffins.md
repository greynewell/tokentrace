## Ingredients

- cooking spray
- 4 cups baking mix (such as Bisquick®)
- 4 pounds pre-cooked sausage
- 16  eggs, beaten
- 4 cups shredded Cheddar cheese

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Coat 24 muffin cups with nonstick cooking spray.
3. Mix baking mix, sausage, eggs, and Cheddar cheese together in a large bowl.
4. Pour into the prepared muffin cups.
5. Bake in the preheated oven until golden brown, about 20 minutes.
