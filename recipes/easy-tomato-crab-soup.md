## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- ¼ cup chopped onion
- 1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed tomato soup
- 1 (14.5 ounce) can diced tomatoes
- 1 (6 ounce) can crabmeat
- 1 pint half-and-half cream

## Instructions

1. In a large saucepan over medium heat, cook garlic and onion in oil until softened.
2. Stir in tomato soup, tomatoes and crabmeat and heat through.
3. Stir in half-and-half and cook until bubbly.
