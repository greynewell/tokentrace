## Ingredients

- 1 (7.2 ounce) package Original Snack Factory® Pretzel Crisps®
- 1 pint cherry tomatoes, sliced
- 1 (4 ounce) package fresh basil, thinly sliced
- ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese

## Instructions

1. Arrange Pretzel Crisps® on a baking sheet.
2. Top with basil, Parmesan cheese, and tomato slices.
3. Set oven rack about 6 inches from the heat source and preheat the oven's broiler.
4. Place baking pan in preheated oven.
5. Broil until cheese has melted, 2 or 3 minutes.
6. Let cool and serve.
