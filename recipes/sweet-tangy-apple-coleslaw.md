## Ingredients

- ¾ cup MIRACLE WHIP Light Dressing
- 1 tablespoon honey
- 1 (16 ounce) package coleslaw blend (cabbage slaw mix)
- 2  apples (preferably red and green), chopped

## Instructions

1. Mix dressing and honey in large bowl until blended.
2. Add remaining ingredients; mix lightly.
3. Refrigerate 1 hour.
