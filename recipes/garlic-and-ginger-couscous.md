## Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon olive oil, or more to taste
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 ¼ teaspoons freshly grated ginger
- 1 ⅓ cups chicken broth
- 1 cup couscous

## Instructions

1. Heat oil in a small saucepan over medium-high heat.
2. Saute garlic and ginger in hot oil until fragrant, about 1 minute.
3. Pour chicken broth into garlic mixture and bring to a boil; remove from heat and stir in couscous.
4. Cover the saucepan with a lid and let stand until broth is completely absorbed, 5 to 7 minutes.
5. Fluff couscous with a fork.
