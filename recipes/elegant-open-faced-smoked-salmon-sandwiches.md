## Ingredients

- 4 slices bread
- 2 tablespoons salted butter, softened
- 1 teaspoon finely chopped scallion
- 1 teaspoon finely chopped fresh parsley
- ½ teaspoon bottled minced garlic
- 8 slices smoked salmon
- ½  cucumber, thinly sliced
- ½  lemon

## Instructions

1. Toast bread slices in a toaster until golden and crispy, 1 to 2 minutes.
2. Combine butter, scallion, parsley, and garlic in a small bowl.
3. Spread each piece of bread with 1/2 tablespoon of herbed butter mixture. Layer with 2 slices of smoked salmon, followed by cucumber. Top with a squeeze of lemon juice. Cut on an angle and serve immediately.
