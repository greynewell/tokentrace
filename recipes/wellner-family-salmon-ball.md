## Ingredients

- 1 (16 ounce) can red salmon, drained
- 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice
- 2 teaspoons grated onion
- 1 teaspoon prepared horseradish
- 1 teaspoon liquid smoke
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- 1 dash Worcestershire sauce
- ½ cup chopped walnuts, or as needed

## Instructions

1. Combine salmon, cream cheese, lemon juice, onion, horseradish, liquid smoke, salt, and Worcestershire in a large bowl; beat with an electric mixer until fully incorporated.
2. Wrap cheese mixture in plastic wrap and form into a ball. Place in the refrigerator for 8 hours to overnight. Roll in chopped walnuts before serving.
