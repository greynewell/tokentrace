## Ingredients

- 2  bananas
- 2  eggs
- ¼ cup cacao powder
- ½ teaspoon chia seeds, or to taste  (Optional)

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Line a muffin tin with cupcake liners.
3. Mash bananas in a bowl.
4. Mix in eggs.
5. Add cacao powder; mix until batter is well combined.
6. Fill each cupcake liner with 1 ice cream scoopful of batter.
7. Sprinkle a pinch of chia seeds on top of each muffin.
8. Bake in the preheated oven until tops spring back when lightly pressed, 11 to 13 minutes.
9. Cool on a rack for about 2 minutes.
