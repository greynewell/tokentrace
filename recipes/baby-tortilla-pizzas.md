## Ingredients

- cooking spray
- 1 (8 ounce) package flour tortillas
- ½ cup pizza sauce
- 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
- 1 (8 ounce) package sliced pepperoni

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Grease a 12-cup muffin tin with cooking spray.
3. Use a round cookie cutter or small mason jar lid to cut circles from the tortillas that are slightly larger than the bottom of the muffin cups.
4. Place 1 circle in the bottom of each cup.
5. Spoon some pizza sauce over each tortilla circle; top with some mozzarella cheese and 1 pepperoni slice.
6. Bake in the preheated oven until cheese is melted and golden, 10 to 12 minutes.
7. Scoop out of the muffin cups using a spoon.
