## Ingredients

- 1 ¾ cups all-purpose flour
- 1 teaspoon baking soda
- 1 teaspoon salt
- ½ teaspoon ground cinnamon
- ¼ teaspoon baking powder
- ¼ teaspoon ground cloves
- ⅓ cup buttermilk
- ½ teaspoon vanilla extract
- 1 ⅓ cups raw sugar
- ⅓ cup shortening, softened
- 2  eggs
- 1 cup pumpkin puree
- ⅓ cup chopped dates

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Grease a 9x5-inch loaf pan.
3. Sift flour, baking soda, salt, cinnamon, baking powder, and cloves together in a bowl.
4. Mix buttermilk and vanilla extract together in a small bowl.
5. Beat sugar and shortening in a large bowl with an electric mixer until light and fluffy.
6. Beat in eggs.
7. Beat in pumpkin puree.
8. Add flour mixture in 3 batches, alternating with buttermilk mixture, beating batter briefly after each addition.
9. Fold dates into the batter.
10. Pour batter into the prepared loaf pan.
11. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean, about 1 hour.
