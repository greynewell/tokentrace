## Ingredients

- 1  Idahoan® Buttery Homestyle Mashed Potato Cup
- 4 frankfurter (5 in long x 3/4 in dia, 10 per pound)s Kosher-style hot dogs
- 3 tablespoons stone ground mustard
- 4 each potato flour buns
- ¼ cup Celery, chopped
- 4 medium (3/4" to 1" dia)s Radishes, finely sliced
- 4 each Kosher pickle spears
- 1 tablespoon Yellow mustard
- 1 tablespoon Red onion, finely diced

## Instructions

1. Grill hot dogs until done.
2. Prepare Idahoan Buttery Homestyle Mashed Potato Cup according to package directions and stir in stone ground mustard.
3. Place not dog on bun and spread potato mixture on top.
4. Add toppings to your taste.
5. No ketchup needed!
