## Ingredients

- 7 cups sliced cucumbers
- salt as needed
- 1 cup sliced onions
- 1 cup sliced green bell pepper  (Optional)
- 2 cups white sugar
- 1 cup white vinegar
- 1 tablespoon celery seed  (Optional)
- 2 teaspoons salt, or to taste

## Instructions

1. Place cucumber slices in a colander and liberally sprinkle salt over cucumbers; set aside until water starts to release from cucumbers, about 15 minutes. Drain and rinse cucumbers.
2. Mix cucumbers, onions, and green bell pepper together in a bowl.
3. Whisk sugar, vinegar, celery seed, and 2 teaspoons salt together in a bowl until dressing is smooth; pour over cucumber mixture and stir until evenly coated.
