## Ingredients

- 1 (1.5 pound) salmon fillet, skin removed and quartered
- 1  lemon, juiced
- 1 tablespoon chopped rinsed capers
- 1 tablespoon chopped ripe black olives
- 1 teaspoon white wine vinegar
- 1 large clove garlic, minced
- 2  roma (plum) tomatoes, chopped
- 1 ½ teaspoons chopped fresh basil

## Instructions

1. Combine lemon juice, capers, olives, vinegar, garlic, and enough water to cover the salmon in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
2. Bring to a boil and add salmon.
3. Reduce heat to medium-low.
4. Let simmer, covered, until salmon is opaque and easily flaked with a fork, about 10 minutes.
5. Remove salmon from the liquid.
6. Let liquid simmer until reduced by half, about 5 minutes.
7. Add tomatoes and basil; cook and stir the sauce until tomatoes begin to break down, about 3 minutes.
8. Spoon sauce over the salmon to serve.
