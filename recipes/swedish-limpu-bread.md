## Ingredients

- 5 ½ cups all-purpose flour
- 2 (.25 ounce) packages active dry yeast
- 2 cups water
- ½ cup packed brown sugar
- 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
- 2 teaspoons salt
- 2 teaspoons orange zest
- 1 ½ teaspoons caraway seed
- 1 ½ teaspoons fennel seed
- 2  eggs, room temperature
- 2 cups rye flour

## Instructions

1. Boil water, sugar, oil, salt, orange rind, caraway seeds, and fennel seeds in a saucepan for 3 minutes.
2. Cool until warm In a large bowl, whisk together 3 cups flour and yeast.
3. Stir in cooled orange rind mixture.
4. Beat with an electric mixer on medium speed for 2 minutes.
5. Blend in eggs.
6. Add 1 cup flour, and beat 1 minute on medium speed.
7. Add rye flour and enough additional white flour to make a stiff dough.
8. Turn dough onto a lightly floured surface.
9. Knead for 8 to 10 minutes, or until smooth and satiny.
10. Shape into a ball.
11. Place in lightly greased bowl, turning to grease  the surface.
12. Cover with a damp cloth, and place in a warm spot.
13. Allow to rise for 1 1/2 hours, or until doubled.
14. Punch dough down, and divide in half.
15. Shape into 2 balls.
16. Let rest for 10 min.
17. Shape into 2  loaves, and place into ungreased 9 x 5 inch loaf pans.
18. Brush with oil.
19. Allow to rise for 1 hour, or until doubled in size.
20. Bake at 400 degrees F (205 degrees C) for 30 to 35 minutes, or until done.
