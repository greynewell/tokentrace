## Ingredients

- 8  fingerling potatoes, cubed
- 1 large onion, cubed
- 2 tablespoons coconut oil, melted
- 2 tablespoons minced garlic
- 1 tablespoon sea salt

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Line a large baking sheet with foil.
3. Toss fingerling potatoes, onion, coconut oil, garlic, and sea salt in a large bowl.
4. Spread mixture onto baking sheet; bake, stirring every 10 minutes, until potatoes are tender, about 30 to 40 minutes.
