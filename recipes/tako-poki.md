## Ingredients

- 1 pound cooked octopus, cut into bite-sized pieces
- 1 bunch green onions, thinly sliced
- ½ cup sesame oil
- ¼ cup kosher salt (such as Morton®)

## Instructions

1. Combine the octopus, green onions, sesame oil, and kosher salt in a bowl; mix well.
2. Cover and refrigerate 30 minutes before serving.
