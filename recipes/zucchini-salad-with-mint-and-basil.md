## Ingredients

- 2 cups water
- ½  red onion, finely chopped
- 6 cups small zucchini, thinly sliced and seeds removed
- 1 tablespoon finely chopped fresh mint
- 1 tablespoon finely chopped fresh basil
- 4 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
- 1 teaspoon lemon juice, or more to taste
- ½ teaspoon finely grated fresh lemon zest
- salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Bring water in a saucepan to a boil, add onion in a sieve and blanch for 1 minute.
2. Remove and rinse under cold running water.
3. Add zucchini to the pot with boiling water and cook for 30 seconds.
4. Drain and put zucchini into a bowl with ice water.
5. Move zucchini around so they cool evenly.
6. Drain well.
7. Combine zucchini, mint, and basil in a large salad bowl.
8. Mix oil, lemon juice, lemon zest, salt, and pepper in a small cup and pour over salad.
9. Mix and refrigerate for 30 minutes.
