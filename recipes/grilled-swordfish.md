## Ingredients

- 4 (8 ounce) swordfish steaks
- ½ cup teriyaki sauce
- 2 tablespoons margarine, softened
- 1 teaspoon garlic powder

## Instructions

1. Preheat an outdoor grill for medium heat and lightly oil the grate.
2. Meanwhile, marinate swordfish in teriyaki sauce, 5 minutes per side.
3. Grill swordfish, basting frequently with melted margarine, until fish flakes easily with a fork, 5 to 6 minutes per side.
4. Season with garlic powder and serve.
