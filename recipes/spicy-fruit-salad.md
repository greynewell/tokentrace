## Ingredients

- 1 (8 ounce) can pineapple chunks, drained
- 1 cup cubed, seeded watermelon
- 1  mango, peeled and cubed
- 1  green apple, cored and sliced
- ¼ cup lime juice
- 2 tablespoons honey, or more to taste
- 1  jalapeno pepper, seeded and chopped
- 2 teaspoons chili-lime seasoning (such as Tajin®)

## Instructions

1. Mix pineapple, watermelon, mango, and green apple in a large bowl.
2. Blend lime juice, honey, and jalapeno in a blender until smooth.
3. Pour jalapeno dressing into the large bowl and mix thoroughly. Sprinkle chili-lime seasoning on top. Chill for 1 to 2 hours before serving.
