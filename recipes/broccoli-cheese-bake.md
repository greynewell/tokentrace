## Ingredients

- 8 cups fresh broccoli
- ½ cup butter
- 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
- 1 small onion, chopped
- 1 ¼ cups milk
- salt and pepper to taste
- 4 cups shredded Swiss cheese
- 2  eggs, beaten

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C).
2. Place broccoli in a steamer over 1 inch boiling water; cover. Cook until tender but still firm, 2 to 6 minutes. Drain.
3. Melt butter in a medium saucepan over medium heat. Add flour; cook and stir until bubbly. Stir in onion, then milk. Bring to a boil and cook for 1 minute. Remove from heat; season with salt and pepper.
4. Mix in cheese and eggs until cheese is melted. Add broccoli and stir until combined. Transfer mixture to a 9x13-inch casserole dish.
5. Bake in the preheated oven until bubbly, about 30 minutes.
