## Ingredients

- 1 (12 ounce) package pork sausage links
- 2  eggs
- 1 cup all-purpose flour
- ½ cup water
- ½ cup milk

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
2. Brown sausage in a large skillet; drain and place sausage in a 9x13 inch baking dish.
3. In a small bowl beat eggs; add flour, water and milk and beat all together.
4. Let sit 5 minutes.
5. Pour mixture over sausage and bake in preheated oven for 25 to 30 minutes, or until top is browned and crusty.
