## Ingredients

- 12 cups all-purpose flour
- 6 cups white sugar
- 12 teaspoons baking powder
- 6 teaspoons baking soda
- 6 teaspoons salt
- ½ cup vegetable oil
- 2  eggs

## Instructions

1. Mix thoroughly flour, sugar, baking powder, soda, and salt. (Can be stored on the shelf for months.) Mix 3 cups baking mix, oil, eggs, and 1 cup vegetables or fruits.
2. Add flavorings, nuts, raisins, dates, etc., if desired.
3. Pour into one 8-1/2x4-1/2 inch or two 7-1/4x3-1/2 inch greased loaf pans.
4. Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 30 to 45 minutes.
