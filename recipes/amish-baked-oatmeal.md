## Ingredients

- 3 cups quick-cooking oats
- 1 cup packed brown sugar
- 1 cup milk
- ½ cup vegetable oil
- 2  eggs, beaten
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly grease a 9x13-inch baking pan.
2. Mix oats, brown sugar, milk, oil, eggs, baking powder, salt, and cinnamon together in a bowl using an electric mixer. Pour into the prepared pan.
3. Bake in the preheated oven until set, 25 to 30 minutes, making sure not to let brown.
