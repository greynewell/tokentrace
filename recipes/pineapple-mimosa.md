## Ingredients

- 1 cup chopped fresh pineapple
- 2 ounces pineapple juice
- 6 ounces champagne

## Instructions

1. Combine pineapple and pineapple juice in a blender and blend until smooth.
2. Pour into 2 glasses and top each with 3 ounces champagne.
