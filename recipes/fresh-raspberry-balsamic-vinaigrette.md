## Ingredients

- 1 cup fresh raspberries
- 1 tablespoon white sugar
- ⅔ cup balsamic vinegar
- ¼ cup olive oil
- 1 tablespoon honey
- ½ teaspoon salt

## Instructions

1. Mix raspberries and sugar together in a bowl; set aside until mixture is juicy, about 10 minutes. Mash berries using a fork until liquefied.
2. Pour raspberry mixture into a clean jar with a lid; add vinegar, oil, honey, and salt. Cover the jar with a lid and shake until dressing is mixed well. Store in the refrigerator.
