## Ingredients

- ½ cup softened butter
- 2 tablespoons sweetened condensed milk
- 5 tablespoons honey, or to taste

## Instructions

1. Place the butter, milk, and honey into a food processor or blender.
2. Process until the mixture is smooth and has turned a light cream color.
3. Refrigerate until ready to serve.
