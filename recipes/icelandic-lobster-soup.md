## Ingredients

- 10 tablespoons butter, divided
- 2 large onions, coarsely chopped
- 5 cloves garlic, coarsely chopped
- 2 tablespoons salt
- 2 pounds lobster tails - shelled, meat coarsely chopped, and shells reserved
- 8  button mushrooms, chopped
- 1  Red Delicious apple, coarsely chopped
- 1  red bell pepper, coarsely chopped
- 4  carrots, coarsely chopped
- ¼ cup tomato paste
- 3 ½ quarts water
- 2 tablespoons ground paprika
- 2 tablespoons curry powder
- 2 tablespoons dried tarragon
- 2 teaspoons chili powder
- 2 teaspoons ground cayenne pepper
- ¼ cup all-purpose flour
- 1 cup heavy whipping cream

## Instructions

1. Melt 6 tablespoons butter in a large pot over medium heat.
2. Add onions, garlic, and salt; cook and stir until onions soften, about 5 minutes.
3. Add lobster shells, mushrooms, apple, red bell pepper, carrots, and tomato paste.
4. Cook and stir until vegetables soften, about 7 minutes.
5. Add water, paprika, curry, tarragon, chili powder, and cayenne pepper.
6. Simmer soup over medium-low heat, uncovered, stirring occasionally, about 2 hours.
7. Remove soup from heat; strain liquid through a mesh colander into a bowl to yield about 2 quarts.
8. Discard lobster shells and vegetables.
9. Rinse the pot.
10. Melt remaining 4 tablespoons butter in the pot over medium heat.
11. Add flour; mix vigorously into a thick roux, scraping the bottom of the pot, 2 to 3 minutes.
12. Add the strained liquid, 2 cups at a time, mixing well until entirely smooth, about 5 minutes.
13. Stir in heavy cream, maintaining a low simmer.
14. Divide lobster meat evenly into serving bowls; ladle hot soup generously on top.
