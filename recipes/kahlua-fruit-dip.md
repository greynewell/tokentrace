## Ingredients

- 1 (8 ounce) container frozen whipped topping, thawed
- 1 (3 ounce) package instant vanilla pudding mix
- ½ cup milk
- ½ cup coffee-flavored liqueur (such as Kahlua®)

## Instructions

1. Mix whipped topping, pudding mix, milk, and coffee-flavored liqueur together in a bowl.
2. Refrigerate dip until set, 1 hour to overnight.
