## Ingredients

- 2 (1 pound) loaves Mexican-style processed cheese food, cubed
- ⅓ cup mayonnaise
- 3 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
- ½ pound cooked and peeled shrimp
- ½ pound cooked crabmeat

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. In a medium baking dish, mix Mexican-style processed cheese food, mayonnaise, Worcestershire sauce, shrimp and crabmeat.
3. Bake in the preheated oven 45 minutes, or until bubbly and lightly browned.
