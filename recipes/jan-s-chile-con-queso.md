## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon butter
- ½ cup finely chopped onion
- 2  tomatoes, chopped
- 1 (4 ounce) can chopped green chiles
- ½ teaspoon salt
- 1 ½ cups shredded Cheddar cheese

## Instructions

1. Melt butter in a skillet; cook and stir onion just until soft and translucent, about 3 minutes.
2. Stir tomatoes, green chiles, and salt into onion mixture; reduce heat to low and simmer, uncovered, until liquid evaporates, about 10 minutes.
3. Stir Cheddar cheese into green chile mixture; cook until melted, about 2 minutes.
4. Serve immediately.
