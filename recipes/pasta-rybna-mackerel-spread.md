## Ingredients

- 6 ounces canned mackerel fillets in oil
- 2  hard-boiled eggs, diced
- 1  onion, chopped
- 1 tablespoon mayonnaise  (Optional)
- 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley, or to taste  (Optional)
- 1 pinch ground black pepper

## Instructions

1. Drain mackerel and place in a large bowl.
2. Add eggs, onion, mayonnaise, parsley, and black pepper; mix well to combine.
