## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons butter
- 1 teaspoon crushed garlic
- 2 tablespoons slivered almonds
- 1 (8 ounce) can sliced mushrooms, drained
- 1 tablespoon brandy
- 1 teaspoon dried tarragon
- 1 (8 ounce) wedge Brie cheese, coating removed

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Melt the butter in a medium saucepan over medium heat.
3. Mix in garlic and almonds, heating until almonds are lightly browned.
4. Stir in mushrooms and cook until tender, about 5 minutes.
5. Cover with brandy and sprinkle with tarragon.
6. Place Brie in a small baking dish.
7. Pour the mushroom and brandy mixture over Brie.
8. Bake in the preheated oven 20 minutes, or until bubbly.
