## Ingredients

- 2  eggs
- 2 cups white sugar
- 6 tablespoons butter, softened
- 2 cups all-purpose flour
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- ½ teaspoon salt
- 1 cup milk
- 1 ½ cups canned sweet cherries, drained
- 1 ½ cups chopped pecans

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. In a medium mixing bowl, beat eggs and sugar together; add butter and beat well.
3. In a separate bowl, sift together flour, baking powder and salt.
4. Add the flour mixture to the egg mixture, alternating with milk.
5. Fold in the cherries and pecans; stir just to combine.
6. Scoop batter into prepared muffin pans and bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 20 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of a muffin comes out clean.
