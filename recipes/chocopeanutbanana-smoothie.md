## Ingredients

- 1  banana, sliced
- ½ cup skim milk
- 2 tablespoons peanut butter
- 2 tablespoons chocolate syrup

## Instructions

1. Blend the banana, skim milk, peanut butter, and chocolate syrup in a blender until smooth.
2. Pour into a glass to serve.
