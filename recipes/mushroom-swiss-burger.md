## Ingredients

- 1 pound ground beef
- ½  sweet onion, chopped
- 1 (4 ounce) package chopped fresh mushrooms
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1  egg white
- 1 teaspoon seasoned salt
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- 6 slices Swiss cheese
- 6  hamburger buns

## Instructions

1. Mix together the ground beef, onion, mushrooms, garlic, egg white, and seasoned salt in a bowl.
2. Form six equal-sized hamburger patties from the mixture.
3. Season burgers on each side with salt and pepper.
4. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat.
5. Lie the patties into the skillet and cook until golden brown, about 3 minutes per side.
6. Top each with a slice of Swiss cheese shortly before removing the burgers from the skillet; serve on hamburger buns.
