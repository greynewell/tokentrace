## Ingredients

- 1 ½ pounds potatoes, peeled and cut into 1-inch cubes
- 1 pinch Salt to taste
- ¾ cup sour cream
- ½ cup mayonnaise
- 2 tablespoons TABASCO® brand Chipotle Pepper Sauce
- 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
- ½ teaspoon finely chopped garlic
- 3 slices bacon, cooked and crumbled
- 2 each hard-cooked eggs, coarsely chopped

## Instructions

1. Place potatoes in a large saucepan and cover with water; add 2 teaspoons salt and bring to a boil.
2. Reduce heat to medium; cover and cook 12 to 15 minutes or until potatoes are tender.
3. Drain and cool.
4. Meanwhile, in a small bowl, combine sour cream, mayonnaise, TABASCO® Chipotle Sauce, mustard, and garlic; mix well.
5. Place potatoes in a large bowl with bacon and eggs.
6. Add dressing and toss to coat.
7. Season with additional salt, if needed.
8. Cover and refrigerate.
