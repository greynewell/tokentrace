## Ingredients

- 2 ½ cups elbow macaroni
- 1 pound squid, cleaned
- ¼ cup red wine vinegar
- 6 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 large onion, chopped
- 1 ½ cups crushed tomatoes
- ¼ cup dry white wine
- ½  lemon, juiced
- 1  cinnamon stick, broken in half
- 2  bay leaves
- ¼ teaspoon dried basil leaves
- ½ teaspoon dried oregano
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- ½ cup grated Mizithra cheese

## Instructions

1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
2. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.
3. In a small sauce pan, boil the squid in 3 cups of water mixed with the red wine vinegar for 8 to 10 minutes.
4. Drain, cut into bite-sized rings/pieces, and set aside.
5. Heat the olive oil in a large heavy skillet.
6. Saute the onion and garlic until onion is tender, but not brown.
7. Stir in the squid, and saute for 2 minutes.
8. Pour in the crushed tomatoes, white wine and lemon juice.
9. Season with cinnamon stick, bay leaves, basil, oregano, salt and pepper.
10. Bring to a boil, reduce heat, and simmer 15 to 20 minutes partially covered, stirring occasionally.
11. Remove from heat, and mix in cooked pasta.
12. Serve topped with grated cheese.
