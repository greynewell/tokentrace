## Ingredients

- 1 cup reduced-calorie mayonnaise
- 1 cup chopped cooked shrimp meat
- 1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
- 1 cup Parmesan cheese
- 1 teaspoon garlic salt
- 2 (12 ounce) packages sourdough English muffins, split

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Line a baking sheet with aluminum foil.
3. Combine mayonnaise, shrimp, Cheddar cheese, Parmesan cheese, and garlic salt in a large bowl.
4. Spread mixture over 1 side of each English muffin.
5. Arrange muffins into a 1 layer on the baking sheet.
6. Bake in the preheated oven until brown and bubbly, about 20 minutes.
