## Ingredients

- 1 cup BBQ sauce
- ½ cup maple syrup
- 2 tablespoons Dijon mustard
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- 4  salmon steaks

## Instructions

1. Combine BBQ sauce, maple syrup, Dijon mustard, and garlic in a large resealable plastic bag. Add salmon steaks and marinate for at least 45 minutes.
2. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Line a baking sheet with foil.
3. Place salmon on the baking sheet. Drizzle marinade over salmon.
4. Bake in the preheated oven, turning halfway through, until salmon flakes easily with a fork, about 20 minutes.
