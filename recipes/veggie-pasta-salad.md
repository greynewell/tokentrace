## Ingredients

- 1 (8 ounce) package pasta spirals
- ¼ cup diced sweet onion
- 1  green bell pepper, seeded and minced
- ½  fresh hot chile pepper, seeded and minced
- 2  tomatoes, seeded and chopped
- 1  cucumber, seeded and chopped
- ¼ cup olive oil
- ¼ cup tomato sauce
- ¼ cup lime juice
- 3 tablespoons red wine vinegar
- 1 teaspoon garlic powder
- 1 teaspoon salt
- ground black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil; cook the pasta spirals at a boil until tender yet firm to the bite, about 8 minutes; drain.
2. Rinse pasta with cold water until cool; drain.
3. Toss drained pasta, sweet onion, green bell pepper, chile pepper, tomatoes, and cucumber together in a large bowl.
4. Whisk olive oil, tomato sauce, lime juice, red wine vinegar, garlic powder, salt, and black pepper together in a separate bowl; drizzle over the pasta mixture and stir to coat.
5. Chill in refrigerator 2 hours; stir to serve.
