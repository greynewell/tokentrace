## Ingredients

- ¾ cup shortening
- 1 cup packed brown sugar
- 1  egg
- ½ cup mashed ripe banana
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 1 cup all-purpose flour
- ½ teaspoon baking soda
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
- ¼ teaspoon ground cloves
- 3 cups rolled oats
- ½ cup chopped walnuts

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Grease cookie sheets.
3. In a large bowl, cream together shortening and brown sugar.
4. Beat in egg and mashed banana, then stir in vanilla.
5. Combine flour, baking soda, salt, cinnamon, and cloves; stir into the banana mixture.
6. Mix in rolled oats and walnuts.
7. Drop by rounded spoonfuls onto prepared cookie sheets.
8. Leave room for spreading.
9. Bake for 8 to 10 minutes in preheated oven.
10. Allow cookies to cool on cookie sheets for 5 minutes before transferring to a wire rack to cool completely.
