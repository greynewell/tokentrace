## Ingredients

- 1 (3 ounce) package ramen noodles (flavor packet discarded)
- 8 ounces frozen cooked shrimp, thawed
- ½ cup julienned red bell pepper
- ½ cup shredded carrot
- ½ cup thinly bias-sliced celery
- ⅓ cup bottled chile-lime vinaigrette
- 2 tablespoons chopped dry-roasted peanuts
- 2 sprigs fresh mint
- 1 pinch cracked black pepper  (Optional)

## Instructions

1. Cook ramen according to package directions.
2. Drain in a colander under cold running water until cool; drain again.
3. Toss together ramen, shrimp, bell pepper, carrot, celery, and vinaigrette in a bowl.
4. Top servings with peanuts, mint, and black pepper.
5. Serve cold.
