## Ingredients

- 1 pound fresh asparagus spears, trimmed
- ¼ cup white wine
- 2 tablespoons butter

## Instructions

1. Place asparagus in a microwave-safe dish. Pour in wine and dot with butter.
2. Cover loosely and cook in the microwave on high until bright green and tender, about 3 minutes. Allow the dish to stand for 5 minutes before serving.
