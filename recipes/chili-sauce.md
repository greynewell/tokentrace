## Ingredients

- 1 cup tomato sauce
- ¼ cup brown sugar
- 2 tablespoons vinegar
- ¼ teaspoon allspice

## Instructions

1. Stir tomato sauce, brown sugar, vinegar, and allspice together in a mixing bowl until thoroughly blended.
2. Pour into a covered container; refrigerate until ready to use.
