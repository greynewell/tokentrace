## Ingredients

- ¼ cup butter
- 2 pounds ground beef
- 2 teaspoons salt
- ½ teaspoon ground allspice
- ½ teaspoon ground cinnamon
- ½ teaspoon ground black pepper
- 4 ½ cups chicken broth
- 2 cups long grain white rice
- 2 tablespoons butter
- ½ cup pine nuts

## Instructions

1. Heat 1/4 cup butter in a large saucepan over medium-high heat. Add ground beef and mix in salt, allspice, cinnamon, and black pepper. Cook until beef is browned, 7 to 10 minutes.
2. Add chicken broth and rice to the saucepan. Cover and bring to a boil. Reduce heat to low and cook until liquid is absorbed, about 20 minutes.
3. Meanwhile, melt 2 tablespoons butter in a small skillet over medium heat. Add pine nuts and cook until lightly browned, 3 to 5 minutes.
4. Mix pine nuts into rice and meat mixture before serving.
