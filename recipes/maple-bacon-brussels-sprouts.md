## Ingredients

- 2 pounds Brussels sprouts, trimmed
- 3 tablespoons olive oil
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
- 3 tablespoons pure maple syrup
- 5 slices bacon, crisp-cooked and crumbled

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
2. Arrange Brussels sprouts in a single layer on a foil-lined 10x15-inch baking pan. Drizzle with oil; toss to coat. Sprinkle with salt and pepper.
3. Roast in the preheated oven for 10 minutes. Drizzle with maple syrup; toss to coat. Roast until tender and beginning to brown, 10 to 15 minutes more.
4. Toss Brussels sprouts mixture with bacon in a bowl.
