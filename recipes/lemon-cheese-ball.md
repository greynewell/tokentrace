## Ingredients

- 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
- ¼ cup butter
- ½ cup confectioners' sugar
- 1 tablespoon powdered lemonade mix
- ¼ cup graham cracker crumbs

## Instructions

1. In a medium bowl, mix the cream cheese, butter, confectioners' sugar and lemonade mix using an electric mixer.
2. Refrigerate for 3 hours.
3. When the mixture is completely chilled, shape into a ball and roll in graham cracker crumbs.
4. Serve on a platter with your favorite crackers.
