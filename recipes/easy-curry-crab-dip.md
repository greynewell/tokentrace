## Ingredients

- 2 (4.25 ounce) cans crabmeat, drained
- 5 tablespoons Japanese mayonnaise
- 3 tablespoons thinly sliced green onions, green parts only
- 1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice
- 1 tablespoon curry powder, or to taste
- 1 pinch paprika  (Optional)

## Instructions

1. Mix crabmeat, mayonnaise, green onions, lemon juice, and curry powder together.
2. Cover and chill until ready to serve.
3. Sprinkle with paprika just before serving.
