## Ingredients

- 6 slices bacon
- 3 tablespoons vegetable oil
- 1 tablespoon vinegar
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ⅛ teaspoon ground black pepper
- 3 drops hot pepper sauce
- 2 medium avocados, cubed
- 2 large tomatoes, chopped
- 1 small red onion, chopped

## Instructions

1. Place bacon in a large skillet and cook over medium-high heat, turning occasionally, until crisp, about 10 minutes.
2. Drain bacon slices on paper towels and crumble.
3. Mix oil, vinegar, salt, pepper, and hot sauce together; pour over avocados.
4. Toss.
5. Stir in bacon, tomatoes, and onion.
6. Cover and refrigerate about 2 hours.
