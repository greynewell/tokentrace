## Ingredients

- ½ cup small pasta shells, uncooked
- 1 (5 ounce) can water-packed tuna, drained and flaked
- 1 large tomato, chopped
- 1  cucumber - peeled, seeded, and chopped
- 1 small red bell pepper, chopped
- ½ cup chopped green onions
- 2 stalks celery, chopped
- 6 tablespoons ranch dressing
- 3 tablespoons chopped fresh dill

## Instructions

1. Fill a pot with lightly salted water and bring to a rolling boil.
2. Once the water is boiling, stir in the shell pasta, and return to a boil.
3. Cook the pasta uncovered, stirring occasionally, until the pasta has cooked through, about 13 minutes.
4. Drain well in a colander set in the sink, and chill by rinsing with cold water.
5. Combine the cooled pasta, tuna, tomato, cucumber, red bell pepper, green onions, and celery in a salad bowl, and toss lightly to mix.
6. Stir in the ranch dressing and dill, cover, and refrigerate until chilled, at least 1 hour.
