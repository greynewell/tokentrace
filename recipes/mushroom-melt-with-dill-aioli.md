## Ingredients

- 1  garlic clove, chopped
- 1 teaspoon lemon juice
- 1  egg yolk
- ¼ cup olive oil
- 1 teaspoon dried dill weed
- ½ teaspoon salt
- cayenne pepper, to taste
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 2 slices whole wheat bread
- 4 thick slices Cheddar cheese
- 2 large mushrooms, chopped
- 1 cup fresh baby spinach

## Instructions

1. Combine garlic and lemon juice in a bowl; mix egg yolk in gently.
2. Add olive oil, dill, salt, and cayenne; stir aioli until combined.
3. Heat a large skillet over medium heat.
4. Butter 1 side of each bread slice; place 1 butter-side-down into the skillet.
5. Layer 2 slices Cheddar cheese, mushrooms, and spinach on the bread.
6. Cover with 2 remaining slices of Cheddar cheese and the remaining slice of bread, butter side up.
7. Fry sandwich until dark brown and crispy, about 5 minutes per side.
8. Serve with aioli for dipping.
