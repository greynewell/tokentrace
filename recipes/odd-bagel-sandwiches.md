## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons peanut butter
- 1  bagel, split and toasted
- 4 slices sliced pepperoni

## Instructions

1. Spread the peanut butter on the bagel while still warm.
2. Arrange the pepperoni on the peanut butter and sandwich between the 2 bagel halves.
