## Ingredients

- 1 pound chicken livers, rinsed and trimmed
- 1 cup prepared yellow mustard
- 1 (4 ounce) can chopped black olives, drained
- 1 pound bacon
- toothpicks

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. In a medium bowl, stir together the mustard and olives.
3. Dip each chicken liver in the mustard mixture, then wrap with a slice of bacon.
4. Secure bacon with toothpicks.
5. Arrange the livers in a single layer in a baking dish.
6. Bake for about 20 minutes in the preheated oven, or until livers are firm and bacon is browned.
