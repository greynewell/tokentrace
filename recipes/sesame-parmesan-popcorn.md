## Ingredients

- ⅓ cup popcorn kernels
- 3 tablespoons butter, melted
- 3 tablespoons sesame oil
- ¼ cup finely grated Parmesan cheese
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon ground black pepper

## Instructions

1. Pop popcorn in a hot air popper or microwave popper according to manufacturer's instructions. Pour into a large bowl.
2. Mix butter and sesame oil together in a small bowl. Drizzle over popcorn. Add Parmesan cheese, salt, and pepper; toss well to coat.
