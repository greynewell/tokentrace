## Ingredients

- 2 cups rolled oats
- 2 cups boiling water
- 1 cup scalded whole milk
- 1 tablespoon canola oil
- ⅔ cup molasses
- ⅓ cup white sugar
- 1 tablespoon active dry yeast
- 6 cups all-purpose flour
- 1 tablespoon salt

## Instructions

1. In a large bowl pour the boiling water over the rolled oats.
2. Add the scalded milk and stir.
3. Pour in the oil and molasses.
4. Stir well.
5. In a separate bowl mix the sugar with the yeast and 1 cup of the flour.
6. When the oatmeal mixture has cooled to lukewarm add the yeast-flour mixture.
7. Stir in the salt and remaining 5 cups of flour.
8. Knead dough for 6 to 8 minutes.
9. Place the dough in a covered greased bowl and let rise until doubled, about 1 1/2 hours.
10. Punch the dough down.
11. Knead for 2 minutes.
12. Divide dough into two pieces and place into greased 9x5x3 inch loaf pans.
13. Cover pans and let rise until almost doubled in size.
14. Bake in a preheated 350 degree F (175 degrees C) oven for 1 hour, or until nicely browned.
15. Let the loaves cool on a rack.
16. Remove loaves from pan; wrap well and store in a cool spot.
