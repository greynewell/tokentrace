## Ingredients

- 8 tablespoons salad dressing of your choice, or to taste
- 2 large carrots, chopped
- 2 (15 ounce) cans chickpeas, drained
- 1 bunch chopped kale
- 4 tablespoons crumbled feta cheese

## Instructions

1. Set out 4 mason jars and start layering.
2. Divide dressing equally between the 4 jars.
3. Add equal amounts of carrots, chickpeas, kale, and feta, in that order.
4. Close jars with a lid and refrigerate until using.
