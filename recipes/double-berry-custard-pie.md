## Ingredients

- 1 (3.4 ounce) package instant vanilla pudding mix
- 1 ¼ cups heavy cream
- 1 (9 inch) prepared graham cracker pie crust
- ½ cup seedless raspberry jam
- 6 ounces fresh blackberries
- 6 ounces fresh raspberries

## Instructions

1. Whisk together pudding mix and cream in a bowl until very thick, about 2 minutes.
2. Spoon mixture evenly into pie crust and smooth.
3. Chill, covered, about 30 minutes.
4. Heat jam in a small saucepan over low heat, stirring occasionally, 2 to 3 minutes.
5. Mix blackberries and raspberries together.
6. Mound berries over pudding mixture and drizzle warm jam over top.
7. Chill, loosely covered, at least 30 minutes and up to 1 day.
