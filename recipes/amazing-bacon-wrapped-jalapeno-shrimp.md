## Ingredients

- 1 quart vegetable oil for frying
- 32  peeled and deveined medium shrimp, tail-on
- 1 (4 ounce) can pickled jalapeno peppers, cut into slivers
- 16 slices bacon, cut in half
- 32  toothpicks

## Instructions

1. Heat oil in a deep-fryer or large saucepan to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Cut the shrimp along the back, nearly through to the front.
3. Stuff each shrimp with a sliver of jalapeno, then wrap with half a slice of bacon.
4. Secure with a toothpick.
5. Repeat with the remaining ingredients.
6. Cook the shrimp in batches in the hot oil until the bacon is crispy and golden brown, 2 to 3 minutes.
7. Drain on a paper towel-lined plate before serving.
