## Ingredients

- 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese
- 1 (10 ounce) box frozen chopped spinach, thawed and squeezed dry
- 1 (4 ounce) container feta cheese, crumbled
- 1 (14 ounce) can artichoke hearts, chopped with hard leaf tips removed
- 2 teaspoons minced garlic
- 1 (1 ounce) package dry ranch salad dressing mix

## Instructions

1. In a large bowl, combine the cream cheese, spinach, feta cheese, artichokes, garlic and Ranch dressing mix.
2. Mix together with clean hands until well blended.
3. Form into a ball or log and set on a serving plate.
4. Done!
