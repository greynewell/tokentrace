## Ingredients

- 3 ½ cups brown rice flour
- 5 teaspoons baking powder
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ¾ cup butter
- 1 cup milk
- 1  egg
- ⅓ cup agave nectar
- 1 tablespoon lemon zest
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice, or more to taste
- 1 cup diced candied ginger

## Instructions

1. Preheat an oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
2. Lightly grease a baking sheet.
3. Whisk together the flour, baking powder, and salt in a mixing bowl.
4. Cut the butter into the flour mixture with a knife or pastry blender until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
5. Whisk together the egg, milk, agave nectar, lemon zest, lemon juice, and ginger in a separate bowl; stir into the flour mixture until moistened.
6. Turn the dough out onto a lightly floured surface and knead briefly, for five or six turns.
7. Pat or roll the dough out into a 1/2-inch-thick round.
8. Cut into 8 wedge-shaped pieces and place onto the prepared baking sheet.
9. Bake in the preheated oven until golden brown, about 20 minutes.
