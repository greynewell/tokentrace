## Ingredients

- 1 ¾ cups dry bulgur wheat
- 1  avocado - peeled, pitted and diced
- salt and pepper to taste
- ½ pound bacon
- 2 tablespoons lemon juice
- 2 (5 ounce) cans tuna, drained

## Instructions

1. Place the bulgur wheat into a saucepan with at least 3 times it's depth in water.
2. Bring to a boil over medium-high heat, then reduce heat and simmer until tender, about 15 minutes.
3. Add water if necessary to prevent burning.
4. When the wheat is tender, stir in a pinch of salt and simmer for 5 more minutes.
5. When done, drain and set aside to cool.
6. Place bacon in a large, deep skillet.
7. Cook over medium-high heat until evenly brown.
8. Drain, crumble and set aside.
9. Place the diced avocado into a small bowl.
10. Season with salt and pepper, then toss with lemon juice to thoroughly coat.
11. In a large bowl, combine the tuna and cooled bulgur wheat.
12. Add the bacon and the avocado with its juice, and toss gently.
13. Serve as a meal or as a salad.
