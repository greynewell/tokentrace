## Ingredients

- 1 cup mayonnaise (such as Hellman's®)
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
- 1 cup chopped fresh basil
- 1 cup chopped fresh dill
- 1 quart oil for frying
- 2 cups all-purpose flour
- 1 tablespoon onion powder
- 1 tablespoon garlic powder
- 1 tablespoon cayenne pepper
- ½ cup heavy cream
- 2  eggs, beaten
- 1 (1 pound) package bacon strips, halved
- 16  small sandwich rolls
- 4  tomatoes, sliced
- salt and pepper to taste
- ½ head lettuce, chopped

## Instructions

1. Make the aioli by stirring the mayonnaise, garlic, lemon juice, basil, and dill together in a bowl.
2. Refrigerate at least 1 hour before serving.
3. Heat oil in a deep-fryer or large saucepan to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
4. Stir the flour, onion powder, garlic powder, and cayenne pepper together in a bowl.
5. Whisk the heavy cream and beaten eggs together in a separate bowl.
6. Dip each piece of bacon into the egg mixture and then into the flour mixture.
7. Repeat the process a second time to assure each piece is heavily coated.
8. Fry the bacon in the hot oil until golden brown, 3 to 4 minutes per side.
9. Set aside onto a plate lined with paper towels to drain.
10. Spread a generous amount of the aioli onto each roll.
11. Add a slice of tomato to each and season with salt and pepper.
12. Finish the sandwiches with a portion of the lettuce and a few slices of bacon.
