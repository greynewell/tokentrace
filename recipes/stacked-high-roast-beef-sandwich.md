## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon Dietz & Watson Horseradish Sauce
- 2 slices sourdough bread
- ½ cup fresh, peppery arugula
- 10 ounces Dietz & Watson Roast Beef, Eye Round
- 2 thin slices  Dietz & Watson New York State Aged Cheddar Cheese with Horseradish
- 4 slices Dietz & Watson Kosher Dill Pickles

## Instructions

1. Spread a small amount of Dietz & Watson Horseradish Sauce on both slices of sourdough bread (add a little extra for an added kick).
2. Layer your sandwich as follows: bread, arugula, Dietz & Watson Peppered Eye Round, Dietz & Watson Cheddar Cheese with Horseradish, Dietz & Watson Kosher Pickles, bread.
