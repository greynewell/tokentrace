## Ingredients

- 2 pounds new red potatoes
- 6  hard-boiled eggs, peeled and chopped
- 3  green onions, chopped
- 2 cups mayonnaise
- ¼ cup evaporated milk
- 2 tablespoons mustard
- salt and ground black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Place potatoes into a large pot and cover with salted water; bring to a boil.
2. Reduce heat to medium-low and simmer until tender yet still firm, about 15 minutes.
3. Drain and refrigerate until cool, at least 30 minutes.
4. Peel and chop cooled potatoes; mix with eggs and onions in a large bowl.
5. Add mayonnaise, milk, mustard, salt, and pepper to potato mixture; stir until evenly coated.
6. Chill salad for 1 hour before serving.
