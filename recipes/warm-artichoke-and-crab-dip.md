## Ingredients

- 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, room temperature
- 1 cup mayonnaise
- ⅓ cup chopped onion
- 1 (14 ounce) can artichoke hearts, drained and chopped
- 1 (8 ounce) package imitation crabmeat, chopped
- 1 cup shredded Monterey Jack cheese
- ¾ cup shredded Parmesan cheese

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
2. Blend cream cheese, mayonnaise, and onion in a bowl using a hand mixer.
3. Gently fold artichoke hearts and imitation crab, Monterey Jack cheese, and Parmesan cheese into the cream cheese mixture; spread into a pie plate.
4. Bake in preheated oven until browned and bubbling on top, 15 to 20 minutes.
