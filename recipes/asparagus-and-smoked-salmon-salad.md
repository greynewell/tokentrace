## Ingredients

- 1 pound asparagus, trimmed and cut into 1-inch pieces
- ½ cup pecans, broken into pieces
- 2 heads red leaf lettuce, rinsed and torn
- ½ cup frozen green peas, thawed
- ¼ pound smoked salmon, cut into 1-inch chunks
- ¼ cup olive oil
- 2 tablespoons lemon juice
- 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ¼ teaspoon pepper

## Instructions

1. Bring a pot of water to a boil.
2. Place asparagus in the pot, and cook 5 minutes, just until tender.
3. Drain, and set aside.
4. Place the pecans in a skillet over medium heat.
5. Cook 5 minutes, stirring frequently, until lightly toasted.
6. In a large bowl, toss together the asparagus, pecans, red leaf lettuce, peas, and salmon.
7. In a separate bowl, mix the olive oil, lemon juice, Dijon mustard, salt, and pepper.
8. Toss with the salad or serve on the side.
