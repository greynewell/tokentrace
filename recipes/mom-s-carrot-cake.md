## Ingredients

- 6  carrots, quartered
- 2 cups all-purpose flour
- 2 cups white sugar
- 2 teaspoons baking soda
- ½ teaspoon salt
- 3  eggs
- 2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
- ¾ cup vegetable oil
- 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
- ¾ cup buttermilk
- 1 (8 ounce) can crushed pineapple with juice
- 1 (3.5 ounce) package flaked coconut
- 1 cup chopped walnuts
- ½ (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
- ¼ cup butter
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 2 cups confectioners' sugar

## Instructions

1. In a small saucepan, cover carrots with water.
2. Bring water to a boil and cook until tender, about 15 minutes.
3. Let cool and mash.
4. In a large mixing bowl, beat eggs.
5. Add vegetable oil, buttermilk, 2 cups white sugar and 2 teaspoons vanilla.
6. Add flour, baking soda, ground cinnamon and salt.
7. Mix in pineapple, 2 cups mashed carrots, nuts and coconut.
8. Pour into paper towel-lined 13 x 9 x 2-1/2 inch pan.
9. Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 55 minutes, or until cake tests done.
10. Place cake on a plate, and allow to cool completely before frosting.
11. To Make Frosting: Combine cream cheese, melted butter, 1 teaspoon vanilla extract, and confectioners' sugar.
12. Spread on cooled cake.
13. Cake tastes best after refrigerating  overnight.
