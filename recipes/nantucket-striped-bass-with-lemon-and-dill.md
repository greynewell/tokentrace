## Ingredients

- 1 (8 ounce) fillet striped bass fillet
- 3 tablespoons lemon juice
- 2 tablespoons chopped fresh dill, divided
- 2 tablespoons mayonnaise
- 2 tablespoons Dijon mustard
- ground black pepper, or to taste
- ¼ cup bread crumbs, or more to taste
- 1 tablespoon butter, thinly sliced

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C).
2. Place bass in a glass baking pan.
3. Combine lemon juice and 1 tablespoon dill in a small bowl; pour over bass.
4. Marinate at room temperature, at least 10 minutes.
5. Bake in the preheated oven until fish flakes easily with a fork, about 8 minutes.
6. Combine the remaining dill, mayonnaise, mustard, and pepper together in a bowl.
7. Mix and smear sauce over the bass.
8. Sprinkle bass with bread crumbs to make crust.
9. Sprinkle butter slices on top.
10. Set oven rack about 6 inches from the heat source and preheat the oven's broiler.
11. Broil bass on high until crust is crispy and golden brown, about 3 minutes.
