## Ingredients

- 2 pounds limes, juiced
- 2 pounds swai fish
- 1 pound Roma tomatoes, chopped
- 1 large red onion, chopped
- 1 bunch cilantro, chopped
- 1  serrano chile peppers, or more to taste  (Optional)
- sea salt and ground black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Pour lime juice into a large bowl.
2. Process swai fish in a food processor until pureed and mushy; transfer to lime juice, stirring until all fish is covered in juice.
3. Add tomatoes to fish mixture.
4. Blend red onion, cilantro, and serrano chile peppers in a blender until desired consistency is reached; spoon into fish mixture.
5. Season mixture with salt and black pepper.
6. Cover bowl with aluminum foil.
7. Refrigerate until fish is tender and "cooked", at least 2 hours.
