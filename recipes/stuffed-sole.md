## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon lemon juice
- 2 tablespoons thinly sliced green onion
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- ¼ cup dry white wine
- 4 (6 ounce) fillets sole
- ¼ pound cooked salad shrimp
- salt to taste
- ground black pepper to taste
- ¼ teaspoon paprika
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 1 tablespoon all-purpose flour

## Instructions

1. In a small bowl, combine lemon juice, green onion, garlic, and wine.
2. Set aside.
3. Lay filets flat, and divide shrimp evenly among them in a band across one end of each fillet.
4. Roll up around shrimp, and secure with toothpick.
5. Place in a baking dish.
6. Season to taste with salt, pepper, and paprika.
7. Pour lemon juice mixture over the fish.
8. Cover.
9. Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 25 minutes.
10. When fillets are nearly done, prepare sauce.
11. In a small saucepan, melt butter over medium heat.
12. Stir in flour.
13. Transfer fish to serving platter ,and keep warm.
14. Pour pan juices into butter/flour mixture; cook and stir until thickened.
15. Pour over sole, and serve.
