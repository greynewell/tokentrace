## Ingredients

- 2 cups all-purpose flour
- 2 cups white sugar
- 1 cup butter
- 1 cup water
- ½ cup shortening
- ½ cup unsweetened cocoa powder
- ½ cup buttermilk
- 2  eggs
- 1 teaspoon baking soda
- 1 ½ teaspoons vanilla extract
- 6 tablespoons milk
- 4 cups confectioners' sugar

## Instructions

1. Sift together and set aside in large bowl the flour and sugar.
2. In saucepan over medium heat bring to a boil; 1/2 cup margarine or butter, 1/2 cup shortening, 1 cup water, and 4 tablespoons cocoa.
3. Pour cocoa mixture over flour and sugar mixture.
4. Stir in buttermilk, eggs, baking soda, and 1/2 teaspoon vanilla.
5. Mix well and pour into a greased and floured sheet pan.
6. Bake in a preheated 400 degrees F (205 degrees C) oven for 20 minutes.
7. To Make Frosting:  Five minutes before cake is done, bring to boil in a saucepan 1/2 cup margarine or butter, 6 tablespoons milk, and 4 tablespoons cocoa.
8. Remove from heat and immediately stir in the confectioners' sugar, nuts (if desired), and 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract.
9. Beat until smooth and immediately pour frosting over cake.
10. Cake may be served warm or at room temperature.
