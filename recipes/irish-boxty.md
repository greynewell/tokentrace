## Ingredients

- 1 ½ cups grated raw potatoes
- 1 cup all-purpose flour
- 1 cup leftover mashed potatoes
- 1  egg
- 1 tablespoon skim milk
- salt and pepper to taste
- ¼ cup olive oil

## Instructions

1. Toss grated potatoes with flour in a large bowl. Stir in mashed potatoes until combined.
2. In a separate bowl, whisk together egg and milk; mix into the potatoes. Season to taste with salt and pepper.
3. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Drop in the potato mixture, forming patties about 2 inches in diameter. Fry on both sides until golden brown, 3 to 4 minutes per side. Drain on a paper towel-lined plate. Serve warm.
