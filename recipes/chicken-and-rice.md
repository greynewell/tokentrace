## Ingredients

- 2 cups instant rice
- 1 (5 ounce) can chicken chunks, drained
- 1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of chicken soup

## Instructions

1. Prepare rice according to package directions.
2. When rice is ready, add chicken to pot and continue to stir over low heat. Mix in soup, and continue cooking until heated through.
