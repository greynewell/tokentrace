## Ingredients

- ½ cup olive oil
- ½ cup butter
- ½ cup minced garlic
- 4 pounds Snow Crab clusters, thawed if necessary

## Instructions

1. Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat and lightly oil the grate.
2. Whisk butter, olive oil, and garlic together in a bowl until well combined; generously brush onto crab.
3. Cook crab on the preheated grill, turning once, until the shell begins to brown, about 5 to 6 minutes.
