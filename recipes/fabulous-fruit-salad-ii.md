## Ingredients

- 2 cups diced apples
- 1 cup sliced banana
- 1 cup sliced fresh strawberries
- 1 cup chopped walnuts
- 1 cup vanilla yogurt
- ¾ teaspoon ground cinnamon

## Instructions

1. In a bowl, mix the apples, banana, strawberries, and walnuts.
2. Fold in the yogurt.
3. Sprinkle with cinnamon.
4. Gently stir just before serving.
