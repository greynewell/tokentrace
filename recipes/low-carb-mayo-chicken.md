## Ingredients

- 10  cucumbers, diced
- 1 ½ teaspoons salt, divided
- 1  whole chicken
- ½ piece cassia cinnamon, broken into small pieces
- 4  black peppercorns
- 4  whole cloves
- 4  cardamom pods
- 1  bay leaf
- 2 tablespoons mayonnaise
- 2 tablespoons light mayonnaise
- 1 clove garlic, crushed

## Instructions

1. Place cucumbers in a bowl; sprinkle with 1 teaspoon of salt.
2. Fill a pot with water; add remaining 1/2 teaspoon salt, chicken, cinnamon, peppercorns, cloves, cardamom, and bay leaf.
3. Bring to a boil; cook until chicken is no longer pink in the center and an instant-read thermometer inserted into the thickest part of the thigh, near the bone should read 165 degrees F (74 degrees C), about 35 minutes.
4. Transfer chicken to a cutting board; cool until easily handled, about 15 minutes.
5. Remove skin and bone; cut chicken into small pieces.
6. Transfer to a bowl.
7. Stir mayonnaise, light mayonnaise, and garlic into the cucumbers.
8. Pour over chicken and mix until combined.
