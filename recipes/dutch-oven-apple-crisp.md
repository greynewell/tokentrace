## Ingredients

- 2 ½ cups Macintosh apples - peeled, cored and quartered
- 2 cups rolled oats
- 2 cups packed brown sugar
- 1 cup all-purpose flour
- ¾ cup milk

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
2. Combine apples and brown sugar in a large Dutch oven.
3. Bake for 30 minutes.
4. Combine milk, flour, and oats.
5. Pour mixture over apples and brown sugar.
6. Bake for 1 hour.
7. Serve.
