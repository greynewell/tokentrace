## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons butter, room temperature
- 2 slices bread
- 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard  (Optional)
- 3 slices Brie cheese
- 1  Fuji apple, thinly sliced
- 4 strips cooked bacon

## Instructions

1. Heat a panini press to medium-high heat according to manufacturer's instructions.
2. Butter 1 side of each slice of bread. Flip 1 slice and spread mustard on top; add cheese. Place apple slices on the cheese. Lay bacon on top. Cover with the other slice of bread, buttered side-up.
3. Place sandwich into the panini press and press down. Grill until lightly browned, about 3 minutes per side.
