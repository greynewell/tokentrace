## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons butter
- 1 small onion, minced
- 2 (8 ounce) cans oysters, liquid reserved
- 4 cups milk
- 2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
- 1 dash garlic powder

## Instructions

1. Melt the butter in a large pot over medium-high heat; cook the onion in the melted butter until tender, about 5 minutes.
2. Pour the oysters with their juices into the pot and bring to a boil; cook 3 minutes.
3. Add the milk, Worcestershire sauce, and garlic powder; stir.
4. Allow the soup to cook until heated completely through, 5 to 10 minutes.
