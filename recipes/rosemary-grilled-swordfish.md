## Ingredients

- ¼ cup olive oil
- 2  lemons, juiced
- 1 tablespoon minced garlic
- 1 tablespoon dry mustard
- 1 tablespoon minced fresh rosemary
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
- 2 pounds swordfish steaks

## Instructions

1. Whisk olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, mustard, rosemary, salt, and black pepper together in a small bowl.
2. Arrange swordfish steaks on a rimmed baking sheet and pour marinade over fish.
3. Refrigerate up to 1 hour.
4. Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat and lightly oil the grate.
5. Place swordfish on the preheated grill rack.
6. Cook until fish flakes easily with a fork, 4 to 5 minutes per side.
7. Serve immediately.
