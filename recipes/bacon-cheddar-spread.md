## Ingredients

- 10 slices bacon
- 2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese
- 1 tablespoon grated onion
- 1 cup creamy salad dressing

## Instructions

1. Place bacon in a large, deep skillet.
2. Cook over medium high heat until evenly brown.
3. Drain and crumble.
4. In a medium bowl, mix together crumbled bacon, Cheddar cheese, grated onion and creamy salad dressing.
5. Chill in the refrigerator approximately 1 hour before serving.
