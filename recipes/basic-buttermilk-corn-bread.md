## Ingredients

- 2 cups cornmeal
- 1 ½ cups buttermilk
- ¼ cup vegetable oil
- 1  egg
- 2 tablespoons butter

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
2. Whisk together the cornmeal, buttermilk, vegetable oil, and egg in a bowl to form a batter.
3. Melt the butter in a cast-iron skillet over medium heat, and swirl the melted butter all over the bottom and up the sides of the skillet.
4. Let the butter heat until it gives off a faint toasted fragrance; pour the batter into the hot skillet.
5. Place the skillet into the preheated oven, and bake until golden brown, 25 to 30 minutes.
