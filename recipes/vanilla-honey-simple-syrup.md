## Ingredients

- 2 teaspoons milk
- 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
- 1 tablespoon honey
- 2 teaspoons butter

## Instructions

1. Combine milk and vanilla extract in a bowl.
2. Heat honey and butter together in a microwave-safe bowl in the microwave for 30 seconds; stir into milk mixture.
3. Allow to cool completely before use. Chill in the refrigerator if too hot.
