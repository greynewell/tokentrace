## Ingredients

- 1 quart safflower oil for frying
- 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
- ½ teaspoon baking powder
- 2  eggs
- 1 cup heavy cream
- 2 cups seasoned bread crumbs
- 4 large Spanish onions, sliced into 1/4 inch thick rings

## Instructions

1. Heat safflower oil in a large, heavy skillet to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
2. Mix together flour and baking powder in a small bowl.
3. In another bowl, whisk together eggs and heavy cream.
4. Place seasoned bread crumbs on a medium, flat dish.
5. Dust Spanish onion rings with flour mixture.
6. Dip one at a time into egg mixture.
7. Coat thoroughly with bread crumbs.
8. Fry coated rings in the hot oil, a few at a time, until crisp and golden brown.
9. Drain on paper towels.
