## Ingredients

- ¼ cup balsamic vinegar
- ⅓ cup vegetable oil
- 1 ½ teaspoons white sugar
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ½ teaspoon ground black pepper
- 12 ounces cherry tomatoes, halved
- 1 (8 ounce) package fresh mushrooms
- 2  green onions, sliced
- ½ cup chopped fresh basil

## Instructions

1. Whisk together the balsamic vinegar, vegetable oil, sugar, salt, and pepper in a bowl; add the tomatoes, mushrooms, onions, and basil; toss until evenly coated.
2. Cover and chill in refrigerator at least 3 hours.
3. Stir before serving.
