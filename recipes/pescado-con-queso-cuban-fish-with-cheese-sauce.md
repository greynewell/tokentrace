## Ingredients

- 1  onion, chopped
- ½ pound shredded Cheddar cheese
- 2 pounds swai fish
- 1 teaspoon butter
- 1 cup milk
- 1 ½ teaspoons Worcestershire sauce
- 1 teaspoon mustard
- 1 teaspoon salt
- ground black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Grease a 9x13-inch baking dish.
3. Mix onion and Cheddar cheese together in a bowl.
4. Sprinkle 1/2 of the cheese mixture into the prepared baking dish; top with swai fish.
5. Sprinkle the remaining cheese mixture over fish.
6. Whisk milk, Worcestershire sauce, mustard, salt, and black pepper together in a bowl; pour over fish.
7. Bake in the preheated oven until fish flakes easily with a fork and the cheese is melted, about 45 minutes.
