## Ingredients

- 32  (3.5 inch square) wonton wrappers
- 1 ½ tablespoons margarine, melted
- 3 tablespoons cinnamon sugar

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
2. Lay the wonton wrappers out flat on a baking sheet. Brush evenly with melted margarine. Sprinkle 1/4 teaspoon of cinnamon sugar over the top of each one.
3. Bake for 5 minutes in the preheated oven, or until crisp. Remove from baking sheets to cool on wire racks.
