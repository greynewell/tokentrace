## Ingredients

- 1 pound ground beef
- 1 pinch garlic powder
- salt and pepper to taste
- 1 (32 ounce) package tater tots, thawed
- 1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of mushroom soup
- 1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of celery soup
- 1 (10.75 ounce) can  milk
- 1 pinch garlic powder

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
2. Crumble ground beef into a 9x13-inch baking pan. Sprinkle beef with garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Cover beef with tater tots.
3. In a bowl, stir together mushroom soup, celery soup, and milk. Pour the soup mixture over the tater tots and beef and sprinkle with additional garlic powder.
4. Bake in preheated oven for 60 minutes.
