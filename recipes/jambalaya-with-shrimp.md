## Ingredients

- 4 tablespoons butter
- 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
- 3  onions, chopped
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- ¼ cup cooked ham, cut into strips
- 2 cups peeled and diced tomatoes
- 1 teaspoon dried oregano
- 2 cups uncooked white rice
- 3 cups hot chicken broth
- 2 pounds shrimp, peeled and deveined

## Instructions

1. In a large saucepan, melt butter over low heat.
2. Stir in flour until smooth.
3. Stir in onions, garlic, and ham; cook and stir until onion is soft.
4. Stir in tomatoes, and cook for 5 minutes.
5. Mix in oregano, rice, and broth.
6. Cover, and simmer until just a little liquid remains.
7. Add shrimp, and stir to evenly distribute.
8. Cook for 8 minutes, or until rice is cooked and shrimp turns pink.
