## Ingredients

- 1 ½ cups shredded Cheddar cheese
- ¼ cup chopped pecans
- ¼ cup bacon bits
- ¾ cup mayonnaise
- 1 teaspoon grated onion

## Instructions

1. Mix Cheddar cheese, pecans, bacon bits, mayonnaise, and grated onion in a bowl.
2. Shape into a ball and wrap in plastic wrap.
3. Refrigerate over night.
