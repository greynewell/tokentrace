## Ingredients

- 3 cups peeled garlic cloves
- ½ cup olive oil
- 3 tablespoons salt, or to taste

## Instructions

1. In a food processor, process garlic cloves and salt together until smooth.
2. Add olive oil in small amounts, processing in short bursts until well combined.
