## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon extra-virgin olive oil
- 1 medium red onion, chopped
- 2 ½ tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
- 3 cloves garlic, diced
- 1 teaspoon beef base (such as Better Than Bouillon®)
- 1 tablespoon ground black pepper, or to taste
- 1 teaspoon dried thyme
- 1 teaspoon salt
- ¾ cup dry vermouth, divided
- 2 pounds New York strip steaks, cut into thin pieces
- 3 cups sliced mushrooms
- 2 cups beef broth, divided
- 1 (16 ounce) package wide egg noodles
- 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
- ¾ cup sour cream

## Instructions

1. Turn on a multi-functional pressure cooker (such as Instant Pot®) and select Saute function.
2. Heat oil in the pot.
3. Saute onion until barely tender, about 3 minutes.
4. Mix in Worcestershire sauce, garlic, beef base, pepper, thyme, and salt.
5. Pour in 1/4 cup vermouth; deglaze the pot by scraping the bottom with a wooden spoon.
6. Add steaks; saute until browned, about 6 minutes.
7. Turn off Saute mode.
8. Add remaining vermouth, mushrooms, and 1 1/2 cups beef broth.
9. Mix together gently.
10. Close and lock the lid.
11. Select high pressure according to manufacturer's instructions; set timer for 10 minutes.
12. Allow 10 to 15 minutes for pressure to build.
13. Release pressure carefully using the quick-release method according to manufacturer's instructions, about 5 minutes.
14. Unlock and remove the lid.
15. Stir in noodles.
16. Lock the lid and cook on high pressure until noodles are tender, about 3 minutes.
17. Allow 5 to 10 minutes for pressure to build.
18. Pour remaining 1/2 cup beef broth into a microwave-safe bowl.
19. Microwave until hot, about 1 minute.
20. Dissolve flour in the broth.
21. Release pressure carefully using the quick-release method according to manufacturer's instructions, about 5 minutes.
22. Open lid and stir in the beef broth mixture.
23. Let thicken, 3 to 5 minutes.
24. Stir again.
25. Add sour cream; stir until completely combined.
