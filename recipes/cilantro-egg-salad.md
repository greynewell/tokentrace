## Ingredients

- 5  hard-cooked eggs, finely chopped
- ¾ cup mayonnaise
- ¾ cup chopped fresh cilantro
- 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
- 1 teaspoon lemon juice
- 1 teaspoon lime juice
- 1 stalk celery rib, finely diced
- 4  fresh mushrooms, finely diced
- salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. In a medium bowl, stir together the eggs, mayonnaise, cilantro, mustard, lemon juice, lime juice, celery, and mushrooms.
2. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
3. Serve on bread or toast.
