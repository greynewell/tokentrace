## Ingredients

- 1 cup shortening
- ½ cup white sugar
- 1 ½ teaspoons salt
- 2 ½ cups scalded milk
- 4  egg yolks, beaten
- ½ teaspoon lemon extract
- 1 ½ tablespoons active dry yeast
- 5 cups all-purpose flour
- ½ cup fruit preserves

## Instructions

1. Cream shortening and sugar together.
2. Stir in salt, hot milk, beaten egg yolks, and flavoring.
3. When lukewarm, add yeast to mixture.
4. Let stand 5 minutes.
5. Add flour, beating well.
6. Knead down into bowl.
7. The dough will be slightly sticky.
8. Let rise 1 hour.
9. Cut off small balls of dough.
10. Roll into rounds in your hands, and place on greased cookie sheets 2 inches apart.
11. Let rise 15 minutes.
12. Make a depression into the center of each roll.
13. Fill with fruit preserves.
14. Bake at 450 degrees F (230 degrees C) until brown.
15. Watch carefully.
16. Cool on wire racks.
