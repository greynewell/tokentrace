## Ingredients

- 1 pound spaghetti
- 1 pound lean ground beef
- 1  onion, chopped
- 1  green bell pepper, chopped
- 2 cloves garlic, chopped
- 1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of mushroom soup
- 1 ½ cups water
- 1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed tomato soup
- 2 cups shredded sharp Cheddar cheese

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook until al dente, 8 to 10 minutes; drain and set aside.
3. Sauté beef, onion, green pepper, and garlic in a large skillet over medium heat until beef is browned, about 10 minutes; drain and return mixture to the skillet.
4. Stir cream of mushroom soup, water, and condensed tomato soup into the skillet. Stir pasta into meat and soup mixture; mix well. Spread mixture into a 9x13-inch baking dish; top with Cheddar cheese.
5. Bake in the preheated oven until bubbly and cheese is lightly browned, 20 to 30 minutes; serve.
