## Ingredients

- 1 cup tomato sauce
- 1 cup diced celery
- ½ cup chopped green bell pepper
- ½ cup diced onion
- ⅓ cup diced carrots
- 2 tablespoons lemon juice
- 1 ½ teaspoons Worcestershire sauce
- ½ tablespoon salt
- 1 pound halibut fillets
- 4 slices  lemon, for garnish

## Instructions

1. In a small sauce pan, combine the tomato sauce, celery, green pepper, onion, carrots, lemon juice, Worcestershire sauce and salt.
2. Bring to a boil.
3. Reduce heat and simmer, covered, for 5 minutes.
4. Spoon vegetable mixture into a shallow 1 1/2 quart baking dish.
5. Place fish filets over vegetables and spoon some of the sauce over the fish.
6. Bake, uncovered, at 400 degrees F (205 degrees C) for about 15 minutes or until fish flakes with a fork.
7. Garnish with lemon twists and chopped celery.
