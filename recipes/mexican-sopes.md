## Ingredients

- 1 ½ cups masa harina (corn flour)
- 3 tablespoons flour
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ¾ cup water
- 1  egg, beaten
- ¼ cup shortening, melted
- 2 cups vegetable oil for frying, or as needed

## Instructions

1. Combine  , flour, and salt in a bowl.
2. Stir water and egg into the masa mixture; add melted shortening and mix well to form dough.
3. Gently knead dough until moist but retains shape; transfer to a bowl, cover bowl with plastic wrap, and refrigerate for 1 hour.
4. Divide chilled dough into 15 portions.
5. Roll 1 portion of dough into a ball, while keeping remaining dough covered to prevent it from drying.
6. On a lightly floured work surface, pat ball into a 3-inch round.
7. Pinch along the edges of the round to make a ridge.
8. Repeat with remaining dough portions.
9. Pour enough oil into a skillet to be about 1/2-inch deep; heat to 365 degrees F (185 degrees C).
10. Fry a couple shells in hot oil at a time until crisp, 1 to 2 minutes per side.
11. Remove sopes from oil using a slotted spoon to a plate lined with paper towel.
12. Add more oil as needed and reheat between batches.
