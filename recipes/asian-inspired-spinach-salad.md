## Ingredients

- 5 cups fresh spinach, rinsed and dried
- 1 cup bean sprouts
- 2  clementines, peeled and segmented
- ⅓ cup sliced fresh mushrooms
- ⅓ cup vegetable oil
- 2 tablespoons soy sauce
- 1 pinch garlic powder

## Instructions

1. Tear the spinach into bite-size pieces and toss in a large bowl with the bean sprouts, clementines, and mushrooms.
2. Whisk together the oil, soy sauce, and garlic powder in a small bowl. Pour over the spinach mixture, toss, and let stand for about 5 minutes to let the flavors blend.
