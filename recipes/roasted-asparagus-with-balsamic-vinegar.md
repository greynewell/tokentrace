## Ingredients

- 2 pounds fresh asparagus, trimmed
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- ½ pinch ground sea salt
- ⅛ teaspoon ground black pepper
- 3 tablespoons balsamic vinegar

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (205 degrees C).
2. Place asparagus in a shallow 9x13-inch baking dish. Sprinkle with oil, salt, and pepper; toss to coat.
3. Bake in preheated oven until lightly browned, 15 to 20 minutes. Drizzle with vinegar just before serving.
