## Ingredients

- 1 cup all-purpose flour
- 1 cup whole wheat flour
- 1 ½ cups wheat bran
- ½ teaspoon salt
- 1 ¼ teaspoons baking soda
- 6 tablespoons molasses
- 2 cups buttermilk
- 1  egg, beaten
- 3 tablespoons butter, melted

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Grease muffin pans or line with paper cups.
3. In a large bowl, combine all purpose flour, whole wheat flour, wheat bran, salt and baking  soda.
4. Add molasses, buttermilk, egg and butter; stir just to moisten.
5. Scoop batter into prepared muffin pans.
6. Cups should be 2/3 to 3/4 full.
7. Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 20 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of a muffin comes out clean.
