## Ingredients

- 8 cups diced rhubarb
- 1 cup white sugar
- 3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
- 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
- 1 cup brown sugar
- 1 cup butter, softened
- ¾ cup rolled oats

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). Grease a baking sheet.
2. Mix rhubarb, white sugar, and 3 tablespoons flour together in a bowl; spread onto baking sheet. Mix 1 1/2 cups flour, brown sugar, butter, and oats together in a bowl; sprinkle over rhubarb mixture.
3. Bake in the preheated oven until browned and crunchy, 35 to 40 minutes.
