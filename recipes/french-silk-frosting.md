## Ingredients

- 2 ⅔ cups confectioners' sugar
- ⅔ cup butter, softened
- 2 (1 ounce) squares unsweetened chocolate, melted
- ¾ teaspoon vanilla extract
- 2 tablespoons milk

## Instructions

1. In a medium bowl, blend confectioners sugar, butter, melted chocolate and vanilla on low speed.
2. Increase speed to high.
3. Gradually add milk; beat until smooth and fluffy.
