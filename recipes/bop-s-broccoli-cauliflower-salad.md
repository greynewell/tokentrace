## Ingredients

- 5 slices bacon, or more to taste
- 1 head fresh broccoli, diced
- 1 head cauliflower, chopped
- ¾ cup shredded sharp Cheddar cheese
- ¼  red onion, chopped
- ½ cup creamy salad dressing (such as Miracle Whip®)
- ½ cup white sugar
- 2 tablespoons white vinegar

## Instructions

1. Place bacon in a large skillet and cook over medium-high heat, turning occasionally, until evenly browned, about 10 minutes. Drain bacon slices on paper towels and crumble.
2. Stir broccoli, cauliflower, Cheddar cheese, and onion together in a large bowl.
3. Whisk creamy salad dressing, sugar, and vinegar together in a separate bowl until sugar is dissolved; pour over broccoli mixture and toss to coat. Refrigerate salad for at least 30 minutes. Stir crumbled bacon into salad just before serving.
