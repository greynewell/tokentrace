## Ingredients

- ½  cucumber, peeled and grated
- 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
- 3 tablespoons chopped fresh dill
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- 10 slices dense white bread
- ½  cucumber, peeled and thinly sliced
- 1 pound deli roast beef, thinly sliced

## Instructions

1. Wrap grated cucumber in a clean kitchen towel; squeeze to release liquid from cucumber.
2. Transfer grated cucumber to a bowl.
3. Mix cream cheese, dill, salt, and black pepper into grated cucumber.
4. Spread cream cheese mixture over bread slices.
5. Divide roast beef and cucumber slices on 5 slices bread and place another slice of bread spread-side down on top to make 5 sandwiches.
