## Ingredients

- 1 pound rotini pasta
- 4  carrots, shredded
- 1  green bell pepper, chopped
- 1  onion, diced
- 1 cup distilled white vinegar
- 1 cup white sugar
- 1 (14 ounce) can sweetened condensed milk
- 2 cups mayonnaise
- 1 teaspoon salt
- ½ teaspoon ground black pepper

## Instructions

1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
2. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.
3. In large bowl, combine pasta, carrot, green pepper and onion.
4. In medium bowl, combine vinegar, sugar, condensed milk, mayonnaise, salt and pepper.
5. Toss salad with dressing and chill 4 hours in refrigerator before serving.
