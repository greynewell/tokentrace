## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons olive oil, divided
- 2 pints cherry or grape tomatoes
- Salt and pepper
- 2  garlic cloves, minced
- 1 tablespoon minced fresh basil

## Instructions

1. Heat 1 tablespoon olive oil in a 12-inch frying pan over medium-high heat until oil starts to smoke.
2. Add tomatoes to hot oil; season with salt and pepper. Saut√©, shaking pan frequently, until tomatoes soften and skins just begin to wrinkle, about 2 minutes.
3. Stir in garlic and continue shaking the pan until garlic is fragrant. Remove from heat; stir in fresh basil and remaining 1 tablespoon olive oil.
