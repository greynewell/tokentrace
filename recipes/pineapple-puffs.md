## Ingredients

- ½ cup butter, softened
- 1 cup white sugar
- 1  egg
- 1 (8 ounce) can crushed pineapple
- 2 cups all-purpose flour
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ½ teaspoon baking soda
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- ½ teaspoon ground cinnamon

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Grease muffin cups or line with paper muffin liners.
3. In a large bowl, cream together the butter and sugar until light and fluffy.
4. Add the egg; beat well.
5. Stir in the crushed pineapple and its juice.
6. In a large mixing bowl, mix together the flour, salt, baking soda, baking powder and cinnamon.
7. Add this mixture to the pineapple mixture; stir well.
8. Scoop batter into prepared muffin cups.
9. Bake 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 20 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of a muffin comes out clean.
10. Cool  for 10 minutes in the pans before removing to a wire rack to cool completely.
