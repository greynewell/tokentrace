## Ingredients

- 1 ½ (16 ounce) boxes dry fettuccine pasta
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 2 tablespoons chopped sun-dried tomatoes
- 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
- 1 tablespoon minced garlic
- 1 teaspoon dried basil
- 2 pounds sea scallops
- 1 cup chopped mushrooms
- 3  green onions, sliced
- 2 (16 ounce) jars garlic Alfredo sauce (such as Catelli®)

## Instructions

1. Fill a large pot with lightly salted water and bring to a rolling boil.
2. Stir in fettuccine; cook at a boil until tender yet firm to the bite, about 8 minutes.
3. Drain.
4. Melt butter in a large skillet over medium heat.
5. Cook and stir sun-dried tomatoes, parsley, garlic, and basil in the hot butter until browned, about 5 minutes.
6. Add scallops in a single layer; cook until they release from the bottom of the skillet, 2 to 3 minutes per side.
7. Transfer to a large plate.
8. Cook and stir mushrooms and green onions in the same skillet until lightly browned and tender, about 5 minutes.
9. Pour in  ; cook, stirring often, until heated through, 5 to 10 minutes.
10. Return scallop mixture to the skillet; stir until coated with sauce and warmed through, 2 to 3 minutes.
11. Serve sauce over fettucine.
