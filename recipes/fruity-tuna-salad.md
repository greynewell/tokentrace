## Ingredients

- 2 (5 ounce) cans tuna, drained
- 1 cup chopped dates
- 1 ½ cups chopped celery
- 1 large apple - peeled, cored and diced
- ½ cup lemon yogurt
- 1 tablespoon minced onion
- 2 teaspoons lemon juice
- 1 teaspoon ground curry powder

## Instructions

1. In a large bowl, mix the tuna, dates, celery and apple.
2. In a separate bowl, whisk together the yogurt, onion, lemon juice, and curry powder.
3. Pour over tuna mixture and gently toss to coat.
4. Refrigerate 1 hour, or until chilled.
