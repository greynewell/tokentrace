## Ingredients

- 24  pitted prunes
- 12  bacon strips, cut in half
- 8  bamboo skewers, soaked in water for 20 minutes

## Instructions

1. Preheat an oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Wrap each prune with one of the pieces of bacon.
3. Thread three of the wrapped prunes onto each skewer.
4. Arrange the skewers on a baking sheet.
5. Bake in the preheated oven until the bacon is crisp, about 12 minutes.
6. Allow to cool before serving.
