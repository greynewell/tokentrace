## Ingredients

- 8 ounces wide egg noodles
- ¼ cup butter
- 1  onion, diced
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- 4 ounces baby spinach leaves
- salt and ground black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook egg noodles in the boiling water, stirring occasionally until cooked through but firm to the bite, about 8 minutes; drain and return noodles to pot.
2. While the noodles boil, melt butter in a skillet over medium-high heat. Saute onion and garlic in melted butter until lightly browned and soft, about 8 minutes. Stir baby spinach into the onion mixture; saute until the spinach is just barely wilted, about 2 minutes.
3. Stir spinach mixture into the noodles; season with salt and pepper.
