## Ingredients

- 1 (8 ounce) container blended strawberry yogurt
- 1 (8 ounce) tub frozen whipped topping (such as Cool Whip®), thawed
- 5 teaspoons strawberry flavored Jell-O® mix
- 3 tablespoons white sugar
- 2 tablespoons strawberry flavored cream cheese  (Optional)
- 2  fresh strawberries

## Instructions

1. Mix the yogurt and whipped topping together in a large bowl.
2. Stir in the strawberry gelatin and the sugar.
3. Mix until well dissolved.
4. If a richer taste is desired, whisk in the strawberry flavored cream cheese until smooth.
5. Garnish with fresh strawberries.
