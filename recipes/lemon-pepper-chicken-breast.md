## Ingredients

- 4  skinless, boneless chicken breasts
- 2 teaspoons lemon pepper seasoning
- 4 tablespoons butter, divided
- 1 cup thinly sliced carrots
- ⅓ cup thinly sliced green onions
- 2 cups chicken broth
- 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
- ¼ cup sour cream

## Instructions

1. Rub chicken breasts with lemon pepper seasoning.
2. Melt 2 tablespoons butter in a large skillet over medium heat.
3. Cook chicken breasts until juices run clear, 7 to 10 minutes per side.
4. Remove chicken from the skillet and cover with foil.
5. Add remaining 2 tablespoons butter, carrots, and green onions to the skillet.
6. Saute until crisp-tender, stirring frequently, 3 to 5 minutes.
7. Whisk chicken broth and flour together and pour over the vegetables in the skillet.
8. Bring to a boil; simmer until thick, about 5 minutes.
9. Add sour cream and mix well.
10. Return chicken breasts to the skillet.
11. Heat until warmed through, 3 to 5 minutes.
