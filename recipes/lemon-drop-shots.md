## Ingredients

- ¾ fluid ounce vodka (such as Absolut®)
- ¾ fluid ounce lemon juice
- 2 ½ teaspoons white sugar, or to taste
- 1  lemon, cut into wedges

## Instructions

1. Combine vodka and lemon juice in a shot glass. Sprinkle 1/2 teaspoon sugar on top.
2. Place 2 teaspoons sugar in a small plate; dip lemon wedges in sugar. Suck on a sugared lemon after drinking the shot.
