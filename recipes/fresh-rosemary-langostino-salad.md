## Ingredients

- 1 (16 ounce) package frozen pre-cooked langostinos (such as Trader Joe's®), thawed
- 1  red bell pepper, diced
- ¼ cup finely chopped fresh rosemary
- ½ teaspoon sea salt
- ½ teaspoon red pepper flakes
- ¼ cup dry white wine
- ¼ cup white balsamic vinegar

## Instructions

1. Combine langostinos, red bell pepper, rosemary, sea salt, red pepper flakes, white wine, and balsamic vinegar, in respective order, in a large bowl.
2. Toss until langostinos are evenly coated.
3. Cover bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate until flavors blend, 4 hours to overnight.
