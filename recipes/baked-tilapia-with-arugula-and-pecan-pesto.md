## Ingredients

- 3 cups young arugula leaves, rinsed and dried
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- ½ cup chopped pecans
- ¼ cup extra virgin olive oil
- ¼ cup grated Parmigiano-Reggiano cheese
- ½ teaspoon ground black pepper
- 1 dash fresh lemon juice
- sea salt to taste
- ½ cup young arugula leaves, rinsed and dried
- 4 (8 ounce) fillets tilapia
- 1 tablespoon grated Parmigiano-Reggiano cheese

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
2. Lightly grease a 9x13 inch baking pan.
3. Combine 3 cups arugula, garlic, pecans, olive oil, 1/4 CUP Parmigiano-Reggiano cheese, black pepper, and lemon juice in a blender and blend until it reaches the consistency of a pesto sauce.
4. Spread 1/2 cup arugula on the prepared baking pan.
5. Place the tilapia fillets on the arugula.
6. Spread the remaining pesto generously on top of fillets.
7. Sprinkle 1 tablespoon Parmigiano-Reggiano cheese on the pesto.
8. Bake in preheated oven until fish flakes easily, about 20 minutes.
