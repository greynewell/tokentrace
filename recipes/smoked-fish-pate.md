## Ingredients

- 8 ounces smoked whitefish, skinned and boned
- ¼ cup mayonnaise
- ¼ cup unsalted butter, at room temperature
- ½ teaspoon Worcestershire sauce

## Instructions

1. Place the whitefish, mayonnaise, butter, and Worcestershire sauce into a food processor.
2. Pulse until the mixture is smooth.
