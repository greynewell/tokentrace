## Ingredients

- 24  frozen dinner roll dough, thawed, divided
- ½ cup butter
- 1 cup brown sugar
- 1 (4.6 ounce) package cook-and-serve vanilla pudding mix
- 2 tablespoons milk
- ½ teaspoon ground cinnamon

## Instructions

1. Grease a 9x13-inch baking dish.
2. Arrange 12 dinner rolls in the prepared baking dish.
3. Melt butter in a saucepan over medium-low heat. Add brown sugar, pudding mix, milk, and cinnamon to melted butter; stir until smooth. Pour pudding mixture over rolls; top with remaining 12 rolls. Allow rolls to rise for 3 hours.
4. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
5. Bake in the preheated oven until rolls are cooked through, 30 to 35 minutes. Cool for 15 minutes before serving.
