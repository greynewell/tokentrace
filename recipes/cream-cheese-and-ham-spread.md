## Ingredients

- 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
- 2 ½ ounces sliced ham, chopped
- 2  green onions, chopped

## Instructions

1. In a medium-size mixing bowl, combine cream cheese, ham, and onions.
2. Cover and refrigerate overnight before serving.
