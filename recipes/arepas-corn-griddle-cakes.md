## Ingredients

- 2 cups warm water
- 2 cups pre-cooked cornmeal
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 tablespoon butter, or as needed

## Instructions

1. Combine water, cornmeal, and salt in a bowl.
2. Mix together and let sit until water is absorbed, about 5 minutes.
3. Form dough into patties about 5 inches across and 1/2-inch thick.
4. Coat a griddle with butter.
5. Cook patties, one at a time, over medium-high heat until lightly browned, about 3 minutes per side.
