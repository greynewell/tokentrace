## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 1  onion, sliced
- 1 tablespoon garlic basil spread (see footnote for recipe link)
- 1 tablespoon Italian seasoning
- 1 cup pickled sweet and hot pepper rings
- 1 cup sliced mushrooms
- 2 tablespoons Marsala wine
- 1 cup beef broth
- 2 pounds sliced roast beef
- 4  ciabatta sandwich rolls, sliced horizontally
- ¼ cup garlic basil spread, divided (see footnote for recipe link)
- ¼ cup prepared horseradish sauce, divided
- 12 slices Swiss cheese, divided

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 500 degrees F (260 degrees C).
2. Heat olive oil in a large pot or Dutch oven over medium heat.
3. Cook and stir onion, 1 tablespoon garlic basil spread, and Italian seasoning in the hot oil until onion has softened, 4 to 5 minutes.
4. Stir in pepper rings and mushrooms; cook and stir until mushrooms have softened, about 5 minutes.
5. Pour in Marsala wine and bring to a boil, scraping up any browned bits from the bottom of the pan.
6. Add beef broth and roast beef and stir until the beef is warmed through, 3 to 4 minutes.
7. Spread each ciabatta roll with 1 tablespoon garlic basil spread and 1 tablespoon horseradish sauce.
8. Top with 3 slices Swiss cheese.
9. Place sandwiches on a baking sheet and bake in the preheated oven until the cheese is melted and the bread is toasted, 3 to 4 minutes.
10. Using a slotted spoon, pile the beef-onion mixture into each sandwich and pour 1 to 2 tablespoons pan juices over the filling.
11. Transfer the remaining pan juices to small bowls for dipping.
