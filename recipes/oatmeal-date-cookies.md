## Ingredients

- 2 cups all-purpose flour
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- ¾ teaspoon baking soda
- ½ teaspoon salt
- 1 cup packed brown sugar
- 1 cup shortening
- 3  eggs
- ½ cup white sugar
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 2 cups quick cooking oats
- 1 cup dates, pitted and chopped
- ½ cup chopped walnuts

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C) and grease cookie sheets.
2. In a separate bowl, sift together flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Set aside.
3. In a large bowl, cream together shortening and both sugars until light and fluffy, using an electric mixer at medium speed.
4. Add eggs, one at a time, beating well after each. Blend in vanilla.
5. Gradually add dry ingredients to creamed mixture, mixing well with a spoon.
6. Stir in oats, dates, and walnuts.
7. Drop dough by teaspoonfuls about 2 inches apart on a cookie sheet.
8. Bake for 8 minutes or until lightly browned. Remove from cookie sheets to cool on racks.
