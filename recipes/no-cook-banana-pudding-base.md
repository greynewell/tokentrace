## Ingredients

- 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese
- 1 (14 ounce) can sweetened condensed milk
- 1 (5 ounce) package instant vanilla pudding mix
- 1 (5 ounce) package instant banana pudding mix
- 4 cups cold whole milk
- 1 (8 ounce) container sour cream
- 1 (8 ounce) container frozen whipped topping, thawed

## Instructions

1. Beat cream cheese in a large bowl with an electric mixer until smooth.
2. Add sweetened condensed milk, vanilla pudding mix, banana pudding mix, milk, and sour cream; beat until smooth.
3. Fold whipped topping into pudding mixture.
4. Chill in refrigerator for at least 2 hours before serving.
