## Ingredients

- 2 pounds ground turkey
- ¾ teaspoon ground ginger
- 1 ½ teaspoons salt
- 1 teaspoon dried sage
- ¼ teaspoon cayenne pepper
- 1 ½ teaspoons ground black pepper

## Instructions

1. Mix ground turkey, black pepper, salt, sage, ginger, and cayenne pepper together in a large bowl until well blended. Form mixture into 8 equal-sized patties.
2. Heat a skillet over medium-high heat; coat with nonstick spray.
3. Add patties and fry until browned on both sides and no longer pink in the center, about 15 minutes. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 165 degrees F (74 degrees C).
