## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons chocolate-hazelnut spread (such as Nutella®)
- 4 slices bread
- 1 cup miniature marshmallows, or as needed
- 1 tablespoon butter

## Instructions

1. Spread chocolate-hazelnut spread evenly over 2 slices of bread.
2. Arrange marshmallows over chocolate-hazelnut spread.
3. Place remaining 2 slices bread over marshmallows creating 2 sandwiches.
4. Spread butter over both sides of each sandwich.
5. Place both sandwiches in a skillet over medium heat and grill until golden brown, about 5 minutes per side.
