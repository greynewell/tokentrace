## Ingredients

- 2 cups peanut butter, divided
- ¾ cup butter, softened
- 2 cups powdered sugar
- 3 cups graham cracker crumbs
- 1 (12 ounce) package NESTLE® TOLL HOUSE® Semi-Sweet Chocolate Mini Morsels, divided

## Instructions

1. Grease 13 x 9-inch baking pan.
2. Beat 1 1/4 cups peanut butter and butter in large mixer bowl until creamy.
3. Gradually beat in 1 cup powdered sugar.
4. With hands or wooden spoon, work in remaining powdered sugar, graham cracker crumbs and 1/2 cup morsels.
5. Press evenly into prepared baking pan.
6. Smooth top with spatula.
7. Melt remaining peanut butter and remaining morsels in medium, heavy-duty saucepan over lowest possible heat, stirring constantly, until smooth.
8. Spread over graham cracker crust in pan.
9. Refrigerate for at least 1 hour or until chocolate is firm; cut into bars.
10. Store in refrigerator.
