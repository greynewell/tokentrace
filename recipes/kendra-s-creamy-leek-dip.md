## Ingredients

- 1 pound bacon
- 15  leeks, roots and leaves removed
- 3 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese
- 2 large cloves garlic
- 2 ½ tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
- salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Place bacon in a skillet.
2. Cook over medium high heat until evenly brown.
3. Drain and crumble.
4. In a food processor, blend the cooked bacon, leeks, cream cheese, garlic, and Worcestershire sauce until smooth.
5. Season with salt and pepper.
6. Refrigerate until ready to serve.
