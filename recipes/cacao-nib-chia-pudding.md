## Ingredients

- 1 cup unsweetened almond milk, or more as needed
- ⅓ cup chia seeds
- ¼ cup cashews
- 5 tablespoons cacao nibs, divided
- 1 tablespoon maple syrup
- 1 teaspoon unsweetened cocoa powder
- 1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
- 1 teaspoon instant espresso powder  (Optional)
- ¼ teaspoon sea salt
- ¼ teaspoon ground cinnamon

## Instructions

1. Combine almond milk, chia seeds, cashews, 2 tablespoons cacao nibs, maple syrup, cocoa powder, vanilla extract, sea salt, and cinnamon, in a high-speed blender.
2. Blend until well combined, 30 seconds to 1 minute.
3. Stir in 1 tablespoon cacao nibs and pour into individual bowls.
4. Cover and refrigerate for 1 hour or overnight.
5. Mixture will thicken as it chills.
6. Sprinkle pudding with remaining cacao nibs before serving.
