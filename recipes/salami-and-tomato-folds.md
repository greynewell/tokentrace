## Ingredients

- 22 thin slices  salami
- ½ cup herb and garlic-flavored cream cheese
- 22  grape tomatoes
- 22  toothpicks

## Instructions

1. Spread each salami slice with a thin layer of cream cheese; fold each salami slice in half over cream cheese layer.
2. Place 1 tomato in the center of each salami half; roll salami around the tomato overlapping the two edges; secure edges to tomato with a toothpick.
