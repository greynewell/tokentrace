## Ingredients

- 4 (6 ounce) fillets swai fish
- 1 pinch Cajun seasoning, or more to taste
- 1 pinch cayenne pepper, or more to taste
- ½ cup minced banana peppers
- 2  habanero peppers, minced
- 1 tablespoon thyme leaves
- ¼ cup unsalted butter, cut into 4 pieces

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C).
2. Arrange swai fish in a nonstick baking dish; season with Cajun seasoning and cayenne pepper.
3. Layer banana peppers, habanero peppers, and thyme leaves on top of each fish fillet.
4. Place 1 piece butter on top of each fish fillet.
5. Cover dish with aluminum foil.
6. Bake in the preheated oven until fish flakes easily with a fork, 30 to 40 minutes.
