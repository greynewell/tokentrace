## Ingredients

- 2 cups all-purpose flour
- 2  egg yolks, beaten
- ⅛ teaspoon salt
- water as needed

## Instructions

1. Mix flour, egg yolks, and salt together in a bowl using your hands, adding water as needed, 1 teaspoon at a time, until the mixture forms a ball.
2. Knead until dough is smooth.
3. Roll dough out on a lightly floured surface using a rolling pin until thin enough be be filled.
4. Cut dough into 7 1/2 by 7 1/2-inch squares.
