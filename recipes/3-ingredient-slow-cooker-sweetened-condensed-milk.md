## Ingredients

- 2 cups milk
- ⅔ cup white sugar
- 3 tablespoons butter
- 2 teaspoons vanilla extract  (Optional)

## Instructions

1. Turn slow cooker on High.
2. Mix milk and sugar together in the crock of the slow cooker.
3. Cook for 3 hours, stirring every 10 minutes in the last 30 minutes to prevent burning.
4. Mix butter and vanilla extract into condensed milk and let cool.
