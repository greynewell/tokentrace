## Ingredients

- 4 cups self-rising flour
- 1 tablespoon white sugar
- 1 tablespoon baking powder
- ⅔ cup shortening
- 2 cups buttermilk
- ¼ cup buttermilk

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
2. Lightly grease two baking sheets.
3. In a large mixing bowl, combine flour, sugar and baking powder; stir well.
4. Cut in shortening until mixture resembles coarse cornmeal.
5. Add 2 cups buttermilk and mix just to moisten.
6. Roll out dough to 1 inch thick and cut into biscuits.
7. Place biscuits on prepared baking sheets; brush tops with remaining 1/4 cup buttermilk.
8. Bake in preheated oven for 15 minutes, or until golden.
