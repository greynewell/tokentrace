## Ingredients

- 1 cup coffee-flavored liqueur (such as Kahlua®)
- 4 ounces Chihuahua cheese, sliced into strips
- 10  large shrimp, peeled and deveined, or more to taste
- 10 slices smoked bacon, or more to taste

## Instructions

1. Bring coffee-flavored liqueur to a simmer in a small saucepan over medium heat.
2. Simmer until liqueur is reduced by half, 5 to 10 minutes.
3. Set oven rack in the middle of the oven and preheat the oven's broiler.
4. Place a slice of Chihuahua cheese on the back of each shrimp and wrap shrimp and cheese with 1 bacon strip; set on a baking sheet.
5. Repeat for remaining shrimp.
6. Broil on the middle rack in the preheated broiler until bacon cooks and cheese melts, about 5 minutes.
7. Transfer cooked shrimp to a plate and drizzle with liqueur reduction.
