## Ingredients

- ¾ cup balsamic vinegar
- 2  English cucumbers, thinly sliced
- 1 pint grape tomatoes, halved
- 1  cooked chicken breast, thinly sliced
- 2 tablespoons olive oil, or to taste
- salt and ground black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Heat balsamic vinegar in a small saucepan over medium heat.
2. Bring to a boil, stirring constantly to prevent burning and reducing heat if necessary.
3. Cook and stir until reduced to a syrup-like consistency, 6 to 8 minutes.
4. Combine cucumbers, tomatoes, and chicken breast in a large bowl.
5. Drizzle the balsamic reduction and olive oil on top; toss to coat.
6. Season with salt and pepper.
