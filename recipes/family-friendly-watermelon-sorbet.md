## Ingredients

- 2 ¼ cups sliced watermelon
- ½  lemon, juiced
- 1 tablespoon white sugar

## Instructions

1. Combine watermelon, lemon juice, and sugar in a blender; blend until smooth and foamy.
2. Pour blended mixture into a glass measuring cup through a fine-mesh strainer, pressing with a spoon to extract as much juice as possible; juice should measure about 1 3/4 cup.
3. Pour watermelon juice into small glass dessert bowls.
4. Freeze until firm, about 2 hours.
