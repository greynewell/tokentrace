## Ingredients

- 2 ½ cups Quaker® Oats (Quick or Old Fashioned, uncooked)
- 2 tablespoons honey
- ¼ cup creamy or chunky peanut butter
- 1 cup ripe mashed banana
- 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon

## Instructions

1. Place oats and cinnamon in large bowl; stir to blend well. Add mashed banana, peanut butter and honey. Stir until ingredients are well blended.
2. Shape into 24 (about 1-inch diameter) balls. Refrigerate, covered, until chilled. Store leftovers in refrigerator, covered.
