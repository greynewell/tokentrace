## Ingredients

- 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
- 1 cup finely shredded Cheddar cheese
- ¼ cup beer
- ½ (1 ounce) package ranch dip mix
- 1 dash Worcestershire sauce
- cooking spray
- 12  wonton wrappers, cut into triangles
- 1 pinch sea salt to taste

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
2. Mix cream cheese, Cheddar cheese, beer, ranch dip mix, and Worcestershire sauce together in a bowl until well blended.
3. Spray cooking spray into wonton wrappers and place them on a baking sheet.
4. Bake wrappers in the the preheated oven until chips are crispy, about 4 minutes.
5. Serve chips with dip.
