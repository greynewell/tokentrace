## Ingredients

- 2  avocados - peeled, pitted, and chopped
- 1 cup chopped tomatoes
- 1 tablespoon lime juice or tequila
- 1 (1 ounce) package McCormick® Guacamole Seasoning Mix
- 1 (8 ounce) package whipped cream cheese
- ½ cup sour cream
- ¼ cup cocktail sauce, divided
- ¾ cup small cooked shrimp, cut into bite-size pieces

## Instructions

1. Mix avocados, tomatoes, lime juice and Seasoning Mix in medium bowl. Set aside.
2. Mix cream cheese and sour cream in small bowl. Spread in 9-inch glass pie plate. Drizzle with half of the cocktail sauce. Top with avocado mixture and shrimp. Drizzle with remaining cocktail sauce.
3. Serve with sliced baguette or crackers.
