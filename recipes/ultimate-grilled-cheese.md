## Ingredients

- 1 slice sharp Cheddar cheese
- 1 slice mozzarella cheese
- 2  thick bread slices
- 1 pinch dried oregano, or to taste
- 1 pinch salt and ground black pepper to taste
- 2 tablespoons salted butter, at room temperature
- 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley, or to taste
- 1 teaspoon chopped fresh chives, or to taste

## Instructions

1. Assemble the sandwich by placing Cheddar and mozzarella cheese on the bread. Season with oregano, salt, and pepper.
2. Heat butter in a pan over medium-low heat. Add sandwich and cook until cheeses melt, 3 to 5 minutes, increasing heat if needed. Flip and cook until golden brown, 3 to 5 minutes more.
3. Cut in half and top with parsley and chives.
