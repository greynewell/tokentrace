## Ingredients

- 1  zucchini, sliced into rounds
- 1  cucumber, peeled and thinly sliced
- â…“ cup balsamic vinegar
- 3 tablespoons olive oil
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice
- salt and pepper to taste
- 1 twist lemon peel for garnish

## Instructions

1. Place the zucchini slices into a steamer basket and set over one inch of water in a saucepan.
2. Bring to a boil, cover and steam for 3 to 5 minutes, or until tender.
3. Remove from the steamer and cool slightly.
4. In a salad bowl, combine the zucchini and cucumber slices.
5. In a separate  bowl, whisk together the balsamic vinegar, olive oil, lemon juice, salt and pepper.
6. Pour over the zucchini and cucumber.
7. Toss to coat, then garnish with lemon peel.
