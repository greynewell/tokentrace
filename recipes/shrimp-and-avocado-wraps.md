## Ingredients

- 3 tablespoons light mayonnaise
- 1 tablespoon ketchup
- 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
- 1 dash hot pepper sauce (such as Tabasco®)
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- 7 ounces cooked shrimp, peeled and deveined
- 4  Boston lettuce leaves
- 4 (9 inch)  whole wheat tortillas
- 1  ripe avocado - peeled, pitted, and cut into 8 wedges

## Instructions

1. Mix mayonnaise, ketchup, Worcestershire sauce, and hot pepper sauce in a bowl.
2. Season with salt and ground black pepper to taste.
3. Add shrimp; mix well.
4. Heat a skillet or grill pan over high heat; pan-fry tortillas one at a time, cooking until softened and browned, about 10 seconds per side.
5. Center a Boston lettuce leaf on each warm tortilla.
6. Add shrimp mixture; top each with 2 avocado wedges.
7. Fold tortillas at the ends to enclose filling, roll up, and cut in half diagonally to serve.
