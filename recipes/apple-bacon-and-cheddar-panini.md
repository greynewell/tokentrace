## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons butter, or to taste
- 3 slices bread
- ¼ cup shredded Cheddar cheese
- 4 slices cooked bacon
- ¼  apple, peeled and sliced thinly

## Instructions

1. Preheat a panini press.
2. Butter 2 of the bread slices. Place 1 slice on the panini press, buttered-side-down. Arrange Cheddar cheese on the bread; lay unbuttered bread on top. Place bacon and apple on top; cover with the remaining slice of bread, buttered side-up. Close the press and cook until panini is browned, about 4 minutes.
