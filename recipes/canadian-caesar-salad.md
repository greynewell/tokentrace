## Ingredients

- 2 cloves garlic, coarsely chopped, or more to taste
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- 1 tablespoon drained capers
- 2 teaspoons anchovy paste
- ½ cup olive oil
- ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
- 1 ½ teaspoons Worcestershire sauce
- 1 teaspoon distilled white vinegar
- ½ teaspoon prepared hot mustard (such as Keen's®)
- 4 dashes hot pepper sauce (such as Tabasco®), or to taste
- 2  egg yolks
- ½  lemon, juiced
- 1 head romaine lettuce, torn into bite-size pieces
- ¼ cup real bacon bits, or to taste
- 1 (5 ounce) package croutons, or as needed
- 4 teaspoons grated Parmesan cheese, or to taste

## Instructions

1. Place garlic and salt in a wooden salad bowl; mash together with a fork until creamy.
2. Mash capers into garlic paste.
3. Mash anchovy paste into garlic mixture until creamy.
4. Whisk olive oil and black pepper into garlic mixture with a fork until well blended; stir in Worcestershire sauce, vinegar, mustard, and hot pepper sauce.
5. Whisk egg yolks into mixture; whisk in lemon juice.
6. Place romaine into the salad bowl and toss with dressing.
7. Sprinkle salad with bacon bits, croutons, and Parmesan cheese.
