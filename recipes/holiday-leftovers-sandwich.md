## Ingredients

- 4  Ball Park® Hot Dog Buns
- 4 ounces cream cheese
- 1 tablespoon chopped fresh chives
- 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley
- 1 cup mixed salad greens
- 8 slices leftover roast turkey, warmed
- 4 tablespoons cranberry sauce, warmed

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees F and toast buns till golden brown.
2. Combine cream cheese, chives and parsley.
3. Spread 1 tablespoon of herbed cream cheese over toasted buns of each sandwich.
4. Then layer with spring mix and sliced turkey.
5. Top with cranberry sauce.
