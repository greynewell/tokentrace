## Ingredients

- 2 pounds imitation crabmeat, flaked
- 2 (8 ounce) packages reduced-fat cream cheese
- 1  onion, diced
- 2 tablespoons prepared horseradish, or to taste
- 2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
- 1 (12 ounce) jar cocktail sauce

## Instructions

1. Pulse crabmeat in a food processor several times until minced.
2. Transfer crabmeat to a large bowl.
3. Blend cream cheese in food processor until creamy.
4. Add onion, horseradish, and Worcestershire sauce to cream cheese; blend until smooth.
5. Stir cream cheese mixture into crab mixture until well-combined.
6. For crab mixture into 2 to 3 balls, cover with plastic wrap, and refrigerate until firm and chilled, at least 1 hour.
7. Place crab balls on a platter and top with cocktail sauce.
