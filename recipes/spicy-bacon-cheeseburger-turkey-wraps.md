## Ingredients

- 1 pound lean ground turkey
- 2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce (such as French's®)
- 1 tablespoon Cajun seasoning, or to taste
- ¼ cup bacon bits (such as Hormel®)
- 2 tablespoons diced jalapeno pepper
- 1 tablespoon minced garlic
- 7  light flatbreads (such as Flatout®)
- 28  dill pickle slices
- 3 ½ cups fresh spinach
- 7 slices pepper Jack cheese (such as Borden®)

## Instructions

1. Mix turkey with Worcestershire sauce and Cajun seasoning in a bowl.
2. Cook bacon bits, jalapeno pepper, and minced garlic in a skillet over medium-high heat until bacon bits start to stick, about 4 minutes.
3. Add the turkey mixture; cook and stir until no pink remains, about 4 minutes.
4. Place 2 ounces of turkey mixture in each flatbread.
5. Place 4 pickle slices, 1/2 cup spinach, and 1 pepper Jack cheese slice in each wrap.
