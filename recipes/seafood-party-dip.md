## Ingredients

- cooking spray
- 3 tablespoons cold water
- 1 tablespoon plain unflavored gelatin
- ¾ pound cooked shrimp, chopped
- 1 (12 ounce) package imitation crabmeat, flaked
- 1 (10.5 ounce) can condensed cream of mushroom soup
- 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
- 1 bunch green onions, thinly sliced
- 1 cup chopped celery
- 1 cup mayonnaise

## Instructions

1. Spray a mold with cooking spray.
2. Whisk water and gelatin together in a saucepan. Add soup and gently heat over medium-low heat until hot, about 5 minutes.
3. Mix shrimp, imitation crab, condensed soup, cream cheese, green onions, celery, and mayonnaise together in a bowl. Add soup mixture and mix until blended. Transfer into the prepared mold. Place in the refrigerator to chill for 8 hours, or overnight, before serving.
