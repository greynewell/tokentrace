## Ingredients

- ¼ cup Almond Breeze Almondmilk Vanilla
- 3  bananas, cut into 1-inch chunks & frozen
- 2 tablespoons peanut butter
- 2 tablespoons chocolate chips

## Instructions

1. In a food processor or blender, mix frozen banana chunks, Almond Breeze Almondmilk Vanilla, peanut butter, and chocolate chips.
2. Blend until smooth.
