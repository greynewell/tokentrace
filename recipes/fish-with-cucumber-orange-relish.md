## Ingredients

- 1  English cucumber, chopped
- 1 (11 ounce) can mandarin oranges, drained
- ¼ cup apple cider vinegar
- 2  green onions, thinly sliced
- 1 tablespoon chopped fresh cilantro, or to taste
- 1 teaspoon minced fresh dill
- cooking spray
- 2 pounds orange roughy fillets
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper

## Instructions

1. Mix cucumber, mandarin oranges, vinegar, green onions, cilantro, and dill in a bowl until relish is well blended.
2. Set oven rack 3 to 4 inches from the heat source and preheat the oven's broiler.
3. Spray a broiler pan with cooking spray.
4. Arrange orange roughy fillets on the prepared broiler pan; season with salt and pepper.
5. Broil in preheated oven until fish is easily flaked with a fork, 9 to 12 minutes.
