## Ingredients

- 2 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened
- ¼ cup milk
- 2 bunches green onions, chopped
- salt to taste

## Instructions

1. In a medium bowl, blend cream cheese and milk.
2. Use more milk as needed to obtain desired consistency.
3. Stir in green onions and salt.
4. Refrigerate until serving.
