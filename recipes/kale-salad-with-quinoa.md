## Ingredients

- 1 ½ cups water
- ¾ cup quinoa
- 1 (10 ounce) package kale, chopped
- 1 bunch grapes, halved
- 2 (6 ounce) containers fresh blueberries
- 1  red bell pepper, diced
- 8  strawberries, halved
- 1  lemon, zested and juiced
- ¼ cup extra-virgin olive oil
- ¼ cup apple cider vinegar

## Instructions

1. Bring water and quinoa to a boil in a saucepan. Reduce heat to medium-low, cover, and simmer until quinoa is tender, 15 to 20 minutes. Let cool completely, 20 to 30 minutes.
2. Mix cooled quinoa and kale together in a large bowl. Add grapes, blueberries, and red bell pepper.
3. Puree strawberries in a blender until smooth. Add lemon zest and juice; blend again. Add oil and vinegar; blend until all flavors are combined. Add dressing to the salad. Serve cold.
