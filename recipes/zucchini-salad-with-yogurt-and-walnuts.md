## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 2  zucchini, grated
- 2 cups plain yogurt
- 2 tablespoons walnuts, chopped
- salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Heat the oil in a skillet over high heat.
2. Cook and stir the grated zucchini for 3 minutes, stirring constantly.
3. Remove from heat and let cool.
4. Mix the zucchini with the yogurt and walnuts, and season with salt and pepper.
