## Ingredients

- 8  potatoes, chopped
- 3  leeks, white and light green parts only, cut into 1/4-inch rounds
- 1  onion, diced
- 3 tablespoons margarine
- 2  chicken bouillon cubes
- 1 tablespoon salt
- ½ teaspoon ground black pepper
- 1 (12 ounce) can evaporated milk

## Instructions

1. Place the potatoes, leeks, onion, margarine, chicken bouillon, salt, and pepper in a slow cooker.
2. Pour enough water over mixture to cover.
3. Cook on High 4 hours.
4. Stir in the evaporated milk.
5. Ladle soup into a blender and blend until smooth.
6. Serve hot.
