## Ingredients

- 1 cup water
- 1 (.7 ounce) package dry Italian-style salad dressing mix
- 1 (1 ounce) package ranch dressing mix
- 1 (.75 ounce) packet dry brown gravy mix
- 1 (3 pound) boneless beef chuck roast

## Instructions

1. Whisk water, ranch dressing mix, Italian dressing mix, and brown gravy mix together in a bowl until smooth. Place the beef roast into a slow cooker; pour the dressing mix on top.
2. Cook on Low until the roast is easily pierced by a fork, 6 to 8 hours.
