## Ingredients

- 1 cup heavy whipping cream
- ¾ cup white wine
- 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon dried parsley

## Instructions

1. Combine cream, wine, flour, parsley, and salt in a medium saucepan over medium-high heat; bring to a boil.
2. Reduce heat to low and simmer until thickened.
