## Ingredients

- ½ teaspoon salt
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- 2 tablespoons minced red bell pepper
- 2  green onions, minced
- 2 small shallots, minced
- 1 tablespoon minced fresh parsley leaves
- 2 teaspoons minced fresh dill
- 1 teaspoon minced fresh cilantro leaves
- 1 teaspoon fresh lemon juice
- fresh ground black pepper to taste
- ½ cup buttermilk
- ½ cup mayonnaise
- ¼ cup sour cream

## Instructions

1. Sprinkle the salt over the minced garlic in the bottom of a mixing bowl; make a paste, rubbing the salt into the garlic thoroughly with the back of a spoon.
2. Add the red bell pepper, green onion, shallot, parsley, dill, cilantro, and lemon juice; mix lightly until well combined.
3. Season with black pepper.
4. Add the buttermilk, mayonnaise, and sour cream to the mixture; beat with a wire whisk until evenly combined.
5. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate at least 2 hours.
6. Dressing keeps refrigerated up to 1 week.
