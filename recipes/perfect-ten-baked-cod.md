## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons butter
- ½ sleeve buttery round crackers (such as Ritz®), crushed
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 1 pound thick-cut cod loin
- ½  lemon, juiced
- ¼ cup dry white wine
- 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley
- 1 tablespoon chopped green onion
- 1  lemon, cut into wedges

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
2. Place 2 tablespoons butter in a microwave-safe bowl. Melt in the microwave on high, about 30 seconds. Stir buttery round crackers into melted butter.
3. Place remaining 2 tablespoons butter in a 7x11-inch baking dish. Melt in the preheated oven, 1 to 3 minutes. Remove dish from oven.
4. Coat both sides of cod in melted butter in the baking dish.
5. Bake cod in the preheated oven for 10 minutes. Remove from oven; top with lemon juice, wine, and cracker mixture. Return to the oven and bake until fish is opaque and flakes easily with a fork, about 10 more minutes.
6. Garnish with parsley and green onion and serve with lemon wedges.
