## Ingredients

- 3 cups Corn Chex® cereal
- 3 cups Wheat Chex® cereal
- 2 cups Chocolate Chex® cereal
- 2 cups bite-size square cheese crackers
- 3 cups cheese popcorn
- ¼ cup butter
- ⅓ cup light brown sugar or molasses
- ⅓ cup light corn syrup
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ½ teaspoon baking soda

## Instructions

1. Mix cereals, 1 cup of the cheese crackers and 2 cups of the popcorn in large, microwavable bowl.
2. Set aside.
3. Place butter, brown sugar, corn syrup and salt in 2-cup microwavable measuring cup.
4. Microwave on High 1 minute 30 seconds.
5. Stir; microwave on High until bubbling.
6. Remove from microwave; stir in baking soda.
7. Mixture will foam up, but keep mixing until baking soda is dissolved.
8. Pour over the cereal mixture; stir until fully coated.
9. Microwave on High 4 to 5 minutes, stirring after every minute.
10. Spread on waxed paper-lined baking sheet.
11. Let cool for about 5 minutes to let caramel fully harden.
12. Stir in remaining 1 cup cheese crackers and 1 cup popcorn.
