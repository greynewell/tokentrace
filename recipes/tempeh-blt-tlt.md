## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
- 4  slices tempeh bacon
- 2 teaspoons mayonnaise, or to taste
- 2 slices whole wheat bread, toasted
- 2 leaves green leaf lettuce
- 2 slices ripe tomato

## Instructions

1. Heat oil in a skillet over high heat; cook tempeh bacon in the hot oil until lightly browned, 2 to 4 minutes per side.
2. Spread mayonnaise on 1 side of each bread slice.
3. Layer 1 leaf lettuce, 1 tomato, and tempeh bacon on the mayonnaise side of 1 bread slice.
4. Top tempeh with remaining tomato slice and lettuce leaf.
5. Place the remaining slice of bread, mayonnaise-side down, on top.
