## Ingredients

- 1 (1.5 ounce) bar milk chocolate candy bar (such as Hershey's®)
- 6 sugar cones
- ¾ cup mini marshmallows
- 6 sheets aluminum foil

## Instructions

1. Build a campfire and allow the fire to burn until it has accumulated a bed of coals. Rake the coals into a flat bed on one side of the fire.
2. Divide chocolate bar into 12 pieces.
3. Fill each cone with 2 tablespoons marshmallows and 2 chocolate pieces.
4. Wrap each filled cone in foil and place on the campfire, indirectly over the heat and cook until gooey and melted, about 5 minutes, rotating halfway through.
