## Ingredients

- cooking spray
- 1 cup mayonnaise
- 1 medium head cauliflower, cut into bite-sized florets
- 1 cup seasoned bread crumbs
- ¼ cup grated Parmesan cheese
- salt and ground black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly spray a baking sheet with cooking spray.
2. Place mayonnaise in a large zip-top bag. Add cauliflower; seal bag and shake to coat.
3. Combine bread crumbs and Parmesan cheese in another large zip-top bag. Add 1/2 of the coated cauliflower; seal bag and shake to coat. Spread in a single layer on the prepared baking sheet. Repeat with remaining cauliflower and bread crumb mixture.
4. Bake in the preheated until tender and golden brown, about 35 minutes.
