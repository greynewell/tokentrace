## Ingredients

- 1 cup heavy whipping cream
- 6 fluid ounces pineapple juice (such as Dole®)
- ⅓ cup white sugar
- 1 teaspoon fresh lemon juice

## Instructions

1. Whisk cream, pineapple juice, and sugar in a bowl until sugar is dissolved; stir in lemon juice. Refrigerate until well chilled, at least 6 hours.
2. Transfer pineapple mixture to an ice cream maker and process according to manufacturer's instructions.
