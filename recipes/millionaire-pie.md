## Ingredients

- 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
- 1 (14 ounce) can sweetened condensed milk
- 1 (12 ounce) container frozen whipped topping, thawed
- 1 (20 ounce) can crushed pineapple, drained
- 1 ½ cups chopped pecans
- 2 (9 inch)  prepared graham cracker crusts

## Instructions

1. Blend condensed milk and cream cheese together in a large bowl; gently fold in whipped topping. Stir in crushed pineapple and pecans.
2. Pour filling into prepared pie crusts; refrigerate for 3 to 4 hours.
