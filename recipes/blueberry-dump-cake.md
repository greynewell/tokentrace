## Ingredients

- 1 (18.25 ounce) package yellow cake mix
- 4 cups fresh blueberries
- ½ cup white sugar
- ½ cup butter, melted
- 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Mix blueberries, sugar, and cinnamon in a 9x13-inch pan; spread evenly in the pan. Cover blueberry mixture with dry cake mix. Pour melted butter over cake mix; do not stir.
3. Bake until the top is light brown, about 30 minutes. Serve warm or cold.
