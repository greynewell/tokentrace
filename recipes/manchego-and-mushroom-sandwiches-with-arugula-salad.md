## Ingredients

- 2 cloves garlic, minced, divided
- 1 (8 ounce) package cremini mushrooms, wiped clean, trimmed, and sliced
- 2  shallots, halved and thinly sliced
- 3 ½ tablespoons olive oil, or more as needed, divided
- ¼ teaspoon salt, or more to taste, divided
- ground black pepper to taste
- 4 slices sourdough bread
- 1 ½ tablespoons fig jam
- 2 ounces mozzarella cheese, shredded
- 2 ounces Manchego cheese, shredded
- 1 tablespoon sherry vinegar
- 3  sweet bell peppers, seeded and finely chopped
- 1 cup arugula
- ½ cup baby spinach leaves

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C).
2. Place 1/4 teaspoon garlic in a large bowl.
3. Toss remaining garlic, mushrooms, shallots, 1 tablespoon olive oil, 1/4 teaspoon salt, and pepper together on a rimmed baking sheet, arranging in a single layer.
4. Bake in the preheated oven until lightly browned and tender, 12 to 15 minutes.
5. Meanwhile, arrange bread slices in a single layer on a separate baking sheet.
6. Drizzle with 1 tablespoon olive oil; flip.
7. Spread fig jam over 2 slices.
8. Sprinkle Manchego cheese and mozzarella cheese over remaining 2 slices.
9. Transfer bread to the preheated oven and toast until golden and cheese is melted, 6 to 7 minutes.
10. Remove from the oven and set aside.
11. While bread toasts, add sherry vinegar to bowl of reserved garlic.
12. Add 1/2 of the bell peppers, remaining olive oil, remaining salt, and pepper; whisk to combine.
13. Add arugula and spinach, tossing to coat.
14. Top cheesy sides of bread with remaining bell peppers, mushrooms, shallots, and a small handful of the salad mixture.
15. Top these with remaining bread slices, jam-side down.
16. Halve paninis; serve with remaining salad mixture.
