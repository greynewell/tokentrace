## Ingredients

- 1 cup light mayonnaise
- ⅓ cup white sugar
- 1 teaspoon red wine vinegar
- ½ pound cooked bacon, crumbled
- 4 cups chopped broccoli florets
- 1 cup chopped celery
- ¾ cup grapes, halved
- ⅔ cup slivered almonds
- ½ cup raisins

## Instructions

1. Combine mayonnaise, sugar, and red wine vinegar in a bowl to make dressing.
2. Combine bacon, broccoli, celery, grapes, almonds, and raisins in a large bowl; stir in dressing and serve.
