## Ingredients

- 1 cup mayonnaise
- ½ cup sour cream
- 1 teaspoon dry Italian-style salad dressing mix
- ⅛ teaspoon curry powder
- 1 tablespoon dried minced onion flakes
- 1 tablespoon dried parsley
- 1 ½ teaspoons lemon juice
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- ½ teaspoon Worcestershire sauce

## Instructions

1. In a medium bowl, mix together mayonnaise, sour cream, Italian-style salad dressing mix, curry powder, dried minced onion flakes, dried parsley, lemon juice, salt and Worcestershire sauce.
2. Chill in the refrigerator until serving.
