## Ingredients

- 1  small cantaloupe, flesh removed and finely diced
- ½  red chile pepper, seeded and chopped
- 10  fresh basil leaves, cut into thin strips
- 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
- 2 tablespoons fresh lime juice
- 1 pinch salt
- 1 pinch white sugar
- 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
- 2 (5 ounce) tuna steaks
- salt and ground black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Combine the cantaloupe, chile pepper, basil, 2 tablespoons olive oil, lime juice, salt, and sugar in a bowl.
2. Heat 2 tablespoons olive oil in a skillet.
3. Season tuna steaks with salt and pepper.
4. Cook tuna in oil for 3 minutes per side.
5. Spoon cantaloupe mixture over each steak to serve.
