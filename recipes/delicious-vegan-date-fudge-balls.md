## Ingredients

- 15  dates, pitted
- 2 tablespoons peanut butter
- 2 tablespoons chopped almonds, or to taste  (Optional)
- 2 tablespoons shredded unsweetened coconut, or to taste  (Optional)
- 1 tablespoon unsweetened cocoa powder
- 1 pinch salt  (Optional)

## Instructions

1. Mash dates together in a bowl until a paste-like consistency.
2. Add peanut butter; mix well.
3. Stir almonds, coconut, cocoa powder, and salt into date mixture.
4. Roll date mixture into balls.
