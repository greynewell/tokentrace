## Ingredients

- 1 (28 ounce) can diced tomatoes with juice
- 1 small onion, diced
- 1 (15 ounce) can white beans, drained
- 1 (15 ounce) can chili beans, with liquid
- 1 (1.25 ounce) package reduced sodium taco seasoning mix
- 1 (1 ounce) package ranch dressing mix
- 1 (12 ounce) package vegetarian burger crumbles
- 1 (8 ounce) package shredded Cheddar cheese  (Optional)

## Instructions

1. Mix the tomatoes, onion, white beans, chili beans,   mix, and ranch dressing mix in a large pot over medium heat.
2. Bring to a boil.
3. Reduce heat to low, mix in the burger crumbles, and continue cooking until heated through.
4. Top with cheese to serve.
