## Ingredients

- 1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
- 1 cup cream-style corn
- 2 teaspoons olive oil
- 1 cup self-rising flour, or as needed
- 3  cocktail wieners
- 1 teaspoon blanched slivered almonds

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
2. Grease a baking sheet.
3. Mix together the Cheddar cheese, corn, olive oil, and self-rising flour to make a firm dough.
4. Roll the dough out into a log shape, and cut into 16 equal pieces.
5. Roll each piece into a ball.
6. Cut the cocktail wieners into 6 slices each (there will be 2 extra slices).
7. Pick out 8 almond slivers.
8. To make the grubs, place 4 dough balls onto the baking sheet, and press them tightly together so they make a lumpy grub with 4 segments.
9. Place a slice of cocktail wiener on top of each segment for markings.
10. Stick 2 little almond slivers into an end ball to make eyes.
11. Repeat for the other grubs.
12. Bake in the preheated oven until the grubs are golden brown, about 15 minutes.
