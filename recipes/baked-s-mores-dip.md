## Ingredients

- 6 (1.5 ounce) bars milk chocolate candy bars (such as Hershey's®)
- 2 tablespoons milk
- 12  marshmallows
- 2 (4.8 ounce) packages graham crackers

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C).
2. Break chocolate bars into a saucepan over low heat.
3. Add milk.
4. Heat, stirring constantly, until chocolate is melted, about 5 minutes.
5. Pour into 8-1/2x6-1/2-inch casserole dish.
6. Arrange marshmallows over melted chocolate.
7. Bake dip in the preheated oven until marshmallows are golden brown, 4 to 7 minutes.
8. Serve dip with graham crackers.
