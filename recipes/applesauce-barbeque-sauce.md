## Ingredients

- 1 cup applesauce
- ½ cup ketchup
- 2 cups unpacked brown sugar
- 6 tablespoons lemon juice
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ½ teaspoon ground black pepper
- ½ teaspoon paprika
- ½ teaspoon garlic powder
- ½ teaspoon ground cinnamon

## Instructions

1. In a saucepan over medium heat, mix applesauce, ketchup, brown sugar, lemon juice, salt, pepper, paprika, garlic powder, and cinnamon. Bring mixture to a boil.
2. Remove from heat, and cool completely, about 20 minutes. Use to baste the meat of your choice.
