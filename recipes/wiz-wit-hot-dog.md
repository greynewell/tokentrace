## Ingredients

- 1  Dietz & Watson Natural Casing NY Style Beef Frank
- 1  pretzel roll
- 2 tablespoons caramelized onions
- 2 tablespoons processed cheese spread (such as Kraft® Cheez Whiz®)

## Instructions

1. Preheat a grill for medium-high heat. Cook beef frank on preheated grill, turning occasionally, until golden brown, 3 to 4 minutes. Place grilled Beef Frank in pretzel roll.
2. Finish with caramelized onions and Cheez Whiz®.
