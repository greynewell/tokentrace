## Ingredients

- 2 cups canned kidney beans, drained
- 1 small onion, minced
- ½ cup mayonnaise
- ½ cup sweet pickle relish
- 1 pinch dry mustard
- 1 dash Worcestershire sauce
- ½ teaspoon white horseradish
- ⅛ teaspoon garlic powder
- ⅛ teaspoon salt
- ⅛ teaspoon ground black pepper

## Instructions

1. Rinse kidney beans; set aside to drain.
2. Stir together onion, mayonnaise, relish, dry mustard, Worcestershire sauce, horseradish, garlic powder, salt, and pepper in a medium bowl.
3. Gently mix in kidney beans until coated with dressing. Refrigerate until serving.
