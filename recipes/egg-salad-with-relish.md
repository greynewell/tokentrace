## Ingredients

- 8  hard-boiled eggs, finely chopped
- ¼  sweet onion (such as Vidalia®), finely chopped
- 1 cup mayonnaise
- 2 tablespoons dill pickle relish
- 1 tablespoon prepared yellow mustard
- 1 dash hot sauce, or more to taste
- salt and ground black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Combine eggs and onion in a bowl. Add mayonnaise, relish, mustard, and hot sauce. Mix until ingredients are well combined. Season with salt and pepper.
2. Chill in the refrigerator for 2 hours before serving.
