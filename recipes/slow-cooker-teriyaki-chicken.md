## Ingredients

- 1 cup chicken broth
- ½ cup teriyaki sauce
- 4 tablespoons brown sugar
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 pound chicken, diced

## Instructions

1. Combine chicken broth, teriyaki sauce, brown sugar, and garlic in a large bowl. Add chicken and toss with the sauce until well mixed. Pour into the slow cooker.
2. Cook on Low until chicken is done, 4 to 6 hours.
