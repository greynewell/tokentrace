## Ingredients

- 3 tablespoons olive oil
- 1 tablespoon yellow curry powder
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice
- 1 teaspoon salt
- ½ teaspoon ground black pepper
- 1 pound cauliflower florets, cut into 1/2-inch slices

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Line a baking sheet with aluminum foil.
2. Combine olive oil, curry powder, lemon juice, salt, and ground black pepper in a bowl. Add cauliflower and toss until well coated. Place in a single layer in the prepared baking sheet.
3. Bake in the preheated oven until golden and crispy, about 25 minutes.
