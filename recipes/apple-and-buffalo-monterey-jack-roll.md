## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
- 1  onion roll, split
- ¼  apple, thinly sliced
- 1 slice Buffalo sauce-flavored Monterey jack cheese

## Instructions

1. Spread mustard onto cut-sides of onion roll halves.
2. Top one half with apple slices and cheese; sandwich with remaining half.
