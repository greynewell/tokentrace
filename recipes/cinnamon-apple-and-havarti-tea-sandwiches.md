## Ingredients

- 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
- 8  slices cinnamon raisin bread
- 4 slices Havarti cheese
- 1 large Granny Smith apple, cored and thinly sliced

## Instructions

1. Spread a thin layer of cream cheese on 4 slices of bread.
2. Top the cream cheese with a slice of Havarti cheese, then a thin layer of apple slices.
3. Place the remaining slices of bread on top to form 4 sandwiches.
4. Using 2 diagonal cuts, divide each sandwich into 4 triangles.
