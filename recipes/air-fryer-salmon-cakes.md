## Ingredients

- 1 large egg
- 2 tablespoons mayonnaise
- 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley
- 1 tablespoon chopped fresh chervil
- 1 tablespoon chopped fresh dill
- 1 tablespoon chopped fresh chives
- ½ teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
- ½ teaspoon Dijon mustard
- ½ teaspoon seafood seasoning (such as Old Bay®)
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ¼ cup panko bread crumbs
- 2 (5 ounce) packages of fresh salmon

## Instructions

1. Preheat an air fryer for 10 minutes to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
2. Whisk egg, mayonnaise, parsley, chervil, dill, chives, Worcestershire sauce, mustard, seafood seasoning, and salt together in a bowl. Mix in panko bread crumbs until combined. Gently fold in salmon.
3. Shape mixture into 4 cakes. Line the air fryer basket with perforated parchment.
4. Air-fry salmon cakes for 8 minutes, flipping at the halfway point. Serve immediately.
