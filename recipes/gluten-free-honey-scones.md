## Ingredients

- 2 cups gluten-free all-purpose baking flour
- 2 tablespoons coconut flour
- 1 ½ teaspoons baking powder
- 1 teaspoon baking soda
- ½ teaspoon sea salt
- ½ teaspoon xanthan gum
- ½ cup butter, softened
- ½ cup nonfat plain yogurt
- ¼ cup honey
- 1  egg, beaten

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
2. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
3. Whisk gluten-free flour, coconut flour, baking powder, baking soda, sea salt, and xanthan gum together in a bowl.
4. Stir butter, yogurt, honey, and egg together in a separate large bowl.
5. Add flour mixture to butter mixture and stir until dough is just combined.
6. Turn dough out onto prepared baking sheet and shape into a large round about 3/4-inch thick.
7. Use a knife to score dough into 8 triangles without cutting all the way through the dough.
8. Bake in the preheated oven until golden brown, about 17 minutes.
9. Cut on the scored lines through the dough to separate into individual scones.
