## Ingredients

- ¼ cup olive oil, or more to taste
- 2 pounds mussels, cleaned and debearded
- ¾ cup Pinot Grigio wine
- 2 teaspoons minced garlic
- 1 teaspoon dried oregano
- 1 teaspoon dried basil
- 5 dashes hot sauce (such as Texas Pete®)
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- ½ cup prepared Alfredo sauce
- ¼ cup unsalted butter, cut into chunks
- 2 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese
- 2 tablespoons grated Romano cheese, or more to taste
- 8 wedges lemon
- 8 sprigs fresh parsley

## Instructions

1. Heat oil over medium-high heat in a large skillet.
2. Add mussels and cook for 4 minutes.
3. Stir in wine, garlic, oregano, basil, hot sauce, salt, and pepper; cover and cook until mussels have opened up, 3 to 5 minutes.
4. Discard any mussels that have not opened.
5. Reduce heat to medium, add   and butter, and cook for 1 minute.
6. Stir in Parmesan cheese and simmer for 2 minutes.
7. Serve with grated Romano cheese, lemon wedges, and parsley.
