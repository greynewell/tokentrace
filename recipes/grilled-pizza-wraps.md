## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons margarine, softened
- 8 (10 inch) flour tortillas
- 1 (16 ounce) package shredded Cheddar-Monterey Jack cheese blend
- ½ cup pizza sauce
- 4 ounces sliced pepperoni

## Instructions

1. Heat a large skillet over medium-low heat.
2. Spread margarine over one side of a tortilla, and place it margarine-side down in the skillet.
3. Spoon a tablespoon of pizza sauce onto half of the tortilla.
4. Sprinkle 1/2 cup of shredded cheese over the sauce, and top with a few slices of pepperoni.
5. Fold the clean half of the tortilla over the filling, and cook until golden on each side.
6. Repeat with remaining tortillas.
