## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 1 small onion, finely chopped
- 5 cloves garlic, crushed
- 6 large tomatoes, chopped, or more to taste
- 1 cup dry white wine
- 1 (6.5 ounce) can clam juice
- 2 pounds mussels, cleaned and debearded
- 1 cup chopped fresh parsley
- ¼ cup butter

## Instructions

1. Heat oil in a large pot over medium heat.
2. Add onion; cook and stir until translucent, about 3 minutes.
3. Add garlic; cook until fragrant, about 1 minute.
4. Add tomatoes with all their juices.
5. Cook and stir until softened, 3 to 5 minutes.
6. Pour wine and clam juice into the pot.
7. Bring to a gentle boil; add mussels.
8. Simmer, covered, until mussels have opened, 4 to 6 minutes.
9. Add parsley.
10. Stir in butter until melted, about 1 minute.
