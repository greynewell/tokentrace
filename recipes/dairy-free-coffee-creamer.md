## Ingredients

- 1 cup unsweetened almond milk
- 2 tablespoons maple syrup
- ½ teaspoon cornstarch
- 2 teaspoons vanilla extract

## Instructions

1. Combine almond milk, maple syrup, and cornstarch in a small saucepan. Whisk until all the cornstarch is dissolved. Continue to stir frequently over medium heat until the mixture comes to a slow boil. Remove from heat and whisk in vanilla.
2. Store in an airtight jar in the refrigerator for up to 5 days. Shake before serving.
