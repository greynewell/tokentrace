## Ingredients

- 2 cups all-purpose flour
- ⅔ cup white sugar
- 1 tablespoon baking powder
- ½ teaspoon baking soda
- ¾ teaspoon ground cinnamon
- 1  egg, beaten
- 1 cup applesauce
- 3 tablespoons butter, melted
- 1.3 cups chopped walnuts
- 1 cup raisins

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly grease a 9x5-inch loaf pan.
2. Stir together flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and cinnamon in a large bowl. Beat together egg, applesauce, and butter in a separate bowl. Stir applesauce mixture into flour mixture just until combined. Fold in walnuts and raisins; pour batter into prepared loaf pan.
3. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center of the loaf comes out clean, about 45 minutes. Allow to cool before slicing.
