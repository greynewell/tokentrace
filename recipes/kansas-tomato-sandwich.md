## Ingredients

- 2 slices white bread
- 1 medium tomato, thickly sliced
- 1 slice white American cheese
- 2 teaspoons butter or margarine
- 1  lettuce leaf  (Optional)
- salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Toast the bread slices to your desired darkness.
2. When done, spread lightly with butter or margarine.
3. Place a slice of tomato on the buttered side of one slice, and top with a slice of cheese.
4. Season to taste with salt and pepper and place a slice of lettuce on if desired.
5. Top with the remaining slice of bread.
6. Enjoy!
