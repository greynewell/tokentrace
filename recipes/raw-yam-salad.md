## Ingredients

- 2 ½ cups peeled and shredded yam
- 1 cup dried whole cranberries
- ⅔ cup chopped pecans
- ¼ cup grapeseed oil
- 3 tablespoons rice vinegar
- 3 tablespoons freshly squeezed lemon juice
- 1 ½ teaspoons dried lavender, crushed
- 1 ½ teaspoons fennel seed, crushed
- 2 pinches freshly ground black pepper

## Instructions

1. Mix yam, cranberries, and pecans together in a large bowl.
2. Combine grapeseed oil, vinegar, lemon juice, lavender, fennel, and black pepper in a jar with a lid.
3. Cover and shake until blended.
4. Pour dressing over yam mixture and toss to coat.
5. Chill in refrigerator at least 30 minutes.
