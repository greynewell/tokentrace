## Ingredients

- 2 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened
- 2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese
- 1 tablespoon finely chopped pimento
- 1 tablespoon finely chopped green pepper
- 1 tablespoon finely chopped onion
- 2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice
- 1 pinch salt
- ½ cup finely chopped pecans

## Instructions

1. Combine the cream cheese and Cheddar cheese in a bowl; use your hands to mix together.
2. Add the pimento, green pepper, onion, Worcestershire sauce, lemon juice, and salt; mix, and shape into a ball.
3. Spread the pecans onto a plate.
4. Roll the cheese ball in the pecans until evenly coated.
5. Refrigerate overnight before serving.
