## Ingredients

- 5 pounds potatoes, peeled, cut into 2 inch chunks
- 3  hard-cooked eggs, peeled and finely diced
- ½ cup finely chopped dill pickle
- 2 cups mayonnaise
- salt to taste

## Instructions

1. Boil potatoes until soft, about 25 to 30 minutes.
2. Remove potatoes to a large bowl.
3. While the potatoes are still warm but not steaming, stir in the hard-cooked eggs with a fork.
4. Mix in the dill pickle, then stir in the mayonnaise.
5. Season to taste with salt.
6. Continue to stir until potatoes are smashed and not many big chunks remain.
