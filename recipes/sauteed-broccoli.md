## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons Country Crock® Spread
- 1 clove garlic, chopped
- 4 cups broccoli florets
- ½ cup vegetable broth
- Salt and ground black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Heat Country Crock® Spread in large nonstick skillet over medium heat. Add garlic and cook 30 seconds. Add broccoli and cook 5 minutes or until tender crisp.
2. Add broth and simmer 5 minutes or until broccoli is tender. Season to taste with salt and pepper.
