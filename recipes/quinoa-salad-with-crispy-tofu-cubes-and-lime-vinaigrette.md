## Ingredients

- 1 large sweet potato, cut into chunks
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- 2 cups chicken broth
- 1 cup quinoa, rinsed
- 1 (12 ounce) package extra-firm tofu, cut into 1-inch cubes
- 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
- 2 tablespoons cornstarch
- 2 teaspoons garlic powder
- ¼ cup vegetable oil
- 3 tablespoons olive oil
- 2 tablespoons lime juice
- 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
- ½ teaspoon garlic powder
- ¼ teaspoon ground cumin
- 1  avocado, diced
- 2 cups mixed greens with spinach
- 1 cup bite-sized broccoli florets
- ½ cup diced red bell pepper
- ¼ cup capers
- 7 leaves fresh basil, cut into strips, or more to taste

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C).
2. Toss sweet potato with 2 tablespoons olive oil, salt, and pepper on a baking sheet; spread out in an even layer.
3. Roast sweet potato in the preheated oven until soft, about 20 minutes.
4. Bring chicken broth to a boil in a saucepan.
5. Reduce heat to low; stir in quinoa.
6. Cover and simmer until quinoa is tender, about 30 minutes.
7. Place in the refrigerator to cool.
8. Bring a pot of generously salted water to a boil.
9. Remove from heat; add tofu cubes.
10. Let soak for 15 minutes.
11. Drain and pat dry with a clean dish towel.
12. Mix flour, cornstarch, garlic powder, salt, and pepper together in a shallow bowl.
13. Toss tofu cubes in flour mixture until evenly coated.
14. Heat 1/4 cup vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
15. Cook tofu cubes in 2 batches until browned, about 20 seconds per side.
16. Place 3 tablespoons olive oil, lime juice, Dijon mustard, garlic powder, cumin, salt, and pepper in a jar.
17. Seal and shake well to make dressing.
18. Pour dressing into a large bowl.
19. Add sweet potato, tofu cubes, avocado, mixed greens, broccoli, red bell pepper, capers, and basil; toss to combine.
20. Divide quinoa among serving plates; top with sweet potato salad mixture.
