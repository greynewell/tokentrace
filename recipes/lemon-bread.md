## Ingredients

- 6 tablespoons shortening
- 1 cup white sugar
- 2  eggs
- ½ cup milk
- 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
- ½ teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- 1 teaspoon lemon zest
- ½ cup chopped walnuts
- ½ cup white sugar
- 1  lemon, juiced

## Instructions

1. Whisk together flour, salt, and baking powder.
2. In a large bowl, cream shortening, 1 cup sugar, and eggs together until light and fluffy.
3. Add milk alternately with flour mixture in two parts; mix well.
4. Stir in grated lemon rind and nuts.
5. Pour batter into greased and floured 9 x 5 inch loaf pan.
6. Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 60 minutes.
7. Allow bread to cool in the pan for 5 minutes.
8. Mix together 1/2 cup sugar and the juice of one lemon to make a glaze.
9. Remove bread from pan.
10. Pour glaze over warm bread.
