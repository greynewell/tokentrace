## Ingredients

- ½ (12 ounce) package bacon, or to taste
- 3 cups broccoli florets, diced
- 1 ½ cups corn kernels
- 1 packet chicken bouillon granules
- 2 tablespoons dried basil
- 2 tablespoons dried oregano
- 1 ½ teaspoons ground thyme
- 2 cups mixed salad greens
- 6 tablespoons butter, divided
- 1 pinch red pepper flakes
- 1 (16 ounce) package linguine pasta
- 8 ounces uncooked medium shrimp, peeled and deveined, or to taste
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- 4 large cloves fresh garlic, chopped
- ¾ cup cream cheese, softened
- 1 cup half-and-half

## Instructions

1. Place bacon in a large skillet and cook over medium-high heat, turning occasionally, until evenly browned, about 7 minutes.
2. Add broccoli and corn; cook until tender, about 5 minutes.
3. Stir chicken bouillon, basil, oregano, and thyme into the skillet.
4. Reduce heat to medium and cook for 2 minutes.
5. Add mixed greens, 2 tablespoons butter, and red pepper flakes.
6. Cook for 5 minutes more.
7. Turn off heat and set aside.
8. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
9. Cook linguine at a boil until tender yet firm to the bite, about 11 minutes.
10. Melt 2 tablespoons butter in another large pan over medium-high heat.
11. Season shrimp with salt and pepper; place into the hot pan.
12. Cook until starting to turn pink, about 30 seconds.
13. Add garlic.
14. Cook and stir until shrimp is opaque, 3 to 5 minutes more.
15. Drain linguine, without rinsing.
16. Return pasta to the pot and leave the heat on medium-low.
17. Add cream cheese and stir until melted.
18. Add half-and-half and the remaining 2 tablespoons butter.
19. Let simmer while stirring for 1 minute.
20. Toss in the bacon mixture and shrimp until combined.
21. Serve immediately.
