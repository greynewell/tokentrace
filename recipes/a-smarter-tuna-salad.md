## Ingredients

- 1 (6 ounce) can white meat tuna, packed in water
- 1 large stalk celery, minced
- 4 medium (3/4" to 1" dia)s radishes, minced
- 1 tablespoon minced red onion
- ½  Granny Smith apple, minced
- 2 tablespoons minced flat-leaf parsley, or more to taste
- 2 teaspoons fresh lime juice
- 3 tablespoons reduced-fat mayonnaise, or more to taste
- ¼ cup minced walnuts, lightly toasted

## Instructions

1. Place the tuna in a bowl and flake with a fork.
2. Add remaining ingredients and mix well.
