## Ingredients

- 1 (9.5 ounce) package Pepperidge Farm® Mini Puff Pastry Shells
- ⅓ cup chopped smoked salmon
- 4 ounces cream cheese, softened
- 2 teaspoons horseradish
- 3 tablespoons minced chives
- 1 dash hot pepper sauce
- 1  Grated zest of 1 lemon

## Instructions

1. Bake, cool and remove the "tops" of the pastry shells according to the package directions.
2. Stir the salmon, cream cheese, horseradish, 2 tablespoons chives, hot pepper sauce and lemon zest in a medium bowl.
3. Spoon about 1 teaspoon salmon mixture into each pastry shell. Sprinkle with the remaining chives.
