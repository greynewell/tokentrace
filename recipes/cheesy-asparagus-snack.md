## Ingredients

- 6  asparagus spears, ends trimmed, cut into 1-inch pieces
- ½ cup mayonnaise
- 2 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese
- ¼ teaspoon dry mustard
- 2  egg whites
- 1  prebaked pizza crust (such as Boboli®)

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C).
2. Bring water to a boil in a saucepan.
3. Cook asparagus until bright green and still crisp, 1 to 2 minutes.
4. Drain asparagus and rinse under cold water to stop cooking.
5. Pat dry with a towel.
6. Stir mayonnaise, Parmesan cheese, and dry mustard together in a bowl.
7. Beat egg whites in a glass, metal, or ceramic bowl until stiff peaks form, 2 to 4 minutes; fold into mayonnaise mixture.
8. Place pre-baked pizza crust onto a baking sheet, arrange asparagus on crust, and spoon mayonnaise mixture on top.
9. Bake in the preheated oven until lightly brown, 8 to 10 minutes.
