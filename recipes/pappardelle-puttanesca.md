## Ingredients

- ¼ cup extra-virgin olive oil, or as needed
- 2 cups sliced mushrooms
- 2 tablespoons minced garlic, or to taste
- 1 cup dry white wine
- 1 (5 ounce) jar anchovy-stuffed green olives, drained and halved
- ¼ cup capers, drained
- 2 tablespoons caper juice
- 2 (14 ounce) cans crushed tomatoes
- 1 pinch red pepper flakes, or to taste
- 1 pound dried pappardelle pasta

## Instructions

1. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
2. Stir in mushrooms and garlic.
3. Cook until the mushrooms have begun to brown, about 4 minutes.
4. Increase heat to high, and pour in wine.
5. Bring to a boil, then stir in olives, capers, caper juice, crushed tomatoes, and pepper flakes.
6. Return to a simmer, then reduce heat to medium-low, and cook for 20 minutes.
7. Meanwhile, bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
8. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain and toss with sauce to serve.
