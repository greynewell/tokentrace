## Ingredients

- 2 large baking potatoes, peeled and cut into 1 inch pieces
- 1 large onion, coarsely chopped
- 1 cube chicken bouillon
- 1 tablespoon margarine
- 3  eggs, beaten

## Instructions

1. Place the potatoes, onion, margarine and bouillon cube into a soup pot, and fill with enough water to cover the potatoes by 1 to 2 inches.
2. Bring to a boil, and cook for 30 minutes, until potatoes are falling apart.
3. You can smash them up a little if you prefer the texture.
4. Drizzle the beaten eggs into the pot, while stirring constantly.
5. Continue to cook and stir for about 2 minutes, until the eggs are completely cooked in long strands.
