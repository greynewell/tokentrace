## Ingredients

- 20  tortilla chips
- ¼ pound smoked beef sausage
- ½ cup Cheddar cheese
- ¼ cup barbeque sauce

## Instructions

1. Arrange the tortilla chips on a microwave-safe platter.
2. Shred the sausage with a cheese grater; scatter evenly over the chips; top with Cheddar cheese.
3. Drizzle the barbeque sauce over the nachos.
4. Heat in the microwave until the cheese melts, 15 to 30 seconds.
