## Ingredients

- 2 cups uncooked long-grain rice
- ½ tablespoon butter
- 4 tablespoons chopped onion
- 2 cups diced celery
- 3 cups cooked, chopped chicken breast meat
- 1 cup mayonnaise
- 1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of chicken soup
- ½ cup blanched slivered almonds
- 1 cup crushed cornflake crumbs
- 2 tablespoons butter

## Instructions

1. In a saucepan bring water to a boil.
2. Add rice and stir.
3. Reduce heat, cover and simmer for 20 minutes.
4. Set aside.
5. Melt 1/2 tablespoon butter in a medium skillet over medium heat; saute onion and celery until soft.
6. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
7. Combine the chicken, rice, onion and celery in a 9x13 inch baking dish.
8. Stir in mayonnaise and soup, then almonds.
9. Top with crushed cornflakes and 2 tablespoons butter.
10. Bake in preheated oven for 45 minutes, until golden brown.
