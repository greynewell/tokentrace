## Ingredients

- 6 cups apple juice
- 3 cups quinoa
- 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
- 1 pint blueberries
- 1 cup almond milk
- 2 tablespoons water
- 1 tablespoon cornstarch
- 1  lemon, juiced
- 1 tablespoon maple syrup

## Instructions

1. Combine apple juice, quinoa, and vanilla extract in a large pot; bring to a boil.
2. Reduce heat to medium-low, cover, and simmer until quinoa is tender and liquid is absorbed, about 15 minutes.
3. Gently fold blueberries into hot quinoa.
4. Pour blueberry mixture into a 9x13-inch baking dish.
5. Cool until quinoa is set.
6. Whisk almond milk, water, and cornstarch together in a small saucepan over high heat.
7. Add lemon juice and maple syrup; cook, stirring constantly, until glaze is thick, 2 to 3 minutes.
8. Pour hot glaze over quinoa to cover it completely.
9. Cool to room temperature.
10. Cover baking dish with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 2 hours.
