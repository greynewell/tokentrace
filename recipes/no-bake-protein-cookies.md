## Ingredients

- 2 cups white sugar
- ½ cup light butter
- ½ cup skim milk
- 4 teaspoons unsweetened cocoa powder
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 3 cups quick-cooking oats
- ½ cup creamy peanut butter
- 2 scoops chocolate-flavored whey protein powder

## Instructions

1. Bring sugar, butter, milk, cocoa, and vanilla extract to a full boil in a medium saucepan. Cook for 1 1/2 minutes. Remove from heat. Add oats, peanut butter, and protein powder.
2. Drop batter by tablespoonfuls onto wax paper and let cool, 20 to 30 minutes.
