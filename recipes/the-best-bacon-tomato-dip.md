## Ingredients

- 1 cup sour cream
- ½ cup mayonnaise
- 4 ounces cream cheese, room temperature
- ½ cup shredded Cheddar cheese
- ¼ cup chopped onions
- 1  tomato, seeded and diced
- 6 slices bacon - cooked, drained, and chopped

## Instructions

1. Stir together sour cream, mayonnaise, cream cheese, Cheddar cheese, chopped onion, tomato and bacon in a bowl until well mixed.
2. Chill for two hours before serving.
