## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 1 tablespoon soy sauce
- 1 tablespoon vinegar
- 1 tablespoon honey
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- 2  tilapia fillets

## Instructions

1. Mix olive oil, soy sauce, vinegar, honey, and garlic together in a small bowl. Place each tilapia fillet in a separate resealable zip-top bag.
2. Pour 1/2 of the marinade into each bag. Seal bags and place in the refrigerator for 1 hour.
3. Set an oven rack about 6 inches from the heat source and preheat the oven's broiler.
4. Remove fillets from the marinade, shake off excess, and place on a broiling pan with a drip pan or baking sheet underneath. Discard the remaining marinade.
5. Broil in the preheated oven for 3 to 4 minutes. Flip fillets and cook until fish flakes easily with a fork, 2 to 3 minutes more.
