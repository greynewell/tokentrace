## Ingredients

- 1 (8 ounce) package processed cheese product (such as Velveeta®), cubed
- 1 cup sour cream
- 1 (4.5 ounce) can chopped green chiles, undrained
- 1 (1.12 ounce) package McCormick® Cheesy
- Mix

## Instructions

1. Mix all ingredients in large microwaveable bowl.
2. Microwave on HIGH 2 minutes.
3. Stir.
4. Microwave 1 minute longer or until heated through.
5. Stir until smooth.
6. Serve hot with tortilla chips.
