## Ingredients

- 1 (16.3 ounce) can Pillsbury® Grands!® refrigerated biscuits (any variety)
- 8 slices American cheese
- 8 ounces thinly sliced deli ham
- 1  egg
- 1 teaspoon water

## Instructions

1. Heat oven to 375 degrees F.
2. Press 4 biscuits into 5-inch rounds on ungreased large cookie sheet.
3. Top each with 2 slices cheese and 2 ounces ham.
4. Press remaining 4 biscuits into 6-inch rounds; place over ham.
5. Press edges with fork to seal.
6. Beat egg and water with fork; brush over biscuits.
7. Bake 13 to 18 minutes or until golden brown.
8. Cut each in half.
9. Serve warm.
