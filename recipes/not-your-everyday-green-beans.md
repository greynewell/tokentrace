## Ingredients

- ½ pound fresh green beans, trimmed
- 2 tablespoons butter
- ½ pound crimini mushrooms, chopped
- salt and pepper to taste
- ¼ cup seasoned almond slices

## Instructions

1. Melt the butter in a large skillet over medium heat.
2. Cook and stir the green beans in the butter for 5 minutes.
3. Stir in the mushrooms.
4. Season with salt and pepper; continue to cook until the beans are tender, about 5 minutes.
5. Stir in the almonds just before serving.
