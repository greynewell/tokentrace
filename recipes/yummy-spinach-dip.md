## Ingredients

- 1 (10 ounce) package frozen chopped spinach, thawed and drained
- 1 (16 ounce) container sour cream
- ¾ cup mayonnaise
- 3  green onions, chopped
- 1 (1 ounce) package dry vegetable dip mix
- 1 (8 ounce) can water chestnuts, drained and chopped
- 2 teaspoons prepared Dijon-style mustard

## Instructions

1. In a medium bowl, mix together chopped spinach, sour cream, mayonnaise, green onions, dry vegetable dip mix, water chestnuts and prepared Dijon-style mustard.
2. Chill in the refrigerator 8 hours, or overnight, before serving.
