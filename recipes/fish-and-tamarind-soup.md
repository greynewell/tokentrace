## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
- 8  shallots, chopped
- 6 cloves garlic, crushed
- ½ teaspoon crushed red pepper
- ½ teaspoon ground turmeric
- 6 cups water
- 6 stalks lemon grass, ground
- ¼ cup tamarind juice
- salt to taste
- 2 pounds red snapper fillets, cut into 1 inch pieces

## Instructions

1. Heat oil in a large saucepan over medium heat.
2. Mix in shallots, garlic, crushed red pepper, and turmeric.
3. Cook and stir until shallots are tender, about 5 minutes.
4. Pour water into skillet, and mix in lemon grass and tamarind juice.
5. Season with salt.
6. Bring the mixture to a boil.
7. Stir snapper into boiling mixture.
8. Cook 10 minutes, or until fish pieces are easily flaked with a fork.
