## Ingredients

- 1  serving cooking spray
- 1 (3 pound) boneless turkey breast
- 1 (10.75 ounce) can  cream of mushroom soup
- 1 (10.75 ounce) can  Cheddar cheese soup

## Instructions

1. Spray inside of a slow cooker with cooking spray and place turkey breast into cooker.
2. Add cream of mushroom and Cheddar cheese soups, cover cooker, and cook on Low for 8 hours.
3. If preferred, set cooker on High and cook for 4 hours.
