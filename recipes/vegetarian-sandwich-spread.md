## Ingredients

- 1 (19 ounce) can vegetarian hot dog links
- ¾ cup sweet pickle relish
- 1  onion, chopped
- ½ cup mayonnaise

## Instructions

1. In a large bowl mash hot dog links using a potato masher or fork.
2. Blend in relish, onion and mayonnaise.
