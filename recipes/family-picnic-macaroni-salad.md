## Ingredients

- 1 (7.25 ounce) package macaroni and cheese mix
- 1 (8 ounce) package elbow macaroni
- ¼ cup butter
- ¼ cup milk
- ¼ cup pasteurized process cheese spread (such as Cheez Whiz®)
- 1 cup thousand island salad dressing
- ⅔ cup prepared tartar sauce
- ¼ cup white vinegar

## Instructions

1. Remove cheese packet from packet of macaroni and cheese and set aside.
2. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
3. Cook both amounts of elbow macaroni in the boiling water, stirring occasionally until cooked through but firm to the bite, 8 minutes.
4. Drain.
5. Pour hot pasta into a large bowl.
6. Stir butter into macaroni until melted; mix in milk and the reserved cheese packet, stirring until thoroughly combined.
7. Mix the processed cheese spread into the hot macaroni and let melt; mix thoroughly.
8. Stir in thousand island dressing, tartar sauce, and vinegar.
9. Cover bowl and refrigerate until cold; salad holds up to 5 days in refrigerator.
