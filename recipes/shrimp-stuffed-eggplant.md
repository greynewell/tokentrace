## Ingredients

- 2 large eggplants, halved lengthwise
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- ½ small onion, grated
- ½  bell pepper, chopped
- ½ pound raw shrimp, peeled and deveined
- 2  tomatoes, chopped
- ¼ cup butter
- 1  egg, beaten
- ½ pound sharp Cheddar cheese, grated

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Scoop the pulp from each eggplant, reserving the shells.
3. Place pulp into a pot and cover with salted water; bring to a boil.
4. Reduce heat to medium-low and simmer until tender, about 10 minutes.
5. Drain and transfer eggplant to a large bowl.
6. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat; cook and stir onion and bell pepper until tender, 5 to 10 minutes.
7. Add shrimp; cook and stir until cooked through and pink, 3 to 5 minutes.
8. Remove skillet from heat.
9. Mash eggplant thoroughly; add shrimp mixture, tomatoes, butter, and egg and mix well.
10. Stuff eggplant shells with eggplant-shrimp mixture; season with salt and pepper.
11. Top each stuffed eggplant with Cheddar cheese.
12. Place stuffed eggplant in a baking dish.
13. Bake in the preheated oven until filling is cooked through and cheese is bubbling, 20 to 30 minutes.
