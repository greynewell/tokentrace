## Ingredients

- 8  whole graham crackers
- ½  chocolate graham cracker
- ¼ cup white sugar, or more to taste

## Instructions

1. Process whole graham crackers and chocolate graham cracker in a food processor to a sand-like consistency.
2. Stir sugar and graham cracker crumbs together in a bowl, adding sugar as needed to reach desired color.
