## Ingredients

- 8 ounces sliced smoked salmon
- 1 (8 ounce) container herb- and garlic-flavored cream cheese, softened
- 1 (8 ounce) package crackers

## Instructions

1. Roll out smoked salmon slices and spread with a layer of cream cheese.
2. Roll up smoked salmon and cut into thin slices.
3. Place one or two salmon slices on each cracker.
