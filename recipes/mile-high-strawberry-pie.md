## Ingredients

- 1 cup all-purpose flour
- ¾ cup chopped pecans
- ⅓ cup margarine, melted
- ⅓ cup brown sugar
- 2 cups sliced strawberries
- 1 cup white sugar
- 2  egg whites
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice
- 1 (12 ounce) container frozen whipped topping (such as Cool Whip®), thawed

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Combine flour, pecans, margarine, and brown sugar together in a bowl until crumbly; transfer to a baking sheet.
3. Bake in the preheated oven, stirring often, until crumbs are lightly browned, 10 to 15 minutes.
4. Transfer most of the crumbs to a 9x13-inch baking dish.
5. Beat strawberries, white sugar, egg whites, and lemon juice together in a bowl using an electric mixer on high speed until stiff peaks form, about 15 minutes.
6. Fold whipped topping into strawberry filling; pour over crumbs.
7. Sprinkle remaining crumbs over the strawberry filling.
8. Cover dish with plastic wrap and freeze until set, 8 hours to overnight.
9. Cut into squares.
