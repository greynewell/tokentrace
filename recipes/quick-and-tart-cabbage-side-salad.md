## Ingredients

- 2 cups coleslaw mix
- 1  tomato, diced small
- ½  cucumber, diced small
- 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
- 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
- ½ teaspoon agave nectar
- salt and ground black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Toss coleslaw mix, tomato, and cucumber together in a mixing bowl.
2. Whisk vinegar, Dijon mustard, and agave nectar together in a bowl until emulsified; season with salt and pepper.
3. Drizzle dressing over the vegetable mixture.
4. Turn the salad with a fork until vegetables are evenly coated in dressing.
