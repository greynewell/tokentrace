## Ingredients

- 3 ⅔ cups all-purpose flour
- 2 teaspoons baking soda
- 4 ½ teaspoons cream of tartar
- ½ teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
- ¼ teaspoon ground allspice
- ¼ teaspoon ground cloves
- ¼ teaspoon ground nutmeg
- ¼ teaspoon ground ginger
- ½ cup butter
- 2 tablespoons molasses (treacle), or more to taste
- 1 cup milk

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C).
2. Lightly grease a baking sheet.
3. In a large bowl, sift together flour, baking soda, cream of tarter, salt, cinnamon, allspice, cloves, nutmeg and ginger.
4. Cut in butter with a fork or pastry blender.
5. Combine milk and molasses in a small bowl; stir into flour mixture until moistened.
6. Turn dough out onto a lightly floured surface and knead briefly.
7. Roll dough out into a 1/2 inch thick round.
8. Cut out circles with a medium biscuit cutter and place pieces on prepared baking sheet so that they are barely touching.
9. Bake in preheated oven for 10 to 12 minutes.
10. Move to a wire rack to cool slightly before serving.
11. For soft scones, cover with a dry cloth for 10 minutes.
12. For crisp scones, do not cover.
