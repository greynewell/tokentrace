## Ingredients

- ¼ cup olive oil
- 1 ½  onion, diced
- 2  carrots, diced
- 3 large zucchini, chopped small
- 1 (14.5 ounce) can fat-free chicken broth
- ½ (16 ounce) bag baby spinach leaves, chopped
- 1 (10.75 ounce) can  cream of potato soup
- 1 quart fat free half-and-half
- ½ cup grated Pecorino Romano cheese
- salt and ground black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Heat olive oil in a pot over medium heat.
2. Cook and stir onion and carrots in hot oil until the onion is translucent, about 5 minutes.
3. Add zucchini; continue cooking until the zucchini begins to soften, about 5 minutes more.
4. Pour chicken broth over the zucchini mixture, place a cover on the pot, and bring to a boil and cook for 8 minutes.
5. Stir spinach into the mixture; continue cooking until the zucchini is tender, about 2 minutes more.
6. Mash the zucchini mixture with a potato masher until a desired texture is achieved.
7. Pour potato soup into the zucchini mixture; bring to a simmer and cook for 5 minutes.
8. Stir half-and-half and Pecorino Romano cheese into the zucchini mixture; stir.
9. Continue cooking until heated through completely, 2 to 3 minutes.
