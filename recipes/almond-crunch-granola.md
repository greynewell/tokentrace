## Ingredients

- 1 cup slivered almonds, toasted*
- 1 cup flaked coconut
- ½ cup honey
- 2 ½ cups rolled oats**
- ¾ teaspoon cinnamon
- ¼ cup almond or vegetable oil

## Instructions

1. Combine toasted almonds with oats, coconut and cinnamon.
2. Blend honey with oil and drizzle over almond-oat mixture, tossing to mix well.
3. Bake in oven at 350 degrees, 30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
4. Remove from oven; loosen with spatula; cool.
5. Microwave Directions:  Combine as above in glass baking dish.
6. Cook on high 8 minutes, stirring every two minutes; cool.
