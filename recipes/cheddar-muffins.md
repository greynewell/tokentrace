## Ingredients

- 2 cups all-purpose flour
- 3 ½ teaspoons baking powder
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon paprika
- ¼ cup butter
- 1  egg
- 1 cup milk
- 1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
- ⅔ cup raisins

## Instructions

1. Sift flour with baking powder, salt, and paprika.
2. Cut in butter finely.
3. Stir in remaining ingredients just until dry ingredients are moistened.
4. Spoon into well-greased muffin pans.
5. Bake in a preheated 425 degree F (220 degrees C) oven for 25 minutes.
