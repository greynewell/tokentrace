## Ingredients

- 2 ½ cups blanched almond flour
- 1 teaspoon baking soda
- ¾ teaspoon sea salt
- ¼ cup coconut oil, melted and cooled
- 2  large eggs at room temperature, beaten
- 1 tablespoon raw honey

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
3. Mix almond flour, baking soda, and sea salt together in a bowl; stir in coconut oil until fully incorporated.
4. Stir eggs and honey into almond flour mixture until a dough ball forms.
5. Scoop dough using an ice cream scoop and arrange dough scoops in the parchment paper.
6. Lightly press each piece of dough to create a biscuit shape.
7. Bake in the preheated oven until biscuits are lightly browned, about 15 minutes.
