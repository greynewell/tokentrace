## Ingredients

- 8  eggs
- ½ cup mayonnaise, or more if needed
- ½ teaspoon mustard
- 1 (10 ounce) package frozen chopped spinach, thawed and squeezed dry
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- 3 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese

## Instructions

1. Place eggs in a pot and cover with water; bring to a boil for 10 minutes.
2. Transfer eggs to a colander and run under cold water until completely cooled.
3. Peel eggs, cut in half lengthwise, and scoop egg yolks into a small bowl.
4. Place egg whites onto a serving plate.
5. Mash egg yolks with a fork until crumbly.
6. Stir mayonnaise and mustard into yolks until smooth.
7. Fold spinach into mayonnaise mixture; season with salt and pepper.
8. Spoon mixture onto the egg whites; top each egg with Parmesan cheese.
9. Refrigerate until completely chilled, at least 1 hour.
