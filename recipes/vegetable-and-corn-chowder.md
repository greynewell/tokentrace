## Ingredients

- ½ pound maple-cured bacon, chopped
- ½ cup diced ham
- 1 (16 ounce) package frozen mixed vegetables
- 4 cups diced potato
- 3 cups water
- 1  onion, diced
- 1 (15 ounce) can cream-style corn
- 1 (14 ounce) can whole kernel corn
- 1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of mushroom soup
- 1 cup milk
- ¼ cup half-and-half

## Instructions

1. Heat a large skillet over medium heat; cook and stir bacon and ham until bacon is crisp, about 10 minutes.
2. Drain bacon and ham on a paper towel-lined plate.
3. Bring mixed vegetables, potato, water, and onion to a boil in a large pot; reduce heat to medium and simmer until potatoes begin to soften, 12 to 15 minutes.
4. Stir in cream-style corn, whole kernel corn, cream of mushroom soup, milk, half-and-half, bacon, and ham.
5. Continue to simmer until potatoes are completely tender, about 5 minutes more.
