## Ingredients

- 2 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened
- 1 (8 ounce) package shredded Monterey Jack cheese
- 1 (8 ounce) package shredded Parmesan cheese
- ½ cup chopped onion
- ½ cup mayonnaise

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C).
2. Mix cream cheese, Monterey Jack cheese, Parmesan cheese, onion, and mayonnaise together in a bowl; transfer to a baking dish or pie pan and spread evenly.
3. Bake in the preheated oven until top is brown and bubbling, about 20 minutes.
