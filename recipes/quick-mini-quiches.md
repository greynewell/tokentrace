## Ingredients

- 15  mini phyllo tart shells
- ½ cup shredded Swiss cheese
- ⅓ cup crumbled cooked bacon
- 1  egg
- ½ cup half-and-half
- ¼ teaspoon dried basil
- ¼ teaspoon dried parsley
- ¼ teaspoon garlic powder
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- ⅛ teaspoon ground black pepper

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Arrange phyllo tart shells on a baking sheet.
3. Fill each shell with about 1 teaspoon Swiss cheese and 1/2 teaspoon bacon.
4. Lightly beat egg, half-and-half, basil, parsley, garlic powder, salt, and black pepper together in a bowl.
5. Pour about 1 teaspoon egg mixture into each phyllo shell.
6. Bake in the preheated oven until egg is set in the middle and lightly browned, 12 to 15 minutes.
