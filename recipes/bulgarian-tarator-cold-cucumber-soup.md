## Ingredients

- 2  cucumbers, peeled and cut into small cubes
- 1 (16 ounce) container plain yogurt
- ¼ cup minced fresh dill
- 4 cloves garlic, pressed
- 2 teaspoons olive oil
- salt to taste
- 1 tablespoon water, or as needed  (Optional)
- 10  ice cubes, or as needed

## Instructions

1. Place cucumbers in a large bowl.
2. Whisk yogurt in a separate bowl using a fork until yogurt is smooth and liquefied; pour over cucumbers.
3. Add dill, garlic, oil, and salt to cucumber mixture; toss to coat.
4. Stir in enough water to reach desired consistency.
5. Refrigerate until chilled, at least 30 minutes.
6. Ladle soup into serving bowls; add about 2 ice cubes to each bowl.
