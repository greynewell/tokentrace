## Ingredients

- 4 (4 ounce) fillets tilapia
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- ¼ cup all-purpose flour, divided
- 1 teaspoon garlic powder
- ¼ cup margarine, divided
- 2 tablespoons lemon juice
- 2 tablespoons capers, drained and smashed
- ½ cup fish stock
- 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley

## Instructions

1. Season tilapia fillets on both sides with salt and pepper.
2. Mix 3 tablespoons flour and garlic powder together in a shallow bowl.
3. Dredge tilapia fillets in flour mixture; shake off excess.
4. Melt 3 tablespoons margarine in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
5. Cook fillets in the hot margarine for 2 minutes.
6. Flip; cook until crust is crisp and flesh just starts to flake when pressed with a fork, about 1 minute more.
7. Transfer fillets to a paper towel-lined plate.
8. Melt remaining 1 tablespoon margarine in the same skillet over medium heat.
9. Scrape the browned bits of fish off the bottom of the skillet with a wooden spoon.
10. Stir in remaining 1 tablespoon flour until thickened.
11. Add lemon juice and capers.
12. Pour fish stock into the skillet; cook, stirring frequently, until reduced by half, 5 to 10 minutes.
13. Return fillets to the skillet and spoon reduced stock on top.
14. Garnish with parsley.
