## Ingredients

- 3  cucumbers, peeled and sliced
- 2 large ripe tomatoes, sliced
- 2  green onions, sliced thin
- 8 leaves fresh basil, chopped
- ½ cup crumbled feta cheese
- ½ cup seasoned rice wine vinegar
- ½ cup honey

## Instructions

1. Layer cucumbers, tomatoes, green onions, and basil in a bowl; top with feta cheese.
2. Drizzle rice wine vinegar over the surface; top with honey.
