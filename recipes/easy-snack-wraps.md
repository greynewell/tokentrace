## Ingredients

- 12 (10 inch) flour tortillas
- 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese
- 1 head lettuce
- 1 (6 ounce) package sliced deli-style turkey
- 2 cups shredded carrots
- 2 cups minced tomato

## Instructions

1. Working in batches, lay a few tortillas on a work surface. Spread a thin layer of cream cheese evenly over tortillas and top with some lettuce leaves. Cover lettuce with a few turkey slices. Sprinkle some carrots and tomato over turkey. Roll up tortillas into wraps.
2. Cut wraps diagonally into bite-sized pieces. Secure with toothpicks.
