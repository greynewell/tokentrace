## Ingredients

- 2 large  cucumbers, peeled and sliced
- 2 large carrots, shredded
- 1 large yellow bell pepper, seeded and cut into strips
- 1 large red bell pepper, seeded and cut into strips
- ¼  red onion, minced  (Optional)
- 2 teaspoons lemon zest
- 1 large lemon, juiced

## Instructions

1. Toss cucumbers, carrots, yellow bell pepper, red bell pepper, and red onion together in a bowl.
2. Scatter lemon zest over the cucumber mixture.
3. Squeeze lemon juice over the salad and toss to coat.
4. Chill completely in refrigerator to serve.
