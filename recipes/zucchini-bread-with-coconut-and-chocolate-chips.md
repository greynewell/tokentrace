## Ingredients

- 3  eggs
- 1 cup vegetable oil
- 1 cup white sugar
- 2 cups grated zucchini
- 3 cups all-purpose flour
- ¼ teaspoon baking powder
- 1 tablespoon baking soda
- 1 teaspoon salt
- ¾ cup shredded coconut
- ½ cup miniature semisweet chocolate chips

## Instructions

1. Preheat an oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Grease a 9x5-inch loaf pan.
3. Beat the eggs in a large bowl until frothy.
4. Stir the vegetable oil, sugar, and zucchini into the eggs.
5. Sift the flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt together in a separate bowl; stir into the zucchini mixture until combined.
6. Fold the coconut and chocolate chips into the resulting mixture; pour into the prepared loaf pan.
7. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean, 45 to 50 minutes.
8. Cool in the pans for 10 minutes before removing to cool completely on a wire rack.
