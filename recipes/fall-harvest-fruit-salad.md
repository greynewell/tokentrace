## Ingredients

- 4 medium ripe pears - peeled, cored, and cubed
- 4 medium apples - peeled, cored, and cubed
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice
- 1 cup sweetened dried cranberries (such as Craisins®)
- ½ cup chopped walnuts
- ½ cup granola
- ½ cup brown sugar
- 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
- ½ teaspoon ground nutmeg

## Instructions

1. Coat pears and apples in lemon juice.
2. Mix dried cranberries, walnuts, granola, brown sugar, cinnamon, and nutmeg together in a large bowl. Toss pears and apples into the mixture.
