## Ingredients

- 1  butternut squash, halved lengthwise and seeded
- water
- 1 tablespoon butter, divided
- salt and ground black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Place squash, cut-sides down, in a 9x13-inch baking dish. Pour 2 cups water into the dish around the squash halves.
3. Bake in the preheated oven until tender and easily pierced with a fork, about 1 1/2 hours, adding more water as needed.
4. Remove from the oven and transfer each half to a plate, cut-side up. Top each half with butter and season with salt and pepper.
