## Ingredients

- 1 (10 ounce) package egg noodles
- 3 ½ tablespoons sriracha sauce
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 tablespoon peanut oil
- 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
- 1 ½ teaspoons dried basil
- 1 teaspoon paprika
- freshly cracked black pepper

## Instructions

1. Bring a pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
2. Cook egg noodles in the boiling water, stirring occasionally until cooked through but firm to the bite, about 5 minutes.
3. Drain and transfer noodles to a bowl.
4. Stir sriracha sauce, butter, peanut oil, rice vinegar, basil, paprika, and black pepper together in the same pot over medium heat until sauce is smooth and heated through, 3 to 5 minutes.
5. Add noodles to sauce and toss to coat.
6. Cool before serving.
