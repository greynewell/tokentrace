## Ingredients

- 2 (8 ounce) packages dry corn bread mix
- 1  red onion, chopped
- 1  green bell pepper, chopped
- 1  tomato, chopped
- 4 cups mayonnaise

## Instructions

1. Prepare cornbread according to the box instructions.
2. Cool, crumble and place in a large bowl.
3. Mix in red onion, bell pepper, tomato, and mayonnaise.
4. Cover, and refrigerate for at least 18 hours.
