## Ingredients

- ¼ cup mayonnaise
- 1 tablespoon chopped fresh dill, or to taste
- 2 teaspoons Dijon mustard
- 3 pinches brown sugar, or to taste
- 1 teaspoon vegetable oil
- ½ pound salmon fillet, skin on

## Instructions

1. Preheat grill for medium heat and lightly oil the grate.
2. Mix mayonnaise, dill, mustard, and brown sugar in a bowl. Spread vegetable oil over salmon skin.
3. Grill salmon skin-side down on preheated grill. Spread mayonnaise mixture over top of salmon and cook until salmon is easily flaked with a fork, 6 to 12 minutes.
