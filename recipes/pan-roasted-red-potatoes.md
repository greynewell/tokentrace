## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon duck fat
- 3  unpeeled red potatoes, diced
- 1 teaspoon dried parsley
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ¼ teaspoon ground white pepper

## Instructions

1. Melt duck fat in a large skillet over medium-high heat until shimmering, about 1 minute. Add potatoes; cook, stirring minimally, until potatoes are golden brown, 5 to 10 minutes. Drain excess fat.
2. Combine parsley, salt, and white pepper in a bowl; toss over cooked potatoes to coat evenly.
