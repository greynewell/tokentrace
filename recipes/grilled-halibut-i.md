## Ingredients

- ½ cup fresh grapefruit juice
- ¼ cup olive oil
- 2 teaspoons chopped fresh marjoram
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ⅛ teaspoon ground black pepper
- 4 (8 ounce) steaks halibut
- 4 sprigs fresh marjoram, for garnish

## Instructions

1. Mix together grapefruit juice, olive oil, 2 teaspoons marjoram, salt, and pepper, in a shallow glass dish.
2. Add fish, turn once to coat both sides, then cover dish.
3. Refrigerate 1 to 2 hours, turning once or twice.
4. Preheat barbecue or gas grill.
5. Reserve marinade, and place fish in lightly oiled wire basket.
6. Place fish 4 to 6 inches above hot coals.
7. Grill for 10 to 12 minutes, turning once and brushing twice with reserved marinade, until steaks are barely opaque in thickest part.
8. Arrange on a platter, scatter grapefruit sections around steaks, and garnish with sprigs of marjoram.
