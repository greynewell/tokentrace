## Ingredients

- 1 (15 ounce) package refrigerated pie crust dough
- ¾ cup peanut butter
- 1 large apple - peeled, cored and sliced
- 8 ounces medium Cheddar cheese, sliced

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Unroll each piece of refrigerated pie dough on a floured surface and cut each into 6 equal pieces.
3. Spoon 1 tablespoon of peanut butter into the center of each piece of dough, and place 2 slices of apple on the peanut butter.
4. Top each square with a slice of Cheddar cheese.
5. Dip your finger in water, and moisten the edges of each piece of dough.
6. Fold the dough over into a triangle shape, and seal the edges.
7. Bake in the preheated oven until the cheese has melted and the pies are golden brown, 15 to 20 minutes.
