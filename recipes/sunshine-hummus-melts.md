## Ingredients

- 8 slices multigrain bread
- 1 cup hummus
- 2  Granny Smith apples, thinly sliced
- 1 cup shredded sharp Cheddar cheese

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven broiler.
2. Lay out the bread on a baking sheet.
3. Broil the bread until lightly toasted, 2 to 4 minutes.
4. Carefully turn the bread over on the baking sheet and place back under the broiler to lightly toast the other side, 2 to 4 minutes.
5. Take the bread from the oven and spread 2 tablespoons of hummus on each piece.
6. Lay the apple slices on top of the hummus and sprinkle the cheese over the apples.
7. Broil the sandwiches open-faced until the cheese is melted and bubbly, 3 to 4 minutes.
8. Sandwich two slices of bread together, to make four sandwiches.
