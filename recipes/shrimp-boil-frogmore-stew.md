## Ingredients

- ¾ cup seafood seasoning (such as Old Bay®), or more to taste
- 2 pounds small red potatoes, scrubbed
- 2 pounds smoked sausage, cut into 2-inch pieces
- 6 ears corn, husked and cut in half
- 3 pounds fresh shrimp, unpeeled

## Instructions

1. Fill a large soup pot halfway with water.
2. Add 3/4 cup seafood seasoning and bring to a boil.
3. Add potatoes; cook for 10 minutes.
4. Add sausage; cook for 10 minutes.
5. Add corn; cook for 10 minutes.
6. Add shrimp; cook until pink, 2 to 3 minutes.
7. Drain; return to the pot.
8. Sprinkle with more seafood seasoning; toss to coat.
9. Pour onto the middle of a table covered with brown paper.
