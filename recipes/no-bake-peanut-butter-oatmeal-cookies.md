## Ingredients

- 2 cups white sugar
- ½ cup milk
- ½ cup butter
- ⅔ cup creamy peanut butter
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 3 cups quick-cooking oats

## Instructions

1. Combine sugar, milk, and butter in a large saucepan; bring to a boil. Cook at a boil, stirring constantly, for 2 minutes. Remove saucepan from heat.
2. Stir peanut butter and vanilla extract into sugar mixture until smooth; fold in oats until well blended. Drop mixture by spoonfuls onto aluminum foil or waxed paper. Cool until cookies are set.
