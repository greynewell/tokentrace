## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 (6 ounce) chicken breasts
- ½ cup chicken broth
- 2 tablespoons lemon juice

## Instructions

1. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat; cook and stir garlic until fragrant, about 1 minute.
2. Add chicken and cook until browned, about 4 minutes per side.
3. Pour broth over chicken and cover skillet; lower heat to low and simmer until chicken is no longer pink in the center, about 30 minutes.
4. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 165 degrees F (74 degrees C).
5. Transfer chicken to a plate, reserving broth mixture in the skillet.
6. Pour lemon juice into broth mixture and simmer until liquid is reduced by 1/3, 2 to 3 minutes.
7. Return chicken to the skillet and cook until heated through, 2 to 3 minutes per side.
