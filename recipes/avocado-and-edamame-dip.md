## Ingredients

- 6 ounces shelled edamame (green soybeans)
- ½  onion, chopped
- ½ cup tightly packed cilantro
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 1 large avocado, peeled, pitted and cubed
- 1  lemon, juiced
- 1 tablespoon chile-garlic sauce (such as Sriracha®)
- salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Place the edamame, onion, cilantro, and olive oil into a food processor.
2. Pulse until finely chopped.
3. Add the avocado, lemon juice, and chile-garlic sauce; season to taste with salt and pepper.
4. Puree until smooth.
5. Refrigerate at least 30 minutes before serving.
