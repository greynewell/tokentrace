## Ingredients

- 1 ½ cups water
- ¾ cup raisins
- ⅓ cup packed brown sugar
- 1 teaspoon cornstarch
- 1 pinch salt

## Instructions

1. Bring water to a boil in a saucepan.
2. Stir raisins into water, reduce heat to medium, and boil until very tender, about 5 minutes.
3. Whisk brown sugar, cornstarch, and salt into raisin mixture and simmer until thickened, about 10 minutes.
