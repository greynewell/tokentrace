## Ingredients

- 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
- 1 cup sour cream
- ½ cup Dijon mustard
- ½ cup mayonnaise
- 3 tablespoons prepared horseradish
- 3 tablespoons garlic powder
- 3 tablespoons onion powder
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice
- ½ teaspoon cayenne pepper, or more to taste
- salt to taste

## Instructions

1. Combine cream cheese, sour cream, Dijon mustard, mayonnaise, horseradish, garlic powder, onion powder, lemon juice, cayenne pepper, and salt in a large bowl.
2. Beat with an electric mixer until smooth.
3. Season with additional salt if needed.
4. Cover and refrigerate for at least 1 hour.
