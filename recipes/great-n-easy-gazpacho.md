## Ingredients

- 1  onion, finely chopped
- 1  cucumber, finely chopped
- 1  green bell pepper, finely chopped
- 1  yellow bell pepper, finely chopped
- 1  red bell pepper, finely chopped
- 3 stalks celery, finely chopped
- 2 (14.5 ounce) cans whole peeled tomatoes
- ½ cup tomato juice
- 2 tablespoons white vinegar
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 1 teaspoon crushed garlic
- salt and ground black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Combine onion, cucumber, green bell pepper, yellow bell pepper, red bell pepper, celery, tomatoes, tomato juice, vinegar, olive oil, and garlic in a large bowl.
2. Blend tomatoes with an immersion blender until mostly smooth.
3. Season with salt and black pepper.
4. Chill in refrigerator to blend flavors, at least 2 hours.
