## Ingredients

- 5  potatoes, peeled and cubed
- 1 small onion, chopped
- 1 ½ cups chive-flavored cottage cheese
- 1 cup sour cream
- 1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese

## Instructions

1. Place potatoes in a large pot of water and boil until tender when pierced with a fork.
2. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
3. Butter a 9x3 inch casserole dish.
4. In a large mixing bowl, combine potatoes, onion, cottage cheese, and sour cream.
5. Transfer mixture to the prepared casserole dish.
6. Top with Cheddar cheese.
7. Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 30 to 40 minutes.
