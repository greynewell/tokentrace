## Ingredients

- 2 cups tequila
- 1 cup triple sec
- ¾ cup fresh lime juice
- ¾ cup sweetened lime juice
- 3 cups ice
- kosher salt
- 8  lime wedges

## Instructions

1. Combine ice, tequila, triple sec, fresh lime juice, and sweetened lime juice in a large pitcher; stir.
2. Pour kosher salt onto a plate. Rub the rim of a glass with lime wedge. Dip the rim of the glass into salt; fill the glass with margarita mixture; repeat for each serving.
