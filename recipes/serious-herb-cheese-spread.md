## Ingredients

- 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- ½ teaspoon prepared mustard
- ½ teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
- ¼ cup chopped parsley
- ¼ cup chopped fresh dill weed
- ¼ cup chopped fresh basil
- ¼ cup chopped black olives
- 1 ½ tablespoons lemon juice

## Instructions

1. In a medium bowl, mix cream cheese, garlic, mustard, Worcestershire sauce, parsley, dill weed, basil, olives and lemon juice.
2. Transfer to desired mold, and chill in the refrigerator at least 2 hours before serving.
