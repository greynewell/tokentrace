## Ingredients

- 1 (12 ounce) can evaporated milk
- 1 (5.9 ounce) package chocolate instant pudding mix
- 1 (6.5 ounce) can Reddi-wip® Original Dairy Whipped Topping
- ½ cup miniature semi-sweet chocolate morsels
- 1 (9 inch) graham cracker pie crust
- 1 tablespoon Additional Reddi-wip Original Whipped Light Cream and crushed candy

## Instructions

1. Pour milk into medium bowl. Add dry pudding mix; beat with wire whisk until well blended and mixture just begins to thicken. Stir in chocolate morsels, if desired.
2. Add contents of Reddi-wip can; stir gently but quickly until well blended. Pour into crust; cover.
3. Refrigerate 6 hours, or until set. Cut into 8 slices to serve. Garnish with additional Reddi-wip and crushed candy, if desired.
