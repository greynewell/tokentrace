## Ingredients

- 2 ½ cups buttermilk baking mix
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- 1 pinch salt
- ⅔ cup milk
- ½ tablespoon malt vinegar

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C).
2. Lightly grease two baking sheets.
3. In a large bowl, stir together baking mix, baking powder and salt.
4. Mix in milk and vinegar until a loose dough forms.
5. Turn out onto a lightly floured surface and knead briefly; about 10 times.
6. Break dough apart into 12 equal pieces and place on prepared baking sheets.
7. Bake in preheated oven for 8 to 10 minutes, or until light brown.
