## Ingredients

- 4 cups chicken broth, divided
- ¼ cup matchstick-cut carrots
- 1 tablespoon matchstick-cut fresh ginger
- 2  green onions, thinly sliced with white and green parts separated
- 2 teaspoons cornstarch
- ½ cup shredded cabbage
- 2  eggs, beaten
- salt and ground black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Heat 3 1/4 cups chicken broth in a saucepan over medium-high heat.
2. Add carrots, ginger, and white parts of green onion; bring soup to a boil.
3. Whisk remaining 3/4 cup broth and cornstarch together in a bowl until dissolved.
4. Stir cabbage and green parts of green onion into the soup.
5. Drop beaten egg, a spoonful at a time, into soup, stirring after each addition to prevent clumping.
6. Stir cornstarch mixture into soup and season with salt and pepper.
7. Stir soup until cornstarch thickens the soup, about 2 minutes.
