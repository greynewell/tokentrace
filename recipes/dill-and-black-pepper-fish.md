## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon vegetable oil, or as needed
- ¾ cup crushed dill-pickle-flavored potato chips
- ½ teaspoon ground black pepper
- ½ teaspoon onion powder
- 4 (4 ounce) cod fillets
- 1 cup buttermilk

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
2. Grease a rimmed baking sheet with oil.
3. Combine crushed chips, pepper, and onion powder in a medium bowl.
4. Dip cod in buttermilk; drain off excess.
5. Place cod on the prepared baking dish and press chip mixture evenly on top of the fillets.
6. Bake in the preheated oven until fish flakes easily with a fork, 20 to 25 minutes.
