## Ingredients

- 3  eggs
- 3 tablespoons heavy whipping cream, or to taste
- 1 teaspoon butter
- 1 ounce cream cheese, cut into small pieces, or to taste
- 2 ounces smoked salmon, chopped
- 1 tablespoon chopped scallions
- 1 pinch salt and ground black pepper to taste
- 1 tablespoon chopped fresh dill
- 2 teaspoons sour cream, or to taste
- 2 teaspoons chopped fresh chives, or to taste

## Instructions

1. Whisk eggs and heavy cream together in a large bowl.
2. Heat a large nonstick skillet over medium heat.
3. Spread butter evenly over the surface of the pan; cook until foamy, 30 seconds to 1 minute.
4. Add eggs; cook until beginning to set, about 4 minutes.
5. Drop cream cheese pieces, salmon pieces, and scallions over the eggs.
6. Continue cooking until eggs are set, about 2 minutes more. season with salt and pepper.
7. Roll the omelet over onto itself; slide onto a plate.
8. Top with dill, sour cream, and chives.
