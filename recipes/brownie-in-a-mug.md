## Ingredients

- ¼ cup white sugar
- ¼ cup all-purpose flour
- 2 tablespoons cocoa powder
- 1 pinch salt
- 3 tablespoons water
- 2 tablespoons olive oil

## Instructions

1. Stir sugar, flour, cocoa powder, and salt together in a mug. Add water and olive oil; stir until mixture is evenly moist.
2. Cook in microwave for 1 minute 40 seconds. Serve topped with ice cream.
