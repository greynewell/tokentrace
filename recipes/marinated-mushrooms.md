## Ingredients

- 4 cubes  chicken bouillon
- 4 cubes beef bouillon
- 2 cups boiling water
- 1 cup dry red wine
- 1 teaspoon dill weed
- 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
- 1 teaspoon garlic powder
- 4 pounds fresh mushrooms
- ½ cup butter, or more as needed

## Instructions

1. Dissolve chicken and beef bouillon cubes in boiling water in a bowl; stir well.
2. Add red wine, dill, Worcestershire sauce, and garlic powder; stir to combine.
3. Arrange mushrooms in the bottom of a slow cooker.
4. Pour broth mixture over mushrooms; top with butter.
5. Cook on Low for 12 hours.
