## Ingredients

- 1 cup whole wheat flour
- 1 cup bread flour
- 3 tablespoons olive oil
- 1 ½ teaspoons baking powder
- 1 teaspoon salt, or more to taste
- 1 cup water, or as needed

## Instructions

1. Mix whole wheat flour, bread flour, olive oil, baking powder, and salt together in a large bowl.
2. Mix in enough water to bring dough together.
3. Turn dough out on a lightly floured surface.
4. Let rest for 10 to 20 minutes.
5. Preheat oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C).
6. Line 2 baking sheets with parchment paper.
7. Divide dough into 6 to 8 pieces.
8. Roll out each piece to paper-thin thickness.
9. Transfer pieces of dough to the lined baking sheets; cut into strips or squares.
10. Bake in the preheated oven until browned and edges curl up, 15 to 20 minutes.
