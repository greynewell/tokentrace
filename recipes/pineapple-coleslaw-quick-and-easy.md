## Ingredients

- 2 cups miniature marshmallows
- 1 ½ cups mayonnaise
- 1 (8 ounce) can crushed pineapple, drained
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- 2 (8.5 ounce) packages coleslaw mix

## Instructions

1. Mix marshmallows, mayonnaise, pineapple, salt, and pepper together in a bowl; fold in coleslaw mix.
2. Cover bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate until flavors blend, at least 1 hour.
