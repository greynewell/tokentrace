## Ingredients

- 1 (2 1/2 pound) beef eye of round roast
- 2 tablespoons Greek seasoning
- 1 tablespoon ground black pepper
- 2 teaspoons salt
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- kitchen twine
- 2 cups beef broth
- 1 teaspoon beef bouillon granules
- 6  Italian submarine rolls
- ½ cup giardiniera, drained, or to taste

## Instructions

1. Cut notches all over the roast with a sharp knife.
2. Rub with Greek seasoning, pepper, salt, and garlic.
3. Cover and refrigerate for at least 4 hours.
4. Bring roast to room temperature before cooking, about 1 hour.
5. Tie up roast with kitchen twine so it will hold its shape while roasting.
6. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
7. Mix beef broth and bouillon granules together in a roasting pan.
8. Place roast on a rack above the broth so drippings will fall into the broth.
9. Bake in the preheated oven until tender, about 1 1/2 hours.
10. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read 140 degrees F (60 degrees C).
11. Tent with foil and let cool for 20 minutes.
12. Pour broth into a saucepan.
13. Bring to a simmer.
14. Slice roast very thinly; dunk slices in the hot au jus and stuff into Italian rolls.
15. Top with giardiniera.
16. Ladle more au jus over each sandwich.
