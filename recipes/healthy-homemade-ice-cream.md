## Ingredients

- 1 ½ cups almond milk
- 2  bananas - peeled, sliced, and frozen
- ½ cup strawberries, sliced and frozen
- ½ teaspoon vanilla extract

## Instructions

1. Blend almond milk, frozen bananas, frozen strawberries, and vanilla extract together in a blender until desired consistency is reached.
2. Transfer mixture to a bowl and freeze until solid, about 4 hours.
