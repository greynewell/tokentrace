## Ingredients

- 1  center-cut slice of watermelon, 1 to 2 inches thick
- ¼ cup plain yogurt
- 1 teaspoon honey
- ⅓ cup fresh blueberries
- ⅓ cup fresh raspberries
- ⅓ cup sliced fresh strawberries
- 1 tablespoon chopped pistachio nuts
- 1 teaspoon shredded coconut

## Instructions

1. Cut the round watermelon slice into 8 wedges, keeping them together for a nice presentation, and place on a serving dish.
2. Spoon yogurt evenly over watermelon, stopping before the rind. Drizzle with honey. Sprinkle blueberries, raspberries, strawberries, pistachios, and shredded coconut evenly over the yogurt. Serve immediately.
