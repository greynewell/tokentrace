## Ingredients

- 1  watermelon, peeled and cubed
- 1  honeydew melon - peeled, seeded, and cubed
- 1  cantaloupe - peeled, seeded, and cubed
- 1  pineapple, peeled and cubed
- 1 (16 ounce) package sliced fresh strawberries
- 1 (12 ounce) bottle poppy seed salad dressing
- 1 (8 ounce) container vanilla yogurt

## Instructions

1. Combine watermelon, honeydew, cantaloupe, pineapple, and strawberries in a large bowl.
2. Whisk poppy seed dressing and yogurt together in a bowl; drizzle dressing mixture over fruit before serving.
