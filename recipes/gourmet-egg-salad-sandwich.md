## Ingredients

- 4 slices bacon
- 4  hard-cooked eggs, chopped
- ¼ cup mayonnaise
- 1 teaspoon yellow mustard
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- 4 slices sourdough bread
- 2 tablespoons pesto
- 4 slices Jarlsberg cheese

## Instructions

1. Place bacon in a large skillet and cook over medium-high heat, turning occasionally, until evenly browned, about 10 minutes.
2. Drain bacon slices on paper towels; crumble.
3. Preheat oven broiler and set rack about 6 inches from heat source.
4. Mix eggs, mayonnaise, mustard, salt, and pepper in a bowl; fold in crumbled bacon.
5. Toast sourdough bread under broiler until lightly browned, about 2 minutes.
6. Spread pesto evenly on each slice of bread; top with 1/4 egg salad mixture and spread to cover completely.
7. Top each sandwich with 1 slice Jarlsberg cheese.
8. Return sandwiches to broiler and cook until cheese is melted, 2 to 3 minutes.
9. Served sandwiches warm and open-faced.
