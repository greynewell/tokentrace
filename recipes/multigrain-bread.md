## Ingredients

- 3 cups all-purpose flour
- 1 cup whole wheat flour
- ½ cup wheat, rye, and flax hot cereal mix (such as Red River®)
- 1 teaspoon salt
- ½ teaspoon active dry yeast
- 2 ½ cups warm water

## Instructions

1. Whisk all-purpose flour, whole wheat flour, wheat cereal mix, salt, and yeast together in a large bowl.
2. Pour in water; stir until well blended.
3. Cover bowl with plastic wrap and let dough rest at room temperature, 12 to 18 hours.
4. Push dough gently to deflate; form into a ball.
5. Transfer to a bread pan.
6. Let dough rest, 2 to 4 hours.
7. Preheat oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C).
8. Make a 1/2-inch cut in the top of the dough.
9. Bake in the preheated oven until crust is well-browned, about 45 minutes.
