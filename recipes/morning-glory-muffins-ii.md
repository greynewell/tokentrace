## Ingredients

- 2 cups all-purpose flour
- 1 ¼ cups white sugar
- 2 teaspoons baking soda
- 2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
- ½ teaspoon salt
- 1 ½ cups grated carrots
- 1 ½ cups peeled and grated apple
- ¾ cup flaked coconut
- ½ cup dates, pitted and chopped
- ½ cup chopped pecans
- 3  eggs, beaten
- ½ cup vegetable oil
- ½ teaspoon vanilla extract

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
2. Lightly oil 18 muffin cups, or coat with nonstick cooking spray.
3. In a large mixing bowl, combine flour, sugar, baking soda, cinnamon and salt.
4. In a second bowl, combine carrots, apples, coconut, dates and pecans.
5. Stir in eggs, oil and vanilla.
6. Add this mixture to the dry ingredients; stir until smooth.
7. Spoon or scoop the batter into the prepared muffin pans.
8. Bake at 375 degrees F (190 degrees C) for 18 to 20 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center of a muffin comes out clean.
