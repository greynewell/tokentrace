## Ingredients

- 1 large sweet potato
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 1 cup diced onion
- ¼ cup chicken broth
- 1 tablespoon jarred minced garlic
- ¼ cup whole milk
- 1 teaspoon dried parsley
- 1 teaspoon dried basil
- 1 teaspoon onion powder
- ¼ teaspoon ground cumin
- ¼ teaspoon ground cinnamon
- 10 slices bread

## Instructions

1. Bring a large pot of water to a boil.
2. Add sweet potato; cook until tender when pierced with a fork, 15 to 20 minutes.
3. Drain and cool until easily handled, 5 to 10 minutes.
4. Peel potato and mash with a fork.
5. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
6. Add onion; cook and stir until browned, about 5 minutes.
7. Stir in broth and garlic; cook, stirring occasionally, until onion is very tender, about 3 minutes.
8. Combine mashed potato, onion mixture, milk, parsley, basil, onion powder, cumin, and cinnamon in a blender; puree until smooth.
9. Toast bread slices; spread potato mixture on top.
