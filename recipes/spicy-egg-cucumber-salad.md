## Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon olive oil, or as needed
- ½ small red bell pepper, chopped
- ½  jalapeno pepper, minced
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 large eggs
- 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
- 1 large cucumber, sliced
- 1 bunch green onions, chopped
- 1 tablespoon chopped fresh spicy oregano
- 1 teaspoon chile-garlic sauce (such as Sriracha®)
- ½ teaspoon liquid aminos

## Instructions

1. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat; cook and stir red bell pepper, jalapeno pepper, and garlic in the hot oil until fragrant, 2 to 3 minutes.
2. Remove skillet from heat.
3. Mix eggs and balsamic vinegar into jalapeno pepper mixture until egg yolks break; return skillet to medium heat.
4. Cook and stir until eggs are scrambled and set, about 5 minutes.
5. Place cucumber in a large bowl; top with egg mixture, green onions, and oregano.
6. Whisk chile-garlic sauce and liquid aminos together in a bowl; pour mixture over eggs.
7. Toss to evenly coat.
