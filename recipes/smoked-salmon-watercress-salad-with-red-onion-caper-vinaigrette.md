## Ingredients

- 3 tablespoons lemon juice
- 1 tablespoon rice wine vinegar
- 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
- Salt and freshly ground black pepper
- ¼ cup drained capers
- ½ medium red onion, thinly sliced
- ½ cup extra-virgin olive oil
- 8 cups watercress (preferably the kind that's already trimmed, rinsed, bagged)
- 6 ounces smoked salmon, torn or cut into bite-size pieces

## Instructions

1. Mix lemon juice, vinegar, mustard and a big pinch of salt and pepper to taste in a 1-quart Pyrex measuring cup.
2. Add capers and onion; toss to coat.
3. Slowly add oil, pushing onion aside and beating with a small whisk or fork to form a thick dressing.
4. Mix watercress and half the salmon in a large bowl.
5. When ready to serve, pour dressing over watercress and salmon; toss to coat.
6. Arrange on salad plates, garnishing each with remaining smoked salmon.
