## Ingredients

- ¾ cup seashell pasta
- 2  hot dogs, sliced 3/4-inch thick
- ¾ cup green manzanilla olives stuffed with pimento, halved
- 4 ounces mild Cheddar cheese, cubed
- ½ cup creamy salad dressing (such as Miracle Whip®), or as needed

## Instructions

1. Bring a pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
2. Cook shell pasta in the boiling water, stirring occasionally, until cooked through but firm to the bite, 8 minutes.
3. Drain and run under cold water to chill.
4. Place pasta in a salad bowl and combine with hot dogs, olives, Cheddar cheese, and creamy salad dressing.
5. Stir in more salad dressing if mixture seems dry.
6. Keep salad refrigerated.
