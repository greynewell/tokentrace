## Ingredients

- 1 (30 ounce) can fruit cocktail, drained thoroughly
- 1 (20 ounce) can pineapple chunks, drained thoroughly
- 1 (8 ounce) can mandarin oranges, drained thoroughly
- 1 (10.5 ounce) package miniature marshmallows
- 1 (24 ounce) container sour cream

## Instructions

1. Mix fruit cocktail, pineapple, mandarin oranges, and marshmallows together in a large bowl; add sour cream and stir to coat fruit completely.
2. Refrigerate 4 hours to overnight.
