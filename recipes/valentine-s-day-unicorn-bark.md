## Ingredients

- 3 (3.5 ounce) bars high-quality white chocolate, coarsely chopped
- ½ (12 ounce) package pink candy melts
- ¼ (12 ounce) package turquoise candy melts
- ½ cup mini marshmallows, or more as needed
- 1 tablespoon multicolored candy sprinkles
- 1/4 (8 ounce) package conversation heart candies

## Instructions

1. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper and set aside.
2. Place white chocolate in a microwave-safe bowl and microwave in 10-second intervals, stirring after each interval.
3. Place candy melts in separate microwave-safe bowls and melt in the same way, stirring after each 10-second interval.
4. Pour the melted white chocolate onto the prepared baking sheet and spread it out into a thick rectangular shape. Add dollops of melted pink and blue candy. Swirl the colors together using a clean spatula to create fun patterns.
5. Add marshmallows, sprinkles, and heart candies while the chocolate is still melted. Refrigerate until set, about 2 hours. Break up bark into large, irregular pieces.
