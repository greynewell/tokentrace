## Ingredients

- 21 ounces zucchini, sliced
- 2  onions, chopped
- 2 cloves garlic, crushed
- 3 cups water, divided
- salt to taste
- 1 bunch chives, chopped

## Instructions

1. Combine zucchini, onions, garlic, and 2 tablespoons water in a non-stick pan over medium heat; cook until zucchini are softened, 5 to 10 minutes.
2. Add remaining water and bring to a boil.
3. Reduce heat and simmer for 5 minutes.
4. Remove from heat and cool.
5. Pour zucchini mixture into a food processor no more than half full.
6. Cover and pulse a few times before leaving on to blend.
7. Puree in batches until smooth.
8. Season soup with salt and garnish with chives.
