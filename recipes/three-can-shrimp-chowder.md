## Ingredients

- 1 (14.75 ounce) can cream-style corn
- 1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of potato soup
- 1 ½ cups half-and-half cream
- ¼ cup bacon bits
- 2  green onion, chopped
- ¼ teaspoon cayenne pepper
- 1 (8 ounce) package cold cream cheese, cubed
- 1 (4 ounce) can small shrimp, drained

## Instructions

1. Stir together the cream-style corn, cream of potato soup, half-and-half, bacon bits, green onion, and cayenne pepper in a saucepan.
2. Bring to a simmer over medium-high heat, then stir in cream cheese until melted.
3. Add the shrimp, and cook a minute or two to heat through.
