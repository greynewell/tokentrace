## Ingredients

- 2 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened
- 2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese
- 1 cup chopped green bell pepper, or to taste
- ½ large onion, chopped, or to taste
- 1 (4 ounce) jar sliced pimento peppers
- 2 teaspoons Worcestershire sauce
- 1 teaspoon lemon juice
- 1 pinch salt

## Instructions

1. Mix cream cheese and Cheddar cheese together in a bowl using your hands.
2. Add green bell pepper, onion, pimento peppers, Worcestershire sauce, lemon juice, and salt; mix well.
3. Shape mixture into a ball and place in a bowl.
4. Cover bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate for 2 hours.
