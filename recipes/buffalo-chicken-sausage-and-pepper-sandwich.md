## Ingredients

- 2 teaspoons vegetable oil
- 4  Buffalo wing-flavored chicken sausages (such as al fresco®), sliced on a bias
- 1  onion, sliced
- 1  red bell pepper, sliced
- 1  green bell pepper, sliced
- ¼ cup blue cheese salad dressing, or more to taste
- 3 tablespoons hot pepper sauce
- 2 cups baby spinach leaves
- 4  hoagie rolls, split lengthwise and toasted

## Instructions

1. Heat oil in a skillet and tilt skillet to coat surface.
2. Cook sausage slices in the heated skillet until nicely browned, 2 to 3 minutes per side.
3. Add onion, red bell pepper, and green bell pepper to the sausages; cook and stir until tender, about 5 minutes.
4. Transfer sausage mixture to a bowl.
5. Stir blue cheese dressing and hot sauce into the sausage mixture, assuring the sausage slices are coated in sauce.
6. Make a bed of spinach in each hoagie roll.
7. Top spinach with the sausage mixture.
