## Ingredients

- 1 quart whole milk
- 2 tablespoons white sugar
- 2 tablespoons vanilla extract
- 2 tablespoons plain yogurt

## Instructions

1. Heat up the oven by turning on for 3 to 4 minutes; turn the oven off.
2. Bring milk to a boil over medium-high heat in a large saucepan. Stir in sugar and vanilla extract until blended. Turn off heat and let sit until warm but not cool.
3. Place yogurt in a large container; pour in milk mixture. Cover and place in the oven until yogurt has set, 8 to 16 hours. Store in the refrigerator.
