## Ingredients

- 3 (1/2-inch) center slices King's Hawaiian® Sweet Bread
- 2 tablespoons granulated sugar
- ½ teaspoon ground cinnamon
- ¼ cup margarine, melted
- 2 ½ cups sliced strawberries
- 3 cups non-dairy whipped topping
- 1 cup Strawberries to garnish

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
2. Blend sugar and cinnamon together. Brush both sides of bread lightly with margarine. Generously sprinkle with cinnamon sugar mixture. Cut into 1-inch cubes.
3. BAKE cubes on a baking sheet with a lip for 8-9 minutes, or until golden brown, stirring once. Set aside to cool.
4. Toss together croutons and berries. Spread evenly into an 8x8x2-inch pan. Spread whipped topping evenly to cover filling*.
5. Cover and refrigerate at least 1 hour. Sprinkle top with cinnamon-sugar, if desired.
