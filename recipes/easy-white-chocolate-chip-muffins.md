## Ingredients

- 2 cups self-rising flour
- ½ cup white sugar
- ⅓ cup cocoa powder
- 1 cup milk
- ¼ cup butter, melted
- 2  eggs
- 1 ½ cups white chocolate chips

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Line a 12-cup muffin tin with paper liners.
2. Combine flour, sugar, and cocoa in a large bowl.
3. Combine milk, butter, and eggs in another bowl. Stir into flour mixture just until moistened. Fold in white chocolate chips.
4. Fill each prepared muffin cup 3/4 full.
5. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center of a muffin comes out clean, about 30 minutes.
6. Remove from the oven and let cool for 5 minutes before removing from the muffin tin. Serve warm.
