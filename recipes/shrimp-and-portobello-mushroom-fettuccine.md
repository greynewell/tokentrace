## Ingredients

- 1 (16 ounce) package dry fettuccini noodles
- ¾ cup butter, divided
- 1 pound baby portobello mushrooms, sliced
- 1 large onion, chopped
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 4 ounces cream cheese
- 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
- 1 pint whipping cream
- ½ cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese, divided
- 2 pounds medium shrimp, peeled and deveined
- 1 tablespoon Cajun seasoning
- salt and pepper to taste
- ½ cup green onions, chopped

## Instructions

1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to boil.
2. Add pasta, and cook until al dente, about 8 to 10 minutes.
3. Drain, and set aside.
4. Meanwhile, heat 1/4 cup butter in a large skillet over medium heat.
5. Stir in mushrooms, and cook until soft.
6. Remove mushrooms, and set aside.
7. Wipe out skillet.
8. Heat 1/2 cup butter in the skillet over medium-high heat.
9. Stir in onions, and cook until soft and translucent.
10. Stir in garlic, and cook 1 minute.
11. Add cream cheese, and cook until melted.
12. Reduce heat to medium low, and stir in flour, whipping cream, and 1/4 cup Parmesan cheese.
13. Stir in shrimp, and season with Cajun seasoning, salt, and pepper.
14. Cook until shrimp are pink and sauce thickens, about 3 minutes.
15. Remove from heat.
16. Fold mushrooms, green onions, and fettuccine into sauce.
17. Sprinkle with remaining 1/4 cup Parmesan, and serve.
