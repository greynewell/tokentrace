## Ingredients

- 1 (10 ounce) can baby peas, drained
- 1 (15 ounce) can sliced beets, drained and diced
- 2 tablespoons chopped onion  (Optional)
- ½ cup creamy salad dressing, e.g. Miracle Whip ™

## Instructions

1. In a medium bowl, mix together the peas, beets, onion and salad dressing.
2. Refrigerate until cold, about 30 minutes, before serving.
