## Ingredients

- 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
- 1 (12 ounce) jar bacon bits
- 1 pinch ground black pepper, or to taste
- ¼ cup diced onion
- 2 (8 ounce) packages refrigerated crescent roll dough

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. In a mixing bowl, combine the cream cheese, bacon bits, pepper and onion.
3. On a lightly floured surface, unroll the crescent rolls lengthwise and form into a long rectangle.
4. Thinly spread the cream cheese mixture on the dough.
5. Starting with the long edge of the rectangle, roll the dough into a long, thin roll.
6. Slice the roll into 1/4 inch thick pieces.
7. Place rolls on a lightly greased baking sheet.
8. Bake in preheated oven for 15 minutes, or until brown.
