## Ingredients

- 2  leeks, chopped
- 1  onion, chopped
- 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
- ¾ cup thinly sliced potatoes
- 2 ⅓ cups chicken stock
- salt to taste
- ground black pepper to taste
- 1 ⅛ cups heavy whipping cream

## Instructions

1. Heat butter in a large saucepan over medium-low heat: add leeks and onions. Sauté until soft but not browned, about 8 minutes.
2. Add potatoes and stock; season with salt and pepper to taste. Bring to a boil, reduce heat to low, and simmer gently for 30 minutes.
3. Puree soup in a blender or food processor until very smooth. Set soup aside to cool; transfer into a bowl or a jug and refrigerate until chilled, at least 4 hours. Gently stir in cream before serving.
