## Ingredients

- 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
- 1 (8 ounce) container sour cream
- 2 cups minced crabmeat
- 1 tablespoon garlic salt, or to taste
- 1 (12 ounce) bottle cocktail sauce

## Instructions

1. In a medium bowl, mix together the cream cheese, sour cream, crabmeat, and garlic salt.
2. Form into a ball, and place on a serving plate.
3. Cover the surface as well as you can with cocktail sauce.
4. Refrigerate for at least 30 minutes before serving.
