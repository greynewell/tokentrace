## Ingredients

- 1 cup mayonnaise
- 1 cup sour cream
- 1 tablespoon dried dill weed
- 1 tablespoon dried minced onion, rehydrated
- 1 teaspoon Beau Monde ™ seasoning

## Instructions

1. In small mixing bowl, combine mayonnaise, sour cream, dill weed, onion, and Beau Monde seasoning.
2. Blend well.
3. Cover ,and refrigerate overnight before serving.
