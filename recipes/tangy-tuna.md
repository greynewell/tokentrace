## Ingredients

- 1 (5 ounce) can tuna
- ⅓ cup water
- 1 teaspoon onion powder
- 1 teaspoon garlic powder
- 1  onion, sliced into rings

## Instructions

1. Place tuna in a medium saucepan and pour water over it, then sprinkle with onion powder and garlic powder.
2. Lay onion rings on top.
3. Cover the pan and simmer about 5 minutes, or until cooked through.
