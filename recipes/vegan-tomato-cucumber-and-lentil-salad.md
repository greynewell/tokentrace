## Ingredients

- 8 cups water
- 1 pound dried lentils
- 1  English cucumber, peeled and cut into bite-size pieces
- 2  tomatoes, cut into bite-size pieces
- ½  red onion, finely chopped
- ½ cup finely chopped fresh basil
- ¼ cup balsamic vinegar

## Instructions

1. Combine water and lentils in a large pot.
2. Bring to a boil; reduce heat and simmer gently until lentils are tender but not mushy, about 20 minutes.
3. Drain excess liquid and transfer to a large bowl.
4. Stir cucumber, tomatoes, red onion, and basil into lentils.
5. Dress with balsamic vinegar.
