## Ingredients

- 1 ½ cups finely ground graham cracker crumbs
- ⅓ cup white sugar
- 6 tablespoons butter, melted
- ½ teaspoon ground cinnamon  (Optional)

## Instructions

1. Combine graham cracker crumbs, sugar, melted butter, and cinnamon in a mixing bowl; mix until well blended. Press mixture into the bottom and up the sides of an 8- or 9-inch pie plate.
2. Follow directions for a baked or no-bake crust.
