## Ingredients

- 1 cup canola oil
- 1 (16 ounce) package wonton wrappers
- ½ cup soy sauce
- ¼ cup toasted sesame seeds
- 1 pound frozen ahi tuna, thawed and cut into 1/2-inch chunks
- ½ cup mayonnaise
- 1 tablespoon dill pickle juice
- 1 tablespoon sriracha sauce, or more to taste
- 1  avocado, cut into chunks

## Instructions

1. Heat oil in a deep-fryer or large saucepan to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
2. Cut wonton wrappers into small triangles 2 to 3 inches in length.
3. Cook in the hot oil in a single layer until crispy and golden, 1 minute per layer.
4. Drain on a plate lined with paper towels.
5. Combine soy sauce and sesame seeds in a bowl.
6. Add ahi tuna; mix until coated with soy sauce and sesame seeds.
7. Mix mayonnaise, pickle juice, and sriracha sauce together in a small bowl to make sauce.
8. Arrange fried wonton wrappers on a large serving plate.
9. Layer ahi tuna and avocado chunks on top.
10. Drizzle sauce on top.
