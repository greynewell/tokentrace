## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- 1 pound skinless salmon fillet, cut into 6 - 3/4 inch strips
- coarsely ground black pepper

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven's broiler to 500 degrees; set the oven rack about 6 inches from the heat source.
2. Combine the balsamic vinegar and the garlic in a small bowl; reserve.
3. Roll the salmon strips, beginning on the thickest side, into 6 pinwheels.
4. Secure with wooden skewers.
5. Drizzle the balsamic and garlic mixture generously over each pinwheel.
6. Place salmon under hot broiler and cook for 7 minutes.
7. Turn pinwheels over; cook until edges are brown, and fish is easily flaked with a fork, about 7 additional minutes.
8. Sprinkle with pepper before serving.
