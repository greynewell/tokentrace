## Ingredients

- 24  pickled jalapeno peppers
- 1 pound Cheddar cheese
- ½ cup yellow cornmeal
- ½ cup unbleached all-purpose flour
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 2  eggs, beaten
- 4 cups corn oil for frying

## Instructions

1. Cut a short slit into each jalapeno pepper.
2. Remove as many seeds as possible.
3. Slice Cheddar cheese into approximately 1/4 inch by 1 inch strips.
4. Stuff each jalapeno with a Cheddar cheese strip.
5. Place beaten eggs in a small bowl.
6. In a small dish, mix yellow cornmeal, flour and salt.
7. One at a time, dip jalapenos in the eggs.
8. Roll in the yellow cornmeal mixture until well coated.
9. Arrange on a metal rack, and allow them to sit approximately 30 minutes.
10. Heat corn oil in deep-fryer to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
11. In small batches, using a slotted spoon, lower peppers into the heated oil, and fry until crisp and golden brown, about 4 minutes.
12. Drain on paper towels.
