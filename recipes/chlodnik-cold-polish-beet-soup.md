## Ingredients

- 3  beets with leaves and stems - beets peeled and chopped, leaves sliced
- water to cover
- 1 cup chicken stock
- 1 small cucumber, grated
- 1  hard-cooked egg, chopped
- ⅓ cup chopped fresh dill
- 3 small green onions, thinly sliced
- 1 pint buttermilk
- ½ cup sour cream
- ⅓  lemon, juiced
- ½ teaspoon ground black pepper

## Instructions

1. Cover beets with water in a pot and bring to a boil; cook until tender about 15 minutes.
2. Remove from heat and stir in chicken stock.
3. Allow beets to cool.
4. Mix cucumber, egg, dill, and green onions in a bowl.
5. Whisk buttermilk and sour cream until smooth in another bowl.
6. Stir egg mixture and buttermilk mixture into cooled beets.
7. Cover and refrigerate at least 2 hours before serving.
