## Ingredients

- ½ cup diced zucchini
- ½ cup diced summer squash
- ½ cup diced mushrooms
- ½ cup diced cauliflower
- ½ cup diced asparagus
- ½ cup diced sweet red pepper
- 2 teaspoons vegetable oil
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
- 1/4 teaspoon seasoning, or more to taste

## Instructions

1. Preheat the air fryer to 360 degrees F (180 degrees C).
2. Add zucchini, squash, mushrooms, cauliflower, asparagus, red pepper, oil, salt, pepper, and desired seasoning to a large bowl. Toss to coat; arrange in a single layer in the fryer basket.
3. Cook, stirring halfway, in the preheated air fryer until tender crisp, about 10 minutes.
