## Ingredients

- 2 (6 ounce) fillets salmon, skin removed
- 2 ½ cups orange juice
- 3 sprigs fresh dill weed

## Instructions

1. Place the salmon fillets in a large skillet over medium heat.
2. Pour orange juice over salmon.
3. Arrange dill sprigs around the salmon.
4. Cook , turning occasionally, 15 minutes, or until fish is easily flaked with a fork.
5. Remove salmon from skillet, and set aside.
6. Reduce skillet heat to medium-low, and continue cooking the orange juice 10 minutes, or until thickened.
7. Pour over the salmon to serve.
