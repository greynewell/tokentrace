## Ingredients

- 6  red potatoes, scrubbed and cut into wedges
- 3 tablespoons butter, melted
- 3 tablespoons vegetable oil
- 1 tablespoon chopped fresh rosemary
- salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). Mix together melted butter and oil, then pour into a 9x13-inch baking dish.
2. Place potatoes into the prepared dish; stir until coated. Sprinkle with rosemary, salt, and pepper. Cover with aluminum foil.
3. Bake in the preheated oven until potatoes are tender, about 30 minutes. Stir potatoes occasionally to ensure even cooking.
