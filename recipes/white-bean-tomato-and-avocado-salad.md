## Ingredients

- 1 (14 ounce) can cannellini beans, drained and rinsed
- 1 pint grape tomatoes, halved
- 1 large avocado - peeled, pitted, and cut into chunks
- 4  green onions, thinly sliced
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 2 tablespoons lemon juice
- kosher salt and ground black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Place the beans, tomatoes, avocado, and green onions into a mixing bowl.
2. Drizzle with the olive oil and lemon juice; season with salt and pepper to taste.
3. Gently toss until combined.
4. Serve immediately or chill overnight.
