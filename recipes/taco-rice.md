## Ingredients

- 1 (14.5 ounce) can chicken broth
- 1 (8 ounce) can tomato sauce
- 1 (1 ounce) package taco seasoning mix
- 1 ½ cups uncooked instant rice
- 1 (6 ounce) can black olives, drained and chopped
- 1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
- 1 cup sour cream

## Instructions

1. Place chicken broth, tomato sauce, and taco seasoning into a large pot; bring to a boil.
2. Stir rice into the pot, cover, and remove from heat. Let the pot stand, covered, for 5 minutes.
3. Stir in olives and cheese; mix well. Serve with sour cream.
