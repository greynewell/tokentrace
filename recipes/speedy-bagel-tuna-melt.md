## Ingredients

- 1 (5 ounce) can chunk light tuna, drained
- 2 tablespoons mayonnaise
- 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
- 1  bagel, split and toasted
- ½ cup shredded Cheddar cheese

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 300 degrees F (150 degrees C).
2. Combine the tuna, mayonnaise, and mustard in a medium bowl, using a fork to mash the ingredients together.
3. Top each bagel half with half of the tuna mixture.
4. Sprinkle the tops with shredded Cheddar cheese.
5. Place bagel halves on a baking sheet.
6. Bake the bagel halves in preheated oven until the cheese melts, about 5 minutes.
