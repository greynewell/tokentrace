## Ingredients

- 4 cups water
- 2 cups Japanese sticky rice
- 1 pound skinless, boneless salmon fillets
- water to cover
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 tablespoon tobiko (fish eggs), or to taste  (Optional)
- 1 tablespoon chopped green onions, or to taste

## Instructions

1. Combine 4 cups water and rice in a rice cooker and follow manufacturer's instructions for sticky rice, 20 to 30 minutes.
2. Place salmon in a large saucepan and add enough water to almost cover; season water with salt.
3. Bring water to a simmer over medium heat; spoon hot water over salmon.
4. Cover saucepan and simmer until salmon flakes easily with a fork, about 15 minutes.
5. Remove salmon from saucepan and flake using a fork and spoon.
6. Spoon rice into bowls and top with salmon.
7. Sprinkle tobiko and green onions over the top.
