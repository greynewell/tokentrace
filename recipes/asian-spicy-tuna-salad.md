## Ingredients

- 1 (5 ounce) can solid white tuna packed in water, drained
- 1 teaspoon grated fresh ginger root
- ½ teaspoon diced green chile pepper
- 3 tablespoons finely chopped onion
- ¼ teaspoon curry powder  (Optional)
- ¼ cup mayonnaise
- ½ teaspoon fresh lemon juice

## Instructions

1. With a fork, flake tuna into a small bowl.
2. Mix in ginger, pepper, chopped onion, curry powder, mayonnaise, and lemon juice.
