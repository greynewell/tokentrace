## Ingredients

- 3 cups vegetable oil for frying
- 4 slices white bread
- peanut butter
- 4 teaspoons any flavor fruit jelly
- 1 teaspoon confectioners' sugar, for dusting

## Instructions

1. Heat oil in a deep-fryer or large saucepan to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
2. Cut bread into 3 1/2-inch rounds using a round cutter.
3. Spread peanut butter into the center of 2 slices of bread, leaving 1/2-inch space around the border.
4. Repeat with the jelly for the remaining 2 slices of bread.
5. Place 1 slice jelly bread and 1 slice peanut butter bread together and press down edges to form a seal.
6. Repeat with remaining slices.
7. Place pies in preheated oil and cook until golden and crispy, about 2 minutes per side.
8. Transfer to a plate lined with paper towels.
9. Dust with powdered sugar and serve.
