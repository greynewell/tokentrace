## Ingredients

- ½ cup butter
- 2 pounds frozen medium shrimp
- 2 tablespoons garlic powder, or more to taste
- ¼ cup water
- 2 tablespoons cornstarch
- salt and ground black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Melt butter in a large skillet or wok over medium-low heat. Add shrimp; cook and stir until shrimp turn pink, about 5 minutes. Mix in garlic powder.
2. Whisk water and cornstarch together in a bowl to make a slurry. Pour into the skillet and stir until butter sauce thickens, 3 to 5 minutes. Season with salt and pepper.
