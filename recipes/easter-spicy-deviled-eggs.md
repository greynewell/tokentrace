## Ingredients

- 6  hard-cooked eggs, peeled
- ½ cup whipped chive and onion cream cheese spread
- 1 tablespoon sweet pickle relish
- 2 tablespoons mayonnaise
- ½ teaspoon spicy brown mustard
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- 1 dash hot pepper sauce
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- 2 tablespoons chopped fresh chives

## Instructions

1. Halve eggs lengthwise and scoop yolks into a bowl. Mash yolks with a fork; stir cream cheese spread, mayonnaise, sweet relish, brown mustard, garlic, hot pepper sauce, salt, and black pepper into yolks.
2. Fill egg whites with yolk mixture; sprinkle deviled eggs with chives. Store covered in refrigerator.
