## Ingredients

- 5 small oranges
- 1  ruby red grapefruit
- 1  tangerine
- 1 bulb fennel, thinly sliced
- ¼ cup thinly sliced sweet onion
- 14 leaves fresh pineapple mint
- 1 tablespoon sherry vinegar
- ½ teaspoon salt

## Instructions

1. Slice top and bottom off 1 orange and set on a flat work surface.
2. Cut downwards from the top, following the curve of the fruit, to remove all of the skin and pith.
3. Hold the orange over a bowl to catch juice; cut both sides of each segment away from the connective membrane and transfer to a separate bowl.
4. Repeat with remaining oranges, grapefruit, and tangerine.
5. Gently toss oranges, grapefruit, tangerine, fennel, and onion together.
6. Add mint, vinegar, and salt; toss gently.
