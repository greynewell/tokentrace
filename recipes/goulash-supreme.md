## Ingredients

- 1 pound lean ground beef
- 1 ½ cups macaroni
- 1 quart stewed tomatoes
- 2 teaspoons paprika
- 1 tablespoon chili powder
- ½ cup chopped onion
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- 6 ounces tomato paste  (Optional)

## Instructions

1. In large saucepan brown ground chuck, drain.
2. Add tomatoes, onions, garlic, paprika, chili powder, macaroni and tomato paste, if desired. Add water, a tablespoon at a time, if mixture seems too dry. Simmer until macaroni is tender, about 20 minutes.
