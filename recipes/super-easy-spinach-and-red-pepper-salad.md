## Ingredients

- 1 (6 ounce) package baby spinach
- 1  red bell pepper, chopped
- ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
- ¼ cup olive oil
- ¼ cup rice vinegar

## Instructions

1. Combine baby spinach, red bell pepper, and Parmesan cheese in a large bowl.
2. Whisk olive oil and rice vinegar together in a small bowl; pour over spinach mixture, toss well and serve.
