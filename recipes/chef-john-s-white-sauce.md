## Ingredients

- ½ cup butter
- ½ cup flour
- 1 quart milk
- 4 sprigs fresh thyme, chopped
- ⅛ teaspoon freshly grated nutmeg
- salt
- 1 pinch cayenne pepper, or more to taste

## Instructions

1. Melt butter in a saucepan over low heat.
2. Stir in flour and cook for about 5 minutes.
3. Stir in 1 cup cold milk, about 1 minute.
4. Stir in second cup of milk for an additional minute.
5. Stir in remaining 2 cups of milk, cayenne pepper, salt, thyme, and nutmeg; bring to a simmer over low heat until sauce begins to thicken, about 10 minutes.
