## Ingredients

- 2 (5 ounce) cans tuna, drained
- 1 tablespoon mayonnaise
- 1 teaspoon butter, softened
- 2 cups process cheese food, melted

## Instructions

1. In a medium-sized mixing bowl, combine tuna, mayonnaise, butter and melted cheese.
2. Let dip cool.
3. Stir before serving.
