## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1  onion, cut into wedges
- 2 pounds squid, cleaned and cut into 1/2 inch rings
- ½  fresh pineapple - peeled, cored and chopped
- 4 stalks celery, cut into 2 inch pieces
- 4 tablespoons fish sauce
- 1 teaspoon white sugar
- 1 teaspoon ground black pepper

## Instructions

1. In a large skillet over medium high heat, heat the and the garlic.
2. Fry until garlic is golden brown.
3. Add onion and stir-fry for 1 minute.
4. Add squid and cook until they just turn white (do not overcook).
5. Add the pineapple, celery, fish sauce, sugar and pepper.
6. Stir fry for 2 minutes.
