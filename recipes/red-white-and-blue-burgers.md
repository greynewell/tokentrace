## Ingredients

- 2 pounds ground beef sirloin
- 1  red onion, diced
- ½ (1 ounce) package dry ranch salad dressing mix
- 4 ounces crumbled blue cheese

## Instructions

1. Preheat an outdoor grill for medium-high heat, and lightly oil the grate.
2. Lightly mix together the ground sirloin, onion, ranch salad dressing mix, and blue cheese in a bowl.
3. Form into 6 patties.
4. Grill the burgers for about 5 minutes per side on the prepared grill, until well done.
