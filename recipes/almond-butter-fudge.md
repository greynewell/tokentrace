## Ingredients

- ½ cup coconut oil, melted
- ½ cup cocoa powder
- ½ cup almond butter, room temperature
- ¼ cup raw honey
- ½ teaspoon vanilla extract

## Instructions

1. Line 12 muffin cups with paper liners.
2. Blend coconut oil, cocoa powder, almond butter, honey, vanilla extract together in a blender or food processor until smooth.
3. Spoon mixture into prepared muffin cups.
4. Refrigerate until set, about 30 minutes.
