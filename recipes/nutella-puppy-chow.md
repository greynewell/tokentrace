## Ingredients

- 1 (11 ounce) package milk chocolate chips
- ½ cup chocolate-hazelnut spread (such as Nutella®)
- 8 cups bite-size corn square cereal (such as Corn Chex®)
- 1 ½ cups confectioners' sugar

## Instructions

1. Heat chocolate chips and chocolate-hazelnut spread together in a large pot over low heat until melted, about 5 minutes.
2. Add 1/2 of the cereal to the chocolate mixture and stir until coated.
3. Transfer the coated cereal to a large resealable bag and add 1/2 of the confectioners' sugar; seal bag and shake until cereal is coated.
4. Transfer puppy chow to an airtight container.
5. Repeat with remaining chocolate mixture, cereal, and confectioners' sugar.
