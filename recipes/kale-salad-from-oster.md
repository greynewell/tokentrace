## Ingredients

- 10 cups thinly sliced kale leaves
- 2 medium red or yellow bell peppers, sliced thin
- 2 large carrots, thinly sliced
- 1 cup dry roasted lightly salted peanuts, divided
- ½ cup extra virgin olive oil
- ¼ cup apple cider vinegar
- 1 tablespoon honey
- ½ teaspoon kosher salt
- 1 pinch crushed red pepper flakes

## Instructions

1. Combine kale, bell peppers, carrots and 3/4 cup peanuts in a large bowl.
2. Place remaining ingredients in Oster® Versa® Performance Blender; add remaining peanuts.
3. Blend on HIGH for 1-1/2 to 2 minutes or until combined. (Do not grind peanuts completely.) Pour desired amount of dressing over kale mixture and toss to coat.
4. Serve salad immediately or refrigerate, covered, up to 1 day before serving.
