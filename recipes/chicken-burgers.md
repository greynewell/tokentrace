## Ingredients

- 4 slices whole-grain bread, torn into chunks
- ½ cup quick-cooking oats
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon dry mustard
- 1 teaspoon celery seed
- 1 teaspoon ground cumin
- 1 pound ground chicken
- 1  onion, finely chopped
- 1  egg
- 2 tablespoons canola oil

## Instructions

1. Process bread in a food processor until you have fine bread crumbs.
2. Stir bread crumbs, oats, salt, mustard, celery seed, and cumin together in a bowl.
3. Mix chicken, onion, and egg together in a separate bowl; add bread crumb mixture to chicken mixture and mix.
4. Divide and shape mixture into 7 patties.
5. Heat canola oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
6. Cook patties in hot oil until browned each side and no longer pink the center, about 5 minutes per side.
