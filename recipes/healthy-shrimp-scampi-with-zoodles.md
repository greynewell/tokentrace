## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons olive oil, divided
- 2 cloves garlic, finely chopped, divided
- ½ teaspoon dried parsley
- ¼ teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
- 1 pinch red pepper flakes
- ½ pound uncooked medium shrimp, peeled and deveined
- 2 medium zucchini
- 1 tablespoon butter
- ¼ cup finely diced shallot
- 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
- 2 tablespoons white wine
- 1 teaspoon lemon zest, not packed
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley

## Instructions

1. Mix 1 tablespoon olive oil, 1 garlic clove, dried parsley, black pepper, and red pepper flakes together in a small bowl; mix well.
2. Add shrimp, turn to coat, and refrigerate for 30 minutes.
3. Cut off the ends of the zucchini and use a spiralizer on the thinnest setting to create the zoodles.
4. Heat remaining olive oil and butter in a large skillet over medium-low heat.
5. Saute shallot for 3 minutes; add remaining garlic and cook for an additional 30 seconds.
6. Add shrimp and cook until bright pink on the outside and meat is opaque, about 2 minutes per side.
7. Add lemon juice, wine, lemon zest, and salt.
8. Cook and stir for 1 minute.
9. Remove the skillet from heat and add zucchini; stir for 2 to 3 minutes.
10. The residual heat will cook the zucchini.
11. Divide onto 2 plates, top with fresh parsley, and serve.
