## Ingredients

- ⅔ cup chopped pecans
- ⅓ cup brown sugar
- ⅓ cup all-purpose flour
- ¼ cup butter, cut into small chunks
- 1 teaspoon ground nutmeg
- 1 teaspoon ground cloves
- 1 teaspoon ground cardamom
- 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
- 2 cups chocolate chips
- 1 ½ cups whole wheat flour
- 1 ½ teaspoons ground cloves
- 1 ½ teaspoons baking powder
- ½ teaspoon baking soda
- 1 cup white sugar
- 6 ounces cream cheese, softened
- ½ cup butter, softened
- 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
- 3 medium bananas, mashed
- 2  eggs

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 9x13-inch baking dish.
2. Combine pecans, brown sugar, flour, butter, nutmeg, cloves, cardamom, and cinnamon in a bowl. Blend with a fork until crumbly.
3. Mix chocolate chips, flour, cloves, baking powder, and baking soda in bowl.
4. Combine sugar, cream cheese, butter, and vanilla in a large bowl; beat with an electric mixer until smooth and creamy. Add bananas and eggs; blend until combined. Mix in flour mixture slowly. Beat batter for 1 minute.
5. Pour batter into the prepared pan. Sprinkle pecan topping evenly over the batter.
6. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean, about 35 minutes.
