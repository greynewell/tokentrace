## Ingredients

- 1 cup pecan halves
- 1 cup almonds
- 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
- 2 tablespoons flax seeds
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- ⅛ teaspoon baking powder
- ⅛ teaspoon baking soda
- ¼ cup quick-cooking oats
- 1 cup pitted and quartered dates
- 5 tablespoons maple syrup
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Spread pecans and almonds onto a baking sheet.
3. Bake in the preheated oven until nuts are toasted and fragrant, 7 to 8 minutes.
4. Remove baking sheet from oven and decrease oven temperature to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C).
5. Combine flour, flax seeds, salt, baking powder, and baking soda in a food processor; pulse a few times.
6. Add pecans and almonds to flour mixture; pulse until nuts are coarsely chopped.
7. Add oats and dates to flour-nut mixture; pulse just until combined.
8. Whisk maple syrup and vanilla extract together in a bowl until smooth; pour over oat mixture and pulse just until combined.
9. Spread oat mixture into an 8-inch square pan.
10. Bake in the preheated oven lightly browned, about 25 minutes; cool to room temperature.
