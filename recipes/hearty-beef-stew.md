## Ingredients

- 1 pound cubed beef stew meat
- ¼ cup all-purpose flour
- 1 tablespoon paprika
- salt and pepper to taste
- 2 cups beef broth
- 1 ½ tablespoons teriyaki sauce
- 1  onion, chopped
- 3  carrots, sliced
- 1 stalk celery, sliced
- 2  potatoes, cubed
- ½ pound mushrooms, quartered
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1  bay leaf

## Instructions

1. Place beef stew meat into a slow cooker.
2. In a small bowl, mix together flour, paprika, salt, and pepper; sprinkle over beef stew meat, stirring to coat.
3. Stir in beef broth, teriyaki sauce, onion, carrots, celery, potatoes, mushrooms, garlic and bay leaf.
4. Cover, and cook on Low 6 hours, stirring occasionally.
