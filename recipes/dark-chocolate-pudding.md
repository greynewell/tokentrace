## Ingredients

- 1 cup white sugar, divided
- 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
- 1  egg
- 2 cups milk
- 4 (1 ounce) squares semisweet dark chocolate (such as Baker's®)
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

## Instructions

1. Mix 1/2 cup sugar and flour together in a small bowl.
2. Mix remaining sugar and egg in another bowl. Add to flour mixture and mix together.
3. Heat milk in a heavy saucepan over medium-low heat until warm, about 5 minutes; do not let boil. Stir sugar mixture into milk and cook over medium-high heat until it starts to bubble, about 5 minutes, stirring quickly the entire time to ensure it doesn't burn. Let bubble for at least 1 minute. Remove from heat and stir in chocolate and vanilla extract until smooth.
4. Pour pudding into serving bowls, cover, and refrigerate for 8 hours, to overnight.
