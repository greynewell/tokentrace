## Ingredients

- 1 pound medium purple potatoes, scrubbed
- 2 bunches red radishes, trimmed and diced
- ½  white onion, diced
- ½ cup crumbled blue cheese
- 3 tablespoons mayonnaise

## Instructions

1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil; add potatoes and cook until tender, about 15 minutes.
2. Drain, cool and dice the potatoes.
3. Combine the potatoes, radishes, onion, blue cheese and mayonnaise in a large bowl; mix until the mayonnaise evenly coats the ingredients.
