## Ingredients

- ½ pound fresh green beans, cut into 1-inch lengths
- 2  carrots, cut into thick strips
- ¼ cup butter
- 1  onion, sliced
- ½ pound fresh mushrooms, sliced
- 1 teaspoon salt
- ½ teaspoon seasoned salt
- ¼ teaspoon garlic salt
- ¼ teaspoon white pepper

## Instructions

1. Fill a pot with 1-inch water and bring to a boil over high heat. Place green beans and carrots in boiling water. Cover and cook until tender but still firm. Drain.
2. Melt butter in a large skillet over medium heat. Sauté onions and mushrooms until almost tender. Reduce heat, cover, and simmer 3 minutes.
3. Stir in green beans, carrots, salt, seasoned salt, garlic salt, and white pepper. Cover and cook for 5 minutes over medium heat.
