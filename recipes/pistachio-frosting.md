## Ingredients

- 2 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened
- 1 cup confectioners' sugar
- ½ cup cold milk, or more as needed
- 1 (1 ounce) package instant sugar-free pistachio pudding mix
- 1 cup frozen whipped topping (such as Cool Whip®), thawed

## Instructions

1. Beat cream cheese in a bowl with an electric mixer until smooth and creamy, about 3 minutes.
2. Gradually beat confectioners' sugar into cream cheese.
3. Beat in pudding mix; add milk until desired consistency is reached.
4. Fold in whipped topping.
