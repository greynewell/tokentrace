## Ingredients

- 1 (14.75 ounce) can salmon, drained and bones removed
- 1 (14.5 ounce) can great northern beans, drained and mashed
- ½ cup bread crumbs, or as needed
- 2  eggs
- ¼ teaspoon garlic powder, or to taste
- ¼ teaspoon garlic and herb seasoning blend (such as Mrs. Dash®)
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- 6  hamburger buns
- 1 tablespoon oil, or as needed
- 4 tablespoons mayonnaise, or to taste

## Instructions

1. Mix together salmon, beans, eggs, garlic powder, garlic and herb seasoning blend, salt, and pepper.
2. Form mixture into 6 patties.
3. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
4. Add patties and cook until firm, about 10 minutes per side.
5. Serve on hamburger buns with mayonnaise.
