## Ingredients

- 1 cup all-purpose flour
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- ¼ cup butter, softened
- 5  fresh basil leaves, chopped
- 3 tablespoons olive oil
- 2 tablespoons water
- 1  red onion, chopped
- 1  tomato, chopped
- 8  anchovy fillets, chopped
- 8  pitted green olives, chopped
- 6  fresh mushrooms, chopped
- 4 ounces goat cheese
- salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. In a medium bowl, stir together the flour, salt and basil leaves.
2. Mix in the butter, olive oil and water until it becomes a smooth dough.
3. Cover and refrigerate for 20 minutes.
4. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
5. Butter an 8 inch pie plate.
6. Press the dough into the bottom and up the sides of the dish until it is about 1/4 inch or 1/2 centimeter thick.
7. Bake for about 8 minutes in the preheated oven, until just starting to brown.
8. Make a layer of mushrooms and onion in the bottom of the pie crust.
9. Top with a layer of tomatoes, olives and anchovies.
10. Dot with goat cheese.
11. Return to the preheated oven, and bake for 10 minutes, or until golden brown.
