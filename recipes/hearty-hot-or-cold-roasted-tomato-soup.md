## Ingredients

- 2 pounds Roma (plum) tomatoes, quartered
- 3 tablespoons olive oil
- 4 cloves garlic
- 1 quart chicken stock
- ¼ cup chopped fresh basil
- ½ tablespoon balsamic vinegar
- salt to taste
- ground black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Place the tomato halves, cut side up, on a baking tray with the garlic cloves.
2. Drizzle with the oil, and sprinkle with salt and pepper.
3. Roast at 375 degrees F (195 degrees C) for 1 hour.
4. Snip the ends off the garlic cloves, and squeeze the insides into the bowl of a food processor along with the entire contents of the baking tray.
5. Add stock, basil, and vinegar; blend until smooth.
6. Season to taste.
7. Serve either hot or cold.
