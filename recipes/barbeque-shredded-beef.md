## Ingredients

- 1 cup chopped onion, or more to taste
- 1 (15 ounce) can tomato sauce
- ¼ cup brown sugar
- 2 tablespoons vinegar
- 4 teaspoons chili powder
- 1 teaspoon soy sauce
- 1 teaspoon salt
- ½ teaspoon dry mustard
- 1 ½ pounds boneless beef chuck roast

## Instructions

1. Stir together tomato sauce, onion, brown sugar, vinegar, chili powder, soy sauce, salt, and mustard in a slow cooker; add roast. Spoon onion mixture over roast to coat.
2. Cook on High for 7 hours. Shred roast in the slow cooker with a pair of forks. Continue cooking for 1 hour more.
