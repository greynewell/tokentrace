## Ingredients

- 1  sweet apple, grated
- 2  carrots, grated
- ¼ cup raisins
- 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
- 1  lemon, juiced
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 1 tablespoon toasted sesame seeds  (Optional)
- 1 teaspoon curry powder, or more to taste
- ½ teaspoon maple syrup, or to taste  (Optional)
- salt and ground black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Combine apple, carrots, raisins, and parsley in a bowl.
2. Mix lemon juice, olive oil, sesame seeds, curry powder, maple syrup, salt, and pepper in a jar or container with a tight-fitting lid.
3. Cover jar and shake until dressing is a uniform yellow color.
4. Pour dressing over salad and mix until evenly coated.
