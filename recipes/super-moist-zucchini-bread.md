## Ingredients

- 3 cups grated zucchini
- 3 cups all-purpose flour
- 3 tablespoons ground cinnamon
- 1 teaspoon baking soda
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 cup unsweetened applesauce
- 3 tablespoons vanilla extract
- 3  eggs
- 3 tablespoons butter, melted
- 1 cup chopped walnuts  (Optional)

## Instructions

1. Preheat an oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C).
2. Grease 2 8x4-inch loaf pans or 2 12-cup muffin pans.
3. Place the zucchini in a strainer and set aside to drain into a bowl or sink.
4. Sift the flour, cinnamon, baking soda, baking powder, and salt together in a large bowl.
5. Create a well in the middle of the flour mixture; combine the applesauce, vanilla, eggs, butter, walnuts, and drained zucchini in the center of the well.
6. Mix until all ingredients are combined into a batter; pour into the loaf pans.
7. Bake the loaves in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean, about 1 hour for loaves or 25 minutes for muffins.
8. Cool in the pans for 10 minutes before removing to cool completely on a wire rack.
