## Ingredients

- 1 cup uncooked quinoa
- 2 cups chicken broth
- 1 cup water
- 3  eggs
- 1 tablespoon peanut oil
- 2 tablespoons chili oil
- 5 cloves garlic, pressed or minced
- 1 bunch green onions
- ¾ cup diced pineapple
- 1 tablespoon chopped fresh cilantro
- ¼ cup soy sauce
- ½ teaspoon red pepper flakes, or to taste

## Instructions

1. Rinse quinoa until water runs clear.
2. Bring the quinoa, chicken broth and water to a boil in a saucepan over high heat.
3. Reduce heat to medium-low, cover, and simmer until the quinoa is tender, and the liquid has been absorbed, about 15 to 20 minutes.
4. Beat the eggs in a bowl.
5. Heat a skillet over medium heat.
6. Cook and stir eggs until scrambled.
7. Remove eggs from the skillet and set aside.
8. Heat peanut oil and chili oil over medium-low heat.
9. Cook and stir garlic for 2 minutes, then stir in green onions to heat through.
10. Stir in pineapple and cilantro, then add the cooked quinoa.
11. Toss with scrambled eggs, soy sauce, and red pepper flakes until thoroughly heated.
