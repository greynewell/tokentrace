## Ingredients

- 2 cups elbow macaroni
- 1 ¼ cups mayonnaise
- 5 teaspoons white vinegar
- 1 ¼ cups diced celery
- 1 large tomato, diced
- 5  green onions, finely chopped
- ½ cup shredded Cheddar cheese
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- ⅛ teaspoon ground black pepper
- 1 (16 ounce) package bacon

## Instructions

1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
2. Cook elbow macaroni in the boiling water, stirring occasionally until cooked through but firm to the bite, 8 minutes.
3. Drain and rinse under cold water until chilled.
4. Transfer macaroni to a large salad bowl.
5. Stir mayonnaise, vinegar, celery, tomato, green onions, Cheddar cheese, salt, and black pepper into macaroni until thoroughly combined.
6. Cover and chill salad at least 2 hours.
7. Place bacon in a large skillet and cook over medium-high heat, turning occasionally, until evenly browned, about 10 minutes.
8. Drain the bacon slices on paper towels.
9. Let bacon cool; crumble when cooled.
10. Mix bacon into macaroni salad to serve.
