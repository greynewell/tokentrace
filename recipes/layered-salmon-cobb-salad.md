## Ingredients

- ¾ cup buttermilk
- ¼ cup mayonnaise
- 2 tablespoons white wine vinegar
- ½ clove garlic, crushed
- 2 teaspoons chopped fresh dill
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- 3 cups fresh green beans, trimmed
- 1 pound salmon fillets
- ½ teaspoon vegetable oil
- 4 cups chopped green leaf lettuce
- 1 pound tomatoes, cut into bite-sized pieces
- 1 (15 ounce) can corn, drained
- 2  cucumbers, halved and sliced
- 5  hard-boiled eggs, halved
- 4 slices cooked bacon, cut into thin strips
- 4  spring onions, thinly sliced

## Instructions

1. Combine buttermilk, mayonnaise, white wine vinegar, and garlic in a jar.
2. Seal and shake well.
3. Stir in dill, salt, and pepper.
4. Refrigerate dressing.
5. Place a steamer insert into a saucepan and fill with water to just below the bottom of the steamer.
6. Bring water to a boil.
7. Add green beans, cover, and steam until tender, 4 to 5 minutes.
8. Drain and let cool.
9. Preheat grill for medium heat and lightly oil the grate.
10. Brush salmon with vegetable oil; season with salt and pepper.
11. Cook salmon on the preheated grill until flesh flakes easily with a fork, 6 to 8 minutes per side.
12. Layer lettuce, tomatoes, corn, cucumbers, green beans, and hard-boiled eggs in a large glass trifle bowl.
13. Place salmon on top.
14. Garnish with bacon and spring onions.
15. Serve dressing alongside.
