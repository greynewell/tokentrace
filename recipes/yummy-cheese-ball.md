## Ingredients

- 1 pound bacon
- 2 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened
- 3 tablespoons mayonnaise
- 1  green onion, chopped
- 1 cup chopped pecans

## Instructions

1. In a large skillet fry the bacon.
2. Remove the bacon from the skillet and drain on paper towels.
3. Chop or break the cooled bacon into small pieces.
4. In a medium-size mixing bowl, combine cream cheese, bacon, mayonnaise, and green onions.
5. When mixed well, form 2 balls out of the mixture.
6. Cover and refrigerate 3 to 4 hours, or overnight.
7. Before serving, roll the cheese balls in the chopped pecans.
