## Ingredients

- 1 (8 ounce) package PHILADELPHIA Cream Cheese, softened
- 1 (8 ounce) package KRAFT Shredded Low-Moisture Part-Skim Mozzarella Cheese
- ½ cup finely chopped pepperoni
- ¼ cup finely chopped green peppers
- 1 (16 ounce) package RITZ Crackers

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Combine cheeses in medium bowl; mix well. Gently stir in remaining ingredients.
2. Spread into 9-inch pie plate.
3. Bake 25 to 30 minutes or until top is puffed and lightly browned. Serve warm with crackers.
