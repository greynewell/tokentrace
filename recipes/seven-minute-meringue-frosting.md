## Ingredients

- 2  egg whites
- 1 pinch salt
- 1 ½ cups white sugar
- ⅓ cup water
- ¼ teaspoon cream of tartar
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

## Instructions

1. In the top of a double boiler,  combine all ingredients but the vanilla.
2. Beat 1 minute.
3. Cook over boiling water beating constantly for 7 minutes or until the icing makes firm peaks.
4. Remove from the heat and add the vanilla.
5. Beat until able to spread.
6. NOTE: You can add coloring with the ingredients.
7. You can also use another flavoring in place of the vanilla.
