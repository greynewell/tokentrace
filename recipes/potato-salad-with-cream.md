## Ingredients

- 2 ½ pounds potatoes
- ½ cup vinegar, boiling
- 4  eggs, beaten
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 cup heavy cream
- 4 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
- 2 tablespoons chopped onion
- 2 teaspoons dry mustard
- 1 teaspoon salt
- ½ teaspoon ground black pepper
- 1 pinch ground cayenne pepper

## Instructions

1. Peel the potatoes and add to a large pot of boiling salted water.
2. Cook until tender but still firm, about 15 minutes.
3. Drain, cool and cube.
4. Place into a large bowl and set aside.
5. In a double boiler, bring vinegar to a boil.
6. Gradually beat in eggs and cook over simmering water until thickened.
7. Stir in butter and cook 1 minute.
8. Whisk in cream, parsley, onion, mustard, salt, pepper and cayenne.
9. Pour over hot potatoes and combine.
10. Cool, sprinkle with parsley and refrigerate until serving time.
