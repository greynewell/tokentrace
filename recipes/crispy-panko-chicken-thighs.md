## Ingredients

- cooking spray
- 1 cup mayonnaise
- 1 teaspoon paprika
- ¾ cup panko bread crumbs, or as needed
- 1 teaspoon garlic powder
- 4  bone-in chicken thighs

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). Line a baking pan with aluminum foil; coat with cooking spray.
2. Combine mayonnaise and paprika in a bowl; mix well.
3. Mix panko and garlic powder together in another bowl.
4. Spread mayonnaise-paprika mixture on each chicken thigh; press into panko mixture to coat. Place in the prepared baking pan; spritz thighs with cooking spray.
5. Bake in the preheated oven until no longer pink at the bone and the juices run clear, about 1 hour. An instant-read thermometer inserted near the bone should read 165 degrees F (74 degrees C).
