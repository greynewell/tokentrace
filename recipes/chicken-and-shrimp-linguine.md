## Ingredients

- 1 pound linguini pasta
- ¼ teaspoon ground nutmeg
- 1 pinch cayenne pepper
- ½ teaspoon onion powder
- ½ teaspoon ground black pepper
- 4 tablespoons butter
- ½ cup milk
- 1 cup heavy cream
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 2 cloves garlic, chopped
- 4  skinless, boneless chicken breast halves - cut into cubes
- ¾ cup white wine
- 1 pound medium shrimp - peeled and deveined

## Instructions

1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
2. Add linguini pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.
3. In a small bowl, combine nutmeg, cayenne pepper, onion powder and pepper; set aside.
4. In a medium saucepan over medium heat, melt butter and add 1/2 of the spice mixture, milk and cream.
5. Heat until sauce thickens; set aside.
6. In a large skillet over medium heat, warm olive oil and add garlic, chicken, wine and remaining spice; cook until chicken is no longer pink.
7. Remove chicken from pan and cook shrimp.
8. Deglaze pan by adding some wine and heating it for 2 to 3 minutes; add the cream sauce and reheat if needed.
9. Pour sauce over hot linguine and add chicken mixture and shrimp.
