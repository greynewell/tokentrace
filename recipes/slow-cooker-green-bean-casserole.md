## Ingredients

- 2 (16 ounce) packages frozen cut green beans
- 2 (10.75 ounce) cans  cream of chicken soup
- ⅔ cup milk
- ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
- 1 (6 ounce) can French-fried onions, divided

## Instructions

1. Combine green beans, condensed soup, milk, Parmesan cheese, salt, black pepper, and 1/2 of the can of French-fried onions in a slow cooker.
2. Cover and cook on Low for 5 to 6 hours. Top casserole with remaining French-fried onions to serve.
