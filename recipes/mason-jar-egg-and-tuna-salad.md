## Ingredients

- 6  hard-cooked eggs, chopped
- ⅔ cup mayonnaise
- ½ teaspoon seasoned salt
- ½ cup chopped pickles
- 1 (5 ounce) can tuna packed in water, drained
- 2 cups arugula
- 4  1-pint canning jars with lids and rings

## Instructions

1. Mix eggs, mayonnaise, and seasoned salt together in a bowl; spoon evenly among 4 pint jars.
2. Layer pickles, tuna, and arugula on top of mayonnaise mixture.
3. Cover tightly with lid and refrigerate.
