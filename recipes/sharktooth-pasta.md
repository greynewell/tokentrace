## Ingredients

- 8 ounces dry farfalle (bow tie) pasta
- 1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of chicken soup
- ¼ cup milk
- 2 tablespoons shredded Cheddar cheese

## Instructions

1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
2. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.
3. Return pasta to pot; stir in soup and milk.
4. Cook and stir over medium heat until heated through.
5. Add cheese; cook and stir just until cheese is melted.
6. Serve hot.
