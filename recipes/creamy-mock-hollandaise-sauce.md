## Ingredients

- 3 ounces cream cheese, softened
- 2  egg yolks
- 2 tablespoons lemon juice
- ¼ teaspoon fine salt
- ½ cup milk, or as needed

## Instructions

1. Mix cream cheese and egg yolks together in a bowl with an electric mixer. Stir lemon juice and salt together in a separate small bowl.
2. Transfer cream cheese mixture to saucepan over low heat; whisk in lemon juice mixture. Increase heat to medium-low; bring cream cheese mixture to a boil, whisking constantly, about 3 minutes. Whisk milk in gradually until desired consistency is reached. Continue to cook, whisking constantly, until smooth sauce forms, 1 to 2 minutes.
