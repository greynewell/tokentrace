## Ingredients

- 1  egg
- ½ cup milk
- ½ cup all-purpose flour
- ¼ teaspoon ground cinnamon
- ⅛ teaspoon baking soda
- 2 tablespoons butter, melted
- ⅓ cup cinnamon sugar

## Instructions

1. Preheat an oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
2. Grease a mini muffin pan.
3. Whisk together the egg and milk in a bowl until evenly blended; set aside.
4. Stir together the flour, cinnamon, and baking soda in a separate large bowl.
5. Pour the egg mixture into the the flour mixture and whisk until smooth; being careful not to overbeat.
6. Fill muffin cups 1/2 full.
7. Bake in preheated oven for 20 minutes.
8. Do not open the oven door.
9. Place cinnamon sugar in a brown bag for shaking and set aside.
10. Remove popovers from oven and let cool in pan for 5 minutes.
11. Roll popovers in melted butter then place in brown paper bag; shake until the popovers are evenly coated.
12. Serve immediately.
