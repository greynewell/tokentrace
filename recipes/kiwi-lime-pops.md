## Ingredients

- 6  kiwi fruits, divided
- ½ cup white sugar
- ½ cup fresh lime juice

## Instructions

1. Cut 5 kiwi in half crosswise and scoop flesh from skins with a spoon.
2. Blend with sugar and lime juice in a blender until pureed, about 15 seconds.
3. Transfer to a measuring cup with a spout and pour into 6 (3-ounce) flat ice pop molds, dividing evenly.
4. Peel remaining kiwi and thinly slice crosswise into 12 slices.
5. Submerge 2 slices in each mold, arranging them so 1 slice presses against each of the mold's wider sides.
6. Cover molds, insert sticks between the slices, and freeze until solid, at least 4 hours and up to 2 weeks.
