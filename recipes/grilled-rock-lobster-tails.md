## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon lemon juice
- ½ cup olive oil
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon paprika
- ⅛ teaspoon white pepper
- ⅛ teaspoon garlic powder
- 2 (10 ounce) rock lobster tails

## Instructions

1. Preheat the grill for high heat and lightly oil the grates.
2. Pour lemon juice into a small bowl; slowly whisk in olive oil. Whisk in salt, paprika, white pepper, and garlic powder until combined.
3. Split lobster tails lengthwise with a large knife, then brush flesh with some marinade.
4. Place lobster tails flesh-side down on the preheated grill and cook, turning once and basting frequently with marinade, until opaque and firm to the touch, 10 to 12 minutes. Discard any remaining marinade.
