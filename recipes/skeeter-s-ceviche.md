## Ingredients

- 1 pound peeled and deveined medium shrimp (30-40 per pound)
- 1 pound bay scallops
- 1 bunch cilantro leaves, chopped
- 3  roma (plum) tomatoes, diced
- 2 stalks celery, diced
- 5  green onions, sliced
- 6  limes, juiced
- 2 small serrano peppers, seeded and minced
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- ⅛ teaspoon white pepper
- ⅛ teaspoon ground black pepper

## Instructions

1. Combine the shrimp, scallops, cilantro, tomatoes, celery, green onions, lime juice, and serrano peppers in a bowl.
2. Season with salt, white pepper, and black pepper; stir and pour into a glass or plastic container.
3. Cover, and refrigerate 3 hours to overnight.
4. Serve the seafood with the lime juice marinade.
