## Ingredients

- 1 pound ground beef
- 1 cup ketchup (such as Heinz®)
- ½ (12 ounce) bottle chili sauce (such as Heinz®)
- ¼ cup chopped onion
- ¼ cup chopped green bell pepper
- ¼ cup brown sugar
- ½ teaspoon mustard powder
- hamburger buns

## Instructions

1. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat. Cook and stir beef in the hot skillet until browned and crumbly, 5 to 7 minutes; drain and discard grease.
2. Mix ketchup, chili sauce, onion, green bell pepper, brown sugar, and mustard powder into ground beef; bring to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer until flavors have blended, about 15 minutes; serve on hamburger buns.
