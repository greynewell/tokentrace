## Ingredients

- 4 ounces thinly sliced prosciutto
- 8  dried figs, or as desired
- 3 ounces goat cheese

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Lay prosciutto onto a work surface.
3. Place 1 fig on the end of each prosciutto slice.
4. Spread a thin layer goat cheese onto each fig.
5. Roll prosciutto around each fig; arrange on a baking sheet.
6. Bake in the preheated oven until prosciutto is crisp, 3 to 4 minutes.
