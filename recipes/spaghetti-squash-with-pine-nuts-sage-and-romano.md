## Ingredients

- 1  spaghetti squash, halved lengthwise and seeded
- ¼ cup toasted pine nuts
- ¼ cup grated Pecorino Romano cheese
- 2 tablespoons chopped fresh sage
- 2 teaspoons butter, melted
- salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Place the squash, cut side down, in a large baking dish.
3. Bake the squash in the preheated oven for 50 minutes.
4. Scrape flesh of squash from the rind using a fork and place in a bowl.
5. Add the pine nuts, cheese, sage, butter, salt, and pepper; toss to combine.
6. Serve immediately.
