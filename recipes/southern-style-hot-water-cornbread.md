## Ingredients

- ⅔ cup yellow cornmeal
- ⅓ cup buttermilk
- ⅓ cup self-rising flour
- 1  egg
- ½ teaspoon salt
- 3 tablespoons olive oil

## Instructions

1. Mix yellow cornmeal, buttermilk, flour, egg, and salt together in a large bowl.
2. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
3. Drop spoonfuls of cornmeal mixture into hot oil; cook until browned and crisp, 2 to 3 minutes per side.
4. Drain on paper towels.
