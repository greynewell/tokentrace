## Ingredients

- 3 tablespoons vegetable oil
- ½  onion, thinly sliced lengthwise
- 1 cup chopped carrots
- 1 teaspoon minced garlic
- ¼ teaspoon ground turmeric  (Optional)
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- ⅔ head cabbage, diced and rinsed
- 2 tablespoons water, or as needed

## Instructions

1. Heat oil in a pot over medium heat. Cook onion in hot oil until translucent, about 5 minutes. Stir in carrots, garlic, turmeric, salt, and black pepper. Cook and stir until flavors are combined, about 7 minutes. Add cabbage and mix well.
2. Cover the pot, reduce heat to medium-low, and cook until cabbage is tender but not completely soft, about 20 minutes. Add water if vegetables look dry.
