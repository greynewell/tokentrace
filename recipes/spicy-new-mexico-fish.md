## Ingredients

- 1 (14.5 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
- ½ cup diced hot Hatch green chiles
- 1 teaspoon white sugar
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- ½  jalapeno pepper, minced, or to taste  (Optional)
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ½ teaspoon ground black pepper
- 2  halibut steaks
- 2 cups sliced zucchini

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
2. Combine tomatoes, green chiles, sugar, garlic, jalapeno, salt, and black pepper in a saucepan over medium heat; bring to a boil.
3. Reduce heat to medium-low; add zucchini.
4. Simmer until sauce is combined, 5 to 10 minutes.
5. Place halibut steaks in a shallow casserole dish; pour sauce on top.
6. Bake, uncovered, in the preheated oven until fish flakes easily with a fork, about 30 minutes.
