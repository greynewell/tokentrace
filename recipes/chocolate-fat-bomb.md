## Ingredients

- 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
- ¾ cup coconut oil, or to taste
- 1 (1.4 ounce) package instant sugar-free chocolate pudding mix

## Instructions

1. Blend cream cheese, coconut oil, and chocolate pudding mix together in a bowl using an electric mixer until smooth.
2. Scoop into mounds or place in a mold to shape.
3. Cover with plastic wrap and refrigerate until hardened, about 30 minutes.
