## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 tablespoon minced garlic
- 2 cups chicken broth
- 1 cup quinoa

## Instructions

1. Melt butter in a saucepan over medium heat. Cook and stir garlic in melted butter until just browned, about 5 minutes.
2. Pour chicken broth into the saucepan; add quinoa and stir. Bring the mixture to a boil, reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer until liquid is absorbed, about 15 minutes.
3. Remove saucepan from heat and rest mixture 5 minutes before fluffing with a fork.
