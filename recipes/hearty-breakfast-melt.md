## Ingredients

- 3 tablespoons butter, divided, or as needed
- 1  frozen hash brown potato square
- 2 slices onion
- 2 slices white bread
- 1 slice Cheddar cheese
- 1  egg
- salt and ground black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Melt 1 tablespoon butter in a skillet over medium heat.
2. Fry hash brown square and onion slices in butter until hash brown is cooked through and onion is softened, flipping hash brown halfway, 7 to 10 minutes.
3. Toast bread in a toaster; butter both sides with 1 tablespoon butter and place on a plate.
4. Place cooked hash brown and onions on 1 slice of toast.
5. Top with Cheddar cheese.
6. Heat remaining tablespoon butter in a skillet over medium heat.
7. Fry egg until white is nearly set and yolk is still runny, about 2 minutes.
8. Gently flip and cook for an additional 15 to 30 seconds.
9. Season with salt and pepper.
10. Place over-easy egg on top of Cheddar cheese.
11. Top with remaining slice of toast to form a sandwich.
12. Slice to allow yolk to penetrate sandwich and serve.
