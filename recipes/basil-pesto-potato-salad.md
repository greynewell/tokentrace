## Ingredients

- 10 small red potatoes, scrubbed
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ¼ cup dry white wine
- 1 ½ tablespoons olive oil
- 1 ½ teaspoons lemon juice
- ½ cup mayonnaise
- 6 tablespoons pesto, or more to taste
- 1 small red onion, finely sliced, or to taste

## Instructions

1. Place potatoes into a large pot and cover with lightly salted water; bring to a boil.
2. Reduce heat to medium-low and simmer until just tender, about 10 minutes.
3. Drain and cool until easily handled, 5 to 10 minutes.
4. Peel potatoes and cut into 1/4-inch thick slices.
5. Place in a large bowl.
6. Sprinkle with salt.
7. Add white wine, olive oil, and lemon juice; toss lightly to combine.
8. Mix mayonnaise and pesto together in a small bowl.
9. Pour over potato salad and toss gently.
10. Garnish with red onion.
11. Chill until serving.
