## Ingredients

- 30  chocolate sandwich cookies (such as Oreo®), crushed
- 1 (14 ounce) can sweetened condensed milk
- 1 cup semisweet chocolate chips
- ¾ cup all-purpose flour
- ¼ cup butter, softened
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 1 cup sweetened flaked coconut  (Optional)
- 2 tablespoons powdered sugar, or more as needed

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). Grease an 8x8-inch baking pan.
2. Mix cookies, sweetened condensed milk, chocolate chips, flour, butter, and vanilla together in a large bowl. Fold in coconut. Press mixture into the prepared baking pan.
3. Bake in the preheated oven until lightly browned, 25 to 30 minutes. Dust with powdered sugar.
