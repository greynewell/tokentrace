## Ingredients

- 2 cups cake flour
- 1 ¼ cups white sugar
- 3 teaspoons baking powder
- ¾ teaspoon salt
- 3  egg whites
- 1 cup milk
- 1 teaspoon lemon extract
- ½ cup shortening

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
2. Lightly grease and flour two 8 inch round cake pans.
3. Sift together; the flour, 1 cup of the sugar, the baking powder, and  the salt.
4. Beat the egg whites until stiff but still moist.
5. Gradually beat in 1/4 cup sugar until soft peaks are formed.
6. Add the milk, lemon extract, and shortening to the flour mixture, beat for 2 minutes, scraping bowl while beating.
7. Fold in the egg whites and pour batter into prepared pans.
8. Bake at 375 degrees F (190 degrees C) for about 25 minutes.
9. Remove from pans and let cool.
10. Fill with lemon filling and sprinkle confectioners sugar over the top or frost if desired.
