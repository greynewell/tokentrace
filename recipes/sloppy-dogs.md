## Ingredients

- 1 pound ground beef
- 8  hot dogs
- 8  hot dog buns
- 1 (15 ounce) can sloppy joe sauce

## Instructions

1. Crumble the ground beef into a large skillet over medium-high heat.
2. Cook and stir until evenly browned.
3. Drain off grease, and reduce the heat to medium.
4. Stir in the sloppy joe sauce, and bring to a simmer.
5. Add the hot dogs, and cook until heated through.
6. Place hot dogs into buns and cover with the sloppy joe mixture.
7. Eat with a fork, or use just a little bit of the sauce and eat like a regular hot dog.
