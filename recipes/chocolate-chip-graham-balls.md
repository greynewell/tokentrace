## Ingredients

- 1 cup finely crushed graham crackers
- 1 cup chocolate chips
- ⅓ cup sweetened condensed milk
- ½ cup chopped nuts

## Instructions

1. Combine graham cracker crumbs, chocolate chips, and sweetened condensed milk in a mixing bowl. Pour chopped nuts into a shallow bowl. Form graham cracker mixture into 1-inch balls and roll in nuts until coated.
2. Chill graham balls until set, about 1 hour.
