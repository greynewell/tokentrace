## Ingredients

- 1  lemon, halved
- 5 tablespoons olive oil
- 12 cloves garlic, chopped
- ¼ cup chopped fresh thyme
- ¼ cup chopped fresh rosemary
- 1 tablespoon red pepper flakes
- 4  whole trout - cleaned, rinsed, patted dry
- 1 pinch salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Cut 1/2 of the lemon first into quarters, then into thin slices.
2. Cut the other 1/2 into wedges; reserve for garnish.
3. Combine lemon slices, olive oil, garlic, thyme, rosemary, and red pepper flakes in a bowl.
4. Rub trout generously inside and out with salt and pepper.
5. Stuff 1/4 of the lemon-herb mixture into each trout cavity.
6. Marinate for 1 hour in the refrigerator.
7. Preheat an outdoor grill for medium-high heat and lightly oil the grate.
8. Grill trout until flesh flakes easily with a fork, about 4 minutes per side.
9. Garnish with lemon wedges.
