## Ingredients

- ½ cup uncooked quinoa
- 1 cup water
- ¼ cup chopped celery
- ¼ cup chopped onion
- ¼ cup low fat mayonnaise (such as Hellman's® Light)
- 1 teaspoon wasabi powder
- 2 teaspoons lemon juice, or to taste
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- 1 (5 ounce) can tuna, drained

## Instructions

1. Stir the quinoa and water together in a microwave-safe bowl.
2. Cover; cook in the microwave on High until the water has been absorbed and the quinoa is tender, about 9 minutes.
3. Uncover and place into the refrigerator to cool.
4. Place the celery, onion, and mayonnaise together in a bowl.
5. Season with wasabi, lemon juice, salt, and pepper; stir to mix.
6. Fold in the tuna and chilled quinoa until evenly blended.
