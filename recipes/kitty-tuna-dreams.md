## Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon vegetable oil, or more as needed
- 2 cups canned low-sodium tuna in oil
- 4 cups bread crumbs
- 4  eggs, beaten
- 2 teaspoons brewers' yeast  (Optional)

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Lightly grease a baking sheet with vegetable oil.
3. Drain tuna and reserve oil; add enough vegetable oil to make 1/4 cup total.
4. Combine tuna, reserved oil, bread crumbs, eggs, and brewer's yeast in a bowl.
5. Drop 1/4 teaspoonfuls of tuna mixture 1 inch apart onto the prepared baking sheet.
6. Bake in the preheated oven until set, about 10 minutes.
7. Cool biscuits and store covered in the refrigerator.
