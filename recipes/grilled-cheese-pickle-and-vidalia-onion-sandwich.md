## Ingredients

- 3 tablespoons softened butter
- 2 slices whole wheat bread
- 2 slices sharp Cheddar cheese
- 1  dill pickle, sliced
- 2 thin slices  Vidalia or other sweet onion

## Instructions

1. Butter one side of each slice of bread with 1 tablespoon of butter.
2. Melt the remaining tablespoon of butter in a skillet over medium  heat.
3. Place one bread slice butter-side-down into the skillet, and top with the Cheddar cheese, pickle slices, and onion.
4. Place the remaining slice of bread butter-side-up onto the sandwich.
5. Cook until crispy and golden brown on both sides, 5 to 7 minutes.
