## Ingredients

- 1 cup mashed potatoes
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 2 tablespoons melted butter
- 2 teaspoons peppermint extract
- 8 cups confectioners' sugar
- 8 (1 ounce) squares semisweet chocolate
- 2 tablespoons shortening

## Instructions

1. In a large bowl, mix together the potatoes, salt, butter, and peppermint extract.
2. Gradually mix in confectioners' sugar; mix in enough to make a workable dough, between 6 and 8 cups.
3. Knead slightly, and roll into cherry-size balls.
4. Flatten balls to form patties.
5. Arrange on sheets of wax paper, and allow to dry overnight.
6. Place chocolate and shortening in a microwave-safe bowl.
7. Heat in microwave, stirring occasionally, until melted and smooth.
8. Dip patties in melted chocolate, and let cool on wax paper.
