## Ingredients

- 6 tablespoons vegetable oil
- 2 tablespoons vinegar
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ½ teaspoon ground black pepper
- 2  cucumbers, peeled and sliced
- ¾ cup mayonnaise
- 3 tablespoons heavy whipping cream
- 3 tablespoons minced fresh onion
- 2 tablespoons sour cream
- 1 teaspoon lemon juice
- ½ teaspoon dried basil
- salt and ground black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Whisk vegetable oil, vinegar, 1/2 teaspoon salt, and 1/2 teaspoon pepper together in a large bowl until the oil emulsifies.
2. Fold the cucumbers into the marinade until evenly coated.
3. Marinate cucumbers in refrigerator for 2 hours.
4. Drain cucumbers and discard marinade.
5. Whisk mayonnaise, cream, onion, sour cream, lemon juice, and basil together in a bowl.
6. Fold drained cucumbers into mayonnaise mixture; season with salt and pepper to serve.
