## Ingredients

- 1 ¾ cups all-purpose flour
- ¾ cup white sugar
- ¼ teaspoon baking powder
- ¾ cup butter, softened
- 1  egg
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- ½ teaspoon lavender flowers, or to taste

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Line a baking sheet with waxed paper and grease the paper.
2. Whisk flour, sugar, and baking powder in a bowl. Stir butter and egg into flour mixture until smooth. Add vanilla extract and lavender; gently mix until thoroughly combined.
3. Drop cookie dough by tablespoon about 2 inches apart onto the prepared baking sheet.
4. Bake in the preheated oven until cookies are fragrant and edges turn light golden brown, 10 to 15 minutes.
