## Ingredients

- 1 bunch fresh asparagus spears, trimmed
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 1 tablespoon dried Herbes de Provence
- sea salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
2. Line a baking sheet with aluminum foil.
3. Toss the asparagus with olive oil, Herbes de Provence, salt, and pepper.
4. Spread the asparagus onto the baking sheet in a single layer.
5. Roast in preheated oven until tender and lightly browned, about 12 minutes.
