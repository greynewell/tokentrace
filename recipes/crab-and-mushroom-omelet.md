## Ingredients

- cooking spray
- ½ cup chopped fresh tomatoes
- ½ cup chopped fresh mushrooms
- 6  eggs, beaten
- 1 (6 ounce) can crabmeat, drained
- 1 ½ teaspoons Creole seasoning (such as Tony Chachere's®)

## Instructions

1. Heat a nonstick skillet over medium heat; coat with cooking spray.
2. Add tomatoes and mushrooms; cook and stir for 2 minutes.
3. Add eggs, crabmeat, and Creole seasoning; cook until eggs are firm, 5 to 7 minutes.
