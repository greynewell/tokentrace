## Ingredients

- 2 ounces whole-wheat angel hair pasta
- 1 teaspoon extra-virgin olive oil
- 1  onion, chopped
- 1 teaspoon curry powder
- ¼ cup fat free, low-sodium chicken broth
- ¼ cup evaporated skim milk
- 2 teaspoons low-sodium tomato paste
- 10 ounces fresh scallops
- ¼ cup fat free, low-sodium chicken broth

## Instructions

1. Bring a pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
2. Cook angel hair in the boiling water until cooked through yet firm to the bite, 4 to 5 minutes; drain.
3. Divide between 2 plates and keep warm.
4. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat.
5. Cook and stir onion in the hot oil until softened, about 3 minutes.
6. Stir curry powder into the onion; cook and stir 1 minute more.
7. Transfer the onion mixture to a blender; add 1/4 cup chicken broth along with evaporated milk and tomato paste.
8. Return the skillet to medium heat.
9. Sear scallops on each side in the hot skillet, 2 to 3 minutes per side.
10. Place scallops atop the angel hair pasta; keep warm.
11. Pour 1/4 cup chicken broth into the pan; bring to a boil while scraping the browned bits of food off of the bottom of the pan with a wooden spoon.
12. Pour the contents of the skillet into the blender; blend until smooth and return the mixture to the skillet.
13. Simmer the sauce until warm; pour over the scallops and pasta to serve.
