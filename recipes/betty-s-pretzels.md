## Ingredients

- 24 ounces bite-size pretzels
- 1 (1 ounce) package dry onion soup mix
- 1 cup butter, melted

## Instructions

1. Preheat an oven to 275 degrees F (135 degrees C).
2. Combine the melted butter and onion soup mix in a bowl; stir.
3. Add the pretzel pieces; toss to coat.
4. Spread the coated pretzels into the bottom of a large baking dish.
5. Bake the pretzels in the preheated oven for 1 hour, stirring every 15 minutes.
6. Allow pretzels to cool completely before storing in an airtight container.
