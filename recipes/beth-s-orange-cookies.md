## Ingredients

- 2 cups white sugar
- 1 cup shortening
- 2  eggs
- 1 cup sour cream
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 5 cups all-purpose flour
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 1 teaspoon baking soda
- 1 teaspoon salt
- ¾ cup frozen orange juice concentrate, thawed
- 2 tablespoons grated orange zest
- ½ teaspoon grated orange zest
- 2 tablespoons frozen orange juice concentrate, thawed
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 4 tablespoons butter, melted
- 1 ½ cups confectioners' sugar

## Instructions

1. Cream white sugar and shortening; add eggs and stir.
2. Add sour cream and 1 teaspoon vanilla; stir and set aside.
3. Sift together flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
4. Add little by little to the creamed mixture and stir well.
5. Add 3/4 cup orange juice concentrate, and 2 tablespoons orange zest.
6. Drop onto greased cookie sheets by teaspoonfuls.
7. Bake at 375 degrees F (190 degrees C) for about 10 minutes.
8. Frost cookies when they are cool.
9. To Make Frosting:  Mix together 1/2 teaspoon orange zest, 2 Tablespoons of the thawed orange juice concentrate, 1 teaspoon vanilla, 4 Tablespoons of melted butter, 1 1/2 cups confectioners'  sugar or enough to make frosting of spreading consistency.
