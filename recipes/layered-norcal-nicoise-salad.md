## Ingredients

- 4 ounces tender, thin green beans
- 1  Yukon Gold potato, peeled and cut into 1/2-inch dice
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 3 ounces oil-packed tuna, drained
- ½ cup cherry tomato halves
- ½ cup pitted nicoise olives
- 1  hard-boiled egg, chopped
- 1  anchovy fillet  (Optional)
- 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley
- 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
- 3  anchovy fillets  (Optional)
- 3 tablespoons white wine vinegar
- 2 tablespoons lemon juice
- 2 teaspoons minced shallots
- 1 teaspoon minced fresh tarragon
- ¼ cup ripe avocado
- ⅓ cup olive oil, or more to taste
- 1 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
- 1 pinch cayenne pepper

## Instructions

1. Bring a pot of salted water to a boil over medium-high heat.
2. Add green beans and cook for just 2 minutes.
3. Transfer to a bowl of ice water using a slotted spoon.
4. Transfer potato cubes to the same pot; simmer over medium heat until tender, but not soft, 5 to 8 minutes.
5. Remove potatoes with a slotted spoon and drain on a paper towel-lined plate.
6. Slide out paper towel and drizzle warm potatoes with about 1 tablespoon olive oil.
7. Drain green beans.
8. Cut into 1/2-inch to 3/4-inch pieces.
9. Place the potatoes in a 1-quart canning jar; even out the layer.
10. Add in this order a layer of chopped green beans, tuna, tomatoes, olives, eggs, and anchovy fillet, evening out layers as you go.
11. Top with chopped parsley.
12. Leave about 1/2 inch space at the top.
13. Close with lid and refrigerate while you make the dressing.
14. Place mustard and anchovies in a mixing bowl.
15. Add vinegar, lemon juice, shallots, tarragon, and avocado, olive oil, black pepper, and cayenne pepper.
16. Blend with an immersion blender until thoroughly emulsified, 1 or 2 minutes.
17. If dressing seems too thick, stir in 1 or 2 teaspoons water or lemon juice.
18. Pour about 1/4 cup dressing into salad jar, or more to taste.
19. Toss with a fork.
20. Or you can shake the jar and transfer dressed salad to a bowl.
