## Ingredients

- 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
- ½ cup cold butter, chopped
- 1  egg yolk
- 3 teaspoons ice-cold water, or as needed

## Instructions

1. Sift flour into a bowl and rub in butter. Add egg yolk and enough water to make ingredients cling together.
2. Press dough into a ball and knead gently on a floured surface until smooth. Wrap dough in plastic wrap and refrigerate for 30 minutes.
3. Use pastry for tarts, pastries, quiches, or other savory goodies.
