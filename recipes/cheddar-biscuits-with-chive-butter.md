## Ingredients

- ¼ cup butter, softened
- 3 tablespoons chopped fresh chives
- ¼ teaspoon cayenne pepper
- 2 cups flour
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- ½ teaspoon baking soda
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- 6 tablespoons cold butter
- 1 ¼ cups shredded extra-sharp Cheddar cheese
- ¼ cup grated Parmesan cheese
- 1 cup buttermilk
- 1  egg, lightly beaten

## Instructions

1. Beat softened butter, chives, and cayenne pepper together in a bowl until well-combined and smooth.
2. Preheat oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C).
3. Stir flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt together in a bowl.
4. Cut cold butter into flour mixture with a pastry blender or two knives until mixture resembles oatmeal.
5. Add Cheddar cheese and Parmesan cheese; toss to coat.
6. Stir buttermilk into flour mixture until dough is just-moistened.
7. Turn dough out onto a floured work surface and gently knead until dough comes together, about 6 to 8 times.
8. Roll dough into a 9-inch square (1/2 inch thick) with a floured rolling pin.
9. Cut dough lengthwise into 4 strips and crosswise into 6 strips.
10. Place biscuits on a baking sheet and brush tops with beaten egg.
11. Bake biscuits in the preheated oven until golden, about 15 minutes.
12. Transfer to a rack and cool until warm, at least 15 minutes.
13. Serve with chive butter.
