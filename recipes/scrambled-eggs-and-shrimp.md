## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon vegetable oil, or as needed
- 1  onion, chopped
- 6  eggs, beaten
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 10  cooked large shrimp
- ¼ cup cocktail sauce

## Instructions

1. Heat oil in a skillet over medium heat; saute onion until translucent, 5 to 10 minutes.
2. Add eggs to onion and season with salt.
3. Cook and stir until eggs are set, about 5 minutes.
4. Stir shrimp and cocktail sauce into eggs; cook until warm, 3 to 4 minutes.
