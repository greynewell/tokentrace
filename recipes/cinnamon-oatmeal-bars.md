## Ingredients

- 1 cup MUSSELMAN'S® Apple Butter
- 1 cup flour
- 1 cup old-fashioned rolled oats
- ¾ cup light brown sugar
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- 8 tablespoons butter, melted

## Instructions

1. Pre-heat oven to 350 degrees F.
2. Coat an 8x8-inch baking pan with vegetable cooking spray.
3. Line with heavy-duty foil, leaving an overhang on two sides to facilitate removal of bars from pan.
4. Coat foil with cooking spray.
5. Mix flour, oatmeal, sugar, and salt in a medium bowl.
6. Stir in butter with a fork until well mixed and clumps form, and then spread half the oatmeal mixture over foiled pan bottom, pressing to form a thin crust.
7. Spread apple butter over crust and then sprinkle remaining oatmeal mixture on top.
8. Bake until crisp and golden brown, 30-40 min.
9. Cool to room temperature.
10. Use foil handles to remove bars from pan.
11. Cut into squares and serve.
