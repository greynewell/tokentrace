## Ingredients

- 1 (21 ounce) can cherry pie filling
- 1 (15.25 ounce) can fruit cocktail, drained
- 3  bananas, sliced

## Instructions

1. In a large bowl, mix together the cherry pie filling, fruit cocktail and sliced bananas.
2. Chill before serving.
