## Ingredients

- 4 (3 ounce) fillets cod fillets
- 2 teaspoons smoked paprika, divided
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- 2  limes, halved
- 1 cup high-protein crisp rice and wheat cereal (such as Kellogg's® Special K®), crushed
- ¼ cup vegetable oil for frying, or as needed

## Instructions

1. Season each cod fillet with 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika, salt, and pepper.
2. Squeeze one lime half over each fillet.
3. Spread the crushed cereal into a wide, shallow bowl.
4. Gently press cod fillets into the cereal to coat and shake gently to remove loose, excess crumbs.
5. Place breaded fillets onto a plate while breading the rest; do not stack.
6. Heat oil in a skillet over medium-high heat until hot but not smoking.
7. Fry coated in the hot oil until the bottom is golden brown, 5 to 7 minutes.
8. Flip the fillets, reduce heat to medium-low, and continue cooking until golden brown and the fish flakes easily with a fork, 5 to 7 minutes more.
