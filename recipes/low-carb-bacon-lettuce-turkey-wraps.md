## Ingredients

- 4  large lettuce leaves
- ¼ cup mayonnaise
- 8 slices bacon
- 12 slices deli smoked turkey
- 4 slices Swiss cheese
- (8-inch) lengths of baking string

## Instructions

1. Dry lettuce leaves on paper towels.
2. Spread each with 1 tablespoon mayonnaise.
3. Place bacon between paper towels on a microwave-safe plate.
4. Heat in the microwave until crispy, 4 to 6 minutes.
5. Top each lettuce leaf with 3 slices turkey, 2 slices bacon, and 1 slice Swiss cheese.
6. Roll up and tie each wrap with a length of baking string.
