## Ingredients

- 1 cup water
- ¾ cup milk
- 1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of broccoli soup
- ½ cup chopped broccoli
- ½ cup chopped cauliflower
- 2 cups instant brown rice
- salt to taste

## Instructions

1. In a medium saucepan over medium heat, blend together the water, milk and cream of broccoli soup.
2. Bring to a boil, stir in broccoli, cauliflower and rice.
3. Reduce heat, cover and simmer for 5 to 10 minutes.
4. Remove from heat and allow to stand for another 5 to 10 minutes.
5. Season with salt to taste.
