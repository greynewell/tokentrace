## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons peanut butter
- 2 slices white bread, toasted
- 3 slices crispy bacon
- ½  apple, cored and sliced

## Instructions

1. Spread peanut butter onto one side of one slice of toast.
2. Place slices of bacon onto the peanut butter and then the apple slices.
3. Top with the other slice of bread.
