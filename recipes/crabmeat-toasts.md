## Ingredients

- 1 (6 ounce) can crab meat, drained
- ⅜ cup butter
- 2 tablespoons mayonnaise
- ½ teaspoon garlic salt
- ½ teaspoon seasoning salt
- 1 (12 ounce) package English muffins

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to broil.
2. In a small mixing bowl, combine crabmeat, butter or margarine, mayonnaise, garlic salt, and seasoning salt.
3. Spread this mixture on the open-face muffins.
4. Arrange the toasts on a baking sheet.
5. Broil toasts for 8 minutes or until toasted.
6. Be careful not to burn them.
