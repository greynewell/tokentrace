## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon paprika
- 1 ¼ teaspoons salt
- 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
- ½ teaspoon brown sugar
- ½ teaspoon garlic powder
- ½ teaspoon onion powder
- ¼ teaspoon ground cayenne pepper

## Instructions

1. Combine paprika, salt, pepper, brown sugar, garlic powder, onion powder, and cayenne in an airtight container. Seal; and shake to combine.
2. Store in a cool, dry place between uses.
