## Ingredients

- 4  peaches, halved and pitted
- 1 pinch ground cinnamon
- ½ cup molasses

## Instructions

1. Preheat an outdoor grill for medium-high heat and lightly oil the grate.
2. Place peaches cut-side down on the grill; cook until browned, 4 to 5 minutes.
3. Flip peaches, dust with cinnamon, and fill each half with 1 tablespoon molasses.
4. Cook until browned on the bottom, about 3 minutes.
5. Let peaches rest before serving, 3 to 5 minutes.
