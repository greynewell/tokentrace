## Ingredients

- 35  buttery round crackers, crushed
- 1 (12 ounce) can oysters, drained
- 2 cups half-and-half cream
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 pinch paprika

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C).
2. Grease a 1 quart casserole dish.
3. Place a layer of crushed crackers in the bottom of the prepared casserole dish.
4. Cover with a layer of oysters.
5. Repeat layers ending with a layer of cracker crumbs on the top.
6. Drizzle the half-and-half over all, and dot with pieces of butter.
7. Sprinkle paprika over the top.
8. Cover, and let stand for one hour to firm up.
9. Bake for 1 hour in the preheated oven, until the top is golden, and the center is firm.
