## Ingredients

- 1 ¼ cups water
- 1 (6 ounce) package quick-cooking stuffing mix (such as Stove Top®)
- 1 (20 ounce) can sauerkraut, drained
- 1 (16 ounce) package skinless, boneless chicken breast halves, cut into bite-size pieces
- 8 ounces shredded Swiss cheese
- 4 ounces shredded Cheddar cheese
- ½ (16 ounce) bottle French salad dressing, or to taste

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Bring water to a boil in a saucepan.
3. Stir stuffing mix into the water, place a lid on the saucepan, and remove pan from heat.
4. Let sit until the stuffing is thickened, about 5 minutes.
5. Fluff stuffing with a fork.
6. Mix sauerkraut, chicken, Swiss cheese, and Cheddar cheese together in a large bowl.
7. Stir enough French dressing into the sauerkraut mixture to moisten to your liking; spread into a casserole dish.
8. Spread stuffing over the sauerkraut mixture.
9. Bake in the preheated oven until browned along the edges, about 25 minutes.
