## Ingredients

- 2 pounds fresh conch, removed from shell
- ½  fresh pineapple - peeled, cored and chopped
- ½ cup water
- 2  lemons, juiced
- 1 cup chopped fresh cilantro
- 1 medium onion, chopped
- 1 medium tomato, chopped  (Optional)
- salt to taste

## Instructions

1. Trim and peel the conch, and cut into small cubes.
2. Place into a large bowl with the water, lemon juice, cilantro, onion, pineapple, tomato and salt.
3. Let stand at room temperature for 1 hour before serving.
