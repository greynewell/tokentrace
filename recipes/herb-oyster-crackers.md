## Ingredients

- 2 (12 ounce) packages oyster crackers
- 1 (1 ounce) package ranch dressing mix
- ½ teaspoon dried dill weed
- ½ teaspoon garlic powder
- ½ teaspoon lemon pepper
- 1 cup vegetable oil

## Instructions

1. Place crackers in a large plastic bag.
2. Season with dressing mix, dill weed, garlic powder and lemon pepper.
3. Pour in the oil, close bag and shake vigorously until the crackers are covered with seasoning.
