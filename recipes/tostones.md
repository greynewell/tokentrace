## Ingredients

- ¼ cup vegetable oil
- 3  green plantains, peeled, and cut into 1-inch pieces
- 1 pinch garlic powder
- salt to taste

## Instructions

1. Heat the oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
2. Add the plantain slices and fry until they soften, 5 to 10 minutes.
3. Remove from the oil and drain on paper towels.
4. Use a tostonera (a press) to slightly mash each piece until it is about 1/2 inch thick.
5. If you do not have a tostonera, use a mallet or place the pieces between a folded paper bag and press down with a saucer.
6. Press all of the pieces before going onto the next step.
7. Return the pieces to the hot skillet and fry until crispy, about 3 minutes per side.
8. Drain on paper towels and season with garlic powder and salt while still warm.
