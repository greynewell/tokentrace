## Ingredients

- 1 (16 ounce) can pear halves, drained
- ¼ cup mayonnaise
- ¼ cup shredded Cheddar cheese
- 6  maraschino cherries

## Instructions

1. Place the pear halves on a plate cut side up.
2. Place a spoonful of mayonnaise into the pit of each one.
3. Sprinkle Cheddar cheese over the top of the mayonnaise.
4. Top each one with a whole cherry.
