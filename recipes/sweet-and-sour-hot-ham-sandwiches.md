## Ingredients

- Crisco® Original No-Stick Cooking Spray
- 1 pound thinly sliced deli-style smoked ham, quartered
- 1 tablespoon Crisco® Pure Vegetable Oil
- ½ large yellow onions, cut into very thin wedges
- ¾ cup chopped green pepper
- 1 (10 ounce) jar Crosse & Blackwell® Premium Ham Glaze
- 1 (8.25 ounce) can crushed pineapple in heavy syrup, undrained
- ⅓ cup firmly packed brown sugar
- 2 tablespoons white vinegar
- 6  kaiser rolls*

## Instructions

1. Coat large skillet with no-stick cooking spray. Heat skillet over medium heat. Saute ham, 1/3 at a time, 15 seconds or until ham is lightly browned. Remove from skillet and place in large bowl.
2. Heat oil in same skillet over medium-low heat. Add onion and green pepper; saute 7 minutes or until tender.
3. Stir in ham glaze, undrained pineapple, brown sugar and vinegar. Heat through. Pour over ham in bowl; toss to coat. Serve on rolls.
