## Ingredients

- 3 ½ cups all-purpose flour
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- ¾ pound unsalted butter, softened
- 1 cup white sugar
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 4 cups confectioners' sugar, sifted
- 10 tablespoons water, or as needed
- 2 tablespoons meringue powder
- 1 teaspoon gel food coloring, or as desired
- lollipop sticks

## Instructions

1. Whisk flour and salt together in a medium bowl and set aside.
2. Cream butter and sugar together in the bowl of stand mixer on medium speed until light and fluffy, about 4 minutes.
3. Add vanilla extract and mix to combine.
4. Slowly add flour and mix on low speed until a dough comes together.
5. Turn dough out onto a lightly floured surface and shape into 2 flat disks.
6. Wrap each in plastic wrap and chill for 30 minutes.
7. Line 3 baking sheets with parchment paper.
8. Roll out one of the chilled dough rounds to 1/4-inch thickness and cut out flowers using cookie cutters of your choice.
9. Re-roll the dough up to 3 times and continue cutting out cookies.
10. Transfer cookies to a baking sheet using a flat spatula.
11. Place cookies about 1 inch apart, fitting about 12 on each sheet.
12. Roll out the second dough round and repeat cutting out flowers until you have about 3 dozen cookies in total.
13. Freeze cookies for 20 minutes to help maintain their shape.
14. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
15. Bake until the edges start to look pale golden, 10 to 15 minutes.
16. Cool on pans for 5 minutes.
17. Transfer to a wire rack and cool to room temperature, at least 1 hour.
18. Combine confectioners' sugar, 5 tablespoons water, and meringue powder in the bowl of a stand mixer and mix on medium until a thick icing forms.
19. Add more water, 1 tablespoon at a time, until desired texture is reached.
20. Divide icing among several bowls and tint with desired gel food coloring.
21. Decorate cookies as desired using a pastry bags and tips.
22. Reserve any leftover icing in an airtight container for attaching the sticks.
23. Let icing dry, 4 hours to overnight.
24. Attach lollipop sticks to the back of the dry cookies with royal icing.
25. Tie together with ribbon or arrange in a gift box.
