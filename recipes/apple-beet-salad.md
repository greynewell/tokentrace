## Ingredients

- 1 (15 ounce) can diced beets, drained
- 2  apples - peeled, cored and chopped
- ¾ cup walnuts
- ¾ cup creamy salad dressing, e.g. Miracle Whip ™

## Instructions

1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the beets, apples, walnuts and salad dressing.
2. Mix together and chill before serving.
