## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
- 2  salmon steaks
- 1 tablespoon Creole seasoning, or to taste
- 1  orange, juiced

## Instructions

1. Heat the oil in a skillet over medium heat.
2. Season salmon with Creole seasoning, place in skillet, and cook 2 to 3 minutes on each side, until golden brown.
3. Reduce heat to low, and pour orange juice around the salmon.
4. Continue cooking 5 minutes, or until fish is easily flaked with a fork.
