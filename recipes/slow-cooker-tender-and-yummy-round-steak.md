## Ingredients

- 3  potatoes, peeled and quartered
- 1  onion, chopped
- 6  carrots, peeled and sliced into 1 inch pieces
- 2 pounds boneless round steak
- 1 (1 ounce) package dry onion soup mix
- 1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of mushroom soup
- ¾ cup water

## Instructions

1. Place potatoes, carrots, and onion in a slow cooker. Cut steak into 6 equal pieces; place on top of vegetables.
2. Whisk together condensed soup, water, and onion soup mix in a medium bowl until well combined; pour over steak.
3. Cover and cook on Low for 7 to 10 hours.
