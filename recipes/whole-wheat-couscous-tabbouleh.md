## Ingredients

- 1 cup water
- ¾ cup whole wheat couscous
- ½ teaspoon salt
- 2 cups halved grape tomatoes
- 1 ¾ cups chopped peeled cucumber
- 1 cup chopped red onion
- ½ cup chopped fresh flat-leaf parsley
- ½ cup chopped fresh mint
- ¼ cup fresh lemon juice
- 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
- ¼ teaspoon freshly ground black pepper

## Instructions

1. Bring water to a boil in a saucepan; stir in couscous and salt.
2. Remove from heat, cover saucepan, and let stand until water is absorbed, about 5 minutes.
3. Transfer couscous to a bowl and fluff with a fork.
4. Mix tomatoes, cucumber, onion, parsley, mint, lemon juice, olive oil, and black pepper into couscous.
5. Serve immediately or refrigerate for 1 hour for flavors to blend.
