## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons margarine, melted
- 2 teaspoons all-purpose flour
- 1 (16 ounce) can canned salmon, skin and bones removed
- 6  saltine crackers, crumbled
- ½ cup milk

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
2. Pour melted margarine into a small glass baking dish.
3. Stir flour into the melted margarine until all the flour is moistened.
4. Flake salmon into the dish; add crackers.
5. Pour milk over the mixture and stir.
6. Bake in preheated oven until cooked through, about 30 minutes.
