## Ingredients

- 2 (16 ounce) packages Brussels sprouts
- ¼ cup olive oil, divided
- 2 teaspoons smoked paprika, divided
- ¼ teaspoon sea salt
- ¼ cup heavy whipping cream
- 3 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese
- 1 tablespoon red wine vinegar
- 1  lemon, zested and juiced
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 teaspoon brown mustard

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C).
2. Trim stems off Brussels sprouts. Quarter large sprouts lengthwise; halve small ones. Rinse and dry thoroughly.
3. Toss sprouts in a bowl with olive oil and paprika. Spread out on a baking sheet and sprinkle with sea salt.
4. Bake in the preheated oven for 20 minutes, removing loose leaves after 5 minutes and turning whole sprouts every 5 minutes.
5. Make dressing: Whisk together heavy cream, Parmesan cheese, olive oil, lemon zest and juice, red wine vinegar, garlic, brown mustard, and paprika in a bowl until well combined.
6. Transfer whole sprouts and loose leaves to a serving bowl; pour in dressing and toss to combine.
