## Ingredients

- 2 (28 ounce) cans baked beans
- 2  onions, cut into wedges
- ¼ cup molasses
- ½ cup barbeque sauce
- 1 tablespoon liquid smoke flavoring

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C).
2. In a 3 quart casserole dish combine beans, onions, molasses, barbecue sauce and liquid smoke flavoring.
3. Bake in preheated oven for 2 to 2 1/2 hours, stirring every 20 minutes.
