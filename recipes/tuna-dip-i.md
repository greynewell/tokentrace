## Ingredients

- 1 (5 ounce) can tuna, drained
- 1 teaspoon mayonnaise
- 1 teaspoon prepared mustard
- ⅔ cup yogurt
- 1 teaspoon fresh lemon juice
- 2 teaspoons sweet pickles, chopped

## Instructions

1. In a medium bowl, mix together tuna, mayonnaise, mustard, yogurt and lemon juice.
2. Blend until smooth.
3. Stir in the sweet pickles.
4. Keep chilled until serving.
