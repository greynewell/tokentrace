## Ingredients

- 1 pound smoked mackerel
- 4 ounces cream cheese
- ½ cup softened butter
- 1  lemon, juiced

## Instructions

1. Remove any skin and bones from the mackerel, and place into the bowl of a food processor.
2. Pulse until no large pieces remain.
3. Add the cream cheese, butter, and lemon juice; process until smooth.
4. Place into a small serving bowl, and refrigerate until firmed, about 2 hours.
