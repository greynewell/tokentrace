## Ingredients

- 1 bunch fresh kale, stems removed and leaves thinly sliced
- 1 teaspoon olive oil
- 1 pinch salt
- 5 slices bacon
- ¼ cup olive oil
- ¼ cup balsamic vinegar
- 1 teaspoon soy sauce
- ½ clove garlic, crushed
- ½ cup sunflower seeds

## Instructions

1. Place kale in a resealable plastic bag; add 1 teaspoon olive oil and salt.
2. Mix thoroughly until leaves are coated.
3. Seal bag and let sit for 30 minutes.
4. Place bacon in a large skillet and cook over medium-high heat, turning occasionally, until evenly browned, about 10 minutes.
5. Drain bacon slices on paper towels; crumble into small pieces.
6. Whisk 1/4 cup olive oil, balsamic vinegar, soy sauce, and garlic together in a bowl until dressing is evenly mixed.
7. Transfer kale to a bowl; add dressing and toss to coat.
8. Top salad with bacon and sunflower seeds.
