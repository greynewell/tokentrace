## Ingredients

- ½  onion, diced
- 1 pound ground beef
- 1 (15 ounce) can pinto beans, rinsed and drained
- 1 (14.5 ounce) can diced tomatoes, undrained
- 2 cups beef broth
- 1 cup frozen corn
- 1 (6 ounce) package Knorr® Fiesta Sides™ - Taco Rice
- 1 tablespoon buttery spread
- ½ cup shredded cheese, or more to taste
- ½ cup sour cream, or more to taste

## Instructions

1. Place a heavy pot over medium heat. Add onions and ground beef and cook and stir until beef is browned and crumbled and onions begin to turn translucent, about 5 minutes. Drain grease from pot.
2. Pour in beef broth; add beans, tomatoes, corn and Knorr® Fiesta Sides - Taco Rice. Stir in 1 buttery spread. Bring mixture to a boil. Reduce heat, cover, and simmer until rice is tender, about 7 minutes.
3. Remove pot from heat; let pot stand at least 2 minutes. Stir. Serve topped with shredded cheese and sour cream.
