## Ingredients

- 2 cups mayonnaise
- 2 cups sour cream
- ½ cup tomato-based chili sauce
- ½ teaspoon cayenne pepper

## Instructions

1. In a mixing bowl, combine the mayonnaise, sour cream, tomato-based chili sauce and cayenne pepper.
2. Mix the ingredients well.
3. Serve and enjoy.
