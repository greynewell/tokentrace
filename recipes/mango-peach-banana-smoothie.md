## Ingredients

- 1 (16 ounce) can mango nectar
- 1 cup peach yogurt
- 1 cup vanilla frozen yogurt
- 1 ½ cups frozen peach slices
- 1  frozen banana, cut into chunks

## Instructions

1. Combine mango nectar, peach yogurt, and frozen yogurt in pitcher of a blender; add peach slices and banana chunks.
2. Blend until smooth, 30 seconds to 1 minute.
