## Ingredients

- 1 cup all-purpose flour
- ½ cup whole wheat flour
- ¼ cup quick-cooking oats, or to taste
- 1 teaspoon baking soda
- 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
- ½ teaspoon salt
- 4  ripe bananas
- ½ cup white sugar
- ½ cup brown sugar
- ¼ cup margarine
- ¼ cup milk
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- ½ cup raisins, or to taste  (Optional)

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C).
2. Combine flours, oats, baking soda, cinnamon, and salt in a medium bowl. Mash bananas into the dry ingredients.
3. Combine sugars and margarine in a large bowl; beat with an electric mixer until creamy. Mix in milk and vanilla extract. Add the flour mixture and raisins; mix well. Pour batter into 4 mini silicone loaf pans. Place loaf pans on a baking sheet.
4. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean, 25 to 30 minutes.
