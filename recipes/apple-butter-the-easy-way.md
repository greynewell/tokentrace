## Ingredients

- 2 pounds chopped apples
- ⅓ cup maple syrup
- ½ teaspoon ground cinnamon

## Instructions

1. In a microwave safe bowl, combine apples, maple syrup and cinnamon.
2. Microwave on high for 15 minutes.
3. Transfer mixture to a blender and process until smooth.
4. Return mixture to bowl and microwave 5 minutes more.
5. Cool and transfer to a jar.
6. Store in refrigerator.
