## Ingredients

- ½ cup white wine vinegar
- 1 ½ tablespoons Dijon mustard
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice
- 1 tablespoon Italian seasoning
- 4 sprigs fresh rosemary
- 1 teaspoon dried tarragon
- 1 tablespoon capers, drained
- 1 tablespoon oil-packed sun-dried tomatoes, drained and sliced
- 1 ½ pounds salmon fillets
- freshly ground black pepper to taste
- 4  kalamata olives, pitted and sliced

## Instructions

1. In a shallow baking dish, mix the white wine vinegar, Dijon mustard, lemon juice, Italian seasoning, rosemary, tarragon, capers, and sun-dried tomatoes.
2. Place the salmon fillets into the dish, and turn to coat with the marinade.
3. Season with pepper, and sprinkle with olives.
4. Cover, and refrigerate 1 hour.
5. Preheat oven to 375  degrees F (190 degrees C).
6. Bake uncovered for 20 minutes in the preheated oven, or until fish is easily flaked with a fork.
