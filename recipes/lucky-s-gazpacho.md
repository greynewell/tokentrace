## Ingredients

- 2  roma (plum) tomatoes, chopped
- ½  cucumber, chopped
- ½  green bell pepper, chopped
- ½  red bell pepper, chopped
- ½ small red onion, chopped
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- 2 cups tomato juice
- 2 teaspoons beef bouillon granules
- ½ teaspoon dried oregano
- ½ teaspoon dried basil
- ¼ teaspoon celery salt
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- ⅛ teaspoon ground black pepper
- 1 ½ teaspoons Worcestershire sauce
- 1 ½ teaspoons red wine vinegar

## Instructions

1. Puree roma tomatoes, cucumber, green and red bell peppers, red onion, and garlic in a blender, about 30 seconds.
2. Add tomato juice, beef bouillon granules, oregano, basil, celery salt, salt, black pepper, Worcestershire sauce, and red wine vinegar.
3. Pulse a few times to mix.
4. Pour into a bowl and chill at least 1 hour.
