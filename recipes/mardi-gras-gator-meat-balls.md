## Ingredients

- 2 ¼ pounds potatoes, peeled and diced
- 5 ¼ pounds alligator meat
- 1 ½ pounds onions
- 2 bunches green onions
- 2 bunches fresh parsley
- 1 medium head garlic
- ½ bunch celery
- 1 quart oil for frying
- 2  eggs
- 3 tablespoons dried savory
- 2 ounces salt
- 1 tablespoon black pepper
- 2 ½ pounds cracker meal

## Instructions

1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
2. Add potatoes and cook until tender but still firm, about 15 minutes; drain.
3. Put the alligator meat, onions, green onions, parsley, celery and potatoes through a meat grinder into a large bowl.
4. Mix in the eggs, savory, salt and pepper until well blended.
5. Shape into 1 ounce balls (golf ball sized) and roll in cracker meal.
6. Heat oil in deep-fryer to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
7. Fry meat balls until golden and crisp.
8. Drain on paper towels.
