## Ingredients

- 2 teaspoons olive oil
- 12 ounces lean breakfast sausage
- ½ cup diced red bell pepper
- 1 cup all-purpose flour
- 1 cup corn meal
- ½ teaspoon baking soda
- 1 pinch cayenne pepper
- 2  eggs
- 1 cup buttermilk
- 1 cup fresh sweet corn kernels
- ½ cup chopped green onions
- ½ cup grated sharp Cheddar cheese, plus more for sprinkling on muffins
- ¼ cup melted butter

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F ( 190 degrees C).
2. Place paper liners in 12 muffin tin cups.
3. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium-high heat.
4. Remove casings from sausages (if not packaged in bulk) and place in skillet.
5. Break the sausage into small pieces with a wooden spoon as it cooks.
6. Cook until the sausage is crumbly and thoroughly cooked, about 7 minutes.
7. Add bell pepper; cook for about 2 minutes.
8. Remove skillet from heat.
9. Push sausage mixture to one side of the pan and blot up some of the rendered fat with wadded paper towels leaving about 1 tablespoon.
10. Let mixture cool.
11. Place flour, corn meal, baking soda, and cayenne pepper in a mixing bowl.
12. Whisk until combined.
13. In a separate bowl, whisk eggs and buttermilk together.
14. Add egg/buttermilk mixture slowly to flour/cornmeal mixture.
15. Add fresh corn kernels, green onions, Cheddar cheese, and reserved sausage mixture.
16. Stir in melted butter; mix just until flour is mixed in.
17. Divide batter among cups in prepared muffin pan.
18. Top with a few shreds of Cheddar cheese.
19. Bake until golden brown and a toothpick inserted into a muffin comes out clean, 25 to 30 minutes.
20. Let cool to warm before serving.
