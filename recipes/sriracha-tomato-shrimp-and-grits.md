## Ingredients

- 1  yellow onion, diced small
- 1 teaspoon olive oil
- 1 gallon water
- 24 ounces regular grits, uncooked
- 8 ounces unsalted butter
- 2 cups shredded white Cheddar cheese
- ½ teaspoon kosher salt
- ¼ teaspoon freshly cracked black pepper
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 1  yellow onion, thinly sliced
- 1  red bell pepper, seeded and thinly sliced
- 1  green bell pepper, seeded and thinly sliced
- 4  andouille sausage links, thinly sliced
- 2 pounds uncooked medium shrimp (26-30 count) - peeled, deveined, thawed if frozen
- 1 (28 ounce) can Hunt's® Diced Tomatoes, undrained
- 4 tablespoons sriracha hot sauce
- ½ teaspoon kosher salt
- ¼ teaspoon freshly cracked black pepper

## Instructions

1. Grits: Heat olive oil in a large pot on high heat; add the onions and brown.
2. Add water and bring to a boil.
3. Slowly stir in grits and cook on medium heat until they are cooked, about 45 minutes.
4. Finish with butter, cheese, salt and pepper; keep warm.
5. Shrimp: Heat olive oil in a large saute pan on high heat.
6. Add onions, peppers and sausage and saute for 5 to 6 minutes.
7. Next, add shrimp and cook until they are cooked through, about 5 minutes.
8. Finish by stirring in diced tomatoes, Sriracha, salt and pepper and simmer for 10 minutes on low heat.
9. Serve the shrimp over the cheesy grits.
