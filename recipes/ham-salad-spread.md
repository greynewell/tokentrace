## Ingredients

- 3 cups ground fully cooked ham
- 2  hard cooked eggs, chopped
- 2 tablespoons finely chopped celery
- 4 teaspoons sweet pickle relish
- 2 teaspoons finely chopped onion
- 1 cup mayonnaise
- 1 tablespoon prepared yellow mustard

## Instructions

1. Mix ham, eggs, celery, pickle relish, and onion together in a medium bowl until combined.
2. Mix mayonnaise and mustard together in a small bowl; pour over ham mixture and stir to coat.
3. Refrigerate until ready to serve.
