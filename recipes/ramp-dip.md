## Ingredients

- 2 (8 ounce) packages Neufchatel cheese, softened
- ¼ cup mayonnaise
- ¾ cup minced ramps (wild leeks)

## Instructions

1. Mix Neufchatel cheese and mayonnaise in a bowl; fold minced ramps into the cheese mixture.
2. Cover bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate at least 6 hours.
