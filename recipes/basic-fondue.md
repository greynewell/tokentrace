## Ingredients

- 2 cups milk
- 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
- 2 teaspoons ground dry mustard
- 1 clove garlic, peeled and crushed
- 3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
- 6 cups shredded Cheddar cheese

## Instructions

1. Mix together milk, flour, Worcestershire sauce, mustard, and garlic in a medium saucepan over low heat. Heat until almost boiling.
2. Gradually stir in Cheddar cheese. Continue heating until all the cheese has melted.
3. Keep the mixture warm and melted in a fondue dish.
