## Ingredients

- 2 cups diced cooked turkey
- 1  onion, diced
- 2  carrots, diced
- 1  potato, peeled and diced
- ¼ cup chopped celery
- 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ½ teaspoon ground black pepper
- 6 cups turkey stock

## Instructions

1. Combine turkey, onion, carrots, potato, celery, parsley, garlic, salt, and black pepper in a large stockpot; add turkey stock.
2. Bring stock to a boil, reduce heat to medium-low, and simmer until vegetables are tender, 2 to 3 hours.
