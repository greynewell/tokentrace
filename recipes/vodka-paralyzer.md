## Ingredients

- 1 cup ice cubes
- 4 fluid ounces cola
- 1 fluid ounce coffee flavored liqueur
- 1 (1.5 fluid ounce) jigger vodka
- 2 fluid ounces milk
- 1  maraschino cherry  (Optional)

## Instructions

1. Fill a highball glass with ice.
2. In the order listed, pour in cola, coffee liqueur, vodka, then milk. Stir briefly and top with a maraschino cherry.
