## Ingredients

- 8 (1/2 inch thick) slices soft Italian bread
- ⅓ cup mayonnaise
- 8 ounces thinly sliced young goat Gouda cheese
- 1 ⅓ cups Sheet-Pan Ratatouille, or other prepared ratatouille

## Instructions

1. Spread both sides of each bread slice with mayonnaise.
2. Layer 1/2 of the cheese, all of the ratatouille, and remaining cheese on 4 bread slices.
3. Top with remaining bread slices.
4. Heat an extra-large skillet, grill pan, or griddle over medium heat.
5. Cook sandwiches until golden and cheese melts, 2 to 3 minutes per side.
