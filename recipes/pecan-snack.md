## Ingredients

- 1  egg white
- 1 tablespoon water
- 1 pound pecans
- ¾ cup white sugar
- 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
- 1 teaspoon salt

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 250 degrees F (120 degrees C).
2. Beat egg white with water in a large bowl until frothy. Stir in pecans and mix to coat. Combine sugar, cinnamon, and salt in a small bowl; stir into pecan mixture. Spread pecans on a baking sheet.
3. Bake, stirring every 15 minutes, in the preheated oven for 1 hour. Store in an airtight container.
