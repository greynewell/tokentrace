## Ingredients

- 4 pounds pink salmon (such as Chicken of the Sea®), drained, flaked and cartilage removed
- 4  large eggs
- 1 ½ cups panko bread crumbs
- ¾ cup mayonnaise
- 2  lemons, juiced
- 2 tablespoons ranch dressing mix
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
- 1 teaspoon garlic powder
- 1 teaspoon dried dill
- 1 teaspoon seafood seasoning (such as Old Bay®)
- ½ cup white cornmeal
- ¼ cup vegetable oil, or as needed
- 16  lemon slices
- 16 sprigs fresh parsley

## Instructions

1. Mix salmon, eggs, bread crumbs, mayonnaise, lemon juice, ranch dressing mix, salt, pepper, garlic powder, dill, and seafood seasoning in a large bowl; divide into 15 portions and shape into patties.
2. Dust each patty with cornmeal.
3. Heat 1 tablespoon oil in a large skillet.
4. Fry a few patties in hot oil, not overcrowding the skillet, until browned, 3 to 4 minutes per side.
5. Repeat until all patties are cooked.
6. Garnish each with a lemon slice and a sprig of parsley.
