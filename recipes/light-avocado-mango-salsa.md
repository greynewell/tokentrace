## Ingredients

- 1 large avocado, diced
- 1  mango, diced
- ½  red onion, diced
- 5  strawberries, diced
- 1  lime, juiced
- Salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Mix avocado, mango, red onion, strawberries, and lime juice in bowl.
2. Add salt and pepper to taste.
