## Ingredients

- ¼ cup sriracha sauce
- 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
- 1 teaspoon teriyaki sauce
- ½ teaspoon five-spice powder
- ¼ teaspoon hot sauce (such as Cholula®)
- ⅛ teaspoon ground Sichuan peppercorns  (Optional)
- 1 teaspoon vegetable oil
- 6  large shrimp, peeled and deveined

## Instructions

1. Mix sriracha sauce, oyster sauce, teriyaki sauce, five-spice powder, hot sauce, and Sichuan peppercorns in a small bowl and reserve.
2. Heat oil in a medium pan over medium heat.
3. Add shrimp and cook until mostly pink on the outside, about 3 minutes.
4. Add the sauce and stir to coat shrimp.
5. Cook until sauce is thick and sticky, 2 to 3 minutes more.
