## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 1 large onion, chopped
- 1  red bell pepper, chopped
- 1  green bell pepper, chopped
- 2 stalks celery, chopped
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 ½ teaspoons sea salt, divided
- 1 teaspoon freshly ground pepper, divided
- 1 (15 ounce) can diced tomatoes
- 1 cup chicken broth
- 1 tablespoon dried parsley
- 2 teaspoons chili powder
- ¾ teaspoon cayenne pepper
- 1 tablespoon tomato paste
- ½ pound sea scallops - rinsed, drained, patted dry, and cut in half
- ½ pound haddock, cut into cubes
- ½ pound uncooked medium shrimp, peeled and deveined

## Instructions

1. Heat olive oil in a large heavy saucepan over medium-high heat.
2. Add onion, red bell pepper, green bell pepper, celery, and garlic; cook until softened, 3 to 4 minutes.
3. Season with 1/2 teaspoon salt and 1/2 teaspoon pepper.
4. Add tomatoes and broth; bring to a boil.
5. Reduce heat to medium-low.
6. Add parsley, chili powder, and cayenne pepper; cook until thickened, about 15 minutes.
7. Stir in tomato paste until dissolved.
8. Season scallops and haddock with remaining 1 teaspoon salt and 1/2 teaspoon pepper; add to the saucepan.
9. Stir in shrimp; cook until they are bright pink on the outside and opaque in the center, about 7 minutes.
