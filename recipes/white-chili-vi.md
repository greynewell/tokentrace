## Ingredients

- 1 (48 ounce) jar great Northern beans
- 1 (16 ounce) jar salsa
- ½ pound pepperjack cheese, cut into chunks
- 1 (8 ounce) can white meat chicken, shredded

## Instructions

1. Combine great Northern beans, salsa, pepper Jack cheese, and chicken in a slow cooker.
2. Cook on Low until cheese has melted completely into chili and the flavors have combined, 4 to 5 hours.
