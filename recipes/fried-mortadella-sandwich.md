## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons olive oil, divided
- 4 cups diced potatoes
- 1  onion, sliced
- 1 cup diced pimiento peppers
- 1 pound sliced mortadella sausage
- 4  Italian-style hoagie buns, split lengthwise
- ¼ cup spicy brown mustard
- 8 slices Swiss cheese
- 8 slices Asiago cheese
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 8  eggs

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 500 degrees F (260 degrees C).
2. Heat 2 tablespoons olive oil in a skillet over medium heat; cook and stir potatoes until browned and crisp, about 15 minutes.
3. Set aside on a paper towel-lined plate.
4. Place onion and pimiento peppers in the hot skillet; cook and stir until onion has softened, about 5 minutes.
5. Add mortadella and cook until warmed through and slightly browned, 3 to 4 minutes.
6. Spread each bun with 1 tablespoon spicy mustard.
7. Layer with 2 slices Swiss cheese.
8. Top with 1/4 of the mortadella and onion mixture and sprinkle with 1/4 of the fried potatoes.
9. Top with 2 slices Asiago cheese.
10. Repeat to make 4 sandwiches.
11. Place sandwiches onto a baking sheet and bake in the preheated oven until cheese is melted and buns are toasted, about 5 minutes.
12. Heat 1 tablespoon olive oil in a nonstick skillet over medium heat; cook eggs, 2 to 4 at a time if necessary, until whites are set but yolks are still soft, about 2 minutes per side.
13. Place two eggs in each sandwich.
