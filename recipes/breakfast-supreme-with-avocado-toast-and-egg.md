## Ingredients

- 4 slices wheat bread
- 4 tablespoons barbeque sauce
- 2 teaspoons butter
- 4  eggs
- 1  avocado
- ¼ cup shredded mozzarella cheese

## Instructions

1. Toast bread in a toaster until medium brown, about 2 minutes.
2. Spread each slice equally with barbeque sauce.
3. Melt butter in a small skillet over medium-high heat.
4. Crack eggs in and fry on both sides, about 4 minutes.
5. Place each fried egg on a piece of toast.
6. Top each with 1/4 of an avocado.
7. Sprinkle an equal amount of cheese on top of each toast.
