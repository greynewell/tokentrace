## Ingredients

- 4  boneless pork loin chops
- ¼ cup apple juice
- ¼ cup maple syrup

## Instructions

1. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat.
2. Brown the pork chops in the hot pan, about 4 to 5 minutes per side.
3. Pour in the apple juice and maple syrup; reduce heat and simmer until the juice has reduced and thickened slightly.
4. Cook until the pork is no longer pink in the center.
5. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read 145 degrees F (63 degrees C).
