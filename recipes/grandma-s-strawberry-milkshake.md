## Ingredients

- 4 ounces strawberries, hulled
- 1 cup milk
- 1 tablespoon honey
- 6 cubes  ice

## Instructions

1. In a blender, combine ice cubes, strawberries, milk and honey.
2. Blend until smooth.
3. Pour into glass and enjoy!
