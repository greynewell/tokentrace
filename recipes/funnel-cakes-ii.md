## Ingredients

- 1 ⅔ cups all-purpose flour
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- ¾ teaspoon baking soda
- ½ teaspoon cream of tartar
- 1 quart oil for frying
- 2 tablespoons white sugar
- 1  egg
- 1 cup milk
- ¼ cup confectioners' sugar for dusting

## Instructions

1. In a mixing bowl, beat together egg and milk.
2. Beat in flour, salt, baking soda, cream of tartar, and white sugar until smooth.
3. Heat about 1 inch cooking oil in frying pan to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
4. Pour 1/2 cup batter through funnel into oil with a circular motion to form a spiral.
5. Fry until lightly brown; turn over to brown the other side.
6. Cook to golden brown, and remove to drain on paper towels.
7. Sprinkle with confectioner's sugar while still warm.
