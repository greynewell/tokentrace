## Ingredients

- 12 ounces frozen shelled edamame, thawed
- 2 tablespoons garlic-flavored olive oil
- ¼ cup grated Parmesan cheese
- ¼ teaspoon garlic powder
- ¼ teaspoon onion powder
- ¼ teaspoon chile powder with lime (such as Trader Joe's® Chile Lime Seasoning Blend)
- 1 pinch salt and freshly ground black pepper

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
2. Toss edamame and garlic olive oil in a bowl to coat completely.
3. Mix Parmesan cheese, garlic powder, onion powder, chile lime seasoning, salt, and pepper in a separate bowl.
4. Add to the edamame mixture and toss until well combined.
5. Spread edamame in 1 layer on a rimmed baking sheet.
6. Bake in the preheated oven until Parmesan cheese begins to brown, turning once halfway through, 10 to 15 minutes.
