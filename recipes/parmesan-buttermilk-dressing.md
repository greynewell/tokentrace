## Ingredients

- 2 cups buttermilk
- 1 ½ cups sour cream
- 1 cup mayonnaise
- ¾ cup shredded Parmesan cheese
- 3 tablespoons white vinegar
- 1 tablespoon garlic salt
- 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley
- 1 ½ teaspoons ground black pepper

## Instructions

1. Whisk buttermilk, sour cream, mayonnaise, Parmesan cheese, vinegar, garlic salt, parsley, and pepper together in a bowl until combined; some chunks are expected.
2. Transfer to a large mason jar and chill until flavors are blended, at least 3 hours.
