## Ingredients

- ⅓ cup Heinz 57 Sauce, Lightly Flavoured
- 2 tablespoons melted butter
- 1 teaspoon Heinz Worcestershire Sauce
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- 4  skinless, boneless salmon fillets
- 1  Granny Smith apple, thinly sliced
- ½ cup very thinly sliced red onion
- 2 teaspoons lemon juice
- 2 teaspoons extra-virgin olive oil
- 2 teaspoons chopped fresh dill
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- ¼ teaspoon pepper
- 4  kaiser rolls
- Lettuce leaves

## Instructions

1. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
2. Line the grate with perforated non-stick foil.
3. Combine 57 sauce with butter, Worcestershire sauce and garlic.
4. Brush half this mixture over salmon.
5. Grill the salmon, basting with remaining sauce, for 4 to 5 minutes per side.
6. Toss the apples, onion and dill with the lemon juice, oil, salt and pepper.
7. Slice rolls in half and toast lightly on the grill.
8. Place a leaf of lettuce on each halved roll, top with a piece of salmon and equal amounts of slaw.
