## Ingredients

- 1 (10 ounce) package frozen sweetened strawberries, thawed
- ½ cup vegetable oil
- 2  eggs
- 1 teaspoon baking soda
- ½ teaspoon baking powder
- ½ teaspoon ground cinnamon
- 1 cup white sugar
- 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
- ½ cup chopped pecans

## Instructions

1. Drain the strawberries, reserving 1/4 cup of the juice. In a large mixing bowl stir together the oil, eggs, baking soda, baking powder, cinnamon, sugar and flour. Fold in the pecans, strawberries and 1/4 cup of their juice. Pour the mixture into your bread machine pan and place it in the machine. Select the Quick Bread cycle and press Start.
2. Remove pan from bread maker and let cool completely before removing bread from pan.
