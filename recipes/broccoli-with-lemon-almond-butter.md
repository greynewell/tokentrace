## Ingredients

- 1 head fresh broccoli, cut into florets
- ¼ cup butter, melted
- 2 tablespoons lemon juice
- 1 teaspoon lemon zest
- ¼ cup blanched slivered almonds

## Instructions

1. Steam or boil broccoli until tender, approximately 4 to 8 minutes.
2. Drain.
3. In a small saucepan, melt butter over medium low heat.
4. Remove from heat.
5. Stir in lemon juice. lemon zest, and almonds.
6. Pour over hot broccoli, and serve.
