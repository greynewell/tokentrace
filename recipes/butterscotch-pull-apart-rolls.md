## Ingredients

- cooking spray
- 20  frozen white dinner rolls
- 1 (3.5 ounce) package cook and serve butterscotch pudding mix
- ½ cup butter, melted
- ½ cup brown sugar
- 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon

## Instructions

1. Spray a fluted tube pan (such as Bundt®) with cooking spray. Place frozen rolls in the pan. Sprinkle with pudding mix.
2. Combine melted butter, brown sugar, and cinnamon in a small bowl. Pour over the rolls. Cover pan with a tea towel and let rise overnight, about 8 hours.
3. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Place fluted tube pan on a baking sheet as mixture will boil over in the oven.
4. Bake in the preheated oven until the buns are golden brown, about 20 minutes.
