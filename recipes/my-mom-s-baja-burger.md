## Ingredients

- 2 pounds ground beef
- 1 cup corn chips (such as Fritos®, crushed
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- 1 (28 ounce) can tomato sauce
- ½  onion, chopped
- ½ teaspoon garlic salt
- ½ teaspoon chili powder
- ½ cup shredded Cheddar cheese
- 8 (6 inch) French bread rolls, split in half lengthwise

## Instructions

1. Mix the ground beef and crushed corn chips; form into 8 oblong patties; season with salt and pepper.
2. In a small bowl, mix together the tomato sauce, onion, garlic salt, and chili powder.
3. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat.
4. Cook the patties in the hot skillet 4 to 5 minutes; flip and cook an additional 4 minutes.
5. Pour the tomato sauce mixture into the skillet as the burgers finish cooking; simmer for another 4 to 5 minutes.
6. Top each burger with Cheddar cheese and allow to melt.
7. Place burgers on bread and ladle sauce on the patties before closing each sandwich.
