## Ingredients

- 3 ounces fresh pasta
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 1 cup chopped fresh pea vines
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- 1 slice cooked bacon, broken into pieces
- 1 tablespoon butter
- ½ teaspoon chile oil, or to taste

## Instructions

1. Bring a pot of water to a boil; add fresh pasta and cook until tender, 3 to 4 minutes.
2. Drain.
3. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat; cook and stir pea vines and garlic until vines are wilted, 3 to 4 minutes.
4. Add pasta, bacon, butter, and chile oil to pea vines mixture; toss using tongs until butter is melted.
5. Transfer pasta mixture to a 1-pint mason jar.
