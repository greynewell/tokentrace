## Ingredients

- 2 cups kidney beans
- 5 cups water
- 1  onion, chopped
- 1 ½ teaspoons salt
- 4 ounces cured pork
- ¼ cup molasses
- 4 tablespoons brown sugar
- 1 teaspoon dry mustard
- ¼ cup ketchup

## Instructions

1. Place beans, water, onion, salt, and pork in a slow cooker.
2. Cover the pot and cook on low for 9 to 10 hours.
3. Drain beans, saving the liquid.
4. Add molasses, brown sugar, dry mustard, ketchup and 1 cup of the bean liquid.
5. Cover and cook on low for 1 hour.
