## Ingredients

- 2 pounds halibut fillets
- salt and pepper to taste
- ¼ cup olive oil
- ½ cup chopped fresh parsley
- 1  yellow onion, thinly sliced
- 2 stalks celery, chopped
- 1  green bell pepper, chopped
- 1 (16 ounce) can diced tomatoes
- 2 tablespoons capers
- 4 cloves garlic, minced

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C).
2. Wash halibut and pat dry.
3. Cut into serving size pieces, and place in a 9x13 inch baking pan.
4. Sprinkle with salt and pepper.
5. Stir together the olive oil, parsley, onion, celery, bell pepper, tomatoes, capers, and garlic; pour over the halibut.
6. Bake until halibut is slightly opaque in the center, about 20 minutes.
7. Remove from oven; let stand for 10 minutes before serving.
