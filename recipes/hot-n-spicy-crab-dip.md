## Ingredients

- 1 (16 ounce) can crabmeat
- 2 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened
- 1 (8 ounce) package sharp Cheddar cheese spread
- 3 tablespoons lemon juice
- 3 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
- 3 tablespoons beer
- 2 tablespoons chopped fresh chives
- 2 tablespoons grated onion
- 1 tablespoon chile-garlic sauce (such as Sriracha®)
- 1 teaspoon cayenne pepper
- 1 clove garlic, crushed
- 1 pinch paprika, or to taste

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Mix crabmeat, cream cheese, Cheddar cheese spread, lemon juice, Worcestershire sauce, beer, chives, onion, chile-garlic sauce, cayenne pepper, and garlic together in a bowl; spread evenly into a 9x9-inch baking dish.
3. Sprinkle crab-cheese mixture with paprika.
4. Cover baking dish with aluminum foil.
5. Bake in the preheated oven for 20 minutes; remove aluminum foil and bake until bubbling, about 5 more minutes.
