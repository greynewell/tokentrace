## Ingredients

- ½ cup canola oil
- 2  onions, chopped
- 6 cloves garlic, sliced
- 1  green bell pepper, chopped
- 3  serrano peppers, chopped
- 1 pound ground beef
- 1 pound ground pork
- 1 tablespoon fresh lime juice
- 2 tablespoons chili powder
- 1 tablespoon cayenne pepper
- 1 tablespoon ground black pepper
- 1 tablespoon kosher salt
- 2 (16 ounce) bottles dark beer
- 2 (16 ounce) cans diced tomatoes
- 20 fluid ounces tomato juice

## Instructions

1. Heat the oil in a large stock pot over medium heat.
2. Crumble the beef and pork into the pot.
3. Cook, stirring frequently, until no longer pink.
4. Drain off the grease, leaving just a thin layer.
5. Cook the onion, garlic, bell pepper, and serrano pepper  in the remaining oil until the onions soften, about 5 minutes.
6. Add the lime juice, chili powder, cayenne pepper, black pepper, salt, and 1 1/2 bottles of dark beer.
7. Slowly add the last 1/2 bottle of beer while stirring.
8. Stir in the tomatoes and tomato juice.
9. Reduce heat to low.
10. Simmer 1 hour.
