## Ingredients

- 1 cup water
- 1 ½ teaspoons vegetable oil
- 1 ½ teaspoons honey
- ½ teaspoon lecithin
- 3 cups white spelt flour
- 3 tablespoons dry milk powder
- 1 ½ teaspoons salt
- 2 teaspoons active dry yeast

## Instructions

1. Place ingredients in order suggested by manufacturer.
2. Bake on normal or basic cycle.
