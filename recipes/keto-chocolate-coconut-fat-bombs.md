## Ingredients

- 1 cup shredded unsweetened coconut
- 2 scoops pure stevia powder
- 3 tablespoons butter, softened
- 3 tablespoons coconut oil, softened
- 2 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa powder, or more to taste
- 1 tablespoon all-purpose blend stevia sweetener (such as Pyure®)
- 5 drops liquid sweetener (such as SucraDrops®)
- 20  roasted almonds

## Instructions

1. Line a small baking sheet with parchment paper.
2. Combine coconut, stevia powder, butter, coconut oil, cocoa powder, stevia blend, and liquid sweetener in a bowl; mix well.
3. Spread out on the prepared baking sheet.
4. Scatter almonds on top and press in.
5. Refrigerate until set, about 30 minutes.
6. Remove from fridge and cut into pieces.
