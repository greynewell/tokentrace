## Ingredients

- ⅔ cup grated carrots
- ¼ cup chopped green bell pepper
- ¼ cup minced onion
- ¼ cup chopped cucumber
- ¼ cup chopped celery
- 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- 2 tablespoons lemon juice
- 1 ½ tablespoons mayonnaise

## Instructions

1. Combine grated carrots, chopped green bell pepper, minced onion, chopped cucumber, and chopped celery in a bowl.
2. Mix well.
3. Combine softened cream cheese, salt, lemon juice, and mayonnaise in a separate bowl and beat until fluffy.
4. Fold vegetable mixture into cream cheese mixture and mix well.
5. Cover and refrigerate at least 1 hour before spreading.
