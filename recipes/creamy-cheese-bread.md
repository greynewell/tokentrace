## Ingredients

- ½ cup sour cream
- ½ cup mayonnaise
- 1 cup shredded Swiss cheese
- 1 (.7 ounce) package dry Italian-style salad dressing mix
- 1 (1 pound) loaf French bread, sliced

## Instructions

1. Preheat the broiler.
2. In a medium bowl, mix sour cream, mayonnaise, Swiss cheese and dry Italian-style salad dressing mix.
3. Spread the mixture generously on the French bread slices.
4. Arrange slices in a single layer on a large baking sheet.
5. Broil in the preheated oven 3 to 5 minutes, or until cheese is melted and bread is lightly browned.
