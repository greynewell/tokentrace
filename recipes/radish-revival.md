## Ingredients

- 1 bunch radishes, trimmed
- 1 teaspoon salt
- ¼ teaspoon ground mixed peppercorns
- 1 sprig fresh cilantro, for garnish  (Optional)

## Instructions

1. Place the radishes in a bowl, and sprinkle with salt and peppercorns.
2. Toss until evenly mixed.
3. Serve garnished with a sprig of cilantro.
