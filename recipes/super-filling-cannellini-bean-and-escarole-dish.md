## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- ½  onion, chopped
- 2 cloves garlic, crushed
- 1 (15 ounce) can cannellini beans with liquid
- 2 (14.5 ounce) cans diced tomatoes, drained
- 1 cup shredded carrots
- ¾ pound cooked, shredded chicken
- 1 (10 ounce) package frozen chopped spinach, thawed and drained
- 4 cups chopped escarole
- ½ cup shredded Pecorino-Romano cheese, or to taste

## Instructions

1. Heat olive oil in a large saucepan over medium heat, and cook and stir onion and garlic until the garlic turns golden brown and smells toasty, about 6 minutes.
2. Do not let garlic burn.
3. Pour in the cannellini beans, diced tomatoes, and carrots, cover, and bring to a boil.
4. Reduce heat to a simmer, uncover the pot, and allow the mixture to simmer until slightly thickened, 5 to 8 more minutes.
5. Stir in the chicken and spinach, bring the mixture back to a boil, and mix in the escarole.
6. Simmer until almost all the liquid is gone, about 10 minutes; turn off the heat, sprinkle with cheese, and cover; allow to stand for another 5 to 10 minutes to melt cheese.
