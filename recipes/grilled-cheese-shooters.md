## Ingredients

- 2 (18.75 ounce) cans tomato bisque (such as Campbell's®)
- 2 tablespoons butter, softened
- 4 slices sourdough bread
- 4 slices aged Cheddar cheese
- toothpicks

## Instructions

1. Pour 1 can tomato bisque into a microwave-safe bowl.
2. Cover and cook in the microwave until hot, 2 1/2 to 3 minutes.
3. Repeat with the remaining bisque.
4. Butter 1 side of each slice of bread.
5. Place 2 slices of Cheddar cheese on the unbuttered sides of 2 slices.
6. Top with the other 2 slices, buttered-side up, to make the sandwiches.
7. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat.
8. Add sandwiches.
9. Heat until toasted and golden on 1 side, 2 to 3 minutes.
10. Flip and toast the other side, about 2 minutes more.
11. Cut off crusts, if desired.
12. Slice sandwiches into 1 1/2-inch squares, 18 squares in total.
13. Thread a toothpick through each square.
14. Pour heated soup into heat-proof shot glasses and top with the grilled cheese squares.
