## Ingredients

- 1 (14 ounce) can sweetened condensed milk
- ⅔ cup chocolate syrup
- 2 cups heavy cream

## Instructions

1. Line a 9x5 inch loaf pan with aluminum foil.
2. In a large bowl, stir together condensed milk and chocolate syrup until color is even.
3. In a separate bowl, whip cream until stiff peaks form.
4. Fold cream into chocolate mixture and pour all into prepared pan.
5. Cover and freeze 6 hours, until firm.
