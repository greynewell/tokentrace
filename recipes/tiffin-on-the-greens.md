## Ingredients

- 1 (6 inch) whole wheat sandwich round, split
- 2 thin slices  Black Forest ham, or to taste
- ¼ cup baby spinach, or to taste
- 1 cup mixed salad greens, or to taste
- 2 tablespoons salad dressing, or to taste
- 2 tablespoons cream cheese, softened
- 2 tablespoons sour cream
- 2 teaspoons white sugar
- ⅛ teaspoon vanilla extract
- 1 cup seedless green grapes
- 1 tablespoon chopped pecans
- 1 teaspoon brown sugar

## Instructions

1. Fill sandwich round with ham and spinach.
2. Mix salad greens and salad dressing together in a small bowl.
3. Mix cream cheese, sour cream, white sugar, and vanilla extract together in a small bowl. Add grapes; toss until evenly coated. Sprinkle pecans and brown sugar on top; toss again to combine.
4. Assemble tiffin or bento box by placing sandwich, green salad, and grape salad into the compartments.
