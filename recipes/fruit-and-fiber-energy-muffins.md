## Ingredients

- cooking spray
- 3 cups rolled oats
- 2 cups milk
- 2 cups chopped apples
- 1 ¼ cups raisins
- 1 cup chopped walnuts
- ¾ cup flaxseed meal
- 2  eggs
- 1 tablespoon ground cinnamon  (Optional)
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 1 teaspoon salt

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Grease a muffin tin with cooking spray.
3. Mix oats, milk, apples, raisins, walnuts, flaxseed meal, eggs, cinnamon, baking powder, and salt together in a large bowl.
4. Spoon mixture into the prepared muffin tin.
5. Bake in the preheated oven until edges are crisp and center is moist, 50 to 60 minutes.
