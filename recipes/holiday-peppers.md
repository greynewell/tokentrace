## Ingredients

- 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
- 1  tomato, diced
- 1 tablespoon chopped pimento pepper
- 1 tablespoon minced garlic
- 1 tablespoon minced fresh chives
- 1 tablespoon minced fresh parsley
- 1 tablespoon dried basil
- 1 large green bell pepper, top and seeds removed
- 1 large red bell pepper, top and seeds removed

## Instructions

1. In a bowl, mix the cream cheese, tomato, pimento, garlic, chives, parsley, and basil.
2. Fill the green bell pepper and red bell pepper with equal amounts of the mixture.
3. Slice each pepper into 4 pieces.
4. Cover, and refrigerate at least 2 hours before serving.
