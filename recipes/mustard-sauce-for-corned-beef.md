## Ingredients

- 1 ½ tablespoons salted butter
- 1 large egg
- ⅓ cup brown sugar
- ¼ cup white sugar
- ¼ cup prepared yellow mustard
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- ⅛ teaspoon ground black pepper
- ¾ cup cider vinegar

## Instructions

1. Melt butter in a saucepan over low heat; remove from heat.
2. Whisk egg, brown sugar, white sugar, mustard, salt, and pepper together in a small bowl. Whisk in vinegar. Stir mixture into the melted butter.
3. Stir sauce over medium heat until boiling. Reduce heat and simmer for 3 minutes longer.
