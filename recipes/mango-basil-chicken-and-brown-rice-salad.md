## Ingredients

- ⅓ cup balsamic vinaigrette dressing
- 2 tablespoons chopped fresh basil leaves
- 2 cups cooked chicken, cooled and coarsely chopped
- 1 ½ cups cooked Minute® Brown Rice, cooled
- 1 cup fresh mango, peeled and chopped
- 1 cup grape or cherry tomatoes, halved
- 4  large green lettuce leaves, washed and dried
- ⅓ cup crumbled goat cheese

## Instructions

1. In bottom of large bowl, combine bottled dressing and basil.
2. Add chicken, brown rice, mango, and tomatoes.
3. Toss well to combine.
4. To serve: Place 1 lettuce leaf onto each of 4 serving plates.
5. Divide salad mixture evenly and place into center of lettuce leaves.
6. Garnish top of each serving with an equal amount of crumbled goat cheese.
