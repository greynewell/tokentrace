## Ingredients

- 1 pound fresh crab meat
- 1  egg
- 2 tablespoons mayonnaise
- ½ teaspoon ground mustard
- 1 dash Worcestershire sauce
- salt and pepper to taste
- 4 ounces buttery round crackers, crushed
- 2 cups oil for frying, or as needed

## Instructions

1. In a medium-size mixing bowl, combine crabmeat, egg, mayonnaise, mustard powder, Worcestershire sauce, and salt and pepper to taste.
2. In a large skillet, heat 1/4 inch of vegetable oil to a high heat.
3. Roll heaping spoonfuls on the crab mixture in the crackers, then place the ball in the hot oil.
4. Flatten lightly with a spatula so that the crab cake is 1/2 inch thick.
5. Fry the crab cakes, flipping to ensure both sides get browned.
