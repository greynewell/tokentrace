## Ingredients

- 1 pound 93% lean ground turkey
- 1 large onion, diced
- 1 (12 fluid ounce) can or bottle beer
- 1 (1.25 ounce) package reduced-sodium taco seasoning mix
- 1 (28 ounce) can diced tomatoes
- 1 (15 ounce) can petite diced tomatoes
- 1 (15 ounce) can black beans
- 1 (15 ounce) can dark red kidney beans
- 1 cup fire-roasted corn
- 1 (4 ounce) can diced green chiles

## Instructions

1. Heat a large pot over medium-high heat.
2. Cook and ground turkey in the hot skillet until browned and crumbly, 5 to 7 minutes.
3. Drain and discard grease.
4. Add onion to the skillet and cook and stir over medium heat until translucent, about 5 minutes.
5. Add beer and  , stirring until beer cooks down.
6. Stir in both cans of diced tomatoes, black beans, kidney beans, corn, and chiles.
7. Simmer over medium-low heat for 1 hour.
