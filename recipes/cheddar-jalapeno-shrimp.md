## Ingredients

- 2 pounds large shrimp, peeled and deveined and butterflied
- 8 ounces sharp Cheddar cheese, cut into strips
- 3  fresh jalapeno peppers, seeded
- 1 teaspoon garlic powder
- 1 teaspoon Old Bay Seasoning TM, or to taste
- 2 pounds thick cut bacon

## Instructions

1. Cut the jalapeno peppers into thin strips.
2. Place a strip or two of jalapeno pepper and cheddar cheese inside each shrimp.
3. Sprinkle the stuffed shrimp with garlic powder and   Seasoning TM.
4. Wrap each shrimp with a slice of bacon and secure with a toothpick.
5. Thread shrimp onto skewers.
6. Grill over medium high heat for 10 to 15 minutes, or until firm.
