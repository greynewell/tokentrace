## Ingredients

- 4 cups water
- 1 cube vegetable bouillon, or more to taste
- ¾ cup red lentils
- 2  bay leaves
- ½ pound carrots, chopped
- ¼ pound fresh mushrooms, finely chopped
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 1  onion, chopped
- 2 cloves garlic, minced, or more to taste
- ¼ pound hemp seeds
- 1 bunch cilantro, chopped

## Instructions

1. Bring water to a boil in a large pot and add vegetable bouillon to taste.
2. Add lentils and bay leaves and simmer for 15 minutes.
3. Add carrots, mushrooms, salt, and pepper; continue to simmer.
4. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium-high heat; saute onion until soft, about 5 minutes.
5. Add garlic and saute until fragrant, 1 to 2 minutes.
6. Stir onion mixture into the soup.
7. Cook and stir hemp seeds in a separate skillet over medium-high heat until toasted and fragrant, 2 to 3 minutes.
8. Stir toasted hemp seeds into soup.
9. Add cilantro and cook 5 minutes more.
