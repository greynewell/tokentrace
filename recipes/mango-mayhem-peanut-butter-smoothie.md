## Ingredients

- ½ cup skim milk
- ½ cup plain low fat Greek-style yogurt
- 2 tablespoons KRAFT Smooth Peanut Butter
- 1 tablespoon honey
- ½ cup frozen chopped mango
- 4  ice cubes

## Instructions

1. Blend all ingredients except ice in blender until smooth.
2. Add ice; blend until thickened.
3. Serve immediately.
