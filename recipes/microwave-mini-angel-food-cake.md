## Ingredients

- 3 tablespoons angel food cake mix
- 2 teaspoons water
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

## Instructions

1. Put cake mix, water, and vanilla in a microwave-safe mug and stir until well combined.
2. Microwave on high for 1 minute.
