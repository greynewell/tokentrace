## Ingredients

- 5 slices hard salami
- 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened, or as needed
- 5  green onions, trimmed

## Instructions

1. Lay salami slices on a plate and spread cream cheese on each.
2. Place 1 green onion at the end of each piece of salami and roll salami around onion; secure with a toothpick.
