## Ingredients

- 1 (6 ounce) can crabmeat, drained and flaked
- 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
- 1 tablespoon prepared horseradish
- 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce

## Instructions

1. In a medium bowl, mix together crabmeat, cream cheese, horseradish and Worcestershire sauce.
2. Microwave the mixture on high 3 to 5 minutes, or until warm and bubbly.
3. Stir well before serving.
