## Ingredients

- ½  onion, minced
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- ¾ cup bourbon whiskey
- ½ teaspoon ground black pepper
- ½ tablespoon salt
- 2 cups ketchup
- ¼ cup tomato paste
- ⅓ cup cider vinegar
- 2 tablespoons liquid smoke flavoring
- ¼ cup Worcestershire sauce
- ½ cup packed brown sugar
- ⅓ teaspoon hot pepper sauce, or to taste

## Instructions

1. Combine whiskey, garlic, and onion in a large skillet over medium heat; simmer until onion is translucent, about 10 minutes.
2. Stir in ketchup, brown sugar, vinegar, tomato paste, Worcestershire sauce, liquid smoke, salt, black pepper, and hot pepper sauce; bring to a boil, reduce heat to medium-low, and simmer for 20 minutes. Run sauce through a strainer if you prefer a smooth sauce.
