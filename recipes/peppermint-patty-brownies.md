## Ingredients

- 1 ½ cups margarine
- 3 cups white sugar
- 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
- 5  eggs
- 2 cups all-purpose flour
- 1 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 24  small peppermint patties

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 9x13-inch baking pan.
2. Mix margarine, sugar, and vanilla in a large bowl. Beat in eggs until well-blended. Stir in flour, cocoa, baking powder, and salt; blend well. Set aside 2 cups batter.
3. Spread remaining batter in the prepared pan. Arrange peppermint patties in a single layer over batter about 1/2 inch apart. Spread reserved 2 cups batter over patties.
4. Bake until brownies begin to pull away from the sides of the pan, 50 to 55 minutes. Cool completely in the pan on a wire rack, then cut into 24 brownies.
