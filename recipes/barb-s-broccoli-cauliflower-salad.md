## Ingredients

- 12 slices bacon
- 1 head fresh broccoli, diced
- 1 head cauliflower, chopped
- ½  red onion, diced
- ¾ cup sunflower seeds
- 1 cup creamy salad dressing
- 1 ½ tablespoons white wine vinegar
- ¼ cup white sugar

## Instructions

1. Place bacon in a large skillet and cook over medium-high heat, turning occasionally, until evenly browned, about 10 minutes. Drain bacon slices on paper towels; crumble.
2. Combine bacon, cauliflower, broccoli, sunflower seeds, and onion in a large bowl.
3. Whisk salad dressing, sugar, and vinegar together until blended. Pour over cauliflower mixture and toss to coat. Refrigerate and allow to chill before serving.
